Dahakara is a music project from Istanbul. Starting out as an experimental black metal act that incorporated atmospheric and industrial elements, Dahakara reinvented itself as a darkwave/electronic rock project with the release of 2018's Other Rooms. Dahakara released the follow-up albums, Happiness Forgets and  Hell is in Hello in 2019 and 2020, respectively.
