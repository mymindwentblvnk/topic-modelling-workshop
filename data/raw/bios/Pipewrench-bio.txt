Pipewrench formed in 2005, when three brothers started playing together in a basement in the suburbs of Pittsburgh, PA. By adding a long time friend to play bass they became one of the most original metal bands to come out of the area. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pipewrench">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
