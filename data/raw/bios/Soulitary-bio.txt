Turkish crossover band that was formed by Goktug Sahin(Vocals) and Sertac Uslu(Guitars)in 1998 and released a demo named Mortelies in the year 2004 containing 6 songs(Down, Serenade of War, Each Time, Hellish Torture, Warning).They've performed in many national festivals and also won the 2nd place in Roxy Rock Music competition and 3rd place in Turkey Jack Daniel's Music Rock competition.
They are still performing and expected to release an album in the year 2007.
