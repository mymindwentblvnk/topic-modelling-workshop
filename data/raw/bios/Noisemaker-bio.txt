NOISEMAKER is an alternative nu-metal band from Sapporo. Since the day of their formation, the band has been looking for a new sound, mixing rock, metal and hip-hop. Their first success was reached with the release of their first full album “The 6 matters of the 6″ in 2009, and shortly after, the band went on a country wide tour. NOISEMAKER are AG (vocal), HIDE (guitar), YU-KI (bass) and UTA (drums).
