Liber Necris is a British blackened death metal band from Leeds, Yorkshire, which formed in 2010. It has released two EPs since its inception: "The Immutable Aversion" in 2011, and "Negative Creator" through Venn Records in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Liber+Necris">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
