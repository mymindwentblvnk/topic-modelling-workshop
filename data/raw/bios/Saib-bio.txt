1. Casablanca-based beat-maker and guitarist. He makes lo-fi instrumental hip-hop with influences from jazz to African music.

2. saiB is a VOCALOID rock producer. His songs are mostly of the alternative rock genre. His songs often contain a thunderous, distorted guitar, along with the lyrics, which show a unique view of the world.
His first posted song was "Kaijuu Gomez no Yuuutsu" which was deleted, and a new verison uploaded, later. His "Bye-bee Baby Sayounara" first reached the VOCALOID Hall of Fame.
On October 2010 he started a YouTube account under the name Johnny&The Ickies, where he uploaded some original songs YouTube exclusively. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Saib">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
