DRESTARATA is a Pagan Black Metal band from Indonesia, that initial concept is a lifting mythology Javanese nuances of the story of Java.

Band: DRESTARATA
Genre(s): Pagan Black Metal
Lyrical theme(s): Javanese mythology
Origin: Pasuruan, Indonesia
Formed in: Maret, 2007
Current label: Budak Hitam corp
