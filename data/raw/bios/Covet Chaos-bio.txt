Covet Chaos are a metal band based in St Helens and are now a two-piece band. The music used to be thrash and has now evolved into a musical collaberation between two people.

Formed in June 2007 as Left-Wing, a Sepultura cover band. After a few line-up changes the band changed they're name to Covet Chaos, in September 2007, and began writing origional material.

Recording started for the band's debut album in December 2008 - January 2009 and is now available to download from the Myspace.

A second album is currently being worked on and will be released mid 2010. This album will showcase the bands darker, sludgier and musical elements. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Covet+Chaos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
