Haruka Kamiyama is the mastermind behind this brutal death metal project.  He started it in Tokyo, Japan in 2008.  He started off with a couple demos.  The first one is simply called First Demo and that was released in 2009.  A couple of years later, the second demo simply called Second Demo was released.  In 2012, he decided to compile both demos and released them.  It was entitled Medic Vomiting Pus.  In the year 2014, he released a full-length album entitled Thoracoabdominal Viscerectomy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Medic+Vomiting+Pus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
