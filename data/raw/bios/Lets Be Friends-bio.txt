Lets Be Friends is a EDM production duo based in UK consisting of John Ford (aka JOYRYDE) and Oren Emanuel. 
LBF released their debut EP named "EP1" in  2013. The band got support from Rob Swire and opened up for Knife Party in the "Haunted House" world tour. During 2013-2015 LBF performed 80 shows a year including the top electronic events such as MysteryLand and TomorrowWorld.
