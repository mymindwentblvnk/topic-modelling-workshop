Unisonic is a German hard rock band created by Michael Kiske (ex-Helloween) with Dennis Ward (bass) and Kosta Zafiriou (drums) of Germany's Pink Cream 69 alongside Swiss guitarist Mandy Meyer who is known for his work with Asia, Gotthard and Krokus. In 2011  Kai Hansen (ex-Helloween), the lead vocalist and guitarist of Gamma Ray joined the band as the second guitarist. Unisonic then proceeded to tour before releasing their EP Ignition on January 27th 2012. On March 2012, Unisonic released their debut self-titled album Unisonic.

A new album is planned for 2014.
