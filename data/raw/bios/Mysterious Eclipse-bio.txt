Mysterious Eclipse is band from Slovakia formed in february 1996 by Michal Hrabovský and Martin Bradáč. First they play melodic north death metal. Later it was death/black metal. They released 3 albums: „Darkness In The Light Of Knowledge“ in 1998, „Deathwings“ in 1999 and in 2005 „ ...And The Long Night Fell, No Morning Followed“
