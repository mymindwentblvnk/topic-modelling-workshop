Sharky Sharky is a power metal / rock band that formed in Aberdeen, Scotland in the year 2013.  It is a side project by members of the band Ascension.  The goal of this side project is to introduce kids to music.  The band features Sharky (Fraser Edwards) on guitar and Baxter (Richard Carnie) on vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sharky+Sharky">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
