There are 3 bands called Nefas exist:

1) Technical death metal band from Italy.

2) Nefas was an all-female band from Cosenza, Calabria. Their style is a unique mix varying from hard rock to epic sounds; themes are often taken from classical literature and myths, like in Vindicta deae. Nefas were: Serena Gambaro - guitar and vocals; Diana Corniola - guitar; Michela De Marco - bass guitar; Annachiara Mele - drums.
