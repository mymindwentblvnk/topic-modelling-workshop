Toxoplasma is a German punk band. The band was founded in the 1980 but still exists today.
Toxoplasma are a typical representative of "Deutschpunk". The music is quite simple, the lyrics concern both political and "funny" issues. Because of their hardcore debut album Toxoplasma and a tour with Bad Brains they got very popular in German punk scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toxoplasma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
