Dutch black metal band consisting of Havoque (from Allfather, ex-Fluisterwoud, ex-Galgeras) on all instruments. 

Heidenland is Dutch for "Heathenland". 

Under the name of Heidenland only one demo is really released; "Triomftocht voor de glorie van Wodan" (Procession for the glory of Wotan) in 1998. The 2000 single "Kerkenbrand Commando" (Churchburn Commando) was never released.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Heidenland">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
