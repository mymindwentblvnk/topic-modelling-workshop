Gunfire is an Italian Heavy Metal band formed in 1981 and released a Demo and the EP Gunfire in 1984.  Gunfire broke up in 1986 and reformed in 2001. They have since released a demo and 2 albums. The newest album Age of Supremacy launched in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gunfire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
