Marasmus is a death metal band from Kansas City, MO featuring former members of Obsidian Shore, Die Section Ate, and The Searing Hour. Formed in early 2008, Marasmus is currently in the process of simultaneously writing new material and playing shows. 

Influences include such death metal and grindcore bands as Origin, Nile, Cannibal Corpse, Vital Remains, Hate Eternal, Pig Destroyer, and Napalm Death.

Members:
Devon Ferrara - Vocals
Tommy White - Guitar
Brandon Rockhold - Bass
