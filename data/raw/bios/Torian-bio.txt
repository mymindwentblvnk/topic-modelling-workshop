Torian is an German Power metal band from the town Paderborn. The started in 2003. Their music is influenced by thrash bands, but also the more traditional 80's metal sound. The songs cover a wide variety of styles, from epic songs to classic metal anthems. In October 2003 they released their first musical attack with a 3 track demo CD "Into the Winter: . In august of the same year they signed a contract with the Italian label Underground Symphony Records. In january 2005 they released their debut cd Dreams under Ice.
