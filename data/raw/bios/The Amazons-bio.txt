There are several the bands with the name The Amazons

1. The Amazons are a four piece alternative rock band from Reading, UK. Their debut EP 'Don't You Wanna' was released in October 2015.

2. The Amazons (formed in Michigan, 2002) is an American avant-garde band, who has the incredible goal of changing the face of modern music. They released their first album, "The Wrong Paths, New & Terrible" in early 2006, and are currently working on a much-anticipated follow-up, entitled "Jazz Hand Mary".
The band is a trio of girls (Zevva, Xavi, & Qug), and they have had absolutely no line-up changes. The group hopes to continue making records long into the future.
