Crustcore from Hannover, Germany. The band was founded in 1998 by three members of the hardcore punk band Recharge and a new shouter.

In January 2000 they made a split EP with Wolfbrigade. In March 2001 they recorded their first LP "Ultima Ratio" in Sweden with Mieszko Talarczyk (Nasum) on the mixer and released it in August 2002.

In September 2004 they released a 12" EP "Music From An Extreme Sick World" with six tracks. The CD version came out in 2005 with 5 bonus tracks.
