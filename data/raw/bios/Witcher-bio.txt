Witcher is a Hungarian black metal band from Szombathely, featuring members from Vrag and Trollheimen. 

Roland Neubauer - drum programming, guitars, vocals

Karola Gere - keyboards

The band has so far released two demos, 'Úttalan Utakon (2010)' and 'Boszorkánytánc (2011)' <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Witcher">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
