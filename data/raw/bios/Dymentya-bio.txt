Dymentya is a heavy metal band from Las Vegas, Nevada, formed in 2007.

Their current lineup:

Chris Garcia - Lead/Rhythm Guitar

Ali Annihilator - Lead/Rhythm Guitar

Anthony Garcia - Bass Guitar


Dymentya disbanded in late 2007 because of personal and availabilty issues within the band, and reformed in early 2008. As of '08, they are looking for a new vocalist and drummer.
