When four guys from school decided to form a band they took the name VENEREA, venerea started out as Venereal Disease back in 1991.
The whole thing started in the small westcoastal town of Falkenberg, Sweden.
Daniel "Dana" Johansson (bass), Johan "Bengan" Bengtsson (vocals/guitar), Mattias "Talle" Toldbod (drums), & Stefan Stenslund (guitar)
