Kan was born in kobe, Japan in 1983 and started to play the piano at 1995. He studied piano & composition at the Berklee College of Music in Boston, USA. His own jazz group performed at Monterey Jazz Festival in 2003 & 2004. Kan has been working as pianist, composer, arranger, producer & DJ around Tokyo since 2006. He appeared on many labels such as Ubeat records, Soulunsigned, Origami Productions, Inter Music, Orange Voice Factory and Mercedes Benz mix tape. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KAN+SANO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
