Charred Walls of the Damned is a power metal band comprising of some of the most talented musicians in the world of metal, and features legendary Judas Priest and Iced Earth singer "Ripper" Owens and bandleader Richard Christy (Death, Iced Earth) on Drums.

The band is filled out by Steve DiGiorgio on bass (Iced Earth, Death as well as thrashers Sadus and Testament) and Jason Suecof (Capharnaum but better known as a producer for such acts as Trivium, Devildriver and Chimaira). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Charred+Walls+Of+The+Damned">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
