Venus Victrix is a psychedelic / stoner / occult rock band from Texas, United States. In the tradition of the most ancient mystery cults, Venus Victrix intends to create a communion with the energy of the immaterial via the alchemical power of their rituals. Debut album titled "Venus Victrix I" was  released in April 2014.
