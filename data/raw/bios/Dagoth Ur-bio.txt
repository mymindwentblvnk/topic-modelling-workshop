Dagoth Ur refers to multiple artists.

Dagoth Ur (formerly known as Winterfell), was the Croatian ambient project of Rugiewit (of Slavogorje), inspired by nature / industry, fiction / reality, modern / historical themes - same as the night and day. Due to the lack of time, the project remains only as a guest in Russian band Arisk Priest.

Dagoth Ur is a fictional character from The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind by Bethesda Softworks. The character's name is used as a pseudonym by mashup artist Young Scrolls. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dagoth+Ur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
