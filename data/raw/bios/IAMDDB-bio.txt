IAMDDB, born in March 5, 1996 as Diana De Brito, is a rapper from Manchester, UK. She mixes urban jazz and soul with hip-hop and r&b. She released five studio projects: Waeveybby, Vol. 1, Vibe, Volume 2., Hoodrich Vol. 3, Flightmode Vol. 4 and Swervvvvv.5. She also made collaborations, such as Leaned Out with Inka and Mira Mira with Diplo, beyond her participations on Jagged Tooth Crook by The Mouse Outfit and on Codeine Bluez by Blind MIC.
