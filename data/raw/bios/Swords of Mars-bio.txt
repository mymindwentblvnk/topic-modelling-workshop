Swords of Mars are a four-rider strong stoner/doom/metal thunder rising out of Helsinki, Finland, formerly known as Erotic Support.

Forged in 2001, first churning out balls-out stoner rock the Swords have evolved gradually slowing down with each release.

Line-up:
Ville - vocals & guitar
Tero - guitar
Mikko - bass
Jussi - drums
