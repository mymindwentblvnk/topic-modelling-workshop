Depressive Black Metal from Auckland, New Zealand, one man project of Mort Formed in 2008 and ended in 2010 . The industrious Mort also cites association with Exiled From Light, Schizophrenia, Funereal, Balthazar, Serpenthrone, Nystagmus, Einsamkeit, Beyond Light and Winds Of Sorrow.

The self-titled debut album was released in 2009 through Self Mutilation Services.
