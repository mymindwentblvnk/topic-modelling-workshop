ZHRINE are a black/death metal band from Kópavogur, Iceland that formed in 2008. They released their debut full-length album 'Unortheta' on April 8, 2016 through Season of Mist Records. Previously known as Gone Postal and Shrine. Ævar (bass), Tumi (drums), Nökkvi (guitars), Þorbjörn (guitar, vocals). In September 2016, Stefán Ari Stefánsson left to dedicate himself to his other band The Vintage Caravan. He was replaced on drums by Tumi Snær Gíslason, formerly of Angist and Ophidian I. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ZHRINE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
