Jamaram is a band from Munich that mixes Reggae, Dub, Ska, Pop, Balkan Beats and Afrobeat. The band has been founded in the year 2000 by vocalist Tomo Lugo, drummer Max Alberti and guitarist Sam Hopf. At the moment they consist of eight members.
