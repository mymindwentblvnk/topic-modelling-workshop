Hiss from the Moat are an Italian band whose debut album Misanthropy was released in fall 2013 in digital form by Nuclear Blast, and more recently in physical form by Lacerated Enemy Records. The band includes drummer James Payne, who is also now in Hour of Penance, guitarist Giacomo Jack Poli, and bass player Carlo Cremascoli, and the album includes appearances by some impressive guests - with vocals provided by Paolo Pieri (Aborym, Hour of Penance), additional vocals on one track by Tommaso Ricardi (Fleshgod Apocalypse), and a lead-guitar contribution on another track by Ryan Knight (The Black Dahlia Murder).
