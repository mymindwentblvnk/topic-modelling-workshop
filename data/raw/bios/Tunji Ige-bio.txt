19-year old Philadelphia native Tunji Ige is somewhere in a basement in Pennsylvania writing songs, and amazing ones at that. A West Chester University student, Tunji dropped his first mixtape, "The Love Project", on December 3, 2014. The tape was to released to lots of hype and positive reviews. Tunji released his 1st EP, "Missed Calls", on March 30, 2016. The album went on to receive an 85 from HNHH and a 6.8 from Pigeons and Planes. On July 8 of the same year he released a mixtape for his birthday, called "King of July".
