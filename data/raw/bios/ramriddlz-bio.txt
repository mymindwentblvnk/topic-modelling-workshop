Ramy Abdel-Rahman, also known by his stage name Ramriddlz, is a Canadian singer and rapper, from Mississauga, Ontario. He dropped out of Ryerson University, looking to pursue his music career. He recently released his studio album "Ramreaper" on April 5th, 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ramriddlz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
