Black metal band from Bordeaux, France formed in 2001.
Discography includes their four-track demo Conqueror, Conqueror...Destroyer (2002), Codex 666 - Infernal Legions Strike (2004), Ten Eyed Nemesis (2005), compilation Spawn From The Abyss (2006), Kinetic Zero (2007), Fuck God-Disease Process (2009), No God, No Satan (2010).
