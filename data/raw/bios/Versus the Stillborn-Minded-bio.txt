VERSUS THE STILLBORN - MINDED was originally formed on dec 1st, 2000 after a local metal show, in a very dazed mood, by Boris Breuer, Torsten Trautwein and Robin Pürschel. after several jam sessions, Florian Satt joined the band as guitar player. In this line-up the band played a few gigs. One year later, Jens Partheymüller joined the band as second guitarplayer, he was the missing link to complete the heavy sound of VERSUS THE STILLBORN - MINDED.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Versus+the+Stillborn-Minded">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
