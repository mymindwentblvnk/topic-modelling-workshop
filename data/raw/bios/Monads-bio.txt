Monads, a crushing funeral death/doom metal band from Belgium consisting of:

R.Polon - Vocals
H.Cools - Guitars
F.Breulet - Guitars
J.Frederix - Bass
D.Degrieck - Drums

The term “Monad” is used to describe the idea of a person as a focal point of consciousness within a sea of greater consciousness, and it is believed that all things that can be considered a unity unto themselves constitute a monad as well.
