Belfast's and Northern Ireland's resident sludge metal kings, Slomatics, formed from parts of numerous local bands including the Naut quickly got to work to play their own brand of dual-guitar doom metal.  They have already released a few CDs, including their debut full-length album Flooding the Weir, of which a follow up is due soon
