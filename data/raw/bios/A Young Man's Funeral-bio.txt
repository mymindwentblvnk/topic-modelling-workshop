A Young Man's Funeral is a doom metal band from Moscow, Russia. The original line-up includes A.S. and E.S., both of which are also members of Who Dies in Siberian Slush. YMF released three albums between 2011 and 2014 and, as of now, are signed to Fono Ltd..
