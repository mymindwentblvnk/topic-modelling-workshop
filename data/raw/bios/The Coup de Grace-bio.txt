Melodic thrash metal band from Minneapolis, whose 1990 eponymous debut was co-produced by Dave Pirner of Soul Asylum. Vocalist/guitarist James Mecherie (aka Jimmy Coup) went on to tour & record with Andrew W.K. from 2000 to 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Coup+de+Grace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
