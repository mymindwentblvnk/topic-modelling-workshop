Disclarity is a metal band from Lahti, Finland. Band was founded in 2011. 
Disclarity has five members.

Vocals - Henri Volanen
Guitar  - Nino Volanen
Guitar  - Markus Saarikivi
Bass    - Kalle Niskanen
Drums - Jori Routo

The band's first release is published in the autumn of 2012 "Lost Integrity EP"
