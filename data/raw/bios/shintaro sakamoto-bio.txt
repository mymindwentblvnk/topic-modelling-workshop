Shintaro Sakamoto (坂本慎太郎) is a Japanese singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. He previously was a member of Yura Yura Teikoku before releasing his solo debut How to Live with a Phantom in 2012, followed by Let’s Dance Raw in 2014 and Love If Possible in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shintaro+Sakamoto">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
