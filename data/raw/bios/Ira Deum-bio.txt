A Bulgarian thrash metal band from Shumen. They formed in 1991 and released two albums entitled "Anger of the Gods" and "Reincarnation of the Immortal Evil" before splitting up in 1995. They reformed in 2011 to release their third and final album "Demoratoria Irae - Back in Dark Ages". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ira+Deum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
