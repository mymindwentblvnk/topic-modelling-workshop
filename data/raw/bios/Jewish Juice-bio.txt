Italian black metal band from Arezzo, Tuscany. Founded in 2006 and as of present day they have split-up. Their musical works are influenced by melodic black, even thrash and doom metal-like riffs. As the name suggests, Jewish Juice is considered to be part of underground NSBM scene.

Last known lineup:
▶ Beaker - bass & vocals
▶ Cernunnos - guitar & vocals
