Belgian stoner/metal band formed in january 2011. Its lineup consists of vocalist Serge Kraven (Leng Tch'e), guitarist Rudy “Pink” Dumont (ex-Stereo Chrome), bassist Alexis Nicolajeff (ex-Stereo Chrome), and drummer Jason C. (TCMFH).
