Gates Of Hopeless is a 5-piece member Metalcore/Deathcore/
Mosh/2-Step/Screamo -band from different parts of Osaka JP. 
The band was founded in the beginning of 2004. And the first demo 
and 4 way split demo was recorded just after the second practice session.

Their debut EP "Beauty In The Eyes Of The Beholder" was released on 1st of march 2009. 

Members:

Ryuji  Hashimoto (Vocal)
Kazunori Sugiura (Guitar)
Toru Shingaki (Guitar)
Taturo Maeda (Bass)
Yuko Yamada (Drums)
