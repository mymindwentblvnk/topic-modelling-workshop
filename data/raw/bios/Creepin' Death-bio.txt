Creepin' Death was a thrash metal band, formed in 1984 in Italy  (Turin, Piedmont). The band recorded several demos before recieving a record deal with LM Records, resulting in the release of their first official album "Errare Humanum Est... Perseverare Diabolicum!!" in 1988, known for its strange sound and abstract cover art. Produced by CREEPIN' DEATH and Marziano Fontana.Mixed by Gigi Guerrieri at Miniree Studios in May 1988.
