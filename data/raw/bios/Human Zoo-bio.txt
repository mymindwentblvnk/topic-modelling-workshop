Human Zoo is a German band formed back in 2004. Their first album was "Precious Time" in a melodic hard rock style - or pure rock, as the group calls it. In 2007 came their second album "Over the Horizon". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Human+Zoo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
