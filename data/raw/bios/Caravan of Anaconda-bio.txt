Caravan Of Anaconda is the technical grindcore band from Bandung, Indonesia Formed in the mid of 2006 with 4 piece guys tried to bombing the scene then Ramdhan (Garpitus) fill the bass line until the end 2008 that he resigned, and then Yusuf changes Ramdhan's position until now .
In musicality spesifically they try to customized technically  fast music with experimental, discordant, and chromatic guitar work blend with lyrics that mainly tells about social issues, life, nature and others

And it's consists of 4 members they are :
Aldead : Guitar  
Yusuf : Bass
Curse : Vocal
XOXO : Drums
