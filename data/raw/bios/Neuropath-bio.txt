Neuropath were a brutal deathmetal band from Sydney, Australia, whose style was similar to Suffocation. They released two demo tapes - "Nefarious Vivisection" and "Desert of Excruciation" (Warhead Records)
They have long since disbanded. The lineup was:

Mark Wangmann - vocals
Daniel Stubbs - lead guitar
Anthony Watson - rythym guitar
Ali Chamas - bass
