Apocalyptic Fear was formed in 1991 by JF Houle

The band existed from May 1991 to September 1994. The band played many shows in Quebec and played with bands such as Obliveon, Dead Horse, Crypt Keeper, Exotoxic, Entowniser, Last Breath...

The band released a demo in 1992 "Decayed Existence"
and another one in 1994 "Dawn of the Ritual"
