Wild Pussy were a speed metal/thrash metal band from Cardiff, United Kingdom. They formed in 1986 and released 'Demo 1987' the next year. Their first official release was a four-track EP in 1989, called 'Mechanarchy'. They broke up in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wild+Pussy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
