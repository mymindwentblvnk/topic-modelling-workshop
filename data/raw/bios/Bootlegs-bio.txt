Thrash metal band from Reykjavik, Iceland, formed in 1986 and split-up in 1991. Their studio discography consists of "W.C. Monster" (1989) and a self-titled record (1990), both have been re-released in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bootlegs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
