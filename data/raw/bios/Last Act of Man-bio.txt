Last Act of Man was a sludge/doom metal band from California composed of members from In Disgust (which they took the name from). Last Act of Man has disbanded as of February 24th 2005 and their only release "The End Of Light" 2004 demo is available for free download at Oscillator Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Last+Act+of+Man">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
