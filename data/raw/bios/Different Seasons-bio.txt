Different Seasons was a death doom metal band from Russia formed in 1995. They released a full length "Bleeding Summer" in 1996 with Mortal Dreams Records and also had an unreleased CD "Innocent Motives" in 1996.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Different+Seasons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
