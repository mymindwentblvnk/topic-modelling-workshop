Non Exist is a Melodic Death Metal band formed by Johan Reinholdz (Guitars. Andromeda, Skyfire, Opus Atlantica), Matte Modin (Drums. Dark Funeral, Defleshed, ex-infernal) and the singer Johan Liiva (ex-Arch Enemy, Hearse, ex-Carnage). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nonexist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
