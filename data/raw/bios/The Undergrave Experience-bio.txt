Italian horror funeral doom project. Italy is known for good horror movie like Suspiria, Zombie, etc... As you know, those horror movie had good music by Goblin. Please imagine those music meets funeral doom, it's TUE music. Extremely slow rhythm, mixture of emotional melodies and eerie ones, deep growl represents depression of mankind... Basically, their music's main influence is Last XX... era Worship. They add some horror movie music elements like eerie piano sounds, dark mood etc...
