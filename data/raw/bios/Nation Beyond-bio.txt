Nation Beyond is a prog/power metal band form Sweden  and is built upon an idea of guitarist Jonas Karlgren (Debase) and bass player, Joakim Hedestedt (Face Down) 

Current line-up:
 
Jonas Karlgren: Guitar, Keyboards
Joakim Hedestedt: Bass
Nielz Lindstrom: Vocals
Johan Helgesson: Drums
Micko Twedberg: Guitar
Sara Heurlin: Guest Vocals
