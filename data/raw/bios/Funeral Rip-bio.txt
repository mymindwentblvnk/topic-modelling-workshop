Funeral RIP (established in 2003) are a Black Metal band hailing from France. Their line up is currently:-
Sabbat - Drums
Loke - Vocals
Zagan - Guitar
Satanik Blood - Bass

Satanik Blood replaced Ulfar (Hasserben, Tenebrare)
