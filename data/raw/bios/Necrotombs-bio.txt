Necrotombs is a one man death metal solo project by Xerberus (everything) that formed in Veneto, Italy and formed in the 2015.  Xerberus is the bassist of Stigmhate  The lyrics are about aliens, human abductions and space.  This project has released one demo and four full-length albums between 2017-2022.  These albums are found on Spotify.  Necrotombs is currently to Dark United Media.
