Somehow it's already September, and you've probably had your fill of "summer pop gems" and "warm weather hits". If that is the case - The Ukiah Drag are the breath of fresh air, chilled - and potentially fog filled - you're yearning for. The band is following up their 7" from earlier this year with a full-length, In the Reaper's Quarters, produced by Ben Greenberg (formerly of The Men). Their debut is a sinister, shadowy, psychedelia that's befitting a group of Floridians who grew up in a DIY scene and decided to record an album in a secularized temple off the banks of the Hudson.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Ukiah+Drag">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
