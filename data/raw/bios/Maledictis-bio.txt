Maledictis is a songwriting project which features rock, choral, and orchestral elements.  Music and lyrics are written by John Marszalkowski (Orphonic Orchestra, The Everyday Motive).  Some notable featured musicians include Mariangela Demurtas (Tristania, Ardours) and Brian Koenig (Luna Mortis, Lords of the Trident). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MALEDICTIS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
