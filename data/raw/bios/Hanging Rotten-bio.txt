Hanging Rotten are a grindcore/sludge band from North Carolina featuring members from Seven Foot Spleen. They released a self titled EP in 2001, a full-length album Your Cheatin' Heart, a split with Verge on Reason entitled At Odds, and contributed two songs to the "Transcendental Maggot" compilation. The time of date in which they broke up is unknown.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hanging+Rotten">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
