Catching Flies is a musician, DJ and record producer from London, England. He has self-released two EPs and done numerous remixes for artists like Wilkinson and Rainy Milo. His sound has been described as sitting on the "smooth, mellow side of electronic music" somewhere "between Flying Lotus and Bonobo" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Catching+Flies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
