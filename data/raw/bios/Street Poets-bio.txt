There are two groups named Street Poets.

1) Boston, MA, underground hip-hop group from the early 90's, made up of DIF Productions, Polecat, and Punch (or Punch Spiked w/ Poison). DJ Buzz and DJ Butterfingers may also be members of the group.

2) UK hip-hop duo consisting of Creative Minds and MC Messi, released two albums on the indie label Aim2Break Records in 2007 and 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Street+Poets">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
