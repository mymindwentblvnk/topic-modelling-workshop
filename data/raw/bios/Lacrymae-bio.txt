Lacrymae is a new progressive metal band hailing from Perth, Western Australia. Our style comes from a mixture of influences, varying from metal to classical music. With a lot of emphasis being placed on melody and vocal harmonies.

Agostino Piscitelli - Vocals / Orchestration / Keyboards
