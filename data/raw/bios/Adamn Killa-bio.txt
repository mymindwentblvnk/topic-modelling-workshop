Adamn Killa is a rapper from Chicago, IL. He was born on October 13, 1996. In 2015, he put out a 5-track EP called Libra Season. A year later he dropped his debut tape Back 2 Ballin. He has since released a number of tapes and has collaborated with artists such as Yung Lean and Bladee. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Adamn+Killa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
