Comprised of members of late 80's crossover greats Wehrmacht and Cryptic Slaughter, Spazztic Blurr were one of the early Earache Records trailblazers. Sounding like a bizarre mix of D.R.I. and Mr. Bungle, Spazztic played high speed, super technical songs that also managed to mock everyone from "hardcore" kids to metalheads. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spazztic+Blurr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
