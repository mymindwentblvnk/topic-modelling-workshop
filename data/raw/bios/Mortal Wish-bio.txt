Mortal Wish, formed in 1988, was also the name the group Mortal was known as in the late 1980's. The group was comprised of Jyro Xhan, Jerome Fontamillas, Ray Tongpo and Wilson Peralta. They produced a six-song demo, Wish Fifteen.

Mortal Wish also a black metal band from Brazil.
