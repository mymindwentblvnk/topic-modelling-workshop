The entity was born out of spiritual impulse of Grom on the freezing winter nights of 2002 on the foothills of Eastern Carpathians, in the city where Europe ends. Deriving inspiration from ancient beech-spruce forests of Bukowina, its majestic and mysterious mountains and rocks, 10 years after its foundation GRANSKOG presents the first demo-tape. 16 minutes of Bukowinian Black Metal frenzy capturing the spiritual essence of this land including a GOATMOON cover. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Granskog">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
