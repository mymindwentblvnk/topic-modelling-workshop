Kafirun are a black metal band that was formed in Vancouver, BC in 2014. They have released two EPs (collected in the compilation "The Worship and Glorification of Holy Death"), a split with Transilvania, and their debut full-length album "Eschaton" which was released in June 2017. The band features members of Antolian Wisdom, Hostium, Nordwind, Kymatica, and Riftwalker. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KAFIRUN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
