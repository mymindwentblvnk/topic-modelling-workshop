NS Black Metal from Russia. Members of M8l8th, Morbus Mundi, Endlosung, Eisigwald and Hagl all participate in this project. Lyrics deal with NS ideology and Aryanism. One demo has been released by this band called Borning Star By Myself in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stella+Arja">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
