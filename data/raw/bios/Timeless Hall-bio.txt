This band formed in Copenhagen, Denmark in 1994 and released a self-titled album that same year.  They had an American singer named Drew Heart and he was in the band Vanderhoof.  A single called True Love was released in 1996.  They most likely disbanded in 1996. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Timeless+Hall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
