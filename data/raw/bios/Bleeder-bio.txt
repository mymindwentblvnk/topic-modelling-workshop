In this case a band from Denmark, leadsinger is PeterPeter (ex. Sort Sol)
PeterPeter started up this band to show and tell the world, that punk or rock is not a mainstream issue. 
Mostly known from  Danish Radio P1 for great hits on "Det Elektriske Barometer", but also a great contributor to soundtrack for films like "Bleeder" and "Pusher".
Discography: Psycho Power (1997)
as The Bleeder Group; Sunrise (2004) and several more releases.
Unfortunately the band stopped playing together, but PeterPeter continues making underground music.

http://www.thebleedergroup.com/
