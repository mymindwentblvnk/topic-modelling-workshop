Ten Walls is a project by Marijus Adomaitis , also known as Mario Basanov, from Lithuania.

Ten Walls released his debut EP "Gotham" in May 2013 through Innervisions. It was followed by "Requiem" in December of the same year. September 2014 saw the release of  "Walking with Elephants". "Sparta" was released in May 2015. 

In June 2015, Ten Walls posted a rant on his Facebook pages condemning homosexuality and comparing it to paedophilia. As a result, he was dropped from several festival line-ups and by his booking agency.
