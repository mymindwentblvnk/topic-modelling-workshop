Omotai is a three-headed thunderous metal-spewing beast from Houston, Texas, formed in late 2009. Members include Melissa Lonchambon on bass, Anthony Vallejo on drums, and Samuel Waters on guitar.

In July of 2010, the band released a five song debut EP entitled Peace Through Fear.
