Chryztyne was a German christian metal band founded by some of the members of a band called Saint Attack, one of the first christian Metalbands in Germany. Olaf Hayer was the band's last singer. They released one album, "Tales of Paradise", in 1993. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chryztyne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
