jhfly (pronounced jay·haitch·fly) is Declan Browne, born 1996. jhfly make lofi, vibey instrumentals. They have a small following on Soundcloud, which is where they started originally uploading music. Most of their music is available on their bandcamp.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/jhfly">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
