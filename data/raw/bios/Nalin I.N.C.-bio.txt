NALIN I.N.C. was the solo-project from Andry Nalin from the German DJ-Team nalin & kane. His best known tracks are "Planet Violet" and "Scream", which contained a hot chocolate-sample. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nalin+I.N.C.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
