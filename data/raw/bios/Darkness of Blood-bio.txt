A symphonic black metal band from Mexico. They heavily rely on keyboards and church organs, making the music very symphonic. They base their lyrics mostly on vampires and darkness.

So far, they have released one full-length album called "A Dream of Vampires in Astral Dementia." The band is still active. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Darkness+of+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
