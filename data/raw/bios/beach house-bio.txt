Beach House is an American musical duo formed in Baltimore, Maryland in 2004. The band consists of vocalist and keyboardist Victoria Legrand and guitarist, keyboardist and backup vocalist Alex Scally.

Their self-titled debut studio album was released in 2006 to critical acclaim and has been followed by Devotion (2008), Teen Dream (2010), Bloom (2012), Depression Cherry (2015), Thank Your Lucky Stars (2015), 7 (2018) and Once Twice Melody (2022). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beach+House">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
