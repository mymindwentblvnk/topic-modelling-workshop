Shades Of Nowhere is dark rock/metal band from Turku,Finland formed in 2005.Band members are :

Emmi - Background Vocals
Matias - Lead Guitar
Jarno - Vocals, Guitar
Laura - Keyboards
Arska - Drums
Jamie - Bass

They describe their music as melancholic, catchy, organ-driven rock-metal, but you can also hear lot of influences on punk rock and maybe some pop stuff too. Lyrics tell stories about life & death.
