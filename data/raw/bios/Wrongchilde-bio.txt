Wrongchilde is a persona created by the criminal mind of Mat Devine (Kill Hannah). His debut album is called Goldblooded and was released in 2014, featuring a wide range of other creative artists who seek to help Wrongchilde in his evil quest to make the world a better place. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wrongchilde">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
