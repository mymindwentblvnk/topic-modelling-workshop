There are multiple artists with this name:
1) Drow is a sludge/stoner metal band from Nyírbátor, Hungary with Szabolcs Szabó (Mortem) on vocals.
2) Drow is a heavy metal band from Spain.
3) Dorw is a hard rock band from Köln, Germany.
4) There is also a psy-trance artist from Mexico with this name.
