Third Dim3nsion is a Power Metal band from Aranjuez, Madrid. Formed in 2005, they released 2 demos, 'The Help of the Gods' in 2008 & 'Ray of Light' in 2011, before their debut album 'Where The Dragon Lies..." in 2014. They site Helloween and Gamma Ray as being there major influences <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Third+Dim3nsion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
