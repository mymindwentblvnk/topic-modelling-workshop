1)Aeternum is a Middle Age&Renaissance music band from Transylvania,Romania.The project called Aeternum is an exquisite one in its hybrid instrumental formula, which throws light on the famous dialogue between the guitar and the other instruments, presented this time in a different way than it used to be performed during the Middle Ages and the Renaissance.

The Aeternum repertoire consists of Medieval and Renaissance musical compositions, extracted and adapted from the Codex Caioni and European pieces of music.
