Fields of Asphodel were a short lived metalcore band from Sheffield, UK. The band played dischordant metalcore with mosh parts a plenty. After starting out in 2007, the band headlined shows around Sheffield with a great deal of following and support. However, as 2008 dawned, the support began to diminish and the band faded in to to obscurity.
