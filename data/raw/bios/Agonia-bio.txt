There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Agonia was chaotic hardcore / noisecore band from Italy, active during mid '80-s. It was one-man project by Zambo (drummer of Italian hardcore band Wretched). Agonia released only one LP called "Nessuna Agonia Ci Farà Morire".

2) Agonia is a Hardcore / Nu metal band from Georgia. It has formed in may 2007. There are three members of band: Ucha Tevdorashvili (bass Guitar), Archil Shioshvili (Guitar and Vokals) and Sandro Kviciani (Drums)

3) Agonia was a melodic crust/hardcore/noise band from San Luis Potosi,México active during the beginning 00's.

4) Agonia was a brutal death metal band from Italy active during the early to mid 1990s. Formed in 1992 they released one demo and one EP before splitting up in 1996.

5) Agonia is a Depressive Black/Funeral Doom Metal band from Russian Federaton.

6) Agonia is a new wave/black metal/sadcore/deathcore band from Denmark that formed in mid-2007. Only a few tracks are availible
