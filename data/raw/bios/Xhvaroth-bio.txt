Xhvaroth are an Atmospheric Black Metal band from Krasnoyarsk, Russia, formed in in September 2009. Their music revolves around the detachment of the human spirit from the physical world, the rebirth of the soul, and the meaning of life. The band have chosen to keep their lineup and number of members secret; no additional information is known at this point.
