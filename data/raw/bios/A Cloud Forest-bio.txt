A Cloud Forest is an atmospheric black metal project from Phoenix, Arizona, USA, formed from the ashes of Contravene in 2009. Vocalist Shelly and guitarist Eric started a new project and self-released 5 song demo CD titled "Do Not Stand At My Grave and Weep" was released in 2010 followed by a full length, 50 minute album titled "Through the Eyes of the Ambivalent" in October 2010. 
In March 2011 a new vocalist was added and, in 2011, the band released its most recent album "These Mournful Days" on Silence & Solitude / Depressive Illusions Records. 2012 they released a 4-song CD titled "October's Overture" on a limited edition digipak CD.
