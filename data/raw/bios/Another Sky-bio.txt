Another Sky are a four-piece band from London, UK, currently signed to Fiction Records.

Comprised of vocalist Catrin Vincent, drummer Max, bassist Naomi and guitarist Jack, the group formed whilst they were studying music together at Goldsmiths University.  Their name is taken from a poem by Emily Dickinson.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Another+Sky">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
