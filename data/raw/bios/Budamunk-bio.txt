Hip Hop producer Budamunk moved to Los Angeles in 1996. He later started DJing at local clubs in LA, and making beats as well. He met 2 MCs, Joe Styles and OYG, and formed a Hip Hop crew Keentokers in 2004. In 2005, DJ Duel from the Spin Cycle joined the crew. They rocked a number of shows and were blessed to have shared the stage with various artists. Budamunky and the Keentokers are also a part of a Los Angeles based Hip Hop crew called Soul Jugglerz. After he moved back to Japan, he won the first official AKAI MPC competition in 2008. He released a number of projects from Jazzy Sport. He was also collaborated with 2 Japanese MCs, S.L.A.C.K. and ISSUGI, as a group called Sick Team. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Budamunk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
