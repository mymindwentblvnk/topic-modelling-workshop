Andreas Kleerup is a record producer, drummer, and member of the Swedish group 'The Meat Boys' hailing from Stockholm, Sweden. His first single "Medication" was released in 2005 but his first mainstream collaboration was as the producer of fellow Swedish artist Robyn's #1 UK single With Every Heartbeat.
