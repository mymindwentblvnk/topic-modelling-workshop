Metal band from Umeå formed in 2007, Västerbottens län, Sweden.

Band members:
Kristoffer Bäckström - guitar & vocals
Nicklas Bäckström - bass & vocals
Jakob Burstedt - drums
Patrik Ylmefors - guitar

Discography:
We all face the dark alone (2008)
Our astral circle (2009)
Rural (2011)
