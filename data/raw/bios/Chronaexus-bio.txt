Chronaexus is a Black Metal band from Sacramento, California.  Their debut full-length album "Algedonic Awakening" was released on Buriedinhell Records in 2010.  Chronaexus' second full-length release entitled "A Tempest of Reticence" was released in August 2011 and is available through BuriedinHell Records.  In 2013, Chronaexus released a split with Dodsferd entitled "Desecration Rites" which is available through Obscure Abhorrence Productions.

Chronaexus is currently writing material for their 3rd full length album which will tentatively be released spring 2014.

Nick Liuzzi - Guitar/ Vocals
Pete Chavez - Drums
