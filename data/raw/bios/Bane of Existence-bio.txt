Bane of Existence has been an active force in the Boston metal scene since 1999. B.O.E. has played numerous shows at local venues such as The Palladium, The Middle East, and countless other clubs with many major metal bands such as Suffocation, Vital Remains, Malignancy, Misery Index, Mortician, Immolation, Overkill, Manowar, etc. In 2004, B.O.E.s full-length CD Humanitys Splintered Salvation was released by Fleshfeast Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bane+of+Existence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
