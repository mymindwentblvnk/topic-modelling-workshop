Gutfucked is a 4 piece brutal death metal/slam band formed in Crestview, Florida, USA in 2011. They are writing really soul crushing material right now and they will be raping the stage as much as possible.

Members:
Josh Smith - Vocals
Anthony Davis - Guitar/Vocals
Nate Flores - Drums
Black Steve - Bass
