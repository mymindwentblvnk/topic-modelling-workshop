Kommand is a death metal band from [placformed]Los Angeles, California.   They started in the year 2015. The band consists of Jesse Sanes (vocals), Ian Logan (guitars), Sam Shriver (guitars), Tim Shriver (bass) and Sam Bosson (drums).  Sam Shriver and Tim Shriver joined the band in 2017.  The band is currently signed to Maggot Stomp.  They have released two demos and a compilation that contains their first two demos.  In 2020, they released their first full-length album Terrorscape on vinyl and digitally.  You may visit their official website or their Metal Archive page for more on Kommand.
