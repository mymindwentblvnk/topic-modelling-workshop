MisfitChris is an independent electronic artist from Connecticut, USA. He specializes in making retro dance tracks and VGM influenced IDM. MIsfitChris also has ties with the growing Chipmusic scene and occasionally releases music in Nintendo Sound File format. Chris currently resides on 3 prominent chipmusic influenced indie labels, Pause, Data Airlines and Bleepstreet.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Misfitchris">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
