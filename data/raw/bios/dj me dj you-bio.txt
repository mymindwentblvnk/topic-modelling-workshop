DJ Me DJ You are an electronica duo consisting of Ross Harris and Craig Borrell.  Their first record under this name was a five track EP entitled "Simplemachinerock" released on Emperor Norton Records.  They later released the full-length "Rainbows and Robots" in 2000 and "Can You See The Music" in 2002, the latter being their most recent release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dj+Me+DJ+You">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
