Viogression was a death metal band hailing from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA. They played death metal in the vein of Obituary, Autopsy and Asphyx. They formed in 1989 and released  "Execution Demo" and "Perception Blur Demo" in 1990 leading them to be signed to a label.

They released their debut album called  "Expound and Exhort" in 1991 via Tombstone Records.

This lead the band into releasing their second and final record "Passage" before splitting up in 1992.
