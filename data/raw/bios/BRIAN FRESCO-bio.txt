Born Brian Allen, the Bronzeville, Chicago-based rapper Brian Fresco split his childhood living at the neighborhood's now-demolished Ida B. Wells Homes and the Near South Side's Harold Ickes projects. In 2013, Fresco released his debut mixtape, "Mafioso". Followed in 2016 by the critically acclaimed 'Casanova' and 2018's "Love Scars". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BRIAN+FRESCO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
