Cataplexy is a Black Metal band formed in 1991. Located in Osaka, Japan, they have been one of the few Japanese Black Metal bands to last through the years, with many bands fading out around the 2000-era. Lyrical themes include Occultism and Satanism. Their style is  fast and agressive, with a depressive touch.
