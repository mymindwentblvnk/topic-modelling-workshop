Remi Wolf is a singer-songwriter whose quirky music incorporates pop, funk, and soul.

∙ Trained as an Olympic skier, she turned to music in high school, became an American Idol contestant in 2014, and later earned a music degree from USC.
∙ In 2019, Wolf won praise for her debut single, “Guy,” which was included on her first EP, You’re a Dog!
