Morg is a death and thrash based metal band from Finland. Founded in 2003 upon the remains of Carcass Head, Morg has recorded 4 demo eps so far: Enter the Morgue (2004), Return to the Soil (2005), Buried (2006) and Monolith (2007). Their current lineup consists of Chris Peake (guitar, vocals), Henri Harell (bass, vocals) and Antti Nieminen (guitar) as Turkka Harell (drums) left the band in late 2008.

More info can be found at their official site http://morg.pp.fi

There are or were also bands called Morg from the US and France, but the US band seems to be defunct by now and the French one has changed their name to Vetha. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
