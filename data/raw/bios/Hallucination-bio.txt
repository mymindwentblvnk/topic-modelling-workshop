Hallucination is (1) a death metal band from Scranton, PA, USA and (2) a trance/psychadelic band. 

Hallucination, the metal band, formed in Pennsylvania in 2009 by guitarist Joe Cruise and bassist/vocalist Jake Black. Their play death metal with influence from black metal and a heavy emphasis on a dark, dreamy atmosphere. They released a demo on December 31st, 2013 and are currently raising money to record their debut album.

The line-up currently consists of Jake Black (bass/vocals), Joe Cruise (guitar), Tyler Marvin (guitar), and Jack Allen (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hallucination">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
