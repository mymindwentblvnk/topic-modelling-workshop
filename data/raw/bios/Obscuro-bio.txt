Cold, traditional sounding Nordic black metal band from Sweden.  Since their creation in 2001, Obscuro has released 3 demos and one full length album entitled "Where Obscurity Dwells", via French label Drakkar Productions.

Obscuro was also the name of a Norwegian black metal band in the early 90s before they change the name to Manes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obscuro">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
