There are multiple artists called Sabretooth.

1. Sabretooth is a psytrance project of Sheffield-based Ben Fraser.

2. Sabretooth is an ambient, chill out and downtempo acid jazz artist on the Acuna Boyz Productions record label. 

3. Sabretooth are a neoclassical metal band from Cape Town, South Africa, that fuses modern metal with more classical stylings.

4. Sabretooth are a deathcore band from Peekskill, New York, comprised of Wesley Robinson, Nico Petrocitto, Ryan Santone, Devon Day and Justin White.
