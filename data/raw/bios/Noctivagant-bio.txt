Noctivagant is a dark ambient project hailing from Jacksonville, Florida, USA. The project, entirely comprised by a student named Jeff Reynolds, is influenced by artists such as Burzum, Narcotic Noise, Brian Eno, and various black metal acts. Noctivagant uses dark themes and subtle ambience to create an atmosphere in which many different worlds are explored. Despite its shadowy demeanor, the project aims to heal, relax, and cleanse through the medium of music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Noctivagant">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
