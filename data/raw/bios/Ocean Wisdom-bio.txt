Ocean Wisdom is a rapper from Brighton, UK.

In July 2014 he uploaded the music video for his song "Walkin'" to YouTube, and it has since amassed over 7 million views.

Partnering with High Focus Records, he released his first album "Chaos 93" in 2015, fully produced by Dirty Dike.
