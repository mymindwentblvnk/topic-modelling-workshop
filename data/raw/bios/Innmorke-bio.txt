Black\Death Metal project from Zheleznogorsk, Krasnoyarskiy kray (formerly) \ Krasnoyarsk (later), Siberia. 

Innmorke suspended its activities in 2012. In the same year Foltath Eternum started the one-man-band project Eoront. After the release of the latter's full-length in 2013, Innmorke was disbanded for good in favour of Eoront. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Innmorke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
