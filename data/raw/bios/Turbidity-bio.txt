Turbidity is a Indonesian slam death metal band. They released a full-length album "Suffering of Human Decapitated" in 2011 and an EP "Vomiting the Rotten Maggot" in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Turbidity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
