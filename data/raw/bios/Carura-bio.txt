Carura are a progressive metal band who formed in Poland in 2007.


The band is:

* Piotr ‘’Pieróg’’ Kierach - lead and rhythm guitars
* Marcin ‘’Marczel’’ Szczypior - bass guitar
* Mariusz ‘’Marian’’ Jastrzębski - drums
