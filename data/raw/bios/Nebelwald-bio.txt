Nebelwald is a melodic German black metal band.  It features members of Dusken, Silberbach, Forgotten Darkness, among others.

Nebelwald has thus far released one full length, entitled "The Ancient Rites of Marghas" in 2004, and a demo entitled "War in Marghas" in 2005.
