Several artist use the name Helcaraxe, one from Argentina, other from Canada and finally one from Slovakia. All of them under the sign of black metal.

1. (argentina) From Bariloche, this band opened for Behemoth (Pol) in their presentation in Cemento, Argentina on December 15, 2004. They've released four demos and a compilation.

2. (canada) They recorded just one 4 song demo tape in 1999 

3. (slovakia) With Line up Hromovlad (guitar), As (guitar) and Sigi (vocals, drums). they released 3 dirty black metal masterpieces and in 2005 changed the name into Remmirath.
