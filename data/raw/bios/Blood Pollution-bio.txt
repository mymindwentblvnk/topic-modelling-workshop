Blood Pollution is a Russian band from Moscow. Their style of music is generally called Thrash N Roll, which combines energetic sounds of punkish Rock N Roll with elements of Heavy Metal and Thrash Metal.

History
The band was formed in 2004 by Nick Thrash on guitar and some unknown dudes on vocal, bass and drums. In 2008, after few years of non-stop lineup changes Nick Thrash takes bass and starts singing,Joseph Kranstain and Ivy Age takes drums and guitar. After recording demo, they recorded first album Armed You!, which was released on tapes (Maltkross) and CD (29a) in 2009. In summer of 2009 Alex Krunch replaced Ivy and band recorded and released 2nd album Metal Zombies on their own label Atomic Carnage in January of 2010.
