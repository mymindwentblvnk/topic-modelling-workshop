Bleed From Within is a metal band from Glasgow, Scotland. The band formed in 2005 and currently consists of vocalist Scott Kennedy, drummer Ali Richardson, bassist Davie Provan and guitarists Craig Gowans and Steven Jones. The band has released four full-length studio albums and three EPs to date. Their fourth album "Era" was released on April 7, 2018 through Century Media in which the band moved towards a melodic metalcore style. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleed+from+Within">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
