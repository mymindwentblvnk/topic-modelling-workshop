Fall From Reality are a Melodic Death Metal band from Nizhny Novgorod, Russia who formed in 2004. For its career the band has recorded the demo «Emptiness» dated to 2005 and the full length album «Withdrawal». The album featured the work of both Russian (Arkady Navaho, Navaho Hut Studio) and foreign (Dan Swano, Unisound Studio, Sweden) producers who have made their contributions to the recording. The album release was preceded by the contract with Chaotic Noiz Records, the division of one of the most major Russian record labels “Soyuz”. The album hit the stores on February 24, 2009.
