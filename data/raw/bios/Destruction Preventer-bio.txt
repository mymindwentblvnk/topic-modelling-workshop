Destruction Preventer was formed in the spring of 2007 by Matt Adams and Mike Carroll after the collapse of their former band, Morning Star.

"The aim was to create a band who weren't tied down to any genre or "core" shit, A band who were just PURE HEAVY METAL."

Destruction Preventer are a Heavy Metal band from Torbay. pleasing local fans with their high octane live shows, while keeping other bands on their toes since day one! With a set packed full of unique, genre crossing originals and many known, unknown and surprising covers
