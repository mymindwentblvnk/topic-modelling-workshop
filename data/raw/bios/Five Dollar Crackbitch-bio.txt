They were a death metal band that formed in Andernach, Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany in 2010.  The band consisted of  Stefan Höfer on vocals,  Bernd Schafer &  Daniel Michel on guitars,  Mark Brenner on bass, backing vocals and Mariusz Samujło on drums.  Their only release is an EP called Dehumanization.  In 2016, they changed their name to Plagueborne <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Five+Dollar+Crackbitch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
