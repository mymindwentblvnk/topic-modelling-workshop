The Myriad Burial is a technical/brutal deathcore band from Ontario Canada. Their common lyrical themes include misanthropy and their music includes many breakdowns, slams, and solos. The band is currently on hold, as the members have created a side-project known as Fetus Feast. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Myriad+Burial">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
