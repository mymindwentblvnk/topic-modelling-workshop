Mayhemesis was an unsigned/independent  thrash/death metal band from Buffalo, N.Y.  Mayhemesis formed in 1989 and released only two demos. They separated a year later in 1990. 

Members were as follows:
Dennis John - Vocals
Joe Morelli - Guitar
Phil Frost - Guitar
Rich Ziegler - Bass
