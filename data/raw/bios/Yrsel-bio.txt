Yrsel is a collaboration between the French musician Julien Louvet, best known for his experimental black metal project The Austrasian Goat, and the Swedish dark ambient musician C-J Larsgarden a.k.a. Ondo. They have released three albums together. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yrsel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
