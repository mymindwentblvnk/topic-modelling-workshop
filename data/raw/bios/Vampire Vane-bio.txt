Vampire Vane is an eponymous black metal act hailing from Toronto, Canada... Not much has been revealed about its history. Founded in the early 2000's by Vampire Vane, all material has been self-produced and in limited pressings. Lyrical themes involve evil, mysticism, dark fantasy, paganism, etc. Musically, the band tends to place more emphasis upon dark and powerful atmospheres than upon technicality. 

Currently active.
