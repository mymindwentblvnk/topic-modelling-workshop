A four piece 'indie/jazz' band from Melbourne, Australia. Featuring Ollie McGill (piano, melodica), Harry Angus (Vocals, trumpet, keyboard), Jules Pascoe (bass, vocals), and Harry Shaw-Reynolds (percussion).

So far two albums have been released by the band. The first, 'Go To The Beach', is distributed through MGM Distribution. The second, 'Hold Your Breath' was launched at Bennetts Lane Jazz Club in Melbourne on the 28th of March 2008. The second album is not distributed but is available for purchase from Bennetts Lane and live shows. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Conglomerate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
