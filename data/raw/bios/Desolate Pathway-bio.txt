Desolate Pathway are a UK doom metal band formed by former Pagan Altar guitarist Vince Hempstead. They released their debut album Valley of the King in 2014 telling a story of a mythical genre with an old English theme similar to A Song of Ice and Fire and The Lord of the Rings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desolate+Pathway">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
