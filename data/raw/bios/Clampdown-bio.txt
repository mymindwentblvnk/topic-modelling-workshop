There is more than one artist with this name:

1.) A technical post-thrash/progressive math metal band from France. Formed back in 1998 and released two demos (one in 2001 and "State of Grace" in 2003) and a full-length album entitled Vision Of Splendor in 2010 on label Season of Mist.

2.) Clampdown is a tribute to The Clash from Auckland, New Zealand. The band features members of City Newton Bombers, The Managers, and The Brysons. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Clampdown">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
