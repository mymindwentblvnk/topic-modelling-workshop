Lil Dap first appearance was in 1992 on Gang Starr's LP 'Daily Operation' together with Jeru the Damaja. He is a member of the Group Home, wich are in the Gang Starr Foundation. Group Home left the Gang Starr Foundation after a discussion with DJ Premier and later Group Home split. Lil Dap signed at a Polish label and in 2008 he signed at Babygrand for releasing his first solo album who is going to be released in mid 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lil%27+Dap">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
