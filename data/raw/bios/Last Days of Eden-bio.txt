Last Days of Eden is a Spanish symphonic power metal-band from Asturias, Spain. It was founded in 2013 with members from the power metal band DarkSun. Their music mixes elements from symphonic metal with celtic music using the sound of bagpipes, whistles and flutes.
Members:
Lady Ani – female vocals
Dani G. – guitars and male vocals
Javy González – bass
Leo Duarte – drums
Sara Ember – violin
