Lucky Tapes is Kai Takahashi, Keito Taguchi, and Kensuke Takahashi. They released their debut album "The SHOW" in 2015, and in 2016 a single, "MOON". 2 new songs were featured in the movie「狼女の子と黒人王子」(Wolf girl and black prince). In July, they released their 2nd album "Cigarette & Alcohol". Their most recent release, "Virtual Gravity" EP, was released in September 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lucky+Tapes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
