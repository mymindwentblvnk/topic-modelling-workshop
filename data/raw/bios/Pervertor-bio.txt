Pervertor are a black/thrash metal band from Wellington, New Zealand.... Pervertor are here to deliver a fist in the face to those that lack any real metal substance and claim themselves to be a "metal" band. Here to bring an onslaught of the finest cuts of black/thrashing metal hell in honor of the creators of this ancient art in the vein of Sodom, Bathory, Venom, Hellhammer, Darkthrone etc.
