1) Mysteriis was a satanic Black Metal band from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, formed in 1998. On June 1st, 2006, they decided to stop their activities.

Reformed in 2003 (with almost the same line-up) as Darkest Hate Warfront, with a more intense sound - 2 albums have been released through Keffer Records (DE) since then.
