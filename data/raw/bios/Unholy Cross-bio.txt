Italian Ambient Funeral Doom

A one man project playing calm and sorrowful music in the vein of Until Death Overtakes Me and Funerary Dirge. Includes both electronica and industrial elements. The sound of gunfire and people screaming gives the music a rather unpleasant feeling.

Current Line Up:
Mirco Pervilli - Everything

Discography:

2004: 'Death Spares Nobody' (Demo)
2004: 'Hoc In Marmore Est Incisum' (Demo CD)
