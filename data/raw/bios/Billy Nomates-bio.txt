Billy Nomates is the solo music project of Tor Maries.

Tor recently joined with Invada, and her first single "No" was released in March 2020, gaining huge support from BBC 6 Music.

Tor has been involved with various bands / music projects across [placefrom]Bristol[/placefrom] and the south prior to starting Billy Nomates just over a year ago. She hopes to embark on her debut tour next year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Nomates">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
