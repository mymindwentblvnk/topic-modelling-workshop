Reg Vermue, a.k.a. Gentleman Reg, is a Canadian indie singer-songwriter.

Members of Royal City and The Constantines regularly appear as Vermue's supporting band on his solo recordings.  He has regularly performed with The Hidden Cameras and Final Fantasy, aswell as contributing vocals on the Final Fantasy LP 'Has A Good Home'.    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gentleman+Reg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
