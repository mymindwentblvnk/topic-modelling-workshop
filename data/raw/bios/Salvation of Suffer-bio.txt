Salvation of Suffer is a german metalband and was founded in 2008.

It once started as a small punkrock project. Through some changes in lineup and style its sound was highly increased to a serious kind of metal with catchy guitarsounds, deep growls, impulsive rhythm and explict lyrics that burns deep into the heads of their listeners and fans. Be prepared!

Salvation of Suffer are:

Patrick Arndt - Vocals/Growls
Robby Schmidt - LeadGuitar
René Fleischmann - RhythmGuitar
Aron Erler - Bass
