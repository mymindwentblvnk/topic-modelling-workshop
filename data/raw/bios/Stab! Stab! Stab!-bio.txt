stab!stab!stab! was formed in Pittsburgh, PA in 2003. They released 2 EPs, "stabracadabra!" and "Alcibiades" before finally releasing their full-length in 2004, "Hell's Coming With Me". They disbanded in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/stab%21+stab%21+stab%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
