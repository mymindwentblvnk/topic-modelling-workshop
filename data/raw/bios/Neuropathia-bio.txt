Neuropathia is a grindcore band from Białystok, Poland.  They formed in the year 1996.  In their early days, they started out as a goregrind band.  Their lyrical themes are gore and humor.  They consist of Konrad (vocals), Rodak (guitars), Czarek (guitars), Jawor (bass) and Psychoradek (drums).  They have six demos, five full-length albums, one EP and several splits.  They are currently signed to EveryDayHate.  In 2003, they were briefly featured in a TV  series called "Biała Sukienka".
