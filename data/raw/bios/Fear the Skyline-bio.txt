In November 2005 Thomas, Damian And Lukas met the first time to do some music together. Quickly they brought up the thought of starting a band. Over a few months they wrote songs and asked Matze to join them. He agreed and with him Steph to play the second guitar. As “Your Tears In Red” they recorded one song before Robin replaced Steph and the Band decided to start over new , This was in May 2006. At this Point they finally decided to name this baby “ Fear The Skyline”. They wrote new songs, recorded their first demo BLOODBATHTRIBUTE and began performing live.
