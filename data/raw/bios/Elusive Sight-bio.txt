Elusive Sight has been created in april 2012 by Jarosław Mendrek and Maks Ciastoń. After two years, and Lost In Nothingness demo release, band has been formed in squad: Gordon (vocal), Jarosław (guitar), Paweł (bass), Krzysztof (drums). Band is inactive. 

Band from: Leszno Górne, Poland
Since: April 2012
