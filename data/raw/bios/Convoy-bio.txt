4. Convoy was a San Diego, California country rock influenced band who released the album Pineapple Recording Sessions in 1999. The group was signed to Hybrid Recordings who released Black Licorice, in August of 2001. The band was formed by some members of Dishwater in the latter 1990's, and the core songwriters of Convoy (Jason Hill & Robbie Dodds) formed Louis XIV soon after the group's dissolution. Prior to breaking up, Convoy recorded "Let's Spend the Night Together," a cover of the Rolling Stones song, for an ad campaign by Sheraton Hotels.
