Daywalker are a 4 piece Indie rock group from Liverpool. They write and perform music that is their own style, combining catchy guitar hooks with throaty gravelly vocals, resulting in songs that get you off your seat and singing along as if you’ve known them all your life!!! 
Team this with an energy and enthusiasm that when played live takes it to the next level!!! With a line up brimming with talent and attitude, Daywalker have the drive, the enthusiasm, but most of all the music to be the next big thing to come out of the UK. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Daywalker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
