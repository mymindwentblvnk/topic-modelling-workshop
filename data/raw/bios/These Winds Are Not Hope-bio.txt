These Winds Are Not Hope is a collaboration project from Kumanovo founded in February 2016.

The musicians on the "Yesterday's Ghosts" album are:
Boban Despotovski [[artist]Timesand, MK]
(Guitars; drum & keyboard programming, mix & mastering);

Marc Durkee [The Chronicles Of Israfel; Rain Over St. Ambrose; Lead Mule, CAN]
(Lead & Backing Vocals) ;

Miroslav Denkovski [[artist]Holocaust, MK]
(Bass)
