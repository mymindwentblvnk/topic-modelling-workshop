Accelerate was a five piece dutch heavy metal band formed in Zwolle, Overijssel. They were formed in 1998 and split in 2007 after a final show on the first of June. They've recorded one demo called Ode to Metal in october 2000 and one EP in 2006 called Predator, both self-released.
Final line-up:
Andre Volkers (guitar and backing vocals)
Bart Fontijn (guitar)
Benny Ras (drums)
Clemens Schaap (bass)
