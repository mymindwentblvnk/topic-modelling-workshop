Cauchemar is a heavy metal band founded in 2007 in Montreal, Canada. Its music is distinguished by its doom metal and hard rock influences, and by the mystical french-language singing of Annick Giroux. The band has released one EP (“La vierge noire” in 2010) and one LP (“Tenebrario” in 2013) on Nuclear War Now! Productions, and will release its second LP, “Chapelle ardente”, in 2016. Cauchemar has played numerous shows in Europe, South America and North America, and one concert in India. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cauchemar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
