Souls She Said is the indie/noise rock alter ego side project of Joe Cardamone (vocals) and Don Devore (guitar) from the punk metal band the Icarus Line. The duo released the Rub the Sleep Out EP on Buddyhead in 2003, followed by As Templar Nites on Dim Mak in February 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Souls+She+Said">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
