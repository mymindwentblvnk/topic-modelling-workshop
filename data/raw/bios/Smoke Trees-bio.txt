Smoke Trees is a producer out of Stuttgart, Germany. He’s acclaimed for his smooth and relaxed beats. He’s an expert on the laidback vibe. Be it jazzy, funky, trippy, classic or even with a more abstract style his music never disappoints.

He is the Founder of Estugarda-Records and also Curator of the Spotify-Playlist Selected Beats [Lofi + Hip Hop].

In February 2017 he released »Into The Deep« via Urban Waves Records (Vinyl, Cassettes &amp; digital)
