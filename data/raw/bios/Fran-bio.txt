There are multiple artists using this name.

1 Fran is Fransiska Degeyter, she was a Belgian singer from Brugge (Sint Andries) °30 April 1960. Died 17 November 1986.

2 Fran is a mononym of singer Fran Shohina, a member of Oliver Koletzki & Fran (aka Koletzki feat. Fran & Oliver Koletzki feat. Fran) & The Koletzkis.

3 Fran also refers to a singer-songwriter pop group from Chicago, active since the mid 2010s.

4 Fran (Francisco Gil)  is a brazilian singer-songwriter who released his first album on 2020, "Raíz". He is the grandson of Gilberto GIl and son of Preta Gil.
