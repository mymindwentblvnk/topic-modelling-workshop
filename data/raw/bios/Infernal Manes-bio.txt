Infernal Manes is a heavy metal band who's style is influenced by such other bands as Iron Maiden, Judas Priest and Black Sabbath. The band formed in the mid 90s. In 2002 vocalist TJ Cobra, who also is involved with the bands Blofeld and Violent Power, joined them and they recorded their debut album Battle Of Souls.
