Songs for Iris is an acoustic folk-pop band from Denmark, well-known for it's tight vocal harmonies and it's groovy arrangements. The music covers a wide field of folk traditions, but is at the same time very contemporary and original. It's grand and emotional, and simultaneously humorous with tongue in cheek rhythmics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Songs+for+Iris">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
