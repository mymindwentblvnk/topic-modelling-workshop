Axevyper were a heavy metal from Italy that formed in 2009.  They are influenced by Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Manilla Road, Running Wild and Shok Paris.  Their lyrical themes include metal, battles, anti-poser war and honor.  They released one split, one demo, one EP and three full-length albums.  Bassist  Andrea "Pandò" Tognetti  left the band and that lead to the end of Axevyper.  They disbanded in 2018.   The last known lineup consisted of Luca "Fils" Cicero  on vocals,  Guido Tiberi on guitars,  Damiano "La Favola" Michetti on guitar and  Niccolò Vanni on drums.  The last label they were signed to was Iron Shield Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Axevyper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
