This blackened deathcore quintet is from Lansing, Michigan and formed in the summer of 2016.  They consist of  Alex DeRose (vocals),  Julian Hendrickson (guitars),  Chase Applegate (guitars),  Michael Weber (bass) and Aaron Gray (drums).  They are influenced by death metal, black metal and slam death metal.  They released two EP's, one single and a full-length album.  They have released their releases independently and they are currently not signed to a label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bog+Wraith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
