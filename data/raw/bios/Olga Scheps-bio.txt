Olga Scheps (* 1986 in Moscow) is a Russian pianist living in Germany
Olga Scheps' repertoire contains her  interpretation of the music of  the Romanticism in particular the works of Russian composers as well as Frederic Chopin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Olga+Scheps">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
