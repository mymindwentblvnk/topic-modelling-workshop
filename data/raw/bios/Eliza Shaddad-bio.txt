Eliza Shaddad is a Sudanese-Scottish folk singer-songwriter based in London. 

Born to Sudanese and Scottish parents, Eliza Shaddad is a descendent of a long line of artists and poets dating back to the 1800’s. Raised across Europe and Africa and now based in London, her four-track EP ‘Waters‘ was produced by Mercury Music Prize nominated Chris Bond — the talent behind Ben Howard — and released on Beatnik Creative. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eliza+Shaddad">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
