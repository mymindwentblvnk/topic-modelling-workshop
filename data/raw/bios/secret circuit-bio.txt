Secret Circuit is a cosmic electric music project led by musician Eddie Ruscha, also of The Laughing Light of Plenty, Future Pigeon, Dada Munchamonkey, Maids of Gravity, and Medicine. Secret Circuit has also done remixes for Nite Jewel, Rainbow Arabia, and Dengue Fever. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Secret+Circuit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
