Cory Marks is a Canadian country rock singer and songwriter signed to Better Noise Music. He has released two albums, This Man in 2015 under Big Star Recordings and most recently Who I Am in 2020 under Better Noise. Since the release the album has accumulated over 100 million streams globally with his debut single "Outlaws & Outsiders" being certified gold in Canada and reaching top 10 at US rock radio, making Marks the first to ever do so as a Canadian country act. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cory+Marks">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
