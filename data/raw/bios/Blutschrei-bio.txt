Blutschrei  was an NS black metal from Finland containing Ravenum (Hammer, ex-Horna on vocals and drums, Shatraug (Horna, Sargeist) on guitar and bass and VilwolfHeim from the French Sombre Chemin on drums. 

Blutschrei has released one album through Hammerbund under the name of The Voice of Forbidden Pride in 2006.
