QFX are a dance act from Hawick, Scotland. Their first album 'Freedom' (1994) entered the UK Independent Chart at number 5 and stayed in the Top 20 for 14 weeks. In 1996, QFX covered the Moby track 'Every Time You Touch Me' with vocalist Heather Allan (ex Ultra-Sonic) - the track peaked at 22 giving the band their first UK Top 40 hit. QFX have also remixed tracks for other artists, including Gala's 'Freed From Desire'. The band are signed to Big Life records and continue to record and perform.
