Blistered Earth were a thrash metal band from Massachusetts, playing in the vein of Megadeth,Metallica,Testament and other bay area thrash bands. The band has changed their name to Graveheart, the lyrics also have changed, the music changed towards a more death/thrash approach, with lyrics about Zombies.

Andy Lobas - Vocals/Bass
Steve Fry - Guitar
Graig Marseglia - Drums
