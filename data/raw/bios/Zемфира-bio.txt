Zemfira, full name Zemfira Talgatovna Ramazanova, is a Russian pop rock singer of Bashkir background. 

Zemfira was born on August 26, 1976 in Ufa. She started her career in 1998 and since then became immensely popular in Russia and other former Soviet republics.
