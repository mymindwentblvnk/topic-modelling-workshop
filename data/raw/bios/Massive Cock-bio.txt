Massive COCK is a death metal/hardcore band from Herentals, Belgium.
The band was formed in 2005 by Dirk Wouters (vocals), Nick Verschraegen (guitar) and Sam Verschraegen (drums). Jasper Boven joined them not much later as their bass player. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Massive+Cock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
