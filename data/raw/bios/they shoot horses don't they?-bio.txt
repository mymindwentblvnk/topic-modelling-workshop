[[i]From wikipedia]They Shoot Horses, Don't They? were a rock and roll band from Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Their final show was on 2008-04-27 at the Astoria in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Their debut album Boo Hoo Hoo Boo was released on the Kill Rock Stars label February 21, 2006. KRS releases Pick Up Sticks on June 5, 2007.

Keyboardist Chris Alscher (also known as Chris-A-Riffic) is also a regular guest host on CBC Radio 3. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/They+Shoot+Horses+Don%27t+They%3F">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
