Midnightdate are a dark gothic metal band from Poland. Their songs focus on vampirism, existential pain and contact with the world beyond. The band released three demos - Date At Midnight (2000), Demo (2002) and Bitter Aftertaste (2003) and featured Agnieszka Górecka (Darzamat) on vocals.

Midnightdate's lineup is:

Mirek (Nathan) - Guitar
Katarzyna Czapran - Bass
Agnieszka Górecka - Vocals
Krzysztof Latacz - Keyboard
Łukasz Marut - Guitar and Vocals
