There are more than one band with the name Cold Grave.

- Death/Black Metal band from Oakland, California. Formed in 2008, their debut album, Lycanthrope, was released on Graveyard Records in 2009.  Cold Grave tries to bring an "old school" esthetic back to Death Metal.

- Cold Grave is a German Black Metal band from Nazgul (CAMULOS, CAEDES, FROZEN ABYSS). Orthodox, minimalistic Black Metal in the tradition of Lucifer is presented here. The music is oriented to oldschool manifested Black Metal.  One album, "Infernal", has been released thus far on NS Propaganda in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cold+Grave">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
