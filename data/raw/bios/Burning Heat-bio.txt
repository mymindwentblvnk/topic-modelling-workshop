Burning Heat is a Boston based band formed in 2007. The band has been playing excessively in the New England club scene since its formation, Giving the most energetic live shows. BH is made out of four young talented and hungry for success musicians, All of which are Berklee College of Music Alumni's. At the moment the boys have written and demoed all the songs which will be recorded as the band's first album later this year. The songwriting level of this group can compete with any of the legendary rock bands out there.

Carlos Adrian Araiza----Guitar and Lead Vocals
Ethan Brosh--------------Lead Guitar, Vocals
JC---------------Bass Guitar
