After remixes of Lana Del Rey, Outkast, The Kooks and many more, french producer and multi-instrumentalist Jean Tonique has broken into the online market through being a regular fixture on Hype Machine's Top 10. 

Jean Tonique is preparing to expand his presence with the release of the You EP - bringing that old school, happy vibe that made us fall in love with funky house. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jean+Tonique">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
