Die Sterne are a band from Hamburg, Germany. They were founded in 1992.

Alongside such bands like Blumfeld and Tocotronic, Die Sterne are one of the most important representatives of the so-called "Hamburger Schule" (Hamburg school). Rock, Soul and Funk are some of the major influences on the music of Die Sterne. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Die+Sterne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
