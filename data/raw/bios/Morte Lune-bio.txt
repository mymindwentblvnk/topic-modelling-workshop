Morte Lune is a one-man Black Metal / Dark Ambient project hailing from the forests of Portugal.
All music written and produced by Lord Astaroth, lyrics by Lord Astaroth.


Morte Lune is part of The Order of the Silver Night (OSN), which includes projects such as Fel, Mannfall, and In Cripte, as well as others.
