Metal band formed in London 2001, featuring Shadow Venger (guitars), Spanky/Cyclonis Niyilka (vocals), Adam (drums), and Chris (bass). Released 1 EP Dance Of The Dragon (2003) and 1 album A  Black Death Dawns (2005) before disbanding in 2006.

Shadow & Spanky went on to form the band Empyreal Destroyer. Shadow left the band at the end of 2007 and formed the 2 solo projects Shadow Venger & Venger's Diary.    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stand+Aside">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
