A band created in 1999 with several like minded musician. Coming from a scene of Gothic/Metal realms.  Taking some influence from Sisters of Mercy, Fields of the Nephilim. Also Nightwish and The Gathering started to play a role in the influence of the band at the time.  Towards the end of the bands existance older Katatonia and some other doom metal started to become the direction of the bands endevour. But for some reason certain members of the band wanted nothing to do with music no more and the band ceased to exist. Certain band members went on to create Eternal Embrace in 2004.
