Apparitions of the End are a UK based thrash metal band, taking influence from bands such as Sepultura, S.O.D, Exodus, Testament and Tankard to name a few.

Despite an average age of 18, they have already supported bands including Napalm Death, Divine Chaos, Tesseract, No Consequence, Carcer City, Gehenna 777, Random Conflict and The Argent Dawn.
