Baton Rouge-based producer James Prudhomme. He's been around online for a couple of years, putting out mixtapes, remixes, and original productions for rappers. The most widely heard of these to date is his beat for “Hurt”, for Swedish teenager Yung Lean. He re-released his mixtape 'Japan' on March 10th, 2015.
