Secret was a highly popular Soviet rock and roll quartet singing in Russian. After Maxim Leonidov (Максим Леонидов), Secret's main vocalist, left the band to start a solo career, they became a trio, but in the mid-90s Secret was finally disbanded. Nowadays, Nikolay Fomenko, one of the ex-members, is a famous actor, TV anchorman. As a race driver, for the best part of 2000s, Fomenko also took part in FIA GT championship, and participated in LeMans 24hr endurance races.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/%D0%A1%D0%B5%D0%BA%D1%80%D0%B5%D1%82">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
