A Gun Called Tension is a band with Dann Gallucci, from the bands Modest Mouse, The Murder City Devils, Triumph of Lethargy Skinned Alive to Death, and Sean Reveron of The Beta Band.
They have released a self -titled album.

The band's sound is a fusion of rap, reggae, and electronica. They are releasing an album titled The Sub Sonic Sessions through RockersNYC Records in July, 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+Gun+Called+Tension">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
