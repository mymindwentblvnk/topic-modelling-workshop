Autumn Clan was a Gothic metal band founded in 1997 in Graz, Styria, Austria. The last line-up before they split up in 2005 was: Gabriel Kiri (bass), Jens P. Hermann (vocals, guitar), Markus “Mexx” Kicker (drums; known also from Black Prophecy) and FrankyB (guitar).
