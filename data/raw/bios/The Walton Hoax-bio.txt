The Walton Hoax is a chilled mix of deep bass, dreamy vocals and mid-tempo beats. 

July 2011 sees the birth of debut EP "Chemical Burn". It features three tracks, including a collab with singer/producer Mimi Page. Six months later, a free compilation comes out, with remixes by producers Sorrow, Enigma Dubz, Ghosts of Paraguay, Ollie MacFarlane, Nymos, TTP, Kilowatts and Sy.ic. The Walton Hoax returned in September 2012 with another release, the "All Recklessness Aside EP", featuring the titletrack, a collab with Nymos and a remix by Galimatias. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Walton+Hoax">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
