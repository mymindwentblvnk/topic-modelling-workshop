Clubber Lang was a beatdown band based out of New Jersey in the mid/late 1990's. They released a demo and one ep. Influenced by the likes of Sheer Terror, Biohazard, Merauder, Dmize, Madball, AF, Warzone, Hatebreed, Darkside NYC. Clubber Lang will go down as one of the most popular NJHC acts of the 1990's.

This band is not to be confused with the current Clubber Lang from the mid-west that was using there description in this space.
