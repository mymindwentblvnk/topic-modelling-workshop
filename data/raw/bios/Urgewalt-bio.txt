Ambient black metal band from Bavaria, Germany, formed in 2008 by 'Larvae' and 'Boandlgrama' after their previous band, Pestreich (which included a third member) came to an end. 

Discography;
2009 - Mystik (demo)
2010 - Geisterhain (demo)
2011 - Skrze Mráz / Rhui'Auros (split w. Korium)
2012 - Traumweber I
2017 - Die Maske des roten Todes / König Pest (split w. Anachoret)
2020 - Traumweber II

Also made a cover of the Lustre track "The Light of Eternity" with quest vocals from Flak, the third member of Pestreich. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Urgewalt">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
