The project was formed by Gregor Mackintosh (Paradise Lost) with the help of his colleagues from At the Gates and My Dying Bride. Their music combines elements of death and doom metal which resulted in the single ‘Desecration’, released by Imperium Productions in 2011 as a seven-inch vinyl limited to 500 copies. Their debut album is titled ‘A Fragile King’ and was released worldwide in 31 October 2011 by Century Media Records. Their second album,  ‘Splinters’ was released in 2014. Their third album is due to be released in 2017 under the name of ‘Fear Those Who Fear Him’. 

The current line-up consists of:

* Gregor Mackintosh (Paradise Lost) - vocals and lead guitars
* Hamish Glencross (My Dying Bride) - rhythm and lead guitars
* Mully - rhythm guitars
* Scoot (Doom, Extinction of Mankind) - bass
