Shibo was founded in Moscow in Fall, 2006. The first show took place at August, 23th 2007. There were several impressive gigs in 2007 and 2008 in clubs of Moscow and Nizhniy Novgorod. The band also supported such bands as The Dillinger Escape Plan, Tesseract, Burst, Buried Inside. The debut EP 'Crabcore' was released 7th March, 2009 through Delicious Music (NiNo). Now the band composes new songs for a full-length album which is planned to be recorded in 2011 for an early 2012 release.. 

Line-up:
Anton Kobedinov - vocals
Eugeny Linnik - guitar
Andrey Kim - guitar
Oleg Sinyavsky - bass
Sergey Belousov - drums
