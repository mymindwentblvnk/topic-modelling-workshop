There are multiple Artists using the name White Trash.

1.   Was a Hardcore Punk band from Colorado, existing in the early 80's. Their first and only record, "Wake Up!", was released in 1983.
Three of the members went on to play in The Fluid...



2.  Was a heavy metal band from Queens, New York. They separated themselves from other bands in their genre with their horn section, called the Badass Brass, which gave them a very funky musical sound. They enjoyed some success in the early 90s before disbanding.


3. Was a rock group from Texas and Louisiana. The first Edgar Winter's band. See Edgar Winter's White Trash. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/White+Trash">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
