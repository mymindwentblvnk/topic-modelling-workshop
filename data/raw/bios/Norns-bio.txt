Norns was a black metal band from Kotka, Finland formed in 2005. They've released one demo In Fog They Appear in 2005 and a split with Kathaarian in 2007, and one EP Pazuzu in 2016. 

Last known line-up

Behemoth - Bass, Vocals (Aske, Kuutar, Path of Darkness, ex-Azazel, ex-Vornat)

Destruction - Guitars (Nattfog)
