One of the South Sumatran tiger began to roaring, started in 2011 with sporadic raids their EP album, Human Being. Just Like Yesterday is a 5 young people who have a common vision & purpose of the musical based on their heart and pure. Just Like Yesterday will haunt your ears with a 4 track EP that is included in this album. With heavy guitars and super aggressive drum, is the evidence of their existence, Dimas (Vocals), Ricky (guitar), Ninoy (guitar), Pani (Bass) and Fahri (drums). Choir sound-replication sounding like typical hardcore track on Sadly Mistake Ironically Different, Great weight guitar sound on the intro Even Blind Can See shows a strong character. Slander, a barrel of which are familiar in the ears of Sriwijaya scenester land and ends with Accolade, invites to relentless head banging with the groove part in mid-song. A reference is right for you who look for cast-sound metalcore sound that successfully presented with the full energy in the land of Sriwijaya, Palembang, Indonesia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Just+Like+Yesterday">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
