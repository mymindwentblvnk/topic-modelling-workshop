A death metal band from Cincinnati, Ohio / Louisville, Kentucky that formed in 2015.  This band has released one demo, one full-length album and five split releases between the years 2015 to 2020.  Surgikill consists of Billy Nocera (vocals, guitars), Vanessa Hexe (vocals, lyrics), William Sievers (vocals), Stevo do Caixao (vocals), Zdenka Prado (bass) and Ash Thomas (guitars, drums).  Hexe usually writes lyrics about death and gore.  They are signed to F.D.A. Records.
