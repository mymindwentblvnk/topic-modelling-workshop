Squeezing 5 people and a load of instruments into a very small car, Shake Shake Go embarked on a busking tour of the UK in 2012 to play their collection of indie pop anthems to the country. 
Finding themselves being played by prestigious BBC Radio presenters Steve Lamacq and Tom Robinson and supporting the likes of James Blunt, Rodrigo y Gabriela, King Charles, Dan Croll, Talisco and more...
Their first single 'England Skies' was released in december 2014 reaching over 15 millions streams on Spotify, Shake Shake Go released their debut EP with Beaucoup Music in March 2015. Their first album will be coming out on the 29th of January 2016.
