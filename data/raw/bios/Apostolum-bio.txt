Apostolum is a Black/Doom metal band that formed in 2004 in Cremona, Italy. In 2006 the band released the EP "Anedonia" featuring 2 original songs and a Katatonia cover from "Brave Murder Day". 

The current lineup of Apostolum is
Stone: Guitars & Bass
Meba: Vocals
