Nirvikalpa is a thrash/black metal band into originals from New Delhi formed in the winter of 2001 by ex-lead guitarist of Misanthrope turned singer-songwriter Rishi aka DarkLord. Right from the early stages till now it has always been a one man band jamming to a drum machine and pre-recorded bass patterns with a few external musicians strictly for rare live sessions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nirvikalpa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
