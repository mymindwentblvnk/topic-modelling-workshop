Atropos Wrath are Melodic Death Metal band from Stierberg, Germany founded in 2013.In February 2015 they released their first album called Omega.They are influenced by Behemoth, DevilDriver, In Vain, Insomnium, Children Of Bodom, Kalmah etc.
Band Members:
-Denn (vocals)
-Phil (bass)
-Fr00dl (drums)
