Draco Aerius is a Black Metal project from Wellington, New Zealand. They formed in 2002 and released a full length album in 2004 entitled 'Serve Chaos' before disbanding in 2005. In 2007 they reformed with a new lineup and in 2008 released the demo 'The Cardinal Adversary.' Draco Aerius are back with a new line-up and will be gigging again shortly.
