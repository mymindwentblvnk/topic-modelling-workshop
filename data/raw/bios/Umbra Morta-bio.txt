Umbra Morta started as a one-man-band in 2010 by Dehumanizer to play Black Metal with themes like Death, Satanism and a Negativist and Misanthropic view on mankind.

In 2012 Don The Blasphemer joined the band as the bassist to record the album A Sombra da Morte.
