Eddie Ojeda is best known for playing guitar in American heavy/hair metal band Twisted Sister. He released his first solo album, Axes 2 Axes, in late 2005. The album features guest appearances by Ronnie James Dio, Dee Snider, Joe Lynn Turner and Rudy Sarzo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eddie+Ojeda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
