Silence The Martyr is an Atlanta-based band that fuses black metal, death metal, thrash, and touches of avant-garde together. They began as a side project for brothers, Donovan and Collin Cox, who have played in Atlanta metal scene for for over seventeen years in various bands. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Silence+the+Martyr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
