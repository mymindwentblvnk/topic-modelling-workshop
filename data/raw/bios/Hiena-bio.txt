A band formed in Porto (Portugal) in November 2005 with members that used to play with or currently play in other bands like ZEN, BLIND ZERO, YELLOW W VAN, JORGE PALMA, OIOAI, GRACE, TRON, CARABINAS, WOOFER, RUBBER SESSIONS, PEACE REVOLUTION, BALLA, BULLET...... In November 2005 the band recorded 10 songs, 6 of wich are in the only record edited at this time wich is an EP called "Sink it All".
The songs contained in the EP are:
Crash
Sink It All 
In Our Dreams  
Trouble  
It Could Be Funny  
It Will Finally Be Madness

The band finished activities in August 2007.
