I Breathe Spears were a metal band from the south coast of the UK. They played a handful of shows in their short-lived time together releasing a split EP alongside Chickenhawk and With Scissors through Southampton based Millipede Records. 

I Breathe Spears were -

Luke Semlekan-Tansey (Stee) - Vocals
Chris Edkins (Fanny) - Guitar
Pete Newberry - Bass
Jamie Rankine (Hag) - Drums
