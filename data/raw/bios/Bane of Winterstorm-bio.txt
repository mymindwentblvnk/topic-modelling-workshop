An Australian power metal band formed in 2009 under the name Winterstorm; which they changed  to Bane of Winterstorm in 2011 to avoid confusion with the many bands of the same name. They describe their sound as dark symphonic metal, and state that their influences include bands such as Blind Guardian, Behemoth, Dimmu Borgir, Dragonland, Dragonlord, Epica, Fleshgod Apocalypse, Howard Shore, Iced Earth, Luca Turilli, Nightwish, Rhapsody, Sylosis, Symphony X, Voyager.
Band member Anthony J. Finch does all of the orchestration as well as the guitars and vocals.
Chris Themelco of Orpheus Omega and Eye Of The Enemy used to play guitars for Bane Of Winterstorm, until he left in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bane+of+Winterstorm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
