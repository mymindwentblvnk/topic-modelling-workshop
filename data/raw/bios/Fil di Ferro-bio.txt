An Italian heavy metal band formed in 1979 in Turin, Piedmont.  After 3 full-length albums in the 80's/early 90's, the band went on hiatus before reuniting in the 2000's and releasing the comeback full-length It Will Be Passion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fil+Di+Ferro">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
