Breathe Electric was an electropop/MySpace pop/powerpop artist from Chicago, Illinois. Started by Grant Harris in 2007, Breathe Electric released 2 EPs (Emotion in 2009, and Lovestruck in 2010), and one album (Honestly in 2008) before changing his name to Goldhouse in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Breathe+Electric">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
