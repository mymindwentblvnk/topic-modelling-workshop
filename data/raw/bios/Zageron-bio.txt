There are 2 artists named Zageron:
1. A crust band from Vilnius, Lithuania

2. Zageron (Adam) is an electronic music producer. He began producing music in April 2007, after joining the popular rhythm gaming site Flash Flash Revolution. 

Adam's musical passion is largely influenced by: soundTeMP, Grant Kirhope, David Wise, Yannis Brown, and 菅野よう子 (Yoko Kanno). Adam first began to appreciate music during his first session playing the Fungi Forest level of Donkey Kong 64 (Fall 2001), the impact of the levels soundtrack triggered a massive musical interest. The next big bang of musical influence hit Adam in 2004 when he began playing the MMORPG Ragnarok Online. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zageron">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
