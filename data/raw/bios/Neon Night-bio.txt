Neon Night was a heavy metal band from Elverum, Norway. The band formed in 1984 with Jo Dølbakken on drums, Erik Busk on bass, Øystein Nysæter on guitars and with singer Leif Knashaug (ex-Spiral Architect, ex-Anesthesia, Twisted into Form).
The band released a demo tape in 1984 and a self-titled album in 1987 before their split up in the same year. In 2010, they reformed for a one night (Aug. 14th) only reunion gig in Elverum. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Neon+night">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
