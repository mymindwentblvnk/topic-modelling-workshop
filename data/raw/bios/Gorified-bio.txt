Gorified is a Goregrind/Grindcore band from Bengaluru, India. They formed in 2004 and are signed to Sevared Records.

Current Line-Up:

Charles Firman Rozario - Vocals
Ganesh B.S - Guitars, Bass
Guyome - Drums 

Former members:
Mahran S. Ali - Drums
