Frozen Forest is a Croatian Black metal band founded in 2005. They released one demo "The Final Eclipse" in 2007 and EP "Demonic Horns Rise" in 2009.

Mihovil "Frost" Konečni - Guitars, Vocals 
Danijel "Delirium" Delibašić - Bass
Matko "Klanje" Staudacher - Drums



There is another band with the same name, from USA. They released three demos during 2005 and 2006, and a split album in 2007. According to Metal Archives their status is split-up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Frozen+Forest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
