Black Metal from Slovakia in the vein of dark, nature-inspired music like Drudkh, Hate Forest, Astrofaes...
It is a one man project of a Slovak artist named P.L. Guest vocalist is Beelphegor from Hromovlad.
In April 2008 Toreva issued their first demo named "Skryte Brany Do Neznamych Svetov..." (profi tape with B/W cover, limited to 150 copies). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toreva">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
