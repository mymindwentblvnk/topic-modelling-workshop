Melodic Black Metal band from Lamer Winkel, Germany consisting of:

Katrin Loffler - Vocals
Eugen Muller - Vocals
Bernd Heigl - Bass
Christian Mahr - Guitars
Franz Eidenschink - Drums
Martin Maurer - Keyboards

The band currently has two full length albums out:

Visions of Darkness (2003)
The Path (2006/2007)*

*The Path was originally self released by the band in 2006, and then officially released in 2007.
