Trenchgrinder is a death metal band from Brooklyn, NY with grind and crust influences.  The band was formed in late 2010 after the demise of New York crust/metal stalwarts Atakke.  Trenchgrinder contains members of Atakke, Mutant Supremacy, Skullshitter, Gang Signs, and Anicon and released their first demo in October of 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trenchgrinder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
