Mixing a wide array of influences ranging from jazz fusion to grindcore into a mold of traditional metal, thrash and death metal, Anatomy of I brings a breath of fresh air into the metal genre.

Anatomy Of I is the brainchild of vocalist/guitarist Michael Dorrian, with longtime friends Dirk Verbeuren(SCARVE, SOILWORK) on drums and Steve Di Giorgio (SADUS, DEATH, TESTAMENT) on bass to complete the line-up.



Line-up:

Michael Dorrian : Guitars & Vocals
Steve Di Giorgio : Bass
Dirk Verbeuren : Drums
