PROMILE is a band from Pardubice, CZECH REPUBLIC and was founded in 2000. Band plays rock style with impacts of punk and metal…style can be called like „crazy rock“ or „punk-metal“. Lyrics are about alcohol, women, life style, global and social probléme, life…about everything. Band recorded (own costs) EP Stará Vránová (2002), CD Těhotný chlapi (2003), SP Buran city (2003), EP Podivnej sen (2006), CD Thrash PONK Punk (2007), CD ...a dááávej! (2011) and SP Singl 2013 (2013).
Current line-up: Bart (bass, lead vocal), Bogas (guitar), Cihla (drums).
