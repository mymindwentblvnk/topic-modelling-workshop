Coma Cinema is Mat Cothran, a musician from South Carolina.   He has been writing and performing music since 2005, self recording three albums on different machines in a variety of basements, bedrooms, and abandoned locales. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coma+Cinema">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
