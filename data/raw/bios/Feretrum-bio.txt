Feretrum were a Spanish Death Metal band made up of Rafa (Zombie Industries) - Vocals, Roger Villalta - Guitars, Maes Brenes - Bass, and Juan - Drums. The name Feretrum is Latin for coffin. They released two demos The Return (1990) and One Step From the Dark (1991) before releasing their first and only full release From Far Beyond in 1992.
