There are 2 current bands with the name Inoculation.

1. Inoculation from Cleveland, Ohio, as a death metal band. Formed in 2011 and actively playing shows all across Ohio.  Currently 2 demos have been released, and they can be found on Sevared Records.

2. Inoculation was a brazillian death metal band. Formed in 1992, they released 4 raw and brutal demo tapes before disbanding. The final line-up before disbanding consisted of Marcelo Benati ex-Impetuous Rage on drums, Guilherme Saldanha guitarist for Incrust and vocalist Zulbert Buery of Headhunter D.C.

'Victim Of Imperfection' 1994  
'Germins In Manifestation' 1995  
'Wake Up To See...' 2000  
'Christophobic' 2002

Other members:
Daniel Arjones - Bass
Max Gomes - Drums
