Rawkfist is a female-fronted symphonic metal band from southern Germany, formed in 2006.

They released their first full-length album 'Stories' in 2007 and followed up with 'Gardens of Elysia' in 2009.

The band's third album, 'Chryseus',  was released on March 4, 2011.

On the August 3rd, 2011, the band split due to internal difficulties.
