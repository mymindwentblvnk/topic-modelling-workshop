Bloodrose was a black metal band from Finland that was formed in 2000. Vocalist Jari Haapasalo's other bands include Scent of Flesh and Solium XI. The band released one full-length album entitled Into Oblivion in 2002 before disbanding. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloodrose">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
