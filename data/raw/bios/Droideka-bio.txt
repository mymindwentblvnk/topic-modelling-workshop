Droideka, real name Ellis Carter, is a music producer hailing from Cambridge, UK. Exposed to dnb via local nights, the Portuguese Padawan started uploading tracks onto YouTube, with ‘Get Hyper’ receiving four million views before reaching 13 on the iTunes chart and getting airtime support from Radio 1’s Zane Lowe and Scott Mills. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Droideka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
