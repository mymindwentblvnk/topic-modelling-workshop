Genital Grinder is a french brutal death metal band formed in 1996. Their line-up includes Balrog from The Order of Apollyon (he was also former Garwall singer and Aborted's guitar player.). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Genital+Grinder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
