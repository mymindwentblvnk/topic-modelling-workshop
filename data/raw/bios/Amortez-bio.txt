Amortez is a Czech melodic-black metal band from Brno foundet in 2003 by Richie and Lord Izual. Current line-up : Axsir - drums ; Richie - guitar and second vocals ; Lord Izual - main vocals ; Bigger - guitar and second vocals ; Radkoz - bass.
