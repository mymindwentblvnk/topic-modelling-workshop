Big Grams is the self-titled debut EP by American hip-hop trio Big Grams, which consists of rapper Big Boi and electronic rock duo Phantogram. The EP was released on September 25, 2015, by Epic Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Big+Grams">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
