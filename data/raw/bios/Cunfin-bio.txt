Cunfin is a black metal band from Switzerland created in 2010. Lyrical themes include Hate, Homeland and Extermination. All lyrics of Cunfin are written in Rhaeto-Romance. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cunfin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
