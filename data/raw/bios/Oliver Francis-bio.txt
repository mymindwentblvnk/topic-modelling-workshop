Alexander Francis Kelly (born June 12, 1992), professionaly known as Oliver Francis, is an American rapper and producer from Columbia, Missouri. His biggest songs include “Ahhyeahh”, “Wwaavvyy” and “Gemini”.

Utilizing the internet and platforms such as YouTube​ and SoundCloud​, Oliver has garnered a dedicated following and a number of 1 million+ streamed songs. As of February of 2018, Oliver had over 208K subscribers on YouTube​ and over 100K total followers on his three SoundCloud​ profiles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oliver+Francis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
