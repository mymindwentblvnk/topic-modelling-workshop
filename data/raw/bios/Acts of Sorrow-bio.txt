Acts of Sorrow are a 5 piece metal band from the Wirral in England. They started out in October 2008 and started gigging in March 2009. Acts of Sorrow have shared the stage with bands such as Napalm Death, Annotations of An Autopsy, Trigger the Bloodshed, Viatrophy and many more. As of 2010, new bassist Alex Joyce (formerally in Unsanctum) has joined. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Acts+Of+Sorrow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
