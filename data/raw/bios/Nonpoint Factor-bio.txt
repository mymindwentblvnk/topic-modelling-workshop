Nonpoint Factor were true frontrunners in the mid - 90's aggro metal race. After gaining a strong underground buzz and several high profile shows (including opening slots for such metal stalwarts as Overkill, Pro-Pain, Circle Of Dust and others) the band gradually began to change and ultimately became known simply as Nonpoint, with only drummer Robert Rivera remaining.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nonpoint+Factor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
