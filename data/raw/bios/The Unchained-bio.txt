The Unchained is a Canadian rock band that formed in Calgary, Alberta in 2007. The band consists of Harry Kim on lead guitar, Michael Hintz as singer and keyboardist, Steve Edgerton on drums, Abraham Le Roux on bass and Brendan Sillito on rhythm guitar. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Unchained">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
