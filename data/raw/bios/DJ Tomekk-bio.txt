Tomasz Kuklicz a.k.a. DJ Tomekk (born October 11, 1976 in Kraków, Poland) is a Polish-born, German-based hip hop DJ. His hits feature rappers like Ice-T, Fatman Scoop, Khia, Xzibit, Fler, Sido, Kurupt, Lil' Kim, KRS-One, Flavor Flav and GZA.

DJ Tomekk's first album was called Return of Hip Hop and was followed by Beat of Life and Numma Eyns (Number One).

He took part in the third series of German reality show Ich bin ein Star – Holt mich hier raus! in 2008 but was evicted by RTL Television for making a Hitler salute and singing "Deutschland Über Alles" in the lobby of his hotel shortly before the team left for the jungle, despite his insistences that he was merely joking. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Tomekk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
