They are a brutal death metal band from Estonia.  They formed in 2014.  They're named after a medical procedure that involves the surgical removal or opening of the hymen.  They released a promo in 2014.  They released the Human Feces Collector single in 2015.  They released a full-length album in 2015 entitled Some Necrophiles Having Sex with Naked Autopsied Bodies in the Morgue. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hymenotomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
