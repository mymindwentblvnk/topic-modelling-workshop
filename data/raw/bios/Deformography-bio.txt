death metal band formed in 2002 in CA, didn't play live until October 2004. members gio- guitar and vocals, ebraheem- drums, andrew- vocals. first demo recorded December 2004, included tracks 'Feasting Upon The Retarded' and 'Send Me Your Fetus' and also the cover song 'Wasting Away' originally by Nailbomb. in 2005 they searched for a full time bassist, finding the second gio (gio buitron) and continued playing live. recorded another demo in 2006, and another in 2010. In 2010 the band changed to 3 members Gio, Gio, and Ebraheem. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deformography">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
