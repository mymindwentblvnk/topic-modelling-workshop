Dean "Clea" Brkić (Osmi Putnik,  Pandora) is a singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, arranger and producer from Split, Croatia.
In the year 2000 he started the work on his ambitious project, a conceptual instrumental rock / metal album The Quarterworlds of Fantasia, for which he wrote the concept, all music, lyrics for narrations & themes.
The album was finally released in December 2008 through Serbian label One Records.
On this album Dean played guitars, bass, keyboards, mandolins and also spoke the narrations, but the main guitar work was done by the respected Bosnian guitar virtuoso Emir Hot (ex Neon Knights and Southern Storm), with some guest appearances from Ivan Mihaljević (Hard Time) and Muris Varajic.
