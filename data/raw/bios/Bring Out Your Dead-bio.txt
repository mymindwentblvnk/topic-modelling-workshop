BRING OUT YOUR DEAD are a 5-piece metal band derived from the centre of the United Kingdom in the Isle Of Man. The band’s name is inspired by a line in the film “Monty Python and the Holy Grail”. Having released their debut single, “The Lady? The Tramp!”, for free on 12th December 2012, the band are currently working on the release of their first EP to be released mid-Summer, accompanied by tours.
