Feast For The Crows are a Melodic Death Metal / Metalcore band from Marburg, Hesse, Germany. 

Current line-up
Simon Kollat - Vocals
Sebastian Kollat - Guitar (Antagaroth)
Max Hufnagel - Guitar (Antagaroth)
Stefan Kraft - Bass (Nailedd)
Moritz Jung - Drums (Nailedd, Hyems)

They are currently signed to Bastardized Recordings, and their debut album 'When All Seems To Be Burned' was released in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Feast+For+The+Crows">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
