1) Austerity is a one-man ambient funeral doom band from Italy which also incorporates drone elements.  The band is composed of Zavo, who plays all of the instruments and provides vocals.  Austerity has released one demo, Perpertua Nox Dormienda Est, in 2005, and released the self-titled debut full-length in February of 2009. 

2) Austerity is a death metal / grindcore band from Finland. They released their first demo Portrait Of A Dying Man in 2015.
