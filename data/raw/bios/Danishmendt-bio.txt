Danishmendt is a French band born in 2004. Their first line-up recorded a live demo. After a few months, new musicians joined, since then, Danishmendt released an Ep, L'Homme est un animal qui a trahi in 2006 and an album, Eaux-Fortes, in 2007.

A live performance recorded in 2007 can be downloaded through their myspace page.
