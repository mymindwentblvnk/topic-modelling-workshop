There are three artistic proposals sharing this name.

The first one is  Massachusetts based sound artist Asher Thal-Nir, who has been making and releasing records since late 2005. He has appeared on labels such as 12k, Leeraum, Conv and Laboratoire Moderne. 

The second, is a 'california-based modern rock band', as they put it in their website.

Finally, Asher is also the moniker of a bassline rap artist from Newport in the UK.
