Horsebeach are an indie, guitar/dream pop band formed in Manchester, UK. With the sunny outlook of American garage rock, such as Real Estate, added to the damp feel of Manchester, Horsebeach are glimmering pop perfection. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Horsebeach">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
