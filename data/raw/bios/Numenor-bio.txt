There are two bands with, more or less, same name: Númenor  and Numenor (France), but for many last.fm spotify both bands as they have the same name. 

Númenor has released the debut full-length ‘’Colossal Darkness’’ (Stygian Crypt Prodactions,2013). The lyrics are based on fantasy literature: Michael Moorcock, J.J.R. Tolkien, Robert E.Howard. The band was found in 2009. Also, before the debut album band has released several EPs, singles and some splits as well.
