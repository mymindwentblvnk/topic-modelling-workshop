Saskatchewan METAL!! 

Based in Saskatoon, HFX has just released their debut album THE RISING!  Available on iTunes, and at evelmerch.com.

Formed in late 2007 by Madison, Geoff, and Adam, HFX was rounded out by Chris and Jorden in early 2009.  After nearly two years of off and on writing, HFX entered the studio in September 2009 in Saskatoon.  The Rising was released on May 20, 2010.  HFX will be touring in support of The Rising during the summer months of 2010 and beyond.  THE Rising has just begun!!! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Harmful+Effects">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
