MORTEL is a six-piece band from Kremenchug (Ukraine) starting the way on Melodic Death Metal/Thrash/Alternative scene
The band was create in February of 2011.
Debut full-song 'Nightmare' was meet the world in winter 2012.

Enjoy US!
