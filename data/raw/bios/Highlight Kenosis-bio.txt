Highlight Kenosis is a progressive / symphonic metal band from Romania, influenced by bands like Dream Theater, Nightwish and Muse. They are one of the most interesting young rock bands from Romania, coming with an original and complex sound and very energetic live shows.

The band now has 4 members:
Oana Stoica - voice
Matei Damian Ulmu - guitar
Adi Sardi - bass
Dan Alexandru - drums

Ex-members: 
Dragos Serbanescu - bass
John Ciurea - drums
Alexandru Berehoi - keyboards

They have released their debut album, Glowing, in 2009. Before they’ve recorded a five songs demo, “I Found Myself In You”, available for download on their myspace page: http://www.myspace.com/highlightrockband.
