PJ Geissinger aka Starkey has been throwing it down in Philly for a number of years on the live pa and dj fronts. He was the 2005 Mid-Atlantic and North American Laptop Battle Champion, and has released music on the Slit Jockey label stateside and the Werk Discs label in the UK. He has performed with many of the key figures in the idm, breakcore, grime and dubstep scenes around the world. Starkey has an array of production releases in the pipeline, so stay locked. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Starkey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
