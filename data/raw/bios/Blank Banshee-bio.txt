Blank Banshee is the project of producer Patrick Driscoll from Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Originally from Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada, he was also a member of the band Shinjuku Mad. He began releasing music as Blank Banshee in 2012 through Bandcamp. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blank+Banshee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
