There are numerous bands called Alatyr.

1) A Gothic metal band from Bristol, United Kingdom. Their first single 'Cursed' was released in 2014.

2) An ambient pagan metal band from Belarus. They released one demo called Agoj in 1999.

3) An ambient pagan metal band from Slovakia. They released one album, Alatyr, in 2007. Members were Igric, Striibog.
