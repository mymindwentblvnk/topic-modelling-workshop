L.P.G. is the name of an underground hip hop duo from Los Angeles, California made up of cousins Dax Reynosa (sometimes known as Theory) and Albert Olguin (aka Jurny Big).  They are the founders of the Tunnel Rats crew.  LPG stands for "Living Proof of Grace." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/L.P.G.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
