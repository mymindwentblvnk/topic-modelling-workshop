There are two bands named Cells:

1) Cells - thrash metal band from Slovakia (Neslusa, Zilina) formed in 2001.

2) Cells - Rock band from Lao People's Democratic Republic (Laos). Cells second Lao album 'Saew' was released by Thai label GMM / Grammy in 2007 and received top 20 radio airplay in Bangkok for hit singles 'Waan' and 'Leuk Leuk'. 

Music video's for 'Waan' and 'Leuk Leuk' have also proved very popular on Thai music video channels and Cells have made several well received tours of Thailand to promote the album.
