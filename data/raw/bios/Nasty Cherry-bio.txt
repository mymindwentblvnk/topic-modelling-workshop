Nasty Cherry is an English-American pop band, formed in 2019. The group was assembled by English singer Charli XCX and manager Emmie Lichtenberg, which was documented in the Netflix series I'm with the Band: Nasty Cherry. The group consists of lead vocalist Gabbriette Bechtel, guitarist Chloe Chaidez from Kitten, drummer Deborah Knox-Hewson, and bassist Georgia Somary, also known as Poodle. They are signed to Charli XCX's imprint Vroom Vroom Recordings and Asylum Records.

They released their first extended play, Season 1, on November 22, 2019, to coincide with the release of I'm with the Band.
