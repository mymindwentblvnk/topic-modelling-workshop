Orestes is a death metal band from Jakarta, Indonesia

Orestes was formed in 2007, consisted of Herdityo Wibowo, Florentino Ariza, Johan Franklin, Fachri Bayu Wicaksono, and Gemuruh Adhityatama. 

Influenced by death metal bands like Deicide, Cannibal Corpse, Origin, and Spawn of Possession.
