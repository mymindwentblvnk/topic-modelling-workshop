End My Sorrow is from Grenaa in Denmark, they were founded in 97' by Guitarist/growler Christian H. B. Jensen and drummer Jesper Skousen, it started out as gothic doom inspired, but has since evolved into a unique style of it's own, with both female vocals, growl and melodic guitars build on a fundament of a tight rythm group. 

End my sorrow are:

Melissa Bjerregaard - Vocals
Christian H. B. Jensen - Guitars/Vocals
Kristian "Snade" Snedker - Guitars
Jens K. Andersen - Bas
