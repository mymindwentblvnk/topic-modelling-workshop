LUH (Lost Under Heaven) was formed in 2012 and consists of Ellery James Roberts (formerly of WU LYF) and Ebony Hoorn. Their debut album "Spiritual Songs for Lovers to Sing" was released in May 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Luh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
