Blind Greed is heavy/thrash metal band from USA, Tuscon in Arizona. The band was formed in 2000.
Current line-up consist of: Clyde - Vocals, Mike Bright - Bass, Rob Fletcher - Guitar, Jim Casner - Drums, Brad Coletti - Guitar . So far the band has released 2 studio albums. The first one is called BLIND GREED and was released in 2001 and the second one is The Almighty Dollar released in 2003
