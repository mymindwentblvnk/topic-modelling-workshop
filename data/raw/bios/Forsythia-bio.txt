There are two bands with the name Forsythia:

1.  Forsythia was founded in the beginning of the year 2010 by Mika Auranen, Samuli Saari and Lauri Annala. During the same year Mirko Lehtinen and Aarne Hyvönen joined the band. Recording of the first demo "Your Delusion" began in December and it was published in 4.5.2011.

Line-up:
Mika Auranen - Vocals, 
Lauri Annala - Guitars, Backing Vocals, 
Mirko Lehtinen - Guitars, 
Aarne Hyvönen - Bass, 
Samuli Saari - Drums

2.  Forsythia is an experimental rock band formed in 2012 in Pasadena, MD, USA.
