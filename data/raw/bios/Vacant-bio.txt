Hailing from South East London, Vacant is an artist recognized for his emotive style in UK underground music. He uses the city and the daily experiences of people as inspiration to create his ambient take on UK Garage. Paving his way through the music scene, his combination of dark vibes and emotional backstory make the music he produces among the most highly anticipated in the genre.
