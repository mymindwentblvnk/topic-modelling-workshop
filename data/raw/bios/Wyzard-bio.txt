1. Wyzard was a heavy metal band that formed in 1983 in San Antonio, Texas. The lineup consisted of vocalist Gilbert Grant Guzman, guitarists Michael Angelo Valenzuela and Marc Anthony Garcia, bassist John Anthony Alvarado, and drummer Rene Cox. They released a single EP titled 'Knights of Metal' on Pazuzu Records in 1984. It was later reissued with a bonus track by Perris Records.
