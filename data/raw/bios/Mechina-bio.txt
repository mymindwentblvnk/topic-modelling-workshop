Though Mechina's history is quite short, their progression in the Chicago music scene has grown to impressive heights since 2004. Over their lifespan, the band has been part of multiple compilation CDs and released their first full length masterpiece, "The Assembly of Tyrants". With multiple minds combining many unique and unorthodox styles of music, combining industrial atmospheres, precision inspired metal, and epic orchestrations, Mechina has created their own style of metal. Music that not only creates an atmosphere around the listener, but also displays a very powerful aura of intensity. Mechina strives to create a deeper and more intelligent form of music. A new age of music has been born.
