Violence Spread is a Thrash Metal band from Trentino, Italy. 
Officially born in late 2008 from the ashes of a cover band called Hatemachine.
They already have some songs that will hopefully be released on a Demo in late 2009 or early 2010.
The Band is formed by DD (Vocals), Deriu (Guitar), Chene (Bass Guitar), Vender (Drums), and Ric (Guitar). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Violence+Spread">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
