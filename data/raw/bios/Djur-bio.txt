Division DJUR was formed in 2002 (for that time it was called Навь) by Zorn and Krukh. There were few rehearsals and home recordings after which band has stoped any activity for a two years. In the end of 2004 division takes its name DJUR and begin to play with new strenght. During this time the line-up has changed. For now there are four members in the band: Zorn - guitars, vocals; Swarm - bass; Troskjender - drums; Kil - guitars. November 2008 - due to some personal reasons Kil lef the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Djur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
