The band was formed back in 1995 as "Bloodshed" and released two demos under that name:"Warpath" in 1997 and "Fairyland" in 1998. When the name changed to Everdome in 2000,"Fairyland" was released again as"Promo 2000". The band released its first full length album in 2004,called "Tales beyond oblivion" mainly influenced by power metal,exploring though many other metal genres. After that release and till the present day,the band consists of two dedicated members:Andreas Sotiropoulos and Spiros Haralambous.Now,the second full length album is about to be released, a concept album called "Afterbirth" featuring Mats Leven as a guest vocalist and mastered at Fascination Street StudiosSweden. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Everdome">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
