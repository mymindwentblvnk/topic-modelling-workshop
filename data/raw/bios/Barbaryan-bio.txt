Barbaryan is a German NS/black metal act from 2004. 

Their lyrics are mostly about war, paganism and nationalism. 

Barbaryan's current line-up excists out of:
Runenwolf - Guitar, bass
Raginhar - Vocals, drums (Vikingblood) 

So far, Barbaryan has released the following:

- Des Toten Tatenruhm Demo, 2006  
- Gram / Das Lied von Blut und Eisen Full-length, 2008
