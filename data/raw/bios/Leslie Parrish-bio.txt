Leslie Parrish is another one of Clara Moroni's aliases.

Clara Moroni is one of the founders of Delta. She has been a vocalist for labels such as Asia Records, Time Records and has also released a solo album called Ten Worlds which contains tracks that are the alternative rock style. Clara Moroni as a vocalist and writer is considered one of the Queens of Eurobeat. Her first track in eurobeat was Help Me under the alias Mela. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leslie+Parrish">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
