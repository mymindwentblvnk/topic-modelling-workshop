Flesh Deformation is a Finnish "schizo" death/grind band. The band was formed in 2003 and has released two demos, "Implore Dislimbed" in 2003 and "Humiliated" in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flesh+Deformation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
