Caos (spanish for «chaos») was a Punk Rock band from Chile. Their members were Lucho, Cabezon, Mota and Gerardo. Their album «¿Caos o Democracia?» was included on Alerce Records's Compilation, «Clásicos del Punk Chileno». <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Caos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
