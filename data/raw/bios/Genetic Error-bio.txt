a thrash metal band from Quebec, Canada that was formed in 1987. They released three demos - "Toxic Planet" in 1988, "In Search of Power" in 1989 and "Superficial" in 1995. They had split up in 1990, then reformed in 1994 and then again split up in 1996.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Genetic+Error">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
