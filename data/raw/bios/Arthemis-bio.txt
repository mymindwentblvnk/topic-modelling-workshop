Arthemis are a melodic Power Metal band who started up in 1994. Originally called "Nemhesis", they established their line up in 1998 and it consists of Andrea Martongelli, Matteo Galbier, Alessio Garavello and Matteo Ballottari. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arthemis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
