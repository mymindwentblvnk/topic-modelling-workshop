Harmasar is a band from Chișinău, Moldova, born in 2013. They play an energetic style of folk metal, having influences from Bucovina, Arkona, Finntroll, Korpiklaani, but also developing an original style through the use of Moldovan folk music. They one album for now, launched in 2016, called Din Pământ (From Earth). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Harmasar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
