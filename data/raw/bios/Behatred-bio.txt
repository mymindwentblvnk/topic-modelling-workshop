Behatred is a portuguese Metal band. 
Created in 2005, and counting with ex-members of bands such as End Of Hope and Blacksunrise, Behatred plays a unique mix of Black metal, death metal, hardcore and symphonic metal elements, decribing himselfs simply as "metal".
An EP called "Black Lotus" was realeased in 2009.
