So the journey goes back to 2007 when George and Nick band mates from a band called Perennial End decide to compose dark ,heavy music,at the same year came the birth of As I Suffer Silently first known as Guilty Thoughts and now with the existing name.At that time Nick left the project for dealing with Perennial End,George start to compose material and the result of his effort was the first album named Mourning for her loss ,four years later came the birth of the second album named Memories from the past and then George Davelas(Deceptive Incarnation) was the second involved at the line up of the project , a year after the third homonymous effort was recorded at bands home studio as also and the two first albums.Now we have open rags to sail for new ideas,be tuned
