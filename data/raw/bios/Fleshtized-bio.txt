Fleshtized was a brutal death metal band from Huntsville, Alabama and formed in 1996.  They released their first demo Tower of Pain in 1998.  In 2000, they released an EP called Divide and Conquer.  They released their only full-length album Here Among Thorns in 2001.   They disbanded in the year they released their only album, but then they reunited in 2004.  They released Promo 2006 in 2006.  After that, they disbanded again.  It is not known when they disbanded the second time.  Members of Fleshtized went on to form Chaos Inception in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fleshtized">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
