Azif is a female-fronted Black Metal band hailing from Venezuela, and formed in 2003. The Lyrical themes deal mostly with protest and reality. Their first release came in August Of 2005 "En Honor a Simón", followed by "Edición Limitada" in December of 2006, and featured 2 songs from the previous release. In September of 2007, "Esquizofrenia", their first EP, was released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Azif">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
