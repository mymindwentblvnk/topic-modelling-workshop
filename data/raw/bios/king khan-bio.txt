Arish Ahmad Khan (born January 24, 1977), better known by his stage name King Khan, is a Canadian musician. He is best known as the frontman of King Khan & the Shrines and for being one half of The King Khan & BBQ Show. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/King+Khan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
