Symposion is a metal badn from Copenhagen, Denmark.

The band was formed in 1996 by the ex-Vocalist/Guitarist Jacob Aagaard, The Drummer Jimmy Christensen and the Lead Guitarist Flemming Svendsen (R.I.P.).

In the fall 2006 Nino Drejer (Rhy/Lead Guitar) joined the band. By spring 2008 it was time for Magnus (Bass) to be involved with the band and since spring 2009 Martin Leth (Vocals) has been a part of Symposion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Symposion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
