There is more than one artist with this name:

1. Mayla was a Latvian death metal/deathcore band from Daugavpils, Latvia, formed in 2013 and named after rare Sanskrit name referring to a "religious gathering". The members had rich experience in various music styles, starting from jazz and electronic, finishing with symphonic metal.

The band split up in 2021.

Final lineup:

Vadims Mamonovs - vocals;
Vladislavs Larionovs - guitar;
Juris Paura (Sacramental) - guitar;
Dmitrijs Panfilovs (Puffin) - bass;
Dmitrijs Suhanovskis (Asthma, Begotten) - drums.

Former members:

Aleksandrs Maklakovs - guitar.

Discography:

Бездна (2020)
