Zephani Jong, better known as Zeph, born on April 18, 1999, is originally from Maryland but has recently moved to Los Angeles, California. 
Zeph's debut single Forever & Always was released in 2018. In the year 2021 she released her first official EP 'Scared of Everything'. 
Other EPs are 'goodnight moon (demos)', 'ache (demos)', and 'crush (demos)', which all include demos of short songs she originally posted on platforms such as youtube and soundcloud. While her spotify has few things from Zeph, she has compiled a playlist on her youtube channel titled 'little songs by me' which include a few short songs and demos of songs she has yet to release a full version of. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zeph">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
