An Old school Death/Black/Thrash metal band formed in 2006 in Singapore, They have released a demo (self titled) in 2008, and a full-length in 2010 titled "Apocalyptic Anti-Human Annihilation" with Infernal Kaos Productions. and also a split with Crown ov Horns in 2010 titled "Glorification ov War, Death & Destruction" with Ars Funebris Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Devour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
