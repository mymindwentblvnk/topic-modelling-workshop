Wainas is a one man project from Lappeenranta, Finland, formed in 2008 by Kanttori Wainaa.  His lyrics deal with darkness and human madness and their consequences, and to date he has released two EPs and one album entitled Tuhon maa. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wainas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
