The Ocean Walker is a post metal/death metal band centered at Geneva College in Beaver Falls, PA, consisting of guitarists Adam Cubakovic, Robert Nolte, and Tom Baker, bassist Nathan Shoup, keyboardist Anna Kundus, drummer Matthew Purnell, and vocalist Josh Mauro. Formed in 2010, the band has released one EP (Into the Depths) and is currently writing material to be recorded and released in 2012 featuring the full lineup.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Ocean+Walker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
