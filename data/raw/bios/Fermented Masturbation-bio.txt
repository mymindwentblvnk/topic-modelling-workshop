Fermented Masturbation is a slam death metal band from Oslo, Norway. It is a side-project of the Funderud twin brothers from Kraanium with Benjamin Wingmark, Dutch guitarist Floor van Kuijk and Italian drummer Alex Venders

In September of 2014, they put out their debut release entitled Perverted Slamicide through Ungodly Ruins Productions. They supported the release with an appearance at Slamming Brutality Fest.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fermented+Masturbation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
