Hevinkelium is a side project of a pop/rock cover band Super Trouper that features e.g. former members of Finland's very first rap band "Pääkköset". Vocals are handled by Taage Laiho from finnish rock band Kilpi.

They make heavy metal versions from Finnish hymns.

LAST KNOWN LINE-UP:

Taage Laiho - Vocals (Kilpi, ex-Altaria)
Amos Siekkinen - Bass
Ladis Robles - Organ
Pastori Lehtinen - Guitar
Sakkeus - Drums
