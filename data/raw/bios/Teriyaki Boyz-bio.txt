The Teriyaki Boyz are a hip hop group from Tokyo, Japan signed to The Neptunes Star Trak Entertainment. The five members are Ilmari, Ryo-Z, Verbal, Wise and Nigo, the owner of the A Bathing Ape clothing company. They have released two albums so far, 2005's Beef or Chicken? and 2009's Serious Japanese. They frequently work with American artists such as Pharrell Williams, Kanye West, Big Sean and Chris Brown and are probably most commonly known for their song Tokyo Drift (Fast & Furious) which was the theme song for the movie The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Teriyaki+Boyz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
