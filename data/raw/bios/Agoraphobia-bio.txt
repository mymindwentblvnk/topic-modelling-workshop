Agoraphobia is:
1. A German death metal band formed in 1989. Released a demo and an EP before splitting. Reformed almost 14 years later, releasing "Sick", their debut album in 2005. They have since changed name to New Born Hate.
2. An idm/dubstep/drum and bass/ambient producer from Newcastle-upon-Tyne.
3. A one-man gorenoise band from South Korea, by Yuying Lee of Sulsa and Gonguri. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agoraphobia">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
