Ire Wolves are a three-piece sludge, post-metal band from Duluth, MN. To date, they have released 4 albums: their debut "The Ascetic" in 2014, their follow up sophomore release "Heirs" in March 2017, "Bleeding Aparte" in April 2019, and their latest "Heritage" in July 2022. All releases are available at https://irewolves.bandcamp.com. For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/irewolves. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ire+Wolves">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
