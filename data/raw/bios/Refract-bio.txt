In 2019 a young new talent arised under the name of 'Refract'. With hard striking kicks and powerful melodies he invaded the rawstyle scene.

With tracks like 'Hit Em', 'Can't Breathe' and 'Dangerous Future', Refract slowly but surely works on his hardstyle legacy, while also making a name for him self by playing at events like Live For This in the infamous 'AFAS LIVE' venue.

With his distinctive sound and refreshing hard beats the youngster, signed to Scantraxx Recordz, makes clear he's here to stay. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Refract">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
