Christopher Saint Victor (born November 16, 1995), better known as Flipp Dinero, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Being from Brooklyn, New York, he was later able to connect with another Brooklyn, New York rapper, Joey Badass who later co-signed him along with the record labels Cinematic Music Group, Epic Records, and We the Best Music group. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flipp+Dinero">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
