Cold World Hustlers are 4-1-So-Sic-Ass-Rell, E-Sic and Big Ric. They are from Hunters Point, San Francisco. They debuted in 1992 with Cold Streets. In 1995 they released Iceland on Black Market Records featuring U.D.I, 11/5, Cougnut, Dre Dog, Female Fonk and others. In 1997 they released the compilation Anythang 4 Money. In 2002. Cold World Hustlers were part of the group The Unexpected with 11/5, U.D.I and Big Mack and released U Didn't Know???. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cold+World+Hustlers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
