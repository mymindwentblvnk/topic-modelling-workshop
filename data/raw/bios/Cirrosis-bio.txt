There is at least 2 bands under the name "Cirrosis".

1. Hardcore punk band from Santiago, Chile, formes in the early 90's. Line up were "Hormiga" Patiño (former vocalist of BBS Paranoicos) on vocals, Jorge Azalgado on bass, Daniel Morales and Joaquín Pérez on guitars, and Daniel Jauregui on Drums. In the year 2000 they releasaed their first album, "Alcohol". After some line-up changes, they released their second album, "Cáncer Social", on 2009.
Cirrosis are still active, now as a trio, always with Alex on vocals and now in bass. 

2. Rock Metal band formed on 1991, in Ciudad Obregón, Sonora, México. First line-up were Chuky, Gordo, Cano, Rafa y Clatck. They retires during 1998-1999, and retorned with Chuky, Osunado and Mirame, until their break-up on 2001. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cirrosis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
