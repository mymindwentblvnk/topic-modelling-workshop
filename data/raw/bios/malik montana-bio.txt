Ghawsi Mosa aka Malik Montana (ed. March 11, 1989 in Hamburg) - German-Polish rapper and author of the Afghani origin. Ghawsi Mosa was born in 1989 in German Hamburg. The father comes from Afghanistan, while the mother has Polish-Greek roots. He was raised in Germany, where he graduated from elementary school. When Mosa was 15 years old, he and his brother moved to Poland, settling in Warsaw's Wrzeciona. The stage name Malik Montana was born in 2008. He began to create English-language hip-hop in collaboration with the German rapper Sentino. Even in 2012, his music was created in English. Over time, it evolved into trap and so-called American rap inspired sounds. modern hip-hop, already recorded in Polish . Malik debuted in Polish rap on August 4, 2016 with the clip "Naaajak". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/malik+montana">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
