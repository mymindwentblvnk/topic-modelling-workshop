Young band from Ekaterinburg, founded in 2007 by Anatoly Perlov. Style-basis of band is melodies of doom metal interlaced with math metal rhythms.

"Asphodel is a depressive sound twisted with lacerated technogenic rhymes in a strange harmony, 
it is a thick and oppressive air of vulnerable human feelings"
