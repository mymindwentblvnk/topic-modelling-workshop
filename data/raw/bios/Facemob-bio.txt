Facemob is a Southern U.S. rap group that was put together by former Geto Boy Scarface. The first album, "The Otherside Of The Law", was released on August 6, 1996. The second album, "Silence", was released on November 19, 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Facemob">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
