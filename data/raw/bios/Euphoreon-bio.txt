Euphoreon is a German/New Zealand metal band, originally formed as a solo project band by Matt Summerville from New Zealand in 2009.

The debut Album 'Euphoreon' was released on the 30th of April 2011. It can be downloaded on iTunes, Amazon and Bandcamp. 

Euphoreon are:
Matt Summerville (Lead Guitar, Harsh Vocals)
