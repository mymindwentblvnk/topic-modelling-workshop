German underground black metal from the early 90's, founded in 1992 by Ancient Blasphemic Grave Invocator, of Baxaxaxa and Ungod fame. Fulgor released a rehearsal/promo and three demo tapes during the years 1993 and 1994. The 1995 EP "Eyequinox" released by Germany's Merciless Records was their last release.
