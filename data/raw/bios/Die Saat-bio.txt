Die Saat is a viking black metal band from Thuringia, Germany. The main member Sturm, is also involved in FJOERGYN.
Eosperaz and Tormentor joined Totenburg and Absurd after their period in Die Saat.
The band does not underline ns-political issues like musicians of Totenburg or Absurd.
