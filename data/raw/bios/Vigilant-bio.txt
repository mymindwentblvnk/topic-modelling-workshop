There are more than one band called Vigilant.

1. Vigilant is a 4 member rock group including Jack (bass), Jordan Ferreira (guitar), Padget Nanton III (drums).  > http://www.vigilantband.com

2. Vigilant was a Dutch Thrash-Metal band from the city of Delft.
They released 2 demos, Dramatic Surge 1988 and Opression 1989.

3. Vigilant was a US power metal band from Cincinnati, Ohio. They released their only full length Hellbound in 1991.
