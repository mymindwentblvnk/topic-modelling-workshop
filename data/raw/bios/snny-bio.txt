Born in the African coastal nation of Ivory Coast and raised in Boston, versatile singer, songwriter, and producer Snny melds together bright pop, R&B, electronic, and hip-hop. Based in New York, Snny earned a contract with Glassnote Records based on a track he'd uploaded to his SoundCloud page. His catchy first single, "Young Boy," arrived in early 2017, followed by another single, "Arizona."  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/snny">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
