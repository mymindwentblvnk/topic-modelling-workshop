A Norwegian Doom Metal group that was formed in 2001. The band has released 3 full length albums, 2 extended plays and 1 demo as of 2007, but it's unknown whether or not there are plans to release anything further. 2 of the members are also part of the Norwegian Death Metal band Invocation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Obscenity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
