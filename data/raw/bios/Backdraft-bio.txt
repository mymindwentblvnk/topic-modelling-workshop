Backdraft can be related to:

1. A hard rock/southern rock band from Stockholm, Sweden. They have released three albums: Here To Save You All (2001), The Second Coming (2007) and This Heaven Goes To Eleven (2011).

2. A group making Breakbeat/Dubstep Dance music from Manchester, UK. http://myspace.com/backdraftuk 

3. A Hard Rock/Metal outfit from the Philippines. They have released two albums so far, a self-titled one and the other is The Shadowland Deep.

4. A straight edge hardcore band from The Netherlands featuring member of Spawn and Feeding the Fire.  The band released one 7 inch on Crucial Response Records in 1994. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Backdraft">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
