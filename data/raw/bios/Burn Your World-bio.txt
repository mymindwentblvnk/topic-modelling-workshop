Burn Your World is a metallic hardcore from Winnipeg, Manitoba. The band is currently a part of the Escapist Records roster. Having previously released several recordings on their own - a demo, split release, and "Forced To Hate" 7" - the band released their Escapist debut, the four-song "Vicious Intent" 7"/Digital EP in August of 2013.
