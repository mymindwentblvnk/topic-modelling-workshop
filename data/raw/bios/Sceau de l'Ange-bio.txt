Sceau de l'Ange is a French progressive metal/rock band that was formed in 2003. Sceau de l'Ange means "Seal of the Angel" in French. The band consists of Céline Wallois (vocals), Thomas Safont (guitar), Raphaël Dutreige (guitar), Florent Demaison (bass) and Florian Jacquet (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sceau+de+l%27Ange">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
