SunStroke Project is a Moldovan dance/pop trio formed by Jay Mon (vocals), Anthone Ragoza (violin), Sergei Stepanov (saxophone) and Mc Myslik.

The act took part in the Moldovan national final of the 2009 Eurovision Song Contest with a song called "No Crime" and in 2010 with the songs "Run Away" (feat. Olia Tira) and "Believe". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sunstroke+project">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
