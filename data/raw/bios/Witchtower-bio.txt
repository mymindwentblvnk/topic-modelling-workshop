1.Witchtower is a death/thrash metal band from Germany, formed in 1998. So far they have released four albums: Anthems of Decay (1999),  Under Fallen Wings (2001),  Faster than Death (2005) and Magnitude Triple 6 (2009).

The current line-up is: 
Ralf 'Nunslaughter' Janssen - Vocals
Andre "Schluz" Schulz - Guitar
Benjamin "Benni" Körner - Guitar
Michael "Micha" Braun - Bass
Stefan "Waska" Waskowiak – Drums
