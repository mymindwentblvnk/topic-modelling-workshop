Toxicdeath is a Technical Death Metal group formed in 2000, hailing from Bosnia & Herzegovina.  After few demo CDs, band released debut "Contamination" in 2004. Following the release, they toured and played shows with some important Death Metal acts like Vader, Vital Remains, Behemoth, Decapitated and Sinister. The band is currently searching for a new label to release their upcoming full-length album "Techniques Of Darkness".
