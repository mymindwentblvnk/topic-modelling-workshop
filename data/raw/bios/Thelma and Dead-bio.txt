A straight up in your face big beatdown metal band hailing from Stoke-On-Trent, UK. Their brand of death metal riffs with un-compromising hardcore parts have stirred up a storm in their home town, winning them some important support slots for big name bands making their way to the city, and they will continue to rise. www.myspace.com/thelmaanddead <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thelma+And+Dead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
