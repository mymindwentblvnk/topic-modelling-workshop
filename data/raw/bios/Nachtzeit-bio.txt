Some unreleased songs that were not released from the former project that involved Nachtzeit. Everything is written and recorded by Nachtzeit, except for "Untitled Project 1 - Untitled Track 1" where Kim (Hypothermia, Lifelover etc) contributed with some guest vocals. «Nostalgia - Dreaming the Day Away» - is the first song Nachtzeit ever recorded. Everything was written and recorded between 2004-2008.
