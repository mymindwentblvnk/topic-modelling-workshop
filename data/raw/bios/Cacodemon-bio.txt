There are two bands known as Cacodemon:

1) A black metal group from Ireland. They released their only demo, Cacodemonic Incantation, in 2000.

Line-up:

Divad the Yeksimoc - Guitars
Lord Balor - Vocals
Joseph Deegan - Drums
Faolan - Bass


2) Influential avant-garde band from Swaziland, but their live members are from Togo and they don't play the ukelele. Their influences goes from The Amenta to Leo Mattioli, with some remains of the pavement Rodrigo Bueno ate that day. 
They use to play live gigs with their friends from Vomit Remnants, The Guanoson and Sepultrula. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cacodemon">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
