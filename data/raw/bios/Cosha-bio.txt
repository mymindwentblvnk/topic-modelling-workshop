Cassia "Cosha" is barely 22 and has already closed one chapter on her life as an artist. The singer and songwriter, born in Virginia to musician parents and raised in a small town near Dublin, used to go by the name Bonzai. She released Do You Wanna Dance in Spring 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/cosha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
