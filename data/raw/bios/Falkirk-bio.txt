Falkirk is:

1) Is a DJ/Producer based in Tulsa, OK. Best known for his blending of Punk, Dubstep, Trap and Electro.

2) Was an alternative rock band from Chicago, Illinois. They released their second single, Karma, which can be heard online now!

3) A French heavy metal band from Paris, formed in 1996. They released three albums and two demos. The name Falkirk was taken from the film "Braveheart" - it is the place where one of the battles took place. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Falkirk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
