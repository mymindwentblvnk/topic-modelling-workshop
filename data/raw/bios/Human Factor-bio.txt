There are multiple artists named Human Factor:

1. Human Factor is a Russian space rock band formed in 2011 in Moscow, Russian Federation.

The band's music is probably best described as modern instrumental rock with major space and progressive rock influences, strong melodic edge, and definite sense of adventure.

http://humanfactor.bandcamp.com

2. Human Factor was a heavy metal band from Santiago, Chile. It was formed in 2002 with members of bands as Witchblade, Bewitched, and Dracma.

3. Human Factor is a drum and bass duo from United Kingdom, comprising of DJ Tisso and DJ Atila who are both originally from Brazil giving their sound a distinctly South American flavour.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Human+Factor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
