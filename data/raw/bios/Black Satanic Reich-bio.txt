Black Satanic Reich is a black metal band from Moscow, Russia. They have released one demo thus far, Dark Wisdom, in 2008. Lyrical themes include Sadness, Hate, Depression and Satanism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Satanic+Reich">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
