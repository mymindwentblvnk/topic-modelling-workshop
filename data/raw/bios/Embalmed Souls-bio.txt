Embalmed Souls are a death metal band from Brazil. Since their inception in 1990 they have released four "studio" demos, as well as a rehearsal tape, a live tape and a DVD. Although they have been active for twenty years the band have never released a full-length album or signed to a label. Instead, all the band's recordings to date, aside from limited cassette pressings of several of their demos, have been self-released.
