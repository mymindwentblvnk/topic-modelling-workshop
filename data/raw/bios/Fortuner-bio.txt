Fortuner was a traditional heavy metal band from Germany  (Bielefeld, North Rhine-Westphalia). They formed in 1984 and recieved a record deal with Metalvoice to release a full-length album. "Two Sides of the Metal" was released soon after, in 1986.Vocals : Hartmut Bleumer, Guitars : Rüdiger Eilts, Bass : Berni Johrmann ,Drums : Jürgen Eilts.
