Tweedy is Wilco lead singer's Jeff Tweedy's musical collaboration with his son, Spencer Tweedy, based in Chicago. Backing vocals come from Jess Wolfe and Holly Laessig (both from the indie band Lucius). Scott McCaughey lends additional keyboard support. Their first album is released in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tweedy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
