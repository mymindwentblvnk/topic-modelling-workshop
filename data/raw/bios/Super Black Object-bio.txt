Super Black Object is a black metal band from Northern Virginia formed in 2009.

They have released two full-length albums: "Rituals of Blackened Perfection" (2010) and "Pleroma Majick" (2012). Contributed in "Nocturne compilation vol.2" with the song "Rapture: Bathe in Blackened Light".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Super+Black+Object">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
