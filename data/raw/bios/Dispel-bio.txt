There are multiple bands with the name Dispel.

1) Dispel (USA) is a brutal d-beat band with lyrics about wizards and how much we are oppressed by the government.  Similar to Discharge and the Lord of the Rings trilogy. 

2) Dispel (Russia), is a dark wave band with synth elements; similar to other bands such as Purple Fog Side and Chiron.

3) Dispel (Australia) is a Thrash Metal band from Sydney, New South Wales.
