Mikkel Christiansen (born 1992) and Simen Auke (born 1991) better known as DJ Broiler are a Norwegian DJ and electronic music duo. Mikkel collaborated with Auke and also from Drammen finding success online.

They often mix electronic music with local humor in recordings and music videos.

They released their beut single "Afterski" in November 2012 that became a hit for them on VG-lista, the official Norwegian Singles Chart reaching number 3 in the chart.
 In May 2013 they released "Vannski" peaking to number 1 on the Norwegian Singles Chart, making it their first number 1 single in Norway.
