The first "Heavy" band in Finland. The band was founded by three Sallinen brothers: "Sepe","Pate" & "Juke". Afterwards the guitarist changed and Jare Renko was taken in the band.

Seppo Sallinen: Vocals & Bass
Juke Sallinen: Drums
