Capo, a rapper from Chicago, Illinois and member of Glory Boyz Entertainment (GBE). Capo was a BD from the set Front$treet. He was the older brother of DooWop and the uncle of T-Slick. He unfortunately passed away on July 11th, 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Capo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
