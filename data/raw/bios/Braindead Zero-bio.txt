Braindead Zero started playing toghether back in 1994. In 1999 the band took a long break so their members could work on side projects. 
After the the tragic death of our true guitar hero Dimebag Darrell BD0 decided to start playing again 
In 2005 the band released their first self produced album, "Drastic Meassures" -2005-, made of new recordings of 14 songs previously released as demos in 1.999. 
Braindead Zero have released on July 2006 their new album "Falling Apart", that was an instant success on IAC's website, where 8 of the new songs have been on the TOP 25 metal charts since the album was released. 

The band works as a trio on the studio and to play live gigs they use a fourth member as a bassist
