John F. Cannibal is a carinthian death metal/grindcore band (founded in may 2009 after several jam sessions).
The line-up consists of Michael "Miguel" Hribernig (vocals), Daniel Schober (guitar), Max Zamernik (bass) and Bernhard Windbichler (drums).
In december 2009 they recorded a demo cd (entitled "Deep Devotion") in the fuzzroom, klagenfurt.
