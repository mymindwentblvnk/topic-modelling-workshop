Noxius Nex are: Rasca Cara (drums, vox) and T. McClelland (guitar, bass -aka Kib Sreng of Brulvahnatu / Antediluvian fame). Their recordings seem to be live rehearsal registrations. Droning, at places sludgy blackened metal.
