Psychon Vex formed in early 2007 as an exploration of that which transcends normal existence. Based in the stagnant cesspool of New Orleans, Psychon Vex offered a debut in early 2008 on Bloated Goat Records entitled "Bleak Rememberance". The band includes members of Ritual Killer and previously of Goatwhore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychon+Vex">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
