Deathomorphine - Russian band, formed in August 2011 by two musicians, experimenters, 
earlier known about his solo project "The Diocese of evil" and «Angelic Grief». 
The main style of this project is Doom / Death Metal. 
But in music you can hear elements of the Gothic / Doom. 
During from September to October 2011 was recorded and released their debut album, which called «Virgin Flower».
