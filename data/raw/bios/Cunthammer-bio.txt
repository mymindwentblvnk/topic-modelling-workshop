Cunthammer was formed in 2004 by Ed Taylor, guitar, vocals(Embalmed, Blastwound) and Chris Stevens, drums(Sintury). Ernie Jaramillo, bass (Irreverence, War Ensemble, and currently Demon Seed) was added and they recorded their 1st demo Fields of Flesh.www.myspace.com/cunthammer to hear tracks. Chris Stevens left the band and was then replaced by Brandon Partain(Psychiatric Reguirgitation) on drums.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cunthammer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
