Christopher Francis is a crooner currently of Olympia, Washington, USA.

He has previously performed under the name Son (not to be confused with the Canadian alternative rock band in the 1990s), as well as Absentee (not the British, or California bands).

More information is available at http://www.myspace.com/sonsongs
