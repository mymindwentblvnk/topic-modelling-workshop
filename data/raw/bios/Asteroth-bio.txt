Asteroth is Mark Slater who hails from Bristol, England. His music is delicately beautiful, filled with cautiously layered melodies and minimal organic sonic structures. He has released only one mini-album, on Float records titled 'Songs From The Belfry' in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asteroth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
