Bea Kristi (born June 3, 2000 in the Philippines), known as beabadoobee, is a London-based singer-songwriter. She has formed her own independent, unique bedroom-acoustic sound pulling heartstrings with every beat. Her sensational melodies are influenced by the iconic artists such as Alex G, Karen O and Florist.

She has released a collection of EPs ("Lice", "Patched Up", "Loveworm", "Space Cadet") and her debut full-length "Fake It Flowers." Her sound has evolved over the years, drawing influence from 90s alternative rock and space rock. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/beabadoobee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
