Acalmbefore, rotorua's only metal band.

The group consists of members who have been old high school friends, stolen from other bands and approached on the street.

Influenced by many of todays metal acts and local bands, acalmbefore are writing good heavy tunes to feed the metal and hardcore fans of the local scene.
