There is more than one band with this name.

1) Death Rides a Horse is a Danish stoner/heavy metal band from Copenhagen. They formed in 2008 and have since released a demo and an EP.

2) Death Rides a Horse are a heavy rock trio from Sheffield, UK. They are Oisin O'Doherty (bass/vocals), Nick Wilderspin (Drums) and Steve McCarthy (guitar/vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Death+Rides+a+Horse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
