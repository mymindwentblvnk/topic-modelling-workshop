Matthew and the Atlas is an English alternative folk outfit based around musician Matthew Hegarty. Having played and recorded as a five piece band since early 2010, Hegarty also tours with a stripped-back set up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Matthew+And+The+Atlas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
