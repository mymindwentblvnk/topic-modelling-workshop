There are several bands/artists releasing music under the name Svarog.

1) Svarog is Oleksa Moroz, a techno DJ and musician from Lviv, Ukraine. 

2) Svarog is a black metal band from Germany.

3) Svarog was also a serbian sludge/doom band formed in 1993. They released only one album entitled Smor, which was recorded live at KST on 5.11.1994.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Svarog">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
