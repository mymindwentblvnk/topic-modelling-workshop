Unsacred Seed was formed by Stefan Perras and Andrew Murray to create original, fresh, metal tunes. 2013 marks the release of their 8 song, self titled, debut album. Unsacred Seed is a combination of death, black, and groove metal, using 7 string guitars. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/unsacred+seed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
