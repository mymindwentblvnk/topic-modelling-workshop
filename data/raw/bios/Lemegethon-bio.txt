Black metal band from Thessaloniki, Greece. One-man project of Deifyer of Morbidity, formed in 1991, self-released two demos and an EP:

1992 - Dethronement of Light (demo)
1993 - Spiritus Locus (demo)
1994 - Demonic Hellhounds (EP)
