Warfield is a black metal band from Mexico, formed by Polo Flores (aka Hellfire) in 2005. Hellfire invited Infernal to play drums and they recorded the first demo, limited to 1000 hand-numbered copies.

In 2009 they released the debut full-length, Conquering the Black Horde.

Later that year Antimo Buonnano (aka Molosh) joined Warfield on second guitar and Damnation joined on bass.

This lineup released the 2010 EP, Trivmvirat.
