Heroic pagan death metal band is from Hungary.
They formed in 2010 in Budapest. The members come from the underground death / black metal scenes from Hungary.
Songwriter is Laszlo Szabo, all lyrics was written by Adam Szalai.

Their first album - Hordes -  was finished in 2014, Tonitrus Studio.
