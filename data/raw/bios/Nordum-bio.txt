Black metal band from France - one-man project of Shériald Nordum (former member of Nihilistic Kaos).
Discography:
- 1999 - Le déclic du Mal (demo)
- 2002 - Ad Honores/le Chipset du Froid (split with Nihilistic Kaos)
- 2002 - Mirror
- 2003 - Old Chapter (demo)
- 2004 - En Attendant La Renaissance
- 2004 - Vengeance humaine / 7 ans de Réflexion (split with Orthanc)
