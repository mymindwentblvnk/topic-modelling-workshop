Initially, the band has name of Terrible Symphonic, a gothic metal band that has been running since 2001 and quite frequent of releasing singles or be involved in some compilations. The band also has long experience in stage performances, but since they had been quite frequent of modifying the line-up, the band had once sleepy and it caused their EP release was suspended, which supposed to be released in 2009.

Last 2010, Terrible Symphonic decided to change its name and start all over again. By choosing the name of Armored (which is assumed can represent material they would offered). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Armored">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
