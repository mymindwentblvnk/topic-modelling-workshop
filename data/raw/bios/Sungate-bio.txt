Sungate are an Experimental Metal band from Bytca, Slovakia. The band is currently unsigned by any label. 
So far, the slovakian band have released two full-length albums on their own.
The band combines elements from genres such as Doom Metal/Gothic Metal and Power Metal with elements of folk and medieval music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sungate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
