Beach Slang were a three piece punk rock band from Philadelphia. Members also play in Weston, Crybaby and Ex-Friends.  They have been described as sounding like  "a one-night stand between The Psychedelic Furs and The Replacements". They broke up in 2021.

Their debut EP - Who Would Ever Want Anything So Broken was released on Dead Broke Rekerds.
