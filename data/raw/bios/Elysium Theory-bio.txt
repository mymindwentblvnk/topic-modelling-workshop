Elysium Theory is an Alternative/Progressive Rock band from Woodstock/Saugerties New York.  Its music is a combination of many genres and it can be heard on all of their compositions. Sometimes heavy, sometimes ambient, sometimes both. Their music transcends many styles of music and it can be hard to categorize at times. High energy,  heart felt music, is a good way to describe the emotions their music creates within the listener. Elysium Theory prides themselves in being a band whos music is about reaching people in other ways than the typical commercially friendly music  heard on the radio every day. Come and experience their music and feel the emotions they bring with their music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elysium+Theory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
