Lil' Wayne and Static Major collaborated on the hit song "Lollipop" off Lil' Wayne's album The Carter III. Static Major is responsible for many of the singing parts of the song, while Lil' Wayne did the rap part of the song. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Lil+Wayne+ft.+Static+Major">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
