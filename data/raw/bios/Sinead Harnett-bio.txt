Sinéad Harnett is an Irish-Thai singer based in London, United Kingdom.

She released her debut single "Got Me" that was produced by Eats Everything & Lukas (EEL). Earlier she was featured on Disclosure’s hits “Boiling”, “What’s In Your Head” and more recently on Rudimental‘s debut album tracks “Baby” & “Hide”.
