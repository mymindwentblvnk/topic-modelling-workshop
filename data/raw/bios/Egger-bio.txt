Dave Ullrich, former drummer for The Inbreds, released his first solo album under the Egger moniker. The record is called Force Majeure and features guest musicians Paul Linklater, Doug Friesen and Don Kerr. All the songs on Force Majeure were written by Ullrich. The album is available from Ullrich's indie music store Zunior <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Egger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
