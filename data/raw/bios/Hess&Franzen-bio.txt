HESS&FRANZEN are good friends and collaborators who have come together to create music for the media and release their own album projects.

They record and produce from their home studios on two continents - Hess in Bristol, UK and Franzen in Porto Alegre, Brazil. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hess&Franzen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
