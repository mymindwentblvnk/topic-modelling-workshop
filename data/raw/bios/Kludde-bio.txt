Belgian black metal band Kludde was founded in 2001, originated in Aalst. The lyrical themes are mainly folklore tales, pain, hatred etc.

Kludde split up in 2009 and reunited in 2014.
Current line-up:
Cerulean - Vocals and lead guitar
Snoodaert - Guitar
Basstaerd - Bass
