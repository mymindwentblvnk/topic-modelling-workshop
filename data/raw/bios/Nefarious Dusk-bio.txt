Nefarious Dusk was formed by Hellfire and Aske on the 5th of september 2008

Nefarious Dusk was first known as Hellfire until the 27th October 2008, 
the date the band was renamed.

Nefarious Dusk spent most of 2007 rehearsing,creating material and covering other bands like Burzum, Mayhem, Bathory and Darkthrone. Also trying too find a decent place too rehearse. Starting off by playing in a bedroom too a studio rehearsal room and finally to their own new rehearsal room just outside of their town.
At this point Sitri joined Nefarious Dusk on the 27th December 2008, as bassist, making the line up complete.
Nefarious Dusk are Mainely influenced by Darkthrone, Bathory, Mayhem and Judas Iscariot,
