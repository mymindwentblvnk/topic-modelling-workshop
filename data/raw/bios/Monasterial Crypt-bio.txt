Monasterial Crypt is a Doom/Death Metal Band formed in 1989 of Czech Republic  (Kralupy nad Vltavou) origin.

Last known line up of band includes:
Milan - Guitar
Payo - Guitar
Kaky - Vocals
Veronika - Vocals
Volfy - Drums (Dissection (Cze))
Sokol - Bass

Former/past member(s)
Kopec - Drums

Discography:
