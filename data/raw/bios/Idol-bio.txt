There are four bands named Idol.

One of them is Ukrainian gothic/doom band. In native language spelled as Ідол.

The second one is Estonian pop-rock band Idol.

The third one is a Romanian hard rock band. IDOL is a girl band with one common passion: rock music. The four girls spend time rehearsing, recording tracks and performing live. The band was formed on the 4th of November 2007. The first concert they were invited to was on the 24th of December, in Tirgoviste, being the opening act for Incipient and TROOPER.
