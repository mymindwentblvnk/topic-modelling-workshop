Maddie Jay is a bassist, producer, writer and multi-instrumentalist based in Los Angeles, CA. She grew up in the wilderness of northern Canada and now spends her days making music in her bedroom studio in the San Fernando Valley. Maddie enjoys jangle pop, synthesizers and daily otters. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maddie+Jay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
