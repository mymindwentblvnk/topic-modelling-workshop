Mt. Wolf is a trio formed by Stevie McMinn, Al Mitchell and Sebastian 'Bassi' Fox. The band was started in 2011 and released Life Size Ghosts, Hypolight EP and Midnight Shallows with founding member and vocalist Kate Sproule before splitting in 2013. On November 2014, 'Red' marked the comeback as a trio. The EP also features 'Hamburg', also included on their 2017 debut album "Aetherlight" alongside 'Hex' and   'Anacrusis' released the previous year. 'The electric' and  'Heavenbound' preceded the May album launch.
