"Loud, Hard and Straight - Rebelhead is moving fouriously between metal and Rock'n'Roll - no compromise"

Rebelhead was founded 2001 in Finland by Jore and Aki. It took several years before they recorded their debut album "Fightback" in 2005.

 Band members:
Aki (vocals)
Jore (bass)
Mikko (guitar)
Ville (drums)
