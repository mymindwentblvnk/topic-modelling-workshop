Crematory Digestor are a Death Metal/Grindcore band from El Paso, Texas. Started as a 2 piece with D. Butcher and Dead in 2010 released their first demo "Embalmed to Perfection" (2012, Limited to 50 CD, now out of print). In 2013 Coagulater joined Crematory Digestor, making them a three piece. Later that year they released a split CD with Decomposing Serenity titled "Bury Her Bracelet with Love / Maggot Planet".

Crematory Digestor's demo, "Embalmed To Perfection" (2012), can be downloaded for free at their Bandcamp http://crematorydigestor.bandcamp.com/
