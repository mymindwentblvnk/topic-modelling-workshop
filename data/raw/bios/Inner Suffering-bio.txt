Inner Suffering emerged from the grave in the winter of 2005 when the first signs of solitude approached Georgio's life. In alliance with the devil and a darkened soul, the torture and lust for salvation had begun. Three years had to pass by in order to create a concept of mysticism philosophy and misanthropy. The first anthems of Inner Suffering arrived under the influence of the mighty demons on August of the holy year 2008 released over the world wide web for free download from Hesperus Records.

Soon after the net. release of Inner Suffering's first album new member was added in order to help with the drum lines of the new anthems. His name George also drummer of the well - known doom metal band Shattered Hope.
