A danish (Copenhagen, DK) one-man electronica project.

Moving away from the digital sound commonly associated with electronic music, Ovia utilizes a unique sound library of strange and wonderful sounds based upon hours of field recordings to compose sonic journeys with a distinct organic feel.

You can download Ovia's album for free on his myspace!
