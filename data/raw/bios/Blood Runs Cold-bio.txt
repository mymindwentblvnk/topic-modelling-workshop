There have been 2 bands performing under this moniker:

1) Blood Runs Cold is a Hardcore band from Russia, Moscow. The band was formed in 2020 in Moscow. They released  "Pain Resistance" EP in 2021 and "The Chaos within" EP in 2022

Alex - Vocals 
Dmitry - Guitar 
Charlie - Bass

2) Blood Runs Cold was a New Jersey Hardcore band from 1999 to 2004 from Trenton, NJ. Members went on to perform in The Concubine, The Valentine, Circa Survive, Summer's End, and several other bands. They released 2 EPs and went on a mini-US Tour before breaking up.
Josh - Vocals (Summer's End)
Sean - Guitar (Daddy Green Jeans)
Prez - Guitar
Scotty - Bass
