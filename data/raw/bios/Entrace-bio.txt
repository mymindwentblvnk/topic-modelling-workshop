Entrace was an Alternative/Metal band from the Philadelphia suburbs of New Jersey.  They self-released a self-titled EP in 2000 and a full-length album, "This Is Me Caring" in 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Entrace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
