US Death Metal band, formed in Midlothian, Illinois, 1989. The band recorded one demo in 1991 before splitting. Two members would later go on to play in Couldron.








Line-up:


Bill Sargis - Drums, Vocals
Jeff Massey - Lead Guitar
Eric Ondo - Rhythm Guitar, Backing Vocals
