TIEFLADER is a German-"SchwerMetall"-band with homebase in Stuttgart: Driven by energy, straightforward, aggressive and hard sounds. After 4 EPs and countless shows, they are now making their first LP: TIEFLADER - "Hier kommt der Hammer" ("Here comes the hammer"). Right now the production is on the way and the release is scheduled for spring 2007.
