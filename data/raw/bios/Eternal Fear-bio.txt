There are two bands that go by the name "Eternal Fear":

1. Bulgarian metal band founded in the year 2000. Members of the band are Stoian Uzunov, Kamen Atanasov, Anton Nikolov and Zaprian Dinkov. The lineup has been changing during the years. The ex-members of the band are Dimo Uzunov (guitar), Ivan Terziev (drums) and Ani Artinian (drums). The band's debut on stage is not until 24.09.2006 but the band is already enjoying the love of Plovdiv's audience. Eternal Fear won 2nd place on a band contest in their hometown. The quartet is currently playing cover songs but they've already started writing their own material.
