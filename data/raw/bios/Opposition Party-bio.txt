Considered the Godfathers of Singaporean HC/Punk formed in 1986. They started out influenced by UK '82 Punk and later incorporated elements of Crossover, Thrash, and Metal while enduring various line-up changes and still existing to this day. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Opposition+Party">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
