Formed in 2011, Dreare is a band from Prague, Czech Republic oscillating between drone and progressive metal structures.

Members are: Vojta Tomasek on bass, Tomas Krigler on guitar and Tomas Hradil on drums.
They released 2 full-length albums: Blank and Forward in 2015 (2x 12" vinyl) and Portaali in 2019 (digital). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreare">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
