High Hypnotic was formed in January 2004, starting with four members. Later on a second guitar player joined. They describe their music as 'attitude metal'. Generally they could be considered groovy post-thrash metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/High+Hypnotic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
