Absone was a French vegan straight-edge metalcore band active during the late 90s and early 00s. They released a split with Decontaminate in 1999 and and ep titled "A Last Kiss Before" in 2001 on Sobermind Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
