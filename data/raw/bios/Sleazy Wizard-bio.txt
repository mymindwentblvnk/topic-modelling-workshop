Heavy metal act from Japan. The band released a demo in the early 90s, named "Violence Seeker". In '92, the band appears on a split named "I Cease Resistance". In '97, Sleazy Wizard released two releases: "Blind & Deaf" EP and their first full-length studio album "Stone Dead", which was released on Mandrake Root. In 2002, the band released an EP named "Under My Spell", and two years later "Forbidden Sanctuary". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sleazy+Wizard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
