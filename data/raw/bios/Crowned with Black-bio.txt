Crowned with Black is a melodic dark metal band from Jyväskylä, Finland. The band was found in September 2009 by it's members: Mikko Ojala -vocals & guitars, Inka Tuomaala -synths and Mikael Saalasti -drums. In the beginning of year 2010 Mikko Heikkilä joined the band as a basist. The Dragon Age -EP will be released in March 2010. EP will include two songs of dark-hearted and melancholic dark metal and it will promote our later coming debut album.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crowned+With+Black">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
