Grindcore four piece founded in 1991 in Winnipeg, Canada. After various releases the politically charged act was signed to Propagandhi's label 'The G7 Welcoming Committee'.   The band dissolved in 2004. Clint, Mike, and Cory continue to play together in Of Human Bondage. Clint also plays guitar for Big Trouble in Little China. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malefaction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
