Melodic Death Metal in tight stockings from Grailham-City.
Grailknights' trademarks are superhero outfits and coloured corpse-paint. The four knights search for the holy grail and fight against evil Dr. Skull. They are supported by their "Battlechoir" and the gorgeous beer-supplying horse "Zapf Beauty".

Line Up:

Sir Optimus Prime: Guitar & vocals
Lord Lightbringer: Guitar & vocals
