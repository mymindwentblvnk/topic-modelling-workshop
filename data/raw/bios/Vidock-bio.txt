Crossover metal band from Ústí nad Labem (Czech Republic).
Current line-up :

Tomáš Eisler - guitar, vocal
David Hradílek - bass
Jiří Hájek - drums

VIDOCK was established in the Czech Republic in January 2007 by Tomáš Eisler
(guitars and vocals), Jiří Hájek (drums) and David Hradílek (bass guitar).
The band plays a mixture of different kinds of metal (thrash, heavy, power, doom etc.).

It has performenced at the big festivals such as Noc Plná Hvězd, Benátská Noc,
Basinfirefest etc. and played several tours throughout the Czech Republic.

The band also co-operates with Miloš DODO Doležal, the producer, whose studio the band
