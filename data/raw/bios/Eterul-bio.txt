Formed in the ages between the years 2008 and 2009, Eterul is an ambient black metal project based in Auckland, New Zealand. Keberous, alongside the talents and tortured screams of Zagovornik explore abstract and ethereal concepts of Misanthropy and the unanswerable questions of existence.
