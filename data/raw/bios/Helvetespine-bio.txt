One-man project of Banehogg (Wiik), producing grim and depressive black metal including occasional ambient elements.
Helvetespine released its first full-length album in 2007, entitled as "Frykten Og Mennesket" ("Fear and man") on Hexenhammer records. The second album was released on Self Mutilation Services on 15th March 2014, entitled as "Forheksede visjoner" ("Enchanted visions"). The album, according to Banehogg, "was recorded during winter 2013, in total solitude". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Helvetespine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
