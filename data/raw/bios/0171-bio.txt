0171 are Joe Bedell-Brill and Georgie Hoare, from Hackney East London.

Their debut EP Redlight, released in 2019, received immediate acclaim from the likes of Dazed, the Fader, iD, Clash, Gorilla Vs Bear, The Line of Best Fit and Noisey; "spellbinding debut EP, pure nostalgic euphoria”

1000 words, credited by the Sunday Times as “a tantalisingly good debut” quickly generated attention from across the globe, receiving radio play from stations such as BBC Radio 6 Music and KCRW.
