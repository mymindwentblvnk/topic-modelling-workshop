Xenolith is the name of multiple artists.

1) Xenolith is a progressive/melodic death metal band from Israel.

Their debut album "Obscure Reflections" was self-released in 2009, and produced by members of the prog-metal band Amaseffer.
The band is currently looking for a label to work with.

2) Xenolith is the name of an industrial black metal band currently signed to Ammonite Industries. They have released three EPs. They are currently working on a full length album and a split with Halo of the Sun and Vortex Syndrome.
