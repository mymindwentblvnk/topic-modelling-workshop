Aberfeldy are in the new school of scottish indie pop in the vein of Belle & Sebastian.  Formed in Edinburgh in 2002 they were signed to Rough Trade to release their first album, Young Forever, in 2004.  The follow-up, Do Whatever Turns You On, was released in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aberfeldy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
