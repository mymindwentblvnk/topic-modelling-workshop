Now defunct. drone/doom metal band from Portland, OR, USA. Released one EP "Spirit of Decay" in 2003 and contributed to "Crushers Killers Destroyers II" in 2004.

Last known line-up:
Jesse - vocals
Josh - bass & vocals
Drew - bass
Tim - drums & vocals
