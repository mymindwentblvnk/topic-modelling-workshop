Moon Lore was founded in Norway, winter 1992. The band has released several demos and one album, "The Spheres Beneath the Heavens" in 1997. The band's musical style has varied from black metal to ambient and they have also experimented with electronic music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moon+Lore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
