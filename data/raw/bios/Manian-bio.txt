DJ Manian (a.k.a. Manuel Reuter, born 1978 07 07 in Bonn, Germany, years active 2001-) - is a German based DJ and along with fellow country man Yanou and another german born but now UK based singer and frontlady Natalie Horler, make up the euro-dance act Cascada.  Other succesful and worldwide known projects are R.I.O. (house project with Yanou and singer Neil Antone Dyer) and Spencer & Hill (with Manuel Schleis, a.k.a. Vengeance). As a solo artist, DJ Manian has had most success in his native Germany on Zooland Records. "Welcome to the Club" saw his first UK release on AATW records.  He has produced a number of singles under various pseudonyms, and maintains a number of different musical projects, including Tune Up!, Bulldozzer, M.Y.C., Ampire, Liz Kay and Phalanx. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Manian">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
