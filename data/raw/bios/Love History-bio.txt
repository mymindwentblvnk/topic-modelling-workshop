Love History were a progressive death metal from Ostrava, CZ.  Their approach on both albums was very experimental and unique, often incorporating acoustics, female and male clean vocals, strange instrumentation and songwriting, as well as male growled vocals and death metal riffs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Love+History">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
