Behead The Lamb is a death metal band from Poughkeepsie, NY comprised of former members of All Out War, Nerve Gas Tragedy, Slugworth, and End of One.  Their debut EP, entitled Messiahlation was released in May, 2008 on Glacial Records and Trip Machine Laboratories. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Behead+The+Lamb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
