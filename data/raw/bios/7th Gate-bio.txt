Electronica dance music project from Sapporo, Japan.

7th Gate's main releases are released from David Angle's Rotation records label out of the UK. However they have also collaborated with Ken Ishii and his 70 Drums label on numerous occasions, as well as the Avex Trax label to remix Ayumi Hamasaki's single "Blue bird". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/7th+Gate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
