The band changed their name to Starkill in December 2012.

Massakren is a metal band drawing influence from various Black and Death Metal bands while strongly emphasizing symphonic and melodic qualities. Blending these sonic aspects together, along with virtuosic guitar work and pummeling live shows, Massakren is pushing the limits of metal to new heights. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Massakren">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
