United Forces is a band formed in 2012 by former S.O.D. members Billy Milano (M.O.D.) and Dan Lilker (Brutal Truth, Nuclear Assault, ex-Anthrax, Lock Up) with drummer Nick Barker (ex-Cradle of Filth, ex-Dimmu Borgir, Lock Up) and guitar player Anton Reisenegger (Pentagram (Chile), Lock Up). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/United+Forces">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
