Fetish 69 formed in Vienna, Austria, in 1987. The band has some ties to countrymates and (initial) labelmates Pungent Stench, whose guitarist Martin Shirenc guested on their Brute Force EP in 1993. Initially their music was informed by hardcore and death metal with industrial undertones, however since the release of Purge the band has moved further into electronic industrial territory,  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fetish+69">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
