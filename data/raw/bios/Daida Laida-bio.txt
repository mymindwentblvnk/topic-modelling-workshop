DAIDA LAIDA is a japanese metal band that was formed in November 2011. The members are NoB (vocal; ex-P.A.F), 白田 一秀 (guitar; ex-GRAND SLAM), MASAKI (bass; CANTA,  ex-animetal), JOE (drums; cycle, ex-sex machineguns) and 清水賢治 (keyboard; THE LOVEROCK VIOLENT, ex-D.T.R.).
Their first mini-album 「風と獅子と罪と魂」 was released 2011.12.27.
