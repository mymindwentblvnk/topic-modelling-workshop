Boisson Divine is a folk metal band from Gascony (France), an alliance of Gascogne identity and heavy metal. A fusion between tradition and modernity, sung either in French or the regional dialect.
The texts are a reflection of the daily lives of the musicians : rugby, legends, songs to the land, rural solidarity, feasts, traditional songs, preserving heritage and tributes to historical figures. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Boisson+Divine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
