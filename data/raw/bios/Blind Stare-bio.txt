Blind Stare is a Melodic Death Metal band from Turku, Finland, formed in 1999. After four demos, they finally released their first official work in 2004, the EP 'The Reborn Genius', and then in 2005 their first album, 'Symphony of Delusions'. Their style is similar to bands like Skyfire, Norther or Kalmah.
