Wolfsschrei are a German black metal band, formed in Exdorf, Thuringia in 2004. They have released a number of splits and EPs, as well as two full length albums: Feasting My Hatred, in 2005, and Demons of My Inner Self in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wolfsschrei">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
