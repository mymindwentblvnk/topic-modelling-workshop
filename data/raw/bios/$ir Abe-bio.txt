$ir Abe, whose real name is Ibrahim Elbalola is a 15 year old underground melodic recording artist. His debut commercial album "Mollyworld" that dropped in August of 2021 put his name on the map. Although his most popular song "Mr. Rager", is what really put his name out there. Being 15, $ir Abe was born November 1st, 2006 in Baltimore, Maryland. His islamic and energetic traits show through his music and attires, growing up in a house of 4. $ir Abe has been making music since he was in 4th grade. $ir Abe's song "OMG, $IR ABE!" reached a total of 15 countries in August of 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/$ir+Abe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
