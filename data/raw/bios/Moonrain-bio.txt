Moonrain is a Swiss female-fronted gothic metal band formed in 2004. 

Tales Of Fantasy
Their first EP Tales Of Fantasy, which contains five tracks, was released in 2007.

New Vocalist
In 2008 the band and former lead-singer Gorana parted ways. In early 2009 Rebekka joined Moonrain as the new vocalist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moonrain">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
