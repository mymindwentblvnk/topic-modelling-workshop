Bones of Giants' unique, uncompromising amalgamation of frenzied, bombastic percussion, seething bass lines, churning guitars, and guttural, primordial screams, yields a relentless sonic assault unlike that of any other group.Their lineup features two ex-members of the now defunct doom band Land of Locusts, as well as one ex-member of the now infamous Portland death-core band Killyourself.

"...Endlessly down the horsemen floated, their chargers pawing the aether as if galloping over golden sands; and then the luminous vapors spread apart to reveal a greater brightness...and the sea-coast beyond, and the snowy peak overlooking the sea, and the gaily painted galleys that sail out of the harbor toward distant regions where sky meets sea." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bones+of+Giants">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
