Gus(taf) Isaacson is a lofi/instrumental hiphop producer based out of Seattle, Washington. His ability to blend samples from old jazz records with modern bass and drums showcases some of his unique sounds. He is also able to create a wavy vibe with his own written melodies, building new tracks from the ground up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Guustavv">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
