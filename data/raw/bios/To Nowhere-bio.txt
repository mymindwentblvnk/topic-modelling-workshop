To Nowhere was created in the Galician country , Spain in 1994 as a Death Metal band.
The band released two demos "Nowhere" 1994, "At the banks of the cosmic ocean" 1995.
To Nowhere takes part in a sampler CD called Millennium compilation (1997), wich collects some of the best bands of the underground in Galician country.

Nowadays, the band comes together with an important change of style.
They play Melodic Metal, and concepts on lyrics change, mixing spanish and english in their lyrics.
