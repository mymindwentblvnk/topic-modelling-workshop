The work of Stefan Neville as Pumice, throughout its twenty year plus existence, remains as one of the most relevant and prolific in the New Zealand experimental music scene, and undeniably significant in the psych/outsider/free folk (or any other dull labeling) scenario. Although abiding extreme lo-fi aesthetics and sonic noise explorations, his work has shifted into different sonorities and styles since the mid nineties to the present time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pumice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
