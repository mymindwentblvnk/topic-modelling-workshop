Ohana Bam was born on the 24th of March, 1995 (age 25 years) in Chicago, Illinois, United States. He is a Hip Hop rapper who previously went under the stage name BK Bambino before changing due to legal reasons.  He has become popular for his albums Tree Up and Pigeons & Plane. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ohana+Bam">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
