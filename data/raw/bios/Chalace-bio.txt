Chalace was an American heavy metal from San Diego, California. A 3-track demo was made in 1985 without a label at that time. They also contributed a song called "Under The Attack" on the compilation "No Substitute For Steel" that same year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chalace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
