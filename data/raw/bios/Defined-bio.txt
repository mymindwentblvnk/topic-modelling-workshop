There are multiple artists tracked as "Defined" on Last.fm.

1) A heavy metal band formed in 2000 by 4 friends in high school. They began writing original material in 2001. The band is comprised of Jon Rioux (vocals), Steve Lajeunesse (drums), Neil Landry (bass), and Dan Levis (lead guitar). 

Their influences include: Pantera, Slipknot, Killswitch Engage, Lamb of God, Trivium, Sepultura, Tool, and Mudvayne.
Official Website - http://www.definedband.com/
