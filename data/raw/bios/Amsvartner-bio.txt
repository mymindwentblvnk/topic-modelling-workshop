Amsvartner was a melodic black/death metal band from Umea, Sweden. The name comes from northern mythology. The band formed in 1994 and are currently active.
Members:
Jonathan Holmgren - Guitar (Auberon, Invader (Swe))
Mikko Savela - Guitars
Albin Johansson - Bass 
Alfred Johansson - Drums (Invader (Swe))
