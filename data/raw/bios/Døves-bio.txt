William Hayward (born February 22nd, 1991) aka døves is a singer, songwriter & producer from Los Angeles, California primarily dabbling in the cloud rap/emo rap genres. døves is a member of the musician collective GOTHBOICLIQUE. døves is best known for his frequent collaborations with Wicca Phase Springs Eternal.
