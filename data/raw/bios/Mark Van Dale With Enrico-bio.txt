A collaboration between the Dutch DJs MARK VAN DALE (Marc Pols) and ENRICO (Erik De Koning). Between 1997-1998 they recorded a bunch of dance-tracks. Their most successfull track was a dance-version of the verve's "Bitter Sweet Symphony". Originally their version was called "Water Verve", later they retitled it into "Water Wave". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mark+Van+Dale+With+Enrico">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
