A grindcore group hailing from Oulu, Finland. Their music is rooted in fast and spastic grincore, but heavy influences from death metal and thrash are present with an innovative grip. 

Pellagra is:
Otto Aleksanteri E. - vocals
Antti K. - guitars
Jaakko S. - guitars
Niko L. - bass
