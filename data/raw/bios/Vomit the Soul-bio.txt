Vomit the Soul was an Italian slam death metal band from Lecco, Lombardy, Italy named after Cannibal Corpse's song "Vomit The Soul". The band formed in 1999. They split up in 2011.

They are noted for their extensive grooves, slam riffs and vocals which consists of guttural croaks and frequent exhaled pig squeals. They released 2 full-lengths before splitting up.

Last known line-up:
Maurizio "Ycio" Orsanigo - drums
Massimo "Max" Santarelli - vocals, guitar
Andrea Anghileri - bass
Massi - Guitar
