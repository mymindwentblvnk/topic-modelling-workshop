Death metal band from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, formed in 1992. After Putrid Mass split-up at some point of the 90's, members Baron Rehak (bass), Brian Rehak (drums, vocals, also from Dr. Shrinker and death/doom band Sickness from Wauwatosa, Wisconsin) and Matt Schmitz (guitars, currently in Sleestak, former member of the same Sickness and Put Her in the Trunk) formed the band Memory's Garden.
