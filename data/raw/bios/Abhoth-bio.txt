Abhoth were one of the first death metal bands in Sweden and formed in the town of Västerås in 1988 under the name Morbid Salvation Army. The original line-up included vocalist Joakim Bröms who left in 1990 to join Afflicted (ex-Afflicted Convulsion). A final demo was recorded in 1999 under the band name Chimera but while in the studio they decided to break up <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abhoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
