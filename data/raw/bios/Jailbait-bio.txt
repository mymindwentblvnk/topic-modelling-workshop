There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Jailbait is a hardcore band from the UK.


2) Jailbait is a sleaze rock band from Sweden with a line up of musicians brought together from the splitting of two of Swedish bands, Sharp and Foxey. They previously went under the temporary name of T.I.T.S and performed a debut show at the Rest In Sleaze festival in 2008. Since then the band have released a new single “We Are The Revolution” and another song (found on the bands myspace) titled “We Own The Night”. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jailbait">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
