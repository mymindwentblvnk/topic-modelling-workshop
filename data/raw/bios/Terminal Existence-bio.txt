Terminal Existence were a D.I.Y. Blackened Crust Punk/Thrash/D-Beat band from El Paso, Texas. They released an 8 song E.P. titled  "Christ Mass" (2008) with Miguel on vocals, Carlos on guitar, and David on drums and also a demo titled "House of Cards" (2009) with Sergio on vocals, Carlos on guitar/back up vocals, Aaron on guitar and David on drums. http://www.myspace.com/terminalexistence <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TERMINAL+EXISTENCE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
