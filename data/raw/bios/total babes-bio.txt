Total Babes is Chris Brown, Gary Spolarich and Cloud Nothings' members Jayson Gerycz and Joe Boyer. The band is part of the Adventure Management music collective in Medina, OH.

Old Flame Records and Continental Mind are pleased to announce the release of Swimming Through Sunlight, the debut full-length from Cleveland lo-fi rockers Total Babes on August 30, 2011.
