Burstin' Out is a Black/Thrash metal band from Kaiserslautern, Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. The name came from the Venom song "Burstin' Out". They formed in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burstin%27+Out">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
