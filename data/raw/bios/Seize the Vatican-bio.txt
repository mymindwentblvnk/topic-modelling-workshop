Seize the Vatican is a brutal metal band from Midcoast Maine, with influences from technical and groove metal. The band's current lineup is Scott Stetson (rhythm guitar), Pat Sawyer (lead guitar), Dustin Faloon (lead vocals), and Joe "Nacho" Ronco (drums). Bassist Jason Moody was murdered in January 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Seize+the+Vatican">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
