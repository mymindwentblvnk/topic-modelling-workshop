Mayhemic Truth was a German Black Metal/Pagan Metal Band from Großbettlingen, Baden-Württemberg. The Band was active from 1992-1997 and after their demise in 1998 the remaining members became Morrigan.

Discography:

    1992: Rehearsal November 1992 (MC)
    1993: Son of Dawn (MC)
    1994: Promo ’94 (MC)
    1995: Cythraw (7’’-EP, Folter Records)
    1996: Demo ’96 (MC)
    1997: Live in Bernhausen (MC, Sombre Records)
    1999: In Memoriam (CD/LP/Picture Disc, Iron Pegasus Records; Reissue 2005 on I Hate Records)
