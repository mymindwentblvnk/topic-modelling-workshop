Asmodee is an occult black metal band from Nantes, Pays de la Loire, France. They made a four songs demo in 1997 which has been released by Drakkar Productions on tape limited to 400 copies. Five years later in 2002 a re-release on 10" limited to 333 copies has been published by End All Life. Spica (vocalist) and Gregor (drummer) are also members of S.V.E.S.T..
