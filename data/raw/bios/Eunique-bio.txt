Eunique (born September 13, 1995 in Hamburg, real name Eunique Cudjo Berkeley ) is a German rapper.

Eunique's career as a rapper started in 2015 when she uploaded a freestyle rap on YouTube, then under the stage name Eunique Kobra . This was successful, and so Eunique was promoted by the established Berlin rapper Fler, among others. At the end of 2016, PULS listed Eunique as one of three promising rap acts for 2017. 

Her debut album Gift was released on April 20, 2018 and entered the German charts at number 7. In 2018 she played alongside Kida Khodr Ramadan and Veysel in the second season of the series 4 Blocks. At the About You Awards 2019, she received an award in the Music category. Since 2020 she appears in the web series Due Date.
