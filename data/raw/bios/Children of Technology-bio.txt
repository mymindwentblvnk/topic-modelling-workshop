Children Of Technology is a post apocalyptic vibrating crossover band born in 2007. Unify a rotten and dirty way of Speed / Thrash Metal with dark side of MetalPunk Children of Technology has no limits on inspirational audio sources by playing exactly what they are. Over the years, C.O.T. recorded different materials on vinyl, tape, CD and release with various stamps. Tour with multiple shows in Italy as well as the UK, Germany, Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Belgium, the Netherlands, France, Spain, Portugal, Czech Republic, Austria and Croatia. The band is currently on hold for live performances, focused only on studio releases.
