Hellwell is a metal band from Kansas, USA. The lineup features the eponymous E.C. Hellwell on keyboards and bass, Mark "The Shark" Shelton of Manilla Road fame on vocals and guitar, and Johnny "Thumper" Benson on drums, guitar, bass, and backing vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hellwell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
