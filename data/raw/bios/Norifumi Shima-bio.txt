Neoclassical heavy metal guitarist from Kagawa, Japan. He is best known for being a band-member of Double Dealer, Concerto Moon, Crystal Clear, Dion and since recently D.T.F.M.. Shima has released two guitar playing lecture DVDs in 2006, through Triumph records. He released his first full-length studio album in December 2008, named "From The Womb To The Tomb", through VAP records.
