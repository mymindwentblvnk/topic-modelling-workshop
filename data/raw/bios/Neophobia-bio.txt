There are several bands with the name Neophobia:

1. Former Australian thrash metal band, active between 1992 to 1998.
They released two albums: Fear of the Future (1993) and Nothing (1996).

2. Symphonic Metal band from Italy. Founded in 2013 as Stormwinds; after major line-up changes, the band chose its new name, Neophobia, in November, 2017.
