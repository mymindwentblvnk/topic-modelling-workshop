Hard rock/metal band out of Indianapolis, Indiana. Something Wicked released their debut album Psychotic Friends Network in 1993.  The band consisted of:

Beau Vann - Guitar
T.J. - Guitar
Taz Devlin - Drums
Craig Otto - Bass
Bobby C. - Vocals
