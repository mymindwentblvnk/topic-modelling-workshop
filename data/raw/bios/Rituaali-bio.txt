Dark metallic stuff in Finnish.

2014, after many years of cooling off and personal infernos Michael and Ville brought Rituaali back to life! Fucking a! They were back. Later, drummer called Antti Tani joined Rituaali's forces of evil! 

A new release titled Kuolonsija will be released in 2014.

Michael Hiekkala: Vocals
Ville Muona: Guitar, bass, programming, vocals
Antti Tani: Drums
