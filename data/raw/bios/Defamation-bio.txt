1) Defamation are a Death Metal band from Worcestershire, UK. They are currently unsigned, but have released a 4-track self-titled EP.

2) Defamation is also a black/death metal band from Thailand. Formed in 2009. So far have released a demo tape and three-way split "Siamese Barbaric Nuclear Warmageddon" with Zygoatsis and Nuclear Warfare.
