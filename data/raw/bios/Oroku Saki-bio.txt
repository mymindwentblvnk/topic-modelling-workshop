Oroku Saki was a Swedish power metal band formed in 2004. They only released one demo 'Oroku Saki'. Oroku Saki is the real name of the villain, Shredder, from The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oroku+Saki">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
