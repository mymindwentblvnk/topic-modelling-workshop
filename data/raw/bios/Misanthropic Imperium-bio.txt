Misanthropic Imperium is Blackened death metal band hailed from Malang, East Java. Formed by Throne of the Moon of legendary Indonesian black metal unit, Ritual Orchestra in 2014. The band hava released their debut album titled 'Cawan Amarah' through indonesian records label.
