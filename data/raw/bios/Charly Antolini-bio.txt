Charly Antolini is the swiss drummer who's played in traditional and swing bands with European, expatriate and visiting American players. He recorded in Switzerland with Bill Coleman in the the '50s, and played in the dixieland group The Tremble Kids in the late '50s, early '[/ag]60s and early '70s. He also played for five years in the German big band of The Suddeutscher Rundfunk. He has recorded with Wolfgang Dauner, Eugen Cicero, Stuff Smith, and Baden Powell, been a bandleader and session musician and played with Lionel Hampton and Benny Goodman in the '80s. He also toured the Soviet Union. Charly lives in Munich - Germany and tours regularly with his own band Jazz Power. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Charly+Antolini">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
