Spawn From Deceit is a socio-political focused grindcore band - with twists of crust and hardcore elements - formed 2009 in Kokkola, Finland. 

The current lineup is 
Robert Björk - Lead Vocals
Kaapro Gröhn - Guitars
Ville Luosa - Bass
Tuomo Outinen - Drums.

Discography
God Less America (Demo / EP 2010)
Promo 2011 (Demo / Promo 2011)
At Least We Did Care (2011)
Woven Promises - Unraveled Victims (2013)
