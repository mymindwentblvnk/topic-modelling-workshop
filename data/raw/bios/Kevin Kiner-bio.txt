Kevin Kiner is a composer of music for film and television best known for his rousing orchestral scores to Wing Commander, Stargate SG-1 (both with themes written by David Arnold), Madison (themes written by Christopher Young), Star Wars: The Clone Wars, and most recently Star Wars: Rebels. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kevin+Kiner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
