Tukaaria is a one-person black metal project from Los Angeles, CA. Formed in 2010, Tukaaria first appears on a split cassette with Black Twilight Circle artist Volahn (co-released by the Crepusculo Negro & Rhinocervs labels). Tukaaria offered it's first full-length "Raw to the Rapine" on March 17th, 2011. An exclusive track was recorded for the "Odour of Dust & Rot" compilation on Rhinocervs, due for release in May 2011. Tukaaria is a Yaqui/Yoeme word meaning 'The Night.' <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tukaaria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
