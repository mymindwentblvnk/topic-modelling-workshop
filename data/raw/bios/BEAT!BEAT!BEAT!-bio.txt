Beat! Beat! Beat! are a young indie band from Cologne. Combining elements of contemporary indie, dance-punk, and electronica.

Beat! Beat! Beat! Having returned from their first Headline tour, playing to sell out shows promoting the debut EP, STARS - five tracks filled with blood and sweat, mixed and recorded by Marius Lauber- the production brain behind Beat! Beat! Beat! – and Mastered by “Fast” Larry Reid from Glasgow’s indie dance masters The Cinematics. Beat! Beat! Beat! are working hard in the Practice room, writing their forthcoming Album.

Their debut “Lightmares” is out due October 22.
