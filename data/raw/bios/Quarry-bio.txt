Quarry refers to multiple artists
1. Quarry is an alternative artist from Tring, near Aylesbury, UK, established in 2019. He takes his name from the quarry in which he works, and this locale has featured in many of his live renditions. His single White Wishy Washy World was featured on Radio One by Annie Mac, and his music has gone from strength to strength since - releasing his debut album Is that what you call love? in June 2021 following EPs Q1 and Q2, released in 2020.
