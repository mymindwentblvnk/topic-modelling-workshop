Divine Sins is an upcoming force in Dutch metal. Combining several metal styles, from death to core and from black to thrash, they blend it to a moshing style of kick-ass metal. When Divine Sins plays live, you will get a pure aggressive act who would do their very best to blow the roof off the venue and to team up with the crowd to create an awesome night for everyone!
