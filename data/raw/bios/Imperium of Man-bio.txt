Imperium of man is a progressive, technical death metal band, formed in mid 2008. The music has had much time to mature and to develop into what it has become. With influences ranging from Bloodbath, Gojira, Necrophagist, Obscura, Opeth and Behemoth to classical, we offer a taste of something else. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Imperium+of+Man">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
