CASTED is a metal band from Oulu, Finland. 
Formed in the early summer of 2014 by drummer Ville and guitarists Henri and Petrus. Later joined by singer Olli and bassist Jim.
As the band was full, songs were forming fast and self-titled demo came out in the early May of 2015.
Their first EP "Maze Of Dread" came out in May 2016.
Their  EP "Beyond the Gates of Silver" was released 9th December 2016
