Fathme Records plague metal artist Surachai has been clearing dance floors for years leaving only true worshippers of metal. Spitting out non-time signatures, melting faces off with disjointed blast beats and relying heavily on modular synthesizers to cave in chests, Surachai has been mending it's own cathartic path. Surachai currently runs the infamous Trash_Audio blog and shade:red record label with Justin McGrath. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Surachai">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
