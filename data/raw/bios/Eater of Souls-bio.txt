There are two artists named Eater of Souls:

1) Eater of Souls is an unsigned Death Metal band from Southern Maine..  The band is currently still writing their own music and has not finished any track, mostly because there is no guitarist, bassist or drummer present

2) Black metal from the Netherlands. Eater of Souls' debut EP, 'Mirror of the World', was digitally released on 29 April 2013. Also see: Wilds Forlorn.
