Grave Siesta is a four piece doom metal collective from Helsinki, Finland formed in 2010. 

Their debut full length album was released on 23.11.2011 by Inverse Records.

Guitar: Sami Lintunen
Drums: Juha Kivikanto
Bass: Mikko Puhakka
