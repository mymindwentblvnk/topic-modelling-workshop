Flo Morrissey and Matthew E. White is a collaboration between English singer and songwriter Flo Morrissey and American musician Matthew E. White. They released "Gentlewoman, Ruby Man", a cover album, in 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flo+Morrissey+and+Matthew+E.+White">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
