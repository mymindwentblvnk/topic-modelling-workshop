Neon Nox is a Swedish synthwave producer who takes a lot his musical inspiration from ’80s films, TV shows and video games.
Nox music can be described as an intense symbiosis of eclectic but always groove outrun and dreamy retro chillwave.

He’s discography includes two EP that’s has been released this year and have already reached top 10 of Synthwave charts, Nox has also been guest at well known radio station Synthetix Sundays. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Neon+Nox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
