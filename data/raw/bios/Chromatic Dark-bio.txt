Chromatic Dark comes from Oulu, Northern Finland. Their music is a versatile mixture of aggressiveness and melody. The biggest influences come from thrash and death genre. The band was founded in 2003.

So far Chromatic Dark has released four CDs: Obsidious EP (2006), Hateballads 2008 EP (2008), Fractures EP (2009) and Inhuman Conviction EP (2011).
