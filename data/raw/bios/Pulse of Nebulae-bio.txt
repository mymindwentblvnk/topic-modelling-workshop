Pulse of Nebulae is a progressive death metal/melodic death metal band from Rīga, Latvia. Formed in 2013 by old friends and collaborators, Mārtiņš Platais and Hicham Chahrouri, with the addition of the drum powerhouse, Dirk Verbeuren. The goal of this project is to associate an extreme array of vocal characteristics with music of a melodic, progressive and groovy composition, orchestrated into a narrative translating the story of the cosmos and all it contains.

Members:

Hisham Chahrouri - vocals;
Mārtiņš Platais - guitars, bass, keyboards, songwriting;
Dirk Verbeuren - drums.

Touring members:

Edgars Gultnieks - guitars;
Aleksejs Makarovs - bass;
Edvards Percevs - drums.
