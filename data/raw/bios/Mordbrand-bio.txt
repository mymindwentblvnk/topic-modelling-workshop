Biography:  
MORDBRAND recorded a 12" split w/ english EVOKE in 2006 as a duo (B. Larsson & J. Rudberg of THE LAW fame). In 2010 P. Boder (GOD MACABRE/MACABRE END) joined the ranks, thus adding a more focused sound to the unit. In 2011, the band penned a deal with US label Deathgasm Records, and on september 6th the MCD "Necropsychotic" will be released upon the masses. More brutality is lurking in the depths, so check this page every now and then.
