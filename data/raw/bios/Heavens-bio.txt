Heavens was an indie rock band featuring Matt Skiba of Alkaline Trio and Blink-182, and Josiah Steinbrick of F-Minus. The duo signed to Epitaph Records and released their debut album, Patent Pending, on September 12, 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Heavens">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
