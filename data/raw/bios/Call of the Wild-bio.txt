Reborn of the smoldering musical wasteland of Redding, CA in mid 2008, Call of the Wild accidentally blends elements of Stoner Metal and D Beat for a sound that is akin (albeit loosely) to Tragedy, on tour with The Sword and High on Fire in the same rusty van, blasting Iron Maiden and Amon Amarth on cassette while playing Dungeons and Dragons on the back of a weathered guitar cab...
