Kindness is the solo project of English musician Adam Bainbridge. They first drew attention from the media with their cover of The Replacements' "Swingin' Party". Their first LP, World, You Need a Change of Mind, was released in 2012, followed by Otherness in 2015 and Something Like A War in 2019.
