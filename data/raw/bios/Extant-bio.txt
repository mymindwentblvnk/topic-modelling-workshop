Visceral, thrashy sludge-grind with crust influences from Eugene, Or. Extant was formed in 2011 and after various line-up changes released Daily Grind with Agro-Culture(SD)'s drummer/vocalist handling drum duties in the summer of 2013. After a hiatus and reuniting with the drummer who played on the first released "What are you doing with your life?" Extant recorded and released "Demo 2014" on 3/23/2014.
