Azahel's Fortress is an black metal band formed in 2003 in Austria (Bohemian Forest) consisting of Demonstorm Azahel as the only member. He released one album in 2005 so far.
Discography:
- ... Und im Wind weht Hexenblut (Demo, 2003)
- Tod der Christensau (Demo, 2003)
- Plagued Screams from The Deserted Dark Turret (Demo, 2004)
- Secrets Of The Forest (album, 2005)
