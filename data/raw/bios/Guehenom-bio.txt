Guehenom was formed in 1992, in São Paulo, Brazil. With the purpose of playing Doom/Death metal, inspired by the icons from the same era. The lyrical conception of the songs ranges between epic themes, mysticism and occultism. The band split up in 1998 but came back in 2013 with a new line-up.

Discography:

1994 - The Phosthumous Meeting - Demo
1998 - Solstice Winter - Demo
2006 - The Fallen Age (1992-1997) - Compilation tape
2007 - Xenomorphic Art in Black - Compilation Demo
2008 - March to Guehena - Compilation Demo
