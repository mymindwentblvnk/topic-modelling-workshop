Night of Solitude is a Black metal band from Worms, Germany founded in 2012 by Samael. 
The current line up is as follows: Skadi- Guitars, Samael- Vocals, Bass, Strings, and Baal- Bass (2015-Present). Past members include: Black Moon-Bass, Martyr- Drums, Berith-Guitars. The bands lyrical themes are misanthropy, hate, and nature.
