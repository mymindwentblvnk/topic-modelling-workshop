Traditional Heavy Metal group from Finland, founded in 2011.

Band released its debut demo 'Spark for the Night' in September 2012. The demo was quickly noticed among the underground metal scene in Finland and other Europe. Soon Finnish indie-label Ektro Records signed the band and the debut EP 'Wordless Wisdom of Lord Fist' was released on vinyl in November 2013.

Band's debut full-length 'Green Eyleen' was released early 2015 through Ektro Records.
