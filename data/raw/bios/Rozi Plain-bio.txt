Rozi Plain is British singer-songwriter Rosalind Leyden (born in Winchester) based in Bristol, United Kingdom, 

Plain belongs to the Cleaner Records collective - a loose assortment of singer-songwriters from the Bristol area, including Rozi Plain, Rachael Dadd, Romanhead, and Greg Ashby. Her debut album 'Inside Over Here' was released on Fence Records in 2008.

She has subsequently released Joined Sometimes Unjoined (Lost Map Records / Talitres, 2012), Friend (Lost Map Records, 2015), Friend of a Friend (Lost Map Records, 2016), What A Boost (Memphis Industries, 2019)
