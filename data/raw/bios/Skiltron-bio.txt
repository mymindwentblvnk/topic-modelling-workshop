Skiltron is an Argentinian folk metal band with power, heavy and celtic influences. A lot of their lyrics are based on Scottish history.

Emilio Souto − Electric Guitar, Mandolin, Bouzuki 
Ignacio López − Bass 
Martin McManus − Vocals 
Matias Pena − Drums 
Pereg Ar Bagol − Bagpipes, Tin whistle
