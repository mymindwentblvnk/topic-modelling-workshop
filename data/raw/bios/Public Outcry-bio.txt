Public Outcry is a suburban Pittsburgh Death Metalcore that formed in early 2008. They take elements of Thrash and Power Metal and soup them up with amazing drumming and dueling guitar harmonies. Public Outcry consists of Lead Guitarist Vince Snyder, Vocalist Zane Davin, Rhythm Guitarist Todd Johnson, Bassist Steve Schmidt and Drummer Josh Douglas.  

In July 2009 Public Outcry recorded their first EP with Corey Perotte Productions, entitled "Past The Point Of No Return".
