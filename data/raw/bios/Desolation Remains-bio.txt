Desolation Remains are a metal band based in Birkenhead, England. The band has a strong work ethic and demonstrates this through gigging, recording and producing their videos. They have supported a number of bands including Napalm Death, Sworn Enemy and Sarah Jezebel Deva.
The band are set to record a full length album in November and their last recording is now available on iTunes.

Members:
Vocal - Gareth Dunn
Guitars - Barry Duckworth
Bass - Gaz Jones
Guitars - Peter Pullman
Drums - Jon Mathews
