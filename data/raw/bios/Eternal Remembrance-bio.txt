Eternal Remembrance was doom metal band, formed in Tuzla (Bosnia and Herzegovina) around 1993. 

Line up was:
Mirza Salihagić - vocals, guitars,
Alen Karagić - guitars, 
Mirsad Nalić - bass,
Vela - drums.

'Til 1994 they had 2 other members Robert Paloš - guitars and Goran (R.I.P.) - bass 

In 1994 band moved to Leipzig, Germany. Later some members returned to Bosnia while others stayed in Germany.

Releases:
- "In Our Garden" demo (1994, recorded in Tuzla, Bosnia)
- "Where Destiny Stares" demo (around 1995, recorded in Germany)
