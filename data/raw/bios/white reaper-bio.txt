WHITE REAPER is a punk rock and roll band from Louisville, KY. The band consists of guitarist/vocalist Anthony Esposito, keyboardist Ryan Hater, bassist Sam Wilkerson, drummer Nick Wilkerson and guitarist Hunter Thompson joined in 2016. The band has released two EPs and three full-length albums, their first album in 2015 White Reaper Does It Again,  and The World's Best American Band in 2017. White Reaper released their third studio album You Deserve Love on October 18, 2019, their major label debut.
