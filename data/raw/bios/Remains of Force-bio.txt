Remains of Force, Power/Heavy metal, Brno/Czech republic

Remains of Force is a power/heavy metal band formed in Brno, Czech Republic in 2011. 

Band Members:
Bruno Matchetka - rhythm guitar, manager
Vojta Zakk Jeřábek - rhythm / solo guitar
Majkl Čejny - vocals
Martin Neu Neužil - bass
Tomáš Indián Vaverka - drums
