A Thrash/Speed Metal band from Pittsburgh, USA.   They formed in 1986.  Their lyrical themes included social issues, politics, horror and life.  They released a few demos, one EP, a compilation, a split and one full-length album.  They disbanded in 1991. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Doomwatch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
