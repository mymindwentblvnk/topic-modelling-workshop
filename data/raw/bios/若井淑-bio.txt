Hajime Wakai is a sound artist at Nintendo. He has worked on many of Nintendo game soundtracks. He is the composer of the majority of the Star Fox 64 Original Soundtrack. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/%E8%8B%A5%E4%BA%95%E6%B7%91">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
