Endless Hate is a brazilian Death/Thrash metal band formed in 2004 in Rio Grande/RS. At first the band only did covers of classic bands like Slayer, Kreator and Sepultura. In 2006,inspired by their passion for metal, they started composing their own songs.
Their first gig was in 2004, and have been playing since then.
