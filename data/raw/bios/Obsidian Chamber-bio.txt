Obsidian Chamber is a Symphonic Black/Deathmetal band from Vienna, Austria formed in 2003.

The name 'Obsidian Chamber' is based on the Mayan mythology, which describes a room in Hell, with rotating knifes made out of obsidian to slay the doomed souls to pieces. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obsidian+Chamber">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
