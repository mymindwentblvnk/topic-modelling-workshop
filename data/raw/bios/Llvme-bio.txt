Llvme is a Doom-Black-Folk band formed in Salamanca (Spain) by members of other Spanish veteran metal bands. The roots of Llvme are the Black Metal band Chaotic Hope, band that reached certain renown in the European underground scene due to their two albums, and K-OS, Spanish thrash metal band that entered in the Spanish metal lists with their first album.
