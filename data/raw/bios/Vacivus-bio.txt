Death metal band from Sunderland/Hartlepool UK. Used to be Dawn of Chaos, renamed themselves Vacivus in 2013 after the vocalist Paul left. Released their debut album Dawn of Chaos independently in March 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vacivus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
