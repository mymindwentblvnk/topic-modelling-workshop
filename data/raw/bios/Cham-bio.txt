Baby Cham (born Damian Beckett in 1977) is a Jamaican dancehall artist, famed for his 2006 single "Ghetto Story" off his major label debut album of the same name. Recently, he has been going by the alias Cham and he is signed to Atlantic Records.

CHAM! is a fictional band featured in the Japanese anime movie "Perfect Blue", comprised of three members: Yukiko, Rei and Mima. They are known for the song Ai no Tenshi (Angel of Love).
