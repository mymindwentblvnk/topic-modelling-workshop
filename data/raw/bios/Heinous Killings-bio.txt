After a long silence one of the underground’s originators of ultra low frequency vocals returns. Hung with Barbwire displays Ex-Clean Flesh vocalist Joe Wolfe’s new wave of extremity which will leave listeners guessing what physical abnormality has taken place to cause such inhuman tones. Spinning straight forward brutal death metal and some of the most outrageous oral blasts ever put to tape Heinous Killings is rebuilding the framework which their peers must stand upon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Heinous+Killings">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
