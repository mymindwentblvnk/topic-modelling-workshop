Insidious Decrepancy is a death metal band from Houston, TX.  Formed in the year 2000 at the hands of one Shawn Whitaker, the project is a fiercely captivating display of pure evil death metal.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Insidious+Decrepancy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
