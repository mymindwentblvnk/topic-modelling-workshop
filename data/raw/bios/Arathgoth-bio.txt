Arathgoth is a dungeon synth act. Very epic and atmospheric at the same time.

Bandcamp: http://arathgoth.bandcamp.com

First album, titled "The Season of Ashes", tells an ancient battle between the obscure Morbelins and the people of the Light Castle. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arathgoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
