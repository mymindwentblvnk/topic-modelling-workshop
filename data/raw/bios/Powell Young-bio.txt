Powell Young is a Chinese guitarist. His Chinese name is 歇斯 (Xie Si). He is the first Chinese guitarist to become offered an endorsement deal with Musicman guitars. He has performed on stage with musicians such as Joe Satriani. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Powell+Young">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
