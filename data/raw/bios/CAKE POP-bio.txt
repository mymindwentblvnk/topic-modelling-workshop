Cake Pop is a side-project by Dylan Brady and features songs with friends and frequent collaborators Aaron Cartier, Lewis Grant, Adam Newcomer, Robel Ketema, Pritty, Night Lovell and Five Star Hotel. The Cake Pop self-titled EP was released on August 25th, 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CAKE+POP">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
