Timo Tolkki's Avalon is a symphonic heavy-metal project founded by former Stratovarius guitar player Timo Tolkki (Symfonia, Revolution Renaissance) with drummer Alex Holzwarth.

Their first album, Land of New Hope (2013) features vocalists Michael Kiske (Unisonic, ex Helloween), Sharon Den Adel (Within Temptation), Elize Ryd (Amaranthe), Russel Allen (Symphony X), Tony Kakko (Sonata Arctica) and Rob Rock (Impelliteri).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Timo+Tolkki%27s+Avalon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
