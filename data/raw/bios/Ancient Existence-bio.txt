Ancient Existence is a German Death Metal band. Formed in 2000, they have released 3 LPs (Night Eternal [2003], Hate Is The Law [2007] and Death Fucking Metal [2008].)

official site : http://www.deathfuckingmetal.net/index.html


Current line-up:

Felipe "Morfeu" Dias - vocals
Johannes Leicht - guitar
Alexander Degraf - bass
Dominik Brodowy - drums 


Former/past member(s):

Stefan Kassner - guitar (2000-2004)
Stephan Schirmer - bass (2001-2002) 
Steffen Rehbock - vocals (2000-2008)
