Uncounted Faces Of Death, often abbreviated to U.F.D., were a German underground crossover (hardcore punk + thrash metal) band formed in 1987, Hanau, Hesse and who released a full-lenght, an EP, a split album and two demo tapes before splitting-up in 1995. Their lyrics were mainly focused on sociopolitical matters.
