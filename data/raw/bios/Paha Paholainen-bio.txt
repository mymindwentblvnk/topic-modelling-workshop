Paha Paholainen was a Finnish alcohol influenced black metal/punk/grind band from Oulu, Finland consisting of Twilight, Epidemica and Porcus.

Paha Paholainen was formed in 2001 by Epidemica and Twilight with Porcus joining them soon after.

Their first release was the demo "Yli paha" in 2002.

They released their second demo "Talavi" in 2003.

Their third and last demo "Kaikista pahin" was released in 2004.
