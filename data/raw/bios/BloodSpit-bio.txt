BloodSpit is a underground band from munich, germany formend in 2004
They play skullcrushing deathcore with technical riffs, blasting drums and demonical vokills
Infulenced by bands like Misery Index, Beneath the Massacre, All Shall Perish and Job for a Cowboy
The first full-lenght album is called "Until We Shrink To Nothing" and will be released in middle of october 2007
The band made it to the bavarian final lap of world wide newcomer contest "Emergenza"
Current Lineup is:
Luke - Vocals
Dani - Guitar
Flip - Guitar
George - Bass
