Suburbia in Ruins is a melodic metalcore band from the Belgian h8000-area formed in Brugge, BE in 2007. They have one EP running called 'Undirected' and they are currently making their full-length. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Suburbia+in+Ruins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
