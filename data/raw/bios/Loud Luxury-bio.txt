Loud Luxury is a Los Angeles-based Canadian music production and DJ duo consisting of Andrew Fedyk and Joe Depace, and was formed in 2012 when both members were still in college. They gained recognition in 2017 when they released a remix of the Martin Garrix single "Scared to Be Lonely".
