Point Of Existence from Stockholm in Sweden plays progressive / alternative / experimental metal/rock, POE is also officially used as shortened version of the bandname. The members of POE are:

Torbjörn Sandberg - Vocals
Juan Gauthier - Guitar, Guitar synth
Stefan Sjöberg - Guitar
Christer Elofson - Bass
Lennart Fröderberg (ELF) - Drums
