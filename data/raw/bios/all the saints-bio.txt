Originally from Alabama, this Atlanta, GA-based trio released their debut album,  Fire On Corridor X , in May 2008 on Killer Pimp. 

All the Saints are: Matt Lambert - Guitar/Vocals; Jim Crook - Drums; Titus Brown - Bass/Vocals
