BLOODHUNT is born in February 2006. They play melodic death/thrash metal. 

Members are: 
Slobodan Stupar (vocals, guitar)
Goran Simic (guitar)
Mario Markovic (drums)
Andrej Mesaric (bass guitar)
Ex member: Ivan Mumlek (bass guitar)

Bloodhunt recorded two demo albums named "A New Dawn Arrives" 2006 and "The Final Betrayal" 2007 recorded in Pozega, Croatia. The biggest succses band had was playing in Hungary w/ Have No Choice and Sorrow Is A Sage and our very first mini tour in Germany and Prague with DROWNED (Brasil).
