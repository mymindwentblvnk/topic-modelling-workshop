State Collapse is a brutal death metal band from Sweden. They were formed in by Kristoffer ( all Instruments, Vocals) and Slampo (Vocals) producing unfamilar Gorthenburg terroritory with influences from slam and death metal scenes.

The band has released "Gothenburg Rape" in the fall of 2008 which was recieved with misconception by some and hailed by others.
