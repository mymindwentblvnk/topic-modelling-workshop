Metal Raign is a band located in Abensberg, Bavaria, Germany. The band was founded in 2004. The style can be best described as melodic metal with progressive influences. The five members are: Michael Pusch aka "Fuck" (lead guitar), Robert Templer aka "TemplerRobs" (rhythm guitar, support vocals), Robert Hofmann aka "Hube" (bass, vocals), Chris Neumeyer aka "Chris" (keyboard, synthesizer), Louis Hammer aka "Louis" (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metal+Raign">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
