An artist who publishes his songs on his YouTube channel and on The Escapist Magazine, Miracle of Sound (born Gavin Dunne) became very popular with fans of the Mass Effect series not long after he released his song [song]Commander Shepard[/song] which quickly spread through Facebook and other social sites.
