The band originally formed in 1993 as Odin (as a death metal band), but was put on hold until 1997, changed lineups and styles, and changed to 'Oathean' in 1997.

Do-Su Kim established and runs Jusin Productions.

Ex-bassist/vocalist, Min-Su Kim, is Do-Su Kim's older brother.

Current Members:

Dosu Kim - Guitars, vocals
Heedoo Lee - Lead guitars
Yonghee Park - Bass
Jinah Lim - Female vocals
