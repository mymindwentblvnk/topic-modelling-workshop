OnceOver is an alternative/nu metal band from Toledo, Ohio that formed in 2001. In 2007, founding vocalist Dave Ayling left to pursue a career in video editing. Drummer and producer Steve Dwyer in turn took over vocal duties. They have self-released 5 albums: OnceOver (2002), These Precious Days (2004), The Albatross (2006), Red Right Returning (2008) and 9 (2010) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/OnceOver">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
