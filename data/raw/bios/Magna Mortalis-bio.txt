Founded in 2006, Magna Mortalis spread their Death Metal with power and groove, full of sick melodies and lyrics about all abysmal depths of human existence. After “Demo 2006” and “Promo 2007”, the first full llength CD-album “Onward” was released in 2009 and the band shared stages with a.o. Kataklysm, Belphegor, Evocation, Excrementory Grindfuckers, Drone or Eisregen. At the moment Magna Mortalis is working on the second album, ready to shake the earth with new merciless compositions and overwhelming live performances!  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Magna+Mortalis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
