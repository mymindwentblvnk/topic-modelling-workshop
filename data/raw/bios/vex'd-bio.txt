Vex'd were a UK electronic act consisting of Roly Porter and Jamie Teasdale, active between 2004-2008. They predominately recorded for Mike Paradinas' imprint Planet Mu,  who released their debut album Degenerate in 2005, followed by the project's posthumous second album Cloud Seed in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vex%27d">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
