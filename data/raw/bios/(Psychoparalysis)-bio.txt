Progressive Death Metal from Uusimaa, Finland. They have 2 demo albums released in 2010, followed by a 2012 demo and a debut album in 2013 Human Disposition.

Deligion (2010)
Forest of Ignorance (2010)
Escalation (2012)
Human Disposition LP (2013)

Members

Anssi Kantola - Vocals, guitars
Bhaadi Partanen - Guitars 
Otto Trygg - Bass 
Mika Sallamaa - Drums
