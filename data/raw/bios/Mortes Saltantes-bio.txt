Mortes Saltantes is a Japanese Black Metal band formed 1994. Their style was a very straightforward, powerful Black Metal approach. Through their career, their demos slowly shifted to the sound we now see in their recent work, first as Magane and now as  Quest For Blood. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortes+Saltantes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
