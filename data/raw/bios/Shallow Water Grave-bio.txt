Shallow Water Grave is a five piece based out of Connecticut.  Shallow Water Grave plays a brand of melodic heavy metal with thrash and hardcore influences. 

They formed in mid-2003 and have released only one EP, The Suspension of Disbelief in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shallow+Water+Grave">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
