The Batallion was formed in the summer of 2006 with the intention of reawakening the spirit of classic oldschool deaththrash. All members are from Bergen, Norway and are well established metalheads and have played in some of the most legendary bands from Norway. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Batallion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
