1. Angelize was band from Sweden. They released only two singles - Strangers Paradise 1988, In It For Love 1988. They played melodic rock / AOR music. 

2. Angelize is a Gothic Metal band from Cremona, Italy. The band was founded by Filippo Ondea (Guitar and vocals) and Fiore (drums). After many line-up changes, in 2003 they finally released their first (and, as of this day, only) demo "Light of a Dying Star".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angelize">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
