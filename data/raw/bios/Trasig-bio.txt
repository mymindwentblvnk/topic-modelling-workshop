A new Norwegian black metal band has emerged in Trondheim. It’s called Trasig and is a solo project started by Anfinn Halland in 2015. Trasig recently released the eight-track album Jammer og Nød, where Halland naturally has done all the work.
The album is available for streaming on all streaming services, as well as streaming/purchase on Bandcamp and iTunes etc.

TRASIG is an electro-duo from Sweden.
They are heavily influenced by a lot of different styles of music, ranging from dissonant punk and 80’s thrash metal to old school hip hop and 90’s rave, as well as contemporary electronic dance music and experimental noise / industrial acts. TRASIG has been an active band since November 2008 and can also be found on their myspace and Facebook page.
