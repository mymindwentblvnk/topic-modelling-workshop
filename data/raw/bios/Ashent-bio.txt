Ashent is an Atmospheric/Melodic Progressive Metal band from Venice, Italy in 2002.

Current line-up:
 
Titta Tani - Clean Vocals
Onofrio Falanga - Guitar
Alessandro Cossu - Guitar
Gianpaolo Falanga - Bass and Harsh Vocals
Davide Buso - Drums
Gilles Boscolo - Keyboards
