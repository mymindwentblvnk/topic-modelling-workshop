Heathen Fist is a black metal band from northern Finland. Their latest demo "Strong spirits and merciless attitude" was recorded summer of 2006. They are currently working with a new demo. It is propably going to be released late 2007 and perhaps a twisted music video from one of their oldest songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/heathen+fist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
