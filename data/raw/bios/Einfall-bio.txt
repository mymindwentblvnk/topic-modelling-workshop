They are come from Saint Petersburg, Russia and formed in 2008. They are signed to Black Devastation Records.
Einfall is German and can be translated in numerous ways: idea, raid, invasion, incidence; just to name some.

Current line-up :
Valdsar - Vocals, bass
Demortuus - Guitar
Stratum - Guitar
A.V. - Drums

Former/past member(s)
Haoth - Guitar
Alacris - Guitar
