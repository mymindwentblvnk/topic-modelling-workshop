Ninjaspark was a Bitpop band from Helsingborg, Sweden consisting of Jim Knutsson and Linus Wadström. The project started in 2005 together with Pär Fredriksson and Erik Berglinden, but before disbanding in 2011 only Jim and Linus where still part of the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ninjaspark">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
