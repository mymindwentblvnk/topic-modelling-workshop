Embludgeoned is a Slam Death Metal/Brutal Death Metal, from Pittsburgh, USA.

Founding guitarist Christian Beebe went on to play in numerous bands such as Enemy Mind, Vow of Hatred, Path To Misery, Ten Of Swords, End Of Humanity, and S.C. Firm
