DJ, producer and label owner of Atypical Farm and Suara. He has reached #1 on Beatport with "El Baile Aleman" (liebe*detail), it was the most sold track of 2009 on Beatport. Has worked with other well-know labels like Diynamic Music, Great Stuff, Saved, 20:20 Vision, Get Physical, Rebirth, Noir Music or Tronic. Won two awards Deejaymags 2009 and was nominated on 6 different categories for 2010. Released a cd-mix compilation "Live & Direct" on Cr2 along Edu Imbernon & Uner. Travelling around the world everytime: New York, Seoul, Helsinki, Bangkok, Montreal, Panama City, Detroit, Playa del Carmen or Tel Aviv. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coyu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
