Tenebrae Semitarius's music can be described mainly as generic/experimental black metal. The band was formed during the days of December 2006 in Greece. At January 2007 they started the recordings for their first release "Behind the Shade of Sun" [EP]. At the end of July 2007 the EP was finally ready and includes 6 songs. At this moment they're working for their new release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tenebrae+Semitarius">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
