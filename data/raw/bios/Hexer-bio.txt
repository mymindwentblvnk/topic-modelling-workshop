There are several artists named Hexer:

1.  Hexer is a stoner/doom metal band from Dortmund/Germany. They have released one EP, one demo and one album so far.

Bandcamp

2. Hexer is a black metal project from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

The two members, Phlegethon and Ansgar, are husband and wife.
