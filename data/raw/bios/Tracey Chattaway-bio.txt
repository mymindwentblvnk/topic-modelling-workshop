Tracey Chattaway's music is themed around instrumental piano, interwoven with ambient nature and orchestral soundscapes. Subtle and uplifting dynamics are also noticable through the use of ukelele, percussive rhythms and layerted textures. Her tracks feature beautiful melodies and journeying anthems that are all memorable, reflective and inspiring.
