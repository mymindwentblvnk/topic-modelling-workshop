Ilya Monosov is an American musician of Russian heritage. He is a member of Mountain Home and The Shining Path, in the duo Monosov Swirnoff, and also records as Ilya Monosov & The 21st Century Punks <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ilya+Monosov">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
