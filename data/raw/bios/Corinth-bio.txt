Corinth from Leeds are a band who feature Ben Corkhill who is also the bassist of BongCauldron. Corinth blends elements of stoner/sludge/post metal and post rock all together within their sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/corinth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
