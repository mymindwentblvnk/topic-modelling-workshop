Fatal Opera was a Technical Thrash/Speed Metal band from Florida, USA, formed in 1993 by ex-Megadeth drummer Gar Samuelson (1958-1999) and his brother Stew Samuelson (guitar). The band released their first demo "Preproduction" in 1994 and two full-length albums in 1995 (self-titled) and 1997 ("The Eleventh Hour").

Gar Samuelson died in 1999 because of a liver failure.
