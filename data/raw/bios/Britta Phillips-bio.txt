Britta Phillips was the singing voice of the hit 80's tv cartoon character Jem of Jem and the Holograms. Later, she was a member of several other "real-world" bands, including The Belltower, Luna, and Dean and Britta. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Britta+Phillips">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
