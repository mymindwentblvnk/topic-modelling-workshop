Louis-Philippe Celestin (born August 30, 1994), better known by his stage name Lou Phelps, is a Canadian rapper and producer from Saint-Hubert, Quebec. He is a member of the Canadian Hip-hop duo The Celestics alongside his brother Kaytranada. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lou+Phelps">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
