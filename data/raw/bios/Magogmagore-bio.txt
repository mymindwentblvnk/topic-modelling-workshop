Magogmagore are a 5-piece Brutal Slamming Death Metal band from London, UK. Formed as a one man project in 2006 and releasing 2 demos in 2007 and 2009, the line-up was completed in 2016 with the release of The Gnostic Mass EP.

Members:
Hadley Sharp - vocals
Stavros Afx - guitar
Andrei Verner - guitar
James Moth - bass
