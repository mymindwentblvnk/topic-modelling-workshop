Dreamfall In Vain start out in September 2007, in Minsk. Characterize their music can be as Melodic Death Metal mixed with Deathcore. The first self-titled demo was released in 2009 and consisted of two compositions. Entries have had positive feedback that has been a trigger further creativity. At the same time the guys start performing at local clubs and writing debut EP Empty Graves Are Waiting For The Names, which appeared in spring 2010. In their songs the band wants to convey the tragic moments: when the most horrible thoughts begin to run, dragging irreversibility. Experiences, love, dreams - it's all available here. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreamfall+In+Vain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
