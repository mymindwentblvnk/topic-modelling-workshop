Darkdeity started late 2008 with the idea of playing Death Metal. Members being heavily inspired by Old School Death Metal, they have dedicated their talent in making Darkdeity "Evil and Obscure..." 

Members:
Isaac - Vocals/Guitars
Luis - Guitars
Saul - Bass
Cesar - Drums

Influences:
Sadistic Intent, Necrophobic(early), Crematory(Swe), Wombbath, Darkthrone(early), Benediction(early), Immolation, Bolt Thrower, Unleashed.
