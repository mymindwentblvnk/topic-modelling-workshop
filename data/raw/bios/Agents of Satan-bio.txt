Agents of Satan (formerly named Radioactive Lunch) is a hardcore/powerviolence band formed in San Mateo, California in 1995.  They have released split EPs with Burn the Priest, No Less & Noothgrush. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agents+of+Satan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
