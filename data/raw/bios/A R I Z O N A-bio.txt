Arizona (stylized as A R I Z O N A) is an American rock and electropop band from New Jersey currently signed to Atlantic Records. The band's debut studio album GALLERY was released in May 2017, peaking at #4 on Top Heatseekers, #24 on U.S. Alternative, and #143 on the Billboard 200 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+R+I+Z+O+N+A">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
