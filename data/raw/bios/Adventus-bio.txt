There are 2 artists going by the name of "Adventus"

1)  Spanish Heavy Metal band in spanish created by keyboardist Manuel Ramil, who has been part of such important bands as WarCry or Avalanch. This is his second musical project after Sauze, and for him he has had almost all the musicians who were part of the first stage of WarCry. 

Adventus are:

Voice: Víctor García
Guitar: Fernándo Mon
Bass: Luis Melero
Keyboards: Manuél Ramíl
Drumms: Alberto Ardines

Adventus publishes in January 2021 his first album, "Morir y Renacer".
