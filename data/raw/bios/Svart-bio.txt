There are several bands named Svart: 1) A Swedish black metal band. 2) A Swedish post-punk band. 3) A French black metal band.

1) Svart is a solo project by Christian Larsson Draug (Shining, Acacia etc). The project started back in mid 2007 influenced by slow, oppressive black metal. More info at http://www.svart.ensamvarg.com

Discography:
Då allt upphör (demo)
Vanära, vanmakt och avsmak 
Våran tid är förbi EP
Namnlös och bortglömd EP
Förlorad 
Det personliga helvetets spiral

2) Svart is a Swedish post-punk band. It consisted mainly of Caiza Almén and Jan Svenungsson. Stranded Records released a 7" EP called "Sår" and an LP called "Gryning", in 1980 and 1981 respectively.
