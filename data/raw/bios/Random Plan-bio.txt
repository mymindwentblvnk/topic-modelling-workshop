Random Plan was formed in November 2006 in Lahti, Finland. The group was previously known as Moment After Scarlet, but dropped the synths and started again with a new style of music. The first EP, titled "Perforated" saw the light of day at the end of June 2007. This EP introduces a new flavour of rock music compared to the old, by adding a saxophone musician to the band.
