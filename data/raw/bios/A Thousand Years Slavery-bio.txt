A Thousand Years Slavery is a melodic death metal / melodic deathcore band formed in 2004 in Montreux, a French speaking town of Switzerland. After an online three-track demo named “An Eternal Three” issued in 2006, they released their first EP “A Fury Named Spartan” in 2008 on Conatus Records.
