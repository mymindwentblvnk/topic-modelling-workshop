Deficit aka Matt Deficit is a UK based underground electronic musician, producer, lyricist and founder of Epoch Records.

Deficit is also young metal band out of Chicago, Illinois that has been together since early 2003. 

Deficit is also the name of a hip-hop artist and founder of FreePoets Entertainment.
