Stakker was Brian Dougans and John Laker.  A London based acid house production team best known for their 1988 hit "Humanoid" a relentless acid house burner that took the US and the UK by storm and remains influential 20 years later.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stakker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
