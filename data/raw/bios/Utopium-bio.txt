Utopium is a death metal/grindcore/sludge band formed in 2007, in Lisbon, Portugal. They released their first demo in 2008 and their EP "Conceptive Prescience" in 2010, preparing now to release their debut album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Utopium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
