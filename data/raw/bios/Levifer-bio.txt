Levifer is a one man black metal project of the guitarist from Peruvian black metal band Goat Semen. Levifer is a meld of Lucifer and leviathan.  "Tribute to the Supreme Beast," released in 2000 is a demo originally released on tape then as a picture disc. It is satanic black metal and so on. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/levifer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
