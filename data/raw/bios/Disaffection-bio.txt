Formed in 2005, Disaffection is a Brazilian Thrash Metal band with Christian themes. They released their debut album, Begin The Revolution, in 2010.

Current line-up
Cleiton Magno: Vocals
Paul Moraes: Guitar
Helô Silva: Bass
Daniel Neves: Guitar
