Hand of Doom is a tribute band to Black Sabbath featuring Melissa Auf der Maur of Hole and Smashing Pumpkins as its front figure.

There was also a German band named Hand of Doom with a sound described as similar to early NWOBHM (New Wave Of British Heavy Metal).  They released an album named Poisonoise, limited to 500 copies, in 1979.
