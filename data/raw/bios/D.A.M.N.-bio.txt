There are 2 artists that are known as D.A.M.N.
1) A female-fronted Death Metal band from Germany
2) A Dutch old-school Hip Hop group. Don't Accept Mass Notion was formed in 1989 in Eindhoven by rapper L. Rock and DJ Bass. They released three albums: Don't Accept Mass Notion (1989), Black And White Thing (1990) and Live Positive (1991). Don't Accept Mass Notion is regarded as the first Dutch Hip Hop album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D.A.M.N.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
