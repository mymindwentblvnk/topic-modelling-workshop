Formed in 1997, Shepherd of Sheol is a Lebanese band that plays Gothic Doom Metal with some Power Metal influences. They have released an album entitled 'Fear Beneath The Tears' in 2000. They are now split-up.

Band Members:

Alain Sfeila (Bass)
David Elias (Drums)
Raja Rizkalla (Keyboards)
Elias Njeim (Lead Guitars)
Nicolas Shebli (Rhythm Guitars)
Dory Naoum(Vocals)
