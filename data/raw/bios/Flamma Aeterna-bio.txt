Flamma Aeterna are a Black Metal band from Portugal. Starting in 2001 and releasing their first demo "Forever Inferno" in 2003. They are also supposed to have another demo as well entitled "Procreation in Fire". 

Members include:

Dominus Sabaoth  :     Guitars, Backing Vocals 

Ignis Nox :      Keyboards
