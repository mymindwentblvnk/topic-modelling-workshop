Black Metal band from Russia. They play fast and aggressive riffs with lots of blast beats. Lyrics revolve around satanism. They released a demo in 2002, a three way split "In Nomine Satanas" with Pogost and Byblis in 2008, a full length album "Raping the Creation of God", split "Grave Ritual" with Black Mass Ritual and split "Ave Satan Morituri Te Salutant!" with Doomguard in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beyond+Ye+Grave">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
