There are multiple artists with this name. 
(1) Doom/heavy-metal band from Paris, France. Formed in 2001 from the ashes of Dark White, they released their first album (Rising Dust) in 2005, and the Taste Of Fire EP in 2008.

Their new drummer is Mehdi, from Arkangel and Hangman's Chair.
