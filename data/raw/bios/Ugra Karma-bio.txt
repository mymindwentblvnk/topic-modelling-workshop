Ugra Karma (Harmful Action) is probably the first old school death metal band from Kathmandu, Nepal. They were formed in 2000 debuting with 6 track demo "The Himalayan Metal of Death". 

The only album "Blood Metal Initiation" is 34 minutes of excellent, nihilistic, obscure, total death metal.
