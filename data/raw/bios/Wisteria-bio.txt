There are multiple artists and groups using the name Wisteria. 

1. A gothic dream pop artist based in Los Angeles, California. 

2. A post-punk/electronic artist, first active in 2022. She combines fuzzy, ethereal guitar work with distorted electronics to create a distinct take on the classic post-punk sound. Currently, her releases include the single “enshrined/charon’s obol” and the EP “Lira de Orfeo.”
