Restrayned is a Classic Modern Metal band that mixes influences from several genres to create their unique sound. Restrayned features a thundering rhythm section and heavy driving guitars topped with soaring vocal melodies and harmonies. Their high energy live shows have made them a favorite with concert goers. 
They have supported bands like Trapt, Lynch Mob, Loudness and Faster Pussycat. Their new full length CD "A Dark New Day"  is currently available on iTunes and Amazon.com and has recently been named SRRocks.com's "Album of the Year" for 2012.
Their Song "Remember" (from the 2010 self-titled 5 song EP) was named "2011 Song of the Year" at SRRocks.com... an internet radio station based in Philadelphia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Restrayned">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
