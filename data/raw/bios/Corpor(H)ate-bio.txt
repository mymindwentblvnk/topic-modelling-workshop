Belgian band on base of a cover band, the band evolves according to its line up and has big evolutions, but finally found its style and imposes it in their first demo 3 titles " The beginning ". Their style of metal is difficult to target because all the members have different influences. Then, followed the departure from both original guitarists Xavier and David with all the compositions in May, 2010, the band was obliged to begin again and to recompose songs which are now very Deathcore and Thrash.
