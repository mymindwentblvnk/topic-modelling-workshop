Storm Messenger are a 5-piece metal band from Brisbane, Australia. They formed in 2013 and play a brand of metal that has elements of Thrash, Death and Black Metal, all tied together with a bit of Rock.

In 2015, Storm Messenger released their debut EP 'Brace For The Storm'. A 5-track EP of aggressive songs that also displayed the variety of their influences.

The current band lineup is:

Tim: Guitar and Backing Vocals
Shayne: Guitar
Paul: Bass
Alan: Drums
