"Professor Van Dusen" is a German series of radio plays which ran from 1978 till 1999. All episodes were written by Michael Koser based on stories by American writer Jacques Futrelle. The series followed the famous scientist and hobby-detective Professor Dr. Dr. Dr. Augustus Van Dusen and his assistant Hutchinson Hatch solving various cases around the world in the early 20th century. 76 episodes were produced (including some live plays). Van Dusen was voiced by Friedrich W. Bauschulte, Hatch was voiced by Klaus Herrn. See also: Prof. van Dusen <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Professor+van+Dusen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
