TSOM are a high energy alternative rock/metal  band from Witney in Oxfordshire. Blending elements of modern metalcore through to classic rock and jazz, their songs are laden with big hooks and vocal harmonies. Still aged only 16 and 17, and based in the UK, the journey so far has included 3 U.S. tours, 4 singles with  U.S. based Toil Records, and playing alongside some major artists at some great venues and festivals on both sides of the Atlantic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Twisted+State+Of+Mind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
