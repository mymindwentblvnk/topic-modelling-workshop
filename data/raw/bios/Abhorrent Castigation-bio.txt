Abhorrent Castigation is Brutal Death Metal band from Augsburg/Bremen currently (2021) consisting of the main members:
Thorsten - Guitars, Bass, Drums, Drum Machine, Background Vocals
Al - Vocals, Lyrics, Concept

Since their inception they have always took a pro pircacy stand, encouraging fans to download and share their music if they did not have enough money to aquire it legally. They have pro-actively shared their music on popular file sharing sites and forums to enhance their reach in the past.
