Ghetto Kids is a Mexican group formed in the Santo Domingo neighborhood, Mexico City, in 2012. The band is formed by two DJ's and a drummer, Alejandro Estrada, Luis Diaz and Jonathan Torres. The group sticks to the urban, alternative and electronic genres. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ghetto+Kids">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
