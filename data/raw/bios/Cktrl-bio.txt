cktrl (“can’t keep to reality”) is British multi-instrumentalist, producer and DJ Bradley Miller from London. 

Raised in Lewisham, south London, Miller learnt clarinet and saxophone, taught himself production, started DJing and got a show on online radio station NTS. 

Miller self-released his debut EP, Forest, in 2015.
