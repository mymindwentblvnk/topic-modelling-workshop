In the aftermath of English Dogs, Graham "Gizz" Butt and some others formed War Dance, who released the "None But The Brave" and "Short Sharp Shock" casettes and then vanished. They played intricate punk music heavily influenced by metal (as with anything Gizz played guitar on) and post-punk. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/WAR+DANCE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
