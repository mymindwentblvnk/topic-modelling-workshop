Forsth are a black metal band from Switzerland. The band split up in 1999 after releasing 2 albums, Winterfrost and Helvetic War. The member Gonahr is now in the band Morgart. Morgart is considered to be the follow up band to Forsth. Forsth at one time was known as Gorgon <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forsth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
