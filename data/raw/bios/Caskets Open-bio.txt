Caskets Open of Finland (Keuruu) is a slow rock band formed in 2004.

Band Members:
~ Timo Ketola (Vocals, Bass)
~ Antti Ronkainen (Guitar, Backing Vocals)
~ Pyry Ojala (Drums, Backing Vocals)

Discography:
~ Buried Upside Down (Demo) 2008
~ But You Rule (Full-length) 2010 Streaks Records
