Ruindom is a quartet of drunks from Valkeakoski. The most important influences are oldskool death metal, beer, booze and excess amount of cigarettes.

Antero Hakala: Vocals
Mikko Kivimäki: Drums
Marko Mäkinen: Bass & background grunts
Pasi Nisula: Guitars

Former members:
Esko Hirvijoki: Vocals

Releases:

'Sinister Calling' EP (November 2010)
'Sadist' demo (summer 2010)
'Animal Victim' demo (2009)
'Headslayer' demo (2008)
