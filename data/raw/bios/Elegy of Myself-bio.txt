Elegy Of Myself is a gothic/death/doom band from El Salvador.

Current line-up:
Joel Escobar - Vocals 
Leonel Viera - Guitars 
Victor Jimenez - Guitars 
Edwin Ascensio - Drums 
Oscar Ponce - Keyboards 
Javier Rivas - Bass
