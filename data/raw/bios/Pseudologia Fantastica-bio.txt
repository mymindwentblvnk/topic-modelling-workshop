Pseudologia Fantastica were a 4 piece band from Borsbeek, Antwerp (BELGIUM). They played death/doom in the vein of Rippikoulu, Sempiternal Deathreign with traces of psychedelia. They self-released a single demo called "Stars of Bitterness" in 1992 during their short-lived career. 

Last Known Lineup - 
Bram Van Emrik - Guitar, vocals
Tist Van Emrik - Drums
Johan - Guitar
David - Keyboards
