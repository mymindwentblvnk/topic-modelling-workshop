Bronz the band were one of the first British Rock acts ( N.W.B.H.M.) New Wave of British Heavy Metal,  to hit America in the mid 80’s, touring their début album “ Taken By Storm ”. Fronted at that time by Max Bacon on lead vocals and playing to 30,000 seater stadiums.
The first album went on to sell well over 200,000 albums worldwide. Which all helped to secure them a solid reputation as a British hard rock band to be reckoned with.
The single “ Send Down An Angel ” featured  heavily on the then fledgling M.T.V. in America and across Europe, with a video featuring the actress Emma Thompson. Reaching a top 10 position in the U.K. rock singles charts and a top 20 in the rock album charts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bronz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
