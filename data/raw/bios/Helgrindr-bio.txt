Helgrindr is a pagan black metal band from France. The band consist of members of Ornaments Of Sin who are a NSBM band.

Dolgren Ev Skjal : vocals, keyboards (Ornaments of Sin)
Svardath - guitars (Ornaments of Sin, guest for Eole Noir)
Halgran - bass 
Draghorn - guitars
Arnorth - drums
