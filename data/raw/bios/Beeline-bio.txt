There are 3 bands under the name Beeline.

One is a Canadian indie rock band formed in 2002 (originally as "Low Gravity") before taking the name Beeline in 2003. They have influences from Radiohead, Muse, Blackmail & Placebo.

The second is a Death Metal / Thrash Metal band from Chengdu (China). They released one demo (...you - 2002) before splitting up. Also appearing on Resurrection OF The Gods compilation. 

The third one is an indiepop music band from Cologne, Germany. Started as a duo in 2006 and grown to a 5-piece-band in 2007, Benji, Simon, Leon, Flo and Lukas are playing concerts together.
