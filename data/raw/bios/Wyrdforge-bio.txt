Wyrdforge is an Extreme Metal band from Brighton, UK. Their sound is a mix of Death, Thrash and Black Metal influenced by the likes of Entombed, Napalm Death, Mayhem, Dismember, 1349 and Doom. 

Their first, self-titled, demo was released February 8 2012 and can be found on their facebook page (http://www.facebook.com/pages/Wyrdforge/113572625350309).  

Lineup:
James - Vocals
Guy - Guitar
Glenn - Guitar
Luke - Bass
