Metal Duck were a thrash metal/crossover band from Manchester, England formed in 1985. They're most known for the Mower Liberation Front/Quack 'Em All split with fellow English thrashers Lawnmower Deth. They also released two demos and a full-length album titled "Auto Ducko Destructo Mondo" in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metal+Duck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
