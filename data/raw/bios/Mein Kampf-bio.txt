Mein Kampf was a speed/thrash metal band from Tokyo, Japan. Among several other Japanese thrash metal bands like Rommel and Rosenfeld, Mein Kampf had a Nazi image for shock value. The band released one limited pressed and hard to find single in 1985. They disbanded shortly after that. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mein+Kampf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
