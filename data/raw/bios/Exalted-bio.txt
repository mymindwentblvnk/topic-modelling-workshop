There are more than one artist with the name Exalted.

1) Exalted is a Black Metal band from Chicago Illinois featuring ex-members of Charles Bronson, MK-Ultra, Ottawa & in the past has shared members with Nachtmystium. They have several self released demos as well as a full length album on Battle Kommand, and upcoming releases on Deer Healer Records & Occult Contemporary.

2) Russian trance artist Ruslan Yusipov
