Witch Ripper are a 4-piece sludge, doom, stoner metal band from Seattle, Washington. To date, they have 2 releases: a 4-song ep in November 2012 and their first full length album "Homestead" in September 2018. For more information visit;
https://www.facebook.com/Witchripper/ or https://witchripper.bandcamp.com 
Members: Curtis Parker (Vocals/guitar)
Joe Eck (Drums)
Brian Kim (bass)
