Rocko Schamoni (* 1966 in Luetjenburg, recognized by public bureaus as Tobias Albrecht, further aliases King Rocko Schamoni, Bims Brohm, IBM Citystar, Mike Strecker and Silvio Strecker) is a german entertainer, musician, author, actor, politician, club owner and member of the comedy ensemble Studio Braun (Studio Brown).
