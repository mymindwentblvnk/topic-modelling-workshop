Hostil is a Thrash Metal band from Lima, Peru. the band consists of LUIS LIZARRAGA ( GUITAR - VOCALS ), JOSE GRADOS ( GUITAR ), YESID ARANDA ( DRUMS ), and Jesus on (Bass). They released there first album Infernal Rights in 2018 on independent label.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hostil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
