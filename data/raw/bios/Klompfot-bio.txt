Metal band based in Trondheim, Norway.
Combines elements from different metal genres.

Consists of:
* Ørjan Lunde - guitar.
* Rune Leraand - guitar.
* Håkon Dalen - bass and voice.
* Sverre Leraand - drums.

Erling Hilstad plays the bass on the recorded tracks.
