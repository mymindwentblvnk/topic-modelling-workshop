Formed in Varberg, Sweden more than a decade ago. In 2004 the debut album "A Decade In The Dark" was released and the follow up album "Eraser" was released in 2008. The members have been/still are involved in bands and projects such as: Eucharist (R.I.P.), Anata, Livin' Parazite, Eternal Lies, The End (Swe), Project:Voodoo & BeerBelly Boys.
