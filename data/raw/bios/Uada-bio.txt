UADA (meaning 'haunted' in Latin) is a black metal band from Portland, USA that formed in October 2014. Their debut full-length album 'Devoid of Light' was released on April 8, 2016 through Eisenwald Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Uada">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
