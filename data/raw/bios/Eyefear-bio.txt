Progressive metal band formed in Melbourne, Australia in 1994. So far they have released 5 full-length albums, an EP and a demo.

Discography:
Edge of Existence, 1996
Dawn... A New Beginning (EP), 1999
Promo (demo), 2003
9 Elements of Inner Vision, 2004
A World Full of Grey, 2007
The Unseen, 2008
The Inception of Darkness, 2012
