Unbreakable Hatred is a Technical Death Metal band from Quebec. Formed in 2008, they have released one full length album in 2011 "Total Chaos," which has been received well with the underground metal community. 
Current members are:
Simon Lapointe (Vocal, Bass)
Philippe Drouin (Guitar)
