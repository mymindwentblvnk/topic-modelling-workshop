Terror Infinitum is black metal band from Vladivostok, Russian Federation fromed in 2011 by guitarist Sorg and vocalist Necron after split-up of the local black metal band "Nebiroth".
Some time later band recruited drummer Krieg and the core of the band formed. With that line-up they recorded their debut album, called Тёмный горизонт (which means "Dark Horizon" in English).

Current line-up:
Sorg - guitars
Tyrant - vocals, bass
Krieg - drums
