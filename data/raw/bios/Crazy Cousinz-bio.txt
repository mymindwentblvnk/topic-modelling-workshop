Crazy Cousinz was formed in November 2007 as a producer collective consisting of two bassline-producing cousins (Flukes & Paleface) alongside two other members, Kodi: Engineer/Producer and Play: Producer/DJ. Paleface owns & runs bassline stalwart Northern Line Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crazy+Cousinz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
