Clor were a five-piece post-punk band from Brixton, England. Their breakthrough came with the release of their self-titled first album, released in 2005 to great critical acclaim. Their music is angular in melody and jerky in rhythm, and is often compared to Devo and Gary Numan. 

The band split up in May 2006. Guitarist Luke Smith has produced and remixed various artists whilst singer.guitarist Barry Dobbin now fronts the band Barringtone. 
The bass player - Max, was recently touring with Ali Love in October 2006, but has since left to tour with Groove Armada in Australia.
