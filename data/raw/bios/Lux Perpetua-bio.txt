Lux Perpetua is the name of several artist.

The brain-child of Justin Wolf from Charlottesville, Virginia. With influences ranging from folk, to garage, and country, Lux Perpetua's music is rooted in abtract dialogue and melodic chord structure. He has recorded and played with artists such as The Extraordinaires, Folkskunde, and Look Alike, and currently resides in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Also a Power Metal band from Warsaw, Poland formed in 2004 and going by the name of Sentinel till 2009. They  released their first EP in 2014 entitled Forever We Stand and an album The Curse of the Iron King in February 2017. The band was named after a historical novel with fantasy elements by Andrzej Sapkowski. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lux+Perpetua">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
