Archaic Decapitator is a 4 man brutal death metal band from the US. They formed in 2007 and have released one demo in 2009 and a full length "Impalement Ceremonies" in 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Archaic+Decapitator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
