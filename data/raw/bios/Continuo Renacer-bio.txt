Continuo Renacer is a Progressive Death Metal band with jazz touches from Basque Country, Spain. In the beginning, Continuo Renacer was a Death Metal band, but the last changes has modified their style to achieve a sound like Cynic, Atheist or Acid Death. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Continuo+Renacer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
