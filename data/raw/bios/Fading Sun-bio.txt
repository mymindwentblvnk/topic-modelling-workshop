Funeral Doom / Death Metal band from Belarus, formed in 2010. Basically a Wormphlegm/Stabat Mater/Hierophant worship band with some slight Evoken hints. Although recorded in a professional studio, the sound is purposely as raw and unpolished as it can be.
Discography:
2011 - Shift off the Shells (Split with DSOLTB)
2011 - Yawning Void (Tape EP)
