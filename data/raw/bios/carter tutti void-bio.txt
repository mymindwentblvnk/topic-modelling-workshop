A collaboration between Chris Carter & Cosey Fanni Tutti (of Throbbing Gristle; Chris & Cosey; CTI; The Genetic Terrorists; Carter Tutti; X-TG) and Nik Void (of Factory Floor). In 2012 they released an album called Transverse, most of it where recorded live @ Short Circuit presents MUTE, at The Roundhouse, London, 13.05.2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carter+Tutti+Void">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
