Waelcyrge is a Flemish pagan thrash metalband. They were known as Wapenspraak & Drinkgelag between 2003 and 2009. 

Their first full-length album called Nithstang was released in May 2012. 

Current line-up: 
Grimgäert - Vocals 
D.K. Skeletor - Bass (also in Hulderfolk, Dark Ages, ex-Demonizer, Gae Bolga, ex-Zwartketterij)
Fynnbrenn Ó Torcklàn - Drums (Taranis, Dark Ages, Strange Facts in the Scalpel Case)
Nerclath - Guitars (ex-Demonizer, Urzamoth, session for Corpus Christii)
