Katari was a Latvian screamo band from Jēkabpils, Latvia. They were active between 2005 and 2006.

The original lineup:

Ēriks Blūms - vocals;
Rolands Virks - guitars;
Edijs Majors - bass;
Jānis Grugulis - drums.
