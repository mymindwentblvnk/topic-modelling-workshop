Violent Devoties is a deathgrind band from Austria. They formed in 2000 by Georg Axmann (Vocals), Gerald Valentin (Guitars and Vocals - Desperate Remember),  Gerd Kollmann (Guitars), ,Alexander “Duffy” Krammer (Bass) and Manuel Rainer (Drums - Desperate Remember and Mystiria).
