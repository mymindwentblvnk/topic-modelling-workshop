Dom Dracul is a one man Swedish Black Metal act formed in 2004 by Lord Therramon. After releasing a demo in 2004 he brought out the full length album "Attack on the Crucified" in 2005. One year later a split with Capitis Damnare, Sarin and Necroplasma followed. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dom+Dracul">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
