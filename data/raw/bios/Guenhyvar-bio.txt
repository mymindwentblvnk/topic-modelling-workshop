Guehyvar is a Metal band from Blackstone Massachusets. They mainly play Progressive and Death metal, but they have a wide variety of other influences. The current lineup is Ross Sted on Vocals and guitars, Lucas Gustafson on guitar, Sprigg on bass, and Anders Nordstrom on drums. They have released a demo entitled "The Gall," and are currently looking to play shows. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Guenhyvar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
