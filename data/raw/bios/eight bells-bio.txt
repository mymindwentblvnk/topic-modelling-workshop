Eight Bells are a three-piece experimental / post-black metal band of Oregon, US origin. They are comprised of Christopher Van Huffel, Melynda Jackson and Haley Weiner.  

The band have one released demo, Isosceles and have played a number of shows, including a support act for atmospheric metal band, Agalloch. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eight+Bells">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
