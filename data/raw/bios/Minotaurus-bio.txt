Multiple artists are known as Minotaurus.

1. Band from 70's that was actually named "Minotauros", performed in Germany but originally came from Greece. They played national, folk, greek music. http://www.mygreek.fm/albums/MTgwODE18081/Rain-Over-Thessalia

2. Psychedelic/classic rock band, West Germany;
only release: "Fly Away" (1978) at the Garden Of Delights label (reissue 2002)

Vocals - Peter Scheu
Guitars - Ludger Hofstetter, Michael Helsberg
Bass - Bernd Maciej
Keyboards - Diedel B.
Drums - Uli Poetschulat 

3. Celtic folk metal band from Germany.

4. Hard trance duo from Germany.
