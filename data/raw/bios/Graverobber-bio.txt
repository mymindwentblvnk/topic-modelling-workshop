A Dutch Old School Death Metal band, formed in 2012 bij Felix and Kalv. They are inlfuenced by Boss HM-2 fueled from Sweden (Entombed, Dismember, Unleashed) as well as legendary bands from their own country (Asphyx, Sinister, Pestilence). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Graverobber">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
