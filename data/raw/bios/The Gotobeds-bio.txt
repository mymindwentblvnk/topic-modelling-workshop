The Gotobeds is an American indie-rock band from Pittsburgh, PA. 

The band's third album, ‘Debt Begins at 30’, features outside contributors on every track. The album’s first single, “Calquer The Hound,” includes Evan Richards, and Rob Henry of Kim Phuc. On “Twin Cities,” Tracy Wilson, formerly of Dahlia Seed and currently of Positive NO!, shares the vocals. The title track includes Mike Seamans and Bob Weston.  Silkworm guitarist Tim Midyett plays on “Parallel". The likes of 12XU label boss Gerard Cosloy, Tre Orsi’s Matt Barnhart, Victoria Ruiz of Downtown Boys, Pittsburgh wordsmiths Jason Baldinger and Scott MacIntyre play on other tracks.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Gotobeds">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
