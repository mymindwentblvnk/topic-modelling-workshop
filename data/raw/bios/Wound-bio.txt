There are three artists who use the name Wound. 

1) Death Metal band from Wiesbaden, Hesse, Germany. Formed in 2011. http://www.ibelieveindeath.bigcartel.com
2) Doom/sludge metal band from Finland, formed in 2010.
3) Solo ambient artist previously known as Wounds. Active since late 2009 with the name change taking place mid-2014. His music is a collision of ambient textures, piano and string sounds, with drone and glitch elements. wounds.bandcamp.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
