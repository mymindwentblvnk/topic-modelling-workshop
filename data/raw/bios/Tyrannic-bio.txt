Tyrannic is a doom/thrash metal band from Sydney, Australia. The band was formed in 2011, followed by the release of their debut self-titled demo at the end of 2012, now supported with shows amongst New South Wales.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tyrannic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
