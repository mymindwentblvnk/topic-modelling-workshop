Based in San Francisco, Temperament is the electronic music project of Andrew McCright. Primarily slow tempo, ambient, instrumental, and electronic, the music evokes moody melancholic atmospheric feelings.

Temperament has released four albums: Chocolate Butter (March 2010) , Noire (November 2010), Vivacious (August 2011), and Deep Blue (February 2013).
