Dazzled Sticks is Dutch rapper Sticks (Junte Uiterwijk, known for Opgezwolle and Rico & Sticks) and Dutch singer, songwriter, guitarist and producer Dazzled Kid (Tjeerd Bomhof, who gained prominence as the frontman of rock band Voicst).

Together they make 'hiphop extraordinaire' in Dutch. Their eponymous debut album Dazzled Sticks was released in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dazzled+Sticks">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
