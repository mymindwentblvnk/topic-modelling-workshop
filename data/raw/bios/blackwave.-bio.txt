Blackwave. is hiphop duo from Antwerp, Belgium composed by Willem Ardui (producer, vocals) and  Jean Atohoun (vocals). In 2019 they released their debut album 'Are We Still Dreaming?'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/blackwave.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
