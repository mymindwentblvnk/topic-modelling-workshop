Goblin Hovel is a folk metal project started by Derek Schultz in the summer of 2010. Live band members include:  Derek Schultz on guitar, cello, and vocals, Sam Kessler on lead vocals and percussion, Prateek Poddar on guitar, and Dylan Hodges on bass.
