Trelleborg is a band from Saint-Petersburg. They play Symphonic Metal with elements of humppa. The band is named after fortress in Denmark built around 1000 year by Danish King Svein Forkbeard during the war with Anglo-Saxons. The circle in the middle part of band's logo is a schematic plan of the fortress from a bird's-eye view.

Trelleborg are : 
Kirill J. M. - vocals, lead- and rhytm-guitars, keyboards
Oksi - accordion
Dmitriy - bass
