Darrva is a Latvian death metal band from Jelgava, Latvia. They have been described as, "grim and serious music of the land to which we belong, of its nature. Maybe pagan, perhaps misanthropic. Nature's big and small victories over people."

In late 2013, the band released their independent debut album Saule Neuzausīs, which gained some buzz and also the attention of local listeners.

Members:

Jānis Tiltiņš (Deodium) - vocals;
Slaviks Murugovs - guitars;
Mārtiņš Jansons - bass;
Brūss Kalniņš (Deodium, Velnezers) - drums.

Former members:

Mārcis Drava - vocals;
Rihards Siksna (Theatrum) - drums.
