a death metal band from Italy that was formed in 1990. The band was originally called Massacrator (Ita), then Dark Lust, then finally Necromass. They released a demo "Shadow of the Lost Entity " in 1992.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Lust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
