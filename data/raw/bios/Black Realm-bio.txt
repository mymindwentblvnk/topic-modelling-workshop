Black Realm was a Raw Black Metal band that formed in 1994 in Bonn, North Rhine-Westphalia Germany. They disbanded in 1997 after releasing one demo "Shadows of the Ancient Times".

Black Realm members

Andreas Funke Bass, Vocals 

Peter Krause Drums 

Vincent Choffrut Guitars
