Zack Uidl is a Multi-Talented Guitarist, Songwriter, Teacher, Producer and Engineer. He has studied and performed with many great artists and continues molding the minds of future shredders to this day. His recent projects include endeavors such as a 24 minute collaboration, a solo album Released in 2008, and the Thoughts Betrayed Album debut. He is continuing to record and write material as well as a series of other projects.
