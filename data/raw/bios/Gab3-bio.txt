Gab3 is an American rapper, founder of “OMG! DUDE! Records”, producer and video director from Los Angeles, California. In the videography world, he is mostly known for his 1990s VHS aesthetic used when shooting videos. He is also a pioneer in the genre of emo rap/emo trap.

He was a member of the rap duo UZi who specializes in visuals for music videos. Gab3’s most notable music video visuals are for Kanye West’s “Famous” and Drake’s 2016 song “Sneakin'.” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/gab3">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
