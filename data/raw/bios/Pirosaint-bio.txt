Power Thrash Music and head-banging feel to your ears since 1989. 
In 2011, Pirosaint has relocated to NY, the band is currently recording new material. 

The band started out as a death metal band from Santiago de Chile that later became Thrash Metal. 

The band was formed in 1989, joining a small but powerful and enthusiastic local heavy metal scene. After releasing two demos in 1992 and 1994, the band issued an EP called New God in 1997. 

To celebrate Pirosaint's first active decade, the group released a compilation called Ten Years From the Emptiness in the year 2000, re-issued in 2004, by Drago Bonacich, allMusic.com

In 2011, Pirosaint has relocated to NY, the band is the band is currently recording an EP, that will be available soon.
