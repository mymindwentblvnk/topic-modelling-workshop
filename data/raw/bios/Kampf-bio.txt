Black Metal from Hellas featuring members of The Shadow Order (Pyrron) and Dodsferd (Wrath). So far one demo has been released called "Nothing But Wrath" released through Regimental Records in 2006. Despite having a member of The Shadow Order this band is not connected with NS ideology. Kampf's ideology is Nothing But Hate. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KampF">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
