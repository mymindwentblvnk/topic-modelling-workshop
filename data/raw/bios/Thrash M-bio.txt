Thrash M is an old school thrash metal band from Poland since 2007. They are trying to mix  the sound of classic thrash metal of the 80's with some modern sound of today's new thrash bands. The band is currently looking for a label. 

Discography:
Just Thrash'em All! - demo 2008
Thrash The City - EP 2009

Lineup:
Łukasz Jezuss
Michał Prue
David Winter
Krzysztof Nordian
