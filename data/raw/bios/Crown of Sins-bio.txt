Today, 21 July 2009 Crown of Sins has launched his brand new album, “To Faith Away” an experimental sound of danceable gothic metal inspired by the sounds of electro-pop and gothic metal. All 11 tracks are free to download from www.hyleon.com without any commercial restraints. For those who prefer other uncomfortable formats such as Audio CD’s, they can be bought only by request made at crownofsins@yahoo.com, via mail or YM.
Crown of Sins is a one man band from Oradea, Romania, the experimental project of Caesar, the owner of Hyleon, the black metal – doom brand. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crown+of+sins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
