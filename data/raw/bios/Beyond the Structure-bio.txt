Beyond the Structure is an Estonian technical death metal band from Tallinn, Estonia that formed in 2012.

Members:

Edgar Balabanov - vocals;
Artjom Balakshin (Freakangel, Ognemöt) - guitars;
Yaroslav Luzin - bass;
Simo Atso (Intrepid, Tankist, Loits, Nihilistikrypt) - drums.

Former members:

Pavel Torpan - vocals;
Mihail Makoshin (Sociasylum) - vocals;
Bartłomiej Kurek - guitar;
Alexey Mattal - bass;
Endrik Salak - bass;
Roman Demchenko (Süda) - drums.
