The group was founded in late October - early November 2009. Participants changed until at the end of the summer of 2011 when a stable and complete the lineup was formed. In early October of the same year a 2 song demo was recorded. In November the band first performed live at a local club "Tarantula". At the end of 2012 initial songs for the debut album were recorded on LES Records. Mixing the album was done in the summer of 2013 at Aglaomorpha studio in Stary Oskol . At the same time frequent concerts were played. In August 2013 an agreement was reached wit Solitude Production setting the release of the disc on March 24 , 2014 .  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Restless+Oblivion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
