Mictlantecuhtli (Pronounced: Meekt-lahn-te-koot-ly)is a Black/Thrash Metal styled musical group from Los Angeles, CA. Their lyrical themes are based on Aztec Mythology. 

They are unsigned at the moment, but have put out two self-produced Albums (Pillars Of Silence, 2004)and ( Warriors of the Black Sun, 2008).
