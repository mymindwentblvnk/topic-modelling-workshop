Dominion Caligula is a Swedish melodic death/black metal band formed in 1998. Lyrics tell about Roman empire and Caligula. Current line-up:
Lars Johansson - Guitars
Dominion - Guitars, (ex-Dark Funeral, ex-Obscurity, Vicious Art).
Joakim Widfeldt - Bass, (ex-Obscurity, Vicious Art)
Emperor Magus Caligula - Vocals, (Dark Funeral)
Gaahnfaust - Drums, (ex-Dark Funeral, ex-Obscurity, Vicious Art)
