Jorrit Popkema, better know as D-Sturb, is a hardstyle producer from Arnhem, the Netherlands. Blazing an inspirational trail to victory at high-speed, this hardstyle dynamo is the definition of a rare breed. His authentic and remarkable production skills, as well as natural on-stage persona are the fundamentals that have built this solidly cemented, full-package artist.  

Holding a rich musical background, the End of Line Recordings-signed act has gained worldwide recognition for his striking sound that infamously merges thrashing kickrolls with atmospheric melodies and a larger-than-life character. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D-Sturb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
