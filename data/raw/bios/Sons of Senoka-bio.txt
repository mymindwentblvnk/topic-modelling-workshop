Sons Of Senoka are a Melodic Death Metal band from La Rochelle, Poitou-Charentes, France. They got together in 2006 and released their debut album 'The Blacksky Stand Alone' in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sons+of+Senoka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
