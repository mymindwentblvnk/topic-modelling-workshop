There are 3 bands with the name Bottom Line.

1) A pop punk band from Cincinatti, OH.

2) Is also a hardcore band from New Jersey on Mache Records. www.bottomlinehc.bandcamp.com

3) Bottom Line (NYC) was a band from New York City and Westchester Country from 2001 to 2006. They released a demo in 2001, a 7" in 2003 (No One's Safe, Stab & Kill Records), and a 4-song sampler in 2004. They played New York style hardcore and appeared frequently in Wilkes Barre, PA, as well as the New York area. Their last show was on August 20, 2006 at CBGB's with Tears of Frustration and District 9. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bottom+Line">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
