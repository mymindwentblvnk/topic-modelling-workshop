Brutal Death Metal band from Paraná, Brazil, formed in 1997. Their lyrics have strong Christian themes and they are amongst the growing number of extreme metal bands to feature a female vocalist.

To date they have released the following:
World in Decay - Demo, 1998, 
Annulling Dark Forces - EP, 2001
Eternal Suffering - Full-length, 2003
None Should Perish - Full-length, 2007

Lineup:

Scarelli - Vocals
Firás - Guitar, backing vocals
Renan - Bass
Franco - Drums
