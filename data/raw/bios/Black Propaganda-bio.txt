Black Propaganda is 1) an electronic producer from Medellin, Colombia. 2) a thrash metal band from Italy.
Formed in 2007.
Location: Turin, Piedmont

Members:
Eric Di Donato - Drums (2007-present)
Ian Binetti - Guitars (2007-present)
Jacopo Battuello - Vocals (2010-2012, 2013-present)
Mirco Rizzi - Bass (2013-present)
