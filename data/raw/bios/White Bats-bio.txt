White Bats is a band from Madrid, Spain, formed in 2014 by Jaime B. (vocals, synths), Victor (drums and percussion, programming), Paula (bass, backing vocals) and Jake (guitar, backing vocals, synths).

They self-released their debut White Bats EP in September 2014.
