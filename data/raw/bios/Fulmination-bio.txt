Fulmination was a Death metal from Avesta, Sweden. Their lyrics follow themes such as: Psychology, Abstract Themes.



formed in 1991







Last known line-up








Mats Berggren - Vocals, Bass (Moondark, Entrails (Swe))
Mats Forsell - Guitar (Uncanny (Swe))
Fredrik Norrman - Guitar (Uncanny (Swe), Katatonia (Swe), October Tide)
Christer Enstrom - Drums
