Iconoclast Contra are a Black/Death metal band formed in Utah, USA in 2007 after Ibex Throne Split-up. The original and current line-up consists of: The Zodiac - Vocals, R. Sodomizer - Bass, LD Heretic Warfare - Guitar, and T. Hatefucker  on Drums. They signed to Red Light Sound and have released one album "Combat Is the Voice of the Heathen" on June 3rd, 2010.

The album has been re-released via Strong Survive Records USA and the band have parted ways with Red Light.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Iconoclast+Contra">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
