Proklet was black metal from Rijeka, Croatia.
Proklet recorded one EP (Forest of forbidden whisphers) and one full lenght album (Mizantropski crni metal)
Disbanded on July 7, 2015.
Lyrical themes:
Misanthropy, Death, Nature, Satanism

Members: 
Drinogor - Drums
Sablast - Bass
Prokletnik - Guitar
