A Symphonic Black Metal band from Johannesburg, South Africa. Formed in 2010 they have release two albums so far: Voyage to Spectral Realm in 2015 and Spectral Witch of the Hex River in 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spectral+Realm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
