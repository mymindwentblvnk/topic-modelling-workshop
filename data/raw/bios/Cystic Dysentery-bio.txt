Cystic Dysentery is a brutal death metal band from Jacksonville,Florida U.S.A  that was formed by ben davis (lead guitar) in 2002 the band recorded it's first demo in 2003 with P.B on vocals and Grover norton on drums..The band is now getting ready to release their first album on DEATHGASM records with the new lineup of BRIAN FITZGERALD on vocals and JIM ROSS on second guitar , with BEN still on lead and GROVER on drums... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cystic+Dysentery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
