Februus are a Synth-Metal band from Edinburgh, Scotland.

Although the band began as the solo studio project of founder Guy Perchard in 2004, over the years Februus has changed line-ups as well as styles to exist today as a 5-piece band.

Februus are an independant group, where all music is produced, recorded and released by themselves.

Februus are:

Rebecca Perchard (Vocals)
Guy Perchard (Synths, Vocals)
Andrew Donaldson (Synths, Stylophone, Vocals)
Callum Arnott (Guitar)
Mark Macgillivray (Bass)

Februus have released:

Fading Memory (2004)
Safe (2006)
The Power of Your Fear (2007)
Februus Doesn't Love You Anymore (2010)
