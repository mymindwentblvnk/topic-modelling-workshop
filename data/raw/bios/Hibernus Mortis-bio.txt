Hibernus Mortis is an old school death metal band from Florida, US. They formed in 1996. They have released a demo  "Into the Thresholds of Dead Winter" in 1998,  a live album "Live Manslaughter" in 2000, another demo "Hail The Apocalypse" in 2003 and a full length "The Existing Realms of Perpetual Sorrow" in 2002.

They split up in 2006 but reunited in 2010.
