Tim Reaper is the performance name for London web developer Ed Alloh, who began producing jungle tracks at home in 2009. His first release was a remix of Trace's Final Chapter in 2010. He has since released a steady stream of singles, including a wide range of collaborations with DJs such as Dwarde, Comfort Zone, Kloke, and DevNull. In 2020 he founded the Future Retro label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tim+Reaper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
