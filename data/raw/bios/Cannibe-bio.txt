Cannibe is a goregrind band from Gonnesa, Sardinia, Italy.  They formed in 2001 under the name Kabhal and then changed their name to Cannibe in Autumn 2004.  The current lineup consist of Ivan Loi (vocals) and Nicola (guitars, bass and drum programming).  Past members include  Dario Caredda (all instruments),  Franco Fois (all instruments), Andreas Eder (bass, drums, guitars) and Joseph di Porto (drum programming).  They have released several splits since 2005.  Their lyrical themes include gore, murder, cannibalism and anatomy.  They are not signed to a record label.
