Cryptic Shift are a thrash/death metal band that formed in Leeds, UK in 2013. Their first EP "Beyond the Celestial Realms" was released on April 1, 2016. The single "Cosmic Dreams" was release digitally by UKEM in 2017. Recommended for fans of Revocation, Vektor, Blood Incantation, Timeghoul, Morbid Angel etc. https://cryptic-shift.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cryptic+Shift">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
