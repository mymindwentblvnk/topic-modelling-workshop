There are 3 artists with the name Wiz:

1. Swedish Power Metal band
2. Rapper from Boston

1: Wiz is a Swedish Power Metal band from Gothenburg and they released their debut album Shattered-Mind-Therapy in 2004. The band was formed in 1996. Lyrical styles are sorrow, hate, love and stories.

2: Wiz (Sam Adams) is an up and coming 21 year old rapper out of the Boston area. He has come onto the scene extremely fast with his hit single "I Hate College Remix", where he has remixed Asher Roth's hit single "I Love College". Wiz is currently a senior at Trinity College and has performed alongside the YIng Yang Twins and many colleges. He is quickly becoming one of the most respectable underground rappers. His first mixtape is set to drop soon, but no dates have been released yet.
