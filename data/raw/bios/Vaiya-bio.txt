Vaiya is an ambient black metal band from Melbourne, Australia. They released a split CD-R - limited to 15 copies - with DR called "Cast" in 2010.  In 2011 Vaiya released the ":W i n t e r m o o n:" EP, which contains an 18-minute track and is available for free at the band's page on BANDCAMP - http://vaiya.bandcamp.com/track/w-i-n-t-e-r-m-o-o-n

In 2012 they released their second EP, "Mudh", which consists of a single, nearly 28-minute track; and in 2014 Vaiya released their first full-length LP titled "Remnant Light" consisting of three tracks, 13 minutes each. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vaiya">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
