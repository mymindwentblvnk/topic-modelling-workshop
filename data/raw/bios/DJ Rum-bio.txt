DJ Rum, a.k.a. Djrum (pronounced 'drum') is a producer Felix Manuel currently living in London. His music is deep and jazzy influenced with a sprinkle of Techno added to the mix, as well as dubstep and future garage. His first LP Seven Lies was released on  April 22, 2013 via 2nd Drop Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Rum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
