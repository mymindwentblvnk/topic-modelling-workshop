Lycanthrophy is a ferocious female-fronted fastcore band hailing from the Czech Republic. They have release a numerous amount of 7 inch split EPs over the years on various labels, and have recently released their first self titled full-length album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lycanthrophy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
