Yasha (夜叉) is a japanese heavy metal (with some "modern metal" elements) band with guitarist Katsuhiko Fukushima who is Akira Takasaki's (Loudness, Lazy, Ji-Zo) first disciple. At the beggining band's releases was titled as "夜叉", later (in 2005, with "Zoku" album) they stared using romanji spelling - "Yasha".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yasha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
