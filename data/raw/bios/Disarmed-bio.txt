Disarmed new album is a fresh mix of the famous drummer Marco Minnemann, Fabio Brinkmann and Mario Trentini influences. Some of the tracks are relax while most of the album rocks. In this CD, Disarmed challenges the norms by playing its unique music. This album will quickly become a "must have" for all people who love the work of those three extremely talented musicians.

Disarmed are also a 3 piece alternative rock band from Essex and originally came together in 2001. They have a new demo out now which sounds like life but more tuneful and is filled to the brim with catchy, gloom tinged hooks.
