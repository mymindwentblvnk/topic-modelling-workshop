Despite it seems a page generated by a wrong tag (perhaps it was so), Metal Band was a melodic death metal band from Madison, Wisconsin (USA). The band could not settle on a band name or song titles, so they decided to call themselves "Metal Band".
They released only a demo before disbanding.
