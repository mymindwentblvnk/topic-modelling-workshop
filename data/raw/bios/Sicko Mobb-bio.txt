Sicko Mobb are a hip-hop duo from Chicago, Illinois, USA. Formed in  2013 , the duo consists of of Lil Trav (Delon Sneed) and Lil Ceno (Maurice Larry). They released their debut mixtape Super Saiyan Vol. 1 in December 2013, and signed a deal with Sony/ATV imprint Stellar Songs/Water Music Publishing the same month. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sicko+Mobb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
