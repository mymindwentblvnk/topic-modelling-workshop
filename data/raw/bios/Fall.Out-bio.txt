Fall.Out began in 2002, featuring Daz Jones on vocals,Hayden and Davey on guitars and Ed Jones on drums.  Fall.Out are currently looking to recruit a new bassist as Splinter has stepped down. 

Fall.Out take influence from both classic and modern metal to create a new metal style.  They were winners of The Mic Award for Excellence in Their Field of Expertise and Winners of the Postmortem Unsigned competition March 2008.
