Populus is a progressive rock/metal band from Montreal, Canada formed in 2003. Rooted in the metal genre, Populus elevates itself through the exploration of deep melodies and instrumentation. Fusing the rich textures of piano and accoustic instruments with distorted guitars, the music creates a vast range of emotions.
