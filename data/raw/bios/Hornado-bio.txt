HORNADO is a Heavy Metal band founded 2005 in Bonn (NRW), Germany. They play old school heavy metal music combined with epic elements. Other bands like Judas Priest, Manilla Road and Accept have a huge impact on their music.

Discography:
- Heterock Explosion (Demo, 2008)
- Wild Temple (Full-length, 2016)
- Supersonic Punch (Full-length, 2019)

HORNADO is:
- Dave Laserchild (Vocals)
- Mad Matt (Guitar)
- Ian Lownstone (Guitar)
- Hornhammer (Drums)
- Moe Rider (Bass)
