Opposing Motion are a Symphonic Progressive metal band based in Birmingham, UK.  With the release of a debut EP (The Illustration) recorded at the famous Robannas Studios in Birmingham 2010, Opposing Motion have rapidly made themselves known in the prog genre. With several high street magazine features and numerous excellent reviews, 'The Illustration' has set the foundations for a full length debut album to be released in 2013 through Iconic Record Label LION MUSIC. 2011 also saw the band feature on covermount CD's worldwide, including the prestigious Terrorizer magazine 'Fear Candy-Fresh Flesh' CD, which show cases the best unsigned metal talent.
