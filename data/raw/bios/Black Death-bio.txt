There is more than one Black Death:

1. The all-black US heavy metal band from Cleveland, Ohio, formed in 1977.

2. The earliest incarnation of Darkthrone, who recorded two demos as a trio in 1987.

3. A recent black/thrash metal duo from El Sobrante, California.
