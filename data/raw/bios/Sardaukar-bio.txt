Sardaukar (from the book Dune, it means "death troopers" in Arabic), was a short lived metal band around 2002-2003. Influences were Year Of Our Lord, Prayer For Cleansing, God Forbid, Children Of Bodim, Darkest Hour, In Flames.

Members:

Brandon Jesse - bass
Thomas Exum - vocals
Dan of the short pants - vocals  (dan davis of kodan armada)
Ryan Shea - drums
Kurt and Kyle - twin guitar action
