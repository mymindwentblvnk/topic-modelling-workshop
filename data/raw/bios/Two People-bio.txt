Two People’s debut record First Body draws its strength from simplicity. Born of former Snakadaktal members – and former teenhood sweethearts – Phoebe Lou and Joey Clough, the duo are a sombre, murmuring descendent of their previous band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Two+People">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
