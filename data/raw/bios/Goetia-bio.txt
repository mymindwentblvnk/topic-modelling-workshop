There are multiple artists/bands using the name Goetia.

1. A Polish Black Metal band: http://www.metal-archives.com/band.php?id=1299

2. Atmospheric sludge/post-metal from Ottawa, Ontario.

Bandcamp

3. Goetia is a young french artist from Montpellier . Native from Marocco, after being the singer of metal band 'Immortal Spirit', she arrives in France in '98, where she discovers the Techno/Hardcore scene.
She decides to get involved in it by composing and creates the Elektronoize association with a friend.
