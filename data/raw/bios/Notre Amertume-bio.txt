Notre Amertume is a black metal project of Bulgarian artist Ivo Illiev, playing all instruments and French artist Arsonist as vocalist. It is located in Clermont-Ferrand, France and born in 2011.
2012 they released a split together with Arsonist's solo project Ars Moriendi titled Agonie d'un Ancien Monde. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Notre+Amertume">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
