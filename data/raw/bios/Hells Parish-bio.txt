Groove Metal band from Portland, OR. Formed in 2011 by Connor Reising, Nathan Shakeshaft, Alex Rosenberg, and Maliq Rodgers, Hells Parish sought to combine the sounds of Lamb of God, Chimaira, and Pantera. Their EP "Shadows Rise" was released on iTunes on Jun 10, 2013.
