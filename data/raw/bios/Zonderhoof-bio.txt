Zonderhoof are based in South Wales. Their music is mostly instrumental and has been described as hybrid of sludge, doom metal and stoner rock. Their latest album 'Hakken!' was released by Undergroove Records in September 2011 and was also included as a free CD with issue #214 of Terrorizer magazine in the UK. 

They first released their 3 track demo in 2006 which led to the release of their debut EP/Mini album 'Zonderhoof' on Sound Devastation Records in 2007.
