ORION CHILD is a band from Durango (Vizcaya-Spain) formed in 2006. Their style combines Melodic Death Metal, with roots of Power Metal and touches of Black Metal. After recording a first demo in 2006, at the end of 2008 they come to NewLife Studios of Madrid to record their first studio album "A New Dark Apology", mastered at Finnvox Studios in Finland by Mika Jussila.
