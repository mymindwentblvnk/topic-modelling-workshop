Southern Germany based producer DISTURBIA started his career with punk/rock and made a turn towards electronic sounds in the year 2009. After signing on different labels and headlining various events in Europe, he is now on the way to become an institution in german drum & bass. His productions concentrate on harsh darkstep and techy neurofunk with intense energy, driving beats and dark atmosphere, often influenced by industrial, hardcore and dark ambient.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disturbia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
