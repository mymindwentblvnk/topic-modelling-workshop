There are multiple bands with the name Assault:

1) Assault was a thrash metal band from Canada. They were active from 1985 to 1988.

2) Assault is a Japanese Hardcore Punk with a few recordings under HG Facts Records, they play hard-driving, rocking mixture of melodic guitars, metallic-edged epic dark crust and Burning Spirits Hardcore.

3) Assault was a streetpunk band from Vancouver B.C. Canada. Although they were only around for a few years, they managed to release an E.P. intitled "We're The Reds".

4) Assault was a black/death/thrash band from Iquique, Chile.

5) Punk band from Santa Maria, CA. Released a tape in 1985 and a few songs on compilations.

6) A death metal band from Singapore.

7) A thrash/speed metal band from Oregon

8) Hardcore band from Bydgoszcz, Poland
