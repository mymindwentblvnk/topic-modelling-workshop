The Cali Agents are a rap group consisting of California rappers, Rasco and Planet Asia. The group released their debut album, How the West Was One (2000), which featured the single "Good Life" (#36 Hot Rap Singles). The group then released the albums Head of the State (2004), and Fire & Ice (2006), as well as the compilation Hip Hop Classics (2003). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cali+Agents">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
