Idris Ackamoor (born Bruce Baker circa 1950, hometown Chicago) is a multi-instrumentalist, composer, actor, tap dancer, director, and producer. He is the Founder and Executive/Co-Artistic Director of the San Francisco performance company, Cultural Odyssey. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Idris+Ackamoor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
