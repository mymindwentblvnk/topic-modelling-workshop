Goreaphobia is a death metal band from the USA. Formed in 1988, their current line up consists of Chris Gamble (vocals/bass, Ancient Rites, Absu), Alex Bouks (guitars, Incantation, Master) and Jim Roe (drums, Incantation, Morbid Sin). They have consisted of many highly-rated musicians in the death metal scene over the years including Incantation's John McEntee and Craig Pillard, and Immolation's Craig Smilowski. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Goreaphobia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
