It plays oldschool death metal with political and social critic lyrics. Sharing about injustice system, corrupt morality, living poverty and doctrine religion.
The meaning of Rottenomicon as translated from Greek and English language. Rotten [putrid, stench], Nomos [law], Eikon [image] "an image of the rotten law" Indonesian subtitle means 'kebusukan hukum yang berlaku di masyarakat'
