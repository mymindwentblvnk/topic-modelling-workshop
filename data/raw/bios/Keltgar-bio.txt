Keltgar was a gothic metal band from Baal, Flemish Brabant, Belgium formed in 1998. They released two demos in the 1990s before releasing their first and only album, Wintermist in 2000. Two more demos followed this, one in 2001 and another in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Keltgar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
