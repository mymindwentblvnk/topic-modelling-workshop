Black metal from Waasmunster, Belgium. 
Formed in 1996 as a band with lyrical themes about Flemish history and trolls while it nowadays has changed their concept to misanthropy, anti-human, anti-YOU!

Present line-up: 
Virus (aka Clauwaert) - Vocals, Electronics, Lyrics (Heimat, Nihil-X, ex-Sereneless)
Storm - Drums, Music (Worthless, Paragon Impure, Heimat)
Noctiz - Guitars (Paragon Impure, Verloren, Worthless, Lugubrum)
Strop - Bass (Heimat) 

Released two demo's (1997 and 1998), one compilation with both demo's on it (2000) and one full length album in 2005. In 2021 they made a comeback and released their first album after 16 years - Zonderlingen. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gotmoor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
