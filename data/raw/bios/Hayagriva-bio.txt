HAYAGRIVA is a band play DARK SHADOW METAL music!!! Hailing from Ipoh, Malaysia. Established in 1993.

The band currently in studio, cooking LATEST 11 Songs for the upcoming NEW ALBUM, to be out in 2011. (Plus One Additional Bunus track)

On this upcoming release, its featured once again, the 4 ORIGINAL MEMBER on the recording of the FIRST DEMO in 1995: AAVIMARGA(Vocal)/ MANTHERAVATHEE(Guitar+Bass) / VENKHATESWEHRA(Keyboard+Synth)/ NHAJASAKTHEE(Drum).
