Czech grind core band from Kojetín. They have split with another czech band Decomposed (Obscene/Epidemie, 1997) and full lenght CD "The Real View of the World" (selfreleased, 2000). They splited up in 2001. Bassguitarist Sam and vocalist Maňa founded gore-grind band Spasm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychopathia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
