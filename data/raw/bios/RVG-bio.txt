RVG are a four-piece band from Melbourne who are centred around singer-songwriter and namesake Romy Vager. RVG list their influences as including the Psychedelic Furs, UK post-punk/neo-psych quartet Soft Boys and Queensland indie icons The Go-Betweens. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/RVG">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
