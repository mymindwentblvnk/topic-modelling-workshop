MESMERISED comes from Fribourg in Switzerland. STYLES: trash, speed, hardcore and power metal.

The band has just recorded its 4th album ! Mesmerised has made many shows in Switzerland during the last 10 Years and they're still more than alive with the brand new 10 tracks album called "obnoxiosus". The CD has been released on the label METALFREAK RECORDS.
