1) Bacchus are a DIY experimental crust outfit from Galway, Ireland. They formed in late 2007 & played their first gig in June of 2008. They blend epic swedish style crust with crushing slow/heavy parts & spacey reverb/delay soundscapes. To date they have released a 3 track ep titled "attica" & a split 7 inch with UK band Burning the Prospect. For fans of HHIG, Perth Express, Zann, Acursed, Remains of the Day, Cult of Luna, Skitsystem etc.
