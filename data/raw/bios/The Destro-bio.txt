The Destro is a  metal band from Dallas,TX USA, formed in 2003 by singer Eric Daughtry, guitarist Nick Emde, bassist Steven Nottingham, and drummer Jono Garrett. In 2006 the Destro were signed to Ironclad Records which realeasd their debut full-length 'As the Coil Unwinds'  in 2007. Second album, 'Harmony of Discord', came out in october, 2009 through Ironclad/Metal Blade. 
Current line-up
Eric Daughtry - Vocals
Nick Emde - Guitars
Jono Garret - Drums
