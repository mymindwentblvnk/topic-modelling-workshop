Black Anal Goat Vomit is a one man blackened metal outfit which began in Brisbane AUSTRALIA in 2007.
Not much is known on who BAGV actually is but is known to play in many live bands within the underground Brisbane music scene.

Black Anal Goat Vomit has released one album 'Heaven' in 2008 and many other one off tracks, BAGV began working on their next in 2012 and is rumored to be the final album, the album is due early 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Anal+Goat+Vomit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
