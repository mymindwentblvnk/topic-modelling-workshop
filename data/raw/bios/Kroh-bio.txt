The artist name Kroh is used by at least 2 artists:

1. Kroh was a metal band from Birmingham, UK. The themes in their music include Satan worship, murder, madness, and the death of Wyrd Doom. The members of Kroh included vocalist Oliwia Sobieszek, Paul Kenney on guitar, Darren Donovan on bass, and Rich Stanton on drums.
