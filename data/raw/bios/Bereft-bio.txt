There are several bands under the name of Bereft:

1) A Doom metal band from Los Angeles, California. Formed in 2008 and still active. 
Members: 
Sacha Dunable - guitar, backing vocals (Intronaut, Graviton)
Derek Donley - drums, backing vocals (National Sunday Law, Graviton)
Charles Elliott - vocals, guitar (Abysmal Dawn)
Derek Rydquist - bass, backing vocals (ex-The Faceless)

2) A power electronics band from Rhode Island formed in 2003. Recently expanded to a two-piece with the addition of The Vomit Arsonist.


3) A black metal band from UK. Released their self-titled debut album in 2012.


4) A Post-black metal/doom band from Madison, Wisconsin. Formed in 2011 by Zach Johnson and Alex Linden.
