Wolfszorn is a German black metal act formed in 2002 and is still active today. Wolfszorn's lyrics deal mostly with national-socialism and paganism. 

Currently, the only bandmember is Skoll. 

So far, Wolfszorn has released the following:

Des Wolfes Zorn Demo, 2003  
Thurisaz / Wolfszorn Split, 2004
