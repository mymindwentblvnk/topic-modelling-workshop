Tour Documentary

Leo Moracchioli runs a studio in Norway called Frog Leap Studios in a lot of his covers he records himself playing every instrument. Occasionally his wife joins in on bass and sometimes he has special guests. Rabea Massaad and Hannah Boulton guest appeared in Africa (Metal Cover) and joined the touring band. Truls Haugen from Circus Maximus became drummer on tours although will occasionally step out, play guitar and sing vocals for Pokemon Theme (Metal Version).

Pete Cottrell made a guest appearance at London Gigs for a rendition of Hello (Metal Cover)
