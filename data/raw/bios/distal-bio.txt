Distal is a DJ and producer from  Atlanta, Georgia. He's founder of the label Embassy Recordings, producing a wide variety of bass music. 

His debut vinyl release was on Soul Jazz Records alongside Mala, Coki and Ramadanman, and followed up with a release on SureFireSound. 

Distal plays in a variety of international scenes including the juke/footwork community, dubstep as well as the house scene.
