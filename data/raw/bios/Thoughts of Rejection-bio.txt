Thoughts of rejection is an Austrian Death/Thrash Band from Dornbirn.Formed in 2009,the group was founded by Simon Gamper and Vivian Konzett.There have been 4 member changes since their inception .The current lineup of the band comprises Dietmar Moritsch (Vocals),Simon Gamper (drums),Vivian Konzett (guitar), Markus Fussi (guitar) and Florian Wohlgennant (Bass)  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thoughts+of+Rejection">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
