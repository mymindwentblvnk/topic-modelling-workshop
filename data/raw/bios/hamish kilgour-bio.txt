Hamish Kilgour is a New Zealand musician notable for founding The Clean along with his brother David in 1978. He later moved to New York City and formed The Mad Scene in the early 1990s <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hamish+Kilgour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
