Celtic Folk/Power Metal/Shred project from Surabaya, Indonesia. Included in the Power of Mirantic in early 2011, her project is a composer, producer on  album "The Conscience". She started playing guitar since the age of 15 years, since college, she began to explore the field of music, especially rock and classical piano. Instrumental demo "Harmonic Twin", she started to create in 2010, and released to the internet in 2011 on ReverbNation site. For a while now maybe her lack of focus on the music, because she recently graduated from S1, and she began working to become a teacher at one of the junior secondary education in East Java. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Annisa+Wulandari">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
