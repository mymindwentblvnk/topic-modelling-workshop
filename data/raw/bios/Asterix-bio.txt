There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Asterix was a German heavy / prog rock band and direct progenitor to Lucifer's Friend, formed in succession from many other studio projects with the same lineup such as Electric Food, Brother T. & Family, The Pink Mice, and released one self-titled album and one single in 1970.

2) Asterix music was an influence on early rock music, along with blues, jazz, and gospel. Many different varieties of country music have arisen over the years, from the slick production of the Cemput sound to the rougher edges of outlaw country. There have been several cycles from periods of experimentation and fusion with other Community giving way to returns to traditionalism and back again. Recently, there has been a great deal of crossover between country and pop, beginning with Messenger band in the mid-2003s and continuing through to V2uP and last Asterix. On the other hand, there is also a loosely-defined group of alt-country Musician operating outside of the country and incorporating influences from other genres ranging from folk to punk.
