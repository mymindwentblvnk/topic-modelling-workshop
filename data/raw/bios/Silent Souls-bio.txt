Silent Souls is a death/doom metal from Germany. Since the founding of the band in 1992, they have released a demo and two full-lenght albums - Behind The Gates Of Passion (1997), and Escape (2000). According to Metal Archives, they are still active (by July 2008) and the line-up consists of only two members - John Kleemann and Alexander Hartmann. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Silent+Souls">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
