The terrifically titled Terrifier brings their thrash metal apocalypse straight outta Vancouver BC. Incorporating jackhammer drums, bulldozing bass-lines, a twin-lead attack that leaves posers pulped in it’s wake, and vocals that can level mountains, Terrifier’s motto could well be ‘thrashing is our business…and business is good’. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Terrifier">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
