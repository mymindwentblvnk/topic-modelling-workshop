OAKS (oh-kzz) n.
The term "oak" can be used as part of a common name formed from members of the Tight Bros From Way Back When, Spermatozoa, and Flocking Eduardo. This genus is native to the Northern hemisphere, and includes species extending from the cold latitudes of Olympia, WA and Long Island, NY.

Oaks is a indie rock duo from Minneapolis,MN. The band consists of Erica Krumm and Jim Kolles. They released their debut self-titled EP  on August 11,2011 featuring the lead track ''Feathers''. On February 16,2013 they released their second EP ''Field Beat''. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oaks">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
