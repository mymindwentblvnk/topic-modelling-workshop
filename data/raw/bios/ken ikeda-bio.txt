Ken Ikeda (池田謙) is an experimental musician and artist producing ambient and minimal sounds. He studied at various music and video schools worldwide since his birth in 1964. He has produced music for many experimental films and art exhibitions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ken+Ikeda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
