Drakwald is a pagan death metal band. Created in 2010 in Tours (France), the band has got for main influences scandinavian death metal and pagan folk… 

Since october 2011, the band is doing great shows on local and national stages. (M-Fest, Cernunnos Festival, Night Of Thanatos…). The band has also been invited in local radio station (like Throne in Thanatos, Radio Campus Angers…) and received some chronicles in media (Metallian…). Forgotten Lands, their first EP of 4 songs, has been recorded and mixed in Tempo Loco Studio. EP lyrics are about scandinavian culture, fantastic tales like Robin Hobb or David Gemmell ones.
