Architect of Seth is a French technical death-metal band from Caen. It was founded in 2006 by Paul Rousseaux. In 2012 he was joined by guitar player Yohann Kochel. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Architect+of+Seth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
