Sarah P. (stage name of Sarah Anna Psalti) is a Greek songstress, lyricist and actress currently based in Berlin. Also known as ex lead singer of electronic-downtempo band Keep Shelly In Athens.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sarah+P.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
