Coming off a very successful release of his third project, “Senioritis,” Ataah King blasts listeners with the hit single “Favorite Rapper (Looking For).” Produced by Abbey Reign, this track instills good vibes to all who are lucky enough to listen. Ataah’s hook inspires a sense of the confidence in oneself that comes from his experience as a rapper, a nostalgic feeling for high school, and the poetic states of mind that come with being back in class.
