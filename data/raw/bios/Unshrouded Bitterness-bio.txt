Unshrouded Bitterness are a melodic doom metal band from the Netherlands. Prior to Unshrouded Bitterness, some members also played in the bands Mortuarium and Eternal Terror. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unshrouded+Bitterness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
