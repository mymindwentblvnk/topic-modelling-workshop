Trevor Daniel Neill (born September 28, 1994), is an American singer and songwriter. His debut EP, Homesick, was released in October 2018 and features the song "Falling", which hit the charts in more than twenty countries after going viral in 2019.  The song was also featured on his debut studio album, Nicotine, released in 2020. Daniel released his next project, That Was Then, was released in November 2021, featuring the singles "Alone" and "Dadada". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trevor+Daniel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
