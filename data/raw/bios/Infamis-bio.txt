There are multiple artists named Infamis:

1) Infamis is a dark ambient / dungeon synth project from Grudziądz, Poland, founded by Wojciech Zięba, owner of the label Beast of Prey. He is also involved with Krepulec.

2) Infamis was a black metal band from Szczerców, Poland, founded in 1996. They released two demos: "Wilcza rezurekcja" in 1997 and "Irremeabilis Unda" in 1999.

3) Infamis is a band from Berlin, Germany,  founded in 1987. They are also involved in painting pictures and building own instruments.
