Dellé (civil name: Frank A. Dellé) is a Berlin-based reggae/dancehall-musician and one of the three frontmen of the German dancehall group seeed.
In 2009 he released his first solo-single "Pound Power" followed by his debut album "Before I Grow Old".

Another artist goes by the name of "delle," specifically lowercased and without an accent.
