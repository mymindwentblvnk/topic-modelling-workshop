14, also know as Štrnásť, or XIV  is a underground rap group from Petržalka - Bratislava (Slovakia, Europe).
Members were Šipo, Tanko, Lektor, Suchý Pes, Momo, Mŕtvy Aleš, Dj Kebab, 
now also Sandra, Dj Mako now also know as Peter Pann.
