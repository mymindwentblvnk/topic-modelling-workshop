Mind Bomb was a Chicago Alternative Rock/Industrial band fronted by local producer/musician Matt Mercado. Their eponymous debut released in 1993 on Mercury/Polygram is their only known album. 

Matt Mercado "Captain Lovejoy" - vocals
Jim Bashaw - drums, percussion, backing vocals
Johnny Checuga - lead, rhythm and acoustic guitars, backing vocals, percussion
