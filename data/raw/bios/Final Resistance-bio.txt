melodic death metal / metalcore from Pszczyna, Poland

The band was formed in November 2011 by four friends. They play modern melodic death metal with some metalcore influences inspired by mostly swedish melodeath bands such as In Flames, At The Gates, Dark Tranquillity, Soilwork, Dimension Zero and so on. 

Bartłomiej Rachwał - guitar
Dawid Komraus - vocals
Maciek Kawka - bas
Bartek Olszewski - drums
