Slartibartfass was founded at the beginning of 2005 by Thomas (keyboard) and Philipp (guitars).
In March 2006, the debut concert with the Munich pagan band “Helfahrt” took place in Ulm. It was a great success and band got a name in the metal scene of Southern Germany.

Discography:

Nordwind - Full-length (2006)

Nebelheim - Full-length  (2007)

Funkenfeuer - Full-length (2009)

Since January 2007 band got additional support by Jessica (bagpipes)
