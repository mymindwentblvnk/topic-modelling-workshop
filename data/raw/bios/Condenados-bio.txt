Condenados is a doom metal band from Concepcion, Chile formed in 2005 by Fernando Vidal. The debut album "A Painful Journey into Nihil" was released through Shadow Kingdom Records in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Condenados">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
