Ttrruuces is an English psychedelic rock band, formed by Jules Apollinaire and Natalie Findlay in 2019. The band was based in London, England, but then relocated to France for a year to fully immerse themselves in creating their self-titled debut album, TTRRUUCES. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TTRRUUCES">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
