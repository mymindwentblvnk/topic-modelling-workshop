French electro house artist Kavinsky teamed with Brazilian lead singer of CSS, Lovefoxxx, on vocals.

The song "Nightcall" was produced by Daft Punk's Guy-Manuel De Homem-Christo and mixed by electronic artist SebastiAn . It features Brazilian lead singer of CSS, Lovefoxxx, on vocals and includes remixes by Dustin N'Guyen, Jackson And His Computer Band and Breakbot. The title track "Nightcall" was used in the title sequence for the movie Drive, directed by Nicolas Winding Refn and starring Ryan Gosling and Carey Mulligan.
