Harøy, Norway (2010 - present)

Skaarv is a metal band from Harøy, Norway formed in 2010. The bands main features are downtuned guitars, heavy drums and raw vocals, inspired by bands like Down, Pantera and Entombed. They have earlier been the opening act for other norwegian metal bands such as Sahg and Audrey Horne. They released their first EP named Seed of Justice in 2015.

Current lineup:
Vocals: André Péter
Guitar: Kurt Idar Harnes
Guitar: Øyvind Vasstein
Bass: Frank Ulla
