There are/were two bands using the Dark Land moniker:

1) Dark Land is a gothic rock band from Germany. They released the album 'Rise and Fall' in 1997.

2) Dark Land is a atmospheric black metal solo project by James Cowman, a 17 year old from the USA, influenced by black metal acts such as Burzum, Mayhem, Agalloch, Agrypnie, and ambient groups. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Land">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
