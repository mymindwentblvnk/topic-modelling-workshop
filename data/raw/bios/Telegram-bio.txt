Telegram are a London based band formed in 2013. The band consists of Welsh frontman Matt Saunders, Matt Wood on guitar, Oli Paget-Moon on bass and Jordan Cook on drums. Influenced by their love of Eno, Syd Barrett, krautrock and late proto-punk, Telegram released their debut single "Follow" (B-side "Under The Night Time") on their own GramGram label in November 2013. 

Telegram have supported Franz Ferdinand, Manic Street Preachers and Temples in the past and are about to join Palma Violets on their upcoming tour in November.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Telegram">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
