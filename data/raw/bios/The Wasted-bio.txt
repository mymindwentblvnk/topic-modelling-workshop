The Wasted is the band founded in 2007 by the previous members of Absolute Spirit - Chmielo, Lokis and Amadi. The musicans are creating their own music style which can be called cold wave music, indie or post-rock.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Wasted">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
