1) Antisocial were a punk band from Livingston, Scotland. They formed in 1979. Band members were AlaN "Scottie" Scott; vocals, Gary "Hainy" Hain; drums, Cameron Stevens; guitar & Gordon "Gogs" Balfour; bass. They Split up in 1982. Scottie & Gogs went on to form Bayonet Babies. Gogs later joined The Exploited and now currently plays with oi! band Bakers Dozen.

2) There was also a happy hardcore group called Antisocial who had several releases in 1997 and 1998. Members included Hixxy and Sunset Regime.
