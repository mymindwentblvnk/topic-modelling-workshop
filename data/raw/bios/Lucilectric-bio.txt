LUCILECTRIC was a german one hit wonder / pop duo from 1993 to 1999, consisting of Luci Van Org and Ralf Goldkind. Their debut single "Mädchen" (German for: Girl) was a huge hit in Germany, Austria & Switzerland.

Luci's other project were: eena (1990-1992), das haus von luci (German for: The house of Luci) (2003-2006) & Üebermutter (2007- ). She also played in some German movies and TV series, wrote a book in 2006 and wrote songs for the German punk band terrorgruppe.
