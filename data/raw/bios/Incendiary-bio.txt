Incendiary is an American metalcore band from Long Island, New York. Their debut LP "Crusade" was released on May 16th, 2009. The band then released 3 split 7 inches with New Jersey's Suburban Scum, Portland, Oregon's Unrestrained and California's Xibalba. Their sophomore full length entitled "Cost of Living" was released in April of 2013 on Closed Casket Activities. Their most recent LP, Thousand Mile Stare, was released May 2017 on the same label.
