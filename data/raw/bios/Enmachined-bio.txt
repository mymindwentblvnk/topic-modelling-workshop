Enmachined is an old school thrash metal band from Bangladesh, formed in the early days of 2011. Influenced by East Coast Thrash and Bay Area Thrash scene, the band determines to bring real thrash metal chaos for the ones who can bear the deadly attack. They often regard their music as bone crushing thrash metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enmachined">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
