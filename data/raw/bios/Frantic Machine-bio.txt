Frantic Machine is a French metal band created under the name of Frantic in 2003. Due to copyright issues, they changed their name into Frantic Machine in 2005. They play some heavy thrash metal inspired by bands like Metallica. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Frantic+Machine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
