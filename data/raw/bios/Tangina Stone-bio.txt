Tangina Stone is a New York City-based rising singer-songwriter who brings her own “rock-inflected soul sounds” (SoulBounce) to the musical table with recent debut “The Fall” – EP; now available on iTunes and at all digital providers. Catch her single “Suntan” (Remix) featured in this season of Love & Hip Hop Hollywood on VH1. Moving to New York City from her hometown of Canton, Ohio at the age of 18, Tangina spent five years pursing her music career relentlessly and has built a loyal and growing fan base, especially among the live music scene. She’s collaborated with many artists and played venues such as Arlene’s Grocery, SOBs, Rockwood Music Hall, Silvana and countless others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tangina+Stone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
