Torka is a Necro Black Metal band. They were influenced by bands like Darkthrone, Bathory, Celtic Frost and others. They formed the band in May 2001.
In 2006 GoatowaRex released their debut masterpiece "Old hatred".
Torka also contributed to the GoatowaRex Compilation "Speech Of Goat" with the track "Abyss Of Hate" (4:26), taken from "Old Hatred".
The band is now defunct. Necros recorded his debut one man band album Ater Era and the other two members are playing in Grimoir. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Torka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
