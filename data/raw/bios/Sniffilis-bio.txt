Sniffilis was a Thrash Metal band from Slovenia. They released ''Sniffilis'' full-length album in 1994.

Tomaz Majdic - bass guitar
Aleksander Belec - guitar, vocals
Bostjan Krivorotov - drums
Rok Zevnik - guitar, vocals

Last label: Independent
