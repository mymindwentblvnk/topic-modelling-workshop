Infectous Arteries is a death metal band from Denpasar, Bali, Indonesia that formed in 1998.[1] Their recent lineup is: Putu Leonk (vocal), Layem (guitar), Sonoger (bass), BooDee (drum). Their music character is a mixture between death metal and thrash metal. The band with different genres, such as Suffocation, Monstrosity, Pantera, Malevolent Creation, Meshuggah, In Flames, Amorphis and Dream Theater give influences to their music.[2] Their first full-length album, Journey to the Unknown, was released in 2003 via their indie label, Infect Art Production.[3] And their second album, Synoptic Oddity, was released in 2009.
