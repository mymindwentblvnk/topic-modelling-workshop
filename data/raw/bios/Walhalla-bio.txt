There are three bands called Walhalla:

1. - Walhalla is a finnish black/thrash metal project formed by members of bands like Soulgrind and Gloomy Grim. They have released a couple of albums through the American label Root Of All Evil Records.

2. - Walhalla is a german RAC band.
