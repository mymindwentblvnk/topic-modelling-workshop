Betonkust is the artist name of a Dutch electronic music producer Swiere Westveen.
He's released music on labels like Dekmantel, Crimes of the Future, 1080p, 9300 Records and more.

While melancholia and dreamy drills always remain a keystone throughout his performances, there are catchy outbursts which provide a welcome element of surprise.
