Agonistica started as a death metal band under the name Oprovio. They later started to play a slower, doom metal style, in the vein of bands like My Dying Bride, and changed their name to Agonistica in 1995. The band have currently released two demos and a live album and are currently on hold. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agonistica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
