Todessucht is depressive black metal band from Germany, formed in 2006 by Fäulnis (vocals & guitar) and Kadaver (drums). In 2007, Euthanasie joined the band (bass) and Dolor followed in the end of 2008 as a guitarist to complete the band's line-up. However, Euthanasie left in 2010.
