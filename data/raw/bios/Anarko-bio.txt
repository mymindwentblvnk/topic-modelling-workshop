Anarko is a spanish thrash metal band formed in 1996. They have so far released three albums.

Line-up:
Arby - guitars, vocals
Pinky - bass, vocals
Fity - vocals
Rus - vocals

Discography:
Anarko - 1998
Degeneración X- 2000
La marcha de los muñecos egocéntricos- 2001
