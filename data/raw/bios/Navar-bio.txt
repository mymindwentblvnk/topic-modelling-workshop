Navar was a metal band from Pardubice, Czech Republic.  They released two albums "Proti vsem" (Against all) and "1621" . Their songs were about Czech and European history.  In novemeber 2008 they broke up and a part of Navar members formed a new band called Sebastien. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Navar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
