Xilentium are a 2 piece from Phoenix, Arizona, USA. They play Necro-Depressive Black Metal. So far Xilentium has had a demo tape called the Forest Ablaze and A Lightscribe CD-R released by Grey Ghost Records that was re-released on pro CD-R by Black Metal Cult Records.  They have also released a live DVD on Black Metal Cult Records that comes in an A5 case handnumbered to 66 copies in blood. There was supposed to be a Pagan Hammer split CD with Xilentium, but it was canceled. Lord Demogogon is the owner of Black Metal Cult Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xilentium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
