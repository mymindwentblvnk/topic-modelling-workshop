David Cutter Music is a Soul and Jazz influenced beatmaker, he makes Chillhop, Instrumental Hip Hop, Chillout, Soul, Jazzhop, Jazz Vibes, Electronic, Lofi Beats, Boom Bap and Bedroom Beats.

Starting out when he got hold of a drum machine at an early age, he was soon hooked with the sounds it made, moving on from the drum machine he became a drummer for a number of bands, since then he has moved back into music production, focusing and developing his own style into what you hear today.

This unique style has led to his music being widely used by some the most influential creators on Youtube,
