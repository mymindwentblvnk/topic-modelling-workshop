Italian Death/Doom band with a similar style to Disembowelment. Assumption was formed by Giorgio and David in 2011. Assumption is part of a quest for transcendence in doom/death metal, striving to make music to be awakened by. Released "Mosaic of the Distant Dominion" on Decrepit Productions.
