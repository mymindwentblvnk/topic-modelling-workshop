Iron Batasuna is a grindcore/hardcore/metal band from Pasaia, Basque country, Spain. They formed in 2004 and the current lineup consists of Villano - guitar, vocals (Hell Division, Anosognosia), Rambo - bass, vocals (Anosognosia) and Etxabe - drums (Anosognosia, Antihero).
