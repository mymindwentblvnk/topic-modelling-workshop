A slice of tremolo-heavy classic rock filtered through the lens of the gunslinging American West, Remo Drive is an indie rock band from Bloomington, Minnesota.

Members are Erik Paulson (vocals/guitar), Stephen Paulson (bass) and Sam Becht (drums).
