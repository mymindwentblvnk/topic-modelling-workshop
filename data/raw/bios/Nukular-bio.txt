Progressive Psytrance Act from Northern Germany.

In December 2005 "kuki" and "canna" from the Grounds of Anarchy Crew started the New Project "Nukular" named by an Homer Simpsons Joke. In 2006 they traveled around Germany presenting their Music Live to the Partypeople.
