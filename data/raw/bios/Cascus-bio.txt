Cascus is a progressive death metal band hailing from Yukon, Oklahoma who formed in 2008. Their influences include Decapitated, Opeth and Metallica.

Members:
Jay Gleason - Vocals and Guitar
Cameron Conyer - Guitar and Vocals
Jeren Martin - Bass and Vocals
