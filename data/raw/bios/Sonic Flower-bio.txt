Sonic Flower (Jpn)  

Not to be confused with Portuguese band, The Last of Them, previously named Sonic Flower.

A side project of Japanese Band Church of Misery.  Formed in Tokyo, their music is a mixture of psychadelic groove, funk, and doom metal.  Music influenced by Cactus, Grand Funk Railroad, Mountain, Sir Load Baltimore, Captain Beyond, Meaters, Graham Central Station, Funkadelic, et cetera.

Members;
J.J. - Drums
Tatsu Mikami - Bass 
Hoshi - Guitar 
Arisa - Guitar

Members are mostly the same from past Church of Misery lineup only replacing their vocalist for another guitarist (Arisa)

Only released one self-titled album with Leafhound records in June of 2003.
