They were a thrash metal/speed metal band that formed in Albany, California and formed in 1985.  They only released a demo in 1986.  Their song The Inquisition makes an appearance on Eastern Front: Live at Ruthie's Inn (Restless, 1986) compilation.  Their last known lineup consisted of James Keith (vocals), Terry Hawley (guitars), Ron Nichols (bass) and Erik Thomas (drums).  Walter Ryan is listed as a past member on The Metal Archives page for Chronic Plague, but it is unknown what his role was.  It is unknown when the band disbanded altogether.  They were never signed to any record label.
