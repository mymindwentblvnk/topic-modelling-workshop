For a detailed autobiography, see his page on the Swedish version (it's written in English).

Lashmush uses synthetic guitar packs and several programs to create a variety of music. He has released several albums and organized several projects, such as his Ultima Eternus work and a Quake soundtrack remix project. Most of his work is submitted to Newgrounds for free download.
