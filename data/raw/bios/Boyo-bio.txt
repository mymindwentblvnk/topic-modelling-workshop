Robert Tilden is a 24-year-old multi-instrumentalist from Los Angeles, California. Under the moniker of BOYO, Tilden creates existential psychedelic-pop that is characterized by harrowing vocals, melodic chord progressions and weathered production. Releasing music at a breakneck pace, Tilden has put out a self-titled EP, a full-length album and a barrage of new singles in 2019. Tilden tours extensively, having supported acts such as Vansire and Hot Flash Heat Wave in recent years and even has an upcoming tour with Broncho in November. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Boyo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
