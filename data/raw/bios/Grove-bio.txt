There is more then one artist called Grove:

1) Atmospheric Epic Doom Metal With Funeral Doom Metal & Space Rock Elements. Grove is from West Chester, Pennsylvania, USA, and began in 2005. The line-up consists of Count Tenebrae (Vocals, Synth and lyrics) and Moongun Wormwalk (All guitars, Bass guitar and Drums). The band released their first demo in 2008, called "Of Moss And Men". They are currently working on new material.

2) A Czech breakbeat/breaks producer.
