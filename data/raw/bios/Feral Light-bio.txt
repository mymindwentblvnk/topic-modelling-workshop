Feral Light are a mysterious two-piece black metal/crust enigma hailing from Minnesota who feature ex-members of Wolvhammer, Empires, Manetheren, and Censor who play grim and sorrowful black metal entwined with savage and unyielding crust punk, with layers of post metal leanings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Feral+Light">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
