Agnostic Front is an American hardcore punk band from New York City. Founded in 1980, the band is considered an important influence on the New York hardcore (NYHC) scene, as well as for pioneering the crossover thrash genre. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agnostic+Front">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
