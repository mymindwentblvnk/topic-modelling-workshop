A Post-Rock/Depressive Black Metal fusion hailing from Cluj-Napoca, Romania. Involving two musicians under the pseudonyms P. and V. Razvan (also in Anxiety Disorder), A.L.S have so far released and EP, full length and a demo (all in 2012).

New 2013 full length release: The Story of a Life.
