Tales From Da Blak Side is the debut album by the rapper Blak Czer from Braddock, Pennsylvania. The album was released in 1994 for Relativity and was produced by DJ Battlecat and Smash Money. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blak+Czer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
