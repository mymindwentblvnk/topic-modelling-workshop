Hellscream is a polish music project, founded by Paul Mazurkiewicz from Gdańsk, playing black metal with influences of dark ambient / ambient. Band consist of him(Azgaroth) and Michał Kociak (Svarog) and was formed in early 2008. Their music is heavily influenced by ambient black metal songs of Burzum. Expect demo at late 2008/early 2009, here, on Last.fm.

Band members:

● Paul Mazurkiewicz - lead vocal, guitar, keyboards (2008 - present)
