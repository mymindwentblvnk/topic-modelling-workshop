Beginning of the End is a name of at least two bands:

[1] A funk group from Nassau, Bahamas, better known as The Beginning of the End.

[2] A defunct South London based crusty hardcore punk band formed in 2006. Their line-up includes ex-members of Doom, Health Hazard, Hiatus, Give Up All Hope and many other bands. The band is just recording their debut 12".Members went on to form Agnosy.
