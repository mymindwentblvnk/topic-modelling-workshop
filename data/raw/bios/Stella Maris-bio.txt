Stella Maris is a superband from the idea of Umberto Maria Giardini (ex Moltheni) and Ugo Cappadonia, the other components are Gianluca Bartolo (Il Pan del Diavolo), Paolo Narduzzo (Universal Sex Arena) and Emanuele Alosi (The Grooming); their music is influenced by new wave sounds, but the guitars keep the main attention but their approach sounds fresher and lighter than the one of the eighties;

Stella Maris is an electronica/electropop duo, comprised of Florian Malicke and Fran Wilson;
