There are multiple artists with this name:

1. Muki is a rising pop star from Sydney, Australia. Growing up in the suburbs of Sydney with a blues musician for a father and psychologist mother, Muki definitely has a unique insight to the world around her and invites all who listen in to her peculiar universe.

2. Muki is Slovak underground hip-hop producer and DJ. His music returns SK rap back into the roots of old school beats and the real meaning of hip hop culture. He put out his mixtape "Hmota pretransformovaná do hudby" in 2008 for free dowload. On this masterpiece are attending original artist icons of underground spiting on commercial and stupid meanings of Slovak hiphop nowadays.  
In 2011 he put out LP "Planetárium" with Planet Asia on featuring.

3. Muki, a.k.a. Luke Mullen and Jules Evans is luscious, laid-back, down-tempo artist with atmospheric grooves, and their latest sounds present Muki's natural musical and personal progression to date.
