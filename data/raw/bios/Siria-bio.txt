There are more than one artist named Siria.

1) The music duo Siria is comprised of singer Natalie Horler of Cascada fame, and one of the DJ/producers from Cascada, DJ Manian. The Siria project has had two singles released into the German and Canadian markets as of July 2006.

2) Siria is a Brazilian musical act from Cameta, which fuses the music of escaped African slaves with indiginous Amazonian sounds.
