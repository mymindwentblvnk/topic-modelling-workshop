Dead Elizabeth was a short-lived metal band from Fresno, CA (2003-2005). They released a self-titled EP and went on a small west coast tour with Light This City and With Passion in the summer of 2004.   After the split, members of Dead Elizabeth went on to form Azathoth: http://www.myspace.com/throneofazathoth
