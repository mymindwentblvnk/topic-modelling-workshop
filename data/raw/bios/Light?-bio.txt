"Light?" is a Progressive Doom Metal/Death Metal band formed at 2002 in Russia (Yaroslavl). Current line-up is: Eugeny Churkin - all instruments, vocals and Alexey Smirnov - session vocalist. 

"Light?" has released two albums: "Mirrors" in 2005 and
