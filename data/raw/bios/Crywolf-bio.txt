1) - Crywolf (formerly Cry Wolf), is the alter-ego of Justin Taylor Phillips, an electronic music producer, vocalist, and multi-instrumentalist from Los Angeles, CA. Since Crywolf's conception just over two years ago, his epic, melodic take on electronic music has been met with support from numerous national acts such as Adventure Club, 12th Planet, Skrillex, Minnesota and more. Recently signed with Le Castle Vania’s label, Always Never, his debut EP "Ghosts" released April 9th, soaring to #18 on iTunes Dance Charts, top 10 on Hype Machine, and #1 on Dubstep.net charts on the first day.
