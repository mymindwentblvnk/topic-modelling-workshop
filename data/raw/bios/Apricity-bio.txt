Apricity is a Shoegaze/Black Metal band from Wichita, Kansas, consisting of Nehemiah Kitchkommie and Xxavier Tincher. They met in highschool and were in bands together throughout the years. They decided after some time off that they wanted to make a project that suited their tastse in music at the time. Thus "Apricity" was born.    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/APRICITY">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
