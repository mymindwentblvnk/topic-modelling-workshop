Knokkelklang are a depressive black metal/ambient/noise band from the cities of Oslo and Toten, in Norway. They have released four demos: Eksistens (2006), Kalk & aske (2008), Vandring (2012) and Avgrunnens klangverk (2013). In 2018 they released their first full-length, Jeg Begraver . <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Knokkelklang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
