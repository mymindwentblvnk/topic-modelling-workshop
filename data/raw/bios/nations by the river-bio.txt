Nations By the River was a joint effort by Luke Steele (Sleepy Jackson) and the Kahn brothers (Gelbison) and also Old Man River. Sarah Blasko also featured on the album.  The album is folk based with great melodies, uplifting arrangements any yet contains darker undertones, which are carried beautifully by Luke's husky voice.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nations+By+The+River">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
