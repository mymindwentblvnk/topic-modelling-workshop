Philipp Poisel (* June 18, 1983 in Ludwigsburg/Germany) is a German singer-songwriter. He was discovered by Herbert Grönemeyer and in 2008 was signed by Grönemeyer's label Grönland Records.
Poisel's songs are produced by Frank Pilsl.

Philipp Poisel's first album "Wo fängt dein Himmel an?" ("Where does your heaven begin?") was released August 29, 2008 and reached place 38 in the German album charts. The first single from this album was the song of the same name. 

His second album "Bis nach Toulouse" ("Unto Toulouse") was released August 27, 2010.
