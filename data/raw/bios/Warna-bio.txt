Warna is a vocal group from Indonesia with members: Sarwana Thamrin or Sarwana (tenor), Stephen Tamadji or Stephen (baritone), Victoria Margareta or Ria (mezzosopran), as well as 2 new members, Rr. Ira Pangesti Haryono or Ira (sopran) and Adsary Zinnia Rugebregt or Ari (alto). These two members replaced the previous members, Dea Mirella and Nina Tamam.
