Death Metal/Metal/Progressive. Hailing from Phoenix, Arizona.

Combining elements of heavy rhythms, melodic riffs and layered vocals is what this band is all about. Whether it's a teeth-grinding solo or catchy vocal melodies with rich harmonies, Wynterborne delivers a stylistic approach that is often likened to popular bands such as Killswitch Engage, All That Remains, Lamb of God, Unearth, Scar Symmetry, Soilwork and In Flames to name a few.
