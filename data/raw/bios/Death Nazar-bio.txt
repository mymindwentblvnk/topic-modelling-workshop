Death Nazar is a little duet from cold Russian Siberia, that is trying to play avant-garde metal.
Debut self-titled album Death Nazar was released 23 June 2015. Second one Slendy Dog: 36 Minutes Forward to Mental was released 8 March 2017. And remastered old demo EP Re-Published Orgasm was releases 8 October 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Death+Nazar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
