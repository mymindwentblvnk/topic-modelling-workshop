Kali Ma is a brutal metal band from Seymour, CT, Consisting of Founding Guitarist Todd Whitlock, Roy(Guitar), Shawn(Drums) & Jay(Bass), Kali Ma has been melting faces with their Lamb of God meets The Black Dahlia Murder blend of Metal all over Connecticut since 2006, Kali Ma has a huge fanbase already in the lower Naugatuck Valley towns, Kali Ma is currently looking for a new singer, someone who is going to be as brutal as the previous singer who had left the band due to some personal issues, it will take some serious effort to fill those shoes, Kali Ma has played shows with bands like - IWRESTLEDABEARONCE, Ligeia, Thy Will Be Done and Connecticut's own Dead By Wednesday. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kali+Ma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
