Day of Reckoning was formed in 2010 by Rusty Cooley and Matt Smith (Outworld). After extensive auditions Brad Lambert (vocals) and Matt Hough (Guitar) completed the lineup. Day of Reckoning combines modern heavy metal compositions with virtuosic musicianship. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Day+of+Reckoning">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
