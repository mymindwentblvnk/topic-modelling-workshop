Though no one can capture the exact chemistry and passion of Johnny Cash & June Carter Cash, and the great music they made together; Joaquin Phoenix & Reese Witherspoon did a wonderful job in keeping their memory and music alive in the Soundtrack to Walk the Line. They sound wonderful together. For the movie 'Walk The Line' they both learned to sing and play guitar. All the songs they made for the film are collected on a album. Unfortunately these songs are not available on Last.fm yet.
