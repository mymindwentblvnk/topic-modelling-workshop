Abominor are a black metal band formed in 2008 in Reykjavík, Iceland. They released their demo EP 'Source of Chaos' in 2010 and released their debut EP 'Opus Decay' on March 26, 2015 through Invictus Productions. The EP was recorded at Studio Emissary and the artwork was created by Matt Salters of Bone Divinations.
