There have been at least two bands called Custard:

1. An Australian indie rock band.
2. A German power-metal band.

1. Custard are an Australian indie rock band formed in 1989 in Brisbane, Queensland. The band is colloquially known as "Custaro" due to frequent misreadings of their name.

2. Custard is a German power-metal band founded in 1987. They have released four full length albums so far (the latest in 2008). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Custard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
