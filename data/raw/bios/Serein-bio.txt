Serein is a Chillstep artist who started releasing music in 2018, when the first single "Awake" was released. Serein is most known for their pieces: Atlantic, Blue - Sappheiros Remix, Avenoir.







In the past, there was a folk rock band from Canton, Michigan featuring Andy Milad, Avery Bruni, Justin Heck, Chris Atkinson, and Tommy Zylka called Serein.  The band formed in mid-2009 and played their final show on July 29, 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Serein">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
