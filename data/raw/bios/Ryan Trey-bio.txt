Ryan Trey was born March 27, 1999 in St Louis, Missouri . He is a R&B/hip-hop artist and is most well known for his song 'Nowhere to Run' featuring Bryson Tiller. His first project EIGHT24 was released in early 2017. Following this, his studio album 'august' was released on 31st August 2018. Recently, Ryan Trey released a new single called 'Rollin' on July 10th 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ryan+Trey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
