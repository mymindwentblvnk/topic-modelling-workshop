Funeral of Soul was a death/doom metal band, formed in 1995 in Alchevsk, Lugansk (Ukraine).

Releases:
Morrows Operetta (Demo, 1995)
Angel Reaper (Demo, 1996)
The Life Underground (Demo, 1997).
