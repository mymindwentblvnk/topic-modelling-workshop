UnoTheActivist is a rapper from Zone 3, Atlanta.

He released his debut mixtape, No More Thotties, in October 2015, which was followed up by Gift of Gab, a mixtape which was released in December 2015. He then released a 4 track EP, Sorry For The Wait (Brooke's Interlude) in August 2017, which was followed by the mixtape Live.Shyne.Die which would be released a month later in September that year.
