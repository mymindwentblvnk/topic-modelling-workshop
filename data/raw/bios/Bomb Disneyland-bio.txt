English Crossover/Thrash Metal band from London. In 1988 they released the single "Nail Mary". Next year Vinyl Solution let out Bomb Disneylanders' full-length record "Why Not". Changed name to Bomb Everything when Disney lawyers filed a complaint with the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bomb+Disneyland">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
