BLACK SOUL HORDE was created by John Tsiakopoulos (guitars) and Jim Kotsis (vocals) in 2012 as a side project. Classic Heavy Metal attitude with NWOBHM and Doom epicness and melody. Also influenced from the occult, the Norse and epic mythology. Signed to No Remorse records they released their debut album "Tales of the old Ones" in 2013  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Soul+Horde">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
