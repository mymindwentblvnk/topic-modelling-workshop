Burning Circle was founded in 2006 in the town of Ruma, Serbia. 
In 2007 the band started with rehearsals and creation of their own music, which is a combination of progressive rock, hard rock, progressive metal and many other musical genres (heavy, thrash, death metal and even classical music) as well. In 2008, Burning Circle started with live shows and recording demos.
