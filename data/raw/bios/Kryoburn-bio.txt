Formed in 1995, Kryoburn began to amass a following in the local Carlsbad metal scene that over time allowed them to grow to the national level. They have been touring the United States continually since 2000, gaining an ever-increasing fanbase along the way. They have garnered critical acclaim from heavy metal publication Metal Maniac's December 2005 edition. Kryoburn released their debut album, Enigmatic Existence, on Candlelight Records in 2005. In May 2009 the band revamped their Myspace site with material from their upcoming album Three Years Eclipsed. After a Lineup change and other setbacks Kryoburn has evolved into a new a force to be reckoned with. Joining the band is Allen Scott(Blessed Disease) on Guitar/Vocals and Sam Logan (Blessed Disease) on Synth and Keys. Three Years Eclipsed is available now were ever CD's are sold.
