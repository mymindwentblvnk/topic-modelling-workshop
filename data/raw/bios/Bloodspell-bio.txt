Bloodspell is a Thrash Metal band from Brescia, Italy.

Their sound is a unique mixture of Bay-Area and German Thrash Metal, with melodic themes, played in a personal way including the typical thrash/metal genre and incorporating keyboards.

Bloodspell self-produced and issued the DEMO "Tales From Hell", available for free and full download also on their website <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloodspell">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
