Tim Presley is an American musician, singer and songwriter. Presley began his career playing with the hardcore punk bands Model American and The Nerve Agents. In 2004, Presley founded the psychedelic rock band Darker My Love. In 2006 he joined The Fall, playing on the LP Reformation Post TLC, and was an occasional guest musician on the band's later albums. Since 2010, Presley has released music under the name White Fence.[1] As of January 2019, he has released seven studio albums, two live albums, and two collaborative albums with Ty Segall under that name[2], as well as two albums with Cate Le Bon under the name DRINKS[3], and a solo album, THE WiNK, under his own name.[4]  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tim+Presley">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
