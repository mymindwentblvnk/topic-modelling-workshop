VINTERSEA are an unsigned Progressive/Melodic Death Metal band from Corvallis, Oregon, United States. Formerly known as Asterion they released the EP 'Constallations' in 2014. As VINTERSEA, the band released their debut album 'The Gravity of Fall' on January 20th, 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vintersea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
