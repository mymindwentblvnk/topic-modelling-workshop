New Orleans rapper Curren$y's popularity increase has led to a signing of new talent to his very own group, Jet Life. Along with previous proteges Trademark da Skydiver and Young Roddy, Curren$y has been able to form a legit group of like-minded rappers. The group also includes Smoke DZA, Sir Michael Rocks, Street Wiz, Corner Boy P and Nesby Phips. Jet World Order was their first release which features all of these rappers. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jet+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
