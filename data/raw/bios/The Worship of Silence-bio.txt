The Worship of Silence was formed in 2004 in Oakland, California

The Worship of Silence is a Doom Metal Band. Taking aspects from several genres and creating large, expansive pieces of sound.

Band Members:

Joshua Baker: Guitar
Sanjeev Brar: Drums
Joseph Hutton: Vocals
