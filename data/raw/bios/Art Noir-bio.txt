There are two bands (at least) named "Art Noir":

1. A German dark industrial - gothic - wave - ambient band

- founded in 1997
- first release "Hinter der Nacht" (Saille Compilation)
- CD Fluegel in 2002
- collective CD release Mondgesänge in 2004 - together with Carlos Perón, Woschofius and Perlentaucher
- CD Silent Green released 2009 by afmusic and Danse Macabre. On this album Art Noir works with La_Nadine as a singer.
