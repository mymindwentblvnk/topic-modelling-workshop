Rhythm of Fear is a Thrash Metal, Hardcore, Crossover band from Jacksonville, Florida, U.S.A. that started in 2010. They released there 1st EP Ancient Deception in 2011, 2nd EP Mass Illusion in 2015, and there 1st full length Maze of Confusion in 2016. 

Band Members are:
