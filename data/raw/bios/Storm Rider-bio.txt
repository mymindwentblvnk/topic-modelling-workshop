Power metal act from Tokyo, Japan, formed in 2005. Probably best known for having guitarist Gentaro Satomura in the band. Storm Rider released their first EP, named "Judgment Day", in 2006. The second EP, "Tear's Color" followed a year later. The same year they also released a three song single. Guitarist Koji plays in thrash metal band Snake, Bassist Naoto plays in power metal band Atomic Tornado and drummer Yu-Ki plays in heavy metal band Junxion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Storm+Rider">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
