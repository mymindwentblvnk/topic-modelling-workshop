Morte Aeterna is an  Black metal band from Holland. Starting out from the ashes of the technical Metalband Myrrha in 1997.
"Euphemisms Of Dogma", their first full-length, was recorded in 2003. Due to priority differences between the bandmembers, the band split up in 2004. Morte aeternA started again in 2010 and is recording a new album with the title Morte aeterna - Morte aeterna , And signes with record label Non serviam records.
