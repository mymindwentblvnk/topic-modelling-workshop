Jester's march is a progressive speed/thrash metal band from Dortmund (germany). They have release 2 full-legth albums; "Beyond" (1991) and "Acts" (1992). Their singer is heavily influenced by the voices of Geoff Tate or Midnight. They are currently split-up <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jester%27s+March">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
