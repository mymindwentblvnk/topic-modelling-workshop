Trauma Field is an atmospheric rock/metal band formed in 2004 in Lappeenranta, Finland. Currently the band is located in Jyväskylä, Finland. In the year 2013 the band released their first full length album titled “Harvest”. You can listen to it for free on SoundCloud, BandCamp and ReverbNation. In the beginning of 2014, Trauma Field’s lineup underwent almost a complete reformation. Future plans involve a lot of gigging and promoting the new record.
