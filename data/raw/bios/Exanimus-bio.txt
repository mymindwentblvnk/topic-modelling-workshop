Exanimus is Raw Black Metal From Rusia (Efremov, Tula) formed in 2007 
this one man project from Exanimus whose do everything All Instruments  and Vocals
"Exanimus" translates as "Lifeless" or "Dead"
Lyrical Tehemes Mostly Talk about Death, Gloom, Depression.

Discography so Far 
Exanimus Demo, 2007
Black Prophet's Bible Full-length, 2007
From the Dead Ground / Oslepni, Oglohni, Sdohni Split, 2008
Taste of Tragedy Full-length, 2009
