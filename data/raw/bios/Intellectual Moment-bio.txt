Intellectual Moment is a classic underground Black Metal band from Brazil which reached some attention (mainly in the hermetic Black Metal circle) in the 1990's. The band split up in May 2006, but regrouped in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Intellectual+Moment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
