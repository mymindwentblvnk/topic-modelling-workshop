Storming the live circuit, first on tour with Bloc Party (he stood in for bassist Gordon) and then as support for Bloc Party & The View, Tommy Sparks continues to showcase his dazzling live show around the UK. Tommy Sparks released his own single "I'm a Rope" in Feburary 09.

Tommy and Liam Howlett went into the studio and created the awesome track ‘Disko Punk’ that will be featured as part of the new Prodigy album package.  Tommy has also remixed the new White Lies & Lady GaGa singles. His debut album was released on May 25th 2009 from Island Records. His hit singles include "I'm a Rope", "She's Got Me Dancing", and the more recent "Miracle".

He is now in a band called Health Club
