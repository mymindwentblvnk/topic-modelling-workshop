There are two bands named R.U.S.T.

1.R.U.S.T. (Romania)  stands for Rock Under the Sign of Thunder. Heavy Metal band from Bucharest formed in 2004. The band members are :
George Popescu (Bass) 
Răzvan Stăicuț( Drums) 
Radu Banu (Guitars) 
Mircea Cristea( Vocals) 
The band is signed on Pure Underground Records
2.R.U.S.T. is a traditional Heavy Metal band from Cyprus that tries to keep the Heavy Metal Flame alive.
