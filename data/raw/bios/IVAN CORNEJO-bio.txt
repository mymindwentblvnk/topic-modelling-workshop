Ivan Cornejo is a singer and songwriter of Regional Mexican Music from Riverside, California. Alma Vacía, his 2021 debut album, was released by  in August, just two months after his 17th birthday; it peaked at number two on the Regional Mexican Albums chart, while the singles "Noche de Relajo," and "Corazón Frio" also registered inside the Top 20. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/IVAN+CORNEJO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
