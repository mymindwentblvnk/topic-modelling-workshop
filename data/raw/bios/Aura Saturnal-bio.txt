Aura Saturnal  is a Black Metal band hailing from Finland, the band formed in 2008 with the sole member Luxixul Sumering Auter, also in the bands Asymmetry & Cosmic Church. 

Discography:
Streams of the Hidden Light - Demo, 2009  
Temple of Lord Sathanas - Demo, 2011  
The Will Return - EP, 2011  
To His Kingdom - Full-length,2012
