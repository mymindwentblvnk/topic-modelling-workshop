Lake Heartbeat is the new project from 90’s Swedish indiepop icon Janne Kask (former member of Brainpool). Pop single “Between Dreams” was produced by D. Lissvik (of Studio) and their debut album, Trust In Numbers, is out on Service. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lake+Heartbeat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
