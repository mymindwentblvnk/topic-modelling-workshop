Shinko Ogata is a Japanese pianist who is best known for her work with Nobuo Uematsu. She appeared on the album Final Fantasy VIII: Piano Collections, performing solo piano arrangements of Uematsu's songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shinko+Ogata">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
