Founded in may 2006.
Formed by Vasna, Hangathyr and Galdor.
Soon Grimmhild joined the band and got the part keyboard/bass.

First demo was released on 09.08.2006 called "Kraft durch Blut". (limited to 50 copies, sold out)

Second Demo was released on 11.11.2006 and is called Serpents of the Revelation.

The full length album "Screams of the Forgotten" was released on 25.06.2008 by Nordsturm Productions.
