Psycho Motel was a British Progressive rock band, formed by then-former Iron Maiden guitarist Adrian Smith in 1996. The band released two albums (1995's State of Mind and 1997's  Welcome to the World)  before they disbanded when Adrian rejoined Iron Maiden with Bruce Dickinson in 1999.

Last Line-Up

* Andy Makin - Vocals
* Adrian Smith - Guitars,backing vocals
* Gary Leidermann - Bass
* Mike Sturgis - Drums

* Former vocalists name was Hans Olav Solli. He comes from Norway, and has been involved in bands such as Sons of Angels, 21 Guns (with Thin Lizzy guitarist Scott Gorham) and many more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psycho+Motel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
