Funebre Hate is a one-man black metal band from Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil, in 2001. 'Rafael Butcher' is the lone member. They have released three demos, their first in 2003, "Por 2000 Anos..." later that same year, and "W.W.A.C. (World War Against Christians) in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Funebre+Hate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
