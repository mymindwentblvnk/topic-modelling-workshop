Desolate Shrine are a death metal band that formed in Helsinki, Finland in 2010. The band are currently signed to Dark Descent Records and they have released three full-length albums: 'Tenebrous Towers' in 2011, 'The Sanctum of Human Darkness' in 2012, and their most recent album 'The Heart of the Netherworld was released on January 13, 2015.
