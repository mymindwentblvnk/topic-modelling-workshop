Slut Sister is a sludgey stoner metal/hardcore band from all around Gilbert, Arizona. Their influences range from bands like Black Sabbath and Pantera to Botch and Everytime I Die. They have two releases. The first is a split with fellow Arizonans Heatstroke released in 09/2007 and the second is their latest EP entitled "Raw Meat".  Raw Meat was recorded entirely in Analog by Bob Hoag in Mesa, Arizona. For more information you can visit their myspace at :
