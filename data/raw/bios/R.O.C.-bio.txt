1:R.O.C. is a hardcore band from Vancouver, featuring Gin&Thomas on guitar and lead vocals, Charlie Horse on bass and backing vocals, and Teddy Spaghetti on drums and backing vocals. Their hits include "Hurry Up (Let's Skate)", "Beer Coma", "Torso Boy", and many more.

2:ROC is Fred Browning (vocals, music), Karen Sheridan (vocals, music), Patrick Nicholson (music). They have released three albums: "ROC" in 1996, "Virgin" in 1997 and "Night Fold Around Me" in 2006. They are based in Brixton in south London.

3:R.O.C is a rapping artist that is under the label of Hatchet House.
