There are three known artists under this name:

1. Virginian producer / singer-songwriter, Ann Alexander Thweatt, is the mind behind electronic, alternative solo act: KID A. 

She has collaborated with great acts such as: dan le sac vs Scroobius Pip, Daedelus, Agoria and those cool Lyonnais bros of Spitzer. As well as performing with the likes of: The Cool Kids,  Girltalk, Danger,  Misty's Big Adventure and GAGGLE . 

https://www.facebook.com/KIDAISKOOL

2. Koncertujemy i pracujemy nad wydaniem debiutanckiej płyty, na której znajdzie się 11 autorskich utworów.
