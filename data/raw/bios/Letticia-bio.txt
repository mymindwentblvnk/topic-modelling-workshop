Letticia is Heavy Metal band from Japan, Iwate perfecture. It's known that they released only one Single - Midnight Dancer (1989).

Members:
Takao Sato - Vocals
Tohru Chiba - Guitar
Shigemi Ito - Bass
Shinobu Obara - Drums
