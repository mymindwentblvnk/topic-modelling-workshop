Black metal band from Malmö, Sweden formed in 2015 consisting of M. A. on bass and vocals, A. L. on drums and O. R. on guitars.

They have released a self-titled demo tape in March 2017 on Änglaslakt Productions. A full-length LP titled "The Spearwound Salvation" was released on Shadow Records the 22nd of March 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ultra+Silvam">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
