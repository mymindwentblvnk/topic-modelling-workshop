The Slow Show is a British indie pop band, formed in Manchester in 2010. 

Their debut EP Midnight Waltz featured use of a colliery type brass band sound, adds an extra dimension to their music.

Drawing on a diverse range of musical inspiration you can hear influences from classic artists like Neil Young. Tom Waits & Bob Dylan echoing through out their music.

Outside of the UK, they are best known in Germany – where their record label Haldern Pop Recordings is based – as well as the Netherlands and Switzerland, based on the number of concerts played in each country.
