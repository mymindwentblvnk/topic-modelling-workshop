Astral Kingdom is a young band from Västerås, Sweden, who plays fast and melodic power metal.

The band was founded in 2003 and they have since then established them as an exciting act on the local hard rock-scene.

They recently released their 1st demo under the name of "Power Metal Through the Universe". You can download it for free on their official website: http://www.astralkingdom.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Astral+Kingdom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
