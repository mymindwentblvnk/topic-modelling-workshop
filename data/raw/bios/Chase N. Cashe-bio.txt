Jesse "Chase N. Cashe" Woodard is an American record producer, rapper, and songwriter born in New Orleans, LA. After relocating to Los Angeles, Chase N. Cashe produced hits for several artists including R. Kelly, Brandy, Flo Rida, Pussycat Dolls, Young Money, Eminem and Lil' Wayne. Once signed under Polow da Don's record label, Zone 4 Inc. as a producer, he released his independent debut album "Gumbeaux" on March 8, 2011. It comes as no surprise that the New Orleans native chose to release his album on the same day known as Mardi Gras Day. Chase N. Cashe makes up a part of the production duo Hit-Boy & Chase N.
