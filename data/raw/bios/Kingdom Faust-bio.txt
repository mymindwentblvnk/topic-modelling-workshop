Blackened death thrash from Laurens, SC.
Delivering some of the most unique and raw death metal with black metal and viking influences. Originally formed by Kyle Hicks and Justin Ruppe as a new project from the ashes of another.

Band members:
Justin "Tzaraath" Ruppe - Guitars
Kyle Hicks - Drums
Michael Parris - Guitars
"Pootie" - Bass & Vocals

Kingdom Faust influences:
Death, Opeth, Pantera, Megadeth, Iron Maiden, Slayer, Emperor, Tool, Mastodon, Cannibal Corpse, Immortal
