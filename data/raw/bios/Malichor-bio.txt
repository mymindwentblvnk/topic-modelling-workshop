Malichor are a relentless black/thrash assault of spikes, bullets and leather influenced by metal gods Desaster, Nifelheim, Bestial Mockery, Destruction, Sodom, Kreator, Venom, Celtic Frost etc. with lyrics inspired by the works of HP Lovecraft and the Cthulhu Mythos.

Discography:

2009 - The Myth (Demo)
2011 - Oath of Dagon (Demo)
2012 - Lurkers in the Crypt (EP)
2013 - Ancient Brew (EP)
2014 - The Serum (7-inch EP)
2014 - The Lost City (Compilation CD)
2014 - Ride As One (CD Single)
