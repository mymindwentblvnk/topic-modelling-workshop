Black metal band from Kyiv, Ukraine created in 2013. Founded by Andrei 'Ekvil' Vereschagin, band's lead vocalist, composer and songwriter. Songs explore dark and mystical themes, music combines classic black metal techniques and modern sound.

Current band line-up: Ekvil — Guitar/vocalFarn — Guitar/back vocalHeydvald — Bass guitar

Before 2015 band was known as Mørkesagn.
