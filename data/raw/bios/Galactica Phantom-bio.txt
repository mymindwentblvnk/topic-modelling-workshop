Melodic power metal band from Japan. The band released their first demo in February, entitled 'Gone With the Hurricane'. They released their first full-lenght studio album, 'From the New Galaxy', on December 26th, 2007, through Kappa Records. Current line-up exists of bassist Takenori ''Ruzer'' Minemura (Sleazy Wizard), drummer Shunsuke Ohyama, vocalist Ray, and guitarist Takeshi Inoue (Jill's Project, Sleazy Wizard).
