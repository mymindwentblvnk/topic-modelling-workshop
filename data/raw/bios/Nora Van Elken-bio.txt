Nora Van Elken is an EDM producer most likely with Dutch roots. She appeared in 2017 with her debut “All I Need” and from there she made several hits such as Stay Right There, Best I Ever Had, and I Wanna Dance With Somebody (Who Loves Me) (Summer Edit) which is nominated as the Alarm Track week 35 by the listeners of Radio Marbella.
