Solsikk are a metal band from London, UK. They finalised their debut E.P. 'Volatile Territory' in 2008, through Klotho Records. The line-up is as follows: Vykki (vocals), Chris (guitar), Mark (bass) and Matt (drums).

Their debut album was released on the second of May 2011 and is called: Solsikk.
