BrazZil were a hungarian based Heavy Metal band, formed in 1994. They released the s/t album in 1995, and then they have split up.

Members:
Zoltán Szokodi - Vocals
Norbert Mentes - Guitars (Moby Dick, Hungarica, Masina)
Balázs Birkás - Bass (Paranoid)
Miklós Jancsó - Keyboards (Moby Dick)
