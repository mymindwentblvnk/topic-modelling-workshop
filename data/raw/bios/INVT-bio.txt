Miami natives, Luca Medici and Delbert Perez first met in elementary school through skateboarding. They've grown up playing together in orchestra, jazz bands, and metal bands. Luca on the drums, Delbert on the bass. During high school they began producing music together as INVT and performing at clubs and house parties. Their live set combines chill synths and dark beats. They performed at the Black Friday Boiler Room in Miami in 2019.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/INVT">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
