Mozzy is an American rapper. Representing Sacramento to the fullest, Mozzy's music is a direct piece in the West Coast gangsta hip hop movement, with bouncy beats coupled with violent lyrics and catchy hooks. After releasing several albums independently, he first gained nationwide traction with his 2015 release "Bladadah". He eventually signed to Empire Records in 2016, with his album "Mandatory Check" being his first label release. Earning cosigns from Kendrick Lamar, YG, Boosie Badazz, Ty Dolla $ign and Lil Durk, amongst others, Mozzy has shown that he's a force to be reckoned with in West coast hip hop. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mozzy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
