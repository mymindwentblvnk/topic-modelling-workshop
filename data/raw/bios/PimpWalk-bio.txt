PimpWalk is a band from Pleszew, Poland that plays a mixture of thrash/death and nu metal. The band features heavy and brutar guitrs, fast drums and melodic vocals. 

Pimpwalk was formed in the summer of 2007 in Pleszew, Poland when  Henry  (drums) and  Gumis  (guitar) decided to jam away the songs of their favorite bands. After Mechanix (Henry's thrash metal band) had gone on hiatus PimpWalk turned into a a fullfledged project.

In 2008 Pimpwalk wrote over a dozen of tracks attempting to create their own unique sound. By summer 2008 the line up had been completed with Mat on guitar, Fabian on bass, and Nupa as the band's soundguy. In December 2008 Maskey - the lead singer joind the band ending the seach for the right screamer.

In February 2009 PimpWalk played their first show receiving positive reviews.

In December 2009 the band released their first official demo entitled  Error 204, which is available for streaming and free download on their last.fm profile page or the band's website.

After the EP release the band change the lineup and with the new bass player and afemale singer they are releasing their sophomore EP in February 2012.
