Dear life is a five piece hardcore/metalcore band from Hacienda Heights, California. Forming a band in December 2003, their fanbase has grown rapidly since 2004; when they released their  first CD entitled "DEMO 2004". They went on their first tour in August 2005, there holds no limits to Dear Life's fanbase now, some fans even settled in parts outside of Southern California. Dear Life remains unsigned, yet they have had the pleasure to tour with bands such as Arsonists Get All the Girls, Catherine, Lynch Thy Beauty, and many more. Dear Life is now preparing for their Summer Tour of 2006 and working on releasing their full length album called "The Framework."  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dear+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
