Atoll Nerat - is a band exploring toltek dark metal music burdened with death and black patterns. It is based in Okhanks (Russia, the Urals, Perm Region). They also offer a more accurate definition of this musical trend: 'psychedelic paranormal reality metal'. This implies constant drifting towards the boundaries of perception, major source of inspiration. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atoll+Nerat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
