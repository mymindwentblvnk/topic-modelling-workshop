Death metal band from Puławy, Poland. Currently unsigned Incarnal released their full length "Where Evil Has Its Beginning" in 2012 an EP "I Have Worn a Bird's Mask" in 2013 and a second full length "Hexenhammer" in 2014. 

Incarnal are:

Krzysztof Kiecana Guitars (2010-present)
Karol "North" Łapczyński Vocals (2010-present)
Mateusz Szymanek Bass (2012-present)
Mikołaj Kujda Drums (2012-present)
