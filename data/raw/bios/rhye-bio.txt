Rhye is the musical project of Toronto native Milosh. Originally conceived as a collaboration between him and Robin Hannibal of Danish electro-soul outfit Quadron, the project has evolved into a music collective led by Milosh and focused around its associated live band.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rhye">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
