Diabless is atmospheric death metal band from Bulgaria. Ivaylo Hristoskov (lead guitar and vocals) and Yavor Alexandrov (guitar) started the band named Frozen Shit in 2007, later Nikolay Lozanov (drums) joined the band. After some changes in band lineup in the beginning of 2010 Petko Hristov (bass) and Georgi Kovachev (vocals) joined Frozen Shit and the band played some gigs. In the beginning of 2011 Georgi Kovachev left the band, Ivaylo Hristoskov took the lead vocal parts and Frozen Shit became DIABLESS.
