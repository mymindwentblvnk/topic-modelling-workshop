Everto Signum (Demon's Sign in Latin), also known as Everto, is an Elemental Black Metal band from Aeminium, Portugal. The band's concept consists of the interconnection of the elements and destructive powers of Nature with human psychology and negative emotions. The musical creations combine a traditional Black Metal sonority with melodic and ambient parts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Everto+Signum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
