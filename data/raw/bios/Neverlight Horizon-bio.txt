Neverlight Horizon, formerly known as Born Of Fire, is a death metal band from Belgium.
The current line-up consists of:

Rebhell (Jean-Louis Lizin) - Vocals (1999-) (Devoured (Bel), Catarrhal, Xhzenat, Bursting)
Loris - Guitar (2000-) (Slaughtery)
Laurent D. - Bass (Slaughtery)
Laurent S. - Guitar
Julien - Drums
