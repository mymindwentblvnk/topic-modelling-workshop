Ashen Epitaph is a death metal band from Senta (Serbia,Vojvodina). Founded in 2003, they've released a demo and a full-length. They're highly influenced by early Swedish, British, and Floridian death metal bands. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ashen+Epitaph">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
