Nick Zammuto is an experimental folk / indie rock musician, formerly one half of the highly acclaimed duo The Books. After he and the other half (Paul de Jong) broke up in early 2012, he released the solo album, Zammuto. Zammuto is currently a four-piece band consisting of Nick, his brother Mikey, Nick Oddy, and Sean Dixon.st A short documentary about the band entitled The Shape of Things to Come was directed and released by Wondermind Pictures spotlighting the band. This documentary received wide critical acclaim appearing in numerous film festivals around the United States. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zammuto">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
