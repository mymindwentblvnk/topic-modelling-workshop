Hollywood Principle is an electronic music group from San Diego, formed in 2013 by Elliott Sencan, Mike Ault and Kayla Hope. Their first single "Find Me Out" debuted in December of 2014. In 2015, the singles Seeing What's Next and Firework were featured in the 2015 game “Rocket League”, as well as Breathing Underwater (Ether Remix) which drew many new fans to the band.

In 2016, their single Solar Eclipses was selected as the theme for the new Rocket League update, “Starbase Arc”, and featured a collaboration with rapper Dr. Awkward.

Hollywood Principle continues to work very closely with the remixer community, such as Sando, Melad, Kev Frey, and Ether.
