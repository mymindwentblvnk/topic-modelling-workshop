Brain Failure (脑浊) is a punk rock band that is based in Beijing, China. The band was formed in 1997. Their music is influenced by bands such as The Clash and Rancid. Their lyrical subject matter covers politics and personal politics. They have toured with Dropkick Murphys, The Business, and The Unseen from Boston, Massachusetts. The band has recently released a split E.P. with another Boston band, Big D and the Kids Table. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brain+Failure">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
