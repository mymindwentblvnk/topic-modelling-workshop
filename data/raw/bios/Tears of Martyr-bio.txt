Tears of Martyr is a gothic metal band formed in 1996 in Madrid, Spain.

Current line-up:
Miguel Ángel Marqués - Vocals & Guitar
Berenice Musa - Soprano (guest in Angeldark)
Doramas Parraga - Drums

Former/past member(s):
Javier Montesdeoca - Guitar (Scandelion)
Armando J. Alvarez - Vocals & Bass (Scandelion, Perihellion)
Yeray Corujo - Keyboards (Archantia)
Carlos "Archeon" - Bass

Discography:
The Essence of Evil, Demo, 1996
Eyes to See, Heart to Feel, Single, 2002
Renascence, Demo, 2005
