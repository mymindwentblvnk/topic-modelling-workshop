Euro-dance ensemble Hampenberg comprises DJ Morten Hampenberg (born in Stenbuk in 1977), Thomas Salling, and Lin Samuelsen. The Danish trio debuted in 1999 with the release of a self-titled album including the hit single "Last Night" and their first smash called "Grab That Thing." Two years later, Hampenberg returned to the European dance music charts with "Ducktoy" and "Listen Up," featured on their second album, entitled Duck Off. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hampenberg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
