Thomas Dinger, born October 28th 1952 in Germany, was a German drummer, singer and songwriter who was active in solo pursuits in addition to having been a member of Neu!, La Düsseldorf and 1-A Düsseldorf. Dinger passed away April 9th 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thomas+Dinger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
