1) A German black metal band who has released two demos and an EP.

2) Refractory from France have to date one self-titled album after several appearences on internationally famous compilations such as St. Germain, Buddha Bar IV & V, Blue Note and Barrio Latino on George V Records. 

The album includes the participation of the Budapest Symphony Orchestra on the track Coincidence. Sailing between Jazz, Trip-Hop, Electro and Latin influences, the first album by Refractory could be a soundtrack of a contemporary urban movie. 

All lyrics are offered by Lissette Alea (discovered on the single Come With Me produced by Alex Kid on F-Communication) and interpreted by the charming and talented jazz singer You Sun Nah, who is signed to Sony in Korea. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Refractory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
