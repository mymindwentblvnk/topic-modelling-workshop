Rosa Antica was born in September 2005 from five members of five Italian Metal bands (Crepuscolar Decadence - Bleed - Dying Awkward Angel - Zenith - Sin.Done).
They are first of all friends then musicians, tryin' to spend good time together, playing and mixing the different kind of music they like (heavy metal, death metal, black metal, thrash metal, grind, nu old school, industrial, alternative,...).
They have recorded their first CD "Looking for something divine" (2006) followed by several shows in their country.
In 2007 they have signed for MY KINGDOM MUSIC and they were actually in studio, recording their first full lenght CD "SEVEN", which now available worldwide.
