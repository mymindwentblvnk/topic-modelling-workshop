Abominattion is a death metal act from Caxias do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Known as Anesthesia from 1994 to 1998, they reformed under their current name in 1998. Abominattion play monotonous tranditional death metal, reminiscent of early Krisiun. Their discography includes Hated by the Flames (Demo, 1999), Rites of the Eternal Hate (LP, 1999), Doutrine of False Martyr (LP, 2003), and Terror Nazarene (Demo, 2008).
