Sorceror is the solo project of Lugburz, Black Scourge and Inferi Diabolos band member Count Gravindak The Feared(Gorthaur in Lugburz). Entrance of Darkness split with Mourning is out now. Originally just black metal, but now has turned into a Dark Ambient project in the Vein of Aakon Keetreh, Moevot, and other ambient black metal projects.
