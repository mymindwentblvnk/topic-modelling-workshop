Nihilion is a melodic death metal band from Münster, Germany. 
Members are:
Christoph "Lusch" Tudzynski (v) 
Wladlen "Wajik" Schöner (g) 
Etienne Onasch (g) 
Christian Demter (d) 
Hans Stiegler (b)
