Immaculate is a thrash metal band from Uppsala, Sweden and also a deathcore band from Pensacola, Florida (USA).

The swedish Immaculate have released their debut album "Thrash, Kill and Destroy" on the Witches Brew label. They are all about retro videogames, death to poseurs and ofcourse thrashing until death. 
www.immaculatethrash.com


Thrash Metal band:

Nino Vukovic - Guitars
Fadi Ghanime - Guitars
Mika Eronen - Vocals
Daniel Kallin - Bass
Oscar Moritz - Drums


Deathcore band:

Owen Henderson - Vocals
David Franklin - Guitar
Logan Thomason - Guitar
Nick Fredrickson - Bass
TBA - Drums

Immaculate has played with acts such as: Impending Doom, Blessed By A Broken Heart, Whitechapel, Suicide Silence, With Blood Comes Cleansing, War Of Ages, Mychildren Mybride, Ambush, Too Pure To Die, Sleeping Giant, Arsis, Here I Come Falling, Before Thier Eyes, Akissforjeresy, The Red Baron, Words in Red, Waking the Cadaver, Rose Funeral, Catalepsy, Left To Vanish, Without Remorse, and many more.
