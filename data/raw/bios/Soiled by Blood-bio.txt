Soiled By Blood started in 2008 with Alex (vocals) and his brother Mick (guitar). A couple of month later Oli joined the band as the bass player  but left the band a year and half later. Antoine arrived in the middle 2008 as the drummer. Rock (2nd guitar) joined in 2010 and D-A (bass) arrived in 2011. Since 2008, SBB shared the stage with bands such as Cryptopsy, Aeternam, Waking The Cadaver, Exhumed, Untimely Demise, Goatwhore, Mesrine, Havok, Fuck The Facts, and more. The band recorded their 1rst demo/EP in 2011 (all songs available on Facebook) and they're working hard to achieve their 1rst album writing ! Keep Supporting QC death metal scene !
