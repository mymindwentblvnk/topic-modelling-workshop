Black Metal band coming from the depths of  Los Angeles, CA. Influenced by Mayan dark art and themes such as depression, suicide, war, and astral projections. Their music ranges from Xibalba, Yoatl Mictlan to Abyssic Hate, Silencer, Luror, Sterbendbesungen. For more info visit their myspace page.
