Klava Koka (Клава Кока, born July 23 1996 in Yekaterinburg) is a modern Russian country pop singer, video blogger, musician and songwriter.

In 2012, Klava appeared on the musical TV project "Factor A" and entered the top six of the category of young performers. The star jury were surprised by the rare timbre and bright individuality of the artist. Koka has recorded covers for different performers, after which her songs were more widely distributed among the younger generation.
