This page references two artists:

1) Raw Black Metal/Noise from Russia. Tchort's music is written during natural shamanic substances and muskarine (toadstool) intoxication. Tchort means devil in Russian.

2) Tchort is the alias of the lead guitarist and creative force behind Norwegian metal bands Green Carnation and Blood Red Throne, among others. He was also Emperor's bassist on their seminal album In the Nightside Eclipse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tchort">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
