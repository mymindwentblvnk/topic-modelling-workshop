There are four artists with this name:

1. An existentialist nightmare band from Hämeenkyrö, Finland.
2. Quickly establishing band born out of Edinboro, PA
3. Vancouver death/crust
4. Technical BDM band from Maryland. Released self-titled EP in 2012.



1. Founded in early 2006, Absurdist is a musical perpetuum mobile whose spiritual home lies in Hämeenkyrö, Finland. During its existence, Absurdist has tried to earn its spurs by releasing a few self-released albums and by playing an ever-growing number of shows, but has not yet been on the lips of mainstream music journalists or eager teenage fan girls. The band is closely involved with Suomu Records, a local huddle of experimental musicians within the Hämeenkyrö region, and is one of the head curators of Paskarok! festival happenings.

Antti Lahti - drums
Kalle Hakala - bass
Timo Korpi - vocals, guitar
Eemeli Hakala - vocals, guitar

2. Absurdist is an quickly establishing band born out of Edinboro, PA.  They play a range of genres varying between crust punk, grindcore, sludge, and hardcore.  The band believes in free distribution of music and releases all of their music independently and free of charge. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absurdist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
