The U.N. is a project led by Harlem rapper Cam'ron, featuring Charlie Clips, Byrd Lady, Vado, Fly Boyz, Begetz, Young Hustle, Felony Fame and Omar 'Iceman' Sharif. The group's debut album, Gunz & Butta, will be released in March of 2010. The group's first mixtape, Heat In Here Vol. 1, was released in May of 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+U.N.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
