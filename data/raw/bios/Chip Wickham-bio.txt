Established member of UK Jazz Scene with three big selling albums. He has an impressive live act that won a JazzFM award for best live act at The London Jazz Festival. An artist equally at home in the jazz club (selling out Ronnie Scott's) as well as playing the big festival stage such as Love Supreme Jazz Festival.

Chip Wickham has worked, written, recorded, and toured with the likes of Matthew Halsall, Nat Birchall, Dwight Tribble, Lack of Afro, Scrimshire, Allysha Joy, The Sorcerers, The New Mastersounds, Craig Charles' Fantasy Funk Band, Rae & Christian, The Pharcyde, Jimpster, Nightmares On Wax, Badly Drawn Boy, Andy Votel, Grupo X and Graham Massey to name a few!
