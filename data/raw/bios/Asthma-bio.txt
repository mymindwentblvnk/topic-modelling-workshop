There's more than one band with the name Asthma:

1. Asthma is a thrash metal band formed in Daugavpils, Latvia in early 2009 by a lead guitarist Max Popov after the disbanding his former project Balaguri.[1]

2. Asthma is an alternative rock band formed in 1998 in El Paso, United States, with Lui and Yiyo.[2]

3. Asthma is a crust band from Moscow, Russian Federation. All the members are vegetarians and adhere libertarian ideas.[3]

4. Asthma was a speed metal band from Spain.[4]
