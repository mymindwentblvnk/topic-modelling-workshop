Death toll is a Death and Thrash Metal group created at the end of the year 2007 by the Brothers Ariel Fernandez and Bruno Fernandez and a friend Daniel Jimenez.

The group start with several names and playing covers. After changes in the members of the group and renaming Death toll we begins a serious project and with own music at the beginning of the year 2009.

Former members Daniel (Guitar and choirs) Jessica (bass) Hiram (bass) RECENT NEWS: The Band expects to bring out to sale the demo " wall ripping flesh" by the middle of April, and to initiate playing with a new member marco alderete in the guitar.
