Namter is a Black Metal act from Italy. It was founded in 2004 and is still active today.

Namter's musical lyrics are mainly about Satanism, nature and misantropy.

Namter himself does all the instruments, vocals and programming. 

So far, Namter has released the following:

- Hostis Humani Generis Demo, 2004  
- Paritur Pax Bello Split, 2006
