Boston, Massachusetts (2007-present)

Ice Dragon is an eclectic and prolific psychedelic/doom/stoner/heavy rock band. The band makes recordings in a basement near Boston, Massachusetts. Works include many albums, singles, and EPs, as well as numerous releases under various other names.
