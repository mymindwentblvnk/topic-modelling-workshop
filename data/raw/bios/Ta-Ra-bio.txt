Ta-Ra (formerly Ta-Ha, Taha, senshi1992) (A.K.A tuaregshawty) is a French Algerian singer songwriter know for her low wave, Japanese inspired, unique beats. Her kicked-backed, dreamlike R&B has had plays on on OVO Sound and Soulection radio shows, and she performed earlier this year at Skepta's Music and Revolution event at London's V&A museum. Not to mention, back in March she appeared in The FADER's Global Issue as one of 25 underground artists around the world that we're excited about. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ta-ra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
