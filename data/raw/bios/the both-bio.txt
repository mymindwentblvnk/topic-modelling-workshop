The Both are an American rock band based in Los Angeles, California. The band is a collaborative project between bassist/vocalist Aimee Mann (formerly of 'Til Tuesday) and guitarist/vocalist Ted Leo (formerly of Citizens Arrest, Chisel, The Sin-Eaters and The Spinanes; also of Ted Leo and the Pharmacists). The band originally formed in February of 2012 after the two toured together under their respective solo guises. Originally known as #Both, the project later became known as The Both. The band's debut album was recorded in Los Angeles in 2013 by Ryan Freeland and producer Paul Bryan. Their first single, "Milwaukee," was released in early 2014. Their self-titled debut album was released in April 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Both">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
