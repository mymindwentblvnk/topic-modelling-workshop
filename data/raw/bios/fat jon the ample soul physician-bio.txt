Fat Jon the Ample Soul Physician (born John Marshall) is an American hip-hop producer and emcee from Cincinnati, Ohio and one of the four members of Five Deez, alongside Pase Rock, Sonic, and Kyle David (a.k.a. Chilly Most).

He has recently gained fame for his work on the Samurai Champloo soundtrack alongside Force of Nature, Nujabes, and Tsutchie. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Fat+Jon+the+Ample+Soul+Physician">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
