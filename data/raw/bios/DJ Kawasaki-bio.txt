DJ KAWASAKI is a well-known Japanese house DJ and one of the biggest forthcomers in the growing Japanese DJ scene. He made his debut in 2006 with his album 'Beautiful', which recieved much positive reception and impressed many international artists who wished to collaborate with him. 

His music is defined by his unique beats and uplifting vocals featuring many forthcoming and well-known artists both in and out of Japan.

Since then he has produced and remixed artists including Kyoto Jazz Massive, Haijime Yoshizawa, Ananda Project, Towa Tei and Gordon Chambers amongst others and covered Herbie Hancock's 'Maiden Voyage' for Blue Note. Shuya Okino's 'Shine', on which DJ KAWASAKI collaborated on the sound production, will be released from 'Defected' records, a UK House label which many regard as the most influential house label world wide. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Kawasaki">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
