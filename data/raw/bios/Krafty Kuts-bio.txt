Since the late 80's Krafty Kuts, a.k.a Martin Reeves, has been one of the world's best-known purveyors of breakbeats and old-skool hip-hop.  Hailing from Brighton, he moved from running a record shop to running around the world as he DJs internationally while producing a vast number of mixed albums and original material, including "Freakshow", released on his label Against The Grain. He also started the best breakbeat night in the country with Skool of Thought.. SuperCharged @ Audio every wednesday brighton uk. DJs at the night now play the best beats and bass music, from drum n bass to dubstep, turntablism to breaks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Krafty+Kuts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
