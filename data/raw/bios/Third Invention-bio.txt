Third Invention from Grimstad, Norway plays heavy metal with some thrash metal influences.
The band has selfreleased one EP, Blackened Dream (2005).

The bands current lineup is:
Erik Jakobsen - Lead vocals and guitars
Martin Berger - Guitars and backing vocals
Kristian Rakvåg - Bassguitar
