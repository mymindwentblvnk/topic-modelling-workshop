1. Disregard is a death metal band from Gothenburg, Sweden. Members are: Daniel Toivola, Oskar Fahlén, John Bergqvist, Magnus Dahlin and Simon Wärnén

2. Disregard was an "emo" type hardcore band from the Philadelphia area from 1991-1994. Members included Justin Phillips, Don Devore, Dan O'Neill, Bill Perri and Nathan Sabatino.

3. Disregard is a Grove Death Metal band from Israel. Members are : Raz Edri, Alon Meirovich, Ray Katlarov, Stav ShemTov
