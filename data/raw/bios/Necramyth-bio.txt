Necramyth was a metal band from Seoul, South Korea. The band was formed in 2001. Having had several line-up changes, guitarist/vocalist Urlock (Pedro Chae) has been the only member who has been in the band since it's inception, and interestingly David Gold (Woods of Ypres) was their drummer while working as an english teacher in South Korea. The band split up in 2009 when Urlock decided to relocate to Madrid, Spain. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necramyth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
