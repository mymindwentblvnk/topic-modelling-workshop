This garage rock band came from Amarillo, Texas and formed in 1962.  Guitarist and songwriter Jim Parker was a member of this band.  They were influenced by British Invasion bands, especially The Dave Clark Five.  They were signed to LHI, which was a record label owned by Lee Hazlewood. They only released one full-length album called Everything But in 1967.  They disbanded in 1968. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Kitchen+Cinq">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
