Nordawn is a solo epic/folk metal project from Canada. The music contains folk, black and death metal influences and seeks to create an atmospheric and epic feel. Lyrically, the songs range from natural romanticism to themes of nationalism, war, honor, and sacrifice. Songs are written in English and in Russian. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nordawn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
