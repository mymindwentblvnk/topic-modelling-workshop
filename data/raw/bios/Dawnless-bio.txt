Dawnless is a Melodic Metal band from Wallis, Switzerland.

They were formed in 2003, and in 2006 their first album was released: A Way Of Escape.
A video was shot in the Swiss Alps for the song 'Winds Of Fate'.
A new album has been recorded and released in 2009. It's called "While Hope Remains"
