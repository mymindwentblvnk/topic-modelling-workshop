Sonic Mass ~ London, England
Psychedelic Rock

Formed in November 2007 and up until 2009 performed under the name Acid Temple, Sonic Mass are a psychedelic hard rock band from London, England. Having extensively gigged around the London circuit, they have performed alongside such acts as Firebird, Litmus and dutch veterans Focus. Sonic Mass approve of LSD psychotherapy. They have never been to the desert.
