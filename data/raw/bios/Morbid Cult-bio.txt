French group formed in 2002 with Ulven on vocals, Hammerfrost on the guitar, Krieg on bass and using session drummers. After recording the 2004 demo 'Christicide' the band called it quits in 2006. Ulver remains active as a solo member whilst the band is on hold. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+Cult">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
