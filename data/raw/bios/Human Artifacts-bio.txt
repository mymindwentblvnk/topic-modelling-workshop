Human Artifacts was a Brutal Death Metal band from USA. They formed in late 2005 by Matt Bishop (Guitars/Vocals, Ex-Lividity) and Robert Brodie on Drums. In November of 2005 Chris Montez and jimbo Kelly joined the band and in january 2006 replaced Chris and Jimbo with Aaron Harper (ex Lividity) on Guitars and Jake Lahniers (Deaden) on bass and Vocals. They released one debut album called "The Principles of Sickness" in 2007 with  Comatose. They recently split up in 2008 to pursue other interests.
