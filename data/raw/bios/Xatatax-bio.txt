Xatatax is a 4 piece thrash/punk/doom band from Massachusetts. The band is made up of Ben Morse (rythym guitar/vocals) Steve Smith (lead guitar) Sam Berg (bass/vocals) and Jeff Leblanc (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xatatax">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
