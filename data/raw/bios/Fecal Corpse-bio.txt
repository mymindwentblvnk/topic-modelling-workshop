With rising popularity from the underground scene since forming in 2000, Fecal Corpse are a New York City based goregrind/death metal band who has released an EP, a full length and a recent split album with E.T.T.S..  The band is currently signed to Will Rahmer's (Mortician) label Redrum Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fecal+Corpse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
