Daorson is a black metal band from Zagreb, Croatia. The first self-titled Daorson EP was recorded in autumn 2014 at Studio Veles and released on the 31st January 2015. 

M. – guitars, vocals 
N. – guitars, vocals 
D. – bass 
B. – drums
