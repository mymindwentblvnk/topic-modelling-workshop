Umoral is a black metal band formed in Norway in 2006.

Members are:

- Zweizz (Svein Egil Hatlevik) - Vocals (Fleurety, ex-Dødheimsgard, Aphrodisiac)

- Teloch - Guitars, Bass (Nidingr, Orcustus, Gorgoroth, 1349)

- Hellhammer (Jan Axel von Blomberg) - Drums (Mayhem (Nor), Antestor, Arcturus (Nor), Carnivora, Covenant (Nor), Jørn, Dimmu Borgir, Mezzerschmitt, Mortem (Nor), Shining (Swe), The Kovenant, Thorns (Nor), Troll (Nor), Vidsyn, Winds (Nor), Tritonus, Age of Silence)
