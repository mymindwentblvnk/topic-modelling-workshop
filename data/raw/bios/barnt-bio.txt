Barnt is German artist and DJ Daniel Ansorge (one third of Magazine and part of Cómeme) 

He released his debut EP on Magazine, which became an instant Modern Kosmische classic in 2010.
He followed with "Geffen" in 2012 which was released on Cómeme's now legendary "The Power Of Now EP" and topped many end of year polls. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Barnt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
