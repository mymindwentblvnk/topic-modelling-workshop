Salomón Villada Hoyos, better known as Feid, is a Colombian singer and songwriter. Born in Medellín, Colombia, he has worked with several well-known singers such as J Balvin, Sebastian Yatra, Nacho and Mackie. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Feid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
