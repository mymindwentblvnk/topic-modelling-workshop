Black metal band formed in Larvik, Norway during 2008, they play in the early/mid 90s tradition. 
They have two releases on Blut & Eisen productions: I elendighetens selskap, the debut released in 2011, and Betrayal and Blasphemy a vinyl-only split 12" with Kirkebrann released in 2012. They are active.
