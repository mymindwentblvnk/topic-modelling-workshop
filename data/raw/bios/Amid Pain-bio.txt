Death metal from Maspeth, New York, formed in 1997. Their only release is the 1998 demo "Endure the Suffering", which had Joe Heller on the bass (ex-Darkside NYC), Dave Penna on the drums (formerly of Kronin and Mystic), John Sheehan on the guitars (played in Revoltage) and Matt Szablewicz as the vocalist (former member of Cerebral Hemorrhage). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amid+Pain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
