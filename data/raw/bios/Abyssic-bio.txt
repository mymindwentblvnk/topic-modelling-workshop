Abyssic is a symphonic funeral doom metal project with ex-Old Man's Child members. The music is dense and sweeping, with walls of riffs and baritone roars punctuated by pianos and mighty horns, contrasting the sparse clean guitar breaks, with dense layers that swell and pummel.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ABYSSIC">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
