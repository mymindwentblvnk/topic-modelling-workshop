Savanah is an austrian Stoner Rock Trio from Graz, formed in 2014.

The band takes influences from Doom, Desert and Psychedelic Rock to create their sound, once described as aggressive Stoner with a deafening bass and powerful riffs. Energetic Live-Performances are part of the bands character and attitude.

Members: Benny, Felix, Jakob
