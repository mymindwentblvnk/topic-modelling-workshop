Duelbarrel is a Death/Thrash Metal band originating from Stockholm, Sweden. Duelbarrel was formed in late 2005 by former Lobotomy  members Lars Jelleryd and Daniel Strachal. Jakob "Jake" Leczinsky later joined the band to fill the spot of lead guitarist.

In June 2007 Duelbarrel's first demo entitled "Desolated Works Of Art" finally saw the light of day.
