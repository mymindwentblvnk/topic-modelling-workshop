Pagan Spirit are a a pagan black metal band hailing from Slovakia.  They formed in the year 2000 with the line-up of:

Ordog - solo guitar, vocals
Dainonion - drums
Hrabie Macina - keyboards
Ohaf - bass
Majster Sfery - guitar

They rely heavily on keyboards to create atmospheric elements and the melodies they use.
