Defuze is a four piece rock band from Rochester Minnesota. Their sound is an explosive mixture of high energy guitar riffs, mixed in with melodic, aggressive vocals. The band has been around for several years, but just as of lately finally started making their presence with the addition of singer/guitar player, Jon Prosch. Currently the band is working on their first full length album, due out late winter/early spring of 2009 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/defuze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
