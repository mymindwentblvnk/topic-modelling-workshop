Dynfari is a black metal band from Iceland, formed in 2010 and recorded their first album in december 2010 and in early 2011. There are two members in the band; Jóhann Örn - vocals, bass rhythm guitar and Jón Emil - percussion, lead guitar. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dynfari">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
