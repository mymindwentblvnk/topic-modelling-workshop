Novembers Fall are a progressive death metal band from Neukirchen-Vluyn, Germany. Founded in 2003 they have released three demos so far, their latest, Mythaeon, being free for download on their website. Their style is coined by the lengthy songs, complex arrangements and various influences, of which Opeth is the most notable.
With the begin of 2008 the band decided to break up and most of the old members started a new project called Phyria (http://phyria.com/). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Novembers+Fall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
