Hate For Revenge is a Brazilian heavy metal band from SÃ£o Paulo. Formed in 1999 by the guitarrist Samuel Matioli and the drummer Sidnei Nonato.

Hate For Revenge has so far released three demos and is actually promoting the first studio album also called Hate For Revenge.

The main goal of the band is to provide modern heavy metal music without losing references from other bands in the genre, resulting in a mixture of agressiveness, melodic and powerful music.

The line-up has changes a few times over the last years and the actual line-up is:

Alex Takayama - Vocals
Samuel Matioli - Guitars
Saulo Peghin - Guitars
Sidnei Nonato - Drums
Willi Schults Borelli - Bass
