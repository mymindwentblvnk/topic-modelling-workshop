Erick Elliott, better known as Erick Arc Elliott or Erick the Architect, is an American rapper and record producer of Jamaican descent from Brooklyn, New York. A third of the hip hop trio, Flatbush ZOMBiES, Elliot has also released several solo projects. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Erick+The+Architect">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
