Skalariak is a band from the Basque Country founded in 1994 by Juantxo Skalari and Peio Skalari. As indicated in the name, Skalariak plays Ska. In most of their songs they play solid Ska, sometimes with influences from Reggae, Punk or Latin music. Skalariak also spreads a political message against globalization and capitalism in their songs. Their lyrics are in Spanish and Basque. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skalariak">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
