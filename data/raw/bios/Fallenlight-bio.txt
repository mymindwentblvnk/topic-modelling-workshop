Fallenlight is an oldschool black metal punk (in the vein of Venom and Darkthrone) from Yogyakarta, Indonesia.

• Current Line-Up:
   Abenz - Amateur riff compiler, bass, rotten vocal.
   M.O.T.F - Monotonous drumming, vocal hell

• Discography :
   'OSBM' Demo Rehearsal 2005
   'Last Man Standing' Demo 2010
