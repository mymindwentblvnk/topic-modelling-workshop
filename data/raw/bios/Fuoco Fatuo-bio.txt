Fuoco Fatuo is an Italian death/doom metal band. Since forming in 2011, they've released 2 EPs, contributed to a split with Black Temple Below, and one full-length. 

Their music will likely appeal to fans of Ataraxie, Evoken, Indesinence, Anhedonist, and/or Runemagick. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fuoco+Fatuo">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
