Murdercar is the band that Ross Robinson was in after Catalepsy, from 1989 to 1990. They have recorded a 9-song album which has never been released, and have a song on "Metal Massacre X" ("Mirage of Blood"). The line-up consisted, with Ross, of Dave McClain (of Sacred Reich and Machinehead fame) on drums, Dave Clemmons on bass (who later joined Damn the Machine with Chris Poland, of Megadeth fame, now fronting the band Jud that have 3 albums out on Nois-O-Lution in Germany) and Shawn Hamrick on vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Murdercar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
