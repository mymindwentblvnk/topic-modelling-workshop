Black Wizard is a psychedelic stoner metal band from New Westminster, a suburb of Vancouver, Canada. The band is composed of Eug, Adam, Fee and Kendo. In 2009 they released a debut self-titled EP, with Mountain Bitch 7" following in suit. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Wizard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
