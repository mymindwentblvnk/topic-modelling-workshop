A side project of Piekielne Wrota, Morbid Insane were a death metal band from Koszali, Poland. Released two demos in the early 90's: "Sicken Crazy" in 1991 and "Bez Przyszłości" in 1992. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+Insane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
