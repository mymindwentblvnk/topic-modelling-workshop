Fathom Amity are a 5 piece Hardcore/Deathcore band based in Victoria, Australia.
All of the members in the band are currently 16 years of age.
With influences coming from bands like As Blood Runs Black, Whitechapel, Carpathian & Emmure, Fathom Amity have an exciting live show and is definately not one to be missed.

They are currently writing for an EP that will be recorded and released in the coming months.

Their current line up includes:

Xav: Vocals.
Jake: Guitar.
Clay: Guitar.
Eli: Bass.
Sam: Drums.
