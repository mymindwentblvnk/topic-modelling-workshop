Phantasmagory was Progressive death metal band from Ukraine. They formed in 1991 under the name "Death Sentence", and played a hybrid of  melodic death metal and doom metal. In 1996, they changed their name to Phantasmagory and began playing in a style similar to Cynic's "Focus" and Pestilence's "Spheres". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phantasmagory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
