Hailing from Upstate NY, USA.....
 This female fronted gothic/rock quintet has The Vocals of Lacuna Coil, mixed with the music of Dream Theater. Twist a dark gothic feel into the mix and you would be close. They are trying hard to define their own genre of Gothic Progressive Metal.
