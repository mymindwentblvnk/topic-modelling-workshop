Nuclear Scorn is a blackened thrash/d-beat infused crustgrind band that was conceived in the late fall of 2010 in El Paso, TX by Frankie[Drums], Josh[Guitar], and Isaiah[Vocals] at show when they talk about forming a band that was raw, fast, dirty ,and above all, crusty.  Around the December of 2010, Marcus[Bass] was brought in and stabilized the current line-up that is seen.Throughout 2011, Nuclear Scorn have toured and made new music, releasing a self-titled demo('Nuclear Scorn'). As of the end of 2012 Nuclear Scorn has split-up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nuclear+Scorn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
