Sacred Paws is a Scottish rock band comprising Rachel Aggs (of Shopping, Trash Kit, Cover Girl) on guitar and Eilidh Rodgers (of Golden Grrrls) on drums. Their debut album Strike a Match won the 2017 Scottish Album of the Year Award.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sacred+Paws">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
