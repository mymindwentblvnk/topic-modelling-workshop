Orgasmic Victim is a goregrind/brutal death metal band from Oktyabrsky, Russia that formed in the year 2010.  They consist of Rinat (vocals), Slam (guitars), Viktoria (bass), Ruslan Rudenko (drums) and Ramil Khaibullin (unknown).  Khaibullin played bass in NeverDie, but it is unknown what his role is in Orgasmic Victim.  They write songs about gore, porn and sickness.  They released three full-length albums and split with Brud and Disentomb (from Ukraine).  Orgasmic Victim is not signed to a record label.
