French producer, Feadz, teams up with Australian counterpart and Mad Decent artist, Kito, for a collaborative EP titled Electric Empire. Blending the two eclectic styles, the forthcoming album will offer four songs, accompanied by a cover art with assorted insignias. Releasing through the legendary Ed Banger Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Feadz+&+Kito">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
