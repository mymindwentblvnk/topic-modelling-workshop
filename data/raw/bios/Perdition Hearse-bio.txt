Perdition Hearse was a black metal band from Sarpsborg, Norway. After the split, Stian Occultus founded Shadow Dancers with Rym (guitars), Jontho (drums), Hr. Sander (guitars) (all three then/later/currently in Ragnarok), and Joy D. (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Perdition+Hearse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
