"Liveride" is the Russian atmospheric black metal / ambient one-man project of Roman Barinov.

02/01/2010 - the work on music started. 02/21/2010 - the first album "Tainstvom Lesov" ("Таинством лесов") appeared on the Internet.

Discography:

2. "Tumanami..." ("Туманами...") (2010) (Demo)
3. "Gryozami Nochi, Pechal'ju Dney..." ("Грезами ночи, печалью дней…") (2010) 
4. "Skvoz' Sibir'" ("Сквозь Сибирь") (2011)
