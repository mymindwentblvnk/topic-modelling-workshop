Deviated Instinct formed in 1984 and were a crucial pioneering band in the early British punk metal scene. Along with bands like Antisect and Amebix, Deviated Instinct’s music has influenced legions of bands and helped to define the sound we know today as “Crust”.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deviated+Instinct">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
