Slovenian black metal band Magus Noctum was formed in year 2000. Magus Noctum hasn't been on the scene for long but it has already managed to achieve a lot. The guys have released a demo "After Many Aeons" in 2001 and a debut "Guardian Of The Desert" in 2003. Beside that they are one of the few Slovenian metal bands known also outside our borders.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Magus+Noctum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
