Zombified Preachers of Gore was a gore themed band formed in Royal Oak (Michigan, USA) in 1988, who played a mix of death metal and grindcore. The band's members were Richard Bush, Jason Ali and Mel Drosis. In 1990 they self-released five song demo tape entitled "God's Vomit". The band began to gain popularity throughout the underground and in 1991, through they released a second demo tape through Wild Rags Records . Shortly after the group broke up and continued on their own. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zombified+Preachers+of+Gore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
