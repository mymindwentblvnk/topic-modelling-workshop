Untimely Demise is a Metal band from Saskatoon Saskatchewan, Canada. They play a traditional style of metal with influences like Megadeth, Children of Bodom, In Flames, Iced Earth, Judas Priest, Iron Maiden, Metallica, 3 Inches of Blood, Death, Trivium, Pantera, Slayer, Rammstein, At The Gates, Into Eternity, Exodus, Motorhead, Arch Enemy, All That Remains.

In 2009 Untimely Demise released an E.P. called Full Speed Metal. Full Speed Metal was produced by Glen Drover (Ex-King Diamond, Ex-Megadeth, Eidolon and touring-member of Testament when Alex Skolnick is unavailabe).
