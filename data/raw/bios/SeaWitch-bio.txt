The heaviest band in Nashville just so happens to be one of the loudest. Seawitch make no bones about their cannabis inspiration, be it lyrically or sonically. Combining the crescendo-building riffs of Electric Wizard with the epicness of Sleep, Seawitch channel the slowest, sludgiest and most detuned aspects of stoner metal in their monolithic and occasionally psychedelic assault. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Seawitch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
