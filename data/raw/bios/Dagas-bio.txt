Dagas is a project of Osvaldo Quintero from Panama City, Panama. The project was born in 1999 and its music is mainly chillout/downtempo electronic music with a goa/psychedellic touch.  

Quintero started and still plays rock music as guitarist for local bands.  Parallel to playing rock,  he started making experiments with synthetizers and computers influenced by music from Vangelis, Jean Michel-Jarre, Juno Reactor, Pete Namlook, Ozric Tentacles, Bluetech, Shpongle and such.
