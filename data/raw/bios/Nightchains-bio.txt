Nightchains is a Lebanese band playing old school thrash/speed metal with pure aggression and electrifying ferocity! The band features members of other Lebanese extreme metal band Damaar.

"I started a band in 2002 but Nightchains found its way in the summer of 2003.

Many line up changes, countless auditions - no one got fucking balls!!

Until I accidently discovered the uncompromising hell raising high pitch of an old drinking partner. Max Joins Nightchains on the spot in september 2004. "Ecstasy" single is released in dec 2004.

February 2005 Nabil an old friend too decides to take it on drums. "Metal to the Bone" is released on the 3rd of June 2005.

15 June 2005 Nadim (aka Heathen), an old time drinking partner and corruption supporter to the fucking bone joins Nigthchains on Bass.
