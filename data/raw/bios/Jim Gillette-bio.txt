Jim Gillette, born James Gillette  - Nov 10th, 1968. is an American heavy metal vocalist. Originally a member of Tuff. he recorded an EP with them, Knock Yourself Out.  He then became the vocalist of the 80s Heavy Metal band Nitro, known for his over-the-top attitude and ear-shatteringly high vocals. He released a solo album, Proud To Be Loud in 1987. He is currently a bodybuilder and was married to metal queen Lita Ford  from 1994 to 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jim+Gillette">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
