Mad Axeman was a short-lived power metal band from Germany formed in 1983. They released a self-titled album in 1984 and an EP titled "Hell Raiser" in 1985 consisting of a cover of a Sweet track and two originals on Mausoleum records before splitting up.

Line-up:
Uli Dreyer - Vocals (Lightnings)
Norbert Kribus - Guitars (Alyson Hell)
Peter Esser - Guitars
Ulli Grzeschik - Bass (Lightnings, Alyson Hell)
