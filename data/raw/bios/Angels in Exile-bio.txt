Angels In Exile was a thrash metal band formed in Albuquerque, New Mexico in 1984. The band released a demo in 1992 before splitting up.

The name is also used by a Albuquerque, New Mexico based metal band lead by vocalist/guitarist Lion-hell. Not much is known about Lion-hell or whether he was a member of the original Angels In Exile. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angels+In+Exile">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
