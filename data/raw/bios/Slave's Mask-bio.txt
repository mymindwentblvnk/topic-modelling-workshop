Slave’s Mask is Finnish industrial project formed by ambient musician Kaarna (Somnivore) and black metal musician SG.7 (Horna, Prevalent Resistance, Turmion Kätilöt, Baptism (live member)) with goal to craft EBM-influenced dark industrial music with black metal vocals.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slave%27s+Mask">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
