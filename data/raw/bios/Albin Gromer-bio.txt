Albin Oskar Ulemark Gromer, born 13 July 1990 in Malmö, is a Swedish musician, songwriter and producer.

He released his first EP "Hemmagjord" in 2006. Three singles followed in 2011 and Albin won Nöjesguiden Malmö/Lund Award for best music. In February 2012, he released his debut album named simply "Album" which was received well by critics and called a "beginning in the Swedish soul renaissance". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Albin+Gromer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
