Progressive death metal band from Rome, Lazio. Formed in 2009 and currently signed to Casket Records, they have released one full length album "The Green Effect" in 2014. 

Last Prototype are: 

Alessandro Menale Drums, Programming (2009-present)

Francesco Menale Guitars (2009-present)
See also: Eyelash, ex-Mesosphera (live)

Davide Santini Vocals, Bass (2009-present)
