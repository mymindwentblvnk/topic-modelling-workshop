This thrash metal band from Hokkaido started as Nigarobo in 1989 but eventually turned to Negarobo after releasing their third demo in 1996. "Emergency" is their first and only full-length studio album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/NEGAROBO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
