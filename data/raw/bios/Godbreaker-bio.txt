GODBREAKER is a Powerviolence Hardcore Band from Siegen, Germany. They are rehearsing since August 2009  in a reasonably acceptable rehearsal room in Siegen, that they have to share with a few  other bands.
In November 2009  Chris Lutter aka. Mobsy additionally  joined the band as a Shouter. By reason of lack of time  Chris had to leave shortly after.
