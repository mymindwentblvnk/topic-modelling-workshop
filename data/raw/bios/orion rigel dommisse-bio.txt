Providence-based singer/songwriter Orion Rigel Dommisse utilizes her talents on piano, synthesizers, and cello to craft haunting songs. The Virginia native released her debut album, "What I Want from You Is Sweet", on the Language of Stone label on September 25, 2007. 
Her album "Chickens" was released in 2012 on What A Mess! Records.
