Invidia are a melodic death metal band hailing from Brooklyn, New York who formed in 2000.  The band was once named A Madman's Desire but quickly switched to Invidia, which is Spanish for envy/jealousy.  They play melodic death metal with many leans towards thrash metal.  Influences include In Flames, Opeth, Sodom, Soilwork, Exodus and many other bands. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Invidia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
