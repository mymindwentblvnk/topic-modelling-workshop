There are, at least, four artists known as Zara.

1) A female singer/songwriter of pop music based in London. In Spring 2011 Zara Kershaw released her debut 5 track EP "Glass Silhouette"

2) A recording / performance name of Neşe Zara Yılmaz (b. 15 Jan 1976, in Istanbul), a Turkish folk singer & actress.

3) A UK female-fronted Gary Numan covers band
