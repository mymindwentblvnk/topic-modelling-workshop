Crank Lucas (born December 31, 1985) is a multi-talented hip hop artist, rapper, producer, comedian, and video content creator on YouTube and Instagram who also calls himself a "rapducer." He distributes and promotes his work through his company Realitary Records.

Crank Lucas was born in Washington, D.C., on the 31st of December 1985, but he currently resides in DMV California. He was previously known by the name Xquiz. He is from Prince George's County, Maryland and was born into a music family with his cousins, uncles and father all creators of music.
Crank has decided to use his skills to make people laugh. For months Crank, a DMV California resident, has been posting content on YouTube, Twitter, and Instagram about the hilarious truths about various rappers.
He has appeared on the MTV series Ridiculousness.
