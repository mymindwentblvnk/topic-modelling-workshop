A speed/thrash metal-band from Tokyo, Japan. Formed in 1985, they appeared on the split-LP named "Far East Thrash Army - Thrash Live in Savagery" (1989), together with Genoa, Drastic Gunsmith, Shell Shock and Vietnam. The band also released two demo-tapes in 1988 and in 1989. After that they disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Airraid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
