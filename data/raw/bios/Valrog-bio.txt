Valrog is a raw and purposefully unpolished Scottish black metal band.
GuuL and Destroyer, these two carve a hole in the SBM (Scottish Black Metal) underground
Also, the band, along with GuuL, an eponymous solo project are members of the elite Satanik Teilung.
