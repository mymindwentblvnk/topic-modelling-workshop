Apex Null are a progressive death/thrash metal band based in Brisbane, Australia. The band consists of Colin Cadell (Vocals), Sam Alcock (Bass), Geordie Ross-Conley (Guitar), Michael Buhot (Guitar) and Paddy Johnson (Drums).
