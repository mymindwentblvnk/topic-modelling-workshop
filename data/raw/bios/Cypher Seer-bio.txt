Cypher Seer is an American traditional/power metal band formed in 2003 under the name of Malakis Reign. They released their debut album in 2007, with singer Michael Grant currently pulling double duty with another US traditional/power band, Crescent Shield. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cypher+Seer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
