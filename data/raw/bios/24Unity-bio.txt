24Unity is a four piece progressive metal band formed in 1998 in Vancouver, B.C.  The band consists of Roy van den Broek (vocals), Kelly Stodola (drums), MMO (vocals), and Mark Rogers (bass).

They currently offer all the songs from their first two albums free for download on their official website.
