Capitol K aka. Kristian Craig Robinson is a British electronic-musician combining IDM, folktronica and lo-fi indie-pop.
He's founder of the Faith & Industry record label and late discoverer of Patrick Wolf, the first artist to be signed on Robinson's label.

Capitol k's output includes the following full-length albums

Sounds of the Empire (1999 planet µ)
Island Row (2000 planet µ; remastered 2002 XL) 
Happy Happy (2004 Faith & Industry)
Nomad Junk (2005 Faith & Industry)
Notes From Life On The Wire With A Wrecking Ball (2008 Faith & Industry)
