Linked Horizon is a Japanese band formed in 2012 by composer and multi-instrumentalist, Revo, as a sister project to the band Sound Horizon. The group was formed for the purpose of collaboration in various media such as anime and video games.

Linked Horizon made their debut with the EP, ルクセンダルク小紀行, on 22 August 2012. The songs from the EP consisted of vocalized selections from the soundtrack of the game Bravely Default. The group later gained international recognition for performing several theme songs for the anime series Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人).
