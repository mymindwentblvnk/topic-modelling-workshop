Satyros is a German Black Metal band founded in 2005.

The group released the debut album "Satyros", the second album "Riven", the EP "A Day Of Rain" and the anthology "The Dirt of Ages". Furthermore, Satyros contributed to a compilation and a split EP.

The lineup consists of Nihil (guitars, bass, programming), Hiem (guitars, backing vocals) and Cerberus (lead and backing vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satyros">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
