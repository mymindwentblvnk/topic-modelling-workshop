MC Kresha is Kosovo's one of the finest Hip-Hop MC's on the scene representing Tavnik City. He got famous in the international scene with songs like "Lej Flleshat" and the collaboration with Dr.Mic "Lyrical Warfare" was quite successful too. 

After that summer he produced his first solo album named "Patikat e Mija". With songs like "Not U", "Poem", "Lej Flleshat" etc. Later he made an album with Lyrical Son in their studio called P.I.N.T.(perinatinjonitjetrit).
This album had collaborations with the famous group NR, Lav'da, Dr.Mic, Don Arbas and it was listened quite much by his fans.
