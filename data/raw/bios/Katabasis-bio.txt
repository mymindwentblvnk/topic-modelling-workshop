There are three bands called Katabasis: 1. An atmospheric metal band from Australia 2. A death metal band from Virginia. 3. A metalcore band from Birmingham, Alabama.

1. Katabasis combines elements of many disparate forms of metal and music in general, as the band seeks to establish an emotional grounding rather than a sonic one. They released their debut, self-titled album in 2011.

2.Katabasis (Pronounced Kuh Ta Buh Sis) is a Death Metal band from Fairfax, Virginia. They are currently working on their first yet-to-be-titled EP. 

3. Katabasis is a metalcore band from Birmingham, Alabama. They have released two EPs. First is "Dreams of Dying Remedies", released in January of 2018. Their second EP "Bright Lights and Violence" was released in November of 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Katabasis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
