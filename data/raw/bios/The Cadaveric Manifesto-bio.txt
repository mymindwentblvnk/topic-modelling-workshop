The Cadaveric Manifesto is a Progressive Melo-Death/Grind entity from Dover, New Hampshire. They were formed in November of 2007 and have since released a demo. 

Their members include:

Brandon Phinney (Vocals)
Billy Norton (Guitars/Vocals)
John Rogers (Guitars)
Taylor Jones (Bass)
