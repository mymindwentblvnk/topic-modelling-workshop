Alfi Hayati is the ex bassist of "ODIOUS" and Rythm guitarist of "Midnight Symphony". He decided to start this side project in late 2009. Drawing from personal life influences and driven by long-loved musical influences, Hate Field was born. The music is very dynamic, not as dark as other bands Alfi is currently involved with. "Scary Fairy tale" is the first debut album,which will be worldwide released on January the 26th .
