Pith is a collaboration between http://soundsagacity.com label-mates  hautlle  and IP Config  that was formed in late 2010.  The duo focuses on ambient soundscapes.  Their first EP "...and Time Stood Still" was released December 26th, 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
