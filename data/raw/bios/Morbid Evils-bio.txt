Formed in 2014, Finnish trio Morbid Evils are fronted by Rotten Sound vocalist Keijo Niinimaa and their massively downtuned, sludge-baked death metal is a turn towards an abject, inhospitable and mentally eviscerating indictment of the human condition whose trenchfoot-inducing ground was laid by early Swans and Khanate. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+Evils">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
