Santa Cruz, California has given birth to an ungodly musical beast known as Dusted Angel. Formed in 2008, the band features Clifford Dinsmore on vocals, Bill Torgerson on drums, Eric Feiber and Scott Stevens manning the guitars and Eliot Young on bass, Dusted Angel’s members are longtime friends from the days when several of the members rolled together in hardcore henchmen Bl’ast.
