Lowered A.D. is a five piece band from Marion, Illinois who formed in September of 2013.  Lowered A.D. play a killer style of music influenced by hardcore punk, crust, and death metal.  Musically, Lowered A.D. can be said to be influenced by bands such as Young And In The Way, Black Breath, Entombed, Obituary and Xibalba.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lowered+A.D.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
