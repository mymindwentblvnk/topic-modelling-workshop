Seandrea Sledge (born March 28, 1994), better known by her stage name Dreezy, is an American singer, rapper, songwriter, and actress. Her debut album No Hard Feelings was released in 2016, followed by Big Dreez in 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreezy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
