Upon Shadows plays a mix of Black-, Gothic- and Industrial Metal and can be seen as a Avant-garde or Experimental band.

Upon Shadows was fromed in Uruguay by Tamara Picardo and Natalia Arocena in 2002 and in the same year they released their first, self-titled EP, "Upon Shadows". After that they went silent for a long while, but released a demo in 2009, "The Huge Circle Of Time", followed by their full-lenght in 2010, "Melodies in Grey Scale". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Upon+Shadows">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
