Nite Swim are an L.A. based duo comprised of Cary Singer and Aaron Childs. They are both students of The USC Thornton School of Music, and each have multiple musical projects in addition to Nite Swim. They released two songs on SoundCloud in 2015, and maintain a minimal social media presence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/nite+swim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
