The Old Garden Geranium is a melodic death metal band based in Modena, Italy.

The band was formed in summer 2010 and has released their first self-produced EP on November 12, 2011. The EP contains 5 tracks of melodic metal, influenced by bands like Lamb of God, At The Gates, Soilwork, Nevermore and In Flames. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Old+Garden+Geranium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
