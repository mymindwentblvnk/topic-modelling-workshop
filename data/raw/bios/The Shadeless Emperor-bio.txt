"The Shadeless Emperor" was created in late 2009 from rhythm guitarist Chris Mitros, vocalist Ethan Tziokas and lead guitarist Tasos Bebes in Larisa, Greece. One of the basic aspects of the band was to blend many musical influences as well as fantasy lyrical themes with Melodic Death Metal and create something innovating.

In 2011, the band started recording their first demo cd under the name of "Homeland Demo" After two new additions, Tasos Karamichalis in the Bass and his brother Michael Karamichalis on the Drums, the band released the demo in January 2nd and it was self-financed. The band did a couple of lives with great success. And the future lies uknown yet exciting...
