Kas is a Dutch MC/Producer and musicvideo maker from Rotterdam, The Netherlands. He is known for collaborating with other Dutch MC's from Rotterdam like VSOP. He has released 2 albums already ("Spiegel Reflex" in 2004 and "Alles Uit De Kas" in 2006) whom can be bought on his website www.kasonline.nl <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
