Meat Wave is an American punk rock band from Chicago, Illinois, United States. Their music is described as post-something, melancholic and aggressive. The band was formed in 2011 after Truman & His Trophy disbanded. The trio set out to play with a more aggressive tone than they were used to in their previous ensembles.

The original lineup:

Chris Sutter - guitars, vocals;
Joe Gac - bass;
Ryan Wizniak - drums.

Discography:

Meat Wave (2012)
Delusion Moon (2015)
The Incessant (2017)
