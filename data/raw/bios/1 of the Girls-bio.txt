Note: This band, "1 of the Girls," is not to be confused with the Chicago-based Irish folk group "One of the Girls."

1 of the Girls was a Cleveland-based R&B group discovered by Gerald Levert. The group's self-titled album was released in 1993, and featured the single "Do Da What", which only achieved moderate success. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/1+of+the+Girls">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
