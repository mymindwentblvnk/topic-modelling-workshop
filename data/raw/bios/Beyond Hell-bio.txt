Beyond Hell formed in 2009 and recorded a rough demo in a few days. Since then, they have recruited Elektrokutioner (Decrepitaph, Festered, Tombstones, Foreboding, Encoffination etc) to take over drum duties. 

Discography:

Beyond Hell (demo, 2009)
And Evil Crept Through...  (EP, 2010 with Cursed Existence records)
The Sleeper Awakens (full length, 2010 with Dark Descent and Skeleton Plague records)
