French power/progressive metal band created in december 2003 by Willdric Lievin, previously founder and compositor of the french band Fairyland. Vocals are led by the famous Spanish woman singer Elisa C.Martin (ex-Dark Moor and actual singer of Dreamaker). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hamka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
