Insanity Alert is a thrash metal / crossover band from Innsbruck, Tyrol, Austria.  They were formed by Heavy Kevy in 2011.  Heavy Kevy is in a punk band called The Apers.  Insanity Alert consists of Heavy Kevy on vocals, Dave Dave Dave on guitars, Green-T on bass and  Don Melanzani on drums.  They have one demo, one EP and three full-length albums.
