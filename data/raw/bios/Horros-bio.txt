Band was founded in the fall of 2008 as a death metal band but after some changes in the line-up the band found it's own sound fueled by bands such as Nuclear Death Terror, Morbid Angel, Amebix and many others. Horros doesnt use any distinct genre name but rather label themselves as "Morbid Crust", bands core sound is Dark and Gloomy Crust Punk.

Horros released few demos after recording a full-lenght album in 2009, after that very limited tape EP.
First 7" EP was released independently 2011 and a brand new 7" album was released 2013. Band had another guitarist Urho, one of the founding members but band is now a trio:
Elias - Vocals & Guitar
Joonas - Vocals &  Bass
Lassi - Drums
