Chaos Omen was a Black metal band from Stockholm, Sweden. Necromorbus's solo project, focusing on a very elaborated form of black metal, with a lyrical content about satanism, selfdestruction and death. He plays all the instruments and records at his own studio. Necromorbus is the owner of Necromorbus Studios and he also runs the label Next Horizon Records.
