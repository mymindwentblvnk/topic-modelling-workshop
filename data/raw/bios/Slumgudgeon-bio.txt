Slumgudgeon was a finnish speed/grunge metal band formed in 1992 in Lappeenranta, Finland.

Like many finnish speed metal bands in late 80's and early 90's, their music had progressive influences. They released their first mini-cd album "Durga" in 1992 through Subvertic Records. Second album "... And on what grounds?" was released in 1993 and third album "Factoraped" in 1995. Both albums were released through Stupido Records.
