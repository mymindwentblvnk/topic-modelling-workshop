Metalforce is the name used by the German heavy metal band Majesty from 2008 to 2011. The name change was announced on 12 july 2008 at their show on the Magic Circle Festival in Bad Arolsen. They released a single self-titled album before taking back their old name. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metalforce">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
