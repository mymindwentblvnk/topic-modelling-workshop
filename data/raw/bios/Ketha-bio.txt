Ketha is a four piece math-metal band from Rzeszow, Poland. They have released two full lengths, the first being (III - ia) released in the US by famed grindcore label Debello Recordings. The second (2nd Sight) was released by Instant Classic in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ketha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
