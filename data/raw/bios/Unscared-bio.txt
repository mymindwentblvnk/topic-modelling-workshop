UnscareD is a old school orientated german thrash metal band from Heppenheim, Germany. In the year 2003 they released the first demo called "Hell Is Now", wich was limited on 300 pieces. The songs from "Hell Is Now" can be downloaded for free from their website (www.unscared.de). In 2004 they released the second album "Mosh Attack". After this record  the singer called Zottel left the band. His successor is Pino.

Current line up: Pino (vocals), Kai (bass), Dirk (guitar) and Christian (drums)
