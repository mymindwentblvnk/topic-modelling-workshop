Impaled Upon the Mountains is a black metal band from St. John's, Newfoundland that consists of two members, vocalist Kristopher J. Crane (Nemophilist, Acorn to Great Oak, Himmelen, Kijc, Grimacing) and instrumentalist Greg R. Sweetapple (Mistwalker, Glacier Frost, Icefog, Inverted Coffins, Grimacing, Defense Force). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Impaled+Upon+the+Mountains">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
