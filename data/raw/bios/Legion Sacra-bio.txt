Legion Sacra are a Melodic Death/Thrash Metal band from Arequipa, Peru who formed in 2000. They have released two full length album, the first being 'Odes Of Sanity' in 2003, followed by 'Tears In Darkness' which was released in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/LEGION+SACRA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
