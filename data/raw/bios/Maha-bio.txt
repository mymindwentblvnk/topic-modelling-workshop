1.Maha: Egyptian singer that played with various bands, including Salah Ragab’s “Cairo Jazz Band” and released one solo album in 1979 called Orkos. Orkos was re-released in October  2022 by Habibi Funk in Vinyl, CD and digital formats and into streaming services. In the mid 1980’s, Maha decided to leave music behind for a different life.

2.MaHA - is the Ukrainian composer and song writer as well as performer providing the first album "IN MY MIND", a great combination of soft vocal and heavy rock guitar riffs, that can be in common determined as the Alternative rock genre. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
