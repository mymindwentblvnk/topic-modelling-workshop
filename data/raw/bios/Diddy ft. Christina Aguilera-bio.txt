"Tell Me" is the second single off Diddy's 2006 album, Press Play. The song features Christina Aguilera. The single hit radio stations on November 7, 2006. The demo track was used to test Making The Band 3 contestants. The video for "Tell Me" was directed by Erik White. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diddy+ft.+Christina+Aguilera">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
