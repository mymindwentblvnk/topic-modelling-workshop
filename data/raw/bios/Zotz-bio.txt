Zotz is a four piece post-punk/g-beat band from Mexico City, Mexico.
Started from the ashes of Mass Death & Destruction and Dïshelldethterrör.

Current members are:

Angel - drums & vocals
Yair - guitar
Branny - guitar
Alex - bass

Former members:

Rick - guitar
Luis - guitar
