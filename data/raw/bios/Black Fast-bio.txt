Black Fast is a thrash metal band from St. Louis,  Missouri which formed in 2010. They have currently released one EP and three full-lengths. Currently signed to E1 Entertainment. 

Lineup

Trevor Johanson - Guitar 
Aaron Akin - Guitar, Vocals 
Ryan Thompson - Bass
