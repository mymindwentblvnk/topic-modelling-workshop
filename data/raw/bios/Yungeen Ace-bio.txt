Keyenta Bullard (born February 12,1998) professionally known as Yungeen Ace, is an American rapper. He is best known for his songs "Pain", which has received over 51 million views on YouTube. During his career he has released 7 mixtapes, he was born in Chicago, Illinois but during early childhood moved to Jacksonville with his family, and he represents Jacksonville, Florida and is considered a Duval rapper. He has also served prison time, and survived a shooting that left his brother and two close friends dead. This inspired his hit single "Pain" which gave his career a major boost. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yungeen+Ace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
