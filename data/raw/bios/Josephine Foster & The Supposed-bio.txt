With The Supposed (Drummer Rusty Peterson and guitarist/bassist/vocalist Brian Goodman), out-of-time parlor siren Josephine Foster leaves the acoustic mountain folk behind and weaves her ethereal voice into a rock roll tapestry harkening back to the Psychedelic Decade.
