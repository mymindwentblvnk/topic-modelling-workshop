The Chosen is a black metal act from Groningen, The Netherlands. The Chosen was founded in 2000 and are still active today. Their main lyrical themes are Dutch history, war and paganism. 

Current line-up:
Arjan - Guitars
Herr Hermann (Herman Kleinsmit) - Bass (Rune (Hol), Northgate (Hol))
Rudolf J. Nijholt - Vocals (Northgate (Hol), Moloch (Hol))
Arend - Drums

So far, The Chosen has released the following:

- Wolfcross Demo, 2002  
- For the Glory of the Empire Full-length, 2004  
- Get Those Pagan Fires Burning (Promo) EP, 2008  
- Get those fires burning / Wolfcross Best of/Compilation, 2008
