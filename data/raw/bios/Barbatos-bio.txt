Barbatos is the side project of Yasuyuki Suzuki, known for being part of the band Abigail. Both bands play a kind of blackened thrash metal with a punk edge, and both bands come from Japan. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Barbatos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
