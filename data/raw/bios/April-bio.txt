There is more than one artist with this name:

1. APRIL (에이프릴) was a South Korean girl group under DSP Media. They debuted on August 24, 2015, with the mini-album Dreaming. The group is currently composed of six members: Chaekyung (채경), Chaewon (채원), Naeun (나은), Yena (예나), Rachel (레이첼) and Jinsol (진솔). The group's original lineup included Somin (소민; now a member of KARD) and Hyunjoo (현주; later became a member of UNI.T), along with Chaewon, Naeun, Yena and Jinsol. The group officially disbanded on January 28, 2022.
