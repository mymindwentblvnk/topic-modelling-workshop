There is more than one artist with this name.

1) Akron is an Italian progressive rock band formed by EnioNicolini, bassist of The Black. They released two albums: "La signora del buio" in 1999 and "Il tempio di ferro" in 2003.

2) Akron is an electro-industrial band from Greece.
