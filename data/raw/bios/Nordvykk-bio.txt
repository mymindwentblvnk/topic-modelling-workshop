Nordvykk is a German melodic black/pagan metal band which was formed in early 2000. In November 2002 they started recording their first demo, which was released on February 4th 2003 . Feb 6th they started the recordings for the next demo/album, which was released in January 15th 2004 by Wandalen-Distro-Germanien. This album was called "Nordland" and it has been re-released in September 2004.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nordvykk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
