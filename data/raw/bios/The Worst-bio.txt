There is more than one artist with this name:

1) The Worst was a four piece hardcore punk band from New York, USA. They released one 7" EP and a 12" EP (entitled "Expect the Worst") on MUTHA Records, plus another single as the Bad Guys.  

2) 90s psychedelic rock band. They released a selftitled LP and two EPs: "The Creepy Thing" and "Play Tunes From the Tomb".

3) There was also a punk band from Preston, UK, called The Worst.

4) Australian hardcore punk band.
