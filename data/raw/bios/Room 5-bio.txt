Room 5 is a collaboration between Vito Lucente, better known as Junior Jack, a house music producer/DJ, and disco artist Oliver Cheatham.

They achieved #1 in the UK's Top 40 charts and the European Dance Charts with the single "Make Luv", a remake of Cheatham's "(Get Down) Saturday Night", released in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Room+5">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
