There are several artists named Faces Of Death:

1) Grindcore band from Perth, Western Australia, Australia. Released two demos and a full-length (1998) through Ecocentric Records, limited to 500 copies.

2) Gangsta Funk/Rap from Baton Rouge, Louisiana active in the late 90's. Also known as F.O.D. or Facez Of Death. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Faces+of+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
