A prolific punk band from Seattle, Washington, who have released many CD-Rs/7"s/cassettes since 2006 on various labels. They are formed of Ani Ricci on drums, Chris Ando on guitar/vocals and Mark Greshowak on guitar/bass/vocals. They are named after a relatives black cat, whom was named after a defunct car model. Their myspace can be found at http://myspace.com/talbottagora. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Talbot+Tagora">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
