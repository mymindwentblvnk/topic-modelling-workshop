Tension Prophecy is a Progressive Brutal Death Metal band from Tehran, Iran and then Washington, Usa.
Band members are mainly inspired by bands like Cannibal Corpse, Deicide, Morbid Angel, Trauma, Bloodbath, Necrophagist, Dying fetus... Lyrical subjects which draw heavily on horror fiction and horror movies, are highly controversial.
