There are two bands named Demonic Sacrifice:

1) Death metal from Rochester, NY, USA. Released a demo-tape called Enthralled to Realm in 1996 and a full-lenght, Enter the Realm of Pure Darkness, in 1997.

2) Black metal from Lima, Peru, formed in 2003 under the moniker Nuclear War Command, changing it to Demonic Sacrifice in 2004. Released the split Goat Black Metal with Sair Orchestra in 2009 and another, titled Bestial Prayers of the Black Goat, with Tyrants Blood & Maleventum <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demonic+Sacrifice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
