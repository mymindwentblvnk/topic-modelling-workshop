Lapus Dei is a Doom Death Metal band from Chile formed in 2005. Using a violin, flute, clean/grunted male and female vocals the band creates a very melancholic atmosphere. They use lyrics in both English and Spanish. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lapsus+Dei">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
