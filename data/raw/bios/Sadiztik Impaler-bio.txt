Sadiztik Impaler was a black metal band from Singapore. The band was founded in 2004 by ZahWarlock (guitars). Sadiztik Impaler was later joined by HANAEL (bass), Sithrawll  (session guitars), Lord Inferno Aryan (session vocals) and Hellbasher (session drums). Sadiztik Impaler released two demo CDs in 2004, Warriors Ov Satan and Beastial Christ Fornication.

In 2005, all sessionist departed from the project and new members joined: SHALWEND SORATHS (vocals) as permanent member, FYRAUN (guitars) and FAUZZT (drums) as session artists for the debut album.

In 26 Sept 2005, the debut album entitled Sadiztik Syonan-to Supremacy was released. The two session artists FYRAUN and FAUZZT left the band after the album was released.
