There is more than one artist with this name.

1) DDT (or ДДТ in Cyrillic) is a popular Russian rock band founded by its permanent lead singer, Yuri Shevchuk (Юрий Шевчук), in Ufa in 1980.

2) DDT is a RAC band from Italy.

3) DDT was a hardcore punk band from Atlanta, USA.

4) DDT is a pop punk band from Spain.

5) DDT is a Polish death metal band from the early 1990s. They released 2 demos (Dethroned Death and Fineness of Brutality) before calling it a day. There is a compilation CD released in 2010 that includes both demos and an unreleased track.

6) DDT was a Canadian ska pop rock band which put out one album, Urban Observer, before disbanding.

7) DDT is an alias of UK hardcore producer Darwin only used for the DanceDanceRevolution series.

8) DDT is a breakcore/hardcore producer from Spain, his Syndrome of Chaos album was released on the D-TRASH records label in 2009.
