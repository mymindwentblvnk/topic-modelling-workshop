A death metal band that started in the summer of 2004 in Lawrenceburg, Kentucky.  Created To Kill was started by Steve Forbes and Greg Brewer.  They have two demos, one EP, two full-length albums, one split, one video and one live album.  Their lyrical themes include death, war and horror.  They are signed to Pagan Pride Records.
