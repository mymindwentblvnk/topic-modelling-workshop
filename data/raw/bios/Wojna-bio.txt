UK Black metal band formed in 2007, released debut full length 'The Long Awaited Silence' in October 2008. Have released 2 demos and 1 EP. Wojna (pronounced voyna) means 'war' in Polish. The band are currently working on a new EP which is due to be released in the form of a split with Whorethorn in September.
