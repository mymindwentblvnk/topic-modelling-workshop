Dividead is a melodic death metal band from Nice (France), formed in 2006. Their main influences are Children of Bodom, Kalmah, Dark Tranquillity and In Flames. Beyond Death, their debut extended play was released on the 5th of September 2007, and contains five songs and an introduction.

Line-up :

Pierre - vocals
Thibaud - guitars
Julien - guitars
Eric - bass
Guillaume - drums
Fabien - keyboards
