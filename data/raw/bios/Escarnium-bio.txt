Escarnium is a death metal band from Brazil. The band plays an old-school style of death metal with strong brutal death metal influences.

The band currently has one release, entitled "Covered in Decadence", released in 2009. 

Line-Up: 
Victor Elian - Vocals/Guitars
Eucini Santy - Guitars
Gabriel Dantas - Bass/Vocals
