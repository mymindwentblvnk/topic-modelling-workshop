a thrash/death metal band from Ontario, Canada that was formed in 1985 sharing some members with Sacrifice, Slaughter (Can), Infernäl Mäjesty. They released a demo "Unholy Alliance" in 1987 and participated in a Godly records split album "Raging Death" in 1987 alongside Xecutioner, Betrayel, Sadus and R.A.V.A.G.E..  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lethal+Presence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
