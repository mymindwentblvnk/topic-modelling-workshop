Polish heavy/power metal band, formed in 2003 in Skarżysko-Kamienna by guitarists Arek Cieśla and Radek Szymczak.

Current line-up:
Krzysztof Sokołowski - vocals
Arkadiusz Cieśla - guitar
Robert Kazanowski - guitar
Artur Pochwała - bass
Dominik Wójcik - drums
