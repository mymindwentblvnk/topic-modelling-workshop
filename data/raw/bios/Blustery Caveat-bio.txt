Blustery Caveat is a Brutal Death Metal from Larissa, Greece. They formed in 2003 by Nickos Dablias (Vocals) , Zachos Karabasis (Guitar also in, Jackal's Truth, Black Winter (Gre)), Christos Trakas (Bass) and George Trakas (Drums also in,  Sinner Rider, Marathon).

They released a couple of demos before being signed to Revenge Productions which released their debut "Payback in Brutality" in 2008. The album is of which fast, brutal and contians most traits of that of the scene. 

They are considered to have influence from such bands as HATEPLOW, SEVERE TORTURE, HATE ETERNAL and DYING FETUS
