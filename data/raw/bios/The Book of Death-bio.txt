The Book Of Death is a Death/Groove Metal band from Myanmar. Currently the band is back to being active, they released one self-titled demo album in 2007. You can contact the band through their Myspace, run by the guitarist. This demo was re-released by South Korean label BVT Records in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Book+OF+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
