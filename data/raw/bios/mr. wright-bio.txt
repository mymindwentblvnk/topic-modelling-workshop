Mr Wright previously known as Always has been making records off and on for more than 20 years. 
   Recording 2 albums in his former incarnation Mr Wright has made a further 5 long players; The Fancy Man, Star Time, Hello Is Anyone Out There, Metropolitan and the yet to be released Diary of a Fool. Drawing on a wide range of influences from the standards of the 30s/40s to the avant garde of today Mr Wright's greatest stength is his songwriting and low key delivery. 
   In the last year he has been working on a new project The Dreamers involving Sarah Nyberg Pergament, also known from Action Biker.
