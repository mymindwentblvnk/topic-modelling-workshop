Black Metal band from Norway with inspiration from the viking-era.
Formed in 1997 by Massacra and Tundra. 
---
Line-up
Massacra - all instruments and vocals
Tundra - all instruments and vocals

Former member(s)
Vinterfrost - vocal arrangements
---
Discography
"Norgesriket" Demo
"Massacra's Funeral" Demo
"Frostmoon" 7"EP (Oskorei Productions)
Split w/ Ork 7"EP 2000 (Oskorei Productions)
As of 2008, the band reports that they are currently working on a new album.
