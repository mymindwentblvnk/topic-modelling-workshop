There are several bands called as Moshpit:

1.German Nationalist Metalcore

2.  French digital hardcore project founded in 2002 by Julien Josserand (formely known as Chaosbringer and Le Bruit Qui Court) and Yannick Delort. It became a 3-members project in 2003 and a live band in 2005. Their musical influences are very diverse, from Death Metal to Punk-rock, from Drum and Bass to Speedcore. http://www.moshpit.fr/

3. Technical mathcore band from Moscow, RUSSIA. Now this band is also known as equal minds theory

4. Indian djent band which changed it's name to Yonsample.
