Johnny Stimson (born Jonathan Graham Stimson on January 14th, 1989, in Dallas, Texas, USA) is a singer, songwriter, and musician. He is known for hooking lines and catchy melodies. His upbeat, more-than-meets-the-ear songs involve varying instruments- from saxophone to guitar to piccolo.
