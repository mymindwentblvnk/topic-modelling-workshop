There are three bands named Kramp. One is from Turkey and another from Belgrade, Serbia. The Turkish band is hardrock, and the Serbian one is grindcore-death metal with two singers, male and female. The third Kramp is a Spanish heavy metal band, founded in 2010 in Madrid.
