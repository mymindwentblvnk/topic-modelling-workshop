Warlust is a Black/Thrash/Death Metal Combo from East-Hesse.
They were formed in 2012 and released one Demo (Unholy Attack,2014) and one Full-Length
(Morbid Exekution, 2016) via Undercover Records.
On Bandcamp there is also one complete live concert. ( Live Crucifixion MMXVII, 2017)
