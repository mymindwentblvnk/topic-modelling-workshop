2) Argento is a Serbian metal band. There is not much available information about this band. The band appeared on compilation "Forces of Anger", released in 2004, with a track named "Mirror". The last known line-up was: Marjana Nesic (vocals), Petar Popovic (bass), Radivoje Bogdanovic (guitar) and Dejan Ignjatovic (drums). In 2012, the band's account was created on youtube.com, with several songs of this band, however with almost no other information.
