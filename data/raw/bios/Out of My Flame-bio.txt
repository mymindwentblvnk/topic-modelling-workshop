Out Of My Flame is a Death Metal Band from Germany. They were founded in 2007 and released their first demo in 2008. Just a few copies were sold to some friends of the band. In 2010 the first official demo "The Upward Path" was released. They played liveshows all over South Germany and they are currently working on new recordings.

Current members:
Phil (vocals & bass)
Andy (lead guitar)
Franky (guitar)
