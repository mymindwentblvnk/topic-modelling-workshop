Tower of Babel were a Goth band from Dublin that were around from 1986 to 1989. They released two singles, Drown / Bite & Sunrising and recorded a session for the Dave Fanning show in 1988 (which you can hear HERE) before disbanding. 
Tower of Babel have a great flickr page of images which contains pictures of gigs , press coverage etc. and also a soundcloud account which has recordings and demos to download. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tower+of+Babel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
