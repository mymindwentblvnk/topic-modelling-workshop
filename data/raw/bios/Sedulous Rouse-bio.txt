Sedulous Rouse are a 3 piece thrash/death metal band from Adelaide South Australia.

Formed in 2012 with an idea to play heavy progressive thrash and Death metal and in anything in between, Sedulous Rouse aim to make listeners stop and think and envelop themselves in the music. 

Sedulous Rouse in 2013 released their debut CD Seasons of Triumph to widespread acclaim.Having air play in such countries as Australia, Lebanon,USA, Mexico and Ecuador. Also featuring on many compilations including the cover mount cd for Heavy Music magazine in Australia.
