Talbot is an Estonian heavy metal band from Tallinn, Estonia, formed in 2008. The group's most recent album, Magnetism, was released on March 27, 2018.

The duo has toured across Europe, Russian Federation, Australia and Japan, among the likes of Karma to Burn and Ufomammut.
