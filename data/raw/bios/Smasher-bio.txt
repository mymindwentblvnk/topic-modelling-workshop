Smasher hails from East London and has come through the ranks earning his strips through advancing from a DJ (for Knightsz of the round table) to a producer to and MC. Catching a lot of attention in 2005 with his first single that featured 'Casell the beatmaker, Demoltion man, Preshus & Jookie Moondo (formally of East Co.) he formed alliances with Quality control where he became Mic spar with Loudmoth spawning the 2006 burner 'Take my advice', and this is where Smasher took the route to becoming a solo MC in his own right. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Smasher">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
