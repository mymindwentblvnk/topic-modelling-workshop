Горячий Шоколад (Goryachiy Shokolad) is a successful pop / r'n'b girl-group from Ukraine. It's members are Tatyana Reshetnyak, Lyubov Fomenko and Alina Pilipenko. The group released it's first song in 2008 and first album ("Beregi") in 2009.
