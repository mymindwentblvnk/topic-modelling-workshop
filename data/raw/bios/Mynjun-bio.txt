Mynjun was a dark progressive/death metal band that was formed in Stockholm,  Sweden in 2002. The band consisted of Adam (vocals/guitars) and Rickard (guitars) both sharing bass duties and Ronnie (drums).
Mynjun released 2 demos "Commencement" from 2003 and "Receding Strengths" from 2005.
A third demo was scheduled for release in 2007, but the band split-up in October 2007 at the same time introducing their new band Concilium. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mynjun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
