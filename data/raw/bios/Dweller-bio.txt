There are three bands named Dweller

1) Dweller are a deathcore outfit from Livonia, Michigan. They've released an EP entitled 'Cut Off From The Universe' and most recently their first full length 'The Reality Vector'.

2) Dweller is a powerviolence band from Cleveland, Ohio featuring members of Ages. Their debut EP and demo was self-released in 2011.

3) Dweller is an alternative rock band from the suburbs of Aberdeen, Scotland. Formed in 2005 under the name 'allstationsdown', the band went through several line-up changes, which influenced significant change in the writing process and the name was changed to Dweller to suit the more mature approach to songwriting.
