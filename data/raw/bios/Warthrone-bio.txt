WARTHRONE, an orchestral Black / Death Metal project straddling Virginia and Florida, was assembled in early 2005 as a high profile collaboration between the LACERATION, WITCH-HUNT, SANGUINARY and DYING FETUS credited drummer Erik Sayenga, ex-WITCH-HUNT, NECROPSY and SANGUINARY singer Richard Johnson and ex-WITCH-HUNT, WARFAIR and DARK PURITY keyboard player / bassist Kristel. The band recorded five tracks in February at the group's own Full Blast Studios for an EP, 'Ghoststorm Eulogy', on their own imprint Full Blast Records.
