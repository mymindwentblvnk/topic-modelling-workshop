Yo Trane is an enigmatic 23 year old r&b singer/songwriter who was born in Liberville, Gabon, raised in France, and has a love for American music. His alluring voice paired with his comforting lyrics, and hallucinatory production equals a mysterious force to be reckoned with, who truly does make the music speak for himself. Yo Trane learned English as well as American music listening to legends like Tupac, Biggie, and Michael Jackson. He began his career rapping in France along side his producer Kalim. After a few years he started doing singing covers that gave him international exposure. He later released his first EP Late Night Drive which has earned him several major artist co-signs and millions of streams worldwide. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yo+Trane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
