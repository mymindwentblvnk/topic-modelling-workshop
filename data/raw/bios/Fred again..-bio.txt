Fred John Philip Gibson, known professionally as Fred Again (stylised as Fred again..) or simply Fred (stylised as FRED), is a British singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, record producer and remixer. As a producer and songwriter, he is known for the range of artists he has worked with including Stormzy, Ed Sheeran, Burna Boy, Headie One, Halsey, Octavian, The xx, Underworld, Brian Eno and Stefflon Don.
