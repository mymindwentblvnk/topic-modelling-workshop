Atolah is a Psychedelic Doom band formed in Perth, Australia in 2007. Pierre relocated to The Netherlands in 2011 and Leeroy and Zaheer were replaced with Marc and Geert. Influenced by Kyuss, Sleep and similar bands of the genre they deliver a great deal of pure heaviness. 

Lineup: 
Pierre - Bass
Geert - Guitar
Marc - Drums

Former:
Leeroy - Drums 
Zaheer - Guitar
JB - Drums (on "Relics")
Rich - Guitar, Drums

Releases:
Relics EP 2009
