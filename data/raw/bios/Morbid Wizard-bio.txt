Morbid Wizard is a sludge/doom band from Cleveland, Ohio. In 2011 they have self-released a debut CD album Lord of the Rats that comes in DVD case with large artwork by Scott Stearns. Lord of the Rats title track can be listened on Land of Smiles soundcloud.
Band members are not new to the scene, they are known from other acts such as Fistula, Accept Death, Sollubi and numerous others.

Bahb - Guitar
Stearns - Guitar
Duncan - Bass
Jesse - Vocals (vokills on DVD case)
