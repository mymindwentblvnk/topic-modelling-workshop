Kataphero is a Melodic Death Metal band from Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil formed in 2009.
Their first debut album was released in 2012 under the title ''Life'', and can be downloaded for free on their website. The band was responsible for opening Rotting Christ's gig in Rio Grande do Norte in 2012.
