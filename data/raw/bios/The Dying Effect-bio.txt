The Dying Effect are a metal band from Sylvan Lake Alberta. Their debut release "Bleed The Night" (Torque Recordings), Features twin vocal attack, raging guitars, and pumelling drums together characterizing this metal/screamo offering. Breakneck verses dissolve into huge towering choruses, and whiplash back and continue on. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Dying+Effect">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
