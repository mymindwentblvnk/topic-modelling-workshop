Hovig Demirjian (Armenian: Հովիկ Դեմիրճյան; in Western Armenian Յովիկ Տէմիրճեան, Greek: Χοβίκ Ντεμιρτζιάν; born 3 January 1989), also known by the mononym Hovig, is a Cypriot singer. He represented Cyprus in the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 with the song "Gravity", finishing in 21st place. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hovig">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
