Ad Baculum is a Black Metal band from Brazil.

The band is the one-man project of Meugninuosoan Hades (ex-vocalist of legendary Brazilian band Mystifier (Wicca)).

Meugninuosoan plays all instruments except drums, Renato Corpse handled drums on debut full-length album Blackness Doctrine released 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ad+Baculum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
