1.) Milestone was a side project of Milo Aukerman while attending college in San Diego (between stints with the Descendents).  They had one album.

2.) Milestone is a punk rock band from Long Beach, CA established in 2004 that plays catchy songs in the style of bands like Face to Face, Social Distortion, and Rise Against.

3) Milestone are an Irish band based around Bradford in West Yorkshire, UK.

4) Milestone is an Indie Rock band from Niskayuna, NY. The five members interests include golf and oil.
