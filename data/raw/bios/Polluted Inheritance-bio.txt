Polluted Inheritance was a Technical Death Metal band from Holland. They formed in the year 1989 and have officially announced their split up in the month of August, 2008. During this time they have released two demos and three full length albums.

Discography - 

1991 - AfterLife (Demo)
1992 - Ecocide 
1994 - Demo
1996 - Betrayed
2001 - Into Darkness
