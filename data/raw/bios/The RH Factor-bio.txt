The RH Factor is a progressive jazz group that combines elements of soul, jazz, funk, and hip-hop. It is led by jazz trumpeter Roy Hargrove and features many popular neo-soul and alternative hip hop artists. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+RH+Factor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
