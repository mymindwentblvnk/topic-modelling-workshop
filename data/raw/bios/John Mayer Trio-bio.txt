The John Mayer Trio is the brainchild of pop sensation John Mayer, who, in 2005, decided to move his music in a new direction teaming up with Steve Jordan,  and Pino Palladino, pursuing a more blues-influenced style and paying homage to the great musicians of his childhood days. In October 2005, they toured as the opening act for The Rolling Stones on some dates. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/John+Mayer+Trio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
