You Follow Me is a collaborative studio album by American singer-songwriter Nina Nastasia  and Australian drummer Jim White. It was released in 2007 by UK record label Fat Cat Records.

You Follow Me is Nastasia's second release through Fat Cat, and her fifth career album. White is the regular drummer for Australian instrumental rock trio The Dirty Three and has long been associated with artists such as Will Oldham, The Boxhead Ensemble, Bill Callahan and Nick Cave, but You Follow Me is the first album to bear his own name. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nina+Nastasia+&+Jim+White">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
