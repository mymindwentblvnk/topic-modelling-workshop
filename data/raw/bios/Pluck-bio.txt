Pluck is the name of a duo formed in New York City in 2011 featuring Wu Fei (guzheng) & Gyan Riley (guitar), who were introduced to each other by John Zorn.

In previous projects, the artists have also collaborated with musicians such as Zakir Hussain, Bela Fleck, Fred Frith, John Zorn, and Terry Riley.
