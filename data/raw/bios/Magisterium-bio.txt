MagisteriuM appeared in Siberian Omsk, Russia, in 2002. Its music is a mix of melody and aggression, modern tendencies and old school sound. It is based on the fusion of Stockholm death school, American thrash, academic female vocals and piano arrangements. The lyrics are mainly describe the humanity’s problems and achievements throughout different époques. The band is positively accepted by the spectacle-hungry audience as it performs expressively with inexhaustible sense of humor, grand improvisation and priceless singing skill. Those effervescent fellows are always looking for new ideas, aims and inspiration. Day by day, they work hard but with real jam only to please the ears and minds of true art connoisseurs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Magisterium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
