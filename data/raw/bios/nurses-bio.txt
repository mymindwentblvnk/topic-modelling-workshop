Nurses is an experimental pop duo, made of childhood friends and longtime collaborators Aaron Chapman and John Bowers.  

The group released released Apple's Acre with indie label Dead Oceans on August 4, 2009, as well as the 2011 follow-up Dracula, touring with The Tallest Man On Earth and Stephen Malkmus and the Jicks. The song "You Lookin' Twice" from Dracula was sampled for a cypher by A$AP Mob  for Pitchfork's Selector series.
