Ritual Carnage is a thrash metal band originating from Japan. Their frontman and vocalist Dan Montgomery comes from the USA. The current line-up is: 

Dan Montgomery - Vocals, Bass Guitars, Guitars
Wataru Yamada - Guitars
Eddy Van Koide - Guitars
Naoya Hamaii - Drums

Discography:

1998 - The Highest Law
2000 - Every Nerve Alive
2002 - The Birth of Tragedy
2005 - I, Infidel

All albums were released by Osmose Productions (France). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ritual+Carnage">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
