Morthona is a Death/Thrash Metal band from Schererville, Indiana.  They were previously named Snuffleupagus, but later changed it to Morthona.  They released one album entitled ...Where all Dividing Lines Become a Blur... in 1996.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MORTHONA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
