a death metal band from Pennsylvania, US that was formed in 1995. Released two demos, "Algonquin War Metal" in 1997 and "Return of the Thunderers" in 1999, and a full length titled "Open Season" in 2000. 

"Mi'gauss" word refers to an ancient form of total war practiced by the Middle-Algonquin warriors.
