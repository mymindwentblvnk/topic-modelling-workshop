Krayzie Bone (born Anthony Henderson on June 17, 1974 and also known as Leatha Face) is a member of the seminal hip-hop group, Bone Thugs-N-Harmony. He, like his other group members, is known for his tongue-twisting rapid pace of delivery and his ability to create harmonic hooks within songs. He is the most laid back, versatile, and soft spoken member of Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, letting his work show for itself. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Krayzie+Bone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
