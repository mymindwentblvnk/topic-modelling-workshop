1.)

Issachar were a black metal band from the United States. They released one demo, Medieval Death Hymns, in 1994. Also extant were two other works, a demo titled Wolves in the Winter, which was never formally released, and Dead, a promo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Issachar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
