The band was formed in Hungary, Miskolc in July, 2005. They played punk music in the beginning but it changed to lyrical rock/metal when the members of the band got stabilized. Their 1st album called "Csak nevess" and it has 12 songs what has been recorded in April, 2006. The 2nd release has been recorded in June/July, 2007 in the rehearsal of the band and it has 5 songs. Their 3rd release has been recorded in 2008 and it called "A világ színpadán". There is 4 songs on it. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Otila">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
