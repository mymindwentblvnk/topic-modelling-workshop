Veneror is an italian black metal band from Italy. They formed in 2008.they have released a demo "Nightwandering..." in 2011 and will be releasing their debut album "Percussimus Foedus cum Morte" in early 2013 via New Era Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veneror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
