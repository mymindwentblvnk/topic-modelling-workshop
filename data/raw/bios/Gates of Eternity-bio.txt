The band has been founded by Seckin and Soykan on 18th of January’05 in Izmir. Our band which has been playing with various members has performed many concerts mainly in Izmir and in different cities like Istanbul,Eskisehir,Manisa,Usak. The first demo “Storm Behind the Gate” has been released in May. Besides “Live Chaos Compilation” DVD includes a live video clip of our band. After some changes in the members of the band, the new demo “New Level” has been released. The band continues live performances and studio works. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gates+Of+Eternity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
