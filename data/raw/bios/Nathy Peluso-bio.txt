Nathy Peluso, is an Argentine singer, songwriter, dancer and pedagogue. Born in Argentina, and raised in Spain, Peluso became interested in the performing arts at an early age, performing cover songs at musical bars in her teenage years. After graduating King Juan Carlos University, Peluso relocated to Barcelona to pursue a professional career in music, with her first releases, Esmeralda (2017) and La Sandunguera (2018), being confected independently. Peluso slowly started to become recognized in Spain. After signing with Sony Music, Peluso started working on her breakthrough album Calambre (2020), for which she won the Latin Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nathy+Peluso">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
