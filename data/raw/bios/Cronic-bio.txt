CRONIC (Oradea - Romania) started back in October 2006 lining up: Ionut Covaciu - guitar, Bejusca "Beju" Andrei - guitar, Iulian "Vampy" Milcomete - bass, Cristi "Slater" Bordei - drums.

    In 2007 the line-up changed: Robert "Robi" Brindus - vocals, and Andrei Bejusca has left the band.

    The year 2008 starts with another change. A new drummer: Vlad Tuta.

    The promo material is entitled “Behind Reality”, recorded at No Stress Records in August 2008.
