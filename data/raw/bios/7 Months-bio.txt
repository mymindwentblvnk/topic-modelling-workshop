7 MONTHS are a progressive metal band from Simi Valley, Ca (USA). Although the band was formed in 1995, it was not until 2002 that the band released their debut album '7 months'. The band at that time consisted of Joe Booe on lead vocals, Chris M Jacobson on guitars and vocals, Garegin "Greg" Kalajian on keyboards and vocals, Shawn "The Dude" Richkind on bass and Barry Magnuson on drums and percussion. 
Joe Booe left the band and was replaced by the 'european' vocalist Danny Veras. 
In 2009  the band released their second album "In Time".
