Widow's Walk is a progressive rock and metal band from Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Their original music blends the melodic, intricate trademarks of classic progressive rock with the hard edge and agression of modern progressive metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Widow%27s+Walk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
