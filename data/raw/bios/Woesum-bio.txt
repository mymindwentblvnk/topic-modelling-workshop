Woesum is a trap and experimental club producer from Stockholm, Sweden. He is an associate of Drain Gang and Sadboys, producing and co-producing tracks for the two groups. His 2021 debut Blue Summer features their members predominantly. Since 2018 he has regularly produced for YAYOYANOH, who also features on several songs from Blue Summer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Woesum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
