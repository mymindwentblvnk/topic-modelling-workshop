There are two Australian artists using the name Airling:

1. Airling is a pseudonym of Brisbane singer Hannah Shepherd, formerly of the band Charlie Mayfair.
http://www.facebook.com/Airling.  Who has collaborated with #1 Dads & Japanese Wallpaper.

2. Airling is a shoegaze project by Melbourne artist Lachie Mathison from the band Lunaire.
