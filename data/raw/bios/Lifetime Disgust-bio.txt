Lifetime Disgust is a Depressive Black Metal band from Italy, formed in 2008 by murian. Currently, the band has two releases, an EP called "Voices, Whispers, Echoes of Absolute Emptiness" which has been released by Beneath the Fog Productions in 2009 and a split tape with Aegrotationis released by Bleichmonde Tonschmiede in 2010.

Line-up
murian - all instruments and vocals
