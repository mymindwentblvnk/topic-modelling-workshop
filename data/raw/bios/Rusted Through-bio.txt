RustedThrough, is A Hard Rock/Metal band based out of West Palm Beach, Fl.

Formed in mid 2007, this band is all about ranging from smooth choruses to heavy thrash like verses with lead guitar driven instrumental sections to tie it all together to form an extreme sound like never heard before!

Rusted Through's latest release "Fe2O3" with its dynamic instrumentals and heavy melodic grooves has a sound for every hard rock/ Metal fan!  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rusted+Through">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
