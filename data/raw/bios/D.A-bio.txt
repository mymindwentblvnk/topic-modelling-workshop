D.A is on the verge of becoming one of Hip Hop's greatest minds. Since realeasing his debut album in 2002, he has made a name for himself on both the american and international underground scenes by collaborating with various artists on projects from across the globe. With connections and fans worldwide, it's only a matter of time before this artist/producer takes the mainstream market by storm. D.A's lyrical style is deeply versatile, combining a fresh mix of poetry and classic hip hop flow while his beats are laced with influences from all genres of music. The Houston, Tx. native credits this to being a "music fan first, artist second" and he is definitely a star on the rise.
