BOARCORPSE is a metal band from Boston, formed at the end of 2008.  They mix elements of death, thrash and black in a mathy style that doesn't fall short on energetic grooves. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Boarcorpse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
