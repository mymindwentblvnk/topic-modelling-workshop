Vae Tertium is a black metal band from Germany that was formed somewhere in 2006 and during the same year they released their first demo, "Reverentia". After that they went silent for a while, but in 2011 they released a new EP with the title "Zweitausendzehn". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vae+Tertium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
