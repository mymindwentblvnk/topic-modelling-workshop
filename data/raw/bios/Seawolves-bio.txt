Seawolves is a swiss band founded in 2004 by Roman Friedrich and Sebastian Jehle. The musical influences of the Band is clearly Swedish Death Metal, mainly melodic but also some relations to Stockholm Old School Bands can be found. The lyrics follow the tradition of viking bands such as Amon Amarth, Thyrfing and Bathory.
