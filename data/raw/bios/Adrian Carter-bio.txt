Adrian Carter is a Sheffield based electronic musician/producer, and has collaborated with US slam poet Daphne Gottlieb, along with Anne Garner, Kate Vale and Pixieguts for the '1000 Rubies' EP. This led to remixes for Anne (Sheffield/London),Titee & Pixieguts (Australia) and Radio Scotvoid (US) and a collaborative track with Penny Broadhurst (Leeds), most recent endeavours have involved plenty of Pixieguts recuts/remixes, currently involved in remixes for Jane Silence/Yo Yo Statix/Jillian Ann. 

EP's are available courtesy of net label Earth Monkey Productions in Cumbria (Magnetic.Gangs,1000 Rubies,Switchkicker and contributions to the Falling and 50:50 compilations).
