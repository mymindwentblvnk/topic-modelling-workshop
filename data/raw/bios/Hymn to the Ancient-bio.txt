Hymn to the Ancient are a doom metal band that were formed in Gothenburg, Sweden in Spring 2003 by former-members of the Candlemass tribute band Ancient Dreams: guitarist Eric Rauti, drummer/vocalist Tomas Eriksson (ex-Runemagic) and bassist Mats Rendlert. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hymn+to+the+Ancient">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
