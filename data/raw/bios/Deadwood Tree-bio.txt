Formed in 2014, Deadwood Tree is a 5-piece sludge, post-metal band from Palma, Spain. They released their debut EP titled "Sovereign" in February 2017. It is available at https://deadwoodtree.bandcamp.com For more information, visit: https://www.facebook.com/deadwoodtree/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deadwood+Tree">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
