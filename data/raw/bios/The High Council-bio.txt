Hailing from the beating heart of the Evergreen State, Seattle, The High Council explores the boundaries of melody and groove. With a blend of transcendent improvisation and earthy rock sensibilities, THC draws on elements of jazz, reggae, rock and psychedelia. Fronted by guitar gurus Zeke Wakefield and Adrian Moore and riding the rolling thunder of drummer Conor Sisk, keyboardist Ryan Standley and percussionist Jeff Pang. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+High+Council">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
