Jasmine Thompson (born 8 November, 2000) is a singer from London, United Kingdom. Jasmine is of half English half Chinese descent. Her acoustic reworking of Chaka Khan's Ain't Nobody made #32 on the UK Singles Chart after being featured in an advert for supermarket chain Sainsbury's for their by Sainsbury's range. She plays the piano and guitar, and dances. Her first radio play was in August 2013 on Edith Bowman’s Radio 1 Review Show in which Jasmine won the weekly national online vote “This is my Jam”. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jasmine+Thompson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
