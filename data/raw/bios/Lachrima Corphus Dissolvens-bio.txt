Doom/Death metal band from La Paz, Bolivia. Active since 2005 and currently signed to Demon of the Sex Productions, have released one full length album. "The truth is out there" in 2009. 

Lachrima Corphus Dissolvens are:

Beyson Q.                 Guitars (2005-present)
Jhoel Vempyrioth Keyboards (2005-present)
Antonio Ortiz         Vocals, Choirs (2005-present)
