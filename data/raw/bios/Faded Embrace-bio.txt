A Metal/Hardcore hybrid act from Freeport, IL. Faded Embrace were formed in October of 2005, and suffered some minor lineup changes early on in the writing process. The finalized line-up is Jon Sanches on Vocals, Dan Eaton on Lead guitar, Keith Romanek on Rythm guitar, Matt "Bobo" Bowen on bass, and Mike Vittorio on drums. The five-piece recorded a 5 song EP in April 2006 and released it on Break the Surface Records on May 20 of the same year. 
Faded Embrace has played with the likes of: As I Lay Dying, Trivium, CKY, Machine Head, Gwar, Jungle Rot, Ion Dissonance, Caliban, Terror, The Black Dahlia Murder, and Sworn Enemy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Faded+Embrace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
