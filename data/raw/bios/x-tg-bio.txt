Formed from the remains of Throbbing Gristle in November 2010, UK and disbanded a matter of weeks later in late November 2010 due to the untimely passing of Peter Christopherson.

Line-up featured Chris Carter, Peter "Sleazy" Christopherson and Cosey Fanni Tutti
