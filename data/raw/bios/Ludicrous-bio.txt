Ludicrous is a Finnish industrial metal band formed around 2001 in Helsinki, Uusimaa, Finland.

The band has released 3 demos so far, the first demo being symphonic black metal and the rest two demos being industrial metal.

Band members:
Father Ludicrous (vocals, guitar)
Super Data Satan (synths, drum programming)
Tritech (bass)

Discography:
December Remnants (demo) [2007]
Bridges and Hills - The Blowtorch Chronicles (demo) [2008]
Promo 2011 [2011]

All their demos can be downloaded on mikseri.net. Their latest release, Promo 2011, can be downloaded in high quality on their SoundCloud profile too.
