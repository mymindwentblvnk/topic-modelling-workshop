Minushuman is a French atmospheric thrash metal band from Bergerac (24, Dordogne). In September 2007, on the ashes of Dark Poetry, Minushuman starts working on original compositions by Thomas Billerey. Finally in May 2008 their  first 9-track album 'Watch The World Die' was released. In August 2011 their second album "Bloodthrone" saw the light of day. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Minushuman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
