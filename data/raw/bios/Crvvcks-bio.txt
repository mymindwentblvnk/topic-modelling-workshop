Crvvcks (pronounced Crookes) is a 29 year old DJ/producer based in Leeds from Nottingham who has found fans in BBC Radio 1, Rinse FM and Amazing Radio.  His music is a perfect mix of high energy upbeat dance tracks with a nod to classic House and Soul and downbeat chill out electronica.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crvvcks">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
