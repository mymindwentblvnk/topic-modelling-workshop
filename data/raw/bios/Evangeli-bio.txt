Following release of a 2004 session, 'Favourable Winds Blow Us Far Towards An End' and then one single and a music video before evolving into Evangeli in 2005. They feature the line-up of vocalist / guitarist Hvergelmer, bassist Stefan, violinist / keyboard player Eldhrimnir and Elgareth on drums. Evangeli's first release is a live DVD/CD called "...in Sin". Evangeli unfortunately split-up in 2007.
