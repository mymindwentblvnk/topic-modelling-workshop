Archenemy (not to be confused with Arch Enemy from Sweden) were an underground death/thrash metal band from Los Angeles who released two rehearsal tapes in 1985 and 1986.

Both have been reissued by Hells Headbangers Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ArchEnemy">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
