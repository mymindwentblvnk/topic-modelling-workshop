Sour Milk Theorem is an American Melodic Death/Doom Metal influenced music project started by Ian Goetchius in St Marys County, Maryland in 2007. Signed to the net labels Kitty On Fire Records and Torn Flesh Records, Sour Milk Theorem has relesed two demos, two EPs, and a full length and is currently recording the next full length titled "A Failing Heart". Influences include Insomnium, Swallow the Sun, Amorphis, Before the Dawn, and Ghost Brigade to name a few... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sour+Milk+Theorem">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
