There are two bands named "Argile":
1. A French Heavy metal band. Side project of Misanthrope's members S.A.S de l'Argilière and Jean-Jacques Moréac. They released their first album in 2002 and the new one called Monumental Monolith in 2010.

2. A French prog rock band of the '70s.

3. A German Afro-Jazz-Rock-Fusion Band founded 1988 by Djembe-player Barry Sangare from Mali and German flutist Dieter Weberpals. They published so far 5 CDs and toured all over Europe and as well in Westafrica. They also firmed under the name "Argile & African Heat" in the beginning <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Argile">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
