1. Aggravated Assault - American RAC/Hatecore band from the mean streets of Atlantic City, New Jersey. 

US hatecore band founded 1991 in Atlantic City, New Jersey. Members have played in other bands, most notably Chaos 88. They've recently reformed in 2012 and are planning a bunch of new shows! It could happen to you!
