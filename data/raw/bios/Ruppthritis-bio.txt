Started in January of 07 Rupp has been gigging and writing songs steadily. With influences that vary between metal, blues, jazz, and every genre in between Rupp has developed a sound that can't be totally placed in any one category. The soul of the band derives from unwillingness to conform, strong friendship, the love for music and the undying passion for riding vintage minibikes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ruppthritis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
