Hiidenhauta is a black metal band from Satakunta, Finland. The band was founded in early 2012 by Fornjotur and Riena. Ihtirieckos, Gastjäle, Marras and Rostiof joined the band in the summer of 2012.

Hiidenhauta's lyrics are written in old Finnish "kalevalametre".

Members:

Fornjotur - Vocals 
Riena - Vocals 
Ihtirieckos - Bass 
Gastjäle - Keyboards 
Marras - Guitars
