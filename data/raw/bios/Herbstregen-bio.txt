Herbstregen is One Man Band Depressive Black Metal  From Germany (Friedberg, Bavaria)  in 2009 Lyrical theme(s) Nature, Autumn, Loneliness,The Man behind this is Inferno - All Instruments and Vocals.
Herbstregen also appears on the compilation "Lieder von Lieb' und Leid" (released by Bleichmond Tonschmiede on tape/100 copies) with the song "Life Failed".

Herbstregen is german for "Autumn Rain".
