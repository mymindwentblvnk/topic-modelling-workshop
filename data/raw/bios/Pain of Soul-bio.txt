Pain of Soul is a doom metal band formed in 2000, in Blumenau, Brazil.

Line-up:
Daniele : Vocais, Flute
Joel Sebold : Vocais, Guitarra
Peter : Guitarra
Felipe : Baixo
Luiz : Bateria

Discography:
2002 - Morpheus (Demo)   
2004 - Live in Pain (Demo) 
2007 - Scarsick (CD)
2011 - The Cold Lament (CD)
2013 - The Rustle of the Leaves (CD)
