Anphisbenah began as a project in 1996 with Carlos Formigueri and Ricardo Lopes. Recorded their first demo in 1998 Entitled "Initiates the Horrendous", later in 2003 recorded their first CD entitled "Infinite Mutable Fundamental Form".
