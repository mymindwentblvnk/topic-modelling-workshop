Soul Demise are a Melodic Death Metal band from Neumarkt, Bavaria in Germany who got together in 1998. They released their first (and only so far) EP in 1998 called 'Farewell To The Flesh'. The debut album 'Beyond Human Perception' was released in 2000, followed by the 'In Vain' album in 2005. The next album release came in 2005 when 'Blind' was released. After a 4 year break, the 2009 album 'Acts Of Hate' was released.In 2010 they released Sindustry. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Demise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
