A Fonds Perdu is a gothic metal band formed in Germany in 1996. 

The current line-up is:

Andreas Morak - Vocals, Guitar
Christoph Suppan - Keyboards
Gronald Mario - Guitar
Thomas Morak - Bass
Michael Gfrerer - Drums

Their first album 2000 Years was released in 1996.
