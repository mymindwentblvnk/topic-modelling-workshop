Ramone Johnson, better known by his stage name Cashis (often stylized as Ca$his), is an American rapper who was born and raised in Chicago, but moved to Irvine, California. He was signed to Eminem's Shady Records from 2005 to 2011. He was featured on the Shady album  Eminem Presents: The Re-Up  and released the  County Hound EP  in 2007. 

In 2011 he was dropped from Shady Records without ever releasing an album.
Anyway, 2012 will see the birth of his long awaited "The Art Of Dying".
