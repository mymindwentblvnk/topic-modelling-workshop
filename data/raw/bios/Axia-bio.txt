The AXIA is a hard rock band out of Central California. With their die hard fans and local radio support they have consistently performed at sold out shows. The AXIA offers a new sound that's been long awaited by listeners everywhere. Recently they have shared the stage with such acts as; TED NUGENT, DROWNING POOL, FILTER, L.A GUNS, NONPOINT, THE BULLET BOYS, DEVON ALLMOND and HIS HONEY TRIBE, and PEOPLE IN PLANES. Eager to spread their music to a rapidly expanding fan base.

Life's sorrows and victories, expressed through their influential music, The AXIA is an experience that no person should be left without...
