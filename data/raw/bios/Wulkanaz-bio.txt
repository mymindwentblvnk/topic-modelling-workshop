Wulkanaz is a project that was created by Kumulonimbus (Wagner Ödegård, Tomhet, Semilanceata, Dughpa, Felon Wind, Dragsjukan, Jaertecken) in Mora, Sweden in 2009. Lyrics are mainly about Dark Germanic heathenism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wulkanaz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
