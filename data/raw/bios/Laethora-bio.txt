Laethora is a death metal band founded in Gothenburg, Sweden, in 2005. Lead by guitarist N.Sundin (Dark Tranquillity), the band also consists of the three members of swedish gothic metal band The Provenance and vocalist J.Nordentsam. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Laethora">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
