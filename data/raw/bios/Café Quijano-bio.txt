Café Quijano is a Spanish Pop group. It is made up of three brothers: Manuel, Óscar, and Raúl, all of whom are signers. They live in León where, in the center of the city they manage the club "La Lola" with their father. Besides having published 4 cd' s with great success, they have collaborated with Disney for soundtracks and have been in promotional photos of Castilla y León for tourism in the region.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Caf%C3%A9+Quijano">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
