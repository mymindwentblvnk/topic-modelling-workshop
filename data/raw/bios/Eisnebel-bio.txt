Eisnebel is a black metal band from Hagenow, Germany
Currently the band consists of only one Member, J.N., who is in charge of all instrumental and lyrical work.
In March 2009, Eisnebel released it's first Demo, entitled "Victoria Immortalitatis"
In spring 2010 the second Demo will be released, which is entitled "Anthems of the rising AntiSun".
