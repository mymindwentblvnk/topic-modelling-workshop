There is more than one artist with this name.

1) Millennium were Polish musical electronic music group and live act active from 1993 to 2002, recorded three records: "Teraz Polska" (in English: "Poland now"), "Self" and "Entropy". Their music was also part of E=mc2 movie soundtrack.

2) Spanish well known macrodisco club/discotheque created in 1998. Is situated in Sils (Girona-Catalonia). The kind of music of this club is Dance and Italodance music and is releasing songs every 1-2 years, also does many shows and gig's of well known artists like: Kate Ryan, Gabry Ponte, Prezioso ft. Marvin, Dj Ross, Victor Ark, Cascada, Basshunter.

3) Millennium were a Gothic Metal group from Almada, Portugal. They formed in 1995 and released a demo in 1997 called "Wilderness in White". They split up shortly after.

4) Millennium, metal band in the Indian Music scenario.
