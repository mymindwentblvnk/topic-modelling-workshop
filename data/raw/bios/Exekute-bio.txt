Exekute is a 5- piece death metal band from Sydney, Australia. Despite only being established for a short time, the band has released an EP and has supported various international bands.

The band was founded in August 2011, by George and Elias. The band was fully complete with the services of Fred, Adrian and Jay. June 2012 saw Exekute sign with Grindhead Records.

April 2013 saw Exekute release their debut EP "Doomsday Mourning", which was mastered by James Murphy. June 2013 saw Exekute support party thrashers MUNICIPAL WASTE. October has also seen the band support Italy's HOUR OF PENANCE as well as BEHEMOTH. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Exekute">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
