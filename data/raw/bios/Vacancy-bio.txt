Vacancy is a Groove Metal band from Finland, Kouvola.
Their debut EP, "The New Dawn" released in 2012.
In 2015 they released one EP, called "Ghosts". It contains 4 new songs.
Vacancy is: Niko Tuominen - Guitar 
Aleksi Holopainen - Bass
 Jonne Kuoppamaa - Drums 
Antsa Lehtinen - Vocals 
Samuel Lindroos - Guitar/Lead
