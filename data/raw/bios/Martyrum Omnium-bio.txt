Martyrum Omnium is one man Black Metal From Spain (Valencia, Valencian Community)
the man behind this band is Nocturn - 4-6 Strings, Vocals, Drums, Keyboard (Voice of Hate, Acriter (Esp), Profundis Tenebrarum).

Lyrical Themes Mostly talk About  Suicide, Misanthropy, Anti-Christian

Martyrum Omnium was created in November 2.001. There are recorded four demos: -Soul Wandering -Human Darkness -Prison -War Code For the moment only are aviable "Soul Wandering", "Human Darkness" and "War Code". More new material soon .
