A music producer hailing from New Orleans, Phelian is swiftly becoming renowned for his ethereal soundscapes. His distinctive Ambient Future Garage sound sees powerful deep bass amalgamate with haunting melodies and soulful vocal allegories where melancholy and ecstasy elegantly coalesce. The multi-instrumentalist intelligently crafts a sonic elixir that is captivating, evocative, soothing- spiritual.
