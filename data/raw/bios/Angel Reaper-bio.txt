Angel Reaper is Hungarian Thrash/Black Metal group, formed in 1987.

Last known line-up:

Szabó Gyula - Guitars, Vocals
Biró Gábor - Guitar
Szeghalmi Tibor - Bass
Dudás István - Drums.

Discography:

Demo I 1987
Végzet Utolér (demo) 1989
Exhumált Világ (demo)1992
