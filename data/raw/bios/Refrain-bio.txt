Refrain is at least 3 bands:

1. Refrain was a heavy metal band from Kuopio, Finland and was formed in 2004. In 2005 they released a demo "Psychosis" and in 2006 EP "Justice for the Undead". Their activity ended in 2007 - 2008. 
In 2017 band had their final comeback show in Ottopoika, Kuopio.
