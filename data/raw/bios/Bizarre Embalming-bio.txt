Bizarre Embalming is deathgrind / goregrind from the Czech Republic.  The band formed in 2001 and has had one full-length CD, titled Necrosadistic Surgery. They're not longer active. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bizarre+Embalming">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
