Extreme Masturbation were a Spanish death metal/grindcore band formed in 1991. They released a demo through Drowned Productions in 1992 titled "Sex Deviousness in Mind". In 1993 they put out a second demo by themselves titled "Sadomasokismo". They also released a live cassette in 1992 and a live performance on VHS in 1993. The band had members of Ephemeral Seed and Spontaneous Combustion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Extreme+Masturbation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
