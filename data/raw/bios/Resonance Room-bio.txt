Resonance Room (formerly known as Fragment) is an Italian progressive / gothic metal band, formed in 2008 in Catania, Sicily, Italy.
The band released its debut album, "Unspoken", in September 2008 via My Kingdom Music. Katatonia, Pain of Salvation, Anathema, Porcupine Tree and Riverside are just some of the many influences that can be heard on the album. According to a press release, the group "succeeds in mixing a heavy prog vein with dark gothic atmospheres and haunting melodies".
