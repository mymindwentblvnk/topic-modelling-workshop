Painful Depression is an 4 piece american depressive black metal band. They have only one release so far, "Keeping the Pain Alive". Line-up is Kold (Lost Inside, Unending Hatred, The Eternal Night, Wilted, Means to an End, Wounds, ex-Hanging Garden), Marco (Ah Ciliz, Meaning of Life, Nostalgie, ex-Hanging Garden, ex-Secretly in Pain, Looking Down, ex-Black Hate, ex-Cold Wind), Elmer (Ah Ciliz, Wintersad) and Maur (Unending Hatred, Suicidal Anorexia). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Painful+Depression">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
