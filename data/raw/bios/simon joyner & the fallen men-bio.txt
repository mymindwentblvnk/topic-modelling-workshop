Simon Joyner and the Fallen Men is an Omaha-based group dating to the early 1990's. Joyner is often considered to be the founder of the Omaha music scene, and both Conor Oberst of Bright Eyes and Beck consider him and his work to be major influences upon their music.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Simon+Joyner+&+The+Fallen+Men">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
