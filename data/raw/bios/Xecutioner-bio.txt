Early incarnation of Obituary. They released one demo under this name in 1986, which featured "Find the Arise" and "Like the Dead." Both These tracks appear on the 1997 reissue of 'Slowly We Rot,' and the former was rerecorded for 'Cause of Death' in 1990. They also appeared on 1987's 'Raging Death' compilation. Xecutioner released a second demo in 1987, which included "Enter the Gate of Hell," "Fight to the Death," and "Like the Dead." The next year, they changed their name to Obituary and went on to become one of the leaders of the death metal scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xecutioner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
