Gardland is Sydney producers Alex Murray & Mark Smith. The duo craft humanly flawed analogue techno with the spirit of spontaneity and rawness that their improvised live shows are renowned for. Smith and Murray are the co-directors of Hunter Gatherer, a Sydney-based techno label.
