Segunda is a Dutch progressive rock band. Members include Mark Volmer (vocals), Mark Visser (guitar), Menno Thomas (bass), Joost Schrijver (keyboards) and BertJan Hobbelink (drums). 

The band combines elements of progressive rock/metal, classic and melodic rock.
Segunda are still largely undiscovered, though the 2003 demo 'Mirror' received positive critiques from the Dutch music press. 
Their latest release, Deus Ex Machina is an EP-demo that is due to be released 3/2007.
Deus Ex Machina is Segunda's first take on creating a concept album. Due to limited budgets the story on Deus Ex Machina is not yet complete, so keep posted for more new material throughout 2007 !
