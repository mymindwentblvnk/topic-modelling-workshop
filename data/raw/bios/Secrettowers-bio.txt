SecretTowerS from Italy started as an Iron Maiden tribute band. In 2010 they released a demo
 Insanity Fair with their own music, although they still play some Maiden covers at their shows. Changing lead singers in 2012 the bassist and backing vocalist Marco Antonio Sergi became lead singer.
