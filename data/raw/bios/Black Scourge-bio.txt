Black Scourge is an experimental/ambient black metal band from south Orange County, California started in the summer of 2008. They play a range of black metal influenced widely from various bands and styles such as Burzum, Wolves In The Throne Room, Summoning, and Nachtmystium. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Scourge">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
