Silk Rhodes is a two-piece soul group that has been recently signed to Stones Throw Records. So far the only material available from the group is the track Face 2 Face that has been featured on the Our Vinyl Weighs A Ton soundtrack. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Silk+Rhodes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
