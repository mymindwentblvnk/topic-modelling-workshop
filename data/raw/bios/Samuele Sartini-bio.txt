Dj & producer, Samuel Sartini is among the few still to push ahead with its genuine style, the movement called "soulfull house." Universally known by that name, just Soufull House, this kind has always been synonymous with high quality both in the choice of sounds that partners such as Soul Boy (Wendy Lewis) and with their voices make the difference to complete the projects.
