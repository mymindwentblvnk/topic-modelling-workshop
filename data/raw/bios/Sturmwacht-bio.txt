"Sturmwacht" was a Black-n-Roll / Blackened Metal project based in Graz (Austria) consisting of "Laschke" on drums and René Müller on guitars/vocals. The band released one EP ("First Strike", 2016) and one full length record ("Four Arms of Vishnu", 2017) and played a few live gigs before disbanding. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sturmwacht">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
