Neetzach is a Black metal band from Sandnes, Norway. It was founded in 1993 by Gidim Xul. He released 1 demo - Pinseltronen - in 1995. Then was dormant until Vrangsinn (Carpathian Forest) and Dirge Rep (ex-Enslaved) joined in 2005. On the 6th day of the 6th month 2006, they released True Servants of Satan - The bands first full-length. It has a unique sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Neetzach">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
