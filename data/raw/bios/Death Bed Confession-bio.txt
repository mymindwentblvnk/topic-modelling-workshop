D.B.C. is a band from the Denver, CO USA. Playing their own brand of Metal and Hard Rock. Forming in 2010 the band has had it's share of successes with the release of their debut album in January 2011. The band is currently working on new music for it second release. The current members, David Groover on vocals, James Chandler and Brian David on Guitar, Paul Henry on Bass and Dingo Holtz on the drums are getting ready to go into the studio.
