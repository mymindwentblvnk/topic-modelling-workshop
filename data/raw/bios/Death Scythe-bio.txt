Death Scythe is a melodic death metal band found in Merida/Yucatan, Mexico in 2004. Picking out death, suffering and pain as central theme they released one demo named "Hollow Darkness" in 2006.
Current line-up:
Gerardo Mendoza - Vocals
Roberto "Tito" Cervera - Guitars
Alexis Castelan - Guitars
Rafael Barreiro - Bass
Mario Gonzalez - Drums
