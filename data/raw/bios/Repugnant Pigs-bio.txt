Repugnant Pigs were a death metal/grindcore act from Spain formed in 1991 whose members were from Maresme, Barcelona, Catalonia. The band only released one demo-tape in 1991, Kraneal Infection (aesthetically spelt "Infektion" on the cover) before splitting up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Repugnant+Pigs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
