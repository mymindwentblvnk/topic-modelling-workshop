Evoken is a funeral doom metal band from Lyndhurst, New Jersey, USA. The band was founded by guitarist Nick Orlando and bassist Rob Robichaud under the name Funereus in April of 1992 but it wasn't until 1994 that they became Evoken. The band name Evoken is taken from a Thergothon song found on Fhtagn-nagh Yog-Sothoth.
