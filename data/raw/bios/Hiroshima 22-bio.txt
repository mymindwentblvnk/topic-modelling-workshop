Hiroshima 22 were a 90's grindcore band formed in Brooklyn, New York in USA.  They played a old school style of grindcore incorporating influences from old Napalm Death and Sore Throat. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hiroshima+22">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
