30 years old music producer, born in Italy and transplanted in the UK. Constantly trying different ways to merge the world of videogame sounds with disco, synthpop and funk. Extremely prolific, RoBKTA collaborated with various labels and artists around the globe, alternating live activity and producing new material, sometimes putting new spin on glorious game franchises like Sonic The Hedgehog, or pushing it further in delivering bouncy originals packing blue funky energy, factor that led one of his compositions to end up even on Billboard. Affiliations of the artist include GameChops, Tiny Waves, DESKPOP, GamerDisco and Business Casual. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/RoBKTA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
