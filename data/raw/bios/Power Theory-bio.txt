Power Theory is an American Heavy/ Power Metal band from Tullytown, Pennsylvania. the band uses various elements of metal such as Bay Area Thrash Metal, Classic Heavy Metal, and a little bit Traditional Heavy Metal influence in their music. Achim Kohler is the front man of mastering the music in the band. they signed in to Pure Steel Records and released two albums until 2013. "An Axe To Grind" and "Out Of Ashes, Into the Fire...And Other Tales Of Insanity".

Current Line-up is:
Bob "BB" Ballinger: Guitar and Back Vocals
Dave Santini: Lead Vocals
Jason "J-Bone": Bass and Back Vocals
Chris Smith: Drums
