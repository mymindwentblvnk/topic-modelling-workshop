Dirty Lyxx is a female-fronted, four-piece hard rock/heavy metal band from Boston, Massachusetts comprised of lead vocalist Lilly Vasquez, guitarist-songwriter Alika Madis, bassist-vocalist Stephen Baker and drummer-vocalist Dan Whitelock.  Dirty Lyxx is a modern band with a strong connection to the classic hard rock and heavy metal of the 1970s - 1980s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dirty+Lyxx">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
