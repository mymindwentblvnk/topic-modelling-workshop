Collar (commonly stylised as COLLAR) is a Hong Kong Cantopop girl group formed from Hong Kong broadcaster ViuTV's reality talent show King Maker IV in 2021. The group consists of eight members: Sumling Li (Sumling), Garie Shum (Gao), Natalie So (So Ching), Winka Chan (Winka), Ivy So (Ivy), Day Hui (Day), Marife Yau (Marf), and Candy Wong (Candy). They debuted on 12 January 2022, with the first single "Call My Name!". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/collar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
