Los Angeles-based singer, songwriter, and visual artist Ella Vos emerged in early 2016, lending her voice to "Rolling Dice," a glitchy EDM track by Australian producer Just a Gent. When the song racked up a hefty amount of spins on SoundCloud, listeners took notice of Vos' winsome voice and soon flocked to her site upon the release of her debut single, "White Noise," which arrived later that year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ella+Vos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
