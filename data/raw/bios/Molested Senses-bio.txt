Molested Senses was a death metal band from USA. They formed in 1990 and split-up in 2003.They composed of  Dan Boone (Vocals), Skidda (Guitars), Bill Lailer (Guitars), , Doug Caron(Bass) and Nota (Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Molested+Senses">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
