Festered is an old school death metal band from USA.

They have released their debut album "Flesh Perversion" via Razorback Records. There sound is that of mid-1980s and early 1990s death metal and thrash metal bands.

The band takes influences from bands like AUTOPSY, POSSESSED, SACRIFICE, MASTER, early DEATH, SARCOFAGO, BLASPHEMY, PUNGENT STENCH, SODOM, as well as 70's and 80's horror and classic comic book style artwork.
