Dictionnaire Infernal is a Black Metal band from Ostrov in Czech Republic,formaly found as Death Metal band Monastery Death in 1995.In 1997 they changed their style to black metal with a new name Dictionnaire Infernal,band made two studio albums and played many live concerts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dictionnaire+infernal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
