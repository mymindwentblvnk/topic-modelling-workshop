Discidium are a Groove Metal/Thrash band from Randolph, New York USA.  The band strongly believe in bringing melody back into the American metal scene as they feel that it has been lost, with new bands discarding melody in their music.  

Band Members:

Mike Buvoltz: Guitar and lead vocals
James Evans: Guitar
Darren See: Drums and backing vocals
Aaron Grover: Bass guitar
Brandon Hawley: Keyboards
