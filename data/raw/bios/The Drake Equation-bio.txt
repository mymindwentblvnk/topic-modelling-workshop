The Drake Equation was a progressive metalcore act from Västerås, Sweden, founded in 2009, dissolved in 2016.

Members:

Adam Janzi - Drums
Dennis ''Kare'' Andersson - Vocals
Anton Svensson - Bass, Vocals
Mattias Tiselius - Guitar, Vocals
