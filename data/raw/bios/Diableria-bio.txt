Diableria are a gothic metal band from Italy, with lyrical themes focusing on dark romance and passion. They released a 3 track, self-financed CD in 1998 (Arte Funebre) and a full length album (Cupae Rosae) in 1999. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diableria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
