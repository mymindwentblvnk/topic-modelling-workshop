There is more than one artist with this name.

1.Dreamweaver aka Jim Granlund is an experienced trance artist, he has a very diverse style and a wide range of influences. Having his roots in the early synth/EBM/electroindustrial sound, he was instantly touched by the energy and complex techniques of trance music in the mid-90's. Dreamweaver's sound is cosmic trance of the space age, filled with drifting sounds and complex soundscapes.
