Gentle Robot (real name Ibrahim Imdad Sheikh) is a solo artist from Lahore, Pakistan, and is associated with acts such as Takatak, Keeray Makoray, Janoobi Khargosh, Ali Suhail and Poor Rich Boy. He writes songs about his personal life experiences and uses music as an emotional outlet. He strongly believes that honest self expression through music connects with people in the most genuine way, and hopes his songs have a positive and sincere impact on his listeners. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gentle+Robot">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
