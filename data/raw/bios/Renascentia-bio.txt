Renascentia is a Russian band which combines elements of funeral doom, gothic and melancholic black. The idea of creating the band originated in the spring of 2008 and the first rehearsals took place in 2009. New members joined the band in 2010. So far, they have recorded a split with Ankhagram, called "The Gardens Of Soulless".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Renascentia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
