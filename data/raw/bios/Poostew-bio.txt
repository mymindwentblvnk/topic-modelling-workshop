Poostew was founded in 2003 by vocalist Christian Veltmann (former member of Hatemate), guitarist Paul Haus and drummer Guido Panrek (former member of Vlad Tepez). Later on Thomas Schindler joined on second guitar and Tim Boersting joined on bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Poostew">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
