There are two artists that share the name Death of Kings:

1) Death of Kings is a hip-hop group that consist of three members: Dvorska Luda (Bar, Crna Gora) - MC/producer, Darzee (Bar, Crna Gora) - MC and Diego the General (Chicago, USA) - MC. They have recently released "Testament EP", which is available for free download at Rotacija.net.
