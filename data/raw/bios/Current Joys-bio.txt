Nick Rattigan, also known as Current Joys and TELE/VISIONS, is an American singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, vocalist, journalist and music video director, originating from Reno, Nevada. He currently releases music under the moniker Current Joys. He also is the drummer and lead singer of the indie surf rock band Surf Curse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Current+Joys">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
