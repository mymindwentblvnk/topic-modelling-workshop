Hellnight is a Thrash'n'Roll band from Haugesund, Norway. Hellnight was founded by Gravlund (ex-Sorgsvart) back in 2005. The band has released two demos so far. You can hear they have been influenced by Running Wild, though the theme is set to be more "horror".
