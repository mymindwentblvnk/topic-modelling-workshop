2 piece black metal band formed in 2014.

The cassette "Solens Kraft" was recorded at Råværket in two sessions in the winter of 2014/2015. 
And was later realeased in 31 copys, on the danish lable Moral Defeat in 2015. 

The album "Skygger" was recorded in 3 days at Råværket.
It was released 29th of August 2017.

Kultleder is still active.
