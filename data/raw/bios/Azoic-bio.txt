1) Azoic is a black metal band from Iceland. It was originally founded as a two-piece by Benedikt Natanael Bjarnason (Offerings, Beneath) and Ragnar Sverrisson (Atrum, Beneath). Today they are a four-piece having added Sigurgeir (Atrum) and Daniel Mani Konradsson (Ophidian I, Malignant Mist) on bass and guitar respectively. They self-released their debut album Gateways in 2012.

2) Azoic is a hardcore band with progressive metal influences. Originally formed in Bandung, Indonesia.
