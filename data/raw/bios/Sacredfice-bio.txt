Sacredfice is the one-man metal project of Francesco Romeggini from Pistoia, Italy. He plays a style of melodic thrash metal and produced a single full-length eponymous album in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SacredFice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
