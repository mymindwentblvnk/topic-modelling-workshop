LENORE is a 5-piece metalcore band from Denver, Colorado that released two albums -- 'The Ever So Fancy' and 'Welcome Home Traitors' -- before disbanding. 

Lenore is also a singer/songwriter from Ontario, Canada.  Songs from her third album, "Another Life", released in 2007, are available on last.fm.  Previous albums include "Summer Dancing" (2002) and her self-titled debut (1997).  More information is available at her website at www.lenore.com

The name is also in use by several other bands, such as a progressive metal band from Pennsylvania who have a new CD to be named.
