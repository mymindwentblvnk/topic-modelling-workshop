This is a project formed by J.A. of Decayed. In 1998 the debut demo ‘Spectral Thorns of Torment’ was released, followed in 1999 by ‘Towards the Astral Twilight’. 

The band signed for Hiberica and released the debut CD, ‘Weakening Divinity’ in 2003.

The year 2013 saw their glorious comeback with the album "Born of Fire", released on CD by Hoth Records, and on MC by ᛉdnirgal Prods. and Puro Ruido.
