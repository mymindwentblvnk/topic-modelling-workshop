Tim 'Mitrumothy' Wolvetang is a young guitarist from the Netherlands. He basically makes Heavy / neoclassical metal.

Tim was born on November 10th 1986, and started playing guitar just after he turned 18. He produced his first CD, Breaking Strings EP in December 2005. 
Tim currently lives in Amsterdam and studies Musicology at the University of Amsterdam.
