Cortez Inc. started in the beginning of 2004. They are a norwegian metal/thrash/hardcore band put together of five 17/18 year old members that had their first encounter with metal trough bands like; Metallica,System of a down, Megadeth, Iron Maiden & Slayer just to name a few. The band just finished their first norwegian tour and got really good reviews and responce and are currently working on their next tour.
