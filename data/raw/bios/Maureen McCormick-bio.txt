Maureen Denise McCormick (born August 5, 1956) is an American actress, reality show participant and recording artist. The naturally blonde-headed McCormick is widely known as a child actor for her role as Marcia Brady, the oldest daughter in the 1970s television series The Brady Bunch, (1969 to 1974). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maureen+McCormick">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
