Demonhammer assembled in the winter of 1999, Under the Sign of Armageddon by guitarist /vocalist "Necrodemon" and drummer "Blaspherion". After appearing in their first live show in 2000, the band performed select shows in the following years such as S.O.T.N.C., and others . In 2006 Demonhammer recorded a demo titled "Barbaric Supremacy". Very few were made and mostly handed out at shows. 2010 brought the "Apocalyptic Doomkult Metal" 7 inch which contained two exclusive tracks only available directly through the band.
