Cryostasium(originally The Abhorrer)is a one-man band started by Strep Cunt in 1999. The music is best described as a mix of Black Metal, Industrial, Ambient, and Hardcore Punk.  The most recent Cryostasium release is the full length-album "Stalwart"\. The  discography to date includes:
