Death/Doom Metal from Athens, Greece, formed in 1996 and featuring members of Disharmony, Dream Devoid and Darkest Oath. Released a one-track promo tape in 1997 (In Tears I Follow) and a demo-tape in 1998 (Visions of Passion and Lust). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grace+of+Despair">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
