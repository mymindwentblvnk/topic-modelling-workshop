WirgHata was formed in the summer of 2008 in Krasnoyarsk, Russia as a true black metal oriented 4-piece formation. In 2009 the band released their first demo and soon after that were disbanded. In the end of 2009 two initial members decided to find new musicians and start over within the same name, adding keyboards to their music. Band's music transformed into a mix of symphonic black metal and melodic death metal. First gigs were played in 2010. During next years WirgHata played in a few other russian cities. In 2011 new demo record were released. In 2012 band opened for Kataklysm, Marduk and Vader in their hometown, played at Metal Hail open-air and started recording of their debut album. In 2013 single "Shuttered Room" from the upcoming album was released.
