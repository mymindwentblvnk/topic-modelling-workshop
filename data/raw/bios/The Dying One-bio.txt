The Dying One is a death metal band founded in 2005 in Bonn,  Germany.

The bandmembers are:

Christian Steffes - guitar, vocals
Thomas Reimann - guitar, background vocals
Jana Maue - bass, background vocals
Nils Rau - drums

Discography:

2006 - dream of an undead
