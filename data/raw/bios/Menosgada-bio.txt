Menosgada is a German viking folk metal band from Tübingen in Baden-Württemberg. They formed in 2004 and are not signed under any label. Their lyrics usually center around Nordic mythology. The current line-up is: Michael Koch (Guitars and Arrangements), Sebastian Schulz (Vocals), Simon Spark (Bass) and Ole Fahnick (Drums). They released one demo titled Mittsommer in 2007. Menosgada was the name of a Celtic colony named by the Greek cartographer Claudius Ptolemäus. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Menosgada">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
