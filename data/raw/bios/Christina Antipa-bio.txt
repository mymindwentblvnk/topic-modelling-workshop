Christina Antipa (born in California) is an American folk americana singer-songwriter based in in Portland, OR.

First trained as an oboist in a family of artists, Antipa had been singing and writing songs since her early teens. Her work is said to be sometimes reminiscent of Chan Marshall or Tara Jane O'Neil. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Christina+Antipa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
