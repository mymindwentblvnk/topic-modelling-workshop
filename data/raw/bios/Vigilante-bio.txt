There are six artists called Vigilante: 



(1) NYHC style Hardcore/Punk band from Sydney, Australia. 2010 "Cavity creeps"

(2) Electronic/Industrial Metal band from Chile formed in 2004.

(3) Progressive/Power Metal band from Japan that was formed in 1988.

(4) Thrash Metal band from Norway formed in 1988. (split up)

(5) Jesse Taub's alias, a video games remixer; strong contributor to OCReMix (since 2003)

(6) Hardcore from Belleville, New Jersey featuring Gutter (founder of Gutter Christ Productions) on vocals.
