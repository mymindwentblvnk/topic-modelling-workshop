Iron Rainbow is a heavy/power metal band from Middle Island, New York. They were created in 1993 and released many demos and split's with bands such as Acrimony. 

Lineup:
Jeff Scott Anderson  - Vocals
Rob Angel - Guitars
"Iron" Brian  - Drums
