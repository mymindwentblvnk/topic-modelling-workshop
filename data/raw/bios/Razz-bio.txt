1. Razz were an American punk band that put out at least four 7" singles between 1977 and 1979. One song can be heard on the Hyped to Death Vol 1 compilation. Members: Michael Reidy - vocals / Abaad Behram - guitar / Bill Craig - guitar / Ted Nicely - bass, vocals / Doug Tull - drums. Later singles list the band as (the) Razz with the word the in parentheses.

2. The Razz were a Japanese punk band that released an 8" flexi disk in 1984

3. Razz is a young Danish hip-hop artist who was also the winner of the 2002 Eurojunior Contest.

4. Razz is a Christian jazz/fusion band from the early-mid 80s.
