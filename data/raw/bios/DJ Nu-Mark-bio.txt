DJ Nu-Mark is a hip-hop DJ mostly known for his membership of Jurassic 5, an American now only five-piece hip-hop crew with a DJ and four MCs, the other DJ used to be Cut Chemist. He is also a renowned hip-hop producer and DJ.

His discography includes all four Jurassic 5 album releases (Jurassic 5 LP, Quality Control, Power in Numbers and Feedback), a Sequence Session Mixtape CD entitled Hands On and his most recent project, collaborating with Pomo to create Blendcrafters. He also collaborated on several tracks of the popular "Mushroom Jazz" series. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Nu-Mark">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
