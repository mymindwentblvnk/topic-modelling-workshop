Abstürzende Brieftauben (word play on "Einstürzende Neubauten"; German for "Falling Carrier Pigeons") was a German fun punk band, lasting from 1983 to 1997 (one single reunion concert in 2002). Forming members were Konrad Kittner and Mirco Bogumil; Oliver Rosthal joined the band in 1993.
