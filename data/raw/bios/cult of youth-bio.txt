Cult of Youth is a neofolk band hailing from Brooklyn, New York, United States, formed in 2007. The band delivers their own style of ominous folk with martial industrial flourishes and the vindicative spirit of raw punk to keep the blood pumping.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cult+of+Youth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
