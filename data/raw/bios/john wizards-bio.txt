South African duo from Cape Town making the sound of seven virtuosos from across a wide variety of styles both traditional and electronic. John Withers (music), Emmanuel Nzaramba (vocals), with live assistance from Geoff Brink, Tom Parker, John Withers, Alex Montgomery and Raphael Segerman. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/John+Wizards">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
