(1) THE FORCE is a Drum and Bass Producer & DJ from the UK signed exclusively to the DSTYLE camp. Over the last few years he’s been releasing on the camp's STEREOTYPE label, with releases including ‘The Story’, 'Something Out There', 'Dropzone', 'What You've All Been Waiting For’, ‘War Games’, ‘Dub 2 Africa’ and ‘Keep Rockin’. THE FORCE now releases on his own imprint Rebel Bass Recordings.

(2) The hardcore punk band The Force featured Hunter Burgan of AFI, Matt Wedgely of Viva Hate and The Circus Tents, Chad Cox (also of The Circus Tents), and Mark Roustabout. They played in and around The East Bay, California area from 1995 to 1998, disbanding after Hunter began to play in AFI full-time. They played a reunion show and released a Complete DIscography in 2008.
