There are multiple artists using this name:

1) The Witch aka Witch (We Intend to Cause Havoc) was a psychedelic rock / zamrock band formed in 1971 in Kitwe, Zambia. This five-piece electric band played a strong electric African underground style. Tons of wah wah-fuzz guitar in every track, African style beats and vocals and all original songs.
