Real Names: Georgette "Sweet" Franklin & Lori "Lo" Esteen

RnB duo with a twist of Hip-Hop, Sweet N Lo' released their single "40 Dog" on Third Stone Records in 1993, before releasing their only album "Pucker Up" in the same year. 

Georgette "Sweet" Franklin was last involved in song writing and (along with several other people) penned the massively successful hit for Jennifer Lopez "Love Don't Cost A Thing" in 2000. 

Lori "Lo" Esteen went into group management after the release of Sweet N Lo's album.
