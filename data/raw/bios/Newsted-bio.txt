Newsted is an American metal band founded by Jason Newsted (Metallica, Flotsam And Jetsam, Voivod, Papa Wheelie, Echobrain, Sexoturica, Ozzy Osbourne, Spastic Children, Rock Star Supernova, WhoCares). They released their first EP, "Metal", in January 2013 and their first album "Heavy Metal Music" in August 2013.

Band members: 

Jason Newsted (Lead Vocals, Bass)
Jesus Mendez Jr. (Drums)
Jessie Farnsworth (Guitars, Backing Vocals)
