Alexander Tsihov (September 10, 1982), better known under the alias Smokey Mo - is a Russian rapper from St. Petersburg. He participated in the project "Wind in the Head" (1999). The project lasted about a year, then he released the album "Senorita. Later, he became one of the members of the group "Dynasty Dee". He launched a solo career around 2003.
