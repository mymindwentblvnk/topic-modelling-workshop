Büyük Ev Ablukada is a Turkish band.

The band was formed in 2008 in Cihangir, İstanbul by Canavar Banavar and Afrodisman Salihins, who recorded at home and released their music on line. Büyük Ev Ablukada has  staged numerous concerts, and they released first album 'Full Faça'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/B%C3%BCy%C3%BCk+Ev+Ablukada">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
