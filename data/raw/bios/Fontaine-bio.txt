There are multiple artists called Fontaine. 

1. Fontaine is an alias of Alina Blanco who was also one half of electro duo Promise Circle with Raul Rodriguez.

2. Fontaine is John Thornfield, an ambient and drone producer from Bristol, United Kingdom. 

3. Fontaine, born Fontaine Weyman in 1979, is an American singer, songwriter and guitarist. She self-released her debut album, The Chemistry Between Us, in 2006. She has shared a stage with Paula Cole, performed at Lilith Fair, and provided back-up vocals for Rob Zombie and Josh Brown. Long Way Home, from The Chemistry Between Us, is featured in the film "All In", starring Michael Madsen, Dominique Swain and Louis Gosset Jr. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fontaine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
