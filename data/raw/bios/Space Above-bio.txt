Aaron Short (of New Zealand indie-electro crew The Naked And Famous) has been working on a new music project called Space Above, in collaboration with fellow NZ musician Sam McCarthy.  Expansive, synth-driven electronic music that is both intriguing and hypnotic.  The group has self-described their sound as “space tech.” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Space+Above">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
