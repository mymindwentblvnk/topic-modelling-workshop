INFERIA, PURE PRODUCT OF FINNISH GRINDCUNTROL SINCE 1989.

Inferia is a Porn/Goregrind (with Death Metal influences) band from Lahti, Finland. They have been around since 1989 and they have released 4 demos, 3 MCD/EP's, 1 live album, 1 7" split and 7 full-length albums.

Their seventh full-length album Grind Cumpaign was released in May 2022.

Current line-up: Jani Huttunen (Vocals), Esko Tikkala (Guitars), Tatu Hämäläinen (Bass) and Kalle Lindfors (Drums).
