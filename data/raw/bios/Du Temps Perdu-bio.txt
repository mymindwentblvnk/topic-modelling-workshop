Du Temps Perdu is an Ambient Black Metal band from Irapuato, Guanajuato, Mexico that formed in 2010. To date they have only released three songs that appeared on the split entitled Humo, Cenizas y Muerte. The four-way split was released in October 2010 and features Astarot, Lux Funestus and Neftaraka. Du Temps Perdu have recently changed their name to Abysmal Depths.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Du+Temps+Perdu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
