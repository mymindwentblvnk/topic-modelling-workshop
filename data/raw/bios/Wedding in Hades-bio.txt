The band was founded in late 2006 in Saint-Brieuc, France by: 
S.Toutain : Bass & Vocals 
V.Lahaeye : Drums 
O.Raoult : Keyboards 
D.Simon : Guitars 

A couple of years after the release of their first self-titled demo, Wedding in Hades signs a record deal with the famous russian Doom Label SOLITUDE PRODUCTIONS/BADMOODMAN MUSIC including the release, the promotion and a worldwide distribution of their debut full-length album "Elements of Disorder" (out in february 2010) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wedding+in+Hades">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
