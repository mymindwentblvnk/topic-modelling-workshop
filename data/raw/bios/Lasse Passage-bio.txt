In 2005 Lasse Passage decided to drop his career at the music conservatory in favor for a simple life as a traveler. By living this plain life of traveling, a coherent acoustic focus began to flourish. 5 continents and 2 years later he came back with a handful beautiful songs in his luggage. Lasse Passage debut album "If you don´t have time to cook, you don´t have time to live" was released 15. of February 2009 on his own label.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lasse+Passage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
