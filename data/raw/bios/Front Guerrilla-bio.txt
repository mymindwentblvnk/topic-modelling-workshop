Heavy/speed metal act from Yokosuka, Tokyo, Japan. Formed in 1986, Front Guerrilla was probably best known for having bassist Kannon "Cloudy" Masuo (G.I.S.M.) in the band. Front Guerrilla released a self-titled demo tape and a vinyl EP in '86, entitled "Fight Back", on Water-Land records. Currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Front+Guerrilla">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
