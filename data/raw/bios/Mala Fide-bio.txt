There are several bands with the this name. One is a 3 piece screamo band hailing from Salem, Oregon. Fuses such artists as Circle Takes The Square, Fear Before The March Of Flames, and HeavyHeavyLowLow.

Another one is a black metal band from Denmark. Mala Fide is the winter project of the main members of Apollyon and Brandpest. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mala+Fide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
