X-box Murder (Named after a murder of six people) was formed in the summer of 2006 in Kharkiv, Ukraine.
After release of 2-songs demo tape and single "Inner Voice", in 2012 X-box Murder released their first self-titled EP. 
Second EP "In The First Circle" was released in the end of March 2014.

The band's music blackened is atmospheric death metal in the vein of Celeste, Gojira, Behemoth, Hierophant etc. that combine fast blastbeats and death-metal riffs with melodic black metal solos and dirty/noisy atmospheric sound.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/X-box+Murder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
