Funeral of God or (FOG.) for short, is a black metal band based out of Ohio (USA Founded by Demogorgon in February of 2014, Creating sonic terror for Lucifer. The EP "Skull Fucking Jehovah's Weakness" was followed by a split with Sivad called "Black Mass Covenant" and the band is currently working on a debut full length. 
With Demogorgon on Vocals, Wargoat (Savage Nekropolis, Bloodwraith) on Guitars, Synnkkyys (Felo De Se, Profound Decadence, SIR, Oupyr) on Bass and Daemonos (Felo De Se) on Drums.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Funeral+Of+God">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
