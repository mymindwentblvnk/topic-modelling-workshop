NKVD is an industrial black metal band formed in Montpellier (south of France) in 2005.  Their lyrical themes deal with topics in totalitarianism covering infamous leaders like Stalin, Hitler, and Slobodan Milošević. They have recorded "Diktatura" EP (2007) and "Vlast" CD (2011).
