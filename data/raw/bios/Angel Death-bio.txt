Angel Death were a death metal band from Rieti, Lazio, Italy.  They formed in 1984.  They played a primitive style of black/death metal, then they became a death metal band on their 2nd release.  They only released 3 demos and 2 singles.  Additionally, a self-titled 2 track 7" was released in 1997 by Cryptic Soul Production.  They were active between 1986 to 1993 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angel+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
