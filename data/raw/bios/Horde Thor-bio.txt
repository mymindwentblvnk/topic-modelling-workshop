Folk-Metal from Medellin, Colombia.
The band was formed in the year 2002 with a different line up and name, it was called Meleth Host, but in the same year because of some language determination problems the band changed it's name to Horde Thor. In 2007 the recording of the coming full-length Fatherland begun.
