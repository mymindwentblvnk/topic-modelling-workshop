There are several artists named Plutonia.

1. Plutonia is the project of Alex Attias and Dennis McFarlane (better know as Dego, half of 4 Hero). Released two broken beat 12" on the Visions Inc. label: "Forever" & "What U Know".

2. Plutonia is radioactive rock band formed in Serbia in 2013, releasing its first song "Vatra" in December 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Plutonia">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
