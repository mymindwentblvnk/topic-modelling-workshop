Run River North is an American indie folk-rock band formed in California in 2011. With brilliant harmonies paired with layered instrumentations, the band consists of Alex Hwang (lead vocals, guitars), Daniel Chae (vocals, strings), Jennifer Rim (strings), Joe Chun (bass, vocals), John Chong (drums, vocals) and Sally Kang (vocals, keyboards). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Run+River+North">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
