Pandemia - czech death metal band, founded in '95. Line-up: Michal (vocals), Vlasta (guitar), Jarda (bass), Pavel (drums). The got first on tour in '97 as a support of Krabathor and Vader. Then Pandemia played witch such bands as Six Feet Under, God Dethroned, Krisiun, Kreator, Napalm death, Incantation and even Death. The debut album was released in '99 (Spreading the message). Pandemia recorded three albums overall - Spreading the message, Personal demon, and Riven - and three demos (Prach na očích, Dance in Vicious Circle, The Message from the Death Metal Empire)
