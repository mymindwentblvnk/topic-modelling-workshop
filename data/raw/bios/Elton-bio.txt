There are several artists with the name Elton.

Elton is a punk rock band from Porto Alegre, RS, Brazil. The band was formed in 2005 and released their first album, "Pequeno mundo que só você conhece" (The Little World Only You Know), in early 2006.

The band was created by Rodrigo Deltoro (guitar/vocals), Didi Gloor (drums), Eddie Hoffman (bass) and Fabinho (guitar) soon after Deltoro and Gloor left Tequila Baby, another punk rock band from Porto Alegre.

Elton is also the name of a German TV moderator and comedian. His career began with his job at the TV show "TV Total" by Germany's comedian Stefan Raab. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
