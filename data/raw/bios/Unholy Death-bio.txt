Solo project of Antichristus (Father Befouled, Prosanctus Inferi) formerly known as Funereal.
Antichristus committed suicide in 2009. The cassette "Antinomian Praise of Death" was compiled and released shortly after his death. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unholy+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
