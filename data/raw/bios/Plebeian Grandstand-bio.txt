PLEBEIAN GRANDSTAND was formed in the summer of 2005 in Toulouse, Midi-Pyrénées, France. Extreme metal blizzard. Violent emotions. Oppressive dissonance. Chaotic structures. Apocalyptic atmospheres. Sadness and rage. Hundreds of shows across western Europe, USA, India and Russia. Their most recent album is 'False Highs, True Lows', released on March 29, 2016 through Throatruiner Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Plebeian+Grandstand">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
