In Aevum Agere is a Power/Doom Metal band from Italy (Napoli, Campania) formed in 2005. and still active today. It is comprised of members:
Bruno Masulli - Bass, Guitars (Rhythm), Vocals
Andrea Cannata - Drums
Marco Ruggiero - Guitars (Lead)

So far the band released a demo (From the Depth of Soul, 2007), Single (Labyrinth of Emptyness, 2009), EP (MMXI, 2011) and a masterpiece debut album "The Shadow Tower" (2012). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/In+Aevum+Agere">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
