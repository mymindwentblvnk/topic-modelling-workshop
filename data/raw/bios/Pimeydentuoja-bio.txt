Project born in summer 2009 from the common passion of three friends for old school death/black metal. Pimeydentuoja is a finnish folk expression translating into something like "He who brings darkness" and meaning the Devil. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pimeydentuoja">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
