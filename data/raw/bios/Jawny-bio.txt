Jacob Lee-Nicholas Sullenger (born December 23, 1995), known professionally by Jawny (stylized as JAWNY; formerly Johnny Utah), is an American singer, songwriter, and producer. He first received recognition after the release of his 2019 single, "Honeypie", and was signed to Interscope Records in January 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jawny">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
