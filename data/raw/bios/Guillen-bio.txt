Guillen is the solo project of Adam Guillen from Rio Grande Valley, Texas, Unites States. The genre of the band can be considered as Avant-garde Black / Death Metal. 'Eclipse' is his first album release which was released on the 26th of June, 2012. ''First album from Guillen, it brings forth a mix of metal and much other influences. An album to be listened from beginning to end, sit back and enjoy, lose yourself, then find yourself in the end.'' Composer/Musician/Programmer - Adam Guillenm, Co-Producer - Esai Uresti, Recorded at SugarPocket Studios. His album is available for free download through his Bandcamp. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Guillen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
