Ornaments of Sin is a black metal band from Sarreguemines, France formed in 2001. The band's name is taken from the Darkthrone song Lifeless. Their lyrics focus on evil, darkness, occult, battles and anti-semitism.They released an EP in 2003 titled "Inhale Zyklon-B"' and two splits in 2004 and 2005 with Ad Hominem and Sombre Chemin.
Current line-up:
Dolgren ev Skjal: vocals
Svardath: guitar, bass
Balgradon Xul (Arno Eikenbroek): drums (session)
