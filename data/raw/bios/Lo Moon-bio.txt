Lo Moon are an alternative pop/rock band from Los Angeles, California. The band's members are frontman Matt Lowell (guitar/vocals), Crisanta Baker (synth/bass/samples), and Sam Stewart (guitar). Lo Moon's debut album will be released in February 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lo+Moon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
