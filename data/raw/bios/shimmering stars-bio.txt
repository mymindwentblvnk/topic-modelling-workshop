Shimmering Stars is a 4 piece dream-pop/noise act from British Columbia, Canada. Their first full length, Violent Hearts, was released in September 2011 by Hardly Art/Sub Pop in North America & Almost Musique in Europe. Their second full length 'Bedrooms of the Nation' was released on August 13, 2014.
