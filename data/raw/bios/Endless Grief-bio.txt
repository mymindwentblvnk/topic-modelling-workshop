Endless Grief was a short-"lived" metal influenced punk rock band from Labin, Croatia. Most of the members are now playing in a local metal band called MUR and one of the guitarist is playing In Useless and Atira.

band members:
Josip : vocals
Robi : guitar
Alen : guitar
Mirza : bass
