Ravenshade is a melodic metal band from Guelph, Ontario, Canada; formed in 2005.  Their lyrical content primarily focuses on love, suicide, etc; their music and content is comparable to Sentenced.

Hear tracks from the bands' album, "Wading" at their offical website, www.myspace.com/ravenshademusic. 
Ravenshade merchandise can be purchased from the catacombs.
