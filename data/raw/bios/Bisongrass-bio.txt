Formerly Bison(uk) Beef Metal from Aberdeen, Scotland. 
Formed late 2010 and featuring former and current members of Dredwater, KCHGH, Ablach, Filthpact, Risactonia and Korpse.

Fudge Awards winners Best Newcomer 2012 & Best Band 2012.

Debut album Bisongrass was released on the 15th September 2012.
