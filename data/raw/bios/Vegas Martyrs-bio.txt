Vegas Martyrs is a band which mixes No Wave, Noise and  Black Metal. It is made up of Joe Potts (Percussion) Dominick Fernow (Guitar) and Richard Dunn (Electronics, Voice).  Dominick Fernow is better known for his Noise project, Prurient, and black metal project, Ash Pool. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vegas+Martyrs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
