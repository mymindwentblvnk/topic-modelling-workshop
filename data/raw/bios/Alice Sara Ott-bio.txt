Alice Sara Ott(アリス=紗良・オット) (born in Munich, 1988) is a German-Japanese classical pianist. After studying at the Salzburg Mozarteum, Ott has won awards at a number of piano competitions, including first prize at the 2004 Pianello Val Tidone Competition. She has made recordings of Franz Liszt's Transcendental Etudes and Frédéric Chopin's waltzes for Deutsche Grammophon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alice+Sara+Ott">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
