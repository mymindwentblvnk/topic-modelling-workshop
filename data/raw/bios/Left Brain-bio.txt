Left Brain is an American producer from Los Angeles, California. Left is a member of OFWGKTA and is one half of the rap duo MellowHype with fellow OFWGKTA member Hodgy Beats. Left Brain is credited as a solo artist on songs from the first OFWGKTA mixtape. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Left+Brain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
