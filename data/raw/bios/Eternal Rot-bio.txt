Eternal Rot is heavy doom/death metal band. Crawled out from the grave in 2012 in London, released demo entitled "Grave Grooves", and featured track "Fatal Offering" for "Vestibule of Hell" compilation. Debut album called "Cadaverine" was released on September 2018 by Godz Ov War Productions. July 2020 brings 2nd album "Putridarium", with addition of Radek Pierściński on drums. 

Eternal Rot is:
Mayer - guitars, bass, drum programming, vocals;
Psychoradek - drums
