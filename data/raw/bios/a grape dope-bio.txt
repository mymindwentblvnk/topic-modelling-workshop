Joining the highly acclaimed roster of Tortoise side projects (The Sea and Cake, Brokeback, Isotope 217), A Grape Dope finds John Herndon straddling the line between hardline beats and loose-limbed kineticism. DoseOne, Kathryn Frazier, and Sally Timms join Herndon for an altogether fresh delivery.
