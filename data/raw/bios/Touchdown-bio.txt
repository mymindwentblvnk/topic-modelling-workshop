There is more than one artist named Touchdown.

1) A  Heavy Metal band from Canada, formed in 1983.  

2) Touchdown (also known as Touchdown Eagle) was a Seattle indie rock band active in the 2000s. After their break-up, singer/guitarist Carrie Murphy went on to form Panda & Angel.

3) A Dutch a capella pop group, founded in 1998, consisting of 7 guys who make their own versions of famous popsongs. The singers all have different musical backgrounds, ranging from classical and pop to musical and barbershop. their combined love for serveral famous popsongs gave rise to the present formula with a very recongnisable sound.
