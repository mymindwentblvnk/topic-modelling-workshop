Opticollide's sound is a melodic, metal edged rock. Opticollide released their debut album  "ELEVEN" in 2005. 

Members included:

Adara Blake - vocals,
Chris Finster - guitar/vocals,
Jackson Coffey - drums,
Brooks Schaper - bass,
