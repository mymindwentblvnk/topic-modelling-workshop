The idea of the band came around November 2010, by January 2011 line up was formed by 5 members that shared the same interests, Ricardo Rodrigues on Vocals, Carlo Rodrigues and Tiago Caldeira on Guitars, Jeff De gouveia on Drums and Percussion and Tobias Rodrigues on the Bass Guitar. Calamity Islet has been rehearsing since and has been working on progressing their music and live performances with hopes of one day entering the professional world of music and live entertainment. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Calamity+Islet">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
