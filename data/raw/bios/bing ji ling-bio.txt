Bing Ji Ling ( which means Ice Cream in Chinese) is the nom de plume of a NYC-based producer/musician whose birth name is Quinn Luke. He's released three solo albums including his latest 2008 release June Degrees in December, the first one recorded since he moved from San Francisco in 2005. He's also been featured on Future Primitive and Ubiquity records compilations, and recorded & collaborated with other like minded musicians including Tommy Guerrero, Honeycut, Band of Thieves and drummer Adrian Young of No Doubt.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bing+Ji+Ling">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
