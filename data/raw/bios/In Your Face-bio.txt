In Your Face is a funk/rock/psychadelic Band from Austria formed in 2009. (http://www.in-your-face.at)

In Your Face were also a hardcore band from New York that existed from 1987 to 1990 during which time they released a demo in 1988 and a 7" titled The Grub on Common Cause Records in 1989. Musically they were not unlike contemporaries Token Entry and Krakdown.

There is also another In Your Face, an obscure punk band, best known for such songs as "Riot Man" and "Everything is Bullshit". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/In+Your+Face">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
