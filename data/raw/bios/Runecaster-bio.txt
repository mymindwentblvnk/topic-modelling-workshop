Runecaster are a Celtic folk metal band from Ulster, Ireland. Mixing normal heavy metal and traditional Irish folk instrumentation Runecaster stand out from any other band in this genre with their totally unique sound. 

It began in the fall of 2001, over the next few years the band played several gigs around Northern Ireland and released several records including “Dethwatch” (2002) “Back To The Edge” (2003) “Joyful Sorrow” (2004) before finding their true folk metal sound with records “Folkishly Fiendish” (2004) and the highly anticipated follow up “When Mortal Spirits Fall…” (2005)
