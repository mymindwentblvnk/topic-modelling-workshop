Yazz Ahmed is a London, UK, jazz composer and trumpeter.

She has collaborated with artists such as Radiohead and Lee "Scratch" Perry, and released her debut album 'Finding My Way Home' in 2011.

She released 'La Saboteuse' in 2017, an album which references Arabic Jazz and has earned comparisons to Miles Davis' Bitches Brew. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yazz+Ahmed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
