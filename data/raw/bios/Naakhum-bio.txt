Naakhum is a musical project from Barcelona which alternates the spiritualism and contemplative atmospheres of Ambient, Ethnic Music, Folk and Extreme Metal.
The single member is Pol Guasch, a Catalan multi-instrumentalist musician who includes instruments such as didgeridoo, jewish harp or even instruments manufactured by himself.
