"Flash Of Aggression" is a musical project formed by Andrey Smirnov (U.D.O., Everlost, Zemlyane, ex-Master, ex-Shadow Host), Dmitry Ignatiev (Tantal, ex-Rest In Pain, ex-Fatal Band), Sergey Serebrennikov (Everlost, Gaina, Dom Vetrov) and Polina Berezko (Grace Disgraced).
The band's music is a mixture of modern and old school thrash and death metal with a lot of melodies and female extreme vocal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flash+Of+Aggression">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
