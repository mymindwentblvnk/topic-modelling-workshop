Decrepit Womb was a brutal death metal band from Manchester, England that formed in the year 2007.  Their last known lineup consisted of Sam Yates (vocals, drum programming), Sammy Urwin (guitars), Geoff Bradley (guitars) and Matt Jones (bass).  Past members include Sean Hynes (vocals), Steve Currier (bass) and Arron Healey (drums).  Decrepit Womb wrote songs about gore and violence.  They only released one demo called Promo 2010 and a five-way split called Fermenting in Five-Way Filth with Cumbeast, Defleshuary, Down from the Wound and Heinous Killings.
