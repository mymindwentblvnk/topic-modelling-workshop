Devilish Trio (stylized as DEVILISH TRIO) was a phonk rap trio from Nashville (2017-2020), Tennessee that began their career on SoundCloud and Bandcamp, releasing singles and mixtapes, they also made visuals on their channel on YouTube. Their style is inspired by black metal vibe. The members were Hydra Mane, Tenngage, and BAKER YA MAKER. In 2020 after releasing some singles, BAKER YA MAKER decide to left the trio to focus on "getting his personal life together". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DEVILISH+TRIO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
