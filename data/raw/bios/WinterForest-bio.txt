Winterforest is 1.man band from the Mountains of Fruska Gora in region Vojvodina/Country Serbia.

Doomhead(ex-Ancient Lament,ex-Meskalith,ex-Salamander Funeral)production and all instruments.

Doomhead rename WinterForest to Vinterforst in 2015.



2010-Elftales demos
2011-Singles demos
2012-When The Raven Scream My Name...Winter Is Here
2012-Samhain Forest EP
2014-The Forest Is My Temple

The Winterforests music is mix between athmospheric black metal,dark ambient,folk metal and Doomhead tag this genre with forest metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/WinterForest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
