Tindalos are a Uruguayan band from Montevideo with an Alternative style which includes Rap and Metal branches. Formed in late 2015, the group is made up of Nahuel Rodríguez (Lead Voice), Lucas Corajoria (Bass), Guillermo Segovia (DJ / Sampler / Voice), Martín Rodríguez (Guitar), and David Barreneche (Drums). The band started their first musical works independently that same year and after that they recorded their first material called Abisal, released in early 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tindalos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
