W.C. Noise was a 90's thrash metal band from Oporto, Portugal. They put out two albums; "Loud & Mad" in 1992 and "Reality Asylum" in 1994. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/W.C.+Noise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
