NYC Sewage is a street punk band that formed from the ashes of the band Humyn Sewage. They're from  New York, NY and were popular in the L.E.S. in the 1990s. They played shows with many major punk bands of the time including: GG Allin , The Casualties, Blanks 77, Total Chaos, and Battallion of Saints.  They have reunited after a long hiatus with NEW members and are currently playing shows again. Spike Polite/VOX n Guitar, Shakes McGraff/Bass n VOX
