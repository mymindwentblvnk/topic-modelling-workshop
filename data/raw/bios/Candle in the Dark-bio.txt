There are known two bands named Candle In The Dark:

1. Candle in the Dark is a newly formed melodic metal band based in Keighley in the north of England. The lineup consists of Lowell Brook (guitar), Jesse Howie (guitar), Oscar Manthorp (keyboard), Bill Newton (vocals), Jim Routledge (bass), Alex Moreman (drums).

The genre is primarily power metal with touches of progressive and other genres of metal. Influences include Sonata Arctica, Stratovarius, Helloween, Dream Theater, Symphony X, Ayreon and Iron Maiden.
