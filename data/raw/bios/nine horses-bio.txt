Nine Horses brings together David Sylvian, his brother Steve Jansen (both ex-Japan), and machine- and rhythm scientist, Burnt Friedman (aka Flanger and owner of the Nonplace label).

Nine Horses recordings feature guest appearances by, among others, Stina Nordenstam, trumpeter Arve Henriksen (Rune Grammofon) and Ryuichi Sakamoto. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nine+Horses">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
