Infernal Chaos is a fast-growing melodic death metal band from Taiwan, stimulating more metal fans from the Eastern Hemisphere since August 2007 with their introduction headline: "At the Gates of Chaos". This thrash-act band was born to follow the black metal scene in late 2006 and is consisted of 5 members: Jesse "the Infernal" Liu (ChthoniC's guitarist) as lead guitarist, Aero as guitarist, Mason as bassist, Shin as drummer, and Cig as vocalist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infernal+Chaos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
