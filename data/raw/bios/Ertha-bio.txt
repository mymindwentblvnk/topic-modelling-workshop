Ertha is a Czech metal band which combines melodic vocal lines of both male and female singers with growl and traditional guitar-bass-drums sound with symphonic, folk, gothic, doom and other elements. It was founded by Eyrah and Ereis back in 2004 in Brno but only in 2010, settled in Prague, they found all the members necessary and could start to perform in public. The band has released the first demo in January 2011 and currently plays in Prague and other venues around the Czech republic.

Band members:
Eyrah (basses, guitar, vocals)
Ereis (vocals, recorders)
Greg (guitars, vocals)
Rea (keyboards)
Zelda (violin)
Petr (drums)
Heimerich (gardener)
