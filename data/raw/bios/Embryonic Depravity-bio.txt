Embryonic Depravity formed in 2007.  They were a London, UK based brutal death metal band playing in a similar vein to Disgorge and Defeated Sanity.   The band's debut full length entitled Constrained by the Miscarriage of Conquest was released through Permeated Records (Italy) in the late summer of 2009.  

They disbanded on November 14th, 2012.
