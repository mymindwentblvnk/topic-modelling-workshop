Corubo is an indigenous black metal band from Ji-Paraná, Brazil, formed in 1999. All songs are singed in Guarany Paraguayo, Nheengatu, Tupi, Yucatec, Brazilian Portuguese and English. This is the "Corubo", where the strength came from deep, from true, from love and hate, your feeling can never be destroyed.
