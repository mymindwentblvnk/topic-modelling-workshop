There is more than one band named Antilles:

1) A Danish synthpop band. They released their debut album Something New in 2008.

2) An American progressive-screamo band from Norwalk, Ohio. Releases include Beholder/Destroyer, Antilles EP, My Empathy EP and splits with Mountain Asleep, The Juniper Wait, Trifle Tower, and Staph. They are currently working on their next LP.

3) A French experimental noise band including Erik Minkkinen and Lionel Fernandez (from noisy legend Sister Iodine) with Lori Sean Berg (The Berg Sans Nipple) on drums.
