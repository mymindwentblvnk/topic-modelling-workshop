FORTY SHADES is a 6-headed atmospheric Metal Band with the front singer Manuela Kraller (Xandria , Ex-Haggard).

Tobias started this project early 2007 with the collaboration of Manuela Kraller , under the name of Nagor Mar. After some years of members searching and songwriting, this project became, in 2010, a band, with the new name Forty Shades, from Biel/Bienne

::MEMBERS::

Tobias Gut (CH)-Keys
