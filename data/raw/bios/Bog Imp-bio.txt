There are two bands that use the name Bog Imp
1) Bog Imp is a Atmospheric/Experimental Black Metal band from Appleton, Wisconsin that formed in 2004. They released their album Muck-Crusted and Waterlogged in June 2004 and rereleased the album in August of 2018. In 2021 it was confirmed that Bog Imp has returned by a YouTuber who uploaded music from the Wisconsin Black Metal scene GnosticKeys. in May 2021 Bog Imp released their album Ancient Chrysalis Perdition
