Oliver „Olli“ Marc Schulz (born on October 15, 1973)  is a German singer-songwriter from Hamburg and frontman of  "Olli Schulz und der Hund Marie" (German for "Olli Schulz and the dog Marie"). He now lives in Berlin.
Olli Schulz had been in the music business for many years (as a roadie and stagehand) until his good friend Marcus Wiebusch from the German band Kettcar asked him to record an album.
