There are multiple artists with this name:

1) A goth/industrial band formed in 1996, when they were signed by German Industrial legends Project Pitchfork for their newly formed label Candyland Entertainment. They have released 4 albums including: The Awakening (1996), Darkwaters (1999), Chimera (2001) and Relics of Desire (2006) as well as featured in countless compilations, including: The Goth Box (1996), StrangeLove (1997), The Carnival Within (1998), Venusa XX (2000) and Weatherhead 1 (2005). 
2) The solo project of Nomi Ruiz from Hercules & Love Affair. (Which is now going under a different name after legal action by Deep Red # 1)
