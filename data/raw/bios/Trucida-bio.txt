Formed in 2005, Trucida have recently finished recording their follow up to the well recieved Trials and Tribulations EP (8/10 Metal Hammer magazine). 
The Weight of Days EP, mixed by Mike Rossiter, offers the listener a fresh and innovative approach to the metal genre. WIth twin guitar harmonies, deft and haunting solos and vocalist Tom Clarkes unique style, Trucida have written a record that will certainly stand the test of time. Now streaming at www.myspace.com/trucida.
