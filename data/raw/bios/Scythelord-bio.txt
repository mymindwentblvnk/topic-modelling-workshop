Scythelord (Stylized as SCYTHELORD) is an international thrash/death metal band founded in 2011 by Joel and Frank. Self described as an "effort that combines the sonic assault of traditional thrash metal with the precision of modern production".


Scythelord released their debut album "Toxic Minds" on December 11th 2016.
"The Dawn of Harvest Demos" is complication of demos and unreleased songs dating from 2011-2014 which released on November 13th, 2017. 
Scythelord released their 2nd album "Earth Boiling Dystopia" on June 25th, 2021.

 Vargskelethor is Joel's solo venture and is parody/humor metal.
