The Japanese House is 25-year-old Londoner Amber Bain. Her debut EP Pools to Bathe In was produced with The 1975's George Daniel and Matthew Healy. She describes her music as "warm, dream-pop sounds that wash over the listener like you're standing in the ocean getting taken over by the waves". Bain released her debut album Good at Falling in 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Japanese+House">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
