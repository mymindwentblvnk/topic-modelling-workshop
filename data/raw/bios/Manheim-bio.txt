Manheim is a three-man black'n'roll band from Zabok, Croatia. The band was formed in 2007 and brought out its first demo in March 2008. In the following year, Manheim won the O'rock fest in Oroslavje, Croatia, after it entered the competition as a replacement for a band that could not perform. Frontman Mamut won the prize for the best performance on Demo festival in Zaprešić, Croatia in summer 2010.

During autumn 2010 the band recorded their first EP entitled "Kreatura". By the end of 2011 the band signed a booking contract with Northwind Promotion and Events GbR.

Manheim appeared on the first edition of Niflheim Festival in Stuttgart at club LKA-Longhorn on 17th February 2012.
Currently, the band is preparing material for a new, yet untitled album. Fans of Vreid, Mayhem, Darkthrone, get ready!
