Formed in Novo Polje, Slovenia in 1985, S.L.O.D. is one of the very first death metal bands from the Yugoslavia area. They released one demo in 1986 before splitting up.

Lineup:
Uros - Vocals
Tomi - Guitars
Simon - Bass
Cvrle - Drums
