DubVision, based in the Netherlands, is an upcoming producer/ dj duo. Providing dancefloors with energetic tunes, DubVision seems to be a big promise for the future. Dubvision consist of two Dutch brothers, Victor Volt and Stephen Noque. Their passion for music has led to great tracks, getting a lot of support at the moment. They are known for their uplifting melodies and big beats. With a style variating from progressive, tech-house to sweet house tunes, the duo covers a wide dance floor market.
