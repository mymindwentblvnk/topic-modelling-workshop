Plymouth based band The Southpaw Jinx began a few years back as a four piece but disbanded in 2004.  Before long, fan of the band Neil Harwood joined up with original vocalist-guitarist Neil Sims to carry on the Jinx name and take it to a new level.  After jamming hard, recording a new album and playing almost 50 gigs within the next calendar year,  changes were made on bass and in came Oxford based Pikey Pearce.
