橋本みゆき (Hashimoto Miyuki), born May 14, 1980, in Saitama Prefecture, just north of Tokyo) is a prominent singer of game and anime theme songs in Japan. Recent works include the Ending Theme 「innocence」 of the anime SHUFFLE! The Opening [Screaming] & Ending [Dust Trail] for the anime Soul Link, and the Opening Theme 「Faze to love」 of anime Gunparade Orchestra.
