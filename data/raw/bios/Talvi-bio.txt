Talvi was Frostheim Kuuraparta's (Nostatus) dark synth music project, formed in 1998. Talvi released only one demo-CDr, called "Ikirouta", limited to 50 handnumbered copies.

Website: http://www.mikseri.net/artists/talvirip.108331.php
