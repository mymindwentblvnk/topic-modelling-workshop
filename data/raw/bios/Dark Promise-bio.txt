Started as a one-man Old School Black Metal project in 2009 . The sound of the band has evolved into something more melodic/atmospheric after releasing a couple of demos . The band has released 2 demos . The first in 2009 "To Violate the Light" and the second in 2010 "Night of Misery" also the band has un unreleased 4 track demo which was recorded in early 2009 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Promise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
