Paul Kandel is an American musical theatre actor best known for his work as the Gypsy leader Clopin in Disney's the Hunchback of Notre Dame. He also has played roles on Broadway in Jesus Christ Superstar as King Herod, Titanic, The Who's Tommy, and The Visit. Kandel received a nomination for the 1993 Tony Award for Best Performance by a Featured Actor in a Musical for his performance in The Who's Tommy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paul+Kandel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
