SOMAN is an tbm / noise act from Dresden, Germany, formed in 2003 and headed by Kolja Trelle. In the start, Kolja Trelle was a sound engineer for many well known bands, including: Absurd Minds, Hocico and Terminal Choice, as well as remixing songs for many other bands. Soman has its very own unique sound mixture of Noise, Electro and Techno, or more often described as 'Industrial For Clubs'
Soman has also played live in over 60 acts and also supported more known bands, like VNV Nation.
