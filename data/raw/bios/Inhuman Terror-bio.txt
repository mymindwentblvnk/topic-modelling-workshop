Inhuman Terror formed around town Liptovský Mikuláš, Slovakia. Appearance in Signal_FM on 04/17/2011 made them more famous nationally and was a step forward for the crew. Before then, they were known mostly in Liptov area. The crew performs regularly on events called Mimoza. Their production is mostly drum and bass.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/inhuman+terror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
