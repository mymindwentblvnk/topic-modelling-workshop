Any Other is the italian indie-rock band started as a solo project by singer-songwriter and guitarist Adele Nigro (after the split of the indie folk duo The Lovecats). The project soon became a trio with Erica Lonardi (drums) and Marco Giudici (bass, synth), while recording the debut album Silently. Quietly. Going Away, released in September 2015.
