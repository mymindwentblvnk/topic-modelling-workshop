Beef Conspiracy are a grindcore / death Metal band from London, England.  They formed in 1998.  They released a demo called Undead, Undressed and Ready for Breakfast in 2000.  In 2004, they released their first full-length album Hung, Drawn and Quarter-Poundered.  In 2016, they released their second full-length album A Jigsaw of a Flock of Geese.  They also released Leftover Pieces which features leftover tracks from their second album, the first recording ever and other leftover material.  They are currently signed to Grindethic Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beef+Conspiracy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
