Mass Creamtion is an old-school thrash/death metal band from Varna, Bulgaria, which was formed in the late 2011 by Neno (Guitars) and Maya (Bass), and took it's final form in the middle of 2012 after the affiliation of Trendo (Drums) and Kalin (Vocals). In Summer 2013 Maya resigned and Kalin took her place as a bassist and vocalist. The band has only one demo record yet, called "Violence", released in October 2012.
