Harvist is a black metal band from West Virginia, US formed in 1996. Their lyrics are focused on hate, suicide, mind and nature. They released 3 albums so far. Dusk is currently the only member of this band.
Discography:
- Promo '97 (Demo, 1997)
- Forn Glimnneraka (EP, 1998)
- A Gleam in the Night (album, 2000)
- Turmoil Of The Seed (EP, 2002)
- Lightning Storm in the Veins... (album, 2005)
- He Who Rises (EP, 2007)
- "Live In Appalachia (Live album, 2007)
- Wolfskin Clad (album, 2008)
