There is more than one band with the name Mount Fuji.

1) Mount Fuji is a German metal band.  Their style is a mix of grunge and groove metal with a doom sensibility. 

2) Mount Fuji is also a mathy 4-piece from Birmingham, UK. You may have known them previously as Conquistadors, but John law told them they had to change their name.
