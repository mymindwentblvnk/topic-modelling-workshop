Defloration was founded by Bertram (Guitar), Marko (Bass), Alexander (Vocals) und Christian (Drums) in 2001. In the beginning they played Punk-Rock but after Uwe entered the band their style changed into Death Metal. In 2002 Alexander left the band and Uwe became vocalist. In 2005 Sebastian entered the band and now the line-up is:

Uwe Rödel - Vocals
Sebastian Lau - Guitar
Bertram Tischendorf - Guitar
Marko Reppe - Bass
