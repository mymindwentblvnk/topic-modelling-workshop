DALETH is the solo project from Joe Merrill (Noah's Apathy, Maniac, Esteem). Unlike his previous efforts, DALETH explores a dark, revealing cycle throughout every song. Each note, faced by doom, follows an epic adventure. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Daleth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
