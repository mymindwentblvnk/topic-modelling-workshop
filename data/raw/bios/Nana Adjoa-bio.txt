Nana Adjoa is an Amsterdam, The Netherlands-based singer-songwriter and sonic explorer, "armed with a deft poeticism, a fierce sense of musicianship, a bold approach to songwriting, an unflinching sense of curiosity and an openness to endless possibility."

Following a string of singles and three EPs since 2017, her first full-length album, Big Dreaming Ants, was released in September 2020.
