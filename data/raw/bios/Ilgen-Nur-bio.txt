Ilgen-Nur (born İlgen Nur Boralı), is a German singer-songwriter of Turkish descent from Hamburg, now residing in Berlin. She released her debut EP 'No Emotions' in 2017, at the age of 21. The magazine ''Musikexpress'' characterized their style as a guitar-heavy indie pop somewhere between Kate Nash and Courtney Barnett. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ilgen-Nur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
