There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Bachus is a black metal / ambient project from Minsk, Belarus, formed in 1997. Members include Konstantin Kouzyrev (all instruments) and Vladislav Buben (vocals).

2) Bachus (Matthew Best) was born in Australia in 1974 and continues to love the music of pop bands such as Bee Gees, ABBA, Hall & Oates, Pet Shop Boys, Depeche Mode and Dandy Warhols. Matthew began DJ-ing in 2008, playing sets dedicated to 70's disco and funk. Not long after, his discovery of Australia's King Curly drove him to contact the band and offer a remix of 2 of their songs. Soon after he came in 4th in the world for "Mis Amigos" for his entry in an official online Dandy Warhols remix competition.
