Fuming Mouth is a death metal band from Milford, MA.  They formed in the year 2013.  They released a 4 song demo the year they formed.  They are currently signed to Triple-B Records.  Fuming Mouth consists of Mark (vocals, guitars, bass),  Andrew Budwey (guitars) and Cayle Sain (drums).  They released another demo in 2016.  In 2019, they released their debut album The Grand Descent.
