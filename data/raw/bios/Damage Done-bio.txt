There are three known artists with the name Damage Done:
1.) an alternative rock/metal band from greece. formed in kos island in 2003. their first album called "stories never told" was released in february 2009 by pan-vox.
the band consists of theodosis chatzistergos on guitars and keyboard miki drosos bass guitar, and grigoris mathioudakis on drums.
