This project will be known as "Alas, Tyranny" from here on out.  Feel free to download the "Alas, Tyranny" demo with updated tags: http://rapidshare.com/files/186938421/Alas__Tyranny_-_2008_-_Alas__Tyranny.zip

Matt Parsons is a solo artist hailing from Athens, Georgia.  His influences include Dimmu Borgir, In Flames, Children of Bodom, Tchaikovsky, Tvangeste, and Anorexia Nervosa.  

Matt Parsons released "Alas, Tyranny" in January 2008.  He is planning on releasing a full length CD sometime this winter titled "Domination Liberation Purification". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Matt+Parsons+Band">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
