SCALPTURE, founded in 2009, is a death metal band from Bielefeld (NRW), Germany.

Discography:
- Born Into Battle (Demo, 2010)
- Border Crossing (EP, 2015)
- Panzerdoktrin (full-length, 2017)
- Eisenzeit (full-length, 2020)
- Feldwärts (full-length, 2022)

SCALPTURE is:
- Thorsten Pieper (Vocals)
- Moritz Menke (Drums)
- Tobias Aselmann (Guitars)
- Felix Marbach (Guitars)
- Niklas Neuwöhner (Bass)

Past members:
- Jannik Anselm Heise (Bass) // R.I.P. 2017
- Max Cailloud (Drums)
