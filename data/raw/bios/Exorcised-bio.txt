Exorcised is an old school death metal band from Serbia, formed in 2011. 
In 2012 Exorcised have recorded “Reflections of Horror” demo. They are working on their first album which is going to be released by Razorback Recordings in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Exorcised">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
