After penning tracks for famous names like Britney Spears and touring the globe in Electrocute, an equally witty and gritty electropop duo, Nicole Morier released her own solo material. Under the name Coco Morier, she released a 5 song EP onto the popular music streaming site Soundcloud, to this date she has released quite a few EPs <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coco+Morier">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
