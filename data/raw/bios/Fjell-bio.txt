There are various bands with the name Fjell.

One of them is a German Black Metal band, which later renamed itself to Nordlys.  Their first demo appeared in 1994 and is entitled Lord of the Unholy Forest.  Nordlys in turn also changed names a couple of times... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fjell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
