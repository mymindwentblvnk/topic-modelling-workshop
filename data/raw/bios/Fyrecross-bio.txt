Fyrecross are a Greek heavy/speed metal band that formed in 2013 in Athens.

Theo Karageorgos: Bass
Charis Kambitsis: Drums, Backing Vocals
Giorgos Skourtaniotis: Lead Guitar, Backing Vocals
Raff Kritoulis : Vocals
