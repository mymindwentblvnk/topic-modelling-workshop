Losing Sun are a powerful, engaging 4 piece Rock/Metal outfit from South Wales, UK. Their sound is a dark and gritty journey through catchy, anthemic songs.

Media interest, magazine coverage, and the bands relentless touring of the UK and do it yourself attitude, have secured Losing Sun with a bright future.

The album “Inertia” which was released under “Copro Records” in 2005 features 9 tracks. The band is currently signed to "GMF" records.

Their new album; “Perspective” has been scheduled to be released on the 20th October 2008. The band has announced that this new album is to be mastered by “Tue Madsen” in Denmark. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Losing+Sun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
