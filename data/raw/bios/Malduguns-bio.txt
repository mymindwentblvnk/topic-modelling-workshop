Malduguns was a Latvian death metal band formed in 2009 in Rīga, Latvia that builds their own music on traditional old school death metal with some technical and progressive elements and lyrics in Latvian. They cites Atheist, Cynic, Death as important influences.

Its lineup comprising current and former members of Tabestic Enteron, Heresiarh, Skyforger, Dark Reign, Frailty, Apēdājs, and Mala.

The band's debut album Pēddzinis was released in December 2011.

They split up in late 2013.

Members:

Jānis Naglis - vocals;
Krišjānis Krūmiņš - guitars;
Artūrs Ļubļinskis - guitars;
Ģirts Kļaviņš - bass;
Lauris Polinskis - drums.

Former members:

Andris Dzeburs - vocals.
