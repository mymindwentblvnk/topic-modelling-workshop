Martin Reverby is best known as a member of the seminal New York punk-era synth/vocal group Suicide. Under the name Martin Rev he has released albums that are usually classified as No Wave, but other work - under the names See Me Ridin, Strangeworld, To Live - are very different in style and genre from the Martin Rev releases. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Martin+Rev">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
