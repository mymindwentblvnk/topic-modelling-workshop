There are a number of artists with the name Tormenta: 

1) Tormenta is a chilean duo consisting of visual artist Begoña Ortúzar (Los Terrys) and producer  Cristián Heyne (who has worked with artists like Javiera Mena or  Gepe).

2) Tormenta is a self proclaimed New Age Trash Metal five piece from the Maldives.
