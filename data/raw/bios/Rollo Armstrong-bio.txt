Rollo's real name is Roland Armstrong, he is an English music producer and multi-instrumentalist and one of three members of Faithless. He released his first record in January 1992. Made with 2 friends of him, Felix and Red Jerry in Rollo's bedroom studio. This record, Don't You Want Me by Felix went on to sell 2.5 million copies . Rollo had a number one hit in Holland and all over Europe with Faithless God is a DJ. Rollo produced hits such as Pet Shop Boys Absolutely Fabulous and Can You Forgive Her, M People How Can I Love U More, Donna Summer I Feel Love. With Faithless he reached notable successes with hits like Insomnia, Salva Mea and Don't Leave.
