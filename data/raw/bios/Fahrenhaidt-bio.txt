Fahrenhaidt is an ambient, modern Nature-Pop project founded by Berlin based music producers Erik Macholl and Andreas John. Their guest singers include danish singers Emmelie de Forest and Amanda Pedersen, and the canadian singer-songwriter Alice Merton. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fahrenhaidt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
