J-Dawg is a rapper from Houston, TX. He is a member of Boss Hogg Outlawz, a group & record label started by rapper Slim Thug. Originating from the Northside of  Houston, he was originally a member of Swishahouse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/J-Dawg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
