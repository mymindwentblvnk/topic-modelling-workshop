The Thorn and Tear is the solo project of Robinton of Denver, Colorado. The music is epic and expansive in nature, and incorporates a blend of black, folk and other sub genres of metal.  It also shows strong influences from punk and classical music. 

Thematically The Thorn and Tear embraces many fantasy related elements, a guise through which human nature and the surrounding world is explored. The majority of the songs tell stories, with common themes being adventure, bravery, death and spirituality.
