Dandelion was a alternative rock band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania formed in 1989 by vocalist/guitarist Kevin Morpurgo, bassist Mike Morpurgo, guitarist Carl Hinds, and drummer Dante Cimino. Bayen Butler was also a member in 1993-94. Due to their sound and image, Dandelion is often associated with the grunge movement of the late 1980s and early 1990s from Seattle Washington. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dandelion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
