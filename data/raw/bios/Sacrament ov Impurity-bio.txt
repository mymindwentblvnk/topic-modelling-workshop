A black metal band formed in 2007 located in the outskirts of Washington. Began with a traditional black metal sound focusing on ancient mythology and winter and has since evolved into a raw and yet melodic direction combining a variety of subgenres, focusing on the cosmos and esoteric.

Lineup:

The Butcher: Guitar, 2007 - present
Naphula: Vocals, 2012 - present
