1) Cabala is an alias of a trance producer Nick Muir. He had four releases under this name.  He is also known as Floorjam, Marshal Stax, Monumental, Nemo, One Note Jam and Sony All Stars.

2) Cabala was a death metal band from Nottingham, England, that released two demos in 1994 entitled "Death Twitch" and "Extracting the Knowledge." They have since split up.
