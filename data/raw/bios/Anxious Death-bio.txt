Anxious Death were a Finnish black/death metal band formed in 2000. A very limited rehearsal tape was circulated in 2002 and the band's only official demo The Great Faceless Beast released the year after. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anxious+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
