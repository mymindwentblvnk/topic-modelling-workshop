Abraham's Meat Plow is a southern metal band from either West Virginia or Pennsylvania or western Maryland, depending on which rumor mill you work at.  AMP have been described rather accurately as: mountain stompin', rippin rompin, crushin' down like a Mack truck. Appalachian armageddon, run to the hills, the sun's a settin'.

Members: James  : Rhythm Guitar,
Tony  : Bass,
 troy  : Lead Guitar,
Mitch  : Lead Vocals,
Jeff  : Drums
