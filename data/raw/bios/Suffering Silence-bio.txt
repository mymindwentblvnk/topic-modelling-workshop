Suffering Silence are an alternative hybrid metal band from London, England, UK.

A dynamic duo of awesome consisting of
Romina 'Pyro' Aileru - Lead vocals, Guitar, Drums, Producer & April 'Apes' Finn - Bass, Backing vocals, Artwork.

The bands musical style has a combination of raw and clean vocals with rap style undertones mixed into heavy/melodic guitar riffs and hard driven drum beats. Their image mainly portrays themselves as animated characters (similar to Japanese Anime/Manga), drawn by the bands bassist April "Apes" Finn.  

Their musical influences include Linkin Park, Lamb of God, Eminem, Pussycat Dolls, (hed) P.E., Daft Punk, Timberland, N*E*R*D and The Lonely Island.

As quoted by the band, Suffering Silence are 'Just a band with a plan, ending the silence with a little something we like to call music'. 

Their fifth album 'BEYOND' is out now and available to download for free.
