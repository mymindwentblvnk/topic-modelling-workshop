Masked Raiders were a heavy metal band from Brighton, East Sussex, South East England.  It is unknown when they formed and disbanded.  They only released one album and one single in 1989.  The last label they were on was Le Silence de la Rue. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Masked+Raiders">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
