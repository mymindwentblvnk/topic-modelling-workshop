Acid Evil is an underground one-woman speed metal act from Ecuador. In 2010, Acid Evil released "Black Space" and also appeared on the 2011 split release "Acid Demons" with Demona. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Acid+Evil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
