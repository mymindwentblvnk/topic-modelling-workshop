Vaults are an electropop trio from London, UK, consisting of lead singer Blythe Pepino, Barney Freeman and Ben Vella. The band formed in 2013 and posted their first ever track 
“Cry No More” on Soundcloud, and subsequently released “Premonitions” in 2014 on the label National Anthem. They performed at several UK festivals during 2014  including Lovebox, T in the Park, and Reading and Leeds festivals. The band is best known for “One Last Night” that featured on the "Fifty Shades Of Grey" movie soundtrack (2015). In 2015, Vaults supported the Everything Everything tour and Paolo Nutini's tour. In November 2016, the band covered Randy Crawford’s 1980 hit ballad, “One Day I’ll Fly Away”, for the UK’s iconic John Lewis’ Christmas advert (2016).
The band’s debut album, “Caught In Still Life” was released on December 2, 2016.
