The Bleeding Harmony is a Melodic Death Metal From Finland . They Formed in 1998 and Released a Demo in 2002 . The band changed their name into "The Wake" upon signing a record contract with Spinefarm in 2002, but the contract was based on the Bleeding Harmony "Promo 2002" demo. There were changes in line-up in the same time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleeding+Harmony">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
