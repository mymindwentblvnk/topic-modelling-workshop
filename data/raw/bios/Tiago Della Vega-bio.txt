He began learning to play the acoustic guitar at the age of five. After 2 years, he began with the electric guitar instead, practicing 16 hours a day. As a result, at the age of 15 he was already giving guitar lessons.
Actually Della Vega holds the Guinness World Record to being the fastest living guitarist in the world (over than 320 bpm).
His name is usually cited on international guitarists' website, such as Steve Vai, John Petrucci and Yngwie J.
