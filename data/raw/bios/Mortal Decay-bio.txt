Mortal Decay is a death metal band from Audubon, New Jersey. Since forming in 1991, they have released three demos (as well as a compilation of them remastered), one split, and four full-length albums. Its core members are John Hartman on guitar, Anthony Ipri on drums, John Paoline on vocals, and Joe Gordon on guitar.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortal+Decay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
