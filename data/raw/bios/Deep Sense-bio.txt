Deep sense is a band from Namestovo - Slovakia. It was founded in october 2004 by Jozef Koleják(guitar), Jakub Ptačin(drums), Martin Balko(bass) and  Fero Grebáč(guitar). In april 2005 Matej Červeň(vocals) joined the band. In december 2006 they recorded their first EP "A little wonder" in EXPONENT studio in Hlohovec..

In april 2008 Martin Červeň was replaced by Anton Krajan(vocals) and later in october they recorded their debut album "A contagion of fear"  in Morpheus multimedia studio in Lučenec.
