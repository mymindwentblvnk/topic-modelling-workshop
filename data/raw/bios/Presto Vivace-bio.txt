Presto Vivace is a Melodic Progressive Metal band from Argentina, formed in 1995. After two EPs and a Compilation CD, they released their first album in 2001, called "Utopías Color Esmeralda". 

The current line up is: 

- Marcelo Pérez Schneider: Electric and Acoustic Bass
- Agustín Pinto: Electric Guitar, Acoustic Guitar, Keyboards
