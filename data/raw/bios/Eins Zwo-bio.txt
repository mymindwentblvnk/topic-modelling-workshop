Eins Zwo (German for "one two") were a German Hip Hop group from Hamburg, founded in 1998. The two members of the group were Dendemann (Daniel Ebel) and DJ Rabauke (Thomas Jensen). They disbanded in February 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eins+Zwo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
