The Hidden were a punk/metal band from Boston, MA and New Bedford, MA. They recorded their debut EP, Hymnal, and subsequent LP, Smash to Ashes, with Steve Albini. In November 2005, they were in Chicago recording their second LP, Winged Wolves, with Albini at Electrical Audio. The album was released on October 28, 2006.
Members:
* Kevin Grant- vocals
* Mario Costa- bass
* James Brockman- right guitar
* Daniel Brockman- left guitar, vocals
* Tanya Paglia- drums
