Dead Wish was started as a one man project in 2004 and in the same year, the demo entitled "From the Land of Forgotten Graves" has been released. It has got a great response from the zines, listeners, and labels. The demo has been distributed worldwide by Oak Knoll Productions and some music companies.

In the beginning of 2007, Dead Wish has reformed as a band and started to work on their debut album. "The Storm of Deadly Winter" is the first promotional track of the band with a line-up and Dead Wish plan to finish their studio recordings for their first album in the autumn of 2007.
