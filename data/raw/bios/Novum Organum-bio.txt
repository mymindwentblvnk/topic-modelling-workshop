Taking their name from Francis Bacon's essay on epistemology and nature, Novum Organum create a similarly heady sound-world of complex, swirling harmonies and shifting rhythms. Absorbing ideas from a rich variety of sources, Novum Organum's music is at once a cerebral and deeply personal statement of instrumental metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Novum+Organum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
