Núria Graham (born 25 June 1996 in Vic, Catalonia, Spain) is a Catalan singer and songwriter who sings primarily in English. She is of Irish and Catalan descent. 

Bird Eyes is Graham's first full-length record, released by El Segell de Primavera in 2015. It's a "solid, classic, timeless corset, addictive. Capital pop.

Núria Graham delves into her own compositional and interpretive framework. This studio album has made it one of the most requested voices throughout Catalonia during 2015. It is a sophisticated record in terms of sound, with the voice and electric guitar in the foreground on electronic environments or acoustic, and inhabited by sensual and captivating lyrics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/N%C3%BAria+Graham">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
