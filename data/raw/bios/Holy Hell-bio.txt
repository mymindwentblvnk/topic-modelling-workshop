There is more than one artist with this name. If you are here for the US Power Metal band please change your tags to HolyHell.

1)  Holy Hell was the original inception of the Finnish doom metal band Unholy. They only released one demo in 1989, titled "Kill Jesus" and changed their name to Unholy in 1990.

2)  Holy Hell is an Italian heavy metal band formed in 2003 from Parma. They only released a demo in 2004, titled "Sounds of Winter".

3)  Holy Hell was a black/thrash metal band from El Paso, Texas, USA, formed in 2007. They only released a demo in 2008, titled "Unholy Sacrifice".

4) Holy Hell is a Welsh solo project described by the artist as 'Shithouse Indie rock B^)'. In 2021 they released "Get The Lead Out".

5) Holy Hell , is a groove/thrash metal band from San Antonio, Texas, formed in 2020. They released 'Blasphemous Transformation' in 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Holy+Hell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
