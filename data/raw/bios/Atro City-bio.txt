Atro City (as opposed to Atrocity), was a heavy/thrash metal band from Denmark. They formed in Denmark  (Århus) in the mid 1980's and recorded their only demo in 1988. The members included: Tomas Pödenpfant - Vocals, Tue Madsen - Guitar (Desexult, Grope (Dnk)), Jan Borsing - Bass, Tomas Christensen - Drums. The band would later change it's name to Pixie Killers and would become one of the better known thrash acts out of Denmark in the late 80's/early 90's.
