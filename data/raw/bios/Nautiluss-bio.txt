Nautiluss is the alias of Canadian DJ and producer Graham Douglas Bertie, raised in Montreal and now based in Toronto. Blending techno, house, and bass, he has releases on labels such as Hemlock, Audio Culture, Turbo, and 3024 and remixed artists like Banks, Aquarian, and Untold. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nautiluss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
