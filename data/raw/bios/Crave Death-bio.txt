Crave Death are an Australian metalcore band from Brisbane, Queensland. Formed in 2016, the group consists of lead vocalist Candice Bankovacki, guitarists Dylan Moore & Tom Belcher, drummer Lachlan Currie and bassist Ashley Lowe. They self-released their debut EP Life After Death - The Demonstration in 2016, followed by singles Suffocate // Burden in 2018 and The Illusion of Peace in 2020.
