Plasmoptysis is a brutal death metal band founded in November 2006 in Bandung, Indonesia. With Argha Riyan (vocals), Dedy Kresna (guitar), Endry Noerdajat (guitar), Sandro D. Armanda (bass), and Hendrawan Saputra (drums --ex-Hydro, Glosalia, Haul) they have released their first promo CD 'Parang Gurinda Panyiksa (Promo 2008)' in March 2008. The band yet has released their debut full-length album entitled "Breeds of the Malevolence" in January 2010 on Extreme Souls Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Plasmoptysis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
