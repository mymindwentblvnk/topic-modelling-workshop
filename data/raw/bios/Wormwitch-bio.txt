Wormwitch is a black metal band from Vancouver, British Columbia.

Since forming in 2015 they have released one EP and one single. In 2017 they released Strike Mortal Soil, their first full-length album.

Will likely appeal to fans of bands along the lines of Regarde Les Hommes Tomber[/artists], Young and in the Way, Hexis,Scumpulse, The Secret, Celeste and/or Nesseria. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/WORMWITCH">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
