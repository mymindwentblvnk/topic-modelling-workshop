There are multiple artists named Darkness: 1) German thrash metal band. 2) British black metal band. 3) Colombian thrash metal. 4) German Eurodance / techno band. Not to be confused with the British hard rock band The Darkness.

1) Darkness was a German thrash metal band, formed 1984 in Essen, North Rhine-Westphalia. They released a handful of demos and three full-length albums. “Death Squad” in 1987, “Defenders of Justice” in 1988 and “Conclusion and Revival” in 1989. In 2005 Battle Cry Records released a live album entitled “Bocholt Live Squad”.

2) Darkness from UK was a unique and obscure black metal band from Middle Rasen, Lincolnshire. Little is known about the band and its sole member Caranthir.

3) Darkness was a Colombian thrash metal band formed in 1987 in Bogotá.

4) Darkness was German techno band in the mid-90s. The group consisted of "True" and "Cassandra" on vocals.  They had one well known dance hit "In My Dreams" in the mid-90s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
