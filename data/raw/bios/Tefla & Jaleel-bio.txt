Tefla & Jaleel are a hip hop group from Chemnitz in Eastern Germany. The group, consisting of Tefla (Soeren Metzger) and Jaleel (Tino Kunstmann), formed in 1997. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tefla+&+Jaleel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
