Elivagar is a German band, playing a mix of death metal and folk elements, with lyrics on paganism and norse mythology. In 2006 they released their first demo, "Pride". In 2007 they released a second demo called "Followers of Ancient Tales", and in 2008 a full-length album called "Heirs of the Ancient Tales". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elivagar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
