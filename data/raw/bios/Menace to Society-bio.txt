Menace To Society [M.T.S.] was born in winter of 2005 in Sarajevo (Bosnia) with an idea to make some mixture of modern hardcore music and some thrash/death metal influences. Altough M.T.S. is young  its members have big experiences with hardcore/metal bands in Sarajevo.Their first selftitled is EP is out now!
