Contra Ignem Fatuum is a two man black metal band from London, England. Until 2005 the band was known as Circumscriber. The band released four demos as Circumscriber and in 2005 upon changing their name to Contra Ignem Fatuum they released an EP called Detritus with three tracks on it, a dark ambient intro and outro and one black metal track. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Contra+Ignem+Fatuum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
