Polyptych is a Blackened Death Metal band based out of Chicago, Illinois. The Band consists of founding Members. Tim Kuhn (Guitar and Vocals)  and Scott Skopec (Guitars and Vocals).  The band is currently finishing up its Debut Album which looks to be done in some time 2011. Appearing on the album is young prodigy drummer Matt Kaminsky of Yildun (Mansfield, Connecticut).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Polyptych">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
