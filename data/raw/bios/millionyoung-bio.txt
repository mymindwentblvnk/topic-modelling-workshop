Out of Florida, Millionyoung is the moniker of recording artist, Mike Diaz. Mixing musical talent with electronic instrumentation, Millionyoung has quickly been established as one of the innovators of chillwave, for writing flowy tracks that enable the listener to slip into a softer state of mind. 

Millionyoung has released three LPs via Old Flame Records over the course of a few years with, Replicants (2011), Amanecer (2012), and Variable (2013) and recent EP Materia (2014)- enjoying critical acclaim for his ability to partner indie/electronica with his unique voice.
