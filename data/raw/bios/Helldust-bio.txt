Helldust is a catalan thrash-death metal band from Lleida (Catalonia, Spain). At first they had a different formation, but now is formed by five members. They began acting on October 2012, playing for the first time in Barcelona. Currently they're recording their first EP titled “Bloody Hole” and presenting their own songs. They're also looking for shows in all Spain.
