There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Saken was a swedish post-punk/experimental band in the early 80's.

2) Saken was a Death/Thrash metal band from Santiago, Chile. They released only one album called Hell Within in 1997. This metal act is already a cult band, their album now is a rare piece of collection, with a limited number of copies on CD and cassetes.
