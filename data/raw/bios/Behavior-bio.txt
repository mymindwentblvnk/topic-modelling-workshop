There is more than one artist called "Behavior":

1) Behavior (a.k.a Michael McCann) is an award winning composer, producer and sound designer based in Montreal, Canada. He is best known as composer for  Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Double Agent (Xbox 360, PS3) from Ubisoft, theatrical trailers for Tom Clancy's EndWar , Regenesis (opening theme), The Foundation (TV series starting in 2009), as well as composing for various shows on MTV, VH1, etc. He is also an award winning sound designer & music editor for films like  It's All Gone Pete Tong and FUBAR. Behavior's track Anywhere But Here is the theme for the dramatic TV series Regenesis, and first appeared on Behavior's 2001 debut album.  The track appears as 'Anywhere X' in the shows credits.

Behavior is currently working on the soundtrack to a major upcoming game, 2 TV series, and has just signed on with new U.S. publisher License To Thrill Music.

Visit Behavior's website for much more info and music: http://www.behaviormusic.com

2) Polish group who play a fusion of rock and hip-hop. They're also known from music in a polish cartoon for adults called "Włatcy móch".

3) New York City-based hypnagogic pop band http://www.myspace.com/behaviorband

4) Death Metal band from Brazil. They released their debut "The Awakening of Madness" in 2012.
