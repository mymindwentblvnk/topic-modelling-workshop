There are two artists with this name.

1.The first is from Quebec,Canada.
Why this name?
Endless Funeral means dying to self each day for Jesus Christ. That doesn't mean to die physically daily but as in a spiritual way. It is basically to put our desires aside in order to do please God.

Influences?
Antestor, Living Sacrifice, Extol, Slechtvalk, Immortal Souls, Narnia, Chris Pusey, Random Eyes, Vaakevandring, Harmony, Dark Tranquility, Nightwish, Jon Hooper.

2. The second is a one man band from Hermosillo, Sonora, Mexico. Funereal's lyrics deal with depression, misanthropy, suicide, and darkness.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Endless+Funeral">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
