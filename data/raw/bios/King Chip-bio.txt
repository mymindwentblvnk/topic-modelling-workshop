The Just What I Am Songfacts reports that Charles Jawanzaa Worth, better known by his stage name King Chip, is an American hip hop recording artist from Cleveland, Ohio. A friend and frequent collaborator with Kid Cudi. The Cleveland native changed his name from Chip Tha Ripper as a result of signing to the Creative Artists Agency in the summer of 2012.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/King+Chip">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
