Beach Goons is a trio of "Vatos Tristes" from el barrio Logan in south central San Diego. They write fast paced, alternative surf punk with an underlying Latin thump that reflects true authenticity unmatched by genre peers. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beach+Goons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
