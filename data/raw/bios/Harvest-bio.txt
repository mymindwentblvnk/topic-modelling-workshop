1) Harvest  was a seminal hardcore/metalcore band from Faribault, Minnesota (later relocated to Minneapolis), formed in 1994. They released two full lengths on Trustkill Records plus numerous 7"s and splits. Members have gone on to bands such as The Hope Conspiracy, Season of Fire, Krakatoa and The Good Fight.
