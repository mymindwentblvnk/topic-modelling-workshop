Ghetto House and Juke legend Traxman has a long history in the Chicago House scene with releases on record labels such as Dancemania, Ghettophiles, BTB and Moveltraxx and hits like 'Get Down Lil Momma', 'Let's Get Diz Party Crackin' and his already legendary 'Pacman Juke' track. He is part of the Geto DJz and Ghetto Teknitianz cliques. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Traxman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
