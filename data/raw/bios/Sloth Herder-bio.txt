Maryland/Pennsylvania based Grind/Black Metal band formed in Frederick, MD, in 2009. After an unreleased demo was recorded in 2010, the band recorded their self released 'Sluggard' EP in 2012. The most recent EP 'Abandon Pop Sensibility' was released 12/21/2012.
