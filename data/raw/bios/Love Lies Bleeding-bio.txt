There are multiple artists with this name: 

1) Love Lies Bleeding was a gothic black metal band from Paris, France founded in 1996. Members included Adrastis Korgan (guitars, keyboards), Ereshkigal (bass) and Emrys (vocals).

2) Love Lies Bleeding was an ethereal goth project from Melbourne, Australia, active in the 1990s. Members included Ben Pattison (electronics) and The Baroness (vocals). Also known as The Baroness and Love Lies Bleeding.
