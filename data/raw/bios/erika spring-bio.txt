As part of Brooklyn based trio Au Revoir Simone, Erika Spring has been playing joyful keyboard-based electronic pop since 2003. She began playing shows for her solo project in 2010 and has recorded with producer Jorge Elbrecht of Violens. Her first single "6 More Weeks" was released in July 2011 on a split 7" with Violens via Friendly Fire Recordings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Erika+Spring">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
