Lex One is a hip hop artist from Miami Florida, also known for being a member of the group Wizard Sleeve, and his work as a producer, DJ, videography, and he is the owner of indie label GTPS (Get That Paper Son), founded in 2006. 

Lex has worked with artists such as Freeway, Rick Ross, and N.O.R.E. and producers StreetRunner and Baby Paul from Da Beatminerz. And toured with Souls of Mischief, Bone Thugs-n-Harmony, Smoke DZA, and shared the stage with Curren$y, AZ, KRS-One, Troy Ave, Trick Daddy, Method Man, Inspectah Deck and Kool Keith.  And enjoyed name recognition from mentions by Kanye West and major hip hop blogs. 

His latest album 'Gold Plaque'  was released in 2016 and featured singles 'Walk Alone' 'They Know' and 'Ima Beast'. You can find it on iTunes, Spotify, SoundCloud and more.
