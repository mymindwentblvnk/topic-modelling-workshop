Faagrim was a German Black Metal band from Pinneberg, Schleswig-Holstein.
In 2009 they released their debut Album "Blutfrost". Their lyrics focus on Northern Germany, Winter and Vikings. They splitted up in early 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Faagrim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
