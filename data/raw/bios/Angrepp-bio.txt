Angrepp is a black / thrash metal band from Stockholm, Sweden, formed in 2006 and releasing its first demo the same year.
The band signed to Abyss Records and issued the Warfare album in 2010. It was mixed and mastered by Dan Swano. The band had a release concert on the fourth of September in Stockholm, Sweden.
