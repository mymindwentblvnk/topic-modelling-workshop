Taken by Trees is the latest project of artist Victoria Bergsman from Stockholm, Sweden.  Bergsman was singer-songwriter for The Concretes for eleven years before deciding to leave the band in July 2006.

The debut album Open Field was recorded in six days, and features Peter Bjorn and John's John Eriksson, Electrelane's Verity Sussman, and Andreas Söderström (of Ass and Tape) on guitar.
