There are many artists who go by the name Nergal:

1. A black metal band from Greece who released their debut album entitled "The Wizard of Nerath" in 1995 through Unisound Records which was produced by the band together with workaholic Magus Wampyr Daoloth. It was engineered by George Zaharopoulos and both recording and mixing took place at Storm Studio, Athens. In 2006 the band released another album entitled "Absinthos".
