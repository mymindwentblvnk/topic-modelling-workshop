Daniel Alan Tidwell was born on May 6th, 1988. At the age of fifteen, he discovered his love for the guitar and hasn't put it down since. Daniel joined bands playing guitar only six months after he began playing the instrument. His online presence began and continues to grow through video and audio of covers and original songs. Currently, Daniel is focusing on several personal musical endeavors.
