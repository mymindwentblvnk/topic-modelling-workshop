Massacra Noise is an old school death metal band from Chapelle-lez-Herlaimont, Belgium. They formed in 1989 and released 5 demos until 1991, soon after splitting off in various other directions. Most notably drummer Laurent Masse, creating (whilst at Massacra Noise) Ackros  (formerly Scatophagus) with Denis Rassart.
