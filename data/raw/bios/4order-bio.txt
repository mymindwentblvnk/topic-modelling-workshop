4order is a gothic metal-band from Helsinki, Finland.

The band describes it's music as "Ghost Metal", and boast a combined male-female vocal range of five octaves. The music is Gothic Metal with some hard rock and progressive influence, and quite peculiar, partly operatic vocals.

Current line-up:
Marie Helsingfors - Vocals
Ozzie Rissanen - Vocals
Kalle Vuorinen - Guitar
Pasi Juvonen - Guitar
Stone Ed - Drums
Norman Bass - Bass
