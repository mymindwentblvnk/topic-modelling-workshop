Piano Interrupted is the brain child of Franz Kirmann and Tom Hodge. What began as a studio project blending their respective disicplines of classical minimalism and electronica has developed, with the addition of Greg Hall on cello and Eric Young on percussion, into a fully-formed improvising quartet. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Piano+Interrupted">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
