UK / Brighton based power prog  trio, active from 2007 - 2009.  
Members were Alex Abrahams bass/ lead vocals, Colin Masterson guitar/ backing vocals and Jennie Howell drums/backing vocals/artwork (went on to join Gorse).
Bantam was a promo track for the band's debut and only EP 'At The Helm Of  Warring Minds and Unimagined Vistas', recorded at Brighton Electric Studios and engineered by Tom New and David Tindale.
