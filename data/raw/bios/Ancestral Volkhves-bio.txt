Ancestral Volkhves is a black metal band from Svidnik, Slovakia focusing on paganism and heathen themes. After a demo in 2001 they released the first full length album "Son O Iriyi" in 2004 and "Perun do Vas!!!" in 2008. Their lyrics are also in rhutenian language, so their are calling their music rhutenian metal.
