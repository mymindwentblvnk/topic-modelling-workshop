Intrusion is yet another alias of dub techno artist Stephen Hitchell - a producer and engineer of minimized to maximized sounds, determined to open a new chapter in experimentation with synthesis and sound design. Vintage, warm, ethereal analog sound, produced and recorded onto reel-to-reel tape.
