Erynies is a french metal band formed in 2000.  The linup consists of Carole Rainsard (vocals), Bertrand Breton (guitar), Jean-Christophe Breton (guitar), Guillaume Baeyens (bass), Yann (drums) and Guillaume Donizeau (keyboard). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Erynies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
