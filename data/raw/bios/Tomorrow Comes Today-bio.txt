Tomorrow Comes Today or TCT as their fans call them is an upcoming metal band from the Los Angeles area, signed to indie label, Infinite Strength.  They are a mixture of heavy metal, thrash metal,and hardcore. When you listen to TCT, you can hear influences ranging from Pantera to Arsis.     <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tomorrow+Comes+Today">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
