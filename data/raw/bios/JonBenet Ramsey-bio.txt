In 1999, this death metal band formed in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.  Their influences include Incantation and Nunslaughter  This band now only consists of two of the original members, MaggotEater who does vocals, guitars and drums and Vomit Spray is the bassist.   For more information about this band, go to their official website www.jonbenetramsey.net. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/JonBenet+Ramsey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
