Alexander Frei (born November 20, 1989), better known by his stage name Crimer, is a Swiss pop singer. His songs are stylistically inspired by the pop music of the 1980s.
Crimer was born and raised in Balgach and lived there until 2017. He started his musical career in a church choir. As a teenager, he founded the band The Axxess and later the band BrefSunAjax . He worked in an advertising agency and did community service. Today he lives in Zurich.
His solo career started under the name Batman. When the company DC Comics threatened a lawsuit, he changed his stage name to Crimer. At the end of April 2017, he released his first EP "Preach" and in 2018 his debut album "Leave Me Baby".
