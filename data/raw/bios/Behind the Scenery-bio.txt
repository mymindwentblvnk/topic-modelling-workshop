BEHIND THE SCENERY is a Göppingen (Germany) Death Metal band. Drummer Uwe Kurz also sessions for Black Metal band TEMPTAMENTUM. Following two albums for Nuclear Blast the band signed to Supreme Chaos Records for 2004's 'Rétroviseur'.

LINE UP:
Holger Spiedel (vocals)
Thomas Hauschild (guitar) 
Till van der List (guitar) 
Andy Kurz (bass)
