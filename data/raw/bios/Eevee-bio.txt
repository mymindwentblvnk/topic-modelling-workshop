Eevee is a 28 year old producer and DJ based in the Netherlands. She began producing at the end of 2013 with a unique lofi hiphop sound, and has released several EP's, including Unexpected and Seeds.

She has also released several beat tapes that are available on multiple platforms. Eevee has gained an international following and is dedicated to performing all over the world.

Her versatile skills as an artist allow her to perform live DJ sets using her sp404, Korg pad, and CDJ's. More notably, she has appeared in an interview on Noisey Vice and Revolt TV, she also has been featured on Nest HQ.
