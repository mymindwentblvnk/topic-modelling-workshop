Finally the Debut album from the Australian entity known as Omnioid, featuring members of Corpseflesh and Impure Violation. Serving up a healthy dose of twisted guitar riffing over schizophrenic drum patterns, spine rattling bass lines and Inhuman Vocals, in 14 tracks of brutal technical death metal. 
For fans of Defeated Sanity, Wormed and Disgorge.
