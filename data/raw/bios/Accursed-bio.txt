There are multiple artists called Accursed.

1. Accursed was a Death/Black Metal band from Wisconsin, USA. They released one EP and shortly after, they released their only album "Meditation among the tombs".

2. Accursed is a black metal band, hailing from Melbourne, Australia. They released one album titled "Weapon Be Thy Name" before the creativity came to an end and the band members left for other projects.
