Eternal Decay is a Black Metal band hailing from Israel (Krayot, Haifa). They were formed in 1998 and released 2 demos in 2001 and 2002 respectively. In late 2007, they posted a few videos on youtube featuring music from their upcoming album. Finally, on July 13th, 2010, Eternal Decay's first full-length album, Beyond The Ties Of Flesh, was released.

Current members:
Monarch Erhelbeta - Vocals, Guitar
Bendor Blackwolf - Keyboards, Samples
