Reamonn is a German rock and pop band. They have recorded six albums.

The band Reamonn began in 1990 in Freiburg, Germany with a classified advertisment from Rea in the paper. "Irish singer is looking for band for recording and tour". It didn't take long for Mike to contact Rea. He knew Sebi, who played in a band with Uwe. Uwe told Phil about the project they were doing and suddenly he was there too. Reamonn was born...

Reamonn is currently on hiatus, with vocalist Rea Garvey focusing on his solo career at the moment. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Reamonn">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
