Black metal band from Moscow (Russia), with some folk-ish elements a la Nokturnal Mortum. Formed under the name Pepel'nyi Svet in 1997, they soon translated their name to Ashen Light. They have released 6 albums so far. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ashen+Light">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
