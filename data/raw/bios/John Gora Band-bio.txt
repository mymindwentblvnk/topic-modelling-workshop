The John Gora Band - the Gorale (Mountaineers) also known as The Music Factory (T.M.F.), has been performing for many years at various festivals, dances and special events throughout the USA and southern Ontario.

John Gora Band can be a five, six or seven piece orchestra with instruments such as: sax, clarinet, trumpets, drums, bass, guitar, violin and of course vocals. Live music all the way no prerecorded - sequenced stuff.

The band will adapt to almost any musical style that is called for at a specific venue. The John Gora Band records only polka CD's but when it comes to live events, you can expect anything and everything. A variety of music to suit North American audiences as well as ethnic flare which includes: Polish, Ukrainian, Czech, Slovak, Slovenian, Croatian, Italian and German.
