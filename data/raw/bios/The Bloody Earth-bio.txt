The Bloody Earth was a doom metal band from Niš, Serbia. Formed in mid 90-s, released one full length Requiem of Weeping Souls in 1996, re-released as CD in 2003. Their style was influenced by My Dying Bride early releases.They disbanded probably soon after releasing their album. They had seven members:

Veselin Milojević - Vocal
Ivana Branković - Vocal
Goran Mladenović - Guitar, Keys 
Vlada Kitanović - Drums 
Milan Jovanović - Keys 
Danijel Tomić - Guitar
