Innocence Lost is a female fronted four member rock band from Hamburg, Germany. Their music is heavily influenced by classic 70's and 80's hard rock bands.

Innocence Lost produced a four track demo EP, "Too Late", in April 2007. They are currently unsigned. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Innocence+Lost">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
