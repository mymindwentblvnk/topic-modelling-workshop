Endless Night hail from Thessaloniki, Greece and were formed in 2003. Their first effort came in 2004 with the demo Demonight. Endless Night remain active and they are preparing their next demo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Endless+Night">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
