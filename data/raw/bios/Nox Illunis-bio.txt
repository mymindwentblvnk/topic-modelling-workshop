Nox Illunis is a black metal band from Treviso, Veneto, Italy. Their lyrical themes are about solitude, darkness, poetry and cosmic infinity.

Line-up:
Noxfero - vocals, guitar
Kaos - guitar
Master - drums
Tomhet - bass (2006-2010)

Discography:
2008 - Demo   
2009 - Occulta Struttura - Nox Illunis - Cenere Muto (split)   
2010 - In sideris penumbra
2012 - Metempsychosis
