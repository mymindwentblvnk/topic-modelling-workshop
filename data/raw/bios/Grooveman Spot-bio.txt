grooveman Spot a.k.a. DJ Kou-G is a Japanese DJ/producer, member of the group Enbull. He is part of the Jazzy Sport label known for its quality Jazz/Hip-Hop-inspired releases. He has released several mixtapes, albums, and EPs including the recent Re:Eternal Development (Gold Star & Silver Star), remixes of his 2006 Eternal Development LP.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grooveman+Spot">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
