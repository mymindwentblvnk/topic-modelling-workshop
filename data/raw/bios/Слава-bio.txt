Slava (Сла́ва - 15 May 1980) is a Russian singer, actress, model and public figure.
She started her career as an actress and model. Her debut in movie character in TV series "Mole".
Later she started singing career. On 1 June 2011 she won on "Muz-TV Award 2011" in nomination "Best song" ("Loneliness").
