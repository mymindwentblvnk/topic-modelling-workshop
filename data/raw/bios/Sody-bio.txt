Following the visceral rollercoaster that is recent single “Nothing Ever Changes” Sody continues her emotional journey through to resolution with “What We Had”, the next track from her forthcoming EP “I’m Sorry I’m Not Sorry”. The track demonstrates this emerging singer and songwriter’s ability to craft heart-rending pop with an emotional sincerity beyond her 19 years.
