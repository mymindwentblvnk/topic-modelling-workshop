SEPTAGON play US influenced Speed/Thrash Metal with power and melody.
Five long time active musicians (Atlantean Kodex, Lanfear etc.) who are old enough to know which way the wind blows! The debut album “Deadhead Syndicate“ should be available later this year.
Line up:
Markus Becker - vocals, Markus Ullrich - guitar, Stef Binnig-Gollub - guitar, Alexander Palma - bass, Jürgen Schrank - drums
