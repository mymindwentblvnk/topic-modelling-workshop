Bio
2004 – Formation under the name "Bards Beyond Time" and first gigs; 2005 – Release of the debut album "Spirit of the Land"; 2006 – Concerts in Switzerland and abroad, among at Kallewerk in Bad Salzungen (D) and at Rock City in Uster (CH). Renamed to "Gates of Oblivion"; 2007 – Release of the "Demo 2007"; 2008 / 2009 / 2010 – Concerts in Switzerland, song writing, several member changes; 2011 – Release of second studio album "Mirrored Reflections".

Members
Aline Bühler - Vocals, Alain Stucki - Guitars, Manuel Wunderlich - Keyboards and Vocals, Benjamin Bürgi - Drums, Sven Burri - Bass
Influences

Gates of Oblivion mixes different elements from Progressive Rock, Hard Rock, Power Metal, Gothic- and classical-influences with their very distinctive ingredients. The result is a throughout unique and characteristic style, full of surprising twists and turns – once powerful and dynamic, once soft and romantic.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gates+of+Oblivion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
