Dawn Of Orion were from Georgia, and played late 90s metalcore, with a heavy emphasis on the metal, not unlike Day Of Suffering and Undying. They released On Broken Wings 7" in 1998 on Undecided Records and full length For The Lust Of Prophecies Undone in 1999 on Immigrant Sun Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dawn+Of+Orion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
