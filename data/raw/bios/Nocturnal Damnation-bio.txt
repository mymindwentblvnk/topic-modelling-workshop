Blasphemous disgusting Blasphemy/Beherit influenced Black/Death from the depths and gutters of Seoul, South Korea. Started in 2010 by Bestial Desecrator, the bands discography has been built by himself and a slough of guest appearances, most notably the legendary Iban de Arana. Nocturnal Damnation have released 2 splits(one being a three way between Seges Findere and Purification Kommando, the other with Nihil Domination), 1 demo and one full length.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nocturnal+Damnation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
