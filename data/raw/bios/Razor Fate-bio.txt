Razor Fate History: Razor Fate was formed in the summer of 2005 when several veterans of the Northwest Arkansas metal scene decided to assemble a new musical concept to resurrect a sound that was true to their hearts- Pure Fucking Thrash Metal! For more information on Razor Fate and the Northwest Arkansas Metal scene, be sure to check out www.nwametal.com.
