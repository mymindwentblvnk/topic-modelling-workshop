2. Calvaria is a metal band from Mexico City. The band was formed by the end of 1998, throughout this time the band has been playing in the main rock stages in Mexico City and some other mexican cities trying to make a name within the Mexican scene.

 The sound takes elements from the classic Metal bands and also from other musical genres like classical and progressive, and the music comes out of heart.

 Calvaria has been opening act for well know bands like Gamma Ray (Germany), Angra (Brazil), Rhapsody (Italy), Shaman (Brazil), Rata Blanca (Argentina) and Medina Azahara (Spain) among others. The band has pleyed many gigs in Mexico as headliner and as support act.
