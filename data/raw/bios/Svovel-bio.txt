Svovel is a Norwegian symphonic metal band, combining heavy guitars, drums and bass with operatic vocals and symphonic keyboard. 
They recently went all the way to the finals of the Emergenza Festival in Norway, and were named favourites in the semi finals. Svovel rocked Rockefeller Music Hall in Oslo and got amazing reviews throughout the course of the contest.
Line up:
Jahn Erlend Holm - Keys
Karine Jæger - Vocals
Robert de Graaff - Bass
Håkon Eikeland - Guitar
