Death metal/grindcore band from Livonia, Michigan, formed in 1987 under the name Violent Intentions, changing it to Putrid Resurrection in 1989. In 1991 they released the tape "Pestering Torment" through Rotting Pus records. They attempted to record a second demo but split-up shortly after. Reformed in 2008 and the then three-piece line-up recorded Multitude of Sins, which consists of both re-worked old and brand new material. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Putrid+Resurrection">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
