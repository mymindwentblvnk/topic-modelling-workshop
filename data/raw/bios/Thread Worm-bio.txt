Metal act from Japan, featuring vocalist Seek from Velladonna fame. Hisayoshi Hiraga (Rosenfeld) also did the vocals in the past. Thread Worm released one album on Hisayoshi's label Skull Crusher, named "Parasitism", in 1995. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thread+Worm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
