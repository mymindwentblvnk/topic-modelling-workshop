Alma Mons is power metal band from Sremska Mitrovica, Serbia. Name Alma Mons is Roman name for mountain Fruska gora (english-Fertile mountain). Lyrics are mainly based on history of Romans,  especially  on history of ancient city Sirmium, the economic capital of Roman Pannonia and one of the four capital cities of the Roman Empire. Band members are: Miloš Krsmanović (vocal), Milan Živković (guitar), Krešimir Jakob (guitar), Zvonko Zablaćanski (bass), Mladen Todić (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alma+Mons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
