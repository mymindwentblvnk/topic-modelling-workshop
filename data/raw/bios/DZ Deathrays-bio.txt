DZ Deathrays are a trio dance punk band from Brisbane, Australia. Started playing house parties, will most likely end at one. Formerly DZ. 

Their debut EP, Ruined My Life was released in 2009. They released their debut album, Bloodstreams, on May 7, 2012.

Their 3rd album, Bloody Lovely, was released 2018. 

Their fourth album, Positive rising pt. 1 was launched in August 2019
