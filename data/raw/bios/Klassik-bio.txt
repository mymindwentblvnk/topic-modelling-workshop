Klassik is an independent hip hop/jazz artist out of Milwaukee, WI. Named "Artist of the Year" by 88.9 Radio Milwaukee, "Hip Hop Artist of the Year" by Wisconsin Area Music Industry as well as his single "Anything" was voted "Catchiest song of the year" by Milwaukee magazine. His first full length album #InTheMaking was released in 2012 to great critical acclaim.
