Salvador Navarrete, better known by his stage name Sega Bodega, is an Irish-born Glaswegian producer, singer, songwriter, and co-head of the NUXXE record label.

Bodega has produced for fellow NUXXE members Shygirl, Oklou, and Coucou Chloe, as well as artists like Zebra Katz, Quay Dash, and Dorian Electra. His own releases include the 2018 EP self*care, its 2020 album follow-up Salvador and his 2021 album,  Romeo <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sega+Bodega">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
