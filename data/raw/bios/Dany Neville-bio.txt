Dany Neville is an award winning DJ/Music Producer that has performed in the entertainment capitals of the world, as well as the leading venues of his home country, the UAE. From collaborations featuring the worlds most respected musicians to selling out the the regions biggest venues, he has seen and experienced it all. 

It started with his passion and determination to introduce a healthy and thriving entertainment industry in the UAE. In the summer of 2000, Dany Neville lands his flagship radio program on Radio One making him the voice of the first urban radio program in the UAE, as well as the youngest on air personality in the region. Sixteen years later, Dany Neville turns a new page by joining the UAE's leading radio station, Virgin Radio Dubai.
