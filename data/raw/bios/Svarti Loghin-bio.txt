Svarti Loghin is a black metal band from Sweden. Formed in 2006 and are currently signed with ATMF. 
They have three full length albums and an acoustic EP and one demo.

S.L - Vocals, Guitar, Keyboards (Faustus (Swe))
Raymond N. - Bass (ex-Död)
Emil - Drums (ex-Död)
