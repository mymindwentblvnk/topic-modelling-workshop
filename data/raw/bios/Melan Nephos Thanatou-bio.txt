Melan Nephos Thanatou was formed in 1999 in Abruzzo, Italy. The band performed live, but only released one demo before splitting up. "Black Ritual For Vempire's Revenge" was released in 2002 with four of their tracks. The last known line-up contained Andrea on vocals, Rikkardo on guitar, Paolo on drums, Domenico on bass, and Luca on keyboard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Melan+Nephos+Thanatou">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
