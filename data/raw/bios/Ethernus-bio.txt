Ethernus is a black metal band from Baia Mare, Romania found by Andrei Sălăgean.
Lineup: Andrei Sălăgean - Drums, Bass Guitar, Keyboards, Synthesizer, Piano, Harp, Lead/Rhythm Guitar, Clean/Harsh/Growl Vocals;
Cristian Bobu (bob vs bob) - Lead/Rhythm Guitar;
Nora Deneş - Violin & Female Vocals.

Composer - Andrei Sălăgean
Songwriter - Andrei Sălăgean
