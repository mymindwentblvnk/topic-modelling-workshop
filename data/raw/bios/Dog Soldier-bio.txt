Three bands by this name:

1. Progressive blues-rock, a spin-off of the Keef Hartley Band featuring Hartley and KHB guitarist Miller Anderson. After their one album (1975, for United Artists), bass player and singer Paul Bliss formed the Bliss Band.

2. Hardcore punk band spun off from Blood Spit Nights.

3. Michigan, USA rockers, that released "Name Your Poison" in 1988.   Their sophomore album "Shoot To Kill" was released in 2022, more than 30 years after it was first recorded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dog+Soldier">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
