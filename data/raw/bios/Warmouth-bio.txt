There are two bands that use the name Warmouth.
1. A Metal/Hardcore/Punk band from Yogyakarta, Indonesia
2. A Melodic Death Metal band from “De Bollenstreek” in South-Holland.

1. Warmouth started as a 3-piece at the end of 2013. Formed by Made Dharma after resigning from his previous Grindcore band (Deadly Weapon), Rahmat Gunawan (Energy Nuclear/Maur) and Iwan Kurniawan. The band has released a 2 track demo in January 2014 and play a mix of Metal/Hardcore/Punk of style.
