Stokeley Clevon Goulbourne (born April 18, 1996), better known by his stage name Ski Mask The Slump God (formerly stylized as $ki Mask ''The Slump God''), and Formerly Yung Ski Mask is an american rapper from Fort Lauderdale, Florida. He is best known for his songs Catch Me Outside and BabyWipe.

Ski previously collaborated with fellow Florida rap artist XXXTENTACION, forming the group Members Only and released three mixtapes and performed on a nationwide U.S. tour. Shortly after, the group fell into hiatus due to beef between the two but was resolved before XXXTENTACION s untimely death.

Outside of Members Only, Ski has three EPs, two commercial mixtapes, and one studio album.
