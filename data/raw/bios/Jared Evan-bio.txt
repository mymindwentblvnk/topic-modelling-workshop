Jared Evan, is an American musician, singer/songwriter, producer, rapper. He was born on September 21, 1988 in Nassau County, New York. The Light Songfacts reports that in 2008 at the age of 19, he won the Brooklyn Hip Hop Festival's Spit 16 competition, which was curated by The Source magazine. Off the strength of his performance, Evan was invited to perform several shows including an opening gig for Lupe Fiasco  in the fall of 2008.
He is currently signed to Zone 4 (record label) under Interscope Records. The song "Frozen" which Jared wrote and produced, appeared on the Lebron James documentary soundtrack entitled "More Than A Game". In 2010 Evan's debut street single "In Love With You," produced by Polow Da Don, was featured on the HBO series Entourage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jared+Evan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
