Htiekal is a musical surname by entertainer LaKeith Stanfield, who has also gone under other monicker Moors. He released his first single under this name “Fast Life” on Valentine’s Day of 2020, with a video to accompany. Later that year, he released “Birds” feat rapper Git, who he has collaborated with before on “Do Better”, which was released under his birth name. The single was released along with a vertical music video. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Htiekal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
