There are multiple artists with the name "In Reverence".

1) In Reverence was a four piece hardcore band formed in Eau Claire, WI in 2004. They disbanded in 2006 and members have gone on to play in Solidarity, Regret, Meridene and Dios Mio.

2) In Reverence is a Swedish metal band from Stockholm. In earlier material they played deathcore, but they eventually evolved into a progressive, atmospheric death metal sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/In+Reverence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
