Black/Death Metal band from Split, Croatia formed in 2010 with lyrics focusing on judeo-christian Blasphemy, Satanism and Desecration... Most of them are written in Croatian. Among many influences are bands like Archgoat, Blasphemy, Black Witchery, Bestial Warlust and Beherit. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cidium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
