Bluten is a Slovakian black metal band with one member of Immortal Hammer. They released some demos and a collection of material titled "Years of Hate and Misanthropy" all around the early 2000s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BLUTEN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
