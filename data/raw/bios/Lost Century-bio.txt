Lost Century is a progressive thrash band from Germany. The band makes use of odd vocal patterns (mixing regular singing with growls) and numerous accoustic interludes. The band had a music video for the song "Like The One Above" and after two albums and an EP the band parted ways <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lost+Century">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
