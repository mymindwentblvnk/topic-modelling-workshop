Shadar Logoth is a Symphonic/Black Metal band from Russia(Moscow-city).  It's one of young Black Metal bands in the Moscow. "Shadar Logoth" is named after a dread city  occupied by a living, mindless, consuming, evil, fog-like entity called Mashadar (in the Robert Jordan's fantasy series The Wheel of Time).
Shadar Logoth was formed in 2003 by Ilya(Regnator) and Alexandr(Vedymak).   
After one year of composing, demo-recordings and some live acts they recorded their first CD, called “Sword above the silence” at Nocturnal MMS studio. At the end of 2005 they recorded “Repent. Believe. Salvation in plague.” (EP), released by N.Production.
