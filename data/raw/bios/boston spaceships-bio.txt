Boston Spaceships is a band led by Guided by Voices frontman Robert Pollard. Other members are Chris Slusarenko (Takeovers, Guided By Voices, Svelt, Sprinkler) and John Moen (Perhapst, The Decemberists, Jicks, Elliott Smith, Dharma Bums).

Their debut album, Brown Submarine (recorded in Portland, OR and Kent, OH in May 2008) was released September 9, 2008 on the Guided By Voices, Inc label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Boston+Spaceships">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
