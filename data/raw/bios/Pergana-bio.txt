Formed in 2007 by songwriter and keyboardist Adrian Benegas together with two friends. Alex Holzwarth and Oliver Holzwarth, joined the band as special guests in 2008 and one year later the french singer Audrey Laure and the paraguayan guitarist Mauro Mercado joined Pergana, replacing the former members.

Later on, in 2010, Pergana released their first promotional single FROZEN HEART (2010), introducing the band to the metal scene and getting ready for the release of their debut EP “The Mirror of Silence” wich was released on June 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pergana">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
