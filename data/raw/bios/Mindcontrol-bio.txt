MindControl is a Hungarian Death metal band formed in 1995 in Tatabánya, Hungary with the purpose to combine death metal with hard core grooves. After many demos (Negative Mechanism, Inside, Stereo Mind Energy, Under Control, Sunflower), concerts, tours (TANKCSAPDA, NECK SPRAIN, WACKOR) and member exchange they disbanded in 2003. 

The band was reunited in 2007 and after finding a new drummer they recored the "This Is The Noise That Keeps Us Alive" EP later this year.

Band members:
Banán - vocals
Jansi - guitar, audio effects
Geri - drums
