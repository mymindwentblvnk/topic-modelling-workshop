There are four groups with the name Nightlight.

1) Nighlight is a Danish gothic rock/metal band started by singer and song writer Martin Steene in 2005. Other members: Nikolaj Ihlemann: Guitar, Hagel: Drums, Martin Lund: Bass, Kasper Langmann: Guitar, *Guest member on the album: Anders Nielsen: Keyboards(from Saturnus). 

official site: http://www.myspace.com/nightlightofficial 

2) Nightlight was also an early-1990s Amiga demoscene musician.  His "breakthrough" track was likely "Chant" from a Kefrens demo, though his tracks "Dirt" and "RainyDay" also garnered some attention.

3) Nightlight is also a Christian rock gospel band from Fife in Scotland. 

4) Nightlight is an indie pop band from Guildford, Surrey, UK.
