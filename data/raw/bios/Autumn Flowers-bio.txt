Autumn Flowers is a Melodic Death Metal band from Brazil formed in 1998.
They released a Demo in 2000 and a full length album titled "Of Essence and Nightmare" in 2010. The band split up after releasing their first album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Autumn+Flowers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
