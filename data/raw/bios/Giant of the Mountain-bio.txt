Giant of the Mountain is a trio that formed in late 2008 in Plano, Texas. They blend many different sounds from various genres of metal to create their progressive black metal sound.

Discography:
'EP' (2010)
'Mother Hydra' (2011)
'Valley of the Rogue' (2012)
'Moon Worship' (2013)
