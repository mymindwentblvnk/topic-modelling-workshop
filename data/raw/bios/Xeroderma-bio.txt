Xeroderma is a Black / Death Metal band from Atlanta Georgia that was formed in late 2007 by Halcyon Way Bassist Kris Maltenieks. Newly reformed with Halcyon Way Drummer Ernie Topran, & Ex-Lead Guitarist from Apocalyptic Visions, Jason Lalonde. Recent to date adding new guitar player Drew Collum to the line up
