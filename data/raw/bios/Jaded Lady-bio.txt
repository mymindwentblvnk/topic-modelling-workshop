Jaded Lady was an all female heavy metal band formed in 1985 in Los Angeles, California. 
The band started under the name Leather Angel.
In 1987, Jaded Lady landed a deal with micro metal label Apocalypse for a full-length album, but the label folded before they even got to record, and the band parted ways soon after.

Last known line-up:
Terry O'Leary - Vocals 
Sylvia Cei - Guitars (1987- )
Cathy Amanti - Bass 
Mary Jo La Roche - Drums (1986- )
