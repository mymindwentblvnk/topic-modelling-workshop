Existend were a Rock/Metal band from Sheffield, England.
Originally consisting of Dave Todd (vocals), Harry Griffiths (guitar), Michael Redman (bass), Sam Brown (drums) and Stefan Blackwood (guitar). Dave left the band in mid 2006 to pursue other personal commitments, and after much auditioning for vocalists, the band found a replacement on October 20th 2006, Ant Leathwood. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Existend">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
