"Neophron" - death-metal band formed in Yaroslavl in 2000 year. 
Over time the band has survived through so many changes in the composition and a few long breaks in their work. In 2002 it was recorded the first demo that has received good reviews in "Dark City" and the Belarusian "M" (ex-Legion). 
Since 2006, the group begins to conduct sufficiently active concert activity. 
May 21, 2009 the band released on their own debut album "Icons' Weeping".
