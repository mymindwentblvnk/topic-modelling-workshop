Burning Winds is a one man black metal band hailing from the USA.

Lyrical themes include Satanism, Death and Destruction. The band has released a multitude of demos and splits but only has 2 full length albums to date.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burning+Winds">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
