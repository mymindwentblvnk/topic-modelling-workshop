Newcomers to the doom capital, Leviathan A.D. came together in 2001 in Hagerstown, MD and combined ultra down-tuned doom metal with post-punk melody and hardcore ferocity. Comprised of guitarist Dave (Beyond Fatal), singer / guitarist Adam (ex-Strong Intention), drummer Andy (ex-Strong Intention), and bassist Nathan, Leviathan A.D. recorded and released the 3 song demo, Verthithrax Apocalypse, in 2001.
