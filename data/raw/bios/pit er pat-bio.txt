Pit Er Pat are a Chicago rock band consisting of keyboardist Fay Davis-Jeffers, drummer Butchy Fuego, and bassist Rob Doran. The band has released three full length albums on Thrill Jockey. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pit+Er+Pat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
