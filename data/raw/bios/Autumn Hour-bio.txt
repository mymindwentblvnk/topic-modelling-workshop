AUTUMN HOUR , the new band featuring vocalist Alan Tecchio ( HADES , WATCHTOWER , NON-FICTION , SEVEN WITCHES ), drummer Dave Lescinsky ( HADES ), bassist Clint Arent ( PAINMUSEUM , SEVEN WITCHES ), and guitarist Justin Jurman ,  released its debut CD, "Dethroned"  . Already garnering attention for its cover of the EURYTHMICS ' "Here Comes the Rain Again" , the group combines a unique blend of metal, hard rock, and progressive music with a lyrical concept about a mechanized future. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Autumn+Hour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
