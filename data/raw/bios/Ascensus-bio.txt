Ascensus is a band from Geraardsbergen (Belgium).
The band originally started as a Thrash/Blackmetalband in 2006.
After the band split-up in 2008 there was a time of silence.Some of the members started a new project under the same name in 2009.

The new band changed their influences and genre. Nowadays they play Old school Rock'n Roll with some Heavy and Thrash influences.

The band split up in early 2010.

2 of the members are currently playing in a Rock'n Roll band called Antagonizer.

Most recent line-up:
Ooghakk(Amaury): Lead guitar and Vocals
Count Clitoris(Floris): Bass guitar
Dikkelul(Thomas): Rythm and Lead guitar
Sweykr: Drums
