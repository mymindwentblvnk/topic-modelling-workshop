There are four bands with this name:

1) An alias of experimental artist Akiko Shikata.

2) Goddess was Elvira Valentine, with production from the Bolland brothers.  
Valentine was also part of duos Say When! and Club Risque.

3) A sludge rock band from Oslo, Norway. (2010-)
