Baptised in Ice is a viking metal band from Brazil, São Paulo, formed in 2000. They've released 2 demo albums, one EP and one full length album.

Line up:
Luciano Scanhoela : Vocals, Guitar, Bass
Marcelo Oliveira : Keyboard
Francisco Lemes : Guitar
