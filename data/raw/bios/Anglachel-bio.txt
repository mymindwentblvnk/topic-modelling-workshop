1) Anglachel is a black metal band formed in N.S.W., Australia by M. originally under the name "Mors Mortis" in 2008. Trise joined on drums in 2009. And finnally Ex. was recruited in 2010. The band plays possibly some of the rawest, evil black metal ever.

Band contact: http://www.myspace.com/morsmortisaus


2) Anglachel was a Heavy Metal band from São Paulo, Brazil, formed in 2001. The band had several names, like Drakeron, before finally choosing the name Anglachel. After multiple line-up changes and poor reviews in Brazilian magazines they decided to halt their activities after the realease of their last record in 2005 due to the lack of interest of the media. The last known line-up included Daniel Quintino as vocalist, Christian Pereira on guitars and Fábio Souza on drums.
