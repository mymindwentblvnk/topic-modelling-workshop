Necrokult of Kronos was a Portuguese Death/Black Metal. Active from 2002-2005, they recorded a demo called Apeiron: The Anaerobic Life Forms and an album - Necrokult of Kronos. After recording, the band split and its members pursued other musical endeavours. Ten years after being recorded, the album Necrokult of Kronos finally saw the light of day.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necrokult+of+Kronos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
