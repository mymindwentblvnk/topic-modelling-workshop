The Devlins are an indie rock band originating from Dublin, Ireland. Their most recognized songs are "Waiting", featured on the Six Feet Under soundtrack and "World Outside", which was included in the 2004 drama Closer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Devlins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
