There are two bands with the name Decreation.
(1) Decreation, Melodic Black/Death Metal, United States.
(2) Decreation, Melodic Death Metal, Sweden.

Decreation (2), from Sweden, was founded in 2010 and released their first demo album 'The Pandemonium Light' in 2011.


Lineup, Sweden (2013):
Lisa Delin - vocals
Axel Gustafsson - guitars
Lars Hultman - guitars, studio session bass
