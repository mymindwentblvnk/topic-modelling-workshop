There are several artists named Yosh.

1) Yosh is a french dub band from Nantes formed in the year 2002. The music of the band is a mixing between rock, drum'n'bass and hip-hop with a heavy dub base.
Yosh recorded a maxi cd 4 tracks, a maxi vynil 3 tracks and an album "Big Trouble" in 2004. The next album will be available this autumn.

2) Yosh is a belgian thrash metalband formed in 1988. They recorded 3 albums.

3) Yosh was a dutch DJ who in 1995 and 1996 had two minor UK Top 40 hits, the first being "It's What's Upfront That Counts" and later "The Screamer".
