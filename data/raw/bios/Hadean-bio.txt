Hadean (pronounced: [ˈheɪdiən], the first geologic eon in Earth's history.)
There are a few artists with this name:

1. Hadean is a Post Metal/Progressive Metal band from New Bedford, Massachusetts USA.

2. Hadean is a Heavy Metal band from Cologne, Germany.

3. Hadean is a one-man project with the main objective of attempting to recreate musical ideas and to improve the following skills during the creation process: Vocals, guitar play, songwriting and home recording.

Beware: The first couple of songs may sound primitive/amateurish/boring/incoherent :>
