Gunbridge is a Japanese power metal band, only known for their speed track entitled "Gladiator". This band has been created by Yama-B (Galneryus, AxBites & Rekion), he performs vocals and all instruments. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GUNBRIDGE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
