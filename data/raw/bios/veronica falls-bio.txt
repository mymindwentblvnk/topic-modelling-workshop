Veronica Falls are an indie pop band which formed in London, England, United Kingdom in 2009. The band consists of Roxanne Clifford (vocals, guitar), James Hoare (guitar, vocals), Marion Herbain (bass) and Patrick Doyle (drums, vocals). The members of the band were formerly in the bands The Royal We and Sexy Kids. The band released several singles in the United Kingdom and the United States throughout 2010. Their debut self-titled album 'Veronica Falls' was released on September 2011 via Slumberland Records.

2012 saw the first two advances from the second album, the single "My heart beats" and "Teenager". "Waiting For Something to Happen", was released on February 12 through Slumberland/Bella Union, and recorded with Rory Attwell.

In February 2013, the band released their second album, Waiting for Something to Happen. It was co-produced by Rory Attwell. The album's release was preceded by the "My Heart Beats" and "Teenage" singles in 2012. A music video for "Teenage" was released in November 2012.

Since 2014, individual members of the band have been involved in separate projects. James Hoare formed The Proper Ornaments with Max Claps and released the album Wooden Heads in 2014. He now co-leads the band Ultimate Painting. Drummer Patrick Doyle worked under the name Boys Forever and released a self-titled album on 5 August 2016. Singer Roxanne Clifford began to release music as Patience in 2016.
Patrick Doyle died in March 2018.
