Mitosis is a lesser known alias for Sam Gonzalez, a hardcore producer & engineer originally from Sydney, Australia. Sam has achieved success internationally with tracks on various Japanese compilations, some of which featured in Konami's DDR series of games and in one of Burger King's advertising campaigns.
