Misanthropic black metal from Spain.  Released a demo (First Assault Against Christianity) in 2004, and a split album with Kingdom of Fog in 2005. The band split up after this release. 
 
Misantrok also participated in the "Misanthropic Spirit Part II Compilation" by Underground Propaganda playing the song "Masacre ejecutada con mano dura, no existe la piedad " (5:10).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Misantrok">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
