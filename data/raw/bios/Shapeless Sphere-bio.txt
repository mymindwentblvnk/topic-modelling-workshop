This death metal / groove metal outfit is from Trieste, Friuli-Venezia Giulia, Italy.  They formed in the year 2015.  They consist of  Filip Ribič (vocals),  Massimiliano Novak (rhythm guitar),  Giona Trame (lead guitar),  Eddie Maffezzoli (bass) and  Oles Deyneka (drums).  Oles Deyneka and  Massimiliano Novak are the founders of the band.  They founded the band after the demise of Tryve. Shapeless Sphere is currently unsigned and they released one album called Shieldead in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shapeless+Sphere">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
