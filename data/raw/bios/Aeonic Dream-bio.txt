Aeonic Dream is a progressive power-trashmetal band from Fredrikstad (Norway). They are very young and talented (average age 15). They have given out one EP/demo called "Into The Realm Nocturnal" which was released summer 2007. 


The band consists of:
Martin Bjølstad (Vocals)
Kristian Bjørge (Lead Guitar)
Ådne Kolberg Stene (Rythm Guitar)
Trym Aleksander Eiterjord (Bass)
Pål Talos Absusdal (Drums)
Jarle Johannesen (Synth)
