Demon in Veils was a raw Pagan Black Metal Band from Schaffhausen, northern Switzerland. Found in 2003, the band split up in 2006 after have released a Demo (Demo 2004) and a four Song EP called "Vintersfrost" (2005). Their most popular song was " A Tale of the Vikings", although it was pretty much Finntroll inspired and not typical for the band.
