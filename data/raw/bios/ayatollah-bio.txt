Ayatollah (Lamont Dorrell) is a hip-hop producer from Queens, New York, with a soulful sound who has provided beats for many of the big names in the genre, perhaps most notably Ms. Fat Booty for Mos Def. He has nine full length instrumental releases, including: Listen, Now Playing, Drum Machine, The Quixotic, Cocoon and the newly released Fingertips. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ayatollah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
