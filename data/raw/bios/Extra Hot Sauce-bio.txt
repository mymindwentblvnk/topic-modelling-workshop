Howie (vocals), Eric Sandalic (guitar), Rob (bass), Dan Lilker (drums)

Side project act of ANTHRAX, SOD, HEMLOCK, NUCLEAR ASSAULT and BRUTAL TRUTH bassist Dan Lilker. EXTRA HOT SAUCE's 1988 album 'Taco Of Death' included a version of BLACK SABBATH's 'Paranoid' compacted down to a mere 30 seconds. Glenn Evans of NUCLEAR ASSAULT acted as producer and contributed lead vocals to a rendition of LED ZEPPELIN's 'Communication Breakdown'.

Erstwhile EXTRA HOT SAUCE guitarist Eric Sandalic later adopted bass guitar for his role in New York Death Metal band EXUVIATE, a band which mutated into GROTESQUEUPHORIA, SANGUINARY CARNAGE then BUTCHERY.
