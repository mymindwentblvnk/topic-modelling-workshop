Secrets of the Sky was  a black/death metal band from Oakland, California. After forming in 2010, they would release two albums:  To Sail Black Waters and Pathway before splitting up in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Secrets+Of+The+Sky">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
