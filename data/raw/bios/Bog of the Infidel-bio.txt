Bog of the Infidel is a raw Black Metal band from Providence, Rhode Island USA comprised of members from the band Nefarious. Currently, Bog of the Infidel have a self titled demo which was released in 2009.

Current Line Up:
Satanist - Vocals (also in the band Nefarious)
Architeuthis - Guitar (also in the band Nefarious)
Bloodfuck - Guitar (also in the bands Nefarious, Sorcery)
Bassassin - Bass
