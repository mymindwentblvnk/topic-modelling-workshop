Revengance is a solo project mixing Techno, Trance, Dance, Electro, Rave, Downbeat, and others such as these. Revengance also works under the name of DJ Threat. Everything is done on a few different computer programs. He refuses to release his name even though some people already know who it is. He has self released everything. His first release was a 9 track split demo with him and a friend of his which they released under their real names. It was released back when they were in middle school. The next release was a 4 track EP entitled The Open Door Fallacy which was under the name DJ Threat. Later this was reissued under the name Revengance and 2 tracks were added.. Revengance is currently working on The Opendoor Fallacy 2 (Title is not final) and it is expected to be released later this year. Influences come from Aphex Twin, Squarepusher, The Chemical Brothers, and Dntel.
