Testator is a thrash metal band from southern Serbia.

Members:
Marko "Pupak" Jocić - Vocals
Aleksandar "Guta" Gutović -Guitar
Danijel "Mastemlord" Stanković - Guitar
Aleksandar "Anta" Antić- Bass Guitar
Milan "Snagg" Savić - Drums
