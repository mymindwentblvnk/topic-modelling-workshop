The Crookes consist of George Waite, Daniel Hopewell, Russell Bates and Tom Dakin. They are set to release their second L.P on legendary indie label Fierce Panda this summer. The band have built up a Cult Underground following known as the "Bright Young Things" and are at the forefront of the nascent NEW POP movement in the UK guitar scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Crookes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
