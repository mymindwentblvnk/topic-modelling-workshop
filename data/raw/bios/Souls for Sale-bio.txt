Souls For Sale is a four piece outfit consisting of former members of The Getaway Drivers,  What Never Dies and Marjorie. The band was formed in 2007 in Bielefeld, Germany. They played their first show on October, 14 2K7 @ the AJZ Bielefeld with Sinking Ships, Ruiner, Just Went Black, Storm and Stress and The Weight. Thereafter they went to record a two-song demo entitled The Gold Demo. The vinyl only release of their debut-EP All My Favorite Love Songs Are By Cannibal Corpse followed in October 2010. Souls For Sale's debut album Scavengers is set to be released in March 2013 via WOOAAARGH, Olympus Mons and Puzzle Records.
