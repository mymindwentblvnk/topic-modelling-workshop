1- Airplay released their self-titled album in 1980, but the members went on to much bigger things. Airplay were David Foster on keyboards, Jay Graydon on guitar and vocals, and Tommy Funderburk with background vocals. They were joined by two members of Toto: drummer Jeff Porcaro and bassist David Hungate. Ray Parker Jr., known for his hit title track from the movie Ghostbusters, provides additional guitar backing.

2- Airplay is a disco act produced by Andreas Martin Krause. Singles: "For Your Love"

3- Airplay are Danny Corten and Eddy Weyns, electronic trance duo.
