Legrev was formed in Santiago, Chile in 2006-2007, as a solo project by Throudos (Not to be confused with his side project  (Drone/Doom) named as himself  ''Throudos''). In the same year, Throudos search other musicians to make live performances, in that year Mørket integrate the band as drummer. In 2007 record their first demo entitled ‘’Winter Seasons’’ wich conteined 4 tracks. In 2008 started reheasals and composings of new songs for a future album.

Members: Throudos: Vocals, All Instruments
