Savage Harmony is a Croatian metal band which gathered in october 2002 under the name "New Millennium". After a lot of hard work the band changed its name to "Savage Harmony" and started to play real power metal. The band was originally formed by Robertino Fabing, Sasa Cvitkusic and Goran Radocaj. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Savage+Harmony">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
