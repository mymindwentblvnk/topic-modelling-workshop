Betrayel is an American thrash metal band that existed from the mid to late 80s. The appeared on the compilation "Raging Death" (Godly Records) with Xecutioner (pre-Obituary), Lethal Presence, R.A.V.A.G.E. (pre-Atheist), and Sadus. They also released two demos in 1987 and 1989. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Betrayel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
