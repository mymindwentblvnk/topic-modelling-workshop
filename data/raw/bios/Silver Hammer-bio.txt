Silver Hammer is a defunct solo project by Isaac Domagalski. The music has elements of folk metal and thrash. A demo called "March Into Battle" has been recorded. Only seven copies of this demo have been made. Another demo called Bloody Sword was made. Isaac describes the project in a blog. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/silver+hammer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
