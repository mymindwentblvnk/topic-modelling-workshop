Omery Rising is a Heavy Metal band from Fresno, CA. Formed by Michael McClanahan and David Ramos in 2007 with the idea to bring back true Heavy Metal. Since the formation of the band they have released their self-titled EP in September of 2013 via MGR records, their very first single "Lady Snow' in April of 2014 and now their debut album "The Rising" in August of 2015. Now with a full length album under their belt, Omery is ready to take over the world... one city at a time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Omery+Rising">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
