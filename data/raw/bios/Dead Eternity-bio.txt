(2007-2010)

Hungarian melodic death metal act DEAD ETERNITY was formed by guitarists Peti Kulcsár and Atheist in 2007.
In summer 2008 the band released their first demo which contains 3 songs and was recorded at a poorly equipped homestudio.
Between 2007 and 2009 Dead Eternity played only five shows due to drummer issues.
