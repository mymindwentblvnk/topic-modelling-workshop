Tyskland is a NS black metal band from Thuringia, Germany. Tyskland means "Germany" in several Scandinavian languages. They released a demo in 2002 and the full length album "Cild" in 2004. Deimos also plays in the NS black metal band Totenburg.
Current line-up:
Metus: vocals, guitar
Deimos: guitar
