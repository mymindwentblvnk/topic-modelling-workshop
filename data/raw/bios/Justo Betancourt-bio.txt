Justo Betancourt (b. December 6, 1940) is a Cuban singer famous for his interpretation of Pa' Bravo Yo. He was born in Matanzas, but has lived a significant amount of time in the United States and Puerto Rico. His music shows Cuban influence but developed in the direction of Puerto Rican salsa.
