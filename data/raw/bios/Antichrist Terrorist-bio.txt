Antichrist Terrorist is a 'one man' black metal band formed in Sternberk, CZ in 1998. The original lineup consisted of 'one man' Loki. He released one demo tape called '.. nikdy nepoklekneme před ukřižovaným'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antichrist+Terrorist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
