Agony Lords - death metal group formed in 1989, in Mexico  (Irapuato, Guanajuato) as Ripping Flesh. Their lyrics are about death, pain, apocalypse. Re-united in 2008. 

Current line-up:

Jesus Bravo - Vocals 
Mauricio Barami - Guitar
Oscar Lopez - Bass
Oscar Pinon - Drums
Jorge - Guitar 

Former/past members:

Hector Garcia: Bass
Saul Flores - Guitar 

Discography:

(as Ripping Flesh)

1990 - Rehearsal/Demo [4/2/90] (Demo)
1991 - Parallel Windows (Demo)
1992 - Mercy (Single)

(as Agony Lords)

1993 - Unions (Demo)
1997 - The Sun Of The Cursed (Full-length) 
1998 - Ab Imo Pectore / Unions (Split) 
1999 - Nihilist Passion (Full-length)
