Mast is the brainchild of multi-instrumentalist/composer/producer Tim Conley. Recently resettling from Philadelphia to the bountiful beat scene of LA, Mast is most know for his main collaborative project w/ RYAT which will be releasing her latest record on Flying Lotus' Brainfeeder label May 2012. With RYAT, Tim has collaborated on 3 records and toured extensively throughout the US, Canada and Europe. He is also known as a virtuoso guitarist and has toured w/ Grammy award winning Digable Planets and recorded w/ King Britt. In 2004 Tim released a conceptual jazz fusion album as composer and guitarist inspired by the ocean and its metaphors titled Ocean Exposition. Along his journey he has shared the stage w/ the Flaming Lips, Flying Lotus, Diplo, Bernie Worrell(P. Funk, Talking Heads) Marco Benevento, Saturn Never Sleeps and members of Thievery Corp etc... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mast">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
