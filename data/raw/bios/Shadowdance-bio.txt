Shadowdance is a progressive power metal band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. They formed in 1999 and released their debut album, Ageless, in 2000. An EP entitled Another Look followed in 2003. Though never breaking up, the band's second full-length album would not come out for another nine years. 

On May 1, 2012, the band released their long-awaited second full-length album, Future Negative Fantasy. 

Shadowdance is currently completely reworking the 1st album "Ageless", completely remixed & remastered with additional recording for a late 2012 / early 2013 release <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shadowdance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
