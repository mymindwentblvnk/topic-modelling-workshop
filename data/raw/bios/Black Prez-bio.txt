Black Prez is a LA / Berlin based rapper, songwriter, and actor. He has performed across the globe including doing shows with Wiz Khalifa, Big Sean amongst others. His music can also be heard on Tony Hawk's Pro Skater, Fast & Furious, Netflix, Call of Duty, Power, Empire, MTV, BET, HBO, NFL Network, and many more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Prez">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
