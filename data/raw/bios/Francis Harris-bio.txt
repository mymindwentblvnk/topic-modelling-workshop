Francis Harris is the real name of Brooklyn based artist Adultnapper. After releasing a number of EPs under his own name, Harris released his first LP in 2012 titled Leland. The album has been sighted for it's extensive use of live instruments, a contrasting feature to his work done under the Adultnapper moniker. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Francis+Harris">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
