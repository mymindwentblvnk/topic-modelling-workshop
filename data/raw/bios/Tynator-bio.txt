Tynator is a Thrash Metal band from Phoenix, Arizona, USA.

They formed in 1987 an relased four demos and only one Full-Length: Living In Pain, in 1989.

As of 2011 the band has reunited, and has commenced working on new material for a fall to winter 2011 release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/tynator">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
