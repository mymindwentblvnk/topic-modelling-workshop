Mossfet was a Dutch band playing a mixture of different alternative music styles.
They released one (self-financed) demo-CD called "Blind Man" in 2004.

Line-up:
Johan "Jo" Vos - Vocals (Size 10)
Johan "Coos" Cozijnsen - Bass, Backing Vocals
Gerben van Aalst - Guitar (Size 10)
Andre - Guitar
Tijs v.d. Boogaard - Drums (ex-Noizgate, Show of Force)
