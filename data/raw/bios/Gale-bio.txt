Gale are a 4-piece doom, sludge, post-metal band from Phoenix, Arizona. They released their debut album titled "Vol. 1" in 2013 followed by a self-titled full length album in 2019. Both are available at https://gale.bandcamp.com.

Members: Brandon Tutty - guitar & vocals, Marcus Bryant - drums & vocals, Nick Gutenson - bass & vocals, Wilson Hensleigh - guitar
