The band was founded in August 2005, since the beginning they play an epic and dark Heavy Metal, raw and noble at the same time.
In October 2005 the band takes over the name "Red Warlock", they begin to work on their first demo cd "Rising From Hell".
In September 2009 the band finishes the recording sessions of a new Promo CD titled "Sabrewolf", and their first full-length "Serve Your Master" is released December 6th, 2010 by My Graveyard Productions.
