There are 2 artists from the United Kingdom and one from Russia with this name.

1) Sapphire are an EDM duo from the UK consisting of Richard Simmonds and Stephen Jones who are also responsible for projects such as Ascension, Chakra, Lustral and The Space Brothers.

2) Sapphire is a talented child pop singer and vocalist who sings many cover songs along with some original songs as well.  http://www.sapphireofficial.com/

3) Sapphire was the alias of liquid drum & bass producer Alexander Golubenko from Yeisk. He has changed his alias to Nuvertal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sapphire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
