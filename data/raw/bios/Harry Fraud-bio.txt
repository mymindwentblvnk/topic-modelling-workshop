Harry Fraud is a Hip-Hop producer from Brooklyn, New York. He's worked with a wide-variety of artists since 2010, including Jadakiss, Prodigy, The Kid Daytona, Sean Price, Action Bronson, Riff Raff, French Montana, Shabaam Sahdeeq, Punchline, Cappadonna, Awkword, Vast Aire, Mayhem Lauren, Maffew Ragazino, Ill Bill, and many more.

Fraud dropped his first compilation under his own name, Adrift, in March 2013. It features rappers such as Rick Ross, Curren$y, Action Bronson and Wiz Khalifa. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Harry+Fraud">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
