ST1M is a German/Russian rapper and the former WiStation member. He is best known for the songs like "I am Rap", "Take A Look At My Eyes", "Der Krieg", and many others. With his former group WiStation, ST1M traveled all over Switzerland, Germany and Austria. He made a great influence in modern hip hop culture.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/St1m">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
