Svneatr is a black metal band from Vancouver, Canada. They play a blend of melodic, atmospheric, and progressive black metal and are, in their own words, "the sound of nature screaming back at you." Svneatr released the demo "Serpents & Storms" in 2015 and their first full-length "The Howl, The Whisper, The Hunt" was released in March 2018.

Svneatr is:
Vitharr Deadwolf: Guitars and Vocals
Hengist: Guitars
Scariot: Bass
