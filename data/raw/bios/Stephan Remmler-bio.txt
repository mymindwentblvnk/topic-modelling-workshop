Stephan Remmler (born 25 October 1946) is one of three members of the German band Trio. 
He performed most of the vocals for the band's songs. He was a member of the band alongside Gert Krawinkel, who played the guitar, and Peter Behrens, who played the drums.
Remmler currently lives with his family in Basel. He is married to an interior designer named Hilana Raitzik-Remmler and has three sons (Cecil, Jonni and Lauro).
