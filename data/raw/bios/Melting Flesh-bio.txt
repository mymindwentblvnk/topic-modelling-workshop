Melting Flesh was a Brutal death metal band from Colombia. They formed in 2001 and released 2 demos in 2002 and 2003, making their debut full-length "Good Addiction Butchery" in 2004 with Dan*s Crypt Records. The band split up shortly after other differences in musical direction. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Melting+Flesh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
