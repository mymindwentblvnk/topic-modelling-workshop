Connecticut-based producer Flavors has been steadily churning out impressive instrumentals for the last couple of years. The prolific beat maker, who blends jazz and hip-hop, has released everything from solo albums, including the dazzling  from this past spring, to snow-themed mini tapes to collaborative EPs. 
As 2019 unfolds, the ambitious artist is starting the year out with summit, a full-length project featuring producer Allem Iversom and rappers Chester Watson and Thiago.
