Formed in the spring of 2001, this quartet from the South Jersey area has come together to bring audiences their soul music, METAL. There are sounds ranging from death to grind to thrash to speed metal at any given moment in one of their compositions. Their formula for songwriting differs in every piece that they execute. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Body+Part+Trophy+Case">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
