King Leviathan are a four piece Heavy Metal band that fuses elements of Thrash and Death metal from Brighton.

Devout worshippers of Speed, Violence and The Occult, King Leviathan have spread madness from their inception, and in a scant few months of existence this insatiable four headed beast has already laid waste to venues across the country. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/King+Leviathan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
