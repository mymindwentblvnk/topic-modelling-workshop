Gorugoth was a black metal act from Tokyo, Japan. Its mastermind was Kouzaburou Kozima. Gorugoth had as session members Wood (Hurusoma, The Under, Gnome) and Darkness Profanation (Vomit Remnants, Deathlike Silence, The Under, Hurusoma, Tyrant).
Gorugoth releases 4 demos, 2 splits and 1 compilation before spliting-up. Not much more is known about the band.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gorugoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
