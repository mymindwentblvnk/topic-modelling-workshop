Metalchrist was a Speed/Thrash Metal band formed in Bucharest, Romania in  1992 and split after the first album in 1994. The original lineup consisted of  Liviu Anghel (vocals), Cristi Dobre (guitar), Sorin Balanescu(guitar) . Luci Sandulescu (bass), and  Gabriel Santus (drums).
