Manokin is a 6 piece band from Meadville/Cochranton Pennsylvania.  There really is no way to describe the genre other than Progressive Metalcore.  There are so many influences that each member brings to the band and you can tell when you listen to the music.  The members are Bill Allen - Vocalist, Matt Allen - Bassist, Dan Pipper - Guitarist, Bear Augustine - Guitarist, Ian Richter - Keyboards, and Dan Crock - Drums.  You can find them on Facebook by Looking up Manokin OFFICIAL. They also have music uploaded on www.reverbnation.com/manokin.
