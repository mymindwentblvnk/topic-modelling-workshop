Dwellers Of The Twilight is a two pieces project formed in 2006, with the intent to create raw and intuitive music. All songs were created/composed in one week during september 2006. Vocals added in march 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dwellers+of+the+Twilight">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
