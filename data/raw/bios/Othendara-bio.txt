Othendara was spawned from the Black Metal band Hvelvengel Av Helvete, in Chicago, Illinois,in 2001.

Othendara play grim old-school Black Metal.

Othendara is
Kriglord Blodmane (guitar)
Noctis Herre (vocals,bass,drums)
