Formed in 1999, Canada's Underlord seeks to denounce the pretentiously artistic Black Metal trends and to reaffirm the flagging faith of the true headbanger.  It's just dirty, buzzy, raw and oh-so-true Canadian black metal inspired by classics like Venom and Hellhammer.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Underlord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
