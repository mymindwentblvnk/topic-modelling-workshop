There are two band with name Eligor:

1) Symphonic black metal band from Italy, who have now dis-banded. Released one demo "Southern Shadows" (2001) and two full-length album:
- 2003 - In Nomine
- 2006 - Ante Lucem

2) Russian black metal band from Chelyabinsk. Released only one demo "Vyzov Bogam" (2000). Has site: http://eligor.narod.ru/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eligor">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
