MENTAL AMPUTATION is a death metal metal band from Germany. 

MENTAL AMPUTATION is: Kay Schwarze (drums), Sascha Omay ( lead Git.), Fabian Guschlbauer (lead Git.), Joachim Strehle (voice), Michael Schuster (bass).

MENTAL AMPUTATION stands for uncompromising, yet melodic Death Metal!!
