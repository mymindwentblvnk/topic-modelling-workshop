Ounce of Self is a heavy metal band from Los Angeles, California. They are known for their appearances in the Knitting Factory in New York. Their song, Drink Up, was featured in the Xbox 360 version of Guitar Hero II. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ounce+Of+Self">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
