Noktiis Eterna is a one-man black metal project from France. The leader behind this music is Sacrystia. He has stated Darkthrone as a big influence, and one of the first bands that got him into black metal. Sacrystia's work is very atmospheric, and has a follow up album to Les Larmes du Tyran titled Luna Nokturnia.

To learn more go to noktiiseterna.com

The site is in French, so all non-speakers should google Noktiis Eterna, and next to the site it should say "Translate this page." The translation works quite well. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Noktiis+Eterna">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
