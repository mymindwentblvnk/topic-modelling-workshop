Raw Black Metal from Australia. Regnans is a one man project from the Yarra Ranges in Victoria, Australia. Regnans takes its name from Eucalyptus regnans, the tallest flowering tree in the world, which is native to this area. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Regnans">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
