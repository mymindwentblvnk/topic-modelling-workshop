J. Mascis (drums/guitar/vocals) plays in Dinosaur Jr. Tim Parnin (guitar) and John Petkovic (vocals/guitar) play in Cobra Verde. Dave Sweetapple (bass/vocals) plays in Witch.

Their album Love & Desperation was released by Tee Pee Records on April 20, 2010.

Their second album The Golden Age of Glitter  was released by Tee Pee Records on April 08, 2014.
