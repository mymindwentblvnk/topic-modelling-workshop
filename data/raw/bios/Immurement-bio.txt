Immurement was a death metal band, having formed in 1994 in Murmansk, Russia. Three demos were released: "Ill Fated Imprecation" (1995), "Immurement" (1996), and "In Hesperus' Castle" (1996) under Gothic Horde Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Immurement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
