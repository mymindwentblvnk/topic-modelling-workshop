Total Terror was a side project of Edge of Sanity members. Their music is described as "grindy/crusty death metal". They made a demo in 1993 but split up soon after. 

In 2008 a remastered version of the unreleased full length "Total Terror" was released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Total+Terror">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
