Centuries Of Deception, formerly known as Myocarditis, is a raw black metal band from California, USA. The band has released one EP, Heic Noenum Pax, in 1998. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Centuries+Of+Deception">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
