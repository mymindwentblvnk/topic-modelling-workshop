Czech progressive thrash metal band formed in 1990 which released one album Lame Gart And... at 1993 and split up.

Line-up:
Vladimír Hauk - Vocals, Bass
Vladimír Brett - Guitar
Pavel Herynk - Guitar
Miroslav Maršík - Drums
