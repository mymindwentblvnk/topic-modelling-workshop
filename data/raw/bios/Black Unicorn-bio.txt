Black Unicorn is at least three distinct artists:

1.) Solo synthesizer project of Akron, Ohio based Curt Brown (of Cane Swords).

2.) Czech band folk-grunge band consisting of: Filip Drlík - vocal, guitar; Anička Zajíčková - vocal, piano, keyboard; Tereza "Lisa" Jakubíčková - vocal, violin; Miroslav "Mira" Novotný - drums, cajón

3.) A side project of the band Thrice, featuring Teppei Teranishi and Chris Jones.
