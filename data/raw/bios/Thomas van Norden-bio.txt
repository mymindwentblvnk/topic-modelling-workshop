Thomas Van Norden is a singer/songwriter from Saarbrücken (germany) who records all his songs at home. All the instruments are played by himself. He played in a couple of living rooms and clubs all over germany and released two records so far:

"Straßenlaternen austreten" (2004/self-released cdr)

"Scheiss auf Sex, ich will Früchte!" (2005/CD on Meudiademorte-records)
