Arenna is born in Vitoria-Gasteiz, Spain at the beginning of the year 2005. His five components come from formations with musical different styles but with a common denominator: the rock of the 70s.

During the first months of essays they are creating the first topics and defining his style, which can include inside the so-called stoner rock, although they prefer being defined as lovers of the heavy riffs.

 In June, 2005 they realize his first live performance with Viaje a 800. Also they have touched with Brant Bjork and The Bros, La ira de Dios, Amon Ra, Her Own Disease, Fungus, Orthodox, Supa Scoopa, Rip KC, Positiva, Los Natas, Neubat, Glow....
