Leeni is a synthpop producer based in Seattle, WA. Formerly a chiptune artist, who created music on Gameboys, she has been performing electronic music since 2006 and has completed 3 full-length albums: "8-bit Heart" (2007), "Labyrinth"(2008) and "The Only Now"(2011). Also find her by her band name "Prom Queen." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leeni">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
