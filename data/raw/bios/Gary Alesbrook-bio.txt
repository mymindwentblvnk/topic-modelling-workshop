Jazz trumpet player, composer, arranger and producer Gary Alesbrook is back in the spotlight with the release of his second album “The Stories We Tell Ourselves,” The album consists of eleven original jazz compositions and features an exciting, diverse range of artists including two time Grammy nominated Guitarist and vocalist, Raul Midón. Gary was a guest of Raul’s at PizzaExpress last year as part of the EFG London Jazz Festival. Tonight he features the incredible vocal talent of Elliott Cole. Elliott rapidly made a name for himself on both the jazz and soul/pop scene, working extensively with the likes of Joss Stone, Solomon Burke, Beverly Knight and Natalie Williams’ Soul Family. Tonights performance also features the inspired rhythm section of Ian Matthews on drums, Will Harris on Double Bass and Anders Olinder on Piano. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gary+Alesbrook">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
