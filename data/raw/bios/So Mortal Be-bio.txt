Named after a Spirit Caravan song, So Mortal Be was a Doom metal band from Doncaster, England. The band was active from 2003 to 2008 and recorded the four song demo "Initiation into Nothingness" in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/So+Mortal+Be">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
