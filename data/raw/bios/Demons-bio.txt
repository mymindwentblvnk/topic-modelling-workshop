There are more than one artists called Demons.

1. Wolf Eyes’ member Nate Youngs and Steve Kenney formed the noise drone duo Demons in late 2006.

2. The Japanese metal/hardcore/new wave band DEMONS released their first album GOETIA in 2007. They were also featured on the Tokyo Dark Castle compilation the same year. An alternate tag for them: デモンズ.

Members:
kiyotodemon: Vocals & guitar
yasudademon: Guitar & chorus
hidekidemon: Bass & chorus
kannodemon (COALTAR OF THE DEEPERS): Drums
