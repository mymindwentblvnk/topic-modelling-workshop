Avernal are a death metal band from Buenos Aires, Argentina, formed in 1993. They released four LP's so far - "Avernal" at 1997, "No Hope" at 2001, "III" at 2004 and "El Sangriento" at 2006.

Lineup:

Christian R. - Vocals
F.A. Ramos - Guitar
Sergio D.C. - Guitar
Cyto - Bass
