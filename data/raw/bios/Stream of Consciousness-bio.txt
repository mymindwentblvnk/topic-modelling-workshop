There are two artists that use this name:

1. Stream Of Consciousness is an independent hip-hop artist currently residing in Wisconsin. While he has been writing rhymes for over a decade, he only recorded his first track in early 2007. Stream Of Consciousness is currently finishing up his solo debut album entitled "The Pentagon Papers" set to be released sometime in 2014.

2. Stream of Consciousness is a progressive metal band from Birmingham, Alabama.  They are comprised of Chuck Lamphear, Wade Beevers, Sara Chesler, and Jon Bernreuter.  They have released one LP and one EP independently, and their music has been featured on National Public Radio.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stream+Of+Consciousness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
