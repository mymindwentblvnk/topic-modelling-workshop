The extreme Dutch doom metal band Bunkur was formed in Tilburg in 2001. Band members are reportedly also active with Flesh Made Sin, Planet AIDS and Sauron. Influenced by bands such as Winter, Sleep and Abruptum, the band is not exactly known for making short songs and some of them last over an hour. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bunkur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
