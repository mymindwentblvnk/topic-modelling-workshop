Brumous is a largely improvisational experimental metal artist from the UK. With his debut full-length released in October 2017, it is clear that atmosphere is at the forefront of Brumous' work, often taking a drawn-out, spacy approach to experimental guitar work. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brumous">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
