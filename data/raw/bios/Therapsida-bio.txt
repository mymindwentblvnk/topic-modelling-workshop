Therapsida is Russian progressive groove metal band from Chelyabinsk city.
They formed in early 2007 and came through some line-up changes since. For today, Therapsida is well-known for their very energetic live performances as well as for their music. It combines classical elements of groove metal, such as memorable guitar riffs by Dima Chizhov, extraordinary slap solos by Kamil Valjeev, ultimate drum work by Paul Jerohin and wide range of vocals by Antonenko Yaroslav, and some other elements sometimes unusual for groove metal.


Line-up:
Yaroslav "D.S." Antonenko - Vocals
Kamill Valejev - Bass
Dmitriy «DeeWild» Chizhov - Guitars
Paul «IMPACTOR» Jerohin - Drums, FX, Gruppenführer.
