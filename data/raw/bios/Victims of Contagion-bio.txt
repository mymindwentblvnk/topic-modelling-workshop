Victims of Contagion is a death metal band from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States . 

"Victims of Contagion are huge fans of blood, chunks of flesh, decapitation , disembowelment , rectal leakage , the physical love of animals , meat , anything dark and brutal , impalement , and metal. "

Line-up:
Antonio (Italiano) Zello - Guitar - Vocals
Chris (Thunderfingers) Edison - Low Freq Boomstick - Vocals
Chad (Leviathan) Neville - Guitar 
Lee Andresky - Drums
