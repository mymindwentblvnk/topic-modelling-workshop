There are more than one artist with the same name:

1. Founded in 1979, Carambolage was all-female German punk/post-punk/NDW band, consisting of Britta Neander on drums, Elfie- Esther Steitz on guitar/keyboard/vocals and Angie Olbrich on bass/vocals. They disbanded in 1985.
