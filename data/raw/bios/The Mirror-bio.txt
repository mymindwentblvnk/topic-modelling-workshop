There are several bands named The Mirror.

The Mirror is a UK psychedelic band, mostly known for the song "Faster Than Light" (1968).

The Mirror is also a black metal project that contributed to the 2010 tribute album to Summoning, "And in the Darkness Bind Them". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Mirror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
