Dark Majesty is a Finnish black/dark metal band formed in late 2003 by Abrymoth and Asmodeus.

Current line-up:
Abrymoth - guitar, bass
Asmodeus - vocals, (guitar)

Former members:
Andaeon - bass (2004-2005)
