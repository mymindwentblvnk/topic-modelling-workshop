Hot Sugar is Nick Koenig from New York, USA.
Nick is the founder of a musical process he refers to as Associative Music (a process involving the manipulation of sounds recorded outdoors and in non-studio environments).
His debut "Muscle Milk EP" was released for free online early in 2011.
