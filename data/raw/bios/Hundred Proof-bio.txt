Hundred Proof Started in 2008 in Lawton Oklahoma. They toured constantly gaining a steady fan base that followed them everywhere and began gaining gigs all over. They released there first album Baptized in Whiskey. In July of 2010 they signed with Turkey Vulture Records and changed their name to The Seventh Rebel. They had stated that the name was already used and copyrighted. Unfortunatly, they broke up before they released there album in the fall of 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hundred+Proof">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
