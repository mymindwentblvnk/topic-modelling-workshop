Good Times Ahead are an electronic music duo from Miami consisting of house, electro house, trap and hip hop producers Julio Mejia and Matt Toth.
Originally releasing music as GTA, the Miami based duo has reverted to what GTA has stood for, Good Times Ahead. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Good+Times+Ahead">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
