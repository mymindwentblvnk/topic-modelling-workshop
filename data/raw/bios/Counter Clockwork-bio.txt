Counter Clockwork are a punk/metal band formed in early 2007

CC Released their first E.P in March 2008
From June – July 2008 CC performed in Venues Throughout Wales trying to promote their new material and E.P. They have played in venues such as: The Point, The Pop Factory,TJ’s and Theatre Bryncheiniog.

Currently CC are writing new material whilst trying to get gigs all over Wales and anywhere Possible. CC currently have 3 brand new songs recorded and will be handing out CDs with them on for free at all their gigs.
