Brian Reitzell (born December 24, 1965) is a musician, composer, record producer and music supervisor best known for his contributions to several film soundtracks. Formerly the drummer for the punk band Redd Kross, he is currently a member of the band TV Eyes alongside Roger Joseph Manning, Jr. and Jason Falkner. In 2003, he was nominated for a BAFTA, along with Kevin Shields of My Bloody Valentine, for the score to Sofia Coppola's film Lost in Translation. Reitzell has since brought his talents to an acclaimed list of features including CQ, Thumbsucker, Friday Night Lights, Stranger Than Fiction, Marie Antoinette, 30 Days of Night and Hannibal. He has also collaborated with the French electronica duo Air, having played the drums on their 2001 album 10 000 Hz Legend. 



His first solo album, Auto Music, was released by Smalltown Supersound on June 17, 2014
