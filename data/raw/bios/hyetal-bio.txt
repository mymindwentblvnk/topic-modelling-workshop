Hyetal is a producer from Bristol, UK, garnering releases on Reduction Records, Soul Motive and Formant Recordings in 2009. 

After making his name with a series of off kilter dance floor releases and collaborative projects – mutant boogie outfit Velour (alongside Julio Bashmore), and a joint release with Punch Drunk boss Peverelist, Hyetal released his debut album ‘Broadcast’ in 2011

His sophomore album, 'Modern Worship', was released on the 3rd of June 2013, through True Panther. The collection features contributions from The Fauns' Alison Garner and UK singer/songwriter Gwilym Gold, who co-wrote three songs.
