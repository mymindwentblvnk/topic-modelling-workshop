Tangorodrim are an alcoholic black metal band from Tel-Aviv, Israel. They are compiled of Mrakobes (vocals/guitar), Tangor (guitar), Darveter (bass), and Alcogolik (drums). They play raw and intense black metal in the vein of old school bands such as Hellhammer, Darkthrone and Celtic Frost. The band released their first couple of records Unholy Metal Way and Those Who Unleashed on their own Agressor records. 2003 saw the release of their third vinyl-only release, Unholy and Unlimited on Southern Lord records. 2007 saw the band's next album, Justus Ex Fide Vivit, released on Southern Lord Records. Forces of Satan Records has re-released (digital only) Unholy Metal Way and Those Who Unleashed in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tangorodrim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
