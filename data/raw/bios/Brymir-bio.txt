Composing of six young musicians, Brymir is an emerging band from Helsinki, Finland, expressing fast-paced epic melodic metal enhanced with huge orchestrations and choirs.

Majestic melodies, intense riffs and aggressive vocals define Brymir. Since their conception in 2006, Brymir evolved rapidly from humble beginnings into a mature band, and released their debut album "Breathe Fire To The Sun" 6.4.2011 through the Spinefarm Records.

Band members:
Sean Haslam (Guitars)
Joona Björkroth (Guitars,Vocals)
Jarkko Niemi (Bass)
Janne Björkroth (Keyboards)
Viktor Gullichsen (Vocals)
Sami Hänninen (Drums)
