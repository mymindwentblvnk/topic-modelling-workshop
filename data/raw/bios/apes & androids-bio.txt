Think David Bowie meets "Bohemian Rhapsody" meets Godspell meets Tron, and you're left with New York-based Apes & Androids. Their debut album Blood Moon is long and loud and everything about it is big: from the massive riffs, the pounding score and the band itself, which towers above everything and everyone in this mega-budget sonic explosion.
