Jaxxon D. Silva was born in LA and raised throughout his teenage years across the UK, acclaiming his "nomadic" status. Alongside his musical endeavours, the musician has established a brand that speaks in tune with the authority of his music, seeking to separate himself and stand out as a multidisciplinary creative. With an esteemed repertoire at the beginning of his career, Silva has achieved recognition through his work with musicians such as Skepta and PHARRELL amongst a catalogue of collaborations ranging from Lil Peep to Sega Bodega. He is member of London based collective LAIGON LIFE. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jaxxon+D.+Silva">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
