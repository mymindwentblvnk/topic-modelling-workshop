Born in Barcelona in 1994. (18yrs)

His first mixes were with old Uplifting trance hits.
When he turned 16 he got a call
giving his first performance at a nightclub.

In 2011 at age 17, the number of performances in different clubs, which enabled him to perfect his refined yet agressive mixing technique.
In mid-2011 began with the production.

Featuring 3 releases and awaiting their last EP Limits, Alek is already working on his upcoming projects.
