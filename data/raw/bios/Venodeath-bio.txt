Venodeath is an old school death/black metal band from Hamina, Finland.

The band was formed in the end of summer of 2007. First line-up was Hiems (g), Venomentor (b) and Inquisitor Zythifer (v/c). Soon after releasing their first demo, vocalist/drummer Inquisitor Zythifer left the band, citing musical differences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Venodeath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
