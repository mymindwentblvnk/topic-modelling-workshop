Nucleus Torn is a Swiss Progressive folk rock band. Earlier efforts were focused on the boundaries of rock music, from silence to raging fury, but later releases exhibit a more minimal modernist folk approach.

The band: 
Patrick Schaad - Vocals 
Maria D'Alessandro - Vocals
Rebecca Hagmann - Cello
Christoph Steiner - Percussion
Christine Schüpbach-Käser - Violin
Anouk Hiedl - Flute
