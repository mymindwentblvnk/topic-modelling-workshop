Robyn Hitchcock formed the Egyptians with former Soft Boys Morris Windsor and Andy Metcalfe. Albums range from Groovy Decay to Queen Elvis, plus the live album and video Gotta Let This Hen Out. Their song "So You Think You're In Love" reached number one on Billboard's Alternative Songs chart in 1991. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Robyn+Hitchcock+and+The+Egyptians">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
