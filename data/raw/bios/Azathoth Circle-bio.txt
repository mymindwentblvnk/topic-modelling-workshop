Azathoth Circle is a female fronted metal band from Kyiv, Ukraine that came together on April 27th of 2013. As of August 7th, 2014 the band consists of Serj Thorzhevskyi - guitar, Mike Yablochkov - bass and guitar, and Alexandra Babiy on vocals. They released their first recordings, a three song EP, in the summer of 2014.
