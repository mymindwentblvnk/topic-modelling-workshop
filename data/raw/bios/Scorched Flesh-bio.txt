Scorched Flesh was a Bossa Nova/Emotronic/Psychobilly band from United Kingdom. Consists of: Ciaran Simmons (Vocals), Neil Whiteley (Guitar), Sarah Whiteley (Drums) and Chris Taylor (Bass).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SCORCHED+FLESH">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
