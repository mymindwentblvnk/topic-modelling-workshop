Biography:
My Material Season is a Japanese Melodic Death Metal band with huge Neo-Classical Metal influences. They're formed in Soka, Saitama, Japan in 2005.

Members:
Tanaka - Bass
Nozaki - Drums, Piano
Ozawa - Guitars
Sakurai - Guitars, Vocals
Hasegawa - Vocals
Akihiro Muto - Vocals


Discography:

[2010] Mind of You Fragile in Vitriform (Full-length)
