Nipenthis is a black metal band from Athens, Greece. The band was created by Revan in 2007 as a one man band. After recording some material, he decided to recruit members and transform Nipenthis into a full member band. Since then Nipenthis have done a lot of live performances and have released a split and an EP. The band is currently awaiting the release of a split-cd while crafting its first full-length album.

  Revan - Vocals
   Setherion - Guitar
  Nihilus - Bass
   Asmodeus - Drums
