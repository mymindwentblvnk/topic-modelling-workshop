Doom Metal band from Kuwana, Japan. Blind Witch was formed in 2007 by  Elizaveat; known for his work with Sabbat and Metalucifer.

Line-up:
Sadaki "Temis" Ieda - Vocals (Sabbat)
Takeshi "Elizaveat" Asai - Guitars (Sabbat, Metalucifer)
Atsushi Asai - Bass
Ryo Koyatsu - Drums

Discography:
