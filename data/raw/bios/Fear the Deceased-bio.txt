Fear The Deceased are a 5 piece band from Hertfordshire, comprising of...
Jean Paul - Vocals
Shane - Guitar
Matt -Guitar
Jeeks - Bass
Oli - Drums

They recorded a Demo EP in late 2007 made up of 3 tracks, 2 of which are featured on their last.fm page (For Honour And Grace and Rememberance).
