Craig Walker is an Irish singer-songwriter. During the 90ties he was the frontman of alternative/indie band Power of Dreams and later, from 2002 to 2005, he was the vocalist of Archive. As a solo artist, he has collaborated with several other artists, and he has released a solo album, Siamese. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Craig+Walker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
