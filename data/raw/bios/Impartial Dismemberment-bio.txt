Impartial Dismemberment was a four-piece from Staten Island, New York consisting of members Jamesgang (vocals), D'Andre (Guitar), George (Guitar), Mike(Bass) and Joey (Drums). They are heavily influenced by a variety of death metal, grind and deathcore. Unsigned.

I.D. formed in late 2007 and they broke up in 2010.
