Plague Survivors is a sludge/doom metal band based out of North Andover, MA. Started in 2011, the band features Matt Willwerth of Pangea on vocals, Chris Querengasser on guitar and Aviator members Mike Moschetto and Mike Russo on bass and drums respectively. They released a three-song CD-R demo in January 2012. Manchester (UK) label Dry Cough released a discography cassette of all the band's recorded output to date in May 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/PLAGUE+SURVIVORS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
