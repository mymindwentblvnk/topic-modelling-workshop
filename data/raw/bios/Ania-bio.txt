Ania (born Ania Dąbrowska on Jan 7th, 1981 in Chełm, Poland) is a Polish singer, songwriter and composer. She took part in a Polish edition of a talent-show Idol which enabled her to sign a contract with BMG and release her debut album Samotność po zmierzchu (transl. Loneliness after dusk). She collaborated with numerous Polish stars, such as: Smolik, Krzysztof Krawczyk, Artur Rojek (Myslovitz).
Her second album Kilka historii na ten sam temat (transl. Few stories on the same subject) was released on October 16, 2006.
