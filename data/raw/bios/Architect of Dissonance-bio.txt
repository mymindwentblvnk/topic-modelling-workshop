ARCHITECT OF DISSONANCE come from Dinkelsbühl and are pretty much the local pioneers of the old proven" ON THE GOB "principle. With refreshing influences from the fields of Slam Death, Brutal Death Metal Beatdown and deathcore here offers a very special experience.
