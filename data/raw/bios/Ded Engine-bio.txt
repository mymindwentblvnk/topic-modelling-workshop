A Heavy Metal band (with some Doom Metal tunes) from Lansing, Michigan, USA.
Formed in 1980, their style is somewhere in between Judas Priest and Cirith Ungol, but on some tracks they sound even heavier than both.

They released 2 full albums:
Ded Engine (1985)
Hold a Grudge (1987)

Band members:
Vocals: Scott Litz
Lead Guitar: Doug Horstman
Drums: G. H. Lorimer
