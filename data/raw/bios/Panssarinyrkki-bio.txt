Panssarinyrkki is a Finnish heavy metal playing group founded in the beginning of 2004. 

The band's first try out -production was the Internet spreaded, sloppy sounding "Sieluton", published in December 2004. The first demo(n) was finished in the beginning of 2006, when the three tracks containing "Piiri" was released.
