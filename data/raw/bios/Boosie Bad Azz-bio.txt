Torrence Hatch, better known by his stage name Boosie Badazz and formerly Lil Boosie (as of October 20, 2014), is an American rapper from Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Hatch was bestowed the nickname Boosie by his family, and he was raised in southside Baton Rouge. Boosie has released five albums and six collaboration albums. A true underground legend, he has been featured on over 830 mixtapes.

Boosie Bad Azz is the cousin of another southern rapper, Young Bleed, who is known widely for his seminal hit "How You Do That" which was released on No Limit Records in 1997.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Boosie+Bad+Azz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
