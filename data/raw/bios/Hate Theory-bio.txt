Somewhere between grindcore and death metal, Hate Theory hails from the late 90s Cleveland Scene.  Often compared to the likes of Soilent Green, Blood Coven, and Brutal Truth, and source of some more extreme side projects like The Pillow Humpers.  The band started in 1995, first album in 98 a self released assault entitled "Your Dead Reflection", the song 'freezing' was also on the national debut on World War III records in 2002 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hate+Theory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
