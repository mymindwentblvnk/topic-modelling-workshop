Thy Funeral is a Black Metal project of Nebulah from Dunaharaszti, Hungary since 2003. 
It changed it's name from Nebular Carpathians (formed in 2000).

Discography:
2001 Antichristian Assault
2002 Thy Funeral
2008 Master Of Rebellion
