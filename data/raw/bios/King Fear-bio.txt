King Fear is a black/death metal band from Germany and was created in 2011. So far they have released 1 EP called "King Fear". Vocalist Nachtgarm sings also in Dark Funeral and Negator among others.

CURRENT LINE-UP:

Mål Dæth - guitars, bass (2011-)
Nachtgarm - vocals (2011-)
