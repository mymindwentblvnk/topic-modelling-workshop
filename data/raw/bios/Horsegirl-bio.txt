Horsegirl is a North American rock band from Chicago, Illinois, U.S.A. The band members are Nora Cheng, Penelope Lowenstein and Gigi Reece. The group originated in 2019 and they self-released their first song, "Forecast", that year. Their first full-length studio album was released in June 2022, titled "Versions of Modern Performance". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Horsegirl">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
