Rachel Zeffira is a Canadian-Italian singer from a small town in Western Canada. She is an accomplished opera singer and multi-instrumentalist. In 2012 she released her debut album "The Deserters". Besides performing solo she collaborates with Faris Badwan of The Horrors in Cat's Eyes, a 1960s influenced two-piece band.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rachel+Zeffira">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
