Paul Smith (born Billingham, Stockton-on-Tees, United Kingdom, 13 March 1979) is an English singer, songwriter and rock musician, best known as frontman of Maxïmo Park. 

Smith released five albums with Maxïmo Park thus far, but also releases music under his own name: Margins (2010) was a proper solo album, Frozen By Sight was a colleboration with Peter Brewis of Field Music whereas 2015's Contradictions was recorded with the backing band, The Intimations, that also played with Smith on the Margins concert tour. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paul+Smith+and+The+Intimations">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
