Korikhor is a black metal band from Davao City, Philippines. Korihor was an antichrist in the ``Book of Mormon``. The band split up and some members went on to form the blackened thrash band MANIAK <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Korihor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
