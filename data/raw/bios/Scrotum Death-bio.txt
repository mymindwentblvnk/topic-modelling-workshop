Scrotum Death was a death metal band from Chile formed in 1988, they released their first demo titled "Revolution of Death" in 1990, a second demo tape was released in 1992 titled "Beginning of the End", the band is split-up and nothing else has been released since.

Band lineup consisted of: 

Jorge - Bass
Rodrigo González - Drums
Mauricio Gómez - Guitars
Christián Aguilera - Guitars
Elmo - Vocals
