The Nice Boys are a powerpop band hailing from Portland, Oregon. They were formed by former members of the Riffs and former Exploding Hearts guitarist, Terry Six. They are signed to San Francisco/LA based record company, Birdman Records and finished an American tour in fall of 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Nice+Boys">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
