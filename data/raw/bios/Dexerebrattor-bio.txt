Dexerebrattor is a Crossover band from the province of Formosa, Argentina. The band was founded by Facundo Balmaceda (Voice) and Brian Galvan (Guitar) in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dexerebrattor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
