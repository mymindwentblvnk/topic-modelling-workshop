Mia Wray (born 28 October 1995) is an Australian pop singer, songwriter and musician from Noosa, Queensland. Born and raised in Noosa, Wray rose to prominence after winning Telstra's Road to Discovery Program in 2011, and released two extended plays before her debut single, "Work for Me", was released on 16 September 2020. Wray's musical style encompasses genres such as pop, bedroom pop, and soul. She has toured with the Rubens and the Teskey Brothers. Following her breakthrough in 2020, Wray received a nomination for Breakthrough Independent Artist of the Year at the 2021 AIR Awards. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mia+Wray">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
