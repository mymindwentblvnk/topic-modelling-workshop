Fractal Universe is a Progressive Death Metal Band from Nancy, France.

Their first EP, Boundaries of Reality was released on February 20th, 2015.

The first full-length album, Engram of Decline, is a concept album dealing with the evolution of consciousness, with lyrics inspired by Friedrich Nietschze's "Also sprach Zarathustra". 

They are currently recording their sophomore album, which is scheduled for release in 2019.
