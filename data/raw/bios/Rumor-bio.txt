Ghanaian rap artist from Accra. He started his career from high school. He later signed with Skillions Record where he released his Friends, Fans and Followers mixtape, as well as featuring on Skillions New Generation Tape. He is currently an independent artsist, his latest project being Poetic Licence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rumor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
