Forgotten Darkness was a black metal band from Velbert, Germany found in 1999.
Discography:
- Traces (Demo, 1999)
- Krieg des Winterleids (EP, 2002)
- Nacht aus Blut (album, 2004)
- Nekrolog (album, 2007)
Last known line-up:
Astyanax - Drums
Corvus - Guitars
Adonai Obscura - Vocals
Mortum - Guitars
