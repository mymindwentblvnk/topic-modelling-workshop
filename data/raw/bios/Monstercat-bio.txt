Monstercat Media is a YouTube channel set up for the sole purpose of sharing and delivering well-known and not so well-known EDM DJs and composers to the forefront of electronic music enthusiasts' minds. This is a great way to keep up with the latest tracks from the EDM scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Monstercat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
