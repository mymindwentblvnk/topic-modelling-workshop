Lethal Guest is a Russian Thrash Death Metal formed by four friends. Nikita `Andriyan` Andriyanov on guitar, Andrey `Zhuk` Zhukov on lead guitar,Sergei `Morteele` Folomkin on vocals and bass, and Max `Sadist` Zuzikov on drums. They formed in the spring of 2002. Their sound is familiar to early Slayer, Arch Enemy, and In Flames.
