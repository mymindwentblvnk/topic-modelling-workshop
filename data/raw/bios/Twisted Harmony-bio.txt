Twisted Harmony are made up of Sam Brooks and Keiron Marshall who combine impressive instrumental prowess with a spine-chilling blend of vocal talent. Each artist's astonishing adaptability enables them as a team to produce truely remarkable original music. whilst also breathing new life into a range of recognisable covers. Wringing every last drop of emotion from each performance.

Twisted Harmony are as memorable as they are unique.
