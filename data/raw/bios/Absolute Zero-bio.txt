There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Absolute Zero is an American avant-prog band combining very different genres. It has released one album, Crashing Icons (2000)

2) Absolute Zero is an alias of drum and bass artist DJ Fresh

3) Absolute Zero is also a technical thrash metal band from Cleveland, Ohio. They released a 4-song demo entitled Piracy in 1988.

4) Absolute Zero is a hardcore band from Bandung, West Java, Indonesia. They offers a mixture of hardcore, thrash metal, blues up to rap-core.

5) Absolute Zero is a nu metal/rapcore band from Havlíčkův Brod, Czech Repubic.

6) Absolute Zero was a hardcore punk band from Portland, OR, which released a 7 inch record called 'No Escape' on Allied Recordings in 1996. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absolute+Zero">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
