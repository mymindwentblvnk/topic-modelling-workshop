Raw black metal band from the Netherlands. Their demo tape titled "Night of Blood Moon"  was released March 15th, 2016 by The Shadow Kingdom. Another version of the demo was released with an alternate cover layout by Tour De Garde in the same year. Their debut full length titled "Aristocracy of Twilight" is set for LP release via Iron Bonehead on December 16th, 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blood+Tyrant">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
