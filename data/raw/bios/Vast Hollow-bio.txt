Vast Hollow Bio

Formed in 2010 by two old school metal scholars, Vast Hollow create epic blackened death doom metal which displays the matured writing skills of long term musicians combined with forward thinking ideas to form aggressive dirge.
 "All Hope Is Driven Asunder" is a glimpse into what will appear on their up coming debut album due in the not too distant future.
"Vast Hollow will tug at your heart strings, then rip them out, weave them into a rope and hang you with it!"
