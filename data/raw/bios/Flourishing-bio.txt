Flourishing is a technical death metal band that formed in 2009 in New York City. The trio bulldozers through massive magnetic riffs, industrial mechanization, realms of dissonance and loose progressive abstraction creating an annihilating rhythmic bedlam.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flourishing">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
