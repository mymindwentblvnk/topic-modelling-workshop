Sense of Loss were an atmospheric doom rock/metal band formed in 1999 in Athens, Greece.
They were influenced by bands such as Anathema, The Gathering, My Dying Bride, Paradise Lost etc.
They released the single My own solitude in 2000 which gained very positive reviews and they recorded an album titled Last Image but it was never officially released.They split in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sense+of+Loss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
