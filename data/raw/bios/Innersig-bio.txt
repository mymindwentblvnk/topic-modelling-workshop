All the way from Malang, East Java, Indonesia comes INNERSIG, malang fastest rising metal act. 
The 5-piece band is comprised of Tommy (lead vocals), Rizki (guitar, vocals), Pede (lead guitars, keyboard), Dimas (bass) and Ical (drums).
INNERSIG was created when the 3 old school friends that were just 20 years old and realized their mutual passion for music, they started this band because they love music, writing it, recording it, playing it, performing it, and improving on it. 
From all kind of music in this world, they choose METAL as the connection, so in year 2007 INNERSIG is formed.  
Their influence comes from many Swedish melodic death metal band like In Flames, Soilwork,
Sonic Syndicate, Scar Symmetry, Arch Enemy, Amon Amarth, etc.
