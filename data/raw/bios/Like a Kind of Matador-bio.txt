Like a Kind of Matador was Leeds, West Yorkshire, United Kingdom based drone and sludge music band, started as the three-piece of instrumental in 2003 by Paul McShane (aka Paul Rauschen, Max Bordenghast), former member of British doom metal bands Gruel and RedRightHand.

The band uses drums, guitar, flute, accordion, organ and various other instruments. They recorded an album Halfway to Dangerous for Tumult Records, released in 2008.

The last lineup :

Sarah McWatt - flute, accordion, vocals; Paul McShane - guitars; Paul Steere - bass guitar; Tom Pollard - drums.

Former members :

Daniel Skevington - drums, keyboards.
