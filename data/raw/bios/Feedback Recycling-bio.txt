Death Metal/Hardcore/Grindcore from Hannover, Lower Saxony, Germany, formed in 1988. The band consisted of Uli Feedback (bassist who also played in deathgrind band Professor), Henning Bosse (drums) and Jochen Gutsch (vocals and guitars, also played in Suckspeed). Discography:
1991 - Gags & Gore (split with Uncounted Faces of Death, Harmonizer, Hardcore 918V and Hypocritical Society)
1992 - New Homes for the Rich / Belly Flop (split with Mutant Gods) 
1992 - Religion Splits Humanity (EP)
