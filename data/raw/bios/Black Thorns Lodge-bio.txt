Black Thorns Lodge was a Gothic Metal band from Rome, Italy. They formed in 1999, and later changed their name, becoming goth/rock band 'Room With a View'.

 Members: 
Alessandro Mita - Guitars
Francesco Grasso - Guitars, Vocals
