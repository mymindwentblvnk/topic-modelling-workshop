Jess Williamson is a singer-songwriter from Austin, Texas, United States, now living in  Los Angeles, California. She has released four solo albums and one album under the name Plains, a collaboration with Waxahatchee's Katie Crutchfield. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jess+Williamson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
