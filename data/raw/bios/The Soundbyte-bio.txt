A musical project by Trond Engum (ex-The 3rd And The Mortal) that mixes ambient, rock and trip hop, similar to the later albums of The 3rd And The Mortal. Engum experiments in ways to mix technology with darker rock music.

The debut album Rivers of Broken Glass was released in 2004. In 2007 came the release of City of Glass; a collaborative effort with poet Paul Irgens, in which a collection of poems by Irgens was set to sound. Trilogy, the third album, was released in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Soundbyte">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
