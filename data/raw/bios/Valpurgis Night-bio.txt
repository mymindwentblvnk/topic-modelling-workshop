Valpurgis Night is currently a 3 peice metal band from London formed in 2008 and signed to  Rising Records in 2010. Released their debut album Psalms Of Solemn Virtue on the 25th of October but delayed till 22nd of November.

An epic mix of doom, thrash and progressive metal.

Band Members
Preacher Edwards - Vocals, Bass
Lance Barrington - Guitar
Rafi Utudjian - Drums
