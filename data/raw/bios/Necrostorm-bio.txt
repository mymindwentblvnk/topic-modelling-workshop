Necrostorm is another project from Charagnanna and Nocturnasz. Charagnanna is known for his involvement in tons of different bands, which he has been doing for the last 21 years!! (a.o. Charagnanna, Eternal Terror, Mortuarium, Warlust, We Bring The Sickness, Israthoum, Bringers of Torture, Hamergilde, Hraesvelgr and lots of others). Nocturnasz has been playing in different Black Metal acts since early '93. His fast drumming can be heard in bands such as Warlust, Pestis, Denethor, Bringers of Torture etc.

Necrostorm plays Minimalist Improvised Satanic Black Metal. One guitar, drums and vocals. NO bass, NO second guitar, NO solos and NO rehearsing. All recordings were done in one take. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necrostorm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
