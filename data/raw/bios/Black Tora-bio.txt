Musicworks/Rock Quarry records is proud to present the next generation of Classic Heavy Metal/Rock. Black Tora

This young 3 piece band from Mcallen, Texas captures the timeless energy of the genre with strong, soaring vocals, ripping guitars and incredible musicianship.

Since the reslease of their debut (Demo) Cd on Retrospect Records, Black Tora's following and recognition have skyrocketed due to their tireless live concert schedule, performing throughout the Southwest, including numerous shows in Las Vegas, and a prime performance at Rocklahoma 2008

Rock Quarry/MusicWorks has recorded their new album produced by Paul Shortino (Rough Cutt, Quiet Riot, etc.)

"This band may very well be the best thing from Texas...just a kickass band all the way, a SERIOUS power trio." (VEGAS ROCKS MAGAZINE)
