There are 2 bands named Others (not to be confused with numerous artists named The Others):

1. Indonesian post-rock band

Others is post-rock band from Surabaya, Indonesia. The band members are Erwin (guitar), Gerdi (guitar), Angga (guitar),  Anton (bass) and Hendro (drums). The band formed in 2008, and they have released only one extended play named Others EP.  MySpace.

2. American gothic metal band

Others is a gothic metal band from New York, United States. Until now, since 2004 they have released 7 albums (including LPs and EPs). The band members are Marquis (guitars/vocals), J.R. Rotting (bass) & Mr. Webb (percussion)
