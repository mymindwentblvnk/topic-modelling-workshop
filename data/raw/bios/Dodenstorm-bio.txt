One man Depressive Black Metal project from The Netherlands. Formed in 2007 and has released two demos to date: "Verdoemd door Tijd en Wanhoop" released on tape limited to 186 copies, and "The Great Tribulation" released on a 3" CD-R in a mini dvd-case limited to 50 hand-numbered copies! The man behind this projects call himself for "S.", and Dodenstorm is currently signed to the dutch label Svartgalgh.

S. is also member of the dutch Suicidal BM band Ora Absentia, and he is an logo-artist, who made the logo's for Draqworks Metal Productions, Mortuus Regnum, Sorti and Draco Donnovan...
