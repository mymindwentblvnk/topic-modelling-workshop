1.Surtr  is Swedish Black Metal Lyrical themes :  Anti-cosmic, Dark Germanic Heathenism Current label : Scythe of Death Productions
'Surtr' means 'The black one' in Old-Norse and He is the ruler and guardian of Muspelheim (the realm of fire).Line Up :
Niding - Bass
Ná - Vocals
Hräsvelg - Drums
Mistilteinn - Guitar

2.SURTR unleash a dark atmosphere of traditional doom metal on the France-based trio's debut World Of Doom. The band paints a gloomy picture of the world using the hallmarks of doom metal pioneers. They set up the basics of upcoming riots through a concept-album using an analogy between the giant SURTR's legend and modern societies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Surtr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
