After The Abduction are a five piece Death metal band from Manchester, with a likeness to bands such as Decapitated and Dying Fetus. The band has been formed since early 2015 and have since been travelling around the UK promoting themselves. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/After+the+Abduction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
