Reexamine is a Groove brutal death band from Japan. They formed in 2003 with Brutal Baby  ( Vocals, ex-Transgressor), Robin (Guitar, ex-Flagitious Idiosyncrasy in the Dilapidation, SUNGODDESS), Deathkaya - (Guitar/Vocals, ex-Massdemise) Fukuda (Bass, BLITZKRIEG(JPN)) and Sekiguchi (Drums, MORQUIDO).
