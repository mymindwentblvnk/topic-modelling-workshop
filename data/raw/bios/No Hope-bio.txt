1) No Hope is a band from Saarlouis, Saarland, Germany. The band has released two albums, with their track "M-Lady" from Deutschland's Doomed Vol.1 compilation.

2) No Hope is a harcore/grindcore-trio from Sweden. They use blastbeats in almost every song and sings some of them in Swedish. 

3) No Hope was a group from Winnipeg, MB. They released a five-track album in 2005. After playing a few shows each member went on to different projects.

4) No Hope is a solo project by Dutch artist Ruben du Pon. He has released his first album "The Shadow Inside" in November 2016
