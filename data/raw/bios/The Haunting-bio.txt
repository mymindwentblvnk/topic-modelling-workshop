There are multiple artists with the name The Haunting:

1. An alternative/post-hardcore/electronic project from Long Island's John Von Braunsberg (Scarlet Signature, Forever The Gentleman) and Scott Kearney (Con The Villain, The Mercurial).  Currently working on their debut album (currently untitled), which is set for release in Q1 of 2013.

2. Tara and Mike Connelly (Hair Police, Failing Lights, Wolf Eyes, Connelly, Gods of Tundra).

3. an atmospheric/ambient side project of Matthew Davis (Ten Grand).

4. A Gothic Rock/Deathrock band from Hamilton, New Zealand who recorded an EP in 1990.
