Astral Aeon are a Black Metal band from Athens, Greece. They formed in 2002 to make 2 rough demos and one excellent Full-Length Debut called, Allegories of Light. Astral Aeon incorporate subtle Synths to their music as they kick your ass with their perfect shrieks, captivating riffs, and superb drum work. Really a band worth your time and more! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Astral+Aeon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
