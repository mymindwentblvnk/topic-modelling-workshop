Brock Hewitt is the Founder & Lead Composer of Stories in Sound Studios, based in the Toronto area. Recently coming back to Canada from Los Angeles, where he was interning at Hans Zimmer’s Remote Control Productions, Brock’s goal is to create a family of composers that help filmmakers & creatives share their stories. As a composer, Brock’s main focus is crafting heartfelt piano and ambient compositions that inspire a deep sense of Hope. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brock+Hewitt:+Stories+in+Sound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
