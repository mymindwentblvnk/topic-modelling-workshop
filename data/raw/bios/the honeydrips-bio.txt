Led by Mikael Carlsson, former lead singer of Dorotea, The Honeydrips hail from Gothenburg (Göteborg), Sweden. The electropop one-man-band is run by Swedish label Sincerely Yours. In 2007, the band released "Here Comes the Future", "I Wouldn't know What To Do", and “(Lack of) Love Will Tear Us Apart” Single. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Honeydrips">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
