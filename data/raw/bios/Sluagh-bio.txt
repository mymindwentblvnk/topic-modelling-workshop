There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Sluagh was a doom metal / sludge metal band from Portland, Oregon. They split up on the 20th June 2014, just before their release of their second full length album "Infinite Madness".


2) Sluagh was a black metal band from United Kingdom.


3) Sluagh is a Doom Metal / Acid Rock / Heavy Metal / Hard Rock from New Jersey, USA. They are going to release their debut full length album "Unwanted and Worthless" sometime in late 2014.
