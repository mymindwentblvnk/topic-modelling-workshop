There are multiple artists with the name Wartech:

1. Wartech is an up and coming Dubstep artist.

2. Wartech was the band the remaining members of Axegrinder created after Matt (bassist) left. After Axegrinder's demise, Wartech adopted a totally different approach compared to Axegrinder, showing a great influence from early Voivod rather than Amebix, leaving behind the allready confirmed and established crust pattern; lyrics involved science, virtual reality and computers. There were realeased two demos: Demo 1 and Demo 2 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wartech">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
