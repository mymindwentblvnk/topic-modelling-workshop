Breath of Life is an American death doom metal band from Sicklerville, New Jersey, formed in 1994. They released a demo in 2002 named "Goodbye (Proud World)". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Breath+Of+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
