Die Roten Rosen (German for 'The Red Roses') is an alias of the German band Die Toten Hosen. Die Roten Rosen are usually activated for albums that are more about fun than about serious songs, according to the band itself. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Die+Roten+Rosen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
