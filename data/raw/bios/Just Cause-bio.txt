Just Cause came out of the Vancouver British Columbia, Canada metal scene in the late 1990's. This ultra-aggressive speed/thrash outfit is the brain child of front man/guitarist Patrick Beaudoin (The Allmighty Punch Drunk)His collaboration with legendary drummer GENE HOGLAN(Strapping Young Lad,Death, Dark Angel ect.) Rico Forester (guitar) lead to the sonicly CRUSHING c.d.
