Clientelle were a New Wave Of British Heavy Metal band who formed in Hertfordshire in 1977 and released one LP, 'Destination Unknown', in 1981.

Vocals, Guitars: Roy Powell
Guitars: Rik Taylor
Bass: Steve Trudgett
Drums: Phil Goodfellow
