Hermh is a Symphonic black metal band from Poland, they formed in 1993 and they are currently in the pagan records label.

Current line-up
Bart : vocals
Hal : bass 
Socaris : guitars
Icanraz : drums 
Voit (Flumen) : keyboards
MacKozer : guitars
