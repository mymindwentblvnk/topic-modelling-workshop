There are more than one artist under this name.

(1) Otherwise is a hard rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada. They have produced three albums : "True Love Never Dies" (2012), "Peace at All Costs" (2014), and "Sleeping Lions" (2017), and an EP : "Some Kind of Alchemy" (2009).

(2) Otherwise was a post-hardcore band from the UK who had a string of releases in the early 2000s on the Firefly Recordings label.  This included the full-length album "Dark Adapted Eye" (2002) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Otherwise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
