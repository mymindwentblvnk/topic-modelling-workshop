1). Zmierzch is a Polish (Depressive) Black Metal band, formed in 2009. Currently, the band has released two demo's, an EP and a split release with Wolfenburg. The Zmierzch tracks on this split are the same as on their demo Kruk.

Discography:
2009 - Wicher (demo)
2010 - Kruk (demo)
2010 - Wolfenburg / Zmierzch (split)
2011 - Anders Suhm (EP)
