There are several bands by the name of Nomad Soul:

1) Nomad Soul is a German heavy metal band. They have released a debut album, 'Pale Horizons'. www.nomad-soul.de

2) Nomad Soul is a jazz/electronic collective comprising four music students from London who have recently gained notice online for their soundtrack for the short video 'Murmuration'. http://soundcloud.com/nomad-soul-collective

3) Howie B, Dobie and Diane Charlemagne released the single Candy Mountain in 1991 as Nomad Soul. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nomad+Soul">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
