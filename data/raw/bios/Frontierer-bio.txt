Formed in 2012, Frontierer is a 5-piece Mathcore band from Edinburgh, Scotland - United Kingdom/Missouri - USA. Members: Chad Kapper - Vocals, Pedram Valiani - Guitars, Dan Stevenson - Guitars, Calum Craig - Bass, Owen Hughes - Drums. Their discography consists in a debut album 'The Collapse', released in 2013. Followed by the second full-length of the band called 'Orange Mathematics', unveiled in 2015. In 2018 they've launched 'Unloved'. Band's latest record named 'Oxidized' out at late 2021.
