About the Black Plague

Coming from another existential void, Darkness Beyond Time is born from the ideas and feelings that runs deep beneath the mind of Jormungand. Brooded to experiment, to feel and taste, to create and destroy. Likewise to express the sense of nothingness that exist deep within his thoughts.
Jormungand have no interest in spreading his music to the masses nor being part of the infamous mainstream. The sole purpose is self-satisfaction and maybe share the feelings and ideas to those who think alike...
