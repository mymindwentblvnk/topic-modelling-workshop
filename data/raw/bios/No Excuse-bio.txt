There are 4 bands named No Excuse.

1. Short lived hardcore punk band from Richmond, Virginia. Started in January 2009. Released a 7 song tape demo a month later. And broke up 4 months later in June.
Cited as 80's revival, but had much more influence from 2000's hardcore (Tear It Up, Cut the Shit, Down in Flames, etc).
Half of the band currently plays in DRY SPELL. check them out.
www.myspace.com/dryspellrva

2. Hardcore band from South Korea. Has a demo released and is signed onto Town Hall Records.
