Brutal death metal band from Loches, France, formed in November 2005 by Yanosh who wanted to start a new project in the vein of Devourment and Artery Eruption. Among the members recruited was Guyome, drummer of Pulmonary Fibrosis.

The current line-up as of 2012 consists of Yanosh (guitars, backing vocals), June (vocals), Ben (bass), Adrien (guitars) and Guyome (drums).
