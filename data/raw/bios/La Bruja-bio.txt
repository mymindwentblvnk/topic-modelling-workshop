LA BRUJA, Poet, Hip Hop/Reggaeton Artist, Activist
In case you didn't know, there's more to this witch La Bruja than her gatita negra that wants the milkman to come by so badly! She's a multi-talent artist, a poet, and an activist for women's rights, in particular sticking up for latina girls growing up in the big city. She's recorded an album, 'Brujalicious', and she performs regularly in the NYC area so go see her!
