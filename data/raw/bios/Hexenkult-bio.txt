Hexenkult is a Black/Ambient band from Germany (North Rhine-Westphalia). formed in 2006. a demo in 2006 and a full length album in 2007 was released. both releases were called "Durch rabenschwarze Nacht" .

The band members are : Tysja (Vocals) and Witegar (Vocals, Guitars, Percussion). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hexenkult">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
