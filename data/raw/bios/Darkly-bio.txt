This is the Last.fm page for Darkly, formed 2010 Stockholm, Sweden.
It is the solo project of Johannes Göthager.

The live lineup is constantly changing, so far, the following band members has played for Darkly:
Johannes Göthager
Peter Borgedahl
Micke Pettersson 
Magnus Thelin 
Henning Runolf 
Justus Björk 
Thomas Aherne 
Toomas Saarne 

Discography:
A Dying Eye (2010 album)
Just One More Flower Please Doctor (2012 album)
Becoming Dangerfield (2014 album)

This artist is not to be confused with the british producer of the same name. Unfortunately, his songs are being scrobbled to this page as well. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Darkly">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
