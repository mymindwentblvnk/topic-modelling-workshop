Chaotic death metal from Auckland, New Zealand. Forced To Submit is fronted by energetic and aggressive vocalist Rhys Owen and has featured members of other well known Auckland metal bands, Dawn of Azazel, Skuldom and Ulcerate. 

The band released their only disc in 2004 entitled 'Endless Hatred', 6 terrifying tracks of blistering death metal and featured as an opening act for touring death metal bands to New Zealand including Deeds of Flesh. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forced+to+Submit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
