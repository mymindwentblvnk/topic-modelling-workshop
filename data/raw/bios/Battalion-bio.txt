There are several bands named Battalion.

1. Death metal band from Belgium.
Discography:
- 2002 - Warstorm (demo)
- 2003 - Nuclear Devastation (demo)
- 2005 - Winter Campaign
- 2008 - Welcome to the Warzone
- 2014 - Only The Dead Have Seen The End Of War
Official site: http://www.myspace.com/battalionsquadcamp

2. Black metal band from Australia (Sydney).
Discography:
- 2003 - Soldiers of Evil (demo)
- 2004 - Into Harms Way
Myspace: http://www.myspace.com/devastatedbyartillery

3. Old school Heavy/Thrash Metal from Switzerland.
   Discography:
- 2006 - The Fight for Metal
- 2010 - Underdogs
- 2012 - Set the phantom afire
- 2015 - Generation Movement
Myspace: http://www.myspace.com/battalionofficial

4. Danish thrash band from 1984. Released 2 demos.

5. American rock band from Sacramento, California. Their latest album, "Passion Is Everything" was released in 2009.
Myspace: http://www.myspace.com/battalionsongs

6. Power metal band from Grand Prarie, Texas. Formed in 1985, and released one single in 1986 entitled "Wings of War", intended to have a full length but the project quickly collapsed along with the band in late 1986. Lead guitarist Edgar Rivera later went on to play in doom metal legends Solitude Aeturnus. Their one & only officially released song can be found on the 2009 compilation "Texas Metal Archives volume 1"

7. An American hardcore band from Jerseyville, IL, used to be known as Sincerely, the End until founding members left, they then got a new drummer and guitarist and are now Battalion.
