NS Black Metal from Russia. Nitberg is part of the Blazebirth Hall circle and is a project of Ulv Gegner (Raven Dark, Vargleide) and Kaldrad Branislav (Branikald, Forest). Nitberg concerns itself with Wotanism, Aryan Mythology and NS Beliefs, upholding the overall ideology of Blazebirth Hall.

Nitberg also has the distinction of featuring Temnozor members as session musicians.

After much delay the first full length of Nitberg, Donner Wetter Donner Wyrd is released. Unfortunately a version of this album leaked on the internet in an unfinished form.
