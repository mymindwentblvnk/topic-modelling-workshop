Fenrirs Thirst is a Canadian Death Metal / Black Metal band from Kamloops, British Columbia.

Band members include Mike Griggs - Guitar / Vox, Rob Nasz - Lead Guitar, Mike Pelton - Bass, and Brandon Poznikoff - Drums
