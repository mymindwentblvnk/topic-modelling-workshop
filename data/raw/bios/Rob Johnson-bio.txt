Rob Johnson (also known under various pseudonyms, including Freshbread) is a Canadian musician currently based in Vancouver, British Columbia. He played bass guitar in the punk and alternative rock bands SNFU and the Wheat Chiefs, in addition to recording his own hip hop and hard rock music in various incarnations. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rob+Johnson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
