Dead Men Dream is a Doom/Gothic Metal band from U.K., formed by Darren J. White (ex-Anathema, The Blood Divine).  They have released two demos in 2001 and 2002, called "Zen Session One" and "Absolute Zero". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+men+Dream">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
