The band was formed in May 2004. The first concert was given in September of 2004. The group has given more than 50 concerts in different clubs of Saint Petersburg and its suburbs. The feature of the band is not only the unusual for Russian metal scene genre, but also an indispensable show, fully endured according to canons of the genre, what has given the band a chance to attract a large amount of listeners from the very first concerts. By the way nowadays Solerrain is treated as one of the most technical and professional melodic death metal band in Saint-Petersburg. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Solerrain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
