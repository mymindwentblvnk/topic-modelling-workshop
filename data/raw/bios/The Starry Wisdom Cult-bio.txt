The Starry Wisdom Cult are a four piece, guitar based rock/metal band with stoner/Doom rock influences. The band are based in Fife, Scotland.The band was initially formed in the late 1990s, taking inspiration from bands such as Black Sabbath, Soundgarden, Hawkwind and Metallica.

The Starry Wisdom Cult are currently racking up the gigs, venturing forth across Scotland, and are looking for new and exciting places to play.
