Hammer on Faith is an influenced Death Trash metal band from Quebec, Canada. They have been together since September 2006, and composed around 8-10 songs so far. Gab the original drum player recently left the band, and Steve aka "Stu" came in to fill the spot ; Wich he does perfectly. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hammer+on+Faith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
