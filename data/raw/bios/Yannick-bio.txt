French one-hit wonder from 2000-2001. Ces Soirées Là is a French remake of "Oh What a Night'.

Big hit in France, but also in Senegal and other French speaking West-African countries and french-speaking parts of North America, particularly Québec. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yannick">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
