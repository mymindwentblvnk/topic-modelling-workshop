Collaboration between xylo and cerror. 

xyce is a project by two guys from Groningen / Nederland (Netherlands) who make music on the Amiga 500 (Protracker 1.3b), Atari 1040STe (Musicmon 2.5e), PC (OpenMPT) and Gameboy (LSDJ).

After they met online and started making music together, the project xyce took off and several years later five albums, numerous liveshows throughout the world and various collaborations are the result.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xyce">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
