Lethargy of Death is a funeral doom metal band from Santiago, Chile, formed in 2000 by Emiaj (all instruments, vocals).
Released two EPs, "Tribulations" in 2005 and "Robe of Death" in 2006, and a full-lengthalbum, "Necrology", in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lethargy+of+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
