Lucid Coma is a 1-person electro-industrial music project created by Erik Hernandez, currently based on  Long Island, NY. Originally an experimental project focusing on various electronic genres, recent songs have included distorted vocals and more emphasis on danceable rhythm.

Influences include Nine Inch Nails, Combichrist, Unter Null, and Skinny Puppy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lucid+Coma">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
