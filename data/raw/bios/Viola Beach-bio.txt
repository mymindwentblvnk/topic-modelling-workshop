Viola Beach were an indie rock band formed in late 2013 in Warrington, England. The band consisted of Kris Leonard (vocals, guitar), River Reeves (guitar), Tomas Lowe (bass) and Jack Dakin (drums). On 13 February 2016, all four band members and their manager were killed in a car accident in Södertälje, south of Stockholm, Sweden.

Their debut single "Swings & Waterslides" was released in August 2015 through Fuller Beans Records, followed by "Boys That Sing / Like a Fool" via Communion Records in January 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Viola+Beach">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
