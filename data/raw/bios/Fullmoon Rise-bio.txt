Fullmoon Rise is a Satanic black metal band from Russia. The band released two demos, one in 1997 and another in '98. They are no longer active, and the year of their origin is unknown.

"The End of Life's Ages" demo 1998 Line-up :
Scorner - guitar, voice (Black Wood (Rus), Xafan, Aspera (Rus))
Macabre - drums
Voron - session bass (live bass for Thy Repentance, Autumn (Rus), Kali-Yuga (Rus))

This demo was recorded at Old Shooting-Gallery in August 1998.
