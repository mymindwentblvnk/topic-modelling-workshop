Svart Crown are a French blackened death metal band that formed in Nice in 2004. They have released four albums to date, their most recent being Wolves among the ashes which was released through Century Media in 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Svart+Crown">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
