Enter Twilight are a female fronted progressive metal band from Melbourne, Australia. Enter Twilight combines soaring melancholic soprano vocals with solid yet intricate music.

Enter Twilight are made up of vocalist Lidia Szymczak, guitarists Damir Sisak and Richard Hausberger, drummer John Wauchope, keyboardist Leigh Askew and bassist Dean Gaudoin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enter+Twilight">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
