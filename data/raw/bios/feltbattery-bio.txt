Ben Trueblood has been quietly home recording and self-releasing tapes of eerie tape experiments and random music musings for years. Beginning with a handheld tape recorder, some broke drums and a larynx, Ben began to reconstruct sound and send it out into the void. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Feltbattery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
