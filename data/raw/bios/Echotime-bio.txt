Echotime is a progressive metal band that was founded in 2002-2013 as Echoovertime. 
The bands name changed to Echotime in 2013 with the release of their debut album Genuine. Echotime hails from Italy and is currently on logic(il)logic Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Echotime">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
