Coming from the Drachten region of the Netherlands, De Magia Veterum is a one man avant-garde black metal/black metal band created by Mories of Gnaw Their Tongues. Lyrical themes include the Old Testament, mysticism and misanthropy.

The music in MP3 format can be found at The Wayback Machine.
