Neuro Massacre is a Thrash band from Virginia. They formed in 2006 under the name Fratricide. After a few years of playing and a couple name changes they released a demo (Demo 6-17-10) under the now permanent name Neuro Massacre. The Killing Game Single was released in 9-22-10. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Neuro+Massacre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
