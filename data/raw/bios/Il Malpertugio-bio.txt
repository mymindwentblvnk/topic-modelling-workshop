Il Malpertugio is a 5-piece from Caserta, Italy. 
The band started activity in 2002, but the musical project was better defined after vocalist Ivan and guitarist Emilio joined the band.
Several tours inside and outside Italy between 2005 and 2009, with the first prize at an Italian rock festival at Ceprano, and the collaboration with film-makers gained the band attention and more than positive reviews and comments on many Italian and foreign fanzine and webzines.
The band has one EP - The Blues Demon's Show and  is currently recording and mastering their debut full-length album.

Current Line Up
Ivan Franzini - vocals
Antonio Zannone - guitar
Emilio Cuoco - guitar
Giuseppe Marino - bass
Gianluca Zannone - drums.
