Violent dirge were a Polish technical death metal band formed in 1990. They released two demos before their debut album "Elapse" in 1993 on Carnage Records. They released a follow-up in 1995 titled "Craving" on Loud Out Records. Both albums were cassette only releases. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Violent+Dirge">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
