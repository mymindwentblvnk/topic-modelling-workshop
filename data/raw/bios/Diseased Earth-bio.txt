Started in 2014, Diseased Earth is made up of former members of several bands local to the Staunton,VA area,including The Leviathan's Mandible,and Death from Massive Damage. Combining elements of doom and sludge with death metal and thrash influence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diseased+Earth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
