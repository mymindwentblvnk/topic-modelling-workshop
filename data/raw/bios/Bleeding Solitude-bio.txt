Depressive black metal band from Alsace (France), formed in 2007 by 'Apathy'. In 2010, the album 'Forever Lifeless' was released on CD by Eisiger Mond Productions, limited to 300 copies. Additionally, the album featured two session musicians; D.L.M (bass) and K.T.B.M (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleeding+Solitude">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
