Rainych Ran is an Indonesian singer who uploads song covers to YouTube. She started uploading her covers in 2015. She mostly sings anime soundtracks, although she has recently covered popular songs on TikTok. She gained attention after uploading a Japanese cover of her favorite artist Doja Cat's hit song "Say So". The Japanese version of the song was released digitally on March 31, 2020. Rainych mixes her covers herself, but the Japanese lyrics in her Say So cover were translated by fellow Youtuber named Datenkou. The cover has gained over 24 million views on YouTube. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rainych">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
