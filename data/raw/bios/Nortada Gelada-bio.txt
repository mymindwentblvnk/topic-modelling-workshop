Nortada Gelada was black metal band from Portugal,Lyrical themes:Cold, Lusitanic-Iberic Pride, War
The band participated on an unnamed seven-way split with Infernüs, Cripta Oculta, Black Howling, Hellvete, Irae and Mons Veneris.
