Monoxide, also known as The Monoxide Child, (real name: Paul Methric) is half of the Horrorcore duo known as Twiztid. 


Cohorts and label mates of insane clown posse, Monoxide is also a member of the Psychopathic Records supergroups Dark Lotus and Psychopathic Rydas.


His solo album is titled Chainsmoker.
