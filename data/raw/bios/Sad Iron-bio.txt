Sad Iron is a dutch heavy metal from Hoorn (Noord-Holland). They formed in 1980.

The Current line-up is:
Herke Van Der Poel - Vocals
Bernard Rive - Guitars
Leo Ockeleon - Bass
Jacques Van Oevelen - Drums

Discography:

Total Damnation. Full-length, 1984
