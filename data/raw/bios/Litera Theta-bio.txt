Litera Theta is a gothic metal band from Varna, Bulgaria and was created in spring 2001 by Emilian Vladev. In the winter of 2003 Litera Theta recorded 4 songs: "The Offering", "Small Poem", "In Dreams" and "Night", which were played in a few radiochannels in Bulgaria.

Litera Theta's style is very similar to music played by Nightwish, Within Temptation, Theater Of Tragedy, Luca Turilli. All music and arrangements are created by Emilian Vladev.

The band was plannig to make a demo record in Finland, in autumn 2005.

Current members:

Veneta Stojanova (Vocal)
Emilian Vladev (Guitar and keyboard)
Nikola Nikolov (Bass)
Nikolaj Todorov (Drums)
