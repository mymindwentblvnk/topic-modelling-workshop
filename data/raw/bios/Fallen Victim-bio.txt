Fallen Victim formed in Medicine Hat, Alberta, Canada in June 2003. Comprised then of Ryan Smith, Kyle Konrat and Sean Seitz, their self-titled EP marked their official debut.

Under the current line-up of Trevor Bolen, Kyle Konrat, Sean Seitz, and Gary Ohrn, Fallen Victim continues to build momentum through regular practice and gigs.

Now in fall of 2006, the writing process for their upcoming demo has officially started.
