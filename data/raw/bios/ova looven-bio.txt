After the 1999 breakup of electronic/shoegaze alchemists Antarctica, Chris Donohue found himself with a new vision. The result: coldwave synths and sparse, delayed guitars woven through lush electronic soundscapery. National Skyline guitarist James Minor joins the lineup, and now three years of experimentation come to light. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ova+Looven">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
