Deep Time is an American indie-pop duo from Austin, Texas.  Composed of Jennifer Moore (guitar/organ) and Adam Jones (drums/synth), the group began in 2006 as Yellow Fever, later amending their name to YellowFever before being forced to change it entirely. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deep+Time">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
