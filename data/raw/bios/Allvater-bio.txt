Allvater is a Melodic Black/Viking Metal band from Germany. The band released one album entitled 'Vater' in 2007. Lyrical themes include Paganism, Nordic Mythology and Nationalism. Allvater is a project of SKD members. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Allvater">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
