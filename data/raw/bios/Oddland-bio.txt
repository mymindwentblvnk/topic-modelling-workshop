Winners of the "Suomi Metal Star" competition whose final was held at the FME 2011 (Finnish Metal Expo). Progressive Metal band from Turku, Finland. Signed to Century Media Records.
First full length album The Treachery of Senses released in April 2012. Planning a European tour for Spring 2013, details TBA.

Sakari Ojanen - vocals, guitar 
Jussi Poikonen - guitar 
Joni Palmroth - bass 
Ville Viitanen - drums
