Adelaide, Australia (2005-2013)

Australian 4 piece Traditional DOOM metal band influenced by Celtic Frost, Trouble, Black Sabbath and many more. Started off as Phil Howlett's one man band before expanding in 2009 to a four piece. Split up in 2013 and went on to form Lucifer's Fall.
Final Lineup:

Phil Howlett- Guitar & Vocal
Sean Wiskin- Lead Guitar
Ben Dodunski- Drums
