Shitkill was a thrash metal band from New York, New York and formed in the year 2009. They released The New Breed in 2014. They changed their name to The Kill in 2017.  Their last known lineup consisted of Josh Musto (vocals, guitar),  Danny Chpatchev (guitars),  Karina Rykman (bass) and  Damien Moffitt (drums).
