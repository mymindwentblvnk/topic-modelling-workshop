We are a Grind/Metal band from Littleton Colorado. We are influenced by bands like Brutal Truth, Warsore, Insect Warfare, Nasum. Our lyrics are political. We play often in the Denver area and have toured the Midwest and the west coast in june of 2009.  All of our releases have except the split with Dethbox and the upcoming Kansas split have been done by ourselves.
