Deprimat is a depressive black metal band from New Zealand from 2012-2020. They have released 3 pieces; Drowning In Razorblades, Misanthropy and Depressive Collections. They are also said to have fascist lyrics and imagery from their vocalist, Patient NS. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deprimat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
