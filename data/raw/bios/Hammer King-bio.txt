Hammer King is Napalm Records' new heavy metal bet. A German band that is not a newcomer to the scene. They took their first steps in 2015 and have three albums on the market published by Cruz Del Sur Music. Their fourth release is titled after the band itself, Hammer King.
The band features Titan Fox V, former lead singer of Ross The Boss and Dolph A. Macallan, former drummer of Saltatio Mortis. The line-up is completed by Gladius Thundersword on bass and Gino Wilde on guitar. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hammer+King">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
