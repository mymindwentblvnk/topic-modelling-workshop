Negative Four was a progressive Metal band that consisted of ex members of The Quiet Room, Jason Boudreau (guitar) and Mike Rice(drums), as well as Graeme Wood (Vocalist) and John Jeffers(Bass). However, due to outisde obligations the band broke up in August 2005. Their music still remains for free download on their website. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Negative+Four">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
