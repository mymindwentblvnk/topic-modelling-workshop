There are three artists active under the name Spheric. 1) A five-piece Melodic Death Metal act from 
Braunschweig, Lower Saxony/Germany. Formed in 2005, Spheric have officially released one Self titled Demo, and their current activity status is unknown.  2) A chillstep artist from London, UK who began producing music in 2010. They have released several singles through Soundcloud and Bandcamp. https://soundcloud.com/sphericsound 3) A lo-fi rapper from Missouri, USA who began releasing music in late 2016, though would not begin to regularly release tracks until late 2017 through soundcloud.  They are affiliated with Chill Life Collective as a producer and both Money Back Music & Ahegao Squad as a rapper and producer. https://soundcloud.com/sphericnoise <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spheric">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
