Skegss are an Australian  surf music and garage rock trio originally from  Byron Bay, New South Wales. Their line-up is  Toby Cregan on bass guitar and occasional vocals, Jonny Lani on drums and Benny Reed on lead guitar and vocals. They have released several extended plays and toured both nationwide and internationally. Skegss made headlines after accusing Reese-Lil Yachty of copying the cover art for that collaboration's single, "[single artis=Skeggs]Do It[/single]", from the Australian group's five-track EP, 50 Push Ups for a Dollar October 2015.

They released their debut studio album, My Own Mess in September 2018. The album peaked at number 2 on the ARIA charts. At the ARIA Music Awards of 2019,  My Own Mess was nominated for ARIA Award for Best Rock Album, losing out to Amyl and the Sniffers by Amyl and the Sniffers. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skegss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
