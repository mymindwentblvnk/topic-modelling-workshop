Death metal band, Ahrimah, formed in 1992 from Warsaw, Poland. Members were Leprous - Vocals, Robsson - Guitar, Backing Vocals, Dader - Guitar, Zajac - Bass and Dabal - Drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ahrimah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
