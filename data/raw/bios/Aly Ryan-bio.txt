Alexandra Eigendorf (born September 25, 1995 in Moscow, Russia), known professionally as Aly Ryan is a German pop singer based in Los Angeles, United States. She lived for a while in London, United Kingdom. Ryan has stated that she is of Irish descent. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aly+Ryan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
