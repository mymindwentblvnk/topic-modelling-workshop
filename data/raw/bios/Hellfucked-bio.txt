Hellfucked, lyrically inspired by darkness and satanism, is a black metal band from Sachsen-Anhalt, Germany found in 1995. The band was formerly known as Sturmlegion.

Discography:
- Sturmlegion - (Demo, 1996)
- Deathecration / 1st Offensive - (Demo, 2001)
- Hellfucked / Vornat - (Split with Vornat, 2002)
- Gods of Negativity - (Album, 2003)

Current line-up:
Unhold - drums
Senseless Violence - guitars
Krematorienrat F - vocals
