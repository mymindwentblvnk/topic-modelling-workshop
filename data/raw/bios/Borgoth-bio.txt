Founded in 2003, Borgoth is a Heavy/Power metal band from Skopje, Macedonia. Their initial demo "Warning from Hell" was released shortly after their debut on the Macedonian metal scene. They recorded some additional material, but we still have to wait for their first album. 
So far they had many live performances in Macedonia, but their best venue so far was in Skopje, january 2006, in front of more than 3000 people. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Borgoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
