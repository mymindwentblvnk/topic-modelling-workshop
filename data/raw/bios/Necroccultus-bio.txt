Necroccultus hail from Mexico, being influenced by the old school Death Metal sound (Scandinavia and South America) from the late 80's and early 90's. They were formed in 2003 and are signed to "Hippieshredder" productions. Their current line up consists of  "Isafas Huerta" on vocals,"Cesar Ponce" and "Santiago" on guitar,"Omar Vera" on bass and "Daniel Sanchez" on drums. Since 2004 they have realeased a demo called "Promo 2004",a single "Mirage of death" in 2004,a full length album "Encircling The Mysterious Necrorevelation" in 2005, "Cemetary sons of Hell" split in 2005, "Supreme occult one.....The Underworld Abhorrence"  EP in 2006, "Inverted Dimensions" split in 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necroccultus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
