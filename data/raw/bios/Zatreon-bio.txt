Zatreon is an Egyptian symphonic black/extreme Gothic metal band. The band was formed In 2006 as an underground band with a different name " Grace of Demons." They played original tracks, hiding under  secret makeup and secret characters because they just wanted to get the best out of themselves and create profesional and  mature music that would leave their mark in the world of metal music.. The band faced a lot of problems and breakdowns during their first two years, with a lot of members leaving the band for mysterious reasons. 
After a while, pepole heard of an Ehyptian band called Zatreon that came out of nowhere, with an EP called " Hope Fails " 2008.
