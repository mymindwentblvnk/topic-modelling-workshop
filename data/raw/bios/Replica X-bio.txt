Replica X were the 2005 winners of Warwick University's Battle of the Bands and were finalists in the 2006 Battle of the Bands competition. They have produced two EPs, "Fear of Infinity" and their most recent, "Replica X vs. The Machines" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Replica+X">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
