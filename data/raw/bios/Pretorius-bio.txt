Pretorius is a death metal band from Central Florida (United States), formed in early 2005. After their full-length debut release "Bury The Hatchet", the band split up and so far hasn't been heard from again. Currently other band members are working in other bands and projects of their own though such as Mongolian Death Worm and Spooky Steve, The Maniacal Musick. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pretorius">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
