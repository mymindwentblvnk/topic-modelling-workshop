Patterson Hood (born Mar 24, 1964) is best known as the leader of the  Drive-By Truckers.  He is the son of acclaimed Muscle Shoals bassist David Hood.  In 2004, he released an album entitled Killers and Stars.  His second solo album, Murdering Oscar (and Other Love Songs) was released in June 2009.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Patterson+Hood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
