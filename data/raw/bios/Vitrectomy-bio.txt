Vitrectomy is a Black Metal band from Surselva (Grisons, Switzerland). 
The band was formed in 2017. There are 2 members in the band, Vomitus Sanctus and Panzerfaust and both are 19 years old (2017). 
The lyrics are written in English and in Rhaeto-Romanic. The music were recorded in an abandoned bunker. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vitrectomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
