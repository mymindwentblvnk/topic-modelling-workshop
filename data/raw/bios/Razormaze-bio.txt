Razormaze is a thrash metal band from Boston, Massachusetts, established in late 2007. In December of 2008 they signed to Hold True Recordings a metal/hardcore label located in Massachusetts. Just months later the band recorded their debut full length The True Speed of Steel and more recently in April of 2010 their EP 'Miseries' which can be downloaded free at http://razormaze.bandcamp.com. You can find their homepage on the web at http://myspace.com/razormazethrash.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Razormaze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
