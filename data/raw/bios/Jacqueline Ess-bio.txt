Jacqueline Ess (ジャクリーン・エス) was a thrash metal band from Japan. Their first release was a self-titled demo tape in 1994. Another demo was released in 1996, titled "Void Damnation". A CD-EP, titled "Under the Soul", followed in 1998, through Bang Your Souls Records, which was owned by members of Japanese thrash metal band Jurassic Jade. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jacqueline+Ess">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
