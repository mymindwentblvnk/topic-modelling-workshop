Crossover thrash band from Texas formed after the demise of Rigor Mortis. Featuring Casey Orr (aka Beefcake The Mighty) on vocals and bass (ex-Rigor Mortis, ex-GWAR, ex-X-Cops), Mike Scaccia (1965-2012) on guitar (ex-Rigor Mortis, ex-Ministry, ex-Revolting Cocks, ex-1000 Homo DJs) and Mike Dunn on drums (ex-Rigor Mortis, ex-X-Cops, ex-Sedition). They released ne album entitled "Leave It to... Blohole" in 1992. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blohole">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
