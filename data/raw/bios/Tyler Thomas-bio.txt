Tyler Thomas is a 21 year old Los Angeles based rapper. The Los Angeles are MC has been making noise on the local scene since 2010, and has recently aligned with the legendary No I.D.'s All Out Entertainment production company. Tyler is expected to accomplish big things after the success of his songs "Whiskey On The Rocks" and "Never". Stay tuned for more on this up-and-coming artist.
