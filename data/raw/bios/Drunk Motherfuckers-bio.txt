Drunk Motherfuckers from Athens, Greece started in 2005 as "The Drunk Earth"and continues so far with completely different line up, mood, philosophy AND OF COURSE NAME! "Returnable Bottle" was the first tape EP of the drunk motherfuckers.
They released a full album entitled "...and Alcohol for All" in 2013 and officially disbanded in 2014
