There are two bands named Infinitas.
1. Infinitas is a solo project that was founded in 2009 by Thorn. It contains depressive and atmospheric influences and also some ambient parts. First EP "Ardeur" out in june 30th. First full length "journey to infinity" out in 2012.
2. Infinitas is a folk metal band from Switzerland founded in 2009. They released their first full-length album "Civitas Interitus" in 2017.
