Grimpen Mire is a Sludge/Stoner Metal Band From Erdington, UK

Rearing its ugly head in 2007 Grimpen Mire has continued to lay waste to venues across the UK.

Effortlessly combining traditional doom with sludge the band have come up with something truly refreshing yet crushingly heavy.
