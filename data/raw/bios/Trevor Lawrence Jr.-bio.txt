Trevor comes from a line of serious musicians (his parents were both original members of Steve Wonder's Wonderland band!), his aunt played with Patti LaBelle, the list goes on. 

Trevor is one of the most prolific drummers out there today. He has over 70 recording credits to his name with everyone from Dr. Dre, LeAnn Rimes, Bruno Mars, Ed Sheeran, Snoop, Quincy, Alicia Keys, Kendrick Lamar, Stevie Wonder, and Eminem. 

He tours regularly with Herbie Hancock and you also can catch him nowadays playing with Terrace Martin in his new group, The Pollyseeds.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trevor+Lawrence+Jr.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
