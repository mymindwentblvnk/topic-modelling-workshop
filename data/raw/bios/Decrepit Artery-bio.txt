Decrepit Artery are a Brutal death Metal band from Spain. They formed from one man, Iban whom does Vocals, guitar, drum progamming and is collaberated with Roger B. whom does backing vocals on recordings aswell. They are just about to release a 3 demo track which will be available to download on their myspace.
