Beers Village, New York based new Metal/Doom band MOTH EATER have just posted some new audio tracks. 

MOTH EATER is a band that consists of Death Metal/Hardcore vocalist John Alaia of YEAR OF CONFESSION, Death Metal/Hardcore guitarist John Conley of SCAR CULTURE (Century Media Records) and both Dave Ardolina on the drums and Buckshot on bass from DIRTY RIG (The Music Cartel and Escapi Music). The blends of style and mixture of sounds between their previous respected bands have made for some interesting new material.

"The Thunder God of Monster Island" Moth Eater's debut EP was released on July 27th, 2010 on all digital platforms through Giddy Up! Records (Sony/Red).
