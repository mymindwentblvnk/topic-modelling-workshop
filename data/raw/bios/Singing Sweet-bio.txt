16 year old Singing Sweet burst on the Jamaican music scene in 1992 and captivated many reggae fans abroad as well. His debut single "When I See You Smile" brought a novel sound and immediately delivered the young man's biggest hit to date. This instant success paved the way for recording his first album entitled "Donna."  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Singing+Sweet">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
