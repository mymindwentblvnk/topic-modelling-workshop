Eingana is an Extreme Metal band hailing from Norrköping, Sweden.

The name "Eingana" comes from the aboriginal mythology. There she is the creator godess and the mother of water and all living things, and beyond that she is also the godess of death.

Ludvig Swärd - Vocals

Mikael Iriarte Molina - Lead/Rhytm guitar

Enkido Ercin - Lead/Rhytm guitar

Karl Ek - Bass

Calle Pajala - Drums
