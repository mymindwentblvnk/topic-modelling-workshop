Deities of Darkness was formed in 2008 out of Elgin, Illinois. They quickly materialized and solidified their unique sound. Deities of Darkness is unholy metal from the Midwest and an ever-growing, uncompromising, dark force that will bring eternal winter to even the deepest depths of hell. Enslaved by Time their debut album was released in 2010. Their follow up E.P. The Forgotten Age was released in 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deities+of+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
