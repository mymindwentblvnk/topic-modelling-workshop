High Priest of Saturn is a heavy psychedelic rock/doom metal band from Trondheim, Sør-Trøndelag, Norway. The band is a three piece consisting of Merethe Heggset on bass guitar and vocals, also of the band Resonaut guitarist Martin Sivertsen and also of Resonaut Andreas Hagen on drums as well as guitars. Complementing the band on their debut album is the sound of Ole Kristian Malmedal's organs. The album, simply titled High Priest of Saturn, was released on 13 September 2012. The band also has a demo dated to 23 August 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/High+Priest+of+Saturn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
