Burial Mist is a Raw Black Metal project from Russia (Novocherkassk, Rostov region) formed in 2007. 

Current line-up:

Discography:
Demo - 2007  
Insomnia - Demo, 2008  
Subway To Light  - Demo, 2008  
Morbid Screams From the Frozen Forests - Full-length, 2009  
Demo IV - Demo, 2009  
December - Demo, 2009  
Insomnia - EP, 2009 

Currently signed to Northern Lights Prod.
