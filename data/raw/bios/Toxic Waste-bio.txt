There are three known Toxic Waste bands.

(1)
The first, probably the more famous one, is a Irish anarcho-punk band from Belfast. Toxic Waste have recently reformed and are playing gigs once again.

(2)
The second is a South African pop garage band originating from Strand, South Africa.

(3)
The third is a French punk rock founded in 1991 (official site: http://toxic-waste.propagande.org/). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toxic+Waste">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
