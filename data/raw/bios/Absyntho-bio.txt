Absyntho was a Brazilian pop rock band from Rio de Janeiro formed in 1982 by Sylvinho, Fernando Sá, Sérgio Diamante, Walderley Pigliasco and Darcy. In 1983 the band released their first album, "Meu Ursinho Blau Blau" whose title song became a national hit. The album went on to sell over 350,000 copies. The band broke up in 1987. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absyntho">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
