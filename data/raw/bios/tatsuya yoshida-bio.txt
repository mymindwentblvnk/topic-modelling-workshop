Tatsuya Yoshida (吉田達也, Yoshida Tatsuya) is a Japanese musician who, as the group's drummer, is the only consistent member of the renowned progressive rock duo Ruins. He is cited as the "indisputable master drummer of the Japanese underground", and a "rhythm section gone ballistic", among other things.
