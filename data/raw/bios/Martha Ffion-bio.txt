An Northern Irish singer/songwriter (full name - Claire Martha Ffion McKay) based in Glasgow, Scotland. Her debut album Sunday Best was released by the Welsh label Turnstile in 2018 and was nominated for the 2018 SAY Award (Scottish Album Of The Year).

The 2nd Martha Ffion album - Nights To Forget, arrived in 2020, this time McKay taking her music in a more ambient electronica direction. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Martha+Ffion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
