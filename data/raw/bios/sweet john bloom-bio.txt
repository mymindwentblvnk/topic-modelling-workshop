Sweet John Bloom gets in and out quickly. Most of their tracks hover around the two minute mark — short bursts of wild, frantic energy that get their point across as concisely as they do strongly. The Massachusetts four-piece make the kind of rock songs that feel effortless, but they’re anything but.
