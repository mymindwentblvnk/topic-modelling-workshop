Black Metal band from Portugal with an obvious punk/hardcore influence in fast tracks - imagine Ildjarn played in depressive-suicidal BM way. Slow songs sound more traditional and Burzum-like, but still could be described as rough and primitive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coldness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
