Buddha! was a hungarian-serbian doom metal duo with guitarist Béla Barabás (Southern Special, Freshfabrik) and Slobodan "Bob" Macura (Nevergreen, Green Division). They released a demo album in 1998. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Buddha%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
