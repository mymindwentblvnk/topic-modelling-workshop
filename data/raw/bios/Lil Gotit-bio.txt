Semaja Zair Render (born August 6, 1999), popularly known as Lil Gotit, is an American recording artist from Cleveland Ave., Atlanta, Georgia, who started making music at a young age with his older brother Lil Keed. The two of them made their first song, “Trap Bunkin,” together.

Gotit has had his music played at various clubs in Atlanta. The name Lil Gotit is derived from his original rap name, Maja Got It, a nickname inspired by him “having everything.” He has cited Young Thug as his mentor. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lil+Gotit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
