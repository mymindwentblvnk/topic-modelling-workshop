Adel Salah Mahmoud Eid El-Tawil (born August 15, 1978 in Berlin) is a German singer, songwriter and producer.
He is well known for being part of The Boyz and Ich + Ich.

In 2013, he released his first solo album Lieder. The first single Lieder went straight to #2 in the German Single Charts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Adel+Tawil">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
