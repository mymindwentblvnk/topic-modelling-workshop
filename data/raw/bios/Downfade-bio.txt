Downfade was founded in 1998in nearby of Oulu, Finland. It's music is a combination of clean melodies with crushing heavy guitars, combining a range of genres from rock to metal. The band was previously known as "Brainfade", but changed their name to DOWNFADE in 2001.

New single "Prize of Freedom" came out in June 2009

The current lineup is:
Mänö, Vocals, 
Janne, Drums
Pekka, Guitar
Ipe, Bass
