The band formed in 1999 out of the ashes of the band Trouble (who have since then reformed) and features Trouble's Rick Wartell on guitar and Mark Lira on drums. The band's vocalist Shane Pasqualla has a voice akin to Alice in Chain's legendary Layne Staley, and also plays guitar for the band.

The band's selftitled album features the band's stoner metal sound intwined with the influences of grunge and hardrock, the album was released in 2005.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wet+Animal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
