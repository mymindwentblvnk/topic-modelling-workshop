A heavy/thrash metal band from Stockholm, Sweden. Formed in 2005, they have released 3 demos and 2 albums
Demos:
Demo 2006 (2006)
Blade of The Sacrificer  (2008)
The Evil Has Arrived (2009)

Full Length Releases:
Extermination Has Begun (2011)
Unable to Tame (2014)

As of 2014, the band consists of 
Linnea Landstedt - Guitar/vocals
Per Lindström - Bass
