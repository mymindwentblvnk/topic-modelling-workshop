Teuta is a side project of Lunaris, ex synth-master of Opera IX.

With the cooperation of other musicians from The True Endless, a cult italic black metal band, Teuta made a split cd with other two italian black metal bands: L'Ordre du Temple and Ringwraith.
The pagan black metal of Teuta is very strong and direct, it's music for celebrate the Ancient Pagan Gods, the ancient People of theyr Lands and the Pagan Creed of White Man. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Teuta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
