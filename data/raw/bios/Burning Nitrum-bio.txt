Burning Nitrum are a thrash metal band from Bari, Italy.  They were formed in 2010.  They released their EP Pyromania in 2012 and their first full-length album Molotov in 2014.  They are currently signed to Punishment 18 Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burning+Nitrum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
