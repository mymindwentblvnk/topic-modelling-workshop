Unseen were a short lived punk band that were on the small UK label Slampt in the mid 90's. They released one 4 track 7" titled 'Youth Hole' in 1994 with Slampt. They were also featured on a couple of the slampt compilations 'Seven Unlucky Sevens' and 'Elastic Jet Mission'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Unseen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
