Wasabi is alternative/mall emo band from Moscow. It is the first Russian mall emo band with female vocal who gathered in 2005. 

The band:
Sonya - vocals
Xodos - guitar
Rage-  Bass 
Misha - drums 

Ovod - management

Turning-point of prompt development of the band was the concert at festival "Alternative Is here!" in the summer 2006 on one stage with such groups as Психея, #####, 7000$, НАИВ and many others.
In summer 2006 the band change its sound style, by adding rigid colors and emotions to their music.
