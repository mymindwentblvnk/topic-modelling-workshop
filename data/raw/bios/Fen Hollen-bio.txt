Fen Hollen was an Australian black metal band from the Hunter region,NSW.Formed in 2005 by Menelyagor and Grond,the band released one demo in 2006 called 'Winterspirit'.Grond left the band in 2007 but Menelyagor continued on with Fen Hollen as a solo project but eventually laid the band to rest sometime later.
