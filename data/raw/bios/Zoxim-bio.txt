Located in the Olympic Mountains of the Pacific Northwest, Zoxim is a Black Metal/Neo-Folk musician who strives to write the darkest music he knows how. All instruments, vocals, mixing and production are done in his home studio. Lyrical themes focus on the occult, nature, misanthropy and nihilism.
