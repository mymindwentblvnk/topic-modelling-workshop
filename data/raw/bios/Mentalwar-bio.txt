Jaimes Vaere is the artist behind Mentalwar. Beginning his musical journey in his hometown of Aalborg, Denmark, in January 2018 under the name Outyrstar. He published 5 albums and a number of EPs and singles on the music creation website Soundation throughout 2018 as well as two EPs and some singles in early 2019 before leaving his music behind to attend Aalto University in Helsinki, Finland. Musically his style is in indietronica and dance EDM style but also has dabbled in classical orchestral music, jazz, instrumental rock, and Japanese-influenced music.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mentalwar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
