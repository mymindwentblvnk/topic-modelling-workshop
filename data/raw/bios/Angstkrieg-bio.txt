New band of former Dimmu Borgir and Old Man's Child bassist Brynjard Tristan.
It's Norwegian black metal band formed in 1999. They have realeased EP named Angstkrieg in 2010. 
Current line-up:
Tristan - Vocals (Bloodlust, Dimmu Borgir, Old Man's Child, Disiplin)
Gape Horn - Drums (Disiplin)
Predator - Bass
B. Carrington - Guitar (Stealer of Souls)
