And Summer Dies - avant-garde doom/death metal band from France (Pays de la Loire) formed in 2003.

Current line-up:
• Frédéric Modine 'Spirius' - Vocals (Nocturn Deambulation)
• David Blusseau 'Darkvid' - Guitars
• Nasrollah Hitmi 'Bersërker' - Guitars
• Jérôme Poissonnet 'Heimdall' - Bass
• Romain Blusseau 'Khronos' - Drums
Former/past member(s):
• Joël Calleau 'Jaw Hell' - Vocals (on the "Eternal Soul" demo) 
• Frédéric Relet - Guitar (on the "Eternal Soul" demo)

Discography:
• Eternal Soul [Demo], 2004
• The Chaotic Chronicle [Demo], 2008
