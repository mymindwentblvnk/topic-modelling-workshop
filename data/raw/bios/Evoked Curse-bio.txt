Evoked Curse was a Black/Heavy metal band from Sweden in 1989. Later moved to Finland. Started as Necrophiliac's solo project in 1989 and supposedly released two demos (first one's called Transmigration, 1990, the other demo's name and release year remains unknown).

All demos are very limited, with about 50 copies of each one made! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evoked+Curse">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
