There are multiple artists by the name of Jonny 5.

Jonny 5 is an emcee residing in Denver, Colorado. He is most known for his role as one of the founding members of the Denver-based Hip Hop group, Flobots. He is an active member of his community, having founded non-profit organizations, such as the internet-based Fight With Tools, that call like minded people together for the purpose of creating a better world for all. Jonny 5 is currently working on a project in which he will write a new short rap for every day in the year of 2010. 

Jonny 5 is a DJ residing in Brixton, UK. He plays at various parties around London, as well as co-promoting Invisible Factors and Dream Machine events. He has also performed in Amsterdam, Berlin and Montreal. He contributed mixes and dj edits to the Cybernetic Broadcasting System. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jonny+5">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
