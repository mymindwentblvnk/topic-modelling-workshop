Vremya i Steklo (Russian: Время и Стекло, Time and Glass) was a Ukrainian pop duo formed in Ukraine by Potap (Потап) from the duo Potap & Nastya (Потап и Настя). The group consisted of Nadya Dorofeeva and Aleksey Zavgorodniy (aka Positiff).
