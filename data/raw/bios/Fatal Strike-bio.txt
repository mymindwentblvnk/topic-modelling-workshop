Fatal Strike is a RAC/White Power rock band from Mikkeli, Finland.

In 1998 they produced a 6-track demo called Days of Glory. They have also two tracks on the compilation Suomi 2002, their Fight To Survive and Lost Friends, neither of which were on the demo album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/FATAL+STRIKE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
