Distres is a Death Metal band from Poznań, Poland formed in 2000. As of 2009 they have released two demos "The Final Battle" (2003), "Zbawienie Przez Ból" (2008) and a split with Polish Death Metal band Warpath "The Order of Aggression" (2008).
