Gutsaw is a goregrind band from Corona, California created in 2003. The Gutsaw sound is like no other american goregrind. The atmosphere is unrelenting at times while catchy and melodic at others. Utilizing reoccurring themes such as necrophilia, cannibalism, murder and hate.

http://gutsaw.bandcamp.com/

Current lineup:
Necro Nick - Guitar / Vocals
