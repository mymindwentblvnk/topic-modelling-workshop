Fixers are a band from Oxford, UK. They are a part of the 'Blessing Force' musical community. Their music is of the psychedelic variety, though they have already displayed notable diversity in the music they have put out so far.
Their Debut EP "Here comes 2001 so let's all head for the sun" was released on 9th May 2011
