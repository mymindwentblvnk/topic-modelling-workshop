Malignant Eternal is a black-/industrial-metal band formed in 1991 in Bergen, Norway. Also known as  M-Eternal.

Members:
T. Reaper - Lead/Backing Vocals
Arve Isdal - Guitars
Tom Stien - Guitars
Even Ormestad - Bass
Roy Ole Førland - Keyboards
Brynjulv Guddal - Drums, Loops, Programming and Keyboards.

Discography:

- Tårnet (1995)
- Far Beneath The Sun (1997)
- 20th Century Beast [EP] (1998)
