This death metal/grindcore band were from Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.  They released one album on Razorback Records, Finger Food Fetish for the Morbidly Abnormal. The album was mastered by Roger Beaujard of Mortician through Primitive Recordings in Pennsylvania, USA. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kutabare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
