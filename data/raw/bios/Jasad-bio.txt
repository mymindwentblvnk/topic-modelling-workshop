Jasad is a brutal death metal band from Bandung, West Java, Indonesia. Since 2000, Ferly (guitars), Dani (drums), Joel (bass) & Man (vocals) agreed to create and play US-styled brutal death metal. Technically and musically, Jasad are influenced by some of the traits of Californian death metal, such as blasting technicality and groove elements. Their influences include Suffocation, Internal Bleeding, and Devourment.

Jasad have currently released three demos and two full-lengths (named Witness of Perfect Torture and Annihilate the Enemy). The band plans to release their third full-length Rebirth ov Jatisunda later in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jasad">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
