Thy Art Is Murder are an Australian deathcore band from Blacktown, Sydney, New South Wales, formed in 2006. The band currently consists of vocalist Chris "CJ" McMahon, guitarists Sean Delander and Andy Marsh, drummer Jesse Beahler and bassist Kevin Butler.They have released five full-length albums and three EPs to date. Their latest album Human Target was released on July 26, 2019, through Nuclear Blast Records. Their second album Hate debuted at no. 35 on the ARIA Charts, making them the first extreme metal band to ever reach the Top 40 of this chart, while Holy War debuted at no. 7, the highest-ever position for an Australian extreme metal act. Vocalist CJ McMahon mentioned in interviews with Bluestribute TV and Aggressive Tendencies that the band, individually, has different inspirations and musicians that influence their particular writing and performance style. He added that the band all together are influenced by bands like Decapitated, Gojira, Meshuggah, and Behemoth. The band have frequently cited Behemoth as a major influence on their style whom McMahon has referred to as "the best band on this planet.''
