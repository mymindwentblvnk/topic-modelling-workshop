Topmodelz is a joint project between Bass-T. Sven-R-G, Franky Tunes and Charly Lownoise. It was founded in 2000 by DJ Pulsedriver. The project is currently signed to Aqualoop Records. They have released six singles and two albums.  Bass-T is currently leading the project. Topmodelz is one of the most successful hands-up scene bands.
