1) Pyromancer is a band from Lexington, KY USA formed in 2013 by Master of Graveyard Torment (of Apochryphal Revelation) and Conqueror Horus (of Tombstalker). They play black/death metal in the style of VON, Blasphemy, Beherit, Sarcofago, and Archgoat. The band released Demo MMXV in 2015.
