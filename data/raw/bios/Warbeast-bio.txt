Warbeast is a Thrash/Death Metal band from Antwerp, Belgium. In august 2008 they rose from the ashes of Necroplant. Instead of bringing Necroplant back to life, they started an entire new band. And they did well! Go and see for yourself at one of their shows.
They recorded their first demo, called Death Metal Thrashers. 
In the beginning of 2010 Ath decided to put an end to his career in warbeast, and he was replaced by Kristof. Warbeast is going to record it's first full-cd in november.
