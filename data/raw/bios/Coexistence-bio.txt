Coexistence was formed in 2015 in Siena, Italy by Mirko Battaglia Pitinello (guitar/vocal). After several changes in 2016 they reach the final line-up with Mirko Battaglia Pitinello (guitar/vocal) Leonardo Bellavista (guitar) , Christian Luconi (bass) and Alessandro Formichi (drums).
