Kammerflimmer Kollektief is a German musical group, founded by Thomas Weber, consisting of Heike Aumüller, Johannes Frisch. They play a mixture of jazz, noise, electronic music, free jazz and experimental music. 

Formed in 1996 in Karlsruhe, Germany (then with following members: Thomas Weber, Heike Aumüller, Johannes Frisch, Christopher Brunner, Heike Wendelin, and D. Wurm), they have released to date nine albums and have appeared on many jazz and electronic compilation albums.
