Torturor is a Slam Death Metal / Brutal Death Metal band from New Zealand. They formed in 1998 by Lindsey (Vocals) ,  Robert (Guitar/ Bass),  Kris (Bass/Guitar) and Fender (Drums). They all played for various band such as Odius Embowel, Molested Entrails, Eviscerate (Nzl), Sinistrous Diabolus and Offal Stench.

They have released a debut self-titled full-length album in 2005.
