Bak De Syv Fjell features Haavard (ex-Ulver), and Kvitrafn of Gorgoroth and (pre-Immortal) Det Hedenske Folk. 2 Songs of melodic Viking Metal with both clear male vocals and etherial female vocals in the vein of Ulver, Vintersorg, Hammers Of Misfortune, etc.

The band's website reads "Bak De Syv Fjell is reactivated.  There will be an attempt on recording an album in studio this summer." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bak+De+Syv+Fjell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
