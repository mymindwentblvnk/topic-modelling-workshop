From the wasteland known as the Ohio Valley comes Doctor Smoke, a four piece doom metal band featuring members Matt Tluchowski (Vocals,Guitars), Steve Lehocky (Lead Guitar), Cody Cooke (Bass), and Dave Trikones (Drums). 

The quartet draws it's influence from the classic, guitar driven sounds of early heavy metal while throwing their own modernized spin on the genre. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Doctor+Smoke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
