Kristina Orbakaite (born 25/05/1971 in Moscow), the daughter of Алла Пугачёва, is one of the most popular Russian female vocalists and actresses. 

Kristina was awarded "World Music Award" in 2002 for Best Selling Russian Artist, and "MuzTV Music Award" in 2005 and 2006 for the best female singer in Russia. She has played leading roles in 3 theatrical productions and over 15 movies.

Her sold out 2006 tour "MY LIFE" was one of the most successful tours ever for the Russian artist in the USA.

Kristina is married to an American businessman, Mikhail Zemtsov. She has two sons: Nikita born in 1990, and Denny born in 1998.
