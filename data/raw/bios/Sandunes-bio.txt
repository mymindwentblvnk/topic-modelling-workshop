Sandunes is the pseudonym of Mumbai based electronic artist Sanaya Ardeshir. Previously a pianist who had played for local bands, she shifted to electronic music after studying music in London. 

She released her first EP, Out of the Box in 2012 and her second EP, Ever Bridge in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sandunes">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
