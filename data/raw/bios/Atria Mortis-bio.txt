We Are A Heavy Metal Band From Greece (Thesaloniki).In 2006 We Have Releashed Our First Demo Cd Called "The Samurai" Which Contains Three Songs (Blazing Skies, The Samurai, Like A Shadow In My Mind). The Demo Line Up Is Bill Skandalis (Vocals), Vagelis Boultoukas (Guitars), Haris Vavalidis (Guitars), Lefteris Kasmiris (Bass) And Dimos Konstantinidis (Drums). We Are Now Recording Some New Material Which We Plan To Release As Soon As Possible. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atria+Mortis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
