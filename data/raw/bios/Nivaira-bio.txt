Nivaira began as a two musician band, featuring Diana Alieva on vocals and keyboards, and David Tordera on vocals and guitars. This simple line up performed four gigs, after that the band was joined by Dani Ruiz (drums), his brother David Ruiz (bass) and David Tordera’s brother Josep (guitar). So far the musicians have released one record, called “The City” (2005), recorded in Bled, Slovenia, at dbrecordings studio.
