KREFT is a new project from (Overlege) Roger Rasmussen/Nattefrost (Carpathian Forest, Nattefrost). He is currently working on the first full length album, with co-musician and producer  (Patolog) Fredrik Falk/Yap Papaya of Batboner. The demo Lommemannen E.P. is available through Neseblod Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kreft">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
