Wale Davies (Tec) and Olumide Ayeni (Ghost), popularly known by the stage name Show Dem Camp (SDC) is a Nigerian music duo comprising two friends who are both rappers. They met in a rap battle scene in the UK before moving back to Nigeria and agreeing to work together and a brand. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Show+Dem+Camp">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
