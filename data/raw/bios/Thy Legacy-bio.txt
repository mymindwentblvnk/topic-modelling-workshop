Thy Legacy was formed in 2002. They play death metal and are heavily influenced by old school bands like Autopsy, Morbid Angel, Celtic Frost, Obituary etc. They have released 4 official demos during years 2004-2007, listed below.

Dismal Symphony  Demo, 2004
Reverse Exorcism Demo, 2005
Blood Work Theories Demo, 2006
