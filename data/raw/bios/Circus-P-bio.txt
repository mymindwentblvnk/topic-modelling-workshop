Circus-P (formerly Doofus-P (デュファスＰ) or matt9five) is a music producer from the United States that focuses on creating electronic pop music. His songs are well known for their emotional lyrics, hard-hitting bass, and most notably for utilizing the vocal synth software “Vocaloid". He has also created a few UTAU voice banks such as Chii and Aido Ishiame, and is the creator of fanmade Vocaloid Kasane Keko. 

His English Vocaloid songs “Copycat” and “Goodbye” are featured in the global version of SEGA/Colorful Palette’s mobile rhythm game, Hatsune Miku: Colorful Stage!. 

His song “Ten Thousand Stars” was the winning pick of the Miku Expo 2016 song contest, which has been performed live worldwide for thousands of people. A variety of his songs have also been featured in “Vocamerica”, a US-based concert project focused on promoting English Vocaloid works. He has also created demo songs for Hatsune Miku, Megurine Luka, Kaito, Cyber Diva, and many others.
