Donald Cumming is an american musician. He's best known as the frontman of The Virgins. The Virgins split in 2013 and Cumming released his first solo album, Out Calls Only, in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Donald+Cumming">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
