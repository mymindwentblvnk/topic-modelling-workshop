Lykaion are a metal band from Italy. Their music is a modern and complex blend of different metal and non-metal genres, influenced by Nevermore, Opeth, Katatonia and Anathema.

Founded in 2000, the band released their first promo CD "Behind... A Whisper" in 2005. In the same year, vocalist Luca Mazzilli left the band. In 2006, Tiziana Palmieri joined Lykaion as a vocalist. In this line-up, the band recorded their second promo CD "The Things I've Left" which was released by the end of the same year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lykaion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
