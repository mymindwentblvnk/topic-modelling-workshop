Amaymon was a death metal band from France that was formed in 1991. They were signed to the label Adipocere Records which was infact founded by Bivel who was the guitarist of the band. In 1993, they had a split EP with the Canadian band Purulence and an Advance Tape, both under Adipocere. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amaymon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
