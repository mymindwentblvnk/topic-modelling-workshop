Effi is Thomas Petritsch from Graz, Austria.  The album ASTRONAUT, which took him three years to write,  is his debut album, co-produced by Alexander Nefzger.  Effi's artwork graced the album, and he played most of the instruments himself. He's already toured in Germany, Russia, Estonia and Latvia, in addition to his time as a street performer in New Zealand, where he developed his accent in speaking English. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Effi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
