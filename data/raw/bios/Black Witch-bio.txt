There are several artists that perform under the name Black Witch:
1) A heavy metal band from Berlin, Spandau, active in the early 1980s
2) A band from Mossoro, Brazil, formed in 2015

2. Black Witch formed in Mossoro, Brazil, in May 2015.  They released their first EP, Aware, the following September.  The current line up comprises of Lorena Rocha (vocals) , Rafaum Costa (guitar) , Fred Nunes (drums) , Jorge Luiz (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Witch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
