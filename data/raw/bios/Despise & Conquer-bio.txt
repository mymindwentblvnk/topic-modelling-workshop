Despise & Conquer are a death/thrash metal band from the Ruhr Area, Germany. They band has been established in June 2008 and consists of:

Udo Erwig (Paranoiz) - vocals
Mario Bergermann (Paranoiz, Citizen Kain) – guitar
Daniel Stöckner (Stormgarde, Treasury) – guitar
Andre Saborowski (Paranoiz, Philantropist) - bass
Meik Bartnick (Paranoiz, Suffocate Bastard) – drums

Release date of their debut album "INVASION", was March 31st 2012. The CD was mixed by Andreas Funke at Metallurgy Tonstudio Germany and mastered by Dan Swanö at Unisound Studios Sweden!
