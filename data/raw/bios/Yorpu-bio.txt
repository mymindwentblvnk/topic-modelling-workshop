Thrash / Grind Core band from České Budějovice, Czech Republic. Formed in 2007 from ex-members of Czech bands Gride and Onanizer. Released a split album with FlatV5 and Pisstolero in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yorpu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
