Jericho Jackson is a hip-hop duo, consisting of underground Detroit MC Elzhi and producer Khrysis. They released their debut project, titled "Khrysis & Elzhi Are Jericho Jackson" on February 23rd, 2018.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jericho+Jackson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
