Female fronted melodic death metal band raised 2003 in Guadalajara, Mexico from the ashes of Melancholy. They have released 2 demos in 2004 and a full length album The Prophecy Of Mhisty: A Prelude Of Chaos in 2011.

Current line-up consists of:

Cecilia Ledesma, vocals
Angela Ramirez, keyboards
Noe Gallegos, bass
Luis Santana, drums
Roberto Perez, guitars and vocals
