Atomchild has been making and performing electronic music since 1998. He is known as Jake Mooshian and performs shows in Baltimore, Washington, D.C., and Westminster from time to time. He primarily uses a waveform editor to create the detialed music rather than sequencing devices, which is a very different method from other electronic musicians. Examples of his more recent music is posted on www.sectionz.com with detailed information and reviews on each song. He can be contacted at www.myspace.com/atomchild <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atomchild">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
