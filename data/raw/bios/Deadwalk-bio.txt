Deadwalk is a metallic hardcore band from Montreal, Canada. featuring the ex vocalist from Too Pure To Die , many have compared deadwalk to  Bury Your Dead  being very similar in tone and sound.

In 2007, Deadwalk's independently released EP, "Powerhouse" in 2008 the band signed to Year of the Sun Records.

in 2009 deadwalk released their debut full length entitled "scandalous"
