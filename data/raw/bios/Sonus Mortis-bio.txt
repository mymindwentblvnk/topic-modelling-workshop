Based in Dublin, Ireland, SONUS MORTIS is a new one-man project from VALEDICTION bassist, Kevin Byrne, playing Dark Symphonic Death/Black/Doom Metal.

A 3 song demo was recorded in July 2013.
Debut album 'Propaganda Dream Sequence' out March 2014.
