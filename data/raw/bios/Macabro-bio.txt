Imaginary melancholic twin of HEZZEL comes back with a new album, bringing us a combination of ambient downtempo with some abstractly vague influences from psybient and idm
******

An electronic project from Latvia founded in 2006. 

As the time moved on the music has seen an evolution from harsher noisy sound to warm ambient atmospheres, drones and field recordings, along with using a huge variety of different sounds and self-made samples, such as bells, whistles, xylophones etc. 

The newer works however take a step further in more rhythmic compositions, with a vast array of influences from different music genres, like psy-chill, idm, and downtempo.
