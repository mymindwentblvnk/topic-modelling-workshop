Arkazum - Muzakra "is a brooding, hypnotic, surreal journey into dark infused Rock territory" from the former Cathedral, Year Zero and Blackstar Rising member Griff.

Muzakra is a 11 track CD released by Yednod Records (yednod 1) on 2nd March 2011. 

Tracklisting: Our Father, Lungland, You Can Be My Hearse, Cold Wind Blows, Black Lipped Grin, Initiation, Contemplation, Deep Joy, Bombiel, Udderbudder, Zumlight.
