Rhenium was a nu metal/hard rock band formed in the early 2000s. They released 3 albums, Ragged Thoughts (2002), Absent Thoughts (2004) and Birth of a Century (2005) before breaking up in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rhenium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
