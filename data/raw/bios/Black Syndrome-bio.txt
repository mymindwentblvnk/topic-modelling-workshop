Black Syndrome (블랙신드롬) was formed by Bak Yeong-Cheol in 1986. A few months later, they changed some members and wrote songs for their 1st album. In 1988, Black Syndrome published their debut album 'Fatal Attraction'. Since then they've made nine albums, a live album and eleven omnibus albums. Over the decade they've played more than a thousand gigs. And also they released their 2nd and 6th album in overseas markets like Japan, UK, and France. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Syndrome">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
