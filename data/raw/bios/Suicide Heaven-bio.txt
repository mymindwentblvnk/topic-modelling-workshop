Suicide Heaven is a Neo-Classical Death Metal band from Sapporo, Japan formed in May 2009.
They have released two albums (Two Demos: Wyvern (2009) and Future Ages (2010)) and some of their songs are on their Official Myspace.
The band is comprised of three members:  Ryogi (Guitar & Vocals), Shuji (Drums) and Shogo (Bass).
