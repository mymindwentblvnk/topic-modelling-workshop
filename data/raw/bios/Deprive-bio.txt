(1) Deprive are a doom/death band from Cantabria, Spain. Formed in 2013 and currently signed to Memento Mori records, they have released two full length albums, "Into Oblivion" in 2015 and "Temple of Lost Wisdom" in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deprive">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
