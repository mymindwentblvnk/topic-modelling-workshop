Vindland was formed in 2005 in Paimpol (Brittany) as a Viking / Pagan / Melodic Black Metal band. After a demo, an EP and a few concerts, the band split up.
New compositions were created starting from 2012 and were recorded in 2014/2015.

Current line-up:
Camille L: Guitars & compos
Marc LG: Drums
Johnny G.R: Guitars
Romuald E: Vocals & Lyrics

Discography:

Ancestors' Age EP [2009]
Hanter Savet [2016]
