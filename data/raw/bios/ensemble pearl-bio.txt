New collaborative album featuring core members of Sunn O))) & Boris and ex-members of Ghost, Jesse Sykes & The Sweet Hereafter, White Heaven.
Recorded by Masato Suzuki. Mixed by Randall Dunn. Mastered by Bob Weston. Featuring artwork by Simon Fowler and Michał Mozolewski. Cosmic heavy rock sounds in an area between Link Wrey, Earth “Hex”, and early Tangerine Dream. Inspired by 50s-70s rock and contemporary music productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ensemble+Pearl">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
