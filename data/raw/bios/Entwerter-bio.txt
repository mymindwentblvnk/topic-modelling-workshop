Entwerter is a thrash/groove metal band from Vienna, Austria. They have released two full-length albums, "...und das ist erst der Anfang" ("...and this is just the beginning") and "Mortal Engine". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Entwerter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
