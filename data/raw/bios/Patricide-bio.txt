Patricide is the atmospheric electronic project of Aqua Moon Coffee, currently based in Seattle, WA. Patricide aims to use texture, repetition, and atmosphere to create trance like sonic experiences with themes stemming from shamanism, astrology, climate change, and isolationism. Her most recent album, Classical Complex Obsession, was released December 2019, with a new album slated winter 2020. Two singles from the album have been released so far. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Patricide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
