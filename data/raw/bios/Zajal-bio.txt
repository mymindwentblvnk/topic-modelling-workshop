Zajal is a Death Metal band influenced by Oriental / Middle Eastern music , it was formed during 2010 in Cairo , Egypt .
The band recorded a demo album and they called it " For The Throne " , It contains 7 Tracks , 6 Originals and 1 Cover .
Currently the band is looking for labels to release their demo " For the Throne " .
