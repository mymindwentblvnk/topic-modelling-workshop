Landed is a "noise/rock" band begun in 1997 by Shawn Greenlee, Joel Kyack and Dan St. Jacques. Since its inception, the band has operated as a performance collective involving a rotating cast of Providence area musicians including Rick Pelletier, Neil Burke, John Dwyer, Mat Brinkman, J. Ryan, Adam Autry, Brian Gibson, Brian Chippendale, and perhaps a few others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Landed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
