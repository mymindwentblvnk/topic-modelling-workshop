Dead Alone from Bavaria, Germany is a metal band with a blackened, melancholic note. Formed in 2004 by Florian Hefft and Martin Hofbauer. Debuted with Slivering Marrow in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+Alone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
