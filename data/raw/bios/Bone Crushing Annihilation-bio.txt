Bone Crushing Annihilation (BCA) was  death metal band based around the Washingtonville, New York area.  They recorded several demos, and played numerous live shows, including the Chance Theatre in Poughkeepsie, NY. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bone+Crushing+Annihilation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
