1. Final Judgement is a Thrash Metal Band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania formed in 2017 releasing a demo album you can check out here:  https://finaljudgement.bandcamp.com/releases 



     2. Final Judgement was an old school Death/Thrash metal band from the early 90's that released one CD, Desolating Sacrilege, released on Anastasia Records.

The band was from Atlanta, GA, USA, and played shows at the Masquerade, Wreck Room, Somber Reptile and other clubs. They also played shows with Mortification, Living Sacrifice, Halloween, Tourniquet, Lust Control and others. The band has broken up and the members have melted back into society. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Final+Judgement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
