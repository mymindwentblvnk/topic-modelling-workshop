There are two artists by the name Men Behind the Sun.

1) A harsh industrial/power electronics project by Max Presch & M. Sägmüller.

So far only one record has been released titled Unit 731 as a limited vinyl 10" 450 copies and as a limited Cdr 100 copies All sounds, noises, samples exclusively captured from the film "Men Behind The Sun" by Fu Chi.

2) A harsh noise duo from Ceder Rapids Iowa consisting of Andrew Huitt and Weston Adam.
