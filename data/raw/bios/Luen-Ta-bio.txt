Luen-Ta was a progressive death metal band from France, Rouen. Formed in 1996 under the name Neuroblast but changed it in 1998, when they released their first, self-titled demo.  Since then they released 2 LP: Ghost Area (in 2003) and Parasited Evolution (in 2006). The band is currently split-up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Luen-Ta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
