Lunatic Medlar are an instrumental (mostly), 4piece heavy Prog/rock project from Athens, Greece. Founded sometime in 2009 after the disbandment of Konkave. Consists of members of Tardive Dyskinesia, Sun Of Nothing & Universe 217, a few of the most active & remarkable bands in Greece at the moment. After a few live appearances the band entered Studio217, with Manos G. & Steve Lado behind the mixing desk, with sole purpose to record their first full length album, titled “Finely Tuned Machine”, consisting of 8 heavy, groovy, atmospheric tracks for fans of Keelhaul, Zozobra, Black Cobra, Mastodon, Baroness, Intronaut & not only... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/lunatic+medlar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
