Female-fronted speed/thrash metal band from Japan, formed in 1991. They released a demo-tape in June 1993, named "Karma", and participated on a couple of V.A. releases. Currently disbanded.
The band had a one day revival in 2014 and released a remastered CD, containing 7 old tracks.
