Massive Destruktion is the re-incarnation of Massive Distortion, a project featuring Rob (Drums, vocals) and Bran-Chi (Lead vocals), founders of the band, back in 2005. The band reformed in 2007 with new guitar players, PJ and Carlos. There were a few changes with bass players until late 2008 when Angel took the duties as bassist. The band has shared stage with Thrash legends Exodus and Hirax, and other prominent bands from the USA  and Puerto Rico.
