Rotterdam from Vienna make unplugged techno. On their acoustic instruments Susanne Amann (cello, flute, electronics) and Michael Klauser (acoustic guitar, electronics) are playing minimally changing patterns to booming techno loops. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rotterdam">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
