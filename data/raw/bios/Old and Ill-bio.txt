Emerging after a trek between high desert and bat city via cenopian passageway, OLD and ILL arrived to the surface gritty and altered from having inhaled tainted ammonia soaked subterranean air only to retreat to danky hideaways to further craft their menacing, paranoid music. The result is a belligerent yet deliberate rawness that is equally at home in guano drenched limestone or remote desert. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Old+and+Ill">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
