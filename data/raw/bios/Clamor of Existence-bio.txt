‪Clamor of Existence are a metal band‬ from Greece.

Clamor of Existence were formed in 2011 by 4 university students in Patras. 
They started covering some songs but eventually they ended up writing their own music, with many influences from many bands.

Members:
Panagiwtis Poulakidas (bass-vocals)
George Delios (guitars)
John Sarris (guitars)
Vaios Sven (drums-vocals)‬
