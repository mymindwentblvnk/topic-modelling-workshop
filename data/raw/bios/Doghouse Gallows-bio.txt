Sludge/Stoner Metal band formed in Utrecht, Netherlands in 2008 by (former) members of Detonation, Orphanage, Kutschurft, Pleurisy, Grindpad, Engorge and Man Made Ivory.

The debut album "Dodgy Deals" was released in spring 2009.

Line up:
Jorre Jansen - Vocals
Mike Ferguson - Guitar
Patrick Velis - Bass
George Oosthoek - Drums
