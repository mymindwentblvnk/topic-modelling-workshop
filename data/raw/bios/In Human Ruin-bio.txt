In Human Ruin are a UK progressive/technical/thrash/metalcore band from Swindon, Wiltshire.

Formed in mid 2007 and have gained some popularity on the local metal circuit. They have a self released, self titled demo which can be heard from there Myspace[1] and is available to buy from their shows.

They are currently national finalists for the Bloodstock Battle of the Bands and hope to embark on a UK tour next year with The Argent Dawn though nothing has been confirmed. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/In+Human+Ruin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
