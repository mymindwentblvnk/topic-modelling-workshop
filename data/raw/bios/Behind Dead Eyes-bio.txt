Behind Dead Eyes is a metal band from Äänekoski, Finland. The band was formed on the spring of 2012. Their music is a nice blend of death metal with some groovy and thrashy vibes as well as some beautiful melodies. Behind Dead Eyes is Tuomo Laulainen on guitar and clean vocals & backing growls, Niki Kuivakangas on drums and backing vocals. Bassists come and go.. The band parted ways with vocalist Joni Hakulinen soon after releasing Dead End. 

On the 6th of August Behind Dead Eyes released it's first demo named Dead End which you can download for free from their bandcamp page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Behind+dead+eyes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
