Let's go one step further in the early death metal world ! Hailing from Rutherford, NJ, SAVAGE DEATH is without doubt one of those cult demo bands that managed to achieve a cult following and an influencial place in the early death metal hall of fame along with their compatriots of POSSESSED. Despite sounding more thrash than death metal, both demo will surely please every metalheads trying to find the roots of death metal !
