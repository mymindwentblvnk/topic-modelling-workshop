Who killed Frank? is a four piece indie/alternative band from Hamburg/Bremen/Hannover, Germany founded in July 2009.

The name has it's origin in the film Donnie Darko where a big rabbit called Frank is characterized as the protagonists' enemy. In the end the rabbit got killed.
The release of their debutalbum "Sex on a festival" will be in november 2010.

Members: 
Hinni Hummers (vocals, guitar)
Michael Kuber (vocals, guitar, keys)
Pascal Benke (bass)
Dennis Oldenburg (drums)
