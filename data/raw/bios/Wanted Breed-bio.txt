Wanted Breed is a heavy/power metal band from Los Angeles, California.  They formed in 1985 and they released an album called Knights in Armor in 1986.  The band consists of  Dave Frederick on vocals,  Paul C. Caspio on guitar,  Scott Snyder on bass and The Gunner on drums.  Scott Snyder is also in hard rock band Great White and was in another band called Accomplice that featured  Johnny Gioeli (who is currently in the bands Axel Rudi Pell, Hardline and Crush 40).   In 2012, they have guitarist Phil Whidden of Scarlet Kreed and released an album called Galactic Warriors. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wanted+Breed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
