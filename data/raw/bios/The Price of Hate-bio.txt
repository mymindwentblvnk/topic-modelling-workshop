The Price of Hate is a thrash/death metal band from Manitowac, WI. They have recorded one demo, The Price of Hate (2007), and are currently undergoing line-up changes.

Current Members (from their MySpace page):

Jacky - Self-destructive laryngitis, Dischordant 6-string manipulation
Trostidos, Man Of Tortilla Shells (Chip For Short) - Heretical axe-grinding
Jim - Crunch and grind
IV - Low-end tonal trauma
