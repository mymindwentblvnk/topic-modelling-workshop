Dark Occult Black Metal from Austria.  Formed in 1992, they released just 2 demos, "Faturek - The Wolf" in 1995, and "Host of Chaos" in 1996.  Their music was similar in to the Scandinavian BM scene at the time, using mostly dark, slow to mid paced riffs and some subtle keyboards, ie- The early releases by Carpathian Forest, Ancient, Manes, Obtained Enslavement, Strid, etc... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Call+Of+The+Four+Gates">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
