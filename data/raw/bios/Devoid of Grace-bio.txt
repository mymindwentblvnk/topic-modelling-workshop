Devoid Of Grace is a death-metal band from Lipetsk, Russia. Formed on summer, 2001 it was heavily influenced by such metal acts as Death, Vader, Cryptopsy, Strapping Young Lad, Emperor, The Crown, Luciferion, Behemoth, Morbid Angel, Decapitated, Dies Irae, Meshuggah and others. The band also brings in original approach to the genre.
