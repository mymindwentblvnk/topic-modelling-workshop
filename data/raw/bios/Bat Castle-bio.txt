Bat Castle is a 5 piece from Denton, Texas. Their style is a mixture of black and death metal with an avant-garde twist. With the release of a six track self-titled demo in 2004, Bat Castle have played a handful of shows since and continue to create music. Most of the members of Bat Castle are also in the band Shaolin Death Squad. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bat+Castle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
