The Rumour Said Fire is an indie/folk band formed in Copenhagen, Denmark in 2008 by frontman Jesper Lidang. On September 21st 2009, they released their 4 song EP, which was later followed up by a re-release with 2 additional songs. 

Although still being a new act on the Danish music scene, they have already made a name for themselves with songs like Love for the Tortured and The Balcony.

On October 18th 2010, their debut album The Arrogant was released.
