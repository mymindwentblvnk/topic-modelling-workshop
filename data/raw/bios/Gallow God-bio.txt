Gallow God is a Traditional  Doom Metal band, based in London, UK and formed in 2009.

Their Debut EP False Mystical Prose was released in 2010. The EP can be streamed in its entirety from the Gallow God website www.gallowgod.co.uk.
Full length The Veneration of Serpents was released in April 2013.
