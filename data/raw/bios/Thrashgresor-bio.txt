Argentinian thrash metal act created from the ashes of a previous band called Martirio, in 2011. Based in Federación, Entre Ríos, they also have members from Concordia. They recorded and released an EP CD in 2012.

Current Lineup:
- Victor Suarez (Bass, Backing vocals)
- Andrés Padín (Rhythm guitars, Lead vocals)
- Emanual Sampayo (Lead guitars, Backing vocals)
