Marching Church was originally a solo project by Elias Bender Rønnenfelt, singer of  Iceage. It is now a full band consisting of Elias, Kristian Emdal and Anton Rothstein of Lower, Cæcilie Trier of Choir of Young Believers, Bo H. Hansen of Hand of Dust/Sexdrome and Frederikke Hoffmeier from Puce Mary.
