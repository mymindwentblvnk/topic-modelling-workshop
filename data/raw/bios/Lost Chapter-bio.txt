Lost Chapter is a five piece metal band from a rural community in South East Ohio. With many influences including current world events, Lost Chapter hopes to shed light on recent shortcomings and despair of the world with a positive, uplifting message. The members of Lost Chapter like to refer to this campaign as "Life Metal" in compression to the counter part "Death Metal". The music produced by Lost Chapter is sure to grab your attention with the catchy, heart pounding, forward march like rhythm and lyrics which give hope to a generation that has seen its share of hard times. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lost+Chapter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
