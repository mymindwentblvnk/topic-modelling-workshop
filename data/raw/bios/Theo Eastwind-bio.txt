Modern day troubadour, Theo Eastwind became an icon on New York subway platforms through years as a Music Under New York performer. The hole worn through the body of his acoustic guitar is a tangible reminder of Theo's commitment to his music. Theo's blend of edgy alternative strumming and graceful melodies quickly hook straphangers and have landed sales of over 13,000 CDs underground. Theo Eastwind was featured on MSNBC.com, earned Big Apple Icon label by the New York Post, and won the 100-musician Williamsburg Live Singer/Songwriter Competition with material from his April 4th 2006 release, The O.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Theo+Eastwind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
