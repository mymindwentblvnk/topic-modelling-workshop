Formed in Belfast, Northern Ireland in 2001, a local groove-metal band blending stoner,thrash and heavy grooves they deffenetly stand out amongst the more generic sounding bands. With only one e.p (The Rise of the Robot of Satan) recorded since there birth and with all 295 copies sold they continue to get noticed outside their local seen,  but the band are constantly plagued with set back due almost constant line-up changes.      <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sixkiller+Zero">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
