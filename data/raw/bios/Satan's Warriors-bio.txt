Satan's Warriors was a death metal / grindcore band that started in February of 1986 in West Newton, Massachusetts.   The band's last line-up consisted of Seth Putnam (who would later form Anal Cunt) on vocals & guitar, Paul Kraynak on guitar and  John Gillis on drums.  Past members include D. J. Infernal Honky Hater as the DJ, Drew on guitar and Satanic Nun Molester on guitar.  They recorded a few demos that were never released, but managed to put out one split with hardcore punk band Psycho.  It is not known when they disbanded.
