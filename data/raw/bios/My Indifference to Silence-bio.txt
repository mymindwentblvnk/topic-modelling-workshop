Side-project of Vladimir Andreev of Откровения Дождя (Revelations of Rain), that plays death doom metal with dark atmospheres. One album was released in 2010 under the name On the Edge of the NetherRealm before the name of the project was changed to My Indifference to Silence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/My+Indifference+to+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
