an old school death metal band from Greece that was formed in 2005. Includes members from Dead Congregation, Nocternity, Embrace of Thorns among others. As of now, their discography consists of : 

Necropeophagus (Demo, 2005)
Revenge of the Booger (Demo, 2006 with Morbid Noise records)
Day of a hot Cannibalistic Orgium (Demo, 2006 with Morbid Noise records)
Who Will Survive? And What Will Be Left Of Them (Best of/Compilation, 2007)
Necrovorous / Meathole Infection (Split with Meathole Infection, 2009)
Crypt of the Unembalmed Cadavers (EP, 2009, with Blood Harvest records).
