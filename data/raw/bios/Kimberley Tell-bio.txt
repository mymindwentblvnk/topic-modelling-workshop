Born March 10th 1989, Kimberly Tell is a Spanish singer and dancer of English-Danish descent, and primarily known for her TV roles as Rose in "Something to Celebrate", Ulrike in "Looking for the North", and Pilar in the award-winning "Hierro" series by Movistar+ .

Since 2018, she has been recording music in a professional manner, mostly in Spanish, and has described her sound as "a bit of R&B but with a mix of many things". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kimberley+Tell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
