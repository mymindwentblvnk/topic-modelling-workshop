Born in the depths of Bangalore, India, Dark Desolation roared into the metal scene in 2010 with their debut demo song, Futile Dogma. The band is a five-piece raw black/grind outfit formed by self-confessed maniacs who profess to have varied tastes in music. Coming to birth after umpteen feuds, Dark Desolation proposes to be a major force in the metal landscape for years to come. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Desolation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
