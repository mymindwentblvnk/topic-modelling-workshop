Swoop G is an American rapper from Long Beach, California, formerly signed to Death Row Records but never release an album on the label. He released Undisputed in 1997 and World Don't Take Me Personally in 1999 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Swoop+G">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
