Amain Berhane better known as Berhana is an American singer-songwriter. His self-titled EP was self-released in 2016. His song "Grey Luh" from the album was featured on Donald Glover's Atlanta. Berhana released his debut album, Han, on October 18, 2019 via EQT Recordings. The project features singles "Health Food", "Lucky Strike", and "I Been (ft.
