1) Brixx is a Melbourne-based producer, composer and DJ influenced by cult cinema, electro, minimalism, post punk, techno and EBM. Her music is underpinned by a darkness that slowly bubbles to the surface, acting as a lens through which to explore the human condition, gender, and sexuality.

2) Brixx was a Danish pop group which represented Denmark in the Eurovision Song Contest 1982, in which it sang "Video, Video".

The group members were Jens Brixtofte, John Hatting, Torben Jacobse, Steen Eljer Olsen and Bjørn Holmgård Sørensen.
