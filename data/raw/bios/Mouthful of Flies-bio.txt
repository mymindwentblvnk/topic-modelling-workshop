London-based band revolving around ex-South Africans, Dan Stevenson  (ex-Bloodstone on leads/rhythm & vox and Deangersmith (ex- 2 Dogs Funking ,  Odyssey (South Africa) , Brothering} on rhythm guit and backing vox.

Formed in 2005. Their album, Ten : Negative : Sixes was released on: 2007-09-01.

Studio Musicians:
Dan Stevenson - Vocals & Guitars / Artwork / Lyrics
Deangersmith - Guitars & Drum Programming, Bass on 'Breeder'
Robi Smith - Bass on 1,2,4, 9, 10, 11
Johan Buys - Bass on 3, 7, 8

Live musicians:
