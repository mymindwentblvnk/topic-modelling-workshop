Black Crane is a Lithuanian progressive rock/metal band, formed in 2004. Starting and current line-up of the band is:
Remigijus "Remzis" Bruzas - guitar, backing vocals,
Adomas "Adis" Rutkauskas - vocals,
Valdas Petkus - bass,
Vygandas "Rock'n'roll Kid" Grusauskas - drums.

The band plays music that is somewhat more technical and progressive than the usual heavy metal, with lyrics centered mainly on the human inner-self. Influences come from wide a range of artists and genres, ranging from jazz and glam rock/hair metal to stoner metal, adult-oriented metal and death metal.
