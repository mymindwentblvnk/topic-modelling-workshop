Tempestora is a British Heavy Metal / Thrash Band From Gloucestershire UK. 
Started writing material under the influence of classic thrash bands such as Megadeth,Metallica,Slayer,Testament in 2009.
Although Clearly inspired by the speed of Thrash metal,Tempestoras sound relies alot on being aggressive and technical but never forgetting about melody! 

Line Up Contains All Original Members 
Sam Shiers - Vocals
Jay Deakins - Guitars
Alex Hawes - Drums

After A Heavy Year Of Relentlessy Gigging The Country With A 4 Track Ep,The Band Entered Bloodstock Metal for the masses competition In Wales,Becoming Regional Winners & Played Bloodstock Festival 2010 alongside Children Of Bodom,Devin Townsend & Evile!
Tempestora Supported GAMA BOMB @ The Croft Bristol.

March 2012 Took Tempestora To Serrated Edge Productions To Begin Tracking The Debut Album "The Battle Begins"
