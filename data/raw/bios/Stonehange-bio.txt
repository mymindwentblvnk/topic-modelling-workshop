Stonehange was a one off project by Incubator guitarists Michael Hahn und Sven Schade. After recording Incubators seminal "Mcgilroy the Housefly " some studio time was left and Stonehange was created in a one week period.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stonehange">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
