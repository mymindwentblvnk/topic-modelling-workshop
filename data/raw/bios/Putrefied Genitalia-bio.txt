Greece Death Metal band formed in 2002 . Have released 3 demos , an EP and a split album with Atavism . They also appear in the "Review Sessions Part 1" double tape release that accompanied the 2nd issue of the Belgian u/g zine Bringer of Gore with the track "Rectal Blast". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Putrefied+Genitalia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
