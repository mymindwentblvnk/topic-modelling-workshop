Four piece Turn-based Stoner Doom from Brisbane, Australia. 

Founded in 2013 by members of The Fevered, FVCK MOUNTAIN, Through Plagues and SMHG. Their debut, self-titled LP can be listened to and purchased through Bandcamp at http://www.lizzardwizzard.bandcamp.com.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lizzard+Wizzard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
