Cuatro Gatos is a Heavy/Power Metal band from Madrid, Spain, formed in 2001. 

Line up:
-Juan Miguel Rodríguez - Vocals
-Covadonga Martínez - Vocals & Corus
-Pedro Vela - Guitars (ex-Ñu)
-Javier Canseco - Bass (ex-Arion)
-Carlos Álvarez - Keyboards (ex-Ñu)
-Joaquín Arellano - Drums (Mägo de Oz, ex-Saratoga (Esp), Muro, ex-Ñu, Jose Andrëa)

Juanmi, played with Txus DiFellato of Mägo de Oz, and Leo Jiménez (ex-Saratoga, ex-Alborde, Stravaganzza) the song "No me digas Adiós", in a Txus proyect called "El Cementerio de los Versos Perdidos".
