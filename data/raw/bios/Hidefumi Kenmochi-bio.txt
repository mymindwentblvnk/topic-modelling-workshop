Kenmochi Hidefumi (born 2 Aug 1981), is a Japanese musician, producer, composer, a member of Wednesday Campanella.

He began his career in jazz rap scene. Once he was found out by Nujabes, he released an album ”Falliccia” (2008) from his label Hydeout Productions.

Since he started “Wednesday Campanella” as music producer in 2012, he’s been breaking new ground with bass music attitude, addictive tracks, and very original lyrics.

He released his first album in nine years in May 2019 focusing on footwork and juke music entitled, "沸騰 沸く ~FOOTWORK〜". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hidefumi+Kenmochi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
