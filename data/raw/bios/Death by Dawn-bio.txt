Death by Dawn is a german death metal band founded 2001 in Aachen, Germany.
The most famous band member is Martin van Drunen, who was the vocalist of Bolt Thrower and who is still actice in Asphyx.
In 2006 the band released their first full length record One Hand One Foot... And a Lot of Teeth.


Discography:
Death by Dawn (2002, demo)
Dedicated to the Oppressed (2003, EP)
One Hand One Foot... And a Lot of Teeth (2006, album)


Line-Up:
Jens 'Jeff' Geldon - guitar
Martin van Drunen - vocals
P.J. Colapinto - drums
Gino Core Leone - bass
Ingo 'Fusel' Fussangel - guitar
