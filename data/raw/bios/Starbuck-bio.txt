Starbuck was a rock band formed in Atlanta, Georgia in 1974. Their debut single, Moonlight Feels Right, reached the #3 position on the Billboard singles charts in 1976. Although the band never re-created the success of their debut, several of their songs did chart in the Billboard Top 100 and their 1977 release Everybody Be Dancin' reached #38.

From 1976 to 1980, the band toured with popular groups of the era including ELO, KC & the Sunshine Band, Hall & Oates and Boston.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Starbuck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
