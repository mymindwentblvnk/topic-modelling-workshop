There are multiple artists with the name Margo:

1. Born in Toronto but based in New York, Los Angeles and Atlanta, Margo (formerly known as M Audio and now known as Margeaux) is a talented singer-songwriter-musician and star of VH1's Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.  

2. Margo is a glitch-pop band out of France that makes quirky pop songs sung by a blissful singer over a blend of minimal beats, general craziness, and lush soundscapes. The band consists of Jean-François Lecoq, Jérôme Naslin, Mélanie Masson. They've released two full-lenght albums: "The Catnap" in 2001 and "Furtives Furies" in 2005, and one remixed album. Also they appeared in some compilations and did remixes for Laudanum and Slick Sixty.
.
3. Margo is a brutal death metal band based out of Wallingford, Connecticut. They formed in late 2004 and released their debut full length 'Corpses Stacked To The Sun' in June 2007.

4. Margo is a Polish pop singer (full name: Małgorzata Gadzińska).

5. Margo is a bulgarian pop-folk singer.

6. Margo is a russian pop-rock band
