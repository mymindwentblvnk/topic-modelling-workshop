Myopic Empire is a doom metal act featuring musician Devon Fern. The band sometimes features guest musicians.

Former members: 
Tom Weber (2013-18): guitars
James Balcombe (2015-18): drums

The band's releases so far:
Myopeia (2013)
Doom EP (2015)
Doom Over Colchester (2016)
Silver Machine (2018)
Split with Lucifer's Fall (2019)
Road to Ruin (2019)
The Last Fire (2020)

Music is available as a download or CD via http://myopicempire.bandcamp.com/.
