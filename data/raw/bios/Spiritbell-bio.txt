Hungary (2010 - 2013, 2013 - present)

SpiritBell was formed in 2010 in Western-Hungary. After three years of activity, self recorded and released materials the band split up in 2013. The guys reorganized in 2016 when they have released their come back EP which was released by the German label Witches Brew.
