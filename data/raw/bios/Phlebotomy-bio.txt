There are mutliple artists with this name:

1) Phlebotomy was a doom metal band from Greece. Began under the name Paralysis in 1992, changed their name to Phlebotomy in 1993 and then finally to On Thorns I Lay in 1995.
They released a Promo Tape in 1993, an EP titled "Dawn of Grief" also in 1993 on Molon Lave Records, and a demo titled "Voluptuous..." in 1994.
