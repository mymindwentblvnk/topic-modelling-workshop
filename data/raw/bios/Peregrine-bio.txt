2) Peregrine is an American anarcho-primitivist,  anti-civilization death/black metal band heavily influenced by down tuned hardcore, crust and black metal. 
Their debut CD, 'The Agrarian Curse', came out on FC Records in 2008. In 2009, they released a split 'Green Scare Benefit' CD with Auryn (Pittsburgh) also on FC Records.
Myspace: http://www.myspace.com/peregrinemetal 

3) Peregrine was an Australian indie rock band from Sydney. Renowned for their strong songwriting, innovative arrangements and rollicking live shows, they've been kicking it for close to ten years, releasing two albums - 2003's "One Big Happy Heart Attack" and 2008's "Stay Inside and Misbehave." 
Myspace: http://www.myspace.com/playthings

4) Peregrine was a British folk trio active in the 1970s. Comprising Steve Torrington (12-String Acoustic Guitar, Vocals), Chris Brighton (Guitar, Vocals), and Neil Bonner (Guitar, Vocals), the trio released the album "Songs Of Mine" in 1972. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Peregrine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
