Complicity were an English goth band formed in 1996 out of the ashes of fellow London goth bands Vendemmian and Altered States.

Original lineup:
Matt Wyatt  - vocals
Steve Williams - guitar & bass

They released the Rosetta Stone produced "Playing God" on Grave News Limited in 1997.
