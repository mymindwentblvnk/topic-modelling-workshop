Blitzspeer was a power metal band, from New York City, active in the late 1980s, early 1990s. They released 2 albums via Epic/Sony records. The first, an EP recorded live at the Limelight in NYC, was aptly titled "Live". The EP consisted of a few Blitzspeer songs, and a cover of the MC5 song, "Kick out the Jams". The second album from Blitzspeer was a studio effort, titled "Saves".

Blitzspeer's lineup consisted of Scott Lano (Vocals & Guitar), Phil Caivano (Guitar, Bass and, vocals), Curt Fleck (bass) and, Brian Essing (drums), David Tritt (drums) appeared on a 45" released independently on American Gothic records in 1993
