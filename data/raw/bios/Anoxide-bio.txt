Anoxide is a London based Death Metal band, initially formed in 2011 playing Groove Metal under the name War Machine. Since their change of name and style in 2012, they have performed alongside influential bands such as Cryptopsy, Arsis, Cerebral Bore, Cattle Decapitation and Bloodshot Dawn. Since the release of their 2015 EP 'Purge The Idols', they have also headlined several concerts in the UK. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anoxide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
