Order ov Riven Cathedrals is an experimental Death metal project from Italy. It consist of two members: "12" - the vocalist and "En Sabah Nur" - all instruments, both contributing to the lyrics. 
Themes of the Order ov Riven Cathedrals focus around Egyptian and Mesopotamian histories as well as Astronomy of the ancient world.
