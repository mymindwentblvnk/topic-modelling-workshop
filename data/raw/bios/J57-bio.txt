J57 is an emcee and producer. He was born & raised on Long Island, NY, then moved to Brooklyn. He worked at Fatbeats from 2004 to 2010. He is a member of the group Brown Bag AllStars. He is a part of Rap Is Outta Control with DJ Eclipse and Torae, on every Sunday night 10pm EST - Midnight on SIRIUS/XM <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/J57">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
