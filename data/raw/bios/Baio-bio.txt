Chris Baio, a passionate lover of dance music with eclectic taste and no allegiance to genre, plays bass in Vampire Weekend. Before that, he ran a radio station, threw basement shows and DJ'd parties at Columbia University in New York. He lives in Brooklyn with an incredibly beautiful cat. His performances have rocked clubs across the globe from Ibiza to Paris to Sydney to Seoul. No one does not leave crying. Ever. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Baio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
