MelanomA is a alternative rock group formed in Noyabrsk (Russian Federation) in december of 2003. Current members are: Deshko Vitaliy - vocals, Nasuta Pavel - vocals, Anzin Sergiy - guitars, Fattahov Ildar - guitars, Matvey Potehin - bass guitar and Radygin Leonid - drums.
