1) Adeline Michèle, artist name Adeline, is a Disco/Funk/Soul singer/songwriter, former member of Band Escort. Born in Paris, France, currently living in New York City, USA.

2) Adeline is an acoustic, pop, rock singer/song writer born in Laval, Northwest France. Currently she lives in Hastings, UK and has been performing since 1999. Her album Promesses was recorded in 2002 in Hastings, UK. All songs are written by Adeline.  Her favorite songs include Même Si and Tear Me Apart.
