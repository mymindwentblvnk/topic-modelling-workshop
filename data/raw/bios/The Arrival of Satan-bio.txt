French underground Black Metal horde, released the fantastic Darkness Dealer and one split EP. Vexing Verses was released in February 2009. Band also has two demos ''Prince Of Darkness (End Of Times)'' (2000) and ''Suicide Tutorials'' (2002) but were never released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Arrival+of+Satan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
