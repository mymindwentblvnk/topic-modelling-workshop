After the former band decided to cease in Spring 2003, Dr.Hatred the vocalist tried to start a new band. He opened up the graves of some former members and raised them up again with forbidden alchemy. The members: Dr.Hatred(vo), Alcohol(g), Vlood(g), Eucharist(b). Then Dr. summoned a zombie who could play drums intensely. The drummer is called GxSx. They started to exist as ZOMBIE RITUAL. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zombie+Ritual">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
