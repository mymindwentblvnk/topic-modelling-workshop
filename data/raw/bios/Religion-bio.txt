Spanish Rock Band formed during the 80's in Cordova, Spain. Members: Jesús Astorga (singer), Jorge Luque (bass), Manuel Carrillo (guitar) and Fermín Gómez (drums). Their biggest hit was "Mirando el fuego". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Religion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
