AMPLIFIRE is a modern metal band from Oulu / Finland that consists of Anssi Vetämäjärvi (Drums), Kakke Vähäkuopus (Vocals), Jukka Järvinen (Guitar), Janne Kauppi (Guitar), Tuomas Hiltunen (Bass) and Soiva Johannes (Keyboards)

Amplifire music consists of aggressive guitars, drum machines, synths and melodic vocals.
