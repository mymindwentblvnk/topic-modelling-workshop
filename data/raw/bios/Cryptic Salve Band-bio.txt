Manchester based improvised Noise/BM/hardcore two piece band. Founded by core members Gareth Howells drums and vocals, Darren Adcock guitar in 2009. Clyve Bonnelle infrequent guitars for gigs. Kelly jones (pwhmobs) is in csb's future plans, some recordings done.
