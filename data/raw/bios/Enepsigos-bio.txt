black metal band from Norway since 2015, as a project of the drummer of Blut Aus Nord, Chaos Invocation, Fides Inversa among others, together with Nordjevel's vocalist. Released debut album Plague of Plagues in 2017, a single Cups of Anger in 2020 and second album Wrath of Wraths in 2020 with Osmose Productions.
