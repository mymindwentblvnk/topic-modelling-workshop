Holy Force is a melodic power metal band whose central member is guitarist Ango Chen. In addition to Chen, the band also consists of bassist Mike LePond (Symphony X), Vocalist Mark Boals (Royal Hunt), and drummer Rhino (ex-Manowar). The band's style is based on European power metal with neoclassical elements, but also draws on melodic traditional metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Holy+Force">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
