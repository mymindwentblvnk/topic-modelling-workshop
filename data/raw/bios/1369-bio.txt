1369 was a Groove Metal band from Evansville Indiana formed in the 90s they released a handful of demos and went through a number of lineup changes including a change of vocalist which brought with it a a bit of Death Metal influence and in 2003 changed their name to Blood Tribe <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/1369">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
