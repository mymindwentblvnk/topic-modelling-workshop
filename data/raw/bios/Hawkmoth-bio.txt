HAWKMOTH are a 4 Piece Instrumental Sludge / Post-Metal Band from Sydney, Australia.

Drawn from multiple sources of inspiration and frustration, Hawkmoth has morphed into an instrumental four piece that creates sounds sourced directly from the bottom of a mysterious sludgy swamp.
