Blood Sweat And No Tears is a death metal / metalcore band from Genoa, Italy.  They formed in 2007.  They are named after the album Blood, Sweat and No Tears by Sick of It All.  They consist of Spank (vocals), Fra (guitars), Morchio (guitars), Dos (bass) and Jack (drums).  They had another drummer before Jack, but his name has not been revealed.  They're not signed to any label.  Their only release is Demo 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blood+Sweat+And+No+Tears">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
