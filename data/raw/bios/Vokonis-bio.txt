Vokonis is a swedish stoner/heavy psych band from Borås. Members consists of Simon (Guitar & Vocals), Emil (Drums) and Jonte (Bass). Previously known as Creedsmen Arise, “certain circumstances” have led them to have to change their name to Vokonis.
