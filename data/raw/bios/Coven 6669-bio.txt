Coven 6669 is a thrash metal band from Seattle, Washington.  They formed under the name Coven in 1985, but added the 6669 after they had a long hiatus.  They added the 6669, because of a legal dispute and to avoid confusion with the psychedelic rock band Coven.  They released two demos and three full-length albums.  Members of Coven 6669 in 1993 were guest musicians on El Duce's Slave to Thy Master album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coven+6669">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
