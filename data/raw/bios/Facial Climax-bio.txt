Facial Climax is a Groove Metal band from Amstetten, Austria. They play a style that can be described as Pantera/Exhorder meets Melodic Death Metal.
So far they have released two full lengths (melanCholeric schizoPhobia - 2004 and A Face Of Gray Pulchritude - 2008) and a demo in 2003. They are signed to the Burnside Records label.
