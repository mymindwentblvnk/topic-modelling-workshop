Progressive/Experimental Metal band, formed in Lund, Sweden in 2008 by Alexander Larsson. 
"The intention behind Forlorn Remembrance is to create a musical mix of various genres including extreme metal, progressive metal and soft non-metal parts including acoustic guitars, piano, violin and clean vocals and a variety of other strange influences to form a unique and original sound."
