Allan Thomson: Vocals
His screams are said to summon bats from over 100 miles away!

Charlie Mckenzee-Stewart: Drums
Dominator of the great anus.

Danny Freeman: Guitar
A young man who knows how to party!

Jamie Cate: Guitar
Columbian drug baron.

Robert Trujillo: Bass
Former bassist of Metallica.
