Eat The Turnbuckle has a hardcore "ultra-violence" sound from Philadelphia PA with one unusually awesome peculiarity, the songs are written about professional wrestling. The name "EAT THE TURNBUCKLE" literally applies to professional wrestling in which a wrestler gets his face bashed into the corner "turnbuckle". 

The group consists of CHUBBS on guitar, SHLAK on guitar, JAG on vocals, ANTMAN on drums, CHRIS FEAR on vocals, and CAPTAIN HOOK on bass. The group Eat The Turnbuckle consists of members of  BAD LUCK 13, CALL THE PARAMEDICS, JAVELINA, HELLBLOCK 6, and many other groups.
