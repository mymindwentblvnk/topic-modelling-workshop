Rejoicer (Yuvi) is a producer from Tel-Aviv, taking influence from funk, hip-hop and jazz. He has fostered a community of artists internationally who contribute to his Raw Tapes label and has used it to cultivate Israel's modern music movement - releasing multiple volumes of "Puzzles", a peak into Israel's thriving music scene.
Yuvi has appeared on many projects in various roles such as Apifera (where he plays keys) and as producer for Liquid Saloon.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rejoicer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
