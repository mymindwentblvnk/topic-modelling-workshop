Jumpin' Jesus was a German death metal band formed in 1989, they released their first demo "Live Wire" the same year. In 1991 they released another demo "Braincramps" along with a full-length album "The Art of Crucifying" on Morbid Music / West Virginia Records. The band has since disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jumpin%27+Jesus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
