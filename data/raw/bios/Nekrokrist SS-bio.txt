Finnish (raw) black metal band based in Etelä-Savo, founded in 2000. Despite the name, Nekrokrist SS is not just another band like what many would assume. The lyrical themes are all about the darkest side of reality: Death, genocide, tyranny, torture, pain, suicide, darkness, Satan, blasphemy, self-mutilation, and disregard every living beings.
