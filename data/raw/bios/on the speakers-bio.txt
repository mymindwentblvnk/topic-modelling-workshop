On the Speakers was a short lived independent-alternative rock band featuring lead singer Ian Sefchick, formerly of Creeper Lagoon.  They released only one album, a 2004 self entitled EP, through Universal Records before parting amicably sometime in 2005.  On the Speakers remains on indefinite hiatus.

Ian Sefchick – Vocals, Guitar
Scott Ford – Guitar
Dan Horne – Bass
Anthony Improgo – Drums

As of 2009, all of the members of On the Speakers continue to play music, Scott as the guitarist for Michael Johns, Dan in numerous bands, Ant in Metro Station, and Ian in his new solo project, Ghost Baby. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/On+The+Speakers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
