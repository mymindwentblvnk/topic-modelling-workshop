Croatan is a two piece noisy punk band from Cincinnati, Ohio. They have performed at shows across the US, released many records, and contributed to several compilation albums. Their music can be heard on underground, punk, and college radio stations all over the world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Croatan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
