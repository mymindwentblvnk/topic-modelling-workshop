橘高文彦 (Fumihiko Kitsutaka) is a Japanese rock/metal guitarist which plays in Arouge, King-Show and now also with heavy/melodic metal band called X.Y.Z.->A where Minoru Niihara from Loudness does vocals. Fumihiko released two solo albums. The first one is "Euphoria" in 1994 and  "Never Ending Story" in 2006 under the Fumihiko Kitsutaka & Friends name. The song "Justice Of Black" appeared on the Japanese heavy metal compilation named "Heavy Metal Guitar Battle" in 1985. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fumihiko+Kitsutaka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
