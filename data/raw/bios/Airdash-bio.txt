Speed metal act Airdash were one of the most popular speed metal bands in Finland in late 80's. Founded in 1986, they released three LP's along with few singles and EP's.

Members
Juha Laine — vocals
Roope Sirén — guitar
Markku Niiranen - guitar (1986–1990)
Tommy Dolivo — guitar
Kirka Sainio — bass
