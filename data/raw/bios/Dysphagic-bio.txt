Dysphagic was formed in early 2009. Featuring ex-members of An Eternal Decree, Anytime This Year and Carnal Deformity, Dysphagic brings a fresh new taste of melodic, technical death metal to the Roanoke area. It's super easy to book us... just email us at dysphagic@gmail.com! We love playing shows and meeting new people! Keep on the lookout for Dysphagic, we'll probably be in your area soon!
