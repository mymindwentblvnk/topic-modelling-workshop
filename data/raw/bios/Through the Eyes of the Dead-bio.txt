Through the Eyes of the Dead is an American Deathcore band from Florence, South Carolina, formed in 2003. The band originally started as a Metalcore band, and have since moved on to a more Deathcore sound in their recent music.


Current Lineup is:
Justin Longshore - Guitar 
Jake Ososkie - Bass
Chris Henckel - Guitar
Danny Rodriguez - Vocals 
Michael Ranne - Drums
