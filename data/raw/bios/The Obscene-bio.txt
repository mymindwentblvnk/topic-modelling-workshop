Formed from members of SALUTETOTHESUN and NEBULA RISING, THE OBSCENE are churning out old school, uncompromising, raw and blasphemous death metal. The band have released their debut e.p THE TORMENT OF SINNERS through Pest Records (Re-Released by UKEM RECORDS in 2012).

The band are now signed with UKEM RECORDS to release their debut full length album, ARCHITECTS OF DELIVERANCE in early 2013
