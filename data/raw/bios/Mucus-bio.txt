There are 2 known artists with the name Mucus: 

1. Mucus is a goregrind / mincecore band from Belgium. They've released splits with bands like Agathocles and Loggerhead - among others - and often use humorous effects / sounds in their songs.

2. Mucus is a  Japanese hardcore  band who were on the This is the life vol 2 compilation CD.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mucus">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
