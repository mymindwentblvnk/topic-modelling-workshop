Genital Gore was a death metal / grindcore band from Pardubice, Czech Republic. Formed in 1993 and ended around 1998. Only put out two releases; the "Vaginal Putrefaction" tape in 1995 and a split 7" with Needful Things. Also shared member(s) with a band from Hradec Králové who had a similar musical style called Agony Conscience. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Genital+Gore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
