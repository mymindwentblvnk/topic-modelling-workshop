Decatur, Georgia rapper Yung Chris aka YC has taken the Atlanta and southern rap scene by storm with his hit single “Racks” featuring fellow MC Future. The bouncy, energetic track with one of the catchiest hooks in some time has gone far beyond the South and is now getting the rapper major buzz and spins in markets far away as New York.
