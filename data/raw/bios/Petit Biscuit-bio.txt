Mehdi Benjelloun (born 10 November 1999), better known by his stage name Petit Biscuit, is a French DJ and music producer. Benjelloun learned to play instruments such as piano, cello and guitar but preferred electro house sounds as he able to produce "unlimited combinations of music". His early musical interests and inspirations included electronica and classical music.
