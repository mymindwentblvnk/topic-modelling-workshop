Nevaloth is a black metal band from Zvolen, Slovakia, founded in 2008.

Members:

Delgrast - keyboard, vocals (2008-now)
Vilozof - guitars, vocals (2008-now)
Nargil - guitars (2008-now)
Andrej - bass (2012-now)
Svjatogor - drums (2008-now)

In 2010 Nevaloth released their debut album "La Diabolica Commedia" thanks to Sonic Temple Records.

La Diabolica Commedia can be ordered at www.nevaloth.com/store.php
