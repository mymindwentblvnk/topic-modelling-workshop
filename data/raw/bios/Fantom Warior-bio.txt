Fantom Warior was a thrash/speed metal band from Union, New Jersey, USA. The band was formed in 1984. and had released one studio album "Fantasy Or Reality" in 1987. Before their studio album 5-track demo titled "Morbid Invasion" was released in 1986.

Last known line-up:

Keith Pires - Vocals, Guitar
Steve Schley - Guitar
John Chernack - Bass
