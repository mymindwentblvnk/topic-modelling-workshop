Maria Ilinca Băcilă (Romanian pronunciation: [maˈri.a iˈliŋka bəˈt͡ʃilə]; born 17 August 1998), also known as simply Ilinca, is a Romanian singer and yodeler. She is well known for her unique yodel. She participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 2017, respresenting Romania, along with Alex Florea. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ilinca">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
