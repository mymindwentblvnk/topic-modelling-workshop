There are/were 2 bands called Amazarak:
1. A brazilian black metal band from São Paulo. So far have release 2 demos and a full-lenght album Ascensão do Anticristo in 2009. Formed in 1997.

2. A french black metal band, which existed between 1997 - 2008. They have released 2 demos, EP and a split EP with Inocculta and Hiemperium. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amazarak">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
