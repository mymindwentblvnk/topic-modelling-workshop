There are several bands and/or artists called New Day :

1) New Day is a Spanish rock band formed by Amparo Llanos, former guitarist from Dover, Samuel Titos, former bassist from the same band, and drummer Jota Armijos, from Fuckaine. They released their first CD, Sunrise, in 2017.
2) New Day is a Dutch Gospelband. 
3) New Day is a South Korean rapper.
