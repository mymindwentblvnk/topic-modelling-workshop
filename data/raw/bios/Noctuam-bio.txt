Noctuam is an experimental instrumental black/dark metal project from Cedar Rapids, Iowa. All instruments and programming are done by K. Cornell. Project started in early 2013. Although Noctuam is mainly instrumental, there was a song recorded with Michael Donovan on vocals titled "In the Depths of Darkness"
