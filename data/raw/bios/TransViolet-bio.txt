Led by agile, feminine vocals, Transviolet is a New York-formed, Los Angeles-based foursome serving up slick electronic pop that falls somewhere in line with Sia and Lana Del Rey. Their seductive debut single, "Girls Your Age," made waves via social networking and streaming spheres in the second half of 2015, and was remixed by indie electronic darling Twin Shadow. ~ Marcy Donelson <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TransViolet">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
