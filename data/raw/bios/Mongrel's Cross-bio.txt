Mongrel's Cross is a Blackened Thrash metal band from Brisbane, Queensland in Australia. Formed in 2009, the band consists of Goet Euryn (Bass, Guitars), Necros Craigos (Drums), Grand Mongrel (Guitars, Vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mongrel%27s+Cross">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
