Symphonic death metal act from Białystok, Poland, formed in 1992 and disbanded in 1995. Released the demo "Medieval War" in 1994 and a live split with Dead Infection, Squash Bowels, Nostradamus and Recrimination recorded at ACK "Sepularium" Club on January 17th, 1995. The band's line-up consisted of Michał "Mały" Ochociński (bass, also played in M.A.R.T.Y.R., Priesticide Lust, Divine Weep, Squash Bowels and Immemorial Ages), Tomasz "Tom" Chmaro (keyboards), Dominik Kulikowski (vocals), Bartosz "Bartosh" Lorenc (drums, currently a member of Incarnated, formerly of Primeval Empire [live] and Persaturam), Marr (lead guitars, also played in Unknown Dimensions, Primeval Empire, Dominium and Anthica) and Robert Gobiszewski (guitars, also played in Immemorial Ages). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parodie+Sacrass">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
