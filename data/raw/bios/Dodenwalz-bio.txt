Dutch Black Metal formation, founded October 2007.

Lineup:
Lord Nihilist - Vocals
Lumen Inmanissimus - Guitar
Ater - Guitar
Hagalaz "De Grijnzende Heerscher Des Duisternis" - Bass
Mortiis Spes - Drums

Band performs their gigs wearing gas masks (except for Lord Nihilist) to keep their identity hidden.

See also www.dodenwalz.nl
