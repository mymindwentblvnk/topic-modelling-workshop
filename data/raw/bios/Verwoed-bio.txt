Verwoed are a Dutch black metal band that formed in Utrecht in 2014 under the name Woudlouper, before renaming in 2015. Though the band is a solo project in terms of recorded material, there is a full live line-up. The debut EP Bodemloos was released in July 2016 on Argento Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Verwoed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
