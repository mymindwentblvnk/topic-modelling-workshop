Raging Fury is one of the first Japanese thrash metal-bands, formed in 1982. The band released only one regular (self-titled) album in 1992 and some demos and singles/EPs since 1986 to 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raging+Fury">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
