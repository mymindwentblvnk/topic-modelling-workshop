Yumi Zouma are friends from New Zealand now living between New York and Paris. Their music is stylish, effortless and intuitive. Simple guitar notes sit atop programmed drums to create a subtle backdrop for lush and exploring female vocals. The four tracks on Yumi Zouma's debut EP are timeless and instantly likable. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yumi+Zouma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
