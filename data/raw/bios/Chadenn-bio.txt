Chadenn is Black metal band from France. Formed in 2004, Current label  - Hass Weg Productions. 
Line-up:
Lux (Hagail) - Guitar
Odarec - Drums
Svart - Vocals
Thurisaz - Bass
Discography:
Chadenn  Demo, 2008.
Aux Portes de la Mort  Full-length, 2010.
Chadenn also appeared on the Black metal compilation "Christ Macabre" with an unreleased track 'Aux portes de la Mort'.
