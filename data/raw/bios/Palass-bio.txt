A Belgian Melodic Heavy Metal band formed in Charleroi, Hainaut. Active from 1988 till 1989, when they changed their name to rescue. Released one full length album titled 'Queen of the World'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Palass">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
