Aaron Abernathy, formerly known as Ab, is a multi-talented musician -- singer, songwriter, producer, and entertainer. With outstanding talents as both a keyboard player and a vocalist, he has acted as musical director for global touring acts Black Milk and Slum Village and collaborated with Grammy-nominated artists including Jack White, The Foreign Exchange, and Eric Roberson. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+Abernathy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
