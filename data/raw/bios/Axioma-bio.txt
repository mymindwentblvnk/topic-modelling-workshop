Axioma is a punishing Dark Doom and post-black metal group, featuring members of Keelhaul, Dark Worship, Time of Death, Brain Tentacles, Ringworm, and (ex-) Morgue. Formed in 2015 in Cleveland, Ohio the band is currently on Translation Loss Records and Hibernation Releases. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Axioma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
