An outer space ride on an interplanetary 70's style chopper from hell while tripping on peyote, wearing corpse paint, a Hell Awaits t-shirt and cupping a bubble butt... shit yeah.

No delusions of grandeur. Just making music we dig.
This started off as a solo writing/recording project by Zack Kinsey that was never meant to see the light of day. Just doing it to do it, but with a little bit of encouragement from lead guitarist Brent Hedtke, we decided to put the songs up on the web available for download. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vulgaari">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
