Fistula are a sludge band from Cleveland, Ohio that have been active since 1998. They've had many line-up changes around the two key members, Corey Bing and Bahb Branca and had disbanded shortly after the split with Burmese in 2004. Since returning in 2006 with the 'For a Better Tomorrow' EP, they've put out numerous albums, EP's and split releases.
