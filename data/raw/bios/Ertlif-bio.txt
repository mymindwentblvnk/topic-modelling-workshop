Ertlif is a Progressive Rock band from Switzerland, active in the early 1970's.

Band members:
Richard John Rusinski (vocals, guitar)
James Mosberder (keyboards)
Teddy Riedo (bass)
Hanspeter "Bolle" Borlin (drums)
Denny Andrey (lead guitar)
