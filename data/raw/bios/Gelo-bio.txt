Havin’ It My Way is the debut album by the rapper Gelo from Pittsburg, California. The album was released in 1994 for Big Action Records and distributed by SMG Solar Music Group.
Havin’ It My Way was produced by Mike Mosley and Sam Bostic from Mob Boss Productions and featured guest appearances by rappers Moot Ditty and SW. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gelo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
