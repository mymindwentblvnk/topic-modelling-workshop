AGGRESSIVE DISORDER is a blistering three-piece thrash/heavy metal band that hails from Philadelphia (PA, USA), featuring former members of the Tornado Recording Group power/thrash metal act Instigation. Words to describe their style: Aggression, uncompromising, driven, intense, and diverse. Together, they create a unique sound in a world set upon by generic clones. AGGRESSIVE DISORDER makes for a dynamic, heavy-hitting experience. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aggressive+Disorder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
