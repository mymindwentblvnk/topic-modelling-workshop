Psyborn Eye - Prog Metal band from Minsk, Belarus. Has formed Autumn 2009 as side project of the Imperitia band members. First EP "Where Time Is Gone" was released at December, and been dedicated to human's mind ability to completely change for only one day. At January of 2010 has released single "Our Motion Capture" with re-recorded track "Motion Capture". Last six months band worked at LP "Fetsus", and it had been released at the beginning of July. Result is 30-minutes song, divided on several parts, its story call in question objective reality. All albums of the project can be free downloaded from Internet. Band members are sWinner (guitar, vocals, samples), Andrew Vermin (bass, vocals, samples) and Alex Brain (lyrics). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psyborn+Eye">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
