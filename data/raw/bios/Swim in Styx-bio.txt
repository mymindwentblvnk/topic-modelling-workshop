Swim In Styx is a technical Post-Thrash band from France  (Bordeaux, Aquitaine)  formed in 2003  and signed on Symbol Musik. Their music is somewhat in the vein of Meshuggah. They have released a full-length album entitled  Zero Kelvin in 2007.

The current line-up is: 
Cyril - Guitar
Renaud - Vocals
Toff - Drums
