With overdriven tube amplifiers, cranked fuzz pedals, feedback, wah pedals, drop tuning, heavy down tempo riffs, hard hitting drums, and powerful bluesy vocals, Astral Cult was formed.  They are a psychedelic stoner doom band from Sacramento, California.

Astral Cult is:
Stefan Henskens: Bass
Andrew DeHart: Drums
Ryan Musser: Guitar
