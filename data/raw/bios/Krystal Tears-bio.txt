Krystal Tears is a melodic hard rock band from Rio de janeiro brazil. They formed in 2000 and have released their debut album in 2005 called "A Brand New Life" and a s/t demo in 2001.
This is the last known line-up:
Roberto Sans - vocals
Carlos Santana - guitars 
Diego Padilha - bass
Rigel Romeu - keyboards
