Velnezers is a Latvian black metal band from Rīga, Latvia, started in 2014 as a solo project of Roberts Blūms.

Members:

Roberts Blūms (Stabs) AKA Svēte - guitars, vocals;
Brūss Kalniņš (Deodium, Darrva) - drums.

Former members:

Dāvis Kovaļš - guitar;
Ivo Piģens - bass.
