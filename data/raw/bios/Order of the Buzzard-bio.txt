Order of the Buzzard is a Hardcore metal Band From Adelaide, Australia with a grindcore and a stoner rock influence drawing from old thrash and death metal influences while combining a more modern approach.

Order of the Buzzard was formed in 2005

Current releases - 
"Haunt the Cross EP" 2006 
"Killed by the Hunted" 2009
