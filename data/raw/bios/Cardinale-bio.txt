Cardinale was an American sludge metal band formed in the last half of 2005. Of the four members, Derek, Keith and Tommy were previously in a band called Sea of Thousand. Tommy also used to be in Dragbody. Keith works with Finner and Derek met Finner through him.

Their first and only album, "31:13", which consists of only one song that is 31 minutes and 13 seconds long, was released on Arclight Records in 2005.

Cardinale were:
Tommy - guitars, vocals
Finner - guitars, vocals
Derek - bass guitar
Keith - drums
