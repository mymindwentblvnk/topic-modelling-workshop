Promethean are a 4 piece Symphonic Death Metal band from Cornwall, UK. Started in 2011 by composer Phil Dunn. Their debut EP 'Eocene' is available for free from their facebook page:

www.facebook.com/PROMETHEANUK

Phil Dunn: Orchestrations, Bass
Simon J Sky: Vocals
Kyle Willoughby: Guitar
