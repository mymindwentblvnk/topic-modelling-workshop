He also colaborated and made arrangements to the group Sierpe in their first few demos (2003). In addition, he elaborates music as soloist in the progressive, thrash, death and neo-classical areas, recording independently an instrumental EP-demo proclaimed his own "presentation card" which is titled "WHEN POWER BEGINS" (2003). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MonSanto">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
