Legacy ID is a Latvian metal band from Rīga, Latvia, formed in 2011.

The band is considered a progressive metal and progressive rock band. Influenced by a wide variety of genres, Legacy ID takes the best of everything to create their distinctive sound and progressive arrangements.

Current members:

Rolands Ignatjevs - vocals;
Artis Apinis (Jar of Kings) - guitar;
Reinis Rijnieks - guitar;
Aivars Lietaunieks (Inspo) - bass;
Ivars Logins - drums.

Former members:

Andis Jēkabsons - vocals;
Artūrs Patetko - guitar;
Agris Siksna (Mary Jane) - guitar;
Vitaly Ganya - bass;
Andrejs Ņikitins - bass.
