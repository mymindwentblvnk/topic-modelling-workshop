Prime Creation are a Swedish metal band formed in 2015. They are signed to Silent Wall Proeductions/Rambo Music. The band released their self-titled debut album on May 20th, 2016.

Prime Creation are:

Esa Englund - Vocals
Robin Arnell - Lead Guitar
Mathias Kamijo - Rhythm Guitar
Henrik Weimedal - Bass Guitar
