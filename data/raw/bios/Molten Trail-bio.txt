Molten Trail is an international heavy metal project led by Lithuanian keyboard player Vytautas Diškevičius (Ex - Ruination, Soul Stealer). The project’s 2014 debut album, Striving for the Sky, features several vocalists and instrumentalists from around the world and combines neoclassical, progressive, power metal, and melodic hard rock elements. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Molten+Trail">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
