There are several artists who use this name

1. Tourettes is a heavy metal band from Sydney, Australia founded in January, 2000. Their style has been described as "a mixture of hardcore, nu-metal and other elements to create an apoplectic and eclectic noise".

Live, the band combines performance with visual elements, which has earned them a loyal fanbase (dubbed as "SikFuks"). They have played at festivals such as Big Day Out and Wacken Open Air, as well as shows in the United States and Canada.

Discograpy:

2003 - Detestimony (EP)
2006 - Sicksense (under the name Tourettes Syndrome
2007 - Treason Songs

2. Tourettes is an emcee and spoken word poet from New Zealand. He is currently working on his third album "Who Said you Cant Dance to Misery" due for release in February. He's won numerous Battles and slams, travelled the world entertaining drunks and had his writing published in everything from zines to 
literary journals.
