Death metal from Australia. The band is currently split up, but the line up included David Moxon (rhythm guitars), Michael Wright (lead and rhythm guitars), Kelvin Sugars (drums), Paul Lienart (bass guitar) and Rowan Harm (vocals), better known as Rowan London - the vocalist and keyboard player of Virgin Black. Also Sugars has played in Virgin Black. In 1993 Discarnated independently released the 8 track album Deus Misereatur. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Discarnated">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
