there are at least 2 artists called "mogli".

(1) Selima Taibi is a german singer-songwriter from Berlin, Germany. In 2012 she gained popularity as a contestant on The Voice of Germany. In 2015 she released her debut album "Bird" under the name Mogli. In 2017 her single "Alaska" became a success in Germany. Her second album "Wanderer" was released in 2017.

(2) mogli david grew up on the eastern shore of nova scotia & spent her childhood speaking french to ducks & bringing frozen reptiles back to life.
since 2009 she has crossed the continent several times playing solo or in bands like snail party, the official suckers, the ash cats, jackfish, ants & anchors...
