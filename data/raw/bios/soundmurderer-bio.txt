Junglist Badbwoy!

Soundmurderer (real name: Todd Osborn) ran the first Drum & Bass record store in the Detroit area, as the ex-owner of the legendary Dubplate Pressure shop. His obsession with drum and bass led to him setting up his own label Rewind (Detroit). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soundmurderer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
