Leon Else born on October 29 1989 on England, is a recording artist and throughout his life he starting uploading melodies and lyrics to myspace a long time ago. He was introduced to some producers and got his first recording studio at Universal Publishing in Fulham. In April 2012, Leon signed a development deal with Turn First Records. Gaining acclaim with his truthful lyrics on his debut EP River Full of Liquor, this singer-songwriter has been featured in L'uomo Vogue and as part of the GQ Style Feature. In 2016 he release a song call "Black Car" in Interscope Records, which belong to his debut album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leon+Else">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
