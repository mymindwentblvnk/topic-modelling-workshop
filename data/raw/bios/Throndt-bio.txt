Throndt is a one man Black Metal project from Nordhessen in Germany. It was formed by Fergen Grimnir in 2001, and since then, it is released 2 split albums, ''Throndt/Idhafels'', ''Ulfsdalir/Throndt'' and a full lenght album which came out in 2005, ''Throndt''. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Throndt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
