Endsight is a metal band from Athens, Greece, founded in 2009. Formerly known as For What Is Worth, the band is known for their fusion style of metalcore and melodic death metal.

They have released two full length studio albums, "...To A Falling World" (2010) and "A Vicious Circle" (2013), while in April 2013 they released a video clip for their song "Circles of Inferno".

Endsight has made many national live shows supporting bands like Hatebreed, Darkest Hour, Integrity, Cataract and in 2012 they performed in Rockwave Festival (Athens), alongside Kreator, Saxon and Carcass.
