It's been about a year and a half since Black Mountain leader Stephen McBean first revealed his Grim Tower collaboration with Yeah Yeah Yeahs collaborator Imaad Wasif, and now the duo are finally set to issue their debut LP. Titled Anarchic Breezes.
Described in a press release as an album of "new acoustic death folk," the 10-song record was tracked by McBean at his home studio in Los Angeles, with additional recording done by Rob Campanella at the Jonestown Studios. McBean and Wasif's songs reportedly home in on a "mutual interest in the darker traditions of song and detuning found in the American Primitive Archives." The duo are joined on the recording by bassist Rob Barbato and drummer Dan Allaire, who both play in Darker My Love <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grim+Tower">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
