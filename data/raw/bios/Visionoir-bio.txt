There are 2 bands called "Visionoir":

1) Visionoir is a doom-mental band from the Grudziadz-Gdansk underground (Poland). Current members are: Paulie Blade (lyrics, vocal manipulation, mental disintegration, vagabonding), Johnny Longplay (music, guitar arrangements, pesimistic behaviour) and Dr Queer (bass, being a lazyass, naughtiness, lollipops). 
The group was formed on the fundaments of Squad Apostolsky, and is currently working on the material for their debut album "Splendid Isolation".
