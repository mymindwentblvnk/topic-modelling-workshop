Rising Danish pop star, with roots in Mozambique, she moved to Vanløse in Denmark as a child and recalls creating her first song in fifth grade.. Barbara Moleko is half Danish half  South-African and has been working on her debut album with Scandinavian Pop producers, Madness4Real and És. 

Her debut album was released on Disco:Wax/Sony Music Denmark in September of 2012.  The title of her debut album translates in English to "Happiness is ..."The music she has released is light hiphop/ triphop inspired pop with Barbara's personal charismatic voice and quirky lyrics on top of  bouncy productions. She was selected to join the Excellent Songwriter Bachelor on the Rhythmic Music Conservatory 2011.
