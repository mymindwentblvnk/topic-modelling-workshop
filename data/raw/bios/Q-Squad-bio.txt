Q-Squad was a power/thrash metal band from Wiesbaden, Hesse, Germany who was formed in 1990 and disbanded in 1997.

The singer of the band was former Exumer  drummer Syke Bornetto.

Q-Squad only released one album, Psyched... in 1995.
