Hong Da-bin (Hangul: 홍다빈; born January 1, 1993), better known by his stage name DPR Live (Hangul: 디피알 라이브), is a South Korean rapper and singer. DPR stands for "Dream Perfect Regime". He released his debut album, Coming to You Live, on March 15, 2017. He then released his second album, Her, on December 7, 2017.
