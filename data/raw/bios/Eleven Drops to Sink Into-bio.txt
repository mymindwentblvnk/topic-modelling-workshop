Eleven Drops to Sink Into* is a Bulgarian death-doom metal project started in 2006 by an individual who named itself simply D. There have been two releases so far: 

To Defeat Death (single, 2008)
A Shelter from Time (EP, 2008)

*) listed on Metal Archives as Eleven Drops but the name used on both releases is the full one. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eleven+Drops+To+Sink+Into">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
