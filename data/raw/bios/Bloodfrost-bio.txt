BLOODFROST plays raw, lo-fi Black Metal in the vein of the LLN bands, as well as MOONBLOOD. Formed in 2008, another project featuring Noctir from NATTSVARGR, BLACKMOON, NOCTURNAL ABYSS, MUTILATION.

First L.P. <<POSSESSED BY ETERNAL SHADOWS>> released in 2010.
