Mizraab is a classical rock/progressive metal band from Karachi, Pakistan, formed in 1997.  They are one of the very few metal bands in Pakistan to receive significant acknowledgment by the press. The band has recorded and released music videos for singles such as "Panchi", "Kitni Saadian", "Izhar", and more. They have released two full-length LPs so far; Panchi (2000) & Mazi Haal Mustaqbil (2004).
