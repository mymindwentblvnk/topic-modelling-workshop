Salami Rose Joe Louis (real name: Lindsay Rose Olsen) is a multi-instrumentalist female producer from the Bay Area in California. Drawing influences from jazz, Captain Beefheart, Stereolab, and R. Stevie Moore, she creates a blend of experimental dreamy sounds with jazz influenced vocals and keyboards. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/salami+rose+joe+louis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
