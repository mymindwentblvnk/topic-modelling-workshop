There are two music groups sharing the name Anton.

1) Anton is shy and makes shy music with his casio.

2) Anton is a hungarian band formed in 2009 under the name Passpartou. They are playing definitely melancholic music with some progressive elements and a trumpet.
They have two releases, Periféria and Origo.

3) Anton is a Romanian band consisting of Ovidiu Anton on vocals, alongside Cristi Gram, Fernando Drăgănici, Dani Vlad and Liviu Sorescu on instruments <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
