There's more than one band with the name Disjecta.

1. Solo project of Mark Clifford of Seefeel with releases on Warp and his own Polyfusia label. Named after Disjecta: Miscellaneous Writings and a Dramatic Fragment by Samuel Beckett (the Latin term "disjecta membra" means "scattered fragments").

2. A Raw Black Metal band from Ancona, Italy.  They were formed in 1999 and are currently signed to Vomitium Niger Productions.  They've released two demos and one full length album thus far.

Repel the Hordes of Lights (Demo) - 2000  
...Da Marmorea Lapide Ombrati (Demo) - 2002  
Da Marmorea Lapide Ombrati (Full-length) - 2006
