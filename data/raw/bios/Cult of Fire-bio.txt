Cult of Fire is a Black Metal band from the Czech Republic with Hindu and Buddhist themes formed in 2010 by Infernal Vlad and Tom Coroner.

Members:
Vojtěch Holub - Vocals (2020-)
Infernal Vlad (Vladimír Pavelka, Death Karma, ex-Maniac Butcher, ex-Zlo) - Guitars, bass, backing vocals (2010-)
Tom Coroner (ex-Maniac Butcher,  Lykathea aflame, Death Karma) - Drums (2010-)

Former Members:
Devilish (Dark Storm, Krvavá práce) - Vocals (2010-2020)
