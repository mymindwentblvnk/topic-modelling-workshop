Storrmbringer were formed in July 2006 in Volos city with only purpose to express themselves into epic metal music..Trifonas(VOCALS),Jim(LEAD & RHYTHM GUITARS) and Tassos(DRUMS) started rehearsals creating mainly their own material but also covering others' songs. By the end of 2007 , Kostas was called in for bass duties . In October of 2008 the band entered the studio and recorded their debut album "Among The Flames Of War" was released by EAT METAL RECORDS in November of 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Storrmbringer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
