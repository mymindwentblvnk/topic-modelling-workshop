yunè pinku is Malaysian-Irish heritage producer and songwriter Asha Catherine Nandy (born in London) . Her stage name mixes ‘yunè’, a nickname she had as a child (translates from Japanese to English as ‘cloudy’), and ‘pinku’' (a nod to the claymation icon Pingu). 

Her debut EP, Bluff, released in 2022, and mixes UK garage, post-dubstep, house, and dance music genres. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/yun%C3%A8+pinku">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
