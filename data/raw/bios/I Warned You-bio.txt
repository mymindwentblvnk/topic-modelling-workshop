I Warned You is a metalcore / death metal band from Karlovy Vary / Czech Republic. In september 2011 they release their first selftitled demo.
In 2012 Frank left the band due personal problems and was replaced by Máza.
In fall of 2012 the band released their first full-length album, named Breathing The Scarlets, including ten songs.

In a present time, IWY are about to release their second album, called HOODS OF INCOMPLETE (fall 2014)
