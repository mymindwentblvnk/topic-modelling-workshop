Todeskult is a depressive black metal band from Nürnberg, Germany formed in 2006. The band's name is German and means "Cult of Death". Lyrically they focus on themes like depression, suicide and despondency. After a demo in 2006 the album "Als die Farben älter wurden..." was released in 2007. The second album Apathy was released in 2009. 
Current line-up:
Neideck - Vocals
Mactar - Guitar
Demas - Guitar
Arges - Bass
Dominion - Drums
