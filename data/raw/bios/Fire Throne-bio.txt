Fire Throne is a raw unblack metal band from Poland featuring the members Fire (Elgibbor) and Unblasphemer (Abdijah). The band recently rereleased their debut CD “Day of Darkness and Blackness” on Son of Man Records. The original was released in a very limited quantity in 2005, and the rerelease features remastered versions of all the songs as well as four tracks from the band’s unreleased 2004 demo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fire+Throne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
