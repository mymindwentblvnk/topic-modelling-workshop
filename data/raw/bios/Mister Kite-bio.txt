Mister Kite is a metal-group from the city Malmö (Malmoe) in Skåne (skaane) in Sweden
Mister Kite started in 1999, with the 3 members Alf Wemmenlind, Anton Johansson and Mats Bergentz. Magnus Kristensson helped the band with som guitarsolos on some record and he later became a permanent member of the band. In 2001 they desided to go in to a studio to record a full album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mister+Kite">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
