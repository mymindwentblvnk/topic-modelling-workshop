Hailing from Moscow, Russia, Symbol of Obscurity epitomizes melodic death metal through their harsh vocals and beautiful melodies. They currently have two albums, their debut album, entitled n.N.i.M.m (New Name In Metal Mythology), released in 2007, and the follow up, entitled R.F.M. (Rotting Flesh Machinery), released in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Symbol+of+obscurity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
