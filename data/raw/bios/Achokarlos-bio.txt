Achokarlos is a musician from Cartagena in Spain, he has played guitar since June of 2000.  While practicing every single day, he learned by teaching himself through covering other songs by his favorite bands.  Once he got better gear he started his solo project called "Achokarlos." He records, mixes and edits all of the music himself. He explores progressive styles, polyrithms, fusion, and creates intense and intricate solos.

He is also the guitarist in the Death Metal band Exquisite Pus.

He distributes his albums for free, check his MySpace/YouTube page for downloads.
