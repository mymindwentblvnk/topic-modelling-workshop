Jolie Holland (born September 11, 1976 at Houston, Texas) is an American singer and performer who combines elements of folk, traditional country, jazz, and blues. She is one of the founding members of The Be Good Tanyas <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jolie+Holland">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
