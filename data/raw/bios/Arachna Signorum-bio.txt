Two member black funeral doom metal project, Arachna Signorum formed in Pyatigorsk, Stavropol krai (Russia) in 2007. The band name was taken from the Celtic mythology is Latin for "Spider Constellation". Their music is extremely dark and mostly slow with some up tempo parts. The lyrics are deal with  Nature, Mysticism, Despair and Devastation. 
The bands first demo, released in august 2008 limited only to 30 copies, and songs are mostly in black metal style. Their debut full length album (The Debris of Winter) released by Satanarsa Records in april 2009 contains 6 new track in black funeral doom genre, and one cover of Forgotten Tomb's Springtime Depression.

Band members:
Siatris - Vocals, guitar, keyboards
Nortal - Guitar, bass
