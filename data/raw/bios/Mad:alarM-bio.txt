Mad:alarM is an Israeli Groove Metal band formed in 2009 by Vitali Voronov (guitar), Shachar Huberman(drums) and Shai Izacov (Bass) in Galillee, IL.
At 2012 the band released it's first demo Wake Up Call Demo containing 3 songs. 
On the same year the new vocalist Max Soulfly joined the team.
After playing many shows & festivals all around Israel, the band is finally released their first full album in 2017, named 'Wake Up Call'.
