Daquawn Michael Harris, professionally known as Shark, is a producer and rapper from Maryland. He is a member of the music collective Anti-World. He has collaborated with multiple underground artists, most notably Sybyr, Leon'sWOLF, Lil Darkie, and many others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shark">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
