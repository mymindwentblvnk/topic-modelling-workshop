Paul Chain Violet Theatre was a doom metal project from Italy formed in 1984 by ex-Death SS guitarist Paul Chain. He recorded under this name with a revolving cast of musicians until 1987 when he began recording as simply Paul Chain. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paul+Chain+Violet+Theatre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
