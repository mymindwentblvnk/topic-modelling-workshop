Psychotic Homicidal Dismemberment is brutal death metal/grindcore project from New Alexandria, Pennsylvania.  It was formed in 2006.  Bob Egler started up the project and he does everything in this project.  The project has released 23 full-length albums, 11 splits and 6 EP's.  Lyrical themes include death, gore, murder and porn.  Psychotic Homicidal Dismemberment is signed to Emocaust Records.
