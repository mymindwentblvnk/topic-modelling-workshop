Trashlagoon are Sebastian Welicki and Niklas Freund from Düsseldorf/Germany. The musicians create new atmospheric soundscapes and melodies that will turn as well dance floors as your living room into fantasy places.

Pushed up by French label Delicieuse Records, two EPs are scheduled for the next months announcing their upcoming album “Blue Empire” that will be out this Winter.
