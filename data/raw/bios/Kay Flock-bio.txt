Kevin Perez (born April 20, 2003), better known by his stage name Kay Flock, is an upcoming drill artist from The Bronx, New York.

He started gaining popularity in late 2020 with the songs “Opp Spotter” and “Shot Down” with frequent collaborator B Lovee. He is also associated with Dougie B, making the trio responsible for “Brotherly Love”, one of their biggest songs to date.

In October 2021, Flock had risen to provenance receiving co-signs and collaborations from the Bronx, New York rapper, Lil TJay and the New York rapper, Fivo Foreign for the song, “Not in the Mood”.

Months after Flock’s rise in the music industry, on December 24, 2021, Kay Flock was arrested for the murder of a 24-year-old man which had taken place in Manhattan on December 16, 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kay+Flock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
