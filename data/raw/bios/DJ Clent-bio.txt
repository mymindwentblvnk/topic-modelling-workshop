DJ CLENT aka THE LOW END LEGEND is a DJ/Producer from Chicago. He has releases on DanceMania, Planet Mu, Juke Trax, Ghettophiles, and many more. He has helped evolve the Chicago House sound from Ghetto House tracks all the way to Juke and Footwork. He is notable for many of his hits including (but not limited to) "Bounce", "3rd World", "Bang Skeet", and "Back Up Off Me". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Clent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
