Guru of Darkness is an Italian Black Metal band from Catania, Sicily, formed in 1995 but due to problems in getting record deal they only played live tracks till 2005 atlast they decided to release their first demo with their own effort, they still release album on their own 

They play solid black metal and their lyrical themes deal with pain,darkness, and misanthropy


Band Members :
Tsade - Vocals
Enlil - Guitars, Keyboards (Doublesion)
Baron Cimetiere - Guitars
Davmass - Bass
Asmodeo - Drums
