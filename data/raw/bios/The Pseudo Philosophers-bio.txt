The Pseudo Philosophers are an up and coming Alternative Progressive Goth Rock band based in Croydon South/East London in the UK.
The band consists of The PhanTom (Vocals and Guitar), Finlay (Lead Guitar), Andy (Drums) and Sam (Bass Guitar), who are currently working hard on their debut album... 

Originally formed in November 2003 and reformed in January 2006, the band have gone through a steady transformation while still holding original members, PhanTom, Finlay and Andy. They have gigged in the likes of Brighton, Reading, Hastings, Central London and sunny old Croydon throughout the years, including venues such as the Black Sheep Bar, the Marlborough Theatre, The Rock Garden, The Standard Music Venue and The Underworld.
