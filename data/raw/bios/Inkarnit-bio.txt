Inkarnit is a local Heavy Metal band straight out of Baltimore Md. Within the last year Inkarnit has made some major progress. They brought a new vocalist by the name Nick Thiess, and he blown them away. They now have shows lined up 1 after another. The band has only 1 album so far, Behold the Revelations!! They will have many more to come. The band consist of: Nick Thiess(vocals), Elliot Pfarr( Guitar), Kristoppher Hardy (Guitar), Joe Cox(Bass) And John Slough IV on the Drums!! A.K.A Jayslow.
