There are 3 artists using the name Stilla. A black metal band from Sweden, an indie pop band from Finland, and a DJ/producer from Guatemala.

1) Stilla is a Swedish black metal act with P. Stille (Bergraven) A. Vidhall (Deranged) A. Pettersson (Armagedda) J. Marklund (De Arma). The debut album "Till Stilla Falla" was released in February 2013 via Nordvis Production.

2) Stilla is a band from Jakobstad, Finland that started as a songwriting collaboration between Daniel Hjerppe and Ulrika Öhman. The style of their music could be described as experimental indie pop. The debut album Stilla was released in February 2011. Currently they are working on an EP-trilogy with producer Petter Näse. The first EP is called Hela havet stormar and was released in February 2012.

3) Stilla (Diego Batres) is a 17 year old DJ and producer from Guatemala who produces future house, deep house, progressive house, and dubstep. He released his debut EP "TAB EP" alongside JARB.
