Al Corley (born May 22, 1956 in Wichita, Kansas) is an American actor and producer. Al Corley became famous for his role as Steven Carrington in television drama series Dynasty in 1982. 
In 1984 Corley released his first album "Al Corley" which included the hit single "Square Rooms". The single became more popular in Europe than in the USA. Album was produced and mainly composed by Harold Faltermeyer. His second album "Face to Face" was more complicated synthpop and it was not so huge success anymore.
