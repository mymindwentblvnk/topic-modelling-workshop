Nosferiel is a Black Death Metal band from Kuala Terengganu, Malaysia. It was formed in 1997 as Sandraguna, and later the name got changed to Nosferiel in 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nosferiel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
