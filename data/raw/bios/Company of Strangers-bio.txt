There are three bands named Company of Strangers, on this page you will find informations about all of them. We have to share this page because Last.fm doesn’t give a solution to recognize different pages for bands with the same name…

1) An Australian supergroup consisting of Simon Hussey, Jef Scott, James Reyne (Australian Crawl) and Daryl Braithwaite (Sherbet) recorded an album together in 1992, which was released later that year. It included the singles "Motor City (I Get Lost)" (#26, September 1992), "Sweet Love" (#21, January 1993) & "Daddy's Gonna Make You a Star" (#35, March 1993)

2) A Texan blues-rock band formed in 2007 featuring Evan St. John (bass) Brit Busby (Guitar, vocals) Byron Lee (guitar, vocals, keys) and Landon Lee Laws (drums). Their first release was a 5 song EP titled "The Barons and Saints" (2008) and they play around Austin, Texas' Red River district. Website at: www.myspace.com/companyofstrangersmusic

3)A Michigan-based celtic/bluegrass/pubrock band, with 2 full-length albums to date, though they have played the festival/pub circuit extensively since 1995.
