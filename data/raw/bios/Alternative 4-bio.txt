Formed by Duncan Patterson from Liverpool, UK, who penned the Anathema title of the same name. Alternative 4 signed to Avantgarde Music in 2011 with the debut album 'The Brink' released in 2011. Mark Kelson(The Eternal) and Argentinian drummer/percussionist Mauro Frison completed the line-up.
In December 2012 singer/guitarist Simon Flatley replaced Kelson in the band.
