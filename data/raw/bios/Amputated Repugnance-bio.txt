Amputated Repugnance is a slamming death metal band from Mons City, Belgium. They were formed in 2008 by Moots (Vocals), JF (Guitars) andSeb (Drums/ Guitars) and their music involves crunchy chuggind riffs, blasting infernos on the drums and deep guttural tones from the vocalist.

Their music is influenced by Devourment, Guttural Secrete, Degrade, Dying Fetus, Condemned, Vomit Remnants, Hate Eternal, Abominable Putridity, Defeated Sanity, Suffocation, Unmerciful, Disgorge and Gorgasm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amputated+Repugnance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
