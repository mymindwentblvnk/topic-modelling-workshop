Grim Reaper started up Bloody Rage in 2013.  The band was originally called Bloody Rager (which was a D-beat/Crust/Hardcore Punk project at first).  Bloody Rage plays various sub-genres of metal including black metal, death metal, grindcore and thrash metal.  The band has released a good amount of music.  You may find the music at BandCamp: https://bloodyrage.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloody+Rage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
