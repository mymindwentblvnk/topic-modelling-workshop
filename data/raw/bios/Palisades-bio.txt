There are, at least, two artists with the name Palisades.

1. Palisades are a post-hardcore band from Iselin, New Jersey comprised of members of Marilyn Is Dead. Band members include: Louis Miceli on vocals, Matt Marshall on guitar, Xavier Adams on guitar and vocals, Earl Halasan on guitar and programming, Brandon Reese on bass, and Aaron Rosa on Drums. For more info, see their Facebook.
