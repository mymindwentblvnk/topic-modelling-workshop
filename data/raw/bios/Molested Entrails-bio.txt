Death metal/grindcore from Christchurch, Canterbury, New Zealand, with two members from death metal band Eviscerate. Their only releases were the "Cunt Gutted" demo-tape in 1994 and a '96 promo rehearsal. Most members focused on more important bands like Sinistrous Diabolus. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Molested+Entrails">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
