MS MR is an dream pop duo which formed in New York City, New York, United States in 2008. The group consists of Lizzy Plapinger (vocals) and Max Hershenow (instruments). The band has issued two albums, "Secondhand Rapture" (2013) and "How Does It Feel" (2015). Plapinger is the co-founder of the Neon Gold record label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MS+MR">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
