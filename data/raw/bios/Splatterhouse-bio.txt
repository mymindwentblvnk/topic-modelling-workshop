Splatterhouse is a Death/Thrash/Grind band from Portland, Oregon. They have a full-length entitled "The House That Dead Built", a split CD with Gruesome Stuff Relish and a split 7" with Birdflesh. They are working on material for a few up coming releases, including a split with Poser Disposer, as well as a second full-length. Members can be found playing in Book Of Black Earth, Menacer, Skarp and Aldebaran, as well as a few others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Splatterhouse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
