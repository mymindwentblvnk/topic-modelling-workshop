Alboys is Heavy Metal band formed in 2001 in Tirana, Albania, but now the band is residing in Italy.

Members:
Petrin Tomori - Vocals
Olsi Face - Guitar
Pol Milo - Guitar, Keyboards
Nikolin Meksi - Bass
Olsi Dani - Drums
