Bloody Blossom is a gothic/doom metal band from Turkey.
Members: Emre Kavaklioglu (vocals), Asli Candan (female vocals), Ahmet Kurtbas (guitar), Evren Emre Öngür (guitar), Nihan Yilmaz (flute), Daglar Kök (bass), Eray Severge (keyboard), Rahim Ertap (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloody+Blossom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
