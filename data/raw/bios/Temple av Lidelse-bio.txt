"Temple Av Lidelse started in 2010 as just another underground BM band playing covers of Burzum and Mayhem (as well as their own material in the vein of early Norwegian BM scene). However, their vocalist became interested in radical leftist politics, that's why they made songs like "El último de los Romanov" or "Sistema de opresión social", dealing with political/historical subjects (not to mention they're against the nazi scene). Apart from this demo, they have released two more demos..." (taken from http://r-a-b-m.blogspot.com/2011/10/temple-av-lidelse-sesiones-en-el.html). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Temple+Av+Lidelse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
