Brickfall is a progressive metal band from Bergen, Norway. The band has been around since 2006 and evolved into a heavy and melodic act. Among many influneces Pain of Salvation, Symphony X, Evergrey, Toto are worth mentioning. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brickfall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
