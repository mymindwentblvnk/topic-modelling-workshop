Wreckless Eric & Amy Rigby is a teaming of husband-and-wife Wreckless Eric (a British rock and roll/new wave singer-songwriter) and Amy Rigby (an American singer-songwriter). They released the album Wreckless Eric & Amy Rigby in 2008. The follow-up Two-Way Family Favourites appeared in 2010. In 2012 they released their third album together, A Working Museum. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wreckless+Eric+&+Amy+Rigby">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
