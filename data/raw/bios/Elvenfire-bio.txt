Elvenfire was formed in 2005 as an instrumental side-project of the band Crystal Fate. Their first release (July, 2005) under the Elvenfire name was "Scenes from the Crusade". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elvenfire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
