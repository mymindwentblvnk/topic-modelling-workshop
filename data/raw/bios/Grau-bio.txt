Hernan Grau (aka Grau) is an artist from Mar del plata, Argentina. Founder of Miniatura Records label/netlabel, Shufflemood Records, and co-owner of Mischievous Musique.Always searching for new sounds. He's somewhere between techno and minimal. Started as a dj, growing,listening and changing , his career has started and began to appear in the top places of the local circuit including local radios. In 2007, he started his career as a producer, creating minimalistic techno sounds with groovy basslines .He is in and behind the scene with his Miniatura Music Beatport exclusive aggregator business. Now he's finishing his degree in certified public accountancy, working in administration, working in his labels, making musik & and playing gigs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grau">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
