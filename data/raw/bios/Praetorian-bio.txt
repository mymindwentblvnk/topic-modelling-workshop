There are more than one artist with the name Praetorian:

1.) A five-piece Metal outfit from Melbourne, Australia. They played a very unique blend of Metal fusing Melodic Death Metal with very ambient sound-scapes and progressive melodies. They released two full-length albums, a demo and an EP. Their last release was the single "Dirge" in 2015.
