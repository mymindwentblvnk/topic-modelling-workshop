Bar Sinister was a thrash metal band from Pearl River, New York.  Their last known lineup consisted of Swillard (vocals), Buddy (guitars, vocals), Doles (bass) and Moonie (drums).  The year they formed is unknown, but they were around in the late 1980's.  Bar Sinister only released two demos during their brief existence.  Sin Now... Pray Later was released in 1988.  Live and Sin was released in 1989.  They were never signed to a label.  The year they disbanded is not known. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bar+Sinister">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
