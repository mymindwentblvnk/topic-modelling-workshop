Necrotion is an obscure death metal band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. Line-up consists,
 

John Litchko - Vocals, Guitar -founding member / composers
Joe Young - Guitar -founding member /composers
 Frank gamble -Drums
 Mike Trush  -Bass

Members later ended up in Namtaru/Perverseraph.
Also released a demo in 2001 entitled "Defiled".
