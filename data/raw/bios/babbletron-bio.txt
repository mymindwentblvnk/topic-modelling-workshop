Babbletron is a hip-hop group from New York consisting of producer DJ Pre and MCs, Cool Calm Pete and Jaymanila. They released their debut "Mechanical Royalty" on Embedded Music in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Babbletron">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
