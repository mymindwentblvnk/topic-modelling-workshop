Sentimen Beltza is black metal band from Spain (Amurrio, Alava, Basque Country) which plays raw black metal with lyrical theme(s) about death.

Current line-up:
Nakkiga, Lúpulo - Oindurht
Urgarun - Drums
Kernunnos, Hellthrone, Nakkiga, Lúpulo, Mortinatum - Vocals

Discography so far : 

Arima Erratuen Erresuma Split, 2006
Iltzeak Full-length, 2008
Split Andromeda - Sentimen Beltza Split album, 2010
Olabezarko Basoen Bakardadea Full-length, 2010
