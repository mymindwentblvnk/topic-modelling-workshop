HALMOS is a psychedelic metal band formed in the summer of 2011. Their music ranges from sludgy jams to stoner rock. HALMOS has released two albums - a five song EP entitled Vicious Cycle (2012), and a full length album entitled EXIST (2014). They are currently planning to release a new EP in the near future.

HALMOS' latest album, EXIST, is available for free download via halmosband.bandcamp.com
