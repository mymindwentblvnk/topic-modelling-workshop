Depths of Tartarus was formed by Munihist, Keres, and Syclen in December of 2007. At this time Precipice was the drummer. With this line up Depths recorded our first album "Protogonos" from Jan-March 2008, which was then released by Dark Forest Productions May 9th of 2008. The current Depths line up is Syclen,Munihist,Keres, and Acheron. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Depths+of+Tartarus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
