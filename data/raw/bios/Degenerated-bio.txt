Degenerated is a Hardcore Punk band from Centralia, Washington. They started out in 2007 with only three members. Josh on Guitar and Vocals, Kyle on Bass, and Dustin on Drums. Over the next 2 years they went through many line ups with the main members being Kevin on Vocals, Dustin on Guitar, Justin on Guitar, Joe on bass, and Jake on Drums. Only recently did they switch out Jake on drums for Sam. They have played with many punk rock bands in the Northwest area including the legends MDC and Capitalist Casualties. Soon they will be playing with Bay Area Legends FANG in October 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Degenerated">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
