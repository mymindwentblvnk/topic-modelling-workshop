There are two bands named Ithaqua

1. Ithaqua is a black metal band from Greece, playing classic Hellenic black metal in the vein of old Rotting Christ, Varathron, Necromantia, etc. They released their first demo, Initiation To Obscure Mysteries, in February 2015 on Iron Bonehead Productions.
