Dying Tears is a French metal band formed in 1998 in Paris, France. Playing guitar-driven gothic metal, Dying Tears has released a demo, "Lovely Nightmares", in 1999, and two full-lengths, "Amnesia" and "Spleen and Hope", in 2001 and 2003, respectively. A few years later, Dying Tears has disbanded, with some members starting a new band called Interria. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dying+Tears">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
