(2005 - present)

Hagall (previously known as Devastated), is a band founded in early 2005 at Leppävirta, Finland. They play folk metal. The members are: Roope Karioja (vocals, bass guitar), Eetu Karioja (vocals, keyboard), Aku Karhunen (guitar), Eetu Kauhanen (guitar) and Ollimatti Kosamo (drums). The band has recorded one demo under the name of Hagall, 'Päättymätön tarina', which includes 3 tracks, 'Elämään todelliseen', 'Sielunvaellus' and 'Päättymätön tarina'. Hagall will publish their next release during summer 2010.
