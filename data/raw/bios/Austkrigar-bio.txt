Austkrigar is a black metal project out of Hamar, Norway. Consisting of one member, Arnjeir, he produces a slow tempoted black metal sound in the realm of depressive. He has one demo out, released twice, the latter as a split with Altus Ultionis]. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Austkrigar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
