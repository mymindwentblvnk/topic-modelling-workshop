Vomi Noir is a  death metal / goregrind outfit from Toulouse, France.  They consider themselves to be "Pathological Grind-Gore" according to their Facebook page.  They started in the year 2015.  Vomi Noir consists of Pierre (vocals, guitar), David (bass) and Laurent (drums).  According to their Facebook page, they are influenced by Regurgitate, Napalm Death, Carcass, Dead Infection Ulcerous Phlegm, Garbage Guts, Gore Beyond Necropsy, Maggut and Catasexual Urge Motivation.  They are not signed to any record label.  However, they have released one single, two demos, two splits and one EP between 2016-2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vomi+Noir">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
