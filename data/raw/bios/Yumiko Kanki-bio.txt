Yumiko Kanki (神吉由美子) is a Japanese composer best known in the West for writing nearly the entire soundtrack of the Super Nintendo game F-Zero. Two songs in the game were written by Naoto Ishida (石田尚人) ("Big Blue" and "Ending Theme"). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yumiko+Kanki">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
