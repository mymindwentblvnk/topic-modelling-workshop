GriseldaxFR or Griselda Records is an American hip hop collective and independent record label based in Buffalo, New York. It was founded by rapper  Westside Gunn in 2012. Him, his paternal half-brother Conway the Machine and their cousin Benny the Butcher make up the core of the group. Other members include: Mach-Hommy, Boldy James, Armani Casear, Rome Streetz, YN Billy and in-house producer Daringer. In 2019, the core members released an album titled "WWCD" (What Would Chinegun Do) under the Griselda group name. In 2020, Westside Gunn talked about making a follow up to WWCD with the rest of the members of the collective, but that has yet to be released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/griselda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
