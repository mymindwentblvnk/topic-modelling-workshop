There are multiple artists for this name:

1) Blunt Force Trauma is a Thrash/ Crossover band from Texas, USA formed in 1999 in Austin, Texas. The band's line up consisted of vocalist Bobby Fuentes, guitarist Slaytoven, bassist Bob Paxton, and drummer Ben Burton. The band has released two albums so far, Blunt Force Trauma in 2000 and Good Morning America in 2001. Ben Burton left the band in late 2001, and the band went on hiatus shortly after. In June 2007, the remaining band members reunited and recruited ex-D.R.I. drummer Felix Griffin to much attention. 2008 would see the band log many shows around Texas, as well as welcoming Alex "Rager" Loughborough on bass in late December 2008. On May 29, 2009 the band released their 3rd album, an EP titled "Hatred for the State", on Shattered World Records.

2) BluntForceTrauma is a British band from London, UK, that combines post-hardcore and metalcore.

3) Blunt Force Trauma is a slamming death metal band from Japan.

4) Blunt Force Trauma is a death metal band from Oklahoma, USA.

5) Blunt Force Trauma is a Harsh Noise / Power Electronics Collaboration between Jonathan Canady of Deathpile, Dead World, Angel of Decay, and Scott E. Candey of Gruntsplatter.
