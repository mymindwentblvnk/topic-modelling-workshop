The current line-up is: 
Marcin "Tur" Turbiarz - vocals 
Mikołaj "Haldarn" Grabowski - guitar 
Grzegorz Jaworski - guitar 
Piotr "Ninja" Jankowski - bass 
Jakub Biernacik - drums 
You can find more info at:
