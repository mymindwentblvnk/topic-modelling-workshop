There are multiple artists named Sticky.

1. Sticky is a UK garage producer

2.  Sticky is a young, Geri Halliwell endorsed funky polyrhythmic no wave four piece. Pop music for bins.

3. A danish rock band mostly know for their tribute song "Ovre i Amerika" about NFL-kicker Morten Andersen breaking the record as the most scoring player in the NFL ever.

 4. A danish band that busked in Copenhagen. The band consisted of a guitar player, a violin player, a contrabas player, a drummer and a singer.
