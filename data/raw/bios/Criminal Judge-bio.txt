Criminal Judge is a thrash metal band from Peer, Belgium.  They formed in 1989 and disbanded in 2000.  Then they reunited in 2020.  Criminal Judge currently consists of Danny Vandelvelde (vocals, guitars), Ronny (guitars),  William Bloemen (bass) and Bart Creemers (drums).  Their past members are Erik (vocals), Reinald (guitars), Tweeva (bass), Steven Meijsen (bass), Erwin Neyens (bass), Inno Beks (drums) and Mario (drums).  They released three demos and two EP's.  All of their releases from 1990-1995 were released independently.  Then they were a part of the Criminal Records roster for a brief period of time in the late 1990's.  They released When It's Too Late... through that label in 1998.  Criminal Records was the last label they were signed to.  Criminal Judge are not signed to any label as of 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Criminal+Judge">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
