US doom metal band that began in 1992, as Sub-Terran.  They changed their name to Wintress in 1993, but not before releasing a demo, titled Wintress.  As Wintress, they released an additional two demos, titled Eternal Seclusion and Into the Firey Dawn, in 1994 and 1997, respectively.
