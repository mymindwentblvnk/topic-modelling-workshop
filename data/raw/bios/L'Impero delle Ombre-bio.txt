L'Impero delle Ombre (meaning The Empire of Shadows) is an Italian traditional doom metal band. Their music, self-described as "cemetery rock", is influenced by 1970s hard rock bands such as Black Sabbath and Black Widow, and Italian dark acts such as Death SS, Paul Chain and Jacula. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/L%27Impero+Delle+Ombre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
