On St. Patty's day 2010, 3 musicians gathered in Deleware OH to lay down some chunky sludgtastic metal. The rest will be history very shortly, at least on the cosmic scale. Prepare yourselves for White Wolves.

Members :
Chris Thompson - Guitar
Chris Burnsides - Drums
Jack Huston - Bass

Home town : Columbus, Oh
