BENDIDA (БЕНДИДА)
Bendida is symphonic power metal band from Sofia, BG
The band is created in 2008 by  Vinnie Atanasov  - (guitar and vocals) , 
Bendida also are :
  Anton Yanachkov - bass;
  Kremena Nikolova - vocals;
  Yana Ivanova - keys;
  Lyuben Dimitrov - drums;
  Viara Grancharova - viola da gamba;
2012 is released the first single of the band - "The Farthest Shore"
2014 official video clip "Prince of Ice" and "Witch and the Devil"
