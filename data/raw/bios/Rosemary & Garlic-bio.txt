Rosemary & Garlic is a musical collaboration between Anne van den Hoogen and Dolf Smolenaers from Amsterdam, The Netherlands. The Kingfisher is their debut EP. It has a northern sound; vintage and romantic. The Kingfisher is a colorful bird that lives near the riverside.  The story of the Kingfisher is about stillness, the changing of the seasons, grief, love and goodbye.
