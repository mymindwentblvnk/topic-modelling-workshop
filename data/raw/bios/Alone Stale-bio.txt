Alone Stale is an extreme metal band formed in 1996. It's expression is based on the reality of the circumstances. His lyrics depict particular peculiar ideas as the pursuit of happiness, freedom and other illusive feelings of human nature. Currently the "band" is composed of only one member, who is involved in the writing of the third record.
