Regulus are a stoner rock band from Sheffield, UK formed mid 2012 by guitarist Ozzy, bassist Carl, and drummer Joe. Joined by vocalist and guitarist Luke towards the end of the year Regulus began gigging in the local area. They released their debut EP, The End, in February 2013 and are currently gigging around the Yorkshire area. In November 2013, Regulus released the Titan Moon EP, a concept EP telling a story of interdimensional travel and revolution. On October 25th 2014 Regulus released their debut album "Smoke". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Regulus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
