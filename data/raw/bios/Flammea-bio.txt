Flammea(Brasil, Distrito Federal), band formed in 1988 playing thrash metal music. They where one of the first bands in brasil who were female members playing thrash music, along side with Volkana, Sepultura, Valhalla, P.U.S, and many more.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flammea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
