Archmage was formed in 2013 by musicians Mariya Solodkaya and Robert Budge. The synth pop band is based in Australia.

Their first Single, The Bonfire Party, was released on April 21st, 2016.

Mariya is a singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist who was classically trained with piano, cello, and flute, but since 2013, got into playing synthesizers and singing. She also does all of the art and design. Robert is the guitarist, bass guitarist, and backup singer. He also does the websites and video editing. Mariya is from Russia, and Robert is from Australia. They are a husband and wife team.
