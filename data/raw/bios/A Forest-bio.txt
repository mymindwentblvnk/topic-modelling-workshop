There are two bands with this name:
- A Black metal band from Novara, Italy formed in 2000 and recorded their first full-length album in 2002, The Spirit of the Forest (To Bleed Northern Spirit). Now they have another EP called Black Clouds, that was released in 2003.
- A German pop band from Leipzig and Berlin, Germany, consisting of two men, a woman and a MacBook.
