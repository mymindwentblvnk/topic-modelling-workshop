Ulises Conti (1975)
Argentinian composer, multi-instrumentalist, sound artist, and producer. Whose music does not rest in any genre. From his own production platform ¨Metamusica¨, several projects have developed, including soundtracks for films, theater, contemporary dance pieces and sound installations.
At the same time, Metamusica works like a label and publishing house, which are in charge of publishing his music in different formats (Vinyl, cds, Downloads, Scores and Books)
He usually performs with different projects in Buenos Aires, as well as in the most prestigious international festivals.
His music is available on national and worldwide catalogues.
