Tormenticon is a new old-school death metal band from Finland. Their influences are Bolt Thrower, Obituary and Asphyx among the others. Band's self-titled debut album was released on May 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tormenticon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
