Trade Mark is a rock band from Rybnik, Poland. In theys music sounds classical polish rock bands like Lady Pank and Perfect with some rock'n'roll energy from the 60's. 

Also, Trade Mark was a Greek Hard Rock band that released an ep back in 1992 called Fatal Blues. Trade Mark were: Agelos Kalantzis (vocals), Sotiris Fegitis (lead/rhythm guitars), Nick Lazakis (bass) and Christos Diakoumis (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trade+Mark">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
