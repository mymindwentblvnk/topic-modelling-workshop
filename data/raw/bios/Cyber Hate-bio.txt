Cyber Hate is a Modern/industrial metal band earlier known as Cyber Hell.

They released a demo featuring 6 tracks, an EP "Synthetic Evolution" also featuring 6 tracks and are now recording their first full lenght album "Beyond Human"
They released a video for their songs "Nightmare" and "Cyborg Creation"
Band played live concerts in Croatia and will be featured in an international compilation that will be distributed around Europe.

Members:
Marko Lovric (Vocals)
Kristijan Bajlo (Bass,Vocals)
Vladimir Gundic (Guitars)
Dean Cvjetanovic (Drums)

Past members:
