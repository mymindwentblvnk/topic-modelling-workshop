Malo De Dentro is a heavy metal band from Phoenix, Arizona.  Their style has been compared to the likes of Soulfly, Sepultura, Lamb of God and Pantera.  Basing their sound on some of the tribal aspects of the Southwest, Malo De Dentro has a unique take on heavy metal, with deep riffs, powerful beats, and vocals that pierce the soul. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malo+De+Dentro">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
