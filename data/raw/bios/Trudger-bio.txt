Trudger are a 4-piece progressive, sludge, post-metal band from Barnsley, United Kingdom. They have two releases to date; a 3-song EP titled "Motionless in Dirt" in 2012 and "Dormiveglia" in 2014. They are both available at: http://trudger.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trudger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
