Chaos Aftermath is a Melodic Technical Death/Thrash Metal band from Singapore. The band had decided to refer to their style as "Futuristic Metal" when the band was formed in 2000; in relation to the decided lyrical conceptual-direction which they have decided to take.

The Band consists of:
Wade (Jun) - Guitars, Keyboards/Synths, Composer, Producer (Founder)
Seelan - Drums (Co-Founder)
Dani - Vocals

Kumaran has joinned the band as the recording-bassist for the upcoming album, "Spirits Encased Within..".

They have previously released the demo 'Slave To The System' and their debut album 'Subtopia' in 2002. The band has been inactive since then, however they have recently reformed and the production of a new album is currently underway.
