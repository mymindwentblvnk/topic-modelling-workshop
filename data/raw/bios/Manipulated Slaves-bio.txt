Manipulated slaves are a melodic thrash metal band formed in Japan in 1994. Manipulated Slaves so far have released four albums, Burst Into Blue Flame (2000), The Legendary Blackjade (2001), Over The Black Ocean (2004), Oath In Black Tears (2006). Their current line-up consists of Yutaka Kageyama - Guitar, Akira Togano - Bass,
