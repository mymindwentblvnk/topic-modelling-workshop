There are two bands using the name Vasco Da Gama:

1. A heavy metal band from Portugal
2. A math rock band from Liverpool

1. The first was an heavy metal band from portugal. They released their only LP in 1988.
The band was formed by:
Carlos Jorge Miguel (guitar)
Gil Marujo (drums)
Tó Andrade (bass)
Luís Sanches (vocals)

2 .The second is a band from Liverpool, England, active since 2010, most commonly described as math rock and citing Maps & Atlases, The Dismemberment Plan, Fugazi, Faraquet and The Mars Volta among their influences.
