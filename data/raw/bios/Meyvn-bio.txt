Meyvn is pure power/progressive metal from the heart of Texas. Meyvn combines stunning musicianship with memorable hooks and unconventional ideas to create an original sound that has been capturing the attention of metalheads and music lovers everywhere. The band has been winning over fans at their electrifying shows all over the world and is steadily becoming a "must see" act wherever they play. 

Meyvn recently signed with KillZone Records and will be releasing their debut album, "Splintered Skies", worldwide through KillZone in September of 2006!  "Splintered Skies" will feature over 70 minutes of original material ranging from shred-guitar metal to epic progressive rock and all points inbetween!  Fans of bands like Dream Theater, Symphony X, Queensryche, and Fates Warning won't want to miss this! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Meyvn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
