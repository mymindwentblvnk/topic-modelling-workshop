Formed in 2009 by Demon of Darkness . The main goal was to play old-school Black Metal but then the band changed it's sound on "To Embrace the Nocturnal Hymns" EP to become more melodic/atmosphereic

Releases :
Birth in Sabbath (Demo 2009)
Cleansed by the Evil Hand (Demo 2010)
Nokturnal Forest / Zarach "Baal" Tharagh (Split 2010)
To Embrace the Nocturnal Hymns (EP 2010)

Original line-up is : Demon of Darkness (Vocals , All Instruments) but the band now has a session guitarist "Azathoth" from the Polish Doom/Death band "Gurthang"
