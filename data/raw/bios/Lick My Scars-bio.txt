Russian melodic death metal\metalcore band from Penza. 

Band formed in 2005 and released one EP, called "Лижи мои шрамы (Lick my scars)" in 2006.

 Band members:
Ilya - voice;
Taras - guitar;
Artem - guitar;
Eugeniy - bass;
Alexey - drums.
