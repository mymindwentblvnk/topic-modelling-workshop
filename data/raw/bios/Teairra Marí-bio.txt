Teairra Marí (born Teairra Maria Thomas on December 2, 1987 in Detroit, Michigan, US) is an American R&B singer. She released her debut album 'Roc-A-Fella Records Presents: Teairra Marí' in 2005 with moderate success. She gained popularity with her first single and summer anthem "Make Her Feel Good" and the second single "No Daddy." In May 2007 she was dropped from Roc-A-Fella Records. 

She is now currently signed to the venture Warner Bros./Fo'Reel Entertainment working on her sophomore album "At That Point". The album will feature production from: The Underdogs, The Runners, Polow Da Don, Tank, Neff-U and a list of others. She has also written songs with Keri Hilson, Solange, Sean Garrett and The Clutch for the project. Her first single is "Hunt 4 U," which features Pleasure P is out now. The second single from the album will be the Runners produced track "Cause A Scene" which features Flo Rida.
