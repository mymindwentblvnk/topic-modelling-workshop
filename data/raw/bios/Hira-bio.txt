Heavy Metal band from the Oiartzun, Basque Country formed in 1998

They won in 2001 the "Euskadi Gaztea" demotapes award, these songs would be included in their first album "Nortasuna".

Discography:
Nortasuna - 2001
Oztopoak gaindituz - 2003
(Sic) - 2006

Members:
Ibai Marin (guitar and singer)
Alex Lobato (drums)
Jon Iturrino (guitar)
Manex Etxeberria (bass)
Mikel Zubeldia (keyboard)
