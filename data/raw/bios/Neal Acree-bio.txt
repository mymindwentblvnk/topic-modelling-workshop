Neal Acree is an award winning film, television and video game composer whose work includes the massively popular game franchises World of Warcraft, StarCraft, Diablo, Chinese MMO Revelation Online, shows like Stargate SG-1, Stargate Atlantis, Witchblade and over 30 feature films.  A verstatile composer with roots in classical, rock, electronic music and world music Neal Acree's music has been recorded and performed by orchestras around the world including the hit concert tour Video Games Live.
