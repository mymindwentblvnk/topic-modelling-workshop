Taken By Wolves is a industrial black metal band hailing from Brimingham, UK.  The band consists of former members of Pitchshifter, Iron Monkey and features current members of Hordes of Satan.  Taken By Wolves uses drum machines for their music.  They released one demo (Howling For War)  that's free to download from their website at www.takenbywolves.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Taken+By+Wolves">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
