Highlow is heavy/power metal band formed in 201 in Lublin, Poland. The band has recorded a four song demo and a single and supported Blaze Bayley at his concert in Lublin. In November 2011 Highlow won 23rd annual Festival of Different Music "Staś".

Members:

Tośka - vocals
Mietek - guitars
Seba - guitars
Jamnik - bass guitar
