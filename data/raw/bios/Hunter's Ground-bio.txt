Hunter's Ground formed in late 2009 by several members of Lynchburg, VA death/doom metal band In Death I Become. They focus on a North-American proto-mythology, and many themes concern the brutality of North American wildlife. Their debut EP, Estrous, was released in 2010 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hunter%27s+Ground">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
