A side-project of Cory Smoot (aka Flatus Maximus), lead guitarist of GWAR. The first EP he recorded, the 15 minute epic 'Enigma', was rejigged by GWAR  for their Bloody Pit of Horror' album, as four separate but interconnected tracks: Zombies, March - Come the Carnivor -A Gathering of Ghouls -Storm is Coming

More releases are planned for the future.

Cory also heads two other bands, in which he plays lead guitar: Mensrea & Locus Factor. The rhythm guitarist for both of these bands is Nate Krishna, who is GWAR's guitar tech. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Cory+Smoot+Experiment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
