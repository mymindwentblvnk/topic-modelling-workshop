American band formed in Orange County, California in 1985. Formerly known as Vengeance.

Members are:

Rusty Dades - Vocals
Blake Hastings - Guitar
Rob Swanson - Bass
Barry McGill - Drums

Discography:

Lixx Array (EP, 1987)
Mud in your Eye (1992)
Reality Playground (1992)
