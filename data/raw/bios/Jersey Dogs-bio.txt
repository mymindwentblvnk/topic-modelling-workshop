Featuring Lou Ciarlo (bass/vocals), John Ilaw (guitar), Mike Benetatos (guitar), and Michael Sabatini (drums), the Jersey Dogs were born when long-suffering New Jersey power metal outfit Attacker finally decided to call it quits in 1989. Not that the Jersey Dogs' already rather dated thrash metal fared much better, and after just one EP, the same year's "Don't Worry, Get Angry!", and 1990's "Thrash Ranch" album, they too meekly trudged off into the sunset. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jersey+Dogs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
