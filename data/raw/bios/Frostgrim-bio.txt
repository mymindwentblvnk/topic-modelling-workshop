Frostgrim was a German black metal band formed back in 2005. They released one album called "Hasserben" in 2007 which is avalaible for free download by the band, here. Afte that they split-up. Two members of Frostgrim, Nikita Kampard and Tobias Jaschinsky play at this time in their new band Der Weg Einer Freiheit.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/FrostGrim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
