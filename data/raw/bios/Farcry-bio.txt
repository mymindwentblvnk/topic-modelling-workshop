1. Farcry is a melodic hard rock band with two albums on Kivel Records available now at http://www.farcryband.net . Embracing the hard rock style of the 80's, FarCry is not afraid to show their monster hooks, soaring vocals, and guitar solos. With those influences, it's no surprise that FarCry has shared the stage with the likes of Bret Michaels, Firehouse, Warrant, Vince Neil, Lita Ford, and many, many, more! Melodic Rock is alive and well, go check them out at http://www.reverbnation.com/farcrytherockband !!!

2.  Farcry is a five-man acoustic based rock band from Portland, OR. Fronted by singer songwriter Andrew Supina, Farcry is dedicated to being true professionals in all aspects and working toward growing their success in the coming years and beyond. 

FryCry has opened for Three Days Grace, Floater, Adam Brody "Big Japan" and played events along side bands Everclear and The Presidents of the United States of America.

Influences include; Days of the New, Matchbox 20, Incubus, Pearl Jam and Soundgarden. 

Members include Andrew Supina, Josh (Joshua) McCormick, Jon (Jonathan) Ruffner, Robert “Bob” Balder and Mikey Smith.
