Bringing G-folk to the UK in a big way, Hak Baker redefines what it means to be a folk singer in the capital, blending grime and gritty lyrics with unadorned vocal talents.

Whether chronicling his own experiences or those of the people around him, Hak Baker’s music offers an indispensable insight into the struggles of the working classes in 2021/22. The injustices, the hustle, the heartache: it’s all there to be discovered as this remarkably unique artist takes to the stage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hak+BakeR">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
