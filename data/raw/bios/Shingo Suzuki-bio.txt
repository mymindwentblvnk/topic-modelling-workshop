Shingo Suzuki, the bassist/keyboardist/track-maker/producer creates quality tracks which are made with his unique track making style, sampling the recording of himself playing an instrument.

The style caught attentions of artists such as Moka Only, Blu, 20syl of Hocus Pocus, and DJ Greem of Hocus Pocus. With the appearances of these internationally known artists, his first album "The ABSTRACT TRUTH" became a hit not only in Japan but also in Europe.
The album has reached TOP10 in Japanese CD shops and iTunes hip hop album charts in Portugal and France. Critics in Yahoo! and Myspace Japan stated that "The ABSTRACT TRUTH" is the biggest surprise in the Japanese music scene of the year 2008.
