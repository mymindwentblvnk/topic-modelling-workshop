2. K-LO is made up of cousins Simon Lam (from I'lls) and Chloe Kaul from Melbourne, Australia. The duo released their first single Make Me Wonder in March 2014. False Calls and Under Lie followed, as an advance of their November released EP, 'cusp' through Year One Recordings.
