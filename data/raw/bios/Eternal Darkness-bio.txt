There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Eternal Darkness was a death doom metal band from Eskilstuna, Sweden, formed in 1990 under the name Necropsy. They released two demos, "Ceremony of Doom" and "Suffering" in 1991, a promo tape and an EP titled "Doomed" (Distorted Harmony Records) in 1992. In 2006 Morbid Wrath Records released a compilation titled "Total Darkness" containing their demos and the previously unreleased "Twilight in the Wilderness" that was recorded in 1992 and was supposed to be released back then by Necropolis Records but that never happened. Official Myspace: http://www.myspace.com/eternaldarknesssweden

2) Eternal Darkness is a Greek black metal band formed in 2004 in Ioannina.

3) Eternal Darkness was a Bulgarian black metal band. Released the demo "Християнска перверзия" (Christian Perversion) in 1995.

Note: "Eternal Darkness" is also a video game Developed by Silicon Knights and released for the Nintendo GameCube, its soundtrack was composed by Steve Henifin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eternal+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
