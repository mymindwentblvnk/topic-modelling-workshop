Gutted Out is a slamming brutal death metal band from Michigan, USA. They formed in 2007 by Nick (Vocals), Jeremy Ball (Bass), Cefe Anderson (Guitars) and Dray (Guitars, and used to be drum programming) and play harsh slamming death metal which has simple yet effective riffs, speeding programming and guttural vocals. The band sounds like Inhuman Dissiliency and Digested Flesh which they draw influences from all kinds of slam death metal bands including Begging For Incest, Embodyment, Heinous Killings and Devourment.
