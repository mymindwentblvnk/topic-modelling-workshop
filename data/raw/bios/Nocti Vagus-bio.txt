Nocti Vagus (Previously known as Nordwind) was a 5-man band from Germany. Changing name in 1998, they released one full length album called "Venture in Sombre Passion" in the same year before splitting up.

Band members include:
A. - Guitar, Vocals (Horna, ex-Die Pest, Nordwind, ex-Nordlys, Fjell)
Kriya - Guitar (Die Pest)
Orkrist - Bass (Vargsang, ex-Graven (Ger), Gorrenje, Nordisches Blut, Nordwind)
Zagan - Synth (Nordwind)
