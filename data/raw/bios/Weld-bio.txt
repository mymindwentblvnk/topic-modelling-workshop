Rock/pop group from Moss, Norway. Startet in 1993, splitted 1996. Released two albums, Natural Tools (1994) og Hello Walls (1995). 
Members: Vibeke Saugestad (vocal/guitar), Jørn Johansen (guitar/vocal), Stig Jøraholmen (bass) and Rune B. Johnsen (drums/vocal). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Weld">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
