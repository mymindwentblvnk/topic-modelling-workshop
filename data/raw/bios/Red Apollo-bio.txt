Red Apollo is a five piece collective from Dortmund, Germany, combining impure and sludgy guitars and translucent melodic tunes soaked by well sedated vocals. Atmospheric synthesizers and sprouting drums round up the sound to an experience in the midst of sagging and unsettled noises! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Red+Apollo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
