Nihdrym, created by VilwolfHeim (Sombre Chemin) in 2008, is an Atmospheric Black Metal musical project from France. The first demo called "Kosmosophia" went out on September 20th, 2008 to Those Opposed Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nihdrym">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
