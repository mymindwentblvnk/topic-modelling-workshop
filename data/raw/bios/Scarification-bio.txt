Scarification, is the name of the project started by the one man musician Rott in fall 2013. Rott writes/produces all tracks by himself. Being inspired by artists such as Burzum, Mayhem, Darkthrone & Immortal the greats. Rott
His debut record "Nocturnal Past" which dropped on Christmas Eve 2013 received positive reviews by listeners for sounding like the old 90s kvlt black metal sound. Nocturnal Past is now available on iTunes, GooglePlay, Spotify & more! In the year 2014 Rott will record more and more music for this one man band by the name of Scarification out of Albuquerque, New Mexico, U.S.A... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scarification">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
