There are currently three artists known that share the name "Nostrum".

1) Nostrum is a hardtrance/acid trance project by the German producer Bernd Augustinski. Starting with Brainchild he has released dozens of singles. Recently he also co-produced with Frank Künne under the name Nostrum & Frank Künne.
Bernd lives in Cologne and is the son of the German actor Peer Augustinski.

2) Nostrum is Croatian Black Metal band.
