Wolfskult is a black metal band from Germany. Wolfskult was born in 2007. Nocturnus is the one and only band member. The lyrical themes are about isolation, winter and emptiness, according to the Metal-Archives.com. 

Has only one release: a self-released and self-produced split with "Des Menschenkindes Sarg". This split has no title.
