Heavy metal/shred musician, best known for being the vocalist from Japanese 80's thrash metal act Rommel. In '85 he played with Sqwier. A year later he played guitar on the '86 vinyl EP "Orgasm", from X (pre- X Japan). Takai is also known as "Shu" or "Jun". Under his own name he released his solo album "Radiant Heat", in 1994. In the late 90's he had another solo project, named Gyorai, where he worked with Kazuhiro "Rob" Umehara, the vocalist from Tokyo heavy metal act Ｍｅｐｈｉｓｔｏｐｈｅｌｅｓ and more. The last few years Hisashi gives guitar lessons. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hisashi+Takai">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
