Kowalsky meg a Vega is a hungarian pop/rock/groove band, originally founded in 1999, members are: Kowalsky (vocals, lyrics, also member of Black-Out and Mantra), Vidák "More" Róbert (guitar, acoustic guitar, vocals), Vajda "Sóder" Péter (drums), Tobak Dániel (acoustic guitar, guitar, vocals), Szórád "Jimmy" Csaba (bass, vocals).
