There are a few bands with the name Armour:

1) Armour, a metal band from Finland, who started in 2006.
2) Armour, a South African post-hardcore band, who started in 2003.

Brought to life in 2006 exceptionally by the members of Black Metal bands, Armour represents the Finnish Heavy Metal scene in its finest old-school vein. The project consists of the singer / drummer from Satanic Warmaster Vince "Werewolf" Venom, the guitarist Mike "Aor Jumala" Slutz (ex-Blasphemous Evil), the bassist Pete "Sixshooter" Talker, and Johnny River who took over the drummer role after legendary Jake "The Goat" Spring from Goatmoon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Armour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
