Yardfield Colony was founded in 2011, by Hoffi, Hogler and Suus. Tom (ex - Dispostion) took the place behind the drums in early 2015 and in July Jo followed (ex - Mental Amputation) on vocals. In 2015 the existing songs got prepaired and new material was collected. This 5 guys come up with their mix of Death Metal, Metalcore and influences from other genres to refresh the German metal scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yardfield+Colony">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
