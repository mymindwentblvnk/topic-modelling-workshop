Indungeon was a melodic black/thrash metal band from Sweden featuring Mikael Andersson (guitar), Jonas Albrektsson (bass/guitar), Stefan Weinerhall (bass/guitar), and Karl Beckmann (drums/vocals).  In 1996 they released a demo titled, "Machinegunnery of Doom" and the following year released their debut album, which bore the same name.  1999 saw their second and last album titled, "The Misanthropocalypse."  Members of this band also played in: Thy Primordial, Niden Div. 187, and Mithotyn (among a few other lesser known bands).    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Indungeon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
