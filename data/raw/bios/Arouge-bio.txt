Heavy metal act from Tokyo, Japan, formed in '80. They were probably best known for having guitarist Fumihiko Kitsutaka in the band, who also had a career with King-Show and X.Y.Z.->A. Arouge released their self-titled full-length studio album in '84 on the Victor record label. It was reissued in 2004 with a bonus disc included. Arouge is currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/AROUGE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
