Barnaby Griffin is a singer/song-writer/guitarist living in the southern Costa Blanca, Spain. He has been playing guitar and writing music for over 20 years. Most of his tracks are love ballads, although he has a variety of instrumental numbers and comedy songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Barnaby+Griffin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
