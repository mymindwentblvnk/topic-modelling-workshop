aeshma - is an electronic industrial project, named in honor of the Persian demon or daeva of wrath and created in early 2010 by dahak (aka morbid). music of the project consists of a experimental mix of electronic and industrial music, as well as elements of ritual and ethnic music from different cultures. the main objective of this music is awakening of luciferian or ahrimanic black flame from the inside. it's dance of adversary who can be inside each one us.
