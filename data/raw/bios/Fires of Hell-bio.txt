Fires Of Hell is a one man black metal project from Oulu, Finland, consisting of Twilight  - Vocals, Guitars, Bass, Drum Programming (Paha Paholainen, Sanctimonious)

Twilight started the project in early 2004.

The first demo "Horrific Satanist Music" was released in the summer of 2004.
