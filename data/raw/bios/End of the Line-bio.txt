1) End of The Line were a hardcore band from San Diego, active in the early 1990's. Three members were in Heroin, one member was in Antioch Arrow, and one member was in John Henry West. Their sound was very San Diego and could be described as a much more aggressive version of Heroin. They released a Self-Titled 12" (1993). "Burning Down", with a different sample in the beggining was on the "Give Me Back" compilation 12" (1991). Both releases were put out by Ebullition Records.

2) END OF THE LINE (sometimes also written as EOTL) was German emo punk rock band founded in 1999 in Bielefeld. The Band is now known as Arcona Comes Also check out: http://www.eotl.de <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/End+of+the+Line">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
