A death metal/thrash metal band from Garnerville, New York.  They formed in 1987 and disbanded in 1988.  Their last known lineup consisted of Damon Trotta (vocals, bass), Glenn Snelwar (guitars) and Rod Zodda (drums).  During their brief existence, they released The Cross Has Fallen in 1987 and Demo 1988 that had a song called Out of Control.  They also released another demo called At War With Self.  Only one song off that demo surfaced in 2011 and it is called Diseased State.  It is unknown when the demo was released and there may be other songs off the At War With Self demo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Syianic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
