There are more than one band using this name:

1) Ashcloud is the electronic avant-garde projet of South-American producer EJ_D (Aurasleep, Dolens Aegrus). His aurasonic streams involve a mixture of soothing synthwaves, glitchy beats and acoustic instrumentation combined with smooth ambient textures to create deep and embracing psychodelic soundscapes. His works of unconventional methods, all self-produced and generated from scratch without following any contemporary patterns or standards. At the moment, EJ_D is producing Ashcloud's first conceptual album, which is completely based on improvisation and experimentation.

2) A Death Metal band existing out of two members. One from England ( Gareth Nash ) and one from Sweden   ( Jonny Pettersson ). The band was formed in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ashcloud">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
