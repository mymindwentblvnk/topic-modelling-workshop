Royce Wood Junior is a electronic musician, songwriter and producer from Brighton, UK. Now based in London, Royce is gaining prominence with DJs such as Mary Anne Hobbs, MistaJam, and Annie Mac. Royce's work takes influences from R&B, electro and trip-hop.

Royce has also contributed to work from  Jamie Woon ,  Kwabs ,  Jono McCleery ,  Denai Moore ,  Rosie Lowe ,  George Maple  and  Sinead Harnett .
