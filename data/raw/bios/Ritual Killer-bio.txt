Ritual Killer is a black metal band from New Orleans LA. The lineup consists of Sammy Duet (crowbar/acid bath/goatwhore), Zak Nolan (ex-goatwhore), Jordan Barlow (cancer patient), and James Harvey (rotfest/aphornon). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ritual+Killer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
