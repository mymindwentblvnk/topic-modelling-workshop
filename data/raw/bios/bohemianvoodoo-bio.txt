bohemianvoodoo (ボヘミアンブードゥー) is a jazz group that began in Tokyo, Japan in 2008. The group consists of bashiry (guitar), 木村イオリ (piano, keyboards), Nassy (bass), and 山本拓矢 (drums).

Discography:
'bohemianbazaar' (2009)
'Lapis Lazuli' (2010)
'Live at Motion Blue yokohama' (2011)
'Scenes' (2012)
'color & monochrome' split with fox capture plan (2013)
