Tavoris Javon Hollins Jr. (born August 17, 1997), known professionally as Vory (formerly King Vory), is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He won a Grammy for his work on The Carters' Everything Is Love and has released several solo projects including the EP Lucky Me in 2018 and the single "You Got It" in 2019. He gained further recognition for his work on Kanye West's album Donda, which earned him an additional Grammy nomination in 2021. He is currently signed to Capitol Records, Electric Feel Entertainment, and rapper Meek Mill's record label Dream Chasers Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/vory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
