Bloodmoon Collective is a progressive metal band from southern Ontario, Canada. Founded in 2009, currently unsigned have released one full length "Limitless Complex" digitally.

Bloodmoon Collective are: 

Mike Amicarelli - Guitars/Vocals - Writer
Gerry Pannunzio - Drums - Writer
Curtis Brown - Drums (Live)
Chris Strange - Guitar
