Formed in 2005, World Under Blood is a melodic death metal band featuring Deron Miller (CKY), Luke Jaeger (Sleep Terror), Risha Eryavac (ex-Decrepit Birth) and Tim Yeung (Decrepit Birth, Vital Remains, Hate Eternal).
The band has released 2 songs as of yet on their MySpace (which can be found at www.myspace.com/worldunderblood), "Dead and Still in Pain" and "God Among the Waste".  There have been two different versions of DASIP on the site, one mixed by Deron, and the later mixed by Logan Mader.  And as of September 21st, The anticipated song "Under the Autumn Low" has been released to their myspace page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/World+Under+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
