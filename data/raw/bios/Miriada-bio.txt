Russian Melodic Power/Speed Metal band, formed in april 2005 but they had started work in 2004. At first Miriada started as a cover band. But then in Janury 2005 they already had authors music. In the end of 2005 they were ready for recording, they even had the idea of building their own studio. So in co-operation with musically trained friends of them 'AlderBand Studio' was born. In March 2006 an agreement with Iron Ltd was achieved so Miriada's debut album was released. However, the symbiosis of different influences created interesting product for the audience.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Miriada">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
