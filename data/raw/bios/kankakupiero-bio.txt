Kankaku Pierrot were formed in 2013 by guitarist Akizuki Takuto (秋月 琢登), drummer Nishio Kenta (西尾 健太), and bassist Takiguchi Daiki (滝口 大樹) from Oosaka, along with guitarist and vocalist Yokoyama Naohiro (横山 直弘) from Hokkaido. They also formed their own label, Jiji Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/kankakupiero">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
