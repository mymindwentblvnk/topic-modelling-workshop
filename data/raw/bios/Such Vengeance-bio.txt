Such Vengeance is a melodic-death/ thrash metal band from Pleasant Grove, UT. Never a band to follow trends, Such Vengeance's debut record "The Time is Now", is a stand out in an all too often tired genre. Formed in 2008, Such Vengeance is currently unsigned with the following members in the line up:

Ian Eskelson - Vocals 
Derrick Dennert - Guitar 
Conor Papineau - Guitar 
Josh Hone - Bass 
Eric Watson - Drums
