LOCRACY is a melodic-death-metal band from Austria.

Visit Locracy at myspace, or become a fan on facebook.
Download the Locracy debut album (2009) free  here.

The band was formed in 2006 in Carinthia, Austria, by Bernd Warmuth (guitar), Stefan Asseg (drums) and others. In 2007, the band moved to Vienna and Georg Jobst (guitar) joined. In early 2008, Bernd Warmuth quit and Jack Jindra  (guitar), Peter Schrammel (vocals), and Matthias Emich (bass) joined the band. During studio recording sessions in autumn 2008, Matthias Emich (bass) was replaced by Stefan Hörersdorfer (bass). In 2009 Stefan Hörersdorfer left the band for another project. As a new bassist, Kevin Attoh was found. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Locracy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
