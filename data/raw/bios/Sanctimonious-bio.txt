Sanctimonious is a Finnish black metal band consisting of:
Twilight (vocals)
Eclipse (guitars)
Epidemica (bass)
Inarcissos (drums)

The band was started in 1998 by Eclipse and Twilight in Oulu, Finland.

Their first demo was recorded in 2001 by the line-up of Twilight (vocals), Eclipse (guitar), Plague (guitar), Epidemica (bass) and Lalli (drums). 

After the demo was released Plague left the band and Lalli was replaced with Inarcissos.
