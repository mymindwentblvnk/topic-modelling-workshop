a death metal band from Lithuania that was formed in 1990. Released a demo "Prayer to Fire" in 1994 and then split up soon. They started with the name "Furija" but changed it to Pessimus in 1993.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pessimus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
