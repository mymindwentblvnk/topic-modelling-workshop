Decadenze were born into a world of the mundane and the unimaginative. They sought refuge in music and became enthralled by the daring, excitement and adrenaline of rock 'n' roll.

Five minds sharing one thought: 'To push their own boundaries and achieve full musical, and artistic, potential'

Welcome to a band beyond obsession. . .
