Native In Black - nordic black metal band from Chelyabinsk, Russland (Cold Ural Mountains). It is not just another black metal band with dark winter philosophy. Black metal of Native In Black is the way, that is full of hatred to modern society, modern punk..rock..metal and e.t.c. bands, that JUST PLAY MUSIC. Native In Black creates a magic. Native In Black do not play music... They create their own world... Native In Black fuck the people in their ears! Cold winds bring their names.
