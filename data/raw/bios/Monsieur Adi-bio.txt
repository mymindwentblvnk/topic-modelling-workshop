Monsieur Adi (aka Adi Balbus, born 22 August 1986) is an electronic musician & producer from Paris, France. Adi has created remixes for many artists including Ellie Goulding, Fallulah, Madonna, The Knocks, Chiddy Bang, and many more. His work is marked by the fusion of futuristic instruments with the classical to create epic, cinematic, and modern soundscapes. The music nods to his various influences ; from the obvious Parisian duo Daft Punk to Icelandic alternative queen Björk to the baroque compositions of Vivaldi.

In 2010, his début original work "We Are The Romans" was released on his label Technofavrik with several remixes. In the early part of 2012 the "Fire Fire Fire" EP is set to be released, followed by the long-awaited debut album "Empire." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Monsieur+Adi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
