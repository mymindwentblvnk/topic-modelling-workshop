Hailing from Myrtle Beach, South Carolina,USA, Circles in Autumn was formed in 2005. They released their first EP in 2006 and have a new release slated for the summer of 2009. They frequently play concerts on the east coast and have played with many bands, including Opeth, The Classic Struggle, Sex Slaves, The Independents, Black Market Hero,  and many others . They are currently searching for a label and will be touring the southeast US this summer.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Circles+in+Autumn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
