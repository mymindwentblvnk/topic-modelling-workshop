Spleen Saint is a melodic black/death metal act hailing from Sri Lanka which was formed in early half of the new millennium. The bands draws its influences from acts such as death,emperor,cradle of filth,dissection and opeth. The lyrics are mainly wound around misanthropy,blasphemy and injustice. The current line up includes Dylan (Vocals and Guitar), Malith (Guitar),Arosha (Bass) and Kasun (Drums).
