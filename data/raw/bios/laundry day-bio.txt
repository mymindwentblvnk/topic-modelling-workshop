LAUNDRY DAY is a band composed of 5 friends from New York. Henry Weingartner, Jude Lipkin, Sawyer Nunes, Etai Abramovich, and Henry Pearl are talented individuals with a shared passion for music and performing. They are currently on their WE SWITCHED BODIES tour, with dates all over the United States, United Kingdom, and Ireland. The band currently has 4 albums out, and have made multiple behind the scenes documentaries for various singles off their latest album, WE SWITCHED BODIES. The official LAUNDRY DAY Instagram account is @daundrylay, with the individual members accounts being @henryelias, @sawyernunes, @henrypearl, @judeciulla, and @thugtai. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/laundry+day">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
