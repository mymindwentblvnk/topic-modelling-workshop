Juliette Armanet (born March 4, 1984) is a French singer and songwriter. Armanet won the Album Révélation of the Year in 2018 at the Victoires de la Musique for Petite Amie. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Juliette+Armanet">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
