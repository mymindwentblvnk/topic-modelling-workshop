Metavenge is a Thrash Metal band from Houston, Texas. Combining the feel of classic bay area thrash flavors with a very modern edge Metavenge is taking the metal scene by the throat they has not let go since their formation in 2008.  They have quickly risen to the to top of Texas metal and the Texas Buzz (FM radio) voted them statewide Best Metal Band of 2009. 
The current line-up:
Anthony Cruz Jr. - Vocals, Rhythm Guitar
Ben Matthews- Lead Guitar
Chris Richardson - Bass Guitar
Brandon Galvan - Drums
