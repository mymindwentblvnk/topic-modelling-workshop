Grave Miasma is a blackened death metal band from England. The band was first formed in 2002 under the name of Goat Molest√∂r and changed to Grave Miasma in 2006. One demo tape, Ancient Barbaric Assault, was released under this name, as well as the Realm of Evoked Doom 7" in 2005, and later re-released on CD with improved sound by Nuclear Winter Records. The band released the Exalted Emanation MLP on September 9th 2009 (9.9.9.) on Sepulchral Voice Records.

After a small break and a handful of gigs and tours in Europe and the USA, Grave Miasma released their debut full-length album entitled Odori Sepulcrorum on 13.9.13. This was released by Sepulchral Voice Records in Europe and Profound Lore Records in the USA. The band embarked on a 14-date European tour with Necros Christos shortly after.
