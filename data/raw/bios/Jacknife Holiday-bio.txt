Jacknife Holiday, a sludge / doom metal band established 2009 in Manchester, within a year released the "Stinkin' n Drinkin'" EP, which received over 500 downloads in the first week. The band has an original sound, delivering catchy songs but remaining brutally heavy and crushing.

The band performed at DOWNLOAD FESTIVAL 2010, and has also shared stages with the likes of SOILENT GREEN, DRIPBACK, MALEFICE, VOLITION, CHARGER and RAMESSES.

Introducing two new members in spring 2012 and swiftly going on to headline the 10th Metal Massacre festival, the band are booking shows and are to go into the studio soon to record a new EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jacknife+Holiday">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
