There are several bands known as Shock Treatment:

1. Shock Treatment is the name of a punk-rock / power pop trio from Madrid, Spain. 

The band was born in 1989, when its members were studying at the high school and discovered their common worship towards The Ramones. In fact, they decided to call their band 'Shock Treatment' after a Ramones song.

They recorded four LPs before 1998, when they ceased their activity: "Adiós, tú", "Punk party" "Operación dragón" and "Están vivos". They also released several singles and participated in a split album with punkrock bands from Italy and Spain. 

2. Shock Treatment were a punk band from Northern Ireland.
