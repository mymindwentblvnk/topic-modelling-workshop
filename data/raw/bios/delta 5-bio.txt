Delta 5 were a post punk which formed in 1979 in Leeds, England. The band consisted of Julz Sale (vocals, guitar), Alan Riggs (guitar), Ros Allen (bass), Bethan Peters (bass) and Kelvin Knight (drums). The band released one album, "See the Whirl" , and five singles before disbanding in 1981. The band were noted for their feminist, political lyrics and  lineup that included two bass guitarists.
