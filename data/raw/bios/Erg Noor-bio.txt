Erg Noor is a one-man doom/death band formed in Germany, Leipzig in 2004 (?).

Plays majestic doom/death with power metal influences, growls and beautiful melodic guitars. Highly reccomended for anyone into epic doom metal.

All albums are released as free mp3s only. This includes Revelation (2004), Voices Of Earth (2004), Seasons (2005), Kosmos (2006), The Story (2007). and Sea Journey (2009).
