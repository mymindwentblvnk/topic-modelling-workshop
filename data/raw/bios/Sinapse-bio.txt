Sinapse is an up and coming emcee out of Philadelphia. This bio does not pertain to any other music uploaded to Last FM under the name "Sinapse". Originally from gulf coast Florida, Sinapse did not make the transition from fan to artist until later in life than most emcees, at age 27. Since 2007 Sinapse has recorded and released 3 projects, the mixtape compilation "Sinapse Volume 1: Recon", the double disc debut LP "Re-Evolution" and the recent short album "Prelude To The Odyssey". Currently Sinapse is buidling his reputation as a performance artist in the Philadelphia area and continuing to build what is quickly becoming a prolific catalog.
