Divultion is a brutal death metal band from Crimea, Ukraine. They formed at a festival in 2006 by Alexandr Zaytsev (Guitars) and Alexandr Prokofyev (Drums). Andrey Hutter (Bass) and Anton Rosovskiy (Vocals) got invited by Zaytsev in late 2006. They self-released their first demo "DEMOralization" in April, 2008.
