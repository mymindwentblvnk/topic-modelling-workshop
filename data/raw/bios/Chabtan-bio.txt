Biography
CHABTAN, metal band formed in 2011 in Paris / France, draws its influences in
metal hardcore, deathcore and melodic death metal. The french combo from Paris will produce its own entirely self made EP in 2012. This first opus based on the concept of mesoamerican mythologies, offers a raw music without any concession. So, see you in the pit !!!!
