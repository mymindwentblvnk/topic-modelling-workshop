Diddy-Dirty Money is an R&B/Hip Hop/Rap trio formed by Bad Boy Records owner Sean "Diddy" Combs. The group was formed In April 2009, with Dawn Richard (ex-member of R&B/Pop group Danity Kane) and singer Kalenna. 

The group released their debut album, Last Train to Paris on December 13th 2010. Preceded by four singles, each of which attained varying success on the Billboard Hot 100, the album debuted at number seven on the Billboard 200 Album Chart. According to the Angels Songfacts, Last Train to Paris is a concept album in which Diddy's character takes an emotional journey from London to Paris and falls in love with a mysterious lady.
