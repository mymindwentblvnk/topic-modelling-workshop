Defuntos is a doom/black metal band from Portugal formed in 2005. So far they've released one demo (Mundo de Lápides), two EPs (Um sofrimento distante, Os Suplicios De Uma Triste Lembranca), two full-lengths (Sangue Morto, A Negra Vastidão das Nossas Almas) and a split with Striborg. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Defuntos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
