Winsome tunesmiths of lo-finest pop. Ruari, Lorna and Eilidh live in Glasgow and transcribe bedroom feelings into strings and vocals. 

Golden Grrrls released their debut 7" mid February 2011 on London label Night School. The second single, following a self-released tour tape, is 'New Pop' and will be released in November 2011.
