The Hideous Sun Demons are: 
Toshi Hiketa- guitar 
James LoMenzo- bass 
Ray Luzier- drums

Starting life as david lee roth's band, HSD released their self titled debut album of all instrumental tracks. From very structured pieces to wide open jams.

Formed by roth's long time drummer Ray luzier the band began to shape up when Japanese guitarist, Toshi Hiketa, was brought on board. DLR bassist, James Lomenzo, became interested in the project and the three musicians began to write music together <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hideous+Sun+Demons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
