DEMON CHILD was a melodic black metal band from Laitila, Finland. The band was formed in 2000, split-up around 2003.

Discography:

Sorrow Cast Upon the Angels (demo, 2001)
Shadow Cult (full-length, 2002)

Line-up:

Deimos - Vocals, Bass (Bestial Ritual)
Charon - Guitar, Bass (Black Blood (Fin), Kadotus, Pyromaniac)
Pyromaniac - Drums (Ancestors Blood, Black Blood (Fin))
E. Heinonen - Synth (Ancestors Blood)
