Doom metal band from Göteborg, Sweden. Formed in summer 2003, three recordings so far; "Bound" (2004),"47" (2005) and "Truth is nothing but deceit" (2008). They have also recorded a song called "Forever Lost" and a cover of Skepticism´s "The Falls", the latter included on "Entering the levitation" - a tribute to Skepticism. 

Members are: Jonas Jörgensen (guitars), Louise Halldin (vocals and bass), Mats Dacke (guitars) and Christian Martinsson (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/It+Will+Come">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
