GANGREL is a Peruvian Thrash Metal band formed in 2007 by Diego Andrés. In 2008 GANGREL recorded their demo debut “Metal Hunters” which was released on 2009.

This Demo consists of five original songs: “Front Page Killer”, “The Screamer”, “Metal Hunters”, “Carnage” and “Gangrel” and was recorded in 4 days and mixed in three weeks on a local studio. 

After the recording of “Metal Hunters” the band begin to play live becoming one of the strongest young bands of the Peruvian scene nowadays.
