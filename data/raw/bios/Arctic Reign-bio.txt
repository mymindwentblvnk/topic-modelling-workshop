Arctic Reign is a Black Metal band from Larisa and formed in late 2015 by Vaggelis and Thodoris Grigoriou.
The first album of Arctic Reign entitled The birth of a Carnival recorded in Audiolab music 
productions studios and will be released by the company Barbatos Productions in mid- August 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arctic+Reign">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
