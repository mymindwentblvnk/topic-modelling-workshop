Runespell is an Australian one-man Black Metal project formed by Nightwolf (Blood Stronghold, ex-Eternum, ex-Necrostrigis). Runespell draws on medieval-themes, which are prevalent both in its instrumentation and lyrical themes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Runespell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
