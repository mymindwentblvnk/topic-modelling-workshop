Hatedrive is a german thrash/death metal band, with melodic parts. The band was founded in 2001. In 2004 they released their first MCD called "Population: 0" and you can listen to it on their web page. In 2005 they played a gig at Wacken Metal Battle, where they made it to the semi finals.

Current line-up:

Dave - Vocals
Bernd - Guitar
Steff - Guitar
Sven - Bass
