Cold Blank Stare is a New Jersey, US based recording project which plays a musically traditional, yet lyrically individualist  variation of black metal.

Humanity Will Come Undone is the debut EP, and was limited to 50 hand made CD's. The CD version is now officially "sold out", and there will be no repress. Download the EP (in 320kbps mp3 with lyrics and artwork) here.

Cold Blank Stare's debut full length debut, tentatively titled The Human Condition: Insights Into Madness is imminent.  Everything is tracked and in the mastering process with a release TBA
