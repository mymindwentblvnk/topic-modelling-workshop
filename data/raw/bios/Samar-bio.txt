Samar Mattar (Arabic: سمر مطر‎) is a female singer from the United Arab Emirates whose talent was discovered by Kuwaiti singer Abdullah Al Rowaished. She has a style of singing that differs from other Arabic pop singers and sings mostly in the Gulf Arabic accent, except in some of her more recent songs which have been in the Egyptian accent.

Samar released her first album in 2000, and has since released four albums. All of her albums are produced by the Arabic music label Rotana. She has also recorded several music videos. Some of Samar's popular songs include "Hobbi Wa Hobbak", "Allah Ala Killee", "Ama Ashogtah", "Ana Fee El Gharam", and "Wa'ed Minni".
