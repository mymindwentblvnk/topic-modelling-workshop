SPIRITUS TENEBRAE consisted of members Zwaznu and Saelok from New Orleans. The Twins.
Saelok (aka Erichte) also was in the Norwegian blackmetal band ANCIENT. 
SPIRITUS TENEBRAE released Temple of Anguis in 2000.
Following 13years the duo disbanded in 2005 following Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans.
