Soon to be Forgotten (STBF) is a metal-band from the Norwegian west coast. 

Members:
Alf Morten “Taaken” Bjørnsen (vocals)
Vidar Nordhus (guitar)
Håkon Brunes (guitar)
Joakim Røgenes (drums)
Hans Olaf Myrvang (bass and backing vocals)
