Loud Silence are an all-girl, rock/ hard rock/ grunge band from London, England. They were formed in 2011 by vocalist Charliy Vaughan, bassist Frances Smith and former guitarist Jaime Lloves.

Their main influences include artists such as Evanescence, Nirvana and Muse, and they are currently unsigned. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Loud+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
