Legal Butchery was a Brutal Death Metal band from Belgium. They released one full length album called "Key To Paradise" in 1996 via Shiver Records. The line up for this album was: Yvan (Vocals), Vogel (Bass), Steven (Drums) , Pim (Guitars) and Tony (Guitars).
