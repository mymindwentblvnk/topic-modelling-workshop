Signist is a Melodic/Atmospheric/Death metal band from Tomsk, Russia. The band was formed by Ilyas Ibraev (vocals, drums, keys) and his brother Konstantin (bass, all guitars). After their first demo they released their debut LP "Of Worlds Endtimely Enshadowed" in the winter of 2006. The album was promoted by the brothers and has never been released under any label. Signist is a Death/Doom influenced band, however, in their debut album you can hear inspiration from many different metal genres. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Signist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
