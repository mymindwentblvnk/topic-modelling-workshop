Whales and Aurora is a Sludge band from Italy. 
This band is a project born from a current member of The Secret and former members of Godless Crusade, Anger and La Peste. Coming from different musical realities W&A focus themselves on the emotional impact of sound in its different expressions, engaging in a musical research devoid of any standards/stereotypes.
