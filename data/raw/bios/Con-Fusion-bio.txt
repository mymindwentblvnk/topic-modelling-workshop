Con-Fusion is a Jazz-Rock band from Georgia. The band started in 2008 may. All members of the band graduated from Tbilisi Paliashvili Music College. Since then the band has played number of concerts, took part in the TV show "Listen to the live music" and by july 2009 finished work on their first album "Four October". Prezentation of the album was held in Magti Club, Tbilisi on 19-th of december 2009. The Music style of the band is pretty different-fusion, jazz-rock, funk, latino, reggae etc, complex measures and interesting harmony is mixed with original melodies wich creates a unique sound of the band.Now the band is working on the new material and looks forward to record it on their second album.
