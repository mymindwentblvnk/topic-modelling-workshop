Shadow Venger is the London based guitarist, creating a mixture of instrumental tracks and tracks with guest vocalists, combining multiple styles of Metal, Rock and Classical, inspired by the philosophy "Absorb what is useful, Discard what is not, Add what is uniquely your own" - Bruce Lee.  

Shadow Venger is also the guitarist of the band  EnkElination  (Symphonic Metal) and founding member of  the bands Stand Aside and  Empyreal Destroyer (aka Kerosys)  (Melodic Death Metal).

Other projects include Venger's Diary, an Alternative Metal project focusing more on lyrics than guitar work.
