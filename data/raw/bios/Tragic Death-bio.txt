Tragic Death is a 3-piece metal band that originated in Madison, WI. Their self-branded 'Apocalyptic Metal' style is an amalgam of Black Metal, Thrash, and Death Metal sub-genres resulting in a furious, unrelenting sound that not only crushes with it's brutality but also hypnotizes with a transcendental atmosphere. Tragic Death has recently relocated to the woods of Northern California to further pursue their chaotic path
