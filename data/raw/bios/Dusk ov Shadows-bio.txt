Dusk Ov Shadows is an Ambient Funeral Doom project fronted by the man who also fronts Funerary Dirge. Music is very heavy, sorrowful, utterly depressive, and might be perfect for the ones who are looking for bands such as Until Death Overtakes Me or Through The Valley.
