Beresith is a black/death metal band from Michigan City, Indiana. The band was started in late 2006 and combines American death metal/thrash with Swedish and Norwegian black metal influences.

 Beresith's lineup consists of Mathew Houghton (guitar), Taylor Patz (drums/vocals), Jacob Heflin (bass ex-Black the Sky and Guilty Minds), Bobby Lee (vocals), and Will Davis (guitar).
 

 Previous members include Jason Schact (bass), Brian Robison (guitar), and Chris Keeney (vocals), and Mike Pagos (guitar),  Brad Ferrel (bass).
