Wicked Sensation is a melodic heavy metal band  founded in Germany in 1999 by Michael Klein (songwriter and guitarist of the band). Robert Soeterboek (the albums Reflected and Crystallized), Fernando Garcia (the album Exceptional) and Boban Milojevic were the vocalists at various points in the band's history . 
The latest studio album Crystallized  that came out in 2010 was initially started as a project by Michael Klein after the band split in 2006. It features guest appearance by Andi Deris on tracks My turn to fly and Gimme the Night co-written by Arjen Lucassen (Ayreon) and Robert Soeterboek.
