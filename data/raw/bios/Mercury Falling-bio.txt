Mercury Falling is a progressive/power metal band from Germany which was formed in 1997 at Bad Salzschlirf. So far they released two demos named "Impressions Of A Dream" in 1998 and "Pantha Rei" in 2001. They also released two regular albums. First of them recorded in 2003, was named "Pantha Rei" likewise the second demo and concised from eleven tracks including all four songs from that record. Their last, but not least ;) full album titled "Human Nature" had been relaesed in year 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MERCURY+FALLING">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
