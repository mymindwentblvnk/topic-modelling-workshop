Corpse Light is a 5-piece sludge, doom, post-metal band from Baltimore, Maryland. They released their debut 4-song ep titled "Without Form" in May 2015 through Grimoire Records. It is available at https://corpselight.bandcamp.com For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/corpselightdoom/

Members:
Aurora Heather - Bass
Kieran Holmes - Lead Guitar 
Jim Webb - Vocals
Don Sellner - Rhythm Guitar
