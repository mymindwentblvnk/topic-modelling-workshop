Soul Grip is a black metal/hardcore band from Beligum. Bands they share similarities in sound with include Young and in the Way, Hexis, The Secret and Celeste.

Their debut EP "Avadon" is available for streaming and/or purchase at http://soulgrip.bandcamp.com/releases.
