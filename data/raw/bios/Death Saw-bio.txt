Death Saw is Serbian rock band from Belgrade, formed in 2006. by Đorđe David Nikolić (vocal), Aleksandar Mitrović - Tommy (guitar), Đorđe Golubović - Johnsy (bass guitar) and Stefan Dželetović - Džela (drums). 
While performing at many bike shows, city squares and festivals, band was working on songs that ended up on their first album Blood Avenger.
Author and arranger, guitarist Aleksandar Mitrović -- Tommy, used different musical styles in composing these songs. There are thirteen tracks on the album, nine in English and four in Serbian language. Songwriters were Malcolm Bridger, Mlađan Dinkić, Dragana Ilić and Goran Sobić. 
Blood Avenger was released in November 2010. through Serbian label Power Music.
