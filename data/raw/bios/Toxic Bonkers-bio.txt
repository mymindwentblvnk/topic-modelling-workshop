political grindcore band formed in 1993 by five young musicians from Łódź, Poland which quickly learnt another musical influences such as: death-metal, thrash-metal, crust, punk, etc. At date the band has recorded six albums and one ep. The band is well know in eastern Europe and has played live concerts next to bands such as: Sepultura, Soulfly, Fear Factory, Benediction, Lamb Of God, Vader and more.
