The New Raemon is an indie pop band from Barcelona, Spain. The band has been active since 2008 and is fronted by the Catalan musician Ramon Rodriguez who sings and plays guitar. He is accompanied by Ricky Falkner, who plays bass, drums as well as being the backing vocals. Pablo Garrido plays electric guitar and Marc Prats plays keyboard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+New+Raemon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
