Miles Kane (born 17th March 1986) is an English musician. He is best known for sharing lead responsibilities and writing with Alex Turner in their side-project The Last Shadow Puppets. He also was part of the bands The Rascals and The Little Flames before he decided to start a solo career. "Colour of The Trap", his debut album was released on Monday 9th May 2011. Kane is the cousin of James Skelly of The Coral. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Miles+Kane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
