MTNS (pronounced 'mountains') are an indie pop-band formed 3 July, 2013 in Brisbane, Australia. The band consists of  vocalist and guitarist Tom Eggert, drummer and sampler Joseph Thiang and keyboardist and bassist Robbie Hellberg. Their debut single "Lost Track Of Time" was released to critical acclaim with online music sites such as Line Of Best Fit, Indie Shuffle, and Earmilk. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mtns">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
