Acranius is a slamming brutal death metal band from Rostock, Germany. Originally a duo who recorded the EP The Echo of Her Cracking Chest in 2011, the band acquired a full lineup in 2012 and released their full-length debut album When Mutation Becomes Homicidal in 2013.
