Manshuria is now a five-piece somethingcore-psychedelic-rock n' roll-labelled band from Sao Paulo, Brazil.
Formed at the beginning of 2006, after Luck Signs broke up.
Their debut EP was recorded on November 2008 and you can find it somewhere over the internet for download. 
The band also recorded a single on July 2009. 
Now they are currently in studio working on a full length album.

Current members 
Cesar: drums
Leo: bass 
Meduza: guitar
Erick: guitar
Felipe: vocals
