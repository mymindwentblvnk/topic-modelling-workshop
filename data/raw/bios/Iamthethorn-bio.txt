Iamthethorn is a sludge band from Seattle. They released their debut EP entitled "You Are The Lamb" via-Goodfellow Records in late February of '07. They are currently working on material for a full length to be released later this year. Iamthethorn features members of Splatterhouse, Book of Black Earth, Skarp and Himsa.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Iamthethorn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
