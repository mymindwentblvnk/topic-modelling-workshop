Tempestuous Fall was founded in September, 2011 by sole member Dis Pater who also fronts other projects Midnight Odyssey and The Crevices Below. The idea for a funeral doom / doom-death project had been lying dormant since 2001. Taking inspiration from the funeral doom legends from Finland, as well as Disembowelment and early My Dying Bride.
