Harma is a Rock band formed in 2006 in Kurikka, Finland.
These days, the orchestra is really diverse. It performs about 25-30 gigs a year.
Festivals, restaurants, pubs, weddings and birthdays are the area of Harman.
The range of music at the gigs is wide, from hit to Finnish rock and all the way to hard rock.
The band is also known as an energetic live performer who grabs all kinds of gigs.
On April 18, 2012, Harma released his debut album "Kaihokaupunki", which was ranked 25th on Finland's official list!
