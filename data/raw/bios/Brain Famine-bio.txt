Brain Famine is a death metal project formed in Weymouth, Massachusetts in 2011, consisting of Chris Leamy and John Gillis. Gillis is the drummer, while Leamy handles everything else. They have both played in other local bands such as Continued Without a Finding, Militianary, Kevorkian's Angels, Hannaford and The Heartstoppers, You're Fired, Milkman, Shot and Pissed On, Ecostench, Nidus The Buzz, Today Is The Day, Anal Cunt, Towel Collector, and more!
