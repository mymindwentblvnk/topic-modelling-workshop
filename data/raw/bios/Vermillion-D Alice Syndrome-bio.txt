Vermillion-D Alice Syndrome is a metal doujin circle based in Japan by 刹那 (Setsuna) of 黒夜葬 and D機関, in charge of arrangement, lyrics, guitar and bass and Rib:y(uhki) of Imperial Circus Dead Decadence on vocals, formed in 2011.
