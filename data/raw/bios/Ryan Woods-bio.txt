Born in Altamonte Springs, Florida and raised in Knoxville, Tennessee, Ryan Woods is a singer-songwriter based in Los Angeles, California. He switched over from public school to online school in order to pursue his dreams of making music and first found success when he dropped “bedroom tapes” on Soundcloud in November of 2019. Soon after, his first official single “Bad Texter” released in July 2020, reached 1M streams. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ryan+Woods">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
