M+A is Michele Ducci,19, (vocals, instruments) and Alessandro Degli Angioli , 22, (instruments). They create brilliant sunny electronic soft/pop music with rather unusual vocals. Their first album ‘things.yes’ was released worldwide on the 7th of November of 2011. Since then, they released their second album 'These days' in 2013, followed by a remix EP as well as "Anyway Milkyway" in 2015, an EP that features 5 new tracks including a collaborative track with Spank Rock.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/M%252BA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
