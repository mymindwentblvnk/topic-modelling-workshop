Anno Domini Mortus is a black metal band formed in New Plymouth, New Zealand, in 2005. The line up consists of Zekenfraut (proclaimer of perversions), Satannos (slayer of strings), and Xantubis (smiter of skins). Members have also been in NZ bands Draco Aerius (bm), The Gentlemans Club, Insidious and Amaranthine (bm).
