Skavenger was a metal band from Finland. They have released one demo Obsessions which features three songs "Death" "Russian Roulette" and "Obsessions of the Divine". The band broke up in late 2007 and reformed a day later under the name Abstraktia.

Band Members:
Kasper Sirenius - Vocals
Andy Mihailov - Guitar
Tomi Kuronen - Guitar
Mitro Saunio - Bass
