Aedera Obscura is an Italian metal group which was started on the 27th of September. 2003 Musically Aedera Obscura are influenced by different genres: Symphonic Black Metal, Classical Music, Death and Gothic Metal. Two of the members, Eric (keys) and Alessio (vocals), come from Catharsis. After some time of inactivity and a change of line up the group returns to perform in full force since september 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aedera+Obscura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
