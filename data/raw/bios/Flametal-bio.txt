Flametal is a California band that combines the Spanish musical style, Flamenco, with metal. Flamenco is a song, music and dance style which is strongly influenced by gypsies, but which has its deeper roots in Moorish and Jewish musical traditions.

Ben Woods: Flamenco Guitar
Thomas Perry: Drums
Brian Spaulding: Electric Guitar
Uriah Duffy: Bass
Angeline Saris: Bass
