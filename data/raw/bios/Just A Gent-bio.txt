Just A Gent is tuxedo-clad Australian producer, Jacob Grant. Over the past three years, he has reshaped the electronic music scene, leaving button-pushing conventions at the end of his coattails. At the age of only 20, he has taken Australian radio by storm with several hit records in heavy rotation on world famous Triple J. With well over 50 million streams worldwide, Just A Gent has amassed a huge digital following with his impressive catalogue of original works and remixes, and classic style. In Fall 2017, he will be heading out on tour in the US in support of Heroic Records artists, San Holo and Droeloe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Just+A+Gent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
