1) M.P. (which stands for MetaPhor) is one of the underground rap artists coming from Alabama.  Hailing from Birmingham. M.P.'s style is greatly influenced by the Screwed-Up Texas style, and he has collaborated with Texas artists such as C-Whiskey and D.S.R..  M.P. is CEO of C.I.A. (Cash In Advance) Records.  His first original project was "Mic Breaka Of The Year".  Some of his past mixtapes include "Waaay Afta Tha Kappa", "Goin Fed-Showin Naked All Flows Vol. 1", "Wreckin Underground", and his latest mixtape, "Legend In Da Makin". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/M.P.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
