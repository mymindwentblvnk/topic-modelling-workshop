Fischmob, from Hamburg, were a German hip hop band in the 1990s. The band, formed in 1993 and split in 1998, included DJ Koze (Stefan Kozalla), Cosmic DJ aka Daniel Somme, and Der schreckliche Sven (Sven Mikolajewicz) and Stachy produced 1 single, 2 albums and 5 MLPs, including their hit song Susanne Zur Freiheit. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fischmob">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
