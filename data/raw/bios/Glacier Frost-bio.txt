Glacier Frost is a musical project based out of the province of Newfoundland and Labrador. It encompasses the stylistic motifs of genres such as black metal, atmospheric black metal, post black metal, blackgaze, blackened folk metal, dark ambient, black ambient and space ambient.

Lyrical content is derived from the popular television series My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. The first album, Depths of Her Soul, will be released later in the summer of 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Glacier+Frost">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
