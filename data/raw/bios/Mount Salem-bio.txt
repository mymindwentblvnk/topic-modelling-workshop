Mount Salem are a psychedelic rock/doom band from Chicago, Illinois, USA formed in 2012. They released their debut EP Endless in 2013.

Current line-up:
Emily Kopplin on vocals
Kyle Morrison on guitars
Cody Davidson on drums
Mark Hewett on bass
