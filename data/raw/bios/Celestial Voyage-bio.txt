Celestial Voyage is a two-piece instrumental technical metal project based in Carpi, Italy. They started with the name "Cadaverium" in 2006, playing old school death metal. 
In 2008 they changed the band name to "Celestial Voyage".
They released their first self-titled album in 2009: it can be digitally downloaded for free at www.jamendo.com.

Now the band is working on a new EP titled "Universe Collapse" which will be released in 2011.

Line-up:
Andrea Zamparo - guitar
Lorenzo Venturi - drum programming
Guest member:
Vitaly Rudenko - fretless bass
