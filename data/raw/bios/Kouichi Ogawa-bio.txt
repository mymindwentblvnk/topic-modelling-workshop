Kouichi Ogawa (小川浩一, Ogawa Kouichi) is Guitarist of Japanese band MIGHTELAYER(1985~1988) and ANGELUS(1982~1985). He was born December 2, 1964. He formed MIGHTELAYER and ANGELUS again in November, 2006 and restarted music activity.
