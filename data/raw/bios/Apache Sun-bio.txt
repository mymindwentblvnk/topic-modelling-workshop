Apache Sun are a psychedelic rock band formed in Glasgow, Scotland in 2014, by singer-guitarist Kris Mitchell and bassist Pete Bruce along with drummer David Noble. Initially the band started as a home recording project after meeting at college. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Apache+Sun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
