1. Toska are a heavy progressive instrumental trio based in Brighton, UK, 
with members Rabea Massaad (Guitar), Dave Hollingworth (Bass) and Ben Minal (Drums).
They released their first EP  "Ode to the Author" in Feb 2016.  
They own a side project with Rob Chapman called "Dorje".

2. Toska are a metal band from Iceland who play a distinctly traditional and melodic style of black metal.
