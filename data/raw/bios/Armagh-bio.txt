Armagh is a polish black/heavy metal band formed in 2012 by Galin Soulreaper and Mark Usurper in Warsaw.

Current line-up:
Galin Soulreaper - Gutiar, Vocs;
Madman - Guitar, Vocs;
Mark Usurper - Bass, Backing Vocs;
