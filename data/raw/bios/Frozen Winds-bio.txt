Frozen Winds is a Black Metal band, hailing from Limassol, Cyprus. Frozen Winds released their first demo ‘Luciferian Calls’ in 2009 and their EP ‘Prophets with Distorted Faces’ in 2011. An album in the making for the past 7 years, ‘Necromantic Arts’ emerges, an obscure and conjuring force, invoking the occult arts into existence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Frozen+Winds">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
