Order Of Tepes are an old school Black Metal Assault from South Wales, they formed in october 09 with the members; Fan, Nattens and Greg
They took their name and the main influence from the factual life story of the 15th century Romanian tyrant, Vlad Tepes. Mixing old school black metal with various elements of doom and atmospheric music Order Of Tepes found a sound fitting to their visions for this concept.
