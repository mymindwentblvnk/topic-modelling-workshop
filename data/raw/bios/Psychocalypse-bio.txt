PSYCHOCALYPSE’s debut album “Crying Moon” is due for release on October, 21th via Buil2Kill Records/Nadir Music.

PSYCHOCALYPSE is a one man band project from Perpignan (France) founded in 2010 by Psychotic which mixes various music styles and mainly influenced by Melodic Death/Thrash/Symphonic and other metal’s styles. With an experience made in several bands and local musical projects, the musician decided to start this new his project expressing his state of mind by using several instruments and different singings.

“Crying Moon” will be released on all major digital markets Worldwide and physical distribution (exclusive digipack limited edition) is set to start as well.
