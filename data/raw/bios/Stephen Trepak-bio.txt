The solo project of the Composer/Drummer for the Experimental Hardcore band, Human Future.

The debut album, 'Egress', is roughly 40 minutes of genre-blending Doom, and brings to mind artists such as Abandon, Scott Walker, Neurosis and Kayo Dot. Recorded with the help of AJ Cookson (Necro Deathmort)  and mixed/mastered by Greg Chandler (Esoteric). It is to be released in 2014.
