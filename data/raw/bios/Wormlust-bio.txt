Wormlust is an atmospheric black metal band from Reykjavík, Iceland, recently partially relocating to London. The band was formed in 2006 and released their debut full-length album 'The Feral Wisdom' in 2013 through Daemon Worship Productions. H.V Lyngdal performs all instruments except for drums which are performed by Bjarni Einarsson (Sinmara, Slidhr). The band played their first live show at Oration MMXVI in Reykjavík with the help of Hafsteinn Vidar, Bjarni Einarsson, Örlygur Sigurðarson, and Sindri Stefánsson. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wormlust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
