Destruction Explosion Total Holocaust were a death/thrash metal band from Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil, formed in 1984 under the moniker Leviathan, which lasted until 1985 when they started using the name Death. In 1986 they finally became known as D.E.T.H., releasing their first demo, "Hell", in 1987. Later the same year, a rehearsal tape was also released. The band had its ties to Holocausto, Overdose and Armmagedon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D.E.T.H.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
