Max Brodie is a young Media Composer and Sound Designer from Chester, in the northwest of the UK. Picking up his first instrument at 6 years of age Max continues to develop his passion for all things music and audio. Inspired by the likes of Slash and media composer Rolfe Kent, Max has already established himself as one of Audio Network’s most popular composers.
Max was originally signed to Audio Network through the ‘Undiscovered’ series where the company nurtures new talent. His first releases were met with such overwhelming enthusiasm Audio Network hastily signed him as a fully-fledged composer! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Max+Brodie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
