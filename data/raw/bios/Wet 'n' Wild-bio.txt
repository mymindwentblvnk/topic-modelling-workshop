Wet'n'Wild was a Rockabilly Band from Switzerland. Founded in 1979 and was active until 1982. " Don't stop the Bop" was their first release in 1980. Heavily influenced by 1950's Rockabilly and Rock'n'Roll artists like Bill Haley, Sonny Fisher, Sun Record artists and the like. The line-up on this recording is as follows (from left to right on the cover): Thomas von Ballmoos (keys),Thomas Wyss (Bass), Daniel Nerlich (brother of Guenter Nerlich) (Guit.), Thomas Graf (Guit.), myself Guenter Nerlich (Vocals), John Otis (Drums) and Urs Roos (Sax).
