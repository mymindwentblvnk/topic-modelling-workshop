Punish is an active Swiss technical death metal band formed in 1996. They have released 1 demo (Three Songs Of Mental Disorder), 1 EP (Four Songs in Morbid Lust) and 3 full lengths (Punish, Dawn of the Martyr and Raptus). They have both thrash and black metal influences. 

Their latest album "Raptus" was released in 2009.
