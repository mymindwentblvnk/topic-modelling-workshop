EIMIN was formed On May 15, 2005.

Inspired by the sound of the bands Visual Kei of Japan in 80's 90's, EIMIN was created. EIMIN is a Visual band western Kei that combines the sound of several styles to create the own one and that is alike his influences.

In 2009 EIMIN changed his style, to a band more of the syle of the 70's-80's Glam Rock (KISS, Queen, Motley Crue) and the Japanese Heavy Metal Wave (X Japan, Sex machineguns, Loudness).

Actual songs of EIMIN sounds like Hard Rock, Speed Metal or Power Metal, a good example of that sound is EDGUY or Rata Blanca.

In 2010 the band went indefinite Hiatus.
