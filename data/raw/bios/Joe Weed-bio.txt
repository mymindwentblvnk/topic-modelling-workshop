Joe Weed records acoustic music at his Highland Studios near Los Gatos, California.  He has released six albums of his own, produced many projects for independent artists and labels, and does sound tracks for film, TV and museums. He recently worked on the PBS film “Andrew Jackson,” which premiered nationally on January 2, 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Joe+Weed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
