Skady is a German pagan metal band, based in Wolgast, Mecklenburg-Vorpommern. The band was founded in 2006 and they chose the name of the Goddess Of Ice and hunt as their name, Skady.
They have released one demo and one full-length album so far.

Members:
Andreas -- vocals
Azkel -- guitar
Antis -- bass
Letis -- guitar
