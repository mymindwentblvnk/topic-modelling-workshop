black metal band from Crimea, Ukraine since 2009. Released debut album Necrocosmos in 2010,  EP On the Way to the Land ov Nod in 2013, album Novum Testamentum: Evangelium Morti in 2013, EP Towards the Abyss in 2018, single Приди погибель in 2019 and third album Антитезис in late 2020 with Cold Breath of Silence records.
