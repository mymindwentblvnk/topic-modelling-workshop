The Mae Shi is an indie rock band which formed in 2002 in Los Angeles, California, United States. The band has had many lineup changes, with the only constants being brothers Tim Byron (vocals, bass) and Jeff Byron (vocals, guitar). The band has released two full length albums: "Terrorbird" (2004) and "HLLLYH" (2008), along with several EPs and demo CD-Rs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Mae+Shi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
