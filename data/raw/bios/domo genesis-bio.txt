Domonique Cole is Domo Genesis, a rapper from Hawthorne, California, USA and a member of the alternative hip-hop collective OFWGKTA.  Cole is known as Odd Future's resident stoner, and marijuana is a very prevalent theme in his songwriting. 

Cole has released two albums on Odd Future's official Tumblr account.  Rolling Papers was released in 2010 and was produced by fellow Odd Future members Tyler, The Creator and Left Brain.  Under the Influence, a mixtape with Cole rapping over "his favorite beats," was released in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Domo+Genesis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
