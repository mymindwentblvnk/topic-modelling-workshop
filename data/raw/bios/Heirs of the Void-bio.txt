Blackened/doom band from Aurich, Lower Saxony, Germany. Currently unsigned have released a demo Evil Spirit in 2013 and a full length Four Horsemen of Doom in 2014. 

Heirs of the Void are: 

Wilke Freesemann Bass (2012-present)
