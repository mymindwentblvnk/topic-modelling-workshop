Disbeliever are a gothic metal band formed in 2008 in Pisa, Italy. Initial lineup included the long time friends Sasha Ciampi (drums), his brother Yuri Ciampi (guitar), and Andy Madonia (bass). They were later joined by guitarist Marko Giampaolini and singer Luis McFadden. 
The debut album, Soul Divided, was released in mid 2010, and combines violent riffs and melancholic atmospheres.
