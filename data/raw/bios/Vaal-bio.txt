2. VAAL is an electronic producer from United Kingdom. Another alias of Eliot Sumner, the same one behind I Blame Coco. VAAL has established a sterling rep as one of the key members of the Afterlife family. Their music has been released via Baastard Records (their own label), Life And Death and Afterlife so far. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/vaal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
