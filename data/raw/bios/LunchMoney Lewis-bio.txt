Gamal Lewis, better known by his stage name LunchMoney Lewis, is an American hip hop artist from Miami, Florida. He is best known for his 2015 single Bills, the song peaked to number 1 for two weeks on the Australian Singles Chart. It also reached number two in the United Kingdom and number six in New Zealand. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lunchmoney+Lewis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
