James Baker (born May 19, 1996), known professionally as, BlocBoy JB, is an American rapper and songwriter from Memphis, Tennessee. He is best known for his song "Look Alive" featuring Drake which peaked at number five on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BlocBoy+JB">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
