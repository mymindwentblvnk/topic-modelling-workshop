Polish Black Metal band, formed in 1995. The members are De Sade on vocals (ex-Wizard (Pol), Opus Larvae, Red Violet) and Crudelis playing instruments and programming. The band has released a demo and the album "NS Satan" and now is already inactive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ecclesia+Satani">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
