1. ARGON (아르곤) is a Korean six-member boy group under MSH Entertainment. They made their debut on March 11, 2019 with their first single album "Master Key".


2. Argon is pioneering Finnish Synth-Pop band (duo) by  Seppo Parkkinen and Pekka Tolonen. They released 1 single (San Salvador) and 1 album (Kone Kertoo) in 1980-81.

3. Argon is a Malaysian brutal death metal band hailing from Johor Bharu.
