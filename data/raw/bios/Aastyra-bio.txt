Aastyra is the side project of Wolven Ancestry guitarist and composer Fraust. The album features artists from around the world, including vocalist Laurarena from the USA and guest composer Michael Koefman of Amidst Haze from New Zealand. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aastyra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
