Agroth is a one-man-project by Argi from Denmark. Agroth play black metal with elements from depressive black, ambient and doom metal. Argi are playing all the insturments and vocals, which includes guitar, bass, drums, keys and vocals. 

The one-man project began in the beginning of 2010. Agroth made the first demo called "Opstandelsen af Det Fiktive", which means "The resurrection of the Fictional"  in February 2010 and he was only 16 years old. The demo  was recorded in only two days. Agroth are still creating music and right know he's writing new material, which is more based on the doom and depressive black metal, but he also has the fast guitar riffs and blast beat

His influences are norwegian black metal and german depressive black metal. His biggest inspirations are Burzum and Wigrid from Germany.
