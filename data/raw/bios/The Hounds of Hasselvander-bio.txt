The Hounds of Hasselvander is Heavy/Doom metal.
Formed in USA, 2007.
THOH is one man band. 
Joe Hasselvander  - Vocals/Guitar/Bass/Drums (Live Vocals/Guitar)
Previous Live members are Gary Isom (drums) and  Katy Vigil (bass), Eric Cabana (bass), and Jean "Boomer' Gernier (drums)
Band have one full-length album, "The Hounds of Hasselvander"
