Intoxicum was founded in October 2006 by Mot, Pete, Benner (former Kolbenfresser) and Stefan (former Deadly Intoxication). Until December, 6 Tracks were written and, from December 22nd until December 30th, recorded in the workband's rehearsal room. The Demo-CD "Back For Hunting You" will be released in March 2007.
Since january 2007 the band has been working on new songs.

You see, not much to write about yet, but we will go on...

The line-up:
Mot: Rhythm Guitar/Growls/Screams
Stefan: Lead-Guitar
Pete:Bass/Grunts
