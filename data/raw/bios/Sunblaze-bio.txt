Sunblaze is a Progressive metal band from Germany. Their style is more specifically described as Progressive metal Orchestra. The band was founded in 1994. The EP Illuminating Heights was released in 1997. The band still exists today, but is currently inactive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sunblaze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
