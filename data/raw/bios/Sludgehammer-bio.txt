There are 2 artists with the name Sludgehammer.

1.The trio SLUDGEHAMMER was formed in 1989 in Pittsburgh. They played some kind of noisy post-hardcore. They disbanded in 1991. Then members went to Don Caballero, Storm & Stress, Karl Hendricks Trio and Thee Speaking Canaries.

SLUDGEHAMMER was: Karl Hendricks (guitar/vocals), Brian Welcker (bass) and Ian Williams (drums/vocals).

2.SludgeHammer is a grindcore/hardcore band based out of Gaithersburg, Md. Badly recorded songs can be heard at www.myspace.com/gaithersburgsludge

3. A one man band, formerly of the San Francisco Bay Area, currently of Brooklyn, New York.   http://www.myspace.com/sludgehammer
