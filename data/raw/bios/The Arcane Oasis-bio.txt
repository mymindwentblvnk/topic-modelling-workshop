The Arcane Oasis is a Progressive Melodic Death Metal/ Deathcore band from Kankakee, Illinois. The band has released a 3 song instrumental demo. They will record their debut EP entitled Celestial in Fall of 2010 and it will be released around spring of 2011. The band's vocalist on the Celesital EP (Gage Arseneau) quit and was replaced by former guitarist Nick Juarez. Nick was replaced by Kyle Snyder. The band broke up in 2011. Their EP is available for free download from this link. http://www.mediafire.com/?mzp5v1x4cyeumj7
