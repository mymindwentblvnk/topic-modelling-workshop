Maxeen was (2002-2008) an indie pop punk band formed in Los Angeles, California, branching out along the likenesses of Motion City Soundtrack and Limbeck.

The song "Please" featured on 2005's EA Title Burnout 3: Takedown, and was an instant success. The band have since gained a lot of popularity, and have started to make waves in the music world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maxeen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
