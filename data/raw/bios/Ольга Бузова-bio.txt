Olga Igorevna Buzova (Russian: Ольга Игоревна Бузова; born 20 January 1986) is a Russian television personality, model and singer. She was born January 20, 1986 in Leningrad, Russia. She is most known for appearing on the TV show Dom-2. In 2011 she began her musical career. In 2016 she released her debut single «Под звуки поцелуев».
