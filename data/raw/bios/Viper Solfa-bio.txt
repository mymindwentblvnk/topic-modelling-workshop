Ronny Thorsen (Trail of Tears, Tristania, Blood Red Throne, Scariot), Miriam "Sphinx" Renvåg (Ram-Zet, Eternal Tears of Sorrow), Morfeus (Mayhem, Limbonic Art, Dimension F3H), Endre Moe (Trail of Tears, Dimension F3H, Cutthroat) and Bjørn Dugstad Rønnow (Trail of Tears, Sphere) have joined forces under the banner VIPER SOLFA, bringing their diverse backgrounds together to create a new brand of extreme metal. The band is currently working on material for the debut album, for which the recording process will start shortly.
