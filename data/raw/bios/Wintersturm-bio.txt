2) German Black Metal act Wintersturm was founded in July of 2006 by David (G) and Patrick (G),soon to be joined by Felix (B).In February 2007, the lineup was completed by Daniel (D), who had to leave the band in April due to personal reasons. Merely a month later, the lineup was completed once more by Philip (D) and has existed in this constellation since. Wintersturm are now in the process of recording their first release, a six-track 30-minute demo laden with hatred and despair, which should be released in the second half of August.
