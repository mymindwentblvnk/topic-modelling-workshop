If there was one thing to describe A Hill To Die Upon, it would be a resistance to die. From the beginning, A Hill To Die Upon has made it a point to do, whatever that may be, the best they possibly can. Starting out as a two piece hardcore act and growing through over a dozen members and several genre leaps, A Hill To Die Upon became a Blackened Death Metal duo of two brothers that would not stop after any hardship that came their way. During this time, A Hill To Die Upon played with such bands as Crimson Moonlight, Frosthardr, Becoming the Archetype, War of Ages, Sotahuuto, Set Your Goals, Winds of Plague, Veil of Maya, Year of Desolation, Thine Eyes Bleed, and many other high type acts. Forward is the only direction and up is the only angle. A Hill To Die Upon is very excited about the future goals and the imminent hard work. But ever higher…
