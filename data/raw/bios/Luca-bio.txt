Born in Berkeley, California, LUCA is a singer-songwriter based in Japan.
Her work includes vocal performances on Ryuichi Sakamoto’s solo album “async”, as well as some recent collaborations with haruka nakamura. In September 2019, LUCA releases the album “Light Waves” in collaboration with Kyoto based singer-songwriter There is a fox. 
After collaborating with various artists, her latest solo album “Japanese old folk songs -  A bouquet of the meadow -“ was released in summer of 2020. 
LUCA currently resides in Kyoto after living in various places such as California, Denmark and Paris.
