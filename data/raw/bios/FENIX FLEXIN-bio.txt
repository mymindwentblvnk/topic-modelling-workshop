Fenix Rypinski, better known as Fenix Flexin, is an American rapper noted for being a lead member of the famous hip-hop collective, Shoreline Mafia, alongside, OhGeesy (Alejandro Coranza), Rob Vicious, and Master Kato. The group released their first mixtape in November 2017, titled “ShoreLineDoThatShit”. He together with the hip-hop group, embarked on the Still OTX tour in May 2018. Before forming the group, Rypinski released tracks such as Vice City, Tommy By Tha Gucci, Home Invasion and In Tha Field together with Ohgeesy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/FENIX+FLEXIN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
