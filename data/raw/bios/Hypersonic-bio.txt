There are several artists that go by the name Hypersonic:

1) Hypersonic is Yoni Salah, age 24. Yoni has a wide musical background from classic to rock, from soul to electro, and he is using elements of 70's psychedelic rock, 80's rock and pop, metal guitar riffs, tribal percussions with live guitars and vocals, all blend into a melting pot of Psy-Trance sounds.

Yoni Salah has released 1 album under the alias Hypersonic and 2 albums under the alias Psydrop.

2) Hypersonic was an alias used by Andre Jacobs & Danny Donnelly (Suburban Base) who released bleep techno in 1990.

3) Hypersonic is also an Italian Melodic Power Metal band formed back in 2006 and released their debut album "Fallen Melodies" in 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hypersonic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
