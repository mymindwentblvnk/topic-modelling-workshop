MIND ERASURE created in 2007 at Corfu, by Lampro, Dimitris and Peter. MIND ERASURE made a very strong debut by opening the show for ROTTING CHRIST in Corfu (EPTA TEXNWN TOPOS) and shared the stage with bands like Psycorepaths, Eden Demise, Tardive Dyskinisia and Palnet of Zeus. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mind+Erasure">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
