Mixel Pixel is a music/video band based in Brooklyn, New York.

Influences include (but not limited to) psych-folk music such as The Byrds, Pink Floyd, and Love, Lo-fi music such as Daniel Johnston, and Beck, and experimental electronic artists Bruce Haack and Laurie Anderson
