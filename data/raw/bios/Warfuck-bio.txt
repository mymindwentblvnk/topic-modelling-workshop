WARFUCK is a power duo grindcore band based in Lyon - France, born in august 2011 and influenced by 90s rock scene and swedish extreme music, we wanna play a fast and groovy music. For the first album "The Weak & The Wicked", drums have been recorded at the "Makhouse Studio", guitars & vocals have been recorded at the "Obnoxious Rehearsal Area", mixed & master by Jeremy Pachoud at "Papaschulz di Papatchouli Studio" and finally released on Feb. 2012, available on free download and soon on CD and vinyls. 

 LINE-UP 
 Nico (also in Obnoxious, Lovgun) on guitar, vocal, design & web
 Mak (also in Obnoxious, ex-Rising Terror) on drums, samples, videos & photos.
