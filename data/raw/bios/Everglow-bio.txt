There are two artists that go by Everglow.

1. EVERGLOW (에버글로우) is a South Korean girl group under Yuehua Entertainment. The group consists of six members: E:U, Sihyeon, Mia, Onda, Aisha and Yiren. They made their debut on March 18, 2019, with the album Arrival of Everglow.

2. Everglow was a pop duo from Newfoundland, Canada consisting of Gordon Huxter and Jon Pike. The band released their one and only album, Ocean Season, in 2015 and broke up soon afterwards. Jon Pike would go on to form BRDGS. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Everglow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
