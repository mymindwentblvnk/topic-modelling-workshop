Dunkell Reiter is a thrash/speed metal band from Brazil. They formed in 1996 but became inactive in 1998 until 2007. They were originally called Kriter in 1994, and changed their name to Dunkell Reiter which is misspelled German meaning "Dark Cavalier." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dunkell+Reiter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
