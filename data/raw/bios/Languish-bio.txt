LANGUISH is a grind/crust/death metal band featuring drummer Zack Hansen (NORTH) and guitarist Matt Mutterperl (NORTH) along with Sean Mears (Territory, Gatecreeper) on vocals. Eric Wagner (Gatecreeper, Sport Utility Vehicle, Chamber) joined the trio in early Fall 2014 on bass and vocals. 10-song LP "Extinction" was recorded in December 2014 by Ryan Bram (Sex Prisoner, The Beautiful Ones) at Homewrecker Studios in Tucson. Wagner left in early 2015 with Bram assuming bass duties. 

Sean Mears: vocals
Matt Mutterperl: guitar
Ryan Bram: bass 
Zack Hansen: drums
