A death metal quartet that hailed from Piława Górna, Poland.  It is not known when they formed.  Bloody Butcher's members were Piotr "Łysy" Dradrach (vocals, bass), Tomek Olszewski (guitars),  Wiesiek Zaraziński (guitars) and  Sebastian "Buła" Pasiek (drums).  They only released Under the Justice in 1994.  The year they disbanded is not known.  Sadly, the drummer they called "Bula" died at the age of 42 from complications of diabetes in 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BLOODY+BUTCHER">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
