Cu Dubh (pronounced Koo-Doo) consists of Elijah Woolcott and Mike McNutt on Highland Bagpipes and David Macejka on percussion and often includes featured performers.  Cu Dubh's first album, Order of the Owl, was released in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cu+Dubh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
