Depth Affect is an idm-band from France. The band is putting together Prefuse 73’s breakbeats, Isan’s electronic lullabies and Fennesz’ sound treatments. Depth Affect is creating a relevant, intelligent and dancing (in a wobbly way) work of art. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Depth+Affect">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
