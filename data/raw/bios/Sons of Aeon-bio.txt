Sons of Aeon is a melodic death metal band formed in Jyväskylä, Finland during 2010. The band features former Swallow the Sun drummer Pasi Pasanen, guitarists Tapio Vartiainen and Ghost Brigade's Wille Naukkarinen, Bone5 singer Tony Kaikkonen and Ghost Brigade bassist Tommi Kiviniemi. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sons+Of+Aeon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
