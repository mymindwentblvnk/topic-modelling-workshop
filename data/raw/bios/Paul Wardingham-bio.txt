An award-winning producer, session guitarist and world renowned guitar tutor, Paul has spent most of his career in the music industry behind the scenes.

The 2006 release of the critically acclaimed "Interactive", a collaboration with australian drummer Grant Collins, finally gained Paul some long overdue recognition as a guitar player. An album at the cutting edge of progressive metal fusion exploration, 'Interactive' demonstrated for the first time Paul's accomplished compositional skills and technical guitar wizardry.

Now, 2011. Paul unleashes his most powerful work to date. Bringing his futuristic metal style to the fore, 'Assimilate Regenerate' is sure to impress any fan of metal guitar legends like Jeff Loomis & John Petrucci, with it's mechanical metal grooves, soaring melodies and complex otherworldly solos.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paul+Wardingham">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
