Opposed is a young swiss band who makes music from Punk-Rock over Punk and Ska to Reggae.
The band was founded at 13. March 2001 in Horben near Frauenfeld in Switzerland.

For nearly 5 years Opposed had 5 bandmembers:
Vocals: Pedro Buzek
Guitars: Sebastian Janesch, Daniel Egli
Bass: Lars Regenscheit
Drums: Chritopher "Grizzly" Longthorn

Daniel Egli left the band in January 2006 cause of "personal problems" and so the other 4 guys goes on and on with their nice music!
