Novercalis is a Melodic Metal band hailing from Switzerland. Formed in july 2006, their music is influenced mainly by early In flames, Dark Tranquillity, Insomnium and Mors Principium Est.

Their first studio demo, called "A Night In Despair", was recorded during september 07 in studio Dan's Project. A second demo is scheduled for end of 2008, with a far more mature and agressiv sound.

As of january 2008, December Escape is the only member of the band.
