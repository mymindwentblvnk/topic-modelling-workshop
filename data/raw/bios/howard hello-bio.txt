Howard Hello is the work of Kenseth Thibideau and Marty Anderson. The band centers its focus on the blissful, linear contrasts between mood-defining ambient music and genre-bending pop perfection.

Along with Howard Hello, Kenseth is currently the bassist for Sleeping People and a member of Pinback. He was previously in Tarentel and math rock legends Rumah Sakit.

Marty was previously in Dilute, has worked with Lazarus and in 2005 he released two solo records ("High Road" and "Low Road") under the moniker Okay. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Howard+Hello">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
