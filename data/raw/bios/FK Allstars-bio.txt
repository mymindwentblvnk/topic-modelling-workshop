FK Allstars is an extension of the German Hip-Hop group Freundeskreis, made up of members of Freundeskreis and other individuals of the German Hip Hop scene (Sekou, Joy Denalane, Gentleman, Afrob, Brooke Russell). They toured extensively in Germany, Austria and Switzerland and released a double live album (En Directo). FK Allstars are primarily known for their political lyrics, in which they advocate equality and international understanding.
