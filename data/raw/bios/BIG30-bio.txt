Rodney Lamont Wright Jr. (born December 28, 1999), better known by his stage name BIG30, is an American rapper from Memphis, Tennessee. He featured on his childhood friend Pooh Shiesty's 2021 single "Neighbors", which peaked at number 51 on the Billboard Hot 100. Big30 released his debut mixtape, King of Killbranch, in September 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BIG30">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
