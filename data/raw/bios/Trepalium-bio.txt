Trepalium (sometimes written as 'TrepaliuM') are a death metal band with various influences formed in 2001 in Bressuire, France. Their first album, Through The Absurd, produced by Gojira's Joe Duplantier, was released in 2004. In February 2006 they released their second album, Alchemik Clockwork Of Disorder which is more groovy, jazzy and melodic than their previous debut album.
