There are two bands recording and releasing music as "Desolation Angels." 

1 is a metal band from London, started in the 1980s and recently revived
2 is an Americana band from Detroit, Michigan, founded in the early 2000s

1 - Desolation Angels formed in London in 1981, and later moved to Los Angeles, California. They are best known for their single Valhalla / Boadicea and their self titled full-length, though they also recorded an unreleased full-length titled While The Flames Still Burn. 

2 - Desolation Angels formed in Detroit, Michigan in 2001, and has released three albums of well received alt. country to date: High in Third Gear (2006), Hell or Highwater (2011) and GBS Sessions (2013) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desolation+Angels">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
