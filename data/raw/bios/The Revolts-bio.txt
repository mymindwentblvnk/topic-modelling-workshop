A band started by Jonathan Velasquez before meeting filmmaker Larry Clark and later appeared in his 2006 film, Wassup Rockers. The Revolts is no more and original members, Jonathan, Carlos and Eddie have sinced mutated their South Central sound into their new band, reVolt. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Revolts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
