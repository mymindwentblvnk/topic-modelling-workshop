Altars of Rebellion is a Melodic Black Metal band from Colombia. They debuted in 1998, on an Independent record label. They have released one demo and an album. In 2001 they released demo entitled "Fuerzas Ocultas" and in 2003 they released their first full length "Infernal Paradise" The lyrical theme is primarily pertaining to; Anti-Christianity.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Altars+Of+Rebellion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
