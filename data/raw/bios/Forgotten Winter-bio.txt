Forgotten Winter is a Portuguese based symphonic black metal band founded in 2003.

Lineup consists of Necrodementis on vocals and guitars and J.T. Fausto on drums, keyboards, bass, trumpet and clean vocals.

They have released so far:
2004. "Quest of Ancient Reigns and Memories of Old Ages" Demo;
2006. "Cold Mysteries and a Black River Lighted by Candles" Demo;
2007. "Pela Glória..." split with Blizzard Winter, Kraft and Nargash;
2008. "Infinitas Estruturas do Desconhecimento" EP;
2009. "Dialéctica Transcendental" full-lenght;
2013. "Origem da Inexistência" full-lenght.
2015. "Vinda" full-lenght.
