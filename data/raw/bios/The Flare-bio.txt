The Flare is a surf pop band consisting of Jocelyn and Emily from San Francisco. 
theflare.bandcamp.com

The Flare may also refer to: The FLARE was composed of guitarist SUGIZO (ex-LUNA SEA) and vocalist YUNA (ex-Lazy Knack). The duo was partly conceptual, with the idealistic aim of spreading positivity to the world. After releasing 4 singles, a DVD and an album they broke up (as announced in December of 2005).
