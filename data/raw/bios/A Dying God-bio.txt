Formed in Essex during the Summer of 2012, A Dying God draw influences from across the spectrum of metal, combining philosophical lyrics and concepts (as well as the odd rant and social commentary) with their own take on the enigmatic musical artform that is Black Metal, creating foreboding atmospheres and powerful riffs to form the "KVNT" which drives their style. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+Dying+God">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
