Degeneracy are a grindcore/pop metal band from Sydney Australia comprising of members from 28 Days and Hell City Glamours. Their debut album was recorded in 2002 but not pressed and released until 2007.
Jono - Bass
Simon - Vocals
Pete - Guitar
