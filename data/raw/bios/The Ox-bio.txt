The Ox is a solo artist/producer from Leamington Spa, UK whose first album  'Distant Shores' was released in 2008. His second album 'Island Life' was released in 2009 and was met with great critical aclaim on both sides of the Atlantic.

Music genre: downtempo, chillout, electronica, lounge, dance.

The Ox's real name is Paul Hogg and he is currently working on other projects with different artisits of varying musical genres.

The Ox's third album 'Bloodlines' is nearly finished and he hopes to release this in April 2011. Please check back here for updates.
