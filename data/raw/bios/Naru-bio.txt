Naru is Kang Kyung Tae (강경태), born in 1984. After seeing his cousin who had a band, he started to learn the guitar all by him-self. He wrote, composed and produced his first album 自家撞着 (자가당착, self-contradiction). he is also called 'Modern Genie'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Naru">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
