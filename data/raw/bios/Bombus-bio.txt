1) Bombus are a heavy metal band from Gothenburg, Sweden. Bombus initially signed to Mourningwood Recordings, releasing 3 singles and their debut album "Bombus" before signing to Century Media on 10 April 2013, releasing another single "Apparatus", on 6 May 2013, and their second studio album "The Poet and The Parrot" on 26 September 2013.
