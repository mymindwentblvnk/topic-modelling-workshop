Fyre was a Sacramento based rap trio that consisted of members G Rider, Mxlplx, and Swank Daddy. The group was active from 1998 to 2003. Their content on streaming includes Fyre XII, Fyre XIII, and Fyre XIV, however the remainder of their music is posted on their bandcamp page, run by swank [this excludes a few singles that have been music videos or featured in the short documentary about the group that was recorded]. Both G Rider and Swank Daddy have continued to release music, and Mxlplx formed Death Grips, adopting the name Ride (often incorrectly titled 'MC Ride') in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fyre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
