The Tie-Dye Quartet were a jazz & funk band active between 2005-2009 in the UK, formed by musicians at Aston University, in Birmingham, UK and with a range of musicians over the years.

They enjoyed a residency at The Yardbird in Birmingham and recorded a full-length feature, Somnesia, at Aeriel Recording Studios in Carmarthenshire.

The Tie-Dye Quartet was:

Ian MacGillivray (aka tiedyeina) - Bass, Guitars & Harmonica
Ju Wilkinson  - Vocals, Piano & Trumpet
Pete Ford - Drums & Percussion
Tim Miller - Guitars
