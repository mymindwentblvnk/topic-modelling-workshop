Born in London UK. Don E is a soul artist and song writer.  Has not had the praise or respect his undoubted talent suggests he should get.  Massive following from lovers of real UK soul.  His album Unbreakable was released in England in 1992, and contained some excellent tracks.  His voice and style are unique and unmistakeble.  Along with Omar Lye Fook, Loose Ends, Soul 2 Soul, Caron Wheeler to name a few,  Don-E  represents the finest in UK soul.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Don-E">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
