Dark Sphere is a Melodic Black Metal band from Slovenia, that was created after Gothmog and Kain left Temacnost. Dark Sphere is influenced by Philosophy of humanity,  Destruction. Dark Sphere's goal is to create an atmospheric form of art, which best represents the symbol of death.

Band lineup:
Jumbo - Vocals
Kain - Bass
Gothmog - Guitars and Mixing
