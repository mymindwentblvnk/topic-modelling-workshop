Gaurithoth was a Black Metal Band From Mikkeli Finland. Gaurithoth formed in 1997 and disbanded in 2007. Band Members Were Nevalainen - Vokals, Lahikainen - Vokals (also in Exit Wounds), Immonen - Guitar, Häkkinen - Guitar, Oranen - Bass (also in Förgjord & Njafar). 

Gaurithoth released 6 Cd's : (1998) Satanic Perverse Black Latex Metal, (2002) Huoravasara 2002 (in english Whorehammer 2002), (2003) Priesthunt, (2004) Huoravasara 7", (2005) Perverse, (2006) Gaurithoth
