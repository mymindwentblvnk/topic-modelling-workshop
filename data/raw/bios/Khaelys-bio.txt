Khaelys is a symphonic metal formed in Strasbourg, France.

Khaelys is:

Marionita Paige - vocals
Damien - guitars
Guillaume - guitars
Jonathan - samples / keyboards
Stephane - bass / vocals

Their first album, Darkest Hours, was released in 2009.
