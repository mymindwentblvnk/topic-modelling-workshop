Call Me Lightning's sound brings together the more frantic moments of The Talking Heads' early days with the freak-out riffs and wild vocal style of The Jesus Lizard. Throw in some lyric styling reminicent of The Minutemen and you have a powerful combination. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Call+Me+Lightning">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
