Highway Superstar is the state your heart is in when you unwrapped that brand new vinyl, wiped the dust off of an 8-bit cartridge, watched "Labyrinth" for the umpteenth time. A musical project formed by producer/synth-junkie Alex Karlinsky, aiming to reach out those who left a piece of their soul back in the eighties. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Highway+Superstar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
