Anointed is a Contemporary Christian music duo consisting of siblings Steve and Da'dra Crawford. Their musical style includes elements of R&B, funk, and piano ballads. The group has won seven Dove Awards, two Stellar Awards and three Grammy Award nominations.The group has also been featured on several Christian compilation albums such as Real Life Music, 1996 and WOW The 90s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anointed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
