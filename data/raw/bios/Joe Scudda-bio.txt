Rapper from Raleigh, North Carolina, USA. He actually moved there from Georgia in 1991. An affiliate of the Justus League since he met with acclaimed producer 9th of Wonder, Joe Scudda's name made a mainstream breakthrough with his feature on Little Brother's smash hit "Lovin It". The main ingredient in his music? As he says himself a "healthy love for shit talkin and fun."

Scudda names Boot Camp Clik, Outkast, Big L, and other artists from  1992 to 1997 as being major influences. His name comes from the word a friend of his used to describe something hot. He is signed to the Hall of Justus label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Joe+Scudda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
