Charlie Christ is a Death Metal band from USA, formed in 1993 with well-known guitarist/vocalist Erik Lindmark (Deeds of Flesh).

They have only 1 full-length named "Symphonies Of Blasphemy" that released by Hummingbird Recordings in 1994.

Line-up until they disband:

David Abbot - Bass
Jonathan Boyle - Guitar
Scott Carson - Drums
