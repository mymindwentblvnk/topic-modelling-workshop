Godfall was formed in 1993 in Jyväskylä.

Originally the band played thrash metal, but they then changed their sound to rocking, groovy death metal in the vein of Xysma, Pakeni and Paraxism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Godfall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
