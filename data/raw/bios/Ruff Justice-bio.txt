Not to be confused with plenty of bands called Rough Justice.

1) - Ruff Justice (Texas), in its many forms existed from 1992 until 1996 and toured all across the Midwest and northern United States. The band released three recordings over their four-year span; two in the U.S., Just Us (live), Wings of Desire, and one worldwide, No Justice/No Peace, before splitting-up in mid-1996. The band has reformed in May 2008.
