Nocticula is a Melodic Black Metal band from Ukraine. They were formed in 1997 and still persist to this day - an unsigned band. Two studio albums have been released; In 2000  their debut album "Obitel' Tsarstva Vechnoy Skorbi" and in 2006 "Venchanie So Smertyu", both of which are in Russian. Their lyrical themes are translated as; Romance, Fantasy and Vampiric-oriented themes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nocticula">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
