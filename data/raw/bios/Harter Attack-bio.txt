Harter Attack was a New Jersey based thrash metal band, that was founded by guitarist Rich Harter in 1987. Their only full-length album "Human Hell" was released in 1989 and featured Dan Lilker, Anthony Bramante and Glenn Evans of Nuclear Assault as guest musicians. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Harter+Attack">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
