Damned Grave are a primitive black metal/crust band from Kamloops, British Columbia in Canada. They have released two full lengths, Pure Fucking Hatred and Blaspheme Culte, and one extended player, The Eternal Sea.

The current line-up is:

Abbadon the Dispoiler of Christian Assholes - Vocals, Guitars
Nos Mafattu - Drums
Fiendrew Von Sodom Monger - Guitar
