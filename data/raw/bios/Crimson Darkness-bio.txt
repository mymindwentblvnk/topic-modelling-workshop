Crimson Darkness is a UK atmospheric black metal band from England. Formed in 1996, Crimson Darkness have since released two albums, two E.P's and a promo sampler. The most recent album is 'The Devourer of All'. The current line-up consists of just one member. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crimson+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
