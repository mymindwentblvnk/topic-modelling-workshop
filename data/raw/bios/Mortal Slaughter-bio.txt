Polish death metal band founded in 1988 in Pulawy, Poland. They released two demos and one full length album. Last known line-up was:

Dariusz Szymanek - Vocals, Bass
Wojciech Wojciechowski - Guitars
Pawel ``Buncol`` Boncik - Guitars
Tomasz Komorek - Drums

Discography:
1990 - Roots of Evil (demo)
1991 - Destiny (demo)
1993 - Lepers
