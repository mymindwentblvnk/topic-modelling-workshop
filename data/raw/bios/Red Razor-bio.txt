Thrash Metal Band formed in 2011 in Florianópolis, Santa Catarina, Brazil, heavily influenced by the Bay Area's legendary 80's thrash metal bands like Slayer, Metallica and Exodus, along with the German Thrash of Kreator, Destruction and Tankard.

Band members:
Fabrício Valle - Guitar (Lead/Rhythm) / Vocal
Felipe Ferreira - Guitar (Lead/Rhythm)
Gustavo Kretzer - Bass
Igor Thiesen - Drums
