Expyre is an American Metal Band from Minneapolis, MN formed in November 2004.

Expyre is a molten mix of thrash, hardcore & death metal.  They Played their first show on December 8, 2004 at the 7th St. Entry in First Ave. and have been gigging consistently ever since.  After years of honing their skills on stage, they released their full length debut "Deadfall of Autumn" on Expyre Empyre Music. Current Line-up: Pat Young - Vocals; Jesse Tolkkinen - Guitar; Austin Larson - Bass; Kevin Baab -Guitar; Ryan "Zeus" Smith - Drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Expyre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
