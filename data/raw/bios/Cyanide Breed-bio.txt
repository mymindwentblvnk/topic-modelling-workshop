Cyanide Breed were a progressive death metal band formed in 2000 at Valley Stream, New York.

Discography:
2002 Demo, 2002  
Perpetual Suffering / Cyanide Breed Split, 2003  
Redemption EP, 2004  
Shoutout to Satan Full-length, 2005 

They broke up in 2007
