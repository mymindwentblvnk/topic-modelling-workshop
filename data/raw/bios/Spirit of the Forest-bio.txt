'Spirit Of The Forest' is a Black Metal band hailing from Montreal/Canada.
Members are:
PHOBETOR (rythm guitars, vocals)
EKINOX (lead and rythm guitars, bass, keyboards, acoustic instruments, vocals)
AUSTER (drums)
LUCYBER (vocals)

They have 2 albums to their name so far titled 'A Brew Of Lightning And Terror' and 'Kingdom of Despair'.
