Eternal Deformity is a avant-garde/doom/melodic death metal band from Żory, Poland founded in 1993. They released 2 demos and 5 studio albums to date.

Discography:
1994 - Forgotten Distant Time
1996 - Promo 96
1997 - Nothing Lasts Forever
1999 - In the Abyss of Dreams... Furious Memories
2002 - The Serpent Design
2007 - Frozen Circus
2012 - The Beauty Of Chaos

 
Current line-up:
Przemysław Smyczek - guitar,
Przemysław Kajnat - bass, vocal,
Piotr Rokosz - keyboards,
Arkadiusz Szymuś - guitar,
Tymoteusz Ciastko - drums
