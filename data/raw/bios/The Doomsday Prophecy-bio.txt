The Doomsday Prophecy is a 5 piece metal band from northern New Jersey. The lineup includes members from “Gotham Road” (Featuring Ex-Misfits Vocalist Michale Graves), “Norman Bates”, and “Eminence of the Undead”. The Doomsday Prophecy is in the process of writing new material for their debut release, which has not yet been titled.
