There are multiple artists named Aili:

1. The Belgian-Japanese Aili is Aili Maruyama. She makes fresh electronic indie music in Japanese and English. Aili is currently making and releasing music with her friend Transistorcake under the moniker Aili x Transistorcake They released their debut Dansu EP in June 2021. 

2. The Japanese AILI is a pop and R&B singer, lyricist, composer and producer under Rhythm Zone, an avex trax sub-label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aili">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
