Kashgar is a black metal/death metal band from Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan.  They formed in 2014.  They currently consist of Alfer (guitars), Ars (guitars) and Warg (bass).  Past members include Blauth (vocals, drums), who was in the band from 2014-2017 and Alex (drums) , who was in the band from 2016-2017.  Kashgar writes songs about occult subject matter and folklore.  They are not signed to a label.
