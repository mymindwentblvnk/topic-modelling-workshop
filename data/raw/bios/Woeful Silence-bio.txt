Woeful Silence is a black metal artist from Winterthur, Switzerland. They have released two splits with Euphrasia and Swamp Flower Rhyme, as well as an EP released in October 2017 titled Shrouds of Decay. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Woeful+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
