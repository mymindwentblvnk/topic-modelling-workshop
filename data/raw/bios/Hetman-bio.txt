Polish heavy metal/hard rock band established in the late 80's. Influenced by acts like Deep Purple, Megadeth, Van Halen, Metallica. They recorded 6 albums in their career, two of them being very unusual: heavy metal interpretations of 1) Polish Christmas carols and 2) traditional Polish folk songs. Currently the band is promoting their new album - "Live" - which contains a new song called "Skazaniec" ("The sentenced"). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hetman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
