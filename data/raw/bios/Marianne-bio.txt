Marianne, pop singer from Mexico that released 3 albums under Sony Music. Although never achieving a proper hit per say, her most famous song is "Enciendo Una Vela" that was used on Mexican Television for various public service announcements as the song speaks of world peace.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marianne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
