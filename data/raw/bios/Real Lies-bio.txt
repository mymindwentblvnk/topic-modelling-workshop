There are several artists named Real Lies.

1) Real Lies are a band from Holloway, North London, formed in 2012 and signed to Marathon Artists / Sweet Exile. The band consists of Kev Kharas, Tom Watson and Pat King.

They released the tracks World Peace / Deeper in 2012 and 2013, North Circular / Dab Housing in 2014, and Seven Sisters in 2015. Their 11-track debut album, "Real Life", landed on October 16th of the same year, as they embarked on a 15 date UK tour including a Foals' November UK leg support slot.

http://reallies.co.uk/

2) Real Lies is a rock band formed in Szeged in 2005. A mixture of grunge, psychedelic and stoner tunes, flavoured with melodic singing, all in Hungarian. Their brand new LP "9" is out now!
