Cryptal Spectres are a black metal band from Slupsk, Poland, that formed in 2006. They released three rehearsal demos in 2007 (Walpurgia 2007, Samhain Night MMVII a.y.p.s., and December Fullmoon MMVII), a split with Styggmyr entitled Ancient Chthonic Union in 2008, and another demo entitled Night Fear from Ritual Krypt, later in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cryptal+Spectres">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
