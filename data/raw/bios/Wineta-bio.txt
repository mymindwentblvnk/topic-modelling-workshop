Wineta is a Polish Black Metal band. It was formed in Koszalin, Poland in 1995 by Black Wind, formerly of Wilkolak. In 1996, Wineta appeared on the second half of a split demo with Gromowladny - 'Barbarzyn Ski Mlot Wojny / Zanim Wstanie Dzien'. The first song off of 'Zanim Wstanie Dzien', "Wspomnienie" appeared on the compilation album "The Night And The Fog", a tribute to Underground Polish Black Metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wineta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
