Firasah is part of the latest batch of hopefuls emerging from the Southeast Asian Metal scene. Combining classic & traditional Heavy Metal music along with lyrics about their oriental culture, legends and philosophy, Firasah strives to create its unique imagery and sound in order to share it with the rest of the world.

Influenced by globally reknowned acts such as Iron Maiden, Deep Purple, Dio, Judas Priest, Grim Reaper, Megadeth, Loudness, Manowar, Scorpions, Rainbow, Europe, Riot, Helloween, Black Sabbath, Ozzy Osbourne, Running Wild, Seikima II, Stryper, Angel Witch, Kiss and the like, Firasah wishes to continue the legacy of Hard 'n' Heavy Rock music left on by local Malaysian acts such as  Crossfire, Search, Febians, Ekamatra, Babylon, Bloodshed, Sweet Charity, Rusty Blade, Lefthanded, Rockers, Junction, Gersang, Wings amongst others... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Firasah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
