(2005 - Present) Gyrdleah is a Black Metal band from Birmingham, England formed by Flagrum. Signed to Polish Death/Black Metal label "Via Nocturna", the latest release is "Passage Into The Night" 2017. This album was mixed and produced by "Chris Fielding" of the Stoner/Doom Metal band "Conan" whilst working at "Foel Studio" wales. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gyrdleah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
