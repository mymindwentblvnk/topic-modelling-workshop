Christian Löffler is an electronic music artist from Greifswald, Germany; and co-founder of Ki Records, who also records as A City Is A Tree .

Christian’s first released the 4-track EP “Heights” on Ki Records in March 2009. In 2012 he released his debut album “A Forest”, followed by “Young Alaska” in 2014; both of them re-released in early 2016.

In 2015 he released “York” on 20:20 Vision and “Lost”, through Just This.
