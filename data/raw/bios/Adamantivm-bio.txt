Excellent Spanish power/thrash metal formed in 2006 in Zaragoza, Spain. The music is made by :

Toño Monzón - Vocals 
Luis Carlos Gallego - Guitar
Juan Antonio Terrones - Guitar
Pako Muniente - Bass 
Miguel Andrés - Drums 

These guys only have one album , Adamantivm (2009), which is a very nice metal piece.
