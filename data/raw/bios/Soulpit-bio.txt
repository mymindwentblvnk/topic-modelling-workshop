Soulpit are a Death Metal band from Italy who formed in 2004. They released the 'Trapped Into The Soulpit' EP in 2006, and the debut album 'Substantial' was released in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soulpit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
