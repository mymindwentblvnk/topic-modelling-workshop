Despair's Tiny Things was a French death doom metal band from Arrasan, formed in the '90s. They released three demos: "First Exutory" in 1996, "Intrinsic Trip" in 1997 and "Third" also in 1997. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Despair%27s+Tiny+Things">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
