Nattlig Stjerne is a one man ambient/black metal project from Gorizia, Friuli-Venezia Giulia, Italy.

Bard Vind is the man behind the band, taking influence from bands like Ulver, Darkthrone, Burzum, Bathory, Immortal, Intemperiae, Nargothrond and Vael.

So far, four full length albums have been recorded, as well as two EP's, two demos and a split with Kelvar. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nattlig+Stjerne">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
