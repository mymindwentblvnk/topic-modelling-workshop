American death metal band formed in 1984 and split up in 1986. Their sound is similar to Possessed.
Their only release is a demo called Faces of Death, recorded & released in 1985.  Their demo and two rehearsals were released as a compilation Terminal Death released 2014 by Shadow Kingdom Records on CD and Hells Headbangers on vinyl.

Lineup:
Shaun Glass - Bass (Broken Hope)
Adan Contreras - Drums
Nick Stevens - Guitars
Ricky Aguayo - Guitars
