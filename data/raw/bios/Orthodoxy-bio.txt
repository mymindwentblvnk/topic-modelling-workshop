Orthodoxy are a death metal band from Valencia, Valencian Community, Spain. They released a debut demo in 2015 followed by their first LP,  Novus Lux Dominus in 2019 and a second LP,  Ater Ignis, in 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Orthodoxy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
