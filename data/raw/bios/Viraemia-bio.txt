Viraemia is a technical death metal band from Arizona, USA. They formed in early 2008 by Chad Eklund (Vocals),  Josh Hernandez  (Guitars), Scott Plummer (Bass) and Mike Collins  (Drums). They sound similar to bands such as Brain Drill, Beneath The Massacre, Necrophagist and Psyopus.

The band released self-titled EP in 2009 with Tony Martinez  now on vocals.
