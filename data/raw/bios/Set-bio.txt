SET is a black/death metal band from the United States that features C. Davis (AKA Drathrul) of such acts as Hour of 13, Anu, and Profane Grace.

SET is currently signed to NWN Productions.


SET is also the name of a hardcore/powerviolence influenced band from Frostburg, Maryland featuring ex and current members of Perfect Future and Dogjaw.
