Ferdinand Sarnitz, known by his stage name Left Boy is an Austrian rapper, singer and producer from Vienna, Austria born on December 17, 1988 in Vienna. In December 2010, Sarnitz released his first mixtape The Second Coming for free download. In mid-2011, he started making music videos for all of the songs. He has since become a signed artist and has released the album 'Permanent Midnight' which is available on iTunes, along with several singles. He cites Wu-Tang Clan, Oxmo Puccino, De La Soul, Atmosphere, Ugly Duckling, Daft Punk, Édith Piaf, Nina Simone, Oumou Sangaré, Gipsy Kings as his inspirations.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Left+Boy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
