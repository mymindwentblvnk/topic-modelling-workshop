From the remains of the two local bands Wolfmoon and Dark Wintermoon DAY OF DECAY were founded in  2003 in Giessen (Germany)  to play Death Metal with influences from other styles of metal.

The band consists of 
Kai Emmerich (vocals)
Christian Roll (guitars)
Anna Förster (guitars)
Jonas Jatsch (bass)
Karsten Szameitpreuksch (drums)

Founding member Frank Müller (guitar)left the band in 2005 , ever since the band only plays with one guitar. The band has shared stages with different local bands so far.

In August 2007 the first EP "Tortured Soul" with 5 tracks + one live track has been released.

Karsten and Jonas are also playing in the hardcore band  OUT FOR A KILL .
