At first, band was Ekvil's one-man project. First material was written in 2005. Reference point is spring of 2006 when Brutal Death band - Exorcism had split up, where Ekvil and Helvetius (present Atterum Ignis guitarist) played. In the end of 2007 young drummer Tantra has joined Atterum Ignis. Final member - bass player Zagrabadanasch has joined the band in 2008. In autumn 2008 demo - Black Fire Pandemonium has been recorded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atterum+Ignis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
