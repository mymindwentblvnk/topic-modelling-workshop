There are multiple artists that operate or operated under the name Virulent.

1. Death metal band from Utrecht, the Netherlands. They were active from 1998 until 2006 and released three EP's.
2. A self-proclaimed experimental deathcore band from Phoenix, Arizona that was active from 2011 until  2019. They released two albums and an EP.
3. A Japanese visual kei band, but their bandname is spelled differently, so any scrobbles for that band here are caused by incorrect tagging. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Virulent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
