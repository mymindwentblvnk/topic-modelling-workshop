Their early stuff was thrash metal and they evolved into a more groove metal sound.
Last known line-up:
Eric Peterson - bass/vocals,
Keith White - guitars,
Jeff Hutchinson - drums. 

Bassist Eric Peterson is not to be confused with the Testament guitarist of the same name.
Later changed their name to Blind Eye Open.
