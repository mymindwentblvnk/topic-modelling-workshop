Sepsis was a krautrock band from Russia. They released only one album called Liturgia Bezumia (Литургия Безумия). Not much else is known about them.

Sepsis is a brutal death metal band from Melnik, Czech Republic.

The band was established at the end of  2002 by band leader and bass player Jirka " Hami " Haramul with the help of guitarist Jiri Kůs and drummer Jana Spilkova. Guitarist and drummer Pavel Fiala joined the band later, but soon was replaced by Honza Pernicka on the  post of drummer and at the same time by Jirka Vataha on the post of guitarist. Approximately  one year later new guitarist Honza Tomandl joined the band.
