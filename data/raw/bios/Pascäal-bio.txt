Raised in Michigan and currently residing in Austin, Texas. Peter Wiley is a small-known electronic producer and creates music that is down-tempo, atmospheric and could be described as simplistic Chill Trap, Future Garage and Glo-Fi.  Wiley released his 2nd EP Fragile in 2013 under his artist name Pascäal, which features 5 songs that flow together into a very relaxed afternoon mix. Pascäal is currently signed to Vresh, a dutch alternative electronic label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pasc%C3%A4al">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
