Charles Smith, better known as Blaqstarr or DJ Blaqstarr, is a Baltimore, Maryland area rapper, singer, producer, and DJ. He is best known for his work with M.I.A. and for his association with Diplo's label, Mad Decent. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Blaqstarr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
