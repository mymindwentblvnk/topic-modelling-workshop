Formed in 2001 by Alexis Chatziioannou (guitar), Dimitris Kanellopoulos (bass/vocals), Stavros Paterakis (vocals) Thanos (drums) and Nektarios Pogkas (guitar/backing vocals).
Stavros and Thanos left the band after a while.
Sofia Sarri (vocals) and John Konstantas (drums) joined the band.
Recorded their first demo CD "Anatolia/All Alone" in 2002.
Recorded the self-financed and self-produced CD "F-Rattle" in 2004 and broke up shortly after.
