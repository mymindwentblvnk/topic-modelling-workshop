Formed in 2008, Nightfear is a Power/Heavy Metal band from Madrid - Spain. Members: Lorenzo Mutiozabal - Vocals, Ángel Fernández - Guitars, Ismael Retana - Guitars, Manuel Moreno - Bass, Osckar Bravo - Drums. Their discography consists in a debut full-length called 'Inception', released in 2012. Followed by the full-length 'Drums of War', dropped in 2015. Latest album entitled 'Apocalypse',  has been unveiled in Feb. 5th 2020 through the label Fighter Records.
