Napalm Unit is a deathgrind duo from Tulsa, Oklahoma, USA. They started out in 2014 releasing a debut demo entitled Intolerable Acts of Aggression.

Members of the duo are instrumentalist Josh Snider of Mortuary Science and vocalist Matt Dorr of Swampgoat, The Great He-Goat, Aske, Sledgehammer Autopsy, Death Inquisition, Autumn Reflections, and Satanic Destroyer 666.
