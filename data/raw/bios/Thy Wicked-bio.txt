Thy Wicked is a German Pagan Metal band, formed in 2000. Their lyrics are based on paganism and nordic mythology in general, the call their music "Midgard Pagan Metal".
In December 2004 they started recording their debut album "Wenn Heimdalls Horn erklingt..." and released their latest - the second one - in April 2009, called "Treu dem alten Weg".


Members:
Lars Kriegel -- vocals, bass
Sebastian Schwarz -- guitar, vocals
Wolfgang Serby -- guitar
Stephan Huprich -- drums
