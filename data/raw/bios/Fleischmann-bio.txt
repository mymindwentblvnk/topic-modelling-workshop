Fleischmann is a German band and was founded in Berlin circa 1989. With a style influenced by bands such as Voivod and Prong they went on to release four full-length albums and one self-financed single from 1991 to 1995. The band is currently inactive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fleischmann">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
