Naoto Ishida (石田尚人) is a Japanese composer best known in the West for writing two songs for the soundtrack of the Super Nintendo game F-Zero ("Big Blue" and "Ending Theme"). The remainder of the songs in the game were written by Yumiko Kanki (神吉由美子). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Naoto+Ishida">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
