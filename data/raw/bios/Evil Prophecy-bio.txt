Evil Prophecy was a German Thrash band that became active in 1986. They would release one demo called Proceeding Decay (Demo I - 1987) before changing their name to Inhuman Conditions the same year. As Inhuman Conditions, they'd release a bunch of albums and EPs (all which are available online) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evil+Prophecy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
