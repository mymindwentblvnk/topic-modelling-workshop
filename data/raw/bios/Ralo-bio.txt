Ralo (Terrell Davis) is an American hip hop recording artist from Atlanta, Georgia. He is signed to rapper Young Scooter's Black Migo Gang, Birdman's Cash Money Records, his own label Famerica Records and 1017 Eskimo Records. Ralo gained recognition following the release of his single, "Can't Lie" featuring rapper Future, which was released in 2015.  In 2017 he was the first artist to sign to Gucci Mane's label 1017 Eskimo Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ralo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
