Death Metal From Germany. Formed in 1991 under the name Decomposition. In 1994 they changed their name to Seirim. Their first full length was released in 1999 titled Empire of the Dead. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Seirim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
