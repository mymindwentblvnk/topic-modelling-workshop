Ariwo are a Cuban/Iranian four-piece focused on intersection between electronic and ancestral music.
The band brings together Iranian electronic composer POUYA EHSAEI and three of London’s most influential Cuban musicians; Irakere's Latin Grammy winning “percussion virtuoso”, HAMMADI VALDES; Sierra Maestra lead trumpeter, YELFRIS VALDES; and godfather of London’s Cuban scene, ORESTE NODA.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ariwo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
