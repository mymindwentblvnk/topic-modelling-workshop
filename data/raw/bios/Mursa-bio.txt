Founded in 2012, Mursa (MRSA) is a 3-piece Sludge, Stoner, Doom metal band from Portland Oregon. They have 2 releases to date, a 3 song demo in January 2013, and a full length titled MRSA in August of 2014. Both releases are available at http://mursa.bandcamp.com/album/mrsa. For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/mursaband
