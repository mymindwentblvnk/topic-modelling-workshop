Area 54 has been combining the sound of old-school and modern rock/metal since 1997.

Area 54 released three albums, No Visible Scars, Beckoning of the End and Bring Out Your Dead and an ep, Fear Inside.

Area 54 disbanded after a final show on September 21st 2007.
