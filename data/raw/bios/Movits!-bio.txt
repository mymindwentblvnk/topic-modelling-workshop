Swedish fusion between Jazz and Hip Hop

“Django guitar, windy street swing; music for both art directors and your mom” is how MOVITS! describe their sound. Well-known in Sweden but unheard of elsewhere, they fold together elements of 1930s big band swing, roma swing and rhythm & blues, then drop hiphop vocals on top for some serious energetic firepower. -Aurgasm <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Movits%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
