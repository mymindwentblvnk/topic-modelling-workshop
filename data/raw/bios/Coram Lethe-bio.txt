Technical death metal act from Firenze, Italy.  Obvious influences from bands such as Death, Dream Theater, Carcass, Cynic, etc.  Lineup: Erica Puddu - Vocals, Leonardo Fusi - Guitars, Francesco Miatto - Drums, Matteo Meucci - Bass, & Filippo Occhipinti (formerly in Dysthymia) - Guitars.

The 2009 album '...A Splendid Chaos' is the bands first album with female vocalist Erica Puddu.
