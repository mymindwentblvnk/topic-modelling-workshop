Haggatha is Doom Metal / Sludge band, formed 2007 in Vancouver, Canada. Acts such as Noothgrush or Burning Witch may instantly come to mind, in comparing both musical, and lyrical similarities of this painfully crushing breed of sludge. Presently, their discography consists of a self titled 7" EP (2009), and a self titled full-length 12" (2010). Haggatha has played and continues to play many shows and festivals, that which have only been in Canada, so far.

Line-up:

Braden - Guitar & Vocals
