Celesterre is a heavy metalband that also uses influences from black and doom metal. Try to imagine raw yet melodic vocals combined with threatening, black metal like music.
There is an overarching theme around the music of Celesterre and that is the greatness of nature compared to the futility of man. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Celesterre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
