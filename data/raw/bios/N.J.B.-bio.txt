N.J.B., earlier known as NIGHT JAMMING BAND hailed from Mönsterås, Sweden - their debut was the 7" single 'Soldier of Love / Run Away' recorded at Metrocord Studios and released privately in 1986 limited to 500 copies. In 1988 they recorded their full-lenght LP 'Heroes' at Danish Krajbjerg studios. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/N.J.B.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
