Astaarth is a black metal/folk act from Burgundy, France founded in 1996 and still remain active. Their lyrics are mostly about Burgundian history and nationalism.

Current line-up:

Lord L. Moloch - Vocals (Abbey of Darkness, Baphomet's Throne (Fra))
Lord Gondebaud - All instruments. (Baphomet's Throne (Fra))

So far, Astaarth has released the following:

- History of Our Yesterday's Pride (Demo), 1998  
- Golden Age of a Dead Empire (Full-length), 2002  
- Gloria Burgundia (Full-length), 2007
