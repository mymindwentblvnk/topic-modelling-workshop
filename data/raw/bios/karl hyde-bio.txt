Karl Hyde (born 10th May 1957) is currently one half of the world-renowned dance act Underworld. He has previously served as the lead vocalist & guitarist of 1980's synth-popper's Freur and also as a session musician in the late 80's/early 90's.

He is identifiable for his unique stream-of-conciousness lyrics for Underworld and also his dabbles in the art world with design company, Tomato. After over 35 years in the music business, Hyde released his debut solo album Edgeland on 22nd April 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Karl+Hyde">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
