Cold Moon, the Northern Californian-based quartet. Vocalist/guitarist Jack Sullivan, bassist Will Levy, guitarist Kevin Ambrose and Mike Ambrose debut their first EP, Rising: a collection of instrumental indie-rock songs that cull influences from legendary emo acts including American Football and icons like Wilco, as well as more analytical post-rock impulses.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cold+Moon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
