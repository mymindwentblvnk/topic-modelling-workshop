Gnarhval is a sludgy, psychedelic doom group from Chicago, originally formed by J. Franco and T. Roberts under the name Carthaginian Peace in 2011. The new name Gnarhval was decided on in the same year. Sean Thompson joined in the summer of 2012 on bass.

To date, they have recorded a demo (Owl, 2011) and an album of rehearsal jams (Saeculum Obscurum, 2013).

Members:
Juan Franco- Drums, Vocals
Tyler Roberts- Guitar, Vocals
Sean Thompson- Bass
