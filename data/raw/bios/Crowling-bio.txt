September 2012, Sly (Lead guitars - vocals) and Judas (Bass guitar - backing vocals) form together the musical project CROWLING !!! An Heavy Thrash Melodic Metal band !!!! They are accompanied with Fab on rythm guitar and Link on drums !!!

Their mission : create more songs and go on stage to see you headbang !!!!
For their fans, they decided to record their first EP « Sweet Bloody Pie » at the Deven Prod Studio with Loran Saulus. This EP will be mixed / masterised by Hk at the Vamacara Studio and will release on the 26th July 2014.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crowling">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
