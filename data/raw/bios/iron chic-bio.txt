Iron Chic is a melodic punk band from Huntington Station, New York started by former Latterman members Phil Douglas and (until 2009) Brian Crozier as well as members of: Small Arms Dealer, Jonesin', Get Bent and Capital. They have released records on Dead Broke Rekerds, Yo-Yo Records, Bridge Nine & now Side One Dummy.
