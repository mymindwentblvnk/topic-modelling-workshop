Maude Latour, 22, is an indie-pop artist from New York City. She currently studies philosophy and political science at Columbia University in NYC. 
After releasing her debut EP Starsick EP and a handful of singles, Latour signed to Warner Records. She stated on Instagram that she owns all her masters, a big win for a budding artist. Her first single under Warner Records, Clean, was released in August 2021, followed by her second EP Strangers Forever EP in October. In November of 2021, Maude announced her first headline tour, with dates around North America in early 2022. In January 2022, Maude began to officially tease her next era, presumably hinting at the release of her debut album. On February 18, 2022, Headphones  was released through Warner Records which serves as the lead single of her debut album. On April 8, 2022 Lola , the second single, was released, dedicated to Maude's close friend Lola. Maude's first full album is expected to be released in the second half of 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maude+Latour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
