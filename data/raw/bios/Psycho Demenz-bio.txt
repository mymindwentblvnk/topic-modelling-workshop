Thrash metal act from Japan. In '89, they released their first demo tape. In '92, the band was opening for Outrage on the "Visions on the Road" tour. In '94, Psycho Demenz appeared on the compilation CD "Extreme Hot Candy - The News From Far East Loud Scene" with their song "Trigger". Other bands on this compilation include Casbah, Garlic Boys, Displaced Person, ＤＯＯＭ, Shell Shock and more. A year later, in '95, a compilation VHS appeared, with live appearances from this tour. Psycho Demenz is currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psycho+Demenz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
