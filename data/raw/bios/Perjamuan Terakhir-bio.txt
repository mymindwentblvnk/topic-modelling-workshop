Perjamuan Terakhir (Eng: The Last Supper) is a version of Black Metal that they name GREGORIAN FAKIR BLACK METAL. They come from Jogjakarta, Central Java. In the local extreme metal scene, these guys known for their mystical ritualistic live performances. The lyrics are heavily-influenced by Javanese daily superstition and mythology.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Perjamuan+Terakhir">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
