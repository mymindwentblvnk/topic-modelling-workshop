Parched is a guitar duo formed by Eraldo Bernocchi and Davide Tiso, of Ephel Duath fame. 

If one were to try and summarize the aesthetics of Parched, one could say "Robert Fripp meets Ennio Morricone at a dinner organized by Ry Cooder" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parched">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
