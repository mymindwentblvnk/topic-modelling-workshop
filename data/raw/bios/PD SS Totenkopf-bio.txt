PD SS Totenkopf is a Pagan Metal band, formed 2002 in Belarus (Minsk) by Pan Daminik - vocals, and Voislav - guitar, bass, keyboards. Their lyrical content includes Patriotism and the Rebirth of Krywia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/PD+SS+Totenkopf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
