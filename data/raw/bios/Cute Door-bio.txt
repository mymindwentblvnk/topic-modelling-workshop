Cute Door is the Dani Arnica's New York based musical project. Their first work, Engagement, was released on July 18, 2016 through SoundCloud. Four years later, on July 17, 2020, Cute Door released independently their first album, Night School. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cute+Door">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
