N2Deep was a Chicano 'gangsta' rap group formed by James Trujillo (Jay Tee) and Timothy Lyons (TL) in Vallejo, CA. They were signed to Profile Records in the early '90s and remained an underground presence in Northern California for the remainder of the decade. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/N2DEEP">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
