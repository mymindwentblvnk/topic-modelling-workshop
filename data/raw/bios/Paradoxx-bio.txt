There is more than one Paradoxx on last.fm:

1. An Australian synthpop group.

2. A Chicagoan heavy metal band who issued one extremely rare 12" EP, 'Plan of Attak' (1985).  Future Manowar guitarist David Shankle joined them after the EP's release, & can be heard on their compilation track "Night Ryder".
