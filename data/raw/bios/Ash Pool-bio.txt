Ash Pool, based in New York, New York, came to life in 2005. The duo which plays black metal in a primitive, yet catchy and melancholic style is comprised of Dominick Fernow (Prurient, Vegas Martyrs, Taylor Bow, Roman Cross, Cold Cave, Vatican Shadow, among others) and Kris Lapke (Alberich, Northern Cross).

To date they have released the following:

First Taste of Power // Demo, 2005
Genital Tomb // Demo, 2006
Black Bondage in the North // EP, 2007
World Turns on its Hinge // Full-length, 2007
Saturn's Slave // EP, 2009
For Which He Plies the Lash // Full-length, 2010
Together They Serve to Define the Process // Demo, 2011
Cremation is Irreversible // EP, 2012
