There are three artists with the name Tush:

1) A disco, soul and house duo and live band from Toronto, Canada, formed by Kamilah Apong (vocals, keyboard) and Jamie Kidd (bass, synths, producer).

2) A Mexican two-piece band, formed by César Antonio Sandoval Herrera (vocals, guitar, keyboard) and Carlos Mario Pérez Talamantez (vocals, guitar).

3) A melodic metal band from Germany, formed by Hans Walter Germroth (vocals), Gerold Hirschler (guitar), Ralf Dietz (guitar), Andreas Eichelberger (bass) and Fred Hoffmann (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tush">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
