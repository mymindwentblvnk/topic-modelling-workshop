This death metal band hails from Kampen, Netherlands and they formed in the year 2007.   Sonja Schuringa in 2006 started up a band called Stone Squad in Utrecht, Netherlands.  After a line-up change, the band was then re-established under the name Dictated and was based in Kampen, Netherland.  Dictated currently consists of Yorick Keijzer (vocals),  Sonja Schuringa (guitars), Jessica Otten (guitars) and Koen Verstralen (bass).  The band writes about war, death and illness.  They are currently signed to Metal Blade Records.
