Blasphemophagher is a Black/Death band from Italy and comprises of:
Necrovomiterror
Atomic Incinerator of Necronuclear Collapse & Plutonium Winds
R.R. Unholy Bastard & Phosgene Wargas

The band is considered one of the favorites of the fans of the genre. They would release a Demo named Atomic Infested Carnage in 2003 which would be 10 songs long and another in 2004 by the name of Nuclear Hell Live - Destroying Rome comprising of 11 tracks.
The band would also do 4 splits which were released in 2004, 2005, 2006 and 2007. Unholy Underground Ritual, would be released in 2004 independently. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blasphemophagher">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
