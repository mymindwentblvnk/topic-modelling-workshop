Repudilation is a brutal death metal band from New York, New York.  They formed in the year 1994 under the name Mangled.  Their last known line-up was Brian Wishin (vocals, drums), Matty Bones (guitars) and Joe Reilly (bass).  Past members include Rich Turnbull (vocals) and Chris Matiuk (guitars).  They disbanded while recording a demo that was entitled Encrypted in Blasphemy and it was never completed.  They only managed to release a demo, a split with Disfigured and a compilation album with all of their recordings.  Their lyrical themes were decay and destruction.  Burning Dogma Records was the last record label they were signed to.  Matty Bones died on January 31, 2015.  Chris Matiuk died in 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Repudilation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
