French black metal band formed in 1991. The band split up after releasing two demos in 1992 (Noixificurc detrevni and Victory is Ours) and later become Baal. The band re-emerged in 2002 and released a split with Ankrismah titled Raw Rehears'hell. Then in 2008, the band once again re-emerged for a self-titled full length.
