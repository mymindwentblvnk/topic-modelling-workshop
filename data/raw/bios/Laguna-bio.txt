Laguna is the Italian DJ Cristiano Spiller, better known as spiller. Under this alias he released in 1997 the tracks "Spiller From Rio" & "Do It Easy". 

Laguna is an Italian eurodance act produced by Stefano Tonelli. It released the single "Crazy Night".

Laguna is a Dutch trance project in the early 2000's consisting of producers Erwin Spitsbaard, Tom Peters and Viktor van Ophuizen.
