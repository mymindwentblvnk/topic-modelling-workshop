Formerly known as Riggz Two Nice, Riggz, the Boston Native, is a singer, MC, and producer with soulful delivery, a grooving flow and clever lyrics about his ranging life experiences. Riggz has dropped two well received projects and several singles. His debut project "Business Casual" a joint effort with his producer, engineer, friend, and business partner, Juice God, was released in 2016. After a string of infectious singles, Riggz came back with the 2018 EP "Last Day of November." The "Last Day of November" EP got its name because the 30th of November is Riggz' birthday as well as the day that his grandmother passed away in 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Riggz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
