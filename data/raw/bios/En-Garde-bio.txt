En-Garde was a Greek Gothic Metal band with female and male operatic vocals. 
The band was formed in 1994. In 1995 they released "Negative Self Image" EP and in 1996 they released "Comedia De l'Arte" album. After that they disbanded. In 2003 a compilation including a cover of "Phantom of the opera", some unreleased songs and the songs of their first EP were released in an album called  "Forgotten Memories".

Line-up:

Dimitris - drums
Jordan - guitars (Sarcastic Terror (Grc), Nocturnal Death)
George - bass
