There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Lingam was a melodic black metal band from Olomouc, Czech Republic, founded in 2002. They released an album titled "Seized by Loved Mother" in 2005. Official site: http://www.lingam.mysteria.cz

2) Lingam was an avant-prog band from Veneto, Italy, formed by Sergio Cacherano Staropoli (vocals, guitars, drums), and former Spirosfera members Giorgio Brugnone (bass) and Michele Brieda (keyboards, synth). They released an album titled "Musiche per un film immaginario" in 1999, influenced by Henry Cow and Art Bears. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lingam">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
