Victima is a power metal band from germany. Until now they are playing some local gigs near their hometowns Emsdetten and Greven. They produced two albums: Passage Of Salvation and The Edge of Twilight. Victima are Frank Wardemann (vocals), Dirk Wardemann (keyboard, backing vocals), Benjamin Feldmann (base guitar), Daniel Strietholt (guitar), Markus Hinz (drums).
