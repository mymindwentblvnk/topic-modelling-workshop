He began to be interested in creating his own music in 2000.
He was carried away by trance sounding, ethnics, he experimented with different styles.
He was listening for the internal response and was searching for the special sound. From 2003 he has been writing soundtracks to order.
After acquaintance with so-called " NuSkool Breaks ", he has felt a fresh stream of ideas...
"In sound I appreciate space... The sound should give birth to some video sequence but at the same time it shouldn’t lose its dynamics. Space and certain futurity, in a combination with energy of movement - that is what I want to hear in my track's.” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Cataclysm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
