Hailing from Glen Rock, NJ, now living in Charlottesville, VA Andrew is currently playing shows along the east coast, sometimes with Family Portrait.  Andrew has distilled the sounds of the his genre, a rainy day at the beach sound recorded in some echo-y underground chamber.

He's released a 7" with Family Portrait and a collection of cassette tapes.  You can also find his track Ad Infinitum on "Underwater Peoples Winter Review". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Andrew+Cedermark">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
