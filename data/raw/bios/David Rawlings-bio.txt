David Todd Rawlings is a professional guitarist and singer. He is best known as the longtime musical partner of bluegrass singer-songwriter Gillian Welch. The Dave Rawlings Machine album "A Friend of a Friend" was released in 2009.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/David+Rawlings">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
