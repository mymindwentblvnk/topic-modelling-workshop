There are multiple artists named Destiny.

1) Destiny Chukunyere (born 29 August 2002 in Birkirkara, Malta) is a Maltese singer. She won the Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2015, where she represented Malta with the song "Not My Soul".
In 2017, she participated in the eleventh series of Britain's Got Talent and finished 6th in the second semi-final and thus was eliminated. She won the second season of the Maltese version of The X Factor and was chosen to represent Malta in the Eurovision Song Contest 2020 in Rotterdam with the song "All of My Love", before the contest was cancelled due to the 2019–present coronavirus pandemic. However, Destiny was selected to represent in the 2022 contest with the song “Je Me Casse” she finished 7th in the final.

2) DESTINY (데스티니) is a four-member girl group under FATE Entertainment. They debuted on September 28, 2018 with the single "Like A Bomb". The group is composed by 1 former Kiss&Cry member and 1 former WANNA.B member, being Bohye and J.C (under the name Jiwoo) and plus two more trainees, being Yooyeong and Sui.

3) Destiny were a metalcore band from Germany that formed in 1997. They released two albums under this name before becoming The Destiny Program in 2006.

4) A German death metal band. They released one demo ("Devotion") in 1995.

5) An Italian heavy metal band. They released one demo in the 80s.

6) A Polish thrash band formed in 1990.

7) A doom band from Slovakia. They released  one demo ("Sense") in 2003.

8) A US melodic heavy metal Band, formed in 1989, which released two demos.

9) Destiny is a heavy metal/progressive metal band from Gothenburg, Sweden, founded by Stefan Björnshög. In 1982, Destiny, one of Sweden's very first real heavy metal bands first saw the light of day. Early influences included Black Sabbath, Savatage and Metal Church.

10) A power metal band from Costa Rica, founded at the end of 2013 by Anton Darusso and Bryan S. Molina. Destiny's debut, 'TIME', was released in 2014. During that year Destiny signed with the German label Powerprog for a digital release of a special edition of 'TIME' that contained two bonus tracks, one with the participation of italian singer Roberto Tiranti.

11) Destiny Nicole Frasqueri is a Puerto Rican independent music artist based in New York.
