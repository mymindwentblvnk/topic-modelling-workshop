Before The Disengaged (ex. Metaphoris) is a four-membered extreme-metal band from Vantaa, Finland. The band consists of original members Timi Tonteri, Timo Salminen, Janne Valtonen and Niki Tonteri. The group began when Timi and Janne wanted to make heavy music. Instruments: Drums - Timi, bass guitar - Timo, guitar - Janne and vocals - Niki. The first Before The Disengaged (ex. Metaphoris) songs were heavy, but later on the music became a bit more  melodic, when the band made it's own "sound". Also, in the beginning, Niki's style of singing was deep growl, but later his style changed with the music to more clear growl and screaming. Before The Disengaged (ex. Metaphoris) has performed on Rockin' hours -gigs organized by the town of Vantaa, where the band got some performing experience and a handful of fans.
