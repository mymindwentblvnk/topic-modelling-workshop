Powergod is a german Speed Metal Band which are specialised on cover versions of great metal bands, such as Slayer, Metallica, Judas Priest a. o. . The band was formally formed out of Hama Hart (drums), Riff Randall (Guitar, Bass) and President Evil (vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Powergod">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
