1) Suzie is a funk pop project from Minneapolis, Minnesota, fronted by Mark Ritsema of Night Moves and featuring members of Carroll and Hot Freaks. Their first album, Born Single was released on September 4th, 2014, by No Problem Records.

2) Suzie, sixties pop singer, born 1946 as Martina Carina Peereboom, in Tilburg, The Netherlands.
Teenage singer who recorded in German (hits: Johnny Loves Me, Du gehst vorbei), Swedish, Dutch.
