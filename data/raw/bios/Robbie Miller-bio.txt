Robbie Miller is an Australian musician from Brisbane. He is a winner of the National Indigenous Music Awards (NIMAs) competition. For this he was flown to Darwin to perform at the NIMAs, alongside some of the best Indigenous artists in Australia.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Robbie+Miller">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
