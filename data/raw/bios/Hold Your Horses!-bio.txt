Hold Your Horses! is a French group of 7 members [Charles, Florence, Robin, Gregory, Gilles, Simon, Igor]. Their most famous song to date is 70 Million.

Starting as a duo in London, Florence and Charles moved to Paris after performing a few gigs.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hold+Your+Horses%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
