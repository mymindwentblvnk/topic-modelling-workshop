Evilthorn (Chinese: 恶刺) was formed in June 2001 by lead vocalist Li Chao (Chinese: 李超) and guitarist Nan Shan. Their songs are about war and modern warfare, genocide and historical massacres. 

Li Chao has another two other projects: 怨 & Zaliva-D
