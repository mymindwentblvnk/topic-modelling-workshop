Blasfema is a band featuring members of Protestant, Cold Blue Mountain, Amarok, Surrogate, and The Makai, with members in Chico, CA, Groom Lake, NV, and Milwaukee, WI. 

The first Blasfema record was recorded with Chris Keene at Cutters Cathedral in Chico, CA in 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blasfema">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
