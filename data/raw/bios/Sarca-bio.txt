Sarca is a pop/rock girl band from Hyvinkää, Finland. Band was formed in 2002 - previously band was named invisibles, until Amanda saw a dream in 2008 where band was called sarca. 
"Kesäbiisi" single is set to release 7.5.2012 and new album in the autumn.

Under this name, band has released two ep's (Kaksio, 2009 & 356, 2010) Videos were made for the songs "Kaksio" & "Kahleet"

Lineup:
Ama - Guitar
Axu - Bass
Tuuli-Aurora - Vocals
Vilma - Keyboards
