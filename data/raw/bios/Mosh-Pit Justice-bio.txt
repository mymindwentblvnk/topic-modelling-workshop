Power/thrash metal band from Burgas, Bulgaria. Formed in 2012 by bassist Mariyan Georgiev, who would be joined later on by Staffa and Georgy Peichev.

Band members:
Mariyan Georgiev: Bass, Vocals (backing)
Staffa: Guitars, Drums, Vocals (backing)
