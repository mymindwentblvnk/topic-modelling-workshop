Neon Warship is a stoner rock/metal band hailing from Dayton, OH  formed in 2011.


Consistings of ex-members of Twelve Tribes and Waking Kills the Dream: Guitarist/Vocalist Kevin Schindel (also currently in Mouth of the Architect) ,  Bassist Matt Tackett and drummer J-Bird Smith.
