There are a few artists called Stalin, not to be confused with the band called Stalin!, 

1 - The band Michiro Endo formed years after "The Stalin" broke up. This was Michiros band in 1989 and through the early nineties.

2 - Finnish punk band Stalin was formed by some really young guys, something between 13-14 years old. They made only one record, 7″ EP simply named ‘Chaos’. Some of bands members later formed hardcore band Nukketeatteri.

3 - Stalin is the name of a german punk rock band.
