The band was founded in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, in beggining of 2012 by Borislav and Tihomir, together with the bass payer Cvetan. Then, a few months later, Todor joined the group and they started working on their own songs.
On April 15th 2014 the band released its first album called NOT IN THIS LIFETIME.
In April 2015 the band announced its new vocal CoBy Rosen; the old one Todor deciding to leave for personal reasons.
December 2016 was the month when the new album - REBORN LP was released.
