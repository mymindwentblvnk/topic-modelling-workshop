With their origins in the defunct Cult Of Catharsis, Deathcon rises renewed with the punishing 'Monotremata'. Their lineup consisting of Aeternus, Helheim, Grimfist and ex-Taake members, Deathcon is a brutal death metal band who take their influences from the heaviest and darkest death and thrash metal bands from the '80s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathcon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
