Roma Radz is a London-based independent artist closely related to the hyper pop scene. She first made herself known by touring with Hannah Diamond in 2019 and 2020. In May 2020, she released her debut single ‘Boyfriend in Every City’.

Roma Radz is a musical project created by multidisciplinary artist Roma Radziunaite. Whilst studying Fashion at University, Radziunaite experimented with the medias of art direction, video, photography and styling. Her love for fashion has influenced the aesthetic and style of her pop star persona, Roma Radz.

Influenced by her peers Hannah Diamond and Alexis Knox, Roma has managed to forge a name for herself within the creative scene, and continues to grow her following whilst also learning more about her own practice and career path.

Roma Radz is going to be a continuous project and is looking to release more singles and a debut EP before the end of the year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Roma+Radz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
