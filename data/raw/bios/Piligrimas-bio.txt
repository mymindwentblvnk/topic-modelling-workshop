One of the first bands on the Lithuanian metal scene, formed in 1981. 

Because of the political system at the time they never had a chance to record a proper album, and so very few recordings of their music exist. 

Not very long time ago Piligrimas reunited and they're active now. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Piligrimas">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
