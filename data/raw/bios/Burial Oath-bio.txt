Burial Oath is a Blackmetal band founded in Cleveland Ohio by Lord Malsumis, Sean Deth, and Lord Orpheus in 2016, Driven by more of a  Scandinavian style of black metal like bands such as Watain, Taake, and Mayhem, taken forms lyrical from death rituals and occult phenomenon within Satanism. Burial Oath  is recommended for listeners of Mgla, Watain, Valkyrja, Taake, Uada and Mayhem <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burial+Oath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
