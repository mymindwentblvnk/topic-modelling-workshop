Formed by members of Krack Sabbat, Apostles of Grief and Muuun. Space God Ritual was started in 2010. Space God Ritual play traditional bluesy doom and are Brendan Butler- All instruments; Alexander Olaff- Vocals.
Founding singer Lee Waldron was replaced by Alexander Olaff.
