... is a grindcore outfit playing out of Gianyar (Indonesia) since 2007. The band has released their debut full length via "No Label Records" in 2011 following a self-released  EP entitled "Demography Berdarah" in 2007. Its grindcore has a particular high technical level, good sound production and some influences such as: death metal, hardcore or crust. About lyrical themes, they use to be related to social issues, politics, ecologism, personal feelings, etc.
