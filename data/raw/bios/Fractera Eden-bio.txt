"Fractera Eden" is a Metal Band from Bremen, Germany and was founded in November 2007 by the two Lead Guitarists "Lord Otz" and "Graf Rhytmus", together with the drummer "Grooveminister". The Bands influences are newer Metal Bands like Killswitch Engage, Volbeat and Rammstein as well as more experimental bands like Opeth.
Other band members are the lead singer "Freiherr von Goth" and "Dr. Ohm", which came to the band in December 2007 and January 2008.

The band tries to offer a entertaining and breathtaking show, while playing live and are always endeavoured to write and present new songs. 

They presented their first demo "Fractera Eden" in April 2008 and are willing to go into the studio and record an album.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fractera+Eden">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
