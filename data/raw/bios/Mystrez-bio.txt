Thrash metal act from Wierden, Overijssel, the Netherlands, formed in 1988. After releasing their first demo tape in 1989, entitled "Delirious Desert", the band's debut album "The Indictment" followed a year later through Bad Taste Records. Another demo was done in 1994. After some time, the band name changed to Change of Seasons, but eventually changed back to Mystrez in the late 90's. Their second studio album was released in 1999, entitled "Out of the Blue". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mystrez">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
