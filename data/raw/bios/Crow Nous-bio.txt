Crow Nous is the one-man project of a person who goes by the moniker, Corvus de Mortuus. It is punk and classical inspired Extreme Metal based in Miami, Florida. Influences include Darkthrone, Emperor, Death, Massacre, Benediction, Bolt Thrower, Celtic Frost/Hellhammer, Sepultura (1984-1992), Hellbastard, Sacrilege, Amebix, Black Flag, Black Sabbath among many others.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crow+Nous">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
