Black/Death Metal band from Queretaro, Mexico.
Formed in 2000, as a side project of Ravager. Involves all of the same members, minus the bassist.

Discography:
Hellbirth (split with Domain) -- [American Lime Productions, 2000]
rehearsal tape 01                   -- [self-released, 2001]
Demonized                               --[Osmose Productions, 2003]

Demonized were initially signed to Evil Vengeance Records (label of Gene of Angelcorpse) to record the debut album, but that offer fell through when the label shut down. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demonized">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
