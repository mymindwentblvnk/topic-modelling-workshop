There are multiple artists with this name:

1. Sacrament is a Christian thrash / death metal band from Pennsylvania, US. They released two albums, Testimony of Apocalypse in 1990 and Haunts of Violence in 1992. After disanding, Mike DiDonato (guitars) and Erik Ney (bass) went on to form a new band called Fountain of Tears.

2. Sacrament is a Death metal band from Christchurch, New Zealand. The band formed in early 2005 as a straight up death metal band. After months of writing and doing small gigs the band began to develop a sound of their own, crossing the genres death and black metal. They currently play as a three piece consisting of drums, two guitars and no bass.

3. Sacrament is a Thrash metal band from São João Del Rei, in the state of Minas Gerais, Brazil. The band was formed in 2006 by Guitarist Felipe Mantuanelli. The band, with a current new line-up released an EP called Face of Lie including 3 songs recorded on studio sessions and they are working on new songs for a full album release.
