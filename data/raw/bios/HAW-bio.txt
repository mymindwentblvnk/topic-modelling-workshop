Haw is a Hungarian band formed in October 2008 by band members of Stereochrist and Igor, who claim to be "countrymen living in the capital". Musically the band can be described with such tags as southern metal, doom metal, sludge, blues metal. Their main influences are Black Sabbath, Down, Led Zeppelin, Crowbar, Eyehategod, Corrosion Of Conformity, Kyuss, Mastodon..... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Haw">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
