There is more than one artist with this name:

1. Lochness is the first only female black metal band in the  history of Czechoslovakia (now Czech republic and Slovakia). Founded in 1989 in Brandýs nad Labem, Czechoslovakia and split in 1991.
They recorded only one demo Strašidla z Canterwille (Ghosts from Canterville) with only one song recorded in studio for the Death Metal Session  sampler - Zduř péro kuř

2. Stoner/doom metal from Montreal, Québec, Canada
