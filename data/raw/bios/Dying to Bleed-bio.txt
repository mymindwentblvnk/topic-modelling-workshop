Dying to Bleed is a melodic death metal band from Port Angeles, WA. They often play shows around the Seattle area. In 2008 they signed with Black Sheep Records and released their first full-length album 'The Decaying Process'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dying+to+Bleed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
