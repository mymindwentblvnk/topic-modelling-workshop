Antifuchs ("Anti fox") is a German female rapper with Russian roots. In 2015 she released her first EP called "Willkommen im Fuxxxbau". Her debut album "Stola" has been released in March 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antifuchs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
