A death metal band from Stockholm, Sweden. Formed in 2010; not to be confused with Putrefied, which is a band from the Netherlands.

Have released a demo; EP and two albums so far: Demo "The Return... of Swedish Old-School Death Metal" in 2010, EP "Whore House Tales" in 2011, first full length album "Spawn of the Dead" in 2011 & second full length album "Neurotic Necrotic" in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Putrified">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
