Allan Kyariga (born January 6, 1994), better known by his stage name Allan Kingdom, is a Canadian rapper and record producer based in Saint Paul, Minnesota. He is a member of Thestand4rd along with Spooky Black, Bobby Raps, and Psymun. He was featured on Kanye West's single "All Day". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Allan+Kingdom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
