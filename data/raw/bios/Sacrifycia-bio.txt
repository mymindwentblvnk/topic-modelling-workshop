Sacrifycia is a black metal band based in Montreal, Canada. The band members are Marquis de Blutmyth on vocals and rythmic guitar, Nekrosis on backing vocals and keyboard, Trepas on lead guitar and Pat on the bass. For the time being, Marquis de Blutmyth also records all the drumming, but the band is currently looking for a drummer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sacrifycia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
