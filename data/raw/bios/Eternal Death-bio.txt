Eternal Death are a male/female duo from Stockholm, Sweden formed in 2013. They consist of vocalist Elin Berlin and instrumentalist Johan Angergård (of Acid House Kings, Club 8 and Pallers amongst others).

They release music via Angergård's own label, Labrador Records. The 12 track self-titled debut album was released on 24th February 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eternal+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
