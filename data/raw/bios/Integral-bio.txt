There are multiple artists called Integral.

1. Integral are a German ambient/IDM duo consisting of David Rotter and Rafael Milatz.

2. Integral are a technical/melodic death metal band from Bergamo, Italy. 

3. Integral were a 1970s rock band from Sarajevo.

4. Integral is a member of the Dubfreaks  group/label.
