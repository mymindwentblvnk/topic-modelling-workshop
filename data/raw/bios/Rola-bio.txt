2) Rola Madirose Hinterbichler (b. February 12, 1990) is a German R&B singer of Ghanian and Lebanese descent. From 2005 to 2010 she sang in the trio Sistanova. Since then, she's active a solo artist. She released her debut album 12:12 in 2018. VENUS followed in 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rola">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
