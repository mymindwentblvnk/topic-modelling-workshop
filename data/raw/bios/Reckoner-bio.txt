There are two bands named Reckoner.

1.) Reckoner is a five-piece progressive metal band from Vancouver, Canada.  Since 2007, they have released an EP, played countless shows and developed a strong local following.  They recently recorded their debut album at Armoury Studios with producer Shaun Thingvold (Strapping Young Lad, Lamb Of God, Misery Signals.)  Find the album on iTunes.
