Sepulchral Aura is a finnish one-man black/death metal band that started out in the spring of 2007 from the ashes of a death metal band Deathmantra.

A 4-song pro-CD demo has been released by Ahdistuksen Aihio Productions. A sample song is available at their homepage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sepulchral+Aura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
