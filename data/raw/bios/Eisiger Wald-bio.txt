Solo-project from Black-Wood-Majesty, who's known for (or part of) a variety of other projects including Défaillance, Ulfang and Glaser. Under the name of Eisiger Wald, only one demo has been recorded with the name ''Sur le chemin de la délivrance''. It was released on tape in 2005, limited to 100 copies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eisiger+Wald">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
