Born in Fresno, California, and being one of the few Thrash bands in the Central Valley,  Originally known as Archaic Destroyer. Atomic Violence was started by guitarist Alfred Lopez and drummer Johnny Valles.

Atomic Violence consists of

Johnny Valles: Drums, Vocals
Alfred Lopez: Lead/Rhythm Guitar
Adam Camara: Lead/Rhythm Guitar
Cody Anderson: Bass
Jake Wilcken: Vocals
