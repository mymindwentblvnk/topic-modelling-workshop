Black Metal two-piece from Grodno, Belarus formed in 2007 by members known as "Igar" who handles stringed instruments as well as "Vietraslau" performing vocals & folk instruments. The musical style is heavily rooted in raw black metal and atmospheric black metal. Gnieu is driven by Pagan mythology, Nature, and the esoteric Cosmos.
