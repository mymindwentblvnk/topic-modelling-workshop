Solarfall (Bulgaria) - is one-man (Hadelfar) band that used to play traditions of ambient-ladden funeral doom metal, formed in 2003. It released a demo titled "In Depths And Despair" and a full-length called "Autumn Came with the Sunset" and after a hiatus between 2007 - 2016 was transformed into an ambient trance / psydub influenced minimal techno project, with two EPs "In Orbit" and "Helium" so far.
