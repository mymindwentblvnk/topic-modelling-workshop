SABINA, a stage pseudonym of Sabina Karwala, an independent artist from Poland who made her debut at the beginning of 2018, presenting her first single entitled "Kołysanka". This track preceded a live session of three original songs, showcasing the artist with her band.

Sabina has a background in acting. She graduated from the Film School in Łódź and until recently performed mainly on theatre stages. She is also a finalist of the 36th Review of Actor Songs in Wrocław.

On 27 June 2018. SABINA's first official video for the song "Panowie w kapeluszach" was premiered, perfectly combining the artist's two closest worlds: music and film.

Intensive work on the artist's debut album took nearly 2 years and resulted in material rich in diverse sounds. The album is called "Lepidoptera" and the UPNDWN producer duo is responsible for the music production. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sabina">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
