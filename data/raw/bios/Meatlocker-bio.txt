While travelling throughout the UK, members of Extortion, Hitlist and Ultimatum felt the urge to start a hardcore punk band. Not long after forming they enlist one of the UKs most notorious and violent hardcore bands frontman - Voorhees vocalist Lecky. They soon recorded 12 tracks at Mutiny studios in Bradford for their debut EP on Trial And Error. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Meatlocker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
