Amaranthine Trampler is a one man Funeral Doom project from the United Kingdom. Lee Paxton (the only member of the band) also has another Funeral Doom/Death project called 'Rot In Despair'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amaranthine+Trampler">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
