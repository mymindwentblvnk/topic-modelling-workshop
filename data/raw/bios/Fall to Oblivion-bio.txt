Fall To Oblivion are a Metal band from Essex, England. Formed in mid-2010, they have set out to create a unique sound bands dont usually partake but yet keep a raw sound that every metal fan loves. With a combined sound of Killswitch Engage, Parkway Drive, August Burns Red, Lamb Of God, Opeth and many more - Fall To Oblivion are set to climb the ladder in the local music scene and then take on the United Kingdom.
