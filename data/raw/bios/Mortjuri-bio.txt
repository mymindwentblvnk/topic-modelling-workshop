Mortjuri is a german Black/Death Metal band from Jena/Thuringia. 
The Band was founded in 2005 and had it's first gig at silvester 2005/2006. In May 2005 the first Promo-CD called "Desoulate" was recorded.
The Band plays a mix of Melodic Black Metal with Death Metal elements.
