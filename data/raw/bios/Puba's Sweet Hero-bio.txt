A deathcore / death metal band from Sidoarjo, Indonesia.  They formed in the year 2010.  They consist of Jeff (vocals), Poenky (vocals), Ojan (guitars, rhythm), Pecuz (guitars, lead), Tjend (bass) and Filsa (drums).  Their first release is an EP entitled Dominion.  It was released in the year 2013.  Their music is found on their ReverbNation page and their SoundCloud page.  For more info, go to their Facebook (page) and their Facebook (profile).  They also have an Instagram and Twitter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Puba%27s+Sweet+Hero">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
