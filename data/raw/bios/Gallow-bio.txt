formed in 2002, under the vision of hidaka (BEAT CRUSADERS) & kubota (BEAT CRUSADERS/kuh/ex-popcatcher) to play "21st century Neo-Acoustic". iwahara (ex-BROKENSPACE/ex-BEAT CRUSADERS/ex-group tamashii) & kato (BEAT CRUSADERS/ex-FUNSIDE) joined the band instead of kubota in 2003, trying to mixture black music & white music with asian feelings. released 1st album "PARKEST!" on 2006, kato leaves the band on 2007, released 2nd album "TOO VIRGIN!!" on 2008, so far. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gallow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
