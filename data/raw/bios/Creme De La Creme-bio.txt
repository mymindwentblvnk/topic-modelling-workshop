Creme de la Creme is a hip hop group from Cologne.

Lyrically, many of their songs are about smoking and taking drugs. Musically, their songs are influenced by hip hop and reggae. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Creme+De+La+Creme">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
