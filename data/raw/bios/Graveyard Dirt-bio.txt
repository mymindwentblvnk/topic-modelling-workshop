Graveyard Dirt was formed back in 1994. They recorded a 3 track demo in '95 and signed to 'Ars Metalli' 96/97. Some time after this they broke up reforming years later. In 2007 they released a 3 track E.P titled: "Shadows of old Ghosts". They are composing and rehearsing new material.

Current Members:

Paul Leyden - Vocals (The Ghost of Genevieve)
Kieran O'Toole - Guitars (Lucifyre)
David Reilly - Guitars (The Ghost of Genevieve)
Gary Sweeney - Drums
