Subsenix is the combination of 2 Artists.  Ben Sage and Subsonik. They are well known from their works on Subsonik Sound Recordings. Their style consists of very melodic motives and usualy includes vocals (more female than male). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Subsenix">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
