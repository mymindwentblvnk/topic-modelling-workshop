Formed in 2006, Disaster’s Gates is a Melodic Death Metal band from Quebec, Canada. After a few modifications from its original line up, the band is now formed of Stephan Provost (Guitar/Vocals and main composer), Dave Pellerin (Guitar), Fannie Martin (Keyboard), Phil Lamarche (Bass) and Phil Goulet (Drums). Disaster’s Gates know has their first record “Beyond The Gates”, released in Febuary 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disaster%27s+Gates">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
