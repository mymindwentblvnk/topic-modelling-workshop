Carolyn Mark is a Canadian who writes and sings alternative country pieces. She is known for her onstage antics and powerful voice.  She has recorded both as a solo artist and as a member of the duo The Corn Sisters with American colleague Neko Case. Previous bands she has been in also include The Vinaigrettes, Show Business Giants, the Fixin's, and the Metronome Cowboys. She has also provided backing vocals on recordings by The Buttless Chaps, Greenfield Main, Neko Case and Frog Eyes. 

In 2009 she co-released Let's Just Stay Here with Toronto's NQ Arbuckle (as Carolyn Mark & NQ Arbuckle). The album was nominated for a 2010 Juno Award, in the Roots & Traditional Album category.[4] The album was also one of PopMatters' top 10 album in their Best Country Music of 2009 list.
