Mental Reborn is Melodic Death/Thrash Metal band, formed in Veliko Turnovo, Bulgaria in 2008. After the split-up of the band Final Flame, most of the members formed Mental Reborn. The band recorded their firs demo in the begining of 2009 which included the song Slaves of greed. 
Official MySpace: http://www.myspace.com/mentalreborn
Members:
- Svetoslav Lazarov – Guitar
- Hristomir Subchev – Drums
- Stefan "Chefo" Stefanov - Guitar 
- Ivailo "Asimo" Petrov - Vocals
