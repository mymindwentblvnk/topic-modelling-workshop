Smosh is an internet comedy duo consisting of Ian Andrew Hecox (born November 30, 1987) and Anthony Padilla (born September 16, 1987). Smosh was founded in 2002 in Sacramento, California but their YouTube-career started in 2005. Ever since the song "We're Gangstaz" Anthony and Ian have continued in making music along with videos posted on YouTube and smosh.com or youtube.com/Smosh. 

Smosh music is available for download on their website at www.smosh.com/videos or on their music Myspace page at www.myspace.com/officialsmoshmusic.

They also have teamed up with I Set My Friends On Fire to make such songs as Four Years Foreplay and Sex Ed Rocks.
