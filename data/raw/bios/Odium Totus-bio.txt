Formed in 2011 by Dyingnysus (Gravecode Nebula), Lupericus Infernale (Gravecode Nebula) and R. Sodomizer (Iconoclast Contra), soon joined by guitarist Khate shortly after.Odium Totus are a traditional black metal band in the vein of Ulver, Ved Buens Ende, Darkthrone (90-95 era), Katharsis, Deathspell Omega and others. Music is coming soon! Keep an eye out for records and shows in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Odium+Totus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
