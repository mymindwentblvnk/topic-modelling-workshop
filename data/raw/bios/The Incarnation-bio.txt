Hailing from Alor Setar, INCARNATION originally formed in late 2005. Over the years the quintet has played numerous shows to spread their wings be it in Penang, Ipoh, KL, Pahang, and Johor Bahru.

If you're searching for brutal and aggressive metal, you've met your match with INCARNATION. Combining elements of hardcore, thrash, and extreme metal - INCARNATION has steadily become one of the leading lights in Malaysian heavy metal.

Stainless Perfect Murder, the 1st full-length from INCARNATION is bound to be that album for the band, as it contains all the elements that is wicked and brutal to feed your ultimate metal music satisfaction, while also managing to drive their sound forward.
