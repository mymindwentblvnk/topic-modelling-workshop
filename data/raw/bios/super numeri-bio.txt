Super Numeri are an experimental instrumental group based in Liverpool, United Kingdom and signed to the Ninja Tune label. The band was created by Pop Levi, Snap Ant and Karl Webb, who have recruited several other musicians in their recordings and live performances.

Super Numeri have recorded two original albums to date and have been compared with German group Can and Jah Wobble.
