One-half of the rap duo Sauce Twinz who performs alongside his partner Sancho Saucy. He has released mixtapes titled Sorry 4 the Sauce, Sorry 4 the Sauce 2 and In Sauce We Trust. He grew up in Houston with a mother who was an addict. Some of his biggest singles include "The Kid That Did" and "Ring Around the Rosies." He has a brother named Dillon Clemons. His father was a professional wrestler. He made waves in the hip hop game after releasing a diss track of Drake. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sauce+Walka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
