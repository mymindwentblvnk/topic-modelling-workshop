Thrash metal band from Mesa, Arizona, whose 1986 Die a Violent Death demo featured alternately growling & shrieking vocals.  Their 1987 demo song "The Definitive Apology" was featured on Metal Massacre Nine. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pedifile">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
