Abzofran is a groove metal band from Podgorica, Montenegro, in 2010. After a 5 track demo in 2014, the group released their first album, Dawn Of Neon, in May 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abzofran">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
