Lushlife is one of a new breed of MCs. Lush's diversity lies within some 20 years of musical education - he spent 13 of them in classical piano training, along with a 7 year course in Jazz kit drumming, he has arranged music for Jazz combos and performed on street-corners and at Manhattan's prestigious Steinway Hall.

Feeling disillusioned after a year of studying music Lush decided to go back to the reason he first started playing music: hip-hop. His debut album emerged as 'Order of Operations'. Lush performed all the vocals, writing, producing and performing himself. This guy is THAT good... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lushlife">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
