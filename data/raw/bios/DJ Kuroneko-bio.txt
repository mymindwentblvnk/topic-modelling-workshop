DJ Kuroneko is an electronic producer and DJ from Ireland. He makes electronic dance music, and works mostly in the genres of jungle, house & UK garage, breakbeat, and drum and bass. He is also an experienced DJ, most often live-streaming from home with fellow Irish DJ & electronic producer Harmful Logic) on their shared Twitch.tv channel (ladsundcanzer), called Lads und Canzer, or less frequently solo on his personal Twitch.tv channel (DJ__Kuroneko).. He also occasionally DJs for virtual clubs/raves and other online events. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Kuroneko">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
