Prog metal band hailing from Paderborn, Germany. Combines the thrashy guitar riffs and the rough, yet melodic vocals, as well as majestic keyboards and lengthy guitar solos. Once described as "Germany's best underground Metal band".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deadly+Sin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
