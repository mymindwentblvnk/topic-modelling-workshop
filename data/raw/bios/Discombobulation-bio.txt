Hong Kong-based prog/sludge/doom metal. Released a self-titled EP in 2007 and their only full length called "Roman's Melancholia" in 2008. Shortly disbanded after wards.

Their last known line up consisted of Kiran Jaya on lead guitar, Terrence Chan on guitar/vocals, Kenji Niimura on bass/vocals and Darryl Yu on drums. This is also the lineup which recorded on their EP and full length.

Their official website is http://www.myspace.com/discombobulationhk
