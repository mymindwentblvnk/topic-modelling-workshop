1. Visceral is a death metal/thrash metal band formed in 1998 in Buenos Aires, Argentina by Tano Romano in guitarrs (Hermética, Malón) and Karlos Cuadrado in bass (Malón) with Willy Caballero at drums and vocals. They had played together previously in Cerbero (Speed/Black Metal, 1984-1987), one of the first extreme metal bands from Argentina.
