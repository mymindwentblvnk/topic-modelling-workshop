Alcohol was formed in 2003 in Budapest, Hungary. Their music incorporates elements from melodic metal, punk and hard rock. They have released 2 albums as of now: Ezen a szinten in 2007 and Házam egy kocsma in 2009. Members are:

Csöves - bass, vocals
Tamás Szabó - guitar
