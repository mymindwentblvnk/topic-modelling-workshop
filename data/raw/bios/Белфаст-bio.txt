Belfast is a russian "rockapaddy" (irish-inspired pub folk punk) band from Moscow, Russia, founded in 4 of October 2001.
Leader of Belfast band - Ivan "Sean O'Brien" Voronov (vocals, guitar).
Other members are Julia Voronova (bass, back vocals), Eugen Ilnitskiy (guitar), Egor Matveev (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/%D0%91%D0%B5%D0%BB%D1%84%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
