Sin Theorem is a metal band from Lansing, Michigan.  The band consists of Chad Miller (vocals), Tony Massey (guitar, vocals), Ronnie Lee Graham (drums), Dave Covert (guitar), and Pete Pedula (bass).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sin+Theorem">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
