Chronzon donated their rendition of CRADLE OF FILTH's 'Chtulhu Dawn' to the 2003 Cleopatra tribute album 'Covered In Filth'. The Chronzon pairing of singer Rob Thoth and guitarist Samuel Dean assembled a new Death Metal band venture in early 2005 dubbed MURDER 101, also including the MALEVOLENT CREATION and DIVINE EMPIRE credited Jason Blachowicz on bass with Cesar Placeres of HIBERNUS MORTIS on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chronzon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
