Failing Existence is a young four piece environmental Metal band. Officially forming in mid 2010, they have been successfully impressing local and non local audiences with their unique sound and energetic live performance. Currently available from Failing Existence is a 3 track demo titled ''Too Weak To Go Alone'' which was recorded in late July by Evan Kane. Since the release of the demo, Failing Existence has fine tuned their direction towards the environment and the impact man has on it. Writing more atmospheric and technical songs, which will feature on their upcoming EP due to be released around mid 2011. 

Tony Gillahan - Vocals .. Harrison Kane - Guitar .. Timothy Salzmann - Bass .. Leo Hayward - Drums .
