1- Crescent Moon is a Doom/Death metal band. Formed in late 2008 by Ahmad and Besher in the Syrian city of Aleppo.
Debut EP released and available for free download through Bandcamp 

2- Crescent Moon is the latest brain child of WEEN bassist Dave Dreiwitz. It is a hard rocking duo of bass and drums, exploring sounds of the past and the future. Crescent Moon features different drummers including TOMATO from the Sound of Urchin, CLAUDE COLEMAN from Ween, JOE RUSSO of Furthur and ERIC SLICK of Dr. Dog. Formed early 2006, Crescent Moon had a successful West Coast summer tour featuring TOMATO on drums. Crescent Moon performs original music by Dreiwitz as well as some rock classics and a few tunes from Dreiwitz's previous bass and drum duo and Chocodog Recording Artist INSTANT DEATH.
