There are multiple artists with this name.
1. Ohio-based progressive metal group Mockingbird began in 2008, composed of three friends Andy, Chadd, and Mike. According to their bio on ReverbNation, “Much of Mockingbird has evolved from the personal necessity to create and break from the chains and illusions that confine our limited everyday senses.”
2. Mockingbird is an Australian alternative rock group whose debut LP, “Faceless,” released in 1996.
3. American producer Mockingbird creates electronic of various subgenres, generally leaning towards a melodic sound. He started releasing music in 2018.
