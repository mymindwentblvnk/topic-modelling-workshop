A power metal / progressive metal band from Grosseto, Tuscany, Italy.

The band formed in 2004

Band members:

Simone Cantini -- Bass
Alessandro Brandi -- Drums (See also: Stillborn, ex-Athena)
Massimo Castri -- Guitars
Fabrizio Muratori -- Keyboards
Lorenzo Ticci -- Vocals

Discography:

Oneiric Tales -- Demo (2007)  
Skydancers -- Full-length (2009)
