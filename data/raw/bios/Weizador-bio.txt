Weizador - Burmese Metal band from Sydney, Australia. Music is influenced by extreme metal genres. Lyrics are inspired by religious beliefs and personal stories. 

The name of the band is derived from a Burmese word referring to immortal wizards from Burmese mythology who are highly revered in Burmese tradition.

Today, Weizador music brings you a dark atmosphere influenced by metal genres on the extreme side as well as Burmese culture and religion. The lyrical themes reflect a person's introspections and fantasies as he travels his own spiritual journey.
