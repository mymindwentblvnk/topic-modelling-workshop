Sarvas is modern Metal band mixing genres like Sludge, Groove, Stoner and Doom into a unique, raw entity of massive auditory goo. They hail from Lahti, Finland. The band was formed in late 2012; after a year of intense jamming and gigs Sarvas recorded their first album (Self-Titled) in November 2013.. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sarvas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
