Kick ass stoner quartet from Finland.
 Enter the Pain started out at Äänekoski as a Death Metal -trio. Original line-up held Tommi in drums, Pupu in vocals and Otto in guitar. Year 2006 saw a turn to Stoner Rock, as bassist Jemo joined group. Band has performed in middle-Finland at few smaller festivals and have recorded one EP in 2006.
