Geert Van Dyck is still the driving force behind Epycenter. Drummer Kris De Bie joined with the old line-up in the winter of 2010, and Dave Powell also re-units with his old band. Newest member to the band is Chris Ketelaer. He will shred through the new songs of Epycenter.

[2010]

Epycenter is a Northern Belgian 'Ravage Power/Prog Thrash Metal' band. The guitar is taking the lead under supervision of Koen Legrand. Bass guitar and vocals are under command of Geert Van Dyck and drums are driven by John Van Tongelen. Synthesizer- and keyboardsounds are generated by Simon Kerbusch.
