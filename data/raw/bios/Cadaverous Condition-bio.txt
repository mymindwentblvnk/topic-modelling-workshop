Cadaverous Condition is a band with two faces. The one side is their Death Metal side; the other side their Death Folk side.

Cadaverous Condition became known within the neofolk scene mostly thanks to their split with American neofolk artists Changes; Time. In 2007 they released a full length Death Folk release; Songs Of The Crooked Path.
