Taphos are a death metal band from Copenhagen, Denmark. The band have released three EPs, and their debut full-length album "Come Ethereal Somberness" was released in June 2018 through Blood Harvest Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Taphos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
