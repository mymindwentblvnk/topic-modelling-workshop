There are mutliple artists with this name:

1. Torture  is a Texas thrash metal band. It was formed in 1986 and released a four-track rehearsal tape the same year. They're known from their "Storm Alert" released in 1989 and re-released in 2006 with bonus tracks.

They are a very aggressive and brutal thrash metal band. Some may say their brand of thrash contains sparse death metal influences.

After a long hiatus, the band has recently reunited and they're writing songs for a new album

2. Torture  is  a melodic death metal band from Germany.

3. Torture  is a dark ambient project belonging to the Black Legions (LLN) of France.

The entire album consists of sounds of a man being tortured with various tools &
objects.

4. Torture  is the rapper now known as Sensational.

5. Torture is a brutal death metal band from Indonesia.

6. Torture is a grindcore / sludge metal band from Massachusetts
