The German band Fvneral Fvkk features members of Ophis, Fäulnis and Voidhaven. After the successful 2017 EP “Lecherous Liturgies”, which received positive responses that mentioned, among others, the wit of the lyrics and the stylistic similarity to Solitude Aeturnus, Fvneral Fvkk decided to reach a new milestone by creating a new epic doom metal piece. “Carnal Confessions" in 2019.
