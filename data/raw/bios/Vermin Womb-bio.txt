Vermin Womb is a three-piece from Denver, USA formed in 2014 featuring current and former members of Primitive Man, Clinging to the Trees of a Forest Fire, Death of Self, Scourge Schematic and Salo. Stylistically, the band combines elements of death metal. grindcore, sludge, and black metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vermin+Womb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
