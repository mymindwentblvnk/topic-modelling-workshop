It was sometime in the fall of 2004, after the inevitable demise of Rune, when we began writing the material which was to become what is now Kenoma. Doug and Jeremie had some material together and sought the collaberation of Eric, who they had known from playing with Harlots the prior year, to further the writing process. It was not long after that Jimmy showed intrest, and the band was formed...

Members are Doug McGinnis: Bass, loops, moog, soundscapes; Eric Dunn: Guitar and sounds; Jeremie Jordan: Guitar and noise; James Magnuson: Drums <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kenoma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
