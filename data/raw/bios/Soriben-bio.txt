Soriben is a Melodic Death/ Black Metal band formed in Seville, Andalusia in 1995.

Last known line-up:

Carlos de la Osa - Guitar
Isaías Reinoso - Vocals, guitar (Corpvs Vorax)
Pablo García - Drums (Guilles de Rais, Corpvs Vorax)
Sergio Carmona - Bass (Binchuka, Subliminal Chaos).

Former/past member(s):

Guitar: Francisco Villena (1995-1998), Jorge James (1999)
Vocals: Alfonso (1995-1999), Daniel Ronmark (1999), Benjamín (Crossfields)
Drums: Mao (1995-1999), Fernando Gallardo (Xendra (Esp)), Jesús Montoya (Ira Regia)
Others & uknown roles: Manuel Ponce de León - Bass (1996-1999), Eduardo de la Osa - Keyboards (1996-1999), Manuel Gómez Marín, Mario Serrato, Manolo “Patilla”
