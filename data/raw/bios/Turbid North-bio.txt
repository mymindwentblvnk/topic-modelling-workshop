Turbid North hails from North Pole, Alaska. Formed in 1999, they set out to change the state of metal as it is today. Playing any show they could get , they have set a name for themselves around Alaska. "No gimmicks for us, we play for the metal people and thats it. We are a heavy metal band, pure and simple." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Turbid+North">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
