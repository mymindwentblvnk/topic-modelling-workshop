French Industrial gothic metal with a strong Type O Negative vibe from Amiens, Picardy. They were formed in 1993 and released the demo 001 a year later, then succeeded by a split tape with caarcrinolas the next year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mental+Oppression">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
