Lunar Pocket is a progressive doom metal band from Serbia. Band members are Saska Miss Jukebox (vocals), Srdjan Brankovic (guitars), IP Maesstro (bass) and Vladimir Djedovic (keyboards). In early 2013 the band released its first full-length album named "In Love With the Fantasy" through Miner Recordings label.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lunar+Pocket">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
