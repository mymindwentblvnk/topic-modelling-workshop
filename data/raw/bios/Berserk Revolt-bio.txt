DEATH-METAL band started in 2003 in Zaporozhye (Ukraine) by Taras Berserk, Alex Machina, Nazgul and Den Pulsar. From the beginning they music was based on death-metal and grindcore with lyrical themes such as: social injustices, religion abuses, politics, etc.  During the 2004 Berserk Revolt took part in series of concerts in his city. In 2004 the song "I Hate The Cross" was recorded. The same year the "Ancient Faith" song was also recorded, which appeared on the "Wolfsong Compilation, Vol. 11".  In 2006 "The Cult Of Forgotten Gods" demo cassette was recorded where upon the band took part in several concerts. In 2011 they recorded the debut long album "Perception Kills" which is the illustration of the modern world, impared by urbanization, wars and lies dictated by Mass Media, where manipulation of public opinion prospers and traditional human values collapse.In 2014 they made a new ep named "Theo-Machist".
