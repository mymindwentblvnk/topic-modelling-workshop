Keep Dying is a Metal band coming from near Frankfurt am Main, Germany. As themselves say, their music reflects their daily fears and anger. Their songs cover a variety of styles, including hard riffs, fast drumming, screams and growls, and lyrics concerning about the all-day's life. Altough they describe their style as pure Metal, one could more specifically call their music a mixture of Death Metal and Metalcore. In the area around Keep Dying's hometown, they are already known for their very powerful performances on live stages. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Keep+Dying">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
