Two Ton Boa is primarily fronted by Sherry Fraser, who has been joined on various songs by a plethora of artists:

Brian Sparhawk, Dan Rieser, Rachel Carns, Radio Sloan (formerly of The Need) Scott Seckington, Dave Harvey, Ian Browne, Rob Allison, Nik Pesut.
