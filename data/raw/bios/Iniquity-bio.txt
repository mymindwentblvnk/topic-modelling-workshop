Copenhagen, Denmark

1989 - 2004, 2012 -

Iniquity is a technical death metal band from Copenhagen, Denmark. During their initial fifteen year lifespan they released three full-length albums in the style of brutal, technical death metal, before dissolving in 2004. In August, 2012, the band announced a return of the 1998 lineup.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Iniquity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
