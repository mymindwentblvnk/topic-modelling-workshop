Baalphegor is a Brutal Death Metal band from Girona, Spain. The current line-up is composed of Alfred Berengena on drums, Jose Hernandez (bass), Marc Plana (guitars), Miguel Herrera (vocals) and David Fernandez (guitars). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Baalphegor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
