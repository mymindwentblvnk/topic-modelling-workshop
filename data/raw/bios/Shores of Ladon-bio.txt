Black metal band from Germany, formed in 2009. The line-up includes both members from depressive black metal band Balnasar and shares members with Grail.

Discography:
2010 - Promo 2010
2011 - Brut und Seuche / Lupercal 
2011 - Eindringling / Durch die Augen des Raben 
2011 - Knochenstaub / Waldlied

'Promo 2010' is released on CD by Schwaerze Productions. The other three demo's are released on tape by Sol Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shores+of+Ladon">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
