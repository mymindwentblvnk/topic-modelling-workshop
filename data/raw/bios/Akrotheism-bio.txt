Akrotheism is a Greek black metal band created during 2012 to perform violent, experimental black metal under matters of nihilism, esotericism, God, Satan and their affection to the nature of the irredeemable human decadence.

The band’s first release is a split along with ORDER OF THE EBON HAND available in all formats.

Akrotheism have also completed the recordings of their first album baptized under the name “Behold The Son Of Plagues”. It  includes 10 blasphemies towards the spiritual chaos of humanity, recorded at DEVASOUND studios, while the mix and master took place at NECROMORBUS studio by Tore Stjerna. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Akrotheism">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
