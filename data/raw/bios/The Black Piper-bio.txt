The Black Piper is a cinematic band. They are based in Provo, Utah. They were formed by producer Sae Sae Norris, film composer Michael Bahnmiller. Their first major release is the book soundtrack for The Way of Kings by Brandon Sanderson, titled Kaladin.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Black+Piper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
