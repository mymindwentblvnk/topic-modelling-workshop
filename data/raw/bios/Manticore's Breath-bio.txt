Manticore's Breath is the changed name of Greek psychedelic/prog rockers Will-O-The-Wisp. They have put out one self-titled 10" EP and one full album (Death Breath) in 2011. Their musical style is more heavy-rock oriented, with the psychedelic elements Will-O-The-Wisp were known for.
