Eskapism is an atmospheric black metal project from Lviv, Ukraine. Created in the spring of 2015, Eskapism represents aggressive guitar riffs and drums inherent music in the genre of Black Metal, melodic and atmospheric keyboards arrangements, poetry on the grandeur of nature and the night, their beauty and originality. 

Members: 
Dyvozor Keyboards, Bass (2015-present)
ZymoborVocals, Guitars, Bass, Programming (2015-present)
Mika SalmiDrums (2019-present)
