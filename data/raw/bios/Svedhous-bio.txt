South Korean Depressive Black Metal, formed in 2005 by G.H. The lyrics are mostly based on suicide. 

- Discography -
2007 - Despair Poetry (demo)
2007 - Astaroth / Svedhous (split)
2007 - Path of Suicide (demo)
2008 - From Despair to Suicide (compilation)
2009 - Sursk (demo)
