Regarded as one of Australia's best thrash metal acts, METREYA exploded onto the metal scene with the 2013 release of their debut E.P. entitled MACHINES OF WAR. Featuring 5 blistering speed metal classics, MACHINES OF WAR has been met with critical acclaim among the global thrash metal community.

METREYA are unequivocally thrash metal, delivering a true late 80's thrash metal master class reminiscent of the best the genre has to offer. METREYA cite thrash metal luminaries such as Exodus, Dark Angel, Anthrax and Kreator as major influences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metreya">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
