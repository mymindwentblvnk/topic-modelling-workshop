Massenvernichtung are a German black metal band. Formed in 2002, the band name means "mass destruction" in German. Massenvernichtung has also been featured on the compilation Ancient Hordes - Volume II with the song Christen sind unser Unglück.

Discography:
▶ 2003 - Christen Sind Unser Unglück (demo)
▶ 2004 - Los von Rom (demo)
▶ 2005 - Magna Europa Est Patria Nostra (split with Darkthule)
▶ 2006/2007 - Gemeinschaftstonträger (split CD with Celtic Blood, Femegericht, Vardemis, Dark Forest Productions) 
▶ 2007 - Im Dienste Des Morddoms (full-length)
▶ 2009 - Die Synagoge Satans (split with Occult Crypt)
▶ 2015 - Eine Abrechnung (full-length)
▶ 2017 - Tokio · Rom · Berlin (split with Reek of the Unzen Gas Fumes  and Via Dolorosa
▶ 2018 - Live im Atombunker - Jugendsünden (compilation)
