Sorrow Of Tranquility are a melodic death metal formed in 1990, hailing from Tokyo, Japan.  The band released an EP "Engagement-Hill" in 2002 as well as a full length "Empire From Darkness" the same year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sorrow+of+Tranquility">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
