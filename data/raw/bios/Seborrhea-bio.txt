Seborrhea was a Latvian grindcore band formed in Rīga, Latvia in 2006.

The band's discography consists of two music reviewer and audience acclaimed albums, the debut album, Different Segments of Human Life, on Trepanation Squad in 2008, followed by digitally self-released Relative Intermedium in 2009.

Members:

Andris Mednis - vocals;
Aigars Baumanis - guitars (currently Witherian);
Jānis Jēkabsons - bass (currently Kastete, Nekad);
Uldis Bušs - drums.

Former members:

Andris Pupuriņš - guitars;
Aigars Māls - guitars (currently Enhet).
