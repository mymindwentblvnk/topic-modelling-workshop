Facilis Descensus Averni is a Russian Black Metal band. The band's releases include 2 albums, 2 demos, a split and a single all recorded between 2002 and 2004. According to Metal Archives the band is still active, Facilis Descensus Averni also have a side project called EROS. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Facilis+Descensus+Averni">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
