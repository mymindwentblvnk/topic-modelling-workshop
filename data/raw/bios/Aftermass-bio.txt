Aftermass were a death/thrash metal band that formed in Saint Clair Shores, Michigan, USA in the 80's. They released one self-titled demo in 1989 before splitting up.

Lineup:
Mike - Vocals
Jason Krause - Guitars
Andy - Guitars
Mot - Bass
Scott - Drums
