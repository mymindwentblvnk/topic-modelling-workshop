A drummer with a CV that includes Vivian Girls, Crystal Stilts and Dum Dum Girls, Frankie Rose opted to strike out on her own.  With the help of backing band 'The Outs' she released a self-titled debut album on September 20 2010.  In 2012, she released her follow up 'Interstellar' under her own name only. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Frankie+Rose+and+The+Outs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
