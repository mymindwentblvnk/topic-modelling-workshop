Based in Denmark, Whorls is an entity formed in early 2012 with the intention of playing an agressive and dissonant form of Black Metal/Hardcore

Bandmembers:
Thomas - Vocals
Rasmus - Drums
Emil - Guitar
Sebastian - Bass
