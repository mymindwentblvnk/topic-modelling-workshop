A heavy metal band formed 2008 in Brooklyn, New York by members: 
Weibust - Guitar and Vocals
The Mexicutioner - Bass
Dino Destroyer- Lead guitar
Tooth -Drums and backups
Namrog- Spiritual advisor

They released a 4-track CD-R demo and a 7"  single entitled "Spider Baby" in 2010.
