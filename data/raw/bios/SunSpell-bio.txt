Sunspell is a one man band created by Doulos who plays all instruments. US based this band plays Raw, Depressive and unrelenting black metal. Sunspell does not stick to one style has this band's demos and splits have shown us that. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sunspell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
