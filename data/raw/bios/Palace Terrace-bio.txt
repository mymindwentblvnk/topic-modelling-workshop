Palace Terrace is a progressive and neoclassical rock band formed by world renowned vocalist Jonathan Marshall and guitar virtuoso George Bellas [ex Ring Of Fire, ex Mogg/Way]. Their music combines superb musicianship, beautifully crafted melodies, unique harmonic progressions, compelling stories, counterpoint, large orchestrations, wide dynamics, odd meters, stunning vocal arrangements and elaborate forms. for more news and updates, visit www.jonathanrmarshall.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Palace+Terrace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
