Mayadome is a Swedish progressive metal band. 

Last known line-up:
Bassel Elharbiti - Vocals 
Fredrik Kjörling - Guitar 
Sebastian Okupski - Keyboards (ex-Loch Vostok)
Eric Grandin - Bass (ex-Loch Vostok, ex-Wuthering Heights)
Teddy Möller - Drums (F.K.Ü., Loch Vostok, Wuthering Heights, Flagellation (Swe), Tradore, Vivaldis Disciples) 

Former/past member(s):
Björn Holmquist - Vocals 
Jonas Hägg - Keyboards 

Teddy, Sebastian and Eric have moved on to Loch Vostok.
A third album, entitled "Into the great big nothing" was recorded, but never released.

Discography:
Paranormal Activity Full-length, 1996 
Near Life Experience Full-length, 1999
