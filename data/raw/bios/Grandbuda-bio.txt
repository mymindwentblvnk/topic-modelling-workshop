Following his 4 year collaboration as one of the New Apple Taste emcees, GrandBuda leaves his jazz fusion training to pursue his projects as producer and DJ. In addition to being a DJ and emcee, GrandBuda is also a fine producer, he has released several EPs available online. The most recent, Nothing At All, was released on vinyl and conveys GrandBuda's impressive potential.
