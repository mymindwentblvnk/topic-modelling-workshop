Dreams Of Tears are a Melodic Death Metal band from Caracas, Venezuela. They released the 'Concience' Single in 2006, and an EP called 'The New Times Of Chaos' in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreams+Of+Tears">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
