Depressive Black Metal from Mexico; formed by Lord Marganor Dungrave (drums and lyrics; from Ereshkigal/Black Empire), Lord Xantotol (all guitars and bass; from Ereshkigal/Black Empire) and Lord Ishtar (vocals; from Moonlight) in 2006.
