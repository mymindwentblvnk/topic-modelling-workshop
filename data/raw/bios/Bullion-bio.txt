Bullion (AKA Nathan Jenkins) is a music producer and songwriter from West London, residing in Lisbon. He has released records on Young Turks, R&S, The Trilogy Tapes and Whities. As a producer and musician, Nathan’s worked with Sampha, David Byrne, Paul Epworth, Westerman and Laura Groves. Remix credits include Todd Terje, Metronomy, Model 500 and Dita Von Teese.

He runs DEEK Recordings - an outlet for collaboration. A series of covers compilations on the label, with contributions from Sean Nicholas Savage, Westerman, Red Axes, Nilüfer Yanya, Kero Kero Bonito and more, reflects the label’s ‘Pop, not slop’ aesthetic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bullion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
