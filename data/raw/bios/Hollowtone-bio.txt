Hollowtone is a melodic death metal band from Belgium, consisting of Bart Goffinghs (vocals) and Kristof Vandebeek (guitars, bass, drums). Their debut album, called "Dead Man's End" was released in the year of 2007.
A new album "MISERY LOVES COMPANY" will be released in 2008. Currently, the band is already recording and pre-producing some of the songs for that album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hollowtone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
