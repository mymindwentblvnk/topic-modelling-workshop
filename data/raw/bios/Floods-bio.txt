Floods is the name of at least 5 bands:

1. Post-hardcore band from St. Albans/Watford, United Kingdom formed in 2009, Floods were a 6 piece band influenced by the likes of Underoath, Alexisonfire, Paramore, Mewithoutyou and Hot Water Music. Their debut EP “Cold Light” was released for free in 2009, and whilst writing their debut full length came one of the finalists for the 2011 competition, Red Bull Bedroom Jam. Their self titled full length was released in October of 2012 on Small Town Records.
