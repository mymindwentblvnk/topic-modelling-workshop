Mouseleum was a death metal band formed 1990 in Istanbul, Turkey. They released a demo "Lunar Caustic" in 1993, played some gigs and went off the radar for years to reform some years ago as Death Symposium. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mouseleum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
