Structure of Lies is a Death Metal/hardcore band from Mesa, AZ,  formed in 2000 by ex-members of unruh, wellington, etc. Ryan now is a member of Landmine Marathon and North Side Kings and was in mercitron for a while. They released two demos, two split CD's (one with Iranach and one with misery index) and a CDep, Abacus. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Structure+of+Lies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
