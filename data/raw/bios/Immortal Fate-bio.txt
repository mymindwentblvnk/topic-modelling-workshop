Immortal Fate were a death/grind band from California, formed in 1989. Members of Immortal Fate have went on to play in No Less and Autopsy. Two of the band's members' little brother is in Plutocracy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Immortal+Fate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
