Deathchurch is a raw / lo-fi black metal band from Chiba Prefecture, Japan, formed in 2002.  They have several demo tapes and a vinyl release on Nuclear War Now / Klaxon Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathchurch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
