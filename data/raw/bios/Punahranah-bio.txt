Punahranah (means 'collateral damage' in Malaysian language) is a four-piece death/grind outfit formed in late 2006 by members of Tyranny and Carnage Majesty. They currently will be releasing an 8 track EP titled 'Collateral Damage'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Punahranah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
