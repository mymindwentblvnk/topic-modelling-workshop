Sons of Fenris is a manifestation of Them, the Primordial Dark Forces named Thurisaz, Giants, Jotuns or Manetanaz, into this aberration named Creation.
Follower of the dark Germanic path, this band is a monument of Dark feelings, Death, Chaos and supports all things which bring the fall of Mankind and the return of the Primordial Chaos named Ginnungagap!!!

Sons of Fenris was formed in 1999 in Lutzelhouse, Alsace France by Werewolf- All music, Vocals
