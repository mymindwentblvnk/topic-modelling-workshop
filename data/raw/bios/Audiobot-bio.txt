Audiobot is a 27 year old DJ/Music Producer from LA
breaking into the scene at age 18 he started his career working with LMFAO and will.i.am on their albums during the early 2010s. Since he has been experimenting with multiple genres through his career from dubstep to edm to chill hop audiobot has shown diversity and range in his music. his latest releases have been very mellow and lofi but his dj sets are the complete opposite.
