Bushy is Jim Bingham of Brighton, United Kingdom. He has released one full-length album to date on Catskills Records (Hiya!; 2002), in addition to several remixes and EPs. His music has appeared on a number of compilation albums, including Fabric 28. His track "Never" was remixed by Bonobo, and he himself has remixed many other artists.

Jim also releases music as Electric Bear, which he describes as "more disco oriented".
