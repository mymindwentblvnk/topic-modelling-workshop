Planas aka Toby Davies is a producer/instrumentalist/label manager for Ranking Records. Collaborates regularly with label contemporaries Ruckspin, Quark, Quantum Soul and Evergreen. You can find more beats at www.myspace.com/tobydavies

He also produces hip hop under the alius Tarqwin Smythe (www.myspace.com/smythebeats), and dub under the name "Landlord" (www.myspace.com/landlorddub)

As well as that, Toby plays bass in the Gentleman's Dub Club and hip hop band, the uber.
