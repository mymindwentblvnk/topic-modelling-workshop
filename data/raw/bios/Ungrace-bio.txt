UNGRACE is an Industrial Death Metal band from Ukraine.

Created in 2009, UNGRACE releases its debut album “Hostile Revival” (DFMO) in early 2010, quickly gaining its fanbase all over the world. In the next two years UNGRACE went on several tours, giving shows in Ukraine and Europe.
In 2011 UNGRACE starts working on its second album. After almost two years of production, “Feed The Demons” is going to be released by the leading underground metal label in CIS, Metal Scrap \ Total Metal Records.
In support of “Feed The Demons” UNGRACE is going on its next tour in Europe
