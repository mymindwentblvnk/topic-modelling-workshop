Goathead is a Black / Death Metal band from Sweden. Goathead came to life in 2019 when members from Hellsingland, Mist of Misery and Necrologium wanted to create the best Black/Death Metal taking inspiration from the early 90's Black Metal scene and 80's Death Metal scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/goathead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
