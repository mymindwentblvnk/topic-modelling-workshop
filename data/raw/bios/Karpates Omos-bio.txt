After the split of "At My Funeral" in 2006, Rust and Morke (also known as Vlad Basarab) formed "Karpates Omos". In summer of 2006 they record just one song and in autumn Morke leaves the band. After one year Rust records "Great Past" with Nymus on vocals, but the demo was never released. In the begining of 2009 Banatian Darkness will release a Karpates Omos - Ordinu Negru split. Karpates Omos will appear on the split with three new songs, with Rust and Fulmineos on vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Karpates+Omos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
