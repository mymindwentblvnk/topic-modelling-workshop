YOTA : Youth of the Apocalypse is a band formed in 2017 which consists of vocalist Jamie Reynolds (Klaxons) guitarist Jeff Wooton (Gorillaz), bassist Seye Adelekan (Gorillaz), drummer Jay Sharrock, DJ Twiggy Garcia and New York rapper Young Lazurus. They released their first single "Drop the bomb (feat. MF doom) on June 1st 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/YOTA+:+Youth+of+the+Apocalypse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
