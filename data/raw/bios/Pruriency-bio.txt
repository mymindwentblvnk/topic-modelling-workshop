Toronto Black/Thrash Metal band, featuring members of Lapidate, Adersarial, and other bands. The band formed in 2005 by members Derek, Erich and Taylor. The band has a self produced jam rehearsal demo and are likely working on new materials.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pruriency">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
