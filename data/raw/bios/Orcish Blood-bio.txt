Orcish Blood was a Russian Melodic Black Metal band, split-up after their first full length, Moral Invalid, in 2001. They released only two demos before the album, 1999's "As All the Gods Do" and "I See No End of a Winter". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Orcish+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
