Folk metal music from the baltic area. One man band formed by Velnias in 2014. 

Currently signed to No Colours Records Romuvo have  released two full length albums, "Romuvan Dainas" in 2014  and "Infront of Destiny" in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Romuvos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
