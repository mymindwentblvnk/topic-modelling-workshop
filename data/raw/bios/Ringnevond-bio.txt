Ringnevond is a Norwegian band who plays fast and raw black metal in the vein of Marduk and Darkthrone. They signed a deal with the brand new native label Edgerunner Records for one album and released "Nattverd" in January 2000 although the material of the album was written in 1993 and 1994. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ringnevond">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
