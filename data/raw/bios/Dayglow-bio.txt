Sloan Struble, known professionally as Dayglow, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer from Fort Worth, Texas. His career began releasing songs on Soundcloud.

After self-releasing an LP (Fuzzybrain) the day before going to UT, he reached 9 million streams and opted out of university. The LP was later rereleased as his debut.

His sophomore album, Harmony House, came out on May 21, 2021.

On June 21, 2022; a year after his sophomore album Harmony House was released, Struble released a single (Then It All Goes Away) along with announcing an upcoming album (People In Motion) releasing on October 7, 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dayglow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
