Eradicated is a technical brutal death metal band with experimental influences from Berlin, Germany.

The band was found in 2007 as a grindcore-oriented band.
After 2 years and several member changes Eradicated to focus on a technically more challenging
style of death metal combined with anything they wanted to add up.

Currently the band consists of

Eric (vox) , Steven (guitars) , Marcel (drums)
