1) Sharda began her career in 1966 with the hit song titli udi.. from "Suraj".She hails from Hyderabad and has sung numerous hit songs in films like "Around the world","An Evening in Paris","Seema" etc.

She won the prestigious Filmfare Award in 1970 for her rendention of Baat Zara Hai Aapas ki..in "Jahan Pyar Miley".

She is one of the few female singers in India who made her presence felt in the "Mangeshkar -dominated" Hindi Music Industry.
