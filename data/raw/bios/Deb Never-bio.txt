Deb Never is an American singer and songwriter from Spokane, Washington. She is based in Los Angeles.

She has released demos and singles on digital platforms from 2017 to 2018, and appeared on Brockhampton's "NO HALO" from GINGER in 2019. She released her debut EP titled House on Wheels on August 30, 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/deb+never">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
