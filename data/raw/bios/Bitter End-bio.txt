1) Out of San Antonio, Texas, this band plays New York heavy metal/hardcore in the vein of Biohazard and Breakdown. Released their debut ep Mind In Chains on Malfunction Records in 2006, their first full-length, Climate Of Fear on Deathwish in 2007, and a self-titled 7" also on DW in 08 . They also put out a tape demo earlier on Next Level Records.  Mosh Hard Texas.
