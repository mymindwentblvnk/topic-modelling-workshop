Communist death/thrash metal band from Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina. Formed in 1989 and disbanded in 1997, they reunited in 2008 for a few shows and release a 2nd full-length before disbanding permanently. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+Punto">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
