Ghostly Kisses is the dreamy pop music of singer songwriter Margaux Sauvé.

The name ‘Ghostly Kisses’ was inspired from reading William Faulkner’s poem  ‘Une ballade des dames perdues’ and seemed like the perfect reflection of her ethereal voice.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ghostly+Kisses">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
