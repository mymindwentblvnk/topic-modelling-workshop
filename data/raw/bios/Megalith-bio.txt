1) Megalith is a Doom / Gothic / Progressive / Dark Heavy Metal band from Mainz, Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany, formed in 1998.  They have chiefly political and critical lyrics, dealing amongst others with the European Union, left-wing extremism or so-called "political correctness".

2) Megalith is a Power / Speed Metal band in St Louis, MO, formed in the early 1980's by Joe Bufalo. Megalith recently reformed with three of the four members from the "Risen from the Grave" recordings. The band is currently working on new material. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Megalith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
