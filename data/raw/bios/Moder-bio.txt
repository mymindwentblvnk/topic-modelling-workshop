The Old School Black/Death Metal band "Moder" from Bremen (Northern Germany) got out their first CD in 2001. If You want to load some songs, watch a little bit or buy stuff, visit www.moder-metal.de 

Discography:

2001 - Moder Demo
2002 - Gates To Insanity
2003 - Valley Of No Light
2005 - Fields Of Devastation
2007 - Ewiger Tod (Eng. "Eternal Death")

Currently bandmembers:

Michi Hate - Guitar
Nekro Nickel - Bass & Vocals
Astaroth - Drums
