1) Electronic/dub/ambient project by Kipp Johnson (from Candiru) and Bill Yurkiewicz (from Exit-13) with the help of guitarist James Plotkin (from O.L.D.). They released two albums ("Empty Nature" in 1997 and "Crystallized" in 1998) through Relapse Records' sublabel Release Entertainment. Their music presents significant resemblance to Mick Harris's long-running electronic/dub project Scorn.
