Ritualistic black metal from Denmark. The band was formed some time before 2004. 
The band has released one single, Witchcraft. Witchcraft was released in 2004 by Ancient Darkness Productions.

Line-up:
Diabolos - Vocals, guitar
Sorgh - Bass
