Lower 13 is a melodic metal trio based in Cleveland, Ohio. Formed in 2006, Lower 13 strives to carve their own identity in the metal scene, blending styles and pulling from their various influences. They create their own brand of metal laced with crushing riffs, grooves, and dual vocal harmonies. Lower 13 has been compared to the likes of System of a Down, Rush, Trivium and Pantera.

Current members:
Patrick Capretta – guitar, vocals
Sean Balog – bass, vocals
Eric Kruger – drums

Discography:
2010 – Rabid
2014 – Reach an End
