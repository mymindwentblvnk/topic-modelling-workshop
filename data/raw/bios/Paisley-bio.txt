There is more than one artist with this name.

1.Paisley is a beat maker from Sydney, Australia.

2.Paisley (2008) was an Anarcho-pop vegan straight edge Twee duo from Bloomington, Indiana. They draw a heavy influence from bands like The Happy Birthdays, and I like Japanese Hardcore. Their first demo was released January 2009. They don't play together anymore.

3. Paisley (Leikas) provides a vision of mid 70's classic pop / rock.
