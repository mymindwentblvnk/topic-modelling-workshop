SunStare are a 4-piece doom, sludge, post-metal band from Lille, France. To date, they have two releases; "Under The Eye Of Utu" in 2015 and "Eroded" in 2018. Both are available at https://sunstare.bandcamp.com . For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/sunstareband/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SunStare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
