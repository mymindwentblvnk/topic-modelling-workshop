Black metal from France. Their lyrical themes include darkness and french medieval mythologies. Released a demo in 2006 entitled 'Hollenfurt'.

In 2010, they released the EP called 'MCDXXXI - MCDXL', which is limited to 333 handnumbered copies.

Current line-up

Reicheran - Vocals
Necrowarrior - Guitars (Duaël)
Dalgrin - Bass (Evohé)
