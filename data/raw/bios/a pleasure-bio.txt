A Pleasure is a project by New York producer Mark Hurst.

For several years Mark was making music and uploading it onto his Soundcloud account. He was discovered by Nikolas Jaar on a chance meeting, while dining out before his infamous Boiler Rooms show in 2013, and signed to his label Other People.

In March 2014 his debute release, a three-track EP titled "Extended Play" came out, and his first album, "Jream House", aired on January 2016.
