Fabulous were a punk band formed by then NME journalist Simon Dudfield.  They made at least two singles in the early 1990's 'Destined To Be Free' and 'Personality Recession'.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/fabulous">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
