Asalto (translated: attack or mugging) is a Rock / Metal band from Zaragoza, Spain with punk ska influences in their songs as well as hard rock.

Héctor (Guitar), Victor (drums), Pedro (Vocals), Andrés (Guitar) and Fran (Bass) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asalto">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
