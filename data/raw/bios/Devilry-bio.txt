There are three bands called Devilry

1) Forged in the northern lands of Finland as Carnival Diablo, Devilry is a black/death metal band. After various demo, EP and Split recordings with lyrical themes according to the roots of the genre(satanism, anti-christian/islam, war, death, now NS), Devilry released Rites For The Spring Of Supremacy(2008) under a straight forward NS banner.

Sir Holm - vocals
Grave - guitars
Tupi - guitars
Luihu - bass
Kobalt - drums

2) Devilry is also a Swedish black metal band formed in 2008, but ended in 2010 due to the death of one of the band members.

Releases:
Heading for Salvation - Demo (2009)
A Covenant Infernal - Demo (2010)
