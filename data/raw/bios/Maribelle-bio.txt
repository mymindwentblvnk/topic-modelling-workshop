1. Maribelle is an emo-punk band from Chicago. Members include E. Aaron Ross, Chris Mills, Patrick Lawlor, and Sarah Ross. 'With Teeth Sharp As Old Friends' was self-released in February of 2010, and is the first LP by the band. The second full-length, "Only An Impulse" is now available on CD through Drugged Conscience Records.

2. Maribelle is a Dutch singer who represented the Netherlands at the Eurovision Song Contest 1984 with the song "Ik hou van jou".
