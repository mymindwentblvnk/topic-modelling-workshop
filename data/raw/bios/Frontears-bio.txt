Frontears is a Progressive Power Metal band from Germany (Bremen), initially started under the name of Neverland in 1994. After releasing one demo entitled "Nothing Left to Change" in 1996, the band changed its name to Frontears in 1997. They have released three albums since:
*Dreamhealer          Full-length, 1998
*Pull Push Power Full-length, 1999
*Commiter/Victim Full-length, 2001
and recorded one more 9-track album titled "Lust", which is awaiting further developments regarding a potential release. Currently the band is on hold.
