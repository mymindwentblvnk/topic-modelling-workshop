Godagainst is a Death Metal band from Chile. The band was formed in October of 2006 in Santiago de Chile. Godagainst's musical creations are characterized by highly complex technical rhythms, combined with a quick tempo on drums and a very powerful voice with lyrics anti-god and anti-Christianity in general, speaking to the many dilemmas that religion can not answer.
