The band, City of God pays homage to the Brazilian roots of most of its members by combining elements of Funk, Dance and Hip Hop to make a unique style all their own. After signing with Bieler Bros Records, a label partnered with Warner Music Group, City of God has been putting finishing touches on their debut album. City of God has been seen performing Internationally, blazing the stage with their electrifying performance along the way. Their intense live show quickly caught the eye of others, leading to sharing the stage with such artists as Shaggy, Ne-Yo, Sean Paul, Ryan Leslie, Flo Rida and more. City of God has received numerous awards for their performance and humility, including a proclamation naming their own “day” in the City of North Miami Beach Florida. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/City+Of+God">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
