Sissy Wish is the project of Siri Wålberg, a female artist from Oppegård, Norway. Her first album "You May Breathe..." received very good reviews. In 2005, she released the album "Tuning In", with the hit "About A Machine". 

In 2007, she released her third album, "Beauties Never Die" to very positive reviews in Norway and abroad.  Although, still fairly unknown, she has completed a few short tours in the U.S.; and even attended the SxSW music conference in Austin, TX in 2008. 

She is currently on track to work with sound engineer/producer/musician, Kato Ådland in Bergen, Norway to work on her next album.
