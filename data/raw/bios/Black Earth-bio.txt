1. A band consisting of current and former members of Arch Enemy with their original lead singer Johan Liiva.

2. A U.S. hard rock band formed in 1997 by Jason "Ving" Calise.  Black Earth have released one album : "That's Right, We're Goin' Ballz Deep " (2008) which features Jason "Ving" Calise, Dave Rangel and Jason Reece.  Previous drummer Shawn Alvear also contributed.
