Colorless Forest is an ambient black metal band from Russia. Having been heavily inspired by Velvet Cacoon. Their sound is unmistakable, consisting of numbing walls of guitar droning, laid back sounding shrieks, and ambient keyboards. The band plays self proclaimed 'Gravity Metal'. The current lineup consists of Abyss (keyboards) and C.S. (vocals, guitars, drums). So far they've released three full length albums and one compilation album consisting of three unreleased demos. Currently they are signed to War Against Yourself Records.
