Cosmo Pyke (born in Peckham, London, UK in 1999) is a songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. His music includes elements of blues, jazz, hip hop, and neo-soul. He describes his lo-fi songs as “spacey, beautiful, and lazy.”  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cosmo+Pyke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
