RAISING FEAR is an Italian Heavy / Power Metal band founded in the year 2002.
In 2003 the band released it`s first “Promo 2003”, which gained them a good deal with Dragonheart/SPV for two albums.
The first, "Mythos", was released in March 2005, and the second, "Avalon", was released in September 2006.

The current line-up is:

ROB DF - vovals
ALBERTO TONIOLO - guitars
FRANK RIDER - guitars
CRISTIAN GALIMBERTI - drums
CORRADO VECELLIO - bass
