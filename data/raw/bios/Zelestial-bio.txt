Zelestial was a Black/Gothic metal band from Ecuador.

Zelestial started their sad journey in 2004 and only released two demos before catastrophe struck the band. In 2008 most of the band-members passed away due to an arson, only two members survived (Daniel Calderón and Andrés Cárdenas).
