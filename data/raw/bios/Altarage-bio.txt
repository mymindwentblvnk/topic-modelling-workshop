Altarage are a blackened death metal band from Bilbao, Basque Country. The band have released a demo tape and four full-length albums, the most recent of which is titled "Succumb" and was released through Season of Mist Records on April 23, 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ALTARAGE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
