Trendkill Method (formerly Gust of Anger) is a Latvian modern metal band from Rīga, Latvia, formed in 2004.

Members:

Nikolajs Arhipovs - vocals;
Deniss Meļņiks (Māra) - guitar, backing vocals;
Vladimirs Korobovs (Preternatural) - guitar;
Deniss Djakons - bass;
Alberts Mednis - drums.

Former members:

Romāns Mudrijs - vocals;
Vitālijs Lopatinskis - vocals;
Jānis Umbraška (Flaying) - vocals;
Aleksandrs Vikļuks - vocals;
Gļebs - vocals;
Aleksandrs Redko - guitar;
Sergejs Karševs (Neglected Fields, Preternatural) - bass;
Deniss Denisenko (Sanctimony) - bass;
Tarass Kuzmenko (Rūsa, Zāle) - drums;
Rustams Guseinovs - drums.
