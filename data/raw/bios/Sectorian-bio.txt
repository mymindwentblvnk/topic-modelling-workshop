Sectorian were a death metal band from Stony Point, NY  Vocalist/guitarist Joe Sibley formed the band with Jason Dean (drums)  and Kevin Brooks (bass).  They formed in 1990 and released 2 demos.  Their first demo was Tears No More.  Kevin Brooks was booted out of the band in 1991 and  Doug Howe became their bassist.  A year later, Phil Lobato (of Nocturnal Soul) joined as a live musician performing guitar and doing vocals.  In 1993, they released their second demo Unheavenly Encounter.
