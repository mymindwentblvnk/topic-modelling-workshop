Hellshock is a crust band from Portland, Oregon that was formed in 2000. Lyrically, the band writes almost exclusively about war. The five-piece has ex-members of Remains of the Day, Atrocious Madness, From Ashes Rise, and Detestation. The band is still active today. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hellshock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
