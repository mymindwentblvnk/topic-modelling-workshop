Scream Of Silence - armenian Heavy/Progressive Metal band.

The official start time is 2004

Main Members:
Khoren Jason Mikaelyan (guitars)
Vladimir Mad-Bugz Oganesyan (drums)
Sarkis Zakk Ovakimyan (vocals)

The band is inspired by the following bands: Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Black Sabbath, Ария, Dream Theater, Rage, Symphony X.
