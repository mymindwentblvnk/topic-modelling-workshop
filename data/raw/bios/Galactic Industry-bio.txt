Group Galactic Industry came into existence at the beginning of 2001 in Czech republic. In 2002 group released debut CD Perfect life from Popron music company. One year later group made the first video Perfect life. In 2007 Galactic Industry released the second album Remake (Redblack/Animal Farm). Group made two videos - Law is nowhere and Lost man. In 2009 GIs releases third CD Key to space Love.

Discography:
Perfect life? (2002, Popron music)
Remake (2007, Redblack/Animal Farm)
Key to space Love (2009, Animal Farm)

Actual line-up:

Loomeer - keys, virtul accordion
Andy - bass
