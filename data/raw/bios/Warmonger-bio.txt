There are several bands named Warmonger:

1) Thrash Metal band from Padova, Italy "did born" in 2000. In first, Warmonger was the "fusion" of other two bands: Warpath [in which Mars (vocals (now) & guitars) and Marco (drummer) played] and Deathfreak [in which Ricky aka Mechlet (guitars) and Silentium (vocals) played]. Releases: "Marching on the Warpath" (2002, demo); "Beer Maniac" (2003, single) and "Mors Tua, Vita Mea" (2004, single).

2) Pagan War ambient artist from Poland.

3) Black metal band from Denmark.
