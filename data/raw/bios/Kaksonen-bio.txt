KAKSONEN is a finnish thrash metal band formed in 2007. Band members are: Koskivaara - bass/backing vocals, Lähdesmäki - guitars, Rinkinen - drums, Vainio - vocals/guitars. Kaksonen's songs are performed in Finnish language. So far, they have released 3 demos: "Kaksonen"(2007), "Tulvat"(2007) and last but not  the least "Tuhkasta"(2008). Currently Kaksonen is making their first album.
