Polish death/thrash metal band signed to Loud Out Records. Released only one demo in 1993, split-up afterwards. Last known line-up was:

Paweł Kornacki - Vocal
Krzysztof Rustecki - Guitar, Vocals
Cezary Abramowski - Guitar, Vocal
Grzegorz Wardzyński - Bass
Janusz Tudek - Drums

Their demo, Epitomizin' was recorded at OKO studio in Warsaw in 1992. Contains 5 songs, fast and catchy.

1. Epitomizin'
2. Desert
3. Paper on the Road
4. Sadness in this Mirror (It's My Face)
