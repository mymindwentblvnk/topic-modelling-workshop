Guerre Froide was in the early 1980’s an obscure post-punk band with the dancefloor hits ‘Demain Berlin’ and ‘Ersatz’. These two songs are from the wanted and hard to find nameless 12” from 1981. The band reunited in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GUERRE+FROIDE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
