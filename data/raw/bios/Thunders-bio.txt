Thunders is a really good time on a really bad trip. A white noise grunge group in the same vein as Nirvana, the Stooges, and Sonic Youth. Their upcoming release, "Beautiful Baby in the Bummer of Love", is an 11 song vacation through near pop song melodies with walls of scuzz fuzz noise.
