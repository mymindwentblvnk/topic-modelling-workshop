Finnish thrash metal legends. A.R.G. (short for Ancient Rotten Graveguards) were formed in 1987 and have released two full-length albums, Entrance in 1989 and One World Without the End in 1991 as well as four demos, two singles, and an EP before disbanding in the mid-nineties.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A.R.G.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
