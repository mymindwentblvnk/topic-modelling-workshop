Intorment were a Black Metal band from Hamilton, New Zealand. They released two demo tapes recorded in 1994 and 1995 respectively. The band consisted of former and future members of Azazel, Blasphantom, Sinfeeder, Masochrist and Ruin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Intorment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
