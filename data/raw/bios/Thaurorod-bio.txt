Finnish symphonic metal where the melody is in the throne of a king!

The band name is in Quenya, a fictional language spoken by elves in J.R.R. Tolkien's books, and it means "Evil Mountain".

Vocals: Andi Kravljaca
Lead guitar: Emil Pohjalainen
Guitars: Lasse Nyman
Keyboards: Harri Koskela
Bass: Pasi Tanskanen
Drums: Joonas Pykälä-Aho
