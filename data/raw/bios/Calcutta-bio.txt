Edoardo d'Erme(born April 19, 1989), known professionally as Calcutta, is an Italian singer and songwriter from Latina. His genre is Indie Pop and he is inspired by Lucio Dalla, Lucio Battisti and all the Italian "old school" artists. He gained success with "Mainstream", an album released in 2015. He's made some appearances on national television and had some gigs on the radio. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Calcutta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
