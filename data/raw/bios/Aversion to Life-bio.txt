Aversion to Life is a slam death metal band from Dallas, Texas.

They formed in 2005 and have released their debut album 'Ritualized Murder' through the label Comatose Music in 2006. They split up shortly after. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aversion+to+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
