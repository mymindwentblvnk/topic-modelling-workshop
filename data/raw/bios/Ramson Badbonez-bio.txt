Ramson Badbonez is a british MC from London who is associated with the rap crew Task Force.

In 2013 he signed to UK record label High Focus Records and released the acclaimed LP "A Year In The Life Of Oscar The Slouch". He followed this up in 2018 with "Jason Bonez", and "Mic Day The 13th" in 2019.

Many of his mixtapes are available for free download via his official Bandcamp page: http://ramsonbadbones.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ramson+Badbonez">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
