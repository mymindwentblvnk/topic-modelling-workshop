Nite Fields is an Australian four-piece band originally from Brisbane, Australia with post-punk, shoegaze and electronic music amongst their many influences. Formed in 2011 by Danny Venzin and Chris Campion, and later joined by Michael Whitney on bass and Liza Harvey on drums, they were one of the first releases on Venzin's label Lost Race.

In early 2015, they released their debut LP Depersonalisation on Los Angeles label Felte.
