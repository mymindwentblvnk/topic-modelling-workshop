Old Goat are an American black metal band formed in Juneau, Alaska in 2000. They are currently signed to Pagan Flames Production.

Lineup:
Stagnator - Vocals
The Doldrum - Bass
Digumdagum - Drums

Former members:
Zimfamforam - Guitar

*Into the Valley of Horns - Demo, 2003
*Chapel of Gore - Full-length, 2003
*Nosferatu & Evildeedlive - DVD, 2005
