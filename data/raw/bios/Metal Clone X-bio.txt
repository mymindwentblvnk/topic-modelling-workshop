METAL CLONE X is a music project of Marty Friedman and Freddy Lim of Chthonic. Their debut album consists solely of J- Pop covers in extreme Metal style. A second album with all-original tracks is also currently in the works. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/METAL+CLONE+X">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
