Low crushing chainsaw guitars, insane "anti-gravity" snare blasting madness, brutal distorted bass, and the gnarliest puke low guttural vocals possible is what makes up this slam outfit. This four piece out of the East Bay Area are new on the scene and are ready to destroy.
