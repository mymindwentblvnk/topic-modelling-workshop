The True Frost is a German Black Metal band, that was established in 1994. The bands lyrical themes centred around satanism and paganism. TTF are known to have been signed to  Hellflame, and other labels, e.g. W.T.C.

The last known line-up:
Chraesvelgoron - Bass, Vocals
S. - Guitar (Sadorass)
Fabian - Drums (Sadorass)

Releases (full length CDs only):
Invoking the Aeon of Satan  - Full-length - 2000
Open The Portals To Darkness - Full-length - 2003
In Eternal Strife - Full-length - 2005
Shadows From The Past - Full-length - 2010
