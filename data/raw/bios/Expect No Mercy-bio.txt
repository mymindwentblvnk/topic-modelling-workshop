Expect No Mercy is a band from Bornholm, Denmark. It is very popular on their home island, but otherwise not very known outside the border of Bornholm. The band members was Bjarne Hjorth on bass Lion on guitar Kalle Brandt vocals and Ruben Hjorth on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Expect+No+Mercy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
