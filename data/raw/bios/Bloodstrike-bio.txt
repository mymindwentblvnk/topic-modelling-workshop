Bloodstrike is a Swedish style old school death metal band from Colorado. They formed in 2013 and released their first 3 track demo, Necrobirth, in 2014. Bloodstrike features current or former members of prominent Colorado based metal bands such as Havok, Silencer, Moth, Nexhymn, Clusterfux and others. 

Members include:
Holly Wedel - Vocals
Jeff Alexis - Guitar
Joe Piker - Guitar
Ryan Alexander Bloom - Drums
Rhiannon Wisniewski - Bass
