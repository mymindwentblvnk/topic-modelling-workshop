Eyes of Fire is a California-based sludge post-metal artcore band formed in 1998 originally under the name Shiva. In 2001 they signed with Century Media Records and have since released one EP and two albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eyes+Of+Fire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
