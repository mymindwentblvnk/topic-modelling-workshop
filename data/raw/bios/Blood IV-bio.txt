Heavy/power metal act from Tokyo, Japan. The band released their first full-length studio album, named "Gain", in 2005 on Bazooka Music. A year later, "Warning" appeared. All band members have or had several other bands where they play or played in. Takashi Inoue is Concerto Moon's vocalist. The drummer, Masayuki Osada, is also from Concerto Moon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blood+IV">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
