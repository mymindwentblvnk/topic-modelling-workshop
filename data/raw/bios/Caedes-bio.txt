There are four bands known by the name Caedes.
 1) A Black Metal band from Germany.

2) a metal/trash/hxc band from Marseille, France, in the line of Dagoba  and  Eths. Caedes' first LP "Parralipses" is in stores since january 2006.

3) a german Matalcore/NewSchool Hardcore band that exist between 2002 and 2007 and release a Demo and a selfreleased Split-CD w/ Mental Illness in 2004. http://www.caedes-hc.de
