Integral Rigor is a Persian band which, since its creation has showed itself dedicated to the technical and rigorous but also so intense variety of metal music. Integral Rigor was founded in Iran in the beginning of the 2009. The band performs diverse of madness and delivers progressive songs blended with dark parts.

"NO MORE ROOM IN HELL"

The first album "NO MORE ROOM IN HELL" was finished in March 2011. The album showed new dimensions of music to the listeners, unique and intelligent in purest form of shape of metal.
