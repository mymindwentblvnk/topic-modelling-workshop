Yvncc, (pronounced as Yunk), is a rapper from the United States. His music features many different styles, such as dark, aggressive beats with unique vocal delivery and more lighthearted songs featuring Yvncc's singing style. Yvncc is a frequent collaborator with producers Mutant Joe, DeliverTheCrush, Ryan C., JaguarClaw and many other notable producers. Yvncc's catalog also features songs in collaboration with artists such as Freddie Dredd, Bloodz Boi, Gizmo, Apoc Krysis, Shark, and TrippJones, as well as a duo album with the latter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/yvncc">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
