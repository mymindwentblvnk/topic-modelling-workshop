FBK is a HorrorCore french group, formed by Kaleed and Kensaay in the Vosge... 
After three know album ("bloody", "atmosfear", "l'horreur est humaine"), the group split and kaleed continue making music in the french horrorcore scene with the name of Kaleedomik and releases "chainsaw attitude".
