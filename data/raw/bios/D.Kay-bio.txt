D.Kay aka David Kulenkampff was born in 1979 in Vienna, Austria and is a Drum & Bass Producer (Brigand Music). In 2001 D.Kay came together with Rawfull and then it was time for Ill Skillz. Tracks from D.Kay are released on Soul:R, Bingo, Renegade Hardware and other, he was working together with Kasra, Marcus Intalex and Black Sun Empire. 2003 the Track "Barcelona" (D.Kay & Epsilon ft. Stamina MC) are landed in the British charts. In the same year he got "Best Breakthrough Producer" prize. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D.Kay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
