The Last Town Chorus are an ever changing band built up around singer/writer/lap-steel-guitarist Megan Hickey. They have recently toured with Guillemots around the UK and the new album Wire Waltz, featuring a cover of David Bowies 'Modern Love' is now available. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Last+Town+Chorus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
