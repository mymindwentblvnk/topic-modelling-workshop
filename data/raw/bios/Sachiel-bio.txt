There are multiple artists by the name of Sachiel:

1) Sachiel was a drone/noise project based in Tampa, Florida.
In all, Sachiel had one live performance, featuring all-original material which was not recorded and as a result, shall never be heard again. The project was put on hiatus following this performance, since it's members felt they had achieved all that they had hoped to.
2) Sachiel is an Ohio based blackened grindcore/powerviolence band, which features imagery from the anime Neon Genesis Evangelion.
