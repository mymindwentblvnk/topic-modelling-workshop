Odium Immortalis is a Melodic Black Metal act with Depressive Black Metal roots - founded on 2001. The name itself means "Immortal Hate". They made three split albums and one full-length album so far. They have a project band with Malus called Somnium Mortuum,a Funeral Doom Metal band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Odium+Immortalis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
