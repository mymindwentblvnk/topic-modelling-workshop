Hidden Silence is a melodic death metal band from Malaysia formed in 2004.  They have released 1 LP, "Black Hearted Spiritual" and 1 EP "Into The Child's Nightmare"

Band members:
Oren (bass)
Sam (lead guitar)
Earth (vocals, drums)
Uronous (rhythm guitar)
