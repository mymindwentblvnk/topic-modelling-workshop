The Cornshed Sisters is a British folk quartet based in Tyne and Wear who weave together pop, folk, ballad and protest music into a unique and distinctive style. Originally comprising Jennie Brewis, Cath Stephens, Liz Corney and Marie Nixon, the quartet are currently Jennie Redmond (Vocals, Guitar and Ukelele), Cath Stephens (Vocals and Guitar), Liz Corney (Vocals and Piano) and Marie Nixon (Vocals and Guitar).
