livetune is a music creator from Japan in 2007 who makes songs which focus mainly with having Hatsune Miku vocals. The band originally consisted of two members, Kz and Kajuki P (かじゅきP), but Kajuki P left the group in March 2009 to work at Capcom. Now kz makes up the entirety of livetune.
