Formed in 2013 black metal band Uitzichtloos specializes in more depressive, very raw melodic form of black metal.

Members of the band are: M (vocals), R (guitar), P (guitar), R (bass), and M (drums).

Uitzichtloos brings in a great amount of sorrow within their raw, repetitive melancholia and melodic sound while also bringing in a dark atmosphere from the melodies. Al the lyrics are in Dutch and covers dark and depressive themes.

Uitzichtloos released the EP "Leeg" (2013) followed by "Nevelmasker" (2014) which is also released on CD by a co-release with Vacula Productions from the Ukraine and Sturmglanz Produktionen from Germany. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/UITZICHTLOOS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
