Running Death is a thrash metal band from Bavaria, Germany.  They formed in the year 2005.   They are influenced by Megadeth, Testament and Annihilator.  They are currently signed to Punishment 18 Records.  They currently consist of Simon Bihlmayer (vocals, guitar), Markus Heilmeier (guitar), Andrej Ramich (bass) and Jakob Weikmann (drums).  For their music, go to their BandCamp site.  They also have a Facebook page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Running+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
