King Travolta is an All-Star sludge band from Cleveland, OH, USA, founded in 2004. Vocalist/guitarist Corey Bing,  Bahb Branca on bass and Jason Corley playing drums, are collaborating with changing guest stars from bands like Rwake, Rue, Bongzilla and many others. To date they've released a self-titled in 2005 and "Kill Fuck Metal" one year later.
