Mortigor was founded in 2001, in San Jose, Costa Rica, by Throgaemn (Diego Guevara) and Nazgul (Guillermo Montiel). They released a single in 2003 yclept Behind the Shadow of the Forest, a single in 2005 called Crimson Night and a demo in 2007 entitled Blackheart Journey. Their last release was Barbarian Darkness, released in 2008. They sing about death, anti-religion and national tragedies. They play a brand of black metal and their name is a result of the combination of Mortiis and Gor. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortigor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
