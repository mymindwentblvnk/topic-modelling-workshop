Florian Ruiz-Picasso, better known as Florian Picasso, is a Vietnamese-French DJ and record producer based in Cannes. He is a great-grandson of the well-known artist, Pablo Picasso. He gained recognition for collaborations with Martin Garrix and Steve Aoki. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Florian+Picasso">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
