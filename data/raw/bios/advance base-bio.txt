Advance Base is American singer-songwriter Owen Ashworth (born April 22, 1977), formerly known as Casiotone for the Painfully Alone. A debut 7", a split single with Hello Shark was released in July 2011. The debut album, "Shut-in's Prayer", was released on May 15, 2012.

Subsequent albums include "Nephew in the Wild" (2015), "In Bloomington" (2016), "Animal Companionship" (2018)
