Formed in 1998 as black metal band from Naberezhnye Chelny, Russia, at this time play dark ambient. Split-up. As a result have appeared recordings (Reh. tape) 1998, Realm Of Blazing Night (demo 1998) and На черных болотах.

Outcome of reunion of band in 2009 developed into new album Отблески тревожных дней (2009). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eldenhor">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
