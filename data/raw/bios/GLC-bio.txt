Leonard Harris, better known by his stage name GLC (Gangsta Legendary Crisis), is an American rapper from Chicago, Illinois. He is best known for his relationship with  Kanye West, being a former member of the Go Getters (alongside West, label mate Really Doe and Arrowstar), as well as being a former signee of West's G.O.O.D. Music label. After various features on Kanye West songs such as "Spaceship" and "Drive Slow", GLC left GOOD Music and finally released his debut album, Love, Life & Loyalty in 2010 on Raekwon's Ice H2O label distributed by EMI. The album featured production and guest spots from West, as well as appearances from T-Pain, Twista, Bun B, BJ the Chicago Kid, John Legend and many more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GLC">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
