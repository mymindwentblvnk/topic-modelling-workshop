THY GREY SKY is a Melodic Death/Thrash Metal band from Oberhausen, Germany, formed in 2003.

Current line-up:
Christian Zanke - guitar
Jan Ehlert - bass
Martin Samse - drums
Tobias Kramer - vocals
Kevin Barz - guitar
