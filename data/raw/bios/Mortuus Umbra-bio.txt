Mortuus Umbra are a black metal band that formed in Tel Aviv, Israel in 2013. They released their first EP 'Catechism' in 2015 on CD through Forneus and Total Rust, and as a limited edition cassette on No Return in 2016. The band features members of Kever, Har, and Sonne Adam, and performed at Oration MMXVI. Lineup: Elad Meidan (guitars), Cohen (guitars, vocals), Asaf Meiden (drums), and Zion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortuus+Umbra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
