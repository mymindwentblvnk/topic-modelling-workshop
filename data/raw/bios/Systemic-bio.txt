Systemic reside by the beautiful Brighton seaside, Systemic takes us through a magical journey of mind, spirit and enlightment. With their E.P already on release, spawning a large amount of controversy Systemic have already gathered a well-rounded english fanbase and even one overseas. They are due to play with Lamb Of God in november at the Brighton Centre.

The E.P has 3 tracks:

1. Mistress
2. Anyone, Anymore
3. Get Away

Systemic Are:

Felix : Vocals
Sam Prior: Guitars
Adam Naiff: Guitars
Jack Eade: Drums
