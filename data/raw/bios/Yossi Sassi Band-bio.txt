Israeli guitarist, producer, composer, arranger Yossi Sassi has released his third album “Roots and Roads” under the moniker Yossi Sassi Band. The album continues the style of previous albums, combining Yossi’s various musical roots – east and west, acoustic and electric, oriental and metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yossi+Sassi+band">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
