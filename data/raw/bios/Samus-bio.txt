The psychedelic indie/weird/metal/experimental collective known as SAMUS debuts with Desangano And The Squat Thrust Coolatron Voyager, a 9 song descent into absolutely twisted stoner-doom-metal/indie rock/ far-out experimental terrain. 
Heavy sludge-rock riffs and swampy slide-blues jams collide with turntable wreckage, spaced out electronica, and killer noisy avant-rock. 

Fans of BOREDOMS, BLACK SABBATH, SONIC YOUTH, ACID MOTHERS TEMPLE, EARTH, and THE BETA BAND take note....
