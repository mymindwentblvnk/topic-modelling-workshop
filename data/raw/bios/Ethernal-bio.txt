Ethernal play Acausal Occult Metal. The lyrics are focused on  Mythology, Esotericism and Dark Gnosis. 

The first demos were made in 2003/04 and released their first album "Grim Ethernity" in 2008. 

After a select few live performances over 2010/11 the band embarked on recording their second full length, 'Arkioas' which was released April 2012.
