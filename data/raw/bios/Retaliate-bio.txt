This page represents 2 bands: a hardcore band from California and a deathcore band from the H8000 scene in Belgium.

- - - - - - - - - - - - -

H8000-band Retaliate revived the Ypres hardcore scene in the beginning of the 21st century and started out as one of the first real deathcore bands in the H8000 scene, which was more metalcore/edge metal in the 90's. They quit in 2006, their last show was on the last Congress gig.
