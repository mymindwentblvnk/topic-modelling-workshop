Soil & "Pimp" Sessions is a Japanese club jazz sextet that formed in Tokyo, Japan, in 2001. They are known for their energetic live performances, having coined the term "death jazz" to describe their music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/SOIL+&+%E2%80%9CPIMP%E2%80%9D+SESSIONS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
