haecceity was started in the summer of 2010 to soundtrack the inner workings of Merle Dunn's head. Taking influence from horror movies to Sunn O))) the idea is to create a atmosphere inside the listeners head and leave them wondering what just happened. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/haecceity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
