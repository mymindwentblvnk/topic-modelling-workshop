Puzzle is the solo project of Fletcher Shears from The Garden, started in 2012 as a separate platform for Shears' work. Since the project's inception, Puzzle has released several albums; including Soaring, Tighten the Reins and Laying in the Sand. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Puzzle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
