Genre: Industrial/Goth/Metal/Rock

Heresia is a finnish band that has been influenced by Muse, Marilyn Manson, Nine Inch Nails, Ismo Alanko and without a doubt many others aswell. Dark and moody, industrial influenced songs, with Finnish lyrics (Tohtori Sarvilahti). Aswell as Glam Rock, Hard Rock and Gothic Metal.
