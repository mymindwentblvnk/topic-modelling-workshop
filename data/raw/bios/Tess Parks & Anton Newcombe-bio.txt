Brian Jonestown Massacre's Anton Newcombe and London-based Toronto songwriter Tess Parks teamed up and released a collaborative album I Declare Nothing on Newcombe's own A Recordings label.

The project had its genesis when the pair met up in Berlin early last year, with a few demoes subsequently circulating on the web, before they released album track 'Cocaine Cat' as a single on Record Store Day. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/tess+parks+&+anton+newcombe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
