1. Skog is a Viking Punk band hailing from the United States.  Formed in 2006.

2. Skog (Norwegian for forest) was a rock band from Bergen, Norway which formed in the early 1990s and folded at the end of the same decade. The group consisted of Anders Waage Nilsen, Øystein Gjærder Bruvik, Eirik Glambek Bøe and Erlend Øye. The two latter later found success as the duo Kings of Convenience.

The band released an EP which consisted of four songs, plus a cover song of Joy Division's The Eternal on a compilation CD.
In 2006, Anders Waage Nilsen, Øystein Gjærder Bruvik and Eirik Glambek Bøe got back together to form the band Kommode.
