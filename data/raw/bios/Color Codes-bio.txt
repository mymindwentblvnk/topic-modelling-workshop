Savannah, GA (2009-2012)

"To note in shrub and tree, in stone and flower,
That intermixture of delicious hues
Along so vast a surface, all at once,
In one impression, by connecting force
Of their own beauty, imaged on the heart."

Color Codes was a band of friends playing a blend of alternative rock with some post-hardcore elements. Ex-members of Sons Of Skies, Maia, Mandelenda and Wild Zero. 

Drew Lagoon - Vocals (Sons Of Skies, Mandelenda)
Walt Shuman - Guitar (Sons Of Skies, Seeking Serenity, Wild Zero)
Tom Schmuck - Bass (Maia, Wild Zero)
Dusty Zahrt - Guitar (Sons Of Skies, Maia, Wild Zero)
Corey Robinson - Drums (Wild Zero)

Drew Lagoon went on to be the vocalist for Native Suns while Dusty Zahrt, Tom Schmuck and Corey Robinson relocated to Orlando, FL to continue their new project, Dead Planets. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Color+Codes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
