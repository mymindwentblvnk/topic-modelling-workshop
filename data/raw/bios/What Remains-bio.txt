What Remains are a female fronted rock band from New York City. What Remains formed during the spring of 2010. They have since been playing non-stop all across the five boroughs of New York City. They are made up of Kellene Catron on vocals; Alex Gil and Pete Nastasi on guitar; Jimmy Dicarlo on bass; and Angelo Modica on drums. In July 0f 2010, The band released their debut EP entitled Lost Wonderland; which features songs such as Sanctuary, Unwanted, and the title track Lost Wonderland. They have played such venues as The Charleston, Ace Of Clubs, Crash Mansion, Fontana's, Gramercy Theathre, and more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/what+remains">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
