Roger Ferguson is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker who works as a Christian Counselor at a large church in the Nashville area. He has been writing pop music for a long time. He has made records with his good friends R. Stevie Moore and Billy Anderson and has co-written with them, as well as Chris Breetveld of The Breetles. This is his first foray into Christian music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Roger+Ferguson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
