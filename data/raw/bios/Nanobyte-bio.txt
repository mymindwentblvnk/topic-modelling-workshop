Cardiff, UK. Nanobyte have been drawing from a range of diverse inspirations to produce some of the deepest, uplifting and melodic Music together since 2008. Known for their beautifully smooth basslines, the duo combine a love of film scores, popular culture and the true system sound to create cinematic soundscapes while staying true to the classic dubplate sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nanobyte">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
