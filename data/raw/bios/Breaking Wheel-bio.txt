1. A band formerly known as Artilery, formed in Boston, Massachusetts.

The first version of their band, Artillery, contributed a song for the 2005 Playstation 2 video game, Guitar Hero. They later changed their name to Breaking Wheel and recorded an EP of the same name. The tracks One For The Road and Shoulder To The Plow are on Guitar Hero 2 and Rock Band 2 respectively.

2. Noise/power electronics from Richmond, VA.
