Ear-Shot is a thrash/groove metal based band from Germany  (Gaildorf, Baden-Württemberg) .  They are in no way related to Earshot.  Formed in 1998 on Dayrunner Records. Members are

Alex Wiedhölzl  - Vocals
Elias Uhlmann - Guitar, Backing Vocals
Robert "Rob" Dörfer - Guitar
Andreas "Andi" Siegl - Bass
Moses "Moe" Uhlmann - Drums
Ill Phil - Keyboads, Samples

Released and EP in 2002, 2004 and first Full Length Album in 2007 The Pain.
