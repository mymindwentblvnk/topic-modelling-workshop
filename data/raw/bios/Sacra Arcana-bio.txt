Sacra Arcana is a hungarian folk metal band. The music is based on hard rock and metal including traditional hungarian folk music and some classical and irish influences. Hard and melodic music complete with a fiddle. The lyrics are in hungarian. Mostly on the same way as Skyclad was, Sacra Arcana is a bit more complex music with the lyrics about the history of Hungary, Huns and Hungarian folklore, legends.
