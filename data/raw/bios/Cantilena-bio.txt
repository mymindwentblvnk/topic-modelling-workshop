Cantilena is a thrash metal band from Tallinn, Estonia formed in April 2009 by musicians from RockCrime and Witche's Brew. Influenced by Pantera, Metallica, Slayer, Anthrax, Testament, Gojira and Lamb Of God, their musical style is characterized as oldschool thrash.

Madis "Mad" Welström - vocals
Slava "Slave" Zalmanov - guitar
Stas Zalmanov - bass
Tõnis "Tonnukaz" Noevere - drums
