Mindfields Factory is a progressive metal band from Budapest, Hungary formed in 2006.

Lineup:
Rob Redway - lead vocals
Thomas Lippai - guitars
Blaze Barrett - bass
Gabriel Pivarchy - keyboards
Peter Matthias - drums
