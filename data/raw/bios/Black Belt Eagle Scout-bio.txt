Katherine Paul is Black Belt Eagle Scout. Katherine grew up on the Swinomish Indian Reservation in NW Washington state, learning to play piano, guitar and drums in her adolescent years.

Her debut album, Mother of My Children, released in September 2018. The album was heralded as a favorite new musician of 2018 by the likes of NPR Music, Stereogum, and Paste, was named as a “Best Rock Album of 2018” by Pitchfork, and garnered further end-of-year praise from FADER, Under The Radar and more.
