9. Caverns were a noise/technical hardcore band from Newcastle Australia. With an inclusion of technical riffs and chaotic breakdowns, Caverns also managed to include melodic post rock interludes in their music. Spanning between 2010 to 2012 Caverns have released two ep's, 'Motion' and 'Stillness' and have played with such acts as The Chariot and Antagonist AD.
