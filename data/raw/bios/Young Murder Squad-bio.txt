Young Murder Squad was a Los Angeles-based gangsta rap group whose members include Young D, Young Hogg and Gangsta Reese.
Biography The group came together in 1995 with Young Money joining forces with Young Hogg and Gangsta Reese. The group became associated to South Central Cartel's record label and released their debut album How We Livin' in 1996. After the release of How We Livin', the group went on hiatus before finally releasing their second album Don't B Scared in 2003.
