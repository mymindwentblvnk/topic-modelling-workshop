Marquis Amonte King, better known by his stage name Shy Glizzy, is an American hip hop artist from Washington, D.C., USA. He has released several mixtapes since 2011, including Law, Law 2, Young Jefe and Law 3: Now or Never. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shy+Glizzy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
