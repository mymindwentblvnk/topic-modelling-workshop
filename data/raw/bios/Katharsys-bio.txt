The Katharsys is born in 2001, when Hugo Figuera, a graduated pianist and Thomas Le labousse, a sound technician, start producing their first drum'n'bass tracks, inspired by the dark grooves and demented beats of artists such as Technical Itch, Konflict... Three years later, after countless hours of waveshaping and savage beats processing, they were ready to give the project a substance . Since then, they've been producing fat tunes and dropping some hard sets troughout wherever.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Katharsys">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
