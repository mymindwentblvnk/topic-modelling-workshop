Lions Den is and Indie/Alternative Rock outfit from southern California's San Fernando Valley.
Formed in late 2012, Lions Den is David Lenhoff (vocals and guitar), Nick Wenzel (lead guitar), Thomas Romero (bass guitar), and Danny Wenzel (drums and percussion).
In April of 2014, they independently released their debut EP "See You Soon".
Physical copies of See You Soon are available now, with digital copies on Soundcloud and Band Camp following in May.
