Musician and activist since the inception of the rave and warehouse scenes.

Founder of 'Rave or Die' and the 'New Flesh Records' imprints, Umwelt has over forty releases to his name, which places him amongst the most prolific, currently active, French electronic producers. The Lyon-based artist, a vinyl and analogue aficionado, continues the work of early electronic pioneers, whilst incorporating his own mode of musical transport. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Umwelt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
