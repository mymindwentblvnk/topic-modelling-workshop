Boundaries are meant to be crossed and two Los Angeles natives are making that happen. With strong visuals, stylish aesthetics, powerful vocals, resounding instrumentation, and a chic musical savoir-faire, this duo is LA's latest sensation. Logan and Zou, put simply, B.C., draw their image and sound from many influences ranging from Gregorian Chants to 90s West Coast Rap and Deep House to Dance/Electronica, these guys do not leave anything out of the equation for their emerging and refreshing sound. With an abstract visual and audible point of view, they are going to be seen, and heard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/B.C.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
