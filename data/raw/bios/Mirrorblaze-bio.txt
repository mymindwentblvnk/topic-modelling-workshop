MirrorBlaze is a thrash metal band from Bangladesh. In 2009, Ovee and Afif formed the band and named it after a split-up project of the same name MirrorBlaze. The band has gone through some line-up changes. Bassist Tonmoy left after recording the first ever single Mass Murder for What in 2010.  Mirrorblaze released it's first full length album 'Triumph of the Villain' in September 2012. The band is all about Tyranny, Ideological Dispute, Abysm of Politics, Mass Struggle, War and All other Villainy this Degenerate System offers.
