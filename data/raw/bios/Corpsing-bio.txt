Corpsing are an extreme metal band from England, United Kingdom. They have previously been signed to Jason Mendoca's (Akercocke) Goat of Mendes Records label and are due to release their next album through Grindethic Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Corpsing">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
