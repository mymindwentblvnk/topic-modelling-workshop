Mann (Dijon Thames) is a rapper from Los Angeles, California, signed to J.R. Rotem's Beluga Heights label.  He's best known for his single "Buzzin" and the "Buzzin" Remix ft 50 Cent.  The song peaked top 5 Radio in the UK and Billboard top 60 in the US.  It was also the Billboard #1 Heatseaker for 3 weeks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mann">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
