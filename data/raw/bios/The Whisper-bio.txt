The Whipser (Johnsy Gonzales) is a Peruvian born musican of native american (INKA) descent. He currently resides in the United States. Johnsy G. plays native wind instruments (panpipes and flutes).  He plays native american, new age and smooth jazz music. He has currently released 8 albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Whisper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
