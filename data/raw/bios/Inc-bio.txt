Led by Los Angeles based brothers Andrew and Daniel Aged, Inc. began as Teen Inc. and debuted in 2010 with a self-released 7" single, "Fountains"/"Friends of the Night". Both songs carried a charming demo-sounding quality with the inspirations a peculiar amalgamation of early- '80's post-punk and mid-to late-- '80's sophisti-pop and R&B.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/INC">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
