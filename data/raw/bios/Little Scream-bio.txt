Little Scream (Laurel Sprengelmeyer) is a one woman band who's recently finished her first album, The Golden Record. It was produced by Richard Reed Parry of Arcade Fire / Bell Orchestre fame.  In 2010 she toured Canada, the U.S. (with Owen Pallett), and Europe. Sprengelmeyer is also an accredited oil painter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Little+Scream">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
