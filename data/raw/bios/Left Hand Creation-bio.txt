Left Hand Creation is a melodic death metal band from Oshawa, Ontario which was formed in 2008. Their first full length album: "Rise...From the Blood of Fallen Empires" is a 9 track album due to come out in late 2012.

Mike McLeod: Vocals
TyDamn Electric: Guitar/Vocals
Ryan White: Lead Guitar
Rob Holden: Bass guitar
Ian Macgregor: Drums
