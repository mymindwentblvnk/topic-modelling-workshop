Aryan Art is a Black Metal band from Bulgaria. 

Current members:
Alexander - vocals, bass, keyboards, drums (Bagatur,Pagan Obsession, ex-Nenavist, ex-Stürm Kommand)

Former members:
Boyan - guitars
Plamen - guitars, drums (ex-Gordost)
I.V.T. - guitars
