FLEURS DU MAL is an historyc italian Rock-Blues band from Roma since 1984. Plays Rock mixed with Blues,Funky,Swing,Funky and Latin, performing lyrics in English,Italan and Spanish.
Over 850 concerts all over Italy and Holland,Switzerland,Germany and U.S.A. too.

Lineup:
Stefano Iguana- Vox,Guitar,harp.
Graziella Olivieri-Sax,Backvocal.
Ale Benedetti-Drums.
Manlio Magno-Bass,Backvocal.
