Payroll Giovanni is a rapper/producer from Detroit, Michigan. Formerly signed to Young Jeezy's CTE label alongside his groupmates Doughboyz Cashout, Payroll is now self-signed to BYLUG Entertainment/Empire Distribution and releases material as a solo artist.

Payroll first came to prominence alongside his groupmates releasing multiple mixtapes inspired by the street life including the We Run The City series.

Payroll is also signed to Def Jam Recordings as part of a group with producer Cardo. The pair have released three mixtapes to date in the Big Bossin' series. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Payroll+Giovanni">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
