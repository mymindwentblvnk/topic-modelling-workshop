Electric Void Summoner is a crushing drone doom metal band from Jyväskylä, Finland. They use no guitars, just two distorted bass guitars through handmade tube amplifiers. 

Members:
Miika Ihanainen: bass and vocals
Jarkko Halonen: bass
Samu Montonen: drums

They have released two demos and one EP
Kosmonautilus (2007)
Penal Colony of Tantalus (2009)
We Are The Black Lizard (2012)

New EP can be streamed or downloaded at  http://electricvoidsummoner.bandcamp.com
