Sculpting Atrocity is a Brutal/Technical death metal band from Houston, TX formed in late July 2009 by Aleq Sheets and Cory Musekamp. They later added bass player Thomas Brantley. After a few shows with the band Tom decided to leave the band and pursue other musical aspirations. In November 2009 they added Dar as their second guitarist and Tim as their new bass player. The first demo was completed early December 2009 and is now available through the bands myspace page. myspace.com/sculptingatrocity <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sculpting+Atrocity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
