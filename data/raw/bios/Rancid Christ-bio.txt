A death metal band from Rhode Island that was formed in 1992.  The band consisted of Mike Barrette (vocals, guitars), Todd Laskowski (guitars), Jim Boz (bass) and Ray McCaffrey (drums).  Lyrical themes included blasphemy, anti-Christianity and evilness.  They released a demo Baptized in Blasphemyin 1993 through the label called Rancid, a label they created exclusively for themselves.  They disbanded at an unknown point in time.  Laskowski died May 19, 2018.
