Eisreflektionen is a melodic black metal band from Denizli/Turkey which is themed with darkness, evil and nature lyrics. Band formed in 2005 by Ensomheth Cold who is also the main member of the band. The current label was Spiritual WarArt Productions before Ensomheth isolated himself completly from the black metal scene. Lately he plays atmospheric black metal under the name of "Nachtvind" and moved out from Turkey.
