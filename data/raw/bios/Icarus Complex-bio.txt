Bio:
Icarus Complex is a Brisbane based Progressive Groove Metal band. Their debut EP 'Dreaming of Night' is set to be released June 8th, so stay tuned for more details as we draw closer to this date.

Icarus Complex have supported and shared the stage with many great bands, including 50 Lions, Lynchmada, Down Royale, Subnuronik and more. 

If you want to get Icarus Complex to play at your show, send an email to icaruscomplexband@gmail.com

Our sound could be described as if Slayer, Lamb Of God, Protest The Hero and Born Of Osiris each had children together, each of those kids then went and had a child together. This child would be called Icarus Complex.
