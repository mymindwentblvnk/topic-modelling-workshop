A Vulgar Picture are a thrash/death metal band from Swansea South Wales.  The band was formed in 2011 and released their debut album ‘The World I Found’ in 2013.  With influences ranging from Iron Maiden to Morbid Angel to The Smiths, AVP are that rare beast which culminates in an organic amalgamation of styles to devastating effect. At once destructive and beautiful, real metal fans will be instantly addicted to their unique take on the genre.
