Chaos Invocation is black metal from Germany (Nisterau, Rhineland-Palatinate), formed in 2004. The lyrical themes are mostly about satanism. Their current label is W.T.C. Productions

Current line-up : 
M. - Vocals  (Bethlehem)
A. - Lead guitar, Vocals
S. - Rhythm guitar
SMD - Bass
T. - Drums
