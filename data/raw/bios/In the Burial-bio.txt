In the Burial are a  death Metal band from Adelaide, Australia. Their sound is very symphonic with elements of black metal and technical death metal. The band was  formed in 2007, and their first full length album, Album page: Born of Suffering was released in 2013. The year after that album, their vocalist and guitarist left the band and have since been replaced by Mel Bulian on lead vocals and Phillip Jarrett on guitars. Both new members are on the 2019 album,  Lamentations: Of Deceit & Redemption.
