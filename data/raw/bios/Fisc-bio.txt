Fisc is a traditional/Heavy Metal band from France  (Metz, Lorraine). They formed in 1981 and had numerous line-up changes including at least three differnt vocalists over the course of their career. They produced four full-length albums, as well as singles and EPs. the band remains split up, with their last Record Label being N.E.W. Musidisc.
