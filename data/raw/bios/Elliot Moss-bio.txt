Elliot Moss is a multi-instrumentalist songwriter, producer, and visual artist from New York. He released his debut album Highspeeds in 2014, which was played, recorded, and mixed primarily by himself. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elliot+Moss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
