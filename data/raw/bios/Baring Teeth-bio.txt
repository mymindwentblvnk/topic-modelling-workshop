Baring Teeth is comprised of Andrew Hawkins (guitar/vocals), Scott Addison (bass/vocals), and Jason Roe (drums). The band's sound melds progressive, technical death metal with strong atmospheric doom and avant-garde influences such as Ulcerate, Gorguts, Dysrhythmia, Deathspell Omega, Playing Enemy, and KEN Mode.
