7 H. Target is a brutal death metal band from Nizhny Novgorod, Russia. They previously went under the name Surgeont but changed their name to 7 H.Target in 2010.
They are currently a three-piece consisting of Konstantin Korolev on bass and vocals, Aleksey Menshov on guitar, and Mikhail Panphilov on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/7+H.Target">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
