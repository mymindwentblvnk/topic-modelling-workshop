Wrathprayer is a Black/Death Metal band from Rancagua, Chile formed in 2006 by God of Torment (guitars; vocals) and Pestifer Fides (drums). Bassist J.M. Retsiela joined in 2010.

The first demo, "In Utter Darkness", was released in 2009 on tape and re-released in 2010 on 7" vinyl.

LP "The Sun of Moloch: The Sublimation of Sulphur's Essence Which Spawned Death and Life" released in 2012.
