A Depressive Suicidal Black Metal act from Bandung, Indonesia as a side project band of Jenath members (Hadesthrone and Blackpraiser). But then disbanded in 2003. So in 2011 Hadesthrone has used Hatelord as his solo project who recorded the single in 2012. And he also involved in several band such as Candlegoat and World to be Ashes.
