Omnia Orta Cadunt is a raw black metal band from Zhukovsky, Russia formed in 2002. They released a demo tape, "The Grand Celebration of Destruction ", in 2004 by themselfes and the EP "Let There Be Darkness " in 2005 under the label Art of Anticreation.
