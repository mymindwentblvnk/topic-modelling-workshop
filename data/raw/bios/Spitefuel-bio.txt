SpiteFuel is a Heavy Rock Band from southern Germany (Heilbronn). 
Founded in 2016 by the ex-Strangelet-Members Stefan Zörner, Tobias Eurich and Finn Janetzky as well as Timo Pflüger and Björn Hessemüller. The five musicians working on a new record and Live shows.
Due to powerful Guitars, Drums, the singing of Stefan Zörner and passionated Live performance SpiteFuel will rock the stages! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SpiteFuel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
