Kurikinton Fox is a Japanese guitar player known for always performing in a mask, this mask is handmade and one of a kind, but they're easy to make. He creates videos of his performances and distributes them on his website at http://kurikinton-fox.com/. Often his performances are composed of multiple guitars, all of which are played by himself in a time-shifted multi-track recording. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KURIKINTON+FOX">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
