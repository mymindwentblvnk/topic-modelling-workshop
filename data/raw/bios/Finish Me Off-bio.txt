A industrial metal band from Estonia. Started out with following the steps of Fear Factory, Finish Me Off has grown to its original style today. Deathy growls and beautiful lead vocals based on industrial metal is what describes FMO. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/finish+me+off">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
