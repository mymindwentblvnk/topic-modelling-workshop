Trademark Da Skydiver is an American rapper from New Orleans associated with fellow New Orleans rapper Curren$y and his group JETS. His debut mixtape, Issue #1, was released by Fly Society as a part of a showcase of their JETS family. It featured appearances from Wiz Khalifa, Young Roddy, Hybrid, Dee-Low and Curren$y. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trademark+Da+Skydiver">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
