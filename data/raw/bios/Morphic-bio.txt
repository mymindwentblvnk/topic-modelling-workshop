This prog-rock / metal act hailed from Royal Oak, Michigan, and featured Wes Alfonsi, John Webb, Josh Devrie, Tomo Milicevic and Matt Morrocco. Thus far, the band has played alongside Powerman 5000, The Used, Trapt, Dark New Day, Cold, Saliva, Kittie, 30 Seconds To Mars, Ra, and Finger Eleven.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morphic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
