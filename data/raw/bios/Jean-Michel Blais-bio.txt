Jean-Michel Blais is a composer and pianist from Québec, Canada. He currently resides in Montreal.

His music is influenced by a range of composers and pianists such as Frédéric Chopin, Sergei Rachmaninoff, Maurice Ravel, classical minimalists (Steve Reich, Philip Glass, Erik Satie), and modern composers such as Chilly Gonzales and Yann Tiersen. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jean-Michel+Blais">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
