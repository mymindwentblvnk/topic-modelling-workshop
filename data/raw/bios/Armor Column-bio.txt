Armor Column is a power-/thrash metal from the United States. Formed in 2007 when SKINLESS guitarist Noah Carpenter joined with HELD UNDER's drummer Johnny G, and vocalist Jeff Andrews. The formation is completed with Ray Russell on guitar, and bassist Tom Barber. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Armor+Column">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
