Bang Lime is a Canadian indie rock duo formed by Joules Scott-Key (drums) and Josh Winstead (vocals, guitar) from the well known new wave group, Metric. Their debut album, and only one released thus far, Best Friends in Love was released in August of 2007 on Last Gang Records. The bands name came from a poem written by Winstead, and they are currently inactive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bang+Lime">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
