2) Sizzle is an IDM/dark ambient/Lo-Fi group from France. Founded in 1994 by Le K. (Marc Reina) and Somniak (Anthony Vega). Marc Reina is also running Facthedral's Hall net label, which issued most of Sizzle releases, as well as releases by other friendly artists. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sizzle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
