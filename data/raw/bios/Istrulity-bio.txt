Istrulity is a death/thrash metal band from Čačak, Serbia. The band was formed at the end of 2011 by Luka Milošević and Stefan Vasilić.
Members:
Dušan Živković - bass
Stefan Vasilić - guitars
Luka Milošević - guitars
Đorđe Miladinović - vocals
