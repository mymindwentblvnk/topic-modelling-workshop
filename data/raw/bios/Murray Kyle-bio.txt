Murray Kyle is an independent solo artist in the Wollumbin Region of NSW Australia, member of The Blissmongers musical collective, and vocalist with fusion group Mystic Beats.
His music is played often throughout the region, with Murray being felt to be a favourite local singer/songwriter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Murray+Kyle">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
