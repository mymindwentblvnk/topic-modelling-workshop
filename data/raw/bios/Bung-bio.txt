Description: Loud, rumbling, guitar-driven rock n' roll with raw, raunchy vocals.

Status Info/History: Bung formed in the early 1990's and quickly became a legendary live band on the St. John's scene. Released the album "Whole" in 1994 and parted ways in 1997, only to re-emerge and play live shows whenever time would allow. New CD released Dec. '05.

Members: Jon Whalen, Phil Winters, Arthur Haynes, Barry Newhook, Justin Hall.
