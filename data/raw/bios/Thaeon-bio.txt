A melodic black-death metal band from Russia. Based in Perm, they released their first demo "In Pride They Lived... With Pain They Died" in 2006. Thaeon are: Deliruim Tremens - guitars/bass/vocals, Sergienko Pavel - guitars, Garr - drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thaeon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
