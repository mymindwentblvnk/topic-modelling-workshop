Dead Flesh is a brutal death metal/ grind band from Florida, USA. They formed in 2003 and their current line up is  Joe Tompkins (Vocals), Dave Carrin (Guitars / Vocals, Vomit Spawn, Phallic Decapitant, Queef Huffer, Dead Centre, Baby Basher, Necrocarnis, Asylumeic Splatterfest) and Kyle Sweeny (Drums (formerly Bass), Cystic Dysentery, Asylumeic Splatterfest).

They released several EPs and demos before releasing their debut full length album "Returning From Decay" via sevared records in 2009.
