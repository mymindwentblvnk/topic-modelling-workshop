Dragens Fange is a experimental ambient  metal band, project of Sventoyar from the band Hvangur. Was formed in Russia in 2007. The original lineup consisted of Sventoyar (guitars and drums), Hynek (vocal) and Ratibor the Dark (bass). 

Album "Human Factor Euphoria" was released on Dutch label Svartgalgh. Some several hand-numbered CDs are distributed in Russia by "Fear and Pray"
