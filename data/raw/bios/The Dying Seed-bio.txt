the Dying Seed was created in 2007 by Loup (ex Krarath) and Sylvain (Axenis) , soon joined by drummer Olivier (314), in the fall of 2008. 
This band form a part of what might be called the Post Metal Progressive scene. 

Stoner, Heavy, Trash, Psychedelic Rock, Doom or Death Metal kind of stuff they have ingurgitate through the years, are now put together to create their own music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/the+Dying+Seed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
