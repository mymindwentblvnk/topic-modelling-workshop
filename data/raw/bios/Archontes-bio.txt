Archontes is a power metal/progressive metal band founded by Andrey "Archont" Fedorenko in 1993 in Moscow, Russia. So far, they have recorded 3 studio albums.

Current line-up:
Andrey "Archont" Fedorenko - vocals, keyboards
Roman Guriev - guitars
Alexander "Zmey" Tsvetkov - bass
