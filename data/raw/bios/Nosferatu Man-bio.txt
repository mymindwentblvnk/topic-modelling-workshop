Nosferatu Man was a grindcore/sludge act from Marquette, Michigan, United States that formed October of 2010. They released one self-titled album and disbanded after a final tour with Bruxism in December 2011/January 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nosferatu+Man">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
