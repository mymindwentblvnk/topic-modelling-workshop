German Power/Speed metal band formed in 1984, Atlain was featured amongst a host of new, young German metal bands that were being snapped up by European independent labels in the Teutonic Metal boom of the early to mid 80s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atlain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
