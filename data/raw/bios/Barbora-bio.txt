Barbora is a Slovak progressive metal guitarist based in London, UK.

She initially gained some popularity performing covers of metal songs on YouTube under the name of "IbanezzShredder". Her original debut EP, "TARTARUS", was released on October 1st 2013, and features guest appearances from Simon Dv (~Obsession~, Nibirus) and Kaya Labonte-Hurst. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Barbora">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
