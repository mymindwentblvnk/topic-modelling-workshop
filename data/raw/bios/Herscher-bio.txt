Herscher is a French drone doom trio from Clermont-Ferrand, France. Their music binds elements from doom metal, noise rock, stoner metal and psychedelic rock.

Since 2010, the band have released a self-titled EP and a full-lenght album Pursuit, both released on vinyl; toured in Europe and North America.

Members:

Bud Ó Broin - synthesizers, patterns;
Mathieu Bresle - bass;
René Cheminat - drums, vocals.
