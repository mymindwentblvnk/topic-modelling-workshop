Manngard is an extreme-metal band from Bergen, Norway. They play complex music drawing influences from most of the extreme metal genres.

Lineup:
Olav Kristiseter (guitars) 
Iver Sandoey (drums)
Einride Torvik (bass) 
Olav Iversen (vocals)

Discography:
European Cowards (Candlelight, 2007)
Circling buzzards (Nocturnal Art Productions, 2006)
