Undoing the End were a progressive metal band from Dunedin, New Zealand. Formed by guitarists Mike Wilson and Mike Lamb, the line-up was completed by Isaac Bernhardt (drums), Daniel Bent (bass, vocals) and singer Cally Hammond-Tooke joined in early 2006. After establishing themselves in the Dunedin scene (including an opening slot for Californian hardcore heroes Strung Out), Undoing the End completed their debut self-titled E.P. In late 2006, Mike Lamb left the band due to personal reasons, and was replaced by David Morgan in early 2007.
