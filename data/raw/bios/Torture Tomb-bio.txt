Torture Tomb is a blackened death metal band from Denton, Texas/Louisville, Kentucky.  They formed in the year 2016.  They consist of Cole Crutchfield (vocals, guitars), A.J. Ross III (bass), Todd Thompson (live bass) and Grant Oehmler (drums).  They are currently not signed to any record label.  They have released a demo, single, compilation and EP.  For their music, head on over to their BandCamp page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Torture+Tomb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
