Unseelie (pronounced un-see-lee) is an
electronic/idm/post-pop project by Shawn Shackelford,
who also worked as part of Pear Eyes, Digita'lis/Phlox as well
as other solo project RENEGADE ANDROID.

He also ran the now retired international net-label
GOZOMBIE Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/unseelie">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
