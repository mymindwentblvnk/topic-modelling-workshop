Lord Tariq & Peter Gunz was a rap duo consisting of Sean "Lord Tariq" Hamilton and Peter "Peter Gunz" Pankey. In 1996 the duo appeared on Whodini's album Six appearing on the track "Can't Get Enough", and assisting writing on 2 other tracks on the album. They had 2 hits in 1998 with the platinum single "Deja Vu (Uptown Baby)" which hit number 9 on the Billboard Hot 100 and was featured on their only album Make It Reign. The album featured collaborations with major rappers such as Big Punisher, Fat Joe, Sticky Fingaz, Kurupt and Cam'ron. But the duo had no further hits and amicably split up shortly after. The son of Peter Gunz, Corey Gunz is also a rapper.

Lord Tariq has a solo album The Barcode that features Peter Gunz and his son Cory Gunz. Lord Tariq had also done a collaboration with Jay-Z and Shaq in 1998 on a song titled "Analyze This".
