Formed in 2015, Chrome Ghost are a 3-piece stoner metal band from Roseville, California. Their music blends elements of doom, sludge, and post-metal. They released their debut album "The diving Bell" in March 2020, and their sophomore follow up "House of Falling Ash" in October 2022. Both releases are available at https://chromeghost-us.bandcamp.com.
