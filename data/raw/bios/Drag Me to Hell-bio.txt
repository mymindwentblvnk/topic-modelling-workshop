Drag Me To Hell are a beautifully designed crabcore band, well known for their superb live performances, where they improvise to put on a fantastic show! From the realms of kenilworth, they have discovered their briliant talents through the art of angeloitus, a great disease, where are born to rock, even with slight heraing impediments.
Formed in 2009 with band line-up of:
Lead Guitar and Vocals - Hugh Shuttleworth
Rhythm Guitar and Vocals - Joe Jones
Bass Guitar and Vocals - Matt Burrell
Drums - Tom Sharpe
Screaming Vocals - Alex Sandell
