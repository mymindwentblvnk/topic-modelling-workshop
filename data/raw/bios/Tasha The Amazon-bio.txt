Toronto producer/artist Tasha the Amazon is releasing her solo mixtape, "FiDiYootDem", spring 2012. No stranger to the game, she's shared the bill with legendary names like Fab 5 Freddy, The GZA, and The Roots. Her brazen swag and production chops have already earned her collaborations with the likes of DJ Green Lantern, Grammy-winner Che Vicious (Wiz Khalifa, The Game) and more. FiDiYootDem will be co-produced by Tasha & Danthrax of Bass & Bakery. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tasha+the+Amazon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
