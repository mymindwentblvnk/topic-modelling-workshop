Created from the ashes of previous band Tasmanian Death.
After the mysterious disappearance of W. Foster and the intentional self overdose of Furious D., P. Dingo decided to pick-up what remained.
After changing his name to BuzzSaw and with the recruitment of old friends Misanthroat and Daemon along with outlaw Bass, they set to reclaim what was lost after the destruction of TD.
