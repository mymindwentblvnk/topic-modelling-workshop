Glass Ghost is Eliot Krimsky on Vocals and Keyboards and Mike Johnson on Drums. They used to play together in 'Best of Boston' at community centers, clothing stores and for kids. Then they played together in 'Flying', and now in Glass Ghost. Their debut album, 'Idol Omen' will be out on Western Vinyl recordings on Oct 27. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Glass+Ghost">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
