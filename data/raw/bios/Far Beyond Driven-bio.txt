Far Beyond Driven first came to be in August 2005, based in Tonbridge, Kent. Taking their name from the 1995 Pantera Album. The band fuses together rock, metal, punk and blues in their songs, and lyrics that tend to be about personal experiance. In May of 2006 they re-named to be called 'The 11th Hour'.

There is also a South African band called Far Beyond Driven (http://www.farbeyonddrivenband.com), started in Pretoria in early 2006. 'You Bleed For No Cause' and 'Never Submit' are theirs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/far+beyond+driven">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
