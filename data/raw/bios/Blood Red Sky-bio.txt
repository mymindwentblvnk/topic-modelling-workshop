Two bands have the name 'Blood Red Sky', one from Germany and one from the United Kingdom.

The British Blood Red Sky is a heavy metal band fronted by singer Sam Mitchinson, with guitarists Ash Gardener and Tim Smith, drummer Chris Parker and bassist Elliot Chambers-Ostler. The band has released a few recordings of their songs, most notably 'Sinful State of Mind', and are presently recording more. Their live performances are highly energetic and distinctive, characterised by extended guitar solos, close interaction between the band and the audience and fast-paced songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blood+Red+Sky">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
