Froststarr are a Black Metal band from Halle (Saale), Germany. They play a relentlessly aggressive and epic Black Metal mix between vomit and sublimity.  Both members, Yuggoth and Natt, are followers of the "true norwegian kill rape torture" movement. So far, they released two LPs, "Gottlos und Stolz" (2011) und "Essenz" (2015), as well a split with Vargsblod (2013), a band Natt and Yuggoth are also part of.
