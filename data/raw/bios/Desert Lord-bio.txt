Desert Lord are a Finnish doom/stoner metal band formed in 2010. They released their first EP "Salvation" in 2011 (available here), and are currently recording a full-length album for 2014. They've also made numerous live appearances, sharing the stage with bands like Goatess and Damngod. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desert+Lord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
