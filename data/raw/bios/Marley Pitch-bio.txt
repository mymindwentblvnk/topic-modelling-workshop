Marley Pitch is a singer, songwriter, and rapper raised and based in Atlanta, Georgia. He first struck a bit of exposure with the release of his song "Habits", debuting as a single in 2017 on all platforms, including Spotify. There, it was streamed over 20,000+ times, landing the star some attention. He released a strand of singles from 2017 to 2019, until further releasing an album in June of 2019, named "Aurora: Journey to Dog Star." This album earned him more media attention. He is said to be working on a new album, given the release of his new single "This Is Bad Timing." Pitch confirmed the new project on his Instagram. The project, called "Are You Havin' Fun Yet?" is expected to drop in November.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marley+Pitch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
