Grabgesang hailing from Germany issued the demos 'Of Medieval Graveyard Frost' in 1995 and 'Blutrausch' during 1997. Bassist Carcharoth and guitarist Asvaaraz are active members of both Nebelburg, issuing the 1994 demo 'Verdammnis', and Nargothrond, releasing the demos 'Im Schatten der Festung' in 1996 and 'Kampf' during October of 1997 with the latter act. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grabgesang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
