Curvature is a female fronted, metal/Gothic band formed in Hemel Hempstead, UK .

Curvature is a refreshingly different new band now based in Buckinghamshire. They combine fast, heavy riffage of traditional metal with massive pop hooks and powerful female vocals.  The band has five members: Matt Jones, Matte Black, Michal Czubaj, Dave Summers, Lisa Swatton
