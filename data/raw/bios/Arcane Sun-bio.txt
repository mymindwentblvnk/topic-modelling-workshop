Doom / death metal band from Dublin, Ireland. Formed in 1994.
Last known line-up was:
Brian - Guitar;
Feargal Flannery - Guitar, bass, keyboards (ex-Primordial);
Mark - Drums;
Paul Kearns - Vocals (ex-Dreamsfear, Fifth Dominion, ex-Eden Obscured).
Albums:
Towards Elysium (Demo, 1995);
Eden Eclipse (Demo, 1996);
