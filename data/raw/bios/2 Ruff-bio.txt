2 Ruff was formed in 1992 in Hamburg, Germany. The group consisted of MC's Lyn-T aka Say-On and Gio-E and producer Simple Simon. They released two albums, "Ruffskillz" in 94 and "Natural" in 99. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/2+Ruff">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
