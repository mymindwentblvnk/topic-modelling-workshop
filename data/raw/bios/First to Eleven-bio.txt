Pennsylvania based pop/rock band First To Eleven was formed in 2009. Audra Miller (vocals), Matthew Yost (guitar), and Sam Gilman (drums) have built up their fanbase via weekly YouTube cover videos and a number of original E.P. releases. F211 have been featured on the Vans Warped Tour and by Alternative Press as one of the “best rising bands under 21”. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/First+to+Eleven">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
