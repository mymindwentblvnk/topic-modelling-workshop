Ontario, Canada (2015 – present)

Suncross is an atmospheric black metal project from Ontario, Canada formed in 2015.

Suncross was founded by Kveldulf, in collaberation with Turenn. The band's lyrical content embraces ancient Indo-European (specifically Germanic) spirituality, myth and ritual, and the exploration of nature.
