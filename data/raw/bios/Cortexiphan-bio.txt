Biography
Cortexiphan is an internet-based technical death-metal band formed in November of 2012, by Steven Rose (US) and Dixon Jong (MAS). Nicolò Paracchini (IT) joined in early 2013 on bass. Cortexiphan is currently writing their first full length album entitled Iniquitous which will tentatively be released by the end of 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cortexiphan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
