Pelagic is a Stoner Metal band from Trondheim, Norway. 

Consisting of :
Espen Johansen - Bass
Espen Ellefsen - Guitar
Håvard Alvarez Herujan - Guitars and vocals
Mads "McTasty" Sødahl - Drums

In November 2009 they released their very first EP named "Psychedelic Dreams". It was recorded live at Skansen Studio, Trondheim, October 26th-28th 2009.

The EP have recieved good response within the Trondheim metal-scene.
