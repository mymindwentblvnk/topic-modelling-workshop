Thrasheta is a Croatian thrash metal band formed in Zadar. It was founded by Matej (vocals/guitar) and Momo (drums) in September, 2008. Ive (bass) joined soon to the band. The idea was to enjoy in music so the band was playing covers, but after few months Thrasheta decided to make their own songs and music. Very soon, the band made their first song called "Slave". Thrasheta's first live perfomance was in February, 2009 in student club called DHM. After that accompanied gigs at metal competition in club called Satir and Moto-party in Zadar. The band is influenced by many thrash metal bands; some of them are Metallica, Slayer, Pantera, Exodus, Annihilator, Kreator, Evile, Motorhead, Sepultura, Black Label Society, Secret South, Black Sabbath... Thrasheta made their first demo album called "Dark enemies" in July,2010.
