Babu are a South African band who make music which straddles the boundary between freestyle jazz and Indian classical music. Babu features Reza Khota on guitar, Kesivan Naidoo on drums, Ronan Skillen on tabla and extended percussion, and Shane Cooper on bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Babu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
