Tristam, real name Leandre Bérubé Bergeron, is an electronic music producer living in Vancouver, Canada but is orginially from St. Isidore, Alberta, Canada. He has been releasing on the label Monstercat since 2011, with the single Party For The Living. His music flows between genres, as his early work is oriented towards brostep and dubstep, and has been changing over the years, turning into a more indie pop/ melodic style.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tristam">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
