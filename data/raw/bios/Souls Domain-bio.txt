Souls Domain are black/death metal group from Norway (Tønsberg). They formed in 1994 year. This band has released only one demo entitled "Travel to the Underdark" in 1994.

Last known line-up:
Joachim Rygg - Lead Vocals, Drums;
Nicolai A. Handeland - Guitar, Piano, Vocals;
Thorbjørn W. - Guitar;

Session member:
Saitham - Bass
