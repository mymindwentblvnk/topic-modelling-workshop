A progressive death metal band that formed in Varese, Lombardy, Italy.  They started in the year 2007.  Soul Rape consists of Larry on vocals & guitar, Tambo on guitar & backing vocals, Guglio on bass & backing vocals and Peterbat on drums.  Their past members are Moloch on vocals, Dema on bass and Maui on drums.  Guglio was once only a live bassist, but now he's the full-time bassist of the band.  In 2008, they released a demo called Primordial Paradox.  Gargoyles was the next release and it's an EP.  Through Punishment 18 Records, they released first full-length album Endless Reign was released in 2015.  Lyrical themes include unconsciousness, psychologies and anti-racism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Rape">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
