Alkehol is a popular pub rock/heavy metal band from the Czech republic. To respect the band's name, their lyrics almost always deal with an alcohol-based theme. Alkehol was founded in 1991 by Ota Hereš (Torr, Kryptor) and Petr “Kuna“ Buneš and sice then they have recorded 13 albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alkehol">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
