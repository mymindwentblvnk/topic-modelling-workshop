There are three bands under this name:

1) Germania is a Laibach side project featuring members of Laibach with Anja Rupel and Iztok Turk. They have not released any albums but their songs have appeared on several Laibach albums. The style of those songs is softer than the style of most Laibach songs.

2) Germania is a RAC band from Sweden.They had released two albums.
