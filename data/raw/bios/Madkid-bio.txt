MADKID is a Japanese boy group consisting of You-ta, Lin, KAZUKI, Yuki, SHIN. 

Their name was first J-Boys 5 but later switched to MADKID. They debuted on May 18, 2014, under
Samuraim Records. 

In 2018 they signed under Nippon Columbia label. Starting 2020 they are also under Future Notes, which is an agency created by MADKID themselves. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Madkid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
