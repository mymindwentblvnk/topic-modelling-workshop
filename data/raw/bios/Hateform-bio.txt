Hateform is a finnish thrash Metal band from the city of Turku. They have been active since the year 2004 with their five-man lineup: Petri Nyström on vocals, Tomy Laisto and Tom Gardiner on the guitar, Joni Suodenjärvi on the bass guitar, and Tuomas Vähämaa behind the drums. 

Their first significant gig was in Klubi, Turku on the 11th of June 2004, with Entwine, Before The Dawn and Stonedeep.

At the end of 2007, Hateform got signed to a new record label called 7:45 Records.
