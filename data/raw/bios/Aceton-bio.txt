Aceton was a Slovakian thrash metal band formed in 1991 in Poprad. They've released 3 demos: "Večný strach" (1992), "Nezmyselný svet" (1994) and "Shadows of the Past" (1995).
Members:
Juraj Slavkovský - Bass;
Peter Zavacký- Drums;
Stanislav Madzin - Guitars;
