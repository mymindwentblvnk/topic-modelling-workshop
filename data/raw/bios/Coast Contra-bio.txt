Twin brothers Ras and Taj Austin, sons of West Coast rap legend Ras Kass and soul songstress Teedra Moses, met Columbian-born Rio Loz and assembled Coast Contra adding bubbling rapper Eric Jamal out of North Philadelphia, PA. Coast Contra’s first single, “Queen & Slim” ft. BJ The Chicago Kid is the featured title track from the blockbuster movie “Queen & Slim” released by Motown Records in 2019. Their follow up single “Legacy” and the “Legacy” album has a soulful, hip hop sound with R&B influences.
