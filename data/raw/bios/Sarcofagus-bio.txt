Sarcofagus is officially the first Finnish Heavy Metal Band. Band's unique musical style is a mixture of metal and progressive. Between 1979-1982 Band released one Single, several albums and an Album Length Video. After 25 years the band was back with a new Progressive Metal Album "Core Values". They definitive "Anthology 1979-1982" was released in 2009. Their final album, Back From The Valley of The Kings was released in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sarcofagus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
