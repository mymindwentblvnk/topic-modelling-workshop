Poisonwood are an American melodic death metal band formed in 2009 in Conway, Arkansas, USA. The band is named after the novel The Poisonwood Bible by Barbara Kingsolver. They released their first EP, entitled Dark Days in January of 2010, and embarked on a small tour on support of the EP in July of 2010. In early 2011 Meghan "Meghan the Metal Queen" Kerin the current guitarist parted ways with Poisonwood to pursue studying at Hendrix College, Matthew Velte replaced Meghan and Andy Honer took over bass duties. Poisonwood  recorded their first full length album entitled Pseudo Utopia during 2010 and 2011, prior to the release of the album two tracks were released Atrophy and Upon the Shore. The album was released through iTunes on the 19th of July, hard copies are available through Poisonwood's online store. Following the departure of almost the entire band, Justin Velte has announced a tour hiatus but reassures fans that Poisonwood is still active and preparing to record their second full length. 

Poisonwood's current line-up consists of:

Justin Velte - Guitar and Backup Vocals
