Ain't No Love, one of the most promising new Canadian groups, is made up of two unique MCs (1990 & Beanz), one innovative producer (Love Thy Brother), and an exceptional singer (Saidah Conrad). Hailing from Toronto & Montreal, they have cultivated a sound that has altered the Hip-Hop and Electro/Dubstep scenes in each city. With a buzz surrounding their self-titled debut EP and many memorable live performances, including showcases at the most recent Pop Montreal and M Pour Montreal festivals, Ain't No Love can't wait to find out what their future holds. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ain%27t+No+Love">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
