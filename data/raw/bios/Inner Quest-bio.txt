The band was formed in Palermo, Italy in 2003 by drummer Riccardo Barbiera, bassist Marcello Butera, guitarist Giuseppe Di sparti and Alberto Butera Marcello’s brother on keyboard.

They play instrumental progressive metal mixed with some classical, thrash, progressive and power.

According to an interview with the band with LebMetal.com regarding upcoming releases, the band expressed the following: "Of course. The second album is ready. We are finishing the pre-productions, so we’ll start recording in September 2010."
