SPLATTA FISH is an up and coming metal band based out of the Dallas area.Active since 2007. we've stayed busy having various opportunities to represent our local scene for quite some time. we have shared the stage and opened for the who's who of the top bands in the genre including a slot on the Sumerian stage at the 2012 Rockstar Mayhem Festival.
