Lucy Elizabeth Dacus is an American singer-songwriter and producer. Originally from Richmond, Virginia, Dacus attracted attention with her debut album No Burden (2016), which led to a deal with Matador Records. Her second album, Historian, was released in 2018 to further critical acclaim. Dacus' third studio album, Home Video, was released in 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lucy+Dacus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
