A psychedelic indie-rock band from Minneapolis Minnesota. Consists according to myspace of Jim Jones (Guitar/Vocals), Charels Manson (Vocals/Guitar), El Ron Hubbard (Bass) David Koresh (Lt. Drone) and Maharishi (Drums). No idea what the real names are.
