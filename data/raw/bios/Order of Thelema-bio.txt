Order of Thelema are a black/doom metal band hailing from Cincinnati, Ohio (USA). The overall sound is dark and atmospheric, melding the brooding tempo and melodies of doom with the raw aggression of black metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Order+of+Thelema">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
