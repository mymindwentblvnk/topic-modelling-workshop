Claudia de Breij (Utrecht, 13 maart 1975) is a Dutch comedian, TV-host and radio DJ.

Last years Claudia de Breij was successful with her work as a radio DJ and hosting the tv show VARA Laat. She did five theater shows. Her third show, 'Hallo lieve mensen' was named after the opening sentence of Mies Bouwman's show, from the time that there was only one tv channel in The Netherlands. 
She also has co-hosts the Dutch tv show called 'De wereld draait door' (The world goes crazy/ goes on). She is the host on the Mondays because the main host Matthijs van Nieuwkerk has an other show which he can't combine.
