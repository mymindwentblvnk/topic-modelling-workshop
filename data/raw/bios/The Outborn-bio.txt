About
Progressive/Death Metal band from Québec City, The Outborn started in 2008 in Gab’s basement. Three tracks / 1 EP (Path of lunacy) has been recorded so far, a complete album is being written by now and should get recorded through 2014 and out in 2015. You can hear all three songs via Bandpage, and through our youtube channel : www.youtube.com/outborning . The Outborn has now a new vocalist, and has a couple of shows ahead !
