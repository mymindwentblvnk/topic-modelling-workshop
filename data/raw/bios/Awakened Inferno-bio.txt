Thrash Metal from Christchurch, New Zealand. Formed in 2008 with the aim of making honest metal, Awakened Inferno practiced and wrote many songs for their debut on February 28th, 2009. Since then they have been gigging all over Christchurch delivering a good set to a wide range of audiences. Drawing influences from the likes of Metallica, Testament, Dream Theater and Death, with a good honest dose of COWBELL! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Awakened+Inferno">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
