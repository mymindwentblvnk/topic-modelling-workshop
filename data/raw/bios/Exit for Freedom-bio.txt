Exit for Freedom are a 5 piece progressive thrash metal band, from Newcastle, Australia. Starting in 2008, and finding a full lineup in early 2011 - Exit for Freedom have played shows with numerous metal/rock bands around Sydney/Newcastle/Central Coast/Armidale and have quickly become known in the Newcastle music scene.

Their self titled debut release, is due late January/late February 2012.

Exit for Freedom is:

Dylan Brockelsby (Vocals, guitar)
Toby Haslam (Bass, vocals)
Blake Manefield (Guitar, vocals)
Luke Wilson (Drums, vocals)
