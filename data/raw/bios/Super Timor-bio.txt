Super Timor is a sludge band from France (Marseille). Notorious for having such a silly name (based on a very old school ad from the Ivory Coast) and not taking themselves seriously at all. Super Timor have released a demo called 'Tout le monde aime Super Timor' (everybody loves Super Timor) which is now sold out.

They will be releasing their long awaited album at the Monarch concert in Paris (20/01/06)
