2) Black Metal/Shoegaze/Ambient/Doom project from Chesapeake, VA
Matt Thyssen - all guitars, bass, vocals, and programming

Formed on April 8th, 2009 as an ambient project, Buer has long since progressed. All music comes from Matt's home studio. The first release was a split with Dark Metamorphosis that was released on tape via Thorn Laceration Records. Buer's sophomore release "My Journey's End" was self released and is available for a free download via Buer's bandcamp page. the latest work is a split cd with Garden of Grief, which was released by Ashes of Paltry Souls in November 2011. A full-length album is in progress. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Buer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
