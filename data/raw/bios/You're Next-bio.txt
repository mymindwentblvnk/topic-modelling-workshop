In the summer of 2010, two pairs of friends from Haifa and Tel aviv, decided to make their dream of having a youthcrew HC punk band, come true. YN was formed and began practicing and had their first show in november that year, followed by many more, with friends from Israel and abroad. Lyrics cover political issues, self-aware way of life and counter culture, supporting Veganism and DIY ethics <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/You%27re+Next">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
