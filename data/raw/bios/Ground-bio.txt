Ground is a name for multiple artists:

1) DJ/Producer from Osaka, JP
He uses Osaka as a base to input the sounds he has met around the world and outputs them to the dance floor. His style has no border and some of his ethereal soundscapes are close to science fiction. 

https://ground-dj.bandcamp.com/

2) Combining crushing riffs, fast tempos, and screaming abrasive vocals, Ground is a band that will rip out your collective throats and leave you wanting more.
