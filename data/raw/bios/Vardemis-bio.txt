German black metal band from Wuppertal, North Rhine-Westphalia. Formed in 1995. 

Current line-up:
Vardemis (Thomas Klarovskis) - guitar and vocals (Femegericht (Deu), Daevas, Strongholde)

Former/past member(s):
The Butcher - Drums (on Verreckt Verräter) (Zadögoat, Temple of Tiphareth, Source of Sorrow, Nebeltor, Wytchscythe, Black Horizonz, Is Love Alive?, Deliverance, Shade of Faith, Somewhere in Nowhere)
