Paper Trail is a Virginia Beach Hardcore band with members from Pushing On and Dead Thoughts Memory. The sound is a mixture of early metalcore (Hatebreed/Merauder) and 80's thrash (Metallica/Slayer). So far, they've released the "It Never Stops" EP, as well as the Raw War 9-track album. http://www.facebook.com/papertrailvb <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paper+Trail">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
