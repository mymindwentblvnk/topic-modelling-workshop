Emma Sameth is a Los Angeles based singer/songwriter.

Her first performances were at open mics around the Hollywood area. Since then, Emma has continued to hone her writing/singing skills, releasing her song “Spin With You” with WOLFE & Jeremy Zucker, which found its way onto 27 New Music Friday and “It’s A Hit” Spotify Playlists. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Emma+Sameth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
