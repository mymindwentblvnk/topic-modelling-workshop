As Stormclouds Gather is a black metal band from Aschaffenburg, Germany that no longer exists. The band was formed in 1996.
Last line-up:
Dalamar - Vocals (Triumph of Dawn)
Corvus - Guitar (Barastir)
Homicide - Guitar
Luciferian - Bass
Overlord (Ruben Strenzke) - Drums (Triumph of Dawn, Raidshelter, Stormhammer).
