History of Anabios begins in 2014 in Kyiv, band was founded by gutarist/vocalist Violencer. Guitarist Jasper and drummer Grin have afterwards joined the band. 

Anabios began to create their own songs playing cover songs of such bands as Kreator, Death, Sadus, Sepultura, Dark Angel and Celtic Frost. Later band began conert activity with their own songs and some covers. 

Basist Morbid at that time has joined Anabios. He played in band less than a year, but recorded a bass for EP "Self-demolition". Work on this album began in November 2015 and lasted till the end of winter 2015/2016.
