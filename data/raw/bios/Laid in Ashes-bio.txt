Laid In Ashes is a death metal / thrash metal band from Bottrop, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. They were formed in 1999. They have recorded 2 demos and 2 full-length albums so far. Now, they are recording the third full-length album, which will be released in October 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Laid+In+Ashes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
