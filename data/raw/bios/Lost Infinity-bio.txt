Lost Infinity is a Melodic Black Metal band with Gothic themes from Ankara, Turkey. Their line-up consists of:

Onur Demirbas - Vocals
Zeynep Akar - Vocals
Andaç Yesilyurt - Guitars
Utku Tantoglu - Guitars
Görkem Turan - Bass
Alptug Volkan - Keyboards
Serhat Özcan - Drums

So far they have released the EP "Laments of Dusk" and the full-lenght album "Raven Loft".

Lost Infinity is oftently reffered to as the "Turkish Cradle of Filth", because both bands have a similar music and vocal style.
