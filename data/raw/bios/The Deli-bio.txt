The Deli, born David Pryor, is a beat maker from Austin, TX making jazzy instrumental hip-hop. He released several beat tapes, Jazz Cat being the latest (released July 6, 2018). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Deli">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
