Death Metal Band with folk elements, since 2010 from Juiz de Fora MG, Brazil. Folk metal band created in late 2010 with lyrical themes based on Norse mythology, European Folklore, Culture, etc. In 2011 the band releases a demo. After the release of a single in 2013, they sign a contract with Sound Age Productions, where their first full length will be released.
