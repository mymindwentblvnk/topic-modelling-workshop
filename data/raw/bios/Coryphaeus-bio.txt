Coryphaeus is a blackened death metal band from Anglesey, UK. The band consists of 4 members, Gareth Thomas (guitars and vocals), Ben Jones (guitars and backing vocals), Andy prowse (bass, backing vocals and synths) and Sion Mason (drums and percussion).
