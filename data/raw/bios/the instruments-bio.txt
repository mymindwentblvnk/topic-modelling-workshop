The project of cellist/composer Heather McIntosh, who has played on various Elephant 6 and other recordings, such as Circulatory System, Lovers, and others.

The songs have something of an Eastern European and occasionally klezmer sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Instruments">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
