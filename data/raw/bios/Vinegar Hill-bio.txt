Vinegar Hill is a melodic metal combo from the green heart of Austria. Since their beginning in 2007, the 5 guys from the edge of the Alps played lots of club- and festivalshows in and around Austria, with well-known bands like Mötley Crüe, Dark Tranquillity, Lamb Of God, Parkway Drive, Mastodon, Insomnium, AHAB, Keep Of Kalessin, Aborted, Deadlock, The Sorrow and many many more...
