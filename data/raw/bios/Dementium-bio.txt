Dementium is an ambient based project which surfaced in 2005 with 2 demo releases (Schism & We Have Lost). Dementiums purpose is to explore ambient and electronic based music as a means of creating specific atmospheres and expressing certain emotions. It is mostly a private project, but makes random public releases as a passive curiosity.
