B.E.T. is a Finnish band from the late 1990's, started from Lohja and nowadays situated in and around Helsinki. Music varies from clean singing easy listening metal to raw vocals and more like trash/death metal. Influences from Metallica, Megadeth, Gorefest and Pearl Jam for example. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bad+Evolution+Theory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
