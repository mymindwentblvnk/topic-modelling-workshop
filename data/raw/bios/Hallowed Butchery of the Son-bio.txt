Original, digigrind incarnation of the solo prog-doom project Hallowed Butchery.

The project was originally a solo project, went on to add an additional performer for Monsters With Machines 2006.
The project was then reborn without "of the Son" in the name first as digital black metal, and then in it's current form. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hallowed+Butchery+of+the+Son">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
