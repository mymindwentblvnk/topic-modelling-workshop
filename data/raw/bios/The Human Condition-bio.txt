There have been at least three bands sharing the name The Human Condition.

1. The Human condition was Jah Wobble's first project following his departure from Public Image Ltd. in 1980. The trio also included guitarist Dave "Animal" Maltby and PiL's original drummer, Jim Walker. The Human Condition toured the UK, Europe, and US in 1981, and made two cassette-only releases of their live shows (Live at the Collegiate Theatre and Live in Europe).

2. Hailing from Nashville, Tennessee, The Human Condition is a melodic metalcore band with melodic aspects tuning in F#. 

3. The Human Condition is a doom metal band from England.
