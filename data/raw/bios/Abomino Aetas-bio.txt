Black Metal band from Norway (Stavanger) formed in 2005. They play Raw and old-school black metal from Norway, their lyrical themes are about "Satanism". Unique in that none of the members have specific roles in the band. They each contribute equally to all of the band's musical aspects. 

Last known line-up:
Consitor Mortuus, Imperator, Wermillious.

Releases:
2006 - Rebirth (demo)
2008 - Sower of Death
