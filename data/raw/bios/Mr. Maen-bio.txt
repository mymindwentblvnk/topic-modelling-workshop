Norwegian producer from Trondheim. Tries not to lock himself down to a given genre, but produces mostly electronic music. Draws a lot of inspiration from the big 80's, and, if all goes to plan, meld it with a modern touch. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mr.+Maen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
