Backstabbing Bastard is a six-piece band from Joensuu, Finland. The band is currently without record deal. The band's thrash-based music is a mixture of brutal and sensitive elements creating an authentic style. The alternation of growling and melodic singing works smoothly bringing appropriate contrasts to the overall picture.
