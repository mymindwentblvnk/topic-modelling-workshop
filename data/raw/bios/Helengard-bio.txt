The duet of Anton Belov (Kauan, Inea) and Alina Belova (Kauan). In 2005 Helengard recorded and self-released a promo “Skiringssal” which is now sold out. After 5 years Anton decide to resurrect the work on Helengard and now they have prepared their first self-titled album, which will be released on Finnish label Firebox Rec. Kvlt

Seven years after their debut, Helengard returned once again with "Firebird" released through Soundage Productions in April 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Helengard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
