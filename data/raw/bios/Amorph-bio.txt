1. amorph (person) is the solo project of Lionel Raymaekers. Based in Brussels, Belgium he started producing music in the end of 2003. His initial compositions began with an acid / techno theme but he soon grew tired of the genre. However, when he discovered the idm / electronica world he quickly fell in love with it. amorph is now producing emotional music which crosses all subgenres of electronica, idm and ambient, focusing on the melodic content and mood of his music.

2. Amorph (band) is Thomas Fanger & Jan Siebert. Trance music (1993 - 2000)
