Abominable Carnivore is a Bangladeshi Black/death metal act from Dhaka. The Band formed in mid 2011. The band supported Orator on several shows including Live onslaught and few other shows. The Band released its first EP " Light Devours Our Lust" through local label "Dust and Guilt Records".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abominable+Carnivore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
