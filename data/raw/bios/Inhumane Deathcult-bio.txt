Finnish black metal band Inhumane Deathcult (formerly known as Path of Chaos) was founded in Kuopio, Finland 2004 by St. Belial (ex Barathrum guitarist) and Xul (vocals). They released their first full-lenght album "To Hell With Your Soul" in 2007, and their second album "On Behalf of Satan in 2009.

Their style has been described as raw and traditional black metal. Lyrical themes are around theistic satanism, misanthropy, and blasphemy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inhumane+Deathcult">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
