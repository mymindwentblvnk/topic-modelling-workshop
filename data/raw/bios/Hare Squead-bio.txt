Dublin boys Hare Squead (pronounced Hair Skwed) have found a way to channel an eclectic love affair with music into an infectious and dominating pop sound, along with an explosive live show. Musicians from a young age, E-Knock, Tony Konstone, and Jessy Rose came together through a love of skating, song-writing, flexing, and bonhomie. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hare+Squead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
