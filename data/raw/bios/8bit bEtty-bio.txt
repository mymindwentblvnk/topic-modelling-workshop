8bit bEtty is an electronic music artist from Wisconsin, U.S.A. who makes 8bit noise into fun complex layers of sound & music. Influences include minimalism, shoegaze/post-punk, baroque counterpoint, and jazz. bEtty currently lives in Brooklyn NY.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/8bit+bEtty">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
