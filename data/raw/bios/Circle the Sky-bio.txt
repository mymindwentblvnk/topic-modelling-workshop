1. (2004-2007) A five piece hardcore metal band fronted by a female vocalist (16 years old), local to the Mornington Peninsula, Australia. The band emerged at the start of 2006 shocking crowds by the petite young lady producing brutal vocals. The band recorded a demo called "Traumatized" with "Melbourne Records" which they sold at their shows.

2. A six piece post-hardcore/screamo band from Essex, England; formed in 2008 consisting of vocalist Fraser Goodwin, guitarist Luke Weaver, guitarist Adam Thorogood, bassist Jack Welton and drummer Seb Dixon. They released their first Demo E.P on June 30th 2010 titled "Summits". With beefy breakdowns, break-neck drumming, powerful singing and gut-wrenching screams these Essex boys are looking to take on the English metal scene in their own way.
http://www.facebook.com/circletheskyuk
