Band, formed by Thomen "The Omen" Stauch, ex-drummer of Blind Guardian and the vocalist of Soilwork Bjorn "Speed" Strid. Combines the modern sound of Bjorn's band with classic heavy melodies, tough sound and great riffs with a lot of synths.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coldseed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
