There are two primary bands known as Ex Nihilo:

1) A progressive power metal band from Nashville,TN. They are currently working on their first album, to be entitled "The Birth of Tragedy".

2) A Christian band formed 2007 at Mars Hills Church. The majority of the bandmembers come from the earlier Mars Paranoids band, The Parsons.

3) A future garage / bass producer from Istanbul, Turkey.
