Industrial Black Metal band from Usa . Blastbeats , dark atmosphere , interesting interludes with influences like Akira Yamaoka , Dissecting Table , Psyclon Nine and others . This album is filled with harsh Industrialized noise , deranged screams and shrieks , screeching Black violence and dispairic ambience .
