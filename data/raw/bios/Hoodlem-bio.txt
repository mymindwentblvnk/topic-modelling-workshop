Hoodlem is an anonymous project blending pulsing experimental beats, soulful vocals and crisp production based in Melbourne, Australia. The duo released their first single Through in July 2013.

Hoodlem, widely known as Australia's front runner of experimental soul and future R&B has recently re-located to Toronto, Canada. Rising to popularity after the release of her globally acclaimed single “Old Friend”, she has received praise by media outlets such as The FADER and Nylon for her captivating production and alluring vocals. In 2017 her latest single premiered on BBC Radio 1Xtra, she performed in Berlin with Majestic Casual and her Saint WKND collaboration “Golden” has reached over 4 million streams on Spotify.
