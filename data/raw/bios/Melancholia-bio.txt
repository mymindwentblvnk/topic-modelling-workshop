There are several artists under the moniker Melancholia.

1. Melancholia is a sludge/doom metal band from Bellingham, Washington, USA.

2. Melancholia is a French black metal band formed in 2016.

3. Melancholia was a short-lived death metal band from California, USA, releasing a single self-titled demo in 1997.

4. Melancholia was also the name of an Italian black/death metal band active between 1996-2003.
