The band DREARY MOWER was founded in 2001 in Poznań (Poland). Band plays thrash metal in a "as progressive as possible" way.

Current Line-Up:
free seat - bass
Rysio Pióro - drums
Krzysio Mazur - guitar
Filip Kaiser - guitar, vocal

Artur Troć - logistic

Past members:
Tomek Tomczak - guitar (now security)
Andrzej Kleeberg - bass
Łukasz Handke - vocal
Sławomir Słomian (SS) - bass
