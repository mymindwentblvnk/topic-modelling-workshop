Blasted Pancreas is a pahthological goregrind band from Larisa-Kozani, Greece. The project was formed by Pedolover, Orgasmic Abortion and Baldcummer in summer of 2008. Main influence comes from Brazilian goregrind legends, Lymphatic Phlegm.
