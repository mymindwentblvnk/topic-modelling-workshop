Vacui Segno (half latin, half italian, meaning "vacuum sign") is melodic black metal band from Finland. Created by J.S in 2001. Vacui Segno have released a few demos back in the beginning of the millenium, now re-activated in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vacui+Segno">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
