Curmudgeon is a hardcore band from Boston, MA. The members are Krystina, Ryan, and Ryan. They released a 7" entitled Human Ouroboros in 2011, a self-titled 7" in 2012 and a single-sided 12" titled "Amygdala" in 2013. They also have a song on the "Wake Up Dead, Vol. 1" compilation 7" on Bad Teeth Recordings.
