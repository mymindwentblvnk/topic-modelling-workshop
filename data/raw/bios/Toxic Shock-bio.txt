There are several different bands with this name.

1.) Toxic Shock is a thrash metal/speed metal band from Eislingen, Germany. Formed in 1986, they released two demo tapes and three full-lenght albums. "Change From Reality" in 1988, "Welcome Home... Near Dark" in 1990 and "Between Good and Evil" in 1992.

2.) Toxic Shock was a speed/thrash metal band, featuring members from Tampa, Florida and New York, New York. They released 5 demos from 1988 and 1992.

3.) Toxic Shock was a thrash metal band from Connecticut. They released one single in 1989.

4) Toxic Shock was an anarcho-feminist bass/sax combo from Birmingham, UK. They released a two 12" singles/EPs in 1984 and 1985, both on Vindaloo Records.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toxic+Shock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
