Sathanas is a blackened death metal band from New Brighton, Pennsylvania, USA. They were formed in 1988.  They write Satanic lyrics.  The current line-up consists of Bill Davidson on vocals & bass, Paul Tucker on guitar and James Strauss on drums.  They released their first album Black Earth in 1996.  After that, they went on to release 9 more full-length albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sathanas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
