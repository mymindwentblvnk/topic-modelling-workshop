In 1996, Judah Bauer, guitarist of The Jon Spencer Blues Explosion began recording under the name 20 Miles with his brother Donovan.  Since than, the lineup features a number of changes, but the style of music has been consistent with the blues rock sound that Bauer is used to. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/20+Miles">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
