PC Music is a record label and music-making collective based in London and run by producer A. G. Cook. It was founded and made its first song available on SoundCloud in 2013. The label's music often features pitch-shifted, feminine vocals and bright, synthetic textures.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/PC+Music">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
