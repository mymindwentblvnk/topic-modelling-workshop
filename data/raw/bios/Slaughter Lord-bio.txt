Slaughter Lord were an Australian blackened thrash metal band formed in Sydney, New South Wales that existed from 1985 to 1987. The compilation "Thrash 'til Death 86-87" was released in 2000. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slaughter+Lord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
