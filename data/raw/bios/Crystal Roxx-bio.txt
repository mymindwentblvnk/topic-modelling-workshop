Crystal Roxx are a glam/heavy metal band formed at the end of the '80s. Hailing from Delaware, the band recorded a string of full-length albums in the '90s before disbanding. The band has a similar style to skid row,  Sven Gali,  Kick Axe,  Firehouse et al.
