Darkness Over Depth (王三溥) is a dark metal project from Cheng Du, China. Formed in 2002, the band consists of Kasasis who plays all the instruments. To date, Kasasis has released 1 demo (2002) and 3 full length albums. Darkness Over Depth (2003), The Dream (2006) & Heng Yun .
