Through the Mist, is a Melodic Death Metal band playing out of Grand Rapids, Michigan (USA).  Founded by brothers Ryan (Guitar and Vocals) and Tim Wicklund (Drums) and Fritz Peterson (Guitar, Vocals), the band has enjoyed modest success on the West Michigan Metal scene. They recently added Wayne Grenier on Bass to complete their line up. Their crowning achievement to date has been opening for the mighty Dark Trainquillity in Grand Rapids.
