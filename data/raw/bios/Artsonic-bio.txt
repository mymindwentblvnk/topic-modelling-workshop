French nu-metal band formed in Paris, in 1992. After releasing five albums, the band split in 2003. The band featured drummers Pierre Belleville (Lofofora, Destruction Incorporated) and Dirk Verbeuren (Scarve, Soilwork, One-Way Mirror, Phazm, ...) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Artsonic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
