Hessian Crucible is a 3 piece death metal band from Springfield, Missouri. The band was formed in 2008 and all original members still currently play in the band. Their sound is a blend of metal, covering almost all forms of the genre. They are currently promoting their debut EP, "Morbidity," and are preparing to record their self titled first full length record at Zombielife Sudios.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hessian+Crucible">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
