There is more than one artist with this name, including; 
1) Frozen Tear was a British folk group from Plymouth that was active in the  late 60s. 
2) Frozen Tear was a Finnish death metal band active in the mid-90s. 

1) Frozen Tear was a British folk group active in the late 60s. Formed in Plymouth in 1963 by Tony Way (vocals, bass), Leeson Burt (lead guitar), John Morgan (rhythm guitar), and Paul Riley (drums), their biggest 'claim to fame' was to be on the same billing as Fleetwood Mac and The Herd at Pennycross Stadium in Plymouth, May 1969. 

2) Frozen Tear was a melodic death metal band from Finland, evolved from the black metal band Thorny Wreath. Frozen Tear released only one demo tape "White With Frost" in 1995. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Frozen+Tear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
