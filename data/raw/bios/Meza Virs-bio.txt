Meza Virs was a metal band from Singapore. Their musical style is a combination of black and death metal with a strong symphonic influence. Formed in 2002, they released one demo and their only full-length album "Vida Sacrificium Meum Est" in 2006. It was announced in June of 2014 that they had split.
