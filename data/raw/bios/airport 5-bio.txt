Airport 5 was a collaboration between Guided by Voices founder Robert Pollard and sometime partner Tobin Sprout. Collaborating chiefly by mail, Sprout provided completed backing tracks which he composed, and Pollard added his own lyrics and vocals. Airport 5 released two 7-inch singles and two full-length albums between 2001 and 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Airport+5">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
