Christian Bertoncelli is the legendary Argentinian vocalist who sings with bands such as Renacer, Azaroth and formerly sung with Horcas and Imperio. He released his first solo album titled "Leyendas de Amor y Sangre" under the name Bertoncelli in 2011.

Current members:

Christian Bertoncelli - Vocals 
Emiliano Ortiz  - Guitars
Charlie Giardina - Bass
Gabriel Wright - Keyboards
