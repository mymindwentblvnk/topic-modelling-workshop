BIO
Kill The Sphinx were born in Summer 2012 from an idea by Daniele Cauda and Mattia Giovanni Accinni.
Fabrizio Prelini immediately joined the project, sharing the band's spirit.
Between September and November they wrote their first three songs and in November they entered to studio to record their first EP: Free Conception.

Free Conception, featuring the tracks Sin Sets Free, Have No Fear and Sunflowers, was recorded at Alarm Studio in Turin (IT).

Music written by Mattia Giovanni Accinni.
Lyrics written by Daniele Cauda.
Programming and synths arranged by Fabrizio Prelini.

Actually the guys are writing new songs for their first full-lenght album
