The Ex in collaboration with some of the world's most powerful and performative horn players for some unbelievable in-the-red swing time. 
Brass Unbound comprise legendary Dutch horn player Ab Baars, Swedish force of nature Mats Gustafsson, Chicago jazz heavyweight Ken Vandermark, Italian wild card Roy Paci and boundary busting classical/futurist Wolter Wierbos. 
This explosive combination of   dynamics, groove, ecstatic noise, free jazz, super tense rock and no bullshit attitude makes it all the more dexterous and dangerous <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Ex+&+Brass+Unbound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
