Depressive black metal from Belarus.
Chernolesie was formed in the Belarusian city of Brest Gantsevichi cold winter of 2007. The group is composed of three members, Sirius - guitar, drums, keyboards; Wintermoon - guitar, bass, Necrofrost - vocals, lyrics. April 16, 2010 released album "Illusion Of Light-Illusion Of Life.
