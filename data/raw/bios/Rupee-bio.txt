Rupert Clarke (born September 10, 1975), best known by his stage name Rupee, is a soca musician from Barbados. He was born in military barracks in Germany to a German mother and a Barbadian father, who was serving in the British armed forces at the time. He is signed to Atlantic Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rupee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
