A band project from Bandung / Parijs van Java formed in 2001 and created by Ôrløck. 

The band consists of two musicians, Ôrløck (Vocal / All Instruments) and Dice (Synthesizer / Programming). 

As a founder Ôrløck collecting piece by piece of Clavicula Nox historical activities and re-united this band become one-man band inviting another musicians for live session/performance.

Clavicula Nox's music spreading from influences become more drone-doom, depressive, and experimentalist.
