Massive Wagons is a British hard rock band from Lancashire who love playing loud hard rock. Signed to Rock/Metal Label Casket Music, their first Album ‘Fire It Up’ was released in 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Massive+Wagons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
