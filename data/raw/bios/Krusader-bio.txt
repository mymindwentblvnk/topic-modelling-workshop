Krusader is a new Brazilian symphonic power metal project which is set to release its first album ("Angus") in 2006.

Official website: 
http://www.krusader.net.

Full Story: 
Rick Ricci, frontman and vocalist for fellow Brazillian ensemble Skyscraper, was soon asked to join a new project named Krusader. Recently, the Symphonic Power metal band released a concept album on a popular fantasy saga known as Angus which has a large "cult" following in South America, Korea, Japan, and Italy.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Krusader">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
