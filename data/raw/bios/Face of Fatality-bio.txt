Face of Fatality are a scotlish death metal band formed in late 2006. Influenced by Death metal bands like Cannibal Corpse, Amon Amarth and Decapitated. A lineup change in 2008 seeing the depature of Greg and Scott Johnston (Original Drummer and Bassist).
