Henoheno is a Toronto-based alternative/bedroom pop artist, who releases music independently and on the Bandcamp label Fox Food Records. Henoheno has also worked on a split release tape in the past released on Slovakian label Z Tapes, which featured other artists pet cemetery, brittle brian, and francie cool. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Henoheno">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
