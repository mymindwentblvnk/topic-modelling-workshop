Dominic David Fike (Born December 30, 1995) is an American Singer and Alternative Hip-Hop artist from Naples, Florida.

Dominic Fike's six-track EP "Don't Forget About Me, Demos [Explicit]" was released on October 16, 2018 on Columbia Records after a label bidding war. Fike's mixture of rock and hip hop with an indie edge has quickly earned him fans across music streaming platforms. As of November 5, 2018, the most popular track on Fike's EP, "3 Nights," had been streamed about 2.7 million times.
