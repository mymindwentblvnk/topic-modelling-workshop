Alice Longyu Gao is a resilient DJ, songwriter and performance art practitioner girl. Longyu means “dragon and universe,” her last name Gao means “Magnificent.”
Alice wrote and produced her debut single Princess of Manifestation in Japanese. The song was premiered by V Magazine and featured by i-D Magazine. Her most notable release is 'Rich Bitch Juice' produced by frequent collaborator Dylan Brady from 100 Gecs.

Her music has been co-signed by Lady Gaga, The Weeknd & Selena Gomez. She toured and opened for Dorian Electra, 100 Gecs, Cashmere Cat, and more.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alice+Longyu+Gao">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
