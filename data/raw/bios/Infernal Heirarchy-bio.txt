Infernal Heirarchy was born in 1988 under their original name; False Prophet & hailed from Greensboro, North Carolina. In 1989 False Prophet released a Tape Demo named "Sign Of The Cross," that 4-Track Demo was followed by a 1991 Re-Recording with an additional 2 new songs named "The Second Death." In 1993 False Prophet came with an incredible 7" Single on Rage Records & a name change; Infernal Heirarchy. The 2 song 7" Single Infernal Heirarchy released was Self-Titled an an astonishing masterpiece of Blackened Death Thrash Metal. That 7" Single came to be Infernal Heirarchy's only release; they then split up.
