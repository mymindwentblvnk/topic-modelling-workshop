Whirlwind Heat is a three piece rock band from Michigan. The band features vocalist/keyboardist David Swanson, bassist Steve Damstra, drummer Brad Holland and no guitarist. They toured with The White Stripes, Yeah Yeah Yeahs, The Kills and Beck. They work with Terry Richardson to design their artworks, video clips and clothes.

Their first album  Do Rabbits Wonder was recorded in four days in 2002 and produced by Jack White from The White Stripes. All tracks are names of colors. Back to studios in 2004, they recorded Flamingo Honey a ten minutes, ten tracks EP. Their new album Types of Wood was released April 24th in the UK and May 16th in the US.
