December Wolves are a Boston, USA based black metal band formed in 1993 in Wilmington, Massachusetts. So far December Wolves have released 3 albums. On newer material the group goes in a more experimental/industrial 'post-black' direction.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/December+Wolves">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
