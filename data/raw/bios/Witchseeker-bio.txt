Witchseeker is a power trio Heavy Metal band formed in late 2012, Singapore. The lineup was officially completed in the first quarter of 2013. Witchseeker features Sharizal on drums, Brandon Tan on guitars and lead by their ring leader, Sheikh Spitfire on bass and vocals. The band is seen returning the sounds of the traditional heavy metal and thrash metal bands of the 80s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Witchseeker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
