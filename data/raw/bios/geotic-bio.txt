Geotic is an alias of American musician and producer Will Wiesenfeld, whose work under this moniker has a pronounced emphasis on ambience and minimalism, unlike his more pop-oriented work as Baths and {Post-foetus}. Many of his records are available via his Bandcamp page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Geotic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
