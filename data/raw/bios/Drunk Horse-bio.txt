Formed in 1998, Drunk Horse is a rock band from Oakland, CA. The band released several albums on Man's Ruin Records before the label went out of business. The band then moved on to Tee Pee Records with 2003's Adult Situations, which was followed in 2005 by In Tongues. Drunk Horse has toured extensively, in the US and Europe, and has played Austin, Texas' South by Southwest Festival several times. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drunk+Horse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
