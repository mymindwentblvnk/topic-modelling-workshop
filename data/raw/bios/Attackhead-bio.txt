Attackhead is an old school thrash metal band from Southern California.  

Attackhead receives regular radio airplay on Internet and Terrestrial radio on over 50 stations worldwide. Attackhead has been featured/interviewed in numerous magazines and webzines throughout the world. The band tours regularly and performs Internationally. 

Attackhead’s musical influences are old school heavy metal such as Testament, Iron Maiden, Slayer, Megadeth and Anthrax, to name a few. Attackhead strives to create an entertaining stage show along with a “just like the CD” musical performance. 

In April 2010 Attackhead headed to Tennessee to record their 3rd CD with Twisted Hillbilly Records. The CD was originally released on Jan 25, 2011 and a remixed and mastered version with all new vocals was released in August 2011. Their fist vinyl was released in March 2012.  Along with our regular shows and national tours Attackhead performs for the US military on a regular basis and has performed numerous times for the Army, Navy, Marines and the USO. 

Attackhead supports the Iraq and Afghanistan Veterans of America, the Wounded Warrior Project and the Melanoma Research Foundation.  Attackhead is endorsed by Spider Energy Drink, Tsunami Cables, Coffin Case, Spear Guitars, Jim Dunlop, Rock and Roll GangStar Apparel and Electro Voice.
