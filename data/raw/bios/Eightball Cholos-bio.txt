Latin-American Christian thrash metal band, originally formed in 1983 as The Warning. They were formerly based in California, though by around the time they changed their name to Eightball Cholos in 1996, that is when the guys relocated to Sandston, Virginia.

Features former Dio Guitarist Tracy G on all music with Burrito performing all Vocals. They released one album in 1996 entitled, 'Satan's Whore" on Morphine Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eightball+Cholos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
