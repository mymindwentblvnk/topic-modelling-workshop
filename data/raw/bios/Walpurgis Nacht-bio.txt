Walpurgis Nacht  was a Portuguese Black Metal band that formed in 1998, their only release was called "Dramas, Fields and Fiends" and was a demo released in 2002 .After the dissolution, Helder and Tiago formed Hordes of Yore.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Walpurgis+Nacht">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
