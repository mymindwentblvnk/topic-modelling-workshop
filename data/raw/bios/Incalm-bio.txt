Incalm is a progressive metal band formed in Blatnice pod Svatym Antoninkem, Czech Republic in the year of 2001.  The band released their first demo, entitled "Emergency" in 2001 and released their second demo, "Save My Soul" in 2004. Incalm has never released a full-length album and is currently unsigned. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Incalm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
