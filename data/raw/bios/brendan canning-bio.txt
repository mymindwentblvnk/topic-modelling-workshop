Brendan Canning (born 1969) is a founding member of Broken Social Scene and a veteran indie rock performer who has been a member of various notable bands including By Divine Right, Blurtonia, Valley of the Giants, Cookie Duster, hHead and Len. His debut solo album, which is part of the "Broken Social Scene Presents..." series, is titled "Something For All Of Us" and was released on July 22, 2008 in America. His new album, You Gots 2 Chill, was released October 2013 on SQE Music.
