Dissector was formed in May 2011 as a old school death metal band.Later because of the same interest of the members,they changed the genre to thrash metal.Dissector performed their first show in 2011 called "Riot of metal" . In 2013 they released their debut ep called "Local Maniac Syndrome".

Current lineup:
Vocals/Bass :  Parvez Shetu
Guitar : Affnan Bin Aman
Guitar : Nafis Iqbal
Drums : Muktadir Anzan
