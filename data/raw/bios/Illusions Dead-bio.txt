A Finnish metal band formed in 2009. Influenced by bands such as Insomnium, Dissection, Anata and Intestine Baalism.

The self-titled demo was released digitally 9th June, 2013.

Lineup:
Akseli Auralinna - drums
Mikael Aalto - bass (2010-)
Johannes Katajamäki - guitars, vocals
Jake Lastujoki - guitars (2012-)

Ex-members:
Victor Slätis - guitars (2009)
