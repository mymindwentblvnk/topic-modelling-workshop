Leah Kate is an American celebrity singer, born on September 8, 1995. Prior to the limelight, she began posting music to her self-titled YouTube channel in September 2018. Her single “Fuck Up the Friendship” has surpassed 62 Million streams on Spotify. She released her debut EP, “What Just Happened?” on October 1st 2021, which includes 7 tracks. Later in 2022, her song “10 Things I Hate About You” went viral on TikTok and it already has more than 79 Million streams on Spotify. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leah+Kate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
