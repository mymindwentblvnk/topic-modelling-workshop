Elizabethan Walpurga was a band from Brazil (Pernambuco state) and they played a sort of Black Metal with melodic points and some heavy riffs. They released the demos "...The Darkness... The Wrapping
Tranquillity..." (demo 1996) and "Desire" ( Cd-demo 2000). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elizabethan+Walpurga">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
