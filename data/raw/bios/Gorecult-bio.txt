Gorecult is a brutal death metal/gore metal band from Argentina (Ramos Mejía, Buenos Aires) , formed in 2007. 

Current line-up: 
Fernando Martinez - guitar
Nekroangel - drums (Necroabortion)
Fernando Mustafa - vocals
Matías Cateura - guitar, backing vocals
Lagarto - bass 

Former/past member:
Martín Mármol - Vocals (Low End) 

Discography:
Blood Retribution - Demo, 2007  
Murder Notes - EP, 2009
