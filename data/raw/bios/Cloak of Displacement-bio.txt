Cloak of Displacement is a black metal/doom/drone/noise project of former Gargotheron, Schlitzkrieg, and Reverend Doom vocalist T. Bjorklund. All recordings are self made and released and detail the end of your existence.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cloak+Of+Displacement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
