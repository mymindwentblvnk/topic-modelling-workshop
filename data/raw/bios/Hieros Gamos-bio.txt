Hieros Gamos arisen at 2002 like side line project of Gudrun Snake (vocal&performing) and 677 (keyboards&programming) - well know members of darkwave band the Ľahká Múza from Slovakia. Hieros Gamos is pure electro-symphonic-ritual music with strong and magic voice of Gudrun Snake. In 2006 they released debut album "Fetix Remix".
