Tokyo based UK native submerse is an esteemed producer/DJ with a distinct and recognizable sound that traverses Hip Hop, Jungle, Footwork and Electronic music whilst being punctuated by a myriad of unique and personal influences from Video Game music to Jazz.

Amidst a plenitude of productions, submerse has also seen support emerge from influential media titans such as Pitchfork and performed on Boiler Room and BBC Radio 1. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/submerse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
