There are 3 bands named Murder Squad.

1)Murder Squad is a Swedish death metal band that was formed in 1993 in Stockholm. It is a side project of two Dismember members and two Entombed members. The band is highly influenced by the American group Autopsy. In fact they started out as an Autopsy cover band. On their second album "Ravenous, Murderous" , Chris Reifert of Autopsy appears as a guest singer.

Members:

Matti Kärki - vocals
Richard Cabeza - bass
Uffe Cederlund - guitars
Peter Stjärnvind - drums

Records and distributed by Def Jam Recordings. The album features production by Ant Banks, DJ Gripp, G$ Money and Prodeje.
