Deniro Farrar grew up in Charlotte NC. Black Flag Records released his first 31 track mixtape entitled “Feel This”. Over the past year Deniro has opened up for Mike Posner, NAS and Damien Marley, Young Jeezy, Public Enemy, Wale, Chiddy Bang, and multiple stops with PAC DIV, he also toured on select dates for the Smokers Club Tour (Big K.R.I.T, Smoke DZA and Curren$y) as well Wiz Khalifa’s Wake in Bake Tour. On his sophomore mixtape entitled "Destiny Altered" Deniro works with some amazing producers, Ryan Hemsworth, David Heartbreak, SPADEZ, Silky Johnson, Storm Watkins, Bilal AMG & Oswin SM. Destiny.altered also features fellow emcees: Shady Blaze, Nacho Picasso, ST 2Lettaz of G​-​Side, Rapper Big Pooh and  Emilio Rojas. His last release The Patriarch, released in February received praise form critics and fans, so much so that The Patriarch II will be released on "June 18th" 2013.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deniro+Farrar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
