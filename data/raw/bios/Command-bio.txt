Command can refer to two different bands/artists:

1. Command, a National Socialist Black Metal group from Brazil. Previously the band was known as Resistencia 88 under which they released a single demo. In 1998 they changed their name to Command and released their first demo Kulturkampf. Members from this band also have a joint project with fellow brazillian comrades from Evil known as Dethroned Christ.
