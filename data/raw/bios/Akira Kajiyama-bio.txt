Neoclassical/shred guitarist from Yamaguchi, Japan. He first started with heavy metal outfit Precious in the early 80's. In 1998, Kajiyama and former Anthem vocalist Yukio Morikawa released a tribute album to Rainbow in the name of "Niji Densetsu". Kajiyama is well known outside of Japan as well, since he worked with Glenn Hughes and Joe Lynn Turner. Also Kajiyama and Morikawa formed Goldbrick in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Akira+Kajiyama">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
