Atomik Destruktor was formed in Braga, Portugal, May 2007, five friends with rage for thrash and rock n’ roll who still to this day have a good time getting high and drunk together.
Right from the start they compose original, aggressive and violent music.
In 2015, they release an album Unnatural Disorder with 8 songs that are pure thrash. Their destruction, intoxication and appetite for thrashing continue.

Atomik Destruktor are:
Turtle – Vocals
Gibson – Rhythm/Lead Guitar
Pedraça – Rhythm/Lead Guitar, Vocals (backing)
Marco – Bass
