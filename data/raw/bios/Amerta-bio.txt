Formed in a fickle weather of Melbourne by the took-it-too-far duo, Raja Humuntar and Auliya Akbar, Amerta ended up in the equator-straddling of Jakarta. Utilizing a noxious blend of doom and post metal, they eventually expanded into a four-piece with Indra Purba and Anida Bajumi joining the group.
