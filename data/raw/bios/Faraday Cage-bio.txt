Faraday Cage are a four piece Alternative/Grunge/Post Rock band from London. They set our on a goal to revolutionize music today as they think it is full of corrupt and commercial puppets not artists. Powered by a raw sound you probably haven't heard before, Faraday Cage's task is to change the face of music and it is even more evident as soon as they pick up our instruments. Our sense of experimentation creates soundscapes, this makes our music Interesting and sets us aside from most bands around today.
