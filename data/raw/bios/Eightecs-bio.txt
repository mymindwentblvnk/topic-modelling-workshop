Influenced by electronic acts Jean-Michel Jarre and Giorgio Moroder as well as rock bands Nine Inch Nails, White Zombie and Rage Against The Machine, Gothenburg, Sweden's Eightecs morphs retro-futuristic synthesizers into a high energy four-to-the-floor burst. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eightecs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
