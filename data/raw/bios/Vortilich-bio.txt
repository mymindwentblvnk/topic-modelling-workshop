Founded in 2009 and inspired by Old school Death Metal, in particular bands such as Autopsy, (Early) Carcass, (Early) Xysma, Repulsion and Abhorrence.
Vortilich (pronounced Vort-e-Lyk) consists of Ty (Vocals/Bass) and Kieran (Guitar).
