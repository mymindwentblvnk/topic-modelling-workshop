Blessed Tyranny is a mix of all types of metal on a search to play shows and bang heads. Caution: The Surgeon General of Extreme Metal warns that the viewing of this band is equal to 666 bloody orgasms with the imminent ripping of faces. Started in late 2010 by Viglundr and Necrohorn, after the demise of their previous band Black Scourge. After searching for a guitarist, Necrohorn knew of his good friend Dan who soon joined, then Cameron joined, who was good friends with Viglundr and Necrohorn.
