Lunar Shadow is German Heavy Metal band Formed in 2014.
They describe them selves as Epic classic metal goes head-to-head with blazing Scandinavian black metal on Lunar Shadow’s firebrand sophomore effort, The Smokeless Fires.
Band Members:
Max ‘Savage’ Birbaum: Lead Guitars
Robert Röttig: Vocals
Kay Hamacher: Rhythm Guitars
Sven Hamacher: Bass
