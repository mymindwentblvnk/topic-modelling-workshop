Naturi Naughton was one of the original members of the female pop trio 3LW alongside Adrienne Bailon and Kiely Williams (who went on to join The Cheetah Girls). She notably played the lead in the remake of "Fame." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Naturi+Naughton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
