Astral Winter was formed in 2008 as a melodic black metal band.
2009 -  Illustratons Of Death (EP/Demo) was released on Immortal Frost Poductions.
2010 - Recording started on the upcoming full length album Winter Enthroned.
2011 - Winter Enthroned was released via Immortal Frost productions in late September
2012 - Perdition, a dark ambient/acoustic album was released
2013 - Material is being recorded for Astral Winter's follow up full length album "Forest Of Silence"
