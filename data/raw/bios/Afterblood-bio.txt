AfterBlood is a female fronted Metal band with a Modern Death Metal style that meets the Thrash Metal sound adding atmospheric Black metal touches.

AfterBlood are about to release their debut album " Of Unsound Minds" co-produced, mixed and mastered by Waldermar Sorychta (Grip Inc., Enemy of the Sun) and sound engineer Dennis Koehne, with session drummer Markus “Makka” Freiwald (Sodom) and guest vocal appearances by the legendary Tom Angelripper (Sodom) and Antony Hämäläinen (Nightrage) and guest guitar solo by Waldemar Sorychta.

Members:

Hel Pyre - Vocals, Lyrics
Cyan - Guitars
Dasno - Bass
Vector - Drums
Stylian - Guitars
