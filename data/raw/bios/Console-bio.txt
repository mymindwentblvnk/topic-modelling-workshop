Console is an electronic music project by Martin Gretschmann, the lead programmer for The Notwist a band from Germany. Relying heavily on elements of electronic music, Console is reminiscent of some electro bands, such as Ladytron and Miss Kittin. The first single "Lady-Bug" was released in 1996, the most recent release "Herself" came in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Console">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
