There is more than one band that links to this page:

1. A band from New Bern, North Carolina, USA, which plays a traditional Style of Heavy Metal, with the inclusion of limited elements not indigenous to traditional Heavy Metal. Three members of the band contribute to the vocals, including a female singer.  Note that (accurate as of March 6th, 2007) all of the preview tracks on the artist page are for this particular band.

2. A Seattle area band that had releases in 1985 and 1986 on CBS Records called 'Gone Too Far' and 'Rockit' and also had a couple of 12" releases 'Bitch' and 'New Romance'. The band was fronted by a female vocalist Robin Taylor, and backed by Karl van der Velden and Kevin Dillon on guitars, Jay Boone on bass, and Geoff Saddler on drums.

3. Ritual is a NWOBHM band often mistakenly referred to as “Widow”, as the cover of their 1983 album “Widow” gave no indication of the band name.

4. Widow is a metalcore band haling from Houston Texas. Formed in May 2012. They have now changed their name to "Darke Complex" as of February 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Widow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
