Mystria is a French heavy metal band from Montpellier, influenced by bands like Megadeth, Metallica, Manowar or Yngwie Malmsteen. During the last years, Mystria played with bands like After Forever, Magica, Nightmare, Kragens, Moonstone, Stereoxyde and more. Their projects for 2007 are the production of their first album "The Dark Crusade" and many concerts in France and maybe in Spain. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mystria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
