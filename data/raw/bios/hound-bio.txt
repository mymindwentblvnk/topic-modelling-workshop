There are three artists that go by the name Hound:

1) Hound is a folk/bluegrass collective from Bemidji, Minnesota.

2) Hound is a 4-piece garage rock band out of Toledo, Ohio and is featured on the “Nowhere At All” compilation. The band is on the Danger Limited Sound Recording label and will release their debut 7" record in February 2012.

3) Hound is a 3-piece heavy metal band out of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, releasing their first album, "Out of Time" in November of 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
