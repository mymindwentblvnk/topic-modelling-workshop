Michael Carlyle Hall (born February 1, 1971 in Raleigh, North Carolina) is an Emmy nominated and Golden Globe winning actor best known for his roles as David Fisher in the HBO drama series Six Feet Under and Dexter Morgan in the Showtime series Dexter.  

He is the lead in the musical Lazarus (2015), which had a limited run in New York, and is currently showing at King's Cross Theatre in London. In October 2016, a soundtrack featuring Michael and the rest of the Lazarus cast was released on Vinyl, CD and MP3.

He also had a successful Broadway career and has starred in films such as, 2003's Paycheck and 2009's Gamer. He has been married to both Jennifer Carpenter (who plays his character's sister, Debra Morgan, on Dexter) and Amy Spanger. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Michael+C.+Hall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
