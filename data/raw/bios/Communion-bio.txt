Deathmessiah - Bass, Vocals (Chainsaw (Chl), Atomicide, Ammit, Ejecutor)
Hellavenger - Guitars

Discography:
Demo I - 2008
Fire-Worship (Adv. Track 2010 A.B.) - 2010
