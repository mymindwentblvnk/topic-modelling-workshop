Headhunter D.C. is a Brazilian death metal band formed in 1987. They released a demo in 1989 titled "Hell is Here" followed by two albums on the legendary Cogumelo Records in 1991 and 1993, "Born... Suffer... Die" and "Punishment at Dawn". Since then they have released two more studio albums and a live album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Headhunter+D.C.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
