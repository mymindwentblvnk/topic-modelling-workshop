SHIELD BREAKER is a NU-Metalcore/Djent band from Baden, Switzerland. Their eerie melodies, hard hitting breakdowns and dynamic and quick screams and raps reflect their emotions in their songs and on stage. Major influences come from bands like For Today, Volumes, DVSR, Hacktivist or even original soundtrack from Nintendo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shield+Breaker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
