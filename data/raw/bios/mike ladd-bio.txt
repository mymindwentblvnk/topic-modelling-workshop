Mike Ladd is a name of at least three artists:

[1]  A poet / rapper / producer who is also responsible for the side-projects The Majesticons and The Infesticons.

Sites: Discogs, Wikipedia, Twitter and MySpace

[2]  A Wayne (Maine, USA), guitarist / singer. Rock sound music speaks of childhood memories and love of the good life back in the day. His tracks "Can't Say Why" and "Homeless" are featured on Assorted Maine Artists' Bridges album (1994).

[3]  A Worcester (Massachusetts, USA), guitarist / singer / songwriter for over 25 years. His 1997 release, The Storm, is a critically acclaimed work of beauty and intensity.  He is currently a member of the The Valves. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mike+Ladd">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
