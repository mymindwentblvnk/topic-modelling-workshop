Last Rosary is a Brutal Deathcore band formed in Houston, Texas. They bled styles from Deathcore, Grindcore, and Technical Death Metal. Their debut album, Price Of Living was self-released in 2009 (and is available at http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/lastrosary). The band's currently active, but unsigned.
