Saprophyte is an old-school hardcore band from Prešov, Slovakia. It was formed in1995. Recent line-up: Marek - vocals, Kari - vocals, Koky - guitar, Marián - guitar, Pajsko - bass & Maťo - drums, percussions. They have recorded three albums: Saprophyte (2000), On the right side (2002) and 3 (2009). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/saprophyte">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
