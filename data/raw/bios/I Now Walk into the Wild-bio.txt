June 2009, guitarists Joey Baker and Fraser Colquhoun recruited front-man Tyler Windich and drummer Tyler Stevenson and thus began the journey of Ottawa's newest metal force. Since then, the pack has been writing, practicing, making scratch recordings and frantically searching for shows. One year from it's conception, the band completed it's line-up with Alex Oulton on bass. This new sonar force is seeking to make a full-fledged assault on Ottawa's metal scene.
