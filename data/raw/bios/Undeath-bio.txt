A death metal band from Rochester, New York.  They released an untitled demo in 2019 and another demo "Sentient Autolysis" in 2019 with Caligari Records.  On October 23, 2020, they released their debut album Lesions of a Different Kind with Prosthetic Records (the label they are currently signed to).
