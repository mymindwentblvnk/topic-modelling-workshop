Pagan Prophecy was founded as a black metal band in 1999 but as the years went by (incl. several line-up changes) Pagan Prophecy have refined their style and now play a mix of technical but melodic death metal combined with heavy and folk influences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pagan+Prophecy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
