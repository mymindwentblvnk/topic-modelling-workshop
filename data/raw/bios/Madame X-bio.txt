Madame X is either:

1) An 80s female electro-funk trio masterminded by Bernadette Cooper of Klymaxx.  The members were Alisa Randolph, Iris Parker and Valerie Victoria.

or

2) A short lived tongue-in-cheek lounge act consisting of Madame X (Diana Young-Blanchard, later of the DT's) backed mainly by members of Impala. They released only one 10" and one 7" plus a couple of compilation tracks, all in 1998 and all on Estrus Records.

or

3) an Italian electro-new wave band consisting of (Alessandro De Benedetti, (Vins Baratta and (Andrea Zuccotti. Their debut album for label Venus, called SUPERSEX DELUXE, was released in 2007. In 2008 they released the official video for the single RUMORE. Currently preparing their second album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Madame+X">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
