Thrash Metal band Objector from Belgium!  Old school sounding from the likes of Kreator, Slayer, Annihilator, Exodus, Demolition Hammer and so forth. Founded in 2007, released their first album in DIY style in 2013 called "Stand Your Ground". Line-up change in 2016.
