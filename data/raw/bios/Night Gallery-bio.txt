Night Gallery is the name of multiple artists:

1. Night Gallery is Aaron David Ross of italo-horror duo Gatekeeper and Adam Griffin of Golden Birthday’s spectral cinematic pop duo who divine inspiration from 80’s synth-pop bands, 80’s fantasy movie soundtracks (think Labyrinth  or Return to Oz ) and generally the 80’s.

2. Formed in Rochester, New York, the band consists of four members: Evan (drums/vocals), Eric (guitar/vocals), Pat (bass/vocals), and Dennis (guitar/vocals).  Considered as experimental and psychadelic rock.  Influenced by Pink Floyd and The Hand Creams. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Night+Gallery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
