Kasino is the codename for Fher Cassini, the main voice of dance music act Kasino, which has Ian Duarte and Fabianno on keyboards and programming. The band had its first hit in 2005, with "Can't Get Over". Their second single, "Stay Tonight", also produced in Brazil, has kept Kasino on the top of dance music charts around the country. After the breakup of the group, Kasino has returned in 2014 with Fher Cassini on vocals, Ian Duarte and Carlos Cassino. The group released the single "So Free", in the beginning of march. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kasino">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
