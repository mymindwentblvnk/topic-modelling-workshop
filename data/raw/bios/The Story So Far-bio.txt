At least a dozen bands have released material under the name The Story So Far. This list is chronological, not by popularity, which is the only way to fairly represent all the bands.

1. An American melodic punk rock band from Chicago, Illinois active from 1998 to 2003. Made up of former members of 88 Fingers Louie, King for a Day and Tomorrow's Gone, the band released their debut EP in 2000 on Hopeless Records.

2. A Welsh post-hardcore/pop punk band from Cardiff, active from 2004 to 2010. The band released several EPs and albums.

3. An American pop punk band from Walnut Creek, California, formed in 2007. Their debut full length "Under Soil And Dirt" was released in 2011 by Pure Noise Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Story+So+Far">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
