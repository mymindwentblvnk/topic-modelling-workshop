Tjuvjakt is a Swedish hip hop group from Lidingö, an island outside of Stockholm, Sweden. The band was formed in 2012 and the current lineup consists of Fredrik Woodz, Olle Grafström, Jesper Swärd, Arvid Lundquist and Kid Eriksson. Jonathan Holmquist quit in 2013 to make his own music. 

In 2013 they released their debut album "Man ska vara seriös," followed up by "Musik är ju kul" in 2014. In 2016 the band released their third album "Välkommen till mångata" and in 2017 the band released their fourth album, "Pojkvän." 

They have collaborated with other Swedish artists/bands such as Albin Gromer, Penga' Per, Organismen, Kalle Gracias, Cristophe Lallet, Ivory, SödraSidan and Donatella.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tjuvjakt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
