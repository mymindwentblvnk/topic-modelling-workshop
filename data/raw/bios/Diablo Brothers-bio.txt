Diablo brothers was a Finnish death metal act. Their music is medolic, progressive but still aggressive. They made some demos and later changed their name to Diablo. Diablo is nowadays one of the biggest names in Finnish metal scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diablo+Brothers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
