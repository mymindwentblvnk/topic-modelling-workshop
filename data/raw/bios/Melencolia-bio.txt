Melencolia is an Italian band, based in Milan, Italy, since 2005. 

Its music is an hybrid between gothic, power and progressive metal. 

Current lineup:

- Eva Marti - female vocals
- Luca Barbaglia - guitars and strings instruments, male vocals
- Giovanni Lonati - keyboards and synths, male vocals
- Matteo Marchetti - bass
- Giulio La Ferla - drums
