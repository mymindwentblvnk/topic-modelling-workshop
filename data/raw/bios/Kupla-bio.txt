Kupla is a London based Finnish electronic music producer with previous experience in composing for dance and hip-hop. He has worked with numerous rappers in Finland and has now started to make his fresh futurebeats known to international audience as well. 

In March 2014 he released a single through a French label Marmorean Records together with prestigious Chinese bass music producer Howie Lee. Kupla's first solo release Subarctic  EP was released on May 5, 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kupla">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
