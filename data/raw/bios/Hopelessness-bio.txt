Hopelessness were a Melodic Death Metal band from Spain. They released the 'Paradise Of Sin' demo in 2004, followed by the one and only album 'Broken Tears In Solitude' in 2005. The band have now split, but the Official Website is still active, and you can download the full 'Broken Tears In Solitude' album for FREE by visiting it. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hopelessness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
