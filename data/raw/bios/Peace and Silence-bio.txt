Peace and Silence is a band from Seattle, Washington. They started out in the very early 1990s, playing shows around Seattle and Portland, and released a full-length album entitled FATHOM THAT in 1993.


PEACE AND SILENCE are:
Travis Bracht | Vocals
Rich Henry | Guitar
Tim Lynch | Guitar
Fred Kitchens | Drums
Chuck Miller | Bass


---- from peaceandsilence.com ----

P&S reunited for a one time show on April 30th, 2005, with Sweety, Turn to Fall and Draghead as their opening acts.
