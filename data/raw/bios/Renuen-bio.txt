Renuen - is a melodic death metal band from Belarus.

Current band members:

Alex Kavaliou - lead vocals (2005-present)
Aleh Huta - guitars, back vocals (2005-present)
Stas Charnushevich - drums (live) (2010-present)
Alexi Bykovski - bass (live) (2010-present)

Discography:

A Letter From Hell (2010)
