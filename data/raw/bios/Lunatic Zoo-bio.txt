Lunatic Zoo was a metal band formed in Stevens Point, Wisconsin in 1993. The lineup on their Insanity CD consisted of guitarist John Lewison, vocalist Bob Vogel, bassist Zeke Ysquierdo, and drummer Mike Rivard.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lunatic+Zoo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
