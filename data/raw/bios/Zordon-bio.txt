ZORDON is young "old school" detah metal band from Pardubice, CZECH REPUBLIC and was founded in autumn 2007 by BART (from band Promile). Lyrics are in English. ZORDON recorded EP Another victim (2008) and EP Blind crowd (2011).

Line-up:
NEKRO - vocal
BART - guitar
THRASH - guitar
BLAZA - bass
