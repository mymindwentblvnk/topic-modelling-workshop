There are several bands named Dorothea.

1) Dorothea is a psychedelic drone doom duo formed in 2012.

Their first release, "Majorana," will be released by Tinnitus Records on cassette on May 1st, 2012. 
http://tinnitusrecords.bigcartel.com

It is also available for download at http://dorothea.bandcamp.com

2) Dorothea is a five piece indie rock outfit from Los Angeles, CA.
Built from an array of instruments and lilting harmonies, Dorothea started as a late-night songwriting experiment around a church piano, a pile of books, and a bottle of Jim Beam.

http://www.wearedorothea.com
