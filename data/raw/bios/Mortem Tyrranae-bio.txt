Mortem Tyrranae is a Black Metal band formed in 2007 by Misanthrolord in Seattle, Washington.  Misanthrolord has since relocated to Germany where "From the Dark Mountains Cometh" was released in early 2009 through Svartgalgh Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortem+Tyrranae">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
