Steinherj is a Black Metal band from Norway (Lillestrøm) 
Lyrical themes
Human Disgust, Anti-Religion

Aadsel: Vocals
Dauskrott: Drums and backing Vocals
Luren: Guitar
General Kvalvågnes: Bass and Backing Vocals
Sam: Bass
