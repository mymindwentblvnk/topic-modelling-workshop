ALKYMENIA, a Brazilian Thrash Death Metal band formed in mid-2003 focused in doing a world class style, has been emerged in Brazilian music scenery.

Featuring of the greatest events in the Brazilian circuit together with national and international artists, ALKYMENIA is being acclaimed due to its professional performance and has quickly gaining space and credibility from media and fans.
