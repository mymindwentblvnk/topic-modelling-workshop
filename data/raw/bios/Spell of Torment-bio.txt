Black/Death metal band from Oulu, Finland. Formed in 2013 by Soraghn and lately with Tomahawk in 2015.
Spell Of Torment embrace Black/Death metal melodies.
And create cinematic and Haunting atmospheres charged with death,sorrow,melancholy,and suicide.
Lyrically they speak from the nature,occultism, mysticism,misanthrophy,satanism,paradoxes,consiousness and dreams,mythologies and war. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spell+of+Torment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
