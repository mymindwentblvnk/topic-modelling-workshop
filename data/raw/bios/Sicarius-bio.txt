Formed in 2009, Sicarius is an earth-shattering metalcore band from Anchorage, Alaska. They have recently released their debut Album, The Antagonist, in September 2012. Having played at Warped Tour 2012 in Seattle, WA and Portland, OR, Sicarius has also received radio airplay and a steadily-growing fanbase on the internet and through their energetic live performances.

Their album has been released and available for free streaming on various websites including Soundcloud and on Spotify.
