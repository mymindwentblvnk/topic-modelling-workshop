Moongarden are an Italian neo-prog band. They employ a wide variety of prog approaches. Their music includes full symphonic prog, vocal oriented prog and much improvisation. Additional instruments they use include the flute and acoustic guitar. Their draw influence from bands such as Camel, Landmarq, Genesis and Pink Floyd. For neo-progressive fans, Moongarden is a band that should not be missed.

"The Gates of Omega", third official release , represents so far the most complex and mature work produced by the band. If you love melodic, symphonic music then this is a must buy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moongarden">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
