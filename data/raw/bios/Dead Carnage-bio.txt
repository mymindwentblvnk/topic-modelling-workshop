The band  DEAD CARNAGE  was founded in July 2013 by guitarist  Milhaus  (exG.ORE, exDisfigured Corpse) and bass guitarist  Chidar  (GORE). In 2014, vocalist Joshua is added to the band,   and in spring 2015 the second guitarist  Yabback  (exEuthanasia, Antigod). Personal changes to the drummer's work were solved by visiting drummer  Ferenc  (Disfigured Corpse, Antigod). 
The band performed some excellent concerts (Guttural Gore Grind Mafia # 7, MetalGate Czech Death Fest VII ...) and July 2015 recorded an EP called " The only thing I ever wanted was to kill the God ".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+Carnage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
