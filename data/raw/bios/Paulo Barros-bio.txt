Paulo Barros is a Portuguese guitarist, from Vila Nova de Gaia, best known for having founded the heavy metal band Tarantula in the 1981.

As of June 2008, he has also released 3 solo instrumental albums:
Vintage (1997)
Gemini (2003)
K:arma 6 (2007)

He played the opening act for Joe Satriani when he visited Portugal to promote the "Musterion of Rock" album, in April 30 and May 1, in Lisboa and Porto. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paulo+Barros">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
