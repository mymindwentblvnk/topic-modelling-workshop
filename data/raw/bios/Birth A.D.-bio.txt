Birth A.D. is a Texas thrash band in the style of D.R.I., S.O.D. and the Fearless Iranians From Hell. They write short, explosive punk/metal songs which point out the illogicalities of modern society. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Birth+A.D.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
