Sleep Terror is a technical/experimental metal band from Seattle, Washington, USA formed in 2002. They fuse the heaviest elements of technical music with the lightest streams of jazz, funk, trance, hardcore, progressive and everything in between.

Members:
Luke Jaeger - Guitars, Bass, Keyboards, Drum sampling
Marco Pitruzzella - Drums 2015--(live)
David McGraw - Drums 2002-2008(Live)

Sleep Terror is now in a transition stage, and is on pause from touring and live performances due to financial restrictions, and Luke wanting to pursue his solo-project. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sleep+Terror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
