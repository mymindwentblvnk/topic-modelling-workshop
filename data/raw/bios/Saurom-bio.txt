Saurom is the new name of Spanish folk/power metal band Saurom Lamderth.

Saurom's music often includes the use of instruments such as bagpipes, flutes and other instruments often associated with folk music. All of their lyrics are performed in Spanish. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Saurom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
