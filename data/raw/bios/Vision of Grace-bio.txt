Vision of Grace (formerly Brainfake) was a doom metal band from Sweden. They disbanded in 1999. They recorded two demos, one cassette and one CD. The CD featured five songs and was over 60 minutes long. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vision+of+Grace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
