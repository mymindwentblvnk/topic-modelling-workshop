Mary Lattimore is a harpist living in Los Angeles. She experiments with her Lyon and Healy Concert Grand harp and effects. Her solo debut, “The Withdrawing Room”, was released in 2013 on Desire Path Recordings. Her most recent album, Hundreds of Days, was released in 2018 on Ghostly International. 

She has collaborated with musicians such as Jeff Zeigler, Chris Forsyth, Maxwell August Croy, Elysse Thebner, Meg Baird and Cody Yantis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mary+Lattimore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
