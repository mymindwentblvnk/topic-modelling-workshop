Leviathanne were a heavy metal band formed in Cambridge in 2003. Comprising of members Tom Shepherd (bass), Jason Fairclough (vox), Luke Braddick (drums) and Ashton Le Bailly (guitar) they broke up in 2005 and Luke and Jason went on to form the more death/grind oriented Gamarra. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leviathanne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
