Death-metal band from southern France formed in 1997. They released two albums : Tormenting The Pacifist (2001) and Demolished (2004). Some (former) members of the band also play(ed) in Agressor, Necrophagist, Addicted, Phazm, Devilium and Belef. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Imperial+Sodomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
