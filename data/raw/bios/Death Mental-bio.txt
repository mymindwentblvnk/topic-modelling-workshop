Death Mental is a one man project from Southeastern Iowa.  It's a combo of death metal/metal like Death/Slayer/Pestilence and also of  progressive stuff like Rush/Yes/Pink Floyd. The lyrics are about personal experiences of various mental disorders and medical problems.
