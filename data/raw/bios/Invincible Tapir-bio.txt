Invincible Tapir is a Taiwanese Metal band from Taipei, which was founded in 2012.

Members:
重口 (K.J) - vocals
韋仁 (Boob Monster) - lead guitar
阿馮 (Sean Feng) - rhythm guitar
文嘉 (Wayne Liao) - bass
花花 (Monkey) - drums
