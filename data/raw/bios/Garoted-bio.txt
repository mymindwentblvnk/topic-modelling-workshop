A death metal band from Beatrice, Nebraska formed in 2007. They have released a demo in 2009, a full length album titled 'Praise Hate, Praise Murder, Praise the Beast' in 2010, an EP titled 'Evil Personified' in 2011, and in 2013 they are coming out with their sophomore release 'Visions of Death and Destruction.' 

 Nolan (drums) and Drew (guitar) were also members of the black/death band Typhon (US). They are both in the Kansas black metal band 'Verrater', and along with D. Stansberry, are in Nebraska's Noise/Grinders 'Chafed Taint.' <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Garoted">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
