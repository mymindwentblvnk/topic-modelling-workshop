Dutch power metal band that formed in 1987 in Groningen, released the MLP "From Factory to History" and disappeared. The record has been re-released recently in limited 160 grams vinyl by WH Productions.

Last known line-up:
Eric Van Der Heide (vocals),
Eddo Pol (guitars),
Werner Hoenders (bass),
Hans Bowman (drums)

Known discography:
Out Of The Tomb  (Demo, 1988)
From Factory To History (EP, 1989)
