A doom metal band from Orange County, California that was active in the mid to late 1980s. Their discography includes a self-released 7" from 1988 and a live album recorded in 1988 but not released until 2006. The live album includes many unreleased songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Child+Saint">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
