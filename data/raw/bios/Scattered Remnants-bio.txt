Scattered Remnants is a brutal death metal band from Massachusetts. Some of their most recognizable influences are Exhumed and Incantation. Jason Hendershaw on vocals, Rob Settergren on guitar, Ron Miles on bass, and Eric Roy on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scattered+Remnants">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
