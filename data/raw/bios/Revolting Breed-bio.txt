Revolting Breed was formed in 2004 in Patras,Greece. Through these three years the band has taken part to several shows in clubs around Greece. In may of 2006 the band produced and recorded on their own their first full-length "Rise Against" that was later mixed and mastered by Andy Classen at Stage One Studios (Germany) and finally is available as a self-financed work.In 2009 they decided to split up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Revolting+Breed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
