Halidom started in mid 2013 as a result of four IDF soldiers growing frustration of reality and the oppressive Military system, a pattern for us to express our wrath and disdain.
Bent on making relentless, uncompromising metal in vein of bands such as Lamb of God and Gojira, Halidom are determined to stir ground and break heads. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Halidom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
