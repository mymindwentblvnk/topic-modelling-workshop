Stoner rock/Doom metal band from Stockholm, Sweden.

"The band was formed in early summer of 2009, but had been the project of Niklas and Chritus (Count Raven, Saint Vitus, Terra Firma, Lord Vicar) for several years. Originally called the Weekend Beast, the band changed name to Goatess in August 2012."

Niklas - Guitar
Findus - Bass
Kenta - Drums
Chritus - Vocals
