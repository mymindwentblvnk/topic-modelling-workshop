Ancient Influence are a symphonic power metal band from Pretoria, South Africa.

They opened for Blind Guardian at the first ever Nightfall Festival, and are currently recording their first album.

Members:
Rian Coetzee - Lead Guitar
Tommie Postma - Vocals
Izak Coetzee - Rhythm guitar
Henko Coetzee- Drums
Ria De Wet - Keyboard
