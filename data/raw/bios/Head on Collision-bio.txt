Head On Collision is a Thrash Metal band from St. Louis, Mo. Formed in 2002, they released a demo called Arise from the Wreckage in 2005 and have a song featured on Beer City Record's compilation Counter Attack called "Phobia". Beer City released the band's debut full-length Ritual Sacrifice in May 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Head+On+Collision">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
