Green Hell is a heavy-hitting hardcore band out of Vancouver and it's suburban regional district. Comprised of two Slingshot band members [ www.slingshotpunk.com ] and two local straight edge kids, green hell is full of inspiration to write songs addressing both personal and political issues.
