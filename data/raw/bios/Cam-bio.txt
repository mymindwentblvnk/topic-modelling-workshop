Camaron Marvel Ochs, an American country music singer and songwriter signed to Arista Nashville. She is best known for the song "Burning House" from her album Untamed. Cam, a gospel singer from Spencer, Oklahoma. He aspired to be an R&B singer and while in pursuit of his dreams, Cam met Jesus Christ. His love for R&B music remains, but rather than seeking fame, his desire is to lift up the Name of the Lord.
