Doedsvangr was started as a project in 2015 by Anti-Christian, Doedsadmiral and Shatraug. In 207 they released their debut album "Satan Ov Suns" through Immortal Frost Productions. After a few years, they recruited guitarist BST (The Order Of Appollyon, ex-Asosoth) and recorded their second album Serpents Ov Old, which will be released 2021 through Debemur Morti Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Doedsvangr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
