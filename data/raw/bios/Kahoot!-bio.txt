Kahoot! is an educational game used primarily in K-12 schools in the United States. Due to it's widespread use in classrooms, it's music has garnered popularity amongst generation Z. It's soundtrack mainly consists of lobby waiting music, but also remixes ranging from Disco to Electronic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kahoot%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
