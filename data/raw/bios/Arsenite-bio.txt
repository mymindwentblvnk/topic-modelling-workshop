Arsenite is a metalband from Sweden influenced by bands like Megadeth, Metallica, Iced Earth, Testament, Exodus, Overkill, Judas Priest, Iron Maiden, Motörhead, Wolf and many more.

Started by guitarist Simon Hagberg and guitarist/singer Tony Classon in 2009, Arsenite released their debut album, a 4 song EP, called High​-​speed thrashing maniacs in July 15, 2011.

Formation:
Tony Classon: Guitar/Vocals
Simon Hagberg: Guitars
Martin Giaever: Bass Guitar
Richard Fredriksson: Drums
