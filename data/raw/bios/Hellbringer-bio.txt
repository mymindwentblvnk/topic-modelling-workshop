Hellbringer are an Australian 3 piece Thrash machine hailing from Canberra and formed in 2007.
Featuring Luke Bennett on bass/vocals, Josh Bennett on drums, and James Lewis on guitar, Hellbringer deliver evil, aggressive tunes in the spirit of the old Gods.
