There are three artists known under the name Chastity.

1. Chastity (Brandon Williams) hails from Whitby, Ontario. The genre bending music of Chastity hovers in a space of oscillating urgency, both melodic and noisey, juxtaposing themes of collectivity and isolation.

2. Female singer Sylvia Bruens from the Netherlands who participated in the national finals for the Eurovision songcontest 2005 with the song Baby It's You.
