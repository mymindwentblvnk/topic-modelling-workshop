Thrash metal act from Osaka, Japan. Formed in 1985, Riverge toured with Casbah, Raging Fury, Jurassic Jade, Fastkill, Rose Rose, Shell Shock and much more. The band released two vinyl-singles, two demo-tapes and their first full-length studio album in 2009, named "Rebirth Of Skull" on Rock Stakk Records. "Raid For Riverging" followed in July 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/RIVERGE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
