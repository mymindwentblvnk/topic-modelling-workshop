Symphonic Metal from Helsinki, Finland!

Enthring released their first full-lenght album ''The Grim Tales of the Elder'' in October 2011 and has received a great deal of awesome feedback from many listeners and reviewers.

Members:
Tommi Kangaskortet  - Guitars & Vocals
Henri Vahtere - Bass
Matti Auerkallio - Drums 

Old members:
Petteri Eväsoja - Guitars & Vocals
Mika Lumijärvi - Drums
Henri Kotipelto - Bass
