Blackened doom band from Bergen, Hordaland in Norway. Formed in 2015, Children of Cain have independently released a single Synderflod, two Ep's Quasimodo in 2015 and Sorgen's Saga in 2016.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Children+Of+Cain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
