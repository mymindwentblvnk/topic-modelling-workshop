Seven bands known under the name Barbarian:

1. Speed/Thrash/Black Metal from Florence, Italy. Formed in 2009, the band released a self-titled demo and full length album as a tribute to the old times when bands such as Hellhammer, Celtic Frost, Bathory, and Venom emerged.

2. Thrash metal band from Spain. The guitarist and vocalist Pep Casas is an old member of the mythical thrash metal band Fuck Off. They play old school thrash metal and released a live cd called 'Seguimos avanzando' that contains some old themes from Fuck Off. They also released a studio album called 'Heavy metal resureccion'. Their songs are in spanish.

3. A Hardcore/Gabber house formation. Their tracks are known for the ultra high speed (up to 500 beats per minute) and heavy sound making them arguably the heaviest house band known.

4. A hardcore band from El Paso, Texas.  Find their music here:
-http://www.facebook.com/pages/BarbarianTX/169062239798710
-http://barbariantx.bandcamp.com/album/world-gone-wrong

5. A hardcore band in the vein of Converge from Edison, NJ. Featuring Ex members of the Gray Lines Of Perfection. www.barbarianhc.com

6. A garage pop band from San Diego, CA, with elements of indie rock and psychedelic surf pop.  Find their music here:
