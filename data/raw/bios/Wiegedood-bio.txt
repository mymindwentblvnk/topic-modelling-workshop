Wiegedood is a Belgian black metal band from Ghent, Belgium that formed in 2014.

The original lineup:

Levy Seynaeve (Living Gate, Hessian) - guitar, vocals;
Gilles Demolder (Oathbreaker) - guitar;
Wim Sreppoc (Living Gate, Oathbreaker, White Jazz) - drums.
