High Fighter formed in Summer 2014 and it started as a total new band project born by former band members of A Million Miles, Buffalo Hump and Pyogenesis. Add a heavy dose of the blues to this volatile cocktail of heavy as hell vocals & riffs fuelled by beer and the mighty weedian riffs, High Fighter deliver some mean and dirty Bluescore, Stoner & Sludge to fuel your appetite with. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/High+Fighter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
