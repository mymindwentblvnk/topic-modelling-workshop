Eradikal Insane is a French death metal band that formed in Marseille, France, in 2004. The band has released three demos and one EP.

Back from a French tour including a show in Luxembourg during May 2015, Eradikal Insane announced Mithra to be out September 7, 2015, which will be followed by a tour in Canada in the same month <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eradikal+Insane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
