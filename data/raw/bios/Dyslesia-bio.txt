Dyslesia is a French heavy power metal band from Lyon, highly influenced by bands like Rhapsody and Stratovarius.
Dyslesia was founded in Lyon and started his discographic
career in 1999.
After the release of three albums and one DVD, the band succeeded
step by step in finding its own path and audience. They forged a
strong "live" reputation by playing with the most successful bands
in Europe as Iron Savior, Vandenplas, Symphony X, Consortium Project,
Rage, Stratovarius, Shaman, Rhapsody and so many more...
They are today a major actor of the French Heavy Metal scene and
they are looking forward to highest grounds...
