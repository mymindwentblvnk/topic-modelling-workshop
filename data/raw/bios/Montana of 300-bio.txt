Montana of 300 is an American hip hop recording artist from Chicago, Illinois. His debut studio album, Fire in the Church, which was released in May 2016, peaked at number 95 on the Billboard 200. His rap style is best known for its extensive use of metaphors and complex lyrics set to versatile trap, R&B, and drill beats, while also making remixes to hit songs that, like his originals, often include religious subtext. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Montana+of+300">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
