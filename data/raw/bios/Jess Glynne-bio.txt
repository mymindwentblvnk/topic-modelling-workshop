Jessica Hannah "Jess" Glynne (born 20 October 1989) is an English singer-songwriter. She is signed to Atlantic Records UK.[4] 

She is best known for featuring on Clean Bandit's 2014 single "Rather Be"; the song reached number one on the UK Singles Chart and Irish Singles Chart. She also featured on Route 94's single "My Love", which was released weeks later and also reached number one on the UK Singles Chart and Scottish Chart.
