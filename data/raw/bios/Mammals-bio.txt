1) Mammals is the folk project by Sydney-born singer-songwriter Guy Brown. His recognizably soft vocals combine with his love of subtle instrumentation, generating a warm and ambient folk vibe. Suffering from depression and anxiety many sleepless nights have been accompanied by Bon Iver and Jeff Buckley - influences present through out ‘Mammals’ flowing nature.
