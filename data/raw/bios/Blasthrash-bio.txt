Blasthrash was formed in 1998 with the objective of playing Thrash Metal like that of the 80s. The band was started by Dario Viola (Guitar/Vocals), Felipe Nizuma (Guitar), Rodrigo Schmidt (Guitar), Diego Nogueira (Bass) and Rafael Sampaio (Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blasthrash">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
