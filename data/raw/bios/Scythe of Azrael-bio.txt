Scythe of Azrael is a female-fronted metal band based in Philadelphia, including vocalist/guitarist Amanda Wyckoff, drummer Sara Neidorf, guitarist Andrew Stubbs, and bassist Brian Stubbs. They were active between Octobter 2005 and March 2009. They released a six-song EP in 2006, and their first full-length album, AD INFINITUM, in late 2008. AD INFINITUM can be found on iTunes and Amazon mp3.
