Estrum is: Zena Mckelvy (vocals), Jason Rufuss Sewell (guitar), Jordan Peterson (guitar), Erik Christianson (keys), Jhon Bellizia (bass), and Brian McClain(drums.) Estrum is a melodic progressive metal band from Austin, Texas. They are in the recording stage and are working on 14 orginal songs. Estrum is also seeking management and a recording contract.

The sound of Estrum is focused on extreme dynamics from soft, beautiful, and haunting, to vastly heavy. It also showcases 21 year old Zena Mckelvy's virtuosic vocal abilities as well as the band's stunning & complex instrumentation with a strong undercurrent of heavy, syncopated drumming.
