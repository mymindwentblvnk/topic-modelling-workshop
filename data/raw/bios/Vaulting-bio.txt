Vaulting is an impressive deathgrind band formed in 2006 by the brothers Sebastian and Matthias in Wiesbaden Germany. They have since released two full lengths ("Epilog", and their most recent "Nucleus"), and EP ("Modus Humanis"), and a demo all for download on their bandcamp page. Their current lineup has existed since the beginning of 2010.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vaulting">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
