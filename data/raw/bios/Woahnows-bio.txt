Fronted by ex-Crazy Arm bassist Tim Rowing-Parker, new Plymouth trio WOAHNOWS are the newest signing to BigScaryMonsters, and describe themselves as 'Anarcho Indie Punk'.

Their new EP 'The Joy Disorder' will be out on 2nd September, backed up by a full European and UK tour.
