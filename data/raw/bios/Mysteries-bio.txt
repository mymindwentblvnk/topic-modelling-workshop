There is more than one band with the name Mysteries:

1) Mysteries were a Polish black metal band active from 1992 to 1995. They released three demos which have been bootlegged several times over the years but never officially reissued. Their singer Leinad (R.I.P. September 2006) played in the band Legion with Rob Darken of Graveland.

2) Mysteries is an anonymous collective that released their debut album, New Age Music is Here, in 2014 on the LA-based Felte label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mysteries">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
