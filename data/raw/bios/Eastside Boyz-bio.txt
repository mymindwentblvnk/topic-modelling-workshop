The moniker of the duo consisting of Big Sam and Lil' Bo, purveyors of crunk rap who appear frequently with Lil' Jon. Because they are primarily collaborators, they are rarely credited or appear separately from Lil Jon himself. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eastside+Boyz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
