Blastera is a five-piece Melodic Thrash Core band from Blansko, Czech Republic.
Current members are:

Jura "Jurais" Kuchař (vocals)
Marek "Mára" Konštanský (drums)
Vítek "Harry" Šmerda (bass)
Radek "Šnek" David (guitar)
Martin "Zborta" Zbořil (guitar)
