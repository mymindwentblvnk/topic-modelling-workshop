There is more than one artist by this name

1) Herética is a melodic metal band from Asturias, in the north of Spain. It was founded in 2003 and its members are Pete (guitar), Javi Vijande (guitar), Pelayo Palicio (bass guitar), Violeta Alonso (keyboards) and Antonio Ramírez from Colombia (drums). The band is currently  looking for a new singer. They have released two albums, “Frente al Tiempo” (2004) and “Aún Hay Quien Llora” (2008).
