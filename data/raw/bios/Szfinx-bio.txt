Heavy metal band from Debrecen, Hungary.

Released two albums:
1993: Vad játszma
1995: Húzd!

Line-up: Ecsedi János - Guitars
Koroknai Árpád - Vocals (his other bands include Aberra, P.Box, Acoustic Gallery and Omen)
Monor Tamás - Bass, Vocals
Sándor József - Guitars, Vocals (also in Múzeum and P.Box)
