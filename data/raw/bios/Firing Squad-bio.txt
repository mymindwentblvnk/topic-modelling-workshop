FIRING SQUAD is a hardcore punk band from Virginia. They have a four song demo out that was released on Agitate Records. They are better than all the other Firing Squads, guaranteed. 

Firing Squad is the name of at least two hundred bands. This page is for the Polish death metal band Firing Squad.

Firing Squad is also an "indie"/punk band from New York, USA formed in 2008. On last.fm they use the id3 artist tag "Firing Squad (NY)"  to differentiate themselves from the Polish death metal band of the same name. In all other contexts they are known simply as Firing Squad.
