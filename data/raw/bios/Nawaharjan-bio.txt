Nawaharjan is a black metal band from Germany, part of Scythe of Death Productions. Their lyrics deal mainly with dark Germanic heathenism. The band has released an EP in 2011, titled "Into the Void." Their name is Proto-Germanic for "army of corpses." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nawaharjan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
