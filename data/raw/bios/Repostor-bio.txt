Death/thrash metal band from Buk, Poland, formed in March 1992. Their only release is a 1993 tape named "Give 'Em Help". Guitarist and vocalist Przemysław "Perła" Wejman would later on become a member of Acid Drinkers <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Repostor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
