Imprecation are a Texan death metal group, featuring members from Infernal Dominion and Blaspherian. 

Note: 
David Herrera, vocalist on "Ceremony of the Nine Angles" demo and "Sigil of Baphomet" EP went on to do guitars and vocals for BAHIMIRON.

Drummer VOMIT went on to create ADUMUS.

IMPRECATION is currently ACTIVE in creating new material and playing live shows.
