Gutted Alive is a brutal death metal band from Rochester, New York. Formed in October of 2012 originally under the name The Gutted. They signed with CDN Records in January of 2014. In March of 2014 they released their first full-length album titled Culmination of Mutilation. Gutted Alive is; Michael Ryan on vocals, Michael Sloma on bass, ason "AHQK The Dragon Slayer" Acquilano on guitar, Brandon Graves on drums. Facebook.
