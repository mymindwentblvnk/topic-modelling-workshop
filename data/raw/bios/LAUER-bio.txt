Phillip Lauer is a producer, musician and DJ based in Frankfurt. 

His first results ,mastered straight from cassette, were released as Plenty Ammo on Inbetween Records and later on the prolific Real Soon label. 
Later he put out some more serious tracks on Separe Recordings and Punkt Music and was involved in remix productions and Arto Mwambe, a project he startet with his buddy CB Funk in 2004. 

In 2006 he founded his own label called Brontosaurus, where he puts out music of his weirdest friends and himself. On his rare and special DJ appearances he tries to preach the ragged side proto dance music. Newest development is him going solo under his surname Lauer. His debut album "Phillips" was released in spring 2012 on Running Back. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/LAUER">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
