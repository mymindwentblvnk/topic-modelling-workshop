There are more than one band with the name Die Hard.

1) Oldschool Thrash/Death metal band fom Uppsala, Sweden. In November 2005 Harry (bass) formed a band with the intention of bringing the spirits of the past back to life. Thrash Til Death.

2) Pre-Integrity Cleveland metalcore band, released a 12" titled Looking Out For #1.

3) A band from Moscow, Russia playing some weird psychedelic stuff.

4) A band from Glasgow, Scotland who formed the Halleluwah Hits record label and play Animal Collective-esque, hazy indie pop. Their debut album comes out on 27th of February 2012 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Die+Hard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
