Drain The Dragon is from Padova, Italy, the 4 guys start to play in 2003, initially born under melodic hardcore the band had grown up changing the style in something that can be defined as a mix between hardcore rythms, melodic (but still angry) guitars, and screaming vocals...The last promo, "The Rise of Madness", recorded in Jan 2006.
