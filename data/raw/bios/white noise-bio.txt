There are multiple acts using this name.

1) White Noise are an iconic electronic band formed in London in 1969 by David Vorhaus, a classical bass player with a background in both physics and electronic engineering. He was initially joined electronic composers Delia Derbyshire and Brian Hodgson of the BBC Radiophonic Workshop.

2) White Noise are also an alternative rock/metal band drom the Burnley area. They split in 2003. They reformed for a short period in 2004 and are currently inactive.

3) White Noise are also a psychedelic trance group formed by Black & White and Wizzy Noise

4) White Noise is also a post-hardcore/metalcore band from Greensboro, North Carolina.  www.myspace.com/whitenoisenc

5) White Noise is also a Punk/Thrash Metal band from Alberta, Canada.

6) White Noise is also an Australian RAC band.

7) White Noise is also a Scottish Christian Hip-Hop act.

8) White Noise is also a mash-up artist from Huntington, New York: http://www.myspace.com/wearewhitenoise

9) White Noise is also a French alternative/electro rock band. http://www.myspace.com/thefoxyblackfox

10) White Noise is also an all-female alternative rock band from Vietnam. The band  evolved from a formerly four-piece band called Lazee Dolls. The current line-up includes Phương Hà (lead vocals, guitar, composer), Minh Thảo (vocals, guitar), Thanh Tâm (bass), and Khánh An (drums).

11) White Noise  is also a Beatlesque XTCesque studio operation which recorded in Southern California from 1990 to the present. Albums include Eclectic Signs (1993), Mad at the Moonlight (2000), and A White Noise Christmas (2012). https://www.facebook.com/white.noise.rhythm

12) White Noise  is also a french post-rock electro band from 2015 to the present. Albums include Dubious (2016), The Case Is Over (2017). https://www.facebook.com/wearewhitenoise and https://www.wearewhitenoise.fr

13) White Noise  is also a kiwi bedroom pop project started in 2021. It currently has not released any albums but singles include
Life, Love, and the Pursuit of Despair(2022),
