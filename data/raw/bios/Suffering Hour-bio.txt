Suffering Hour are a death metal/black metal band from Minneapolis, Minnesota. The band originally formed in 2011 as Compassion Dies, before renaming to Suffering Hour in 2013. They released their EP "Foreseeing Exemptions to a Dismal Beyond" in January 2014, and released their debut full-length album "In Passing Ascension" in May 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Suffering+Hour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
