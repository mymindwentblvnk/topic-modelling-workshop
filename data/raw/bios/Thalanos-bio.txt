In early 2010, Thalanos was founded by 5 people who knew each other way too long. Inspired by their previous musical activities together they decided to start a studio band.Thalanos was born.

The musical activities from the past resulted in the album 'Tears of Valiance'. From there on out the band released singles like 'Burn and Plunder' and the future masterpiece 'masterpiece'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thalanos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
