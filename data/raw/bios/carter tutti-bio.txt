Carter Tutti is the duo of Chris Carter and Cosey Fanni Tutti, both members of Throbbing Gristle, who have also gone by Chris & Cosey and CTI.  The pair has adopted this new name for live performances and their most recent albums Cabal (2004) and Feral Vapours of the Silver Ether (2007). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carter+Tutti">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
