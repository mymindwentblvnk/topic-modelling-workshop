Lechia is an NS pagan metal act from Poland, formed in 1998 and still active to this date. Lechia's main lyrical themes deal with things as Aryanism, paganism and ancient values. 

Currently, the only member is Warterz Drzewit Neur, who does all instruments and vocals by himself. 

Lechia has released the following over the course of years:

Pomorski Orze³ Demo, 2000  
Lechicki Heros Demo, 2001  
Lechia Demo, 2003  
Strażnicy Aryjskiej Krwi Split, 2004 
Warriors of Pagan Victory Split, 2006
