Incoma is a 3-piece instrumental post-rock band from Uzhhorod, Ukraine. They released a full length titled "Reasonable Doubt" in 2014 and a 2-song ep "The Ocean Never Ends" in 2016. They are both available at https://incoma.bandcamp.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/InComa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
