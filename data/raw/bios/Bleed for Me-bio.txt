Bleed for me is a new Swedish metal band with members who appeared in various metal bands and metal projects in the past.
The band currently consists of Håkan Johnsson (vocals), Martin Sjöstrand (bass), Conny Pettersson (drums), Mattias Svensson (guitar), and Oskar Palmer (solo guitar). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BLEED+FOR+ME">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
