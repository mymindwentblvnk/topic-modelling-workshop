"Big" Jim Martin (born James Martin, on 21 July 1961, in Hayward, California) is mainly known for being  Faith No More guitar player from 1983 to 1993 on the albums We Care a Lot, Introduce Yourself, The Real Thing, and Angel Dust. In 1997 he released a solo album, Milk And Blood.

He also played lead guitar in EZ-Street (alongside future Metallica bass player Cliff Burton and Faith No More/Ozzy Osbourne drummer Mike Bordin), Vicious Hatred, Agents of Misfortune (also with Cliff Burton), Recluse and Pigs of Death.
