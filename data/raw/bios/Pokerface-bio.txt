First one is the swedish electronic artist Stefan Gustafsson. Music styles ranges from trip hop via movie scores to psychadelica and progressive.

Second one is a band from Holland, previously "The sweet lake sinners". Music best described as heavy rock and influenced by blues, rock, metal, grunge, stoner rock and more. For video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FR428txLwVo and for more information: www.pokerfaceonline.nl
