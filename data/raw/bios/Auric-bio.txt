Auric is a sludge metal band from Fayetteville, Arkansas. Their multi-faceted sound includes elements of sludge, doom, post-metal and black metal. Their self-titled debut album was released February 4th, 2014. The follow up album titled "Empty Seas" was released on July 16th, 2015.
