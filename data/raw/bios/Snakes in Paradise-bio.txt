Snakes in Paradise is Swedish, Stockholm based melodic Hard Rock band.
So far they have released four albums; "Snakes in Paradise" (1994), "Garden of Eden" (1999), "Dangerous Love" (2002) and a compilation album "Yesterday and Today" (2001).
