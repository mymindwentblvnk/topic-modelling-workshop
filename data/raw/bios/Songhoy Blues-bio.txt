Songhoy Blues are a desert blues-punk band from Timbuktu, Mali. The band were exiled to Bamako during the unrest in Mali. The band features Aliou Toure, Oumar Toure, Garba Toure and Nathanael Dembele. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Songhoy+Blues">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
