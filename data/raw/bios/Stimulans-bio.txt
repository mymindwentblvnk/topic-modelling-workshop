STIMULANS is a heavy metal band, formed in Split, Croatia, in 2001 by Mario Čuljak (vocals, guitar) and Ivan Dabro (bass).
Current line-up was formed in 2004:
Mario Čuljak-Čuljo  - guitar, lead vocals
Darko Grubišić - guitar
Ivan Dabro - bass
Alen Đurašinović-Đole - drums 
They have released two demos - Promo 2004 (2004) and No Words (2005).
Their first  album Dust was released December 22nd 2010 through the Serbian label One Records.
A video for the song Omiški Gusar (the band's only song in Croatian) was  released a few months prior to the album.
