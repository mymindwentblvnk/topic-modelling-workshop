Eighth Day Descent are a metal band based in Leamington Spa (nr Coventry) UK. They formed in June 05,playing out in the local area before recording a 5 track demo in March 06 and set out an short UK Tour during July which was then followed up by "The Switch Has Melted" e.p in November of the same year. 

The band features members of : 

Brutal Justice/ Snarf Takes Up The Challenge, 
Naliam, 
A Day Remains,
