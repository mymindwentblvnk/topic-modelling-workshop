Connie Constance (1995) is a singer-songwriter based in London, which debuted in September 2015 with the 3-track, Blue Daisy produced release, "In the Grass", via Black Acre. It contains the tracks Stars, Euphoric and Kingdom. The follow-up, Answer , was released in November 2015.
