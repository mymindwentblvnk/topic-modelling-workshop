Death metal band from Italy, formed in 1996. They released 3 demos (Mantrap in 1995, From the Deep in 2000 and Alhazred in 2002) and one  album (Shudder in 2002). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/EXIDIA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
