Kuldrin is a one-man black metal project of L.R. Galway from Ottawa, Canada. The band's first full-length album 'The Sorcerer' released on November 30th 2014.
Inspired by Paganism, magick, medieval & Scandinavian history, Thelemic philosophy, and Celtic folklore. Several tracks were originally recorded on cassette during the summer of 2004, deep within the forests of Labrador. Everything has been rewritten and enhanced to ensure that all tracks have consistent sound quality. 'Sorcerer' is the first album to be released by the one man project, Kuldrin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kuldrin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
