The Quarter After, who hail from Los Angeles, offer bewitching psychedelic pop songs that shine with an obvious love for the classic sounds of The Kinks, The Byrds, and The Beatles, and contemporaries like the Brian Jonestown Massacre. The band is lead by Dominic Campanella and his brother Rob. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Quarter+After">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
