Formed in 1990, Brooklyn, New York based power metal band Gothic Knights released their self-titled debut album "Gothic Knights" in 1996, and a followup in 1999 entitled "Kingdom of the Knights" both on the Sentinel Steel. For 2003's self-released Up from the Ashes, which included a cover version of Duran Duran's "Hungry Like The Wolf", the original members; guitarist John Tzantis, bassist Mario Cosentino and vocalist Rick Sanchez were joined by former Virgin Steele drummer Frank Gilchriest. In 2004, Gilchriest was replace by drummer Kevin DeDario (The Great Kat), and in 2007 for the first time since the formation of the band 17 years earlier a second guitarist was officially added to the band's roster, with Dave The 3rd. The band is currently in the midst of recording their latest album entitled "Reflections from the Other Side" which is set to be released in July 2012.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gothic+Knights">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
