Sabacthani is a melodic death metal with speed and heavy metal influences. Vocals alternates from death metal grunting to vocals as in the one from 3 Inches Of Blood. Their lyrics thems are existence, war, nature. Band from Mexico formed in 1999.

Line-up:
Ayu Dávalos - Vocals 
Omar Rufino - Guitar
Noel Avecilla - Guitar
Jorge Lémus - Bass 
Alejandro Melo - Drums

Discography: 
"Immense Dark Horizon" (demo) released in 2002.
"The Reformer" (demo) released in 2002.
"Winding Forest" (demo) released in 2003
"A Rising from Here" (album) released in 2007. (which contains all the tracks from the demos on higher quality)
