Formed in early 2013 by Nevarym (Drums, Backing Vocals) and Angmaer (Guitars, Bass, Vocals) from Wolverhampton, West Midlands. Inspired by many black metal bands, including Dissection, Bathory, Satyricon and Darkthrone. They released their first demo in September 2013 entitled 'Possessing The Chapel (Demo I)' and then in April 2014 released 'Demo II' which included the songs off of Demo I but was re-recorded and there was an added song. In September 2014 they released their first EP 'The Gate' which included 6 new tracks including an intro and outro.
