There are several bands named Enthrone.

1) Norwegian Black Metal Enthrone was formed in 1990.
The known line-up of the band consisted of Lord Nosferatu (Nattefrost) - Bass, Keyboards, Vocals and Lord Karnstein (J. Nordavind) - Bass, Guitars, Keyboards, Backing Vocals.

They released the cassette demo 'Black Winds' in 1991.
In 1992 Enthrone changed name into Carpathian Forest
