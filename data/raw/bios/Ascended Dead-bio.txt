Death metal band from San Diego, CA, USA, Ascended Dead formed in mid 2011 by JR and IL from the ashes of Decay, their previous band. CK was recruited on drums, JM was recruited on bass, and the 4-piece has sought to create primitive, visceral, psychotic Death Metal, without limitations.
