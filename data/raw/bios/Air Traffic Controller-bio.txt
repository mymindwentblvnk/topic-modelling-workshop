Air Traffic Controller is an American indie pop band formed in Boston, MA, in 2009 by lead singer and songwriter Dave Munro (who had previously served in the US Navy as an air traffic controller, inspiring the name for the band) and  singer and songwriter Casey Sullivan. 

The band's debut album, “The One”, was released in 2009, produced by Bleu.
