Charakter is a music project made by Wesley Cannibal (last.fm username: XaviersPast, formerly WCannibal). As of now, he has three releases, one of them being an instrumental black/folk metal demo EP entitled Memoirs of a Dream Long Forgotten. You can download that EP here: http://www.mediafire.com/?fny02mn1xeyzlen
Charakter's first full length album, Sielu, was released in January of 2011, under Svartgalgh Records. To download it, click here: http://www.mediafire.com/?298nbcss8si90d3
The second full length, Into Eternal Solitude, is a digital only release and can be downloaded here: http://www.mediafire.com/?x8mawayn3zl5qmp
