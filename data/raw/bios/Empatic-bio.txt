Empatic - polish thrash death metal band formed in December 2005 in Otrołęka, Poland, by vocalist Maciej Rochaczewski and basist Włodzimierz "Włodas" Małaszek. In 2007, they released their first demo entitled "Promo". The same year, they played at the Panzerfest II Festival in Warsaw next to Hate and Crionics. 
In 2010, band released their first full length album "Gods of Thousand Souls" in Poland. In 2012, thanks to Terrasound Records album has been released across the world.

Current line-up:
* Maciej Rochaczewski – vocals 
* Włodzimierz "Włodas" Małaszek – bass 
* Przemysław "Sesyl" Cikacz – guitar 
* Krzysztof "Criss" Bendarowicz – drums
