Formed in Portland, OR, the Metal Shakespeare Company can best be described as a mixture of early Iron Maiden and the works of one William Shakespeare. They list Manowar, Dio, Judas Priest, and Mercyful Fate, as well as Iron Maiden and The Bard as their other influences.
