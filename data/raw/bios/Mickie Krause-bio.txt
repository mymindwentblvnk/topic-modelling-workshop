Mickie Krause (born 1974 as Michael Engels) is one of the most popular singers in the German "Schlager" music business. Most of his songs have rather simple, vulgar and/or insulting lyrics and deal with topics such as women or drinking - often with ironic subtext. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mickie+Krause">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
