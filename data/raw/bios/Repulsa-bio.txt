1. Repulsa (real name: Tambre Bryant) was a thrash-punk/metal parody artist from San Francisco, CA. Besides performing on stage, Tambre was also a band, nightclub, and music studio recording manager. Although known for her abrasively raw, over-the-top, gruff, angst-ridden vocals, she is a classically-trained singer. 

The band was originally formed in 1991. Other past band members include Devon Walker, Randy Markum, Carlton Melson, Scott McKenzie, Tom Hunting (of Exodus), Jon Torres, and Mark McGee (of Vicious Rumors) .
