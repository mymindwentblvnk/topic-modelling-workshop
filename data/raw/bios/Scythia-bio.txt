Scythia is:
An up and coming indie/alternative rock band from Tameside, Manchester. Their debut LP is scheduled for release Autumn/Winter 2008.

A metal band from Vancouver, BC, who combine haunting folk melodies with the driving force of modern progressive metal to form a raw and powerful sound that is their own. Their debut album, "...of War" is a chilling story of triumph and tragedy.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scythia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
