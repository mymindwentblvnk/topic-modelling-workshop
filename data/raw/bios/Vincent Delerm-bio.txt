Vincent Delerm (born August 31, 1976 in Évreux, France) is a French singer, songwriter, pianist, and composer. He is the son of the writer Philippe Delerm.

His self-titled debut was released in April 2002, and received praise from critics for its intimist moods. The song Tes parents received notable attention. Delerm's second album, Kensington Square, was released in April 2004, and a third, Les piqûres d'araignée, in September 2006. All three albums were released on the record label Tôt ou tard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vincent+Delerm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
