Blackened sludge/crust/doom band from Oakland, CA, USA, formed in 2010.
Debut album "Tomb Of Feathers" was released on CD by The Path Less Traveled Records in 2012 and again in 2014 on 12" LP by 7 Degrees Records, Shove Records and Trendkill Recordings. Sophomore LP "Wound Empire" was released in early 2015 by Vendetta Records, 7 Degrees Records, Fragile Branch Records and Shove Records.
