Distilling Pain borns in the summer of 2007 with the purpose of create extreme metal with no creative barriers. The great diversity of musical influences both within extreme metal and within many other different genres no related with extreme metal of each of its members originates a unique style which, although it does remind of some classic extreme metal bands in its roots, it clearly shows this band pursues a distintive sound which is currently being developed. Current lineup: Yova (Vocals), X. Alonso (Guitars), A. Veiga "Tuco" (Guitars), Carlos Pensado (Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Distilling+Pain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
