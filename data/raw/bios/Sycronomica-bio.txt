Sycronomica is a Melodic Black Metal band hailing from Munich, Germany. Formed in 1996, they released the Demo "Durch das Geaest" the same year. In 2000 they released a Live Demo, followed by a Promo Demo in 2002. In 2004 they finally released their first Full-Length album "Paths". In fall 2006, their latest album "Gate" has been released. Their lyrical themes are Feelings and Fantasy Tales.
