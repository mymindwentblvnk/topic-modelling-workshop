Paragoria is a band that continued the work of a band named The Scourge. Their music is defined as death/thrash/groove metal. The first big festival apppearance of this band was at Paranoid Open Air festival in 2009, right after releasing their first demo cd From the Ashes. In 2011 the band renamed and changed their drummer and genre of music, applying some new progressive metal elements. The new Paragoria introduced new songs and line-up in July 2011 at Metalcamp festival in Tolmin. A month later they performed at Metal Mania Open Air festival in Komen. In 2012 the band worked on the layout of their own studio, where they self-recorded their new album. The album is currently in production phase and is expected to be released in 2013. Current band line-up: Matjaž Parfant (vocals, guitar), Jernej Štorgelj (vocals, bass), Primož Brezovnik (guitar), Matija Kadliček (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paragoria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
