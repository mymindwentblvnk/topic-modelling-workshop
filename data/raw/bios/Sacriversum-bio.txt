Sacriversum started as a death metal band in Lodz (Poland). But soon introduced female vocals, keyboards and changed style into more gothic and melodic. 


Discography (from Encyclopaedia Metallum):

The Shadow Of The Golden Fire (Full-length, 1994)
Soteria (Full-length, 1998)
Beckettia (Full-length, 2000)
Shadow Of The Golden Fire - Early Days (Best of/Compilation, 2001)
Mozartia (Full-length, 2003)
Sigma Draconis (Full-length, 2004)
