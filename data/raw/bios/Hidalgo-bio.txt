Hidalgo are a German Band, making music somewhere between PJ Harvey, Sonic Youth, Pixies and english New-Wave Band.

Formed in 1999, the band consits of Betty Muggler, Hans-Christian Fuss (Drummer of "Robocop Kraus" and Andy Necker (Bassist of Multiboy).

The Debut called "Sing guitar sing" was released in 2002, the followup named "I want a girlfriend" was published in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/HIDALGO">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
