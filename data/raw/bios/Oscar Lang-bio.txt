Oscar Lang is a British indie rock artist born May 4th, 2000 in London. He got signed to the UK label Dirty Hit in 2017 and began releasing music under the moniker pig. In early 2019 he dropped that name and went ahead releasing music under his real name. After releasing a few EPs and a mixtape, his debut album Chew the Scenery was released in mid-2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oscar+Lang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
