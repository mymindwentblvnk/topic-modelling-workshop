Flaccid Clitoris is a gore- influenced brutal death metal band from Italy. The band fromed in 2007 by Tom (Vocals) and Vince (Guitars/ Drum Programming). They play a impetigo, Mortician inspired sample brutal death metal with Vulvectomy and Viral Load slam to groove influences.

They released a two-track demo called "Lymphangitic Carcinomatosis" in 2008 which has the work of Inveracity meeting Stabwound and Putrid Pile.
