Death Power is a thrash metal act, from France  (Orange, Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur). They formed in 1986, and released two official Demos, as well an EP, and one full-length album. Their EP "Death Power" was released in 1989, while their only full-length "The Bogeyman" was released in 1990 on Musidisc records.

The lineup consisted of:

Vocals, Bass: David Thimel
Guitars: Philippe Tourville
