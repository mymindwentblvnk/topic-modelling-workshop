There are multiple artists using this name.

1.  Danton is an English DJ based in london, formally known as Wam Way who used to promote in Norwich. Mixing alot of house, garage, techno and future sounds. 

2.  German state of the art screamo/post-hardcore combination. With Benjamin on the guitar, max on the drums and Fabian on the bass this trio is making its way through the modern screamo sound. This band is from Wuppertal, and are close friends to another screamo band named Faced moment from the same region. They bring very emotional screamo with lots of breakdowns. Their shows are best described as intense, powerful and memorizing. Their main influences would be their general thoughts and feelings but like very other. They also like screamo, hardcore and general rock. They don't have a record label but they released a demo called "demo 2006" existing of 6 amazing tracks. 

3.  Danton were a heavy metal from Germany and released one album called Way Of Destiny in 1988. Frontlined by Peter Dirkschneider, Brother of ex-Accept's acclaimed singer Udo Dirkschneider. Peter Dirkschneider later founded another heavy metal band called Vanize. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Danton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
