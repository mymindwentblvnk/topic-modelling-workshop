Formed back in 2015, Cranial Contamination is a Slamming Brutal Death Metal duo from Bridgwater - Somerset, England. Active members: Will Willcox - Vocals, Ellwood Newbon - Guitars. Joined by live musicians Jake 'Mettle' Ettle-Iles and Roy Feyen - Bass, Andrew Bolton - Drums. Their discography consists in a couple of EP's - The debut one released in 2016, called 'Engineered Human Obsolescence' through Sevared Records, and another one named 'From the Absence of Existence' unveiled at early 2018. Their latest record called 'Into the Warp' out now throughout Realityfade Records.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cranial+Contamination">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
