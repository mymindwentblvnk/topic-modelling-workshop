Contusio is a death/thrash metal band founded in Tuzla in 2007. after hardcore metal band Sacrifice broke up. They have recorded their first rehearsal demo  „Final Murder“ in 2007. and home studio demo/EP  „Trauma of Detonation“ in 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Contusio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
