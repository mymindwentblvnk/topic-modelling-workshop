York, North Yorkshire, England (2009 - Present)

Shadows Lie Within are a five-man metalcore group from York, England which formed in 2009, consisting of vocalist Nick Marsh, guitarists Danny Munn and Gavin Seargent, bassist Ollie Altham and drummer Kenny McGeachie.
Their influences include Chimaira, As I Lay Dying, Meshuggah, All That Remains, Lamb Of God, Killswitch Engage..etc <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shadows+lie+within">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
