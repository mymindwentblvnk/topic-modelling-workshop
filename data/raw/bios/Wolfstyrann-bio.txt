Wolfstyrann is a black metal band from Germany. It's founding date is unknown but the band is still active at the moment. 

The current line-up excists only of Blutzorn (N. Jürs). He plays all instruments and does the vocals. 

Wolfstyrann has completed so far:

- Aus den Tiefen dunkler Wälder Demo, 2004  
- Zeit des Blutes Demo, 2005  
- Vereint Unter Dem Banner Der Ahnen Split, 2005
