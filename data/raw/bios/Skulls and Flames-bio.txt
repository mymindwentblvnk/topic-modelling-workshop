Skulls And Flames (S.A.F.) was a five piece  thrash / crossover / hardcore band from the Netherlands started back in 2000. Inspired by lots of bands, S.A.F. found the roots of their music in 80's styled thrash / hardcore / crossover music. Their aim was try to sound like those bands, but without all the fancy stuff. During their existence, which ended in 2006, they released several records and have done many touring throughout Europe.

Band members are/were involved in : Waking The Dead, Mary Bell, Rupsband, Vandersloot, Tuco Ramirez, Villa Fungus, StoorZender, Valser, and Hotel Fire. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skulls+And+Flames">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
