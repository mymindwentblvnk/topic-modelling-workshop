Fate Keeper was a brazilian power metal band who recorded the track "Theatre of Pain" for the Blind Guardian tribute "Tales from the Underworld", released in 2003.

Band members were:
Eduardo Leal - bass, keyboards, vocals
Rodrigo Novaes - guitars, backing vocals
