If Dracula formed a progressive power metal band... Mortanius (Lucas Flocco - Vocals ‡ & Jesse Shaw - Bass ‡) is a progressive power metal band from Plymouth Meeting/Conshocken, PA with symphonic and neo-classical elements that is working towards creating a unique sound that will set them apart from other bands. Highly recommended for fans of Angra, Symphony X, King Diamond, Moi Dix Mois, Circus Maximus, Malice Mizer, Royal Hunt, Mercyful Fate, Galneryus, Dream Theater, Versailles, Light Bringer, Rhapsody of Fire. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortanius">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
