Indie band 'No Mans Land' hail from the diamond of the north, Preston. The Band consists of: 
Stuart Blundell (Rhythm Guitar/Vox)
Joseph Wilkins (Lead Guitar/Backing Vox)
Daniel Fitzsimmons (Bass Guitar/Backing Vox)
Matthew Maher (Drums/Backing Vox)
