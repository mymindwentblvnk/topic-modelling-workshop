Mal Rojo (spanish for Red Illness) takes form on march 2009, when Sebastian Ortiz (Guitars for Dias de Ira) and Andres Jamillo (former Drummer for Asepsia) decide to focus their musical efforts under one band. After a while Andres Espinosa (Former guitarist on Asepsia and former Humano Frontman) and Mauricio Cornejo join this new project and complete its line up.
