Rotten Specimen is original Thrash/ Metal/ Experimental band formed in 2007 by Benny Rodrigo. The band has had several musicians but the most latest/power line up has been Benny Rott: Composer/Lyrical Writer/Guitarist Jessica Rizz on melodic bass/lyrical Writing and Chris Reyes on lead compositions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rotten+Specimen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
