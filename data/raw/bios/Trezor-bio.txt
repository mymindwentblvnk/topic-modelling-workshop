Trezor is Czech Metal band. It's similar to bands like Helloween, Running Wild, etc.
Most of songs lyrics are in czech language. This band release 5 albums - Síla Života (1990), V Srdci Tvém Piští (1991) Fight Your Heart (1992) Máš Svou Šanci! (1994) Tváři v Tvář (1996). The most famous is Máš Svou Šanci! released in 1994. This band was on the top of czechoslovak' metal stream with band Kreyson, singer Ľáďa Křížek, Arakain,...
