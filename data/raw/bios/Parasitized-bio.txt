Wrathful and Destructive Death Metal from Hull, England.  Parasitized released their debut EP "Existence Unveiled"  online in February 2010.  This was followed by a physical release through Blast Head Records in February 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parasitized">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
