Altar of Profanation is a one-man black metal act that was founded in 2012 by Monty Johnson in Valdosta, Ga. This artist is affiliated with Pagan Pride Records, Sylvan Screams Analog, and Against PR. There is a demo, 2 full length albums, and an ep available for purchase and download through the band, labels, and various online sources.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Altar+of+Profanation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
