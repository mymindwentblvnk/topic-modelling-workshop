Schlaflos is a Black Metal solo-project formed in Schwedt, Germany in 2005 and is led by M.v.V. The music is mostly instrumental and atmospheric. The project often works with clean and distorted guitar-only parts.

M.v.V. is also the sole member of Veineliis

Releases:

"s/t" Demo 1, 2007
 Avsked / Schlaflos Split CD, 2008
"s/t" Demo 2, 2010
