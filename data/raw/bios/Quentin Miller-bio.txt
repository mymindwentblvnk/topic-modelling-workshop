Popular rapper who became the center of controversy in 2015 for allegedly ghostwriting songs for hip-hop superstar Drake. He has collaborated with Drake on songs amidst the controversy, and put out his own three-part mixtape series called Hey Thanks Alot.

He was working in a bakery when he got on the phone with Drake and his life changed forever.

He joined a group called WDNG Crshrs. He formerly went by the stage name of Milla, a name in which he released mixtapes called Recreational Activities and Max's Endeavors.
