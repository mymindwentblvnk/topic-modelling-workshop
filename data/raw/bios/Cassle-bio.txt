Cassle was a heavy metal/progressive rock band from Los Angeles, California.  They formed in 1979 and it is not known when they disbanded.  They released one single, one EP and one compilation.  They appear on a split called Forgotten Metal Collector's Series: Outstanding Metal Gems Vol. 9. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cassle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
