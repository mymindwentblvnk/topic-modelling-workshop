Celticmoon was a German black metal band from the mid 90s that released one demo in 1996 titled "My Illusion of an Eternal Winter". The sole member also had a project called Nightwolf that released a split with Ravenclaw (another obscure band featuring a member of Moonblood) in 1996, as well as playing bass and guitar off and on for the German black metal band Paria and playing session guitar for Deathgate Arkanum. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Celticmoon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
