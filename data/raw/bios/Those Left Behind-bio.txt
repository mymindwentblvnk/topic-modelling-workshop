Those Left Behind is a death metal band from Norway. They were formed in 2010.

They released their debut album "Horror Classics And Other Tributes To The Darkside" via Horror Pain Gore Death Productions in 2011 with notable covers of Napalm Death, Sepultura and oddly The Beach Boys. The album features twisted rythms, devasting snarls and blasting fills.
