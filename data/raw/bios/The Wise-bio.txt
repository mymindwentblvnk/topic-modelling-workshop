There are several bands called The Wise: 1) An Italian indie/folk rock trio; 2) An Indonesian rock band.

1) The band was founded in 2012 by Pietro Porretto. In 2014 they took part to the 8th season of the Italian version of The X Factor. Their first album, "DOOE", was released in March 2017. Their sound is heavily influenced by Fleet Foxes, CSNY, Kings of Leon, Kings of Convenience and Bon Iver.

2) THE WISE INDONESIA (Surabaya), the band was formed in last Year 2012.GEA was formed by raditya saksono (electric nation) , erwin zakiy ( freaky frydie) angga yudha ( the lounge ) and alvino heboh ( berlin night club) .the first single "Time Machine" was released on january 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Wise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
