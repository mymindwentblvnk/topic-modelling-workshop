INVAIN, a band from Padua, Italy, has attracted the audiences of many Italian cities, from Bolzano to Catanzaro, in a lot of astounding live performances.

The young trio of INVAIN has established itself by the distinctive drive towards music of Alessandro, Pierpaolo and Christopher. Together they have prepared a self-produced 9-track CD and an extract album consisting of 3 tracks and 2 promo songs recorded in 2006 at the Red House Recordings studios, under the artistic direction of David Lenci, and which is herewith submitted to your attention.

The diverse musical influences of the band include grunge, stoner, prog. Metal e cross-over. The band has not signed any discographic contract and is presently looking for collaborations and proposals.
