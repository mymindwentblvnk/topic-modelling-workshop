Alexis "Mutagene" Glass was born in Canada and now lives in Japan.  His music is defined by an electronic style that draws heavily upon dub reggae, but with a distinctly chilly urban ambience.  Currently, some of his work is posted at http://trilete.net/ as is his Doctor Who remix project and his work with the Gas Station All Stars.  Major influences / similar artists include Scientist and Amon Tobin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mutagene">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
