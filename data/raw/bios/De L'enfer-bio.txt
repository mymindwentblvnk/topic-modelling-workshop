De L'enfer is a Russian composer and keyboardist. His discography comprises one instrumental album "Castletown" composed in a genre of soundtracks for adventure movies. Also he has sent several tracks (such as his own remixes of main themes to "Pirates of the Caribbean" and "Transformers" to Hans Zimmer's Studio where the tracks were accepted well.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/De+L%27enfer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
