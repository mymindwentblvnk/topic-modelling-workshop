Psychopathy is a five piece death metal band out of London Ontario. The band has been together since September 2009 and has gone through many line up changes in this time. Right now we are working on new material to keep the London metal scene thriving! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychopathy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
