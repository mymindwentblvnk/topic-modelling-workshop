Introspectre is a thrash metal band from Milwaukee, Wisconsin

Members:
Rod Wadzinski - vocals, guitars
Rick Edward - guitars, vocals
Adam "XaQ" Granatella - bass
Steve Johnson - drums

They released an album on Megalithic Records in 1996
