Rising from the small town known as Gainesville, Texas, where the only music you can find with a hard and heavy edge to it has the suffix -core, The Great Hanging is a nostalgic dose of ass kicking Heavy Metal taking influence from genre staples such as Judas Priest, Slayer, Black Sabbath and Holy Grail the band has begun it's metallic assault on north Texas and beyond. The Great Hanging currently has a three track live demo out. The Great Hanging currently has performed with artists such as Megatherian, Fierce Band! and Baring Teeth to name a few.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Great+Hanging">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
