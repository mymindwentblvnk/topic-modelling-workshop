Unconventional Disruption is a brutal five-piece deathgrind band from Italy formed in 2007. They released a five song demo called "In a Life of Death to Nothing" in 2009.

Their upcoming full lenght is called "Profusion to Euthanasia"

Members:

Max - Guitar
