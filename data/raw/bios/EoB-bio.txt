EOB is English guitarist Edward John O'Brien (born 15 April, 1968 in Oxford), a member of alternative rock band Radiohead. O'Brien makes extensive use of effects units to create atmospheric sounds and textures, and provides backing vocals. His first solo album, Earth, was released in 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/EOB">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
