Juan Carlos Ozuna Rosado (born March 13, 1992), known simply by his surname Ozuna , is a Puerto Rican Latin trap and reggaeton singer who is regarded as the " New King of Reggaeton". He has released two successful albums Odisea (2017) and Aura (2018), and since the beginning of his career, he has sold around 15 million records, making him one of the best-selling Latin music artists of all time.
