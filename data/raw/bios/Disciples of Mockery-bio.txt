Disciples of Mockery was a Brutal death metal band from USA. They were formed by 3 of the four members of Incantation. Including vocalist Craig Pillard.  They released one album Prelude to Apocalypse" which composes much of the same elements as Incantation but with more crisp touches added to the performance.

The band later did a split album with Womb in 1999. After the band split to go and join the reminder of Womb after most of those members left. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disciples+of+Mockery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
