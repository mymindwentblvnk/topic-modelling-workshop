Heather Woods Broderick is an American musician and composer. She has been part of Efterklang, Loch Lomond and Horse Feathers tour band. Currently (2012) she's a member of Sharon van Etten 's backing band.
Her debut solo album "From the Ground" was released in October 2009. 

She is the sister of Peter Broderick, also a solo artist and Efterklang collaborator.
