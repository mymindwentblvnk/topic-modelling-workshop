Sofía Reyes, born September 25, 1995, is a Mexican singer residing in Los Angeles.

Her first single, which is sung in both English and Spanish, is called “Muevelo” and it features Wisin of the former reggaeton duo, Wisin y Yandel. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sofia+Reyes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
