BLACKLAND RAIDER KLAN

Raider Klan is founded by SpaceGhostPurrp. Members include (or included) SpaceGhostPurrp, Yung Simmie, Denzel A.K.Curry (Raven Miyagi), Mike Dece, Key Nyata, Sky Lexington, Amber London, Young Renegade, Grandmilly, Eddy Baker, Dough2x, Lil Champ FWAY and many others. All this shit is dope, most of their music is inspired by the 90s artists from New Orleans, Miami, Memphis, California, but they add their own originality to it.
