Auroboros is a metal/rock band from Washington, DC. They formed in 2010, and have since released a demo in 2010 and a self-titled EP in 2011 through Australopithecus Records.

Members:
Brian Blickle: vocals, guitar (ex-Baroness and Accursed Dawn)
Jake Smith: guitar (ex-Gnarwolf)
Rob Moore: bass (ex-Salome)
Tommy Parry: drums
