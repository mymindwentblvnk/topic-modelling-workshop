MAW, who entered the market with his single called BMW on 7 March 2018, joined "RedKeys Music" team in a short time. On 7 January 2019, MAW and his team mate EF released a single. On March, he released his first major label single called HOTEL under RedKeys Music & Basemode Records/Sony Music label and goes silent for a short time. After this silence, the young star signals that the preparations of his album continued with releasing his new single on 7 December called "KARAMBOL" which he defines as a letter to his mother and which displays his current status and moral. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MAW">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
