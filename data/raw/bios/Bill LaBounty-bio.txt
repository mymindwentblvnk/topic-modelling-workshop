Bill LaBounty is an American musician. 
He was initially a singer-songwriter in the soft rock genre, first as a member of the band Fat Chance, and later as a solo artist. 

As a solo artist, LaBounty recorded six studio albums, including four on Curb Records/Warner Bros. Records. 

In the 1990s shifted his focus to country music and has co-written several songs for artists such as Steve Wariner.
