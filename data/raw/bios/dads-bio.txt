Dads was an American emo band originating out of Piscataway, NJ. The group consisted of John Bradley (drums, vocals) and Scott Scharinger (guitar, vocals), with Ryan Azada (bass) joining a couple of months before their break up in June 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dads">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
