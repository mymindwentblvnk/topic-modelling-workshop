There are several artists called England

1) England, operating in England during the 70s. The most famous was a four piece progressive rock group that released an album called Garden Shed in 1977 plus a collection of tracks from about the same time released in 1997 called Last of the Jubblies. The group reunited in 2005 to play some live date.

2) The other England is less well-known, a straight rock band that released a self-titled album in 1976.

3) England is a country. This page can be used for things such as traditional English songs that have had their authors forgotten over time, and the English national anthem.
