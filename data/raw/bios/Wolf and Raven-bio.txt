Wolf and Raven is a team of two brothers working as one to create interesting aesthetic synthwave music. Starting in June of 2014, with the goal to spread their music across the globe, the duo is Influenced by the 1980s culture, movies, video games, cartoons, and music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wolf+and+Raven">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
