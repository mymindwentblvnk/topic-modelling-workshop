Temilade Openiyi (born 11 June 1995), better known by her stage name Tems, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and record producer. In May 2021, Tems was ranked number 1 on the Billboard Next Big Sound chart and number 5 on the Billboard Emerging Artist chart. In September 2021, Tems was signed to RCA records and announced as Apple Music Up Next artist of the month. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TEms">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
