Released in English as Nu Virgos.

Ukrainian girl group singing in Russian. Created and managed by well-known record producer Konstantin Meladze, who is also the brother of famous Russian-language singer Valery Meladze. The group name means “Viagra” and “The Band “Gra” simultaneous - it’s play on words. Girls look pretty sexy on stage and singing the corresponding songs. This is the one of the keys of their extraordinary popularity both in Russia and Ukraine.

Members as of October 2020 are Ulyana Sinetskaya, Sofia Tarasova, and Ksenia Popova. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/%D0%92%D0%B8%D0%B0+%D0%93%D1%80%D0%B0">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
