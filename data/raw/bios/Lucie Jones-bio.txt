Lucie Bethan Jones (born 20 March 1991) is a Welsh singer, actress, and model. Jones first came to prominence while competing on series 6 of The X Factor UK in 2009, where she placed eighth. She will represent the United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest 2017, with the song "Never Give Up on You". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lucie+Jones">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
