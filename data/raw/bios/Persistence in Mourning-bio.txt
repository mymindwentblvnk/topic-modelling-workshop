Persistence in Mourning is a solo project by A. Lippoldt that plays minimalistic funeral doom that verges on ambient at times. This project has relesed one album, several EPs and Splits with many well known artists such as Rigor Sardonicous and Worship . <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Persistence+in+Mourning">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
