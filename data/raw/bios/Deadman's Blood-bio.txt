Deadman's Blood is a solo UK Death Metal band. Matt Bass (Wicthclan - UK Black Metal band) is the man behind this Old School Death Metal project. His aim is to destroy your eardrums with raw catchy death metal, the way he loves it. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deadman%27s+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
