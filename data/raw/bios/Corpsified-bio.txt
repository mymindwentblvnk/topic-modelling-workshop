Corpsified is an Indonesdian one-man slamming brutal death metal band located in Blitar of East Java, which was formed in year 2014. Now he's under Dismembered Records and released a demo called "Demo 2014" which includes an intro and a track called "Stench of Decomposed Flesh". The demo was limited in 50 copies and extremely rare in physical form. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Corpsified">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
