Thousand Year Rain is an earthshaking goth powerhouse—replete with sledgehammer riffs, mournful acoustic passages, and cryptic symbology.  Drawing on the influence of artists ranging from Motörhead and King Diamond to Dirk Serries and Kvitrafn, Sankt writes and records in a private studio near Concord.  And the end result is a grim, down-tuned wall of instrumental combustion. — Kirsten Ashwood, TOH Media <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thousand+Year+Rain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
