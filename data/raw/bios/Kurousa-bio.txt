Kurousa-P (黒うさP), also known as WhiteFlame (ホワイトフレイム), is a popular VOCALOID music writer. Prior to producing VOCALOID music, he had been an active amateur musician for quite some time. He debuted with the release of six VOCALOID songs in a single month, receiving high acclaim for his third song, "Shirayuki." His following work, "Cantarella," became a hit, establishing his popularity. However, Kurousa-P is best known for Hatsune Miku's mega-hit "Senbonzakura," released in 2011. As of June 2021, it has received over 14,900,000 views on Niconico, claiming its spot as the second most-watched VOCALOID song on Niconico. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kurousa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
