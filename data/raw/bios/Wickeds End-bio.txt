Wickeds End was a Christian thrash metal band from Upland, California.  They formed in 1992, released one demo (Temporal Pain) in 1993 and then split up.

Members of the band include:

Robert Powell - Drums
Dave Chafee
Joe Prainito
Brett Caddell - Vocals
Glenn Rowlands

Former members included:

Chadd Felts - Vocals
