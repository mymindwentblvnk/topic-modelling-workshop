Proprietors of Pure Virginia Metal. Croatoan hails from Falls Church, Virginia delivering a unique, punishing brand of speed, thrash, and groove. Taking their name from the oldest piece of American history and the folklore that has developed around it, the band prides itself on bringing their venomous message to anyone who stands in their path.
