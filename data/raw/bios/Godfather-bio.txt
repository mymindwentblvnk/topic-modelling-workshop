Origin: Cambridge, England, UK
Members: Real Deal, Tiny Monkey, Jellyroll, Bassman, Colonel

Formed in 2002 by guitarist Real Deal (Tom Bainbridge), Godfather released their debut album, Money Shot, in February 2002. The majority of the songwriting is from Real Deal who is also their recording engineer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Godfather">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
