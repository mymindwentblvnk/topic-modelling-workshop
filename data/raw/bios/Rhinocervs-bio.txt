Rhinocervs is a label based out of Southern California, often releasing uncredited and/or untitled cassettes. The label prioritizes art over artist - not the cult of personality attached. Anonymous releases are differentiated by catalog number and artwork. Not all of Rhinocervs output is untitled, bands signed to the label include Odz Manouk, Tukaaria, Absum, Nihilobstat, and Glossolalia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rhinocervs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
