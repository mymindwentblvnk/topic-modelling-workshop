ddp (until 2005 known as Der Dicke Polizist) is a german punk band, which was founded in 1993 in Koblenz (near Bonn). ddp's music can be discribed as critical, direct and political punkrock.
After many shows in their town, ddp produced their first demo tape "Keine Gerechtigkeit, keine Liebe" (1994). In the following 3 year the band became more and more popular and plays shows with Wizo. Fuckin' Faces and Dödelhaie. Then they produced their second demo tape, called "Wir habens satt". In 1998 the next demo tape "Was den lieben Nachbar stört" and their first album "Es geschah am hellixten Tag" had been produced. ddp produced the next album in the year 2002, which is called "Und die Hoffnung stirbt zuletzt". In 2005 the next album "ddp" had been published. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DDP">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
