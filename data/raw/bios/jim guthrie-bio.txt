Jim Guthrie is a Canadian singer-songwriter. He used to be in the band Royal City until they disbanded in 2004. He also worked with Islands on Return to Sea. He was born and raised in Guelph, Ontario and currently lives in Toronto. Guthrie first made a name for himself by releasing a series of self-produced cassettes, and subsequently released albums on Three Gut Records. He was nominated for a Juno Award for his album Now, More Than Ever. His song "Hands in My Pocket" appeared in a television commercial for Capital One's Canadian branch in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jim+Guthrie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
