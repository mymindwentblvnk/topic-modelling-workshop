Light Denied was formed on March 2011. in Belgrade by  Alex Sindjelic and Marko Gospavic. After having created dozens of songs they have decided to record their first three to give people of Serbia some different kind of Death Metal. 
Bassist Dusan Jakovljevic and guitarist Nenad Krpesic are now full time members of the crew.
