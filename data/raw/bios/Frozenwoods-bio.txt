"Frozenwoods" is the forest black metal project by two men from two different Siberian cities - Novosibirsk and Krasnoyarsk.

Members:
Malzus (ex-Side of Despondency) - vocals, lyrics
Foltath (Innmorke, Breath Of Beherith, EORONT ) - all instruments, music
