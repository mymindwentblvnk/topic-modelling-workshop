Formed in 2011 by Scott A.(A.Morbid) He is the conductor and main writer of the band. A 6 song demo was recorded using a drum machine and was released on Tape via Tridroid Records. The first Preteen Deathfuk show was played live on 11/22/2012 with two session members but ended up not becoming permanent members. In 2013 B.Devastation was recruited to play drums and soon they both recorded their first full length album called "I Don't Remember..." and was released on February 2013 By Hidden Marly Prod. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Preteen+Deathfuk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
