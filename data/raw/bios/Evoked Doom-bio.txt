German thrash metal band formed in 1984 and split-up in 1987. Not much is known about the band, bassist Oliver Barth aka Sherron is a member of Cluster Bomb Unit and played in Decontrol and Furious Rankness, while a man known as "Zombie" was the vocalist. Released two demos: Deadrehtape (1985)  and Evoked Doom (1986). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evoked+Doom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
