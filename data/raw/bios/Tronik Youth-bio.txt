Tronik Youth is an alias for DJ/remixer/producer Neil Parnell.
He has released two singles on Back-Yard (home of Gossip & Chromeo) and recent remix work with Rex the Dog, Shinchi Osawa and The Filthy Dukes. The new single Disko Suks set for release in sept and album imminent featuring guest vocals from Ali Lov and My Tiger, My Timing Amongst Others. In the past year his D.J'ing has taken him to the U.S.A, Mexico, Brazil and Europe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tronik+Youth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
