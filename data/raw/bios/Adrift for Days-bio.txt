Hailing from Sydney, Australia, Adrift for Days are an underground stoner/doom quintet playing a diverse mix of styles described as "smoked-out psychedelic dronefuzz".

The band mixes a heavily psychedelic blues-based foundation with stoner rock, doom, sludge, post-rock, post-metal and drone. They cite their influences as Earth, Jimi Hendrix, YOB, The Doors, Neurosis, Pink Floyd, Rosetta and Boris - amongst many others.

After forming in 2009 August 2010 saw their 71-minute, self-released debut "The Lunar Maria" was met with almost universal critical acclaim. July 2012 saw the band release their sophomore album "Come Midnight...", which also received very positive press. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Adrift+for+Days">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
