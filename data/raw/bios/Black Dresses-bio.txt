Black Dresses is the collaborative noise pop project of Devi Mccallion (Girls Rituals) and Ada Rook. The duo had disbanded in early 2020 after ongoing  harassment, but the two have resumed the project with the disbanding as a part of the Black Dresses branding in 2021 after the release of Forever In Your Heart. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Dresses">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
