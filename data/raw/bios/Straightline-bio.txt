There are 2 bands named Straightline:

1) Straightline are a melodic hardcore band that formed in Munich, Germany in 2001. In 2005 they released their debut album "No Way out". The band has shared stages with Satanic Surfers, Red Lights Flash, Venerea and Ignite.

2) Straightline are a nationalistic rock band from Greece.
