Zithma is a heavy metal band from Panama, formed in 2004. Their style goes through many variations, from alternative rock, progressive rock and power metal. They've been influenced by a great variety of styles and genres: from metal to jazz, new age, jpop/jrock among others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zithma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
