Empyrean Throne is a technical blackened death metal band from Lake Forest, California.  They formed in the year 2011.  They consist of Andrew Knudsen (vocals), Mike Brennan (guitars, backing vocals), Spencer Strange (guitars) and Leviathan (drums).  Their lyrical themes include battles, darkness, demons and fantasy.  They are signed to M-Theory Audio.
