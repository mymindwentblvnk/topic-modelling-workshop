Moses Peter Pelham (* 24. Februar 1971 in Frankfurt am Main) is a German Rapper and Founder and Managing Director of the Pelham Power Productions (3P) music label.

After the band Rödelheim Hartreim Projekt disbanded, in 1998Moses Pelham released his first album "Geteiltes Leid 1". In 1999 Pelham was named Producer of the year (1998) in Germany. "Geteiltes Leid 2" followed in 2004. Also on the label Pelham Power Productions the soul singer Xavier Naidoo released his successful album "Nicht von dieser Welt". This was also produced by Pelham. 

Currently Pelham produces acts such as Glashaus, UnterWortverdacht, Illmatic, Sabrina Setlur and Sebastian Hämer.
