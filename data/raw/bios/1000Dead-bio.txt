1000Dead is a metal band formed in early 2013 in Rethymno, Greece. Their initial effort is comprised by a 3-song promo (Promo 2014), which was highly acclaimed both by local and foreign music press. The band's "thrash-meets-hxc-meets-southern metal-meets grind" self-titled debut album was unleashed globally by Ukrainian Metal Scrap Records in March of 2017. Up to this point, they been highly active on stage, and thus far has co-existed on a live setting with acts such as Sabbaton / Wolfheart and Ravencult to name just a few.. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/1000Dead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
