Collapse 7 are a Melodic Death Metal band from Austria (Vienna). They released the debut album "In Deep Silence" in 2004,  followed by 'Supernova Overdrive' in 2007.

Current line-up:
Werner: guitars (Hollenthon)
Gerry: guitars (Possession)
Mario: vocals/bass (Pungent Stench, Hollenthon)
Mike: drums
