Van Gimot (Agos, Devotion Omega, ex-Carrion, ex-Acherontas, ex-Cthonic Aura, ex-Nihasa, ex-Shibalba)
first formed the band in 1994 as Carrion, playing black metal. A one-track demo tape was released one year later. The name was changed into Virus of Koch in 1997. Only one gig was performed at this time. After a long hiatus the band reformed.
