Garreth Broke (Garreth Brooke) is a British born composer, pianist, teacher and curator of the contemporary sheet music project Upright, based in in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. He studied music at the University of Oxford, earning an MA. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Garreth+Broke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
