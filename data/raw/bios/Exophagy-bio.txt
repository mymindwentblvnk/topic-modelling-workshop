Exophagy is a 5-piece progressive death metal band from Fort Walton Beach, Florida. Their tracks were self-recorded by drummer Wesley Gibson. With influences such as Deeds of Flesh, Dying Fetus, and Hate Eternal Exophagy is sure to keep your head spinning and your bowels loose. Exophagy will be performing at the 2010 Gathering of the Sick Fest in Albequerque, NM. Check them out at myspace.com/exophagy850 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Exophagy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
