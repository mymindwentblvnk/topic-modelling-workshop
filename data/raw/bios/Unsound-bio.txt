10) UNSOUND was formed in Greenville, N.C., by guitarist Todd Paddock, and bassist Charles "Cheech" Massey in 1991. The soon to follow addition of Todd Houck to lead vocals and Trey Willits on drums completed the formation of UNSOUND as the present whole. Relocating to Raleigh, N.C. in 1998. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/unsound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
