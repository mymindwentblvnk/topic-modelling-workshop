Polish death metal/grindcore band from Bydgoszcz, formed in 1991 under the moniker Traitors (released the demo "Fuck Luck!"). In September 1992 they became Beastie Noise, releasing two demos in 1993: "Grindsters" and "Roller Death". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beastie+Noise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
