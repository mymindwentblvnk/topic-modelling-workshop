Efence is an electronic retrowave / outrun artist from Germany. His music is influenced by 80s sci-fi and space movies, games, and shows. Composition is inspired by, and contains nostalgic sounds of the 80s, cyberpunk vibes, and retro synths from the future. In September 2017 Efence released his debut album called Lost Future. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/efence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
