B.B.P is a shorthand name for Bassid, Boobytrip and Pradox. All three are dutch, and are psy producers pur sang. They have collaborated on the track Tide for the first time, and have succeeded to make a crystal clear, and spacious atmosphere with a heavy feel for outdoor sound.
