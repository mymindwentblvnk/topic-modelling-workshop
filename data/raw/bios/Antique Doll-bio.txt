Power/thrash metal band from Tokyo, Japan, formed in 1995. Antique Doll (アンティークドール) released two EP's, "Pleasure Land" and "Maze", in 1996. "Escape", a single, followed a year later.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antique+Doll">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
