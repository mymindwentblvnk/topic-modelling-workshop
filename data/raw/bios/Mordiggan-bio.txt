Mordiggan is a French progressive heavy metal band. They sing in their native tongue, and they have come up with ten good progressive metal songs on their 1st album called "Métamorphose". Mordiggan consists of Sophie on vocals and flute, Jérôme on vocals, Serge on lead guitar, Matthieu on guitar, Sandro on drums, Pierrick on bass, Loïc on keyboards and Mike on background vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mordiggan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
