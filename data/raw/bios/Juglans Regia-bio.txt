Juglans Regia is a solo project by Catalan musician Sergi Marcet. His music combines traditional Catalan instruments with electronic and trip-hop influences. He is part of the emerging Mediterranean roots music scene and is a member of the Catalan folk group Els Groullers.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Juglans+Regia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
