There are 3 bands/groups that share the name 'The Blizzard':

1. Norwegian trance producer duo (Lars-Christian Nyheim & Tore Vatle Jensen). Debut single "Kalopsia" released in 2007, receiving support from many of the world's biggest DJs.

2. Dunedin, New Zealand based drum'n'bass/techtronica/dubstep producer and DJ.

3. Italian independent goth metal group, active from 2000 until 2006.
