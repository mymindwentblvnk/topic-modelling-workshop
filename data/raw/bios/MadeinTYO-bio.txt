Malcolm Jamaal Davis (born April 12, 1992), known professionally as Madeintyo (stands for Made In Tokyo), is an American hip hop recording artist from Atlanta, Georgia He is best known for his debut single, "Uber Everywhere" from his mixtape "You Are Forgiven".
