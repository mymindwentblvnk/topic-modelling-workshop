Ezophagothomia is a Ukrainian brutal death metal band from Kyyiv. They originally formed 2002 but the project only lasted a few months before disbanding. Lev Kurgansky (Drummer 2002, 2008 - Current) reformed the band with a new lineup in the summer of 2008. The band self-released there first demo Ezophagothomical Invasion in July 2009.

Members:
Lev Kurgansky - Drums (2002, 2008- Current)
Vitaliy Drozdov - Vocals (2008- Current)
Vladislav Shekmar - Guitar (2008- Current)
Yury 'Crab' Kanivets - bass - Bass (2013- Current)
