Burialmound comes from Finland and plays brutal Death / Black metal influenced by such acts as Incantation, Morbid Angel, Grave, Behemoth, Bolt Thrower, Darkthrone etc.

The band started in 1994 as Utgard, they changed their name to BurialMound in 1998. However, in 2005 Utgard was resurrected as a separate band, and Burialmound has been laid on ice since then. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burialmound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
