-English Information-
Grondh was founded in the beginning of 2008. Because of the desire to play heavier and more evil sounding black metal, band immediately started to write their own
material. Band’s line-up has remained stable, with only slight changes, in order to represent black metal lifestyle and satanic philosophy with maximal passion, denying
any form of religiosity. Their music is based on the traditional black metal, in order to continue develop new possibilities and borders for already approved black metal
values. The word Grondh means battering ram. It is declaration of war to modern society’s values and church, placing the idea of superhuman = inhuman above everything else.
