A band appeared in town Vyshniy Volochek (Tver region) in 2006. Carry out hard antichristian Black Metal. Rehearsal demo "Izmoroz'" (Hoar-Frost) published in 2008 by self prod.. In summer 2010 the record of debut album begins on the Tver studio Craft. After a year, on August, 29, 2011 album "Odium" went out on label Musica Prod.
Lineup:
Belial - Bass, Vocal
Sagoth - Guitar, Vocal
