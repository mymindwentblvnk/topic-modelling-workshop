Miami, Fl

KALAKAI is a five piece Melodic Death Metal band formed in early 2006 out of the remnants of numerous local South Florida metal bands.
With influences ranging from Pantera, At the Gates, Cannibal Corpse, and Sepultura. Kalakai combines a unique taste in the new wave of American Metal.
