Notre Dame are two artists:

1) French based Notre Dame is a young promising electronic dj/producer. He notably remixed Avicii which gave him a lot of attention and played in several French events including the famous "Printemps de Bourges" festival. Notre Dame loves to run across different genres also as a composer which allowed him to sign deals with Stil vor Talent & Moblack Records recently. 
https://www.instagram.com/notredame.music/
https://www.facebook.com/notredamemusicfr

2) Notre Dame was a Swedish metal band formed in 1997 and disbanded in 2004. Members included Snowy Shaw (vocals), Vampirella (vocals), Jean-Pierre de Sade (guitar and bass) and Mannequin de Sade (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Notre+Dame">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
