Acerbic Angel is an upcoming danish thrash metal band from Horsens. The band released its first demo called "In Your Face"in may 2011 with three tracks, "Dead By Dawn", "Vivisection" and "Wall Of Death". 
The band consists of five members: Morten "IQ666" Quist (vocals), Thomas "Hævi" Møller and Søren Kammer (guitars), Søren "Skæg" Bregenov (bass) and Søren Ryan (drums).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Acerbic+Angel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
