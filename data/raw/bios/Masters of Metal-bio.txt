There is more that one artist with this name:

1.) Masters of Metal is a new band formed by members of Agent Steel: Juan Garcia (guitars), Bernie Versailles (guitars and lead vocals), Robert Cardenas (bass and backing vocals) and Rigo Amezcua (drums).

2.) Masters of Metal is a hair metal tribute band formed in Auckland, New Zealand in 2003. They play covers of Poison, Bon Jovi, Guns 'N Roses, Iron Maiden, Black Sabbath, and so on. The lead singers of The Managers and Malevolence are members of the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Masters+of+Metal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
