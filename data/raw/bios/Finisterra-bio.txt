Finisterra, Latin for 'end of the land', formed in 1982 as a Saltarello project, creating a religious-themed mix of rock, folk, wave and metal. They usually perform at medieval and fantasy festivals and events, with appropriate costumes and masks, playing instruments from the Middle Ages and performing fire juggling acts.
