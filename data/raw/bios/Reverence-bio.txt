Reverence are an Industrial Black Metal band from France. They formed in 1998 and released a few demos and splits between 2001 and 2004. Their 2003 split with fellow country men, Blut Aus Nord, is probably their most famous one. 

Discography:
- 2001 - Winds of North (demo)
- 2003 - Wenn Die Nacht Kommt... (demo)
- 2004 - Decorporation... (split with Blut aus Nord)
- 2004 - Anti Life Terror (split with Sons of Fenris and Wolok)
- 2005 - Industrial Mental Concept
- 2007 - Dissociated Human Junction (split with Blut aus Nord, Bloodoline and Karras)
- 2007 - Chamber of Divine Elaboration
- 2009 - Inactive Theocracy
