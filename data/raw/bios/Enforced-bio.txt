There are multiple artists using this name.

1.  A heavy metal band  from Solo, Central Java, Indonesia.  They are signed to Bloodlies Records.  They have a self-titled demo (released in 2008) and an EP entitled Bersama Sang Martir - Tersulut Api (released in 2011).  The band consists of  Arya Pradana (who is also in a band called Sporadic Bliss) on vocals,  Angga Satrio & Cahyo Y. on guitar,  Rizki Mahendra on bass and  Dwi P. Adi on drums.

2. A crossover thrash band from Richmond, Virginia. They formed in 2016 and have two EPs and two full length LPs, At the Walls and Kill Grid. The band consists of Knox Colby on vocals, Zach Monahan & Will Wagstaff on guitar, Connor McCormick on bass, and Zack Hullinger on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enforced">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
