Walter Williams, better known by his stage name ZelooperZ, is an American hip hop artist from Detroit, Michigan, USA. He released his first mixtape Coon N the Room: Eating Ramen Noodles While Watching Roots on Bootleg in 2011, and later linked up with Danny Brown and the Bruiser Brigade. He released his second mixtape, HELP, on June 13, 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ZelooperZ">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
