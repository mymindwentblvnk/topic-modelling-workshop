The Beast Of The Apocalypse is a Dutch Black Metal band formed in 2009. They released their first album ''A Voice from the Four Horns of the Golden Altar'' in that same year. They describe their music as dark mystical occult black metal and are inspired by bands such as Beherit , Mayhem and Archgoat. Their lyrics are about revelation and apocalyptic themes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Beast+of+the+Apocalypse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
