Phylactery is a death metal band from Brno, Czechia and was founded in 2013. Current members are:

Dominik Kuře - Lead Guitar, Backing Vocals
Aleš Krupica - Main Vocals
Jan Blaha - Bass
Tomáš Melichar - Rhythm Guitar
Mirek Hanák - Drums
