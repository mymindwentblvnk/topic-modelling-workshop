Black Kronstadt was an Anarchist hardcore/crust punk band from Victoria, BC, Canada.  Active from 1992 to 1997. Former members went on to form Iskra, who play a fusion of black metal and crust punk. A style they coined "blackened crust" Which could be seen in some of Black Kronstadt's later songs.
