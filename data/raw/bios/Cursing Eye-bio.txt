Cursing Eye is an industrial black/death metal band from France, Arvernia. Made in 1998 by Sirdek (Vocals, Guitar, Keys & Programming) also in Tryoxyn & Tempus Fugit and Migreich (Guitar).In 2000 they released they firs LP "Manipulated by Opposites", and in 2005 they first (!!!) demo "Awekening Live". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cursing+Eye">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
