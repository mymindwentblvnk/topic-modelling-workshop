Gold & Youth is a band from Vancouver and Toronto. They take an electronic sound to a live band presence. The band is known for their live show throughout Canada and have opened for bands like The Temper Trap and Metric. For their debut album 'Beyond Wilderness' they struck a deal with the label Arts & Crafts.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gold+&+Youth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
