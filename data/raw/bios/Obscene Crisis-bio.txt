Obscene Crisis is a death/grind band from Quebec, Canada that was formed in 1990, playing a perfect mix of death metal and grindcore. As per the information in Metal Archives, the band first split up in 1997/1998, then reunited in 2003 to make a new release. They split up again in 2004/2005 without releasing anything new, then reunited in 2007 again. They released an album named Silence of the Mind in 1995.

Their discography consists of : 
Modern Hypocrisy  (Demo, 1994)
a Promo/Demo "1995" cassette.
Silence of the Mind (Full-length, 1995)
