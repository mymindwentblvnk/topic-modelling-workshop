Bleed In Vain is a melodic death metal band from Italy. Originally established in 1999, it was a symphonic black metal band called Glory and is inspired by the Swedish melodic death metal scene. During the last four years they became one of the most innovative and important metalcore band from Italy and in the summer of 2005 they have been the first band from Italy who recorded in Studio Fredman with the producer Fredrik Nordstrom. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleed+in+Vain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
