Fruit Salad, from Boston, MA, played fast hardcore that infuses grind and thrash influences with powerviolence and youth crew elements. They formed in 2003 and played their last show in August 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fruit+Salad">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
