Silength was formed in Chihuahua, Chihuahua, México in 2004.

Their first EP, Desires from the Deep, was released in February 2007.

Members include:

Victor Hernandez (Vocals)
Eliel Mendoza Wong (Guitar)
Carlos A. Gonzàlez Olvera (Guitar)
Galia Mireles (Bass guitar)
Omar Ruvalcaba (Keyboards)
Manuel Alberto Perez (Drums)
