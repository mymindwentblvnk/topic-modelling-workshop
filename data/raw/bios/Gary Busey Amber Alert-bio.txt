A Technical/Melodic/Brutal Death Metal band from Sacramento, California who formed in 2011.  They only released one demo called 2011 Winter Demo in 2011.  They disbanded in 2013 and changed their name to Alterbeast. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gary+Busey+Amber+Alert">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
