Swamp Devil is a rock band from Portland Oregon USA.

Although pulling from many modern influences, its core sound is rooted in the power and depth of early heavy metal. Swamp Devil is Jesse Darling-bass and vocals, Steve Nobles-drums and vocals, Devon Rogers-Lead guitar and vocals, Chris Bowden-rhythm guitar and vocals.     <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Swamp+Devil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
