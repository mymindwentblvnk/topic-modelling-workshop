Killing Roots formed in West Seattle, Washington in 2000 as a groove metal band with hardcore influences. 

In 2007 Killing Roots and Black Sheep Records partnered up to release 'Black Water Death Grip', a honed collection of Killing Roots material and the group's first full length release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Killing+Roots">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
