Riko Azuna (安月名 莉子 Azuna Riko, born December 3, 1993) is a Japanese singer and songwriter who is affiliated with Kadokawa. She made her major debut in 2018 with the song "Kimi ni Furete" (君にふれて Touched By You), which was used as the opening theme to the anime series Bloom Into You. Her music has also been featured in Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World and Boogiepop and Others.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/%E5%AE%89%E6%9C%88%E5%90%8D%E8%8E%89%E5%AD%90">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
