357 a is three member band that are brothers. This southern rock band with a mix of modern rock. These three brothers are all under the age of 18. Austin is 17 and sings lead vocals and plays all the guitar parts for the band. Eric is 11 and sings backup vocals and plays bass. Tate sings backup vocals along with play drums. The band has now been together for three years. With their 1st album recorded by M7 Studios they are ready to take the journey of their own written  and composed music out to the world.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/.357">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
