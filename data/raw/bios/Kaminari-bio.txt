Kaminari is a Toronto-based duo who play, according to their MySpace page, "Sitar & Didgeridoo's World Electronic Fusion".  Their 2003 release, "Triangle," features ten instrumental tracks.  Influences include Portishead, Aphex Twin and Ravi Shankar.

Band Members
- Yoshi Yamano (Sitar, Guitar, Shamisen, Sample, Computer)
