Progressive Metal/Metalcore Band from Providence RI. If you like Soilwork, Nevermore, Iron Maiden, Queensryche, Into Eternity etc. Released 2 EP's with Heavyweight Metal Producer Zuess in the mid 2000's!  The band is currently in active but is planing a reunion in 2014! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hypersolid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
