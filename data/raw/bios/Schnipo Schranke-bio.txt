Schnipo Schranke was a indie-popband from Hamburg. It consisted of the singers and instrumentalists Daniela Reis and Fritzi Ernst. 
The band's genre was described by the paper Intro as "HipHop-Chanson-Fuck". 
Their most successful time was in 2014/2015 when their song Pisse, a song about unhappy love, was spread via social media. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Schnipo+Schranke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
