Winter Axe was formed in 2002 by Sacris (Drums) , Exacre (Guitar) , and Valkaezir (Bass/Vocals) , under the name of Black Dimension, then changed singer/guitarist with Exacre and joined with Kent the Destroyer (of Xiphias) to form WINTER AXE. Burn Abe of the Bow held bass duties shortly, but now Kent the Destroyer again has taken this position. Sacris has left the band. The current line-up is Exacre (vocals/guitar), Kent the Destroyer (bass) and Skelvaarg (drums). Exacre is also part of gothic metal band Thee I Worship and thrash metal band Last Eclipse <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Winter+Axe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
