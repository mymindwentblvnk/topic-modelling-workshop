NEVER COMES SILENCE is a Atmospheric / Gothic Metal band from Datteln, Germany, formed in 1999.

In the summer of 2002 the band released their first full length album "RED OCEAN". In 2005 NEVER COMES SILENCE presented their 2nd CD "ONE SECOND ETERNITY".

NEVER COMES SILENCE played a various number of concerts all over Germany, the Netherlands and Belgium.

Current line-up:

Guitars, Vocals : Thomas Droste
Guitars : Sascha Regmann
Drums: Fabian Regmann
