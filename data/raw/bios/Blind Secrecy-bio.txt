Blind Secrecy is a Finnish  band from Etelä-Karjala, Lappeenranta formed in 2006. Blind Secrecy plays melodic heavymetal with progressive influence. 

Anssi Salokannel - vocals
Marko Heikkilä - guitar
Markus Sepponen - guitar
Tommi Virolainen - drums
Heidi Saarelainen - bass

Band has released two demos; Crushed in 2006 and Of The Dying Land in 2007. In 2008 BS released promotional CD called Miscast Angel (recorded in Astia Studios).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blind+Secrecy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
