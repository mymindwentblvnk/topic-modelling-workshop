Raven Tide is a female fronted Symphonic/Gothic Metal band from Italy.

The band was formed in 2009 by Cheryl and Shark, with the accompany of Mark and Fred, and during the same year they released a EP, "Ever Rain", which is said to feature songs from their upcoming full-length.

Since the release of their EP they've been featured on various Compilation-albums and they are currently working on their debut album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raven+Tide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
