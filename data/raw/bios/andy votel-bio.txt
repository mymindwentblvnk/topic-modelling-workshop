Andy Votel (real name Andrew Shallcross) is an electronic musician and producer from Marple Bridge in Stockport. 

In 1997, he set up Twisted Nerve Records with Damon Gough (AKA Badly Drawn Boy). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Andy+Votel">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
