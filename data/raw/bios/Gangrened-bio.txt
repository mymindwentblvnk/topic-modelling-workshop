Gangrened is a Finnish sludge band from Kokkola, Finland formed in 2012.

Members:

Ollijuhani Kujansivu - vocals;
Andreas Österlund (I, Captain) - guitar;
Jon Imbernon - guitar;
Ari Kuorikoski - bass;
Owe Inborr (Ondfødt, Dispyt) - drums.

Former members:

Axel Vienonen (I, Captain) - bass;
Ilkka Kumpulainen - bass.
