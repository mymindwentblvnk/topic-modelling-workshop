Bastardizer are a Black Thrash band from Sydney, Australia. 

Formed at the beginning of 2013, the band began writing material and recorded 'Demo(n)s Unleashed' in February.

Upon writing more material and making their live debut in Sydney in May, the band recorded their first full-length 'Enforcers of Evil' in September which was released in January 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bastardizer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
