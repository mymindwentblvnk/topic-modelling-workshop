Formed in Bursa in 2003, Edna is a Turkish metal band stepping up to smash metal boundaries and meld the agressiveness of the metal with a geniunely prolific sound which is a compendium of metal sounds and genres in between. Now here it is! Edna has just released their demo - album entitled ‘Günışığına Masallar’ (Tales in the name of Daylight) consisting of nine English and Turkish songs, and ready for making their mark on the music scene with the new album.

Line up of the band is: 

Pınar Tekin- Vocals
Eren Mete - Rhythm Guitar
Oytun Özcan - Lead Guitar
Berkay Aycan - Drums
