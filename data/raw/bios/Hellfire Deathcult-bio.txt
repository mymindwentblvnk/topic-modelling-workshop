Hellfire Deathcult was formed in 2013 by R. Perez and E. Rodriguez. 

In 2014 Hellfire Deathcult self released their debut album "Ave Mors" on CD, followed by a pro tape release by Behold Barbarity in early 2015. This was also the year Hellfire Deathcult became a three piece with the addition of A. Alcantara on Guitar and R. Perez moving to Bass and Vocal duties. 

In late 2015 Hellfire Deathcult completed recording of their second release "Culto A La Muerte" released in early 2016.
