Septic Cemetery were a Melodic Death Metal band from Austria, who formed in 1995 and have long since disbanded. They released the 'Diabolos Rebellion'  EP in 1997, followed by their first (and final) album 'Shattered' in 2000. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Septic+Cemetery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
