Nebula Disrupt is a 5 member extreme death/black metal band from the Western-Cape. The band was formed over a period of 2 years, finding the right members to create the sound that is "Nebula" today. Influences range from the fast technical riff's and drumming of bands like Origin, Fleshgod Apocalypse, Belphegor, Hate eternal, Behemoth, Dimmu Borgir and also a dark touch of atmospheric black metal. Regular to gig around the Western-Cape of South Africa and has performed at shows like Metal4Africa SummerFest "14. Die-hard fans of the band are to be found at almost every show and some are not even from a metal background.
