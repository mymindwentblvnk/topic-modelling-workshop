Svetlana Serhiyivna Loboda (Ukrainian: Світлана Сергійiвна Лобода, born 18 October 1982 in Kyiv, Ukrainian SSR), known as Svetlana Loboda (Ukrainian: Світлана Лобода is a Ukrainian singer and composer. Loboda represented Ukraine in the Eurovision Song Contest 2009 and finished in 12th place with 76 points.
