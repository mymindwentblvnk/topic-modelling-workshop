Helix Nebula is a melodic death metal band with some progresive sound from Rivas Vaciamadrid, near Madrid, Spain. The band was founded in 2008 and since then, they have edited an EP, called In The Beginning of Time in 2013 and a demo called A New Era. In 2016 the band edited their first LP  The Last Lights of a Dying Universe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Helix+Nebula">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
