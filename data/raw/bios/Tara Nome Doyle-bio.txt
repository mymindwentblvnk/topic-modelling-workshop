Tara Nome Doyle is a Norwegian-Irish singer/songwriter born and bred in Berlin, Germany.
She grew up between doors, between city life and untouched nature. Her music embodies these inner conflicts, varying between slow, sweet melodies and ecstatic crescendos with lyrics that speak of reflections on crisis and belonging <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tara+Nome+Doyle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
