1. Russian metal-band Montezuma's Revenge or Месть Монтесумы. 
mntzm.bandcamp.com

2. Montezuma's Revenge was an a capella group from the Netherlands. It was founded in 1985 by a group of students and their teacher. They quickly became a professional band combining music and theatre. Most of their repertoire consists of covers. The membership changed significantly over the years. Resulting in a large variation of themes for the shows. In 2007 it was announced that they would stop and on 5th February 2008 they gave their last public performance. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Montezuma%27s+Revenge">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
