Features former guitar player of cult band Legion of Death, Mike McFarlin. The Lack of a heartbeat has yet to stop Asystole. After the "Higher Form Of Hatred" demo, Asystole spent 1992 searching for suitable members to complete their line up. As the year came and went, they decided to stop letting their line up situation hosten the release of their next demo. The result, intense death metal with a flavor of impiety and debauchery. In 2000, Asystole changed their name to LORD OF SHIT and released this obscure 3 song demo with the members Mike and Bob.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asystole">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
