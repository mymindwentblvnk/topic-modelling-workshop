HWLS is the music project of Australian born producer Justin Elwin. With two EPs out on Future Classic and Remix work for artists Joji, Tokimonsta, Seekae, Hermitude among others. HWLS has toured the world playing a range of venues with artists Flume, Ta-ku and more. The versatile producer has provided production credits for Denzel Curry, Ty Dolla $ign, Jaden Smith to name a few and production collaborations with artists like Flume, Esta, Losco, Shockone, Noah Breakfast and many more. With a wide range in production, dj experience and music knowledge this artist has seen through the many changes of musics evolving patterns while still staying true to his sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/HWLS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
