1. Huginn is an Icelandic DJ/producer.

2. In 1993, sole member "M" (AKA Marco DeRosa) created Huginn.

Over its existence, Huginn released 5 demos, a split album with The True Endless, and a full length CD titled "Back From The Old Hills, which was a compilation of 3 demos.  The band ceased to exist after this release. 

Marco DeRosa also plays in various other Italian metal bands, such as Opera IX, A Forest, Skoll, The Blessed Hellbrigade, and many more.
