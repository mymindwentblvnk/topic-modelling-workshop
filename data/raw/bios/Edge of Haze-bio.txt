Modern sound and open-minded approach.

The keywords above describe especially well what Edge Of Haze is all about. Formed in 2010, the Finnish five-piece has self-released two records to date.

Their 2014 entry, Illumine, has been praised for its unique blend of genres and has received a highly positive reception in the global metal community. The album was voted as the Album of the Year 2014 at the UK-based site Rock N Reel Reviews.

Illumine and its 2015 spin-off EP, Lights Decay, have been mastered at 4D Sounds by Acle Kahney, from the band TESSERACT.
