There are two artists with the name Lacerta:

1) Lacerta formed in Hobart, Tasmania in late 2005. With influences from Death to Steve Vai.
The main sound of Lacerta is old death metal/thrash, with black melodies and death/black vocals.

Their first album "Mind Processor" was released in 2010


2) Lacerta is single music maker, living in Finland. His influences are different kinds of ambient sounds and fantasy/sci-fi genres. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lacerta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
