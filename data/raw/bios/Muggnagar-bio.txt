Muggnagar was formed in Norway in 2001 by Count Darcon from Diabolical Breed. It is a one man project created as an musical outlet from the black metal of Diabolical Breed. Inspired by bands like Windir, Thyrfing, Children Of Bodom and Finntroll. Muggnagar is a melodic folk metal band, with catchy melodies and songs to shake your head to.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Muggnagar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
