Hellcannon is a thrash metal band that hails from Buffalo, New York.  They formed in December 2008.  They also have death metal influences in their sound.  They currently consist of Ryan Florita (lead vocals, rhythm guitar), Jim Nickles (lead guitars), Joe Leising (bass) and  Marcus London (drums).  Their lyrical themes include death, war and destruction.  They  are currently signed to Vile Records.
