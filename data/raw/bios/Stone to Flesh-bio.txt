Stone to Flesh was a black/death metal band formed in Belgrade, Serbia in 1996. They released 2 demos ("Serbian Woods" and "Killed by the sun") and one full-length album ("Some wounds bleed forever").
In 2001 they changed their name to The Stone.

Last known line-up was:
Kozeljnik - guitars
Nefas - Vocals
God of Perversion - bass
