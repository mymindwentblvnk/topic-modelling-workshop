Fran De Mork (From the Dark) is an Extreme Metal band from Arizona with Fast depressive melodies and Doom ambience Undertones. Lyrics come from the darkest parts of human emotions portraying the beauty of pain, sorrow and misery. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fran+De+Mork">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
