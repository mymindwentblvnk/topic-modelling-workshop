1. Hiraeth (USA) - Female Singer-Songwriter, Neofolk, Folk, Shoegaze, Dreampop

Hiraeth is an acoustic solo project that aims to express the feelings associated with the rough translation of the Welsh word, which means "a homesickness for a home to which you cannot return, a home which maybe never was; the nostalgia, the yearning, the grief for lost places of your past."  She describes her music as "ghost folk."
