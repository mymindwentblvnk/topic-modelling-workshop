Darkened Horizons was​​ formed in the early winter of 2011 by Vacillation guitarist Adrian Socaciu and vocalist Josh Ellis as a project dedicated to producing progressive death metal. As soon as the band was formed, preparations for the recording of the first EP "Darkened Horizons" were set up. The recording initially began in March and lasted several months until July of 2011 with all of the instruments and vocals produced by Adrian Socaciu and additional vocals and lyrics by Josh Ellis. The EP was released independently online that fall for free. In May 2012, the self titled EP was released on Torn Flesh records. The second release, a full length, "Surreal Devices" is to be released winter 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Darkened+Horizons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
