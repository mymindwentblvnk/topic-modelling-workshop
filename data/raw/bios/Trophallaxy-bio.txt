Trophallaxy was a symphonic power metal band formed in 2007.

Lineup:
Yannick Rey - Guitar
Valentin Magnenat - Bass
Joëlle "Jo" Graz - Vocals, Cello
Jonathan Pellet - Keyboards
Gaël Regamey - Drums
