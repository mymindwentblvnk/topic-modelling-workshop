Formed in Minneapolis, Minnesota, in 1990, The Game Hens were a death/thrash metal band who released two demos in the early 90's: a self-titled demo in 1991 and "Something Fowl" in 1993. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Game+Hens">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
