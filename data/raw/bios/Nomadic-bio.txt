Nomadic is Cuban artist Alain Hernandez, he released a track on the SUBsidence comp a while back called Anxiety -on touchin bass.
Nomadic produces electronic beats of rare beauty, twisted melodies, shimmering broken rhythms, throbbing bass lines and dark volcanic tones.Nomadic has also recorded a new artist album which will be coming out on Touchin Bass later on in the year.
Nomadic experiments in an unusual way of song writing. He manages to somehow tell a mysterious story layering Broken down Beats, Blanked Melancholic Melodies, Glitches, Stitches,white noise melodies and not to mention Bass!.Nomadic manages to be both experimental and enchanting at the same time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nomadic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
