Despite only releasing one album and a couple of demos, they still have a heavy underground following. Anvil Bitch are typical of East Coast metal in the mid-80's. They play primitive speed metal with vocals that are mostly high-pitched wails and falsetto screams. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anvil+Bitch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
