Maahlas started as a project in 2012 by the Turkish composer and guitarist Cuneyt Caglayan after he moved to Oslo from his home city Istanbul. 

The upcoming Maahlas album with the working title "Evolutions" is the work spread over a year in a no compromise process. The final recording sessions will be done in Istanbul in November and the album is excepted to see the dark of night around January 2014.

Band Members:

Cuneyt Caglayan, Guitars 
Levent Ultanur, Vocals
