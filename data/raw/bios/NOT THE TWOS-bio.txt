NOT THE TWOS is American producer Tim Maxey from Chicago and currently based in Atlanta.  

Maxey did production work on Baby Rose’s 2019 album ‘To Myself’ and subsequently on Kendrick Lamar’s album ‘Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers’.
