2) Metrix is a 3 piece Thrash Metal band from Lancaster PA.  The band formed in 1999 and has been the same line up since there formation.  Band Members are Jon Dochterman (Lead Guitar & Vocals) , Andy Dochterman (Drums & Backing Vocals) & Dan 'Dagon' Dochterman (Bass Guitar & Backing Vocals).
