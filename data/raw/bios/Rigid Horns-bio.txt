Rigid Horns is an instrumental metal band from Finland, compared to the likes of Primordial, Summoning, Nokturnal Mortum and Emperor. 

History:

- Project initiated by Korpi in 2005
- Guitarist/bassist N joined in 2006
- First demo "Nightrhymes", 2007
- Second demo "Triumph of Pan", 2008
