Stoner rock band with members of CRUEVO, CHRIST ON PARADE, GRINCH, A MINOR FOREST and ELVIS '77. Their album Better You Than Me was produced by Alex Newport of Fudge Tunnel & Melvins, and featured a guest appearance by Dave Edwardson of Neurosis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/High+Tone+Son+of+a+Bitch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
