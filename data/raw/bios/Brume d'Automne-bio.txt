Brume d'Automne is a pagan black metal band from Quebec, Canada, formed in 2003 by Nordet (vocals, guitars) and Athros (guitars, bass, drums, mandolin, also member of Forteresse).
Their name is French for "autumn mist" and their lyrical themes are about Quebec nationalism, traditions and history.
