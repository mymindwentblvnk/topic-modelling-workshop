Polish solo black metal project from Łódź, formed in Bełchatów in July 2013. Only musician and composer back then was Michał Bentyn, known as Nay. Lyrics deals with their own interpretation of nature and its original image. After two years of silence he gathered musicians to form a full band. In late 2015 next EP was anounced. "Fall of All the Divine" was released in 25.03.2016.
