Horgoth is Melodic Black Metal from France - formed in 1997. They've released one EP in January of 2008 called "It Finally Comes..." with five songs running in a total of twenty two minutes. It is self-released material and a long gap of time since we've last heard from them. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Horgoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
