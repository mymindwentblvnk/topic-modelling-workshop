Det Hedenske Folk was a norwegian black metal band, with Tyr as the main man of the band. Abbath(Immortal) was a sessiondrummer for the recordings of the few releases made by the band. Kvitrafn (Gorgoroth) joined the band on drums but they split-up before anything was released.

The lyrics are focused on Heathenism and nordic pagan culture. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Det+Hedenske+Folk">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
