Success Will Write Apocalypse Across The Sky (SWWAATS) was formed in 2006. They reside in Tampa, Florida.

Current line-up
John Collett - Vocals (Dehumanized)
Matt Simpson - Bass
JR Daniels- Drums
Ian Sturgill - Guitar, Vocals (Bodies in the Gears of the Apparatus, Filth Porn)
Jen Muse - Samples (Filth Porn)
Shaun De Leon - Guitar

SWWAATS features members of Bodies in the Gears of the Apparatus and Dehumanized.
