Cauvery is a brazilian metal band formed in 2013 in Belo Horizonte. Earlier they were known as Eternal Crossing. Their first album, entitled "We Are the Plague" can be downloaded at their official website www.cauvery.com.br/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cauvery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
