Habit of Force is a three piece Metal band from Chicago. Having many influences, HoF delivers a wide spectrum of styles in an attempt to keep their fans guessing and satisfied. Inspired by life, Habit of Force draws a positive and motivational line with their music. Always striving to achieve the next level, HoF gives you their all in the studio or on the stage. Always looking to have a good time and tear it up, Habit of Force is coming for you!
