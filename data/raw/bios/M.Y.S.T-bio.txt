M.Y.S.T is a heavy metal band from Istanbul / TURKEY. The band was formed in 1994 under the name Antidote and got the name M.Y.S.T in 1999. M.Y.S.T became a major band in the Turkish underground heavy metal scene in the 2000's. The music is a combination of American style power metal with an melodic and progressive approach. The band is well known for their live performance. So far they released an E.P. called Sand and Time. The song Fortress of Time entered the radio billboards on #1 and stood there for 8 weeks. The band is still active working on their new songs. For further information visit: www.mystmetal.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/M.Y.S.T">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
