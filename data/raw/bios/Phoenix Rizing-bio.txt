Phoenix Rizing was the name of a Norwegian power metal band that started in 1996 and split up in 2001. The band recorded a demo in May 1997 in Panser Studio, and released two albums. In 2001 the band split up, but reformed with a new vocalist under the name Highland Glory. Phoenix Rizing is regarded by many as one of Norways first power metal bands.

On Saturday 14. January 2006, the band held a reunion show at John Dee in Oslo, together with Highland Glory.

Discography:
* Demo (1997)
* Rise From The Ashes (1998)
* Eternal Crusade (2001)
