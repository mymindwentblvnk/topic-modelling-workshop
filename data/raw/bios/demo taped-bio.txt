Demo Taped, the moniker of Atlanta native Adam Alexander, is an Electronic Soul solo project dedicated to spreading love and good vibes. Adam's upbeat rhythms, soft vocals, and unique synth melodies combined with dreamy, electronic R&B textures spread a general feeling of happiness and love and soul to reveal the universal groove within us all, allowing people to access its power. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/demo+taped">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
