Infamous Sinphony were a thrash metal/crossover band from San Diego active from around 1989 to 1994. They released two albums and appeared on a Wild Rags Records sampler LP with Bloodcum, Natas and Outcasts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infamous+Sinphony">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
