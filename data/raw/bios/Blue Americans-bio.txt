Blue Americans was formed by Daniel Morgan Ball and Kris Platt, former members of the band More than Conquerors. In September 2017, their track Bull on Venice Beach was featured as the Sentric Music Blog's "Track of the Week". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blue+Americans">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
