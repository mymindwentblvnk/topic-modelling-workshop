As the Sea Parts is a death doom metal band from Woodbridge, Virginia, United States, formed in 1995. They released one demo titled "Trilogy of Sorrow" in 1996. The band features members of While Heaven Wept, Witch-Hunt and Rain Fell Within. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/As+The+Sea+Parts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
