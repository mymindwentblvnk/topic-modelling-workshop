Despirited were a black/death metal band from Calgary, Alberta, Canada. They released two demos in 2000 and 2001 respectively, and were featured in different compilations.

They recorded an album titled "Forest of Suicides", but it is unknown whether it was released or not. The band disbanded at some point. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Despirited">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
