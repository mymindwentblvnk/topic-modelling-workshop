Stockholm, Sweden (2012 - present)

Adam Kanyama, born in 1995, is a rapper and songwriter who grew up in Norsborg, Stockholm, Sweden and has been rapping since he was 11 years old. His mother's swedish and his father's tanzanian. Adam is also included in a group called "The Basicz" with Jordan Peltier and Chris Kipa. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Adam+Kanyama">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
