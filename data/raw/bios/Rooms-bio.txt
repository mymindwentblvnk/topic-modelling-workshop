there is more than one band by the name of rooms

1)Alternative/indie/punk/grunge -rock group founded in 2003. Originally from Jyväskylä, Finland; nowadays Tampere/Vantaa, Finland.

2) Band from Los Angeles, CA consisting of Alex Wisner, Sean Friday and Christopher Null. They are no longer a band but sean friday and chris null continue making music with friend sonny moore. 

3) Band from Indianapolis, IN active from 2004-present consisting of Matt Askren, Kevin Toole, Ben Traub, and Andrew Greenburg, all veterans of the Indianapolis indie music scene, having played in such bands as Thin Fevers, Letters In English, Saraswati, and many more.

Rooms formed in early 2004, took a hiatus while Askren spent some time on the west coast, and reformed in 2007. They released their self-titled full-length Rooms in August of 2009 and their second album Drawing the City Walls in January of 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rooms">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
