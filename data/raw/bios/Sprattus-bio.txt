Sprattus is a Heavy Metal band from Buenos Aires, Argentina. Founded in 1984 and still active in 2009. They embarked on a short European tour at the end of 2009 playing some selected concerts.

Current line-up:
Claudio Partenzi - Guitar & Vocals
Luis Seoane - Drums
Gustavo Di Yorio - Bass
Victor Martin - Guitar

Discography:
Soy Lucifer
Su Majestad
