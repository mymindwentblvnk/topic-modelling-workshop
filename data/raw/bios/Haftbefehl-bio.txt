Aykut Anhan (born 16 December 1985) known as Haftbefehl (meaning "arrest warrant" in German) is a German rapper[1] of Kurdish[2] descent born in Offenbach. He was signed to his own record label Azzlack, before he was signed to the label Echte Musik. In June 2017 he began making chicken business in Offenbach. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Haftbefehl">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
