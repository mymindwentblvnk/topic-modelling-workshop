Taking nods from their numerous experiences in the melodic death metal and metalcore scenes, Shadow of Corvus incorporates crushing, galloping riffs with razor sharp melodic lines, underscored by a powerhouse of percussive force. Paired with vocals that are kaleidoscopic and range and technique, the band cunningly weaves between genres like a phantom on Separation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shadow+of+Corvus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
