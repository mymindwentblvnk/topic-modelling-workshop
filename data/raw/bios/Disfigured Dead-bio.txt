Disfigured Dead was a death metal band from Moravia, New York.  They formed in 2008 and disbanded in 2014.  They only released two full-length albums.  They last label they were signed on to was Butchered Records.  They wrote songs about horror, death and torture.  Their last known lineup consisted of Dave Gruver (vocals, guitars), Ryan Kratzer (guitars) and Kyle Kratzer (drums, vocals).  The last known lineup went on to form NilExistence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disfigured+Dead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
