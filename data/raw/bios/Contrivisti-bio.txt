Moderately blasphemous black metal act formed by John Gelso and the late Aragon Amori. Cut from the same mold and zeitgeist as Profanatica, Havohej, Incantation, and Revenant, they built leaning towers of discordant melodies far removed from the conventional and safe world of modern music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Contrivisti">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
