Candy Cane were an avant-garde metal band from Tampere/Helsinki, Finland.  They formed in the year 1995 and disbanded in 2013.   Early on, they were a post-punk band and then became an avant-garde metal band.  They released four demos, six EP's, three full-length albums, three splits and one compilation.  Their last known lineup consisted of  Marko Neuman (vocals, guitars, percussion), MS (guitars, backing vocals), JL (keyboards, backing vocals) and TI (bass).  Witching Hour Productions was the last label they were signed to.  "Anal Us" is an unreleased song that they have and it is featured on  the Rabbit Ilsn Records Collection Vol.1 CD-r (Rabbit Ilsn Records 2010) .
