OGMA is a prog metal band formend in the end of 2008 on Brazil.
The band counts with Joel Pagliarini (drums), Bruno Mello (guitar), Roger Werner (bass) and Marcos Follador (vocals).
Ogma makes a heavy and agressive sound, with many influences of brazilian music, passing throught
dramatic and nostalgic moods, to heavy and headbanging parts.
In the end of 2010 the first album’s band, A ROYAL CRIME
