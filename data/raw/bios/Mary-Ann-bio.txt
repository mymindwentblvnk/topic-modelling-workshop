Mary - Ann is a punk/rock/gothic band from Finnland. Formed in 1993, this band realises one album named "Mary-Ann" and EP "Depper Sin".
After these actions, band changed their music style to Gothic Metal and name to To/Die/For releasing the album called "All Eternity" in 1999. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mary-Ann">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
