There are several artists called Jara. In chronological order:

1. American emo-hardcore band from Goleta, California that released two 7"s and an LP between 1994 - 1995.

2. Jara is an Icelandic hardcore band, which featured ex-members of Downcast and Manumission.

3. Jara is a Dutch indie pop artist, who released her first EP 'Sky Collector' in 2013.
