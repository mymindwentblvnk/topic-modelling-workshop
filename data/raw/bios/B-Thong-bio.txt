B-THONG came in 1990, from Gothenburg,Sweden, when the band was originally tiled CONCRETE STUFF. B-THONG recorded and released the 'Skinned' debut album in 1994. After second album 'Damage' frontman Tony Jelencovich quit B-THONG in November 1995 to form TRANSPORT LEAGUE (releasing the 'Stallion Showcase' album in early 1996). They are influenced by Warlock, Motorpsycho, Kyuss. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/B-Thong">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
