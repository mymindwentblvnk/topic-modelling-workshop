Christian Epidemic is a black metal band from Hungary since 1997. Formerly known as Euthanasia. Existed as Epidemic for a couple of years (2006-2009), but then continued as Christian Epidemic.


Line up:

László Nagy - Bass & Vocals
Zoltán Kocsis - Guitars
Barnabás Hummel - Keyboards
Imre Kocsis - Drums 
Former members:
Gábor Borhidi - Guitars
József Pósfai - Vocals
Antal Nagy - Keyboards
Tamás Vénusz - Guitars
Zsolt Széles - Bass & Vocals (earlier also guitars)
