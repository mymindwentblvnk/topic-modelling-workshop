There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Jailbreak is Heather Leigh Murray  + Chris Corsano

2) Jailbreak was a hardcore band from Detroit, Michigan.  They released a demo and a 7" which was available on Brokenglass Records.  The demo was reissued on 7" on Brokenglass Records.  Members went on to play for RZL DZL(Razzle Dazzle).

3) Jailbreak is a Swiss rock band.
