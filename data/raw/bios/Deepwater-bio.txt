Deepwater was a Straight Edge emo band from the US. They released a Self-Titled 12" in 1993 on Break Even Point. Members of the band were also members of Encounter, Point Of View, Alloy and Policy Of 3. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deepwater">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
