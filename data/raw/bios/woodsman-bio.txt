Woodsman is Brooklyn/Colorado based trio that plays heavily manipulated psychedelic rock. 

They've released several EPs and LPs thus far, slowly gaining recognition in the obscure indie scene for their experimental nature which incorporate krautrock grooves, lengthy epics, and pop sensibilities.
