Camerom Avery (born 21 April 1988) is an Australian multi-instrumentalist, musician, singer and songwriter. He is best known as a member of the bands Pond and Tame Impala, and for fronting his own band, The Growl. Cameron released debut solo album Ripe Dreams, Pipe Dreams on March 10, 2017 via ANTI- Records, channeling right from the heart of his interests, which range from his love for Tom Waits, Nick Cave, Russian literature and 60s French cinema. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cameron+Avery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
