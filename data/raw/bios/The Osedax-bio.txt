The Osedax are a Stoner/Doom/Sludge/Hardcore Metal Band from McLean, Virginia, USA. The band came together in 2008. They released their debut album "Delayed Response" in 2010.

Line-up: 

Guitarist - Scott Coldwell
Bassist - Mike Horn
Vocalist - Josh Albright
