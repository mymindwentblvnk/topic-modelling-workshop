There are multiple artists who perform under the name Blasphemia:

1. A black metal/noise band from Bischmisheim, Germany. They've released a multitude of demo and split albums, all within the years 1994-1995.

2. A black/thrash metal band from San Felipe, Chile. They have released two demos and one EP within the years 2001-2005.
