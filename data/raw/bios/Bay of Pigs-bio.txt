Art punk from the post-punk paradise that was San Francisco circa 1980. 
Bay of Pigs is probably one of the more “No-Wave” sounding releases from the whole Bay Area scene.
 
Bass – Joseph T. Jacobs
Drums – Stephano Paolillo
Engineer – Richard Kelly
Producer – Michael Fox
Steel Guitar – Ron Morgan
Vocals – Andrew Hayes
