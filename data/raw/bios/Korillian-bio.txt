Korillian is a progressive/groove/death metal band from Pensacola, FL. The members are.

John Lay - Drums
Sam Bare - Guitar
Derek Rowell - Bass
Scott Johnson - Vocals

They record with Ultra Mega Studios, based out of Pensacola. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Korillian">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
