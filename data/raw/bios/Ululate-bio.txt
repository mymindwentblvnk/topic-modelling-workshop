History

Ululate was formed from the ashes of another Black Metal band, which Spectre was a part of. His first CD, "We Are Going To Eat You", was released in 2003 through Dying Art Productions. Lyrical themes include horror and zombies.

The second CD, "YaZi", was named for the town the songs on the album arebased off of. The album also includes live songs from a gig in Wuhan 2003 was released through Dying Art Productions in 2004.
