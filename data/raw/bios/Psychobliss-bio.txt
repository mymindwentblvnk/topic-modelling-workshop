PsychoBliss is an innovative metal duo from Phoenix, AZ. Their sound incorporates themes of the black, death, and gothic metal genres. Most fans and critics simply summarize the sound as gothic black metal or "dark metal".

Dan Stollings is the main song writer for PsychoBliss, providing all instrumentation. Kyla Pavelski is the front-woman, equipped with vocal diversity that's widely unheard of in the metal world.

Although PsychoBliss is influenced by a large array of heavy metal, the band has managed to forge their own distinct sound, which ranges from atmospheric to heavy and melodic. PsychoBliss aims to provide music that's something...a little different.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychobliss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
