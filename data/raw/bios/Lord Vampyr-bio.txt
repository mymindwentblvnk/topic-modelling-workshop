Lord Vampyr is an Italian, vampiric themed, black metal artist who for much of his career was the originator and lead singer for the band Theatres des Vampires. He left that group in 2004, and in 2005 produced his first solo album under his own name Lord Vampyr entitled De Vampyrica Philosophia.

Since this release of this first solo album Lord Vampyr has been involved in other projects, including fronting the band Shadowsreign. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lord+Vampyr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
