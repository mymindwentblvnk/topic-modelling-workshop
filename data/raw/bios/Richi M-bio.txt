Richard Moldovan (born 22nd March 1974 in Hungary) is a swedish producer and DJ mostly known as Richi M. He made a name for himself in 1997 and had after that a couple of successful years with more hits. Richi has even remixed songs by among E-Type, Ayleen, Bodies Without Organs and Van <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Richi+M">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
