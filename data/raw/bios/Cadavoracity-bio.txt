CADAVORACITY is a Brutal Death Metal band from Bekasi - INDONESIA, formed on early 2012 by the will Deddy (ex-ASPHYXIATE), Ryo (ex-ABONATION), Nano (VOMIT LARYNX). CADAVORACITY itself is a mixed words for Cadaver Voracity, We use a drum programming for this project due we don't found the right drummer to fill it. We started this by the promo stuff 2012 and now we write some new materials for our debut album with more brutal tunes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cadavoracity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
