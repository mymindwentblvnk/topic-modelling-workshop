Sean MacDonald: Bass, Lee Williams: Vocals, Matt Olivo: Guitar, Bill Bradley; Drums. 

Another absolute classic grindcore band out of Flint, Michigan. They were active in the mid 90s and recorded a demo and a live radio show in 1993-94. Matt is in Repulsion. Sean was in many bands in Flint (Repulsion and Tempter with Matt and Scott Carlson). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dejecta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
