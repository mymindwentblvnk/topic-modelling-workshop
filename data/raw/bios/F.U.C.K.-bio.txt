F.U.C.K. is a dutch musical duo. This name is one of the many aliases of the band 2 from keen. Their most popular songs are 'F.U.C.K. Theme', 'Miskoop' and 'Koken, Roosteren, Braden op een Spitje'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/F.U.C.K.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
