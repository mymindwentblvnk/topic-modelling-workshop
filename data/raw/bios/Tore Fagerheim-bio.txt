Tore Fagerheim, born in Norway, 1991, is the man behind the Youtube channel "Metal Guitar Stuff", which started in 2011.
He is also the guitarist and composer of the band Shadowmind. Tore has been playing guitar since 2007 and has since then released several albums with Metal Guitar Stuff and Shadowmind. His inspirations are from various metalcore bands, symphonic metal and epic film scores. This combines into a mix of catchy, heavy and epic sounding tracks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tore+Fagerheim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
