California pop, Phil Spector style, with a twist of The Smiths and a whole lotta Beatles. Bikeride conquers Prog, country, and just about every other musical genre on The Kiss. They are today's indie heroes.

In March 2008, singer/guitarist Tony Carbone passed away from cancer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bikeride">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
