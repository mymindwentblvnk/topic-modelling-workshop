Winter's Wake is a five piece Viking Metal band from Staten Island, New York. They have released one demo so far entitled "Yellow Moon". They are inspired by all genres of music, not just metal. They blend harsh extreme metal passages with beautifully crafted folk and orchestral pieces.

Current members:
James Battle - Vocals, Bass (Live for Today, Denim Burke, End of Eternity,  The James and Chris Experience)
Chris Warner - Keyboards, Guitar (Denim Burke, Live for Today,  Krypt1k, End of Eternity,  The James and Chris Experience)
Chris Bartoloni- Drums (2015 - Present)
Sean Battle - Guitar (2016 - Present)

Ex Member's
Mike Cregg - Guitar 2010 - 2016
Brian Cregg - Drums 2010 - 2014
