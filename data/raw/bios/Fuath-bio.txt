Fuath is a one-man atmospheric black metal project from Scotland formed in 2015 by vocalist and multi-instrumentalist Andy Marshall (Saor).

Lyrically the project deals with winter, melancholy, and mysticism. "Fuath" means "hatred" in Gaelic.
