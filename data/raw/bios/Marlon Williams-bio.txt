Marlon Williams (born 31 December 1990) is an award winning singer-songwriter and guitarist from Lyttelton, New Zealand. He has performed solo as well as with current backing group the Yarra Benders. He first came to attention as front-man of The Unfaithful Ways and has also performed and recorded as a duo with Delaney Davidson. William's music style straddles folk, country, soul, bluegrass and the blues. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marlon+Williams">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
