Nordverg is a folk/viking metal band from Yekaterinburg, Russia. They were formerly known as Dragon's tears but changed their name to the one that reflects their style and ideas better. "Nordverg means "northern way" and that's the way we will take!" states the band. Their debut album "Багровый рассвет" (Crimson Dawn) was released in December 2011.
