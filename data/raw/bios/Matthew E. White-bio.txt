Matthew E. White (born August 14, 1982 in Virginia Beach, Virginia) is an American songwriter, producer, arranger, and founder of Spacebomb Records. White was named eMusic's Breakthrough Artist of 2012, Paste Magazine's Best New Act of 2012, and a Consequence of Sound Rookie of the Year.

As well as his solo work, White is known as the leader of the avant-garde jazz big band Fight the Big Bull (for whom he composes and arranges all music) and a former member of rock band The Great White Jenkins.
