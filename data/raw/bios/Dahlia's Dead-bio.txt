Dahlia's Dead was a doom metal band from Shirley, Massachusetts, active from 1995 to 1996, in which time they managed to release a 4-song demo tape.
Members were: 
Krista Van Guilder - vocals/guitar, 
Hilary Meyer - guitar, 
Michelle McCormick - drums, 
Mark McCormick - bass
