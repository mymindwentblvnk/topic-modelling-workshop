Brutal hardcore with classic grindcore and black metal influences.  This one off project features Dave Witte of Discordance Axis, Municipal Waste, and his former Black Army Jacket bandmates Carlos Ramirez and Andrew Orlando. 'Year of the Leper' is their only official release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hope+Collapse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
