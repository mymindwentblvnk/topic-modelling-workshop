Raptile is a German rapper from Munich. He gained mainstream success through his singles Make Y'All Bounce feat. Xzibit and Strong Arm Steady, as well as NevaEva which was featured in the game NBA Live 2007.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raptile">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
