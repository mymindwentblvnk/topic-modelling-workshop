Calderah are a very newly formed metal band from Brighton, England who currently consist only of guitarists Henry and Will, and drummer Eddy. They are currently writing and recording very rough demo tracks and looking for a bassist and vocalist to complete the bands line up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Calderah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
