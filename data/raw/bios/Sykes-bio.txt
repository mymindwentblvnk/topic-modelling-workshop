There is more than one artist with this name, and the tracks/releases listed are mixed up.

1. Former name of synth pop three piece, PLYA from London, UK

2. Stage name of Dutch DJ and vocalist, Mike Everling from Rotterdam.

3. John Sykes  of Blue Murder released several solo albums under the name of Sykes.

1. SYKES is the former name for  PLYA, a three-piece synth pop band from South London. The band consists of singer/keyboardist Julia Sykes, guitarist Kris Taylor and drummer William Brown. As SYKES they released three EP's: Gold Dust in October 2014, Younger Mind in July 2016 and Glimmer (Remixes) in March 2017. Since 20th October 2017 all new releases have been under the name PLYA, which include various singles and an EP, Still In Love, released in June 2019.

https://soundcloud.com/sykesband / https://soundcloud.com/plyaband
Twitter: @sykesband / @plyaband

2. Sykes is a DJ, producer and vocalist based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Growing up listening to artists such as Donny Hathaway and India Arie and being inspired by electronic artists like James Blake, Bonobo, RJD2 during his teens, Mike Everling began producing his own electronic music under the name Sykes. He has been performing live sets with Beau Zwart in the Netherlands and together they released their Hold Tight EP in July 2020. In February 2021 Sykes released his debut solo extended play, We and Us EP.

https://soundcloud.com/mike_everling 
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/Sykes0001/ 

********

3. John Sykes is an English musician, songwriter and producer. He is best known as a member of Whitesnake, Thin Lizzy and Tygers of Pan Tang. He has also fronted the hard rock group Blue Murder and released several solo albums. Under the pseudonym Sykes he has released three albums; Out of My Tree, 20th Century and Nuclear Cowboy as well as Loveland under his full name John Sykes.

Official website: https://www.johnsykes.com//
