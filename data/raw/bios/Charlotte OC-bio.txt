Charlotte OC (full name, Charlotte Mary O'Connor) is a singer songwriter from Blackburn, UK. Her debut single, "Treasure Island", was released in September 2011 with her debut album, "For Kenny" following in November 2011. With 3 subsequent EPs under her belt - "Colour My Heart" (Nov 2013), "Strange" (Sept 2014) and "Burning" (March 2015), Charlotte OC is due to release her second album late in 2016.
