Guitarist Ron Sachs, vocalist Kevin Jones, drummer Dave White, and Jon Wilson on bass make up the hard and heavy progressive metal band Rattleface. The band formed in 2001 in Orange County, California and has three albums to date. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rattleface">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
