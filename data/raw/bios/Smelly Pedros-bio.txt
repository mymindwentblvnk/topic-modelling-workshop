Smelly Pedros is a Canadian thrash metal band from Sherbrooke, Québec.  Formed in 2008 the band can also be categorized as "pirate metal" since their lyrical themes mostly revolves around pirates.  The band writes songs both in english and french and releases a full length album on December 5th 2009 entitled "La Mer À Boire" which includes 7 tracks.

Current line-up : 

Adam Rousseau (Beard Light Lime) - Vocals
Olivier Laroche (Redbeard) - Guitar, Backing Vocals
Vincent Compagna (Beard Dry) - Bass, Backing Vocals
Dominic Allard (Greybeard) - Drums, Backing Vocals
