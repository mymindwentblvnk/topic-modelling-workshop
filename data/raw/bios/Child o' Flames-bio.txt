Child O' Flames is a band from Curitiba, Brazil.
The band was born July 2004, playing with Sad Theory.
The band may be describide as modern thrash metal, and has influence of heavy metal, gothenburg sound and hardcore, still, not sounding as metalcore.
The band played with some well known bands like Sepultura, Korzus, Grave Digger and many others brazilian metal bands.

Line-up: 
William Nicolaio: Guitar
Douglas Souza: Vocals
Tiago Miranda: Drums
Leandro Soares: Bass
