Dover Trench is a thrash metal band from USA (Sierra Vista, Arizona), formed in 1985.  They released a few demos (from 1987 to 1989) and one full length album, Exhibition of Speed, in 1991.

The current line-up of Dover Trench is:
Eli Freeman - Vocals
David Chaney - Bass
Matt Pimple - Drums
Tom Bianchino - Guitar
