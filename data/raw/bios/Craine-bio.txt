Craine are an indie band from Sheffield, England.

Their debut single 'End Of The Line' was released on download on 23 June 2008, and on physical format on 30 June 2008.

Their Single 'Divine Intervention' was released for download on March 30th 2009 on i-tunes and all major download sites. 

The Debut album 'Sleeping Vixen' containing 11 tracks followed April 13th 2009. Available from HMV in the physical format. Downloads of the album also available. 

The band consists of:
Steve Craine - Vocals
Chris Roberts - Guitar
Dan Roberts - Bass
Tom Stebbing - Drums
