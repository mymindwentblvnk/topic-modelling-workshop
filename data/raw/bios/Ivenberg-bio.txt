Detached from stale schemata and attitudes, inspired by modern viking, technical black to straight death metal, onto atmospheric or riffloaded pieces with influences by Dissection, Immortal, Bathory, Wasp...


After Morsan had collected many different experiences in other bands, he decided to realise his piled up ideas under the banner Ivenbergs.
In his lyrics he digests the daily fight for survival. Just like before, the humans wrestle for life, but sword and shield aren't the weapons of modern warriors. Especially tongue and mind serve the preservation of one's own way of life and ghost.
