Dronckaerdt was a black metal project of ex-Astharoth-member Ecthelion on all instruments and vocals. Inferis (of (Lycanthropy's Spell, Sarmak, Funeral Forest and more bands) plays session drums on the track 'Cun Lerren' which is a tribute to Sarmak who died 11/09/05. 
The project was founded in 2003 in Westmalle, Belgium. Ecthelion has released one demo until this moment; 'Hoorn van ontwaken' in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dronckaerdt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
