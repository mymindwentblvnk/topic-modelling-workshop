Metal/punk band from Osaka, Japan. Formed in 2008, Sex Messiah takes their music approach like Zouo and G.I.S.M. did, and also feature influences from the thrash and black metal side. They released a self-titled demo tape in 2008. Their first vinyl EP, entitled "Wish You Were Dead", was released on Criminal Trap Records in March 2009. In early 2011, the band released another EP, this time on High Society Satanic Records, entitled "Abysmal Ritual".
