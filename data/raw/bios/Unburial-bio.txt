Unburial is a Melodic Death/Black Metal band from Mallorca, Spain. 

The band started playing together early 2006.

The lyrics are highly influenced by classic ancient era (Greece/Rome).

Their debut album, Bellum Internecinum, was released by Godeater Records in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unburial">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
