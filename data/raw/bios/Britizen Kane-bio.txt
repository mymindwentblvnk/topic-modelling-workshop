UK lyricist Britizen Kane blends witty wordplay, rapid fire flows and a delivery of pinpoint accuracy to create fresh sounding hip-hop music over the most up-to-date production. Raised in Manchester, and taking influence from the likes of Sway DaSafo, Joe Budden and Tech N9ne amongst others, Kane started writing and self-releasing his raps in his late teens. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Britizen+Kane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
