Bladesmith is a Black metal band hailing from Vantaa, Finland. Their lyrical themes are about War, Glory, Pride, Celtic Beliefs and Odinism. There's only one member of this band, Erik Wray. In January 2007 the demo "Darkness Remains Unsent" was released followed by the split with Ohvrikivi titled "The Tenebrous Journey" in May 2007.
