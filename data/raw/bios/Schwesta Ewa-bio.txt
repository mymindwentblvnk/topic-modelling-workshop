Schwesta Ewa (* July 16 1984 in Koszalin ; civil Ewa Müller) is a German rapper of Polish descent from Frankfurt am Main.

Ewa Müller was born in Koszalin 1984. She lives in Germany since 1987 and grew up in Kiel, where she at age 19 in 2003 on a job as a waitress at a red light bar with the milieu came and contacted in 2004 to Frankfurt am Main moved to work as a prostitute. 

2012 she rapped on the songs "Beifall" of Xatar's "Album 415" and "Frauen" on the Album "Hinterhofjargon" from Celo & Abdi.
