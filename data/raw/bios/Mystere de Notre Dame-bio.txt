Mystere de Notre Dame is an Italian Progressive Metal formed in 1993 by Ruggero Cesana, Joe Lacquaniti, Roy and Luciano Marinetti.

In 1995 Luciano Marinetti Joined the band changing the sound of the band.

Their first record was released in 1996.
