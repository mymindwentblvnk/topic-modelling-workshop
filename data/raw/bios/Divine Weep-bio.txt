Started in the 90's as a black-metal band in Bialystok, Poland. Played around 30 gigs at the time.
Now re-activated in the heavy-metal genre with two members from the original lineup and three newcomers. First official release is the Age of the Immortal album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Divine+Weep">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
