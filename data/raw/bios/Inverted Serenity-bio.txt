Formed in 2009, this band started jamming together in high-school. Over the next couple years, several shows as well as lineup changes would occur but, the determination would not cease. Musically, their style contains a variety of different forms of death, thrash, progressive, technical, and black metal grooves. Lyrical themes for Inverted Serenity's music include; philosophy, consciousness, mathematics, enlightenment, science, fractals, metaphysics, and nature. Over a dozen songs have been written and a full-length album is in the works. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inverted+Serenity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
