Nol is a name transliteration of russian band Ноль.

The band "Ноль" ("Nol'", Zero) is the Russian band of Fedor Chistyakov. Using the mix of soviet punk and some of folk methods in their music makes them something special. F. Chistyakov some times ago have been discharged from the psychiatric clinique and said that he is never gonna take drugs, but even knowing that the music he played was made in the drug-drunk condition it is really interesting and distinctive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nol">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
