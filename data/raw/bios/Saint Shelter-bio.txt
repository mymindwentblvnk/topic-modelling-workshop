Saint Shelter was founded in March of 2007. Since then the band's constantly moving forward with great success. Musically they move between Death-, Thrash- and Core-Elements and are known for ther energetic live performances.

In April 2009 they released their first EP 'Apocalypto' which was promoted throughout the summer with many shows.

Up to now they shared the stage with bands such as Neaera, We Butter The Bread With Butter, Fear My Thoughts, Cataract, Misery Speaks, Deadlock, and others.

Their Demo-CD 'Caged Inside' with their new singer Norman Ley will be released in November.
