Clean Heart makes a heavy metal with some power/melodic influences. Samuel, the soul of the band, is, at the same time, the guitarrist and vocalist and their concerts are usually full of energy. This brazilian band has only one release named “Fight In The Name of God” with 4 tracks and the same release in a Special Edition, with all the songs remastered and a live extra track (Fight In The Name of God SE). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Clean+Heart">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
