KorovaKill is an avantgarde black metal band from Innsbruck, Austria since 2000. It was originally formed in 1990 under the name Korova.

Line up:
Moritz Neuner - Drums (Abigor, Necrology, Siegfried, Dornenreich, Angizia, Enid, Darkwell, Evenfall, Korova, Graveworm, Atrocity, Sternenstaub, Leaves' Eyes, Shadowcast, Dark Embrace, Angry Angels, Golden Dawn, Samsas Traum)
Christof Niederwieser - Vocals, Guitar, Bass, Keyboards (Korova, Shining of Kliffoth, Angizia)
Renaud Tschirner - Keyboard (Elend)

Christof Niederwieser is chief editor of the webzine www.avantgarde-metal.com.
