Sixpounder Teratoma is a goregrind band from Russian Federation. The band was formed in 2007 under “Sixpounder” name as a thrash/death metal project. With time style of music is changing the porn gore grind with brutal death elements. In early 2011, the band changed its name to “Sixpounder Teratoma”. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sixpounder+Teratoma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
