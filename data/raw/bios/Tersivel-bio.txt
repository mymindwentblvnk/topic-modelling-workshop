Tersivel is an Argentinian band, influenced by a great list of REAL music, from Celtic music, Sicilian Folk music, Ancient Military music and of course from many great extreme metal bands of all times, bring us this Folk Extreme Metal charged with epic and glorious passages, extreme and heavy atmospheres and a clear and strong message in their songs:
Glory, Honour, Bravery and Pints of Beers!
In Lian's words: "Our Music is not Pop or Rock music, you can find it easily in any bathroom out there, this music is composed around the story. We try to take you with us, to face the enemy and to help you to open your eyes and mind" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tersivel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
