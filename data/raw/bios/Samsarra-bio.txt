Spawned on the sun-drenched shores of Santa Barbara, California, in 2003 by lead vocalist and occasional guitarist "Reverend Scott" Braden Schwartz, Samsarra continue to evolve through line-up changes,both expanding and focusing a relatively unique approach to modern metal. 

Samsarra released their third album, "Coming Forth By Day" in january of 2012. It is available on itunes and amazon.
