From what War Injun has released thus far it seems that they can best be described as a healthy cross between High On Fire and Red Giant, equal parts heavy southern rock riffs and stoned out doom/blues. Definitely worth checking out for those fans of the stoner rock persuasion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/War+Injun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
