Aki Kato is a musician, who plays Avant-Garde metal. Began with doom, death, black metal in 2007 and collaborated with many bands.
Later took aim at a wider audience and in 2014 released his first full-scale album, which received good reviews. The music is full of emotions and can make you reflect or take to faraway unknown worlds.
