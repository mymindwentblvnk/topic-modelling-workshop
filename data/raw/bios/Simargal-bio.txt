Simargal originates from Trstenik, Serbia. Band started off at the end of 1995 by the name Abadon, but just after a few rehearsals, the name was changed into Simargal.

Simargal, is a Slavic god defending the light from the dark, good from evil, day from night, etc. Simargal's symbol is the same rune used nowadays as the symbol of White Power movement. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Simargal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
