a black metal project formed in Venezuela in 2010 and later shifted to Chile. Releases so far - Veritas Filia Temporis demo in 2011, split with Aversio Humanitatis and Nihil in 2013, another split with Holbach in 2014, An Ominous Landscape EP in 2015, s/t debut album in 2017, Secular Compendium compilation in 2018, a single The Guardian of the Final Plague (Hirilorn cover) in 2018 and second album Relatos de angustia in 2020 with Debemur Morti productions.
