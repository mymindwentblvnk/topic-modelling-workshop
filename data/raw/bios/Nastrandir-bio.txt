Pagan/Viking Metal Band from Luebeck, Germany. Mixture of folk and epic metal music with choirs and violin melodies.

Nastrandir was founded in April 2005. 
The current line-up is: 
Vocals/Bass/Acoustic guitars: Grimnir
Guitars: Fjoellnir
Violin, Drums, Lyrics: Ragnar
Keyboards: Vidir
