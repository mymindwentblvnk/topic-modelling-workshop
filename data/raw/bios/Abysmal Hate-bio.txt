New Black Metal project from the headmaster behind Lost Apathy (Drone Doom), Gut (AGH on Last.fm), and one of his friend, Assatur(also known as Wild-Snake on Last.fm).
Abysmal Hate consist in a Black Metal Band, influenced by legends like Burzum, Darkthrone, and some recents groups like Xasthur, Nortt...
