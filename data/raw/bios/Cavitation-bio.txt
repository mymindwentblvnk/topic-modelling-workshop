Cavitation is a progressive death metal band from Amersfoort the Netherlands.
The band started with serious songwriting in 2010. Before that it was a kind of jamband.

Vocals: Michael
Guitar: Bram Hilhorst
Guitar: Jeroen van Aernsbergen
Bass: Walter
Drums: Olle (blastfabriek)

Influences: Death, Gojira, The Haunted, Necrophagist, Obscura
