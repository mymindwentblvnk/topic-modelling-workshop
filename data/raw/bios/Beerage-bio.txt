Beerage are Thrash Metal band from Ra'anana, Israel. 
Beerage was formed in late summer of 2008 by Yarin Swid, Bennet Stern, Ted & Saar Hasson. Preformed all over the state of Israel, in the cities Ra'anana, Herzeliyah, Tel Aviv & Be'er-Sheva. Recorded in August 2009 and self-released first 6-track EP (Nuclear Pesticide) in May 2010.
