BLASTMASTERS, the Florida-based death metal band led by DIABOLIC/UNHOLY GHOST drummer Aantar Lee Coates entered D.O.W. Studios on July 25 2005 with producer Juan "Punchy" Gonzales (MORBID ANGEL, UNHOLY GHOST) Jessie Jolly Bass - Vocals/Jeff Parrish - Guitars and RJ - Guitars to begin recording their full-length debut, entitled "Twisted Metal".
