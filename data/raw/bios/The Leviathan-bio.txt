The Leviathan is a progressive blackened death metal band from Manchester, England. Since forming in 2011 they have released one full length album, In Darkness it Crawls in 2013 on Asgard Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Leviathan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
