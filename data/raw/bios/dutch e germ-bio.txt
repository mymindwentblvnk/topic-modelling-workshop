Endlessly curious and often challenging, the music that drummer/producer Tim Dewit makes as Dutch E Germ has cemented his reputation as a revered figure in Brooklyn’s underground electronic music world—not that he didn’t already have shine: he was one of the founding members of Gang Gang Dance and left just before the release of the band’s fourth album, 2008's Saint Dymphna. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dutch+E+Germ">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
