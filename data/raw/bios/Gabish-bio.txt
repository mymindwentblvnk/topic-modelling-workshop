Death/thrash metal act from Japan. They released their first full-length studio album "The End of the World" in 1994 on Stinger Records. Next to that, they are featured on several compilation vinyl LP's and CD's. They also appeared on the "Skull Smash Vol. 1" compilation VHS, together with bands like Xenolith Oger, Raging Fury, Hellchild and more. Gabish is currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gabish">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
