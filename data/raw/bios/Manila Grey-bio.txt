MANILA GREY is a hip-hop/R&B duo originating from Vancouver, Canada formed by childhood friends Soliven and Neeko. The duo started releasing music in 2016 in collaboration with South Asian music producer “azel north”. The duo has recieved acclaim for bridging modern Asian aesthetics with atmospheric R&B sonics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Manila+Grey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
