Dutch acid, psychedelic rock band formed in 1964 in Landgraaf, Limburg (Netherlands).

Dutch Group, partly as a Shocking Blue (particularly voice and vocal style Diana Leemhaus like Marishku) but with pop-psychedelic bias.
