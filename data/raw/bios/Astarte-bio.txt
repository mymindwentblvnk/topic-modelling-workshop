Astarte was a female fronted black metal band from Elefsina, Greece formed in 1995 by Tristessa, Kinthia, Nemesis. The group split up in 2014 after the death of founding member Maria Kolokouri (Tristessa) due to complications from leukemia.

Line-up at time of split:
Tristessa (aka Lady Lilith) - vocals (R.I.P)
Derketa - synths
Hybris - lyrics
Ice - drums 
Lycon - guitars/bass

Former members:
Kinthia - vocals, acoustic guitar, keyboard
Nemesis - guitars, keyboards, acoustic guitar
Psychoslaughter - drums
Iraklis - keyboards
Katharsis - keyboards, effects
Ivar - drums
