Swampbeast is an extreme metal band from Los Angeles, California- the members of which play a mixture of death metal, grindcore, and black metal. Their first demo, "Path to the Oasis," was released in 2017. Since then they have released a split with Void Terror and a full length album (in 2019 and 2021, respectively). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Swampbeast">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
