This trash / death metal band from Sweden, consisting of Torgny Johansson (guitar), David Gabrielsson (vocals), Niklas Andreasson (guitar) and  Henrik Petersson on drums was founded late '90's with  with one intention, delivering straight up metal.

They are currently working on their third album.
