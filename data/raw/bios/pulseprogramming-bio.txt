Pulseprogramming is a multi media collaboration between musicians Joel Kriske and Marc Hellner, graphic designer Hans Seeger, and video/film artist Eric David Johnson. Pulseprogramming originated in Portland, OR, however, all but Eric have moved to Chicago. Upon arrival, Marc joined L'altra for a short time, left the group, and has since rejoined (while always remaining a collaborator and contributor to all their recordings). Pulseprogramming have toured the U.S. twice, once independently, and once with L'altra. Last year, they headlined the aesthetics showcase at Sonar and were featured on John Peel's BBC show to support their most recent full-length 'Tulsa For One Second' <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pulseprogramming">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
