There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Daemonium is a Mexican Death/Grind band from Nuevo Leon.

2) Daemonium was a French Black Metal band, changed name to Akhenaton. He appears sometimes as Akhenation. Under the name Lord A. Vincent Daemonium, he published a first CD in 1994, "Dark Opera oh the Ancient War Spirit (Or Search of Light)". Then, under the name Lord V. Akhenaton, he made "Divine Symphonies" (1995). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Daemonium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
