Christine and the Queens is the musical project of French singer-songwriter Héloïse Letissier. 

Letissier released his debut full-length album as Christine and the Queens, "Chaleur humaine", in 2014. An English language version of the album, titled "Christine and the Queens", was released in 2016. His sophomore album "Chris" was released in september 2018 and received universal acclaim. He has also released eight EPs and several singles.
