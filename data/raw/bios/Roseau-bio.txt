1. Singer from London, UK
Writer, Producer, Musician.

Roseau named so after the capitol of her father's home country of Dominica is a singer-songwriter from London. She previously performed as, Kerry Leatham In 2011 she formed Peter and Kerry with Tape Club Records label mate, Peter Lyons releasing the acclaimed album, La Trimouille in 2012. The duo also released 2 EP's together. Under the moniker Roseau, she strays from her acoustic roots and embraces a more robust, electronic sound on her solo debut album, Salt which was released in Fall of 2015 on Big Dada Records.
