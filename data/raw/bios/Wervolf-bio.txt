Wervolf is a Black Metal band from New York who plays a style of Early 80s NWOBHM driven Black Metal(Venom/Mercyful Fate/Sodom) and also some Raw and Depressive songs.

Started in 2006 by Frost the only member of the band. He is also the owner of Black Mass Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/WerVolF">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
