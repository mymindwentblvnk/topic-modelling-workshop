Valefar was an obscure Lithuanian black metal band, formed by members of Anubi and Nahash. The band has ceased to be in existence, in actuality. 

Last known line-up:
Agalyarey - guitar, keyboards, vocals
Abigor – bass, keyboards
Sargatanos (aka Lord Ominous of Anubi) - vocals
Lorey – drums

Discography:
