Nidvind was founded in 2006 in Sweden by Ve (Elände).

The band's lyrical content revolves around Anti-cosmic chaosophy, Black Magick and Death.

In March of 2010, Nidvind released the four-track EP Skördetid (Harvest Time), which was self-released and limited to 99 copies.

Later in 2010, Nidvind was joined by new drummer; Trish (Hinsides, DödsÄngel, Gate to Khaos, Skitliv)
