An electronic-tinged Nu Metal band out of Brooklyn, New York who released 2 full length albums ("Dust To Dust" in 2001 and "Sick" in 2003). The first album ("Dust To Dust") was released through Sanctuary Records and the second album ("Sick") was released through Psyclone Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dust+to+Dust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
