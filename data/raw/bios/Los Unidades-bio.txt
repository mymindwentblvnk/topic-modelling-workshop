Los Unidades Superband or super-project around  the members of Coldplay (Chris Martin, Will Champion, Jonny Buckland, and Guy Berryman) and several guest stars  among them are Pharrell, Stormzy, David Guetta, Stargate, Cassper Nyovest, Wizkid and Tiwa Savage.
Started in december 2018 with an EP "Global Citizen". On the first single " E-Lo "  you can here the voice of Chris Martin.
It's a dance track with traces of World Music.
