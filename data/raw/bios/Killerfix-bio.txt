A metal band from London, UK. Their debut album "Bridge of Disorder" was released in Germany, Austria and Switzerland on 25th November 2011 and the UK release is set for 2012 with the rest of the world to follow.

Members:
Craig Wilson - Vocals
Christian Lloyd - Guitar
James Fitzpatrick - Guitar
Mat Curr - Drums
Kim Rogers - Bass
