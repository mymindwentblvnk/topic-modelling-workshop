Audio Push is an American hip hop duo from Inland Empire, California, credited for popularizing the West Coast hip hop dance, Jerkin'. The group consists of Oktane (Julian Brown) and Pricetag (LJ). In 2009, the duo were signed to Interscope Records. The duo had their first hit single with "Teach Me How to Jerk". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Audio+Push">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
