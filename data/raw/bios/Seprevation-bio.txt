Formed in 2011, Seprevation began on the Bristol scene playing their first gig in December of that year and have since gone on to support bands such as Exhumed, Gorguts, Anaal Nathrakh, Wormrot, Dyscarnate, Bloodshot Dawn and Flayed Disciple aswell as playing at Bloodstock 2012. 

Executing old school death/thrash with influences such as Sadus, Death, Possessed, Morbid Angel and Terrorizer. Their first EP Ritual Abuse was released in summer 2012.
