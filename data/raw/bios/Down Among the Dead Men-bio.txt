Down Among the Dead Men ia a band formed by Rogga Johansson (PAGANIZER, RIBSPREADER, FONDLECORPSE, HUMANITY DELETE, THE GROTESQUERY) and Dave Ingram (Bolt Thrower, Benediction, Downlord) with Paganizer and Ribspreader musicians Dennis Blomberg (guitar) and Brynjar Helgetun (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Down+Among+the+Dead+Men">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
