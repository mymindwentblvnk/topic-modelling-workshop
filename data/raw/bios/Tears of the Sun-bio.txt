Tears Of The Sun  - funeral doom/death/gothic metal from Ukraine. One man band. Founded by little depressive boy, Den aka Solncevorot, in winter of 2005. Band has a several demo's (Dreams Of Sorrow, Autumn Sun) and almost ended full-lenght album.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tears+Of+The+Sun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
