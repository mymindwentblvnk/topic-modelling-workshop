Moorhead, Minnesota, United States of America, 2002
"Think of S.O.D. meets Anal Cunt and both raped Euronymous".

Blackened death metal grindcore act WWJD (Why Won't Jesus Die) released their self-titled demo in 2003. In 2011 they released their self-titled full-lenght. Lyrics are about blasphemy, misogyny, death and attacks on other metal bands.
