A Crows Glory is a metal hardcore band from Vancouver, BC. The band minus frontman KC Lange, formed in Vernon, BC and relocated to Vancouver in 2005 where they changed their name from Beyond the Battlefield, to A Crow's Glory, and joined up with Lange to create the explosive music. Their only release to date is a demo CD recorded in the spring of 2005, which has been recognized critically for its combination of hardcore vocal, and breakdown styling, and progressive metal writing. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+Crow%27s+Glory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
