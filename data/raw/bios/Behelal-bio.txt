1999 - present

Behelal is Black Metal band from Greece. The lyrical themes are Occult Arts, Satanism and Black Magick. 

Band Members: Daemon Nosferius (Spiritual Wintermoon)
Daemon Lethaemon (Ezgaroth/Esgaroth (Grc),Bethor (Grc), Imperial Darkness (Grc), Medieval Demon)
