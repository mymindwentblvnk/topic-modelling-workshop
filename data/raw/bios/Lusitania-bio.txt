There are multiple artists by the name of Lusitania:

1) Lusitania was a folk duo from New England, known for their dark tone and unusual instrumentation.  The primary members were Wyatt Shibley and Ben Richter.  In existence around 2003-2007, the band was connected to the scenes surrounding The Dresden Dolls, Mischief Brew, and Johnny Hobo and the Freight Trains.
