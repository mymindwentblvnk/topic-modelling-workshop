Ego apartment is an alternative band formed in 2020. Comprised of producer-bassist, Dyna, guitarist-vocalist Peggy Doll, and guitar-vocalist Zen. The three piece band has a diverse background of influences including R&B, EDM, Pop, and Rock. A fusion of infectiously catchy pop melodies and an alternative D.I.Y. sound, ego apartment is paving its own direction in music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ego+apartment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
