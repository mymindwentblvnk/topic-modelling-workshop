Rockstar (vocals),  T.J. Quim Quimby (guitar),  Blue Cheese (guitar), Ram Rod (bass) and Pat the Rat (drums) are from Hobart, Tasmania.  They are a brutal death metal outfit and formed in the year 2011.  They are signed to CDN Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GAPE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
