Chicherina, a band lead by Russian pop-rock artist Yulia Chicherina, officially Yulia Dmitrievna Chicherina (Russian: Юлия Дмитриевна Чичерина). The Band was created in 1997 by Yulia Chicherina with Azat Mukhammetov, Aleksandr Buriy, and Aleksandr "DrOff" Aleksandrov and has been performing since then.  Chcherina is part of the wave of Uralic rock along with Nautilus Pompilius and Chaif. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/%D0%A7%D0%B8%D1%87%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%B8%D0%BD%D0%B0">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
