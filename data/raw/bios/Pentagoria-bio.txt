Pentagoria was a brutal death metal band from Minnesota, USA. The band was formed in 1998 with Scott Bibus (vocals), Tom Heikes (bass), and Tony Williams (drums, guitar, and vocals). 

In 2000, Pentagoria released a demo entitled, "Spermatophore" along with their only full length recording, "...And The Sky Bled Gore". They were also featured on several compilations. In 2001, Pentagoria split up and disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pentagoria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
