The earlier incarnation of Mundanus Imperium. They released their demo 'Ode to the Nightsky' in March 1996. It was reissued on CD under the new band name in 1997 by Impure Creations Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nattefall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
