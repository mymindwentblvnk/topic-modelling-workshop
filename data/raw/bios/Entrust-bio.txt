A heavy metal / thrash metal band from Brno, Czech Republic. Formed in November 2003 by singer Lukas and guitar player Viktor after their previous band, Krysi Smich, ended. They had several concerts in Brno, and they cooperated with famous Czech instrumental band called Seven, so Entrust appeared onstage in Prague and Plzen with them. Unfortunately, Lukas has left the band in summer 2006, but before end of the year 2006, he decided to return. Current lineup: Lukas (vocals), Viktor (el. guitar), Tom (el. guitar), Pavel (bass guitar) and newest member Tom (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Entrust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
