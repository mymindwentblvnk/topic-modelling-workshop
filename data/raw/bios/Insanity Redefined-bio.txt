Insanity Redefined is a Bulgarian metal band that mixes several metal styles in their music. The band was created at the end of 2013. Recorded an EP called "Dim Frontiers" in 2014 and released it in January, 2015.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Insanity+Redefined">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
