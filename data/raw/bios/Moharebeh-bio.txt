Moharebeh are US black/death metal band formed in 2014, which is notoriously known for their anti-Islamic lyrical themes.\

Moharebeh is a term that is treated as interchangeable with ḥirabah in Arabic lexicons. The related term muḥārib (محارب) has been translated by English language Iranian media as "enemy of God". In English-language media sources, Moḥarebeh in Iran has been translated variously as "waging war against God,"  "war against God and the state," "enmity against God." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moharebeh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
