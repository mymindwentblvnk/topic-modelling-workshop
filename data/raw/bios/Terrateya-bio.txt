Terrateya is a Symphonic/Gothic Metal band from Regensburg, Germany. Their debut album "Whispers Torn from a Raven's Dream" was released on September 25, 2011, to great critical acclaim. In their issue #77, German metal magazine Legacy gave the album 14 out of 15 points and summarized their review with the words: "This is what modern Gothic Metal should sound like". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Terrateya">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
