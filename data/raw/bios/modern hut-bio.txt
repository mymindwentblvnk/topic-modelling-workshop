Joe Steinhardt is the co-founder of New Jersey's Don Giovanni Records, home to Waxahatchee, Screaming Females, California X, and others. His debut LP as Modern Hut, produced by Screaming Females' Marissa Paternoster, is a sparse, straightforward anti-folk record. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Modern+Hut">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
