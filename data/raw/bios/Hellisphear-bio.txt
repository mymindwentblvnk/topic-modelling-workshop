Hellisphear was founded on January the 24th 2003 by Jeroen Gilhuys, Danae Koenen & Nick Tolenaars in Drunen. Through the years hellisphear has grown alot and experienced multiple member-replacement.

The name originates from the word Hemmisphere, the Hemmispere is one of the layers in the Ozon layer, the Hellisphear is a place somewhere between subconcience and reality.

The music Hellisphear makes can be categorized in the New Age Metal style, we try to keep our music variated from heavey and sometimes difficult to simple and mellow.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hellisphear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
