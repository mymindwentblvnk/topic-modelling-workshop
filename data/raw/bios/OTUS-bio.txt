OTUS are a slduge doom band from Rome Italy born in 2012, which takes elements of Ambient\Post metal bands as Isis and Cult of Luna, passing through the Black Sabbath's doom up to the experimental music of Tool, Om and Sunn O))). The band enrich their sound playing ethnic instruments, building their own synthesizers, chanting mantras and taking particular care of their visual and symbolic identity, from show posters to onstage visual projections.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Otus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
