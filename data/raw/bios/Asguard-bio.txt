Asguard are a Belarusian melodic death metal band hailing from Mogilev. They are one the first bands from Belarus to gain international recognition.
Throughout the years their sound has evolved from technical "classic" melodic death metal to a more mainstream-friendly music style through incorporating various electronic and industrial elements in their music. However, they are still recognizable and respected.
