Dave Okumu (aka E-40), is a member of the London, UK jazz & jazz fusion scene, working with musicians such as Django Bates & Finn Peters - whilst, also, being a member of the London based fusion community F-IRE Collective. He has lead The Invisible, since it's formation, in late 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Okumu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
