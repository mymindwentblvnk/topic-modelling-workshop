Seversun is a Russian Prog/Groove metal band that was formed in 2015. They have released 2 albums, “New World Chaos” in 2017, and “Headlong Through Blood” in 2021. The current members of the band are Arsen Abdullaev (Vocals & Bass), Shamil Usarov (Guitar (lead) & Vocals), Maxim Krasilnikov (Guitar (rhythm) and Eugene Mazur (Drums). They are not currently signed to a label. Bandcamp is here: seversun.bandcamp.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Seversun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
