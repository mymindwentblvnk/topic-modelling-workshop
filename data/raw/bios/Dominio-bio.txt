Dominio is a collective based in Valencia (Spain) that has been organizing gatherings dedicated to electronic, experimental music and audiovisual art since September 2018. They support artists and young people by establishing new spaces and opportunities for their enjoyment and work that are usually located off-radar or non-represented locally or even nationally <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dominio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
