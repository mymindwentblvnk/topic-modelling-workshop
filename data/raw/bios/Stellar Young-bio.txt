A multi-genre infused indie rock group based out of albany, ny, Stellar Young blends their catchy, melodic signature with such varied influences as ambient rock, dance, jazz, hip-hop and folk. Stellar Young feature the talents of John Glenn (vocals/keyboard), Erik Flora (guitar/vocals), Kyle Hatch (guitar) and Dave Parker (bass/synth). The group has shared the stage with The Dirty Heads, We The Kings, Kaiser Chiefs, Bret Michaels, San Cisco, Terraplane Sun, The Features, Civil Twilight, Weerd Science, and The Front Bottoms. Property of Zack called the band "...Engaging enough to garner mainstream indie rock fans attention and intricate enough to please music snobs." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stellar+Young">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
