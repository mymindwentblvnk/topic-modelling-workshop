Morpheus Tales is a black metal project that started in Thessaloniki, Greece. It started as a side project of Paris and then it became a two-man project, adding Anestis on the line-up. After the release of one EP (Samples of Reality) and one full-length album (Secular Noir), Anestis left the band. Giorgos P  joined the project and the second full-length album, titled ''II'', was released on 19.06.2017. The last member to be added was Lee, who joined the band at the production phase of "II".

Members:
Paris: Guitars, drums/ keys programming
Giorgos P: Guitars
Lee: Vocals
