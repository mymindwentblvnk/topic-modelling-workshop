MINCE GRINDCORE band formed in 1990 in Rome, Italy. They recorded an only one EP with 10 tracks ultra-short, ultra-fast and ultra-simple old school grindcore like early Napalm Death, Agathocles or Fear Of God. They had very good friendship with the veteran mince-core label "SOA Records".
