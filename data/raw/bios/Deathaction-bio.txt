Biography:
DEATHACTION is a young band presents THE NEW WAVE OF METAL and whose main distinctive feature lies in powerful stage performance and the sheer quality of their material. The band exists for 3 years at the moment and has already had a 24 concerts and also at the large festivals. The band already has three singles ,3 video-clips and much more new material is on the way...
For more Information check:http://www.facebook.com/Deathaction

 In 2010 "Deathaction" supported such bands as "RAGE","SONIC SYNDICATE","CALIBAN","KISS FOREVER BAND" AND "RAGNAROG" on their concerts in Russian!!!

 In 2011 released first official video "Run...Please...Run" 
 and in summer we begin to record first album [The Diaries] which was released in March 2012!
