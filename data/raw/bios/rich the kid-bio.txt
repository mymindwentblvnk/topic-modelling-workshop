Dimitri Leslie Roger (born July 13, 1992), professionally known as Rich The Kid is an American rapper. Signed to Rostrum Records, he released his debut studio album,The World is Yours, on March 30, 2018. His second studio album, The World is Yours 2, was released on March 22, 2019, and his third studio album,Boss Man, on March 13, 2020 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rich+The+Kid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
