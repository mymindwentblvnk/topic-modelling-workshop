1) DARKENED is a DEATH METAL band with members from the UK, Sweden and Canada. They released an EP in 2019 and have their debut full-length scheduled for release on September 11th, 2020 through Norwegian label Edged Circle Productions.

2) Darkened is an icelandic melodic Metalcore band currently working on their debut album, they are aged 15-17 and they are strongly influenced by bands such as Avenged Sevenfold, Metallica, Anthrax, Children Of Bodom and The Devil Wears Prada.

3)  DARKENED is a Washington DC area dubstep artist whose full-length album Flipside will be released in Fall 2009. Darkened blends dubstep with glitchy junkyard, african/voodoo, german brass cabaret, and found sounds.
