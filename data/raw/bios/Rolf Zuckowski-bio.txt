Rolf Zuckowski sings songs for and with children to make them happy. 
The little kids in Germany love him.
He has been writing songs since the late seventies gaining popularities in the 1980s with LP such as Schulwegparade or Radio lollipop . 
He is writing songs that promote children's rights, and road safety as well as other educational topics and seasonal events. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rolf+Zuckowski">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
