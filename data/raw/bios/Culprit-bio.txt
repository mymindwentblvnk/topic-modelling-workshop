There are currently two artists sharing the band name Culprit:

1. Culprit is a heavy metal band formed in Seattle, USA in 1981. They are widely known for their 1983 release Guilty as Charged.
The band is currently signed to Hellion Records, listen to their music at their official myspace page:  http://myspace.com/culpritlives.

2. Culprit is an alternative rock/indie band from Los Angeles, California. They are currently signed to Easy Killer Records and are following up their debut EP, Analogue, with an untitled release set to come out this summer. http://facebook.com/culprit <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Culprit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
