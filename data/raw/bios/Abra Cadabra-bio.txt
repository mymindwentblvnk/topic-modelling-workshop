Abra Cadabra (born 4 April 1998) is a British rapper and singer from Tottenham, London. He is a member of the UK drill group OFB. He is most known for his single "On Deck", which was released in July 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abra+Cadabra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
