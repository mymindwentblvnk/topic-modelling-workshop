Atrox Trauma is a thrash metal band formed in 2008, Hungary. 
In the beginning, Imre Török (vocal/rhytm) and Kristóf Andruskó (drum) started to play songs from Slayer, Kreator and Sepultura. Then they completed with Máté Bódog (bass) and András Székely (lead guitar). 
The band started to write own songs too. You can listen 3 of their songs in their new demo, called Fojtogat (Strangles).  //you can find the demo at their websites: www.atroxtrauma.com  or  www.myspace.com/atroxtrauma
Nowdays they give concerts and work on their new songs.
