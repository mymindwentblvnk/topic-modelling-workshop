Mark Forster is a German singer-songwriter. He was born on January 11, 1984 in Winnweiler (near Kaiserslautern) Germany. He was signed to the record label Four Music in 2010. His album discography comprises:
- Karton (June 1, 2012)
- Bauch und Kopf (2014) - which featured the highly successful "Au Revoir"
- Tape (2016)
