New Jersey metal band featuring former Ripping Corpse/Dim Mak drummer Brandon Thomas along with current and former members of Subzero and Cattlepress. Their style is a mixture of thrash and death metal, with technical yet memorable guitar playing and insanely creative drumming.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Dying+Light">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
