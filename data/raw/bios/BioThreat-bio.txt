BioThreat are a band coming from The San Fernando Valley, adjacent to Los Angeles. They play fast and aggressive crossover-thrash and like to party. They have recorded only one EP that can be bought at there shows, but there have not been any official recordings out. The members of BioThreat as of 2011 are currently writing new material and plan to record by early next year. Check out their official Facebook for more information on BioThreat.
