Moving Oos is a retrospective music collective from Trondheim.
The eight members hails from various bands that have made a name for themselves over the past few years. They are definitely a Trondheim “all-star” team with members from New Violators, The International Tussler Society, Cadillac, Dadafon, Nullskattesnylterne, Fuckface among other bands. Their music is inspired by the fantastic years between 1967 and 1974. Rock, gospel, funk and soul are common denominators. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moving+Oos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
