During January/February of 2011, Dis Pater began writing music for a new project, The Crevices Below. The album, entitled “Below The Crevices” was released on the 23rd of May through Nordvis. Set in an underworld city, it documents the slow fall and deterioration of a subterranean monarch. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Crevices+Below">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
