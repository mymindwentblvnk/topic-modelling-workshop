Nacho Picasso is a rapper from Seattle that has released four albums exclusively produced by Blue Sky Black Death; "For The Glory" (2011), "Lord Of The Fly" (2012), "Exalted" (2012) and "Stoned & Dethroned" (2015) after releasing the solo album "Blunt Raps". 

He is affiliated with Cloud Nice and is 1/3 of the group BAYB.
