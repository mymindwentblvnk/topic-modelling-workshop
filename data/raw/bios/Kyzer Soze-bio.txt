Taking the intensity of both modern death metal and traditional black metal, Queensland destroyers KYZER SOZE are a primal force. The five piece deliver a hungered live performance, to be expected from a band that have taken their name from the merciless crime lord in the film The Usual Suspects, and cite bands such as THE BLACK DAHLIA MURDER, BEHEMOTH, DEICIDE and HOUR OF PENANCE as foundation influences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kyzer+Soze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
