There are 3 artists named Ossification. 

1.)  An American brutal death metal band from Moreno Valley, California.  They formed in 2007.  They consist of Damian Martinez (vocals),  Frank Zelada (guitars), Cris Gutierrez (guitars), Salvador Valadez (bass) and Albert Rios (drums).  Their lyrical themes include murder, violence and hatred.  They are not signed to a record label.

2.)  A Brazilian death metal band that formed in 1994 and only released one demo in 1994.  They disbanded at an unknown point in time.

3.) An Italian death metal band that formed at unknown point in time.  They also released one demo.  It was also released in 1994.
