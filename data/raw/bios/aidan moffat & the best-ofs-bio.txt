Aidan Moffat & the Best-Ofs are a four piece band fronted by Moffat. They played their first gig supporting American band Slint, at the Glasgow ABC in Scotland, where they debuted the songs "Big Blonde", "The Last Kiss", "Ballad of the Unsent Letter", "Living With You Now", and "Atheist's Lament". They released their debut album 'How to Get to Heaven From Scotland' on Valentine's Day in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aidan+Moffat+&+The+Best-Ofs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
