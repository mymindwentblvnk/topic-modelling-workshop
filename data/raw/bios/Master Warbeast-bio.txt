MASTER WARBEAST were a death/thrash metal band that combined progressive influences. From Kidderminster, England they consisted of;
Dan Hinton - Guitar (2006-2010)
Andy Morgan - Vocals (2006-2008)
Jim Maitland - Vocals (2008-2010)
Karl Bicknell - Bass (2006-2010)
Ran Edwards - Drums (2006-2010)
Earlier material was more grind influenced, they split up in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Master+Warbeast">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
