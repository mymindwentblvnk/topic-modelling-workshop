For the classical composer, see Antonio Salieri

Salieri is an band from Andalucia (Spain), created in 2001 by former members of bands such as Sick Buzos, Sr Chinarro, Zonites....

Their first album -"Electric Doorbells"- was realeased in 2006, by Discos Liliput/ El diablo, and produced by Antonio Luque (Sr Chinarro).

Their second album, "Frogs, lemons, sea", (Embajada de Liliput/ El diablo) has been chosen by several magazines, such as GoMag or Arto de Madriz, as one of the best albums realeased in 2008. It was produced by Paco Loco, and masterized by Kramer in Florida (USA).
