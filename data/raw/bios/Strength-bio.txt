1) Strength are an acclaimed trio from Portland, Oregon who produce infectious dance pop. Their full length debut "Going Strong" was released in August of 2006.  In September 2010, their sophmore album "Mind-Reader" came out. Mixed by Jacob Portrait at Dandy Warhols Odditorium studio in Portland, Oregon, "Mind-Reader" continues in the same vein of catchy, hook-laden dance pop.
