Started in 2009, Cover of Night is a one man atmospheric, black metal project from Littleton, Colorado. All instruments and arrangements are performed by Andrew Malone. To date, he has released 2 full length albums; a self-titled in 2015 and Walkabout in 2016. Both albums are available at https://coverofnight.bandcamp.com For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/coverofnightmetal <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cover+of+night">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
