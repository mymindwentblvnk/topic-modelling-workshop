A UK producer, who started out as a pioneer of the bootleg craze, releasing limited edition singles under as Girls On Top between 2001 & 2002. He signed to Virgin Records as a result of the interest in the early Girls On Top singles & released his debut album Presents His X-Factor, Vol. 1, in 2003. He has now become a successful record producer, producing hit singles for artists including Sugababes, Kelis, Liberty X & Rachel Stevens. Remixing work hace been with Trent Reznor's Nine Inch Nails, New Order, Pet Shop Boys, Gwen Stefani, Depeche Mode & others including Poppy & The Jezebels.
