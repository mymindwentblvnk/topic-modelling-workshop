Manfred Mann Chapter Three was a British experimental jazz rock band founded by South African keyboard player Manfred Mann in 1969. The band was short-lived and disbanded in 1970 after two albums. Mann went on to form Manfred Mann's Earth Band in 1971. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Manfred+Mann+Chapter+Three">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
