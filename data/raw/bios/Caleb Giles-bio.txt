Caleb Giles is a rapper from the Bronx who plays saxophone in Standing On The Corner. Tower is Caleb Giles' debut statement. His 2018 project, There Will Be Rain, was hailed by Pitchfork as "a lovely melange of jazz, hip-hop, gospel and soul". In 2019, he released his third studio album, Under the Shade.
