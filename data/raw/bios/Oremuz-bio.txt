Oremuz is an all Filipino band from Manila Philippines. It was founded in 2004 by guitarist Rams Aquino,then vocalist Ronell Lanuza and Debi Mendez. Their music is variously described as gothic metal, as well as symphonic metal in some media.But the band describe their music as experiment/dark metal. Years had passed,the band changed their lineup many times Until 2008. The members became stable playing gigs up to now. The present lineup are Weneath Onate, Shekaina Gabitanan, Rams Aquino, Russel Jean Gabitanan, Kris Paul Katigbak, Lovel Ajok and John Raphael Gonzales.
