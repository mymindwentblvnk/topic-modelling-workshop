Concedo Nulli is a deathmetalband from Rotterdam. By mixing personal influences with deathmetal the band defines its own sound.

All bandmembers are inspired by numerous bands, artists and authors, but choose a unique approach for Concedo Nulli.

The result: independent deathmetal.

Our demo Critique Of Pure Treason is available on the sound section.
