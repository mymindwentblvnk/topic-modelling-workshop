Bringing influences from all genres of music and coming from all walks of life; A Texas Funeral's sound will not only to catch you off guard, surprise, and challenge your ear. ATF is composed of 4 musicians from diverse backgrounds ranging from Prog, Metal, Hardcore, Indie, Rock, Punk and Pop and from various parts of the country. They are curently based out of Montreal, QC. With a new album slated to be released on fight the mentality records summer 2008.
