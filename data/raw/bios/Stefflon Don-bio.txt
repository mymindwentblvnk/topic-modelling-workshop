Stephanie Victoria Allen (born 14 December 1991), better known by her stage name Stefflon Don (or Steff London), is an English rapper and singer. Stefflon Don was born in Birmingham, England. She is of Jamaican descent. She has six siblings. When she was four years old, her family moved to Rotterdam in the Netherlands, but at the age of fourteen, she returned to Britain to study at a London school. She worked as a cake decorator and hairdresser before her career in music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stefflon+Don">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
