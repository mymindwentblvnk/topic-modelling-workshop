There are mutliple artists with this name.

1) The Slow Death is a melodic death doom metal band from Blue Mountains, Australia with both male and female vocals, formed by Stuart Prickett (mastermind of Elysium and Stone Wings) in 2007. Their style is ultra slow death/doom with melodic riffs, powerful deep growls, grandiose female vocals and tragical lyrics. The atmosphere is mournful and dark. The guitars are magnificent with perfect riffs, amazing melodies and rhythm changes. The melodies are melancholic passages of perpetual depression.
Their debut album, titled "The Slow Death", was released in September 2008. Follow-up "II" saw light of day in 2012.

Current line-up:
Gregg Williamson - vocals 
Mandy Andresen - vocals, keyboards 
Stuart Prickett - all instruments

2) The Slow Death is a country-tinged punk rock from Minneapolis. Ex-members of Pretty Boy Thorson & The Falling Angels, The Ergs!, and Rest of Us.
