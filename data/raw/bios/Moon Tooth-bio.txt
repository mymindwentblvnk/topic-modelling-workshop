The new project of long-time collaborators Nick Lee & Ray Marte. MOON TOOTH formed from the dissolve of their previous band, Exemption. The two quickly found kinship with John Carbone (Rice Cultivation Society) and Vincent Romanelli (Give Up The Goods) while embarking on their new musical journey. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moon+Tooth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
