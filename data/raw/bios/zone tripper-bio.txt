Zone Tripper is a retro wave synth project by Roy Julian Digre aka Technomancer, focusing on retro danceable beats and catchy melodies. Unlike his other projects, it is largely instrumental, featuring sparse to no vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/zone+tripper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
