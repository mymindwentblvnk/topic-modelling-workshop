Consisting of former members of Malnurture, Ashes of Babylon, Winds of Leng, and To Be Eaten. Officially established in the fall of 2009. Shortly after the Hiatus of Malnurture, Rhythm guitar player Curt DuRocher, Josh Perrin, and drummer Brian Miller began work on Deadspawn.
