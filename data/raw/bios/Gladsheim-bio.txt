Gladsheim was a one-man black metal act from France whose sole member was Skoll. The band used to belong to The Hate Korp along with numerous other bands such as Cavaticus, Brume, Svart Hat, Charogne, Votum Mortis and Yperite. Common lyrical themes of this band included war, hate and misanthropy, which can be heard on its only demo «War Wolf Spirit» released in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gladsheim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
