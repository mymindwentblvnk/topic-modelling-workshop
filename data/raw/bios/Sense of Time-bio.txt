Sense Of Time is a band from Graz/Austria. Since 2005 they rocked many stages. The guys played together with ZSK, Man Alive, The M.A.S.S, Jaya The Cat, Boom Boom Kid, Skafield an many many more great bands.

Now the first EP is finished. It was recordet at the Stressstudio together with Tom Zwansger (Antimaniax) The CD is an exciting mix of Punk, Rock and Ska-Elements! So enjoy the songs. And if you like the music buy the CD over www.sot-music.de or www.myspace.com/senseoftime <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sense+Of+Time">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
