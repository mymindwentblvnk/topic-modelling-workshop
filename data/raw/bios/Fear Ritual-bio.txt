Brazilian death metal band formed in 1991 in Caxias do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul. They released only one self-titled EP in 1995. 

Line-up,

Cipriano Maffei - Drums (Dies Irae (Bra), Abominattion, The Sceptic)
Evandro S. de Brito - Guitar
Paulo Schroeber - Lead Guitar (Dies Irae (Bra), Almah)
