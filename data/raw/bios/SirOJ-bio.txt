SirOJ (real name Joris Titawano born 10-11-1987) is known for his remixes of hundreds of national and international artists, aswell as his productions for major Dutch hip hop artists. He released his debut producers album on the two biggest hiphop labels of The Netherlands in August 2010. Early 2011 he produced the movie score for Dutch movie 'Rabat'.  Currently he is producing Dutch hip hop artist Dio's second album.
Over the years SirOJ became one of the most divers producers of the Dutch rapscene. He managed to release on several labels, collaborate with all the major singers and rappers and releasing instrumental work on a international scale as well.
