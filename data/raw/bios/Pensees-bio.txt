Pensees is an electronic duo from Rostov-on-Don, Russian Federation. Their music embraces future garage, dubstep, trip-hop, and other types of downtempo music. Their debut album 4.35 am was released on February 7, 2016 and is available via the official Bandcamp page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pensees">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
