Cade is a Melbourne-based musician/multi-instrumentalist. His music is a medley of indie/electronic sounds with tinges of post-rock. An avid audiophile, he surrounds himself with assorted forms of instrumentation as he creates music from his cosy lair in the inner city. Cade also moonlights as a freelance graphic designer and is presently engaged in experiments with visual installation and new media.
