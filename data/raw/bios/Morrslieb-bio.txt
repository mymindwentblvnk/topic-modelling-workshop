Morrslieb was a heavy-metal/alternative band based in Bonn, Germany. Founded in 1998 by Peter Tagliabue (vocals) and Stephan Schmidt (guitar), from May 1999 the group included Philipp Ruhland (bass) and Alex Schröder (drums). The group performed in clubs in the Bonn-Köln area. Albums include "The Pressure Principle"  (EP 1998) and Liquid (EP 2000). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morrslieb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
