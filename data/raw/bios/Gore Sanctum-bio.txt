Gore Sanctum is a one-man brutal death metal/ slam death metal project by Stu (Vocals and all Instruments/programming) from the UK. He takes influences from brutal death metal bands such as Disgorge (US) and Lividity, also providing old school slam influences such as Devourment and Dying Fetus.

The debut album "Flesh and Organ Consumption" in 2009 by Sevared Records. The debut beautifully molds death metal with slam with extreme vocals attached.
