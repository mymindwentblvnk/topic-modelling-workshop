Reign of Terror is a US band playing what they claim to be "european" Power Metal, which may be a fairly accurate description. Singer, song writer and producer Michael Vescera is known from the Japanese band  Loudness, with whom he recorded 2 albums, Soldier Of Fortune (1989) and On The Prowl (1991), and from his appearances with Yngwie Malmsteen.

To date two Albums has been released, the debut Sacred Ground and Conquer & Divide.

Apart from Vescera, the band consists of Joe Stump (Guitars and song writing), Matt Scurfield (drums) and Jay Rigney (bass)
