Ambeon is a project consisting mainly of multi-instrumentalist composer Arjen Anthony Lucassen and young singer Astrid van der Veen, both hailing from the Netherlands. The music incorporates dark ambient, progressive metal and celtic melodies with Astrid's lush, melodic voice accompanying it. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ambeon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
