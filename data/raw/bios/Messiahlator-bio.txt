MESSIAHLATOR is a crust/grind/thrash band from Edmonton, AB, Canada. They have released a 10" EP, "Blizzard of Saws" (2010), and a demp tape, "Abose" (2012).

Lineup:
Brett - Vox/Guitar
Pavs - Bass/Vox
