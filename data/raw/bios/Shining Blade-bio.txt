Shining Blade is a little-known Speed Metal/Heavy Metal band from Italy  (Bari, Apulia). Their catologue consists of only 3 official releases, with only one of these being a certified full-length: 1986's Touch the Night (Released on  LHDLM). One of the band's tracks was featured on the Heavy Metal sampler "Speed metal hell Vol.I" compilation (1985). The band is currently split up.

Last known line-up: 

Francesco d'Elia - vocals
Jimmi Troccoli - guitar
Fabio Pignataro - guitar
Francesco Collela - bass
