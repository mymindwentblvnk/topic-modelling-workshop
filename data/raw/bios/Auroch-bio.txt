There are two bands named Auroch. The first is a death metal band from Vancouver, Canada, that formed in 2008. Their music focuses on themes from H.P. Lovecraft's mythos. They have released a number of EPs and split releases as well as two full-length albums, with their third album Mute Books due to be released on October 21, 2016 through Profound Lore.

Members:
Montesi- Guitars/Vocals
Ouzounov - Guitars/Vocals
Chandler- Drums

The second Auroch was a French heavy metal band that released an EP in 1985.
