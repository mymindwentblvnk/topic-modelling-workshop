Toby Fox is the developer and composer behind the 2015 hit indie video game Undertale.

In 2018, he released chapter 1 of a new game titled Deltarune, which he also composed the music for. Chapter 2 was released in 2021.

Fox has also composed music for Homestuck under the alias Radiation. Not only this, he also composed music for hit Nintendo titles Super Smash Bros Ultimate and Pokemon Sword and Shield. According to his  Twitter he has also composed a track that will be starring in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toby+Fox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
