El Kaso Urkijo is a mince grindcore group of Viladecans, Catalonia (Spain). Formed in 1987 by Popeye, Warheads and Obeja was the first spanish group managed to release an album of grindcore in 1991. After more than a decade after its dissolution, the band has reunited with changes in training. The Kaso Urkijo published in 2009, its Cd back, with the name of "La bañera fué su nicho". His music moves certainly in search of the essence that gave name to this style of music, pure, simple, direct and radical Grindcore unceremoniously or foreign ornaments have been a reference for many groups both inside and outside of Spain, with a sound own very particular.
