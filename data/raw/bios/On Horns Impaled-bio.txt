On Horns Impaled is a German black metal act and was founded in 2007. They are still active today. Their musical themes deal mostly with anti-christianity. 

Their current line-up exists out of:

Scapegoat - Vocals
Steelcrusher - Bass
Ays - Guitar
GoatSodomizer - Drums

On Horns Impaled has released the following:

- Perversion and Desaster Demo, 2007  
- On Horns Impaled / Samrt Split, 2008  
- Total World Domination Full-length, 2009
