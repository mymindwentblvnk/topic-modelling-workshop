Eyes Of The Dead is a Melodic Death/Thrash Metal band from The United States, Connecticut.  The band consists of former members of the bands Aggressia and Deadwait. 

So far they  have released one self-titled demo, two full-length albums, ''What Nightmares Are Made Of'' (2006) and ''The Weak And The Wounded'' (2009), and an EP of cover songs titled "Let's Play Drink The Beer!" (2010).


Their current line-up:

Frank Connors - Vocals
Matt Talarczyk - Guitar
Brian Greene - Bass
Chris "Junior" Tallerdy - Drums
