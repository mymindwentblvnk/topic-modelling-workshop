7 Year Rabbit Cycle was a noise rock band that started with Rob Fisk, Kelly Goode, Miya Osaki, Steve Gigante (performed under the name Owls). Subsequent lineups included Ches Smith, Jamie Stewart, George Chen, Devin Hoff, Cain Blanchard.

Fisk subsequently started the solo project Common Eider, King Eider and enlisted Chen for the live band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/7+Year+Rabbit+Cycle">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
