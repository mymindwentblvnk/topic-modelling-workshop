Lauren Michelle Jauregui Morgado (born June 27, 1996 in Miami, Florida, US) is a Cuban-American singer-songwriter, best known as a member of the girl group Fifth Harmony. As of 2016, Jauregui has begun a solo career collaborating with artists such as Marian Hill, Halsey, Ty Dolla Sign and Steve Aoki. His first solo song was "Expectations". In 2021, his first EP called "Prelude" was released, bringing together tracks like "Don't Wanna Say", "Falling" and "Scaterred". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lauren+Jauregui">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
