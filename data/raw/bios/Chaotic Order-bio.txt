Chaotic Order is a hardcore metal band from South Burlingon, Vermont. They consist of Adam Wing (lead singer) Greg Hughes (guitarist) Tucker Bean (drums) and Dylan Hughes (bass) they have shared the stage with many amazing local New England bands and continue to get more and more attention through myspace and word of mouth. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chaotic+Order">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
