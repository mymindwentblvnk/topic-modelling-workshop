There are two or more bands with this name.

1. Silent Voices is a progressive metal band from Finland, founded in 1995 by guitarist Timo Kauppinen, bassist Pasi Kauppinen, drummer Jukka-Pekka Koivisto and keyboardist Henrik Klingenberg.
At the time their main influences were Dream Theater and Rush.

Henrik originally also performed vocal duties, but this role was later filled by Michael Henneken. Since the album "Building Up the Apathy", however, Michael Henneken has parted ways with the band. Band released a new album "Reveal The Change" on november 2013. They also announced that their new singer is Teemu Koskela who is formerly known from Winterborn.
2. Silent Voices was an 80s industrial/electronic band from Nuremberg, Germany.
