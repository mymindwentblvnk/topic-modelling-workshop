Sergent Garcia is the group formed around the French singer Bruno Garcia, a former guitarist of punk band Ludwig Von 88.

Sergent Garcia mixes reggae, salsa, raggamuffin, rock and other trends in a new style he defines as the salsamuffin.

In 2011, he released his sixth studio album, Una y otra vez (Cumbancha / The Gwagwita), recorded in France, Spain, Cuba and Colombia.

Together with Colectivo Iye Ife, Sergent Garcia started a world tour that began in Paris in March 2011 and made appearances in through Europe, the United States, Canada, Mexico and Colombia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sergent+Garcia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
