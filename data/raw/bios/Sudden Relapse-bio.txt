Sudden Relapse was a thrash metal band from New Orleans, Louisiana.  The year they formed is not known.  Their last known lineup was Andre Karst (vocals), Danny Guidry (guitars), Joey Larson (guitars), Jesse Dover (bass) and Ross Sandoz (drums).  Will Langford (bass) is the only past member of Sudden Relapse.  Dan Guidry died at an unknown point in time.  They only recorded one demo in 1992 called Demo '92.  After they released that demo, they split-up at an unknown period of time.  Their demo can be found at this site. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sudden+Relapse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
