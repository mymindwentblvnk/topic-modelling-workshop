Born in Venezuela now living in Spain Maddie Moon is a self produced , singer-songwriter inspired by the universe and the futuristic retro aesthetics of the 80s, introduce the listener in a new dimention with her magic songs and empowering melancholic lyrics. Inspired by genres such as Indie pop and Bedroom Pop, Maddie Moon evokes a magical atmosphere connecting each individual to their inner soul. In 2019 she was selected for the first phase of The Voice Spain with her cover of Disintegration by The Cure. Later on she won the 4th place among 600 people in the Cover Nation Melanie Martinez contest, with her cover of Playdate, Now is working on a New EP and waiting for her NEW SINGLE ¨Fairies¨ in April 2nd. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maddie+Moon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
