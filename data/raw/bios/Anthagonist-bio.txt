Anthagonist were an old school death metal band from Canada. They released two demos before splitting up.
Last known line-up
Frédérick Simard - Bass & Vocals
Sébastien Beaulieu - Guitars
Pierre Champagne - Guitars
Luc Laporte - Drums

Their discography includes

Enigmatic Pleasure of Death - Demo, 1989
