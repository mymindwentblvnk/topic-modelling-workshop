Gutted Pig Genocide, often abreviated GPG, is a dutch goregrind band, wich was formed in 2006 by Corpsificater (Fredrik Van Dijk) as a solo project. Fredrik was inspired by late Last Days of Humanity and Cock and Ball Torture. The first album, called Skinned Alive, was released in 2008. All the instruments, except the drums, are played and recorded by Fredrik.
