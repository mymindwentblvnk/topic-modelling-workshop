Sevenday Curse is from Boston, Massachusetts.  The band is Scott McDonald (Vocals and Guitar), Dan Lozzi (Guitar), Pat McKenna (Bass), and John Crawford, (Drums).  They have one full length out "After the Storm" with Tortuga Records that came out in 2001 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sevenday+Curse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
