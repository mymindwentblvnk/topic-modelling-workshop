Ommani is a progressive metal band from Lille, France. Their music blends elements of deathcore, djent, and mathcore with polyrhythms and a mix of clean and heavy vocals. To date, they have 2 releases; a 4-song ep titled Help Is On The Way in 2006, and a full length titled Apology in 2009. Both are available at https://ommaniband.bandcamp.com For More information, visit: https://www.facebook.com/ommaniband or https://twitter.com/ommaniband
