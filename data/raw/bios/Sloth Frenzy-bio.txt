Sloth Frenzy was a short-lived Heavy Metal band from Tempe, Arizona. They released one album, Slow Murder, in 2000 before disbanding. Band members included: 

Michael Wolff - Guitar
Chris Defelice - Vocals (R.I.P. 09/05/2000)
Dave Dukart - Guitar
Max Wolff - Drums
