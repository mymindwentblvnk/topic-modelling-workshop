JUNGLEPUSSY, born Shayna McHayle, is a rapper and occasional singer from Brooklyn, New York City, USA. She garnered internet attention with her first track, ''Cream Team'' in 2012. Her debut mixtape, ''Satisfaction Guaranteed'' was originally intended to be released in summer 2013. The release was pushed back.
JUNGLEPUSSY released ''Satisfaction Guaranteed'' as her debut album on June 10th, 2014. The album featured previously heard tracks ''Satisfaction Guaranteed'', ''Picky Bitch Checklist'' and ''Curve 'Em'', plus 8 new tracks, including one bonus track.

JUNGLEPUSSY is funny, sexy and often provocative; she freely expresses her sexuality and has been compared to fellow female rap provocateur Lil' Kim. She listens to artists such as Missy Elliott, Ladysaw, Kelis, Busta Rhymes, Vybz Kartel, Movado, The Veronicas, Metric, Gossip & Soulja Boy, but has said that she cannot pinpoint one key influence. ''Artists are human, and they have all existed, so they are all there to learn from and be appreciated and observed.'', she told Interview magazine.
