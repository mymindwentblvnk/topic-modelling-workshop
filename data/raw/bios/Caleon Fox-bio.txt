Caleon Fox is a YouTuber and musician from San Francisco, California. He has gained popularity for his skits on his YouTube channel SenseiLeFox, as well as some of his multiple solo singles, including "No Swag No Swag", "The Durag Song", and his collaborations with fellow Bay Area artist Wade08. Fox released his debut solo EP If Only You Knew on August 3, 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Caleon+Fox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
