Anna Burch is an indie pop/folk musician hailing from Detroit, Michigan. She was signed to Polyvinyl Records in the summer of 2017 after being referred by her future labelmate Fred Thomas. Her debut album "Quit the Curse" is scheduled to be released in February 2018.
