Putrid Skull are a band formed in Arica, Chile in 2005.
They play old school Death Metal a la Repulsion. Other noticeable influences are Suffocation, Carcass, Unleashed, etc.

Line up:
Eduardo L. - Lead guitar
Vicente G. - Guitar, vocals
Rodrigo A. - Bass guitar
Francisco C. - Drums

They recorded a Demo in 2006 and a Live album called "Live in Putrid School", recorded in 2007.
