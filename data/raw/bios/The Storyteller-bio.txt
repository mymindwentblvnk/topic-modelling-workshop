The Storyteller is a Swedish heavy power metal band founded in 1995 by L-G Persson and Fredrik Groth. Its lyrics are often based upon old folk lores.

Members

L-G Persson - vocals
Fredrik Groth - guitar
Martin Hjerpe - drums
Jacob Wennerqvist - guitar
Johan Sohlberg - bass
