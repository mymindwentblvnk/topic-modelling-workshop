Coldsteel is a thrash metal band from Long Island, New York.  They formed in the year 1986.  They currently consist of Troy Norr (vocals), Eddie Campbell (guitars), Fred Canelos (guitars), Rick Lopez (bass) and Hal Aponte (drums).  Coldsteel has released three demos, one full-length album, two EP's, one single, one split and two compilations.  They disbanded in 1993, but they came back in 2012.  They are currently signed to Stormspell Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coldsteel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
