Animi Vultus is a black metal/ambient band formed in 2002 in Russia  (St. Petersburg). This band participated in the Burzum tribute album released in 2006, covering the song "Ea, Lord Of The Depths".

Discography:
Ia Tenebrarum (Demo, 2002)
Angels of Disharmony (Demo, 2003)
Falling (EP, 2004)
And The Six Wolves Ate Mankind (Split, 2007)
