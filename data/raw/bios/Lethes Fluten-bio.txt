Lethes Fluten is a german Progressive Black Metal band hailing from the mighty forests of Trier. 
The band was founded out of the remains of the now disbanded band Broken Messiah. After some changes in the lineup, the band is now recording their first album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lethes+Fluten">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
