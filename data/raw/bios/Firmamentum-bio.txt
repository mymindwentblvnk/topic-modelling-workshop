Progressive Death Metal Band from Montréal, Canada.

Their style was based largely on Gothenburg Melodic Death sound on the 90s. Lyrically, the band were of the Romantic Metal tradition. 

2001-2003

Members:
Rob Breger - Bass
Chris Dancel - Drums
Diego Lobaton - Vocals
Adam Reider - Guitar
