Hung on Horns are a 3-piece progressive rock, post-metal band from London, United Kingdom.  Formed in 2014 from members of Obiat  and The Osiris Club, they released their debut album "Slaves" in 2016. It is available at https://hungonhorns.bandcamp.com.
