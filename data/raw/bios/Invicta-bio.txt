Invicta, Founded on February 14, 2017, are a Melodic/Brutal Thrash Metal Band from Kitchener, Ontario, Canada. Members include: Kyle Edissi - Guitar/Vocals, Jonah Kay - Guitar, Shareef Hassanien - Drums, and Steven Rowlands - Live Bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Invicta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
