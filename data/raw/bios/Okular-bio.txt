Okular is a progressive death metal band from Norway, founded by compositionist Andreas Aubert.

Members:
- Andreas Aubert: Composition & lyrics, backing vocals
- Marius S. Pedersen: Guitars & vocals
- Bjørn Tore Erlandsen: Drums
