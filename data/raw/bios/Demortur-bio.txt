A Death Metal band from Quebec, Canada that was formed in 1990. They released a demo "Oblation" in 1991, which they re-recorded the next year.

Line-ups:
Ben Savard - Bass & Vocals
Dominic Morin - Guitar & Vocals
Pascal Madore - Guitar
