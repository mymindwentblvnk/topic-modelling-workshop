Raw and crusty oldschool Death Metal from Östergötlands län, Sweden. Tormented is a Death Metal project from members of Tortyr and Edge of Sanity.

Current line-up
Andreas "Drette" Axelson - Vocals/Guitar (Incapacity, Marduk, Infestdead, Edge of Sanity, Tortyr)
