Define is a underground hip hop activist/rapper from Philadelphia, PA. With impressive lyrics, a grinding flow, Define shows promise, unique ability and style. From appearing on mixtapes with Tech N9NE and Bone Thugz n Harmony, to ripping almost any emcee who steps on tracks with him, Define has come a long way since he began flowing in 2004, and at the age of 20, he has awhile to go.

Define is also italian Post-Hardcore band from Verona. Current members are:
Nicola Bicego - Vocals 
Filippo Bertoncelli - Guitar/Vocals 
Stefano Bonafini - Guitar 
Federico Accordini - Synth/Vocals 
Damiano Ambrosi - Bass 
Alberto Fontanabona - Drums
