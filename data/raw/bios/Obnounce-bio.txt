Obnounce is a metal band from Slovenia. The band was formed in the middle of autumn 1998 by the musicians that many years played in other bands. The music they wanted to play, had many influences and ideas joined with their common wishes for aggressive and melodic metal. Today is Obnounce known as band that plays extreme & melodic death/black metal with influences of other metal genres. They have released one album called Remnants of Humanity and participated on two slovenian metal compilations so far. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obnounce">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
