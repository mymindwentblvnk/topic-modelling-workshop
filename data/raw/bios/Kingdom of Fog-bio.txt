Kingdom of Fog, was a Raw Black Metal band hailing from Montilla, Andalusia Spain. Formed in 1997 and now has disbanded. The sole member WarHatred did All instruments, Vocals. Kingdom of Fog put out one demo Humanity Eternally Dying in 2004 and a split cd with Misantrok in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KINGDOM+OF+FOG">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
