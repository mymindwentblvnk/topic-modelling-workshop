inc. is a duo formed by brothers Andrew and Daniel Aged based in Los Angeles. Accomplished musicians, they have also recorded and toured with a number of high-profile artists, including Raphael Saadiq, Beck, and Cee Lo Green.

inc. began as Teen Inc. and debuted in 2010 with a self-released 7" single, "Fountains"/"Friends of the Night" That same year, the Aged brothers featured on Nite Jewel's title song for her Am I Real? EP.  After dropping "Teen" from their name, the brothers signed to 4AD, where they released a three-track EP, 3, in 2011. 

Their debut album,"No World", was written, recorded and mixed in the winter of 2011 and released on 19 February 2013.
