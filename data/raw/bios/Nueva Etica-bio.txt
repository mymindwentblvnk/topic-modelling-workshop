Nueva Ética (New Ethic) is a metalcore band formed in Buenos Aires, Argentina in 1998. They follow the vegan straight edge philosophy and are one of the first Latin American hardcore punk bands to tour the world. Named after an Earth Crisis song, Nueva Ética's music has been described as a mix of Hatebreed and Terror. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nueva+Etica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
