A collaboration between the 2 german DJs tom novy and eniac. Together they recorded the club-tracks "Superstar", "Someday > Somehow" & "Pumpin'" between 1997-2000.
"Superstar" was a big club-hit all over Europe in '97. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Novy+vs.+Eniac">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
