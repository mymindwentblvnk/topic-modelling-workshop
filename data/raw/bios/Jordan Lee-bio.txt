Jordan Lee is a young adult/multi-instrumentalist who writes pop songs using whatever instruments are laying around his room.  He currently lives in Austin, Texas and also plays in a rock and roll band named Black Cock.  

He recorded Front Porch in 2007 and Bedroom Rock 2008.

The label Crippled Light has been gracious enough to put out both of these CD's.
