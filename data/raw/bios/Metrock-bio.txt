There are two Romanian bands named Metrock. The first was active in the early 80s and released the album "Castelul de nisip" in 1982. The other was a power/thrash metal band that released the album " A Kind of Hell" in 1994. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metrock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
