There are 2 bands called "Muerte" 

Muerte is a hardcore band from  Mexico City, With influences of goth punk. It's formed by members of Inservibles, Crimen and Ratas del Vaticano. Their demo tape is about to be released by Cintas Pepe this year.

Muerte was founded in 2003 in Warsaw, Poland. As they said, their goal was to create something that each of them would like to listen. According to the lead guitarist of the band, Kala, their sound is hard rock with a touch of Southern kick, but people also assiociate Muerte with thrash metal and stoner rock. Muerte disbanded in 2011 after releasing two EP albums and a music video.
