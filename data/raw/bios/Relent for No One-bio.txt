Relent For No One is a band that has taken huge strides in their music writing since their conception in 1998. Since then they have had their ups and downs due to lineup changes and were out of the local scene for a year and a half which devastated their fan base. Now after their first show back in January 2006 and hours of hard work, they are ready to take over the world one venue at a time with their unique sound that draws from influences that range from progressive metal to death metal. Relent For No One is a band that is passionate and dedicated to their music, and they prove it every time they take the stage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Relent+For+No+One">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
