Schwarzer Tod is a German Black Metal band. 

Current line-up: 
Porcus - Drums (Permafrost (Ger), Blutrache (Ger))
Sabnock - Guitar
Furor - Vocals

Additional notes:
"Schwarzer Tod" is "Black Death" in English; also known as Black Plague or the Bubonic Plague.
