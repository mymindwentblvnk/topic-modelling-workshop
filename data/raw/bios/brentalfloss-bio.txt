brentalfloss is a songwriter, comedian, and nerd culture icon. He first gained notoriety in 2008 when he added lyrics to the title theme from Mega Man 3 and created the "With Lyrics" series on YouTube. He has since become a feature on ScrewAttack.com and grown a Youtube subscriber base of over 90,000. He has been compared to such artists as "Weird Al" Yankovic, Jack Black, and Stephen Lynch, but his most popular work involves video game music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/brentalfloss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
