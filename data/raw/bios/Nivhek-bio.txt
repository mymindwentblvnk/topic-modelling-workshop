Nivhek is Liz Harris. 

In February 2019 she released the album After its own death / Walking in a spiral towards the house. The music was created during and after residencies in the Azores, Portugal and Murmansk, Russia. It also includes work Harris made at her home in Astoria, Oregon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nivhek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
