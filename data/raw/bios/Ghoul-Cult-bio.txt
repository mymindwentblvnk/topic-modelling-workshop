Ghoul-Cult is a Death/Thrash metal band from Asker/Oslo in Norway.
The band was formed in 2001 by  Johnny Tombthrasher.

The current line-up:
Johnny Tombthrasher - Drums and vocals (also plays drums in Faustcoven since 2007)

Live members:
Sergeant Salsten - Bass (Deathhammer, Warfield (Nor))
Marcofago - Guitar
Lund - Guitar
Sadomancer - Drums (Deathhammer, Black Magic (Nor))
