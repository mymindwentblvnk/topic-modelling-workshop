Bound by blood are a metal/hardcore band from staffordshire (UK). They have released 3 CDs Bloodshed Means Nothing 2006, Storm of Swords 2008, 3 Track 2009

They've shared the stage with Terror, Parkway Drive, Full Blown Chaos, Bring Me The Horizon, xBISHOPx, No Turning Back, The Berzerker +many more
