Inside Tragedy are a relatively new Japanese metal band - their first live was in 2004 and currently only have a Demo CD.

The band consists of;
~Kow (Vocals, Guitar)
~Sayako (Keyboard, Vocals)
~Usshi (Guitar)
~Hiroki (Bass)
~K-Ichi (Drums)
