Bad Lizard was a power metal/heavy metal band from Belgium, active in the 1980's. The band formed in Bruges in 1982, and recorded their first single "Hot Girl" the same year. This was followed by a refined effort in the "Killer on the Loose" EP, on Proma-Sound Recordings in 1983. Their best known work is the solid full length "Power of Destruction" released in '85 on European metal stable Roadrunner Records.The last known lineup consisted of: 

Guy de Nolf - Vocals (R.I.P.)
Johan Vandenberghe - Keyboards
Frank Deprest - Lead Guitar
Lou Michot - Bass
Patrick de Troyer - Drums

Former members include:

Peter Sabbe - Vocals
Eddy Termote - Vocals (Run4H)
Patrick Hollevoet - Drums
Erwin - Guitar
Guido Verhaeghe - Bass
