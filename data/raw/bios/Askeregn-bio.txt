There are two bands named Askeregn: a black metal band from Germany and a black metal band from Norway.

Askeregn (GER) is a soloproject of  Varg Belial. It is raw and agressive UGBM from Germany, Thuringia. It was formed in 2008. Belial released the first demo in 2010. Lyrics are about Satanism, Occultism, Nihilism and Nature.
