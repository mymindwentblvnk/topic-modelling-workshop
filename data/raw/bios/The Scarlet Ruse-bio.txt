The Scarlet Ruse is a Death-core band that was formed in mid 2007. They are a six piece band that resembles sounds brought to you from multiple influences and genres. They love playing music and it shows through their song writing. The Scarlet Ruse is currently being managed by Joel Haston of Active Management (same company that manages Terrorhorse, Wretched, Fullujah, and More).
