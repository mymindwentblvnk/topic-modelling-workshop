Pesthauch is a four piece black metal band from Regau, upper Austria who formed in 2003. Still unsigned they brought out an self-released album in 2005. Pesthauch means "breath of pestilence". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pesthauch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
