Side-project of Vladimir Andreev of Откровения Дождя (Revelations of Rain), that mixes death doom metal with a gothic metal atmosphere in the vein of Novembre. One album was released in 2010 before the name was changed to My Indifference to Silence. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/On+the+Edge+of+the+Netherrealm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
