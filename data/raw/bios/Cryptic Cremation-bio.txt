Cryptic Cremation was a death metal band from Quebec, Canada. They released a demo in 1995 and the 'profanation' EP in 1996. Two of the members went on to form Demental.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cryptic+Cremation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
