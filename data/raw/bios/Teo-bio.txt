There are three artists called Teo:

(1) Yuriy Vaschuk (Russian: Юрий Ващук; Belarusian: Юрый Вашчук; born 24 January 1983), better known as Teo (stylised as TEO), is a Belarusian singer that will represent Belarus in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen, with the song "Cheesecake".[1] Teo also participated in the Belarusian national selection for the Eurovision Song Contest 2009 as Yuriy Vaschuk in a duet with singer Anna Blagova.[2]

(2) Teo is a fresh pop/rock/goa-psy/funk band from somewhere around Calea Grivitei, Bucharest.
