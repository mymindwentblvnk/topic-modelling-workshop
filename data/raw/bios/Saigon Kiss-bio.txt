Saigon Kiss are a Norwich based, 4-piece metal band.

Taking a heavy dose of Metal, adding a dash of punk and fueled by low budget horror films they terrorize music venues across the UK. 

In February Saigon Kiss will be releasing their first full length studio album titled 'Death... And Hell Followed' so be prepared!
