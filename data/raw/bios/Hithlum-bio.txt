Californian pagan black metal band formed in 2004. They only came out with one album so far "In the Land of Mordor, Where the Shadows Lie" and lyrically inspired by the Lord of the Rings movies and/or book. Also uses samples of the movies in their music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hithlum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
