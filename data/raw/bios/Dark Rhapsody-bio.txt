Death/Thrash Metal band formed in Kryvyi Rih, Ukraine in 1992. They've released a 1994 demo Funeral Mournful Song after which they've disbanded. After trying themselves in another project the members of the band reunited. The last documented recording of Dark Rhapsody is dated 1997. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Rhapsody">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
