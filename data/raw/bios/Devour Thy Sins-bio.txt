Devour Thy Sins was a five-piece band from Northwest UK, formed from the ashes of past bands in 2006. They are currently disbanded and several members have founded a new band, Ashes of Illusions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Devour+thy+sins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
