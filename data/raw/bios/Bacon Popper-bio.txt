BACON POPPER is a dance project created by the Italian DJs and producers Paola Peroni, Pieradis Rossini & Ottorino Minardi. Their biggest hit was "Free" in 1999, a song based on samples from u2's song "New Year's Day" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bacon+Popper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
