A rolling G-funk trio from Flint, Michigan, Top Authority debuted with Somethin' to Blaze To on Trak Records. The following year brought Rated G, and Ichiban picked up distribution through Wrap for third album Top Authority Uncut (The New Year). ~ John Bush, All Music Guide <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Top+Authority">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
