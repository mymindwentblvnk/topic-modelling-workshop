Refiloe Maele Phoolo (born 16 December 1990 in Mafikeng), also known as Cassper Nyovest, is a South African recording artist and record producer.[1] His debut album Tsholofelo was released on 18 July 2014. He is currently signed to his own independent label and media company, "Family Tree". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cassper+Nyovest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
