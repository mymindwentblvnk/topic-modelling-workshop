The Nibiruan is a five-piece metal chain from Helsinki, Finland. The band consists of guys who are by no means newcomers to the Finnish metal scene, although "The Nibiruan" is a fairly new group. The first EP will be released during February, 2010 and it contains three diverse, groovy and epic songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Nibiruan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
