ILOSTHEBALCONY is a four-piece metal band, originated from Samara, Russia. Having been founded in 2009, the band managed to release their first EP "Balmageddon" in 2013. ILTB are now working on the new material which seems to be sufficiently different from their first EP.   

Band members:

Alexey Kuznetsov - vocals
Alexander Aksenov - guitar
Artyom Monakhov - guitar
Dmitry Boubochkin - bass
