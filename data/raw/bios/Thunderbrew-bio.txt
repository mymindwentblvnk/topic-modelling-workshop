Thunderbrew is a four piece hard rock/heavy metal act from Wilmington, DE.  They have been together since 2004 and released their first album, "Red, White, and Brew" in that year.  They have a sound that has been compared to a cross between Metallica and Lynyrd Skynyrd.

Line up: Daran Amos (lead guitar and vocals), Rick Land (lead guitar), Bob Beird (bass guitar), and Dan Kwasizur(drums).
