Deep Silence is a four-piece alternative rock band from Liepāja, Latvia formed in 2009.

The original lineup :

Gints Gertners-Purgailis - guitar;
Kristaps Žilinskis - guitar;
Kristaps Šēnbergs - bass guitar, vocals;
Kristaps Karps - drums.
