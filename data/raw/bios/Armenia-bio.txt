There are multiple artists named Armenia;
1. Japanese metal/crust band with female vocals; 2. Noise artist from Equador; 3. Band from Colombia; 4. Canadian Symphonic metal artist Armenia Sarkissian. 

1 )  Armenia is a Japanese metal/crust band with gothic rock influences and female vocals, formed in 1997 in Tokyo.
Members:
Mayumi - vocals, 
Shinichiro - guitar, 
Kawachi - bass,
Alabaster - drums.

Discography:
2000 - Prodigal Flowers
2002 - Cocytus 

2 ) NOISE artist from Equador called Leonardo Sabatto who make experimental music since 1994 and he has recorded 90 albums plus 5 ep´s ans several tracks in NOISE compilations.  He pioneered the meso-American noise scene with his own label "Bizarre Audio Arts".  Armenia's style consists of nearly pure harsh noise from Japan 1979 like Merzbow, although he collaborates with many grindcore and crustcore artists on his releases. Many works of him are avaible totally free in Archive.org, Bandcamp or similar sites.

3 )  Armenia es una agrupación colombiana conformada por Juan Antonio Toro (voz y guitarra), Ricardo Laverde (teclados), Juan Simón Ramírez (guitarra), Samuel Huertas (bajo) y Daniel Cardona (batería). El proyecto se oficializó como banda con el sencillo "Violeta" en febrero del 2019. La agrupación se consolidó, con su primer álbum titulado "Armenia", como uno de los proyectos musicales más importantes del año según varios comentarios en medios como El Espectador, Shock, Rolling Stone Colombia, entre otros.
