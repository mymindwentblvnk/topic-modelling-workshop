Visions of the Night from Toronto, Canada was formed in 1999 and has since performed tours in Canada, U.S.A., Australia, Japan and Taiwan. They have 4 releases (1 ep + 3 full lengths), with the newest being on Butchered Records out of Buffalo, NY.

The new album 'Guerrillas within their Midst' features sessions drums by Todd Hansen (THE BERZERKER, ROME, FUC) , a guest vocal appearance by Rob Urbinati (SACRIFICE) and was recorded by Matt Connell (ex-EXHUMED, ex-FUCK THE FACTS).
