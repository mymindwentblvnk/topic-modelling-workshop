There is more then one artist with this name:

1. Ragnor is a black metal band, founded by the Norwegian musician Khorne, and other two colleages during an early morning on the summer of 2005 in Fjellhamar, Norway.

On the next year, the Spanish guitarrist Overlord joined on the band as guitarrist and vocalist.
After a lot of work, they have released their demo “Hat og Nag”.

After some legal problems, the band is going to do a gig around Spain with some very special shows.
