They are a new old school death metal band from Szeged, Hungary.  They formed at unknown point in time.  They are signed to Xtreem Music.  They consist of Disguster (bass), Churchburner (guitars) and Blasphemy (drums, vocals).  Coffinbirth has only released an EP entitled Beneath the Cemetery.
