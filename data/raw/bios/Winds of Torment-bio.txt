Winds Of Torment is a young french Melodic Death Metal band from Saint Etienne. The band formed in 2000.
In 2007 The band released their debut album Delighting In Relentless Ignorance.

Line up:
Xavier - Vocals 
Bertrand - Guitar
Alban - Bass
Jean-François - Drums
Jérôme - Guitar
