December's Cold Winter are a self proclaimed "Intensive Melodic Metal" band from Costa Rica. They released their first full length album in 2004 under the name of Decaying Recollections. 

December's Cold Winter became a reality in early 2000. Despite a praised stage debut, winning the Costarican "Battle of the Bands" on their second gig. December's Cold Winter has shared some headbanging with bands like Dismember, Behemoth, Sepultura, Fear Factory & Death by Stereo. After being signed by Freak World Entertainment and let the "Decaying Recollections" album in hands of Steve Austin for the mix and master process, December's Cold Winter is ready to reach a new level through the Music and Stage Performance that characterizes the band!
