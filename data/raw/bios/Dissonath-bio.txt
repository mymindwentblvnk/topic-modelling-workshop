Dissonath is a Death Metal band from Venezuela. They we're formed in 2006 and have released an EP (Infected World - 2006) and a LP (Destruction Statament - 2007).

Dissonath:
Daniel Haddad - Vocals (Noctis Imperium)
Ellis Martinez - Guitar (Scythe (Ven))
Alejandro Lobo - Guitar (Lignum Crucis)
Luis Malave - Bass (Bloody Insanity (Ven), Murdered)
