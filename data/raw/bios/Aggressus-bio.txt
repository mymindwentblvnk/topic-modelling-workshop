Aggressus are a thrash metal band from Dunfermline, Scotland. Formed around the end of 2007, they originally compromised of Dave Ralston (Guitars and Vocals) Dom Hardy (Drums) and Ryan Gourlay(Bass Guitar) and recording and self releasing a demo in 2008 entitled "Cleansing of the Mind", as well as gigging as regularly as possible around the Central Belt of Scotland.

By 2009, the band had brought in Eddy Morris in as another guitarist, and Callum Cant to replace Ryan on bass duties as well as take over on lead vocals from Dave.

2010 saw the release of the music video for "Arrogance of God", and the departure of Dom, leaving the band to put activity on hold until a suitable replacement is found. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aggressus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
