The core of Molokh was founded in Oktober 2004. After the split of the melodic death metal band "Devastating Art" Martin Arzberger, Wolfgang Pfneisl and Max Reif continued to play together. But songwriting went into a much more heavy direction. In 2005 bassist Alexander Meyer joined the band. With this lineup the band recorded one demo cd in 2006, the full-lenght debut album "Feuertaufe" in 2007, and played some gigs in Austria.

In the year 2008 the lineup of Molokh drifted apart. Max Reif went to Switzerland and Alexander Meyer decided to go to Carinthia.
