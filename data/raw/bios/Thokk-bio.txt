Thokk was an American black metal project with a vampiric theme, the members were The Lord Kaiaphas (ex-Grand Belial's Key, ex-Ancient) and Thegn Damieus The Marauder. Formed in 1994, released one album in 1997 titled Of Rape and Vampirism and disbanded shortly after that.
