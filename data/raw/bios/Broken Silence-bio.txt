At least 3 bands share this name:

1)- Broken Silence is a HipHop band/collective from the United States (Utah). Formed in 2003, they have established themselves in the western US as a "top 100 group to watch". Their most recent album is available to stream and download, entitled "We See Everything". Full Wiki Bio is available on that album page.

2)-Broken Silence was a Canadian New Wave band, mainly formed by Edward Kay and Jim Lysowycz. Released the single "Night Stalking" in 1984 and the track "Part of the machinery" in the "Great Ontario Modern Rock" compilation.
