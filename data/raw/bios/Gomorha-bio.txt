Death metal from Goor, Overijssel, Netherlands. Released a demo in 1989 named "Gomorha Hordes". The band consisted of Jan "Verpus" List (the mastermind behind Exmortes) on the drums and Theo Loomans (long-time member of Asphyx until his death in 15 August 1998 and also part of Evoker, Swazafix, Soulburn and Napalm Deterioration) as vocalist and guitarist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gomorha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
