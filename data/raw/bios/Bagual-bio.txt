1) Bagual is a Desert Rock / Stoner Rock band from Santiago de Chile, formed in 2011.
*2014 I: Viento sur
*2015 II: Viento norte

2) Bagual is a project of Chilean producer Rodrigo Planella, settled in Patagonia. He is been creating for several years a mysterious electronic music, difficult to classify, but that surprises. Their icy sounds, that bring to the memory the old school dub and electro, have been played in different places in the world.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bagual">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
