Fleur East is a London singer, rapper, songwriter and model from London, UK. She participated in the 2014 series of The X Factor UK and finished in 2nd place. She released her debut EP, ''She'', in late 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fleur+East">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
