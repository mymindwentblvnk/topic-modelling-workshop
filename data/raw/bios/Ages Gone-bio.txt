Ages Gone was a Death Metal band from Cologne, Germany, active between 1996 and 2003. They played a unique experimental version of Death-Metal, a style they named "Free-Death".

Members moved on to form the new band Creatures. 

Their latest album is available as a free download from the official Creatures site.
