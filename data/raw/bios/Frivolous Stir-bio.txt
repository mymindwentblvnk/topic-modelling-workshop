Frivolous Stir is a gothic black metal band from Split, Croatia formed in 2002. The bands influences include Cradle of Filth, Dimmu Borgir, Old Man's Child, Behemoth and many similar bands. In 2002 they released their second demo 'Gnostic'.

Members:
Zrinka Maras - vocals
Darko Sipic - vocals
Josip Markovic - keyboards
Gordan Grubisic - guitars
Tomislav Margan - guitars
Ante Kodzoman - bass
