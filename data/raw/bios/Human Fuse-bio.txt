Human Fuse is a metal band from Tennessee. They play many shows around Knoxville and Jefferson City and Morristown.

Andrew Bledsoe - Vocals
Mitchell Trueblood - Guitar
Joe Rowland - Bass Guitar
Ben "Hans" Eller - Guitar
Derek Tingle - Drums and Percussion
