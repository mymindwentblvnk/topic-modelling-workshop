Thrash/death metal from Middle-Finland. 

Founded in 2006 under name Wastage. Shortly name changed to Mindration. Few demos came out in 2008-2009. After that band fell into oblivion.

Founded again in 2013 with new line-up.
Mindration's new "Mindration" promotional EP saw daylight in Sebtember, 2014.
