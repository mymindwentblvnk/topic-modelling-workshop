Rather Be Dead are a six piece band born from the Central Coast of Australia. Formed in 2003, Rather Be Dead have developed their style over time to forge their own sound. Utilising dual vocals, Rather Be Dead plays what could be described as Death-Hardcore. RBD have been performing live since September 2004, playing local shows as well as gigs in Newcastle, Sydney, Scone & Adelaide. Rather Be Dead released their S/T demo June 2005.
