From the Who Killed ET? blog: This is some good thrash metal. I don't know if they have had any sort of punk influences. But they have a punk edge to their sound. This EP was their only release before changing their name to Destroy Babylon. At one point in time, their drummer Spikey drummed for English Dogs when Pinch had left the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ALTERNATIVE+ATTACK">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
