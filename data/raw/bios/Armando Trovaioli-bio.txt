Armando Trovajoli (also "Trovaioli") (September 2, 1917, Rome - 28 February 2013) was an Italian film composer with over 200 credits as composer and/or conductor, many of them jazz scores for low-budget exploitation films. He collaborated with Vittorio De Sica on a number of projects, including one segment of Boccaccio '70.

Trovajoli was the husband of Anna Maria Pierangeli.
