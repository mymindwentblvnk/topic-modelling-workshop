Morgorth is Black Metal band from Croatia formed in  2000 and exploring themes as Satanism and NS. It is a one-man project of person who goes by pseudonym Morgorth. Several demos were released but those were very obscure and only a few close friends and comrades had opportunity to hear Morgorth's art. In 2010. Morgorth decided to do a split with Raw Black Metal band 1389 that plays similar music and that was the first time Morgorth's art became available in the Black Metal underground.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morgorth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
