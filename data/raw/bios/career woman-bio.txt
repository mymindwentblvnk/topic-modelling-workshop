career woman is the project of 18 year old Los Angeles native Melody Caudill. Melody describes her songs as public diary entries for the world to interpret. She is influenced by Snail Mail, Samia, Soccer Mommy, Cavetown, Phoebe Bridgers and Beach Bunny. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/career+woman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
