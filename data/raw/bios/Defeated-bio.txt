There are two artists by this name.

1) Defeated is a doom/sludge one-man-band from oulu, Finland. 
The man behind this project is a member of the hardcore bands Brutopia and Tuhkaus, and is also behind the one-man-band called Epätasavalta. 

2) Defeated is a black metal band from Vyškov, Czech Republic.


Discography:
*Confession of Murderer, 2007 
*Znovu Živí, 2008
*V Bahnech, 2010
