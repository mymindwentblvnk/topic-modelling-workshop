Forcedown was founded in september 2002 and the band-members are from Gmünd/Austria and Ceske Velenice/Czech Republique.

It is very hard to describe their style or to put them into a certain category but Forcedown’s style is a mix of several different musical influences (e.g. Hardcore, Metalcore, Swedish Melodic Metal). 

Forcedown performed more than 40 gigs(in Austria and the Czech Republic , Slovakia ) with bands like Madsen(D), Hypnos(Cz), Flowers for the whores(D), Skwor(Cz), Satisfucktion, Locomotive,….in the last 2 years.
