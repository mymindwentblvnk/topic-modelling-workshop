"Formed late-autumn 2000 when jimibudah (Philadelphia) sent pieces of audio (wave files) to Shok (Hollywood) via aim.

The end result would be a dozen songs which have had an online life.  The music never made it into mass ears, yet has seen positive reviews in traditional print and online.

jimibudah may still reside in Philadelphia and Shok continues to write and record in Los Angeles as well as having his own two bands, Zeitmahl and Red Light District" - J.
