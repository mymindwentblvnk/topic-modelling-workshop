Marawa is one-man thrash/power metal project from Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. Senad Marawa (ex-member of Bombarder, D. Trone and RapidFire) started this project around 2006. Released one full length in 2009 called "Gate to the next world". Senad played drums, guitars and bass on his first solo material.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marawa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
