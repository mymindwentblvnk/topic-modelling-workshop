Doom/Death Metal/Noise band with members of Esoterica and Krieg from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA, founded in 2003 as Coffin, but renamed to Lithotome in 2011.
They released their self-titled debut in 2013. Current members are Alex Poole playing all instruments and N. Imperial on vocals.
