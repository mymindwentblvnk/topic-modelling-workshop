Jagannath is from Netherlands (Nijverdal, Overijssel) formed in 1985 playing Thrash Metal. They released only 2 demos in 1988 and 1989. Jagannath is the name of the god of universe, the mighty of the mighties, on the Indian mythology. This name was translated to english to Juggernaut.
The band did an exclusive reunion gig in 2005, on the Raise Your Fist Festival in Holland.
