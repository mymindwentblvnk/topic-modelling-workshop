22 year old producer from The Netherlands. He only started producing music early 2014. His name is a combination from Theo Parrish and Alex Omar Smith whom have always inspired him. He draws his inspiration from labels as L.I.E.S, Minimal Wave and Finders Keepers. He feels experiment is more important than a regular 4/4 rhythm.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parrish+Smith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
