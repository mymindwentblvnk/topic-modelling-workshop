Masteroid was a stoner doom metal band from Helsinki Finland formed in 2008. Masteroid was Tommi Ojansivu/vocals, Tero Pantsar/guitar, Janne Vornanen/bass, Ville Ristolainen/drums. The band disbanded shortly after the release of their first full-length studio album "MMXIII" in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Masteroid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
