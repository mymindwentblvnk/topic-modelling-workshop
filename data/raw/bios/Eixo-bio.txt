Eixo is an American songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and producer born in Brazil. For him, the emotion in the song is everything.
Influenced by the sonic innovations of modern pioneers such as Phantogram, Halsey, Miike Snow, Lana Del Rey and Lorde, Eixo follows the trend of producers like Major Lazer , David Guetta, Zedd and Calvin Harris, teaming up with different singers to realize his musical vision.
