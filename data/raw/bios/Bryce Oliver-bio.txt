San Bernardino native, Bryce Oliver, is an independent American singer, songwriter and rapper who started off by making voice recordings on his phone and taking them into the studio for live recordings. One of those recordings inspired his debut single “Situations” which gained him recognition following the release of his first mixtape “You Owe Me” in 2016. 
 
Since Bryce’s first release, Bryce opened for Jaquees and Kevin Gates in June 2016, has been recognized by Spotify on The United States Viral 50 (#39), Fresh Finds and Release Radar, and  released his latest single “26th Floor” which gained press coverage from HotNewHipHop, Sway’s Universe and an interview with Dash Radio on the XXL station.
