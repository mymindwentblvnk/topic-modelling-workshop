Raaz is a Bollywood film released in February 2002. Directed by Vikram Bhatt staring Dino Morea and Bipasha Basu. It is a romantic thriller and has a beautiful soundtrack. Music is by Nadeem Shravan, Lyrics by Sameer. It has a variety of playback singers including Udit Narayan, Alka Yagnik, Abhjeet, Sarika Kapoor, Jolly Mukherjee, Bali Brahmbhatt and Suzzan. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raaz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
