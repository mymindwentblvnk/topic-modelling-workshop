Parasitic Eve is a Chinese melodic death metal band from Kunming, Yunnan Province. The band's Chinese name is "寄生前夜". They release a debut EP in 1st December, 2013, that named "没有立场的生命毫无意义". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parasitic+Eve">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
