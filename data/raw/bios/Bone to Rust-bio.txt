BONE TO RUST were formed in august 2006 by Mikebass(bass-vocals) and Alex(quitar)!a couple of months later,Stavros(drums) joined the band. After 5-6 months of rehearsals the band arranged some gigs in Athens. In september 2007 Bone To Rust had their first demo cd recorded.It contains 4 songs(WAR,SEEKIN' SALVATION,MEATBALL,MARKED FROM EMPTY WORDS).It is distributed FOR FREE.Anyone who might want it,just e-mail the band and ask for it! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bone+To+Rust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
