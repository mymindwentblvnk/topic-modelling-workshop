OMAAF is a Progressive Death/Grind band from Birmingham, Alabama. They have been together since 2007. In 2012 they self released an EP entitled "Ethnospheric Decay" which was recorded, mixed and mastered at Mana Studios by Brian Elliott. They have recently recorded a full length "Born In a World Where They Still Fear Gods" also at Mana Studios with Brian Elliot. OMAAF have played several national live shows in the area with the likes of Dillinger Escape Plan, Suicide Silence, The Faceless ,Veil of Maya, As Blood Runs Black, Animals as Leaders, and Conquering Dystopia just to name a few. Flowing through death, doom, progressive, and black metal, sometimes within a single song. OMAAF has something for any metalhead!
