Thyresis is a Brazilian Death Metal band formed in 2006 by Josué de Queiroz (V), Danilo Rufino (G), Andrei Targino (G) and Victor Hugo Targino (B).
Later on, Andrei Targino left and joined Soturnus, João Pachá (Guitars, Rest in Disgrace) took the job and Demetrius Pedrosa (Drums, Metacrose) joined the band to release the first demo, Journey Beyond Infinity, in early 2008. After several shows that y...ear, Josue left the band and bassist Victor Hugo took his place as singer.

Now, in 2011, with Eduardo Borsero replacing João, the band is set to release its debut album, self-titled, produced by the band's bassist and singer Victor Hugo, and mastered by the experient and renowned producer Jens Bogren (Opeth, Katatonia, Paradise Lost, Amon Amarth). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thyresis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
