MX Machine was a crossover / trash band from Boston, Massachusetts in the late 80's. Manic Panic, their only album was released in 1987 on Restless/Enigma Records.

Band members: 

Mitch Rellas: Guitar 
Lee Kaiser: Vocals 
Diego Negrete: Bass
Liquid Dan (Danny Anaya): Drums
