Nilfgaard is a metal band from Bydgoszcz, Poland, formed in 2007. Their music is a mixture of heavy and melodic playing. They released their first EP in 2011. Their debut album "Enter Fiction" was released on the 6th  of January, 2014, and it is available for free download.

Line up:

Adrian - guitars
Konrad - guitars
