OVERPAST is an industrial band from Kiev, Ukraine. Formed from INMOST alternative doom band they lost solo-guitarist, bassist and vocalist. The new band changed its style to industrial rock combining live and sampled percussions, keys, strong guitar riffs and violin parties. In fall of 2002 drummer has left the band.
In 2003 OVERPAST recorded their demo-album "Half-Life".
