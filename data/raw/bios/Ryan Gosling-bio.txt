Ryan Gosling (born November 12,1980 in London, Ontario, Canada) mostly known for his acting in indie and studio movies, has deep roots in song. 

Gosling hasn't released much, but often performs at jazz clubs. He did background vocals on the Canadian group soulDecisions 2000 release, still most of what the public has are clips from the movie Wild Roomies (2004) in which he created much of the soundtrack. 

His current project is a vocalist/guitarist for the band Dead Man's Bones alongside vocalist/guitarist Zach Shields.
