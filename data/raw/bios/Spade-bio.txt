There more than one artists named Spade.

1) Spade Is Elmar Ivatarov (born 1984), a Russian born psy-trance producer now based in Israel.  
He was a member of Vibe Tribe along with Stas Marniansky for their successful first album Melodrama.  Elmar left around the time of their second album to pursue a solo career.  He is currently signed to Israeli label Utopia Records and is working on his debut album as well as frequently collaborating with other full-on artists such as Vibe Tribe, Brain Damage & Freedom Fighters.
