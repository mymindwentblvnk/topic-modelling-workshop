Early contributors to the eco-spiritual style of black metal in and among the Cascadian region. Recorded and self-released a tour edition of By Blood And By Earth in 2004, though an official pressing was never released.

Members also appear in Fauna, Echtra, Fearthainne, Alethes, and Sadhaka. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Threnos">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
