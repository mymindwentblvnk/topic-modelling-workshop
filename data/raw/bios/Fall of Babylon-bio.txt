Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania's FALL OF BABYLON is a three-piece death metal / deathcore band officially formed in 2021 by Tad Logero (Guitarist/Vocalist), Evan Weston (Bass), and Logan Cussan (Lead Vocals). Their debut single “Reduced to Ash” is on all streaming platforms. Album coming early 2023. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fall+of+Babylon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
