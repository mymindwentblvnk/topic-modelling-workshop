Satan's Massacre was a heavy metal / black metal band from New York, New York.  The band formed in 1986.  Their last known lineup consisted of King Evil (vocals) and Black Legion (guitars).  Their lyrical themes include rape, Satan and evil.  They only recorded two demos.  A demo in 1986 (not too much is known about it) and Robbing the Graveyard and Raping the Dead.  There are no bass and drums on Robbing the Graveyard and Raping the Dead.  It is not known if the band ever recorded any bass or drums on the first demo.  They disbanded at an unknown period of time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satan%27s+Massacre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
