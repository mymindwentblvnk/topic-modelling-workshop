French heavy-metal band formed in Paris between 1981 and 1986. Blaspheme reformed for one concert in July, 1997 in Bars, France. They also reunited in 2007 in order to play at Paris Metal France Festival II (13 january 2008). They then decided to go on and enter the studio to record a new album, "Briser le Silence".
