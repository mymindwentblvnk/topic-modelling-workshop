Formed in 2011 in Dublin, Ireland, The band was originally founded as 'Akraga' but was renamed to 'Atominated' during the mid 2011 month's.
The band have released one Homemade demo entitled 'World Without Flesh Demo' and week's after it's release and rise in popularity, the band hit the studio and re-recorded 'World Without Flesh' which was released worldwide Via Slaney Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atominated">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
