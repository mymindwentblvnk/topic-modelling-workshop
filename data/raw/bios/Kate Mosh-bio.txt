There are two artists using this name:

1) Kate Mosh formed in Berlin in 1999. The first LP (Flash Gulash, 2001) was a self production. Some years later in 2004 they released their debut album "Life Is Funfair" and went on tour around Europe. An EP followed in 2005 titled "4 A.M. And It's Already Hell". The next Album "Breakfast Epiphanies" saw the light of day in April 2006 and they went on tour again, this time across Germany.
