There are 4 known artists with the name Disabuse:

1. Disabuse was a one-off crust punk / d-beat project that featured members of Disrupt and Extreme Noise Terror. Their only release was a self-titled CD / 12'' EP, put out by Vinyl Japan in 1994.

2. Disabuse was a short-lived crustgrind band from Halifax, Canada that eventually became Existench.  

3. Disabuse was a Dutch hardcore punk band
