Michael Da Vinci (real name Robert Douglas) is a rapper from Chattanooga, Tennessee, best known as a member of the collective TheHouse, alongside YGTUT, ChrisP., Brian Brown and Isaiah Rashad. He formerly went under the stage name Rob December, and a few years later in 2014, would be mainly known for his appearance on Isaiah Rashad's critically acclaimed EP Cilvia Demo, on the song "Brad Jordan". Since then, Da Vinci had released a wide variety of singles including "Get Money", "Foes", "Priorities" and "High Face, Baby Face".

On January 29, 2021, Michael Da Vinci released his debut project Roses, a collaboration with producer/rapper Lege Kale.
