Scott Bradlee's Postmodern Jukebox is a rotating music collective founded by arranger and pianist Scott Bradlee in [yearfounded]2011[/yearfounded] in New York City, New York, USA. They are known for reworking popular modern music into different vintage genres, especially swing and jazz.

Several former American Idol finalists have found success as part of the ensemble, including Blake Lewis (season 6), Melinda Doolittle (season 6), Haley Reinhart (season 10), Casey Abrams (Season 10), and DeAndre Brackensick (season 11). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scott+BradLee%27s+Postmodern+Jukebox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
