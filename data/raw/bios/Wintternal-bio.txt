A Progressive / Melodic Death Metal from Madeira, Portugal, known for their melancholic riffs and chorus.

Released in 2013 their first EP called "The Monologue" which brings you through the monologue of a guy who is left behind by his own self, leading his life to destruction. Available for free download at http://wintternal.bandcamp.com/album/the-monologue

Members: 
António Santos - Lead Vocals / Rhythm Guitar
Cláudia Nascimento - Drums
Pedro Rodrigues - Lead Guitar
