UNDERHER is a sensual electronica/melancholic act created by @kaldenbess , already in the techno circuit since 2009 as ‘Kalden Bess’.
Now among the most successful Tunisian and Canadian electronic acts over the the last couple of years. Having Released with artist such as Moby, Books Shade, U2, Maya Jane Coles and many others. The sound that defines UNDERHER, a mixture of sensuality, melancholia and sorrow has easily reached and touched many types of public. Supporting live bands such as has SOHN, HVOB, Haelos.

This passion of making music not only made for the dance floor saw fruits when Netflix starting to pick up the music. Now with over 7 tracks on 6 differents TV Shows. Khaled Bess is creating a new path for himself in the Hollywood world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Underher">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
