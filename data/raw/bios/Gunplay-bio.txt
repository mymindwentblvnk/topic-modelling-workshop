Richard Morales, Jr. (born July 18, 1979), better known by his stage name Gunplay, is an American hip hop artist from Carol City, Florida, USA. He is signed to Def Jam and Maybach Music Group. 

He was a member of the Southern hip hop group Triple C's, which was founded by Rick Ross. He has contributed to three compilation albums (with Maybach Music Group), and has released eight solo mixtapes, his first being Sniffahill: First Gram  in 2008. His debut studio album Living Legend is scheduled for release in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gunplay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
