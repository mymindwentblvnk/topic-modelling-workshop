Face of Agony is thrash metal band from Púchov, Slovakia. Many of the band's songs focus on politics, war, and the military. To these days they have released four albums. Mostly influenced by bands from San Francisco Bay Area. Last stuff  was "Hunting season opened " in 2009. 

The line-up: 
Viktor Mareček - Vocal
Martin Kucej - Guitar
Martin Lajcha - Guitar
Marcel Chleban - Bassguitar, Vocal
