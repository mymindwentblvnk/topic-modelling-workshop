Explosive Diarrhea was a Grindcore/Death Metal from Houston, Texas. Formed in 2004 they released the first full-length on 2004 called "Shits in the Shadows" and an EP in 2005 called "Morbid Molestations". The band currently is disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Explosive+Diarrhea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
