Mondo Loops is a Guitar Player and Producer from the North West [England] specializing in warm cinematic Lofi Hip Hop beats. Known for his organic and cinematic style, he brings foley and found sound textures to his production in a unique and infectious way. Releasing with many labels in the Lofi scene, most notably, Lofi Records [Lofi Girl], Universal Music, Sleep Tales, Stereofox & many more.
