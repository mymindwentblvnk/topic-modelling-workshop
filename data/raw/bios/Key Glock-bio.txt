Markeyvius LaShun Cathey, better known by his stage name Key Glock, is an American rapper and songwriter. A protégé of Memphis rapper Young Dolph, he rose to fame in 2017 with his mixtape Glock Season. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Key+Glock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
