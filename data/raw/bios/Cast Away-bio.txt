Cast Away is a German heavy metal band formed in Heppenheim, Hesse in 2003.

Discography:

Promo 2004  Demo, 2004
Cast Away  Full-length, 2005
Mind Domination  Full-length, 2008

Cast Away could also refer to a 3-piece hardcore anddeath metal band. Formed in the year 2008 inHouston, Tx. Consisting of Cameron on vocals and guitar, Chad on drums, and Drake on bass.
