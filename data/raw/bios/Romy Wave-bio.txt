Switzerland (1992 - present)

Romy Wave is a Swiss singer-songwriter. She started with covering songs in 2011 on Youtube, but also has her own songs. In 2016 Rolling Tide was her first single being released on Spotify and in 2017 her first solo album, Brave, was released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Romy+Wave">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
