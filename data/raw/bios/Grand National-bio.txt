Grand National is an indie band that consists of London duo Rupert Lyddon and Lawrence 'La' Rudd. Taking influence from their previous band, a Police/Sting covers outfit, Grand National manage to mix eighties pop, nineties dance, indie, electro and ska into fresh, experimental pop songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grand+National">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
