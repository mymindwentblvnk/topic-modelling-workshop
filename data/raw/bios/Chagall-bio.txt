Chagall is an Amsterdam-born, London-based electronic music producer, songwriter, singer, performer and early adopter of the mi.mu gloves. 
Since the release of her Stray Flux EP (2015) she has been playing all over Europe showcasing her audiovisual live set incorporating gestural technology, reactive visuals and choreography.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chagall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
