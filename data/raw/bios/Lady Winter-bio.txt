Symphonic gothic metal band from Russia, formed in 1998. The band consists of Olga "Winter" (vocals), Nikolay "Fa Dey" Fadeev (bass), Oleg "Boroda" (guitar), Alexandr "Light - Darkness" Starov (keyboards), Dimitriy Akopov (guitar) and Alexey "Irish" (drums). They released their first demo in January 2000, called "Ты Увидишь Свет...", and in the year of 2007 released their first full-track album "This Rain Can't Be Forever".Today the band itself describes their style as melodic-gothic metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lady+Winter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
