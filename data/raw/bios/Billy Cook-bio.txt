Billy Cook is an American R&B singer, songwriter and record producer from Houston, Texas. As well as his own releases, he's also known for providing vocals for tracks by fellow Texan rappers such as Bun B, Z-Ro, Chamillionaire and Lil Keke. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Cook">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
