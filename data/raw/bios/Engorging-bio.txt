Engorging is a Death Metal band originating from Samarinda, Indonesia, a long journey on the underground scene, formed in 1997, keeping the feel of pure death metal, Engorging had experienced periods of changing the band name and mutually exchange personnel, Engorging previously called Sarcasm.  And Engorging now have 8 songs including: Dendam Kebencian Menghitam, Santapan Hewan Pemakan Bangkai, Apocalypse Slaughter, Bloody of Massacre, Kaki Tangan Neraka, The Evil, Keterunan Iblis, Terlahir Untuk Menjadi Liar. This year, Engorging ready to release his first album in titled Apocalypse Slaughter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Engorging">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
