Tenowar is a Black Metal band from Hungary. They formed in 2006 releasing two demos and one album. 

"The Darkness Of A Hungarian Forest" is the name of their album and is an epic, obscure album. 

The band writes about Darkness, Winter, Nature and Medieval times all infused to a Hungarian point of view.
