Jullian Nott is a British film composer. He is most known for his composition of the theme for the "Wallace and Gromit" shorts & motion picture, as well as scoring them. He graduated from the Film & Television School of the UK in 1990.

In addition to his music, he has also directed a number of short films, along with a full-length comedy, entitled "Weak at Denise." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Julian+Nott">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
