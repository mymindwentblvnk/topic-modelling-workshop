Annie Hayden was one of four members of the 90's indie rock band Spent. In the year 2000, she released her first solo disc, The Rub. Her sophomore solo release followed in 2005, also released on Merge Records, titled The Enemy of Love. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Annie+Hayden">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
