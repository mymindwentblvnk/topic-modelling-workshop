Mega NRG Man is a eurobeat vocalist.  His real name is Tomas Marin, and he works for the eurobeat labels A-Beat C and Go Go's Music.  Most of Mega NRG Man's music is fast and aggressive, with additional electric guitars (sometimes done by guests, such as Kiko Loureiro in the song "Rock Me").

Other eurobeat aliases of Tomas Marin include Derreck Simons, Mr. Groove, and Mike Skanner.  As a hyper techno vocalist, he uses the alias "DJ Zorro". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mega+NRG+Man">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
