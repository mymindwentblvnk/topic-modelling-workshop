In the summer of 2005 Plastic Gods was concieved in the shadows of Reykjavík. Dagur and Ingólfur, the founding members, were at first planning a stoner/sludge project but as time passed, things slowed down considerably and Doom was added to the mix. .. 

Members: 

Dagur - Bass 
Ingó - Vocals 
Gummi - Guitars 
Finnbogi - Guitars 
Hössi - Drums

They released their debut album in december 2008, entitled "Quadriplegiac".
