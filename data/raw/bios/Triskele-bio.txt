There are four bands named Triskele, one is black metal from Canada, another is an Estonian folk group, another is an NSBM group from Scotland, and the last is an American jungle producer.

The Estonian Triskele started its activities in 1997 with the aim to perform Estonian folk music, concentrating on ancient runesongs and folk hymns. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Triskele">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
