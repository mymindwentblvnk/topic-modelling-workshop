Ekser was a Death Metal band from Phoenix, Arizona. Ekser released one full-length album, Patterns of Reprisal, in 2007. Their last known lineup included:

Nick Radlovic - Guitar, Vocals
Kyle Brandstatter - Lead/Rhythm Guitar
