Bloodkrieg is a melodic death metal w/ folk influences band from Walnut Creek, CA. Formed by the remaining members of Mizeria after they split in July 2009.

In January 2010, the band was resurrected from an indefinite hiatus as a one-man Warcraft themed melodeath/power metal band.
