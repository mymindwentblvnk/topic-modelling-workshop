Marcos Cabral is an American electronic music producer, half of the production duo called Runaway. Marcos has released records on L.I.E.S., DFA, Rekids, Mule Musiq, Endless Flight, Permanent Vacation, Wurst, I'm a Cliche, Chinatown, Aus Music, Crue-L, and his co-owned label On The Prowl.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marcos+Cabral">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
