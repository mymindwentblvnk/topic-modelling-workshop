Las Vegas based thrash metal band active in the late 80's-early 90's. They released two demos, Double Standards in 1989 and Legends in 1990. Vocalist Gary Guerin was known for his over the top vocal style inspired by original Exodus vocalist Paul Baloff.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/O.T.+Factor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
