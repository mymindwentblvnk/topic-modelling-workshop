Witchclan is a Black Metal band from the UK. The band formed in 1990, released 2 demos in 1993 and split up in 1995. In 2009 the band was resurrected and a new demo was released in 2010. Come early 2011 the band had signed to UK label Darkness Shade Records for a debut album. A promo tape for the album was released in April 2011 and the debut album entitled 'Misanthropist' is due for release on October 31st 2011. Witchclan play Bestial Hell Metal exclusively and are recommended for fans of early Bathory, Darkthrone, Darkened Nocturn Slaughtercult and Burzum. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Witchclan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
