Dutch death metal trio who released the Praising Death demo in the Summer of 1986.  Their vocalist & guitarist, Patrick Harreman & Boudewijn Molendijk, went on to form the thrash metal band Usurper in 1987. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sepulchral+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
