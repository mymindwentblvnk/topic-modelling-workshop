Early in 2006 Aron and Samuel thought that there wasn't enough heavy music in the world so they started a band and started to try and make the heaviest music they could think of.
Naturally this is a pretty hard task without a basist so we started to look around and eventually found Rolle, and now we are all part of Sonic Stone trying to make heavy riffs and making people bang their heads to what we call music.
