GATT is a 3-piece Grind/Crust from Jakarta, Indonesia. They play dark, heavy grindcore-punk like discharge or dystopia meets old school grindcore like Napalm Death or Unholy Grave with lyrics mainly focus on political and social issues. Expect nothing but raw, in your face, old school grindcore with fast-paced power chords, D-beat and loads of blazing tempos.Grind As Fukk...!!!
