MORTAL RATION was Thrash/Death Metal band from Velká nad Veličkou, Czech Republic formed in 1995

Members:
Testy - vocals
Kurry - guitars
Petr Bila (Billy-q) - drums (Sad Harmony)
Slacha - backing vocals
Kocar - guitars, bass
