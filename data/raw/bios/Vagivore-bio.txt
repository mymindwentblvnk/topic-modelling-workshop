Vagivore started in January of 2009 as a 5 piece deathcore band hailing from Bloomington, IL. They have shared the stage with several national acts such as: The Tony Danza Tap Dance Extravaganza, Misery Index, Rose Funeral, Born of Osiris, Attila, Destroy the Runner, Blind Witness, I Am Abomination, Harp and Lyre, Catalepsy, Woe of Tyrants, Psyopus, War of Ages, Wrench in the Works, A Hope for Home, The Destro, Suicide Silence, Bury Your Dead, After the Burial, Cattle Decapitation, Spineshank, A Life Once Lost, Thy Will Be Done, Oceano, Broadcast The Nightmare, This Or The Apocalypse, xLiferuinerx, A Plea For Purging, etc. Vagivore still continues to play a large number of shows, tour, and keep searching for a serious label to back their music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vagivore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
