Svartedauen was a norwegian punk band from Rognan, Norway. The band existed between 1979 and 1980. During their day they played concerts together with bands like Mitti Skritti and Hjertesvikt A/S. As a result of their somewhat controversial lyrics they made some fuzz in the local press. The band did a one off reunion at the UFFA house in Trondheim on september the 29th 2007.
