Sumeru are a 5-piece psychedelic, stoner rock/metal band from Sydney, Australia. It features members of Blkout, Lomera, Firearms, Shake Your Blood and No Apologies. To date they have two releases; a 4-song self titled ep in 2013 and a full length titled Holy Lands in 2014. Both releases are available at http://sumeru.bandcamp.com/ For more information, visit: https://www.facebook.com/mountsumeru

Members:
Matt Power - Vocals
Peter Bursky - Guitar
Chris Wilson - Guitar
Reg Barber - Bass
