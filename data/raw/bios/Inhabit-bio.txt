There's more than one band/artist named Inhabit:

1) Inhabit is a one-man brutal death metal band from Michigan, USA. They released their debut album "Pray for Nothing" in 2011.

2) Inhabit was a thrash/groove metal from Texas, USA. Released "Feed the Lie" (EP, 1996) and "If These Scars Could Speak" (Full Length, 2001).

3) In Habit band was formed in 2004 in Miami. They have released two EPs since then. You can read more about them at www.inhabitmusic.net.
