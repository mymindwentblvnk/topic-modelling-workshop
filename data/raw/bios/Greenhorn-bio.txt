Greenhorn is a Doom Metal outfit from the UK, who have two self-released EPs: The Plague Doctor's Mask (2013) and Doom Hawk (2014).  Outstanding riffage.  Third Wave Ska fans should look elsewhere.

GREENHORN is a studio project from Roger Lima (Less Than Jake/Rehasher) and fellow Gainesville, Florida musician Jennifer Vito (NoMore). In July 2014 they released their debut 6 track EP 'I WANT MORE' on Anchorless Records (US) and Saint November Records (UK).

GREENHORN Is also a Columbus, Ohio band of the late 80s/early 90s, country tunes played with a grunge style. Lead singer Dan Spurgeon later formed, and still performs in, The Bush League All Stars. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GREENHORN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
