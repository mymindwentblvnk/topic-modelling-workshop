There are three artists with the name King Felix.

1)1980s progressive/art-rock band from Shreveport, Louisiana. The band was formed by Geoff Walden, Keith Rowley, Wade Ozment and Mitchell Player. Their album “Rivers & Veins” was released in 1987. 

2) Los Angeles based music group formed in early 2012. King Felix creates a variety of  different musical styles including, EDM, electornica, Glitch, progressive/house/rock, and DEM (Dark Electronic Metal). http://www.beatport.com/artist/king-felix/255903 
http://www.youtube.com/kingfelixmusic 

3) Dance music oriented alias of Laurel Halo. Her debut under this name “Spring EP” was released in April 2012 on Mute’s new Liberation Technologies imprint. http://www.laurelhalo.com/
