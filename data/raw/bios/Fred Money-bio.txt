The younger brother of fellow Harlem rapper J.R. Writer, Writer's Block/The Diplomats own Fred Money watched his elder Brother J.R. Writer gain recognition in the underground New York rap scene.This influenced Fred Money, at the young age of 16, to begin writing and performing music as well. Since that time, he has received numerous guest features on his brother's projects. He soon signed to independent label Babygrande Records and geared up for his first solo album, which was released June 2009, titled 'Money Rules Tha World'. It contains features from many of the Diplomats and Writer's Block members. He has maintained a steady presence within the Writer's Block and Thunderbird movements with his prolific lyrical skills, appearing on over 70 mixtapes so far in his career. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fred+Money">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
