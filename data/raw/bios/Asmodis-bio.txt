Asmodis is a Thrash Metal band from Hamburg , Germany.

The band formed in 1985 by Slaughter Ed Crowley (Drums) and Oscar Juan Le Rocque (Guitars) and, after a number of demos, they released one album in 1993 called Fahr zur Hölle Pfleischmütze.

They reformed in 2009 and played some Gigs in late 2009.
