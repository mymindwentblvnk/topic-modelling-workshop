Depraved Murder is brutal death metal band from Pare, Kediri-East Java, Indonesia, founded in mid-2011.
They have always had and maintained one point since their inception, which was to play original music that mixed every particular death, violence, gore and more rapid and forceful rhythm down tuned section and a distinctive sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Depraved+Murder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
