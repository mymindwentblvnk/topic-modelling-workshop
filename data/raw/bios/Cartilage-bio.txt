There are multiple artists with this name:

1. Cartilage was an early Death Metal band from Finland that formed in  the city of  Vaasa  in 1991. The band has released 2 demos and a split LP with Swedish Death Metal band Altar.

2. Cartilage  was a Death Metal band from France. They  formed in 2000 and released one demo since departing.

3. Cartilage  was a hc-punk band from Winterthur, Switzerland.  They made several albums and became well-known at the local music scene for football songs and provocative lyrics.

4. Cartilage self-styled 'Glasgow filth merchants'. Percussion and bass duo. Released tape/download/stream Lung Butter EP on 28 March 2019.

5. Cartilage  is a US deathgrind band from San Francisco, California. They released their demo It's Necrotic in 2015, and debut LP Dialect of the Dead in 2017.
