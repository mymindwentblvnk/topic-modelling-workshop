Celebratum is a Norwegian black metal band formed in 1996.  

Current line-up:
Wharrl - Guitar
Ole "Vargon" Nordsve - Drums (The Embraced, Cor Scorpii, Legiones, Hellstorm (Nor))
Nefast - Guitar
Scython - Vocal (Previous Guitar)

Former/past members:
Vocals: Romul
Guitar: Morchyon
Bass:Telal, Vlad Tepes
Drums: Stig 

Discography:
Carpe Noctum - Demo, 1999  
Spirits - Demo, 2000  
Mirrored Revelation - Full-length, 2001  
Instinct - Full-length, 2005
