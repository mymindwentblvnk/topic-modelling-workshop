Lords of Decadence is a melodic death metal band from Austria formed in 2000.
The current line-up includes Norbert Leitner - Guitar and Vocals, Andreas Tuma - Bass and Vocals, Bernd Kladler - Guitar, Paul Tikal - Drums <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lords+Of+Decadence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
