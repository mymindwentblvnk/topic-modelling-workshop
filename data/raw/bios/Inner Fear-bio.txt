There are multiple artists named Inner Fear: 1) Czech gothic black metal band. 2) Chilean heavy metal band. 3) German thrash metal band. 4) Greek thrash metal band. 5) Dutch death metal metal. 6) American thrash metal band. 7) American nu metal band.

1) Inner Fear is an extreme gothic metal / symphonic black metal band formed in 1997 in Brno, Czech Republic. Members include Anghlelina (vocals), Khopec (guitars), Paraghus (bass), Marthus aka Martin Å karoupka (drums).
Released four full-length albums, "Akhu" (2000), "Thanalogy" (2002), "Symbiotry" (2003) and "First Born Fear" (2012). 

2) Inner Fear was a heavy metal band from Santiago, Chile, active between 1999 and 2003.

3) Inner Fear was a thrash metal band from Werne, Germany, active between 1992 and 1999.

4) Inner Fear was a thrash metal band from Athens, Greece, formed in 1990.

5) Inner Fear was a death metal band from Bruchterveld, Netherlands, formed in 1993.

6) Inner Fear was a thrash metal band from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, United States, active between 1990 and 1992.
