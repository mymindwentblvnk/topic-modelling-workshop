Chicago, Illinois Heavy Metal act.
The solitary HATRIK album, 1985's 'The Beast' released by Roadrunner Records,
sees a cover of the GRAND FUNK RAILROAD hit 'We're An American Band'.
In early 1988 HATRIK resurfaced under the new guize of TECHNOKILL.
