Nether Regions is a metal/sludge/psych band from Portland, Oregon formed from members of Portland's Ditchliquor. Current members include Joe Wickstrom (bass, vocals), Todd Pidcock (guitar), Colin Stout (guitar), and Noah Baxter (drums). The band released their debut album Into the Breach in 2011 and are currently recording their follow up for a 2013 release date. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nether+Regions">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
