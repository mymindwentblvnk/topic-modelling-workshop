From Rohnert Park, CA

Death's Eminence began playing together at Chris Carlin's house mid-August of 2006. Which started as trio that consisted of Pat Baxter: vocals/lyrics, Jesse Pinske: drums, and Chris Carlin: guitar/composition. Near the end of 2006 Tyler Williams A.K.A. Scot joined the band as a second guitar player and began writing as well. Chris and Tyler played in Jagermonster together which ended a year earlier, Pat and Jesse were and still are in their original band DBGC. The band remained a four piece ...
