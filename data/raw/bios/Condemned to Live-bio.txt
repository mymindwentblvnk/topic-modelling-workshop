Formed in 2007. Based out of San Jose, California (Bay Area) (well that's where the drummer's kit is!) Condemned to Live is a 4 piece Death metal band.
CTL blends Death Metal, Thrash, Black Metal, and some Grindcore into a brand of its own. CTL has various opinions on Nihilism, Agnosticism, Atheism, Regicide, Suicide, Deicide, - if there is an ism or a cide, CTL has something to say.

CTL is available to play arenas, clubs, bars, and even at your neighbor's barmitzvah.
