Sword of Judgement is a four-piece metal band formed in Tacoma, Washington.

They consist of:

Taylor Enloe Guitar / Vocals
Kenny Bender Bass
Jack Darms Guitar
Jeremiah Gandy Drums

According to their Myspace page, their sound, "takes influence from many veteran metal acts such as, Megadeth, Iron Maiden, Testament, Forbidden, Heathen, Stormwarrior, and Helloween. To create a blend of Power, Thrash, and Classic Metal to ring a style of music to Seattle that has remained docile since the mid 80's." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sword+of+Judgement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
