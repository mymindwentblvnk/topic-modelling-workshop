Ohvrikivi is a Pagan Black Metal/Dark Ambient band hailing from Estonia. There is only one member of this band, Berg. They released a Demo entitled "Forgotten Screams" in 2000, and an EP with the same name in 2006. Their latest release is a split album with the band Bladesmith called "The Tenebrous Journey." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ohvrikivi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
