Infernal Pussy is an all-female trash metal band from Cali, Colombia.
The band was founded back in 2010. They have released one demo so far called “Asesinas de la Noche”

The current line-up (August 2011) consists of:

* Momia Dark – vocals
* Ms Thrasher – guitar
* Vikinga – drums
