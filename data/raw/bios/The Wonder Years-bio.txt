The Wonder Years is an American pop punk band from Lansdale, Pennsylvania, United States that formed in June 2005. Since their conception, they have released six full-length albums, two EPs, and several splits/compilations. The group is currently signed to Hopeless Records. Their name originates from a paper that lead vocalist, Dan 'Soupy' Campbell, read written by his after-school education teacher titled "The Wonder Years"

Current members

    Matt Brasch – guitar, backing vocals
    Dan "Soupy" Campbell – lead vocals, acoustic guitar
    Casey Cavaliere – guitar, backing vocals
    Mike Kennedy – drums, percussion
    Josh Martin – bass guitar, backing vocals
    Nick Steinborn – guitars, keyboards, piano, backing vocals
