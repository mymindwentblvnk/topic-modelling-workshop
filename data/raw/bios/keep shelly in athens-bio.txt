Keep Shelly in Athens are an electronic / downtempo duo from Athens, Greece. Their debut 12" vinyl EP, "In Love With Dusk" released in 2010 and quickly sold-out.  It was followed up with a 7" single "Hauntin' Me" and another 12" vinyl EP "Our Own Dream".

The original singer was Sarah P., but she left the band in 2014. The most recent singer on board is Jessica Bell, who has also released music under her own name, as well as the moniker BRUNO. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Keep+Shelly+In+Athens">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
