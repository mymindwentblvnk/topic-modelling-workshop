Ant Clemons is an American singer and songwriter from Willingboro, New Jersey. After moving to Los Angeles in 2017 in pursuit of a career in music, he found himself couch-surfing and writing songs in exchange for somewhere to stay at night. A year later, Ant gained the public's attention for his guest feature on Kanye West's '"All Mine" alongside Ty$. Shortly afterwards Ant signed his publishing deal with Universal Music Publishing Group, and has since had releases with such artists as Beyonce, Jay Z, Chance The Rapper, Childish Gambino, G Eazy and many more. In 2019 Ant signed a distribution deal with Human Re Sources and began releasing his debut solo music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ant+Clemons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
