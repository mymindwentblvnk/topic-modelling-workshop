Maegi is the power-prog metal project of Oganalp Canatan. Powered by Steinberg Media, debut album "Skies Fall" features very important names from the metal scene: Hansi Kürsch (Blind Guardian), Chris Boltendahl (Grave Digger), Tim Ripper Owens (Judas Priest, Iced Earth, Yngwie Malmsteen), Zak Stevens (Savatage, Trans Siberian Orchestra, Circle II Circle) and Jerry Outlaw (Jon Oliva's Pain).

Oganalp Canatan is the singer of Dreamtone and Dreamtone & Iris Mavraki's Neverland.

With Dreamtone and D&IM's Neverland, he has released 7 singles and albums, also collaborating with metal world's famous people such as Hansi Kürsch (Blind Guardian), Jon Oliva (Savatage, JOP, Trans Siberian Orchestra), Tom Englund (Evergrey), Mike Baker (Shadow Gallery), Gary Wehrkamp (Shadow Gallery), Edu Falaschi (Angra), Urban breed (Tad Morose).
