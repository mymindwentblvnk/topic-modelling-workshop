Sapata​ is a Finnish progressive heavy doom metal band based in Tampere, Finland.

Formed in 2014, the group has released two full length albums; ​"Satanibator​" (2017), "No Sun To Embrace" (2021) and the EP​ "Reflections" ​(2018). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/sapata">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
