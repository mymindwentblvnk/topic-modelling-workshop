J.U.S.T.I.C.E. (which stands for Just Undeniably Some of The Illest Composers Ever) League is an American record production trio of producers and instrumentalists from Tampa, Florida. The group consists of Erik "Rook" Ortiz, Kevin "Colione" Crowe and Kenny "Barto" Bartolomei. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/J.U.S.T.I.C.E+League">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
