Norwegian death/thrash-metal outfit Slogstorm founded in 2003, and recorded their first demo in 2004 followed by a series of demos in the wake of their first. They are currently scheduled to record their first full length album in the winter of 2007/2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slogstorm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
