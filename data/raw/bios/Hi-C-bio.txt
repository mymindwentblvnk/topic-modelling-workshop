Hi-C is a recording artist and producer from Nashville, Tennessee. He was formerly a member of Diamondsonmydick's collective Reptilian Club Boyz from 2016 to 2021. His debut album, No More Heroes, Vol. 2, was released in July 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hi-C">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
