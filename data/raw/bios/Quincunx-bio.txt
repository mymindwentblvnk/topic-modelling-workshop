There are multiple artists under the name Quincunx:

1. Quincunx is an electronic musician who has been creating music in various genres since 1993, but currently not making music.  His main genres tend to be noise, ambient and lo-fi/old-school electronica, but he has ventured into quiet soundscapes, grinding cacophony, acoustic tracks, and complex sequenced multitrack recordings as well.  He has completed an Album-a-Day twice (http://crapart.spacebar.org/aad/).  He has also recorded under the monikers Meister G, Calculator Heist and Contraholiday.
http://moonatnoon.com/music.html

2. Quincunx is a black metal/crust/grind band signed to Colloquial Sound Recordings, releasing the Quincunx S/T cassette in late march of 2013 as CSR026.
