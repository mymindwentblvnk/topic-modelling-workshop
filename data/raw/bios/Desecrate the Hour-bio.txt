Desecrate the hour was formed early in 2004 with Nick Shabatura,  Brian Gilbert, Bryan Gonzalas, and Don Sweeney. playing shows all over the local scene the drew a following playing shows with many headline bands. eventually Bryan quit to pursue college at columbia university. while looking for a new rythme guitarist the band did not play shows for about two years. they found their  bassist  Nole Schuyler and have changed their name  to CHAPTERS after not playing shows for so long they decided that a new start would be needed. feel free to follow them at www.myspace.com/desecratethefuckinghour  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desecrate+The+Hour">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
