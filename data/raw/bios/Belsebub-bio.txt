Belsebub was a death metal band from Motala, Sweden.  They formed in the year 1989 and disbanded at an unknown point in time. They were one of the first death metal bands to use dual vocals.  They only released a couple of demos and one EP before they disbanded.  Their last lineup consisted of Mica Savimäki (vocals, bass), Peter Blomberg (guitars) and Jonny Fagerström (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Belsebub">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
