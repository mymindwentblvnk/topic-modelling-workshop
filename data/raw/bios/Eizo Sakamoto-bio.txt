坂本英三 (hiragana: さかもとえいぞう/romaji: Eizo Sakamoto) One of the most famous metal singers in Japan. He headed to sucess with the heavy metal band Anthem in 80's and since 2001 when Naoto Shibata invited him back to group, later with Animetal (in 90's) ultil now. He also participated on Jam Project and started his solo career in 2000 with "Metal Icchokusen" album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eizo+Sakamoto">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
