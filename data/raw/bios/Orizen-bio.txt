Orizen is a  pagan black metal band from Switzerland and was formed in 2005 by Richard Leishman.

In 2009 a first self produced CD The Path into Revelation was released. Until now the band has been a one man project of Richard Leishman, but Orizen is currently being formed to a complete group.
