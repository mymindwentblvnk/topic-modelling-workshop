Nini Music is a folk-fusion artist from Taiwan. She found considerable fame live-streaming on Reddit covers of videogame, film, and rock music using Chinese traditional instruments such as the San Xian, Ruan, and Liuqin. She currently has one EP of original music, Rise Up, and is producing a second focusing on Chinese history and Daoism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nini+music">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
