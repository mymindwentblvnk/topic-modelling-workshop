Evil Shepherd is a Belgian Thrash Metal band.

The band was formed in 2008,plays live regularly, and has released two demos.In 2010 they released a full length, 'Sowing Death',that received great reviews,for instance in Terrorizer mag and in Rock Tribune mag.
By adding a new drummer and a 2nd guitarist to play the leads,new songs are made,trying to give them more diversity but always keepin' the thrash feeling.To check new songs,or more, go to the Myspace+Facebook+Reverbnation+Twitter profile of the band.
You can also see us E.S. live on youtube...check the video's "evil shepherd @ t-klub","breaking the oath+priesthunt" and others.   
The plan is to record a second full album in february 2012.
The current line-up is: Jonah(leadvocals),Maes(killerriff creator,backingvocals),Lemmes(leadguitar),Tommy(thunderbass)
