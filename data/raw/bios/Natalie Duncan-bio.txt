Natalie Duncan is an English soul singer/songwriter from Nottingham, signed to Verve Records. She was featured on BBC2's Goldie's Band: By Royal Appointment, and released her debut EP in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Natalie+Duncan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
