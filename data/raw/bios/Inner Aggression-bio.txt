Inner Aggression is a German Melodic Black Metal with Thrash influences. Now evolved  as a Melodic Black/Death Metal band.

Their music consists of melodies in contrast with heavily paced drums, creative dark keyboard works and harsh screams and growls.

Band formed in: 2004
Current line up:
Sebastian Panzer : Vocals
Fabian Panzer : Guitar
Stephan Schafferhans : Lead Guitar
Christian Simmerl : Bass
