Glorytellers are a mostly-acoustic indie-rock outfit formed around singer/songwriter/guitarist Geoff Farina (Karate/Secret Stars) in 2005. They released their self-titled debut album on the Southern label in 2008 and most recently their third record, 'Current Resident', in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Glorytellers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
