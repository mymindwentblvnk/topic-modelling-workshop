Formed in December 2012 when Nick and Christos met drummer Alexandros. After a few jam sessions, Pavlos was added to the band. Combining their love for classic 70s rock and classic heavy metal bands like Iron Maiden, the band wrote 5 songs, that were recorded during the months of May to July 2013. The EP titled Amrak was released in September 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
