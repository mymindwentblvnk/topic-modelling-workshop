PATTON is an instrumental hard rock band from Pittsburgh. After discovering a mutual fascination with WWII hero Gen. George Patton, the band set out to dedicate their stoner blues to our General's hubris, bravado, and bravery. Quotes from the biopic film are ensconced within.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Patton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
