Beneath the Flesh is a death metal band from Bartlett, Illinios, originally formed in 2004. They have one full length album out called "Desecration" and several shirts, which can be purchased from http://www.myspace.com/beneaththeflesh7. The current line up is Mark Smedbron (vocals), Matt Ashley (Bass, Backing Vocals), Mark Harrison (Guitar, Backing Vocals), and Huvi Chagoya (Drums). Influences include Megadeth and Pantera. They've played with many big names such as God Forbid, Testament, Jungle Rot, Macabre, Over Kill, Vader, Abigail Williams, Lividity, Devildriver, and Napalm Death.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beneath+the+Flesh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
