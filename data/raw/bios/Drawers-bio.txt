Drawers is a 5-piece stoner, sludge, metal band from Toulouse, France. To date, they have 3 releases, their debut full length titled "All is One" in 2011, a split with Hangman's Chair in 2012, and a self-titled full length in 2014. All releases are available at https://drawerskvlt.bandcamp.com. For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/drawerskvlt. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drawers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
