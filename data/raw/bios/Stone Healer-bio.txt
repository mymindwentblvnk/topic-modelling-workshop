Stone Healer is a one man progressive, death metal project from the mind of Dave Kaminsky of Mansfield, Connecticut. He released his debut album titled "He Who Rides Immolated Horses" in 2015. It is available at https://stonehealer.bandcamp.com[/link]  For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/stonehealermusic <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stone+Healer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
