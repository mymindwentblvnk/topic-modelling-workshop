There are three bands named Echoes of Silence; 1.) Is an Italian gothic rock band, 2.) a symphonic black metal solo project and 3.) Dark Ambient, Dungeon Synth one man project by Pillar from Morocco

Echoes of Silence is a experimental symphonic black metal solo project that was created in 1999 after Leviticai left Dark Divinity. The first demo "Lust For The Grave" was handed out at metal shows. The band was first signed in 2003 by Anno Satannas Records. Many releases came to be throughtout the years featuring many session musicians as well.

Leviticai has worked with musicians like Lord Vampyr and Stefan Traunmueller (Golden Dawn) on his latter releases to achieve a experimental yet symphonic sound. Latest of which will be "The Pale Enchantress" which will define the band's dark sound and eerie melodies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Echoes+of+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
