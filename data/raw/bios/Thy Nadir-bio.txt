Thy Nadir is an atmospheric and melodic black metal band from Tokyo, Japan.  The band has only one member, who is also in Shoutin' Corpse and Deathscythe.  In 2008, Thy Nadir released a split with Blasphemous Legion called J.B.M.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thy+Nadir">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
