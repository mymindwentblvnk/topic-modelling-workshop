Formicator are a Black Metal band, formed in London, England mid 2010. Their line up consists of;

Vogkt - Vocals
Herewulf - Guitars
Vargrimst - Guitars
Heretic - Bass
Icaeros - Drums

The band originally started as a small two piece project between Vogkt and Herewulf, who recorded the only currently available tracks themselves before getting a full line up. These tracks can be found at www.myspace.com/formicatormetal
