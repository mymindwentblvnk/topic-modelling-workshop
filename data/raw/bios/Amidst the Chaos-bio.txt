Amidst The Chaos is a metalcore band from Missoula, MT. They were formed in November of 2011. They have already played many shows across Montana, including one with known artists, Memphis May Fire and Born of Osiris. They have also played with Righteous Vendetta, A Past Unknown, Spoken and others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amidst+The+Chaos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
