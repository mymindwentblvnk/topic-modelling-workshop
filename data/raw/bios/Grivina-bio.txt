Grivina is the stage name of Grivina Daria Romanovna (born July 22, 2000 , Abakan , Russia ). She is a Russian singer working in the genres of deep house and abstract hip-hop . Her most famous song is Я хочу. Grivina has a team that includes songwriter and music producer Denis Gaziev (the main songwriter) and concert director Farhad Veisov. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grivina">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
