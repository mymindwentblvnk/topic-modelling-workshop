Hollow Hills is a Blackened Doom Metal band that formed in the winter of 2014 in Yarramalong Australia, Jed Berry the founder of Hollow Hills had the band name in his head ever since 2010, always waiting for the perfect genre to suit the name, After hearing bands such as Woods Of Ypres and Type O Negative he knew the path that Hollow Hills would take. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hollow+Hills">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
