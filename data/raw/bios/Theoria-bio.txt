Theoria is an Atmospheric Black Metal band formed in 2012 by Ahmad and Besher in the Aleppo, Syria.
Influenced by the likes of Darkspace, Chaos Moon, Sun of the Blind, Lunar Aurora, Blut Aus Nord.
The band's debut album is available for streaming on bandcamp:
