Voreaphilia are a two piece goregrind/mincegore band from st. john's, newfoundland featuring Eric Johnson on Guitar/Vocals and Ryan Collins on Drums that pull influences from every end of the extreme music world. The band started in July 2006 and broke up in January 2007, playing only 8 shows and a few parties. Later in early 2008 they re-united, with friend Adam Sharr on Bass/Backing vocals and played shows for another year or so, again calling it quits in early 2009.
