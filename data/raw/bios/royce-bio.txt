ROYCE are the new team up of Nicolas Masson, label and event manager of PALM OFF, and June One from Glen Check. Together they produce Nu Disco and organise monthly parties at CAKESHOP. We met the two super nice gentlemen on a warm Indian summer evening in a small café in Hongdae.
