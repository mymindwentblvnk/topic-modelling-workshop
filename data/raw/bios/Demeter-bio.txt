Demeter is an intimate, acoustic/electric guitar folk duo from Slovenia, consisting of Miha Zbašnik and Jure Engelsberger. It has "grown from the ruins of the Slovenian punk band Racija", of which both Zbašnik and Engelsberger were previously members. Their first album is titled Uvod v strastno (Introduction to Passionate). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demeter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
