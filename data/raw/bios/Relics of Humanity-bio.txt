A brutal death metal band from Minsk, Belarus.  They formed in the year 2007.  Relics of Humanity currently consists of Flo Butcher (vocals), Sergey Liakh (guitars), Pavel Lapkovskiy (bass) and Vladislav Vorozhtsov (drums).  They write songs about ungodliness, cataclysms and anti-religion.  They have released three demos, two full-length albums, two singles, one compilation, one EP and one video.  They are signed to Willowtip Records.
