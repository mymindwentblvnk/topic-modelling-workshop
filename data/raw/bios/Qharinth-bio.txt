Qharinth is a symphonic black metal band which appeared around 2004 in Kedah, Malaysia. Currently, the quintet consists of vocalist-bassist Rator Flames (also in Narrator and Azariath), guitarist Fuad Ghastly (also in Ephemeral and Ghastly of Gills,  drummer Ej Hadeas (also Azariath, Narrator, and Bravery), and keyboardist Mat Stygian (also in Nefarious Azarak and Angelixia). Now they are signed to Crambeat Records which released their first EP
