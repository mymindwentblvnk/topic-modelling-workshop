In Memorium (1997 - 2010) is a black metal band from Seattle, Washington USA. Members are or have been in other bands such as Lord Gore, Fall of the Bastards, Inquinok, Menacer, Stahlmantel and Fallen Angels.

Releases:
An Ancient Word EP on Moribund Records in 1999
From Misery... Comes Darkness full length self-released in 2003 and rereleased in 2005 by the Moribund Cult.

Current lineup:
Nihilist - Vocals
Michael Krutsinger - Guitar
Steve Fournier - Drums
Rob Steinway - Guitar
Eric Close - Bass
