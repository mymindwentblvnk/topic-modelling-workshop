Eating Machine was a grindcore band from Florida. It was composed of only Phil Taylor and released a split tape with Godstomper then got a drummer named Bill Crate (who now plays in White Guilt and Blunt Guts) and released a 7". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/EATING+MACHINE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
