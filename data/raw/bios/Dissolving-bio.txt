DISSOLVING, formerly known as ANTERIOR DAWN had their stage-debut in late 2008. One year after, looking back upon many ups and a few downs, the quintet has finally found its future path. The new name together with the upcoming Demo 09, which features almost all the tracks that were played live so far, will be the band a base for the musical progression that took form over the last few months. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dissolving">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
