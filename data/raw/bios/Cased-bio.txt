There are multiple artists called Cased.

1. Cased were a death/black metal band from Denmark. Formed in 1992, the following year they released their only recording, a promo tape called Promo 1993, featuring two tracks: Resusciated from the Christians and Crusade .

2. Cased are an emo band from Chicago, USA, formed around 1998. Their first album was recorded and produced by Kerry Brown in Chicago at Chicago Trax. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cased">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
