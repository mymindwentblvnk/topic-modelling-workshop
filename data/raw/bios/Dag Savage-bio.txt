Dag Savage is hip-hop duo from Southern California, which consists of Johaz of Deep Rooted and Exile of Emanon.

Dag Savage first premiered on Exile's mixtape Intro to the Outro, which was released in 2011. Dag Savage released a mixtape titled Salvation on November 19, 2012. Dag Savage is scheduled to release their debut EP on January 15, 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dag+Savage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
