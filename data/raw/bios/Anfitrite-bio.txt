Symphonic pagan metal band from Venezuela. The band received the beautiful name of Anfitrite (in spanish) in honor of the Greek goddess of calm sea Amphitrite, his lyrics are inspired by mythology (especially ancient Greece) and the cult expressed by women in ancient pagan cultures.
