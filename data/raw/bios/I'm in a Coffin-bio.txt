American depressive/suicidal black metal consisting of Members Adorable and Sad-Ist. Their music has the power to actually physically unsettle people and genuinely affect the mood of the listener.

The band went silent after 2008 when they released their debut album One Final Action until a demo tape surfaced in 2012 sent out to record labels showcasing more recent material that included the songs "Suicide Eyes" and "Too Fargone to See Any Light" 

As of October of 2014, the band has confirmed that they are working on a new full-length album to be titled "One Last Final Action". The band has started a brand new Facebook page announcing their return, and have also made a Bandcamp page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/I%27m+in+a+Coffin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
