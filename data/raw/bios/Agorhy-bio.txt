grindcore band from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, started by three young musicians in 1997. Unlike most local bands of the genre, they sing mostly in Portuguese and use to speak about: social issues, politics, anti-war, against prejudices, etc. They have recorded two albums plus an ep.
