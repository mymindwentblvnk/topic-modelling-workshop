KeKe Wyatt (born Ketara Shavon Wyatt, March 10, 1982) is an American R&B recording artist from Indianapolis, Indiana. She gained popularity after a highly successful collaboration with R&B singer Avant on his platinum album, My Thoughts. This led to her debut Certified Gold album Soul Sista in 2001, followed by a promising solo career with MCA Records. Currently signed with Shanachie Entertainment, she recently released her fourth and latest album, Who Knew? on Feb 23, 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KeKe+Wyatt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
