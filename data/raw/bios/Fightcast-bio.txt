Fightcast is a metalcore band from Cesena, Italy, formed in 2004.  Musically, the band has a diversified sound, regularly utilizing elements of traditional metal, alternative metal, and melodic death metal, as well as the breakdowns and low thuds of nowadays metalcore.  Screaming, growling and clean vocals are seen in the bands material. 

Discography:
Killer Goodfellas Family (2004, self-produced) 
Breeding A Divinity (2008, Kolony Records)

Members:
Filippo Tellerini - Vocals
Stefano Casadei - Guitar
Francesco Vicini - Bass
Alberto Casadei - Guitar
