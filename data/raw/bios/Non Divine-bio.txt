Non Divine was a German black/death metal band from Dortmund, North Rhine-Westphalia who released a demo in 1996 "Darkened World" and probably nothing else. Some of the members play nowadays in Lifeless. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Non+Divine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
