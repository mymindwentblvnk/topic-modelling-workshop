Krlja was founded in April, 2009 in Samobor, Croatia. With influences from its band members styles and many bands worldwide, they formed their own variety of death/grind/crust metal. Their first album is called "Grind World Order", available for order from the band's MySpace. And their second album "Psalms of  the eater god"  which is also available on their bandcamp as the first album.

Line up:
Mitja - drums
Metod - guitar/back vocals
Nikola - vocals
Ivan - bass
