Ukrainian melodic death metal band formed by the merger of the two bands of Kiev in febrary 2012. First band played a brutal death, which included Max Frolov (vocals), Roman Korotkov (drums), Andrey Loboda (guitar), Igor (guitar). Second band played melodic death, which included Max Frolov (vocals), Andrey Loboda (guitar), Vitaly Dmitrienko (guitar), and Alex Redchenko (bass), without drummer .

Cantabile Wind is : 
Max Frolov - vocals 
Andrey Loboda - guitar 
Vitaly Dmitrienko - guitar 
Alex Redchenko - bass guitar 
Roman Korotkov - drums
