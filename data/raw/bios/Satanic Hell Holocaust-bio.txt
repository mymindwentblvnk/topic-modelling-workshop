Satanic Hell Holocaust is a raw black metal / death metal one man band that is from São Carlos, São Paulo, Brazil.  The project started in 2015.  Satanic Hell Holocaust is not signed to a label.  Lyrical themes include Satanism, war, death and desecration.  So far, the project has released two EPs and two singles independently.  Final Holocaust, War (Single) and You Will Be Gutted were released in 2015.  Stamped In The Crucifix (Single) was released in 2016.
