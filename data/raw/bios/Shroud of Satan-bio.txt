Shroud of Satan is black metal band from Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, Germany.  They formed in 2012.  They consist of B (vocals, drums), S (guitars, keyboards) and M (bass).  They write songs about misanthropy.  They have released one EP, two full-length albums and two splits.  They are signed to Sol Records.
