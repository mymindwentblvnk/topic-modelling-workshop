Alrakis is a one-man atmospheric black metal/ambient band from Berlin, Germany. 'A1V' started the project in 2007, his debut album, "Alpha Eri", was released in 2011.

The album "Omega Cen" is not an official release. It consists of 8 unreleased tracks which have been stolen and uploaded on the internet by someone. Many of the tracks appear on "Alpha Eri".
