Norwegian Amateur Metal Band from Kyrksæterøra. Currently inactive, awaiting ressurection. Recorded a demo in the summer of 2006: Liquid Metal. 
Lineup as per summer 2006: J. Strandheim, Guitars & vocals; B. Lomundal, guitars, lead vocals; V. Enge, drums; P. Graff, Bass, vocals; T. Strandheim, Keyboards.
