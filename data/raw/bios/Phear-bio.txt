Phear is a progressive group of individuals from Dhelshins, Iowa.  They dabble in creating "audible experiences" that leave the listeners a quivering pile of terror.  The only way for someone to understand the sheer horror that these tracks bestow upon their listeners is to simply put in your headphones, turn up the volume, and begin to feel absolute fear that you can not comprehend. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
