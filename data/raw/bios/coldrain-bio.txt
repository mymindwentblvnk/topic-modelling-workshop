coldrain (コールドレイン) is a Japanese post-hardcore band from Nagoya, Japan, formed in 2007. The band consists of five members, two of which are half American.
The band's musical style is often described as a mixture of post-hardcore and metalcore.
However, some songs feature elements from alternative metal,  alternative rock, nu metal and even pop punk.
The band's biggest influences are Linkin Park, Incubus, and Papa Roach.

In 2009, the song "8AM" was used as the ending for the anime series "Hajime no Ippo: The Fighting! New Challenger". 
In 2010, the song "We're not alone" was used as the opening theme for the anime series "RAINBOW: Nisha Rokubou no Shichinin".
In 2017, the song "FEED THE FIRE" was used as the opening theme for the anime series "Ōsama Game The Animation".
In 2018, the song "Revolution" was used as the main theme of the game "Mobile Suit Gundam: Extreme Vs 2".
In 2019, the song "MAYDAY" featuring Ryo Kinoshita of Crystal Lake was used in the popular anime series "Fire Force"  (En'en no Shōbōtai), which made a huge impact on the band's recognition.
Later in 2019, the song "Elevator" was featured on the "ROTTENGRAFFTY Tribute Album ~MOUSE TRAP~".
In 2022, the song "Bloody Power Fame" was used as the opening theme for the Netflix anime series "BASTARD!! Ankoku no Hakaishin".
