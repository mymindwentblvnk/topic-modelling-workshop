A Finnish band calling their music 'space sludge', because of the psychedelic effects used on bass guitars, and occasionally synth. Phenylketonuria stands for 'an inherited form of mental retardation due to a disorder of amino-acid metabolism'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phenylketonuria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
