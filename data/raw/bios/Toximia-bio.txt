Toximia was a thrash metal band from New York, USA, formed in 1985.  They released one full-length album, War, in 1987, which was a follow-up to their 1986 demo Another Beautiful Day.  The band officially broke up in 1989. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toximia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
