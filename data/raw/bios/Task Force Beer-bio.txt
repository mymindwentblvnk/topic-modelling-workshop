Task Force Beer (aka T.F.B). is from Trier, Germany.  They started on November 9, 2015.  They combine death metal, grindcore and groove metal.  The band consists of guitarists Oos & Pinch, bassist Matze, drummer Kring and vocalists Daniel & Mario. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Task+Force+Beer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
