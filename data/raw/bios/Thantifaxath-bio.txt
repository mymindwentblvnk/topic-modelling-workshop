Thantifaxath are a black metal band based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The members wish to remain anonymous. They released their self-titled EP "Thantifaxath" in 2011. Their debut album "Sacred White Noise" was released by Dark Descent Records in 2014. Their second EP "Void Masquerading as Matter" was released in November 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thantifaxath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
