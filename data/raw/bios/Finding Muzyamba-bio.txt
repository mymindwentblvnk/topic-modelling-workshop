Finding Muzyamba (Real name: Joseph Muzyamba) is a male man from Norway whose genre-bending approach draws from a range of styles, from synth-pop and trip hop to electronica, contemporary hip-hop and R&B. 

In June 2013, Finding Muzyamba released his first official recording, an album titled "The Experience", crafted by himself, CRONIKL and PVCIFY. In the autumn of 2014 he got his demo "Clusters" premiered on Noisey by Vice.

Muzyamba is planning to release his second album, "Dusk Till Dawn", in 2015.
