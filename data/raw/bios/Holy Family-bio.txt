Holy Family is the duo of ROOM40 label operatives Lawrence English and John Chantler coming together for some bass heavy twin modular synth attack - ELEH lineage tonal stasis, chaotic bomb clusters and bristling distortion.

Their first release came out as a ltd C25 on Digitalis in early 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Holy+Family">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
