Margenta is a project by heavy metal poetess Margarita Pushkina. Margenta songs are based around Pushkina lyrics and performed by various guest artists. Arthur Berkut, Valery Kipelov, Sergey Terentyev (all formerly from Ария), Kirill Nemolyaev and many other notable Russian metal musicians participated in it. Composers included Alik Granovsky (Мастер), Vitaly Dubinin (Ария) and Gennady Matveyev.

Since 2007, Margenta released a series of albums Dinastia Posveshonnyh (Dynasty of the Consecrated) with lyrics based on medieval history. The main composer on this albums is Sergey Skripnikov. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Margenta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
