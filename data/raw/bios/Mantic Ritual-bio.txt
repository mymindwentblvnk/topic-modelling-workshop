Mantic Ritual (formerly known as Meltdown) is a thrash metal band from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. They released a full-length called Executioner under the name Meltdown in 2007.  In 2008, they signed Nuclear Blast Records and changed their name to Mantic Ritual, to avoid confusion with other bands using the name.  They disbanded in 2012, returned in 2013 and then disbanded again.  They came back again in 2020.  They officially announced their return on their official Facebook page and they also announced working on a second album.  Visit their official Facebook page for more on Mantic RItual.
