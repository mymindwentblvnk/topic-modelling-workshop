Tox was a german Melodic Heavy Metal band from Stuttgart in the early 80s. They've released two albums: Prince of Darkness (1985) and Tox (1986). 

Members:
Werner Dannemann (Vocals, Guitars)
Wolfgang Kallert (Bass)
Peter Garattoni (Drums)
