Elektrum have a unique style of music, fusing together many styles to create a totally original sound which has a wide appeal. Claus is known worldwide as a composer & guitarist. Hurdy Gurdy & Secret Oyster are just two of his great cult bands. Ian Macdonald was with The Ginger Baker Group, Furniture, Separate Energy to name but a few. Elektrum are said to be one of the hardest working bands on the circuit and their live performances are breathtaking.
