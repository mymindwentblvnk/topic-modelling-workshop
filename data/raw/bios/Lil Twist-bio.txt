Christopher Moore (born in Dallas, Texas), better known by the stage name Lil Twist, is a rapper and singer signed to Lil Wayne's Young Money Entertainment. He is also a member of the rap duo Young Hot Boyz with Lil Chuckee. He has released one mixtape, 2009's The Yearbook. 
Since then, Twist has been working on his upcoming mixtape Wake Up. In August of 2013 he released his single featuring Chris Brown titled  I Don't Care. Also, he released Twerk featuring Miley Cyrus and Justin Bieber. During a ustream he play the intro to another single titled  UFO.
