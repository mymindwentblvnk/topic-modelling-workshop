Hymn Above Traumatic Emotion is a doujin metalcore/melodic death metal circle formed in Japan by KUHLE (lyrics, arrange, mixing, mastering) and tac-t!s (artwork, lyrics) in 2012. They released their first EP, The Opening of HATE in 2013 at  the M3-2013 Spring even the single TRAUMA XIV was released at the M3-2013 Fall event. A second EP, Mors Certa Hora Incerta and a split with オレオレウサギ, Never Odd or Even was released at the M3-2014 Spring event.
