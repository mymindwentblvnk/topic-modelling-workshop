Yancey Boys is a hip-hop duo from Detroit that consists of Illa J and  Frank Nitt. Their debut album, Sunset Blvd., was released on October 29, 2013 via Delicious Vinyl, and is entirely produced by J Dilla. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yancey+Boys">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
