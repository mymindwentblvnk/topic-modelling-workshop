Fractal Generator is a death metal side project featuring 3 members of Wolven Ancestry. Lyrics focus on science, theory, infinite concept and mathematics. The Cannibalism of Objects is the first demo originally recorded in 2008, and finally digitally released through Archaic North Entertainment in August 2011.
