Hip-Hop Artist hailing from Pasadena California, now working and living in Spokane, WA. First 2 Albums: "Dark night of the soul" and "Setting the Bar" were recently followed up by his most recent release "The Odyssey" available on itunes and on his website www.wildcardqm.com. The music video for the album's title track was released in March 2012. Watch it here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_detailpage&v=RODyosRWyHI. Wildcard has previously collaborated with Smoke from Old Dominion, Simple from the Sandpeople, Dead Poet and Krayzie Bone from Bone Thugs and Harmony. Fans can follow his show tour and updates on his website and facebook page. www.facebook.com/wildcardqm
