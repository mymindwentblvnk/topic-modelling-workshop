Cloud Of Shadows, Melodic Death Metal Band formed in July 2006 in Quilmes, Buenos Aires, Argentina. Their main influences are based on In Flames, Arch Enemy, Dark Tranquillity and other bands from Gothemburg, Sweden.


The musicians of the band are:
Gaston Fernandez: Guitars, backing vocals
Gabriel Lema: Guitars
Natalia Penzi: Bass
David Garaycochea: Vocals

Nowadays the band is recording their first studio album, which will have aproximately 10 tracks.
