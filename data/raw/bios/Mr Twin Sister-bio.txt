The quintet indie pop band from Long Island, NY formerly known as Twin Sister since their first EP, Vampires with Dreaming Kids, in 2008 have resurfaced with the release of their self-titled, sophomore album, Mr Twin Sister.

Playing lead by singer Andrea Estella, Mr Twin Sister consists of Eric Cardona, Gabe D'Amico, Udbhav Gupta and Bryan Ujueta.
