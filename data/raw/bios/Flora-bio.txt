There are six artists who go by Flora. 

1)Flora Pereira da Silva, populary known as Flora is a character in the brazilian soap-opera A Favorita, the character is played by actress Patricia Pillar. In the soap, Flora is a pop-electronic-country artist, formerly one half of the duo Faísca (aka Donatela Fontini, played by actress Claudia Raia) & Espoleta.  She's also a maniacal psycho killer. Her hits include: Beijinho Doce, Velha Safada and É Tudo Meu.
