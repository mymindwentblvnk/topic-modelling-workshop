Abs Conditus aka Obscenus Aryanhord was a one-man NS Black Metal band from Poland. After leaving NSBM scene turned to Buddhism and later became a poet. Konrad Turula commited suicide on June 3, 2008 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abs+Conditus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
