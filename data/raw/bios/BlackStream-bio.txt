Blackstream aka Mårten Svartström is a Finnish singer, composer and radio voice that took part in the Finnish preselection of the 2010 Eurovision Song Contest with a song called "Divine". The song didn't make it through the sms voting phase to the semifinals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blackstream">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
