The Dark Lords of Stonehurst is a thrash metal band from Pennsylvania. 
They are unsigned, but they play live shows often around Philadelphia and have released their first CD, called Serpent Witch.
Band members include:
Jeff the Fuck Tyrant (vocals) 
Shifty Shane (drums) 
Blunker (bass)
