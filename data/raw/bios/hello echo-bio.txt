3-piece alternative rock band from Santa Monica, CA, originally from Boston.  Formed in 2008 and led by ex-Apollo Sunshine member Sean Aylward (guitar/vocals), Hello Echo is hot off their debut release Hello, and are just starting to gain some notoriety on the indie scene.
