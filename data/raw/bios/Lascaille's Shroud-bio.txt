Lascaille's Shroud is a progressive death metal band from Saint Petersburg, Florida led by Brett Windnagle. The debut album "Interval 01: Parallel Infinities, The Inner Universe" features two guest musicians; Patrick Parris of Project: Roenwolfe (Clean vocals) and Tyler Sherrill of Steamforged (Key solos on final track)

The name Lascaille's Shroud is taken from the Revelation Space sci-fi novels by Alastair Reynolds. The name refers to a vast spatial anomaly created by aliens which is an important part of the plot in the series. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lascaille%27s+Shroud">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
