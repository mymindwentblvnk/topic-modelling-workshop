Swedish folk/black metal.

Line-up:
Eihort - Guitar
Abhoth (Pierre Wilhelmsson) - Bass
Nyarlathotep - Guitar
Cthugha (Erik Grawsiö) - Vocals, Drums

This is a side-project by members of Månegarm. Lyrics deal with the writings of H.P. Lovecraft and horror. The music itself is very close to that of "mother band" Månegarm.

Cthulhus Scorn released one 2-track demo in 2000 before being put on ice. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cthulhus+Scorn">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
