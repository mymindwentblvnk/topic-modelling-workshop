There is more than 1 band named Drawback

1. Drawback's a 4 piece street punk band from the good ole Constitution State.

The current members are:
Eric - guitar and lead vocals
Tom - lead guitar and backup vocals
Mike - bass and backup vocals
Tommy - drums

We've been on a bit of a hiatus, but we're back with a vengeance! We're currently working on a new album that will be out in the fall of '09. In the meantime, check out the Obsessive Compulsive Demo. All our songs are posted for free download.

If you want to book us for a show, drop us a message here or email drawbackpunx@gmail.com

2. Hardcore band from Sydney. They were around in the mid-late 1990's and had a NYHC-inspired sound.

Band was:
Vocals - Alfred
Guitar - Anthony
Bass - Paul
Drums - Alcides

3. For the Los Angeles ebm darkwave band featuring DJ Amanda Jones and Mike Larrabee circa 2000-2003 please see the DRAW.BACK Last.fm entry.
