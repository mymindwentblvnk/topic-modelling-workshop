State Of Destruction are a Melodic Death Metal band from Bremen, Germany who formed in 2004. In 2006 they released their debut album 'Cleaning Out This World' and the single 'Unholy Enemy'. In 2008 they released the 'Global Punishment' album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/State+of+Destruction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
