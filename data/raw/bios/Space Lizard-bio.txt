Space Lizard is four-piece from Southern Finland playing doom metal influenced semi-psychedelic rock. The band was formed already in the 90's, but the current line-up saw the light of day in august 2008. Space Lizard's music is a mixture of groovy rock and gloomy doom with a touch psychedelic elements and raw sounds. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Space+Lizard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
