12 Gauge Dead is a band hailing from Linköping, Sweden. Their music is a blend of a lot of different elements in metal, classic death aswell as progressive influnces. Their discography consists of three demo releases:
Greyscale (2005)
Beneath Our Wings (2006)
Circles (2007)

Members are: Magnus Nilsson - Vocals, André Axell - Guitar,  Joel Holmgren - Guitar, Joel Ekman - Bass and Aksel Homgren - Drums.
