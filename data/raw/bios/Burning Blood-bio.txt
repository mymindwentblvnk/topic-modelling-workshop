Burning Blood is one man Raw NS Black Metal act from Virginia (USA). The band was founded in 2003 and is still active today.

The band focuses on themes like war, satanism, Nazism, WWII and Armageddon. 

So far, Burning Blood has released the following:

Forest of the Damned Demo, 2005
Unholy Demo, 2006  
Rehearsal 6/6/6 Demo, 2006  
Death Cult Of The Totenkopf Split album, 2009
