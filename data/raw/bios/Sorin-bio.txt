Sorin is the newest brainchild of Nicolas Sorin, an artist with a long and prolific background in the industry worldwide, received several Awards and Nominations (including 4 Latin Grammys nominations , 2 premios Gardel , 2 Premios Cóndor & Premio Clarin for film scores and productions) He has worked in Europe with artists like Miguel Bosé, Shakira, Alejandro Sanz, Juanes, and has conducted prestigious orchestras such as the “London Session Orchestra”, the Mexico Symphony Orchestra and the “Henry Mancini Orchestra” among others. Nicolas was the orchestra conductor and one of the 3 composers of Argentum, the 40-minute Suite that premiered at the G-20 Summit at Teatro Colón (Nov 30th. 2018) attended by World Leaders and Presidents from 20 countries . He’s been actively composing and producing as well as performing with groups such as” Sorin Octeto” , “Fernandez 4″, ”Santiago Vazquez” “Octafonic” and he is currently preparing the premiere of “Sinfonía Antártica” and orchestral work which he composed in Antarctica between 2013-19 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sorin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
