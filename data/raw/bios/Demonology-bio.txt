Demonology were a Death Metal band from El Paso, Texas that formed in 2000. They released a self-titled demo in 2002 and a full-length CD titled "The Veil of Perception" in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demonology">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
