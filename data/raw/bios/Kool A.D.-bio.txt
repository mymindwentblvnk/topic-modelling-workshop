KOOL A.D. (real name Victor Vazquez) is a Bay Area-based solo rapper and the drummer of hardcore band Party Animal. He was previously one half of Queens-based rap outfit Das Racist and one fifth of electro-pop group Boy Crisis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kool+A.D.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
