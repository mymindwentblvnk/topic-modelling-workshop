Mist Of Nihil is a 5 piece melodic death/doom band stationed in Athens,Greece. The band started 1 year ago with so far 3 line up changes.The current lineup is: Panos Leakos-Vocals Thanassis-Rythm Guitar Marios-Lead Guitar Makis Vouros-Drums Nikos Gerakios-Bass Mist Of Nihil draws inspiration from themes such as: nature,darkness,vanity and sorrow and bands such as : Opeth,Dark Tranquility,Insomnium,Belakor,In Flames,Swallow the Sun,Ghost Brigade,Lamb Of God,Omnium Gatherum We just released our single,titled Eternal Curse taken from our upcoming EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mist+of+Nihil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
