Subliminal Messages is an instrumental progressive/post-rock band from Kaunas, Lithuania. Inspired by space themes and other post-rock and instrumental performers, they are exploring their own paths while the performances are enhanced by custom visuals and audio samples and the music varies from spacey guitar parts to energetic, almost noise parts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Subliminal+Messages">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
