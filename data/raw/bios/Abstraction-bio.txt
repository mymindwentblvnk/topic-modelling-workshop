Abstraction may refer to:
1) A Progressive Death Metal Band
2) A French dark ambient/8-bit artist
3) Scottish Indie Band 
4) Bulgarian progressive power metal band
5) American Downtempo/Ambient/Chillout artist

1) Abstraction is a melodic progressive metal band with roots in Death Metal. Formed in 2005 by John Brisentine (Guitar and Vox) and Brian Vandegrift (Drums). Later members include Ryan Thall (Guitar and Vox) and William Denne (Drums). Remote Viewing was released on July 30th, 2014. 

2) The French Abstraction released 'Abstraction' in 2011. It's downloadable here: http://www.jamendo.com/fr/album/88938

3) The Scottish Indie band Abstraction come from Loanhead a small town just outside Edinburgh. They released 'Optics And Alice' on the small Scottish Indie Label: Lone Head Records.
http://loneheadrecords.bandcamp.com/track/optics-and-alice

4) Progressive Power Metal band Abstraction from Zlatitsa, Bulgaria. First single Wolf released in the summer of 2013. Debut album End of Hope released in 2014. http://www.abstractionband.com

5) American Abstraction is Benjamin Burnes. Residing in breezy Minnesota, Ben is a solo artist who has been independently writing and releasing music since 1998. Starting in 2014, Ben has been writing music for video games and short films.
