The project was born in 1997 as Iluvatar.Lately name was replaced by Inis Gwenva.
From the beginning the project consisted to mix Metal with Traditional Music influences, by using instruments like the Bagpipe and the Irish Whistle.
Discography:
"The Call Of Revenge" (demo) - 2003
"IInd. Invasion" (demo) - 2005
After a tragic loss of Iluhain in december 2005 the band decided to change their name to Valuatir.
