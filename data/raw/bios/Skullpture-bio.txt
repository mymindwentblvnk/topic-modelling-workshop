There are 2 artists with the name Skullpture:

1) Skullpture is a trio (from Turku, Finland) playing experimental improvised music. Their soundscape consists mostly of - but is not limited to - effected guitars and various percussion elements. The band was formed in the spring 2002 by Novaro, PT and Wikgren. Since 2005 they seem to be non-active...
Band's site is still available
