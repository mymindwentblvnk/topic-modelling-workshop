Cale Parks is most famous for his current role as the multi-faceted drummer, percussionist, pianist and vibraphonist of indie band Aloha, on Polyvinyl Records. 

In the summer of 2006, Cale Parks released his first full length album, Illuminated Manuscript, on Japan's imprint Stiff Slack. 

In addition to performing solo and with Aloha, Cale Parks is also known for his contributions of recording and touring with various other bands and solo artists, including: CEX, Chin Up Chin Up, Joan of Arc, Love of Everything, Owen, Pit Er Pat, and White Williams.
