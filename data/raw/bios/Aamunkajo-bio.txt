Aamunkajo is a one-man funeral doom metal band from Finland. The first song of Aamunkajo (Ammuttu) saw the day light in autumn 2006. In october 2008 small ukranian label Boyanov Gimn released Aamunkajo's first album Unen päättyessä (At the end of dream).
