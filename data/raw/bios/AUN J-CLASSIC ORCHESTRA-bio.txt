AUN J-Classic Orchestra(AUN J クラシック オーケストラ) is a musical collective made up of young musicians working to express traditional Japanese instruments(koto,shamisen,shakuhachi etc.) in new & more popular ways. They cover film scores, videogames, and other such avenues in the traditional style but in a J-Classic manner.
