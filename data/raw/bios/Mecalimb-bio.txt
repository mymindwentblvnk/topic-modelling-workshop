Mecalimb is a thrash metal/hardcore band from Fredrikstad that consist of five members: 
Kenneth Liliegren: Vocal
Ole Olsen: Guitar
Torben Svendsen: Guitar
Dag Kopperud: Bass/ Backing vocal
Marius Pettersen: Drums

At fall of 2008 they released their first album: Mechanical Recipe
