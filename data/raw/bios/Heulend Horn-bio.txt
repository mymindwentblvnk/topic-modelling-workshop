An epic viking metal band from Argentina, Buenos Aires. Formed in 2002. All instruments are played by Friedrich Curwenius. Lyrics deal mainly about viking mythology and history. Musically influenced by such bands as Summoning and Graveland. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Heulend+Horn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
