Lamented Despondency is a project band from Povazska Bystrica, Slovakia created by Peter Hutyra (Moonfog guitarrist) in 2006 to produce slow and mournful music. In January 2008 Juliana Novo from Porto Alegre, Brazil joined the project.
In April 2008 Juliana quit the project.
Discography:
2006 - Foul Slumber (6 tracks of slow Funeral Death/Doom Metal)
2008 - Ornaments of Death
