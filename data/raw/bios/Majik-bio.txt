Majik refers to multiple artists:

1. Majik were a funk disco group formed by the Bayyan brothers. Prolific singers, writers, producers and musicians. They also were in the bands Forecast, Atlantis & The Kay-Gees. They released one album with Armenta as Majik titled 'I Wanna Be With You' in 1985. As well as one single as Majik and a further two with Armenta. 

2. Majik (often stylised as mʌȷık) are an indie duo based in London, UK creating "chill"-sounding electronic pop songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Majik">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
