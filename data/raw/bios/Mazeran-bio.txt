Heavy metal act from Osaka, Japan, formed in '85. The band released their first vinyl single, named "Wild & Roll", the same year. A vinyl split EP with Killer May was released in '86. In the same year, Mazeran released two more vinyl singles, named "Can't Stop My Dream" and "Outsider". In '87, the band released "Moving Lips" on 12" vinyl, through Night Gallery Records. Their last, self-titled album was released in September 1989 on J.A.P. Records. This album featured a more hard rock style. It had a different line-up (2 Japanese and 2 American members) as well. Mazeran is currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mazeran">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
