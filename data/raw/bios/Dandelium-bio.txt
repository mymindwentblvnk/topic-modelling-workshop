Dandelium is a spanish female-fronted Symphonic Metal project.

The actual composer is Erik Monsonis, who composed by himself until newly-added band members co-wrote songs with him.

Current line-up:

Bella Dianez - Female vocals
Erik Monsonis - Rhythm guitars, growls, keyboards
Alex Zamora - Guitars
Adrian Martin - Bass
Christopher Perez - Drums

Discography:
