Azbuk formed in Brazil in June of 1997. With three demos under their belt, "Divine Forces" (1998), "Nasferatus Darkness Earl" (2001), and "Ancient Secrets of the Bible" (2004) all receiving high praises from a number of South American magazines and zines.  Now 10 years later, Azbuk & Sullen Records will celebrate 10 years of Azbuk with the release of "Compilation for Eternity". "Compilation for Eternity" will highlight the last 10 years of Azbuk and make their material available to a much wider audience world wide.

"Compilation for Eternity" is set for release in August 2008, and will be limited to a mere 250 units world wide.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Azbuk">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
