dot(.) is a sludge / stoner doom metal band from Tokyo, Japan, formed in 1998. They are signed to Blind Date Records.

Members:
Kohki - vocals, bass
Rei - guitar
Dan - drums

Former members:
Kan - drums
Morita - drums
You - drums
Dan - drums
Jonas - drums
Ucchy - guitar, vocals
