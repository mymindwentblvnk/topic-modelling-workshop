GODHELPUS kicked ass in the local music scene for years, opening for some big names like Superjoint Ritual, Soilent Green, Goatwhore, Bludgeon and Macabre. Throughout their jamming career, they released two full length albums and were on the verge of a third when inner conflicts caused them to disband.
