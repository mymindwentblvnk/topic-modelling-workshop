NOKTURNAL HELLSTORM was formed in the frozen wastes of Rochester New York in 2010 by veteran Bassist Danny Lilker to pay homage to the best of the 2nd wave of Black Metal.  Lilker soon enlisted a cadre of like-minded blasphemers to round out the lineup.

Members:
Dan Lilker - Bass
Bryan Rague - Guitar
Mike Welden - Guitar
Joe Lionti - Drums
Ron Blackwell - Lead Vocals
Colin Hanna - Keys and Vocals

Past Members:
Eric Quartaro - Guitar
Matt Greenwood - Keys and Vocals
