Heavy metal band Madd Hunter formed in the late '90s, with singer/songwriter/guitarist/bassist Phil Grasso and drummer Chuck Fitzgerald, both formerly of Steel Assassin and Sacred Reich. The band's debut album, 1999's "Silence of the Lambs", was released on GME Records, with distribution by The Orchard.

Grasso also works with the group Hells Felons, while Fitzgerald, a former member of Warhead and Poseidon, later formed Tongue-N-Grove with Paul Wabrek and Ralph Whittemore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Madd+Hunter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
