Rancidectomy is a band from Slovakia playing death metal with goregrind elements formed in 2006. The members of Rancidectomy are the same like in Sperm of Mankind. They released demo called "Surgical cuts and other tales from the cold hell" and 3 song EP "We dont need weapons to blast your head off" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rancidectomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
