There are three different artists by this name:

1. Early American power electronics project by Michael Moynihan (now best known for Blood Axis). Operated at various times out of both Cambridge, Mass. and Belgium. 

A number of planned releases & compilation tracks never saw the light of day, including: 
- Coup De Grace/Club Moral live collaboration cassette (originally intended to be released on Cthulhu Records as CR05). 
- Coup De Grace "Last Words" memorial LP (originally intended to be released on Desecration/Stimulation Recordings from Belgium). 
- Coup De Grace retrospective CD (originally intended to be released on Cthulhu Records as CR14).

2. Coup De Grace was a Hungarian hardcore-punk band.

3. Coup De Grace is also an art/prog group from Syracuse, New York that relocated to Wilmington, North Carolina. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coup+De+Grace">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
