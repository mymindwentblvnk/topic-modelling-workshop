Prettiest Eyes is an electronic garage punk trio from Los Angeles, featuring drummer/vocalist Pachy García, bassist Marcos Rodríguez, and keyboardist Paco Casanova. John Dwyer of Thee Oh Sees signed the band to his Castle Face label. The band's second album, Pools came out in 2017. Their third album, Volume 3, was released in June 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Prettiest+Eyes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
