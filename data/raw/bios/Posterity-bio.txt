The group was formed in 2007 in Utena, until then, there have been intensive search of musicians and music style, finally the group stopped at death metal style. The group played many concerts in various cities in Lithuania and continues it's intensive activity. 
Posterity released their debut full-length album "The Price of Greed" in 2010.

Lineup:

Mantas Gurkšnys – Rythm guitar/vocals
Vytautas Budvytis – Lead guitar
