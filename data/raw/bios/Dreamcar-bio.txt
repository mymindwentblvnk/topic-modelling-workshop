DREAMCAR Is a New Wave supergroup formed in 2016.
Their first single Kill For Candy premiered on KROQ's Kevin & Bean show on March 2, 2017. Dreamcar's self-titled debut album Dreamcar was released on May 12, 2017 on Columbia Records. It was produced by Tim Pagnotta (Neon Trees), and is heavily reminiscent of early 80s New Wave and New Romanticism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreamcar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
