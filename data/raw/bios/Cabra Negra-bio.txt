Cabra Negra are a Black Metal band from Colombia who formed in 2004. They released a self-titled demo in 2004 followed by a self-titled EP in 2006. A promo CD entitled 'Lucifer An Endless Dream' was released in 2008. Their debut album 'Ultimate Sacrifice' was released in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cabra+Negra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
