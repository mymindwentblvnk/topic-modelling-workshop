All I Could Bleed is a melodic death metal band formed in 2004 and are from Chelyabinsk, Russia.They released their first album "Burying the Past" at June of 2011.Their members are:
Ivan Stroev: Bass
Alex 'Morblack' Sibrikov: Bass / Drum programming / Guitars / Vocals
Max Melnikov: Guitars
