Combining trollish foot-stomping and Carnivale-esque quirkiness with Metal (and a healthy dose of musical humour), TROLDHAUGEN's oddball antics have been turning heads and raising eyebrows since 2008. 

Hailing from Wollongong, Australia, the Five-piece originally began as a duo, primarily focusing on blending traditional Scandinavian Folk music with Heavy Metal. It wasn't until 2010 that a band was formed and TROLDHAUGEN blasted onto the live scene.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Troldhaugen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
