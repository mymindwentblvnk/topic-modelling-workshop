There are multiple artists with this name.

1) A German heavy metal band. (1981-1982)
2) A rock band from Lindenhurst, Long Island, NY. (2011-present)  (http://breslau.bandcamp.com)

2) Breslau from New York consists of David Weckerle (Vocals/Guitar), Chris Schafer (Guitar/Vocals) who was in the late 90s band, The Waiting Process, John Mee (Bass), formerly in Iron Chic & Anthony Vito (Drums) who was also in The Waiting Process & is currently in Broadcaster.
