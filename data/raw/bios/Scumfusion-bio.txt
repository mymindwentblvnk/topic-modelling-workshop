Scumfusion is a grindcore band formed in Finland in 2000. The original lineup consisted of Antti. From 2003 into 2004 Taito was handling the lyrics and vocals. Timo joined the band in 2006 Spring. The band is studio project band only.

Music style mixes grindcore & death metal. The band started taking steps towards scifi realm with 2006 release ("under exoriant light") and changed completely into scifi grind, both musically and lyrically speaking, with 2010 release ("a Lossidian threnody"). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scumfusion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
