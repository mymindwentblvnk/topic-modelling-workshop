Ft. Lauderdale, FL  (2007-2008)

Sons of  War is a three piece Death/Black Metal band. 
Formed in 2007 same year they released there only self titled E.P "Sons of War" recorded at
Simplekill Studios.They later took part of several festivals all over the Southern Florida.  

The band later disbanded in 2008 due to personal issues.

Sons of war released two albums.

2007 - Sons of War E.P
2008 - Slaughtering at the Talent Farm


Roger "Total Gore" Garcia - Lead Vocals

Vic V  - Guitars/Vocals
