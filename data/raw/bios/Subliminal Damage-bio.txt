After a 2 year collaboration in the band Almost In Range, Vlad Borcan, Mircea Ciocoiu and Cosmin Isar founded a new band Subliminal Damage in August 2012. Being influenced by bands such as Mastodon, Tool, Lamb of God, Animals as Leaders, Death, Paradise Lost, the band focused towards a more progressive approach to their metal style. Each member equally contributes in the writing process. The band members are: Vlad Borcan (guitar and vocals), Mircea Ciocoiu (bass guitar), Cosmin Isar  (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Subliminal+Damage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
