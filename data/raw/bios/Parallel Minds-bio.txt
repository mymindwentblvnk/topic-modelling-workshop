Parallel Minds is a French progressive/power metal band, founded by cousins Stéphane Fradet and Grégory Giraudo. They released their debut album "Headlong Disaster" in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parallel+Minds">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
