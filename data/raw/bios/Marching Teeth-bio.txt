Marching Teeth is a heavy rock/metal band from Brooklyn, NY. The founding 3 members are originally from Louisville, Ky (Aaron Baumle, David Cook, Damon Meena). Carmine Laietta (Hull) and Drew Mack joined the line up in 2014. 

Marching Teeth's first album was released in the fall of 2012. The second album will be released in winter of 2015. 

Also Check Out https://www.facebook.com/marchingteeth .

David Cook - Guitar
Aaron Baumle - Skins
Damon Meena - Wide Rails & Vocals
Carmine Laietta - Guitar
Drew Mack - Guitar
