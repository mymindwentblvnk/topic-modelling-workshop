Disease Process was formed in Perth, Australia in 2010. Disease Process plays raw grindcore. Disease Process's lyrical subject matter deals with anarchism ideals and beliefs, discontent and hatred towards society. They are inspired by 80's UK hardcore, crust, and black metal.
