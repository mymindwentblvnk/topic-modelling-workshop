Svart Lotus started as a soloproject by Tor R. Stavenes in 2012.Songs were written and with the inclusion of drummer Eivin Brye the project started a slow journey toward becoming a band.Sigve Jordheim joined in 2013 as a second guitarist. The Svart Lotus EP was recorded in 2015 and will be released in May 2016. The band is planning live performances, and have recently recruited bassist Øyvind Kaslegard.
Line up:
Eivin Brye - Drums
Sigve Jordheim - Guitar
Øyvind Kaslegard - Bass
