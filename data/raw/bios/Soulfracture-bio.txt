Soulfracture are a 5 piece death/thrash metal band from Leeds, England. Influences include Pantera, Carcass, Slayer, At the Gates, The Haunted, Lamb of God and Dying Fetus. They have a mp3's @ http://www.myspace.com/soulfracture and a website @ http://www.soulfracture.co.uk. Soulfracture recently signed to Thirty Days of Night Records, an excellent metal label based in Leeds (http://www.thirtydaysofnightrecords.co.uk). Soulfractures debut album 'Ashes of Existence'  was released in February 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soulfracture">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
