Psalm Zero is an experimental metal band from Queens NY, led by Charlie Looker (formerly of art-rock band Extra Life) and featuring current members of Kayo Dot. Their debut 7" came out on Last Things Records in November 2013. Their debut full-length, The Drain, was released by Profound Lore Records in 2014. This was followed by Stranger to Violence in 2016, and Sparta in 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psalm+Zero">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
