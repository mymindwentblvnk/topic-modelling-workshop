Karbonsoul is an  band from Portugal, formed in early 2007, composed by musicians with former projects. The main influences reside in bands like, my dying bride, katatonia, lacuna coil and the gathering.

Muffy on Vocals , Rstein on Guitar , Rvid on Bass , Hellfish on drums are currently working on the first studio recordings.
