Desecravity was formed in 2007 in Tokyo, Japan by Yuichi, and then began playing shows in the summer of 2008. In 2009, Desecravity was able to tour the Philippines. It was very successful and led to another opportunity of performing with Exodus and Dying Fetus at the Painkiller Metal Festival in Beijing, China. In 2010, they started recording their first full-length album "IMPLICIT OBEDIENCE" which mixed and mastered by Erik Rutan (Hate Eternal, ex-Morbid Angel) at Mana Recording Studios in Florida, and then, Desecravity signed with Willowtip Records (USA) in 2011.

In 2012, finally long-awaited their first full-length album was released via Willowtip Records. It has been highly acclaimed not only in Japan but also in United States and Europe. Then,from spring to summer, they did their own tour "IMPLICIT OBEDIENCE ASIA TOUR" and "IMPLICIT OBEDIENCE JAPAN TOUR".
