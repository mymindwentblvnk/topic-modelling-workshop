There are a couple of bands using the moniker 'Talion'. One of them is a death-thrash metal band formed in 1998, hailing from Växjö, Sweden. The current lineup features Protector's former singer Martin Missy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Talion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
