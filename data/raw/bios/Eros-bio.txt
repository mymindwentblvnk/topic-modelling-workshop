There are multiple artists under the name Eros.
1) A Brazilian thrash metal band founded in 1985. Released one album, Road to Wisdom, before splitting up.

2) A hardcore punk band from Hayward, CA. (EROS) The band is currently based in Bay Area, California.
http://www.facebook.com/eroshardcore

3) EROS / DEATH, also known as Eros, a San Francisco, CA-based post-punk/goth project.
http://erosdeath.bandcamp.com/

4) Eros, R&B singer/songwriter from Houston, Teaxs is the latest artist from the Milky Wayv camp to deliver a tope new body of work, Stratus. The project is entirely produced by Bobby Earth, with vocal guest features from D.C.-based pop singer/songwriter Jen Miller as well as Houston-based emcee Brice Blanco and soul singer Solace. Get ready for an eclectic, sonic journey through the stratosphere as the god of desire himself, Eros, uses the gift of melody to detail his quest for true love. https://iameros.bandcamp.com/
