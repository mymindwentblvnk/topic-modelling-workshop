Lichium, a Black Metal band from Nagasaki/Tokyo, Japan. Lichium formed in 1992, with lyrical content dealing with satirical comedy and parodies of the Black Metal genre/scene. Lichium is currently inactive but over their short career released two full-length albums and a single demo.
