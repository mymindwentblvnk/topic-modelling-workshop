Black metal band from Japan.
Discography:
- 2001 - Demo 1 (demo)
- 2002 - Funeral Elegy (demo)
- 2003 - Rehearsal Demo 2003 (demo)
- 2004 - Sargeist / Funeral Elegy (split with Sargeist)
- 2008 - Vicious and Cruel Symphony (contains all songs from demos + 5 unreleased live songs.)

Members: 
Hakuja - Vox + Guitar (Hakuja, Cataplexy.
Psychoblaze - Bass (Infernal Necromancy)
Shiho - Drums.
