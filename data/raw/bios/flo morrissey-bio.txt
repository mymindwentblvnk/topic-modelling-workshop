She has released several albums and singles and has toured internationally. She has also recorded with American musician Matthew E. White as Flo Morrissey and Matthew E. White, and, most recently, as the duo The Clementines with husband Benjamin Clementine. 

Morrissey discovered the likes of Joanna Newsom, CocoRosie, and Devendra Banhart in her dad's car when she was nine or 10 years old. 

In December 2014 Morrissey released debut single "Pages of Gold", followed, in 2015, by her debut album, Tomorrow Will Be Beautiful, written and recorded in California and produced by Noah Georgeson (Devendra Banhart, Joanna Newsome, Little Joy) and Philippe Zdar. 

In 2017 she recorded an album of covers with American musician Matthew E. White as Flo Morrissey and Matthew E. White.
