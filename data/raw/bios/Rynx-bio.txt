Evan Fong (born 31 May 1992), known online as VanossGaming and Rynx, is a Canadian internet personality, and music producer. He posts montage-style videos on YouTube of him and other his friends playing various video games. Aside from this, he produces music under the name Rynx. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/RYnx">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
