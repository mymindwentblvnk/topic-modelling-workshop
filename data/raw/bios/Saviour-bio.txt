Saviour are an Australian Metalcore band from Perth, they combine melodies with blasting breakdowns like no other. The peaceful combination in their debut album, 'Once We Were Lions', of these melodies and heavy breakdowns creates something truly amazing. 

In August 2013, it was announced that Saviour had signed to UNFD with their second album 'First Light To My Death Bed' to be released on October 4th that same year. 



There are 3 other lesser known acts under the name Saviour:

1) Saviour is a London based post hardcore band.

2) Saviour is a Belgian hardcore band. They come from Oostkamp.
