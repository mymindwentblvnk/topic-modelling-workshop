Rich Gang is an American rap supergroup formed in 2013 composed of rap mogul Birdman, Young Thug, Rich Homie Quan, various Cash Money Records artists, Young Money Entertainment artists and friends in the music industry. The debut album of the group was released on July 23, 2013, titled Rich Gang: Flashy Lifestyle in addition to several singles including 'Lifestyle', 'We Been On' & 'Tapout' and mixtape Rich Gang: Tha Tour, Part 1. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rich+Gang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
