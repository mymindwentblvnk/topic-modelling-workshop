Total Negation is a depressive black metal band from North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany that formed in 2007 by Wiedergaenger (vocals, guitar, bass, drums, keyboards).
Debut full-length album titled "Zeitenwende" was released in 2009. Second full-length titled "Zur späten Stunde | Zeiträume" was released in 2013.

Wiedergaenger also played bass on the Through The Pain album "Time Heals Nothing".
