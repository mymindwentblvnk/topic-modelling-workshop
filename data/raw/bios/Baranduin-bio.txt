'Fantasy Metal' from Finland. The band is named after the river 'Baranduin' (or Brandywine) from J.R.R.Tolkien's Middle-Earth.
They are inspired by bands like Bal-Sagoth, Moonsorrow, Rhapsody, Symphony X and Wintersun

The bonus track from Ancile (A Warrior's Death) is downloadable at Mikseri.
