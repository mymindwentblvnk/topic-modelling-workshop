Lord Vampyr's Shadowsreign is an italian gothic death metal band formed by ex lead singer of Theatres des Vampires Lord Vampyr with ex members of VII Arcano and other members of the Roman extreme metal scene

Lineup
Lord Vampyr - All Vocals 
Sk - Guitars 
Nepesh Ra - Guitars 
Nighthorn - Bass 
Aeshla - Drums
