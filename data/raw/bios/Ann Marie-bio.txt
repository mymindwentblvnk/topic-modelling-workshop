Ann Marie is an American singer-songwriter, from Chicago, Illinois. She is signed to Interscope Records. She is best known for her single "Secret", which has garnered over 50 million views on YouTube and peaked at number 22 on the Billboard Hot R&B Songs chart. Her debut was on July 10, 2019 with the EP Pretty Psycho. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ann+Marie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
