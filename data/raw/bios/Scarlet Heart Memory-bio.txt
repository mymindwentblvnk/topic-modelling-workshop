Formed in Mexico City, 2005.  Visionary Hugo L.S., who writes all of the music and lyrics, released Scarlet Heart Memory's debut album "Angels and Children" on September 2006.  Currently, Hugo L.S. is recording Scarlet Heart Memory's second album, which will be called "Magician in the Stars".  The second album will be much more experimental, and greatly influenced by Hungarian composer Bela Bartok. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scarlet+Heart+Memory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
