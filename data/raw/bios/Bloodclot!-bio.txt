Straight edge hardcore metal band. Bloodclot!'s sound consists of mixing the intensity of hardcore with the precision and brutality of metal. The line-up consists of singer John Joseph (the former frontman of Cro-Mags and Both Worlds), drummer Danny Schuler (a founding member of Brooklyn's Biohazard), bassist Rick Lopez (Merauder), guitarist Scott Roberts (a former member of Biohazard and Cleveland hardcore legends The Spudmonsters) and guitarist Eric Klinger (from Pro-Pain and The Spudmonsters). Their debut album is titled "Burn Babylon Burn"
