NOME is a Belgian Duo most noted for their song "Le Choix" - which they released in 2019. Their song "Maria" was remixed by The Wickeed. Since their debut in 2019, they have released 5 individual singles, with their most recent work being "Derniere Fois" which was released in April 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nome">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
