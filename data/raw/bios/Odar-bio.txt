Odar (ex Capricorn Silhouette) was formed in the beginning of 2006, by Zlo (guitars), Odron (vocals), Neath (bass), Infidel (guitars) and Kurvar (drums). 
The idea was to play black metal. Since the beginning band is playing they own songs. During the 2008. Neath and Infidel left the band, on their place comes Kob (guiters) and Orkus (bass) and the band changed their name into Odar 
The band finished their firist album called "Zavjet dalekom snu"  which is released during October 2008 for Walk Records label on tape and also on CD for Sonic Temple Records (Slovakia).    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Odar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
