Distributor was a progressive metal band from Ireland. They disbanded in late 2010. Guitarist Ian Rockett, bassist James Dunne and drummer Ross McMahon formed the band Shattered Skies in early 2011 and joined up with vocalist Sean Murphy. Their last.fm is:
