Howling Giant are a stoner metal/psychedelic rock band from Nashville, Tennessee, USA. Active since 2014, the band offers an eclectic style of metal inspired by early sci-fi and horror. All three members of this power trio share vocal duties, with lyrics inspired by space travel. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Howling+Giant">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
