Ten Tonnes (real name Ethan Barnett) was born on the 23rd of August 1996 in Hertford, United Kingdom. He is the younger brother of George Ezra, who drew his attention to music and Bob Dylan's discography. George also taught his brother to play the guitar.
His first 3-song EP "Lucy" was released in 2016. The following year he released his next EP, titled "Born To Lose" and his breakthrough single "Cracks Between". In 2018 he continued releasing new singles - including "Lay It On Me" and "G.I.V.E." which had its first radio spin on the 2nd of August at BBC Radio 1. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ten+Tonnes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
