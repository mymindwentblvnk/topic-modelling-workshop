Bio
In Salvo was formed in 2002 and have, so far, recorded two demos:
- Waltz For The Blackend Heart (2006)
- As It Withers (2007)
Both demos are recoreded in BD STUDIO, Gvarv.
After some changes to their line up, In Salvo is back again, and are currently working on new material for the upcomming debut album.
