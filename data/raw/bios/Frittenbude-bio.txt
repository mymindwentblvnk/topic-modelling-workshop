Frittenbude is a German electronic/electropunk Band from Geisenhausen.

They were founded in 2006 by Martin Steer, Johannes Rögner and Jakob Häglsperger.

They are signed to Audiolith.

They released three albums: "Nachtigall" in 2008, "Katzengold" in 2010 and "Delfinarium" in 2012.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Frittenbude">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
