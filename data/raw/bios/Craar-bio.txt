Craar originated from the mind of Hermit, in the winter of 2009. It started as a small one-man project with no specific aim. Just a way to unleash his thoughts into music. As time passed, Hermit felt the urge to break the boundaries of his solitary project. He revealed his music to two close friends. At that moment, Smerter and Dyscia became the voices of Craar. For two years they worked together to create songs, inspired by their innermost thoughts. What remains is their debut album: In solitary minds <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Craar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
