AUTARKEON, of Haskel project has succeeded another one-man-band, “ZERO 0” (which also worked occasionally as a duo and trio).

Haskel has worked as a singer / vocalist in various bands Garage, before starting, self-taught on guitar.

Singer-songwriter of AUTARKEON, Haskel handles registrations.

He is assisted by Spike to finalize the programming drum lines and mixing.

The “old school death” of AUTARKEON also borrows from other currents of extreme metal.
