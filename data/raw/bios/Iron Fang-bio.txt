Iron Fang is a Power/Speed Metal band from Kicevo, Macedonia.The band is formed in 2006 by solo guitarist Gorazd. At the beginning the band starts as a cover band and plays some gigs in Kicevo, but the band stops with work when some members leave the band. In 2007 come new members and band starts with work and is back on stage. Now the band works on their first demo album.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Iron+Fang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
