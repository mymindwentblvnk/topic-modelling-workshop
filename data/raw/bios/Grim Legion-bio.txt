Grim Legion is a Death metal band from Manalapan, New Jersey. They formed in 1988. The band re-activated in the year 2011 with the addition of Mike Keller on drums. They played their first show back on October 8th, 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grim+Legion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
