Burn In Silence was a Metalcore band from Boston, Massachusetts.

The band formed in 2003, and a year later they released their debut EP Pure as Your First Day. They would later sign a contract with Prosthetic Records who released their debut album Angel Maker which was well-received by fans and critics. Extensive touring followed, and they were in the process of writing and recording a new album. However in 2008 Prosthetic Records announced that the band had decided to break up. No reason has been given as to why they broke up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burn+In+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
