Founded in 2011 Texas Toast Chainsaw Massacre has been tearing up the Chicago DIY scene. Combining crossover thrash metal, comedy, pop culture and wrestling. Having shared the stage with bands such as The Mentors, Psychostick, Cross Examination, and Spellcaster these up and comers are beginning to make a name for themselves.
