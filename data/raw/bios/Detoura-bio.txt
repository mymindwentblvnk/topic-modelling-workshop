Detoura is a melodic thrash metal band from Orlando Florida. They are known for fast drumming, guitar virtuosity and dynamic vocals! Members consist of Drew Jarosz (vocals/guitar), Zack Robin (lead guitar), Roach Rose (bass guitar) and Shaun Kimmerling (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Detoura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
