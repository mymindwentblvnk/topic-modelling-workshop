Sorrow Leads To Salvation is the project of a Kyiv-based sound producer known under the alias Adam Maylow. Speaking of the sound, the artist tries to make it manifold, combining a lot of genres to reach a unique eclecticism across ambient, techno, instrumental hip-hop, IDM etc. Not so long ago he released his LP Million Years Of Sorrow through ULTRA VAGUE RECORDINGS UVR <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sorrow+Leads+To+Salvation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
