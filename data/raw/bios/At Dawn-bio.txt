At Dawn is a band from Yuba City, Sacramento,California, United States. The and was formed in 2004,and  then broke up in late 2009, then they reformed in 2010.Their style of music they play ranges from Melodic Death Metal, to Thrash Metal and Metalcore.

Current Band Members:
Paul Collier - Bass
Bob Swanson - Drums
Kyle Hansen  - Guitars
Mark Underwood - Guitars
Salvador Fernandez - Vocals 

Discography:
Undead EP 2008  
Rapture EP 2009
