Stonechurch is a heavy metal band from Gotland, Sweden, since 1999 consisting of Karl-Oskar Wiman (bass and vocals), Ronny Westergren (guitars), Pontus Lennander (guitars) and Jonas Johansson (drums). So far, they've released three albums, all self-financed under their own label Goodevil Records.

The albums:
* Visions (2001)
* Goodevil (2002)
* Amplified (2006)
