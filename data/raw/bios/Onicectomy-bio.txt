Onicectomy is an italian brutal death metal band from Bari, Italy. They formed in the year 2007 and have released a promo ("Promo 2008" ) and an EP ("Maniacal Epidemy") in 2008. They have released their debut album called "Drowning for Salvation" in 2011 via Coyote Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Onicectomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
