Abrania was formed in Västerås in 1999 by singer Jonas Lindh. The band started as a black metal band but changed the style of music around 2002 in melodic death metal. join their facebook: http://www.facebook.com/?ref=home#!/pages/Abrania/174943112547228?v=wall

They have released ten discs to date:
Calling My Name  (2001)
One Way Ticket To Death  (2003)
At The End Of The World  (2004)
dyin' screams (2005)
Rest In Peace (2006)
me vs. the world (2006)
nine years of mental tears (2007)
make my hate more productive (2008)
Breed My Hate (2009)
My Demons, My Sorrows And My Endless Guilt (2010)
