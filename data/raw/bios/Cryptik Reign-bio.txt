Band started with 2 guitarists ( Onni and Late ) and with a drummer ( Olli ) long time ago. Bassist ( Topi ) came to band in 2006 summer. Late left the band in late 06. In early 07 Topi started to do vocals. Band made two demos in 08, From The Abyss and Witching Warfare.
