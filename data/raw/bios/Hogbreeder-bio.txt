Hogbreeder is a death metal band from the United States (Central Florida). The band was formed in 2001, after a couple releases and various live performances, the band split-up sometime between 2008-2009.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hogbreeder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
