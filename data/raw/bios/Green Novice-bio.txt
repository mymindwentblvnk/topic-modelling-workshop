Green Novice are four young lads coming from the land of woods and lakes, Latgale. Formed in 2001 in Preiļi, Latvia, they are playing traditional folk influenced metal and singing in the language of their forefathers.

Members:

Alvis Bernāns - guitars, vocals (currently also Skyforger);
Zigmunds Čeksis - guitars;
Vitālijs Piskunovs - bass;
Valdis Metlāns - drums (currently also Inde Indive).
