1) MG 42 was a black metal band from Germany. It was formed in 2007 and is currently non-active.

MG 42 is short for Maschinengewehr 42 which was a
machinegun developed by Germany during World War II.

Götz von Tod  does all the vocals and instruments himself. 
He is also a member of the German group Pestschwadron.

MG 42 has so far released:
- Die Säge Mäht Noch Immer Demo, 2007
