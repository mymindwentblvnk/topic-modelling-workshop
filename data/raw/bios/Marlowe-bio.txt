Marlowe is a hip-hop duo from North Carolina comprising MC Solemn Brigham and producer L'Orange. They released their debut, self-titled album on Mello Music Group on July 13th, 2018. Their second collaborative studio album "Marlowe 2" was released on August 7th, 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marlowe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
