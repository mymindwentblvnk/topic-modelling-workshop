Alan Khadzaragov (Russian: Алан Хадзарагов) born on April 20, 1995 in Vladikavkaz, Russian Federation) better known under the pseudonym MATRANG, is a Russian musician. Since 2017 Khadzaragov has been member of the creative association "Gazgolder" which also is the label that releases his music. 

Khadzaragov rose to fame within Russia in 2018 with the hit "Meduza" (Jellyfish), which was one of the most played Russian songs that year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MATRANG">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
