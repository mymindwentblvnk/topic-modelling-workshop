"Ben Reilly" may refer to two different artists:
1. Ben Reilly, contemporary rapper and member of group "Abstract Media."
2. Ben Reilly, a Canadian trap artist from 2009 

1. Ben Reilly is a rapper from Atlanta and a member of up-and-coming group Abstract Media, which consists of Miseye, Orongello, Luke Royal, and himself. His first ever project, FREELANCE, was released on June 16th, 2021 through Abstract Media Group.
