Kolac is raw black metal band from Belgrade, Serbia, formed in 2006.
Their lyrical themes are about blasphemy and satanism.

Current line-up:
Zlorog - Guitar, Vocals
Grob - Bass, Lyrics
L'Hiver - Sesion Drums
