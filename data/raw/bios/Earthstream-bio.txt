Earthstream is a melodic death metal band from Japan, Tokyo. They formed in 2003. Their sounds are influenced by symphonic black metal. 1st demo "Evil Crusade" released in 2004, and they recorded 2nd demo "The Symphony of Tragic Sorrow" in 2006.Official website: http://sound.jp/earthstream/
