YAMAGEN'S DEVILELIET is a melodic death metal solo project effort from Japan headed by artist YAMAGEN. YAMAGEN has also provided vocals on albums for the Touhou Project arrange doujinshi bands Pizuya's Cell x MyonMyon and CROW'SCLAW.
