There used to be at least three bands with same name:

1) BLOODPATH was a straight edge harcore/punk band from Umeå, Sweden. They released a demo tape, put out a split 7" with another swedish hardcore band called Forced Into and appeard on the now classic «Straight Edge As Fuck Vol. 3» CD.

2) BLOODPATH was a thrash metal band from Joensuu(Finland) formed in 2006. They released only one demo (Demo 2007). Band changed their name to ARMA in 2007.

3) BLOODPATH was a hardcore band from Canada. Band existed 2003-2005.
