Christian Mejlænder Krogsvold (Oslo, Norway, 1989) is an independent chiptune/techno/breakbeat/orchestral  producer and professional video game music producer known for creating the complete soundtrack for the popular arcade game Castle Crashers. 
Waterflame used the nickname “chainsaw_09” before becoming “Waterflame”.
