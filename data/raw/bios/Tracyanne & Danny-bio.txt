Tracyanne & Danny is a new collaborative project between singer-songwriters Tracyanne Campbell (Camera Obscura) and Danny Coughlan (Crybaby). Drawing on their shared love of The Roches, Lou Reed, The Flamingos, Serge Gainsbourg, and The Style Council, their tracks have been produced by the likes of Edwyn Collins, Sean Read (Dexys) and Francis Macdonald (Teenage Fanclub). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tracyanne+&+Danny">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
