Ella Mai Howell (born November 3, 1994 in London) is a British singer and songwriter from London, England. She won a Grammy Award in 2019 for Best R&B Song with "Boo'd Up". She is signed to DJ Mustard's label 10 Summers Records where she released three extended plays and one studio album.
