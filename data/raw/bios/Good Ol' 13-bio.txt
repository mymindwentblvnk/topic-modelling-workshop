Kirkkonummi, Finland (2012 - present)

Good ol' 13 is a metal band from Finland. Their music could be described as Southern Metal, but there's a lot more in it. The band combines various styles of rock and metal into a tight package of exploding rage, usually singing about the cause of... and solution to all of life's problems: alcohol.

Members:
Unlucky Jack - vocals/guitar
Blacktooth Steve - drums
Bentham Bruce - bass/backing vocals
