There are currently two artists by the name of Favorite:

1) FAVORITE (Hangeul: 페이버릿), known pre-debut as Astory Girls, is a 6 member South Korean girl group that debuted on July 5th, 2017 under ASTORY Entertainment.
The members are Gaeul, Saebom, Seoyeon, Sugyeong, Junghee and Ahra. 

2) Favorite (born Christopher Alex, 18th Febuary1986 in Essen, Germany) is a German rapper. He is known for his self-deprecating and manic music style, as well as the work with contemporaries such as Kollegah and other known artists signed to Selfmade Records. In total he released five full length albums from 2007 to 2017 with frequent stretches of him disappearing from the public inbetween. His fourth album Neues von Gott reached the top of the German charts upon release.

After more dispute between Favorite and his label, he left Selfmade Recods in 2019 to found his own label (BuyOrDie-Records). On 7th June 2020, Favorite oficially announced the end of his music career for the time being.
