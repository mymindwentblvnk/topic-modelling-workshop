Ben, Alex, Albert & Murph. Melbourne based rock band.

If you like melodrama, hi 5's and vegetarian pasties you may be inclined to like Slowly Slowly. Their debut LP 'Chamomile' came out in August 2016.

Slowly Slowly are on tour with Something For Kate throughout November and their sophomore LP 'Aliens' is due for release in early 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slowly+slowly">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
