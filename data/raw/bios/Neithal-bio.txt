2004 - Act of creation. Marrath Sattis on bass, Shenloc on guitar. The first demo "Reig Namor" is released. DrVVAL joins as session drummer. RHOS handles synths, Red handles vocals.

2005 - DrVVAL joins as regular member. Red leaves the band. 

2006 - "Through the world of paradox" EP is released. Chmura joins as guitarist, Marrath Sattis handles the vocals. First gig on April 1st.

2007 - Chmura leaves the band. His place is taken by Sargon. Band plays gigs with Hate, Crionics and KAT.

2008 - RHOS leaves the band. Recording for "Residuum" is completed.

2010 - Recording for "Realis" and "Imaginalis" is completed.
