Arcana XIII is a melodic death metal band formed in Tokyo, Japan in 2003. They have Shin on vocals and guitar, Keitaro on bass and Ens on drums. They released only one single so far, "Reborn in pain" in 2003 but their status is active still <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ARCANA+XIII">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
