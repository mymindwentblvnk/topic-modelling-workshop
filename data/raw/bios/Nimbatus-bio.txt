Nimbatus is an one-man-metal-project focussing on instrumental songs that translate darkness and sadness into music. All the different stages of musical creation were done exclusively by Nimbatus.

The debut album called "Cyclus One" was released on April 2010 and features 8 songs conceived between January and March 2010. The following album "Cyclus Two" was released on November 13th 2010 and contains 8 new songs. On March 20th 2011 Nimbatus released the third record called "Cyclus Three". All albums can be heard in full length on MySpace and Bandcamp. Download is for free. The new EP "Transitions" is exclusively available on bandcamp and was released November 2011. On April 2012 a new single track was released called "Times of Yearning".

Furthermore Nimbatus has started a musical collaboration with spoken word artist Malinconica. The first song from this musical fusion can be heard in the song "Destruction". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nimbatus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
