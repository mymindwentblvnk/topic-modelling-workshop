1. A US electro-rock duo (Sierra Kusterbeck and Blake Harnage) from Port St. Lucie, FL and Brooklyn, NY. Formerly VersaEmerge but then changed names after leaving their former label Fueled By Ramen.

2.Versa is also a dubstep producer who has worked with Disciple Records, Circus Records and DPMO Records. Recognizable with his noisy, heavy and futuristic sound, he makes some reference to computer hardware in these sounds.
