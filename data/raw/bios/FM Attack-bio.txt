Originally signed by Tiga with Turbo Recordings, producer Shawn Ward (based in Vancouver, Canada) created the dreamy synthpop project "FM Attack" in 2009.

His debut album "Dreamatic" received critical acclaim leading to a publishing deal with the legendary Chrysalis Records and remixes for such artists as PNAU, Erlend Oye, Sally Shapiro, Tesla Boy, Visitor, Trans-X, Richard Marx and Tegan and Sara. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/FM+Attack">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
