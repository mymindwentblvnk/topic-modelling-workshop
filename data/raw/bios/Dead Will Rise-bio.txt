Dead Will Rise was a 5 piece Deathgrind band from Ohio, USA and were signed to Twelve Gauge Records. They flawlessly combined elements of Death Metal and Grindcore to create an arsonal of Grinding and Thrashing Brutality. The band broke up in 2008 mid tour. Some members went on to join Rose Funeral and  Struck By Lightning. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+Will+Rise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
