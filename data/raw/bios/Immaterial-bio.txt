Immaterial was a Melodic Death Metal band from Rehovoth, Israel, active between 2005-2009. The band released its debut EP "The Beast Remembers" on June 25, 2009. The bass tracks for the EP were recorded by Elad Manor of The Fading and ex-Acropolis.
It was announced on December 26, 2009, that the band would be breaking up. Bassist Hezi Menashe has then joined brutal death metal act Spawn of Evil and Guy Liloof and Maor Fisher plan to continue playing Immaterial's music with their upcoming new project.
