7 Minutes Dead, real name Alex Gulikers, is an American electronic music producer signed to Monstercat and Bitkins. He made his debut on Monstercat in 2013 with his track Sidewinder. 
In 2014, he released his Peacock EP. He also collaborated with Varien on the Mirai Sekai EP, which was released on September 1.
