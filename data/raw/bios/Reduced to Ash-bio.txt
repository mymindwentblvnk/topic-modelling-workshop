Melodic, Gothic-tinged Metal from Mikkeli, Finland featuring members of Natsipaska and Ruoska. Vocalist Patrik Mennander also sang male vocals on Battlelore's two first albums.

Reduced To Ash released one album, Club Demonique, in 1999 as well as a couple of singles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Reduced+to+ash">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
