The Hand of Glory is the dried and pickled hand of a man who has been hanged, often specified as being the left hand.

Hand of Glory is a solo endeavor of LAM also of Imperial Trumpet, Mongrel, Sanguine Eagle, Vorde, Winds of Gladsheimr, and Zarabanda Moon.

He also ran House of First Light label.
LAM passed away for unknown reason at 6th january 2021.
