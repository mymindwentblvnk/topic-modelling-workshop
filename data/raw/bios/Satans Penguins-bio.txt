Satan's Penguins is a Black Metal band from Sweden, consisting of Killerpenguin - guitar, bass, saz, and Fishbutcher - guitar. After disbanding in 2003, the group was reformed in 2006 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satans+Penguins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
