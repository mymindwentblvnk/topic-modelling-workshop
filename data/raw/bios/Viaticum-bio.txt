There are three artists under this name:
1) Black metal band from Charlotte, North Carolina, USA.
Formed in 2007. So far just released an EP, titled "Reliquary of the Horned One" under the label of Satanic Propaganda Records. Lyrical themes deal with orthodox satanism.
Current Line-Up:
Bime — Guitar / Vocals; Sariel — Vocals; Chavajoth — Bass / Drum programming

2) Death metal band from Flanders, New York, USA.
Formed in 2006. Only released a demo in 2007. Formerly known as Lynched.

3) Psychedelic rock band from Winterthur, Zurich, Switzerland.
