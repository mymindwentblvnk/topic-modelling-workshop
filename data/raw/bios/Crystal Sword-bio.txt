Crystal Sword is a thrash/melodic black metal band from Chernigiv, Ukraine, formed in 1999.

Their first gig was given in june of 2000. They played with FATAL BLADE, NATURAL SPIRIT and GATEWARD then. A month later they played also with Black Inquisition.

Current members:
Viktor Antonenko - Guitar, Vocals
Dmitrij Gaponenko - Bass
Artem Rey - Drums
Yurij Isachenko - Session Guitar

Ex-members:
Diana Garus - Keyboards
Roman Dzhoga - Drums
Pavel Khalimon - Guitar
