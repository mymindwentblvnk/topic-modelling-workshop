A brazilian thrash metal band formed 2001 in Santo André, São Paulo. They have released one demo "Attack".

Current line-up:

Cleber Orsioli - Vocals, Guitar (Andralls)
Marcio Garcia - Guitar
Bruno Amorim - Bass, Vocals
