The Swedish thrash metal band Brejn Dedd was formed in Finspång in 1988 by the young musicians Dan Swanö and Michael Bohlin. After three demo recordings they split up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brejn+Dedd">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
