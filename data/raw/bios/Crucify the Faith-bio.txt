Metal band from Finland. Music is melodic modern metal with metalcore influences. Band was founded in 2014 and spent the first 3 years releasing their first EP and playing various shows. In 2017 they decided to slow things down and plan on whats next. In 2019 they were back with some changes in their lineup and a new single 'Last Goodbye'. The band decided to delete their old songs from all platforms, because they wanted to "start from a clean table".

Members
Aki Juvonen - Vocals
Jere Kontiainen - Guitar
Valtteri Estola - Drums
