3 Days Of Silence is a Black Metal band from Switzerland formed in 2011, mixing classic BM with electronic. With its first LP "Sodium/Sulfur", 3 Days of Silence drags its listener in two distinctive landscapes. Hypnotic riffing and inventive sampling are the trademark of ambient construct "Sodium" while "Sulfur" plunges in an industrial yet melodic black metallic furnace. 


Sodium/Sulfur was produced by 3 Days Of Silence with Drop (Sybreed) and mixed by Drop in Geneva, Switzerland

The lineup consists of :
Faex - Bass, Synths
Tom - Guitars, Drums, Synth
AsCl3 - lead and backing vocals
