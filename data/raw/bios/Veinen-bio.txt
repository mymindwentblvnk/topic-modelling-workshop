The creation of Xardas from Lebanon - recorded one track, Black Hypnosis, in 2003. It was sent out to two or three individuals so they could decide if they were interested in recording the vocals.
After reading an interview with Pisk from Aska (Swe) on Mother Goat webzine, where Pisk wrote: " ... One of the things that'll be on top of the list is an ultra-monotonous, possessed Black Metal in the Von-spirit ...", Xardas immediately sent the "Black Hypnosis" track to Pisk. After hearing the track, Pisk joined in 2004 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veinen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
