Situated in Lofoten, Northern Norway. The name taken around 2000. Songs written 1995 - 1999. Recorded with bad equipment at the very beginning of the millenium. 

Draugsang means "the song of draugen". Draugen is a ghost of a dead fisherman lost at sea. When a sailor heard the song of Draugen he knew he was going to die.

Antik- vocals, guitars, programming
Orv- bass, additional guitars, programming
Sophie Isabelle-vocals

Discography:
Seil På Skyggans HavEP, 2005
Seil På Skyggans Hav/Den Sista UtfärdenSplit, 2006
