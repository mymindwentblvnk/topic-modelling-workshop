Garcharot is a Finnish Brutal/Old School Death Metal Band. Their first and till now, the only album is called Core of Despair although they have released two demos too. Their lyrics are mostly on the subjects like Cannibalism, Gore, Mutilation, Perversions, Terror.

Current Line-Up consists of :-

Lasse Heinonen - Vocals
Markus Franssi - Guitar, Vocals
Janne Korkeakoski - Guitar
Ville Koiviola - Bass
Ville Rissanen - Drums
