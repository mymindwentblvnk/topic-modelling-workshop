There are several bands known as Black Angels:

1) Black Angels: a Hard-Rock/Heavy-Metal band from Schaffhausen, Switzerland formed in 1979 which is still active today.
Releases:
* 1981: Hell machine
* 1983: Kickdown
* 1984: Black Angels (EP)
* 1985: Black and White
* 1985: Broken Spell


2) Black Angels: Heavy Metal Band from Kongsvinger, Norway which released a two track single and a demo in 1984. In 1986 they released the E.P. On the Run which was produced by former TNT singer Dag Ingebrigtsen. 

3) Black Angels: a Heavy Metal band from Kyyiv, Ukraine.

4) Black Angels: a melodic power metal band from Benguela, Angola.
