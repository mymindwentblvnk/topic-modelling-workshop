Modern death metal/deathcore band from Lithuania. Formed in 2013 from the remains of Posterity by vocalist and ex guitarist of other famous bands of lithuanian death metal scene: Saples and Paralytic.

The original lineup consists of:
Mantas Gurkšnys
Vytautas Budvytis
Daumantas Vizbaras
Dominykas Kaukėnas
Džiugas Šukys



In March, 2013, Exile Into Suffery debuted and reached national finals of Lithuania Wacken Metal Battle 2013.
