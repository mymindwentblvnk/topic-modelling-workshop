Hexenwald (initially Gallows Doom) is a black metal band hailing from Germany. The band formed in 2001, but only started releasing material in 2005. The band has released two full lengths, 2 demos and a split with Polish band, Tedd Deireadh. Lyrical themes include Occultism, Witchcraft, Violence and Death. The name Hexenwald is German for "forest of witches". 

According to Metal Archives, Nachtjaeger (guitar, vocals and lyrics) is currently located in Colorado, USA in collaboration with a group called the "Trollskogen Black Circle". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hexenwald">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
