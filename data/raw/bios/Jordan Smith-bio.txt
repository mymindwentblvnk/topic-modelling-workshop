Senatobia, Mississippi - January, 03 2011 - Jordan uses muted hooks with uncomplicated finger-picked guitar to somehow churn out undeniably gorgeous music. With standout tracks like "How Can I Live Without You" look for Jordan Smith to be a major presence in the folk landscape for years to come. 

Jordan Smith is an aspiring Singer/Songwriter from North Mississippi who is taking the plunge like so many other artist in the music industry today. With many opportunities as well as competition from others like him, it is a hard and difficult decision but one that Jordan is determined to take. His music is his number one priority in his life and it shows in such of his tracks like, 'Dearheart' and 'Smiling Heart'. He has a repetoire he has been building on the internet. From Facebook to Youtube, it can clearly be seen that Jordan Smith is bound and determined to 'get his hands dirty' and perform his dream.
