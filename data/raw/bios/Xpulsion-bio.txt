Xpulsion is a Death Thrash band from Temecula, CA. Formed in 2009, they started out as a Crossover Thrash band, but made the switch to Death Thrash with the release of their most recent demo. They have released two demos, Pizza Incision (2011) and Hunted (2013), and one EP, War is Imminent (2012). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xpulsion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
