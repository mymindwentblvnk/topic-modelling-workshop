Kurt Rockmore is an artist and entertainer from Long Island, NY. Boasting fantastic stage presence and charisma, Kurt has toured the country with artists like, G-Eazy, Mike Stud, Rockie Fresh, and many more. Kurt makes conceptual songs, that are both catchy and potent. With no gimmick, Kurt prides himself on making music we can listen to for years.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kurt+Rockmore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
