Brutart was formed in 2001 and recorded the Arab Dream EP in 2005. In 2006 signed with an indie label On Parole Productions and in the December 2007 released the album Mimic. 

The bands name comes from the outsider art principle Art Brut, and the style is  on code with the Horror Vacui artform. This would be also the name of their blog which can be founf at:  http://www.brutart.com/index.php?doc=horror <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brutart">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
