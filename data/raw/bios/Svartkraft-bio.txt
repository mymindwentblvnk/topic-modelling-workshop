Svartkraft is a black metal band from Finland.
Current Line-up:
Godslayer Vassago (Tomi Kalliola) - Instruments & Vocals

Svartkraft has released four full lengths, called "Den Onda Pesten",  "II - The Twilight Void" and "III - Lord Of Vermin" and IV Ruin.
