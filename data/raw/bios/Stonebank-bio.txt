Stonebank, real name Michael Stonebank, is an electronic music producer from Birmingham, United Kingdom. 

He has a few other aliases, such as Mike Modulate, Mike Stonebank, Modulate, and Rocket Pimp. He is also 1/2 of the duo Zero Hero along with Darren Styles.

Stonebank holds the record for most releases as the first song of the year on Monstercat, with four. (2015: Stronger, 2016: All Night, 2018: Sky Is Falling, and 2019: The Government)

His first off-Monstercat release was in 2017, and was an official remix of Delta Heavy's track Nobody But You. His first off-Monstercat single, Skank In The Rave, was released in 2020 and was a collaboration with Darren Styles on the label Rave Culture.
