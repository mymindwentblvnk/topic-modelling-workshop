Hailing from Sweden is Bishop Garden, a melodic metal band.  They released two albums: Garden of Relief in 1997 and Superpillow in 1999.  Members in the band are: Niclas Wilhelmsson on drums
Fredrik Johansson on vocals
Stefan Olsson on guitar
Johan Bergkvist on bass 
Mattias Cederlund on keyboards (only for Garden of Relief)

It should be noted that Mattias also played keyboards with Veni Domine.
