LXST CXNTURY real name Ivan Beresnevich, is a Belarusian music producer and songwriter. 

Beresnevich's production style combines elements of drift phonk, Memphis rap, ambient, and wave music. His songs tend to feature hip-hop vocal samples, often from Memphis rap songs, which is a common element of phonk music. His melodies tend to be heavily inspired by wave and ambient music, as his music often features relaxed and eerie synthesizer melodies like pads and synth leads along with heavily reverbed pianos and strings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/LXST+CXNTURY">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
