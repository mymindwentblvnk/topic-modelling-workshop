There are multiple artists known as Glacial:

1. Huge riffs, spacey sounds, epic songs. Glacial falls somewhere between Led Zeppelin, quicksand and failure. If you like like guitar oriented rock, with smart lyrics, and well thought out song writing, then this is right up your alley. Recently signed to Exigent Records, their new self Titled album will be out December 1, 2006.  

2. Glacial is also the name of a one man electronic artist (now known as GlacialSpoon from Ipswich, UK. A separate page can be found under Glacial (happysalad).
