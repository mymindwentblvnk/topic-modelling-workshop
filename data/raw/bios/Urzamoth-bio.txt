Urzamoth is a blackened thrash metal act from Belgium formed in 1999. The band split-up in 2004 but reunited in 2008. They recorded a demo in 2002 and a self-released album in 2010 "Strength, Persistence, Achievement" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Urzamoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
