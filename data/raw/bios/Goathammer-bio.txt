Goathammer is a Finnish ambient/industrial-influented black metal band from Kouvola, Kymenlaakso. It's a one man project of Roudantuoja who also plays in Metzgen.  Goathammer was found in 2005. Now Goathammer is working on a new material, new EP will be released 2009.

Releases:

Demo Anno Jew 2006
Demo Anno Jew 2006 (re-release through Wolfsvuur Records 2009)
Demo Hellish Battalions 2009
