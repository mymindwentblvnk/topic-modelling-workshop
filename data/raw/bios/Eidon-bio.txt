Eidon are a Symphonic Metal band from France formed in 2008. Their first release was 'Fantasia', an EP released in 2010, a full-length album 'Crystalight' followed in 2012.

Lineup:
Geoffrey Fourmis - Bass
Damien Morand - Drums
Thibaut Lemoine - Guitars (lead)
Sébastien Gané - Guitars (rhythm)
Cécile Vollet - Keyboards
