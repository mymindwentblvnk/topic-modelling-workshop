Formed late 2005 / early 2006, Blacklung is the brainchild of R.A. Basterfield, and is presently based in Leeds.
The second and latest E.P. has shifted attention to a more Drum & Bass approach, after originally starting in more of a chillout manner. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blacklung">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
