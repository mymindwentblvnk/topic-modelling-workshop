In June 2008, Hadoth was born. 3 Months later, a split was released with Torrance CA Band Black Plague. The 2008 tracks were recorded with one microphone to all instruments. A year later in September 2009, Hadoth re-recorded the tracks due to their inferior production, and even included a new Black Plague track with new artwork and a new logo. The 2009 tracks were recorded properly and were produced/mastered by H. In After a year long hiatus, Hadoth returned in 2011 and is now making music once again, preparing to create and record a debut. Aug 2011 saw the release of the 2009 cover EP titled "Painful Assemble". In Oct 2011 Hadoth joined Deep-Pression as a session guitarist.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hadoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
