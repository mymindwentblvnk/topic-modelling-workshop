Germ Bomb is a Swedish band from Gothenburg, formed in 2009.
Germ Bomb released their debut album Infected From Birth 2011 at Area Death Productions and was acclaimed both nationally and internationally in the music press (Close-Up Magazine, Forbidden magazine). Germ Bomb mixes metal and punk. The music is not as commercial, is relatively large in the sub-cultures in some countries such as Brazil, Germany and Japan among others. The genre called right metal punk. During a recent album Fading Sun came in 2015 and was released by Czech label DOOMENTIA Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Germ+Bomb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
