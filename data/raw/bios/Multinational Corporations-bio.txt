Multinational Corporations aka MxCx is a Grindcore band from Lahore, Pakistan. The band consists of Hassan who does vocals and writes lyrics, and Sheraz who does all the instruments as well as mixing the tracks.  Since 2010 they have released a demo tape "Equality" (2011) and "Jamat-al-Maut" EP in 2014.  The band has put their work available for free download on different internet platforms.
