Polish Club is a rock duo from Sydney formed in 2014. The duo is made up of David Novak (vocals, guitar) and John-Henry Pajak (drums). Their debut album Alright Already was nominated for the 2017 ARIA Award for Best Rock Album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Polish+Club">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
