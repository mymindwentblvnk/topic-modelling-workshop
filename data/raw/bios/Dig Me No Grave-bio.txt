Death metal band from Vologda, Russia. Formed in 2010 with a demo "Shrine of Blasphemy" released a full length album in 2014 "Cosmic Cult" on Satanath records.

Dig Me No Grave are:

Stanislav Timofeev Bass
Anatoliy Schenikov Drums
Nikita Smirnov Guitars
Anton Pyatakov Guitars
