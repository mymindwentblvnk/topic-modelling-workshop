There are multiple entries for Maki.

1) Maki can refer to the artist who performs the opening theme, I~jan! Yuujou, or い～じゃん！友情, of the TV anime series Doujin Work. She is also a prominent vocalist on the EXIT TRANCE label.

2) Maki can also refer to the completely unrelated producer affiliated with the Clothes Horse Records label out of Saskatoon, Canada. Most noted for production of the Epic and Nomad album, his discography includes collaborations with artists such as soso, Neila, Nobs, Escape Artists, Audio88, MC Homeless and others.
