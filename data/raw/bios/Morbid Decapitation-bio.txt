Hardcore-influenced death metal band from Charlemagne, Quebec, Canada. The band was active from 1986 to 1992. Released the demos "Dead World" (1987) and "Poser Slaughter" (1988), as well as a full-length named "Death Anthem" in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+Decapitation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
