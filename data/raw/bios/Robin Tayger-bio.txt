Swedish house sensation Robin Tiger Caminger, better known as Robin Tayger, is racking up millions of streams with his tantalizing melody hooks and infectious basslines. Not bothered by any limitations when it comes to underground versus mainstream, Robin keeps true to his style and is consistently pushing the boundaries for what defines house music.   Having only been in the scene for two years, the Stockholm-born house prodigy has already received personal support from the biggest stars in the world (Martin Garrix, Don Diablo and Sam Feldt only to name a few). After signing deals with Warner Music, BMKLTSCH and Black Hole Recordings - it’s safe to say the route to stardom has only begun.
