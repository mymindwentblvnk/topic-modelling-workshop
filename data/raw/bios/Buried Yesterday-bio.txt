Buried Yesterday is a brazilian deathcore band from Curitiba, Paraná. They were formed in march of 2007. Their first EP called "Only salvation for us" was released in January 2008.


Members:
Flávio (Pako) - guitars
Júnior (Catarina) - guitars
Felipe (Fê) - drums
Caio - bass
Kelvin - vocals

Ex-members:
Diego - vocal
