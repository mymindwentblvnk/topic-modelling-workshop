BIIPIIGWAN are a sludge metal band from Ontario, Canada. Born in 2010, their aggressive and abrasive, yet sometimes slow and heavy music blends elements of doom, and grind. To date, they have 3 releases; God's Hooks (2010), Nibaak EP (2012), and Something for Everyone; Nothing for Anyone (2013). All releases are available, name your price, @ http://biipiigwan.bandcamp.com/.
