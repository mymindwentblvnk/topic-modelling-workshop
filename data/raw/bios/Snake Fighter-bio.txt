Biography:

Formed in the heart of the Ozark Mountains in 2008, Snake Fighter combines elements of classic heavy metal, thrash metal, and southern hardcore to create high energy music with thundering riffs and flowing melodies. Cultivating a love for playing shows, rocking hard, and having a good time, Snake Fighter delivers nothing but the best, and never a dull moment.

Members: Austin Sanders- Guitar, B.C.- Bass, Billy Rosemund- Vocals, Eli Stillings- Drums

Hometown:Forsyth, MO
