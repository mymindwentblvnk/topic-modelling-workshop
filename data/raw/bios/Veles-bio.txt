Nationalistic Black Metal from Poland. The name Veles is named after a major Slavic god of earth, waters and the underworld. Previously the band was called Belthil. Rob Darken from Graveland consistently plays keys with Veles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veles">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
