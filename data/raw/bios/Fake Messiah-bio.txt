Fake Messiah is a female fronted Progressive Death/Groove Metal band from Switzerland who formed in 2007. In 2010 they recorded a demo EP entitled 'For A Greater Cause'  followed by the 'I Want To Scream' EP in 2011. The debut album 'Sip of Madness' was released in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fake+Messiah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
