There are 2 bands (at least) named Squealer:

1. German heavy-metal band now known as Squealer A.D..  They have since changed their name back to Squealer since the 2008 release of the album The Circle Shuts.

2. A french heavy metal band formed in 1980, which released:
- Shame (Single, 1984)
- D.F.R. (1987)
- Squealer's Mark (1989)
- This Is What The World Is All About ! (1991)
