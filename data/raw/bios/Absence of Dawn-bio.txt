Absence of Dawn was a death doom metal band from Argos, Greece, formed in 1992. They released two demos, "And the Sun Is Fading..." in 1996 and a Promo tape in 1998. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absence+of+Dawn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
