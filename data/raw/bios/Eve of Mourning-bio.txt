doom metal band from Palos Heights, Illinois, USA formed in 1991.
The last known line-up consists of:

Scott McElheney - Vocals, Guitar, Programming
Joel Leonard - Guitar
David Holland - Bass
Brian Whited - Keyboards

They released two demos:
Acheronian Winter - 1993
The Raven - 1996


If you're looking for the doom metal band from  Staten Island, New York, USA,  see Grey Skies Fallen.
