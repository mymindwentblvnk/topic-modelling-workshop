An electronic music producer and composer from Azerbaijan. Playing violin and piano since the age of six. Strongly influenced by funk / nu jazz, combining funky grooves with punchy hip hop beats. Signed for US based Cold Busted record label and released several albums to date.

Constantly hitting Beatport's top funk and chillout charts with such tracks as Woman, Southern Man, Cut A Few, El Gran Duelo, Walkin' 2gether and many more.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Akshin+Alizadeh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
