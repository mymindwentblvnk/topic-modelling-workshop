The band Vrankenvorde from Frankfurt(Oder) Germany, shoot your music now since 1994 in the world. Many faces looked at the band already. Many styles traversed the Vrankenvorde own music and wants and will still not seem to fit in any drawer.
1998 already released (the demo "Es waren die Jahre ..."). Followed in 2005 only then the album "Einst". 2007 volunteered Vrankenvorde much harder than before, returns with "Schlachtensang". Currently are working on the new album ("Menschenschicksal"), which still 2010, to appear.
