Mischief Night was a black metal/”mischief metal” band founded in 2009 by members of the prolific Baltimore-based Noxia Nox collective. They released one split album and three full-lengths before disbanding in 2013.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mischief+Night">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
