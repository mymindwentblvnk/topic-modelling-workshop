The Lazy Eyes are a 4 piece [tags]psychedelic rock[/tags] band based in [cityformed]Sydney[/cityformed].
The band are Harvey Geraghty (vocals, guitar, keyboard), Itay Shachar  (vocals, guitar), Noah Martin  (drum kit) and Blake Wise  (bass guitar).

The band started playing together in high school.  Their first official release was "EP1" in 2021.  In a band interview with Monster Children, they stated that they took their name from the lyric "She's the one with the lazy eye"  in the song "Common Girl" by Beach House.
