Axewitch was a Swedish heavy metal band founded in 1981 that began life with a thrash-metal approach and went glam-rock shortly before its demise in 1985. The lineup was Anders Wallentoft (vocals), Magnus Jarls (guitar), Mikael Johansson (guitar), Tommy Brage (bass), and Mats Johansson (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Axe+Witch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
