A split-up, two-member NSBM band from Bavaria, Germany that formed in 2002; the line-up consisted of Demon Of Darkness (vocals, guitar) and Divine Vision (drums, bass). They made three demos titled Adlig Wald (2003), Divine Wind War (2004), and The Spreading Of Sadness (2004). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/KamiKazeKrieg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
