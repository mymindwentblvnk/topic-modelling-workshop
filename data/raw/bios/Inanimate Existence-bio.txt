Inanimate Existence is a progressive death metal band from Santa Cruz, California.  They have released two full-length's since forming in 2010. They are currently signed to Unique Leader Records. The band's sound can be compared to the likes of Decrepit Birth, Arkaik. Augury, Neuraxis and Spawn of Possession. 

Cameron Porras - Guitar/Vocals
Joel Guernsey - Guitar
Max Zigman - Bass
Ron Casey - Drums
