Holy Knights is a symphonic power metal band from  Palermo, Italy , formed in  1998 . The band recorded the demo Gate Through the Past, in 1999, shortly after releasing his debut album, A Gate Through the Past, the band was put on hiatus, which lasted until 2010, in 2012, the second album, Between Daylight and Pain, was released through Scarlet Records. 

Members: 
Syl Raven - Low 
Mark Raven - Vocals, Keyboard 
Danny Merthon - Guitar 
Jorgen Claus - Drums 
Federico Madonia - Guitar
