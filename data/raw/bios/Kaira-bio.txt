Kaira (also known as Каира) are a female-fronted symphonic heavy metal band from Gomel, Russia. 

The band was formed by guitarist Paul Drozdov and vocalist Kaira Drozdova in January 2006. and released their first full-length album "The Will of Fate" in the same year. 

In 2007 the band were joined by a number of session musicians from Belarus and recorded a music video for the song ‘To the Verge of Everything’ and released their 2nd album “The Wheel of Fortune” 

Kaira's third album "The Huntress" was released in 2008. The band also recorded a soundtrack to the film “To Escape from Destiny” and wrote a song for the renowned Zapashny Brothers circus troupe  (“The Legend of the Brothers of Camelot”). 

Over a period of 3 months the band then recorded their fourth album, “Child of Fire,  which was released in 2009. Child of Fire album showcases a new style for Kaira, described by the band as Symphonic-Industrial-Sex-Metal.

Free tracks can be downloaded from their official websites at www.kairamusic.com and www.myspace.com/kairarussianband.

On June 4th 2013 Rock Alive Records released KAIRA - Not For Angels worldwide. This epic metalcore album is meant to be loud.
