Black/Death act from Seattle, Washington. Originally forming in 2012 under the moniker "Warpvomit", changing to Crurifragium in 2015, display a sound similar to bands such as Proclamation, Bestial Warlust, Blasphemy and Beherit.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crurifragium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
