Iraqi Death Police are a Grindcore / Death Metal band from Durban, South Africa. The three-piece consists of Brynn "Kadar Pussydestroyer" on drums and vocals (although he also contributes guitar, piano and bass parts on recordings), Grant "Ramiz Intestinegrater" on vocals and guitars, and Chris "Nazir Facecrusher" on guitar and vocals.

The band released their debut album entitled "Allah Will Fuck You" last year and are planning to begin recording their second album in April / May this year... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Iraqi+Death+Police">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
