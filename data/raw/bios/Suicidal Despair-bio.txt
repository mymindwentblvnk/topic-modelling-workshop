Suicidal Despair is a raw depressive black metal project from Quebec, Canada. The band was formed in 2004 by its only member "AX", who is also active in Puretée Ancestrale and Coeur Noir.

Discography:
2004 - Amertume (demo)
2005 - Hanged (demo)
2006 - Suicidal Despair (album)
2007 - Atrophie (EP)
