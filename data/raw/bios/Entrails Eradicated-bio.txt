Entrails Eradicated  are a brutal/ technical death metal band from Perth, Western Australia. They have recently released their first EP Viralocity which was recorded at Oracle Studios in April 2010.  Cadaverous Inhuman Form (single) was released in 2012.

Band members include:

Jim Parker - Guitar & Vocals

Lynton Cessford - Guitar & Vocals

 Joshua Wasley  - Bass

Tarren Whitfield - Vocals
