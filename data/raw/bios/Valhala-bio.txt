Valhala is the hard rock / heavy metal band from Pancevo, Serbia, heavily influented by the works of Ronnie James Dio and Black Sabbath.

Valhala are:
Drasko `The VIKING` Patic - vocal
Jovan Jelicic - Jesa - guitar
Vladimir Zec - bass
Zdravko `The Hammer` - drums
Nemanja Mijatovic - keybords
