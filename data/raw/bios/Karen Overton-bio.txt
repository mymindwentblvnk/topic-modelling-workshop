Karen Overton was born in 1983 in Brighton, England. She attended the K-Bis School for Performing Arts where she began writing music. After a couple years she moved to London where she began modeling. While modeling she kept writing songs and working with artists like Kelly Le Roc and Big Brovaz.

She is known for her voice in the very popular Progressive House track Your Loving Arms, the Club Mix of which was mixed into DJ Tiesto's "In Search Of Sunrise 5" (2006) and Armin van Buuren's "A State of Trance 2006". She's also a pop singer, and she writes her own songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Karen+Overton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
