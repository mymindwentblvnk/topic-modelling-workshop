Turkish heavy metal trio founded in 1998. Their debut album which was released in 2000 has 9 songs  including an instrumental  version of FAHIR ATAKOGLU's 12 MART. The band had several gigs after they released the album and also played at the Rock Station 4 festival with the lineup Arda Basoglu (Guitar), Bulent Aksoy (bass, vocal), Bugra Karabey (Guitar), Onur Ertem (drums). The band split the same year. They did not have a chance to play or produce again after the guitarist Arda Basoglu left for military obligation.  Bulent Aksoy (bass, vocal) joined Crossfire later on. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tayga">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
