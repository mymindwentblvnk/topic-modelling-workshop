(1) Hand of God is a Greek power/melodic gothic metal band formed by guitarist Theodore Ziras and the vocal sensation Marissia. The project began taking shape when Lefteris Moros (drums) and Alex G (bass) signed on.

The band is set to release a CD single, titled „Start Again”, also a full CD will be released afterwards.
