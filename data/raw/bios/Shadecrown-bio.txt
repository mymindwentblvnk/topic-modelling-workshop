Shadecrown is a five-piece melodic death metal band from the heart of Finland. The group consists of Henri Rahm on guitar & vocals, Tony Tammelin on guitar,  Saku Tammelin on Keyboards, Janne Salmelin on Bass & clean vocals and Kalle Varonen on drums. The main composer of the band is the keyboard player Saku Tammelin. 

So far the band has released 1 demo (ST) and 1 EP (Chained). The band is currently working on their first full-length album. 

The music of Shadecrown is influenced by such bands as Sentenced, Insomnium, Pantera and In Flames.
