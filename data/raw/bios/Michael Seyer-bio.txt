Currently in Los Angeles, singer-songwriter Michael Seyer was originally born in the Philippines. The 23 year old musician produces everything in his bedroom and takes pride in it. With hazy guitars, airy synths, and introspective lyrics, descriptions of his music have ranged from atmospheric, bittersweet, and emotional.
