Orthodox black metal from the UK. The band has released a rehearsal demo in 2007, called Orthodox Black Ritual and a self-released demo titled The Temple Of Diseased Flesh.

Soley written by Xeper, included various other members for session recording and live instruments, however, now relocated to California, USA, it has become just Xeper again.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Haizum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
