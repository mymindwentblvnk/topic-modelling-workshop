Ninesin (also typecast "NinesiN") is a five-piece Metalcore/Melodic Death Metal band from Seoul, South Korea. Originally formed in 2004 under the name Asiaeffect, but changed their name to Ninesin in mid-2006. They have released one EP and one full length as of 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ninesin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
