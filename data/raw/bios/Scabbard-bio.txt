Scabbard is a death metal band, formed in Spytihnev, Czech Republic in 1992.

They've released three demos:
1992 - Soudny Den (Judgement Day)
1993 - Nonsense War
1994 - Better to Die
and two full-length albums:
1996 - Beginning Of Extinction
