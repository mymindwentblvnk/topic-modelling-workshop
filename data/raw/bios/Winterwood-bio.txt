Winterwood are the New Zealand-based husband and wife duo of Zac and Holly Winterwood. They draw on their diverse musical backgrounds in classical violin, opera, and sound experimentation to create a unique blend of acoustic atmospheric folk, ambient soundscapes, and epic long-form drone pieces. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Winterwood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
