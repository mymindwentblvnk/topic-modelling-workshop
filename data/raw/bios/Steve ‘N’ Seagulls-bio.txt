Steve 'n' Seagulls is a Finnish country band, playing bluegrass versions of well-known hard rock and metal songs. The band became known in the summer of 2014 with the publication of videos on YouTube. Especially popular was the band's version of "Thunderstruck" by AC/DC which received over 25 million views on YouTube. The band's debut album Farm Machine was released through Spinefarm Records in May 8, 2015. In November 2015, Steve 'n' Seagulls were featured on The A.V. Club Undercover covering Sabotage by the Beastie Boys.
