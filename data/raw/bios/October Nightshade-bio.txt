www.octobernightshade.com

October Nightshade is a finnish band that was formed in winter 2005-2006. Band derives it's influences from various folk music, doom, progressive and black metal bands.

Recordings of the first promo CD started in summer 2009. It was released 15.01.2011 and it contained the following tracks:

    1.Tenebris Ad Infinitum
    2.Särjetty Sielu, Rikottu Ruumis
    3.Tie Tuonpuoleiseen
    4.Muistosanat

Promo was also released on myspace: http://www.myspace.com/octobernightshade
Two tracks can be downloaded from mikseri.net: http://www.mikseri.net/octobernight 
Few copies of Promo 2011 are still available and can be ordered from octobernightshade@gmail.com for a price of 5 euros.

Recordings of an album length release have started and a CD is expected to be released at the end of 2011.

Current line-up:

O. Hytönen - Vocals, Bass guitar
S. Lahtinen - Guitars
H. Niskanen - Guitars
N. Hämäläinen - Drums
I. Rahikainen - Synth
