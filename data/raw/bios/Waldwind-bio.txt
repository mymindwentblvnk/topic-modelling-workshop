Waldwind is a folk black metal band coming from Munich, Germany. They formed in 2008 and they are stiil active. This is their first release, a demo which was released in June 6th in 2009. The songs speak about nature.  "WaldWind" is "Wind in the Woods" in English.

They released their debutalbum "Lohe" in 2011. In the same year, they released their first official videoclip and played at the "Thorhammerfest II" in sao Paulo, Brazil. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Waldwind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
