Two Feet is the name of the musical project by Bill Dess (born June 20, 1993 in New York). Dess played locally in jazz and blues ensembles before forming Two Feet. After his single "Go Fuck Yourself" went viral, he signed with Republic Records. The single reached #36 on Billboard's Hot Rock Songs chart. His next single, "I Feel Like I'm Drowning", reached #1 on Alternative Songs in 2018.
