There are 2 bands with this name:

1. The Hessian (Southern German) heavy/power metal band of four "BlackDust" was founded a few years ago, mainly by Max Lassmann (b, voc) and Nicolas Keil (g), who have known each other long before. After a few changes, the present line-up has emerged in June 2004.

2. A French extreme Gothic metal band which was formed in 1993. They released one demo in 1996 and a full-length album which was recorded in 2005 (but not yet released on CD). Their music has elements of Gothic, black and thrash metal. The vocals are highly influenced by Cradle of Filth. The band is still active.
