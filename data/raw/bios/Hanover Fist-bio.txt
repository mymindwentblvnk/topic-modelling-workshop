There are two bands called Hanover Fist.

1) Heavy metal band from Canada that released two full lengths. The self titled came out in 1983 and two years later Hungry Eyes was released. Mike Terrana was the drummer of this band and later have played in  Gamma Ray, Yngwie Malmsteen, Savage Circus, Rage (Ger) and Metalium to name a few.

2)A synthpop band from Minneapolis, USA. They released a few singles, including "The Tyger" (1985), "Razor Garden" (1986), "American Dream" (1987) and "Love Kills" (1988), but no full-length album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hanover+Fist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
