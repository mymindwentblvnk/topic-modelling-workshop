I Am Three People is an instrumental rock/metal solo project from Helsinki, Finland operating on and off since late 2008. Debut album called Explore the Vertical was released on 25 February, 2012. Available as a free download at http://iamthreepeople.bandcamp.com. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/I+Am+Three+People">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
