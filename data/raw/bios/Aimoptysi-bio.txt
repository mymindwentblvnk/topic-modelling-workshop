A death metal band from Greece that was formed in 1995. Released three demos, "Horizon"  and "Dark Aimolith" in 1995, "Mortem Obire" (initially named as "666") in 1996, and a full length "Searching The Myths Of The Past" in 1996.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aimoptysi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
