Little T is a British rapper from Blackpool. He is best known for being involved in the 2016 Blackpool Grime scene. He is also known for beef with another Blackpool based rapper, Soph Aspin. He has collaborated with Afghan Dan and various other rappers. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Little+T">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
