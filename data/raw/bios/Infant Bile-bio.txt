Infant Bile are an Extreme Metal Band formed in 2005 in Birmingham England. Their songs seem to always have a humorous intro. They have guttural vocals and they use a drum machine. "Grotesque First Action" was their first album and then "Promo 05". They are currently active. They focus on themes of Wife beating, Stella and Gore.
