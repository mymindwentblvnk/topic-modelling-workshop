Sintury is  a Texas Death Metal band from Cedar Hill, USA.  They formed in 1997 and only released Disgorging the Dead in 1998.  The band consisted of Kevin Clark (vocals, guitars),  George Crounse (guitars),  Emmitt Gillaspy (bass, vocals) and  Chris Stevens (drums).  Their lyrical themes included death, gore and murder.  The status of the band is unknown and the last label they were signed to was Corpse Gristle Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sintury">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
