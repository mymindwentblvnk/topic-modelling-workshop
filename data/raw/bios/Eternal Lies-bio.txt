Eternal Lies are a melodic Death Metal band from Varberg, Sweden. They released one album "Spiritual Deception" in 2002. They reformed in 2015 and three years later released a new album "Burning the Nest", composed of songs written before the band broke up.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eternal+Lies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
