Alasdair Roberts from Appendix Out, Jason Molina from Songs: Ohia (now Magnolia Electric Co.) and Will Oldham (Bonnie 'Prince' Billy, Palace etc..). They each perform two songs individually and feature on an extra song sung by all three members entitled 'I Will be Good'.  This song is featured as a hidden track on the CD version.

The vinyl version of 'Amalgamated Sons of Rest' features an etching depicting a sperm whale, two boats and men and fish floating above or in the vinyl. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amalgamated+Sons+of+Rest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
