KIRARA is a South Korean electronic producer and DJ. Formerly under the name of STQ Project, she changed to KIRARA in 2012 because "it was too long."

Often experimental and varied in styles, KIRARA carries a unique, loud, and energetic sound across her productions. She describes her music as "pretty and strong."
