There is more than one artist with this name: 

1. Boar is a Finnish doom metal band from Oulu, Finland that formed in 2009.

Members:

Petri Saarela - guitar, vocals;
Jari Montonen (Stolen Kidneys, Visto) - guitars;
Petri Henell (Seith) - bass, backing vocals;
Kalle Huttu-Hiltunen (Arson Under the Sea) - drums.

Former members:

Toni Turtinen (Seith) - guitar.

Discography:

Boar (2010)
Veneficae (2015)
Poseidon (2018)
Hulluuden Mandala (2021)

http://www.facebook.com/boardoom

2. Boar is an American harsh noise project from Dubuque, Iowa, United States. It was started in 2006 by musician Alex Nowacki that runs also other noise projects such as Centuries Behind a Gate, I Watched You Die, and Phantom Rib.
