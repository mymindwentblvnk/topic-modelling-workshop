Mood:Doom is a doom metal band formed in January 2010, in Finland, by the members of  Scumfusion. The band is studio project band only. Music style is doom metal mixed with dark ambient / space elements. All the lyrics deal with space'n'scifi; thus the band themselves have titled the music as scifi doom metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mood:Doom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
