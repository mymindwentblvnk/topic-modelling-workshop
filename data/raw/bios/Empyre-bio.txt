Empyre, a British rock band formed in 2014.

Having known each other for years in different bands, Henrik Steenholdt (Lead Singer/Rhythm Guitar) & Did Coles (Lead Guitar) are the original founders and stem cells of Empyre.
The original four piece first entered the studio to record 'EP1’ in 2015

The band released their second EP 'Something Remains’ on 1st March, 2016

The band signed their first management deal in April 2017 with RPM
