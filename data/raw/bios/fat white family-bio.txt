(The) Fat White Family are a band from Brixton, South London, known for the dark subject matter of their lyrics. The previous band of the brothers Lias and Nathan Saudi was The Saudis, while Saul Adamczewski came out of The Metros. Their debut album Champagne Holocaust was followed by the 2016 album Songs For Our Mothers. Lias and Saul also moonlight in The Moonlandingz. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fat+White+Family">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
