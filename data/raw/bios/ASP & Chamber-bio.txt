Asp & Chamber are both Gothic bands who came together once in a lifetime to play a Tour. The tour was called "Once in a lifetime" and was recorded on the album "Humility". 

Each band has released several albums as a stand-alone group. Asp is one of the most famous German gothic bands. As is the band Chamber.
