Trysail (トライセイル) stylized as TrySail is a Japanese voice actress girl group. The members of the group are Natsukawa Shiina (Yellow), Asakura Momo (Pink), and Amamiya Sora (Blue); all of them are also voice actresses who are all managed by Sony's Music Ray'n subdivision. Their single whiz - EP reached the fourth place on the weekly Oricon Singles Chart.

In 2017, the group's single オリジナル。 ("Original") was used as the opening theme song for the anime Demi-chan wa Kataritai. TrySail moved from Aniplex to the new record label Sacra Music (both under Sony Music Entertainment Japan) in April 2017. Their single adrenaline!!! is the ending theme of the anime Eromanga Sensei.

In 2018, the group released the singles WANTED GIRL and Truth.. Both songs are used as the second opening theme of Time Bokan: Gyakushuu no San Akunin and the second opening theme of Beatless, respectively. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trysail">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
