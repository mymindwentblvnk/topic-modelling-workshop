Eloquent is :

Steven Cochran - lyrics and vocals
Randall Erkelens - music programming


Eloquent have perfected their own approach to deep-rooted influences of Pet Shop Boys and Erasure.

This duo brings you Modern Synthpop at it's best. Ultra-catchy synth hooks with great melodies, combined with thought provoking lyrics, will appeal to anyone who likes great pop songs.
