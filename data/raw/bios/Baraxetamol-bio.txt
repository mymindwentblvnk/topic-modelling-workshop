Primitive, harsh and brutal black metal from Finland. 
Formed in 2005 by Antikvlt and Terrrorist as a grindcore project, later started to grow more darker though the grindcore influences are still there.

DISCOGRAPHY:

2008: Narsissistik Misantropikal Supremacy (demo) 

2009: The Unholy Trinity (3-way split with Unatas and Land Ov Frost)
