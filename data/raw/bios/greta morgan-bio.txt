Greta Morgan (born Greta Morgan Salpeter on 12 February 1988) is an American singer-songwriter and musician based in Los Angeles, CA. Her career began in 2005 as singer/pianist of the Chicago-based band The Hush Sound. She later formed the band Gold Motel (debut album released on June 1, 2010). Together with La Sera's Katy Goodman, Morgan formed the duo Books of Love; they released the song "Space Time" in 2013. Since 2014, she has performed and tecorded under the name Springtime Carnivore. In 2018, Morgan joined the new live line-up of Vampire Weekend on keyboards, guitar and vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Greta+Morgan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
