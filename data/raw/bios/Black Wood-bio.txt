Black Wood was formed in the end of 1996 year on the ruins of Black Widow band. Featuring Skull on vocals, Nazg on strings & keys, and A. Nemerowski from Skullthrone on bass. Not to be confused with the black metal band from Peru. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Wood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
