Experience the contagious and moving sound of Red Rain. Taking its origin in New Zealand and touring the world throughout the year, this new generation rock band is committed to bringing a message of life, love and hope through their music.

Motivated by a deep love for God and a genuine desire to see His love impact the world in a real way, Red Rain travel extensively though New Zealand, Australia, Europe, USA, United Kingdom, Africa and Asia.

Playing in many different venues and locations including city events, festivals, churches, outreaches and crusades their vision remains the same. To create an atmosphere for people to realize the reality of God.
