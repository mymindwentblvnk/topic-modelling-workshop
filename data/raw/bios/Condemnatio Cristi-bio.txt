Condemnatio Cristi are a progressive/symphonic black metal band from Slovenia. The band was founded in 2009 and they have already recorded their first album in 2010 which will be entitled Incests of Vatican. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Condemnatio+Cristi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
