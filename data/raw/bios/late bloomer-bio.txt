For fans of Dinosaur Jr., Jawbox, Superchunk, Husker Du and anything that kicked a lot of ass in the 90s.

The band formed in in early 2012 and features ex and current members of Pullman Strike (NC), Meth Mountain, Horse Thief, Obstruction, Scout and Alright. 

The band has released two full lengths, s/t 2013, and 2014, "Things Change," as well as a split 7" with Old Flings.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Late+Bloomer">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
