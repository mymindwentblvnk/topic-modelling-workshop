1. Parricide is a brutal death-grind band from Poland. Characteristic with very downtuned guitars and aggressive drums-often blast beat and really raw dual kick drums.

2. There was also another Parricide from Rhode Island, USA, who released a 4 song mini-CD entitled 'A Future of Suffering' in 1995. The line-up had just 2 members, Bob on guitar, bass, vocals and programming and Bill  also on guitar. They played an energetic, vitriolic deah/thrash style of heavy metal.

3. Parricide is death/thrash metal band from Tuzla,  Bosnia and Herzegovina who released their only demo "Buried Alive" in 1991 and formed in 1990. 

4. Also a Czech band founded in 1992 existed, split in 2002. Website in czech

5. PARRICIDE – tai vilniečių grupė, užsirekomendavusi sugebėjimu sukurti brutalią atmosferą scenoje. 1993 m. grupė atidarė „Death Comes 3“ festivalį. Tais pačiais metais įrašytas ir demo albumas „Liberation“. Nors grupė savo muziką užfiksuoti įraše bandė ir anksčiau, pirmasis (ankstesnysis) demo narių nuomone buvo gana nevykęs – įrašytas kažkokioje skylėje per 7 val., tuo tarpu „Liberation“ įrašai jau atlikti „Plokštelinėje“. Sugrota dar keletas didesnių ar mažesnių koncertų. 1994 m. išleistas dar vienas demo "In Brutality".

6. UK Thrash Metal band from Nottingham who released their only demo "Self Inflicted Biopsy" in 1990.

7. UK Death Metal band from Wales who released their only demo "Infection" in 1995.

8. German death metal band from Lower Saxony who released their only demo "Inner Relation" in 1990.

9. German death metal band from Rhineland-Palatinate who released their only demo "Emotionless Hope" in 1995 and changed their name to Parryzide in 1997.
