This is a side project of Twisted Sister's vocalist Dee Snider. This band relased an album called Occulus Infernum, A Helloween Tale in 2003, which is conceptual. This project has many elements of power metal, and symphonic metal, with choirs and symphonic orchestration and synths, similar to Savatage, Therion or Rhapsody Of Fire. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Van+Helsing%27s+Curse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
