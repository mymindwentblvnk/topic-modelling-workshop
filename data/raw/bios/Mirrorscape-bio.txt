Mirrorscape is a St. Louis based Melodic Metal band and the brain child of R. Smithee and M. Stinehart. With a strong focus on good song writing, melodic hooks and Majestic Chorus's to form a unique sound. Currently working on their first full length album blending their staple sound with the ethereal qualities associated with the Post Movement. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mirrorscape">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
