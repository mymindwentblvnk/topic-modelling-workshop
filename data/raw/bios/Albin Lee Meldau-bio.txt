Albin Lee Meldau is a 27 year old Swedish singer and songwriter from Masthugget in Gothenburg, recently released the EP 'Lovers' in his own name. He is also lead singer of The Magnolia who won best soul band on Scandinavian Soul Awards 2014.

Albin was born and raised in a musical family and is the son of Annika Irene Blennerhed which is known as one of the strongest female jazz voices, and it is the voice that also distinguish him from others. A voice that can easily induce goose bumps. The music is naked and leaves the expression that his deep voice particular accents with an impressive clarity. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Albin+Lee+Meldau">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
