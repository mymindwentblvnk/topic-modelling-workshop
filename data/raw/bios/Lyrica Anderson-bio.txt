Lyrica Anderson is a singer-songwriter from Los Angeles, USA. She is signed to Mosley Music Group,  record label created by Timbaland.

In September of 2012 she released her debut mixtape titled "King Me" including 11 tracks, with features of J-Doe, A1 Bentley and Timbaland. Before it's release, first track titled "Mixtape", leaked and had Missy Elliott on it, while the one included on the mixtape album is a solo version.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lyrica+Anderson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
