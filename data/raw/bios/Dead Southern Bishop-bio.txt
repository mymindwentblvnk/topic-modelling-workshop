Dead Southern Bishop are a harsh sludge metal band from Georgia. The band incorporates elements of Southern rock, groove metal, stoner rock and doom metal into their music. Their music is available for free download on their bandcamp page: http://deadsouthernbishop1.bandcamp.com/

From their Bandcamp page: "Dead Southern Bishop was born deep in the backwoods of southeast Georgia, late 2007. In May of 2008, we played our first show and from that day forward we were possessed to spread our plague through the plastic scene surrounding us." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+Southern+Bishop">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
