ANAMAI is a freak-folk band from Toronto, consisting of Anna Mayberry (who also plays in punks HSY) and David Psutka. They released their debut LP, Sallows, in 2015, and followed it up with What Mountain in 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anamai">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
