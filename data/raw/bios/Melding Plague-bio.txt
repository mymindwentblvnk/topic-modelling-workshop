Melding Plague combines elements of extreme metal with industrial aesthetics. Essential influences are fellow northmen Immortal and Arcturus, along with industrial pioneers Skinny Puppy and Ministry. The debut album Cataclysm Archives heralds a dystopian future and apocalypse of mankind's own device. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Melding+Plague">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
