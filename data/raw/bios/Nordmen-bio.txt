Quebecois black metal whose sole member is Athros (Forteresse, Sacrenoir, Starlight Ritual, Déliquescence, ex-Teutates, ex-Winterwitch, ex-Ur Falc'h, ex-Brume d'Automne, ex-Blutskrieg).
