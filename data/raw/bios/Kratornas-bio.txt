Kratornas is a black death metal band in Cadiz City, Negros Occidental, Philippines. It started as a 3-piece band but after a short time, it ended as a one-man band. the entity behind the band is Bruno Zamora. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kratornas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
