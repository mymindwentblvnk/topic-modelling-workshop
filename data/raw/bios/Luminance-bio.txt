LUMINANCE is a project of DA (also guitarist of band Soror Dolorosa) , founded in Brussels in 2012 . The music is a mix of new wave , synthpop , electronic with cold and cloudy vibes floating around it , inspired by cold wave , synthpop , post punk , and all kinds of music from the 80' but definitely bathing in the present moment .
