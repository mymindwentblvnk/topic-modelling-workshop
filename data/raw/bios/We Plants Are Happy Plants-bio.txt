We Plants Are Happy Plants is a one-man act formed by Peter Bergmann in Budapest, Hungary in 2006. 
Since 2008, he has released 6 full albums of emotional, harmony-driven dance music influenced by space exploration, both outer and inner. Besides official remixes for great Hungarian bands such as Zagar, Peter made countless unofficial remixes for Daft Punk, Radiohead and most famously Hans Zimmer, who even shared WPAHP's remix of the song 'Time' from the Inception movie soundtrack on his own official channels. 

His most recent album, the self-titled  We Plants Are Happy plants was released on March 4th, 2018.
