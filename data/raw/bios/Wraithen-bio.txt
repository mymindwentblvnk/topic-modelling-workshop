Wraithen was a black metal band from Portland, Oregon that lasted from about 1997 - 1999.  It is unknown when they exactly disbanded. 

Wraithen was comprised of members of defunct band Vein, and vocalist Nihilist from Thy Infernal, Wraithen was short lived, but recorded two demos: Legions of Wormwood and Cult of the 11th Dawn. Wraithen also played shows with bands like Cannibal Corpse, Angelcorpse, Impaled and others. It's members went on to perform in Lord Gore, Abazagorath, In Memorium, Frightmare, Fornicator and other projects. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wraithen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
