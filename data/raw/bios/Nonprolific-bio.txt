Nonprolific is a five-piece extreme metal group from Bratislava, Slovakia. Their music is a mixture of death metal with various other more experimental and different influences along with gentle use of samples. Weird and dissonant parts are often used in their music. 

Current line up:
Kiko - vocals
Twisted - guitars, samples
Kunda - bass
Zrzo - drums
KaoS. - guitars, vocals

They recorded their first EP called Dissolution. It is available from April 2011.
