Hidden In The Basement is a four-piece southern-stoner metal band, formed in Larissa, Greece in October 2007. The band consists of Filipos Zoukas on vocals and bass, Kostas Tsiavalias on guitar and vocals, Thanos Vouvouras on guitar and Thanos Dimkos on drums.

They released two studio albums, called "Ego" (2012) and "Behind the Shadow" (2016), as well as one EP, "Hope for a New Day" (2010).

The band has made many lives throughout Greece and has shared the stage with bands such as Clutch, Sepultura, Orange Goblin, Karma To Burn , Burst and Nightstalker.
