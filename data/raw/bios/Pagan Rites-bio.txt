Pagan Rites is a black metal band from Halmstad, Sweden formed in 1992. Besides a live-album in 2000 and several smaller works they released two albums: "Mark of the Devil" in 2003 and "Pagan Metal - Roars of The Anti Christ" in 2006.
Current line-up:
Lenny Blade (Venom) - Bass
Thomas Karlsson (Unholy Pope) - Vocals
J.Voltage (Hora) - Guitar
