Nocturnal Invocation is an atmospheric black metal project from Romania. The band's sole member is Fulmineos, who also plays in Fogland, Argus Megere, Ordinul Negru, Ultima Candela and is the owner of the record label Banatian Darkness.

Releases:
2006 - "Darkrooms" (split with Ordinul Negru)
2007 - "Under Ruthven's Shroud" EP
