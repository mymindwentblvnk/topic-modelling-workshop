Chaosick is a brutal death metal band from Canada that released two demos before splitting-up. Formed in Sherbrooke, Quebec, Canada in 1992, Chaosick released In Chaos for Eternity in 1994 and Kill The Corruption in 1995.

Last known line-up included,

Steve Fontaine - Guitar 
Louis Plante - Drums 
J-F Truchon - Bass

Former/past members,

Donald "Bimbo" Boisvert - Bass
Patrick "Tex" Cantin - Drums
Stephane Côté - Guitar
Pascal Langlois - Vocals
