Firstryke was a collaboration of two of the best Tulsa, Oklahoma rock bands in 1984.  The band toured the midwest United States throughout the 1980's and record an album titled Just A Nightmare in 1986.  Firstryke was the opening act for Metallica on the Master of Puppets tour as well as opening for many other major rock acts.  Firstryke rereleased Just A Nightmare on Retrospect Records and also on a 25th anniversary limited edition collectors CD.  Firstryke played Rocklahoma 2009 and have signed to Rocklahoma 201.  They are in talks to play many other future shows and festivals.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Firstryke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
