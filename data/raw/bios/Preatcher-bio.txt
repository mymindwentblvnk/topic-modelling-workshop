Preatcher is a brazilian band from Pernambuco state (Northeast region) formed in middle of 2002 by Pedro Henrique (ex-Triumph Divine and Stay Wild) and Vinicius Barros (ex-Abhorrer and ex-A Gothic Heaven) together with Leandro Tavares in Bass, Leonardo Tavares on guitar and Pablo Jr. on vocals ones. 

Two years later Leonardo, Leandro and Pablo give post to the Raj Ranjan on lead guitar, Leonardo Silva (ex-Triumph Divine) on Bass e Raphael Dantas (ex-Triumph Divine and ex-Steel Blade) on vocals. The band released a EP "FACE THE FACTS", in september of 2006. 

Influences: Running wild, Grave Digger, Destruction, Overkill, Kreator, Accept, Iron Maiden, Saxon, Mercyful Fate, Sanctuary, Judas Priest, Metal Church, among others.
