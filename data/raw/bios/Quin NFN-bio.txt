Quinlan Sharif McAfee (born January 18, 2001), better known by his stage name Quin NFN (formerly Quincho), is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from Austin, Texas. He is best known for his singles "Talkin' My Shit," "Straight Thru," and "Poles" featuring NLE Choppa. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Quin+Nfn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
