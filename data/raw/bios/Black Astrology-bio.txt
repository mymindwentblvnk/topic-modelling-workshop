Black Astrology is an Russian underground avant-garde (some may call it black death metal) black metal act. They started off in 2003, in St.Petersburgs, and are still active today, even though they have only released one full album, entitled "Kingdom Of Ghosts" [2005]. They are currently signed to Taiga Records.

Members:

Kill - Vocals (Sanctum Regnum (Rus))
Nik Golubev - Guitar (Koma, Boney NEM})
Artem Serebryakov - Guitar (Koma)
Sergey Zelenyuk - Bass (Bestial Deform)
