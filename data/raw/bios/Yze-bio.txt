Yze is a psychedelic black/doom metal project from Kentucky. Taking a lead from the second wave black metallers, he has produced an EP that combines the atmosphere of Burzum with musical stylings in the vein of early Pink Floyd. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
