Josh Kramer is an indie composer and musician from Ohio currently residing in Turkey. His cinematic sensibilities have been described as a mix of Thomas Newman, Max Richter, Rachel Portman, and Alexandre Desplat. His work has been used in productions around the globe including dance, short film, documentary, and various commercial spots. Most recently, his work has premiered alongside Olafur Arnalds and Nils Frahm in a series of major ballet performances at the National Theatre of Japan in Tokyo.
