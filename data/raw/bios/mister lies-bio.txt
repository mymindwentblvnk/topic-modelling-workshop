New York-based producer, composer and sound artist Nick Zanca has been releasing music under the name Mister Lies since 2012. Over the years, his sounds have inhabited a specific intersection between intimacy and atmosphere through a maximalist mosaic of field recordings, sample-based textures, glitchy soundscapes and nocturnal pop songs.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mister+Lies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
