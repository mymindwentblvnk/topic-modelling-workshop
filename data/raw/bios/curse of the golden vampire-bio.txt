In 1997-1998 Alec Empire & Techno Animal collaborated on the album The Curse of the Golden Vampire.

This 2003 follow-up was created solely by members of Techno Animal, performing under the name Curse of the Golden Vampire, without assistance from Alec Empire. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Curse+Of+The+Golden+Vampire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
