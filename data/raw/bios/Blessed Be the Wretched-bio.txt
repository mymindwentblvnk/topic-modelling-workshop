Blessed be the Wretched is a black metal/grindcore band formed in 2006 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Their current line-up is: 
Logan - Vocals
Kyle the Vile - Guitar
Floyd "Boo Boo Wretched - Guitar
Jason Wretched - Bass
Mop - Drums
