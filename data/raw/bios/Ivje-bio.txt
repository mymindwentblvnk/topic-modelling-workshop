Ivje is black metal band heading from Menges, formed in 2001 by guitarist Tine Bregar and drummer Tine Horvat under name Malison. Tilen Benda joined in 2002 as second guitarist. Soon after Andraz Mocnik was recruited for bass guitar. After a while, Andraz left the band. In the beginning of 2004 Luka Prijatelj joined as new bassist and Ziga Lumpert as vocalist. In one year, band changed name to Ivje. Band is curently working on their first demo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ivje">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
