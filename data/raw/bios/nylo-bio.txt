NyLo (easier to find under HateNylo) is a LA based singer songwriter from Chicago signed to Epic Records. She released her debut EP "Memories Speak" last year followed by "Indigo Summer" released in July of 2013. She is planing on releasing a follow-up mixtape titled "Indigo Rose" this fall.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nylo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
