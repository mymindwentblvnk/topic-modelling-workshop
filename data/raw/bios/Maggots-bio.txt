1) Maggots was a Thrash metal band from Switzerland.

2) Maggots was a Brutal death metal band from France.

3) Maggots is a  Death Metal band from Germany.

4) Maggots was a Brutal death metal band from Holland.

5) Maggots is a death metal band from Mexico

6) (The) Maggots were a punk rock ‘n’ roll band from Ortonville, Michigan, USA that existed in the 1990s. They were one of the central bands of the OVRR (Ortonville Rock ‘n’ Roll) scene with all three members spitting off, joining other bands. (Pat Witherup [Negatives, Rulers, Left Out], Johnny Bourbon [Plan 3] and Jack Virginia [Rulers, Left Out, solo]). All three members were also founding members of the Vel-Crows and Sloppy Mothers. Their major release was 1996's Ghettoblaster.
