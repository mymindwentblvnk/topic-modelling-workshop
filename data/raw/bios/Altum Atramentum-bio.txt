Altum Atramentum is a Black Metal band formed in Germany (Saxony) in 2008.  

Current line-up: 
Maik "Flecky" Fleckeisen - Drums (Persecution (Deu), Black Swan (Deu), Coldun)
Robby Kunz - Guitar (Andras, Persecution (Deu))
Michael "Micha" Schneider - Bass (Seelenschmerz, Rhetorical)
Matze - Guitar (Dawn of Eternity (Deu))
Enrico Wolf - Vocals (Seelenschmerz)
Alex Bratsche - Violine (Dawn of Eternity (Deu))
