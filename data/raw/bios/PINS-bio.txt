PINS is a new wave/alternative/metal band from Ukraine, founded in November 14, 1999.

PINS are an all-female three-piece who make noisy, textured pop, mixing their stated influences of Hole, The Jesus and Mary Chain and Beach House into a thrilling brew. PINS are singer/guitarist Faith Vern, guitarist Lois MacDonald and bassist Kyoko Swan. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
