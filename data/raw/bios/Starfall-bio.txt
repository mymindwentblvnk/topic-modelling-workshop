Starfall is a 17-year-old vocalist and producer from Hawaii. Inspired by his artist friends, Starfall was motivated to make music in late 2018. Although his sound currently leans towards pop and R&B genres, Starfall prefers to experiment and try new things. His ethereal voice can be heard on collaborations with quannnic, Noahh, AViT, and more, demonstrating his ability to adapt to any sound. Starfall hopes to eventually put out a larger project as his growth continues. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/starfall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
