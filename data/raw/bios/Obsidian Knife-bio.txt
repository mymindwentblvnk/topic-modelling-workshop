Obsidian Knife is a Cologne (Germany) based band that plays black metal.
You can listen to their first tape at their bandcamp site and also there's a free streaming on youtube.
Their tapes are very unique and all of them are handpainted. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obsidian+Knife">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
