Burn The Yeti is a DIY female fronted doom metal band from the UK, formed in March 2010 by Jim Males and Bruce Hamilton and takes influence from traditional doom metal, sludgecore, female fronted synphonic metal, blues and punk rock to name a few. 

After a year of searching for a line and writing material the band started work on recording an EP - Out of the darkness, its out now, please message the band on facebook to get your copy!
