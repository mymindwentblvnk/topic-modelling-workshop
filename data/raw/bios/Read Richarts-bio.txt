Alabama born, Texas raised, Read Richarts started writing around the age of 11. Now in his early 20's, its became a passion and lifestyle with the desire to succeed. A realist as well as a dreamer, he knows that you only get as far as you drive yourself, and he's looking to go all the way to the top. He is now writing and producing his debut mixtape "Read Between The Lines" which should be available soon. He is also writing for other local R&B artists, focusing on his his own production and publishing company. Hoping to make a name for himself, and his city, he welcomes the challenge and thrives off pressure. His influences include artist such as Nas, Jay-Z, Lupe Fiasco, Kanye West and many others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Read+Richarts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
