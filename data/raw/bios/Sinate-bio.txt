Sinate was formed by brothers Matt Sheppard and Sam Sheppard in 2002 in Auckland, New Zealand. Matt and Sam are also ex-members of the death metal band 8 Foot Sativa. Sinate's current lineup is: Matt Sheppard (vocals and guitar), Sam Sheppard (drums), "Munro" (bass) and Matt Fawcett (guitar). Their first full-length "Beyond Human" was released in 2005 and "Violent Ambitions" followed in October 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sinate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
