Deadsoil is a german Hardcore / Metalcore band which were founded in 2002 by Boris Pracht and Christian Bass. The band contains of Friedrich Weber ( Vocals ), Boris Pracht ( Guitar ), Jens Basten ( Guitar ), Andreas Schüssler ( Bass ) and Christian Bass ( Drums ).

Stefan Eutebach, the former bass-player left the band to concentrate on his "normal" Job. He was replaced by Andy Schüssler, known from "Since the day" and "Reincarnated".

Deadsoil's newest album, "Sacrifice", was released on 26.05.06.
