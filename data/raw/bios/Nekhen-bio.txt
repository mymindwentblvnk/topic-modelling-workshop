Italy (2014 – present)

Nekhen is an experimental metal group from Italy with dark ambient influences.

Since the group's inception in 2014, they have released two albums. Their EP Entering the Gate of the Western Horizon in 2015, and in 2017 their full-length album, Akhet. Seth Peribsen (named after the 2nd dynasty ancient Egyptian pharaoh) is the sole member of the group. He describes Nekhen as, "… mixing metal and ethnic musical genres with ancient Egyptian themes and spirituality."
