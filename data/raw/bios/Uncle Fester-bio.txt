Uncle Fester is a "junk rock" band from Blackpool , Lancashire, England formed in 1999 with Rat Fink (ex-Alien Sex Fiend) on drums, Dunk Rock on vocals, Johnny Volume on guitar and Chad Spandosa on bass. The band released the albums "Frump Experiments", "Total Sonic Mayhem" and "Offensive" before changing their name to UFX in 2006 

The name Uncle Fester was taken from the member of the fictional Addams Family as played by Jackie Coogan (famous as Charlie Chaplin's sidekick in The Kid, 1921), in the television program, and by Christopher Lloyd in the two movies, and by Pat Thomas in Addams Family Reunion.
