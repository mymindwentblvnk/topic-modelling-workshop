Funeral doom metal band from Easthampton, MA, USA. Originally a one-man band of Josh Moran that began in 2011, a full band of musicians was brought in in 2013 to complete a recording and touring line-up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vacant+Eyes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
