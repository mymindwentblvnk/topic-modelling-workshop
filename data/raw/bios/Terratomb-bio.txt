One of the most well known extreme metal bands to come out of Halifax, Nova Scotia. After a lengthy period of inactivity, Terratomb returned with the release of their second full length "Rebirth Through Destination" in late 2006.

Their current lineup Mike Fedora on vocals(played bass until 2006), Clayton Hart on Guitar, J Quinn on guitar (live guitarist for Dead Brain Cells), Ross Vicars on Bass (played drums until 2006, also of Thy Flesh Consumed and Maelstrom) Graham Ferguson on drums (also of Hellacaust, ex-Realms of Dispair)
