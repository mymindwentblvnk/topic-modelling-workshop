Antemasque is a USA band of ex-The Mars Volta members: producer Omar A. Rodriguez-Lopez (also ex-At the Drive-In), Cedric Bixler Zavala (also ex-At the Drive-In) and drummer Dave Elitch, together with Michael Balzary (bass - aka Flea of Red Hot Chili Peppers). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antemasque">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
