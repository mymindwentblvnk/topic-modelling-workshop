Yajin is an independent Austrian music producer.
It hisses, crackles and pops. It plucks and whistles. Underneath are hypnotic beats and sometimes a poppy voice emerges. This is the delicate and experimental music of Yajin. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yajin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
