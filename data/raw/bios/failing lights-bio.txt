NOISE project started individually by Mike Connelly, who also plays in: Wolf Eyes, Hair Police, The Cove, Entry, Human Liberation Technology, Lasting Hunt, Mike Connelly, New Device, Stoning, Under Cloven Spell, Zombie, etc ... NOISE all of them oriented projects. Their discography is super wide (over 20 albums), plus 20 splits with other bands NOISE (Sick Llama, Basket Case, Jason Zeh, Religious Knives, etc..). And parallel in 2000 created his own label to distribute their recordings: "Gods Of Tundra".
