Noekk is the duo F.F. Yuggoth (Markus Stock - Ulf Theodor Schwadorf) and Funghus Baldachin (Thomas Helm), both of Empyrium. They specialize in a dark and symphonic style of progressive metal.

To date they have made three albums: The Watersprite (2005), The Grimalkin (2006) and The Minstrel's Curse (2008). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Noekk">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
