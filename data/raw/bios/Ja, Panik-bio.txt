JA, PANIK (German for: Yes, Panic) is an Austrian Indie-Rock-Band consisting of Andreas Spechtl (vocals, guitar), Stefan Pabst (bass) & Sebastian Janata (drums).
They recorded 5 albums: "Ja, Panik" (2006), "The Taste And The Money" (2007), "The Angst And The Money" (2009), "DMD KIU LIDT" (2011) and "Libertatia"(2014)
