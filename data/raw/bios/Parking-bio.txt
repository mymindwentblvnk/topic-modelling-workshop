One man band project from a spanish musician. During the 90's he was involved in various alternative and indie bands. Left music for quite a while and come back with this solo recording (Parking).

Technicaly everything was recorded with a 4 track recorder, trying to strectch its possibilities to a maximum not involving any piece of technology other than a guitar and a microphone. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parking">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
