Offlaga Disco Pax are an Italian spoken-word band from Reggio Emilia, Italy.

They are:
Enrico Fontanelli: bass, keyboards, graphics, weak thought
Daniele Carretti: guitar, bass, quinquennial loan
Max Collini: vocals, lyrics, ideology
