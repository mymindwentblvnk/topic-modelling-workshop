Dawn of Glory awoke from slumber in the frosty winter in the year 1999, although the first tendencies of a proper band could be seen already in Autumn the very same year.  Dennis Nielsen,  Max Hult and Rasmus Bäckström started to jam together.

Current members:
Petter Nilsson - Vocals and guitar
Dennis Nielsen - Guitar
Linus Nielsen - Bass
Matthias Klauser - Drums

Free downloadable songs and updates on the band can be found on their site www.dawnofglory.net
