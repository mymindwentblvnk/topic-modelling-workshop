Rainwound is a progressive rock / metal band that formed in 2005. They released an instrumental demo version of Shrouded Destiny. The full vocal version was released in 2006. They followed that up with the release of The Ivory Chapel in May of 2009.

Rainwound released their United EP on 9/11/11, and a Deluxe Edition on 9/11/12 which is available on iTunes, Spotify, Amazon MP3, and other digital distributors.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rainwound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
