Atlanta native KEY! (real name Marquis Whittaker) has been making waves in the music scene for some time. He is one of the visionary artists responsible for Atlanta hip-hop group Two-9 and has been an early collaborator with artists like Playboi Carti, ILoveMakonnen, OG Maco, A$AP Mob, as well as producers like TrapMoneyBenny, Mike WiLL Made-It, and FKi 1st. KEY! is most known for his album "777", a collaboration with Kenny Beats, containing some of his biggest hits to date. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Key%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
