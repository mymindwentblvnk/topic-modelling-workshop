Veilburner is an Experimental Blackened Death Metal duo from PA. Formed in 2013, they have released 4 full length albums, the first 3 comprising a trilogy on the subject of transhumanism.Their experimentation with dissonant melody, psychedelic and quasi-industrial overtones in a black/death metal context mirror their lyrical themes of esoteric storytelling and arcane subject matter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veilburner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
