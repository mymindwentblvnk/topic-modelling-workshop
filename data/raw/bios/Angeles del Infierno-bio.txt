Angeles Del Infierno is a Heavy Metal band from Spain, formed in 1980. They achieved success in their home country and Latin America during the 1980s and the early 1990s. In 2003, the band reformed and signed to Warner Bros, releasing Todos Somos Angeles later that year.

Band members

    * Juan Gallardo : Voice
    * Robert Alvarez: Guitar
    * Ix Valieri: Guitar
    * Foley: Bass
    * Gerardo García: drums
