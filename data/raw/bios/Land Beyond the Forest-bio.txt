Land Beyond the Forest was a black metal band from Saint Petersburg, Russia.
They formed in 1996, and split up in 2000.  They released one demo, entitled "On Road to frozen Stars", and a split album with Древедь entitled "Страхокрест". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Land+Beyond+The+Forest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
