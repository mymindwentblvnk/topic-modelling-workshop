Formed in 2007, Calgary area trio Bloated Pig play beer fueled sludgy Heavy Rock / Metal with an in your face attitude. Band members include: Hemlocks Wolfbane - Bass, Wilhelm - Drums, Vaahgner - Guitars and general verbal abuse.
