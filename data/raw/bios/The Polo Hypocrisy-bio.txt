The Polo Hypocrisy is a Canadian melodic death/modern metal band from Montreal. Formed in 2004, recorded 4-songs demo in 2005.

Alex - vocals
Dave - lead guitar
Eric - rhythm guitar/vocals
Danny - bass
Derek - drums

Split up in may 2007.
