There are several artists named Hospice:
1. Hardcore punk band from Boston, Massachusetts.  http://hospice.bandcamp.com/
2. A progressive death metal band from Baranovichi, Belarus, active from 1991 to 1996.
3. A Symphonic Black Metal band from Volgograd, Russia formed in 1997. Thus far, they have only released one album, "Land of Eternal Night" released in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hospice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
