Amendfoil's music is a combination of metal, metalcore and stoner rock. Amendfoil were formed in Äetsä, Finland in 2007. Amendfoil relseased two full length albums, Skyline Escape in 2013 and Empyrean & Ophidian in 2016. In 2019 they changed their name to Palehorse.
