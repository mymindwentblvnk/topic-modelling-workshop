Swearin' is an American band based in Philadelphia comprising singer/guitarist Allison Crutchfield (ex-P.S. Eliot), singer/guitarist Kyle Gilbride, bassist Keith Spencer, and drummer Jeff Bolt. The band released two albums and an EP before initially splitting in 2015 (with Crutchfield embarking on a solo career). In November 2017, Swearin' reunited.  

Some of the band member's other musical endeavors include: PS Eliot, Big Soda, Bad Blood Revival, Bad Banana, Big Eyes, Dear Marje, and a number of others.  Having been involved with with the DIY scene for a long time, the band record their own songs, make their own merchandise, and they self-released their first tape "What a Dump" in 2011.
