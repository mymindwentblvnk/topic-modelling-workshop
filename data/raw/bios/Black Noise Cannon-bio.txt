Black Noise Cannon is a sludge band from Seattle, Washington. Members include E.Greenwalt (formerly of Christdriver and Subvert), P.Petrocelli (formerly of 3D House of Beef), B.Ward (formerly of Christdriver), R.Webb (of Knife Cuts Voice). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Noise+Cannon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
