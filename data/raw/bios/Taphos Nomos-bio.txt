Formed in early 2015, Taphos Nomos is a death/doom metal band from Pittsburgh, PA.

Their debut recording, "West of Everything Lies Death" was released on Caligari Records.

Members are :

Tectonic Mastication:Bass, Vocals (2014-present)
Arthur Rainnbridge:Drums, Percussion (2014-present)
Canyon Shifter:Guitars, Vocals (2014-present)
Sylvan Spectre:Synths, Vocals (2014-present)
