Upstate New York native Michael Okon, better know as Rawhead, is a music producer and performer, as well as co-founder of Sub-Bombin Records. Musical styles range almost as much as his influences, from the, raw, lo-fi hip hop sound found on his Beatwise & Alchemy series to the textural, ambient soundscapes heard on his 2011 LP, Psychopomp.
