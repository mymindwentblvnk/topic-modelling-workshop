EDGEND began its activity in 2005 as a heavy power metal band with a neo- classical touch and symphonic composing. The name 'EDGEND' is a portmeanteau of the words Edge End meaning the sharpest point of the blade.

At the end of 2006, EDGEND released a demo titeld “Attached by Insanity”, after recording 4 songs with their former vocalist.

At 2008 work began on the first album,  "A New Identity".
The album was produced by Kevin Codfert and now its
available by Nightmare Records worldwide.

Band Members:
Rami Salmon - Vocals
Ben Metal - Bass
Asaf Rosenberg- Drums
Sharon Halachmi - Guitar
David Ezuz - Keyboard
