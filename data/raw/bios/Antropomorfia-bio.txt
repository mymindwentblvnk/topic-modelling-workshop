Antropomorfia were a brutal death metal group from Madrid, Spain. The band formed in 1989 and are considered to be the first Spanish death metal band. After 14 years, the only remaining band member released the debut album Engendro in 2003. Antropomorfia is currently disbanded.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antropomorfia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
