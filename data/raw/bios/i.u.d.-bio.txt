There are 2 artists named I.U.D. 

1) I.U.D. was a punk // new wave // post punk group from Orange, California; Leslie Barrie and Zoo Barrie were its members.  The duo self-released  a 7" ep in 1981 that contain the songs "Precious", "Charm" and "Bon Appetit". 

2) I.U.D. is the duo of Lizzi Bougatsos (Gang Gang Dance) and Sadie Laska who make, in the words of Artforum, “minimal, contagious noise-music on two drum kits and two microphones.”  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/I.U.D.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
