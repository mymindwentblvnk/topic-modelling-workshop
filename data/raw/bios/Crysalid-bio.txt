Crysalid is a band hailing from Saint-Jean de Tholome, France, near Geneva, Switzerland. Crysalid's music is an energetic blend influenced by nu-metal and progressive metal.

Formed in 2003, the band released one album, Come Closer, in 2008, the band announced its split in June 2009.
