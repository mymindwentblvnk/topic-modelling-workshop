Several artists by the same name:

1) Symbiote is a metal band from Prince Albert Saskatchewan. Formed in January 2006, Symbiote plays a mix of many heavy metal genres, primarily thrash, death, and black metal.

2) Demoscene artist. Released an eponymous psytrance web-EP in 1997. 

3) Symbiote is a Hardcore Punk band from Atlanta, Georgia. They have released two EPs (self titled, 2018 & Privilege of a Daydream, 2020) and a split cassette (P.D.C., 2019). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/symbiote">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
