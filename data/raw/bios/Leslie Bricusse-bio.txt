Leslie Bricusse (born 29 January 1931) is a British lyricist and composer.

Although best known for his partnership with Anthony Newley, Bricusse has worked with many other composers such as Henry Mancini and Frank Wildhorn. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leslie+Bricusse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
