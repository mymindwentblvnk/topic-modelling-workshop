Fibroma is a live project by Don Campbell (USA, Georgia) consisting of sound/feedback looping and manipulation as well as guitar playing and delayed analog synthesizers, gameboys, static and wall noise, television and radio, clarinet, kitchen appliances anything he can get his hands on.
