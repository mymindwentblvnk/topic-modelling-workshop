Caducity are an old-school death metal band from Belgium.  The band released two albums in the '90s, both classics in the underground death metal realm.  They combine influences from Bolt Thrower, Napalm Death, and Dismember into a sound of their own, complete with fantasy-based song titles and concepts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Caducity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
