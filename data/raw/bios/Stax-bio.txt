There are a couple of artists who use the name Stax.

1. Stax is a demoscene music composer who produced music on an Atari Falcon with a rave style. Lost Blubb is a demo for the Atari Falcon that features music composed by Stax.

2. Stax was an early eighties act who recorded hip hop for the Philadelphia record label P&P owned by disco producer & remixer Patrick Adams.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stax">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
