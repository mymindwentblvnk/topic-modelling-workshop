Technical brutal death metal in the veins of Morbid Angel, Suffocation, Hour of Penance with some influences of scandinavian death and black metal.

The Malice started as a project in 2014. The vision was to create a band that plays the american style of death metal like Deeds of Flesh, Nile, Hate Eternal, Morbid Angel among others.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Malice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
