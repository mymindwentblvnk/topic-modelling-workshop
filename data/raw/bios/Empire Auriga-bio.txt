Empire Auriga are a black metal band from Lansing, Michigan in the USA. Formed in 2008, they released thier debut full-length Auriga Dying in the same year on Moribund Records. They play black metal with martial industrial influences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Empire+Auriga">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
