Snoezelen (2009-present) are an instrumental trio from Perth, Western Australia. The group's characteristic guitar-driven sound features soft/loud dynamics and minimal vocals reflecting the influence of The Microphones, Do Make Say Think, Slint, Slowdive, Carissa's Wierd, Early Day Miners and Fugazi.

Snoezelen is also czech rock band from Cvikov.
