Winter Of Agony is a melodic black metal band.
The members are from Quebec City in Canada,
and the band was created in 2004.
Their first demorecording is done, and it's now on sale.
 

Nicolas Garneau - Guitar, Vocals
Francis Latulippe - Lead Guitars
Renaud Fortin - Drums (ex-Twisted Minds)
Symon Marcoux - Bass
Alexandre Garneau - Keyboards
