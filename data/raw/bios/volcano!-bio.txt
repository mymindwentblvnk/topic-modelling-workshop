volcano! is a three-piece rock band from Chicago, Illinois formed in 2005. It draws influences from classic rock, post-punk, free jazz and noise rock. 
Lineup: Mark Cartwright (bass), Sam Scranton (drums), Aaron With (vocals).

The Leaf Label released the trio's critically acclaimed debut, Beautiful Seizure, in the USA in late 2005 and worldwide in early 2006. In 2006 the band did a Take-Away Show session with Vincent Moon.

As of 2020 the band is inactive.

Sam Scranton creates experimental/spoken word/ambient music.
