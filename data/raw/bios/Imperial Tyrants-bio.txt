Imperial Tyrants is a black metal band from Singapore, that formed in 2005.  In 2007, they self-released their demo entitled 'Tyrannikal Inquisitions'.  In 2008, they released a split entitled 'Amalgamation of Imperial Demonization' with Infernal Execrator. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Imperial+Tyrants">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
