Los Wálters es un colectivo de ideas electro-musicales que surge de la colaboración inalámbrica entre San Juan, Puerto Rico, y Philadelphia, EEUU. Este experimento a larga distancia se distingue por la fusión de sonidos oscuros, melodías brillantes y voces distorsionadas, elementos de donde emergen recuerdos de los comienzos de la música electrónica sazonados con un toque caribeño. 

This is a project made in 2011 with a lot of love between Puerto Rico and Phily... Now the work is being made between Phily and Barcelona, because live changes and we need to move a little. Two Puertorrican tropical dudes that have there own lives in different cities decided to connect with soft/strong experimental melodies but, with voices in spanish to make it with the elegance of that language that we appreciate very much.
