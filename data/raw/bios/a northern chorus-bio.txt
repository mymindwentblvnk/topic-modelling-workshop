A Northern Chorus was a Canadian band from Hamilton, Ontario. They released four records on the Sonic Unyon label, Before We All Go To Pieces, Spirit Flags, Bitter Hands Resign and The Millions Too Many. Before We All Go To Pieces was originally released by Black Mountain Music. A Northern Chorus disbanded in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+Northern+Chorus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
