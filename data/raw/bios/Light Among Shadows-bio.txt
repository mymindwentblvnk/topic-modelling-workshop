Light Among Shadows is a metal/gothic band formed in Algeciras, Cádiz, Spain in 2010.  Their style is a mixture of Gothic-Symphonic Rock with some of the New Progressive metal tendencies.  The band was formed by the German female singer Tatjana Klee and 3 Spanish musicians, Adrien Fowl - Lead Guitars,
Alejandro Tineo - Drums and Christopher Red - Bass.

In March, 2011, they released their first album, called “At the Gates of Dawn”. This album was recorded at Prometeo Records, Cádiz (Spain), and produced, mixed and mastered by Antonio López. The album recevied great reviews from the media, getting a mark of 88/100

After the release of "At the Gates of Dawn", the band toured around Spain, supporting several Spanish major bands, such as Saratoga, Legendaria, V3ctors and Phoenix Rising.
