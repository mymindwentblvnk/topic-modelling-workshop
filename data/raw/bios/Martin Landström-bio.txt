Since moving to Stockholm in the late nineties Martin Landström (a.k.a Martin Persson) is regarded as one of the hardest working musicians/arrangers/producers in the business. His track record includes over 400 songs as arranger for the TV-show “Glee”, In-house producer for the most popular television programme in Sweden “Melodifestivalen”, touring musician with Belgian/Canadian artist Lara Fabian, arranging and composing alongside Rasmus Faber for the Japanese anime show “Asterisk Wars”, music producer for "Mamma Mia! The Party”, working with Björn Ulvaeus of ABBA, numerous musical commitments for international TV-shows (American Horror Story, American Crime Story, Feud, Kally’s Mashup and others) and also music for games and apps (Planet Coaster, Toca Boca). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Martin+Landstr%C3%B6m">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
