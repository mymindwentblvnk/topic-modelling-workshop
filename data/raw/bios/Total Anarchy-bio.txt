Total Anarchy is an Indonesian Hardcore Punk band originally from Bekasi, West Java, formed in 1995. The band's sound has evolved considerably through out the years of playing, taking on a much lighter brand of hardcore punk with thrash-metal influences as the years passed. The second full lenght album "Frustasi Global" released on September 2009 under Suburban records are fully armed with 10 tracks of aggresive hardcore punk songs contained social critics, war and all shits they can get.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Total+Anarchy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
