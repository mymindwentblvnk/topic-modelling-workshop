Bible Belt Byproduct
Death doom/sludge band from Atlanta, GA.
Atlanta heavy hitters who shared the stage with Corrosion of conformity, Crowbar, EYEHATEGOD, Morbid Angel, Six Feet Under, Obituary, Clutch & many more.

Released one extremely heavy Debut CD in 1998 on Reprobate Records.
