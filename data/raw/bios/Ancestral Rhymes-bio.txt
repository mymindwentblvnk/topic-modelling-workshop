Ancestral Rhymes is a Hellenic Black Metal project from Trikala, Greece consisting of 2 members. 

Dimitris: All Instruments, Programming, Vocals
Sophia: Keys

Ancestral Rhymes plays Epic, Atmospheric Black Metal.
