Guf is the stage name of Alexey Dolmatov, a Russian rapper. Alexey was born 1979/09/23 in Moscow. He started his carrier in 2002 in project called Rolex-x.
He realized that he has to move and opened a new project with his friend Princip, the project called Центр (Centre), in 2004.
Started in 2002 Guf is working under his own album "Город дорог" which saw the light in April 2007.

Discography:

1. Город дорог (2007)
2. Дома (2009).
