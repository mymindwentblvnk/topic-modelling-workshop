An Improv/Jazz band in the style of AMM and similar artists. They were Paul and Limpe Fuchs from Bavaria, who, alongside Tangerine Dream, recorded for the German Ohr label back in the sixties. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anima+Sound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
