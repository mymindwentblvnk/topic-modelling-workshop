NIHIL NOCTURNE entered the black underground in 1994 when they founded the still infamous SOLUZEN Magazine. After publishing five issues they stopped releasing it in 1998. Two more years passed until the first demonstration tape "Black Hatred" was recorded in December of 2000. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nihil+Nocturne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
