Palmaria are a song writing duo from a small town in Italy, that have made London their home for creating their distinctive Hypnotic Pop. Their music poetically transports the audience on a journey of dreamy landscapes, uplifting experiences, and positive energy to dance with your soul. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Palmaria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
