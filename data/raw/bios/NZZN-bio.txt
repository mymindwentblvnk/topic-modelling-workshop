NZZN started as a Van Halen & AC/DC cover band.

The name NZZN comes from the member names:
Necas
Zica
Ze Nuno

NZZN released the first portuguese heavy metal single, ``Vem Dai``, in 1980

The band ceased to exist in 1982.
