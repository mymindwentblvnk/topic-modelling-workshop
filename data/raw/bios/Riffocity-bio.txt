Riffocity is thrash-oriented band from Serres at North Hellas (aka Greece). The band had been found by Dimitris Kalaitzidis at late's 2013.Riffocity already self-released the ep called "Disciples of the Storm" and at 2017's Spring will release in collaboration with Soman records their debut album entitled "Under A Mourning Sky"
Current Line-up
Thomas Trabouras-Vocals
Dimitris Kalaitzidis-Guitars and Backing Vocals
George Lezkidis-Guitars
