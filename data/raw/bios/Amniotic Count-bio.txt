Amniotic Count is a band from Milan (Italy) which was born in 2001 as the continuation of the Goth-Death Metal band, Evil Bards.
They plays an original Death Metal influenced by many different genres and bands, such as Black Metal and Progressive, and melodic stuff like Dimmu Borgir and Children Of Bodom with wonderful piano pieces.
Tracks are either very aggressive and atmospheric thanks to the mix of the keyboard work, harsh vocals and the death-metal-riffing.
Amniotic Count realeased their official demo in 2005 with excellent reviews. The demo with 4 tracks is titled 'Not Born Yet'. They are now testing new members in order to release a new demo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amniotic+Count">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
