Rapper/ Dj -known as F. M.C (From Many Countries ) Being Half English, Half Russian born in Copenhagen, and growing up in Oslo, Norway he was certainly exposed to traveling and experienced the ways of different cultures. He began writing lyrics at the age of 14, being inspired by the amount of energy, and expression that came from hip hop. Tupac, Snoop Dogg, Dr.Dre, Cypresshill and A tribe called quest were constantly coming from the speakers. But Rock and blues was also big part of his teenage years. His father a classical musician, he was exposed to music at an early age. He originally started with the group TimeOut being a trio, though later in life he wanted to travel and study abroad which led to the group brake up. It..s easy to hear that his lyrics come from a conscious and humorous approach to life and his surroundings. His music style is influenced by many generas from -Hip hop,soul, jazz, funk and rock, to his fathers classical music. His dj sets show great knowledge of the different genres. Today he is putting all his energy into music, working on new songs, perfecting his skills as a rapper and dj.
