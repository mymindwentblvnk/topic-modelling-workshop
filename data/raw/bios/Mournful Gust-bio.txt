Mournful Gust is a doom/death metal band, founded 1999 in  Kryvyi Rih, Ukraine. Their debut album "She's My Grief" was recorded in 2000, then the  band broke up. After the release of the album in 2005 they reunited and started working on new material. Their second album "The Frankness Eve" has been released in 2008.

Mournful Gust consists at the moment of the following members:
Vladislav Shahin - vocals
Evgeny Vecher - guitars
Rojas Gaman - guitars
Saho Maljovaniy - bass guitar
Stas Mischenko - keyboards
Vjacheslav Kapusta - drums
