Absolute Grey was a jangle pop band which formed in 1983 in Rochester, New York, United States. The band consisted of Beth Brown (vocals, guitar), Matt Kitchen (guitar), Mitchell Rasor (bass) and Pat Thomas (drums).

The band released three albums - "Green House" (1984)," What Remains" (1986) and "Sand Down the Moon" (1989) - before they disbanded in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absolute+Grey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
