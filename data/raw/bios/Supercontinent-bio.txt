Supercontinent was a metal band from Ann Arbor, MI. Their first and only album, 'Vaalbara', was released in 2008. 

Current and past members include:

Matt Gauntlett - Guitar/Vocals
Adam Tatro - Auxiliary Instruments/Recording Mastermind
Justin Bean - Drums
Matt West - Bass (2008-Present)
Chris Plumb - Guitar/Vocals
Dave Lock - Guitar
