There are multiple artists under this name:

1.) Corrupt (aka James Caudle) is an electronic music producer from Columbus, OH. 

2.) Corrupt is a Swedish old school thrash band in the vein of early Slayer and Kreator. They have a few MCD's and demo's out, lots of songs are available to download on their webpage, www.shotgundeath.tk.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Corrupt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
