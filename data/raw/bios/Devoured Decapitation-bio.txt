Devoured Decapitation is Colombian brutal death band formed in 2003, Medellin. The band name is influenced by the violence shaking Colombia and the titles of two songs by Colombia-based band Internal Suffering (Catacombs Devourer and Decapitation of the Weak.) The members are Milton Restrepo (guitar, bass, vocals and drum programming) and
