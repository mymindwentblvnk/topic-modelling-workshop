Sarpedon is a Norwegian band.

Fronted by Stan Sylvander, the band has supported bands such as Communic, Battered, and Crest of Darkness, and has had tracks on a number of samplers.  Their  music is for the most part written by guitarist Torgeir P. Krokfjord. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SARPEDON">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
