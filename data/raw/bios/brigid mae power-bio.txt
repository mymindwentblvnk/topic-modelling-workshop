Brigid Mae Power is an Irish singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. 

Born in London, England, to Irish parents, her family relocated to Ireland when she was twelve years old. She learned to play the button accordion before starting to sing and play piano as a teen. 

Power began releasing music under the name Brigid Power-Ryce in 2010. 

Her debut self-titled album, "Brigid Mae Power", was released in 2016. In an interview with Uncut, Power stated the album was inspired by her experiences as a single mother. Power promoted the album with a tour of the UK and Japan. 

Her second studio album, "The Two Worlds", was released in 2018.
