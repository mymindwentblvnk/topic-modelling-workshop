Keys to Eternity, spanish thrash-death metal band located in Madrid, formed in 1996, with three demos edited: Beyond abstractions" (1999), "Spirit in Flames" (1999) and "The Warder is coming" (2006). Keys to Eternity is: Victor Martin (Guitars and Vocals), Tripas (Drums) and Ricardo Moreno (Bass). At the moment they are on the project to self-produce the first band's official CD, for being edited in 2010, and playing live everywhere where it's possible.
