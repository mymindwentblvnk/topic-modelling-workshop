There are multiple artists named WEI:
1) WEi (Korean: 위아이) is a six-member South Korean boy group formed by OUI Entertainment in 2020, consisting Jang Dae Hyeon, Kim Dong Han, Yoo Yong Ha, Kim Yo Han, Kang Seok Hwa and Kim Jun Seo. The group debuted on October 5, 2020, with their first EP titled Identity : First Sight.
