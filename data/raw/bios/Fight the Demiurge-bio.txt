Fight The Demiurge is a Pittsburgh, PA based  Brutal Death Metal/Slam Death Metal band, Comprised of Vocalist/Guitarist/Bassist/Drummer Samantha Michelle Smith.
The band is known for it's extremely heavy yet groovy riffs, and Samantha's utterly bestial growls and snarls.
Fight The Demiurge is NOT Deathcore, no matter what Last.FM says.

Members:
Samantha Smith - Drums (2012-2013, 2014-Present), Guitar (2012-present), Vocals (2011-present), Bass (2012-present), all production, recording.

Former Members:
Timothy Saveall - Guitars/Drums (2011)
Zach Krauss - Drums (2013)
