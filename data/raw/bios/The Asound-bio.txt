Psychedelic doom metal band from Connellys Springs, NC, formed in 2009. They released two EPs (2010: The Asound, 2013: 4 Song Casette) and a split with hungarian doom metal supergroup Magma Rise.
Members are: 
Jon Cox (ex-Seven Foot Spleen): bass
Michael Crump: drums
