With black metal roots and a sound somewhere between Shining and Meshuggah, Eldrimner creates a new branch in their genre. So far the band has released a critically acclaimed EP (9/10, Close Up-Magazine) and the debut album is to be released this fall. The band promise it to be something unique and a fresh breeze for the modern metal scene <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eldrimner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
