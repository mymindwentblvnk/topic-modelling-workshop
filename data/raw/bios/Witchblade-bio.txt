Witchblade is the the name of a number of bands, most of which are metal acts.

1. Witchblade are a heavy metal band from Santiago, Chile, formed in1999. The band currently consists of Freddy Alexis (vocals), Sergio Villarroel (guitars), Claudio Aguayo (guitars), Felipe Vuletich (bass) and Juan Pablo Hermosilla (drums, percussion). The band have released three studio albums, I in 2001, MMVI in 2006 and Ignition in 2007.

2. Witchblade were a short-lived side project of Kat Bjelland, also featuring members from her band Katastrophy Wife. The project formed in 1999 to record a number of songs for the soundtrack to the comic book series of the same name. The album, eventually released as Songs of the Witchblade, also featured other artists such as Buzz Osbourne of the Melvins, Lydia Lunch, Miho of Cibo Mato, Girls Against Boys and Dave Ogilvie of Skinny Puppy.

3. Witchblade are a black metal band from Canada.
