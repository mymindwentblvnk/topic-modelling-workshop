Broken Betty - Polish rock band formed in Gdynia in 2006. The band self-released a 30-minute EP in 2009. Their style may be described as a mixture of stoner rock and grunge (they like to call it "desert rock") and their mail influences are The Melvins, Helmet, Kyuss, QOTSA, Mike Patton, Mondo Generator and Sonic Youth.

Style:
Stoner Rock, Grunge, Hardcore, Punk, Alternative, Metal....

Band members:
