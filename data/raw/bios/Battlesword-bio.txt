Battlesword is a melodic death metal band from Mönchengladbach, Germany. Formed in 1999 they released one demo in 2001 and the album Failling in Triumph in 2003.

Current line-up:
Andreas Klingen - Drums (Bloodstring)
Björn Kunze - Guitars (Bloodstring)
Chris Birx - Vocals (Motorjesus)
