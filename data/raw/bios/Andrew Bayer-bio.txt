Andrew Bayer is an American DJ/Producer from Washington, DC. He is known for his unique progressive house productions on the Anjunadeep and Anjunabeats labels, also incorporating elements of idm, progressive breaks and other genres. He is also known for collaborations with Norwegian producer Boom Jinx (as Boom Jinx & Andrew Bayer), BT, and the now inactive progressive trance group Signalrunners with Alan Nimmo. Bayer has also collaborated with fellow Berklee College Of Music attendants Matt Lange and Kerry Leva.

His debut artist album "It's Artificial" was released on 25th July 2011 on Anjunabeats. Two tracks - Counting The Points and From The Earth - have subsequently been released as singles.

Andrew's second artist album "If It Were You, We'd Never Leave", featuring the singles Lose Sight and Gaff's Eulogy was released in 2013 on Anjunabeats accompanied by huge critical acclaim. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Andrew+Bayer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
