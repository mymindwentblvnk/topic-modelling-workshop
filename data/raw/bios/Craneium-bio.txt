Craneium is a stoner/desert/fuzz rock band formed in Turku, Finland, late 2011 and released their first album "The Slowerdrive Tapes" on green cassette in 2013, a 12" split with 3rd Trip in 2014 and the first full-length record "Explore The Void" was released in the fall of 2015. Their sophomore album "The Narrow Line" was released in 2018 and their third and latest album "Unknown Heights" was released in 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Craneium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
