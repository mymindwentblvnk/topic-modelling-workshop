2- Rasta were a thrash-metal band coming from Belarus. They play a powerful, heavy melodic style and are often using keyboards in their music (as Children of Bodom or Fear Factory).
Rasta has nothing to do with reggae or anything associated with it.
The band gains more and more popularity and will perform on concerts and gigs in Poland, Finland and Germany throughout 2006.

The band is signed under Go Records.

A new 2007 album is entitled "Meridium (single)".
