Daniel McBride, better known under the moniker Sheep, Dog & Wolf, is a multi-instrumentalist, vocalist, and composer based in Wellington, New Zealand. His official site states: "All music is written, performed and recorded by myself in my bedroom."
McBride released his first EP under this name in May 2011, at 17 years old. The EP received critical acclaim internationally, including a number one single on German radio, and saw him signed to Lil' Chief Records for his debut album, the LP "Egospect", released August 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sheep,+Dog+&+Wolf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
