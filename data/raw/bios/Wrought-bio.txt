Wrought is a four-piece self-produced metal band from Northwest Arkansas. Vocals: Jason Hurley, guitar: Dan Robinson, bass guitar: Eric Halsell, drums: J Hollingshead.

Wrought recieved the Ozark Music Awards: Best Metal Band 2005, Best New Band 2005, and was the only metal band ever to be nominated Artists of the Year.
