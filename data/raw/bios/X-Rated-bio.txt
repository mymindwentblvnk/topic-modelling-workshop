Another band named X-Rated was a project that existed since 1994. It was a 5-pieces from Verona, a city in the North-East of Italy.
The band were:
* SJ (singer)
* ZAMPI (drums, keyboards)
* PIGA (bass, noises)
* CESCO S. & BOSCA (guitars)
Every single member of the band came from a different background: from classic Hard-Rock, to Metal, to Pop, Punk, Psychedelic.
