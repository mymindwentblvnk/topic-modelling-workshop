There are several artists with this name.

1. The Regulars was the former name of Reggae Regular. http://www.last.fm/music/Reggae+Regular

2. The Regulars were an indiepop act formed in 1996 in Birmingham, UK. The key line-up comprised Pete Green, Rob Harris, Paul Roach, Stu Fletcher and Chris Green. Before splitting in 2002 they released two singles on the Bearos label, which issued a posthumous compilation album, Effortless, in 2003.

3. The Regulars are currently a rock 'n roll band in New York City. Joe Benoit - lead vocals, guitar  Sam K.
