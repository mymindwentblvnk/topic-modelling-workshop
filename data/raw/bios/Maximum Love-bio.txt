Maximum Love are a British electronic band, consisting of 2 brothers.

They released their debut EP "Midnight Astronaut" in February 2014. Followed by three singles and their second EP "Dead By Dawn" in March 2016.

In November 2016, the band announced that they were working on their third EP entitled "Vision". Which has since been released in January 2017.
