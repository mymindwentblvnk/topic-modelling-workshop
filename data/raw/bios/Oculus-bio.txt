There are multiple artists called Oculus.

1. Oculus are a black metal band from Santiago, Chile, releasing two demos: Two Serpents Kingdom (2004) and Axis Mundi (2011).

2. Oculus are a black metal band with members drawn from Switzerland, the United States and Serbia. They released the album The Apostate of Light in 2017.
