South London producer Paul White and Florida vocalist/rapper Eric Biddines have joined forces to form new duo Golden Rules.

Ahead of their full album, due later this year on Lex Records, the pair are sharing their debut Golden Rules song, Auntie Pearl’s House, an offbeat ode to Eric’s rural upbringing. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Golden+Rules">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
