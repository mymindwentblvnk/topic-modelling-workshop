Fredrick Dewon Thomas Givens II, known professionally as Fredo Bang, is an American rapper from Baton Rouge, Louisiana. He is best known for his tracks "Oouuh" and "Top". In April 2020, he released his debut studio album, Most Hated, through Se Lavi Productions and Def Jam Recordings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fredo+Bang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
