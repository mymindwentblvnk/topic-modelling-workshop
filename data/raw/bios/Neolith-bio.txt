a death/doom band from Poland that was formed in 1991. In their later part of their career they've been playing death/black. Released the demos "Death Comes Slow" in 1993, "Journeys Inside the Maze of Time" in 1995 and a promo tape in 1996 and the full length "Igne Natura Renovabitur Integra" in 1998. After reforming again around 2002, they released two more demos and full lengths.
