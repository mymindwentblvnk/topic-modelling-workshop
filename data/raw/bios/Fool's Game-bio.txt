Fool's Game is a combination of Progressive, Thrash and Power Metal featuring many top musicians: 


Lars F. Larsen - Vocals
John Macaluso - Drums
Nick van Dyk - Keyboards
Matt Johnsen - Guitars
Matt Crooks - Guitars & Bass


Equipment used:
John endorses Tama, Sabian & Vic Firth. Nick endorses Conklin Guitars. Matt C. endorses Jackson Guitars & Mesa Boogie Amps
