A Heavy band inspired by the metal as it was in the 80's, when appellations as NU-metal, Industrial an whatever-metal was not a part of the peoples vocabulary. Double Devil does not submit to any sub metal genre. They just play Heavy as they think it should be played. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Double+Devil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
