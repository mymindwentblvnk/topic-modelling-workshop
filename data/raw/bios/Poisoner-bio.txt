Poisoner is a Danish thrash metal band formed in 2011 by Lauritz Andersen. Poisoner became a band in 2012, when Emil Deleuran joined in as a drummer. Guitarist David Bjørndam and bassist Lasse Sølling joined the band in January 2014.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Poisoner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
