Amalgama is the name of at least two different musical ensembles:

1. A cooperation between Spanish flamenco musicians and Indian classical musicians.
2. A Spanish Heavy Metal band. They've released 4 albums since they created the band.
3. An American Thrash Metal band. They were formed in 2005 in Albany, NY and so far they've released 1 EP, 1 full length album and 2 live EPs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amalgama">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
