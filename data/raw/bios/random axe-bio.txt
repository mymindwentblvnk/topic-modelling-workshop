Random Axe was a hip-hop super-group composed of Black Milk, Guilty Simpson, and the late Sean Price. 
Signed to Duck Down Records, the group released their self-titled debut and only album Random Axe on June 14 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Random+Axe">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
