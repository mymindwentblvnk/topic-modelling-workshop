Selefice was a death metal band from Athens, Attica, Greece, founded in 1990. They released a demo tape in 1991 and a full-length album titled "Where Is the Heaven" in 1993. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Selefice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
