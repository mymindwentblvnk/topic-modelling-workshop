Joe Boston, professionally known as stage name Shallou, is a Los Angeles based producer, singer, and environmentalist, who produces ambient, house melodies and soulful vocals that have carved a niche in the Electronic and Indie scenes. His debut EP, All Becomes Okay, was self-released on May 12, 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shallou">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
