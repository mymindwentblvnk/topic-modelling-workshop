Forlorn Emotion were a Melodic Death Metal band from France. They formed in 1994 and released five demos before their debut (and only) album 'The Sweet Decline' was released in 1997. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forlorn+Emotion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
