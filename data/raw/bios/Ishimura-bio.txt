There is more than one artist with name «Ishimura».

1) Ishimura is a brutal death/black/noise metal band From United States that formed in 2011. Their debut release, a split with Filtheater, was released on December 31st, 2011 on Speed Ritual Records.  In 2013 Ishimura released their first studio album entitled Detestation Aggregation.  An EP came out later that year entitled Eclipsing the Abyss.

2) Ishimura is Königsberg-based post-metal band. They've released their first single «Dignan's Plan» at 10 of November, 2012. Single is available at  http://soundcloud.com/ishimuramusic/ishimura-dignans-plan <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ishimura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
