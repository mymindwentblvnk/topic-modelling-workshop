Tyler Hudson (AKA. BBY GOYARD , or Shannon), is a rapper coming from North Carolina who makes rap with hints of hyperpop, or as he calls it, Shannoncore. Goyard's most recognizable song's are The Ugly Truth, Vamp Fangs, Mystery Box, and most notably "Run Shannon Run", his highest streaming song with nearly 5 million streams. He currently has released 4 EPs & 4 albums. He is the GOAT music-wise. Notable artist Goyard's worked with are people such as Destroy Lonely, and David Shawty. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BBY+GOYARD">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
