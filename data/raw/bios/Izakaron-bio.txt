Izakaron is an Symphonic Black Metal band - from Russia. They formed in the year of 1997 with multiple keyboarders/synths. They've also been known to excel in implementing high dominating Metal keyboards and Symphonic elements. So far they have recorded one full-length album titled;  "Chaoschrist", which was released in 2000. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Izakaron">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
