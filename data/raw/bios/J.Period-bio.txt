J.Period(born February 12, 1975 in Los Angeles, CA) is a Brooklyn-based American Mixtape DJ, remixer and hip-hop producer. He is best known for producing Official Mixtape collaborations with Nas, Big Daddy Kane, Lauryn Hill, The Roots, The Isley Brothers and Mary J. Blige, and an "audio-biography" style that incorporates personal interviews with these artists into his “Best of” compilations.[4] His original production and remixes have also recently appeared in film trailers for Universal Pictures' American Gangster (2007)and Fox Searchlight's Street Kings (2008).
On 10 Februari 2009 J.Period presents... The [Abstract] Best was released, a mixtape wich highlights the career of Hip-Hop legend Q-Tip. It is available as a free download. Within 2 weeks after its release more than 80,000 people did download it.
