Former Butterglory and current White Whale leader, Suggs put out a few solo albums on Merge Records, 2000's Golden Days Before They End and 2003's Amigo Road.  His solo work sounds very much like Butterglory and wouldn't sound out of place on that band's Are You Building a Temple in Heaven? <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Matt+Suggs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
