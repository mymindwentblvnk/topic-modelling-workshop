Esser is an indie rock artist from Essex. He released his debut album, Braveface on 21 March 2009 in the UK, and on 11 August in the US. Before releasing Braveface, Ben Esser supported the Kaiser Chiefs on their 'Off With Their Heads' tour, before touring on his own, supported by Young Fathers. He also appeared at the Glastonbury Festival. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Esser">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
