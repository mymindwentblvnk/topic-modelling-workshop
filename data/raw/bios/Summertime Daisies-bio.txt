Summertime Daises is an Old School Death Metal band from Ontario, Canada.

They've released one demo in 1995 called Gathering of Vermin and an independently released full length album called The Clarity of Impurity in 1996.

The last know line up is - 

Pat Masters - Vocals
Colin Marshall - Guitar
Gerry Caldwell - Drums
Barry Topley - Bass/Vocals
