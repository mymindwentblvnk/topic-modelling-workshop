Spillage Village is an American musical collective from Atlanta, Georgia. The group is composed of rappers, singers and producers, and was founded by the members of EarthGang. Current members are EarthGang, JID, Hollywood JB, Jurdan Bryant, Mereba, 6LACK, and Benji.. The Spillage Village's goal is to "inspire stories aimed at rebuilding and revolutionizing our communities for the future".
