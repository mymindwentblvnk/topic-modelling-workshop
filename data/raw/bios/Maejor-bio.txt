Brandon Green, better known by his stage name Maejor, formerly Bei Maejor and later Maejor Ali, is an American music producer, songwriter and recording artist from the Detroit, Michigan area. Maejor has written and produced songs for several artists, such as Trey Songz, Justin Bieber, Austin Mahone, Tinie Tempah, Keri Hilson, LeToya Luckett, Ciara, Sean Kingston, Cody Simpson, Lloyd, Sammy Adams, Chrisette Michelle, T-Pain, New Boyz, Ne-Yo, Wiz Khalifa, Bun B, Monica, Ginuwine, Plies, TiÃ«sto, Three 6 Mafia, Iggy Azalea, and Kid Ink. As well as producing music for national campaigns including Pepsi and the Boston Celtics, Maejor has also scored film soundtracks for Bratz: The Movie in 2007, The Princess and the Frog in 2009 and Think Like a Man in 2012. He received his first Gold Plaque for production on Bun B's Trill, in 2005, while still in college at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. He has also been nominated for Grammy Awards, for his work on Trey Songz's album in 2010 and Monica's album in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maejor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
