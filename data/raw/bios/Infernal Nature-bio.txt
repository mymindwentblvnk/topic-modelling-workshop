infernal nature is a side project of "niv mazkeret" from sitra achra.
in 2003 was recorded his first demo "satan is within me" and in 2005 was published the split with "Mucous Scrotum" that is called "Calls From The Underground".
at 2007 another split album was released together with "Crux Infernum" entitled " Dehumanization - Black Metal Blitz".
