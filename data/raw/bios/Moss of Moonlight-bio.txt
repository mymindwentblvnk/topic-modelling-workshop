Moss of Moonlight is a neofolk metal band. They consist of two members: Jenn Grunigen (drums, percussion, female vocals) and Cavan Wagner (growls, male vocals, guitars, bass, mouth harp, programming, other instruments). The band was formed in the summer of 2010; they write music about the earth.

Moss of Moonlight released their debut album "SEED" in 2012 via Cascadian Alliance, a indie label that supports artists from the bioregion of Cascadia. 2013 saw the release their EP, "Winterwheel."
