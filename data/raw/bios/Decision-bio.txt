Decision is a rapper hailing from Southern California. After gaining fame on YouTube through his own songs he then set his sights on World of Warcraft parodies. Now, in an attempt to push his career forward he has dropped the parodies and focused on making his own, real music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Decision">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
