Akihiko Narita (成田 暁彦 Narita Akira)  is a Japanese video game composer. He is employed by Capcom. Narita is notable for working on a few games from the Monster Hunter franchise and Resident Evil 5. He recently led the music team of Resident Evil 6. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Akihiko+Narita">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
