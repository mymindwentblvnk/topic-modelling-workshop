The Threat Is Real, is a four piece band, formed in Red Wing, MN, & networking around the Twin Cities(Minneapolis/St.Paul), MN area. All members of the band are influenced by different artists in the Metal scene. There sound is often described as a Metal, Punk, & Thrash mixed vibe.
