Explosive band from Karlstad, Sweden.

Sparzanza have been delivering hard, heavy and groovy metal to the people since 1996. They have toured Scandinavia, Germany, France, Belgium and played at the biggest festivals in Sweden - Hultsfredsfestivalen 2003, Arvikafestivalen 1999, 2002, 2005 and Metal Town 2006. 

Albums:
Besides a couple of compilations, a bunch of singles and splits, they have released six albums to date: Angels of Vengeance in 2001 and Into the Sewers in 2003. Banisher of the Light was released in 2007 and on February 25th, 2009, In Voodoo Veritas came to the world. 2011 saw Folie À Cinq, followed by their latest album Death Is Certain, Life Is Not in 2012.
