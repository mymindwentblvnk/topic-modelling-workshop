The New York-via-Boston indie rock band Hooray For Earth broke up last year, and now former frontman Noel Heroux has gone solo, taking on the name Mass Gothic. Sub Pop released Mass Gothic’s self-titled debut in February 2016, entirely written and recorded by himself, getting help from Zambri’s Jessica Zambri. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mass+Gothic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
