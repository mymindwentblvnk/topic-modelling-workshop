Originating from Karlskrona in the south of Sweden, Mister Green formed in late 2004, at the time named Schizophrenia. With a blend of Forbidden, Vio-Lence, Testament, Megadeth and early Metallica, they are a promising addition to the swedish thrash metal community. Mister Green are currently writing their upcoming debut album.

Releases so far:

- Mirror Punishment (EP, 2007) 
Tracklist: Frankie's Debt, The Machine, Day of the Rope
