Between the Frost is a black metal metal band from Tarragona, Spain. They formed in 1993 and have released two albums: Instinct of Surliness (1998) and Realms of Desolation (2007). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Between+the+Frost">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
