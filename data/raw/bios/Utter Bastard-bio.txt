Utter Bastard are a death metal/grindcore and powerviolence/hardcore band from San Francisco, California, formed in 1995. They split-up in 2008, were then active from 2009 to 2010 and reunited in 2014. Discography:

1996 - Utter Bastard (EP)  
1997 - Split With Deadbodieseverywhere
1998 - Split With Cause for Effect
1998 - Cash, Gash, & Thrash (Split With Bad Acid Trip)
2002 - Split With Irritate
2007 - Rising Sun Fuckers / Kusottare Yankee (Split With Gesewa)
