Down To Ruins was a Quebec Death Metal born during 2008. They released an EP on 11th august 2010. The band lineup did not change. It is known that the band is now working on a new CD, but they have not realeased-it so far. In the meantime, they changed their name to Atonement, but there is no information on this new band yet. However, the lineup remains the same.

Vladimir Eskandari: Guitars and 12-string guitar
Xavier "Toth" Berthiaume: Screams and growls
Sacha Lopez: Guitars
Jean-Mathieu Gareau: Drums
