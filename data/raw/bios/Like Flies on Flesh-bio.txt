Like Flies on Flesh was an American female fronted doom metal / death metal band formed in 1999 in Fort Worth, Texas, United States. The band's music can often be hard to place into a single genre as it tends to take on different forms as the songs progress and the vocals seem to switch between angry and mournful.

As main lyrical themes is an anger, inner struggles and destruction.

They currently only have one release: Asunder / Like Flies on Flesh split, released under the Life Is Abuse Records label in 2000.

The original lineup :

Jamie Myers - bass guitar, vocals (South Mouth, Hammers of Misfortune, Wolves in the Throne Room);
Westbrook Johnsen - guitar, back vocals;
Sean Marsden - guitar;
Tony Diaz - drums, back vocals.
