Kaeos is a band based in Hamburg, Germany. Founded in December of 2007, the band has been working together ever since. The band consists of three members:

F. - Guitar
T. - Drums and Vocals
M. - Bass and Vocals

Their first release is a demo-recording titled Wolves for the Throat - Ravens for the Eyes which was performed and recorded in their rehearsal room in Hamburg. All production, promotion and artwork has been done by the members themselves.

This demo is available for free download from their last.fm page or from the band's official website at http://totalkaeos.com where you can also buy a real life copy of the demo.
