Formed in 2008, Highpriest are a 3-piece instrumental, post-metal, post-rock band from Oakland, Maryland. They self-released their self-titled debut album in January 2012. They recently released their follow up 4-song ep titled "Shift" in November 2013 with Baltimore based Grimoire Records. It is available @ http://grimoirerecords.bandcamp.com/.
