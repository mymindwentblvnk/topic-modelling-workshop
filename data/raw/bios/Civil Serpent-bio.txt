Members: Ody-guitar, J Core-guitar, Brad-bass, Tomac-drums, Roy-vocals

Sounds Like: Pantera, Slayer, Iron Maden, Metallica

Label: Civil Serpent

Manager: David Mikeska

'Civil Serpent' started in the summer of 2009. 'Civil Serpent' has played many local and out of town shows including "Metal at the Moose" in Wisconsin, 'Phoenix Lounge' in Sioux Falls SD, and "Jitters" in Mitchell SD just to name a few. In Aug 2011, metal music producer David Mikeska joined forces and started recording a new album as well a redoing some of their older tracks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Civil+Serpent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
