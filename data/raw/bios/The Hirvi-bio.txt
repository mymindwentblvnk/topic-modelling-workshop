The Hirvi is a thrash metal band formed in 1987, Finland.
So far they have only released one demo in 1989 and a full length called "Old School Killspree" in 2018

A MLP was planned for 1990 but was never released and the band never signed with Remedy Records.

"Hirvi" is Finnish for "moose".
