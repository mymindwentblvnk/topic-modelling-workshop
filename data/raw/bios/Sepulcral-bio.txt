Sepulcral from Italy started in 2009, by E, L, G (all from the more "technical" old school thrash band Torment) and T (Inlansis) to play more raw and unpolished stuff in the vein of Hellhammer, Nunslaughter, Goatlord, Derketa etc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sepulcral">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
