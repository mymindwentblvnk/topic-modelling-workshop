This band are now known as Flight Of Kites.

WinterKids, now working under the name Flight Of Kites, are a 5 piece Indie Art-Pop band from a small Countryside Village called Peaslake in Surrey, England. They write music for all to sing and dance to. Their fusion of catchy melodies, electro sounds and artistic stage performance has created quite a stir, and the band released their debut single via Little House Records in July 2006.
