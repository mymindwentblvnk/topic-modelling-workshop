A death metal band from Christchurch, New Zealand. (Currently inactive)

During the band's time before disbanding they recorded a 4 track demo named "Surge Of Despisement" and played as an opening act for Skinless on the Christchurch leg of their New Zealand tour in 2006.

Brutalized Mortality:

Tom Patchett - Guitar and Vocals
Adrian Patchett - Bass
