Reap is a Thrash Metal Band from Austria formed in May 2000. Their music is influenced by bands like Sepultura, Soulfly, Slayer or Fear Factory.

In 2004 Reap played as support for Chimaira at a show in Vienna's "Planet Music".

After several line-up changes they recorded their first album "Therefor we are" in 2005 and are now working on their second L.P. which will be released in late 2006.

Current Members:
-Philipp Wintersberger (guitar, vocals)
-Simon Tossmann (guitar)
-Hannes Schauer (bass)
-Matthias Noebauer (drums)
