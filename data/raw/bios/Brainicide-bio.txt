Brainicide was an American thrash metal band from Akron, Ohio. It was the first of many bands Tim "Ripper" Owens fronted in his music career. They recorded two demo tapes called "Brutal Mentality" and "Damage Daze". They also recorded a song for the "Heavy Artillery" compilation called "Payment In Blood".


The band:

Tim "Ripper" Owens - Vocals
Dan Johnson - Guitar
Steve Trent - Guitar
John Wyler - Bass
