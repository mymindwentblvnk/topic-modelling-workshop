There are multiple artists named Bianca:

1. A Brazilian Latin pop singer, signed to Sony Music.

2. Bianca Claxton, performing under the name Bianca, is a pop singer-songwriter from the United Kingdom, formerly part of the British five-piece girlband Parade before her departure in February 2013 caused the band to split. In September 2014, she resurfaced as a solo artist with her single Step It Up. She released her debut EP, Hi 5, in May 2015. She is currently unsigned. She has featured on the DC Breaks single Faithless, and provided backing vocals for Leona Lewis' "One More Sleep". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bianca">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
