South Memphis Rapper Blac Youngsta got his start with mixtapes in 2012 before signing with major label Epic in 2017 and releasing a pair of charting full-length projects, 223 and F*ck Everybody (both 2018).

Younsta returned in 2019 with the three-song Cut-Up EP.
