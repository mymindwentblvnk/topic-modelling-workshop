1/ Calvaire is a five piece alt-rock post-hardcore experimental fusion of musical orgasmus. The band's line-up consists of Shane Steponas (vox), Joe Pascal (guitar), Chris Morais (guitar), Jason Nobrega (bass), and Robin Jones (drums).  

2/ Calvaire is also the name of a french band composed of members of As We Draw, Birds in Row and Pigeon.
