Led by their singing she-wolf Amara Avodem, VISIONATICA headed off to the first league of Symphonic Metal in 2016. With her fantastic und crystal clear soprano vocals, combined with the powerful grooves, melodic guitars, as well as impressive orchestral elements of her allies, this musical pack blows up a lot of dust in the scene with its debut “Force Of Luna“.

Band Members:
Amara Avodem: Vocals
Manuel Buhl: Guitar
Gerhard Spanner: Drums
Georg Büchs: Bass

Hometown:
Nürnberg

Record Label:
Dr. Music Records
