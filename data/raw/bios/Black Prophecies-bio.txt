Black Prophecies was a Black Metal band which formed in 1987 in Italy  (Rapallo (Genoa), Ligury) 

Lineup:
Achille "Dark" Testa - Guitar
Maury - Vocals
Ingo - Bass
Rob - Drums

Discography:
Azathoth - 1988
Unholy Rehearsal - 1989
Turning the Crosses Towards Hell - 1991

An album titled "Abominations From the Abyss" was set for release by Wild Rags (with the catalogue number WRR020) in 1989 but never emerged. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Prophecies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
