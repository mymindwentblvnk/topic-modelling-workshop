Renny Wilson, musician formerly known as Sugarglider spent almost ten years working on debut album Sugarglider, a collection of glassy sounds and deeply insular atmospheres. Somewhere between the wildly funky bedroom disco of Arthur Russell and the megalomaniacal crash of contemporaries like Ariel Pink and Mac DeMarco, Wilson finds a unique place on the map in this album of dance-oriented shut-in brilliance. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Renny+Wilson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
