Fluisteraars  (whisperers) are an atmospheric black metal band from the Netherlands. 

Discography: 

Beringheim (demo, 2009) 
't Hondslog (demo, 2010) 
Dromers (full-length, 2014)
Luwte (full-length, 2015)
Gelderland (EP, 2016)
De oord (split album with Turia, 2018)
Bloem (full-length, 2020)
Gegrepen door de geest der zielsontluiking (full-length, 2021)
