No Gods No Masters are a chaotic, hardcore, sludge band from Twente,  Netherlands. They are heavily influenced by bands such as Down,  Cathedral, Goatsnake, and Celtic Frost. They self-released their 4 song, self-titled debut EP in September 2013. It is available, name your price, @ http://nogodsnomasters.bandcamp.com/.
