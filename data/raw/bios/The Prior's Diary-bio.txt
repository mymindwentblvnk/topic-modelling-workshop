A Gothic Death Metal band from Germany.
The Prior's Diary were founded in late 1991 by Dominik, Uwe and Günther. Later Chris and Grogor joined the band. In 1992 Günther died. The first Live Demo was recorded with female singer Carmen in 1994. Late 1994 the band recorded one track for the Metal Under Germany Vol 2Sampler with new female singer Patricia.
