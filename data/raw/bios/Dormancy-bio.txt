What started as a simple spoken-word midwest emo project became the genre-hopping monstrosity that is now Dormancy. Drawing influences from La Dispute to Opeth to Isis to Deafheaven to Animals as Leaders, Dormancy crafts eyebrow-raising instrumentals while delivering the essence of lyrical sadness.
