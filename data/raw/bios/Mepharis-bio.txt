Mepharis ( www.myspace.com/mepharis )

Thrash/heavy metal band was established in 2007 - at first named Dedzi - by Cygan & Strażak. Name Mepharis oficially accepted in 2009. In that time rehearsals was took part in Karpniki - in his family village . After continuous problems formed a stable team which (Cygan, Daimos (left in April 2010) Pojeb, Strażak, Maden) recorded - between february and april 2010 - the first professional demo - Suicide Avenue <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mepharis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
