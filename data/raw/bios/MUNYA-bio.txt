Munya is the stage name of Québécois musician Josie Boivin. Munya initially garnered attention in 2017 performing at the Pop Montreal music festival. The following year, she issued her debut EP, North Hatley. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MunYA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
