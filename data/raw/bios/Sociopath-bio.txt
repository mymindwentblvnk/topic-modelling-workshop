Sociopath is a suicidal black metal project from Turkey that Invidiosus planned in October 2006. Sociopath has become active when Exitosus joined as bassist; Cynicus Bryonia Marsi joined as guitarist & assumed composing music.

Musically & lyrically, the band embraced a depressive style; personal psyhcologic crisisis and depressions; the suicides at the deep of personal imagination. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sociopath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
