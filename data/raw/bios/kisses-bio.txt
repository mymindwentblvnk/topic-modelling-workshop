Kisses are Jesse Kivel and Zinzi Edmundson, a married couple from Los Angeles, USA.

They released their debut album, The Heart of the Nightlife in 2010. Which had a breakout single Bermuda. Afterwards they gained a lof of traction. Their follow-up, a concept album about rich Bel Air teenagers, Kids in L.A. (co-produced by Saint Etienne's Pete Wiggs) was released in 2013. They released the single Funny Heartbeat from it beforehand, and gained a lot of follower from it.

They made a third album in 2015 named Rest In Paradise. Which has a few funky jams as: The Nile, A Groove, and Control. You can see they have their method very refined by this point. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kisses">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
