Brazilian Thrash/Death Metal Band based in Campinas/SP, under the influence of Destruction, Deicide, and the 80's and 90's Death and Thrash Metal with war and religious themes.

The band has just lunched the album THE WAR IS NOT OVER by Kill Again Records.
