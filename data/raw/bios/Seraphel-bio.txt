Seraphel is a black metal band from Italy formed in 2004 by Sytry - guitars and Amroth Eligor - bass.

The first demo had immediately positive reviews from magazines and webzines and the band played with Slayer, Dissection, Children of Bodom and other metal artists at Metal Camp 2005 in Slovenia.

Seraphel released our first album “Remembrance” by Swedish label Khaoz Star Records in 2006.

With a renewed line up Seraphel returns with changing in the sound, mixing death and black metal. 

On the official site www.seraphel.com you can listen to the new promo Post Human God out in 2009.

Seraphel is:
Silent - vocals
Sytry - guitars
Eligor - bass
Doroth - synth
