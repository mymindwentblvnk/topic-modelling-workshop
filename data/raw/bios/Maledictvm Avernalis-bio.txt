Maledictvm Avernalis, formed in Columbia in 2007 is a fast-paced Black Metal band featuring anti-christian lyrics. Shortly after having assembled their line-up, they released their first Demo "Satanic Raw Cult", consisting of 3 songs. Their second Demo "Maledicta Vulgata Maligna" is scheduled for a release in 2008.
