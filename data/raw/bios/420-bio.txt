1) Galcher Lustwerk has released a new self-titled album under a new alias, 420.

Galcher told Resident Advisor he made the 16 tracks on 420 over the past month during quarantine. Each of the 16 tracks is four minutes and 20 seconds long and cost $4.20 apiece. The total length of the album is 69 minutes and, naturally, the full LP is available for $69. It was mastered by Josh Eustis, AKA Telefon Tel Aviv.
