Recently much has been made of Washington, D.C.'s emerging hip-hop scene and one of the people at the forefront of all the hype is a well-rounded new face by the name of Lightshow. Coming off of being listed as one of Complex Magazine's "Top 25 New Artists To Watch Out For" Lightshow prepares to make his Livemixtapes debut with his latest effort "Life Sentence 2".
