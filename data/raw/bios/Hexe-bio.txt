There are multiple artists with the name Hexe:

1. A virtual singer (Auxuman). She was previously used as an AI player in a computer game by Bazookai.

2. HEXE (헤쎄) was a five-member girl group under Serenity Music. They made their debut on October 16, 2015, with "Hexe", their second self-titled digital single. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hexe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
