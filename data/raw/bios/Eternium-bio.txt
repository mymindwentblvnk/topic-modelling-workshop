1. Eternium is a symphonic Black/Death metal band from St. Louis, Missouri. The band was founded by Markov Soroka in 2010, and after many line up changes, Markov decided to do it as a solo project from 2010-2011. The Aura Titian demo was being recorded in this time. After some time, Markov scouted out members Brent Dossett (Decapitated Saints, Maggot Feast) Chad Griffin, (Recrudescence), Gabe Price, and Cole Sandel (Gormogon)
The music style is a mix of melodic black/death metal with subtle symphonics. Since then, the band has found Ryan Kelley (Archon) for drumming duties, and The band is currently planning to release their first full length on September 26th. All music up to this point has been written and recorded by Markov Soroka. S
