The CATS describe their sound as Tropicalia Pop, whatever that means. Without aiming towards a specific style, they always leave traces of Bossanova, jazz flute and gospel folk harmonies. Their sound cultivated from the annals of southern culture; a mix of sand and sea governed by the golden state. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cristobal+and+the+Sea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
