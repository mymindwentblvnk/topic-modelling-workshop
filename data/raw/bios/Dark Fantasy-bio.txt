The radio show:

Dark Fantasy was a radio series with tales of the weird, adventures of the supernatural, created for you by [[artist]Scott Bishop.It aired as a horror drama on NBC between 1941 and 1942.

Dark Fantasy was dedicated to dealings with the unknown. Originating from radio station WKY, Oklahoma City, it was written by Scott Bishop (of Mysterious Traveler and The Sealed Book fame) and was heard Fridays over stations. Tom Paxton served as announcer. The shows covered horror, science fiction and murder mysteries. Although a short series, the shows are excellent, with some stories way ahead of their time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Fantasy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
