Sadhaka is a atmospheric / Cascadian black metal band from United States. The band formed in 2012 by Vines, Echtra and Jennifer Christensen. They have members from Fauna, Vradiazei, and Møllehøj. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sadhaka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
