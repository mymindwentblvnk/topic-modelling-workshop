Melodic-death/doom metal band from Kuřim, Czech Republic was founded in 2012 by base members Jindra Bubeník (guitar) , Aleš Krupica (vocals), Martin Zejda (guitar), Jan Šmerda (bass) and Petr Hejč (drums).
