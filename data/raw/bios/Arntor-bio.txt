Arntor is a french pagan black metal project formed in 2007 in Angers, Pays-de-la-Loire. Previously known under the name of Raziel (2006). The band was named after the famous Windir album called "Arntor".

Current line-up
Faust - Vocals (Hanternoz, Mordheim, Ordo Blasphemus (Live), Breizh Occult, Odium Diabolicum)
Requiem - Lead Guitar, Backing Vocals
Hraesvelg - Guitar
Beorn - Bass
Hrungnir - Drums

Former/past member(s)
Dumnacos - Vocals (Hanternoz)
Celot - Bass
