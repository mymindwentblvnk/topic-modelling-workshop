Code:Red is a Norwegian local thrash metal band stationed in Haugesund. They do not have a record so far and are only to find on: http://uhort.no/artist/CodeRed, the "metal from Haugalandet" CD and on their own printed CDs.

They picked up a lot of their inspiration from the three Teutonic thrash metal bands (Destruction, Sodom and Kreator)

They have played on some local gigs and some in Oslo (Betong) and Sandnes, all in Norway.
