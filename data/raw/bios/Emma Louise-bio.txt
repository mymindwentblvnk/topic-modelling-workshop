Emma Louise is a critically acclaimed singer-songwriter based in Brisbane, Australia. She released her debut EP ' Full Hearts & Empty Rooms' in early 2011. 

Emma Louise won 'Breakthrough Independent Artist' at the 2011 Independent Music Awards, whilst collecting awards for 'Song of the Year', 'Best Pop Song' and 'Best Singer-Songwriter' at the 2011 Queensland Music Awards. Her haunting debut single 'Jungle' featured on Greys Anatomy and promos for ABC series "The Slap".

She has toured with the likes of Josh Pyke, TiGERTOWN and Boy & Bear.
