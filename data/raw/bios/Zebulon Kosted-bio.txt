Zebulon Kosted is an experimental black metal band formed in Bozeman, Montana in 1999. The band comprises of sole member, Rachid Abdel Ghafour, who records and produces everything including vocals, electric guitar, bass,drums, noise and electronic work. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zebulon+Kosted">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
