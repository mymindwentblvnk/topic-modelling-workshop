The Bottrops is the new name for the 'Terrorgruppe Ohne Archi' after Archi left the Band. The replacement for Archi is Benno, who formerly played in a band from Berlin called 'Xarecrows'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Bottrops">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
