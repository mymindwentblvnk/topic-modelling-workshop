Mordheim is a black metal band from France which was found in 2007. Lyrical themes surround Anti-Christianity and darkness. It's only member is Comte Faust von Vlachfölds.
Logo and artwork done by Jerry from Legion Mortifère.

All releases have been released on tape by Dernier Bastion (France).
