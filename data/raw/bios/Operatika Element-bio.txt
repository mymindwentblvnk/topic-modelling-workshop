Operatika Element is a symphonic metal band formed in 2002 in New Jersey, USA.  It's music combines melodic, dramatic and powerful metal with female vocals.

MembersSlava Popova—lead vocals
Anna Dok—vocals, keyboards
Boris Zaks—keyboards, violin
Richard Fischer—guitars, vocals
