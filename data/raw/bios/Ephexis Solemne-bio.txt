Ephexis Solemne is a raw black metal/ambient band formed in 2004 by Imperator Solemne from San Diego. California. The band only released 1 album which is the A Previous Design of Indifference and Nature demo released on February 2nd, 2005. The band currently is on hold. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ephexis+Solemne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
