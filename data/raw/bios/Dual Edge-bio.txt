Dual Edge was an early Christian metal band hailing from Minneapolis, MN.  With their demo, they were the first band to sign with Intense Records (which would later sign Sacred Warrior and Deliverance).  Members of the band were:

Scott Turner – Lead Vocals
Rick Wald – Guitars
John Avery – Bass
