Sonne Adam is an old school death metal band from Tel-Aviv, Israel that was formed in 2007.  The name is in Hebrew and translates to "Hater of Humanity". As of 2016 the only remaining member of the band is guitarist/vocalist Tom Davidov. Their first release was the 2010 EP 'Armed with Hammers'. Following this, they released their debut full-length album 'Transformation' in 2011. They released two further EPs in 2012, 'The Sun Is Dead' and 'Doctrines of Dark Devotion'. In October 2012 they released an anthology of their three EPs as well as three new tracks titled 'Messengers of Desolate Ways'.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sonne+Adam">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
