Akphaezya is an "Eklektik Metal" band formed in 2002 in Orléans, France, featuring the female singer and pianist Nehl Aëlin.
Mixing each kind of metal with jazz and other styles freely.

line-up :

- Stephan H: guitar
- Nehl Aëlin: vocals / piano
- Loic Moussaoui: drums
- Stephane B: bass
