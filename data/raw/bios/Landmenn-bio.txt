.. Landmenn was founded by the two musicians Bill Cox (Bass,Vocal,Lyrics and concept) and Norwegian Nobody (Guitar,Percussion,Keyboard and Vocal) in the late winter of 2006. Both musicians have been busy with various band projects until Landmenn was formed. Bill Cox was the original bass player of Slogstorm, and Norwegian Nobody was one of the originators of the old Black Metal Band Frostmoon. Their first EP: "Døde menn gaar i land" was released digitaly in 2010. The EP "Døde menn" is available on Napster, Spotify and iTunes. 
They are now working on the  2012 release Fangstliv.
