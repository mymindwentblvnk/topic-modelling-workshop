Spanish Death Metal band from Ourense, Galicia, formed in 1992. The four-piece band counted with musicians from many other local death metal acts, including Detestor and Dismal. Discography:

1992 - Promo 
1993 - Promo 
1994 - Avowals (split with Dismal and Absorbed)
1995 - Promo 
1995 - False Light Garden demo 
1997 - Throne of Anguish 
2004 - LIFE compilation (tracks 1-4: Throne of Anguish mcd, tracks 5-8: from the "Avowels" split cd, tracks 9-11: False Light Garden demo 1996 and tracks 12-16: Live in Bilbao, 1997) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unnatural">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
