Auspicium is a Black Metal band from Penobscot County, Maine. The singular vision of Patrick Hasson, Auspicium was formed in 2004. The music is described as thalassic black metal, due to the predominant nautical feel of the first recordings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Auspicium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
