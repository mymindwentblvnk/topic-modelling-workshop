Immiserate is a melodic death metal / thrash metal band based in Seattle, Washington. They teamed up with Black Sheep Records to release their first self titled EP "Immiserate" in 2007.

Since the release of this EP the entire lineup of the band has changed, and while they still have a strong live presence they have not released any material with the new lineup.
