Born July 13, 1988 in Aix-en-Provence (13)
SOKAR is in production environments since 2004.

His first music label Entrac sound and outputs of their famous instrumental mixtapes are about him, at the time of these mixtapes aujourdh'ui includes a total of 1 Million Miles 254 downloads around the world ...

Signed to the label as hip hop in the PACA region (Le'Vice. Music), he produced compositions for artists such as: Xtaz, Mister Yelo, Mr. Stiff, Mister Coffee, The Warning, Mahaline, Mc Hoss, Smoki, Madiness, Holldop, Ladea, N'zano, Nino The imfame, Chnawak, and many more ...

Compositions with sounds unique to his style are produced thanks to the famous sampler / sequencer AKAI MPC 2000XL, a Yamaha Motif Rack, multiple sound libraries and software Pro Tools and Reason. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SoKaR">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
