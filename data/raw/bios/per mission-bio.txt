Per Mission is a solo project from Jason Noble, formerly of Rodan, and currently a member of Rachel's and Shipping News.

A Ritual Loop was released in 2001 on Monitor Records and features contributions from members from his other projects including Kyle Crabtree, Rachel Grimes, Christian Frederickson, Greg King, Kevin Coultas, and Jason Mueller.
