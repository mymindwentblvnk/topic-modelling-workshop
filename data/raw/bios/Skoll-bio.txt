Skoll are an Italian two man band formed in 1994. They sing about things such as Ancient pagan times, heathenism, mythology and they're a mixture of black and viking metal. They have released a variety of demos with several different band members over the years, and in 2005 a compilation album entitled '11 Years of Mist'. The band is still active. 

The name `Skoll` comes from norse mythology and is the name of a wolf that chases the chariot that pulls the sun. http://www.myspace.com/skollmusic
