Selvmorrd, formed in 2006, is a german Suicidal Black Metal Band from lower Saxony.

The current members are:
Nightwolf - Lead guitars, drums, vocals (also in Godless Cruelty)
Sturmgestalt - Rhythm guitars

They have released two full-length album so far:
Bis zum Ende (2006)
