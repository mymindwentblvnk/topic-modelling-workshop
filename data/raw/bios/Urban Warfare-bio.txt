Urban Warfare was a Hardcore band from Limburg, Belgium mixing old school with beatdown and a pinch of hiphop. after releasing their demo "Break it Down" the band quit after a few months. They changed their name to Turn Away with new members and did a few shows, after that they called it a day for good. Guitar player mici now plays bass with For My Enemies, Tommy now plays drums with Point of Impact <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Urban+Warfare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
