Fossegrimen are a two piece Doom Metal band from the US. Dylan Vatne (vokills) lives in New Jersey and Paul Schofield (instruments) lives in the Pennsylvania. A demo has been released and a full length is in the planning process.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fossegrimen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
