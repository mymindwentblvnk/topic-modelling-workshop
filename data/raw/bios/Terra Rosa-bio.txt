TERRA ROSA (80's JAPANESE Heavy Metal Band) 

It's  founded by Kazue Akao(vocal,ex.Wizzard)&Masashi Okagaki(keyboards,ex.X-Ray,Magic)in the summer,1982.

Many great musician have joined in their works.
Such as,Yousuke Miyake(guitar),YOU ADACHI(guitar),Suzuki Hiromi(Guitar),Koji Yamaguchi (bass), Masaaki Tano(drums),Noburo Horie(drums)...an so on.

Disbanded in 1992.
