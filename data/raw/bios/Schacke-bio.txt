Martin Schacke began as a pioneer on the Copenhagen industrial and noise scene via countless releases under countless pseudonyms on his label Moral Defeat. Recently Schacke’s work as a techno DJ and producer has taken him to renowned clubs around the world, including a residency at St Petersburg’s Клуб club, and his tracks Make Them Remember (on Ectotherm) and Automated Lover (on Kulør) have become two nailed-on classics of the Copenhagen scene. His DJ sets are similarly agile, noisy, trancy and unruly <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Schacke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
