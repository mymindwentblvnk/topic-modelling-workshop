Black Empire is the name of at least 2 bands, both playing black metal; one from Mexico, and one from Canada.

1) Black Empire Mexico
This band come from Ciudad Nezahualcoyotl, and have 3 album known releases, 1 EP and 2 split albums.Their myspace page is at http://www.myspace.com/thetrueblackempire 

2) Black Empire Canada
This band originate from Rouyn-Noranda, Quebec, and have been active since 2001. They released their first album "2000 AD" in 2013.
