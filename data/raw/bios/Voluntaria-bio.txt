Voluntaria is a Depressive Black Metal band from Germany, formed by Wintersturm (vocals, lyrics, concept, music), Azeroth (guitars, concept, music) and Martel (bass) in 2004.

Discography:
2006 - Trist (demo)
2007 - Solitary Songs to Leave this World Behind (demo)
2011 - Wintersturm (split with Trauer)

- The demo Trist was rereleased in 2008 (again on tape) by Self Mutilation Services.
