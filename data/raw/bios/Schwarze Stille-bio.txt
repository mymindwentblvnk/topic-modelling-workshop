Schwarze Stille was a black metal band from Germany formed in 1997. The band released two full-length albums, Licht und Schatten in 2002 and Anderswelt in 2004, before disbanding in 2006. Three members would go on and form the black metal band Hangatyr in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Schwarze+Stille">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
