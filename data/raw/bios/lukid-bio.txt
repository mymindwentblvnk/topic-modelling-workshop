Lukid (real name Luke Blair) is an electronic music producer from London, England, UK. He released his first four albums Onandon, Foma, Chord, and Lonely at the Top between 2007 and 2012 via Werkdiscs. He works in a variety of electronic music genres, such as abstract, dubstep, IDM, etc.
