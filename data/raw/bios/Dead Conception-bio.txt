Dead Conception is a death metal band, which was formed at the beginning of 2014 in Poland in Tomaszow Mazowiecki, at the initiative of drummer Jakub Woskowski and guitarist Kamil Jachym. The theme of songs are stories from the life of murderers and mentally ill people as well as misanthropy or finding strength in helplessness.


Current members:

Jakub Woskowski - drums (2014-present), vocals (2015)
Kamil Jachym - guitars (2014-present), bass (2015)
Michał Kowalczyk - bass (2015-present)
Michał Jankowski - guitars (2015-present)
