Progressive Death Metal with Jazz influences from Calgary, Alberta. Formed in mid-2010 by Denver Bergreen and Jake Reimer as a casual "play-anything" project. Not long after, both Clay Steadman and Tristan Peterson were recruited to join the line-up. Signed to Minotauro records released their first full length Calculations of the Ancients in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Super+Massive+Black+Holes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
