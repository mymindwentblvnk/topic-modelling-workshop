Through The Pain is a one man depressive black metal project from Germany, formed by Baelvect in 2006, the first appearance in the scene was with a split release Ft. Trist entitled "Black Veil" in the late of 2007, the main theme of the band is personal advancement & experiences through pain.

Current Line Up:
Baelvect - Vocals, Guitar (Ignifer)
Wiedergaenger - Bass (Total Negation)
Richard - Drums (Hypothermia, Sitra Ahra) (Session)
