Lily Lizotte, known as The Blossom, is an alternative artist from Sydney, based in Los Angeles. They are signed to the Video Store record label owned by Kevin Abstract and Romil Hemnani of Brockhampton. Recently they've changed their artistic name to THE BLSSM. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Blossom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
