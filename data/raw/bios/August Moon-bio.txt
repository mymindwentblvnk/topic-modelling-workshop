1) August Moon was a death metal band from Finland.  The band featured two members from fellow Finnish band As Serenity Fades.  August Moon self released a demo in 1993 titled "Rosegardens" and then split up.

2) August Moon is a band from louisville kentucky that plays fast, upbeat instrumental music. They have a few demos and one proper EP released. They are currently writing new music for an EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/August+Moon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
