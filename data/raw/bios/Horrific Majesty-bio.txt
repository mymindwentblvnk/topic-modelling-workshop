The US band Horrific Majesty began in 2003 as an ambient project. By fall of 2004 the music has evolved into a dark and original form of black metal, including themes of Christianity, depression, sorrow, and regret. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Horrific+Majesty">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
