G Mills is the jazz-inspired hip hop project of Brooklyn-based producer and drummer, Gabe Millman. From a young age, Gabe has been deeply engrossed in music, which led to him formally studying jazz, classical, and audio engineering. His experience and intuitive musicality shines through creative drum programming/playing, smooth chordal structures, and unique sound design <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gary+Mills">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
