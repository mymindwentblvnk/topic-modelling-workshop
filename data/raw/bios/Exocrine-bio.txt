Exocrine is a Progressive Death Metal band formed in Bordeaux, France. 
Exocrine have evolved in modern and technical metal styles, mixing influence without losing sight of the true essence of their music.

Their debute "Unreal Existence" combine grooves with lots of technical riffs followed by melodic leads.
It's also to find through the label Great Dane Records, hit it up on google or bandcamp.
