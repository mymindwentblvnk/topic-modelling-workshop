Nekrofrost was a black metal project from the United States formed by Count Inferno, also known as S. Inferno. The project was ended in early 2009. 
S. Inferno participated in several hard rock bands before returning to the black metal scene with the resurrection of a previous band, Vampiric Tyrant.
