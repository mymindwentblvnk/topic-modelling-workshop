Dark Sun Rising is a dutch progressive/melodic death metal band from the Eindhoven area. The former band started as a coverband. In the summer of 2011 two bands combined their forces and Dark Sun Rising was born.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Sun+Rising">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
