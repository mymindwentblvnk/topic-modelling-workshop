Burning Tears is one of the most promising Gothic Metal bands from Chile. The band cultivate a very unique sound, mixing the brutality of Black Metal, with the dark and melodic sound of gothic influences, and the inclusion of a transverse flute. 

Actually they have just released their debut album titled "Tales From A Disturbed Soul", a powerful material wich has allow them to share stage with several bands such like Azotth, Denun, Six Magics and Xandria. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burning+Tears">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
