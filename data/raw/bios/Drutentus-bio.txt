Black Metal band from Greece composed of Voreas (ex-Dark Mighty Thought, ex-F.R.O.S.T., ex-Goathrone, ex-Βάκχειος) and Hierophant (ex-Tatir). They only released one demo in 1998 - In frigidis silvis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drutentus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
