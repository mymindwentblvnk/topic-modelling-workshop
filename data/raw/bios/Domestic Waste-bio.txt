Domestic Waste is a death metal band from Wrocław, Poland, formed in 2003. They released a 5 track demo in 2005, which got many positive reviews in press and webzines.

Their current lineup is:
Banan - vocal
Szymon - guitar
Bogdan - guitar
Mareczek - bass
Bandaż - drums
