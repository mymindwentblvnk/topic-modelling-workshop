Black metal band from France formed in 2010. The band made a compilation appearance with the track "Contre le Temps" on Flammes de France (Darker than Black Records, 2017).

Current lineup:
▶ Urwiss - guitars (2010-present)
▶ J. - vocals, bass (2010-present), drums (2017-2018)
▶ Freissler - drums (2018-present)
Past members:
▶ Exekkutioner - drums (2012-2014)
