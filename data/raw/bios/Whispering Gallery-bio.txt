Whispering Gallery (1995-2008) was a doom metal band from Amersfoort, Netherlands. They use a mixture of clean vocals and grunt. Lyrical themes are life's issues such as loneliness and pain. Band pictures feature religious symbols, but it's unknown if they're religious.

The last known line-up:
- Huber ter Meulen: Guitar / Vocals;
- William van Dijk: Guitar;
- Barry van Trigt: Bass;
- Pascal Spierings: Drums / Vocals;
- Fred Provoost: Synthesizer / Piano.

Former band members are:
- Reinier Vreeswijk: Grunts.
