There are two artists with the name CRO

1. Cro (Carlo Waibel; born 31 January 1990) is a German rapper, singer, producer and designer. He was represented by Chimperator Productions until July 2018. He describes his music as a mixture between rap and pop, which he calls "Raop".

2. C.R.O. (Tomás Manuel Campos; born 11 September 1998) is an Argentinian singer and rapper, born in Neuquén. He belongs to Bardero$ crew, and him and Homer el Mero Mero have released several hits. He started with rock music at a very young age, and moved on to rap when he found freestyle competitions. He has now collaborated with several Argentina Trap powerhouses like Duki, Neo Pistea and Ysy A. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cro">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
