NECROTIC WOODS is a Black Metal band founded 2004 in Steinheim (NRW), Germany.

NECROTIC WOODS is:
- Thorn (Vocals)
- Brew (Guitars)
- Leech (Drums)
- Spitt (Bass)

Discographie:
- Exaltation of Death (2008)
- The Nameless Dark (2017)
