Amritpal Singh Dhillon, known popularly as AP Dhillon, is an Indian singer, rapper, songwriter and record producer associated with Punjabi music. Five of his singles have peaked on the Official Charts Company UK Asian and Punjabi charts, while "Majhail" and "Brown Munde" have topped the chart. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/AP+Dhillon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
