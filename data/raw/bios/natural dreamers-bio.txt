Natural Dreamers is an abstract, instrumental pop (clearly influenced by 'math rock') group on the independent, San Francisco-based Frenetic Records label. Its members include guitarists Chris Cohen and John Dietrich (Deerhoof & Gorge Trio), and drummer Jay Pellicci (also of Dilute). In 2004 they released their first, self-titled, LP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Natural+Dreamers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
