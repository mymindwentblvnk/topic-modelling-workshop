Voice of the Soul is a death metal band based in Dubai that started in Kuwait in late 2007. After playing several shows in Dubai since January 2011, the band opted to relocate to Dubai. 

Voice of the Soul released their third EP, “Into Oblivion”, live on SOS Heavy Metal Radio in Portugal in August 2011. 

In April 2014, the band recorded their debut full-length album "Catacombs" at Haven Studio in Dubai.
