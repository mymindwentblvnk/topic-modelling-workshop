Tairach is a black metal band from Germany which was formed in 1999. Skjeld (Nyktalgia, Sterbend) and Galagrm (Wintersturm) are the people  of the last known line-up. Tairach released a demo in 1999, but they broke apart <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tairach">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
