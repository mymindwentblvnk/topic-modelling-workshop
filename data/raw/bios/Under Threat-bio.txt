UNDER THREAT is a melodic death metal band from Colombia South America, the band was formed in 1997 by Nicolas Bermudez,(guitars),David Bermudez (bass) Alejandro Rojas (drums) and John Perez (vocals).With 5 releases under their belt , and with Nicolas on vocals since 2011, the band is currently promoting their latest album "The prison within". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Under+Threat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
