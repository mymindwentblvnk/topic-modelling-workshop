The Brothers Highhorse started as a four-piece band in 2009, drawing on their roots in metalcore while incorporating elements from death and black metal. Fast-forward to 2011, and the Brothers have released their long-awaited EP, INVASION. Despite numerous setbacks and member changes, the Brothers Highhorse have persevered, maintaining a strong presence in the North Texas scene.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Brothers+Highhorse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
