1) Death metal act from South Korea, formed in 1992. Sadhu has released their first full-length studio album, entitled "The Trend Of Public Opinion", in 1996. The guitarist from Sadhu, Lee Myeong-Hee, also played in Scoundrel.


2) Dubstep producer from Berlin, Germany, formed in 2011. Sadhu is well known for his monstrous drums and raging synthesizers.
