Azgard is a 5-piece Ukrainian progressive deathcore band, formed in 2009. They've released their debut EP "Bogdanchyk's Porridge" in 2011 and their debut album "At the Break of the Day", which was fully recorded, mixed and produced by the band itself on May 28, 2015.
Their music is a mix of aggressive grooves, progressive and djent'y riffs and shredding leads. Band's main lyrical themes are social problems and politics.
