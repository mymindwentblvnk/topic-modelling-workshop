Anvil Of Doom is a Melodic death band from south of Spain Jerez de la Frontera, started in 2002. The first demo was named "Died Before dawn", which earned the band recognition. The band then signed a record deal with Xtreem Music.

In January 2004 the band started recording their astonishing album named "deathillusion". It was ready that same year in October. Anvil Of Doom is growing in Spain and has made some remarkable gigs, reviews and interviews. The band is growing fast!  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anvil+of+Doom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
