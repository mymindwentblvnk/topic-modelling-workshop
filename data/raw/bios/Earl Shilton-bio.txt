Earl Shilton comes from the mind of Alex Thomas, who plays all instruments on the albums.  In live performances, his brother plays bass and his sister plays drums.  Alex Thomas played drums for Bolt Thrower on the album Mercenary, and used to play with Groop Dogdrill as well.
