Zcerneboh is Black Metal Band from  Poland (Slupsk) Formed in 2008,Current label Ancient Order Rex.
Lyrical theme(s)
Witchcraft, Pagan Evil, Dark War, Ancient Rituals
Current line-up
Alastor Wolrad - guitars & vocal (Cryptal Spectres, Hatenwar)
Wened Wilkinus Slawibor - drums & vocals (Aetheres, Archandrja, Angrenost (Pol), Lechia, Birkut, Demiurg Blyskawicy, Wilczy Demon, Cmentarny Zew, Venedae, Hatenwar, Cryptal Spectres)
Additional notes
Zcerneboh means Devil based on Black God from Slavonic mythology.
