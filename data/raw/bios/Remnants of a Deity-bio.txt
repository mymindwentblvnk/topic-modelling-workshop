Remnants Of A Deity is a Technical Death Metal band from Ottawa, Canada.

Originally formed in the fall of 2003, R.O.A.D is comprised of Roch (drums), Scott (guitar/vocals), Vittorio (guitar), Gareth (vocals), and Jeremy (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Remnants+of+a+Deity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
