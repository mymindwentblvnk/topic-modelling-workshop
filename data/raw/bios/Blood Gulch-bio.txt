Hailing from Richmond, VA, Blood Gulch play their own spin on metal, mixing thrash, metalcore, hardcore and grindcore. Their lyrics range from the Dateline host Chris Hansen, to video game characters like Snake (Metal Gear Solid). They currently have one demo out, available for download upon request from them on their myspace.
