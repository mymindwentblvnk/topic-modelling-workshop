Formed in 2011 in Colombo, Sri Lanka, Genocide Shrines is a blackened death metal quartet, half the members being also in Manifestator. 

They are currently signed to Cyclopean Eye Productions, through which they have already released an EP Devanation Monumentemples (2012) and a split CD Mahabharat Terror Attack (2014) with the above-mentioned Manifestator.
