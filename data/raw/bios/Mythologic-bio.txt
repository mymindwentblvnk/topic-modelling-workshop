American progressive metal  band from from Pennsylvania.After the split-up of band Andeavor, guitarists Steve Matusik and Chris Rodler formed Mythologic with Chris` wife Melissa and brother Brett.
Current Line-up:
Steve Matusik - Guitar (Andeavor)
Melissa Rodler - Vocals (Razor Wire Shrine)
Chris Rodler - Guitar, Bass (Andeavor, Razor Wire Shrine)
