Theories Divide are an unsigned metal band from Dublin, Ireland that play Groove Metal infused with some old school flair.

They released their debut EP, "Revolutions" in August of 2013, followed by a release of a single "Reborn" in 2014 and finally in 2016 they released their debut album "The Outcast".
