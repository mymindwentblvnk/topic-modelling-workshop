There are two artists named 'Above'.

1. ABOVE is a new project involving Samael's members.
For the story, ABOVE was formed on the sixth of June 2006, it is the fruit of a collaboration between creative minds who wanted to focus on a common project.
Described as "Merciless over the top Metal with an intensity rarely achieved", you can check the first song "Black Hole" on the band's MySpace.

2. This band 'Above' exists since summer 2005 and is stationed at Dokkum, Netherlands.
