Holopaw is an American indie band in Gainesville, Florida. The band was named after the town of Holopaw in Osceola County, Florida, although none of the members of the band has ever lived there. One of its members, John Orth, joined up with Modest Mouse frontman Isaac Brock and others in 2002 to release the album Sharpen Your Teeth under the band name Ugly Casanova. Another member, Michael Johnson, went on to join Kurt Heasley's Lilys. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Holopaw">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
