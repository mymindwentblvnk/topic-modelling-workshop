Hailing from Western Sydney, Damarill are the finest exponents of progressive heavy music the scene has to offer. Citing a wide range of influences, Damarill’s aim is to make the best heavy music in Sydney. Forming in late 2004, Damarill consists of Weege on vocals, Yonn on drums, Cam on bass and Chris on guitar. The lads have been honing their skills for years playing in several other bands and have come together to make music that moves people. Pretention and clichés aside, Damarill offer an engaging and explosive live show full of passion and conviction. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Damarill">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
