Founded in 2008, Xenocide has seen many line up changes until 2011 when the writing for their first album "Galactic Oppression" began. Based out of Vancouver, BC, Canada, Xenocide brings space based technical death metal to the masses with lyrical content spanning Ascension, Colonization of other worlds, and holographic universes.
