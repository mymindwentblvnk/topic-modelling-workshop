The band was formed in January 2003. Its music is influenced by many styles varying from melodic death and modern metal to progressive metal with art rock touch. The music is built on Anastacia’s screaming supported by heavy riffing, melodic solos and atmospheric keyboards.

The band has played over 50 gigs, released one demo-album and a music video under the name «Sluchayniy Gost». And also released as «Riverain» in April 2009 EP «The Dream». New full-lehgth, «Overstepping the Verge», has been released in June 2010 by Irond ltd. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/RiveRain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
