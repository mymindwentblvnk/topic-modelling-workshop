Somnae is a Dark Melodic Metal band rising from the overcast lands of the Pacific Northwest. Their sound can be defined as a blend of somber metal atmospheres, deep haunting themes and yet very distinctive and memorable passages. 

Members: 

Jesse Heidner - Vocals, bass.
EJ Oropza - Guitars
Alexander Linder - Drums
Rob Steinway - Guitars (live)
Jeffrey Stephens - Keyboards (live)
