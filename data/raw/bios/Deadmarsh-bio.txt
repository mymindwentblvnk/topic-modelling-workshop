Deadmarsh is a legendary Thrash Metal band from Minsk, Belarus, formed in 1998.
The current line up of the band is:
Alexander Knysh - vocals
Alexey Bumin - guitars/back vocal 
Valeriy Zvariko - guitars
Oleg Patskevich - bass
Andrey Adamtsevich - drums
