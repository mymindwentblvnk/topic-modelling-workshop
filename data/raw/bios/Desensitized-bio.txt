Desensitized is a New York City hardcore band. They play fast, straightforward hardcore with an upbeat edge in the vein of SOA, Negative FX, Gorilla Biscuits, and Bad Brains.

Desensitized released their 2010 demo on October 15th, 2010. It was recorded and mixed with Glen Lorieo and James Williams at Night Owl Studios in NYC.

Irish Voodoo Records (Southern CA) released Desensitized's "Bad Apple" EP in July 2012. Bad Apple was recorded with Eliot Geller at Geller Cellar Studios in Mahopac, NY. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Desensitized">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
