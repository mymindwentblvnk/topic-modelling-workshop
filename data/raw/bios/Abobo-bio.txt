Abobo is a retrowave artist based in Italy. His music is the result of the musical background developed during his childhood and early adolescence, which includes funk, italo disco, 80s pop, movie and videogame soundtracks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abobo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
