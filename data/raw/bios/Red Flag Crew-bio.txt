Red Flag Crew is a pirate metal band from Zaragoza, Spain, formed in 2013. In 2014 they released the album Black Sails on the Horizon (EP) and in 2015 Tides of Blood (full-length), where four songs are re-recorded from the EP album.

Román Ramirez "Captain Rom": Keyboards (Captain), 
Guillermo García "Tormentor": Drums (Surgeon), 
Alejandro García: Vocals (Quartermaster), 
David Prades: Guitar (Stowaway),
