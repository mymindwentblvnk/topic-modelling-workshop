Post-rock/doom metal/symphonic project of multi-instrumentalist Joe Hawker, hailing from the UK dealing with melancholic, simplistic-yet-powerful tracks with intricate layering and expansive atmospheres. To date he has two EP's that can be downloaded for free from Bandcamp. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Of+Solitude+and+Solemn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
