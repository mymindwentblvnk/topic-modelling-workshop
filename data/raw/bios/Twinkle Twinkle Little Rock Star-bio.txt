Twinkle Twinkle Little Rock Star is a music collective which creates lullaby instrumental versions of the popular artists since 2010. A request for the new album of a specific artist can be submited at their official webpage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Twinkle+Twinkle+Little+Rock+Star">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
