Daemonius is a one man black metal band from from Alunda, Sweden. Formed by Angramain'yo (Azhubham Haani, Dysentery, De Vermis Mysteriis) in 1992. He released only one demo Rehearsal recorded in 20/09/1992. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Daemonius">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
