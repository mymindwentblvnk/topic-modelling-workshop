Spreadsheet of all Siriusmo songs
Siriusmo (Moritz Friedrich) is from Berlin, and is signed to Monkeytown records. He has released a handful of EPs over the years, including Diskoding, Allthegirls, Minirock, and the Sirius EP. He works regularly with Modeselektor, either through collaboration or remixing. 

After having said he would not DJ or perform live, due to conflicting interests, he finally gave in when he performed his first live mix on a radio show in 2010. After this, he has also been seen in a few prestigious German clubs performing live.  It has been said that he is interested in VST production and is a prolific graffiti artist. After years of steady work and copious amounts of remixes, his debut album, Mosaik, was released on February 25th, 2011.
