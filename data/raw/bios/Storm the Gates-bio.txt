Storm The Gates are an unsigned five-piece heavy/thrash metal band from London, England. First forming in the summer of 2008, they quickly took London by "storm" and plan to be recording their first album in the summer of 2010. Their influences include Iron Maiden, Metallica, Pink Floyd and Mastodon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Storm+the+Gates">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
