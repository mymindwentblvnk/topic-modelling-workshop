Vultra is a heavy metal band from Spokane, WA. Band members are: Craig "Axeman" Rudder (lead vocals/rhythm and lead guitar), Tony Nelson (drums), Amity " The Blood Queen" Sowers (Background Vocals) and Jack Cutler (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vultra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
