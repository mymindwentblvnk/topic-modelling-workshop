Deferum Sacrum is a black metal band hailing from Ukraine (Kyyiv) formed in the year 2005.

Current line-up: 
Athamas - Vocals, bass (Raventale)
Morthvarg - Guitar (Balance Interruption, Semargl, Den of Winter)
Yar - Drums (Balance Interruption, Den of Winter)
Astaroth - Guitar (Raventale, Den of Winter)
