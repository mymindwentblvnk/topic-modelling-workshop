Wolves Guard My Coffin plays black/thrash or "War Metal" in the vein of Destroyer 666, Desaster, Absu, and Gospel of the Horns. The Northern Virginia band started in the summer of 2009. The current lineup consists of World Eater (Guiars, Vocals), Plague Tyrant (Guitars), Hammer of Conquest (Bass) and Consuming Fog (Percussion).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wolves+Guard+My+Coffin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
