Fortuna Fugaz is Adrián Méndez's solo project. This started the first week of December 2007, and the first songs written by Adrián were Fortuna and Todo es Fugaz. 

During the following months, the songs "Turambar, Amo del destino", "No eres nadie (Sangre Azul cover), "Cuando quieras olvidar" and "Más allá del valle de la oscuridad were added to the list.

The Fortuna Fugaz project is full of emotion, love and passion. And, in Adrián's own words: "This album is a fragment of life and thoughts that, just like me, will probably never be the same again. This is a bubble locking a moment in history, MY history, and the history of those who accompanied me and are identified with this work".
