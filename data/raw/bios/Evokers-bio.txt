Evokers are a Brazilian brutal death metal band formed in 1997. In 2001, they released a demo titled Scephalyc Retaliation, and released a split with Maggots a year later titled Splattered Manifestation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evokers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
