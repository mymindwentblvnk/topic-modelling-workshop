Clamath is a thrashmetal group from Finland. Turo Niskakoski on bass and vocals, Mikko Syväterä on guitar and Panu Koskinen on drums, this trio is pretty much of an armageddon on stage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Clamath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
