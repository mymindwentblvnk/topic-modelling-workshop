Another Vendetta started around October 2006, when they were asked to play on a school venue and after that, the band was formed.

Another Vendetta is a hardcore/metal band from Nijmegen, Netherlands. They write about political issues, opinions and emotions. The songs are fast and melodic with clean/grunt vocals and fast melodic guitar riffs.

Another Vendetta is influenced by bands like: Rise Against, Boysetsfire, Antillectual, Metallica, Opeth and many more! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Another+Vendetta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
