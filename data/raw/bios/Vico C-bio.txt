Vico C (Born Armando Lozada Cruz on September 8, 1971)is a Puerto Rican rapper and singer who is one of the founders of the Reggaeton Genre. He was born in New York but raised in Puerto Rico. Vico C entered the charts with the super hit "La Recta Final" (The Way To End, literally: "The Final Stretch") and become famous across the Americas. He started touring to places like New York, Chicago, Miami, El Salvador, Venezuela, Dominican Republic. He is known for recently hits like "Desahogo" "5 De Septiembre" dedicated to his daughter & many more. The most famous song is "Tony Presidio". He has also the best song ever  " Me Acuerdo".
