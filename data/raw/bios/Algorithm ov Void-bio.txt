Algorithm ov Void is a one-man Ambient Black Metal band from Poland. He released an EP entitled "We Are All Nothing" in 2010 with Depressive Illusions Records. Marek is also behind the one-man black metal act Garden of Sadness. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Algorithm+ov+Void">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
