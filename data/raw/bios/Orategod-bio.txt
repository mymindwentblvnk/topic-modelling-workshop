Orategod is a brutal death metal band from Santiago, Chile that was formed in 1999. It's worth noting that their lyrical topics deal with mysticism, apocalypse, thelema,etc as opposed to the usual topics of gore,murder,torture,etc of this genre..  

Their discography consists of : 

pentamuerte (Demo, 1998)
From the Dark Immortals (Demo, 2002)
Fall Slowly Until The Hell Abyss (Full-length, 2004)
Courts Ophion (EP, 2009)
