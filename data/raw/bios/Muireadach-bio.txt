BIO:
Muireadach was born in December 2005. 
Its  work shows the style and trends of baroque and classical composers and several metal artists such as Yngwie, Helloween, Hangar, and Angra.

During 2006, the members dedicated themselves to the writing of the music, saving live presentations for a close future. The result were twelve songs, and four of them are part of their first discographic material “Omnine Secundo”, released on December 21th of 2007.

Their Debut was the same night of “Omnine Secundo” demo’s launching, as a support-band for HANGAR’s Show during the Conviction Tour (of “The reasson of your conviction” material).

Today they are working in a CD, hopefully to be release in 2012 with the reintegration of Xiaxia in the Keyboards.
