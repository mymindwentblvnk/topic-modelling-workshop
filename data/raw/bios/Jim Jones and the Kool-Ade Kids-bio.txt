Crossover band who formed in 1983 and released a single album and two demos before splitting up. They played a form of punk influenced thrash metal with a prominent doom influence, sounding similar to Celtic Frost and Hellhammer. The band had a second LP planned, however they could not find a proper record deal and as such split. Their unreleased album was to be called Contrafusion and was released as a demo.

Releases:

- Adventures in Distortion (Demo, 1985)
- Trust Me (Full Length, 1986)
- Contrafusion (Demo, 1988)
