There is more than one Tectonic.
1) A thrash metal band from Romania Bucuresti formed in 1987. They released the album "Anomalia" in 1995.
2) Tectonic are Janette Stuart and Gavin Charles, an electronica/pop outfit from Melbourne, Australia.
Formed in 1996, they released 2 EP's, Big Bang and Loud and Clear. 
For more information and to listen to their music, go to http://www.home.aone.net.au/~tectonic
