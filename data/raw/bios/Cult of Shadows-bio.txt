An extreme Gothic metal band from Italy. Not much is known about this band aside the members and the two demo releases. The band was formed in 2002 and released two demo albums. The band is still active and additional albums are yet to be released. Their music embodies not only Gothic metal, but also has elements of melodic death metal and symphonic black metal as well. Their albums are very rare even in Italy.

Releases:

2003 - From Maelstrom - A 4 track demo. This is the debut demo release.
2004 - Sinking Through the Dark Eyes - This is their second demo. It has 5 tracks.
