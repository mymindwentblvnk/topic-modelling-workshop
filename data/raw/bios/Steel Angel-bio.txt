Steel Angel was a power/heavy metal band from France  (Bordeaux, Aquitaine) . They formed in 1983, and released several minor releases before their legendary full-length pieces "And the Angels were Made of Steel" and "Kiss of Steel" were released in 1985 and 1986 respectively. Lineup:

Pat Montero - Vocals
Michel Guerrero - Guitars
Patrick Bonnifet - Guitars, Vocals
Paul Breitner - Bass
