Weird Light was a doom metal band created by M.Blacklord (vocals, guitars, music) and F.H.Faust (bass-guitar, backing vocals, lyrics) in May of 2004.

They released one demo, "Doomicvs Vobiscvm", in 2007 and split up soon after that. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Weird+Light">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
