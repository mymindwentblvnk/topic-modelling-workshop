Thees Uhlmann (born 16th April 1974) is the singer and guitarist of the german indie-rock-band Tomte and the owner of the label Grand Hotel Van Cleef. In 2011, he released a self-titled solo album under his own name.
Since then, he released two further studio albums: #2 in 2013 and 'Junkies und Scientologen' in 2019. Furthermore, he released his first novel 'Sophia, der Tod und ich' in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thees+Uhlmann">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
