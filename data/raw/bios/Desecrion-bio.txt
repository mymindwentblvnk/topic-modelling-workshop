Brutal Death Metal from San Jose, California. Here to slay the untrue and spread the message of hate throughout the world in the name of death. In late December of 2008 through early 2009 Desecrion released The Scourge E.P. containing 3 tracks: Prolonged Laceration, Scourge, and Mental Mutilation. Desecrion is currently working on its self-titled full length album to be completed in the near future. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desecrion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
