Stargod, born Isaac Ntim, arrived into the world on the 1st of May, 1995, in Koforidua, Eastern Region of Ghana. He was born to Ghana parents Mr. and Mrs. Ntim, who raised him in Koforidua. According to records, Stargod attended Pentecost Preparatory School (PPS), from where he went on to Koforidua Secondary Technical school for his secondary school education. He then moved on to Further his education at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Kumasi where he pursued his degree in Computer Science.

In his little time on the microphone, the singer has proved to be one of the most promising artist to come out soon.

The Afrobeat songwriter launched his career in late 2017 unofficially but came out officially in 2018 where he formed a group called RicoMob with his Day 1 friends of 6 doing music together in the eastside of Ghana. He dropped his first Single titled: “Pengting” in 2017. The following year the Singer released another single titled: “Night life” to the excitement of many music lovers.
