Mega Beardo is the moniker of Ryan Postlethwait, lead guitarist of Descension Rate. His debut album, "Mega Beardo: A Mega Man 2 Guitar Tribute," was a loving tribute to one of his favorite things of the last 20 years. He continues his journey into Nintendo nerdom with the release of his second album "Belmont," an original concept album based on characters from the first Castlevania game. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mega+Beardo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
