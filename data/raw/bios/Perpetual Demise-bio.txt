Perpetual Demise was a death metal band from the Netherlands, formed in 1989. They released two demos, "Massacre to Be" in 1992 and "When Fear Becomes..." in 1993, and a full-length album titled "Arctic" in 1996 on DSFA Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Perpetual+Demise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
