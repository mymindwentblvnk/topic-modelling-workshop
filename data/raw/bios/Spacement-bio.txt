Spacement is a band located (only physically) in Athens, Greece and formed in the hot summer in 2012. The sound of the band, emerging from the emptiness of deep space, contains a fair amount of stoned and psychedelic riffs, passing from doom and sludge, to post influences and with heavy fuzzy tone.The group consists of Nikos (vocals),Stathis (Guitar),Thanasis (Bass) and Tsak (Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spacement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
