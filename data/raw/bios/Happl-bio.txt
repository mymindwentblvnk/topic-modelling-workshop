Happl was an Austrian heavy metal band formed by Gottfried Happl. They released 1 album "Signed" om 1991 on Wollvieh Productions. The band has also recorded the "Never Gonna Stop" album in 1989, which was "released" in 1 or 2 copies without the band's permission. The band was also featured on the "Made In Vienna" compilation with two tracks: 'At the Top' and 'Never Gonna Stop'.

Lineup:
Gottfried Happl - Vocals, guitar
Robert Gösi - Bass
Tom Kräutner - Drums
Wolfgang Czeland - Keyboards
Bobby Kapsarev - Keyboards, flute
