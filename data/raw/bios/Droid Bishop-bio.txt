Droid Bishop is an American synthwave musical artist, keyboardist and songwriter. 

Bishop is the brother of musician Sam Sparro. His music combining the 80's dream with a futuristic soundscape, Droid Bishop brings a unique approach to the world of synthwave, sometimes reminiscent of artists like Jean Michel Jarre or Vangelis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Droid+Bishop">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
