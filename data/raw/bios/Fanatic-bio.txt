1.Fanatic is a project by R&B/Hip Hop songwriter/producer. Andreao Shaunee Heard. He has worked with 702, Lil' Kim, Olivia Longott & The Notorious B.I.G.. In 2005 the single 'Stupid Like U', featuring award winning Soul/Dance diva Deborah Cox was released through a deal with EMI Records. Because of label politics the project was shelved no further releases followed. However, many bootleg remixes of the songs can be found online.
