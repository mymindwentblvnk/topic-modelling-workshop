In 2001, after close to nine years, more than 350 shows and 3 CDs, 
forced to decay felt like doing something new! 
thorstens new bands are GHOULS & THE OTHER. 
carsten, marc und daniel are playing in IDEOBLAST und ONCE A DEMON 
forced to decay is dead !!
