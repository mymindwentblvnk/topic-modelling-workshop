Black Well is a Kuwait-based metal band that consists of:

Adam Benobaid (Vocals/Guitar)
Ahmed Al Zubaidi (Guitar)
Monish Shringi (Bass)

Following the disbanding of Gates To Valhalla, Adam and Ahmed  got together to write new material, which led to the creation of Black Well. Black Well later recruited Monish Shringi to take up the position of bassist in January of 2010 and completed recording bass parts for 'The Human Condition'.

Black Well started writing and recording material in September of 2009, starting off with 'Before I Forget You' and as of May 2010, recording and mixing of 'The Human Condition' was completed. 'The Human Condition' is yet to be released and the release date is yet to be decided. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Well">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
