Alcoholica is a heavy metal / thrash metal / rock band from Świerklaniec, Poland.  They formed in 2004.  They started as a Metallica cover band.  Their current lineup consists of Wojciech "Sasim" Sasimowski (vocals, guitars), Marcin "Kęda" Kędzierski (guitars), Michał "Misiek" Flak (bass) and Michał "Misqot" Skotniczny  (drums).  They are not signed to a record label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alcoholica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
