Rabenholz is a german Black Metal Band.
The Band was founded in 2006 by Svarog and Totenfrost, in 2009 they were joined by Zythifer and Hevnbrann to complete the Band.
Their first album "Akt 1 - Auf welken Schwingen" was released in 2010.

Lineup:
Svarog: Guitar, Bass, Vocals
Totenfrost: Vocals
Zythifer: Guitar
Hevnbrann: Drums
