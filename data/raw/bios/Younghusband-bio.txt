Younghusband are Pete Baker (drums/pads), Adam Beach (guitar), Joe Chilton (Bass/vocals) and frontman Euan Hinshelwood (vocals/keyboards/ guitar), who is also a member of Emmy The Great.

Since forming they have steadily built a growing fanbase that includes Zane Lowe, Marc Riley, Guy Garvey and Jarvis Cocker. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Younghusband">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
