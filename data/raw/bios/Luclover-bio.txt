Ryan Mounfik (Better known as his stage name Luclover) is a 17 year old artist based in Houston, Texas, his family orignally hailing from Morocco. He was born on January 9th 2005. Before music, Luclover had a passion for Basketball, which fell through in early 2019, leaving him to go to his next best option, making music. His parents were supportive of his decision and decided to help him set up an in home music studio, where he still records. He released his first single “999” with Dmxn at just 14 years old. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Luclover">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
