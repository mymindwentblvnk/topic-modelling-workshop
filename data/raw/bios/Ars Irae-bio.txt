Black metal band from Germany(Rosenheim) formed in 2004.

The band released two EPs:
Ignis Fatuus 2005
Verwelkt 2007

Band members:
Michi - Vocals
Flo - Guitar
Stephan - Guitars
Toni - Drums

Former members:
Ronny - Bass

The band is currently unsigned to a label.
