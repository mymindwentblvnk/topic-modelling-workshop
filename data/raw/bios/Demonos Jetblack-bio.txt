Side project of Demonos Sova, better known as the mastermind of Barathrum, and Jetblack Roima, former Barathrum guitarist. Demonos Jetblack was active between 1991-1992 and released two demos, "Ange De Mort" in 1991 and "Necro Sodomy" in 1992. The music style is very similar to old Barathrum material, old school doomy black metal.

"Ange De Mort" tape features "Winter of the Black Snow" a song also on Barathrum's "Witchmaster" demo tape 1991 and the debut album "Hailstorm". Demonos Jetblack was one of the first bands to record extreme metal with Finnish lyrics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demonos+Jetblack">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
