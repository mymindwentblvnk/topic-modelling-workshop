Sandrider is an unearthly loud and brutal RAWK band from Seattle, WA. The trio is comprised of Jon Weisnewski (Akimbo, Homo Eradicus, White Jazz) on guitar,  Jesse Roberts (Ruby Doe, Kid Congo) on bass and Nat Damm (Akimbo, Tight Bros From Way Back When, Automaton) on drums. 

They recorded their Self Titled debut with Matt Bayles at The Red Room in 2010.
