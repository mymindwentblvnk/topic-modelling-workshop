Warpstone is a progressive melodic death/black metal band from Nice, Provence-Alpes-Côte-d'Azur, France.  They were formed in 2012.  They consider their music to be called "Occult Dark Metal."  The band consists of three members.   Anthony Malaussena on guitar & vocals (clean),  Michaël Raverdino on bass & vocals (harsh) and  Jean-Baptiste Pol on drums.  They released Daemonic Warpfire in 2012.  Their latest album is Into the Phantasmancer Celestial Castle and that was released in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Warpstone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
