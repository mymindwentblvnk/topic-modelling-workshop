Coptic Light was a band from New York, NY started by Jeff Winterberg (ex-Antioch Arrow), Jon Fine (of Don Caballero fame, also a member of Bitch Magnet) and Kevin Shea (who shared a band - Storm & Stress - with another former Don Caballero member Ian Williams) in early 2000's. Coptic Light Broke up in 2006. 

The band released a self-titled 7" on volumeone records in 2003, a self-titled CD on No Quarter records in 2005 and a posthumous self-titled EP CD (a shared effort of No Quarter and dotlinecircle records) in 2007.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coptic+Light">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
