At the tender age of only 19, Shirin is a young inspirational singer songwriter who is a rising star. 

Shirin has performed to audiences in excess of 25,000, at events such as the Danson Festival in London during 2006 and 2007. She has followed her music to other countries including trips to Norway, Cyprus and the USA where she sang her brand of Pop and R&B.

Her experience of recording has included work with top producers in Los Angeles, USA, such as Benny Cassette and KC Porter.

Back in the UK, Shirin continues to hone her craft with emerging Nast Boi Productions, whilst consideration for performances at Londons top venues takes shape. Appearing on ITV Television in England and recording several Radio interviews, plus making her debut on an album in Barbados when she was just 14 years old.

In 2010/11 Shirin did a tour of schools in the UK to promote her music to high school students.
