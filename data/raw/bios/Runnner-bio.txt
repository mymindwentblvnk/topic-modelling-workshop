Runnner is the project of songwriter and producer Noah Weinman. Sometimes working alone, other times acting as a musical collective with a close group of friends, Runnner fuses intimate songwriting with experimental production to create what he calls “bandcamp songs for soundcloud kids.” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Runnner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
