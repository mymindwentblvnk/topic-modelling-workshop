Jake Fairley is a Canadian techno artist now residing in Berlin, who had a number of releases (including an album) on that city's famous Sender label before moving on to release an album of bolshy pants-off electro-rock with 2004's 'Touch Not The Cat'. He has an accompanying live show which he has toured extensively around the world.

He also records under the alter-ego Fairmont, releasing an album 'Paper Stars' on Cologne's Traum label and 'Gazebo' 12" on Border Community. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jake+Fairley">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
