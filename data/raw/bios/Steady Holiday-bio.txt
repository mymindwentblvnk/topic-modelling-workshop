Steady Holiday is American singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist Andrea "Dre" Babinski. Based in Los Angeles, and formerly of Miracle Days and Dusty Rhodes and the River Band, Babinski released her debut album, "Under the Influence", in 2016. 

Following the disbandment of Miracle Days (formed with guitarist Edson Choi), Babinski worked as a touring musician with Hunter Hunted. 

Her first solo single, "Your Version of Me," was released in 2015. Additional singles appeared leading up to the April 2016 release of her debut album, Under the Influence. Her follow-up album, Nobody's Watching, was released in 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Steady+Holiday">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
