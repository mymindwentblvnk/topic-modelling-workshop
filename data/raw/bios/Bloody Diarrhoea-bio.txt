LO-FI electro gore grind band from Děčín, Czech Republic. B.D. use gabber elements to their gore grind, automatic drum machine and harmonized vocals. 

Discography:
- Shitstorm (Sounds od Pneumatic Clyster) - CDr (2003)
- Tribute to Boneyard CD (2006)
- Cybergrind Pornogoremageddon - 4 way split w/ Fucksaw, Psychosadistic Haterapist, Tumour - CD (2006)
- Kings of Shit - 4 way split w/ Reek Of Shits, Hovní Puch, Sračka - CD-r (2006)
- Tenesmus - full CD (2010, Bizarre Leprous Prod.)
   and some compilations...

Actual line-up: Halbi - guitar, Francek - guitar, Bambus - vox, Mlaďas - basstard
