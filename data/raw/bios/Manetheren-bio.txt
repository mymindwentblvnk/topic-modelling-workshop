Manetheren is a two man band from Minneapolis, Minnesota in the United States of America. Formed in the year 2003. They have 3 albums, The Seven Realms of Manetheren, The Absence of Light, and Solitary Remnants. The latest album is due to be released in 2012. Accompanied by Gionata Potenti, and Music from Peter Anderson of Raison D'etre.

Manetheren is in process of a new album for 2011, with live drums from Gionata Potenti of Frostmoon Eclipse/ Glorior Belli/ Former Handful of Hate/ Former Nocternity sesstion drummer, on the latest album. The album is six tracks long and has shifted towards the atmospheric black metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Manetheren">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
