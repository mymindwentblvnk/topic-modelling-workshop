Jacques Palminger and the Kings of Dub Rock are Jacques Palminger (one of the founders of Studio Braun, theaterdirector, actor and audiobookauthor), Viktor Marek (part of Knarf Rellöm Trinity, part of The Boy Group and was part of 8doggymoto) and Rica Blunck (former dutch naked dancer of a russian club near the french border of Luxembourg, later choreograph of theater, opera, video-clips and movies. Singer for (e.g.) Die Zimmermänner, Adolf Noise, International Pony and Studio Braun. Their debut "Mondo Cherry" is out on PIAS. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jacques+Palminger+&+The+Kings+of+Dub+Rock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
