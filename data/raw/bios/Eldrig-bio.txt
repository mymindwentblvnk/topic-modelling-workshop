A solo project by Eldrig of the band Fanisk, playing similar but more guitar oriented epic black metal with serious influences from the works of Savitri Devi, Miguel Serrano, C. G. Jung, and Friedrich Nietzsche. 

So far his official releases consists of four full-length albums:

Kali (2007)
Everlasting War Divinity (2007)
Mysterion (2008)
Urlagarne (2012)

He also recorded two demos:

Beyond the Strength Of Kings (2003)
