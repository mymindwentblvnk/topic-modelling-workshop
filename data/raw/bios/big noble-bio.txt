Big Noble is an experimental duo formed in 2014, consisting of Daniel Kessler (of Interpol) and Joseph Fraioli (Datach'i). They have collaborated before, notably when Fraioli was invited to engineer Kessler's remix of Not Even Jail for a special edition of Antics. 
Big Noble's debut LP, First Light, is due to be released in early 2015.
