Aloha From Hell was a pop punk band formed in 2006, in Aschaffenburg, Germany, consisting of Vivien Eileen "Vivi" Bauernschmidt (vocals), Moritz "Moo" Keith (guitar), Andreas "Andi" Gerhardt (guitar), Max Forman (bass), and Felix "Feli" Keith (drums). They were the winners of a new-band contest sponsored by the German teen magazine "Bravo."

The group's debut album  No More Days to Waste was released on January 16, 2009.

On July 15, 2010, the members announced the breakup of the band.
