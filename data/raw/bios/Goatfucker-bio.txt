Goatfucker is a raw black metal band from Franklin, Tennessee.  Goatfucker started in the year 2004.  It currently consists of one person.  Graven Hatred does vocals, guitars, bass and drum programming.   Yamaha Goat the Unholy was the drummer only in 2004, he's only on the demo and first EP.  Graven Hatred writes songs about gore, anti-Christianity and violence.  Goatfucker is currently signed to Inverted Cross Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Goatfucker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
