Varus is a symphonic melodic death metal band from Bamberg, Germany. The band name is a reference to Publius Quinctilius Varus, a Roman general whose army was defeated and annihilated by Germanic tribes in the Battle of the Teutoburg Forest (9 AD). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Varus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
