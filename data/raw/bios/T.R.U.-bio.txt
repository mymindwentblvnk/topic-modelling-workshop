T.R.U. (The Real University) is a hip-hop group from Atlanta, Georgia. The main members are Skooly, Sleepy Rose, Worl, and Hott LockedIn, all members of 2 Chainz' record label. 2 Chainz often appears on their releases.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/T.R.U.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
