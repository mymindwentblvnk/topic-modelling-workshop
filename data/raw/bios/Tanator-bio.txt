Tanator was given birth in 2012 by Alligator and Egor Petrov. Located in St.-Petersburg, Russia. 
Later incorporated members of Pyre, Bolo, Mortal Virus, etc. 

The first album was DIY recorded in 2014 and released on DIY tapes. 
It was released in 2016 on CD by Grotesque Records.
line-up: Alligator - guitars/voc
