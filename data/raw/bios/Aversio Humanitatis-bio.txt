Aversio Humanitatis is a black metal band from Madrid, Spain, formed in 2010. Releases include debut album Abandonment Ritual in 2011, split EP Three Ways of Consciousness with Selbst and Nihil in 2013, another split with Primigenium in 2015, an EP Longing for the Untold in 2017 and second album Behold the Silent Dwellers in 2020 with Debemur Morti productions.
