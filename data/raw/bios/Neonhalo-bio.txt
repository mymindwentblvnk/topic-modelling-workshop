NEONHALO are a death/extreme metal band from Chatham, Kent. Locally renowned for their high energy, frantic paced live shows- NEONHALO have quickly become one of the areas top metal acts, landing themselves a spot at Bloodstock 2013 by winning the Kent region Metal 2 the Masses competition.
