There is more than one artist that goes by the name Negativa:

1. A short-lived technical/experimental death metal band from Quebec, which was a successor to Gorguts while that band was inactive (2005-2008). Released one self-titled EP in 2006.

2. A solo black metal project from Spain. Sole member DB has other solo projects (Hässlig, Déliriant) and is the owner of Bile Noire (record label). Negativa is signed to the well-known underground avant-metal label Mystískaos and is associated with Sentient Ruin Laboratories.
