Allison Crutchfield (born in 1989) is an American singer and guitarist who previously co-founded Swearin' and P.S. Eliot (the latter with her twin sister Katie Crutchfield). When Swearin' initially split in 2015, she had already self-released the EP "Lean in to It" in 2014. In 2017, she released the album "Tourist in This Town".
