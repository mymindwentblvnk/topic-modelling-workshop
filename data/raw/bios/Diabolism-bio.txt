Diabolism is a Pagan Black Metal band from Bulgaria (Burgas). They were formed in 1994 and are currently split-up. Last known line-up was Agarvaen - Vocals, Bass, Lord Deimos Infernal Frost (aka Deimoz of The Revenge Project) - Guitars, Angrist - Drums. Former/past member(s) are Ivan Ganchev - Drums (Drummer in Almost Dead right now), Radostin Dimitrov and Zombie. Their only full-length album is Endless Darkend Saga (1997 - Wizard). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diabolism">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
