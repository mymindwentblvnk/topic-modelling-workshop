There are 2 bands that go by the name of Akela:

1. Hungarian thrash metal band from Budapest. Akela is the name of a wolf in Kipling's "The Jungle Book".

Members:
Katona László „Főnök” – vocal (1990-)
Kovács Attila „Pókember” – guitar (2003-)
Király Zoltán „Piócamen” – drums (2007-)
Tóth Károly "Kártmen" - guitar (2012-)
Mezőfi József "November" - bass (2012-)


Biography:

1990: The band was founded by László Pálvölgyi(bass), László Katona(vocals), Gyula Pavelka(guitar) and József Sobry(drums).
1991: Second guitarist István Györki joined the band.
1992: Sobry left the band, new drummer: Miklós "Retek" Ratkai.
1994: Retek leaves, new drummer: Gábor Kertész.
1995: Sobry rejoins now for the 6th time.
1996: New drummer: Gábor Szalay.
1998: Band disbands.
2002: Reunion.
2007: Second disband of the band. Last live show: 31.10.07.
2009: Final reunion. New lineup.
2010: 20th year anniversary gigs.
