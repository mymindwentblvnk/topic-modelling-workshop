Samomor ("Suicide" in band's vernacular) existed in less palpable forms since before 2008. But it wasn't until that year that some of us had the privilege to witness the advent of its musical expression and therefrom plunge our artistic drought into its melding of ecstasy and complete self-destruction. Musically expressed in the gloomy gloss of depressive black metal, and lyrically inspired by the uncanny verses of the Slovenian poet Kosovel, - the result: a dark journey that set its controls for the darkest night of the soul -  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Samomor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
