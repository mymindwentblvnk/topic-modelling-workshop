Longobardeath is an heavy metal band which sings all of its songs in Milanese language. The band plays in little clubs near Milan, and is fronted by the singer Ul Mik. Other members of the band include Ul Robert (Guitars), Ul Teo (Bass) and Ul Giurgin (Drums).
