Sochitta Sal, better known by her stage name Honey Cocaine, is a Cambodian-Canadian rapper from Toronto born on October 22, 1992. Honey Cocaine is signed to Tyga's label Last Kings; the first artist signed to the label. She first received recognition with her debut collaboration "Heisman", featuring Tyga. She recently released her first mixtape, titled "Fuck Yo Feelings." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Honey+Cocaine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
