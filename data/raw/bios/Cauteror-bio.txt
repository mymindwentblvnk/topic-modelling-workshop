Cauteror was a Dutch death doom metal band from Drachten, founded by Maurice (Mories de Jong, now involved with Gnaw Their Tongues).
They released two demos, "To Atonement I Am Owed" in 1991 and "They Fell...." in 1993. Later changed name to Soulwound.

Line-up:
Wieger - vocals, guitar, bass
Maurice - guitar, bass, keyboards, backing vocals
Carine - female vocals, keyboards
