Rioux is the musical project of electronic songwriter/producer, Erin Rioux (pronounced ree-yoo). With a left-of-center pop sensibility, Rioux crafts ornate, psychedelic productions with organic instrumentation. After a creative excursion to Berlin, Rioux has returned to Brooklyn with a new live band and a series of upcoming releases throughout 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rioux">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
