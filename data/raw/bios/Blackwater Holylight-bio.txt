Blackwater Holylight are a Portland based psychedelic rock band. Comprised entirely of women, the band gained a quick following and subsequently were signed not long after forming. Their first album dropped in 2018 to positive reception with a second album quickly following. The band incorporates elements of psychedelic, shoegaze, pop and doom into a sound that is heavy yet melodic & dreamy.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blackwater+Holylight">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
