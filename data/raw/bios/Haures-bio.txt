Haures- Polish band playing melodic death metal. The band was formed in October 2010, by two guitarists: Phobos and Marcus. In January 2011, they invited the vocalist, Samael, to cooperation. In March, the drummer, who left the band, was replaced by the new one: Shark. Two months later, Marcusâ€™ brother joined the group as the bassist. 
The name of the group is taken from the name of the fallen angel: Haures.
