Phalon "Jazze Pha" Alexander (pronounced Jazzie Fay) (born 1975 in Memphis, Tennessee) is an American songwriter, rapper and record producer.

He is known for his collaborations with Nappy Roots, Nelly, Ludacris, Trick Daddy, T.I. and Lil' Wayne, among others.

He has signed a production deal with Cash Money Records, and also has his own record label, Sho'nuff, which signed Ciara and Jody Breeze. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jazze+Pha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
