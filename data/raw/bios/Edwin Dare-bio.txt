Edwin Dare is an American metal band from Ohio.  Their album Can't Break Me (1994) was recorded with Bryce Barnes on vocals, Jeff Kollman on guitar and backing vocals, Tom Kollman on Drums, and Kevin Chown on bass and backing vocals.  Their style is reminiscent of Queensryche, with a slightly more metallic edge that occasionally borders on a Metallica vibe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Edwin+Dare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
