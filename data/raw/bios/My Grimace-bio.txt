My Grimace is a five-piece metal band hailing from Jyväskylä, Finland. The band was formed in 2007 but went through several lineup changes and a short hiatus before evolving into it’s current state in 2011. Current lineup consists of vocalist Jere Hämäläinen, guitarists Juha Kumpulainen and Aleksi Salojärvi, drummer Roope Salminen and bassist Joonatan Jaakonaho.
