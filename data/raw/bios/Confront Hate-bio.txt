Confront Hate is a progressive/groove metal band from Faro, Algarve, Portugal. The band has started in 2003 after the dissolution of an old project called Full Of Noise. Until 2012 the band has released 1 Self-titled Ep (in 2007) and one album called Diabolical Desguise of Madness (2011), recieving good cristicism by both national press and general public.
The band members are:
David Rosa - Vocals
Pedro Faustino - Guitar
Ricardo Albino - Guitar
Pedgoi - Bass
Pedro Costa - Drums
