Pollux is the brainchild of Arnaud Barbe, a hyper-productive boy from France. He has put out more tons of releases on numerous labels and has also founded his own net-label named "Sirona-Records". He mostly produces ambient and drone but has also produced some noise, electronica, soundscapes, idm and experimental music too.
