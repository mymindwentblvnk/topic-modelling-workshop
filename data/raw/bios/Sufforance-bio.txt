Sufforance are a young band from Poole, Dorset, England. They consist of Fil - Vocals, Ollie - Guitar, Ed - Guitar, Dan - Bass and Dylan - Drums.

They are heavy in the worst way and bring to mind the likes of Dead To Fall, Chimaira, Job For A Cowboy and Beyond The Buried and Me.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sufforance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
