Nadia Reid (born Nadia Reid-O’Reilly on 23 August 1991) is a New Zealand singer-songwriter based in Auckland. Her albums, "Listen to Formation, Look for the Signs", and "Preservation", have received wide acclaim at home in New Zealand and internationally. Reid performs live with a regular band of musicians consisting of Sam Taylor (guitar), Richie Pickard (bass), and Joe McCallum (drums). 

Reid’s first single from her album ‘’Preservation’’, "Richard", saw her nominated for the APRA Silver Scroll award in 2017, regarded as New Zealand’s most important songwriting award. Reid herself was nominated for Best Solo Artist, Breakthrough Artist of the Year and Best Alternative Artist.
