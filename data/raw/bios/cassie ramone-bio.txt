Cassie Ramone was the lead singer and primary songwriter of the Vivian Girls. Cassie and friend musician Kevin Morby of the band Woods are also in a band called The Babies. Her solo album, called "The Time Has Come," was released on August 26, 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cassie+Ramone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
