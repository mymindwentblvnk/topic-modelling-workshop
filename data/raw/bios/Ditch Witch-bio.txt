Ditch Witch was an indie band under the Grass Records label out of New York (bought by Wind-Up Records in 1996), putting out just a few albums.

Members:

Brian Coughlin - Drums
Shannon Mercer - Bass and Vocals
Chris Gebhardt - Vocals and Guitar
Barry Hibdon - Guitars

with the inclusion of:

Saylor Craig - Viola (Steal This Town)
