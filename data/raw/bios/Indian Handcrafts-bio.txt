Indian Handcrafts is a three piece rock band from Barrie, ON, Canada formed by Brandyn James Aikins and Daniel Brandon Allen in 2010. The band's first release entitled Indian Handcrafts was self released in 2011 in January of 2012 they signed to US Based label and management company Sargent House and released their first full length album, Civil Disobedience for Losers in 2012. Their second full length album Creeps, was released roughly 3 years later, in 2015. The band has since added a third member,  Leland Burmania, and is working on a string of EP's, slated for release in 2018. The first of these EP's "Bastard" features one 14 minute track, and was released on May 11th, 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Indian+Handcrafts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
