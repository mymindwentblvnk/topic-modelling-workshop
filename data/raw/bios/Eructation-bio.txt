Eructation was a Death/Thrash metal band from Alafors, Sweden. They only released one demo in 1992.
In April 2016, Spanish label Memento Mori released a compilation CD The Fumes Of Putrefaction (1992-1995) which includes their '92 demo tape, and 7 unreleased tracks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eructation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
