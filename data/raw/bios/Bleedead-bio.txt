Bleedead was a Brutal/Technical Death Metal band with heavy Goregrind influences. Formed in 2009 and split up in 2015, the band released one demo in 2011 entitled  Demo 2011  followed by their self-titled full-lenght in 2012. Following these releases, a single,  Marion Phauna  and an EP  Mustma Dorcheme  were released in 2013 and 2014 respectively. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleedead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
