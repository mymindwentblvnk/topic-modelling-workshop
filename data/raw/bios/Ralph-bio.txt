Raffaela Weyman was born October 20, 1990 in Toronto, Ontario. Raffaela most known as “Ralph” is a Canadian singer and songwriter from Ontario.

Ralph is signed with 604 Records (Ontario). After being signed, Ralph began releasing singles for her debut EP, “Ralph”. Her song titled "Cold to the Touch" was released on May 25, 2016 as the lead single from the EP.

After the release of her debut, Ralph began releasing singles for her debut album “A Good Girl” with titles such as, “September Fades, Girl Next Door featuring TOBi, Tables Have Turned & Weather. “A Good Girl” was released on September 28, 2018.

Her latest project’s include two new singles, “Gravity” & “No Muss No Fuss”, both released this year. Ralph will begin touring alongside Canadian pop star, “Carly Rae Jepsen” on the Canadian Leg of The Dedicated Tour this August.
