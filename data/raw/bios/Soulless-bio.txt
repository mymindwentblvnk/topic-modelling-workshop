There are multiple artists with the name Soulless:

1) Soulless are a death/thrash metal band from Cleveland, Ohio. Their music is a bit more melodic than other bands of this style. 

2) Soulless were a Polish death metal band from Katowice. They released handful of demos as well as two full-lenght albums "Total Desecration" and "Summoning Heresy" in 2003 and 2006 respectively. Currently, they are play under a name Soulless Profanation.

3) Soulless are a death metal band from Greece. They were formed in November 1996. They have released 3 demos and a promo cd.

4) Soulless were a Portuguese death metal band from Leiria. They formed in 1995 and released one demo 1997 before their split-up.

5) Soulless were a gothic metal band from Switzerland that have now split-up.

6) Soulless are beatdown hardcore band from Melbourne.

7) Soulless is a one man dsbm/atmospheric/post black metal artist from West Sumatera, Indonesia
