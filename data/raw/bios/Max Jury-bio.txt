Max Jury is an American singer-songwriter from Des Moines, Iowa currently living in London, UK. 

A love of great American songwriting from the likes of Bob Dylan, Gram Parsons & Paul Simon, has helped Jury hone and craft his own skill. He has been managed by Marathon Artists since 2013.

His debut EP, Something In The Air, features his single Christian Eyes. 

Subsequently,  he released All I Want: The Sonic Factory Sessions.
