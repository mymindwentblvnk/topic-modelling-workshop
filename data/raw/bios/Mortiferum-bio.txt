A death/doom metal band from Olympia, Washington, USA formed in 2016. Released their first demo altar of decay in 2017, a KTDF promo in 2018 and their first album, Disgorged From Psychotic Depths in 2019 via Profound Lore Records. Their sophomore LP,  Preserved in Torment was released in 2021.
