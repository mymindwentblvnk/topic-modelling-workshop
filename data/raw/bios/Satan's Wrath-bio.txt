Greece (2011-present)

Satan's Wrath is a Greek black metal/thrash metal band formed in 2011, made of guitarist Stamos K and Tasos "Tas" Danazoglou, best known as one time bassist for doom metal band Electric Wizard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satan%27s+Wrath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
