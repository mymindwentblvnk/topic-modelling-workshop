Way Through are Chris and Claire phasing in and out with guitar, samples, damaged drums and vocals. They play prism punk; ragged intuitive songs shot through with technicolour noise. Using repetition, swift interplay and happenstance they have a sound which is both fun and challenging, a sound that can do anything and can go anywhere. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Way+Through">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
