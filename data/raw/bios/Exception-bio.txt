a] THE EXCEPTION was british rock group formed in Birmingham, in 1967. Members of the group are: Alan Eastwood (vocals), Roger Hill (guitar) and Dave Pegg (bass). They only have one album from 1969 - The Exceptional Exception. Robert Plant is rumored to play the tambourine. 

b] Exception was a symphonic rockband from The Netherlands in the seventies.
