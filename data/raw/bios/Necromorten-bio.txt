Brazilian Thrash Metal band from Fortaleza (CE), formed in the mid-nineties, influenced by 80's thrash metal like Slayer, Kreator, Destruction, Exodus, Anthrax etc. Has one promo released,  "After the Holocaust", and is recording the first full-length to be released in early 2009.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/NecroMorten">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
