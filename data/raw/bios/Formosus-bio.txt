FORMOSUS is a polish melodic death metal band which was formed in 2007 in Cracow, Poland by two guitarists of Goblin Market: Krzysztof Stanisz and Maciej Jagiello. They hooked up Marcin Turek on vocals, Piotr Kaczmarski on bass and Marcin Pazera on drums. The band finished its first material in July 2008. The EP have been uploaded in the internet for full preview.
