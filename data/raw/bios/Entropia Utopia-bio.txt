Entropia Utopia is a progressive rock band from Thessaloniki, Greece. Their music combines elements of jazz, progressive metal, progressive rock sounds of the 70's and avant garde music in general as well. So far they have released two demos: Entropia Utopia (2004) and Weirdom (2005).

The band members are:
Stamatis Pratsinakis - Vocals, Flute, Sax, Keyboards
Paris Papadopoulos - Guitars
Kostas Georgiadis - Guitars
Stelios Mikedis - Bass 
Kostas "Guss" Pasaxidhs - Drums
