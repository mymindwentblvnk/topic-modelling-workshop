Since the age of 17, he has been working as a DJ. In 1999, his single "Welcome to the Club" was a success, and he got on with "Living on a Better World." 


You can hear him at radio Sunshine Live, every Tuesday, from 10 to 12 pm.


In 1989, he met Enrico Zabler and formed a group called Masterboy which had success in the mid 90's in songs such as "Feel the heat of the night" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Klubbingman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
