There are several artists with the name BTK:

1.) BTK (Vinicius Honorio) a brazilian Drum & Bass DJ, producer and label owner of Dutty Audio.

2.) A beatdown hardcore band from germany

3.) A five-piece Psychedelic Hip Hop band out of Toronto, Canada. Released their single album, "birth thru knowledge" in 1998. The band consisted of Lo-Ki (Vocals), Stone Groove (Vocals), DJ Spinz (Turntables), Adam Carlo (Bass) and Matt deMatteo (Drums). 

4.) BTK is Hungary's answer to the german NDW band, DAF. Probably it was the first band who played EBM/electropunk in Hungary in the early 80's.
