Hinatazaka46 (日向坂46) is a Japanese idol group and the fourth sister group of Nogizaka46 (乃木坂46). The group was formerly named Hiragana Keyakizaka46 ( けやき坂46), until they announced their independence on February 11, 2019. Hinatazaka refers to a place specifically in Minato, Tokyo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hinatazaka46">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
