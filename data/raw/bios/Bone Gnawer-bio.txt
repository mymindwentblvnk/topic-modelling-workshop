Bone Gnawer is an American and Swedish collaboration playing old school death metal.

The line up includes some well known figures in death metal namely Kam Lee (Massacre and ex-Denial Fiend), Rogga Johansson (Demiurg, Ribspreader, The 11th Hour and Paganizer to name a few), Ronnie Bjornstorm (Ribspreader, Hate Ammo) and Morgan Lie (Naglfar).

They released their debut album "Feast of Flesh" in 2009 via Pulverised Records.

After their success they released a split with Bonesaw (including ex-Ablach members) in December 2009 by Aphelion Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bone+Gnawer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
