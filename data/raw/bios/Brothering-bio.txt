Brothering was a funk metal band from Johannesburg, South Africa.  They released a demo collection of songs, simply titled Brothering in 1997. It was recorded at Mega Music Studios, Johannesburg, South Africa. Mixed by Leon 'Sonic Terrorist' Erasmus, produced by Brothering.

Deangersmith - Vocals & Guitars
