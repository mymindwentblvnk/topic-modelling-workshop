Highlord is a power metal band from Torino, Italy formed in 1996. The band currently consists of Andrea Marchisio (vocal & backing vocals), Stefano Droetto (guitar), Diego de Vita (bass), Alessandro Muscio (keyboard, piano & harpsichord), Luca Pellegrino (drums).
