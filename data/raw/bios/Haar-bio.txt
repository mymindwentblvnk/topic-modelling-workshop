There are multiple artists with the name Haar:

1) Haar are a progressive black metal band from Edinburgh.  Formed in 2008, they self-released two EPs in 2010 and 2012.  Their 2012 EP was then re-released by Todestrieb Records.  Their CDs can be purchased from  http://haar.bigcartel.com/

http://www.facebook.com/haarsounds

2) Haar, not to be confused with others, are brothers Gary and Ian Smith, from Edinburgh, Scotland; their album, is published, released, available; East Coast Chill embraces the digital chillout genre and combines great guitar instrumentals with well crafted and balanced compositions. 

3) Haar were a black metal band from Australia.  They released in 1997 the Demo "The Flickering Light of a Dying Flame" in small numbers. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Haar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
