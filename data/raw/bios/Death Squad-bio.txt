There are more than one artists using the name Death Squad:

1) A Death Metal / Thrash Metal band from The Netherlands (started in 1991).

2) Death Squad was a power electronics/noise project by Michael Nine. Death Squad ended in 2000 then evolved into the current project mk9.

3) A hardcore band from Bulgaria. 

4) A Thrash / Speed Metal band from Olympia, Washington.

5) A now defunct Scandinavian hardcore infuenced band from Albany NY fronted by the one and only Mark Telfian of Hail Mary fame.

6) A Thrash Metal / Crossover band from St. Paul, Minnesota.
