SNØ("snow" in Norwegian) plays progressive, eclectic rock. Their music explores extensive soundscapes soaked up with melancholy and bursting with energy. Those deep spaces of ambient are merged with heavy rhythmic riffs and toped with a powerful soprano. The Norwegian sextet thus creates a captivating atmosphere that dynamically oscillates in intensity and depth, triggering a flow of emotions.

With lyrics in both norwegian and english they will never stop surprising, and they have great things ahead of them.


Karla  was in Oslo to study opera singing. Suddenly, she had enough of the conservative “rules” and moved back to Bergen to start a band. After a few years of creative testing, they found the six golden persons they long wished to gather. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Sn%C3%B8">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
