Intergracia are a 4-piece melodic heavy metal band from Wellington, New Zealand. They formed in early 2008 as a new experimental project by sole composer/song writer Tim Mekalick. The music is a mixture of progressive melodic metal with slight celtic influences and acoustic breakdowns. Intergracia have played a number of gigs across New Zealand and released a demo towards the end of 2008.
