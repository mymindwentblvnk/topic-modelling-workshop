Virulency is a brutal death metal band from Spain.  They formed in 2011.  The band consists of J on vocals, Asier on guitars,  DisJorge  on fretless bass & guitars and Edgar on drums.  They have released two demos, an EP and a full-length album.  Their lyrical themes include sickness, gore and pathologies.  They are signed to the record label New Standard Elite. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Virulency">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
