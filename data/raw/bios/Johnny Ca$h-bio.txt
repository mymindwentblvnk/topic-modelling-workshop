T.I.P (Thizz in Peace) Johnny Ca$h 8.11.1981 - 3.29.2007, The Fast Gunna aka CA $ was shot to death in the face in Vallejo, Ca which is the home city of Mac Dre & E-40. Johnny Ca$h was an upcoming Bay Area artist from Richmond, Ca and was under Mac Dres record label Thizz Ent.  He was in a rap group called MONEY GANG MOB aka MGM with Pittsburg, Ca artist Rydah J.
