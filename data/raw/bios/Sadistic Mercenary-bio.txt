Sadistic Mercenary is a blackened thrash metal band from Linares, Chile, formed in 2011.

Line-up:
Diego Yañez Hernandez: Vocals
Kako Caroca Araya: Bass and Backing Vocals
Pablo San Martín: Guitars
Emilio «Xaedo» Aedo: Drums
