The Kill Junkies are a 5 piece Stoner Metal /Hard Rock band from Ventura, California with a hard hitting sound firmly rooted in classic metal with modern sensibilities. Formed in 2005 TKJ have released 2 albums "Metal Against Your Will" 2006 and "Steamroller" 2010.

The Kill Junkies are:

Chris Savoy: Bass/Lead Vocals Jason Amelio: Guitar/vocals Zach French: Drums Chris Cromelin: Lead Guitar Sean Harris: Guitar
