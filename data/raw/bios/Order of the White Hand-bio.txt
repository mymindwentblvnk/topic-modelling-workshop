NS Pagan/Black Metal band from Finland.
Order Of The White Hand was founded in 2006 by Koomastroyer. O.W.H. stands for the Nordic heathen pride, anti-christianity and nationalism.

Current line-up:
Immakkel - Guitar, bass, vocals
Koomastroyer - Drums, vocals
