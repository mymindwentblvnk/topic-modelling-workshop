There are two bands named Ironheart, both from the UK.

1) Stylized IronHeart, are a power/heavy metal band from Belfast. They released their debut album "Revolution Calls" in 2015.

2) Stylized Ironheart, are a NWOBHM band from the mid 80's, that released one single called "Running Away," in 1984. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ironheart">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
