Hell Trucker is a Brazilian thrash / death metal band. They hail from Belo Horizonte (Minas Gerais) and some of their main influences are Pantera, The Black Dahlia Murder and Exodus. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hell+Trucker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
