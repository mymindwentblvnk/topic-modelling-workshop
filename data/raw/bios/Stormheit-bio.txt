Folk/Black Metal band from Finland, formed in 2005.

Current line-up:
Stormheit - Vocals, guitar, bass, keyboard (Animal Foreskin, Uncreation's Dawn, Wounds, Evil Witch, Empire of Tharaphita)
A.O. - Session drums (Animal Foreskin, Infiltrator (Fin), Wounds, Evil Witch)

Discography:
Calling The Spirits Of Hate - Demo, 2006
The Awakening Of The Conqueror - Full-length, 2006
Caelic Weold Finnum - Full-length, 2009
Kvenland - Full-length, 2009
Chronicon Finlandiae - Full-lenght, 2011
Syksy sieluni yössä - EP, 2011
Pohjanmaa - Full-length, 2014
