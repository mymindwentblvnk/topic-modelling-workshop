Cristian Gamboa, a performer under the name of Yum Kimil is a young, up and comer artist closely associated with Psychedelic Movement, a evolutionary music . He is an 22 yr old pioneer from Guadalajara Jalisco, who established his name back in 2009, the time when he was first introduced to the art. In 2010, a completely focused Cristian decided to pursue his true passion towards the music industry. He devoted countless hours working full nights at performing psychedelic Full on Night music.. With the support of Dementia Society Records Seal,It was not until 2014, when the name Yum Kimil was born, preceded by his continued hard-work and dedication to the world of hi-tech psychedelic music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yum+Kimil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
