Digby Jones is a musician specialising in jazz & funk / groovy music. His tracks have appeared on TV shows, trailers, commercials and albums for clients such as Dreamworks, CSI, Kia Cars, Banana Republic, Playstation, Cafe Del Mar. His day job is running Sublime Music in London.
