Traumatism is a brutal death metal/grindcore band from Canada, forming in 1994. They have released two albums: Truculent in 1997 and Grossly Unfair... in 2000. The band is currently on hold working on other projects. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Traumatism">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
