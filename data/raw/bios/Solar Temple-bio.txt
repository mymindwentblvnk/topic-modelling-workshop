Solar Temple are an atmospheric/psychedelic black metal band that was formed in the Netherlands in 2016 featuring members of Turia and Fluisteraars. They released their demo Rays of Brilliance on May 24, 2017 through Fallen Empire Records and Haeresis Noviomagi, and their full-length debut  Fertile Descent on September 28th, 2018 though Eisenwald.
