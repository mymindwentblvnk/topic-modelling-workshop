Cory Reid (b. March 30, 1994), better known by his stage name Nebu Kiniza, was born in Atlanta, GA. Widely known for his song “Gassed Up.” Nebu Kiniza started gaining fame around 2015-2016.

Another song that’s widely known is “Myself.” Kiniza also produces some of his own beats and buys or uses beats from Mexikodro.
