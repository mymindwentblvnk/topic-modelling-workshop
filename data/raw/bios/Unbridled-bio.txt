Unbridled are a Portuguese Deathcore band founded in October 2006 and to date have played around 50 gigs throughout Portugal and Spain.
Unbridled have released the EP "Corrupting the Core of My Soul" and the album "Deathcore Glamour" (2009, Hard As Hell Records). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unbridled">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
