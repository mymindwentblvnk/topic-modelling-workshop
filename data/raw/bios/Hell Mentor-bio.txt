Hell Mentor is a Belgian Black/Thrash/Punk band mainly based in West-Flanders.

The band was formed in 2009, after changing its name from Corpus to Hell Mentor.

Hell Mentor's first demo, titled 'Hang Over',  was released in 2010. It contains 4 tracks and is available from the band.

Line-up:

K. Moshrat - Vocals
Lorre - Guitar
Koen - Bass
