There is more than one artist with this name.

1) Sick Stoner Rock from Sweden.

2) A Serbian thrash metal band formed in 2004. They have released one full length album entitled "Piece of Hell" in 2006. Line up consists of:
Aleksandar Manja - Vocals
Ivan Srdanovic - Guitar
Milos Broceta - Bass
Branko Babic - Drums

3) Tommy L. was born and raised in Düsseldorf, Germany. As a teenager, his first steps in making music were to write texts whithout any knowledge about reading music. He just remebered the melodies by reading the words of the text. Then came years writing and singing in many bands, each with an other focus on style and vision. When he got his first PC in 1999 he started to work on his material only in an electronic manner. He was listening to electronic bands before that came from his hometown Düsseldorf, but till then he always intended to make electronic music. In 2001 he released his first Album "Potential dreams" under the pseudonym FLUSH.

4) A moniker used by The Shirehorses in 1996 for their cover of the Lush song "Single Girl", in this case titled "Single Bloke", and actually released as one of the b-sides to the original track by Lush. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flush">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
