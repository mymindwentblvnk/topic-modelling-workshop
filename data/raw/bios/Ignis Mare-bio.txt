Ignis Mare is a heavy metal band from Salo, Finland. Founded in 2009, the band plays a grooving blend of rock and metal that draws influences from it's members' extensive interest in various sorts of music. In Ignis Mare's music tight riffs meet catchy melodic hooks, spiced up with nuances on a scale from traditional hard rock to pop and from prog to speed metal.
