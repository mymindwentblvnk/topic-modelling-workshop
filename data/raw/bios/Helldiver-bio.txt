1) Helldiver is a stoner-doom metal band. Helldiver was born January 2007, consisting of members of Purified in Blood and The Rape of Lucrece; the musical path has already been set; hard and heavy crushing metal. Already a month after forming the band, they recorded a three track demo. Picking up where Purified in Blood left off, the music is just as hard and even more metal.
2) Helldiver is a classic hard rock/heavy metal band from Hungary. The band was born in 2008 but their first music video only got released in 2014 and their first album "A Túlélés Záloga" in 2019.
