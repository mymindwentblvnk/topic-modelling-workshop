DJ Hell is a pseudonym of Helmut Josef Geier (b. 6 Sept 1962 Born in Altenmarkt an der Alz, Bavaria, DE), who began DJing in Munich, Germany, in 1978.  He is one of the best reputed German 90's DJs.  His eclectic style has been developed from numerous genre : darkwave, goth, Detroit techno and electro - though, he is mostly known as a 'punk DJ'.

Geier runs the International Deejay Gigolos label and was resident at the Ultraschall club (Munich).  He has also collaborated with many others including:  Laurent Garnier, Billie Ray Martin and Mijk Van Dijk.
