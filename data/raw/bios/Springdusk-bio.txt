Springdusk is a one man Doom/Death Metal band hailing from Sweden. Formed in 1999, the band consists of Henke [Vocals, Guitar, Bass, Drums] and has released two demos, "A Journey Through Misery" in 2002 and "Pierced By Mournful Memories" in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Springdusk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
