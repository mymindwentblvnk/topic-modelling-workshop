Perunica was a Pagan Black Metal band from Płock, Poland formed in 2000. They released their work under Eastside Records label. The last-known lineup was: Ostry (guitar and vocals), Michał (bass) and Swaromir (drums). They released one demo, Święte Dziedzictwo Przodków (2001) and a full-length album, Triumf Odwiecznych Prawd (2007) which was recorded in 2003 but released in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Perunica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
