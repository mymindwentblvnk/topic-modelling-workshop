Dyessence is a Melodic Death Metal band from Finland. The band released a demo called Follow the Line in 2006. The members are Toni Kammonen (bass), Tommi Häyrynen (drums), Mikko Turunen (guitars) and Niko Häkkinen (vocals and guitars).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/dyessence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
