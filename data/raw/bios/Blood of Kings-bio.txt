Blood of Kings is a Seattle, Washington based Traditional/Speed/Thrash Metal band.  In '11 they released a self-titled demo before unleashing their debut, 'Starvation' through My Graveyard Productions in April 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blood+of+Kings">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
