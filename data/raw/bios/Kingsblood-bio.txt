Formed in the bowels of Columbus Ohio's metal scene, Kingsblood, is a metal band on the verge of decimating the world as we know it. With their epic onslaught of ear exploding riffs to their barrage of drumming perfection no one is safe! Whirling up imagery of epic battle scenes from days long since past you will more than likely want to hoist your axe up high and charge into the battle with them.
