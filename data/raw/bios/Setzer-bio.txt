There is more than one artist under this name:

1. setzer is a Japanese guitarist, best known for being a member and the co-founder (along with a2c) of MintJam.

2. Setzer (Dan Watton) is a Canadian IDM producer. With only one split EP with Kettel released, Setzer became quite famous for his high quality remixes for artists like Sense and Ilkae. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Setzer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
