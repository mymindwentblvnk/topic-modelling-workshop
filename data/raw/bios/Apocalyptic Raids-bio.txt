Apocalyptic Raids was a blackened thrash metal from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil in 1997. They later changed their name to Apokalyptic Raids in 2001. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Apocalyptic+Raids">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
