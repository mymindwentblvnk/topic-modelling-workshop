Ave Sathanas is an anti-religion black metal band from Norway found in 2002. The two brothers Frediablo and Fug are currently the only members of this band. They released one album, "Religion of Pity", in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ave+Sathanas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
