Ex Members Of Malak & Cave Moth. Nebz formed in late 2010 with many scattered ideas and concepts, over time and after acquiring a full line-up Nebz started to write their debut E.P. "Imaginary Images". Taking influence from vintage sci-fi films, games and novels; Contacting Nebulas have started to acquire a unique sound with an even more unique fanbase. Now a midst writing their full-length with an accompanying original science fiction short story, Nebz plans on changing people's opinions on genres and musical prejudice. Expanding our horizons and being open to new concepts is the only way to progress modern music. Nebz is influenced by countless musicians ranging from Metal - Jazz - Funk - Post Hardcore - Grind & More.
