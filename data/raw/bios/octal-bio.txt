Octal is the solo project from Jeff Reilly, bass player from Magnog. His first album, "Stairs Leading Up" (also known as simply "Octal"), was released in 1997 by Space Age Recordings, the record label of Spacemen 3's Sonic Boom.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/octal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
