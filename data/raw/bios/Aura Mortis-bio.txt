Aura Mortis is a black metal band from Velika Plana, Serbia. It consists of two members: Pakost (vocals) and Maledictor (all instruments, lyrics). The band has been formed in late 2013.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aura+Mortis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
