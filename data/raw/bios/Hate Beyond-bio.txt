Japanese deathrash band who have 6 albums released. Debut album Perpetual Pain 2003. Then no albums were released for 10 years, until Cage of the Sorrow 2013, then Bonded in Hell 2015, Verge of Death 2016, and Ruthless Aggression 2018. Newest album is Strangled Existence 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hate+Beyond">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
