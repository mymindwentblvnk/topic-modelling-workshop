Dresden is a thoughtful indie rock group from Warner Robins, Georgia. Signed to Famined Records, Dresden comprises of John Buice (Vocals), Joe Dempsey (Guitars) & Dustin McDearmid (Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dresden">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
