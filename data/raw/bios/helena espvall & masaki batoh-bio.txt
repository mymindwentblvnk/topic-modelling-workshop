Helena Espvall & Masaki Batoh was the pairing of Swedish-American musician Helena Espvall and Japanese musician 馬頭將器 (Masaki Batoh). They recorded two acoustic albums that range in style from folk to classical and medieval music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Helena+Espvall+&+Masaki+Batoh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
