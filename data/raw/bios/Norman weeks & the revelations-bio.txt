Norman Weeks has been playing the piano since he was six years old. His influences are Oscar Peterson and Joe Sample. Norman is gifted in all areas of music from classical to Gospel to Jazz and all the genres in between. Not only is Norman a musician but he is also a producer and composer.  

The Revelations consisted of Patricia Thompson (alto), Michael Blue(tenor) and Deborah Taylor(soprano) for the song "Hold On". The singers for "I Just Wanna Sing For You" were Norman Weeks(baritone), Patricia Thompson(alto), Ocatavia Willis(soprano) and Allen "AppleJack" Redd(drummer).

The Revelations disbanded in 1984. Norman is now producing another group called Norman Weeks and Groove Tyme.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Norman+weeks+&+the+revelations">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
