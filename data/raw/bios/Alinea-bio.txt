ALINEA (ah-lin-ee-ah) means “new train of thought” which describes how they feel about their music. Their original music and sound is considered alt rock with a touch of surf. They are currently working on completing their first album. Band members all reside in the Southern California.

They recently released their first single “She Doesn’t Get It” which they debut on National TV in the Rose Parade 2019.
