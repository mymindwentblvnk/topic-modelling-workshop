Parracide from Tuzla (Bosnia and Herzegovina) was one of the very first death metal bands in ex-Yugoslavia. Formed in 1988., they have recorded one demo - Buried alive (1989) and than changed name to Perryside (metalcore, one album, 1999., self-named). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parracide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
