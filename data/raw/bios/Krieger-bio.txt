1. The german band KRiEGER, based in Dresden (Germany) & providing industrial songs, coming with various influences (oldschool rock, country, punk..) such as Johnny Cash, Ramones, Queens of the Stonge Age,...
Their first gig took place in Dresden (Germany) in 2005 before being a part of the legendary Wacken Open Air Festival in 2006.
Their debut album is scheduled for release on August 24th, 2007.
Official website: www.krieger.eu
