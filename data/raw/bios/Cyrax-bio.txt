Cyrax is a rapper based out of Richmond, Virginia who is apart of the internet collective Divine Council. He released his Lone Bwoy EP in July of 2015.

Cyrax is an italian prog metal band formed in early 2013. Main influences are Symphony X, Dream Theater, Iron Maiden. The first record, "Reflections", is released in 2013 for Musea Records (http://www.musearecords.com/boutique/anglais/index.php?ficheprod=1&artiste=CYRAX&refp=39493)

With the same name there is also a German Trance, Techno & Acid Artist. He played in all great areas in NRW called Turbinenhalle, Soundgarden, Tor3, AcidInferno and many many more.
