'Demencial' is the debut album of the Chilean metal band KRUDO, the band was formed in 2001, after released two demos, The band is ready to take over the world with their technical thrash metal. 

The album was recorded, mixed and mastered by Mario Ruíz at Acid Records, San Antonio, V Región, Chile and the art design was done by Mauricio Javier González.

KRUDO is: 
Cristian Arriola: vocals 
Giordano Moglia: guitars 
Mario Ruíz: guitars 
Lucio Pérez: bass
