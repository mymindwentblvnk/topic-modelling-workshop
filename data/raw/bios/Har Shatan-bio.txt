Har Shatan, a one-man-project of Zepar is a German black metal band formed in 2002 in Neef, Rhineland-Palatinate. They have released two demo tapes, two splits (with Front Beast and Wolfsschrei) and a full-length. Har Shatan is currently signed under the label  Black Devastation Records.
