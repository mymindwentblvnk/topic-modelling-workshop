Gastrorrexis was formed in 2003, in San Gabriel, Ecuador, by Romel (vocals), Carlos (guitar), Alex (bass) and Marcelo (drummer). They released a demo in 2007 called Blood Splatter and a full-length album in October 26th, 2010 called Grotesque Human Disfigurement. It was released through Sevared Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gastrorrexis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
