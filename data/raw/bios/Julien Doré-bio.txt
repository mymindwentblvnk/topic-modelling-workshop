Julien Doré, born July 7th 1982, is the 5th winner of the French reality tv show "La nouvelle star" (the "French Idol"). Julien Doré sings, plays the guitar and the ukulele. He is a member of two bands. His work is influenced by many different musical genres, among which are rock/folk as well as various experimental artists. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Julien+Dor%C3%A9">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
