"Little" Nell Campbell (born Laura Campbell) (born May 24, 1953 in Sydney) is an Australian actress and singer. Her roles include The Rocky Horror Picture Show (as Columbia), Jubilee, Shock Treatment (as Nurse Ansalong), and Pink Floyd: The Wall (as a Groupie). She recorded a few singles, too, including Beauty Queen, Fever, and See You 'Round like a Record.

Campbell was a member of the Australian swimming team for the 1972 Summer Olympics; in the early 1980s, she owned a nightclub in New York called Nell's. She starred in Summer of Secrets (as Kym).
