Acephalous was a melodic Black/Death Metal band formed in the year of 2003,
(Belgium (Buizingen, Flemish Brabant))

Last known line-up
Kenneth Keysers - Guitars, Vocals, Drums (Steengruis, Serial Butcher)
Tom Van Steenwinkel - Guitars
Bart Govers - Bass (Time Out (Bel), A Trail Of Horror, Earection)

Former/past member(s)
Ketter - Drums (Morrigu (Bel), Kludde)

After the release of Divine Purity , Nico (Serial Butcher) joined Acephalous on drums but they never had the chance to do a live show with him behind the kit due to a split up <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Acephalous">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
