Marcus Schmickler belongs to the few artists constantly working for A-Musik. His most recent works in contemporary electronic- and/or 'mechanical'-music file under such names as Wabi Sabi, Sator Rotas, or his most recent work "Param".
His interest for different fields of musical understanding finds a way into projects like Pluramon or Corvette.
