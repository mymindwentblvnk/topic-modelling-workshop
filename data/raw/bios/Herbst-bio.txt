There are multiple artists named Herbst:

1) Herbst was a German black metal / neofolk band from Rheda, Germany, formed in 2005 by Markus Siegenhort. He released a demo in 2006 and a full-length album in 2007 titled "I: Prolog".

2) Herbst is a music composition major's bedroom melodic black metal project. Recorded two full-lengths, changed name to Vyhskt, branching away from black metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Herbst">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
