Arima Ederra (Soul Beautiful) given name Genet. Born in Atlanta, GA but raised in the sin city of Las Vegas, NV. Arima has been singing for awhile but never really considered herself a singer. From singing in the shower, fundraisers, talent shows, etc; Arima Ederra always considered herself a poet who sang.

With just a few years of writing her own music, songs she wrote are described to be a feeling. From her Jazzy, HipHop(y), and Rhythm & Blues sound, her electric felt songs have a powerful feeling.

Arima Ederra grew up in the 90s R&B and Hip-Hop era with legends such as Erykah Badu, Lauryn Hill, Jill Scott, Mary J +more. All of these artist were very influential and inspiring not only in Arima talent but in R&B, and Hip-Hop in general. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arima+Ederra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
