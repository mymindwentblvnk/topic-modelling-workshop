Hindvir is a Pagan Black Metal band from France, formed in 2003. They have released one demo "Vigrid Vollen" in 2005 and one full-length album "Roth Cingetos Taxonaria" in 2008.

Current line-up:
Efnys Yersina - Vocals/Guitars
Walanir - Guitars
Arkasian - Bass
