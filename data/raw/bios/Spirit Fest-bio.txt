Spirit Fest are a supergroup built around Saya and Ueno, aka the Japanese duo Tenniscoats, Markus Acher best known as singer of The Notwist, Matt Fowler, who plays with Jam Money and Bons, and Cico Beck, who's making music with Joasihno. The overarching mood of Spirit Fest is one of lightness and joy, of experiment and pure pop pleasure.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spirit+Fest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
