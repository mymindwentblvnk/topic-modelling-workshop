Age Of Fury are a Melodic Death Metal band from Sweden who formed in 2004. They released their first demo in 2005 along with the 'Betrayed' single. A second demo followed in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Age+Of+Fury">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
