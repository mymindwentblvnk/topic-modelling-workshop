Funeral Age is a Blackened Thrash Metal band formed in 2003 from Seattle, WA.

Current Line Up:
Kevin Bedra - Vocals/Guitar
Henry Bale - Guitar
Victor Hamilton - Bass
John Devos - Drums

Funeral Age have released two albums; their 2004 debut,  "Fistful of Christ" and their newest release, "Thy Martyrdom Come", both of which have been self released. To help promote these albums Funeral Age has opened for many bands, including Exodus, Kreator, The Accused, Cannibal Corpse, Impiety, Gravehill, Decapitated, Decrepit Birth, Fleshgod Apocalypse, Marduk, 1349, Weapon, Withered, Destroyer 666, Enthroned, Vader, Possessed, Korpiklaani, Moonsorrow, and many more! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Funeral+Age">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
