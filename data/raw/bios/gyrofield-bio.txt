gyrofield is the alias of Kiana Li, an electronic musician born from Hong Kong and currently based in Bristol. She's mainly known for their unique take and sound design toward genres like neurofunk and drum & bass, as well as dabbling in other genres alike. She also has been receiving supports from acts like RL Grime, Noisia, Fracture, and more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/gyrofield">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
