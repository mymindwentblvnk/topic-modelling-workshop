A collaboration between Portland's Liz Harris (Grouper) and Australia's Lawrence English. The project has been self-described as being born from "a mutual fascination with horrific depictions of the human present and future". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slow+Walkers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
