Wagars is a Latvian crust punk band formed in 2012 in Rīga, Latvia. By admiring the wilderness and untamed, mysterious power of nature, they chime post-apocalyptic moods - in a simple, fast, and sharp manner. Like a hail spurt driven by the Northern winds.

Members:

Jānis Zvērs (Trauma Moralis) - guitars, vocals;
Tomass Beķeris (Anal Punishment, Nekad) - bass;
Filips Batarags - drums.

Former members:

Māris Kaurāts (Trauma Moralis) - vocals;
Edgars Felkers - drums.
