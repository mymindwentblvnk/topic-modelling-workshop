There are six bands currently using this name:

1) Carcharoth (Germany) a two-piece black metal band whose albums are centered around Tolkien-themes on the first album and later around space. Their last EP and album is available here:
http://blogs.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendId=368647459&blogId=465192480

2) Carcharoth (Australia) a one man progressive melodic death/black metal band from Melbourne, Australia. They've released a demo entitled The Raven's Cry that is available for download at this location: http://www.megaupload.com/?d=CLW2DU4Y
The band also released an album entitled From the Cavern Come in 2008.

3) Carcharoth (Hungary) A folk/pagan black metal band.

4) Carcharoth (Spain) a pagan black metal band.

5) Carcharoth (US) a Sylvan Black Metal band from rural California, with themes combining Tolkien's Middle Earth with Paganism, Satanism and nature supremacy. https://carcharoth.bandcamp.com
