There are several artists with this name.

1) Behind Bars was a punk band from Oslo, Norway that consisted of members from Bøyen Beng, Lost Luggage, Svart Framtid, Stengte Dører and Manna. The DIY label Siri Rekkårdz released an LP with the band in only 100 copies. All on white vinyl with white labels and no cover.
