Formerly known as Chad  now The Chad Hollister Band (formerly just Chad) is an alternative rock band from Burlington, Vermont. Band members include Jeremy Goldsmith--lead guitar/vocals, Aaron Flinn--Bass, Paul Wolstencroft--Piano/organ/vocals, Steve Benson--mandolin/lap steel guitars, Drew McKeon--Drums, and Chad Hollister. The band has opened for popular musicians such as Bob Dylan, Phish, Blues Traveler and the Violent Femmes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chad">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
