Iron Harvest is a death metal band from Utrecht, the Netherlands, featuring members and former members of Weltschmerz, Korpse, White Oak, Monuments and Once Begotten. 

In 2012 the band was formed by Michiel van der Werff (guitar), Matthias Ruijgrok (bass), David Schermann (drums) and Andy Wit (vocals). In 2013 they were joined by Ruben Schmidt (guitar). Musically the band can be described as extreme death metal drawing inspiration from bands such as Dying Fetus, Hate Eternal, Decapitated and Cryptopsy.
