Apocalypse Command is a bestial black metal / war metal group formed in Tampa, USA in 2006.  The group consists of Gene Palubicki of Angelcorpse on guitar, bass, and vocals, as well as Steve Hughes on drums, a former member of Sadistik Exekution.

Apocalypse Command is on Nuclear Abominations Records.
