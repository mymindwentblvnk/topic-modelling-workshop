Noein is a Norman cyber-metal band, created in 2007 in the reunion of already experienced local musicians. The band starts playing live very quickly, and after having shot a music video early 2009, started work on a 5-tracks-EP called The Initial Tale which was released in April 2010.  Their first full-length album. Infection - Erasure - Replacement was released spring '13. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Noein">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
