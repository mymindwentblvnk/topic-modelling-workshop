Metal, experimental, hardcore band from Baltimore.

"People always said we were ahead of our time. well what time is it now? kind of began in 1993 and kind of ended in 2001, with lots of cool stuff accomplished and dismantled in between.(greg) we're back because the dead will rise again. ---although we never made a real commitment to being dead---(paul) --- Like a phoenix rising from the ashes... (lou)---compression...the free masons of the metal world...(albi)..."
