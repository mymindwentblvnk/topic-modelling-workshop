Divine Pustulence started in Tulsa in 2004 inspired by brutal bands, books and films they love.

Citizen X demo was recorded in February 2006

Human Monsters (REVERB) was released in January 2007, with liner notes by true crime author Corey Mitchell, infamous cover art of Gary Ridgway the Green River Killer by Nico Claux and insert art by Richard Ramirez.

Surgical Defilement 2007 promo / split with Gore Blast was recorded on Friday April 13th 2007 and mixed April 20th.

New full length CD is nearly done being recorded and waiting for mastering and is being planned for an early 2008 release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Divine+Pustulence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
