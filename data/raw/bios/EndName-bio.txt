EndName is a sludge band from Moscow, Russian Federation, formed in 2007.

Since its inception, the band operates with such genres as post-metal, drone and doom metal. At the heart of creativity process lie devastating guitar riffs, rough song structures, pure feelings and emotions. Instrumental canvas is filled with agression and lyrics at the same time.

Members:

Sergey Kaplin - guitars;
Sergey Podgorny - bass guitar, patterns;
Dmitry Musatov - drums.
