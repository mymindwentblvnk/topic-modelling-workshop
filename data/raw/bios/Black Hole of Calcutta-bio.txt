Black Hole of Calcutta is a grind/punk band from Chico, CA.  They've released several split 7" records with bands all over the world including Gatt, Wojczech, Lycanthrophy, Human Error and a split 10" with Bloody Phoenix.  Their website also lists several more upcoming split 7" records.

Black Hole of Calcutta consists of Ian Makau (guitar), Jesse Shreibman (drums), Ben Terhune (bass) and Spider (vocals).  Jesse and Ian are also members of the metal band The Makai.
