Killteam, formed in 2006, is a five-piece band from Groningen (Netherlands) with their roots in 90's progressive/alternative, metal/rock-bands like Prong, Helmet, Alice in chains, Crowbar etc... After about six months of rehearsing they recorded the 2007 EP, to introduce themselves to mankind.
Currently they are working on their first full length album, to be released in september 2008.
