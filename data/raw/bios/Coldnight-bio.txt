Coldnight is a Black metal solo-project from Colombia, lead by the woman known as Hypotermia and was created during the summor of 2008.

The band started of as a Depressive Black metal project and Hypotermia recoreded the first full-lenght, "My Dying Soul", in 2008 but it didn't see the light of day until 2010. During this time, Hypotermia also slightly changed the sound of the project to a more ambient-sounding approach, describing her music as "Ambient Depressive Metal".
