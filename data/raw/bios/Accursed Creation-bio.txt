A death metal band from Ontario Canada, Accursed Creation officially started in the early spring of 2009, and since then have been writing and performing like wildfire. 

Influences include Cannibal Corpse, Autopsy, Decapitated, Bolt Thrower, Behemoth, Children of Bodom and Lamb of God.
