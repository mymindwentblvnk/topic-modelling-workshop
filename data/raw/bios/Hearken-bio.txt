A Christian Death Metal band  that started in 1996. Stated aim is to preach the word of the Lord through their music. Also notable for contributing the song O Come All Ye Faithful to the compilation A Brutal Christmas: The Season In Chaos. Currently on hiatus from playing live after releasing their first full-length Nuclear Negligence. 

Releases:

Arise (Demo, 2001)
It is Written (Demo, 2001)
Nuclear Negligence (Full-length, 2003)
