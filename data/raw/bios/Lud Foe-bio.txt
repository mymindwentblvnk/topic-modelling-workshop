Lud Foe is an up-and-coming rapper from Chicago, Illinois. He was co-signed by fellow Chicago rapper Lil Durk, who remixed his song “Cuttin Up”. Lud Foe gained more exposure when “Cuttin Up Remix” was featured on Episode 26 of OVO Sound Radio. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lud+Foe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
