Jack Latham is a UK electronic music producer and DJ who performs under the alias Jam City. He has been active since 2010 and records on the label Night Slugs. He has released two full-length albums: 2012's Classical Curves and 2015's Dream a Garden. He has also produced for artists such as Kelela, Troye Sivan, and Gaika. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jam+City">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
