Your Smith is a solo project of Minnesota-born, Los Angeles, California-based musician Caroline Smith.

Starting out with a folk album at the age of sixteen, Smith's music has continued to evolve through her career. From folk rock with Caroline Smith and The Good Night Sleeps' first album, through indie rock with their second; now working her way through an alt-pop/RnB period with her solo work.
