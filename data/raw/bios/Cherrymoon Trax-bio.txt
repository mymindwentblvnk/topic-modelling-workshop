Belgian Hard Trance project active in the mid 90's - the golden age of rave. One listen to tracks like 'The House of House', 'In My Electric House' or 'Conflictation' and you'll realize it's one of the most skillful music projects of the genre. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cherrymoon+Trax">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
