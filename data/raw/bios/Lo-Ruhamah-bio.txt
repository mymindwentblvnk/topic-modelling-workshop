Lo-Ruhamah is a death/black metal band with post-rock influences. In 2005 they released an EP MCD, and gained attention in the underground. In 2007 they released their first full length cd, The Glory of God. With this release Lo-Ruhamah gained even more attention. Lo-Ruhamah hopes to grow musically and lyrically and generate an even better response with future albums.

The Lo-Ruhamah line up is

Mathew-guitar and backing vocals
Jonathon-bass and lead vocals
Harry-drums and clean vocals
