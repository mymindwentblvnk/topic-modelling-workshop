Tiny Masters of Today are an American indie punk rock band, consisting of teen sibling duo Ivan, born February 21, 1994 and Ada, born March 4, 1996 both from Brooklyn, New York.

In the band both members play guitar however Ivan also plays bass and drums. Ada plays keyboards, bass and drums. The duo first began playing music in 2005 and released their debut single in July 2006 on UK indie Tiger Trap.

The band signed to Mute Records and released their debut album 'Bang Bang Boom Cake' in August 2007. Their brand new album 'Skeletons' was released June 2009.
