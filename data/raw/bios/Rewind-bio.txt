There is more than one artist with this name:

1. REWIND is a hardcore/death metal band from Ankara, Turkey.
2. REWIND is a Bay Area underground hip hop artist.


1. REWIND is a hardcore/death metal band from Ankara, Turkey. The band was established by Metin Atahan (Hazy Hill), Baris Tarimcioglu (Hazy Hill) and Yakup Senem (Crestfall) back in 2002. REWIND recorded 2 demos in 2005 and 2006. After some line-up changes the band recorded a 3 track demo in 2007, after Emek Ataman (Pera, emekataman) joined the band. The band is currently working on an album, and continues performing on local shows and festivals.

2. REWIND is a Bay Area underground hip hop artist.  He has amassed a following with millions of youtube plays.  His style is a rugged rapid pace of tounge twisting.  His lead single Baycat has 350,000 youtube plays as of this writing.  He currently lives in Castro Valley, California with his girlfriend and daughter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rewind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
