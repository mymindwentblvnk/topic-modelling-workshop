Heavy/Thrash metal band from Ankara, Turkey.

Formed in 1988. One of the very first bands, started thrash metal fire in Turkey in late 80s alongside with Pentagram. The band released a compilation in 2000. Since then, they are suffering a state of hiatus despite they are recognized as active.

Line-up:

Ufuk Önen - Vocals/Guitars
Zafer Altundağ - Guitars
Ekim Can Bayram - Bass
Mete Kutes - Drums
