Speedpig are a hard rock band from Bridgend, South Wales.   
Gaz & Mungo formed the band in 2009, and recorded 2 tracks (Kill You To Death & Rise Up And Die) with the intention of recording an EP.  

After 2 years in the making, the EP 'Hog Roast In Hell' will be released in April 2011 as a free download.   

As of 2011,  Speedpig have started gigging after recruiting 2 additional members Len (Bass) and Mungos brother, Tom (2nd Guitar).
