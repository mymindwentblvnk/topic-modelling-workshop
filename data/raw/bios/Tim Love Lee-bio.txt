Tim 'Love' Lee (born January 1970 in Cambridge, UK) is an English musician, record producer & DJ. He is co-owner and creative director of the Tummy Touch Music Group. He is most notable as a pioneer of electronic dance & downtempo trip-hop music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tim+Love+Lee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
