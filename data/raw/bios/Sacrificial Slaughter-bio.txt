SACRIFICIAL SLAUGHTER is an Extreme Death Thrash band that was formed in 2001 by vocalist Steve Worley. SACRIFICIAL SLAUGHTER has spent years of hard work building itself into a powerful outfit in the underground metal community across the world. SACRIFICIAL SLAUGHTER now consists of Necro Nick - Guitar/vocals, James Pavey - Guitar, Jim Harris VI - Drums, Aaron T. Whitesides Bass/vocals and Steve Worley - Vocals.
