PROVOCATOR - the Russian musical studio duet working in a genre of electronic music. PROVOCATOR's creativity  was influenced by such executors as Kraftwerk, Rockets, Prodigy and others.
Into structure PROVOCATOR enter Ren Suomy and Alex Corg, for the first time met in 1993.
Musicians it is a lot of hardworking in studio, took part in other projects.
It is considered to be the first serious PROVOCATOR work album MUSIC INFORMATION written down in 2004 and published In 9 years.
