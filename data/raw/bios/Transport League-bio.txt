Transport League was formed by B-Thong singer Tony Jelencovich.

The band stated at August 31, 2005 that they had broken up. Members continued as M.A.N.

The lineup for Superevil reunited for shows in 2009, only performing songs from the first two albums. The first reunion show took place on May 15th in Gothenburg <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Transport+League">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
