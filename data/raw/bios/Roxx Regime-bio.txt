Roxx Regime was the former band and name of todays Stryper founded in Orange County, CA in 1983.
The band changed their name to Stryper when bassist Tim Gaines joined the band.

There were no official releases under the name of Roxx Regime until Stryper released The Roxx Regime Demos in 2007 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Roxx+Regime">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
