A Stoner Rock / Doom Metal band from Turkey, Humbaba was formed in 2013 and made their debut in June, 2014 with their 2 song EP "Possessed by Visions/The Ritual". 

The band is currently looking for a label and has already started working on new songs for an upcoming album to be released on the last quarter of 2014.

Emre Can Serteli - Vocals
Ege Çaldemir - Guitars
Kıvılcım Çilingir - Bass
