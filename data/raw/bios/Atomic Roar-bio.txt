Atomic Roar is a band which was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil in 2003. The band consists of 4 headbangers:

Vinícius Hellpreacher - Vocal 
Victor Whipstriker - Bass
Victor Poisonhell - Guitar
Léo Witchcaptor - Drums

They have a two albums under their belt. The first was being "The Warfare Merchants" released in 2009 and the new album "Atomic Freaks" released in 2011.
