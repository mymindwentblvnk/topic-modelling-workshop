Gateway was a jazz trio consisting of John Abercrombie on guitars, Dave Holland on the double-bass, and Jack DeJohnette playing drums.

The Gateway trio released two influential modern jazz albums in 1975 and 1977, and reformed in 1994 for a recording session which yielded another two albums. All four albums having been released on the ECM label. The first eponymous album was recorded in 1975, Gateway II in 1977, and Gateway III and IV in 1994.
