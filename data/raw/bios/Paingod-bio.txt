Paingod was a American thrash-metal band from Florida. They split-up after releasing their first album in 1997. The band featured drummer J.C. Dwyer (later in Pro-Pain) and guitar player John Paul Soars (Malevolent Creation, Divine Empire).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paingod">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
