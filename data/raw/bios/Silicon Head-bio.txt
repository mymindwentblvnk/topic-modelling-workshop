Silicon Head is an experimental thrash metal band from The Netherlands which was formed in 1996. It was started as a side-project by musicians from Orphanage, Frozen Sun and Pleurisy. The line-up consisted of Luciano Sirigu on vocals, Axel Becker on guitars, Eric Hoogendoorn on bass and Erwin Polderman on drums.

In 1997 their first full length album Bash was released on the label DSFA. The album contains guest performances by Lex Vogelaar (Orphanage), Sharon den Adel (Within Temptation), George Oosthoek (Orphanage) and Marco Torres (Laberinto). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Silicon+Head">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
