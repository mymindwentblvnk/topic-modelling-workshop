Forest Stream is a symphonic black / doom / gothic metal band from Chernogolovka, Russia, formed in 1995. Their music is generally considered highly depressive and has alot of symphonic elements. Forest Stream's music combines elements of doom metal, death metal, black metal and gothic metal, and has been compared to Katatonia's "Dance of December Souls" album.

Discography:

Last Season Purity (demo, 2001)
