There are several bands called Urgent.

1)  US Urgent - was a short-lived  melodic rock band from New York City featuring former and future members of Sterling, Vendetta and Diving For Pearls. Founding member Donnie Kehr and guitarist Yul Vazquez have both gone on to highly successful stage, movie and television acting careers. They released two albums: 1984´s 'Cast the First Stone', including the huge hit Running Back and 1987´s 'Thinking Out Loud'.

2)  The Canadian band Urgent - they released only one album: 1984´s Timing, which is often wrongly connected with the US band Urgent.

3) The French band Urgent - Formed in France in the early 90's. They Released a mini CD in 2002 - Hold On. They have released two albums to date:  Out of Time was recorded in 2004 & released in 2005. The newest album Asylum was released on April 11, 2011. This is all according to the official website http://www.urgentmusic.com. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Urgent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
