daigoro789 is an online composer and musician who specializes in writing and performing music for the piano. He primarily performs music from video games and anime, but occasionally covers music from different genres. He has covered a lot of music, from Zelda to Studio Ghibli and Final Fantasy to Naruto.

His greatest inspiration is Masashi Hamauzu, who is famous for composing the soundtracks for the Final Fantasy franchise.
