Ingvar is a one man project, based in Athens/Greece. The music is created in the pc with help from an additional keyboard. His first album "And then I woke up..." and his first Ep "Whispers on an Endless Plain" are provided for free in his myspace account  /ingvarsounds.  He is currently writing new material for an upcoming EP named "A system waterfall" and a piano solo album released sometime in 2010.
