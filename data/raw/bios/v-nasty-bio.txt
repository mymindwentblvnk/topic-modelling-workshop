Vanessa Renee Reece (born November 11, 1990), better known by her stage name V-Nasty, is an American rapper from Oakland, California. She is best known for her association with Kreayshawn, her controversial use of the word nigga, and her collaboration album with Gucci Mane. Reece's father is Vietnamese, and her mother is white. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/V-Nasty">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
