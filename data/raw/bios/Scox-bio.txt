Scox Band Biography

Scox is a brutal death metal band from Iran. The band was formed at the year 2010 ,now stronger than ever on its way to achieve its goals.Not only with what the members are doing now but also with what they're going to do in the future they might be able to fulfill the expectations of their fans. The distortion of guitars, the kind of sound which is used in guitars and blast beats, twin pedals of drums all and all are demonstrators of the brutality of the music.  The meanings and feelings of the music and lyrics may be understood in various ways depending on the age group,beliefs and thoughts of the person who
listens to them. There are so many unusual ,secret and hidden meanings to every song. 
At the moment the band is Iran based as all members work hard altogether to become an international well-known band admired and liked by metalheads from all over the globe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
