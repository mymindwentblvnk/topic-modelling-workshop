One of many aliases of house music luminary Maurice Fulton, Boof is used to output jazzy off-kilter house material. Fulton first presented it to the public with "Life Is Water For Gerbadaisies When They Are Dancing" album back in 2000. "A Soft Kiss By A Rose" followed in 2006, while the latest, flower - themed of course, "Shhh... dandelions at play" is out 2011 on  Bubbletease communications. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BOOF">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
