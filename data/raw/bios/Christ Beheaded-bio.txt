CHRIST BEHEADED is a Satanic black/death band featuring Bob Fouts (drummer for THE GATES OF SLUMBER, guitar in CHRIST BEHEADED) and Dustin Boltjs (drummer for DEMIRICOUS).

The "Open The Gates Of Hell" CD/EP is available now. The effort was recorded at Basement Rage studios with Fouts as the engineer.

For fans of DISSECTION, WATAIN and CRAFT, CHRIST BEHEADED is on the old man's path to destroy all things god loving and holy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Christ+Beheaded">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
