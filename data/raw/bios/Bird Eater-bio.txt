Members of Gaza, Pilot This Plane Down and Day Of Less. Bird Eater was formed in 2004 after half of Day of Less (DOL) had moved on. Last drummer for DOL, Kel, and new guitarist Tino joined remaining original members of DOL, Chris and Jon, with new sounds and intentions. 1 member of Bird Eater is a former member of Gaza, while 2 others currently play in Gaza.

The Utah EP was released on Exigent Records and is currently the only released material that exists. New recordings are in the works and will be out shortly on a bigger label, though it is not yet clear who is picking them up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bird+Eater">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
