Lunatic Paradise is a lithuanian Death/Thrash Metal that formed in 1992 in Ukmergė.

Last known line-up
Smauglys (Valdas Tonkunas) - vocals
Remigijus (Remigijus Semeta) - drums
Saulius (Saulius Antanavicius) - guitar
Figaro (Darius Morkunas) - guitar/bass

Guests:
Laura - Violin
Agnė - Violin
