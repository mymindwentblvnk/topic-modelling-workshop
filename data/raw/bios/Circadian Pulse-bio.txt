Circadian Pulse is a progressive metal band from Melbourne, Australia; in the sense that Dream Theater, Fates Warning, Queensryche and Symphony X are progressive metal bands. Formed in early 2008, the band released their debut EP ‘In the Blink of an Eye’ in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Circadian+Pulse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
