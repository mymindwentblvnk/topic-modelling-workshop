Hellbox is a blackened thrash metal band that formed in Turku, Finland in 1998 and are currently signed to Deviance Records. They have released 8 demos and 2 full length albums so far. Most of the demo material can be downloaded for free in the media/MP3 section of their website at http://www.hellboxhell.com while the full length albums can be purchased from places such as Record Shop X.

Current lineup:
Hellvis Perversley - Vocals
Evil Hellman - Guitar and Vocals
Hell D.Satansson - Bass and Vocals
Len Hellkill - Drums

Former members:
Ramses Demon - Guitar
