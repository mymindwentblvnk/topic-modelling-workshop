90's grind/metalcore, featuring Chris Colohan of Cursed, Left For Dead, Ruination, and Fucked Up. 

Also a drum and bass act (by Dominic Glynn & Stephen Root) who wrote the soundtrack to video game Forsaken, and a band from Derby signed to Fight Me Records.

Also an Australian rock/psych group from the late 80s featuring the core of Ash Naylor and Matt Cotter (who would go on to form Even), and Francis Leach (who would become a well known Triple J personality, and later an ABC and SEN radio DJ). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Swarm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
