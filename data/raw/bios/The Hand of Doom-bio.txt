The Hand Of Doom was a heavy rock band from Bad Hersfeld (Germany) which released just one album in 1979.
"Poisonoise" has been re-released with bonus material as a 2CD set by Shadow Kingdom Records and as 2LP plus 7" by High Roller Records in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Hand+Of+Doom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
