Electronic Circus are musicians and composers Chris Payne, Paul Johnson Rogers and Michael J. Stewart. All three studied at Chichester College of Music. Payne and Rogers were part of the original members of Electronic Circus and released a single called 'Direct Lines' back in 1981. Now, thirty two years later they release single number two called 'Roundabout'. Direct Lines has recently enjoyed a bit of a 'cult status' and has been released in Portugal and Spain and soon on to be released on Medical Records in the USA.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/the+electronic+circus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
