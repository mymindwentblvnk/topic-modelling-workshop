Inner shrine is a symphonic / gothic metal band from Florence, Italy, founded in 1995 by Luca Liotti and Leonardo Moretti. They mix opera and classical music with metal.
They released a promo tape in 1996 and than three albums under Dragonheart Records: "Nocturnal Rhymes Entangled in Silence" (1997), "Fallen Beauty" (2000) and "Samaya" (2004).
The current line-up comprises Luca Liotti (guitars, synths, programming, vocals), Leonardo Moretti (bass, vocals, lyrics) and Cecilia Boninsegni (soprano).
