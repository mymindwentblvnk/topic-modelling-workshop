Envinya are a female fronted Melodic Heavy Metal band from Germany who formed in 2006. Their first demo 'A New Dawn' was released in 2007. The 'Beyond The Dark' EP was released in 2010. The debut album 'Inner Silence' was released in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Envinya">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
