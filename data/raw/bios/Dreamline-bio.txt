Dreamline (UK) are a talented duo producing fresh Euro Dance and UK Hardcore.

Dreamline is the brainchild of producers Tim Gosden (Reflex) and Simon Robinson (Simon Royale).
They brought Dreamline together in early 2008 and have been co-producing songs together to date.
Recently signing two songs to Aftershock Records the future is looking bright for these two young producers, aged at only 17 the talent that is shown from the pair is unreal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreamline">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
