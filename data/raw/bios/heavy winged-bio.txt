Experimental/psych/guitar-noise band Heavy Winged started in the fall of 2004 in Brooklyn, NY.  They have released albums on labels such as Type, Three Lobed, NotNotFun and many others.  Despite being spread across three states and two coasts, they continue to record and play live when time allows. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Heavy+Winged">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
