All Restored is a Christian metalcore-band from Finland. The music of the band varies from thrash-riffs of heavy guitars and growling to melodic clean vocals. All Restored, founded in 2006, has already played several gigs with good attitude. Songs are sung in English. 

The four-song EP of the band is named Illusions. For the following year 2008, All Restored is willing to have several gigs and to build their music to fullfill their inner desires.
