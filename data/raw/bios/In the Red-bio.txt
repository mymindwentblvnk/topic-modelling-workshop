Gainesville institution Mike Hale (formerly of Gunmoll) brings along best friend Matt on drums and Luke on bass to create In The Red, a grown up-take on the signature Gainesville sound. Still as powerful and driving as ever, the band's confident playing style and melodic vocal touches display the best aspects of a polished and mature band. 2009 brought their sophomore release "Volume 2", put out on Suburban Home Records.
