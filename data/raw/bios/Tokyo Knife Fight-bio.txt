Tokyo Knife Fight was a short lived experimentation into the inner workings of pop and computer generated electronic music. Written, recorded, and produced during the fall of 2004, Music for Hallways is the culmination of said experiment.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tokyo+Knife+Fight">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
