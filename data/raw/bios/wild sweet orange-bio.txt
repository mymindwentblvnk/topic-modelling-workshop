Wild Sweet Orange was an American indie rock band from Homewood, Alabama, that formed in 2004. In early 2008 their song "Land of No Return" featured on the soundtrack of the TV show "Grey's Anatomy". In 2010, the band announced on their Facebook page that they were calling it quits.

Members:
Preston Lovinggood: vocals, guitar 
Chip Kilpatrick: drums
Taylor Shaw: guitar
