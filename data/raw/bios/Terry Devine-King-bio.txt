Terry Devine-King is a leading composer in the television world. 

Terry was formerly an experienced performer and player with groups ranging from The Cutting Crew to The Style Council 

His credits includes commercials for Guinness, Rover, Citroen, Orange, Daily Telegraph and Barclays, as well as numerous TV documentaries.

He is a founding composer shareholder of Audio Network .
