Kriegersseel means 'Spirit of Warriors'

Kriegersseel is a black metal act from Berlin, Germany. 

They were founded in 2000 but are currently split-up. 

Last known line-up:
 
Alexander - Vocals
Jens - Guitar (Wahrer Krieg, Ewige Wiederkehr)
Christian - drums (Feuersturm, Ewige Wiederkehr)
