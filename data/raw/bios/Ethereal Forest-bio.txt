Ethereal Forest are a Black Metal/folk metal band from the haunted shores of England. Comprised of Azrael (Vocals, Guitars) and Theobald (Drums, Voices) who played together in a Black Metal Band called Heathen Deity. The first Demo, titled simply "Ethereal Forest" was recorded in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ethereal+Forest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
