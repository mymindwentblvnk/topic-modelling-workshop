US Black Metal band from Suffolk, New York. Solo project of Fars Morder.

Has released one four-track demo 'Hilsen Fra Vinland' that includes a cover of the song Master of the Universe originally by British stoner rock band Hawkwind. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elderhead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
