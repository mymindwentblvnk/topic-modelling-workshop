Guttrench are a Death Metal 5-piece based in Dublin. Formed in the wake of 2010, most of that year was spent writing songs and sculpting their brutal, guttural, groove, and slam laden blend of death metal and then gigging extensively all around the country. 2011 Seen the release of their debut EP titled "Capture Release Kill" as well as securing support slots with acts such as Desecration, Putrid Pile, Cerebral Bore and a mini tour with Cancerous Womb. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Guttrench">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
