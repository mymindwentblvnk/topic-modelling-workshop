Karlheinz Zöller was a German flutist, and principal in the Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra between 1969-1973 and 1975-1993. Zoeller was born 1928 in Höhr-Grenzhausen (Westerwald, Germany), he died on July 29, 2005 in Berlin.
