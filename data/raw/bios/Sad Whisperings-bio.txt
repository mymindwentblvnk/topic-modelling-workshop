Sad Whisperings was a death doom metal band from Groningen, Netherlands, started in 1990 as Desecrate (Hol) but changed their name to Sad Whisperings in 1992.
They released a self-titled demo in 1992, a full-length album titled "Sensitive to Autumn" in 1993 on Foundation 2000 Records, and three promo demos each in 1995, 1998 and 2003. The band existed on and off during the 1990s and restarted in 2002 with a female lead singer, but finally changed name to The Bleeding in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sad+Whisperings">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
