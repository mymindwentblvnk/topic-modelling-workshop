1)From Vancouver, Canada, No Kids is a trio comprised of 3/4s of the critically revered pop band P:ANO, No Kids are multi-instrumentalists Julia Chirka, Justin Kellam, and singer/songwriter Nick Krgovich. "Come Into My House" is the first release out on Tomlab in February 2008.

No Kids will be touring the west coast with Parenthetical Girls this spring.
