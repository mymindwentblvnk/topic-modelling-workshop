DVBBS is an electro/house duo from Orangeville, Canada. Alex & Chris write and produce bangers that are sure to keep the party going. Their unique style allowed them to quickly build a loyal fanbase, which in turn allowed them to release their EP "Initio" in March, 2012. You can check out their website at DVBBS.com. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DVBBS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
