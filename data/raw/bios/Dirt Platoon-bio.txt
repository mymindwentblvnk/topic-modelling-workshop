Dirt Platoon is a hip hop duo from Baltimore Maryland. The group began in the late 90's and continues to shock the world with every project released. Most would agree that the duo provides their fans with “Real hip hop” music. 
Dirt Platoon's recent project “War Face” released in 2013 and has received positive reviews. Dirt Platoon will be launching “War Face” tour this fall in Europe to promote the album. The album was released under Shinigamie Records and Don't Stop The Music licensed by Modulor. The executive producers for the project were Kyo Itachi and Randy Mc. Dirt Platoon collaborated with artist such as Ruste Juxx, Dro Pesci and others during the recording of the album.
