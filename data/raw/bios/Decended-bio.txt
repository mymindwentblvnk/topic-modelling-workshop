Decended is a five piece, thrash and symphony influenced power/melodic metal band from Joensuu, Finland. The band was created in the end of year 2005.

Petteri Soila (Guitars)
Sami Saravo (Guitars)
Tommi Ilonen (Keyboards)
Aki Jalkanen (Bass)
Olli Vainionpää (Drums)

ex-members:
Tony Turunen (Vocals)
