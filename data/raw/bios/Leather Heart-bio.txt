Leather Heart is a young and well established band of Hard Rock/Heavy from Madrid, Spain, which keeps attracting a great attention from the national and international media with their powerful live show and amazing performance of a classic and organic sound which although fresh, it seems to be recovered from an old vinyl.
​Their debut LP, Comeback, was released worldwide in September of 2015 in CD and vinyl format. Nowadays Comeback has sold more than 1500 copies around the world and it has been considered one of the best metal albums in Spain of 2015. Line up: Adrián González, Alejandro Gabasa, Jorge Piñero, Manuel Rueda, Sergy Alvarado. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leather+Heart">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
