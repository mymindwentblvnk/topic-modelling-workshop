London via Wyoming, Alo Lee released her debut EP "Twice Burned" on May 5th, 2015. Alo's R&B vocals coupled with dark production have garnered press and fan comparisons to Aaliyah with the sensibilities of Frank Ocean. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alo+Lee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
