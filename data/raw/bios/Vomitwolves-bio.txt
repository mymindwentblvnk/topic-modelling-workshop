Vomitwolves is a two person black metal band from the United States.  Instruments are handled by Jared Moran and the vocals are done by Jill Moran.  The band has two releases on Speed Ritual Records, a self-titled EP released in 2013 and a full length album entitled Chapel of Rot which was released in 2014.  An EP was released on October 1st, 2014 entitled Ceremonial Offerings.  This was the third release for the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vomitwolves">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
