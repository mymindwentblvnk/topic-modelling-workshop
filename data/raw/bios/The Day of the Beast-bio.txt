Formed out from the remains of various metal bands within Hampton Roads, VA, The Day of the Beast draws influence from a wide variety of extreme metal styles. Guitarists KC and Eric along with drummer Jeremy, vocalist Steve, and bassist Justin, joined up to forge a dark and aggressive sounding thrash metal band with mild death and black metal undertones.
