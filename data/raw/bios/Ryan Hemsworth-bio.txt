Ryan Hemsworth is a Canadian DJ and producer. Raised in Halifax, Nova Scotia, he attended University of King's College, where he studied journalism. His speciality is electronic music and sampling. He has produced tracks for Deniro Farrar and Shady Blaze, among others. He has also remixed works by Grimes, Frank Ocean, Tinashe and Cat Power. He released his first album 'Guilt Trips' in 2013 on Last Gang Records, which was followed a couple of weeks after by a free mixtape of remixes called '☺RYANPACKv.1☺'.
