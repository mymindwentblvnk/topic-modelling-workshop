Stowed away in the sprawling corner of Los Angeles, SPAZZKID aka Mark Redito quietly constructs his map of seemingly disparate beats and melodies into perfect harmony. With influences that span from punk to jpop to video game music and everything in between, Spazzkid's sonic fancies are evident in his tracks as they find the listener riding high on percussive waves of jazzy synthpop or feverishly gnawing on crafted candy electro pop. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spazzkid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
