Laura Brehm is an American singer and songwriter. She has collaborated with Monstercat artists such as Rogue, Feint, Varien, TwoThirds, Ephixa and Draper. 
She formed her own label, Electric Bird Records, in November 2016 to support indie artists in the electronic-folk genre.
Laura is also known outside of Monstercat for collaborating with producer TheFatRat, and doing a lot of live performance video on her YouTube channel. Aside from singing and producing she likes to read books about psychology and to spend her time with friends. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Laura+Brehm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
