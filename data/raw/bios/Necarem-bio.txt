Necarem formed as a three piece in the summer of 2004. Through it's existence, Necarem has performed with bands such as Misery Index, Thine Eyes Bleed, Dead Jesus, etc. Activity halted with the tragic passing of Adam White in early 2006. An  EP release using Adam's surviving recordings was released in 2011.
