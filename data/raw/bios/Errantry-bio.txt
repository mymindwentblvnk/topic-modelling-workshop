Errantry was a Latvian rock band from Rīga, Latvia, active between 2014 and 2016.

"Journeying in search of chivalrous adventure."

The original lineup:

Emīls Mālkalns  (Chomsky Chess Club) - guitars, vocals;
Laura Gribuste - guitars, vocals;
Katrīna Maslova - bass;
Ieva Zeltiņa - drums.
