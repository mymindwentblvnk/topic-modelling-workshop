1. Drab is a lo-fi, grungy, twangy, poppy, discordant two piece/ sometimes 3 or 4 piece based out of Boston and Northampton, MA.  The band features Tanya Pearson (vocals, guitar), Jason Layne (vocals, drums), Sophia Cacciola (bass).


2. Drab is Boston-based electronic artist Andrew G Nault making cinematic ambient related music.
