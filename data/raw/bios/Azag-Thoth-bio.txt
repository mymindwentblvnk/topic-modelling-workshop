Obscure black metal band from Zurich, Switzerland. Released one album "Reign Supreme" in 1996. Originally known as Pazuzu and released one demo "Conjurations" under this name. Not much information readily found, but the band is no longer active. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Azag-Thoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
