Deathnir is a 3 piece heavy metal band based in Hamilton, New Zealand. The main influences of their music include old school metal, thrash and power metal, such as: Black Sabbath, Iron Maiden, Megadeth, Metallica, Judas Priest, W.A.S.P, Dio, and Manowar. They've been active since July 2013.

Andrew Carter - Vocals and guitar
Adam Johnson - Bass guitar
