Belle Lawrence is a pseudonym used by Belle Erskine when she records  for Almighty Records.  She records dance tracks in Hi-NRG style, many of them covers.

She is featured on the first Handbag Series , titled Handbag Heaven: Boulevard Of Broken Dreams.
