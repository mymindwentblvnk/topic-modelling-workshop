Excommunication is a melodic death metal band from Sammamish, Washington, USA. 

Influences:
    Music: In Flames, Dimmu Borgir, Children of Bodom, Hypocrisy, and Lamb of God.
    Lyrics: Cannibal Corpse, Six Feet Under, The Project Hate, Immortal, and Deicide.

Their first [independant] album "The Legacy: 666" was released December 22, 2006.

Lineup:
Guitar/Leader-Bryan Lidin
Drummer-Michelle Nuno
