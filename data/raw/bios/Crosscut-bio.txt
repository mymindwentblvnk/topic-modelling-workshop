Crosscut is a German metal / hardcore band founded in 1992 by Frank Holtmann and Holger Czysch. They released their first Demo "Mindgroove" in 1997, the LPs  "God Given Time" (2001), "Nonesizefitsall" (2002) and "Director's Cut" (2004) followed. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crosscut">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
