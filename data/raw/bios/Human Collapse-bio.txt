There are multiple artists using this name.

1) Human Collapse is a melodic death doom metal project from Saint Petersburg, Russia, featuring vocalist from Vin de Mia Trix (Kyiv, Ukraine), formed in 2011. Debut album "Darkness to Fall" was released in April, 2014 on Bandcamp.
http://humancollapse.bandcamp.com

2) Human Collapse is a solo project, created by 18 year old, Jordan Jongema, putting forth depressing melodic tracks, melancholic ballads, and ambient/instrumental sadness.
Jordan J, decided to start Human Collapse back up as a solo project, covering guitars, drums, vocals, and piano. Through trying out many genres, he has settled down on the sole purpose of making instrumental (but at times with vocals), depressive-ridden songs full of diminished chords, and astray solos. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Human+Collapse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
