There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Lustre is a Swedish ambient black metal band, formed in the autumn of 2008 by Nachtzeit (ex-Hypothermia, Durthang, Mortem Parto Humano, Life Neglected, Nihilum). https://lustre.bandcamp.com/music

2) Lustre is a black metal band from Turkey. Their lyrical themes are anti-christianity and cannibalism. They released one album, titled "Vaticannibalistic".

3) Lustre was a power pop / rock band from North Carolina, USA, active from 1995 to the early 2000s.
Comprised of Will Marley, John Ray, and Greg Clayton. They released a self-titled album on A&M records in 1996. Will went on to form The Nickel Slots. Greg reformed with his brother in ANTiSEEN, John Moved to nashville to form Phenobarbiedoll. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lustre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
