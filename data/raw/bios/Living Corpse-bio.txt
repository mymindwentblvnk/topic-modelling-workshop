LIVING CORPSE is an Italian death/thrash/ hardcore metal set up on 2000 year. They record the first mini-ep The Redline on August 2005 and fix some gigs of their first official tour. The band also open the "Hellbound revenge tour 2005" in the only Italian show of the 25 TA LIFE,BRUTAL TRUTH, SADIST, CRIPPLE BASTARDS, ALL SHALL PERISH, BRUJERIA.

In June 2009 they start to record their last masterpiece “Metaphysical Collapse”, that will be in stores worldwide for February 2010 with Coroner Records.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Living+Corpse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
