Kromestar is an alias for a Dubstep producer from South London. He is also known as Ironsoul which is the name under which he releases grime. Under the name Kromestar his most proflific releases have been Kalawanji and Surgery on the Deep Medi Music record label.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kromestar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
