Landskap ~ London
Psychedelic Rock

London based heavy rock project mainly influenced by late 60's and early 70's psychedelic rock featuring George Pan (Father Sun, Dead Man's Band), Frederic Caure (SerpentCult, Thee Plague Of Gentlemen), Paul Westwood (Fen, Skaldic Curse), Kostas Panagiotou (Pantheist) and Jake Harding (Dead Existence, The Death Letter).
