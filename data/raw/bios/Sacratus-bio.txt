Sacratus, (formerly known as Dolor Al Reves) are a death/doom metal band from Cherkessk, Karachay-Cherkessia, Russia that formed in 2002. 
They are currently signed to Darknagar Records, and have two full lengths out. 

Current Line-up:
Armand ‘Blackeyes’ Quenteres - Drums, Back Vocals
Serge Lords - Guitar, Vocals
Maximilian Del Rio - Guitar
Roland Mephisto - Keyboards
Julietta ‘Fox’ Lorencetti - Bass
