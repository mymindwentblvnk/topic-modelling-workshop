Stangala is a stoner doom metal band that formed in Quimper, Brittany, France in 2006, combining traditional doom metal in the style of Black Sabbath with celtic influences and lyrics written in Breton. They released two demos, .. dans les bois and Lutun Salvia Delirium in 2008, and a full-length album, Boued Tousek Hag Traou Mat All, in 2011 under Solitude Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stangala">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
