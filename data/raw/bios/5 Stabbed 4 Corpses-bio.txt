5 Stabbed 4 Corpses is a brutal death metal/goregrind band from Augsburg, Germany. The band was founded in October 2009 by Slava, Alex, Rico and Zapf. After some Death Metal experiments, they decided to play grindcore. Nowadays they call their music "Bloody Samba Grind". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/5+Stabbed+4+Corpses">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
