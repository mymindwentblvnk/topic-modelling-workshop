StoneGazer is a stoner band hailing from Tampere, Finland. The band was formed in 2011 and its members have since then dedicated themselves to delivering grooving stoner rock in the vein of Orange Goblin, Kyuss and Down. StoneGazer has released two demos, "Vol I" in October 2012 and "Vol II" in March 2013.  
The band is currently writing new material as well as looking for publishers for their next release.

StoneGazer:
Juho - Guitar
Late - Guitar
Jussi - Vocals
Tomas - Bass
Jallu - Drums
