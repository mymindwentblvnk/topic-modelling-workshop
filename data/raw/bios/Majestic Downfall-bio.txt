Majestic Downfall is a one-man death/doom metal band from Mexico. The band started in August 2006 with the idea to recapture the feeling of the 90s European doom metal scene, while mixing it with some more modern doom metal overtones. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Majestic+Downfall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
