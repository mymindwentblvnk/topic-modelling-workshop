Deathrider is a speed metal band from St. Paul, Minnesota with influences ranging from Slayer to Metallica. They recorded 10 songs that made up the Requiem CD. The band did a limited distribution before breaking up in the early nineties and have not played since. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathrider">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
