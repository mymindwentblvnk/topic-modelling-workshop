History: Unsphered was formed from the remains of 'Quell the Skies' in 2008, including T.Nurminen, S.Rantanen and K.Virtanen A.Laakso joined the band as a vocalist bringing new kind of aggressiveness and vocal expression to the vile mixture of tight drumming, brutal riffing and twisted melodies.

Members: A.Laakso - Vocals T.Nurminen - Guitar K.Virtanen - Guitar S.Rantanen - Drums
