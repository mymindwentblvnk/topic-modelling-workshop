There are two artists that go by the name Maro:

1) Maro is an artistic name of Mariana Secca, a 27 year old multi-instrumentist and composer from Lisbon, Portugal. In 2018, she released her first solo album, in 3 volumes: Maro, Vol. 1, Maro, Vol. 2, and Maro, Vol. 3. All songs are organized chronologically and are sung in Portuguese and English. Maro started studying classic piano at the age of 4, later completing the Conservatory Diploma at the age of 17, along with her High School Diploma.At 11, Maro wrote her first song. At 12, she started playing the guitar as an autodidact, today a key instrument in her composing process. Later, her voice and the electric bass also became important in Maro's musical activity.
In 2022 Maro released her latest album Can You See Me?.

2) Marwan Daou (born August 2, 2000), better known as Maro, is a Ukrainian-Lebanese singer-songwriter and YouTuber from Beirut. He started his musical career posting cover videos on his YouTube channel in 2015, and gained a following in 2018. In 2019, Maro caught the attention of tech-music label SNAFU Records, which led to him signing a deal as an original pop artist. Maro's debut single "Carsick" was produced by Carl Falk and Albin Nedler, and released in August 2020, followed by "Take It Back", released in January 2021 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maro">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
