The Eschaton Creed are a three piece Death Metal Band formed in 2004 and based in Glasgow, Scotland.

The lineup is:
Frazer - Lead Guitar, Vocals and Drum Programming,
Alasdair - Lead Guitar,
Ian - Bass and Additional Vocals.

The self-titled EP is the only release by The Eschaton Creed.
