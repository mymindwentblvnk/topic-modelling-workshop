John "Julius" Knight worked with Pomeroy and Pomeroy's partner, Brian Tappert in a cover of the Stevie Wonder song "All I Do", released under the name Cleptomaniacs with vocals by Bryan Chambers, which reached the UK Top 40.  He has also worked with Tappert and Pomeroy under the name Eminence, in the track "Give It Up", with vocals by Kathy Brown. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cleptomaniacs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
