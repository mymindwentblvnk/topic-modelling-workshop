I Shall Devour is a 5 piece Australian Deathcore/Death metal band from Gold Coast, Australia. Formed in 2009, they were placed on their first tour supporting Rise For November on the Dead Fest tour through south east QLD, they quickly stepped up their game sharing the stage with such acts as Thy Art Is Murder, Make Them Suffer, Impending Doom, Sybreed, Signal The Firing Squad, Aversions Crown, State of East London and many more. They released their debut EP Manipunation in June 2013 through Skull & Bones Records and Shock Entertainment.

Jarrod Eagleton - Vocals
Jem Mcpherson - Guitar
David Wilder - Guitar
Matt Thomas  - Drums
