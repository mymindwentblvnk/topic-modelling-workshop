Budapest, Hungary (2007 – present)


Ego Project is a Hungarian heavy metal project formed by István Belobrek in 2007. They released three albums, and also have English versions of their songs on their "Ego II." and "Puszta ököllel" album.

Members are:
István Belobrek  - bass
Zsolt Belobrek  - drums
Zoltán Kiss - vocals
Norbert Jung  - guitar
Zoltán Lahovicz  - guitar

Releases:

Ego (2008)
Ego II (2010)
Puszta ököllel (2012)

For more information (in Hungarian), please visit their official website:
