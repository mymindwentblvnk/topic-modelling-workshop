Tim Fite (born Timothy Sullivan, formerly of Little T & One Track Mike) is an American musician, singer-songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist, living and working in Brooklyn. His past releases have run the gamut from indie to alternative country to hip hop. His label albums include Gone Ain't Gone (2005), Fair Ain't Fair (2008), and  Ain't Ain't Ain't (2012).

"Under the Table Tennis" (2010) and "Change of Heart" (2009) are available on Tim's website (http://www.timfite.com) for free, along with several other self-released works, and a collection of Tim's artwork. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tim+Fite">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
