Wald Geist Winter (translates roughly into "Wood Spirit Winter") is a Black Metal band from Germany (Thuringia). Current members are: Herr Eichman (guitars, bass, vocals) and Sucellus (drums, guitar, bass). In 2006 they've released a demo called ''Siegreich ist das Eis'' under Raging Bloodlust Records label. Their latest demo "Des Nebels langer Weg" was released in 2007 and is limited to 222 copies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wald+Geist+Winter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
