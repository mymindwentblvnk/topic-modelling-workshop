Alex Hepburn (born December 25, 1986) is a British singer and songwriter from London. 

In 2011 Alex Joined Grammy winner Bruno Mars on his European tour & brought her even more attention and new devotees.
She released her eponymous EP in June 2012.  
On April 2013, she released her debut studio album, called Together Alone.
