Serbian death metal band from Trstenik, formed in 1990 and disbanded in 1995. Released the full-length "Art Of Butchering" in 1994, which was later released in 2007 as part of the "Legions of Gore and Death" split with Disfigured. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necrophiliacs+Terror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
