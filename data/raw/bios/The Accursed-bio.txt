1) The Accursed is a UK '82 band from Kent, England. All of their releases of 3 LPs (1 of which is half a live album) and 1 EP were all released on Wreck 'Em Records between 1983 and 1984.

2) The Accursed is a melodic death meets blacked thrash band that formed in 2002 from the ashes of New Bedford, MA band Again Without Feeling. For the better decade they have been touring along the eastern coast and have played such noteable acts as the Milawakee metal fest and have been booked with bands as noteable as Candlemass, Dark Funeral, TestAmenT, and Finntroll.

In 2006 they released their debut album entitled Seasons of the Scythe, and are currently in the works to release a second full length.
