African Brothers Band (renamed to African Brothers International Band in 1973) was a Ghanaian band and concert party formed in 1963. It was led by Nana Kwame Ampadu. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+African+Brothers+International+Band">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
