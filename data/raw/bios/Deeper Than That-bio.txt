Hardcore metal band with english lyrics, from Brazil
They started out back in 99, playing metal influenced hardcore. Due to some lineup changes they hadn't had the chance to record anything up to now, september 2004. they have recorded our debut full lenght entitled "The Threat That Comes From Within". This effort contain 8 songs of metal hardcore influenced by bands like shai hulud, at the gates, in flames, iron maiden, killswitch engage, undying, caliban and many others... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DEEPER+THAN+THAT">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
