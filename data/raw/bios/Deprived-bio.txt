Deprived are a Portland punk band that existed from 1989 to 1992. They evolved into  In their 3 years together they released two E.P.s "Resistance" and "Reject The Illusion... CLASS WAR NOW!" as well as a split with Resist (A fellow PDX anarcho band)
In 1993 Deprived evolved into Defiance, the influential anarcho punk band on Punkcore Records.
