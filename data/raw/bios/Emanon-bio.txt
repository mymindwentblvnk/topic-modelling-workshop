There is more than one artist with this name: 


1.  Emanon is a hip-hop duo consisting of DJ Exile and MC Aloe Blacc from Los Angeles, United States formed in the 1990's.



2. Emanon was a Latvian hardcore punk band from Saldus, Latvia formed in 2004. They have released their debut full-length record called Behind the Walls of Melody on OSK Records in 2006.

Members:

Mareks Jēkabsons - guitars, vocals;
Anete Burve - bass;
Mikus Alksnis - drums.

Former members:

Mārcis - vocals;
Krists - vocals;
Klāvs Zariņš - guitars, vocals.

Discography:

Behind the Walls of Melody (2006)
Emanon (2008)
