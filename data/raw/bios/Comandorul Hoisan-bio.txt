Comandorul Hoisan is a power-industrial metal band, which was formed in 1998 in Bucharest and disolved in 2003. Line-up members: Bogdan Hladiuc (vocals), Liviu Hoisan (vocals, bass, programming), Antoniu "Tony" Muresanu (bass). Discography: -1998-LP-Onoare, suflet si credinta (MC-Promusic Production); -2000-LP-Extremism; -2003-Deratizatorul. The band's lyrical themes were: militarism, nationalism, Romanian Iron Guard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Comandorul+Hoisan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
