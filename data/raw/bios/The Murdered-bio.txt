The Murdered is a Hardcore//Metal band from Atlanta, Georgia.  Made up of Ryan Shea, Corey Hunter Bledsoe, Justin Smith, Matt Karaglou, and Barry Henthorn... The Murdered bring action packed and fast shows to the stage packed with sing-alongs and great dancing.  With their Debut album "...And the Maggots Shall Inherit the Earth" (Tribunal) coming out 6-6-06 The Murdered are bringing some fresh metal to the Atlanta and World-Wide hardcore scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Murdered">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
