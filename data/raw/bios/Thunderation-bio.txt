Thunderation are a Polish/Dutch band based in Cracow, Poland. The band came to life in spring 2015. In 2016, the band recorded and released their debut album "Where Are the People", including the lead track "Rift".
Band line-up:
Phil - Guitars/backing vocal
Dave - Bass
Martin - Guitars/backing vocal
Danimal - Drums/lead vocal
