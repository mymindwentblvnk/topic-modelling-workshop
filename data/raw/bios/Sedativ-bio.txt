There are multiple artists named Sedativ:

1) Sedativ, also known as Sedative, was a Russian post-punk band formed by Roman Sidorov (also mastermind of Старуха Мха, Der Golem and Fatal) and his wife Alya. The music of Sedative is characterized by mid-tempo guitars, and an atmospheric, melancholic, sparse and hypnotic sound. Roman himself described his music as "psychedelic suicidal post-punk".
A first demo CD-R, simply titled "Sedative", was released in 1999. It features Evgeny Voronovsky (Cisfinitum) on synth.
A full-length album  was written and recorded sometime between 2002 and 2003, and reportedly the only copy was discovered in the wake of Roman's suicide on September 23, 2003.

2) Sedativ is a Croatian thrash metal band founded in 2012 in Čakovec, Croatia.
