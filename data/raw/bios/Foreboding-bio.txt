Genre: Death Metal

Members:
Rogga Johansson - Guitar, Bass, Vocals 
Elektrokutioner - Drums, Vocals

This project is done via the internet with Rogga living in Sweden and Elektrokutioner living in the United States. In the middle of the recording of the demo, the band was offered a deal with Asphyxiate Records to release their debut album in late 2008 or early 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Foreboding">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
