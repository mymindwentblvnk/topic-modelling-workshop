Cathis Ord is a one-man-band from Great Britain.

(From Cathis Ord myspace):
Dark and melodic prog from the home of all things slow and heavy. Cathis Ord combines elements of Doom, depressive rock and acoustic passages centered around mythical landscapes and epic songs. The debut album 'The Far Shore' was released in January 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cathis+Ord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
