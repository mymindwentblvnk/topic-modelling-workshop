'Khazali supplies effervescent vocals under-pinned by a very real sense of emotion.' - Clash Magazine

Part of London's elusive music collective and independent label Slow Dance, Khazali is a vocalist who works with and against house, soul, hip-hop and R&B cliches to create fantasies based on reality. 

With early support from Clash Music Magazine, Khazali teamed up with East London band VUURWERK in late 2016 to provide vocals. With debut collaboration 'Because I Feel Things' and NME Magazine and acclaimed 'dizzying' video for the BBC Radio London endorsed single 'Face It ft.
