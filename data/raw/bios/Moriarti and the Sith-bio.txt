Moriarti and the Sith was a gothic metal band from Durham, England. Formed in 2002.

The band came to an abrupt end in 2006 when Dark Lord Moriarti took his own life. 
Only one album was recorded by the band.

Last known line-up:
Dark Lord Moriarti - Vocals (R.I.P. 2006, suicide)
Violent Christ - Guitar/Vocals
Paul T Geist - Guitar
La Mort - Bass
Enrique Doom - Keyboards
