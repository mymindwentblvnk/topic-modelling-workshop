Jaymes Young is a 27-year-old Los Angeles singer-songwriter born and raised in Seattle.
 
Released in May, 2013, Young’s Dark Star EP received immediate label interest and blog acclaim upon its release – being featured by the likes of Nylon Magazine, Earmilk, and Noisey, as well as topping the #1 spot numerous times on Hype Machine’s “Most Popular Tracks” list.

Having just finished a North America & UK tour this fall with British indie pop trio London Grammar, Young sets to work on his much anticipated debut album, set to be released in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jaymes+Young">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
