Verhern is a German black metal band, formed in 2004 by L.S., T.Nif. and Eternal. In 2008, the band released their self-titled album through Eternity Records and Fimbul Productions on both CD and LP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Verhern">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
