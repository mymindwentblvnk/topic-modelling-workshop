The Dweller, aka Robin Björkman, has participated in a number of constellations throughout the years. His first music project was the indie-pop duo nincompoop, formed in 1994, which still leads an active life. 1996 to 1998 The Dweller was one of the members of the pop orchestra Dinsdale. Starting of as a lead guitarist and singer he later also played bass. Between 1999 and 2002 he was a part of the quartet lloyd. Besides his solo career as The Dweller which started in 2003, he still is working with constellations as Rodel, Stockholmsförsöket and nincompoop. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Dweller">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
