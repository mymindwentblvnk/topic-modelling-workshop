Kingsbane was a Progressive Metal band from Canada. After the release of their demo tape the band changed their name to Seven Years and later to In The Name. Their demo was released in 1991 and re-released in 2010.

Fabien Madeleine - Vocals 
Mark Stewartson - Guitars 
Matt Draper - Guitars 
Tom Rokicki - Bass
Danny Russel - Drums
