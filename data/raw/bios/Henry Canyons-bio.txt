LA based rapper, Henry Canyons, transcends your typical boundaries of hip-hop identity. The self-described “Brooklinite-French-Jew” took to music at a young age; from his training as a jazz saxophonist to his teenage years spent in Brooklyn cipher circles, his melodic style and bilingual lyrical integrity reflect his diverse musical background.
