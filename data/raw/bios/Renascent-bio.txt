Renascent is a Finnish symphonic melodic death metal band formed in 2003.

Current Lineup:

Eero Tertsunen - Vocals/Guitar
Voitto Rintala - Bass
Mikaela Akrenius - Keyboard
Markus Saarela - Drums
Pekka Taina - Live/Session Guitar

Previous Members:
