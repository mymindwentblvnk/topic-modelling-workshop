Nonamen was formed in 2010in Krakow - Poland. After some changes and reshuffles within the band the present members are:
Maciej Dunin-Borkowski - composer/guitars
Grzegorz Kozikowski - drums
Edyta Szkołut - vocals
Agnieszka Reiner - violin
Grzegorz Janiga - bass
The musicians wander through different corners of music genres, from progressive rock/metal to classics. The unique sound of Nonamen’s music is strenghtnened by a use of violin and expressive female vocals.
