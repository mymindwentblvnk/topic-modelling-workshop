Tom Purcell, or Wave Racer, is an Australian electronic music producer from Sydney who makes mellow and purposefully cheesy sounding electronic music that can be loosely described as future bass or tropical bass. He claims that he likes "vocoders and arpeggios". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wave+Racer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
