Music was Szymon Borzestowski's life. He loved it, lived it, breathed it. A bright, brilliant talent, the young Newcastle artist would lose himself in his bedroom studio for days, honing his humble yet hypnotic artpop.
Sadly, Szymon lost his life at just 23. Lovingly curated by family and friends, 'Tigersapp' is the record he would have released, a glimpse into the vibrant world he hoped to share. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Szymon+Wydra+&+Carpe+Diem">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
