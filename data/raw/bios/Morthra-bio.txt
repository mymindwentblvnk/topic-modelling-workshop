Morthra was a Death Metal band from St. Anthonis/North Brabant, Netherlands. Morthra played no-frills, ancient Death Metal, at times like the rosy-cheeked kids from Sweden, and at times like the rosy-necked hicks from Florida. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morthra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
