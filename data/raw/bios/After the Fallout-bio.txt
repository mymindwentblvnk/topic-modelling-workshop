After The Fallout was formed in Seattle in early 2010 by Allen Mattila and Jason Northover (formerly of Subjekt 2 Change) and Shannon Higgason, Robert Hansen, and Daren Weaser (formerly of Moral Sacrifice). Their first album, Chaos Unleashed, was released in September 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/After+The+Fallout">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
