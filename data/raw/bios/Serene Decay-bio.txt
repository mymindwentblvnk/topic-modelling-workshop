Serene Decay is a recently started Finnish metal band from Helsinki. A two-song self-release Promo has been made, and material for the first full-length album is under work. The music will have influences of gothic and several other metal and rock genres.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Serene+Decay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
