Pahuldip Singh Sandhu (born 5 February 1987)[citation needed], better known by his stage name Steel Banglez is a English producer and solo artist who is signed to Warner Bros. Records. Best known for his production work with artists including Mist, MoStack, J Hus and Wiley, Steel Banglez achieved his first chart hit as a lead artist when his track "Bad" reached the top 30 on the UK Singles Chart in February 2018.

Born Pahuldip Singh Sandhu in Forest Gate, Steel Banglez grew up in Newham, east London. Raised in a Sikh family, he was surrounded by traditional Indian instruments such as harmoniums and tablas as his mother was a music teacher. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Steel+Banglez">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
