"My Useless Life" is a depressive black metal project of two musicians, F. and S., from Sacramento USA. The idea of the project belongs to F., he created it in September 2011, later S. joined the band (November 2011) as acoustic guitar player and vocalist... The demo was recorded in December 2011 and published for free. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/My+Useless+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
