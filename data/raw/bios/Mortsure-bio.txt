Mortsure is a Franco-Bolivian band that comes searching a fusion between rock and symphonic music and the project starts officially in 2011. The main influences range between Within Temptation, Apocalyptica, Lacuna Coil, Epica, Nightwish and other bands like Sepultura, Metallica, Jimi Hendrix, The Mars Volta.
