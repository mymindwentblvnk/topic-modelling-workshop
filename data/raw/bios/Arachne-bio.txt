Arachne is an old school death metal act that formerly originates from the Czech Republic. It was formed during year 2000 and self-released two full length albums named respectively "Severe Life Reality" and "Hate and Pain" in year 2004 and 2009. The band still appears to be active nowadays. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arachne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
