Irencros is an Italian power progressive symphonic metal band formed in 1998 by Gianfranco Attardo (guitar), Johnny Bozzari (bass) and Davide Lima (drum).
The Line-up changed various times through the years and only on January 2000 the definitive one was born with Francesco Ilona (keyboards), Simone Campione (guitar) and Giulio Di Gregorio (vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Irencros">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
