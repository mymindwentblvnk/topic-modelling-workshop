Twilight Moon is a black metal band from Pietarsaari, formed by Mathias Lillmåns and Joonas Lönnbäck. 

Current line-up:

Mathias Lillmåns - Vocals, earlier also bass (1998-) (Carnaticum, Chthonian, Degenerate, Finntroll)
Jonas Frilund - Guitar (2003-) (Chthonian)
Mikael Mannström - Guitar (2000-) (Carnaticum)
Mats Backström - Bass (2005-)
Kenneth Nygård - Drums (1998-) (Tinnitus)
Karin Thörnfeldt - Synth (1998-2001, 2005-)

Former members:

Joonas Lönnbäck - Guitar (1998-2003)
Caroline 'Syssi' Ahlvik - Synth (2002-04)

Discography:

Demo (1998)
Aether (2000)
Shattered Reflections (2002)
Delusional (2003)
