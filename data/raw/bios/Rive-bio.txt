Rive is essentially Bryce Bresnan, an electronic artist from Lakeland, Fl. 

Classically trained, Bresnan began studying the piano at the age of four. He now resides in Satasota Florida where his music has been focusing on utilizing glitch. 

More information about Rive can be found on the official Myspace profile.
