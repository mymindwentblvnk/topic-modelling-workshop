Scyron is a german death metal band which was formed in fall 2007 by Matthias Buckel (Vocals), Johannes Mottl (Guitar), Edgar Prigoana-Mueller (Guitar), Maximilian Reichart (Bass) und Alexander Knoll (Drums).  

Their first 4-track CD was released in fall 2008.
