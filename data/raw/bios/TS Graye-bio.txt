With her Dua Lipa-esque vocal and mature, fully-formed pop sound, it's hard to believe TS Graye is just a teenager from the West Country.

Debut single "MY2" is an atmospheric introduction to the brand new star, featuring skittering beats against an organ-like synth backdrop. Elevated by production from Louis Mattrs, her vocal is a strong asset to the slick, sophisticated sound she's already cultivating, underpinned by trip-hop influences from nearby Bristol. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TS+Graye">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
