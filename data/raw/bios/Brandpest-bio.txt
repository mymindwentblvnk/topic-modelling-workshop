BRANDPEST, forged in Vejle during 1996, are fronted by Vrykolatios, also singer for MALA FIDE. The band is in fact comprised of all the members of APOLLYON, vocalist Vrykolatius, guitarist Diabolos (a.k.a. M. Bisgaard) and drummer Sorgh.
 
Diabolos also has ties with GOAT WORSHIP whilst Sorgh as association with DENIAL OF GOD. Vrykolatius has links to AD NOCTUM in a session capacity. 

BRANDPEST debuted with a June 1996 demo 'Helhesterad'. The November 1998 'I Fanderns Vold Og Magt' single was limited to 333 copies of which 111 were transparent vinyl.
