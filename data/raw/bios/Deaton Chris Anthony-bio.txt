Deaton Chris Anthony is a R&B singer and producer from Olathe, Kansas 
he is best known for his song RACECAR with Clairo & Coco & Clair Clair. 
He independently released his Debut album BO Y on the 20th of November 2019 and released his debut EP BOOGY WOOGY the following year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deaton+Chris+Anthony">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
