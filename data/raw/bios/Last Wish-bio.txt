Last Wish was a rock band from Houston, Texas, most notable for being the first band of Blue October frontman Justin Furstenfeld. The band had a strong fanbase in Houston, where they played several sold out shows. Furstenfeld left the group in 1995 while they were working on their third album. Furstenfeld still plays Last Wish songs during his solo shows, and Blue October has played Last Wish songs on occasion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Last+Wish">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
