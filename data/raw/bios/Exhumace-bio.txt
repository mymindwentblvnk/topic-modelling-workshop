Southbohemian band formed in 1989 by  Kalíšek's brothers. Members: Kalous (vocals), Marek (bass), Zbyňa (drums) (ex-Ingrowing), Rafak (guitar). First demo released in 1991, it is called "Malý hřbitov" . Following album called "Morbid" - released in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/exhumace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
