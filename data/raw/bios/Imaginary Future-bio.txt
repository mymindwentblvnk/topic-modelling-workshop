Imaginary Future is the stage name of singer-songwriter Jesse Epstein.

He has released an EP titled "The Imaginary EP" and followed up with an album called "Fire Escape". He frequently tours with singer Kina Grannis who also provided background vocals for his last album.

Epstein's style leans toward slower melancholic songs that can be described as acoustic folk pop.
