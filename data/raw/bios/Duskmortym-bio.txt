Duskmortym is a metal band with avant garde elements (synth, black, folk) from Victoria and Nanaimo, British Columbia, Canada. Everything done in the band is by Dusk, or E. S. Bowes, whether that is the instruments, lyrics, or artwork. Duskmortym tackles themes like: wilderness, the sublime, cultural decay, and mythology. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Duskmortym">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
