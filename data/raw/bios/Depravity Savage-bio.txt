Depravity Savage, a death metal band based in Bandung, Indonesia. Was formed in 2006 and there are many great artist influence the band in writing their music, which include Disinfected, Jasad, Suffocation, Cannibal Corpse, Dying Fetus, Malevolent Creation, Terrorizer, etc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DEPRAVITY+SAVAGE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
