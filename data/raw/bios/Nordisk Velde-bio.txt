Nordisk Velde is a black metal band from Wilhelmshaven, Germany formed in 2004. They released a demo in 2004 and the full length album "In Manch' Dunkler Nacht" in 2006. Berg Ravn (Guitar, Vocals) and Surtr (Bass) are currently the only members of the band.
