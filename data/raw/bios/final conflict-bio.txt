There is more than one band with the name Final Conflict (in order of date of formation):

1) Final Conflict is a hardcore punk band from Long Beach, California formed in 1983. They have gone through various lineup changes with the only constant member being original guitar player (and founder) Jeff Harp.

2) There was also a Midwestern hardcore band named Final Conflict around the same time as the Californian band. The Minneapolis based band released their lone EP in 1983 and was featured on the Lung Cookies compilation. The EP was titled " In the Family". They were infamous at the time for playing the final show at Goofy's, a local hardcore hangout in Mpls., which ended up in a riot.; as written up in the "City Pages"  weekly in Sept. of 1983. Noel Morgan was the vocalist, Joe Morgan played guitar, Paul Roberts (Skid-O) played bass and the drummer whose last name I forget was Chris.

3) Final Conflict is a UK prog-rock band formed in 1985 who have released many original albums over their 22 year history: Redress the Balance, Quest, Stand Up, Hindsight (2003) and the latest album "Simple" (2006).
