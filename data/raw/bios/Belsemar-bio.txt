Black metal from Boden, Sweden. 
Project of Aemelgoth (Pär Johansson) and Azazel (Magnus Alakangas) of Satariel, started in 1993. First demo "De svarta gudarnas sömn" was released in 1993 and the second (and last) "Epistles Of Pain" in 1995.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Belsemar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
