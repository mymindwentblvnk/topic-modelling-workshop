Dubh (pronounced 'Duv', and Irish for 'black') is ambient dub electronica produced by Belfast-based artist Jonny McEwen.  Two albums have been released by Dubh: Fractured Broken and Beautiful (2009), and Insurrection (2010) - the latter an EP which features songs from the Insurrection Tour with Peter Rollins and Pádraig Ó Tuama.  An album was also released in 2005 on behalf of Belfast-based emergent group ikon called ikon Dubh, which was a collaboration between Jonny McEwen, Paul Hutchinson, and Pádraig Ó Tuama (credited as Pádraig Twomey).

Dubh's releases are available at Bandcamp or his label Proost.

The 2005 album ikon Dubh is also available at Proost.
