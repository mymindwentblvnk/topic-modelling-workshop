There are two artists with the name Heldentod.

1. Heldentod is a project begun in 2005 by Jeff Williams. Primarily influenced by death industrial and power electronics, Heldentod looks to create the most suitable expression of its key themes, including ghosts, ancestor worship, human sacrifice, neolithic fertility rights, Iron Age religion, mediæval literature, unseen dimensions, inhuman intelligences, and all manner of Forteana. 

2. Heldentod is a hungarian NSBM band since 2005.
