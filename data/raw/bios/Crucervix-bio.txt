Crucervix is a black/death metal one-man band hailing from Dunmore, PA, USA. All instruments are played by Peter Syslo. Two albums have been released: "My Dark Angel" in 2007 and "Blood" in 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crucervix">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
