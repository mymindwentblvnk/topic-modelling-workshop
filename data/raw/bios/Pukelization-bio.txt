Pukelization hails from Japan. The band plays, so-called death metal, but in their own way. Pukelization stands for "civilization of puke". The band has been there since 2000. Released 4 demos so far. Played in almost any areas in Japan and toured Indonesia 4 times. New album "Storm of Resurrection" (Obliteration Records) is now available. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pukelization">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
