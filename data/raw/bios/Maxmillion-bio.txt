Maxmillion sees the reuniting of Jason and Bobby from 16. This record represents the next step for Jason and Bobby and has seen them join together with Chris Jones (bass) to create a monstrously heavy, thick and evil sound. Comparisons will be difficult, but Maxmillion pull together elements of sludge, metalcore, doom and thrash resulting in one severe experience. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MaxMillion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
