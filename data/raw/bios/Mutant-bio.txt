1.Mutant is a west London-based thrash metal band who have been going for 4 years. 
They have had their music on various compilation CDs, and gig regularly.
Their most popular song to date, "Psycho Surgery", was released on the Earache Records "Thrashing like a Maniac" compilation last year. 
They are currently working on new material.
