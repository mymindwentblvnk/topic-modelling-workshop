Show Me a Dinosaur is a Russian instrumental band from Saint Petersburg, Russian Federation that formed in 2011. Their music is a blend of post-rock and post-metal with elements of ambient and shoegaze.

Show Me a Dinosaur lineup:
Artem Selyugin - guitar, vocalsPavel Volkov - guitarPhillip Chernonog - bassDaniel Kourdakov - drums
