Japanese noisy black metal with Woods (Hurusoma, Gnome, Gorugoth), and Dust (Deathchurch, SSORC). Released a cassette (Blissful Night of Abortion) and are featured on the Various artist album called Black Eye's Aggression with song Dead Human Trees. It was released by Weird Truth Prod. and Cosmic Garden Prod.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Under">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
