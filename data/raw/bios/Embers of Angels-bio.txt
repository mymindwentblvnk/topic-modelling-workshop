Embers of Angels are a melodic metal band based in Hertfordshire, England. Formed in 2006 by Guitarist Sam Clarke and Basist Simon Marston, the band has seen a variety of line-up changes over the years. 
Present Members are Lead Guitarist/Songwriter Sam Clarke, Basist Simon Marston, Vocalist/Lyric Writer Joe Mould, Drummer Simon Longe and Keys Player Chris Blow.

The band released a 4 Track EP titled Bloodwork on Febuary 1st 2011 featuring  four tracks that were contributed by former guitarist/songwriter John 'Mac' Stacey and featuring former Guitarist Azzy Smith.
