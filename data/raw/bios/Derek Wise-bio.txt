Derek Wise is a Canadian hip hop recording artist from Toronto, Ontario. He is under the XO label and collective,[1] founded by singer The Weeknd. Wise is also a part of 88GLAM, a duo formed in 2017 with 88 Camino. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Derek+Wise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
