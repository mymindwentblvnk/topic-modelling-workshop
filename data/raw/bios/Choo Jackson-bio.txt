Choo Jackson is an American hip hop recording artist, and producer. He is a Florida native from Vero Beach who later moved to Chambersburg, Pennsylvania. He worked at ID Labs studio in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania where he was discovered by Mac Miller who signed him to his Most Dope label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Choo+Jackson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
