Forgotten Venezuelan old school Death Metal band from Caracas. Baphometh was one of the earliest extreme metal bands from Venezuela, releasing a total of four demos from 1988 to 1993. The band suffered several line-up changes, with only Franklin Zambrano remaining as the original member.

A compilation featuring all four demos was released in 2003 by Peruvian label NecreoNauseA Records. It features the following line-up on the back cover:

Hugo Fuguet - Guitar
