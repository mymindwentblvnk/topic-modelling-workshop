The Dutchess and the Duke was a folk band consisting of singer/guitarists Jesse Lortz and Kimberly Morrison which formed in Seattle, Washington, United States in 2007 and disbanded in 2010. The duo was signed to Hardly Art Records and released two albums, "She's The Dutchess, He's The Duke" (2008) and "Sunset/Sunrise" (2010) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Dutchess+and+the+Duke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
