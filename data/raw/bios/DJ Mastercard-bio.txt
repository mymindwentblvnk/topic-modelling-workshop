DJ Mastercard is a member of Mall Music, an internet crew that's also home to [aritst]DJ Orange Julius, DJ Instant Message, DJ Boy Drama and DJ Paypal, their ringleader. Their music transcends the self-aware irony of their identities: over a series of digital releases and collaborations with other labels, the crew have laid out a glossy, information-age take on footwork, trading in the soul and R&B influences of Teklife for something more synthetic and futuristic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Mastercard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
