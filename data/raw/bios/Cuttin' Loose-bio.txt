Cuttin' Loose was formed in the summer of 2007. They released a 6 track demo early 2008 including "Ghetto Chillin'" which will also be featured on 2011 EP release "Skate To Hell". With influences which range from a variety of music including early/new punk, thrash, metal, and hardcore. Drawing on these influences they've managed to create their own style and unique sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cuttin%27+Loose">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
