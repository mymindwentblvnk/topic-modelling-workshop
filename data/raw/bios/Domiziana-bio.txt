Domiziana Gibbels, known simply as Domiziana (born April 9, 1977 in Freiburg im Breisgau, Germany) is a German hyprerpop singer. She grew up in Sicily, Italy, and moved to the German capital, Berlin, at the age of 17. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Domiziana">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
