ASRA was a Grindcore/Death Metal band formed in United States of America  (New York City, New York) in 2006.

Last known line-up:

A. Hernandez (Drums/Vocals)
M. Paradis (Guitar)
M. Hukill (Bass/Vocals)
H. Christos (Vocals)

ASRA stands for "Alleged Satanic Ritual Abuse".

They broke up in 2008.
