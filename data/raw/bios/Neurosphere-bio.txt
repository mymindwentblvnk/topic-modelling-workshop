Neurosphere are a Italian heavy metal band, most known for their song "Last Man Standing" featured in the intro screen of the Doom 3 co-op mod of the same name.

Information about the band can be found at their Myspace/Band Site. All their music can be downloaded from the media page on their main website for free.

Main site: http://www.neurosphere.it/
Myspace page: http://www.myspace.com/neurospheretheband
