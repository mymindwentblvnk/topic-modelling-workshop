Formed in 2014, Broken Glass Sanctuary is a death metal band from California's Bay Area with the sole mission of bringing a new perspective of modern death metal to the scene. BGS strives to forge a sound that encompasses the best of all dimensions the genre and it's subgenres have to offer from old school to technical death metal. With influences such as: Cannibal Corpse, Skeletonwitch, The Black Dahlia Murder, Whitechapel, Enfold Darkness, Suffocation, The Zenith Passage, The Faceless, Soreption, Fit For An Autopsy, Aborted, and so much more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Broken+Glass+Sanctuary">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
