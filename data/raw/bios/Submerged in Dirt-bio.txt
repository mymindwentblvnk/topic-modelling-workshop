Technical turbo-charged riffs are held together by incredibly precise and even faster drumming while even more extremely aggressive vocals combine to create a sound that can decimate stereotypical standards placed on most bands of this genre (Death/Progressive/Thrash Metal) . 
Submerged in Dirt is a band that can attract a wide fan base outside of it's local area. Recording obscurely at home for a combined total of over 8 years, it has developed production techniques very similar to those used in professional studios. Submerged in Dirt have the intention to create a new (higher) standard in metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Submerged+In+Dirt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
