Toshinori Hiramatsu (平松俊紀), who also goes by the name Sword of Justice, plays heavy metal arrangements of game music. He performs in several soundtracks of Gust games: the Atelier Iris ETERNAL MANA Arranged Tracks DECEITFUL WINGS alongside S.S.H. and also four tracks from Mana-Khemia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Toshinori+Hiramatsu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
