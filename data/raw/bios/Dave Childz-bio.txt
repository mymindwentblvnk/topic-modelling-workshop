Dave Childz, born in the birthplace of hip hop the Bronx, NYC to an African American father and a Cuban mother. After listening to various hip hop artist such as Notorious B.I.G.,  Jay-Z, Tupac, DMX, Outkast, Nas and countless others; He started his fixation on rap music at the age of 13 and through the years he has dedicated himself to perfecting his craft, meanwhile participating in various other hustles. At age 22, he decided to make music his main focus and began dropping mixtape after mixtape experiencing growing success with each offering. Now in 2013, he plans on transcending his career to that of commercial success with his new mixtape "Underrated Genius" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Childz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
