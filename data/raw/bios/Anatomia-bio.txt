There are two bands known as Anatomia:

1.  Anatomia is a death doom metal band formed in 2002 in Tokyo, Japan and features members of Necrophile (JPN) and Transgressor (JPN).
Heavily influenced by bands like Autopsy, Nihilist, Derketa, diSEMBOWELMENT, Rottrevore, Winter, Death and early Cathedral.
