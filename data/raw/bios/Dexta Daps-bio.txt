Louis Grandison (born January 12, 1986), known professionally as Dexta Daps, is a Jamaican reggae singer known for his dancehall songs as well as ballads. His songs include the 2014 ballad Morning Love, 2015's 7Eleven and the 2017 song Shabba Madda Pot. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dexta+Daps">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
