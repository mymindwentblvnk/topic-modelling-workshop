GutFuck were founded in 2012 In Ulm, Germany, by Chrissi (Guit.) and Pete (Voc.). A few rehearsals later Maddin (Drums) completed the line up and then the real songwriting process started. Oak (Bass) joined the Gang Bang Crew in 2014. Since then the line up is entired.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gutfuck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
