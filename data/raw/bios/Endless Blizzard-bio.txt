Hailing from the US, Endless Blizzard is the Black Metal solo project of Roskva from Lightning Swords of Death.  So far, a split with Gromm called In the Glare of Black/Beyond the Frozen Gates (2006) and a full length entitled Remember Your Death (2008) have been released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Endless+Blizzard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
