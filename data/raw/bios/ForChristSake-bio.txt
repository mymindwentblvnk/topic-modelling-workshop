FORCHRISTSAKE--Early 90's thrash/grunge/secular band from Rockford, Illinois U.S. Toured North America '92-'93, playing the Sunset Strip in Hollywood, Ca., to featured venues like the Satyricon in Portland, Ore. and CBGBs in New York City. Recording in Rockford and Vancouver B.C. Canada during those years produced the Demos 90-95 sessions. The band recently reformed for selected US Midwest dates in 2010.
