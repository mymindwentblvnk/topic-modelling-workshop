Giving Up rose from the Iowa mud in the summer of 2006. Howls, yelps and charming keys emanating from a midwest basement. The band consists of Jenny Rose, Sean Roth, and Mikie Poland. you can find their albums/cassettes at Sophomore Lounge. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Giving+Up">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
