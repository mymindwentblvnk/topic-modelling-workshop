Bleak Flesh is a band from Santiago de Chile that mixes different styles and elements of Metal and Rock resulting in a very technical, melodic and powerful sound. Despite his short career the band has performed at various venues in and out of Santiago highlighting the performance of December 16 2010 as support act for Cradle of Filth at the Teatro Caupolican. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleak+Flesh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
