Spectral Birth is a Death Metal / Thrash Metal band from Melbourne, Australia.

Formed in 1986 and soon after one demo release changing their name to Spectral Birth in 1988. They released two demos in1990 and 1992. Later releasing a Best of in 2004 to mark the end. And split up after that in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spectral+Birth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
