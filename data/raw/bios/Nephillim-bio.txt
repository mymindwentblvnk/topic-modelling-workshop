Nephillim was a black metal band from New City, NY that formed in 2001.  They released 2 EP's, a demo and split with Zircon.  They mostly performed live in the tri-state area.  They once performed at Hellfire Manifest in Huntsville, AL.    It is not known when they disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nephillim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
