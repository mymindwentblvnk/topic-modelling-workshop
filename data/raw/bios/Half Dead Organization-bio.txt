The group became known locally in the mid 90s with the release of a demo of 4 songs. Members were originally Smittyroe, JP Wood and Wood then joined the group for the release of their eponymous album in 1997. A second album is planned, "Resurrection - The Spirit of Wood". The band's style is typical of southern hip-hop show of horrorcore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Half+Dead+Organization">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
