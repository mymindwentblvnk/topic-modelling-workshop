Veil of Nithael was born in Fresno/Clovis, CA. Originally known as Northern Throne. Symphonic/Melodic Black Metal with Folk elements. The band has since split up due to personal differences.

Veil of Nithael consisted of... 
Kulden Ettall: Vocals 
Justin Blair: Guitar
Adam Camara: Guitars
Sarah Zacky: Keyboards
Johnny Valles: Drums
Eric Huselid: Bass
