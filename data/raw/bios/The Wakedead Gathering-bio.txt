a one-man old school death metal band from Ohio, US that was formed in 2007. A demo "Ars Notoria " was released in 2008 whose tapes were released by Skeleton Plague records and a full length "Tenements of Ephemera" was released in march 2010. Presently signed to I, Voidhanger Records.
