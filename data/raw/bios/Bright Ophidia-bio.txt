Experimental metal group found in 1998, Bialystok, PL. Connected with other band - Dominium. 
i.e. Korn meets Dream Theater

Band members:
Cezary Mielko - drums
Michał Franczak - keys
Przemek Figiel - guitars
Adam Bogusłowicz - vocals
Mariusz Rusiłowicz - bass

Past band members:
Mariusz Maciejczuk - guitars
Piotr Romaniuk - bass
