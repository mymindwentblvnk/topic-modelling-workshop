Metal / alternative band from Slavicin, Czech republic. The group was started in 1992. Their music is heavily influenced by the progressive, core music scene - Dream Theater, Meshuggah, Devin Townsend, Soilwork. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Substance+of+Change">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
