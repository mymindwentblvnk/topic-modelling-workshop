Devilish Distance is a Death Metal band from Russia. They formed in 2004 by Stoker (Guitars/Vocals), Goger (Guitars), Pavel Potapov (Bass also with Terror Stricken) and Krut (Drums). The band quickly signed to Mazzar Records and released "The Black Mountain Call" in 2005 and a year later released "Under The Shroud Of Lie",  where the band shows progression and technicality in their music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Devilish+Distance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
