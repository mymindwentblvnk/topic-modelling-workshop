In Death... are a 5 piece death metal group from Brisbane, Australia. The band formed in 2008 and released a self-titled E.P. the next year. In 2013 they released the E.P. "Thanatos" which was mixed and mastered by Logan Mader (Machine Head/Soulfly). 
The band toured nationally and throughout the U.S.A. in the years that followed and in 2017 will release their first full length album which is mixed and mastered by "Hertz Studio Poland" (Behemoth/Decapitated/Vader). They are on the artist development roster for "The Flaming Arts" Europe and have a view to continue touring internationally. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/In+Death...">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
