Cromleh is a Symphonic Black Metal founded in 2002 in Romania [Galati] under the name of Sepulcral. The band quickly changed its name to Cromleh, in 2004, releasing one demo in 2007, Denuntul Trãdãrii.

Current Line-up:
Cristian Hainic - Vocals, Guitars
Andrei Zala - Bass (Y-Grec)

Former/past members
