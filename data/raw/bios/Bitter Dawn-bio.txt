Bitter Dawn is a depressively-raw black metal project by Dietrich (B.C. Canada/WA, USA) and Mikael (originally from Connecticut, USA) and are currently based in the Puget Sound area of Washington, USA. 

  Bitter Dawn strives to create a dark, brooding atmosphere brought on by malcontent, depresssion, self-loathing, and contempt for sub-human scum. 

 Line-up: 

Dietrich: Rythem guitar, bass, battery, vocals and audio engineering.
