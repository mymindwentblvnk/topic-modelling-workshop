Zot is a group from Huntington Beach, CA that released one album in the 80s.  Their song "Uranium" got some play on MTV, but the band quickly faded into obscurity.Zot Members

Randy Wayne: Vocals, keyboards
Patrick Knowles: Guitar, vocals
Chris Stewart: Drums, vocals
Bryant Simpson: Bass, vocals

others:

Tom Calderaro: Keyboards, arranger
Andy Thomas: Synth, PPG
Eric Merienthal: Saxaphone
Dave Diamond: Synth
