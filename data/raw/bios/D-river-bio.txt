D-River is a Death Metal/Grindcore formed in Japan in 1997.  They are currently on Bellewood Records

Current line-up includes: Dess-Kawa on lead vocals; Miura on lead guitars; Satoo on bass; Kaz on drums 

The Band toured in Japan with Stormtroopers of Death / S.O.D. and Brutal Truth, and call their Style "Bushi-Core" (Bushi means: Japanese Warriors of Ancient Times)

The Band also released 2 Songs ("Birth of Ignorance" & "Walking Corpse") on the Brutal Truth Tribute Album "Choice of a New Generation" (2000 / Zero Negative Records) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D-river">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
