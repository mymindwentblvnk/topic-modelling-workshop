Progressive thrash metal band from the Netherlands, formed in 1990. Genetic Wisdom released a demo tape entitled "Trivial Destiny" the year after. Their first full-length studio album entitled "The Fear Dimension" was released on Mascot Records in 1993. In March 1994, the band released their second and final album "Humanity on Parole" through Pavement Music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Genetic+Wisdom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
