Everlasting Dawn is a Female Fronted Melodic Symphonic Death Metal from  Graz/Styria Austria.

Members: 
Lisa - vocals
Dave Slut - guitar/vocals
Richard - guitar
Adrian - keys/synths
Chris - bass
Wulf - drums

The band started as a project run by multi-instrumentalist Dave Slut (guitar) in autumn 2010 and made its way up as a Symphonic-Death-Metal Band after the entry of leadsinger Lisa, Richard (guitar), Chris (bass), Adrian (keys/synths) and Wulf (drums). Now this band is ready to spread their musical power among the Smphonic-Melodic-Death-Metal scene through daunting guitar riffs, charming vocals and catchy but also complex keyboard arrangements.
