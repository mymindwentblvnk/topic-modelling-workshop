Caught somewhere between epic 80's metal and modern progressive rock lies Dartanian. They try to combine catchy maiden-esque guitars, victorious sing-along anthems, odd-metered but accessible riffs and straight up breakdowns. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dartanian">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
