Formerly known as Evan B, Stacy Money is an underground rapper from New Jersey that has amassed a following on Soundcloud and Apple Music/Spotify. His music is melodic, with airy production, and lyrical content that focuses mainly on women and drugs. Stacy Money has also produced a couple of songs for Playboi Carti, which jumpstarted his success and got his name out there. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/stacy+money">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
