A black/death metal band formed in 2003 originally from Denmark that relocated to London, England. Between 2007 and 2013 they released 3 EPs and 3 Demos and in 2014 through Black Plague Records released their first full length recording "Word at War 1914-1918". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sturmtiger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
