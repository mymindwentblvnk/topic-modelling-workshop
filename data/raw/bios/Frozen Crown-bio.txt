Frozen Crown is a new female fronted Melodic Power Metal band infused with classic heavy metal vibes and themes.

 Frozen Crown is an Italian power metal band formed 2017 in Milan, Lombardy. In 2018, their debut album, 'The Fallen King', garnered considerable media exposure. Four tracks from the album were also released as singles, notably Kings, which has around four million views on YouTube. The band's second album,  'Crowned In Frost', was released in 2019. In the following year three of the album's tracks were made into videos, with Neverending at over seven million views on YouTube.

In November 2020, the band announced that their third album was officially in pre-production. In January 2021, it was announced that Alberto, Thalìa and Filippo departed from the band in 2020 to focus on personal projects. On 18 February, new band members Fabiola "Sheena" Bellomo, Francesco Zof and Niso Tomasini were announced.

On 23 April 2021, the band released its third studio album,  'Winterbane', having been preceded by its lead single Far Beyond already on 26 February.
