Best known for influential hip-hop/garage project The Streets, Mike Skinner's surprisingly prolific newest musical output comes under the guise of 'the darker the shadow the brighter the light'. Releasing a couple of songs in 2016 and coming to prominence with 'Bad Decisions in the Night', in March 2017, Mike has since released over 15 songs under this name, as well as a collaboration with Grim Sickers and Ghetts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/the+darker+the+shadow+the+brighter+the+light">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
