Rema (birth name Divine Ikubor)  is a Nigerian afrobeat and hip-hop artist from Benin City, Edo State. His critically acclaimed EPs have changed the wave of hip-hop/trap in Nigeria, with the songs "Iron Man" and "Spiderman" were featured on Obama's music list in 2019, while "Dumebi" was a global hit. His debut album Rave & Roses is set to be released on March 25, 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rema">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
