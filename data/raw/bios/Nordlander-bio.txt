Nordlander is a folk metal band from Huntsville, Alabama.

They play folk metal reminiscent of bands such as Heidevolk, Korpiklaani, and Otyg.

Their first demo was released March 13th, 2009. It was titled "To The Battlefield!" Stephen Hughes performed all vocals and instruments on it.
