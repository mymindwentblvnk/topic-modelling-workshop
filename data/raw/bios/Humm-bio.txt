Humm is an Italian post-black metal band from Piedmont, originally conceived as a one-man project by Fabio Gatto in 2014. They are currently signed with Polish record label Via Nocturna.

Described by the critics as "black, harsh, psychedelic, intimate, lacerating and unique," Humm released their opera prima No Empathy Before Doomsday on June 9th, 2014 through Haarbn Production. It was then quickly followed by their first single Metzengerstein: 1Q84, released independently on September, 2nd of that same year.

In 2017, Humm self-released their first EP To Shame Mankind digitally through their Bandcamp page on March, 13th as well a collaboration with Theta, the split record Humm/Theta on August, 22nd.

On November 1st, 2018 Humm released their second EP, entitled Way of the Serpent, digitally once more through their Bandcamp page. It was produced, recorded, mixed and mastered by Fabio Gatto, as well as having written all of the songs and lyrics.
