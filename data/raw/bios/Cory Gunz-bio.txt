Cory Gunz (born June 22, 1987) is a rapper, the son of rapper Peter Gunz. He is currently signed to After Platinum Records/Young Money Ent/CMB/Universal Records. He is a native of the Bronx. More recently, Jay-Z shepherded him into a joint venture through Island Def Jam Music Group. He was featured on a remix of Rihanna's "If It's Lovin' That You Want". He also contributed a verse to the original version of Lil' Wayne's A Milli before it was replaced with new verses by Wayne for the album version. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cory+Gunz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
