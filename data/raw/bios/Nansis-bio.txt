Geneva, Switzerland

Personal project created in 2008 by Ivan (previously Destronork) with the aim of creating dark and atmospheric black metal.

The one man band gets bigger in 2009 when Haître (lyrics and vox) (previously Destronork). The name changes from "Horizon", to become "Nansis".

In 2011, Nansis gets a full line up with Glycy (previously Destronork) on drums, Gino (Deadly Sin Orgy) on guitar and Anaïs on bass guitar as well as diverse designing activities.
