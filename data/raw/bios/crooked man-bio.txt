Crooked Man is the latest moniker of Sheffield electronic producer Richard Barratt (a.k.a. Richard Parrot, DJ Parrot) who is/was a member of The All Seeing I, Sweet Exorcist, Hoodoo, Surface Mutants, Tooth Faeries, White Trash and various house music projects from the late 80s/early 90s.
