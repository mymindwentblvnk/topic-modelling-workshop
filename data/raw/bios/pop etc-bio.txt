POP ETC is an American indie band from Berkeley, California. The band was formerly known as The Morning Benders. 

As The Morning Benders the band released a number of EPs and two full-length albums: Talking Through Tin Cans (2008) and Big Echo (2010). 

On March 25, 2012, the band posted on Facebook and Twitter that the band's name would be changed to POP ETC. Their first album under the new name is the self-titled POP ETC (2012).

The band consists of Julian Harmon, Tim Or, Jonathan Chu and Christopher Chu.
