Tyrant Goatgaldrakona is a death metal band, formed in 2009 in Szeged, Hungary.
Members:
Grave Desecration and Necrosodomy - guitars, bass 
Hellfire Commander of Eternal War and Pain - drums, vocals 


Discography :
-Sign Of Moloch (EP, 2010)
-Horns in the Dark (LP, 2013) 
-Marquis of Evil (EP, 2019)
