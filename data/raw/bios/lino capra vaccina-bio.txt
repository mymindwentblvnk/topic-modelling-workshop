Lina 'Capra' Vaccina is a cult Italian artist who came to prominence in the 1970s. 

He studied percussion, piano and composition, and started his musical carrer with the band Aktuala and also worked for Franco Battiato. 

He formed a short-lived group project with Battiato, Juri Camisasca and others called Telaio Magnetico. 

In 1978 he released a solo album, 'Antico Adagio' before disappearing until the 90s, when he returned to musical production.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lino+Capra+Vaccina">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
