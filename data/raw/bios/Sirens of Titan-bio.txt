"Very interesting and unique band, who blend Trouble, Soundgarden and King Crimson vibes with soulful female vocals that have been compared to Grace Slick and Anne Wilson. A refreshingly original album and band." -The End Records

Sirens of Titan were formed in Minneapolis, Minnesota in 2005, and were named after Kurt Vonnegut's novel "The Sirens of Titan" from 1959.
They released an eponymous album in 2007, and split up in 2011. Their vocalist, Felicia Mitchell is now in a project called Amyrrhond. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sirens+of+Titan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
