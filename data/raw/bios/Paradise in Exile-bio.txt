Paradise in Exile is an Australian deathcore band from Perth, Western AU. They formed in 2010 and have so far released a five-track demo entitled Desecration in 2011 and currently are preparing an EP for a 2012 release. 

The current line-up for the band is:
Vocals: Dustin Bonfield 
Guitar: Lewis Dodson 
Guitar: Travis Degois 
Bass: Cameron Broad
