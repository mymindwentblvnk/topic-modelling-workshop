Detroit garage band which appeared during the early/mid 1990s.  They are known for their cross-dressing skin-bearing stage presence.  The band consists of Danny Doll Rod [guitar, vocals] (a.k.a. Dan Kroha formerly with the Gories), Margaret Doll Rod [guitar, lead vocals], and Christine Doll Rod [drums].  The band has released 3 LPs and a number of singles, the first in 1994.  They are still rockin' today. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demolition+Doll+Rods">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
