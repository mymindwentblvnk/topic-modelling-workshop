Space Frog is a rave, hard trance German band formed by Cet Merlin, Robin Otis & James Walls. Cet Merlin co-founded the Frankfurt Energized Records in 1993 and released Phases (1-3) in 1993 and Lost in Space (Maxi Single) in 1994. Space Frog released their full album Welcome All Species in 1998 for Dance Pool German label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Space+Frog">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
