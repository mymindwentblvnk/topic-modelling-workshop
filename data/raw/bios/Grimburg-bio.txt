Grimburg is a Pagan Metal band from Germany. It was founded in 2003, but is now split-up.

Their music is about nature and paganism. 

Grimburg's last known line-up existed out of:

Ilska - Guitars (Heidenzorn)
Runatyr - Keyboard, Drums (Heidenzorn)

Grimburg has released the following:

- Naturherrschaft Demo, 2005  
- Promo Demo, 2007  
- Naturherrschaft EP, 2008
