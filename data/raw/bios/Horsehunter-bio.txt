Horsehunter is a four piece sludge/doom/stoner metal band from Melbourne, Australia formed in 2012.  After having had the honour of supporting High On Fire (US), Kylesa (US), Beastwars (NZ), King Parrot (AUS), Conan (UK), and Windhand (US), as well as playing very successful shows with many talented local acts (Child, Watchtower, Clagg, etc.), Horsehunter finally recorded their first piece of music and released their full length LP titled "Caged in Flesh" in September 2014 and is available in digital format at Bandcamp.
