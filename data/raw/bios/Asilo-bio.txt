2. asilo is a sludge crust four-piece band from buenos aires, argentina. 

members and ex members of uroboros, stilte, gran cuervo, abolition of impediment, etc.

their line up includes two bass, drum kit, vocals and electronic analogue devices. 



asilo is:

manuel p: backing vox / bass / analogue devices (2010-)
gabi: lead vox (2014-)
sebaxxxtian; bass (2010-)
sebastian f: drums (2013-)

former members
fahsbender125: bass (2010-2011)
adrian m: keys / fx / noise / ambiance (2012-2013)
nacho dp: lead vox (2013-2014) (drums 2010-2013)
