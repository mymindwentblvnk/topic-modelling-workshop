Acrostichon is a Death Metal band. They originate from the Netherlands (Tilburg) and were formed in 1989. They are also famous for female, growling on vocals.
Reunited in 2009. Although not in full active duty, Acrostichon will play occasional gigs.

Official site: www.acrostichon.nl

Line-up:
Richard Schouten - Guitar (Disembowel, Robotmonster, Black Melody, Master (US), Spina Bifida) 
Serge Smolders - Drums (Outburst (Hol)) 
Corinne van den Brand - Vocals, Bass 
Jos van den Brand - Guitar (Outburst (Hol), Master (US))
