Hammerschmitt was a German tradtional heavy metal band formed in 1984 in Falkenstein, Hesse, Germany. The band released their self titled album in 1985 and then split up afterwards. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hammerschmitt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
