1) Level (US)
Level is a alternative metal side project band from Los Angeles, CA featuring Bobby Reeves (lead vocalist from Adema).   Their self-titled debut album, Level, is available for download on iTunes.  The song "Disasterproof" has been featured in Twisted Metal: Head On in addition to television airplay.  Level's songs can also be heard during trailers for movies such as "Batman Begins", "Disturbia", "Walk the Line".
