Fatal Castration is a brutal death metal band from Olongapo, Philippines.  They formed in the year 2009.  They consist of Red (vocals), JP (guitars),  Quinn Carabeo (bass) and Renz (drums).  Red and Renz are also in the band Guillotined.  They released two full-length albums.  They are currently signed to the label Metallic Media. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fatal+Castration">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
