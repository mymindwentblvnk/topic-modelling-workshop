Dave Adams is a pop musician from North Carolina. He fronted the band Glass Moon in the early 80s, who were best known for their cover of the Hollies’ classic “On A Carousel.” In 1985, he released a solo album, “Dancing in My Sleep,” and worked as the touring keyboardist for a local NC band called the Fabulous Knobs. He re-formed Glass Moon in 2004 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Glass+Moon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
