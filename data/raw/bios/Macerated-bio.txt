Macerated is an underground grind/death outfit from Virginia Beach, Virginia. They still hold true to an old school DIY punk ethic, while playing super fast , brutal grinding death metal.The 2 songs up on this page are from their 1st real studio recording, at Sniper Studios in Moyock, NC.
