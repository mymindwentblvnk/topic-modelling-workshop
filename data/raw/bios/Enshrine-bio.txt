Enshrine were a Death/Black Metal band from Hamilton, New Zealand.
They existed in the early 90's and released two demo tapes: "Equality Is For Equals" in 1993 and "Burning" in 1994. Their songs were short blasts of hate.
Featured Sean (ex-Azazel, Intorment, Sinfeeder and currently Gawj and Coffee Rage), Dane (ex-Ruin, Animosity, Gore Story and now also in Gawj and Coffee Rage) and Rory (Blasphantom and ex-Azazel, Sinfeeder and Asscharger). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enshrine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
