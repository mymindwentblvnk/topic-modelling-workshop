Aone are a progressive metal group hailing from Lille, France and are composed of Yann Messemanne (singer and guitarist), Johann Hugot (guitar) and Simon Dubreu (drums).
Their debut album 'The Age of Aquarius' was recorded in 2008, and is released in April 2009.
Influences : Metallica, Pantera, Opeth, Dream Theater, Tool, Gojira, Led Zep, The Devin Townsend Band
