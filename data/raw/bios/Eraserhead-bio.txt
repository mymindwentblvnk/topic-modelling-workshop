There are, at least, 4 artists named Eraserhead:

Eraserhead is a Hardcore Punk band from Ontario, Canada.

Eraserhead is an electronic music producer from Bristol, UK specialising in breakcore, hardcore and melodic glitch. Best known for the bootleg Warp Records Memorial Mix from 2005 he has since produced music for net-labels Marionette Records and Love Love Records. His work has been remixed by fellow breakcore artists weyheyhey !!, Stazma the Junglechrist and Broken NVEP. An Eraserhead remix of TechDiff's Sim Stim will be appearing on an upcoming Peace Off release.

Not to be confused with the other Eraserhead, Pete Atkins, producer of the 1994 happy hardcore track 'Life Is A Mystery'.
