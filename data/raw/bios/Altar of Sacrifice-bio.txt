"The Destroyed" is only song by thrash/death band, ALTAR OF SACRIFICE.
It is pure, raw energy recorded in only one take.
The vocals of RobbKong were harsh because, as the story goes, he was hungover when the vocals were recorded.
I hope you enjoy.
