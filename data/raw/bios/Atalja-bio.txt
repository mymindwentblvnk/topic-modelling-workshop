Atalja was a heavy metal band coming from Central Finland. The band was formed 2002 and it disbanded 2003. Atalja's music is characterized by clear vocals and emotional, moody lyrics combined with traditional metal influences

Members:

Jami Haavisto (vocals)
Matti Puranen (guitar)
Matti Alonen (guitar) 2002-2003
Ville Luoma-Aho (guitar) 11/2003-12/2003
Hannu Hokkanen (bass)
Anssi Räisänen (drums)


Musical influences: Children Of Bodom, Dream Theater, HIM, Megadeth, Metallica, Pain of Salvation, Pantera, Slayer, Symphony X.
