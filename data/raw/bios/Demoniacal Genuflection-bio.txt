Demoniacal Genuflection is a Darkened Death Metal band from Texas. They embody a style of death metal that is hardly played anymore. Brutal, fast death metal with blackened doom atmospheres. 

2012 Lineup Consists of:
Ronnie Allen - Vocals
Sean Gary - Guitar
Anthony Contreras-Guitar
Keegan Kelly-Bass
