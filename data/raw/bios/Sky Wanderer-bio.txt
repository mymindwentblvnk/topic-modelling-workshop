Sky Wanderer is a hard rock/power metal band from Guadalajara, Jalisco. Their sound is based in a hard and dynamic work in the guitar execution, a powerful rhythm base and melodic voices.

Principal influences to the band are european metal, academic composition, progressive rock and classic literature.

Guitar - Fernando Farías and Juan Mexía
Bass - Brandon Garcia
Drums - Santiago Zamorategui
