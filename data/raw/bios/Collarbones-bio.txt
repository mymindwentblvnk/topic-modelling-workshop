Collarbones are a long distance Australian electronic pop/r&b collaborative duo, comprised of Adelaide’s Travis Cook (production) and Sydney’s Marcus Whale (vocals, sampling), beginning in 2007. Their debut album Iconography was released in March 2011 on Two Bright Lakes, who also put out the followup, Die Young in 2012; as well as their third album, Return, released in November 2014. Other releases include a 2009 EP Waiting for the Ghosts, 2010 singles Kill Off the Vowels and Beaman Park, a 2011 mixtape entitled Tiger Beats and a 2014 EP entitled Atlantis 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Collarbones">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
