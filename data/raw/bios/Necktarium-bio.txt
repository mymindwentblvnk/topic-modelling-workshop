Necktarium is an Atmospheric project from Rawalpindi, Pakistan. Started in Autumn of 2010 by Abbas. Borrowing influences from the shoegaze sound & aesthetics, songs about dreams, night time sensations.
The demo after being released as a free download, has been released on CD by Khrysanthoney(USA) and Tapes by Valse Sinistre Productions(Rom).
