Manuel Fogliata, aka Nuel, is part of a new wave of techno coming from Italy, led by studio partner Donato Dozzy. Togheter Nuel and Dozzy have released two highly sought after Aquaplano EPs, now selling for hundreds of Euros in Discogs. The Aquaplano sounds explores hypnotic techno and meditative ambient in equal measure. Nuel is continuing to expand his musical horizons, with his latest EP exploring electro and drum and bass. In late 2011 he will release his debut album on Further Records , a beautiful and deeply introspective work. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nuel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
