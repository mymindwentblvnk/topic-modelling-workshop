The Owls are an indie pop band from Minneapolis, Minnesota. Their songs consist of cute, catchy singalong melodies and soft background instruments. They released an 8-song EP in February of 2004 called "Our Hopes And Dreams". http://theowls.net


The Owls are an indie rock band from Newcastle, Australia. Formed in 2008 they have developed a unique sound that infuse rock, blues and country. They released a 5-song self titled EP in 2010 and following up with a single "Better Off Deaf" prior to the release of their first album "Swap Love" on November 9th 2012 http://www.theowlsonline.com/.
