Planet Eater is a power slop band from Albany, NY. 

Dustin Horan - Vocals
"Diamond Ray" Mike Mullin - Guitar
Nate "Bareback" Glass - Guitar
Mike Kuehn-Bass
Ryan Kenney-Drums

Originally started as a bowling team, Planet Eater was known for their winner takes all attitude, their 17 gutterball per game average, and their enormous bar tabs. Once Planet Eater was banned from most local alleys they decided they needed a new hobby.  After a failed cooking class and an incident at a Kung Fu dojo that they don't like to talk about, the dudes got a practice space in nearby Guilderland, NY.
