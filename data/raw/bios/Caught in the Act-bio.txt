Caught In The Act (CITA) were a boyband created in 1993 by producer Cees Van Leeuwen, who had previously worked with Dolly Dots and Berdien Stenberg. They consisted of Bastiaan Ragas and Eloy De Jong from The Netherlands and Benjamin Boyce and Lee Baxter from England. With little success in The Netherlands where they were based, the band  were a big success in Germany and went on to sell more than 4 million records around the world. After 4 albums, the band split in 1998.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Caught+in+the+Act">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
