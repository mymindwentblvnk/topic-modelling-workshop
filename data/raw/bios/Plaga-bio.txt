Plaga is a Polish black metal band from Olsztyn formed in 2008.

Plaga means "The Plague" in Polish. Members of Plaga wanted to stay anonymous.

"Plaga is Satanic black metal madness inspired by glorious hand of the Devil. We dedicate our work to Satan and underground metal psychopaths who proudly represent him. No line up, no interviews, no trends...".
