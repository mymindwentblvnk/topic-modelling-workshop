There is more than one artist with this name:
1. Brume (Christian Renou) is a prolific French musique concrete composer/sound organizer who has been releasing CDs and cassettes worldwide and he focuses today mostly on vinyl media. Renou is remarkable because he engages in elaborate concrete compositions without the academic pretension that surrounds much of the work in this style.
