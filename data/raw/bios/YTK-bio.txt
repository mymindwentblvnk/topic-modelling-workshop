YTK (Yonge Toronto Kids) is a Toronto-based hip hop group consisting of Keysha, Steph J and Jimmy B. They released their first single "Say Somethin'" in January of 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/YTK">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
