TrenchHead are a Brutal Death Metal/Slam Death Metal band from South London, England. After disbanding in 2007, the undergound death metal act are back with a vengance! but this time only with  vocalist jamie (Hunting Humans, Bun Dem Out) and founding second guitarist louis (Rotten Divinity, kola bear night terrors: the bellarom dextrose sleep deprivation experiment, Bun Dem Out). Not to forget the new addition to the band, a drum machine. The 2009 comeback will bring a new and more brutal stlye, more suited to the influences of the current members.
