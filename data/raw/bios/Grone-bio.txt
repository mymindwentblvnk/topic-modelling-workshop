Grone is a deathmetal band in the vein of Sepultura, Fear Factory, Obituary, Nailbomb, and Celtic Frost.

Grone was active from 1987 to 2001 and is currently on an extended and indefinite hiatus. They haled from East Brunswick NJ (USA).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
