Caustic Thought was Byron Stroud's first main band formed in 1987. The band also featured Jed Simon and Devin Townsend. Devin joined Caustic Thought to play with Byron after Jed left. In 1995, Devin asked Jed to join his latest project, Strapping Young Lad (aka SYL), and Jed has been a part of SYL ever since. Stroud also plays in SYL. Caustic Thought's only official release is their self-titled album released in 1994. Album also featured Ian White and Jason Vaughn both guitar/vocals and Bob Wagner in drums. They also have three self released demos called "Demo", "Sore" and "Cleaned Tape".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Caustic+Thought">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
