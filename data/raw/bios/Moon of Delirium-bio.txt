Moon of Delirium is the band formed by Alex Jorgenson (The Obliterate Plague, Desolate Realm, Terra Noir, Discard The Day) in the fall of 2009 and later joined by now ex drummer Russell Kummer (Gravecode Nebula, Lord Beherit, Pazatzu) as to be the creation of something new and different from previous musical works. 

Moon of Delirium is a band directed towards the subjects of the weird and paranormal as well as life and what lies beyond.

The name Moon of Delirium is loosely based on the 2009 film MOON.

New members Hadley Davidson, Helen Mehan, and Eric Bausch joined the lineup in spring/summer 2011. 

Debut album is intended for release in spring 2012.
