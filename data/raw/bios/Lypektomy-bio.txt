Lypektomy is a brutal death metal / Slam Death Metal band from USA. They formed in 2001 and have released two demos called "Promo 2005" and "Guttural Groove Slamm Gravity Blast" in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lypektomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
