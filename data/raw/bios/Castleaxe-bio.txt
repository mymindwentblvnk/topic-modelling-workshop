CastleAxe is a heavy/speed metal band from Salt Lake City, Utah. The lineup consists of Manchester on vocals, Hölger on lead guitar, Grög on guitar, Sürt on drums, and Prybar on bass. They originally formed as Speitre in 2006, changing their name to CastleAxe in 2011. As Speitre, they released the 'Speitre' demo in 2008, and the 'Metal Meal' demo in 2010. The 2011 'Castlemaster' EP is their first release as CastleAxe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CastleAxe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
