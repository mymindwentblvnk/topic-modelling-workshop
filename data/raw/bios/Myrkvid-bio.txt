Myrkvid is a black metal band from France formed in 1999. The lyrics deal about Destruction, Chaos & Nihilism. 
Line-up: 
Acheron - drums,
Nothgot - bass,
Hraesvelg (formerly Svalinn) - vocals, guitars.
