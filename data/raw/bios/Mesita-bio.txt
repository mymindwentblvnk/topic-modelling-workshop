Mesita is the name of more than one artist:
1) US indie pop 
2) Uruguay rap/trap 

1) Mesita is American multi-instrumentalist James Cooley from Colorado. He released his debut album Cherry Blossoms in June of 2008. His second album Here's To Nowhere was released March of 2011. His third, The Coyote was released April 3, 2012. His fourth album Future Proof was released October 8, 2013. Mesita's album The Phoenix was released right before New Years on December 30, 2014. It features the single 'The Villain'.
