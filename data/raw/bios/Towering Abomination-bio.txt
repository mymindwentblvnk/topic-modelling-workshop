Towering Abomination is a brutal technical death metal/grindcore band from Milwaukee, Wisconsin.  The band consists of  Gus Thome  on vocals,  Stevie Schmidt on guitar & vocals,  Scott Lyles  on bass and Nicholas Bradshaw on drums.  They are signed to Twin Town Tyrant Records and released their self-titled debut album in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Towering+Abomination">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
