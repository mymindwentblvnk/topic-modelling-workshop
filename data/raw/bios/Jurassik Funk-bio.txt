JURASSIK FUNK is a 4 piece band playing old school funk all the way from France.
Band members are Thomas Ottogalli (lead vocals, gtr & songwriting), Jorris Guilbaud (Keys & bg vocals), Seb Husson (bass & bg vocals) and Christophe Regonini (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jurassik+Funk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
