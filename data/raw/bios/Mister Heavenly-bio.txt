Spurred on by a mutual appreciation for doo-wop and doomed love songs (aka doom-wop), Nick Diamonds (Islands/The Unicorns) and Honus Honus (Man Man) set out to record a one-off instrumental 7" but soon found themselves writing an entire album within a span of a few late-night sessions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mister+Heavenly">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
