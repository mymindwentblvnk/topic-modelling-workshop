Vado is on a fast track to becoming the next rap sensation out of Harlem. Teeyon Winfree aka Vado grew up in what is known as the “Hall of Fame” area of Harlem, the same streets that spawned hip hop icons Big L, Cam’ron, Mase, Max B and Jae Millz. He is the first artist signed to the U.N., Cam’ron’s newly formed music group.

Vado’s versatile talent, unique rap skills and undeniable swag set him apart from many up and coming artists. He has been featured on a DJ Kay Slay album and has found success with Cam’ron on the well-known DJ Drama “Gangsta Grills” mixtapes. In addition, Vado’s recorded several tracks with hip hop artists like Rick Ross, Gucci Mane, Mase, Young Chris, Yung LA and Cassidy. He’s also been putting in countless hours in the studio working with producers such as Arab and AR15. This newcomer has even been seen on Sub Zero DVD.

The New York rapper proudly reps his “Most Hated” crew and is poised to take his career to the next level. Vado was first introduced to the game by legendary battle rapper Jae Millz, who is also a childhood friend.
