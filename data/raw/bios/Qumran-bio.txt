There are multiple artists using the identity Qumran.

One is a demoscene tracker from Sweden. He has a blog but it is mostly in Swedish. He has been known to collaborate with dknation.

He was born in Orsa, Sweden 1981. His influences are a mix between egyptian folkmusic, electronical music and ambient. 

He has made some songs for computer games (eg. Camel Ride, Hero in dungeon) but the games had no success on the market.

In the early 00's he and dknation was well knowned at the music tracker site http://www.traxinspace.com at the time when that site was strictly tracker music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Qumran">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
