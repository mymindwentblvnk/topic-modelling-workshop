Ardkore were an Crossover/Thrash band from Teesside, North East England. They were founded in 1985 and existed until 1991. In this time they released one demo and a LP (Napalm Stix to Kids/1989.

Line-Up:
Scott Pope - Vocals
Steve Hoggart - Bass
Lash - Guitar
Dave Gregory - Guitar
Ian Williams - Drums
