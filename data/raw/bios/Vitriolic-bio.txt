Powerful metal riffs with soaring vocals over the top. Vitriolic the unsigned supergroup is made up of...

Darren Curnow - Vocals (formerly Stephensons Rocket)
Mike Fricker - Guitar (formerly Stephensons Rocket)
Johnny Walker - Bass (formerly Ozena)
Marc Randall - Guitar (formerly Covered in Blood)
Kurtis Johnson - Drums (formerly R41M)

Vitriolic have so far supported Skindred, Breed 77, The Wildhearts, Thunder, Twin Method and the Ravonettes to name a few; and gigged as far away as Los Angeles USA. Vitriolic have played The Viper Room USA, Plymouth Pavillions UK, London Astoria UK, Carling Academy UK. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vitriolic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
