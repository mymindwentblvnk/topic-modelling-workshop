Getsemani was an Italian band from Brescia, originated in 1997. They played a Doom/Extreme Metal with elements of Gothic. They had only 2 releases. A demo Dreaming To Fly and a kind of EP with 5 tracks named Under The Light. The main member was Roby Sora, who played violin, some vocals and was in charge of all keyboard and synthesized parts, including drums. Cinzia Hunecke Rizzo was the female soprano voice on the band (who later took part as guest vocal to bands as Rhapsody, Luca Turilli, Avantasia among others) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Getsemani">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
