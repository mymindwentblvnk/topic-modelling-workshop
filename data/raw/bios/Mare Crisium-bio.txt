Mare Crisium is a Slovakian (depressive) blackmetal band from Trenèín, formed in 2005 by members from Korium and Morana. The band released the demo Bolesť in 2006 on tape and CD-R, both in a very small and strictly limited quantity. A nameless split with Holomráz and Semai was released in 2009 on CD-R as well. The band lyrical theme's cover subjects such as depression and nature.

The band also performed live during the Blackened Emotions vol. 1 concert which was released on DVD-R in a limited quantity (performance together with Korium, Trist and Festa desperato.

Discography:
2006 - Bolesť (demo)
2009 - Holomráz / Mare Crisium / Semai (split)
