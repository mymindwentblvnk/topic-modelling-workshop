There are multiple artists using this name.

1. A Hardcore band From New Zealand.

2. Post Hardcore / Screamo / Emo from Cleveland, Ohio.

3. A soul / funk band from the late seventies that recorded only one full album, with the traditional Salsoul Records Label. Famous session bassist Bob Babbitt played on the album.
