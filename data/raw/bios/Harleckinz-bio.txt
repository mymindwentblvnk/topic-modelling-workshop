Rap-crew from Berlin with american and spanish roots. Members: Doubleface, Big Sal (RIP), Boogieknight. Raps in american-english, some german features. 

Dropped their debut "Now We're Talkin" in 2000 on wea/warnermusic. The tracks "What Time Is It? (Zeitgeist 2000)" and "Berlin Love" got the most recognition, also in the top40.
