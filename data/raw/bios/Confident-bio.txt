Fast & intense thrashcore band from Szczecin, Poland who issued two studio demos & a rehearsal tape between 1988 &1990.  Their bassist/vocalist Jarosław "Cypil" Kędra is now a mathematics professor at the University of Aberdeen. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Confident">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
