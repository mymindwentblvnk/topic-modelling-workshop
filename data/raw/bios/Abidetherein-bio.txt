Abidetherein is a Black Metal band formed in Damascus, Syria in 2010 by Goraal Guitars ,Bass, vocals , all music and lyrics , and then Typhon joined as a drummer and also a guitarist . 

Abidetherein plays black metal with some oriental influences .

Lyrical themes: Anti-Religion , Horror , Darkness .

Session members :
 Amer Salek : Lead guitar 
Hussam Alshaikh Ali : Oud
 Ameen Armoush : Darbuka
