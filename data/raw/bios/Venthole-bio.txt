Tuomas Kauppinen - Guitar      Venthole, if you're not familiar with them, is a five-piece metal band hailing from the heart of Finland, Jyväskylä. Although their music could broadly be categorized as metal, the songs carry a vast amount of influences stemming from the members' different musical preferences.
