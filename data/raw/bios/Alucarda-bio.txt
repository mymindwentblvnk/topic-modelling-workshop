To the max on the fuzz and the bizarre, Alucarda worship 70's bad acid culture and reminisce on that dead generation that abandoned their careless everything goes ways in favor of baby booming and suburban mortgages. Their lyrics speak with total abandon of their taste for vice and the macabre and their music blasts away, via deranged screaming and lo fi doomy garage rock, a bygone era of damaged thinking and total hopelessness.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alucarda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
