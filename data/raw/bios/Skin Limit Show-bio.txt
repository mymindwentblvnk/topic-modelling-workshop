Grind/doom/industrial created by Stuart Toolin, once of (early) Pitch Shifter, Taken by Wolves, Hordes of Satan, Execrate (UK), Slughunter etc. Formed in Nottingham (UK) in 1993, split-up in 1996.

Features two singers -  members of 3rd Stone and Hard To Swallow on the first LP Wound Freeze.

Discography:
Wound Freeze /LP (1994)
Walking Knife /EP (1995)
