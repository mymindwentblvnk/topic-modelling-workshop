Mucklers Circle is a 5 member metal thrash band from West Townsend, MA formed in 2010. Bringing the best of old/new school metal together due to a father/son team and ages of the band ranging from 21-43. The first self-titled EP Mucklers Circle was released in September of 2011 with 11 tracks 


Many ask the meaning of our name..

Once upon a time, long, long ago people had a different way of dealing with scumbags, thieves, rats, & other various undesirables. They would be surrounded and "taught" the meaning of justice or revenge. This surrounding and the accompanying "lesson" was called the "MUCKLERS CIRCLE".
