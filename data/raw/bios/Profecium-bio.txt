PROFECIUM is a communist black metal band formed in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in 1993 and disbanded in 1998. It was the first known RABM band, promoting anti-fascism, anti-christianity and socialism from a satanist point of view. In 2013 the band returned and recorded a new CD in 2014.
