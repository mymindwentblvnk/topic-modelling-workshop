Repuked is an old school death metal band from Stockholm, Sweden. They formed in 2007 ,and have to date, releasing 2 demos  and a debut "Pervetopia" in 2011, Also, in 2013 the band released their second full length "Up from the Sewers". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Repuked">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
