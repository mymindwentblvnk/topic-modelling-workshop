Razor White was a heavy metal band from New Orleans, Louisiana.  They formed in the year 1984.   They are best known as the band that Phil Anselmo was in before he joined Pantera.   However, Anselmo does not appear on any of Razor White's official releases.  Razor White released a demo in 1988 and one full-length album in 1991.  They disbanded in 1992.  Their last known lineup consisted of Troy Marks (vocals), Jimmy Hipp (guitars), Matt Thomas (guitars, keyboards), Will Buras (bass) and Steve Joseph (drums).   Past members include Craig Cazaubon (bass), R.C. Holmes (bass), Danny (drums) and Phil Anselmo (vocals).
