Formed in 2006 as Autograder this band has been renamed to Zoliborz before the release of their debut EP "Poincare Conjecture" in 2014. In fact the band has no a regular lineup and consists of the guitarist Vitaly Mimoladov in one hand and session musicians/guests in other hand.

Debut EP "Poincare Conjecture" released in 2014 consists of the earliest themes (dated with the years 2002 - 2006). The main songwriters of these old themes were Alexander Razhev (former Autograder member) and Vitaly Mimoladov. With the aim to record this debut EP Vitaly Mimoladov had attracted some session musicians (extended info is available at metal-archives.com etc.) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zoliborz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
