Dreaming Dead are a female fronted Melodic Death Metal band from (Los Angeles, California) United States. The band was formed in 2006 and were formerly known as Manslaughter, They released their debut album 'Within One' in 2009. Their second album 'Midnightmares' was released on April 20th 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dreaming+Dead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
