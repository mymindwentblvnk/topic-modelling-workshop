Making Fuck are a 3-piece doom, sludge, metal band from Salt Lake, Utah. They released a 2-song, self-titled demo in 2013 and their debut full length titled "A Harrowing End" in 2016. Both releases are available at https://makingfuck.bandcamp.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Making+Fuck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
