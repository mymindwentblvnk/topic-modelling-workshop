Amorphia is a popular underground soundtrack death-black metal music band  from Ukraine; their music style is called “soundtrack metal” because all their songs is based on the cult thriller movies (such as “Seven”, “Omen”, “Angel Heart” etc). They come from Zaporozhje city in southern Ukraine, and they formed in 2005. They released their debut album EP “The Name Of The Rose” in 2007. New album “Dramatis Personae” is being recording.
