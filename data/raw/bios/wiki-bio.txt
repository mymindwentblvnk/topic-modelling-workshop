Wiki is the pseudonym of Harlem rapper Patrick Morales, one third of the group RATKING. On December 7, 2015, Wiki released his first solo mixtape, Lil Me. Along with Sporting Life, a number of heavyweights produced the album, including Madlib, Kaytranada, and Harry Fraud. Wiki also put out a video for “Livin' with My Moms,” the lead single from the solo project.
