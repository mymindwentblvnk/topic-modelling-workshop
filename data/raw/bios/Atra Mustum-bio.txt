"Atra Mustum" band was created 30.08.2000 as a result of separating of Pagan-Black band "Ashen Light". The band was consist of musicians of "Ashen Light": Wsegard, Gmur, Niyan, Smurd and new comming guitarist Visionary. In September of the same year Smurd left the band because of differences in musical interests. New drumer(Rodento) came in only 28\10\2000. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atra+Mustum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
