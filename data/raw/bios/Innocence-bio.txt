There are multiple artists using this name:

1. Innocence is an Urban Soul outfit headed by the lead singer Gee Morris.
The first album, Belief, was a success based upon the hit single Natural thing. The follow up album, Build, less successful  and moved to a  more ambient sound.
Gee then disbanded the group, hoping to concentrate more on producing artists and music, but she's kept up her singing and is currently working on a third album.

2. Innocence is Franco-Spanish actress and singer Geraldine Larrosa, who is married to Il Divo member Carlos Marin.

3)  Innocence were an Austrian Heavy/Power Metal band. Their S/T LP was released only in Germany through distribution of the IMS from Hamburg.

4) A Canadian one-woman noise/black metal project helmed by Chariot. Lyrical content pertains to childhood abuse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Innocence">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
