There are multiple artists called Newbreed:

1)  Newbreed (also capitalised as NeWBReeD) are a Polish metal band.

2)  NewBreed are a British rock band from the Midlands, who have played numerous gigs around the UK and also in the US. They recently released their first EP, Be Your Own Saint, on independent label Wolf At Your Door. They have since changed their name to Envy of the State.

3)  newbreed is an experimental/metal project of  Juraj Marko([http://www.last.fm/user/jurajjr]) from Slovakia(aka newmachine/REACH). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/NewBreed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
