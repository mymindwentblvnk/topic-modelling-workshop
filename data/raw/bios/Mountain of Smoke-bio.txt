Mountain Of Smoke are a 3-piece doom inspired sludge, post-metal band from Dallas, Texas. To date, they have have 5 releases; their debut "Gods of Biomechanics" in  July 2018, "Future Sins" in December 2019. "Replicated in December 2020, " Gods of Biomechanics 1​.​5" in June 2021, and "Imprinted" in April 2022. Allreleases are available at https://mountainofsmoke.bandcamp.com.
