1)  Future Shock (not to be confused with UK electronica duo Futureshock) are a hip-hop group from San Diego, California. Debuting in 1996, it includes members Sojourn, Ahred, Capture, Ajax Starglider and DJ DNA. They are former members of the Tunnel Rats crew and have worked with artists including KRS-One, Soup The Chemist and Pigeon John.
