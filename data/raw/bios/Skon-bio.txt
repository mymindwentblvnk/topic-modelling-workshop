Skon is the name of more than one artist
1) Slovakian Doom/Death Metal/Depressive Rock
2) Polish one-man project

1) Slovakian Doom/Death Metal/Depressive Rock project from Carpath ((Warmarch (Svk), Carpathia (Svk), Oldblood, Inferno (Cze), Evil (Svk), Slavigrom).

2) Skon is a unique one-man project based in Poland. With roots in old school industrial he is  influenced by more modern electro, gothic and folk artists as well. He released demo called "Nic Realnego" (Polish for "Nothing Real") which is a mixture of harsh electronic beats and folk music. This artist has not released music for some time, hopefully we'll hear from him again in the near future. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
