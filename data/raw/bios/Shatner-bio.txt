SHATNER are a Leeds based band with two critically acclaimed albums under their belts, "Energise" (2005) and "Thirteen O'Clock" (just released).
They were formed in 2003 as guitarist/songwriter Jim Bower plus bassist brother Rick, keyboard player Chris Minz and drummer Dick Sharp came out of retirement with no plan except to be as good as possible. They have recently been joined by Sarah Niven and 'Planet' Claire Gibbons on backing vocals.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shatner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
