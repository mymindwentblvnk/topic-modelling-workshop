Hyatari is a four-piece drone / doom metal band from Huntington, West Virginia, United States formed in 2004 by Mac Walker and Chris Tackett, both formerly known as creative center of Chum.

Current lineup:

Chris Tackett - guitar, bass guitar, keyboards, sequencing;
Mac Walker - guitar, keyboards, sequencing;
Brett Fuller - sounds, programming;
Jude Blevins - drums, moog.
