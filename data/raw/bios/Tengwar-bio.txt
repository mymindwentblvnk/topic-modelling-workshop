Tengwar are a folk/power metal band from Argentina. Their lyrics are themed around Tolkien's Lord Of The Rings, and they have thus far released two EPs, The Pilgrim's March and Tengwesta Quendion, and the latest full-length album titled "The Halfling Forth Shall Stand" released on March 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tengwar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
