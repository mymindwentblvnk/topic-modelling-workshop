Steel Aggressor is a heavy/speed-metal band from Indiannapolis, USA formed in 2010 by  Rick Cope. Debut album 'From Ruins To Dust' is now available from Stormspell Records. Current line-up: Rick Cope (Rhythm, Lead Guitar, Vocals), Bob Fouts (Drums), Damon Black (bass, session). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Steel+Aggressor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
