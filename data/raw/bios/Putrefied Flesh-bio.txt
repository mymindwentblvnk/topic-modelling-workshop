Putrefied Flesh, hailing from Pennsylvania, US, are a band who formed in 2003 under the name Nihilistic.  The band, in the past, played a more death metal sound but today blend death metal and metalcore elements. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Putrefied+Flesh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
