Genre: Pagan Black Metal
Country:Germany
Active since 2005 and present

They  released two album : My oaken chest (2009) and Upon Bleak Grey Fields (2012)

Formed after the break up of Twilight Prophecies by Bjorn and Jan, the band is continuing to search for members to complete the line-up and continue their creative process.

The Winding Path and My Oaken Chest both are free to download off of the band's official website.

From Latin, Timor et Tremor loosely translates to "Fear and Trembling."
