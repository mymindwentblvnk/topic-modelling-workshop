Nathaniel Kai Oda, better known by his stage name lil ricefield, is a rapper from Oakland, California. He belongs to the local rap group “the.ism.”

He started off making music back in 2016, but didn’t gain much traction until 2018 with the success of his breakout song “Trapanese.”
