Cult Belgian thrash . 
A band that truly defies classification, this 80's Belgian act combined the sloppy blackish speed metal of early Hellhammer, N.M.E, Carrion (Che), early Voivod, etc., with the fury of early 80's crust/proto-grindcore punk. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/X-Creta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
