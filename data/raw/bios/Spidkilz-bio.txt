Spidkilz are a heavy metal band from Italy. The band was formed by vocalist Elisa Over in 2010 after she split with fellow Italian metallers White Skull.

Spidkilz plays a fast-paced mixture of old-school thrash and heavy metal. They released their debut five-track demo "The Ultra Demo" in May 2011.
