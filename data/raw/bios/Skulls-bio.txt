There is more than one artist named Skulls (also see The Skulls):

1.) A UK indie-rock act (related to Fleeingnewyork/Fleeing New York, who have released material with Sotones (2007) and Stuck Up Music (2005), now active in the US as 'SKULLS', then as Band Of Skulls)

2.) A Jamaican roots reggae harmonies trio active in the late 70's - early 80's.
