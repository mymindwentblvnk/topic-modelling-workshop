Elysia Paula Crampton Chuquimia is an Amerindian Aymara electronic musician. Her work touches upon themes of Aymara survival and Latin culture, queerness and its historic roots in aforementioned Aymara history, naturalist themes, and frequent utilization of samples and arrangements from varying sources. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elysia+Crampton">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
