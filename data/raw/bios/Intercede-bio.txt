Intercede were a American metalcore band from Burlington, Wisconsin. The band has released one demo and split album with Castahead. Bill Kurth, Joe Dalen and Mark Clark went on to be in Endthisday and Temo Rios went on to form 7 Angels 7 Plagues. 

 Band Members:
▪Temo Rios – Vocals
▪Mike Pagliaroni – Bass
▪Mark Clark – Guitar
▪Joe Dalen – Guitar
