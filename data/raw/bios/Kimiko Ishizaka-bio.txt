Recordings of Johann Sebastian Bach's Goldberg Variations, first published in 1741, played by the renowned, award-winning pianist Kimiko Ishizaka, who lives in Cologne, Germany. These recordings are released in the public domain under the Creative Commons Zero license.

Lossless FLAC and mp3s of the music along with the history of the project and its funding through a Kickstarter project can be found at its homepage. Streaming via Soundcloud is also available on the site.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kimiko+Ishizaka">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
