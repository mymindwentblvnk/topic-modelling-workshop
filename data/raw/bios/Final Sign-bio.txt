They are a heavy metal / power metal band from North Carolina.  They formed in 2013.  Final Sign consists of Shawn Pelata on vocals,  Brian "Hellstorm" Williams on guitar,  Jim Hunter on bass and Kevin Haverlah on drums.   Howard Baker was their bassist before Jim Hunter.  Final Sign released their debut album Hold High the Flame in 2015.  Their lyrical themes include allegorical presentation of human issues and fantasy.  They are currently signed to Divebomb Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Final+Sign">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
