Dopefight were a British sludge / stoner metal band from Brighton that formed in 2009. They released a self-titled demo, a split CD with Dead Existence, a full-length 'Buds' (the vinyl version was released by StoneStallionRex/Funeral Industries), a split 7" with Gurt, a split 7" with The Fucking Wrath and a split 12" with Lex Rhino. The band split in 2012.
