There are multiple artists called X-Ray.

1. X-Ray was a  Heavy Metal band  from Kansai, Japan formed in March of 1982. Their first full-length studio album named "Hard Section" was released on June 21st of 1983.

2. X-Ray  is a techno collaboration between the Belleville Three; Kevin Saunderson, Juan Atkins and Derrick May

3. X-Ray  is a hip hop producer, he was a member of Monsta Island Czars with MF Doom, MF Grimm. & others.
