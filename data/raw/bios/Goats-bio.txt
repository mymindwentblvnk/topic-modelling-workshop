Goats is a technical death metal band from Japan.
They have released two albums, those being "Breath" in 1995 and "Collective Unconscious" in 2002.

Line-up.
Satoru Hatashi - Vocals
Takayoshi Hashimoto - Drums
Hiroki Kosaka - Guitar
Atsushi Noguchi - Guitar
