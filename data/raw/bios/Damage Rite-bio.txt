Damage Rite is a Lebanese metal band who released their first album in 2017. Because of their unique combination of thrash metal, death metal and groove metal (with some blackened tinges here and there), they are one of the few bands whose most fitting genre descriptor is extreme metal, an otherwise very vague term.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Damage+Rite">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
