What happens when four crazy fellows join forces in the heart of the Greek metal scene? Blood-pumping, fast-paced bay area thrash combined with that notorious teutonic drive; all under the veil of the much appreciated old-school feel.
 
Toxic vocals is the answer to the duo's hellish riffing, yet keeping the solos melodic and imaginative, while the aggressive but also groovy drum blast leaves enough space for technical basslines to make their statement.
