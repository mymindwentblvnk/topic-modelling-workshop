Thorngoth is a Black Metal band from Germany. The band was founded in may 2003 by Sorath, Grond, Corpse & Akhorahil. Vulgrim joined Thorngoth in 2008 as a live member. In 2010 he became a permament member of Thorngoth. 
In June 2010 their third album "Leere" was released via Folter Records.
