Islet are an experimental rock band from Cardiff, Wales. Their debut album "Illuminated People" was released in January 2012 through Shape Records, following two 2010 'mini-albums' 'Celebrate This Place' and 'Wimmy'.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Islet">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
