Buffalo Witch is a three piece band hailing from the icy depths of Michigan. The mix sludgy stoner metal with doom and psychedelic. The consist of members Nicky Thunders, Christopher Hell, and Eric Morin.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Buffalo+Witch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
