DISPARITY 

is a newly formed melodic death metal band from Široki Brijeg/Mostar (Bosnia and Herzegovina). Formed in the end of 2013., the band consists of Denis Leko (drums), Zvonimir Mandić (vocals), Toni Grbešić (guitar), Ante Dujmović (guitar) and Filip Musa (bass). Their first recorded song "Horn of Nephilim" recieved amazing feedback and they are currently working on their first EP, which should be finished in the 3. quarter of 2014.
