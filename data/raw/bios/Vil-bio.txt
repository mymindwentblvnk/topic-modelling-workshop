1)Vil - Mihaly Botyanszki (12.31.1972.)
Hungarian composer. He has two favorite occupations. The music, and the computers.
He heard electronic music in the 80 years first.
In the 90 years, as the active member of the demoscene, founded a team with some friends of his. And made stuffs for compos on demoscene parties.
Todays he works as a software developer. And he writes music in his free time.
The musics which can be listened to much more easily interest him now.
