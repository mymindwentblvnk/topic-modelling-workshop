Deviator is young Ukrainian one man-band from Khmelnitskiy city. It has been formed as dark-ambient project but lately style have changed to atmospheric black/death metal. Music includes hooked death metal riffs with atmospheric sound of black metal, expressive screaming and also melodic doom metal with folk acoustics influences. Lyrics themes are connected with Death, Darkness, Sorrow, Hatred and Pagan beliefs with strong Nordic influence.
