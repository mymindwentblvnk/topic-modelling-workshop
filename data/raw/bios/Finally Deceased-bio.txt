A German death metal band founded in 1991. The band released an EP called "Blessed by Demons" in 1994. They continued playing live shows, including the1994 Wacken Open Air, until a split up in early 1997. They finally released their self titled album in February of 2011 after reuniting in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Finally+Deceased">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
