Brazilian heavy metal band from Curitiba. It's singer (Raphael Mendes) got fame on youtube by covering songs from many bands with Bruce Dickinson's voice (including Maiden and Bruce solo works). The repercution on youtube gave the band it's debut album recorded by italian label Frontiers Records.
