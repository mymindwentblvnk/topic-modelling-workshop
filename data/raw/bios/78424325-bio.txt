78424325 is a Mexican depressive black metal band formed in 2008 by B.G. (Vocals and Guitar) and M.P. - (Drums and Vocals). 78424325 means suicidal. So far, the band has only one release; a split called "Three Ways to Live in Pain". This split (Managarm, Sterbenzeit, 78424325) has been released by Self Mutilation Services in 2009.
