F-Rontal, born in Mannheim in the mid-90s, developed an interest in the electronic music scene early on. Since the beginning of 2014, he has managed to create his own style and thus gain a foothold in the international scene. With releases on record labels like KD RAW, CODEX or SAYWHAT? he has already made a name for himself. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/F-Rontal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
