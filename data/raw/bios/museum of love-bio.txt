Museum of Love is the new project from Pat Mahoney (of LCD Soundsystem) and Dennis McNany (AKA Jee Day) new project. Their first single was titled "Down South", followed by an eponymous debut album in October 2014.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Museum+of+Love">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
