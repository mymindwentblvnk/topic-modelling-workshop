1. Buffalo-based Hardcore Punk and Thrash Metal band with Garrett Klahn on lead vocals, Glenn Szymanski on guitar, Bob Whiteside on bass and Jon Salemi. The band originally formed as Split Decision in 1990 before changing their name to Support later that year. After splitting up, Bob and Jon would continue making music in Snapcase. 

2. Hardcore Punk band that released "Can't Leave It Behind" in 1999 on Breakthrough Records.

3. Punk Rock band from Austria that formed in 2001. Line-up consisted of Pezi, Sepp, Chris, Paul and El Mikele
