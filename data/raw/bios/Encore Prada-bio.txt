Encore Prada is a deathcore/grind band formed by the ex-members of "chema y andrea" in 2007.Later that years, the band drastically changed both in lineup and style. Herz  started recruiting a Rocko, Rafa, Zeuz and Tom from diferent hardcore/Metal bands such as FP y DFM to continue this proyect. Encore Prada boasts Mexico DF's native talents:

Zeuza Strecci-Screaming vocals (with the occasional pig squeal)
Herz Strecci-Guitars
Rafa Strecci-Bass
Yulie-Guitars
ChikiTom-Percussion
