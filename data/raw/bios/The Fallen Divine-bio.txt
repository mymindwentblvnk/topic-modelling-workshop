Founded in the late summer of 2009, The Fallen Divine is a new Norwegian Melodic Death Metal band from Sandvika, Akershus. Their sound contains elements elements from a wide specter of genres, including folk music, power metal and thrash metal. All these genres combine in a mashup consisting of furious fretwork, thunderous bass, fast drumbeats, sweeping orchestra sounds and harsh growling vocals as well as clean singing. 

Right now, the band is in a process of both songwriting and performing in various clubs. They have plans of recording a demo containing 3-4 songs which will be released sometime in 2010. Some of their songs can be heard on MySpace. Be sure to keep an eye out for The Fallen Divine.
