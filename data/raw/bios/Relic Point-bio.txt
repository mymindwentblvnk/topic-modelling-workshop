Band formed in mid 2010. Relic Point is a raw, dark  music  influenced by many genres such as post metal, sludge metal, drone metal, black metal. They released their first demo, "I', at the end of  2010. The first full-length album “VIXI” was released in February of 2014. The second album "Self Punishment" was released in February of 2020.
