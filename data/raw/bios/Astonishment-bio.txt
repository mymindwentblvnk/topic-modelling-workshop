Astonishment is a danish duo from Copenhagen, consisting of Stinne Lender (vocal)and Thomas Eltorp (vocal, guitar). 
It was formed in 2003. The first release, "Disillusion, here we come", came in 2005. The second album, "The rest is silence", was released in 2007. In 2008 came the no-named ep.
We can expect the next release from Astonishment in the summer of 2009.
