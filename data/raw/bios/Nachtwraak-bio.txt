Nachtwraak is a raw Black/Thrash Metal band from the Netherlands. They were formed in 2003 in Sliedrecht(The Netherlands). There on hold now for a unknown time.

Releases under the old name Tempus Fugit:

- Shadowlords
- Realm Of Suffering

Releases under the name Nachtwraak:
- Burzum Tribute Attakk ( covered the song: A Lost And Forgotten Sad Spirit)

Before they started to take a break for a unknown time they recorded a promo cd for a album wich was planned to record in the future. the promo was never released.
