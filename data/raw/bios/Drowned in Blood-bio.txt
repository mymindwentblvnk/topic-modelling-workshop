Drowned In Blood was born in April 2003 in Mexico City. Characterized by creating a Death Metal with powerful and raw riffs, fast and crushing drums, used in the lyrics concepts of war, destruction, gore and death.

They recorded an LP titled "The Warfare Continues" and the Split "Impalement Of Mankind" with Warcrusher, now working on their second full-length, have shared the stage with bands like Vital Remains, Avulsed, Disgorge (Cal), Incantation, Severe Torture, Putrid Pile, Impaled, Monstrosity, Grave, Vader, among others, and has participated in important festivals in Mexico and Central America.
