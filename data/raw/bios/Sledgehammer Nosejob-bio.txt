Boom! In your face! That’s Sledgehammer Nosejob .

A Dutch metal project with Massive Assault  members Carl Assault and Fredde Kaddeth. Assisted by session musicians Marco Prij (Distillator ) and Caspel Le Gras to fulfill live shows and studio work.

Sledgehammer Nosejob’s music style varies from bombastic Death Metal to groovy Rock & Roll and from energetic Thrash Metal to angry Crustpunk. Sledgehammer Nosejob is known for their energetic live performance and excellent songwriting skills.
