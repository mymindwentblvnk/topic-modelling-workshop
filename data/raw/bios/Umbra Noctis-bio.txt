Umbra Noctis is a black metal band formed in Mantova, Italy in 2004.

Members:
Filippo Magri - Vocals (Maestus)
Tiziano Valente - Guitars (Grinning Shadows, Angryon)
Davide Bottoli - Bass
Omar Maghella - Drums

Former Members:
Michele 'Shon' Leali – Guitars
Lorenzo Leasi – Guitars
Daniele Cerutti – Guitars
Suicide 'Loki' Barda – Guitars (Suicide, Inhuman)
