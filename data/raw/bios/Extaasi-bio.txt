Extaasi was formed in 1995 in Turku, Finland. Having played for over ten years, they have reached a stable position in the underground scene with their Finnish, metal-influenced rock. Extaasi has often been compared to leading Finnish rock/metal bands, such as Kotiteollisuus and Maj Karma. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Extaasi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
