1. Jungle/breakcore project from New Zealand, active circa 2000.

2.  END OF MAN is pure heavy, aggressive music born of the Philippines. They draw influence from Heavy Metal, Thrash Metal, Hardcore and Death Metal music.

Formed last quarter of 2002, END OF MAN is no stranger to the underground scene. The group is composed of ex-members of Mass Carnage, Seven Stitches and Thrash Metal Gods, Discant X.
 
END OF MAN offers Crossover Music to the Underground scene.
