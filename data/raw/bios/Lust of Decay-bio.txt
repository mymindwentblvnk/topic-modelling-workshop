Lust of Decay is a brutal death metal band from North Carolina whose guitarist, Steve Green, owns the death metal label Comatose Music.

Lust of Decay formed in the year 2000 with only one thing on their minds: to play pure and gory death metal.  With members collected from various amateur bands, a lineup was put together and the band released their debut full-length Infesting The Exhumed in 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lust+of+Decay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
