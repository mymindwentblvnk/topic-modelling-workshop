Die My Will was a hardcore band from Connecticut. Die My Will released a seven song self-titled CD in 1997 on Drawn & Quartered Records. Later that year they release two new songs on a split 7” with All You’ve Lost.  In 1998 Die My Will released a nine song cd entitled “…and still we destroy” on Pin Drop Records. In 1999 Die My Will release three new tracks on a split CD with Piecemeal on Wonder Drug Records. 

Die My Will was:
Brandon Dubrosky (Guitar & Vocals)  
Rob Wisner (Guitar & Vocals)
Todd Reynolds (Bass) 
Jeff Mielcarz (Drums)
While Jeff was on tour with Hatebreed for a short stint Justin Foley (Killswitch Engage) filled in on drums.
