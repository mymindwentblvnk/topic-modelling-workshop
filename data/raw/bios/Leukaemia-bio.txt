An 80's english punk band.
Leukaemia formed in 1982. Hometown Stamford and Peterborough. Original line-up was Tucker-vocals, Tim Head-Drums, Graeme Baker-Guitar and Geoff - Bass.


Also a black metal act from China formed 2008. With several releases out.
