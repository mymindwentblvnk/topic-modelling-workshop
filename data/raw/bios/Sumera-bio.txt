Sumera is a Dutch singer-songwriter. Her first EP entitled 'Stop Heart' was released in 2012. Late 2012 she supported Marina and the Diamonds on the 'Lonely Hearts Club' Tour in Europe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sumera">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
