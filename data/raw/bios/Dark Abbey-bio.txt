Formed in 1989 in Sollentuna, Sweden with Johan Enochasson - Vocals, Drums, Nicke Hagen - Lead Guitar, Bass, Manne Svensson - Rhythm Guitar.  The band released one demo tape, Blasphemy, in 1990 before changing the name to Epitaph in 1991. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Abbey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
