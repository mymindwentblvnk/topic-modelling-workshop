Clinging To The Trees Of A Forest Fire formed in 2006 and self-released an EP that same year entitled "Harmonies From Bleeding Mouths".  Their first full-length, "Omega Drunk On The Blood Of Alpha" debuted in 2008 on Debello Recordings.  Their newest album "Songs of Ill-Hope and Desperation" was released in 2010 on Prosthetic Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Clinging+To+The+Trees+of+a+Forest+Fire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
