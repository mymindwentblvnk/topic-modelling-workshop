1. Nebulah is an black metal band from Germany.

2. Nebulah is an extreme metal band of Lleida city (Catalonia-Spain).
They play a fusion of brutal death and black metal, a combination of slam and darkness with old school and new elements.
On December 2007 they finished their first demo entitled "Paths of Carnage".
