Formed In June 2011 Distant Shores is a Four Piece Metal Band Originating From Guernsey, Channel Islands. Having Played A number of eventful shows on their home island of Guernsey & neighboring island of Jersey, Distant Shores has built up a loyal and enthusiastic fan base and looks to new horizons to expand their music.

Distant Shores is:

James Holmes - Vocals & Guitar
Lee Oliver - Guitar
Joe Le Long - Bass
