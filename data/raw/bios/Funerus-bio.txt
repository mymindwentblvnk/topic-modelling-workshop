Great Death Metal band hailing from Pennsylvania, USA.
Current members:
Jill (Daily) McEntee - Bass, Vocals
John McEntee - Guitar, Vocals (Incantation, Mortician (US), Immolation, Revenant, Goreaphobia)
Sam Inzerra - Drums (Mortician (US), Morpheus Descends, Deadspeak, Undivided)

Discography:

Utter Dismemberment (Demo, 1991)
Dismembered Again... (Demo, 1992)
Rehearsal Demo (Demo, 1994)
Festering Earth (LP, 2003)
Reduced To Sludge (LP, 2011)
