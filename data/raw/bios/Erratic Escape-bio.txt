Erratic Escape was formed in the summer of 2007 in Thessaloniki Greece. 
The band played mainly cover songs and some of their own until January 2008 that they recorded their first demo. 
In July 2008 they played their first live show and many more the following winter of 2008.
 In April 2009 they recorded their first EP called "Just Erratic" which had six songs written by the band.
 After some member changes they played more shows until the end of 2009 and in March of 2010 they recorded their second EP called "Unleashed" with 2 new songs, and two old songs re-recorded with the new members of the band.
