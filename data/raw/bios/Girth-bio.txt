3) Girth was formed in 2003 by Dave Webb and Peijman Kouretchian. Their first record, "Living in Truth," can be downloaded at brutalprog.com

in 2005 keyboardist Andrew McInnis joined. "Sleeper, Awaken!" was released by Web of Mimicry Records July 18, 2008.

The final lineup of girth (circa 2006) included Mark and Blaine of the Swarming Hordes as well as Dave and Peijman. The album this line-up recorded was released September 15, 2009 on Trademark Recordings.

Dave is now playing with Wah Wah Exit Wound and ASVA.
