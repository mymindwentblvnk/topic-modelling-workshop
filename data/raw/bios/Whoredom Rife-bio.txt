Whoredom Rife are a black metal band that formed on the outskirts of Nidaros, Norway in 2014. They released their EP 'Whoredom Rife' in 2016. They released their debut full-length album 'Dommedagskvad' in June 2017 through Terratur Possessions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Whoredom+Rife">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
