Japanese black metal band, probably best known for having drummer Ironfist from G.I.S.M. in the band. Started in Tokyo (2000) and currently active through Armageddon / Obliteration Records. The band has released their first demo in 2004, named "Black Metal Terrorism". Their full-length studio album, named "Infidel Eternal" followed a year later. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SSORC">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
