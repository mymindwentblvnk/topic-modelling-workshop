3 piece stoner/doom rock band from Oxfordshire, who's influences include Kyuss, Sleep, The Melvins and Black Sabbath.
 The band describe their sound as music to have a good smoke and drink to, while nodding your head to the slow hypnotic grooves.

Pete - Bass/Vocals
John - Guitar/Vocals
G - Drums
