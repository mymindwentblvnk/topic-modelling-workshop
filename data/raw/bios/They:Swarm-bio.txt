They:Swarm was an Israeli death metal/grindcore act.

in early 2008 Whorecore announced that their lead singer Tomer Jones is leaving Israel to work in american porn industry, but revealed shortly afterwards that it was a prank and that the band changed it's name to They:Swarm, as well as the joining of Sven De Caluwe of Aborted as a Vocalist. the band recorded one EP as They:Swarm named "The Mundane Corruption", and in late 2008 rechanged their name back to Whorecore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/THEY:SWARM">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
