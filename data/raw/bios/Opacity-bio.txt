Opacity is a Finnish band from Espoo, started in 2001. They played industrial/ambient/noise until 2005 when they started to incorporate metal elements into their sound. The band describes their style today as futuristic electro metal. They have released a demo "Of Chaos and Beauty" in 2002, first album "Evolution of Mind" in 2005 and another album "Syndicate in Blood" in 2007.
