Photophobia consists of members from Finland and Italy.
The band has released 2 Full Length albums by now under Immortal Frost Productions.
Humana Fragilitas "2010" & The Seven States Of Mourning "2012"
Their lyrical elements waver around Suicide, Murder and Anger, among other horrible things.

There is also a brazilian progressive / darkpsy project named Photophobia.
