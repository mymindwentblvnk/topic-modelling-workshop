UK Death Metal band, formed in 1983 by vocalist / guitarist Andy Barnard, who was a member of Electro Hippies and Metal Duck. Although active for 25 years, the band has only released 3 albums and 1 EP, however they are a highly regarded underground act, and have toured with numerous bands, including Master, Paradise Lost, Benediction and Carcass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nightmare+Visions">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
