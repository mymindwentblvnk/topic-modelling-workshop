Cystem are a Death Metal band from Vancouver, Canada. 

In 1997 they contributed a song called Divided Process to the GRRRR!! Extreme Music From Vancouver - Volume 1 compilation, which was compiled and produced by Devin Townsend of Strapping Young Lad fame. The line-up on this recording is:

Craig Campbell: voice
Cory McBain: guitar
Bernie Wempe: guitar
Rick Struve: bass
Jason Hagan: drums

The song Divided Process is from the then (1997) forthcoming album, Option Anxiety - A Wempe Story. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cystem">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
