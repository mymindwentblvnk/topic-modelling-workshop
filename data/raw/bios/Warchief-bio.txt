There are more than one band by this name

#1 Warchief is a four piece melodic death metal band from Chivilcoy, Argentina.
They first started in 2007 under the name En Force, but changed their name to Warchief in early 2010, just before recording their debut release. The band consists of Nahuel Martiniello (guitars), Pablo Godoy (vocals), Ezequiel Corrao (bass) and German Neme (drums).
