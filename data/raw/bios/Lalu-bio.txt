Lalu is a progressive rock/metal conceptual outing from French composer Vivien Lalu (Time for a Change, Sad Warden, Shadrane, Chris Catena, Mind's Orchard...) initiated in 2003 with the help of Dutch and Canadian musicians. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lalu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
