Motherstone are a Groove/Thrash Metal band from Rome, Italy who formed in 2003. They released their first demo 'Just a Little Dose...' in 2003, followed by the 'Through The Paths Of Insanity' EP in 2006. Their debut album 'Biolence' was released in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Motherstone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
