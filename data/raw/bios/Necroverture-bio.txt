Necroverture are a five-piece progressive death metal band, based in Brisbane, Australia. 
The band formed in 2009 and their debut EP was released in mid 2010.

Line-up:
ZEKE: Guitar
JOEL: Guitar
JANNICO: Bass
ROBB: Vocals
