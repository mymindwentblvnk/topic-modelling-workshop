Bonefleshdolls hails from Tampere, Finland. The dynamic trio focuses on experimental alternative metal combining the everlasting forms of jazz, funk-, rockabilly, death metal, progressive metal and pretty much everything in hands to create music which does not exist yet. The Bonefleshdolls debut "album" This is most definitely a pineapple was released on late 2006 with some financial aid from finnish indie-label eWolution Media.

Currently Bonefleshdolls is recording the second album with the project title "My schlong is so long that is has to be measured in kilometres" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BoneFleshDolls">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
