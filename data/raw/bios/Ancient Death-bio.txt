There is more than one artist/band named Ancient Death:
1) Ancient Death is a death metal band from Massachusetts. They have released one single, Labyrinths of Self Reflection, from their upcoming debut album Sacred Vessel. The single was released on released June 3, 2022.

Ancient Death is:
Jerry Witunsky - Guitars & Vocals
Ray Brouwer - Guitars
Jasmine Alexander - Bass
Derek Malone - Drums

2)  Ancient Death is a black/thrash metal band from Ecuador. The band was formed in the year 2003. They released two full-length albums in 2016, Oda a la obscuridad and Cult to the Death. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ancient+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
