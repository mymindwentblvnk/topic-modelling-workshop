Tleskač was Ska band from Plzeň, formed on 1st January 2000 and disbanned on 29th November 2013. Former members were:

Jirka Sýkora (ex Požár mlýna) - Bass guitar, Vocals;
Míra Cisler (ex Požár mlýna) - Vocals;
Karolína Jägerová - Vocals;
Karel Kolesa (Ausgerechnet) - Trumpet;
Honza Ženíšek - Guitar;
Honza Mráz  (Maxipes fík, ex Mrtvý řízek) - Keyboards;
Ondra Žižka - Drums;
Michal Sloup - Trombone;
Patrik Havlán - Tenorsax;
Pavel Halík (Rastabilly´s Kang, Feromoon, ex My nevíme) - Altsax, Barytonsax, Clarinet;
