Kraken Duumvirate is a somewhat experimental black metal band from Finland that uses nautical-themed concepts for their releases. Their lyrics is about oceanic myths, concerning monstrous beings.

So far, two EP's have been released, both consisting of three tracks which together form one sentence. The second track is the album title as well.
