Shah Alam, Selangor, Malaysia (1998 - )

Formerly known as Zirkon.

Additional release: Hell Hordes of Darkness (Demo 2002). 

The band was "on-hold" for some years because Malaysian police arrested some members due to their involvement in the local Black Metal Scene.
