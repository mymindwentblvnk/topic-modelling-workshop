(2004-2008)
One Dead Dream were a 5-Piece Metal-Mosh/Hardcore band from the Mornington Peninsula. 

One Dead Dream have gigged with such bands as Parkway Drive, Carpathian, Behind Crimson Eyes, The Getaway Plan, Bodyjar, Antiskeptic, Shotpointblank, In Name and Blood and many more <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/One+Dead+Dream">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
