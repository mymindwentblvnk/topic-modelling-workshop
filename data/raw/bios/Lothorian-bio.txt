Lothorian is a four piece stoner/sludge/doom band from Limburg. The band airlifted in august 2011 with a slow, heavy and dirty set. They create a sound like Weedeater, Orange Goblin, Sleep, Goatsnake, ... They recorded their debut-album "Welldweller" in january 2012 at Safehaven Recordings.

The current line-up consists of:
Jeroen Op 't Roodt - Bass
Jurgen Martens - Drums, Vocals
Thomas Francot - Guitar, Vocals
Niels Fatalist - Guitar
