Internal Harvest were an Experimental Post-Black/Doom Metal project that was active between September 2009 and December 2013. The musical style comprised elements of doom metal, post-rock, shoegaze, black metal and progressive music amalgamated to create a unique, dark, droning and melancholic sound. The lyrics expressed intense themes of personal struggles, self destruction, sorrow and inner pain & suffering.
