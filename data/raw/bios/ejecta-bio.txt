Ejecta (now known as Young Ejecta) is a synthpop duo from New York City, USA, consisting of Leanne Macomber (vocals) and Joel Ford (instruments, production). On 20 November 2013, they released their debut album Dominae on Driftless Recordings.

Joel Ford was involved in the project Ford & Lopatin, and also worked with Oneohtrix Point Never, Autre Ne Veut and Tigercity. Born in Texas, Leanne Macomber is the touring keyboardist of Neon Indian and also a member of the band Fight Bite.
