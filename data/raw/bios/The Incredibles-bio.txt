The Incredibles is the name of more than one artist.

1) a mid 1960s soul group from Los Angeles. Members were were Cal Waymon, Carl Gilbert, Alda Denise Edwards and Jean Smith.

2) a house group consisting of  Gregory Cocco, Peter Hantke and Stefan Heinemann.
