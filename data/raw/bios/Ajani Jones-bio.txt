Ajani Jones is an MC from Chicago's Southside. After attending College at the University of Iowa on a scholarship he left it all behind to pursue his life's passion in music. With a razor-sharp wit and tight delivery his raps are often written with the purpose of entertainment by way of thought stimulation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ajani+Jones">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
