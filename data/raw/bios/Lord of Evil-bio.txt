Polish black metal from Gdynia, Pomorskie. They started out under the name Rotting in 1991, changed their name into Lord of Evil in the same year. Their debut demo is "I Fuck the Mother of Christ" which was recorded in 1991. In 1995, this band later changed their name to WAR 88 after developing NSBM themes on their lyrical themes.

▶ Desecrator of Souls - bass, drums, vocals
