Formed November 2004 Dead Element creates a growl that soars to the stratosphere of the sonic soul. Led by singer Steve Thiriot, and guitarist Jason Hughes. Dead Element is an energetic metal band with a unique sound full of rage, emotion, and substance. Their name is a combination of a fascination for the word Element and the dreary connotation of the word Death.
