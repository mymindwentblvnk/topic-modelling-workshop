Corpus Rottus is a death metal band from Wilmington, Delaware.  They formed in 1989. 

Their discography consists of -

Intensified Gore (EP, 1991, with With Your Teeth records)
Rituals of Silence (Full-length, 1991, with With Your Teeth records)
Demo 1991 (Demo, 1991)
Nihilism (EP, 2009, with Outlaw recordings)
