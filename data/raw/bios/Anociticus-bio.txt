Anociticus is a Melodic Death/Black Viking Metal band from Owensboro,Ky. 

After a few genre/name changes they settled on Anociticus.

Current Members:
Trent Williams - Rhythm Guitar
Logan Blankenship - Lead Guitar
Jodi Hart - Vocals
Jesse French - Drums
Chris Pyle - Bass

Former Members:
Blake Welsh - Bass
