Yestalgia is a beatmaker, producer, and multi-instrumentalist from Copenhagen, Denmark. His first solo project is inspired by the expanding lo-fi movement, blending imperfect and organic drum grooves with soul and jazz influences.

Following 2 years of regular releases, starting with his debut single 'See You Tomorrow Sunshine' and his EP 'Yes', Yestalgia has started collaborating with the biggest labels in the genre, like Chill Beats, Chilled Cow, Lofi Jazz Records, and more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yestalgia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
