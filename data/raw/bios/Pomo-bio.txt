Pomo is a Canadian born multi-instrumentalist now residing in Montreal, QC, Canada.
With absolute vigour, Pomo has taken the electronic music scene by the collar and dragged it back onto the dance floor with dreamy synths, glittery arpeggios, buoyant beats and twangy samples. There really is only one way to describe Pomo's music... 

Sunshine flavoured ice cream for the ears.
