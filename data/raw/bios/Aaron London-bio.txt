Aaron London (born; October 17, 1993 in East Ham, Newham, London) is a British singer, songwriter, producer & rapper. Aaron London first started singing and producing beats at the age of 13 after learning to play the piano and electric guitar during his childhood. Aaron's recent production credits include Channel AKA's #1 Urban hit 'Think' by Intact. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aaron+London">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
