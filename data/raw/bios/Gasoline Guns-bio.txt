Gasoline Guns was formed in 2013 in Chernihiv city (Ukraine). Genre: Heavy Metal/Hard Rock with a harsh vocal. Influences: Motorhead, Helltrain, Chrome Division, Malignant Tumour , Metallica.
Lyrical themes: rock 'n' roll, Booze, devilry, horrors, post-apocalyptic dystopia.
