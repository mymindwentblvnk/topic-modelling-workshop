Hwimory (휘모리 in korean) is a Melodic Power Metal band from Inchon, South Korea.

They've released an album in june 2010, named as the band's name, Hwimory.

Band members:
Jae Wook Lee (guitars) 
Jae Moo Lee (guitars) 
Ji Bum Park (vocals)
Eun Sik Jung (drums)
