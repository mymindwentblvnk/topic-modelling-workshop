Men An Tol is a 3 piece Black Metal band from Christchurch, New Zealand. Formed in the late summer of 2009 and spewed forth from the swamps of Marshlands. Taking influences from across the board including: Immortal, Woods of Ypres, Wolves In The Throne Room, Peste Noire, Reverend Bizzare and a lot of 70's rock. Our debut album is due to be released later this year so watch this space.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Men+An+Tol">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
