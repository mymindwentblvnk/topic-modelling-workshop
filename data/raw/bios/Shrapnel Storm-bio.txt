Shrapnel Storm is a death metal band from Tampere, FInland. The band is influenced by old school/mid tempo death metal. A few melodies mixed into the riff-based rhythms create the Shrapnel Storm -sound.

Founded in 2007, the band recorded it's first demo, "Battlefield", in early '08. New demos have been released, "Bullet Trails", in late 2009, "At Dawn" in 2011 and "We come in peace..." (2013).. Now the band is focusing on recording it's first album. More information is available via the official web site.
