There are multiple artists with this name.

1) Sear is a blackened death metal band from Helsinki, Finland. They have released 2 full length albums, which are "Begin the Celebrations of Sin" (2005) and "Lamentations Of Destruction" (2007). They are currently signed to Dynamic Arts Records, who hosts a number of other notable artists including Impaled Nazarene, Deathchain, Deathbound, Shade Empire and Scorngrain.

2) Sear is an electronic/trip-hop project by Michelle Richfield, who appeared as the lead female vocalist on the first two albums of Antimatter and as a guest vocalist on My Dying Bride's "34,788% Complete" and Anathema's "Eternity". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
