The are at least two bands named Nephthys: 
1) Nephthys is a dutch doom/death metal band who only released a promo recently.
2) Nephthys is also band from Slovenia formed in 2008 by Dorian Žulič (vocals, guitars, keyboards) and Matej Kržišnik (drums).
