Lucifer's Heritage is a Speed Metal band from Krefeld, Germany. They changed their name to Blind Guardian after recording two demos symphonies of doom in 1985, and battalions of fear in 1986. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lucifer%27s+Heritage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
