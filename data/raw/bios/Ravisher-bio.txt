Ravisher is a new Thrash Metal band from Topeka, Kansas. The band was originally formed by brothers Hunter (Guitar) and Colton Strathman (Bass) along with Jaiden Smith (Drums) in late 2008. This trio began writing and practicing songs together for several months, developing their sound. It wasn't until the summer of 2009 that the band was complete with the addition of C.J. Mills on vocals.

In the fall of 2009, Ravisher recorded their 4 song E.P., Ravish Me, which was produced by Gerren Andres of Diskreet.

Ravisher is heavily influenced by 80s thrash metal bands such as: Slayer, Testament, Megadeth, Nuclear Assault, Anthrax, Metallica, D.R.I, and many others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ravisher">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
