Nathan Ball is a singer-songwriter formerly of acoustic duo The Ballinsky Project and the band Nayfumble. He has been writing songs since his teens and at the age of 20 won the North West Song Writing Competition. Until 2008, Nathan played as The Ballinsky Project with cellist Adrianne Wininsky during which time they released “The Control Z Sessions”. 

Currently based in London, Nathan now performs as a solo artist and will be at several UK summer festivals in 2009, including Small World, Sunrise Celebration, Glastonbury, Secret Garden Party, Fiddler's Green Folk Festival, Big Green Gathering, Broadstairs Folk Week and Buddhafield. 

Nathan's EP “Footprints” will be released on 14 June 2009 at The Green Note Cafe, Camden.
