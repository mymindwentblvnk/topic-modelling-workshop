1) A black metal band from Saxony-Anhalt, Germany, currently comprising of five members: C.K. (vocals), B.M. (guitars), Deimos (guitars), STK-Kommando (bass) and C.H. (drums). The band play an unusually aggressive brand of suicidal black metal, rather than the slower, more lethargic approach which is the prevalent variety of the style. The band has since its formation in 2004 released a couple of demos, a compilation, a split EP with Weltbrand and a full-length entitled "To Welcome Death (by Heart and Soul)".
