Morbid Fog is a solo black metal project from Karditsa, Central Greece. The project was established in 2009 by Commander Ov Thirty Demonic Legions and released a demo tape "Wolves Under The Winter Fullmoon" in 2009. Now, in 2010 Morbid Fog are going to release their first full length album "Aggelon Apokathilosis"(Deposition Of The Angels). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+Fog">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
