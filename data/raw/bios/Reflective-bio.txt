Reflective was a metal band from Joensuu, Finland. The first demo, Erasing Memory, was released in 2003 and sold out very quickly. Because of musical differences they decided to quit the band. They made their second and final demo, Ghost Logic, in autumn of 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Reflective">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
