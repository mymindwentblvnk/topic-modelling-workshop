Black Chalice is a doom metal band from Maine. They started as a raw death metal band on their demo, Prayers for Our Lord and Savior. Subsequent releases saw them moving to a brand of doom metal mixed with death metal and funeral doom metal elements. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Chalice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
