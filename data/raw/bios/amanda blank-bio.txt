Amanda Mallory (a.k.a. Amanda Blank) is an MC/Rapper from Philadelphia. She is best known for her overt sexuality and collaborations with Spank Rock and Aaron LaCrate in 2005.  Her popularity at events like South by Southwest Festival can be attributed to her carefree attitude and lyrical flow. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amanda+Blank">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
