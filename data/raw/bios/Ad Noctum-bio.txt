Ad Noctum was a danish band playing Occult Black Metal!

Formed back in 1996, in Fredericia, Denmark by drummer Galheim (Denial of God, ex-Exmortem, Livjatan, Hellsermon, Mareridt) and guitarist Impaler...

3 demos has been created in 1997, 1999 & 2000
And they were re-released on cd in 2004 by danish label Ancient Darkness Prod.

Now in 2006 the debut album has been created with the line-up:

Galheim - Drums
Impaler - Strings
Constrictor - Strings
Holy Poison - Vocals (also in Church Bizarre and Victimizer)

Watch out for the 'ARROGANCE' album in 2008!!
