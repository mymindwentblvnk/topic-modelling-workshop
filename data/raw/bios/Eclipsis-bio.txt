Eclipsis
At the moment a one-man musical project from Turku, Finland. 
The music consists of metal music features combined with melodic themes. Strong guitars and drums tracks are supported by symphonic and/or atmospheric instruments. 
Themes vary in different types of fantasy, such as fictional mythology, natural conditions, melancholy and horror. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eclipsis">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
