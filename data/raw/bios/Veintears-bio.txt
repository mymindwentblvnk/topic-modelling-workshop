Veintears is a depressive black metal band from Thessaloniki (Greece), formed in 2009 by Father Alex, Lau and Thanasis. One year later, the band made their first release with the album 'Redemption of the Soul' (Selbstmord Kommando Produktionen, limited to 100 copies on red CD-R in a DVD-case).
