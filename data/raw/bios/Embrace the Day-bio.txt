Embrace the Day  is a melodic hardcore band formed in Tilburg, The Netherlands in 2012. They are hoping to bring a new insight to today's heavy music scene, showing everyone what it means to put passion into your music. Their 5-track debut EP Wanderlust was released on May 23rd, 2014. "The music sounds hopeful but viciously and feels like an enrichment for the Dutch and international music scene" (Error Error Ezine). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Embrace+the+Day">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
