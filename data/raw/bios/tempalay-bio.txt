Tempalay is a Japanese psychedelic pop band formed in June 2014 in Tokyo, Saitama. The band consist of Ryoto Ohara on the vocals and guitar, Natsuki Fujimoto on the drums and precussions, and later, joined by Honami 'Amy' Furuhara, known professionally as Aaamyyy in 2018 as Background Vocalist and synth player. The bassist Yuya Takeuchi left the band in 2018 for personal reasons.
They made it to ROOKIE A GO-GO at FUJI ROCK FESTIVAL ’15 and made their debut from P-VINE with an EP titled “Instant Hawaii”. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/tempalay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
