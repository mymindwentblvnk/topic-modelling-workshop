There is more than one artist named Asphyxiate:

1. An Indonesian  brutal death metal band. hailing from Bekasi, Indonesia. it was started by the initial formation of Josh (guitar), Dhana (bass/vocals) and Reno (drums). 

In Current Line-up: Deaddy (Vocalist), Yoseph (Guitars), Indra (Bass), Reno (Drum).

2. A New Zealand death metal band fronted by Tim Leth of Shallow Grave and featuring Paul Kelland of Ulcerate.
