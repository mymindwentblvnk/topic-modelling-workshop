The Endoparasites were a death metal band from Rio De Janerio , Brazil in 1989. Like many early 90s band, they had prominent thrashy and doomy parts in their music. 
They released one demo to be followed by their only full-length "Iconoclasty of Flesh" to be followed by another promo tape.

The Lineup:
Adelson C. Souza - Drums
Marcelo Rodrigues - Vocals, Guitar
Daniel Lessa - Bass
