S.E.T. = Street Extreme Team is bulgarian hip hop, folk, jazz, blues, hard core, punk band. Members: Kubrat, Slavi, Yanko, Kozunaka, Panaiot, Vendo, Yavor. Their first song is Makedonska Nadenica. Please don't mistake them with E.S.T.
