As Profecies is a defunct black metal band from the state of Minas Gerais, Brazil. They recorded a demo called Prophanum Antiphona in 1995 and in the the following year they released a 7''EP called Igne Natura Renovatur Integra via Heavy Metal Rock Records.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/As+Prophecies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
