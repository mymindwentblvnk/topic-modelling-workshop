Satani Infernalis was formed in Arizona around 2000. Dantalian was the sole member with the
exception of some other temp musicians. Now Satani Infernalis is located in Los Angeles, CA .
Dantalian had recruited drummer Chutriel in 2005, and long time friend and guitarist 
Lord Plegathon to the ranks. Satani Infernalis is Infernal Infantry! They are currently working
