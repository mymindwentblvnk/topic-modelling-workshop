Mesoamerican/prehispanic Death/Doom Metal. Tzompantli is Nahuatl for “skull rack” which was used for the public display of human skulls, typically those of war captives or other sacrificial victims. Tzompantli embodies the brutality and ritualism of the Mexica in their sound and style.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tzompantli">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
