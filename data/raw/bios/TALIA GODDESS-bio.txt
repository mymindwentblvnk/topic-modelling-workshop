Hailing from East Flatbush, Brooklyn, Talia Goddess is an 18-year-old multi-hyphenate creative: singer-songwriter, rapper, producer, DJ, model, and community leader. Although young, she has earned her spot as one of the top go-to DJ’s in NYC’s youth scene. Since the early age of 6, she has been engaged in the arts, using her talents to express herself and bring people of all walks of life together.  As of recent, Talia has graduated Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School, started her record label and creative agency TRANCE, and has collaborated with several world-renown publications and brands such as Vogue, Wonderland, Refinery29, Tommy Hilfiger, MCM, i-D, Converse, AWAKE NY, and MCM.
