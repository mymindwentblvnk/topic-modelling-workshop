Etherial Winds was formed in 1991 in Nieuwleusen, Netherlands under their first name called Embitter, the band changed their name into Etherial Winds in 1992. In 1993 the band recorded their first promotape which contains three songs and they also recorded a few songs for the D.S.F.A. compilation cd.

The "Saved" mcd came out in may 1993 on Malodorous Mangled Innards Records (Germany) and the full-length "Find the Way... Together" cd was released in january 1995 by Cyber Music (Holland).

In 1996 the band split up when three members left the band to form Cimmerii.

Last known line-up:
