King Fungus was a heavy rock band from Thessaloniki, Greece

Obviously influenced by Acid Kind to whom they make  a clear reference in their name. 

The album "Reign Over The Cactus" can be downloaded at their Jamendo page http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/38865.
