Murmurs are an unsigned black metal band from Sweden with themes of folklore, nature, occultism and spiritualism. Formed in 2008, they have released 4 demos to date.

Murmurs is also a punk band from Seattle, featuring members of Snuggle! and No High Fives to Bullshit. They released their debut 8-song EP, "Fly With the Unkindness," on cassette in 2012. 2013 will see the album reissued on vinyl (as well as digitally) by Dead Broke Rekerds.

Murmurs is a correct tag, please do not redirect to The Murmurs as they are two completely separate artists.
