Gorebutcher is a death metal / grindcore band from Grobbendonk, Antwerp, Belgium.  They formed in 2007.  Gorebutcher has one EP entitled  Unleash the Butcher and it was released in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gorebutcher">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
