Jeckyll & Hyde is a jumpstyle duo from the Netherlands. The members are Maarten Vorwerk (also known as vorwerk) and Ruud van IJperen (also known as DJ Ruthless).

Jeckyll & Hyde have brought many famous songs, such as Kick This One and Precious Dreamer, but these songs never reached the big audience. They became famous when they released Frozen Flame (end 2006), which reached rank 11 in the Dutch Top 40, and Freefall (March 2007), which reached rank 1. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jeckyll+&+Hyde">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
