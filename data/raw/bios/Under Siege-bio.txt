There is more than one Under Siege.

1. Fast grinding hardcore trio from Scarborough, North Yorkshire.  Their only appearance on record was a split EP with Killercrust in 1989.

2. German Metallic hardcore band, extant 2003-08.

3. Indian progressive metal sextet.
