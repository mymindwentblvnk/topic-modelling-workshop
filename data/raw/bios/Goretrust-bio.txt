Goretrust was formed 2005 in Budapest, Hungary.The year was spent by song writing and playing gigs. In the beginning of 2006 they had a tour over South Finland. This trip was a big adventure and experience for the band. Later they released the next demo called "My Apocalypse". Year of 2007 was the time when the band recorded their debut album, which was mastered in october at TNL Studio, Hungary. The album was entitled "Last Revolution" and contains eight songs.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Goretrust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
