Grimfaug is a Black Metal band formed in Antwerpen,Belgium in 2001. The lineup consists of Norgaath, Crygh and Mork. 

In March 2002, they began work on their demo Rex Draconis, which was released in July. The demo was noticed by Pestilence Records and Painiac Records, who would later release Blood Upon The Face Of Creation cd and vinyl in 2004.

Free tracks can be downloaded at their offical website, www.grimfaug.tk.
