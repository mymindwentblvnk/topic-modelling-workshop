Sahrana is the oldest black metal band in Banja Luka, Bosnia and Herzegovina, formed in 2005. Many members and musicians pass through this band. But from the very start, till this days the main shafts of band are the drummer Jovan and the vocalist AlekSS . All the music is made by Jovan, and all the lyrics are written by AlekSS. Music and lyrics are about Death, Slavonic Paganism, War, Occult and Witchcraft  with  satanic influence.
