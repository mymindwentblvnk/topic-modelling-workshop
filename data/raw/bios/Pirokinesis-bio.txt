Colombian death metal band formed in 1987 in the city of Medellin. They released a demo called "Fuera De La Realidad" in 1988 and a Rehearsal Tape in 1989. This band finally disbanded in the early 90's without a trace. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pirokinesis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
