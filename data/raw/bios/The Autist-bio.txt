The Autist is a female fronted metal project with prog/symphonic elements from Portugal/Russia featuring Polina Psycheya as main vocalist. For fans of truly melancholic, tragic and conceptual music that was inspired by deep emotions we all go through on our lives. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/the+autist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
