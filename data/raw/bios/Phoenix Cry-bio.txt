Phoenix Cry is a small metalcore band from Germany which has started at the beginning of 2006.Band Members are Max Kuechenmeister (Guitar/Vocals), Andre Rubbert (Guitar), Christian Grieb (Vocals), Christian Schroeder (Drums) and Manu Skotarek (Bass). They just started their career but they offer a lot of strength by their own sound.Their homepage is www.phoenix-cry.de .  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phoenix+Cry">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
