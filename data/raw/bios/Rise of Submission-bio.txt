Rise Of Submission is the band Skon formed before collabarating with Toras to form Arc Fatalis. Skon released March Through Frost as a demo in 2004. He completed the EP, Descent in February 2005. Skon stopped working on Rise of Submission when he and Toras created Arc Fatalis. Rise of Submission's music can be described as ambient and melodic. Skon used instruments such as bodhrans and acoustic guitars. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rise+Of+Submission">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
