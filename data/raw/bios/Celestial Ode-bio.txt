Celestial Ode is a Power metal band from Athens,Greece,and were formed in 1996.

Current line-up:

Dionisis Koutis - guitars, bass, backing vocals
Manos Xanthakis - vocals
Themis Gourlis - guitars, backing vocals
Nikos Argiropoulos - drums
