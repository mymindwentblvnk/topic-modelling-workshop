Heavy metal-band from Osaka, Japan. Formed in 1983, Hurry Scuary featured famous guitarist Hideaki Nakama who has also played with Anthem. The band released one full-length studio album in 1988, named "Break It Up". Other bandmembers play(ed) with Jill's Project, Sleazy Wizard and Hell N Back. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hurry+Scuary">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
