Chronicus is a brutal death metal band from Chile. The band formed in 2006 by Luis "Chicho" Escobar (Vocals, Caos), Claudio Gray (Guitar, Caos),  Felipe Soto (Guitars, Caos and Trimegisto) and Jonathan Matamala (Drums, Caos)).

The band released their debut EP "Revenge, Natural Reaction  in late 2006. Soto left the band soon afterwards. The band are currently touring around Chile and other South American countries.
