NOBRO is a punk rock band from Montreal, Canada. The band members are Kathryn McCaughey (vocals, bass), Sarah Dion (drums), Karolane Carbonneau (guitar) and Lisandre Bourdages (percussion).  Dion and Bourdages also play in Les Shirley. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/NOBRO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
