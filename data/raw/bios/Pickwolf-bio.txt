Pickwolf was formed in Malang, East Java, Indonesia in mid of 2007. They choose the path of rock music, which is more toward the southern rock and stoner metal. Each personnels came from different band with different genres. Pickwolf's music heavily influenced by bands like Every Time I Die, Maylene and The Sons of Disaster, Cancer Bats, Fight Paris, etc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pickwolf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
