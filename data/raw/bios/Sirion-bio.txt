There are multiple artists with the name Sirion.

1. Sirion was founded in 1999 as a solo project. In 2000 Dark Forest Promotion released first instrumental album "Quenta Silmarillion". Next album "The Long Journey...Through the Lands and Forests" was released by the same label in 2001. It contains harsh black metal vocals. A final album was called "Lorien" and was self released in 2004.

2. Sirion is a melodic death/power metal band from Los Angeles, California. They put out their first EP, EP 2014, in February 2014.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sirion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
