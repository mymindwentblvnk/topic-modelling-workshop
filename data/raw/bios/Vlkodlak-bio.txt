Vlkodlak is black/folk metal band from Neuchatel, Switzerland. They were formed in 2000. Word "Vlkodlak" means werewolf in czech language. They have released one demo Temná cesta and one full length album Prokletý kámen (names of the both releases are in czech language and mean "Dark journey" and "Damned stone").  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vlkodlak">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
