1) DJ Dizzy (real name Mark Joergensen) is a danish DJ, most known and recognized for his 2007-hit "Miracle", which he wrote and recorded with his swedish partner, DJ Monty. DJ Dizzy is also a well-known face in the danish gaming-culture, where he is widely acknowledged for especially his skills in the so-called MMORPG's. His motto and all-round saying is "It's pure muscle!".
