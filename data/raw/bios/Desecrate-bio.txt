According to Metal Archives, there are eight different bands named Desecrate.  They hail from Colombia, Czech Republic, Spain, France, Germany, Holland, Turkey and the US.  With the exception of the German and American Desecrate, they all play some variant of death metal (be it straight death metal, melodic death, death/doom or brutal death metal).  The German Desecrate plays black metal, and the US Desecrate plays heavy metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desecrate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
