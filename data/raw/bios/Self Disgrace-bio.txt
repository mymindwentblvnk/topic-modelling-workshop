Self Disgrace is an Italian Thrash/Death Metal project that was founded in 2007 by 80's guitarist Isa Brutal (ex Ice Age, Rock Goddess, Original Sin). Though the band has had a few lineup changes, they are back better than ever with their album Partners In Crime - now available on their bandcamp!
