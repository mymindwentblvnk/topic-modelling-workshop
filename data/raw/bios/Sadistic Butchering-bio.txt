Sadistic Butchering is a one man brutal death metal band from Texas, USA. John created the band in 2005 when he decided to get some material down with him playing Guitars, Bass & Vocals with  Drum Programming, helping him along. 

He has released a split with Infected Guts, Tamakeri and Soaking in Entrails in the highly anticipated "Slam season - vol 1" by Coyote Records.
