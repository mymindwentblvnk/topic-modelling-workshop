1. Lethal Dosage is a four-man band from the Philippines, consisting of R2 van Tooren on vocals and rhythm guitar, Mig Isada on lead guitar, Lendell Pag-Ong on base and Paolo Bragais on drums. They were formed sometime between 2006 and 2007, and held their first official gig in May 2007. Mostly a cover band, they've done various songs by Nirvana, the Foo Fighters, AC/DC, Tenacious D, Led Zeppelin, and other such bands, with Nirvana and the Foo Fighters being their personal favorites.
