The project founded by Lord Demogorgon (Black Shadow) in winter of 2006. First album, called "Blackest Empire Of Satan", released at 06.06.06 by S.B.M. Records. Second album, called "Satanic March Of Death", released at 06.06.07 by S.B.M. Records. 

The name of this project symbolizing six names of chaos. After recording of six albums (each of them will contain six tracks) H.E.W.D.A.T. will finish it's existense.
