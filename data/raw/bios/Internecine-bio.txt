Internecine is a Jared Anderson creation, who is known for his work in Morbid Angel and Hate Eternal. Having written all of the songs, you'll also hear Jared play all Guitar, Bass and Vocals. While on Drums we have Tony Laureano (Angelcorpse and Nile) and Derek Roddy (Hate Eternal) on two tracks. It also has some guest solos from Erik Rutan (Morbid Angel, Hate Eternal, Alas), who also produced this album.
