Matt McGhee is an 18 year old recording artist, producer, and engineer from the Washington, DC metropolitan area. He has been making music since he was 12 and released his first full project "'93 Til Infinity..." in 2010. After gaining a small buzz in the area, McGhee joined the local label DeLorean Music Group with Nike Nando at the head. McGhee released another mixtape entitled "Dress Rehearsal" which was the prequel to his first album "The Graduation." "The Graduation" was released online for free in August 2011. Recently, he has released a mixtape called GIRLS, joined forces with rapper/producer rMell to create the Grammy Family, and plans on releasing an album called AWARD very soon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Matt+McGhee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
