Pearls are a three-piece band from Melbourne, Australia. They have been together since the start of 2011, releasing the split 7" single At Home With You in May on Glaswegian label RE:PEATER Records.

Since 2011 Pearls have had the pleasure of supporting international acts such as Sons & Daughters (UK) and Marnie Stern (Kill Rock Stars – USA), in addition to performances for Melbourne Music Week, The Thousands and Melbourne Fringe Festival.

Pearls have also shared the stage with great local acts Lost Animal, Total Control, New War, The Ancients, Peak Twins, Super Wild Horses, ZOND, Fabulous Diamonds, Brous and Teeth & Tongue.

Recently, Pearls were hand picked by Warner Australia to contribute a recording of The Standells classic Dirty Water’ for a special album commemorating the 40th anniversary of classic garage/psych comp Nuggets. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pearls">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
