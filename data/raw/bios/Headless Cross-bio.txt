Young Bay Area style Thrash Metal band hailing from the Birmingham, United Kingdom. Although unsigned, they have gained a many fans and lots of respect throughout the UK metal scene and have even played at the country's biggest metal festival, Bloodstock Open Air 2006.
They were set to play at Ragnarok 2006 in Scotland before getting taken off the list for various reasons, the festival was later cancelled.

Dave Silver was the main man for Headless Cross, and he went through many lineup changes before eventually putting an end to the band in early 2007
