Dimension Shifter is a European rock band from Karlsruhe, Germany, formed in 2014. The band’s line-up includes Tobias Lichtenstern (vocals), Dennis Haitz (guitar), Alex Koska (bass guitar) and Tim Timson (drums).

Formed after the dissolution of their previous band, Look Behind The Scenes, Dimension Shifter plays a distinctive style of fuzz-loaded heavy rock music.

They incorporate a variety of different influences such as Clutch, Fu Manchu & The Atomic Bitchwax and released their first four-track EP “High Octane” in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dimension+Shifter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
