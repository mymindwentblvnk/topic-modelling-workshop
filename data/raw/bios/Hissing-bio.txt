Hissing are a black metal/death metal band which formed in Seattle, Washington in 2014. To date, the band have released one demo, two EPs, and a split EP with Sutekh Hexen. Their debut full-length studio album "Permanent Destitution" will be released on October 26, 2018 through Profound Lore Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hissing">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
