Calloused here refers to at least four artists.




1) Calloused was a crust punk band from Minneapolis, MN.  The band released a CD split with Shitlist, an LP called 'The Masquerade,' and had some tracks released on compilations before splitting up.





2) Calloused are a new hardcore band from the Netherlands, focussing on midtempo rhythms and melodic riffs. For fans of Inside Out, Outspoken and Burn.





3) Calloused also refers to a hardcore band from Boone, North Carolina. They write sad songs and drink a lot.



4) From Cincinnati, OH, Calloused just released their debut album "The Distance Between Us" on April 4th, 2009. Their website is myspace.com/callousedrock. Their songs "No Wonder", "I Am", and "Cut Me Down" have garnered radio airplay and are crowd favorites.
