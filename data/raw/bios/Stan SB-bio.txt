Stan SB is now known as Fox Stevenson.

Stan SB (Stanley Stevenson Byrne) is a electronic music artist from London, UK. He was most widely known for his Drum and Bass and Dubstep tracks. Stan also performed live band sets with fellow artist Buster Charles (aka Bustre).

Stan SB made, according to his soundcloud page, "hyper melodic dnb, dubstep and breaky stuff". Most of his vocals were performed by himself, making each of his track a unique masterpiece. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stan+SB">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
