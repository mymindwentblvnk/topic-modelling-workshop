Black Thai is a metal band from Boston, MA USA formed in 2009. The band consists of Jim Healey  (We're All Gonna Die) - vocals and guitar, Scotty Fuse (x Fast Actin' Fuses, Cortez) - guitar, Cory Cocomazzi - bass and Jeremy Hemond - drums.
