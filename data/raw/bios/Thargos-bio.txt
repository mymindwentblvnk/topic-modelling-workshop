Thargos is a German Black Metal/Thrash Metal band from Langerringen.
They released 2 full-length albums so far: Killfukk and Salem City and a 7" called Black Metal Punkz. Several rehearsals and demos has been done in the past.

THARGOS is on hold currently, since vocalist Front left the band. Distortion (Bass) followed soon. Cy Ghul (see also: Cultes Des Goules) is the only lasting member. Currently he thinks about reinventing THARGOS.
