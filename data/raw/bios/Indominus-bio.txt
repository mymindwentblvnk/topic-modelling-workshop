Melodic Death Metal from Northern Ireland

Indominus was formed in mid 2016. The band has since release its debut EP “Legion Within” as well as appearing at multiple international festivals in locations such as Holland, Malta and Switzerland. Their style of music is Melodic Death Metal, however there are elements of various other genres such as prog, black and thrash metal scattered throughout the songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Indominus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
