Wirethrone is a melodic death metal band from Tampa, Florida.  They formed in 2011.  The band consists of Josh Delgado on vocals, James Harper & Adam Mehdaova on guitar, Chris Horan on bass and James Smith on drums.  They released their first album The Sun Betrays Us To The Night in 2013.  On March 16th, 2016, they released a single entitled A Rebirth. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wirethrone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
