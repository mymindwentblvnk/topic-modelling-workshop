Amazingly raw, rough, essential punk (and slightly post-punk) rock from Louisville, Kentucky.  Psychic godfathers to the Crain/Rodan 90's L-ville explosion.  The group's recordings were reissued by Drag City on April 20, 2010 on CD, vinyl, and are also available in several download formats,  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Endtables">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
