Greed Killing is a Death Metal/Grindcore band from North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. They formed in 2007 by Sascha Lubenow (Vocals / Guitars, Soil of Decay, Invoid), Evangelos Gizis (Bass/Vocals, To Die) and Sven Kleis (Drums, To Die). The band sound like Cannibal Corpse, Tard and any old school death metal or old school grindcore band.

The band self-released their debut EP "Police State " in 2007 playing catchy grindcore and old school death metal, with catchy beats, growling vocals, backing screaming vocals and choppy riffs. The band officially made it possible to download their EP in september 2008.

The band are currently looking for a new Bassist.
