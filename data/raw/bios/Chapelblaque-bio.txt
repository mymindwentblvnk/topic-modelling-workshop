Two guys from Baltimore named Tony (aka Cancer the Crucifuct aka Psyphre Chirst) and Tom (aka Tahmn) formed a collective called Chapelblaque, proclaiming it as the "Church Of The Sexually Insane", whilst creating strange harsh dark wave with atmospheric keyboards, metal-ic guitars and a drum computer. They released only one album so far: Hymnals of the Lastdays <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chapelblaque">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
