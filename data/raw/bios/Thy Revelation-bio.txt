Thy Revelation was a melodic death metal band from Staten Island, New York, formed in 2005. The band released a 4-track demo in 2006 entitled The Fallen Immortal. The band's lineup included:

Eric Lau-guitar 
Bret Peters-drums 
Rich Simeone-Vocals
