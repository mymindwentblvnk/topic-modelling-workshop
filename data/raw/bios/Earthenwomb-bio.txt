Earthenwomb is an experimental black metal/noise/drone band begun in 2006 by Kuranes. The band has seen releases on a variety of labels, such as Altar of Waste, Depressive Illusions, Parkbench, Glossolalia, and Cvlminus.

Kuranes is a founding member of the bands Arkhum, Gromkult, Alkmorhilyion, Willowbrook, Skeld and Darkosis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Earthenwomb">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
