This page refers to four bands:

Drunk (US): An American acoustic/indie band (http://www.jagjaguwar.com/artist.php?name=drunk). Members of this band went on to form Spokane (http://www.jagjaguwar.com/spokane/) on the same label with a similar sound.

Drunk (NO): A Norwegian punk band (http://www.drunk.no/). Drunk consisted of members from Life... But How To Live It?, Bannlyst, Captain Not Responsible, So Much Hate, Manna and Barn av regnbuen.

Drunk (BE): A Belgian crossover Thrash metal band born in the pubs of Antwerp.  (http://www.myspace.com/stompingdrunk)
