Zombieslut is a Death Metal Band from Kraichgau, Germany. The band was founded in late summer of 2008. Their lyrics (as their name already says) consist of Zombie, Brains, Ressurrection and the Living Dead. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zombieslut">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
