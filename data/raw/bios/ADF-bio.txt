A Hiphop group from New York. Formed in 1995, ADF stands for All Different Flavas. They are known for working with Todd Bangz and for their high devotion to street ministry.

The group disbanded in 2006 and several of the members have released solo projects. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/ADF">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
