Mortiferik is a one-man-band of funeral doom metal from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil founded in 1998 by Anderson Morphis.

Discography:
1998 - Memory (demo)
2000 - The Silent Voice (demo)
2012 - Agony in the Silence (demo)
2013 - Empire of Sadness Returns (EP)
