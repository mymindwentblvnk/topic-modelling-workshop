Capricorns is an 'instru-prog-metal' band on Rise Above Records. Their debut full length album is called 'Ruder Forms Survive'. The members of this band also play in Orange Goblin, Iron Monkey or Bridge & Tunnel. They released their second album, River , Bear Your Bones in August 2008. Their sound mixes elements of post-rock, post-metal, stoner metal.
