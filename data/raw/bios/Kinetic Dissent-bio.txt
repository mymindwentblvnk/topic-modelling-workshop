Kinetic Dissent had it's origin in Atlanta (GA) and was a long-lost progressive thrash band, along the lines of a Forte or a Inner Strength. Only one album was released, as the band broke up while working on a followup.

Members:
Dwight Bales - vocals  
Stephen Danyo - guitars  
Rick MacConnell - guitars  
Troy Stepens - bass
