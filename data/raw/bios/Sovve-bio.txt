Sovve is a young girl, hailing from Hudiksvall, Sweden, born in 1990. You recognize her music by her unique progressive sound, with a sweet touch of hypnotizing melodies. Her latest track "Rising" is already widely known and supported, and her future in the world of trance music looks even better than before. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sovve">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
