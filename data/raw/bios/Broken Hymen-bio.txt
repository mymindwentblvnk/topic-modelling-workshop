Broken Hymen was a four piece death metal band from Kathmandu, Nepal formed in the early 2012. Seeking the influences from Slayer, Cannibal Corpse, Opeth, Binaash, Ugra Karma, Behemoth and many other bands, they stood up as one of the most promising death metal band in the Nepalese metal scene although they tagged themselves as a Blackened Death metal band . The band participated in Nepfest Band Competition and were ranked 6th among 26 participating bands.
The band was doing well in small local shows, before their guitarist was struck by a disastrous accident, paralyzing the lower half of his body. This led them to a hiatus that lasted several months, amid their studio work and exact prior to the release of there EP  Shrouded By Death . The band announced a split-up after the release of the EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Broken+Hymen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
