Squadda B AKA Squadda Bambino is a member of the Oakland native Green Ova collective and 1/2 of Main Attrakionz, AKA "The Best Duo Ever". Squadda is mostly known for his drugged-out, spacey production and vocals. He has garnered a lot of internet popularity as of recent with the release of his mixtapes "I Smoke Because I Don't Care About Death" and "The Shady Bambino Project" with Shady Blaze, both of which dropped within weeks of each other.
