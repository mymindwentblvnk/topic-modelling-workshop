Kelsey Byrne (born in 1990), also known by her stage name VÉRITÉ, is an American pop singer-songwriter based in Brooklyn, New York. Her first single "Strange Enough" was self-released in July 2014, reaching No. 1 on Hype Machine and becoming the #1 Most Viral Twitter Artist the week of release. Described variously as indie pop or alt pop, Liza Darwin of Noisey wrote that her 2014 Echo EP is "packed with crisp, delicate vocals, soaring melodies, and glistening production."  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/V%C3%A9rit%C3%A9">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
