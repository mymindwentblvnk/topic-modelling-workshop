The Breathing Effect is the music of Harry Terrell and Eli Goss, two lifelong friends and partners in music for over 10 years. Whatever melodies and rhythms exist in their minds ends up on their records. There are many influences and familiar sounds, including jazz fusion, experimental rock, funk and soul.
