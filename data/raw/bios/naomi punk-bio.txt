Naomi Punk are a minimal punk trio from Olympia Washington USA. Notorious for loud and intense live performance. Hardcore Punk and Avantgarde concepts inform oblique Pop music songwriting, evoking a spirit and ethos transcendent of bland revivalism or traditional rock tropes. 12 inch LP "The Feeling"  was released on Couple Skate Records on April 24th 2012. It quickly sold out and was repressed by Brooklyn's Captured Tracks in November 2012. Seven inch EP was released by Canadian label Cruising USA in November 2012.
