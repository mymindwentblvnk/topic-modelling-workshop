Satan's Blade are a new heavy metal band from San Jose, California whose style borrows from classic bands like Iron Maiden and Mercyful Fate. They are Steffan Salas (Bass), Mario Diaz (Drums), and Andrew Webb (Guitars). They have a single instrumental demo released which has four tracks. They are currently without a vocalist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satan%27s+Blade">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
