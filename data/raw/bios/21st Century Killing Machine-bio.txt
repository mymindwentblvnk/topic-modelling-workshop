21st Century Killing Machine is a thrash metal band formed 2005 in Germany. The band was first named Bloodpit, but as another band already owned that name, they changed it to 21st Century Killing Machine.
In 2006 they released their first demo Sarcastic Rhymes.


Discography:
Sarcastic Rhymes (2006, demo)


Current Line-Up:
Rudi Görg - vocals
René Hauffe - guitars(Ger))
Kevin Waldick - guitars
Jörg Knoche - bass
Magnus 'Maggi' Stoermer - drums


Former Members:
Jason Kubke - drums
