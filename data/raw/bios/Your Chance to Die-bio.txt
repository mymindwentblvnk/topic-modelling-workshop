Your Chance To Die are a Melodic Death Metal Band from Columbia, South Carolina, USA. They formed in 2007 and released a self titled demo in 2008 followed by the Verdict Of The Gods EP in 2009.  In 2010 YCTD went into the studio with renowned producer Jamie King (Through the Eyes of the Dead, Between the Buried and Me, The Human Abstract) and recorded their second full length entitled 'Suscitacio Somnus' which has yet to be released.  The first single from the album, 'Slaughter of the Innocents' is available on all digital music sites for purchase.
