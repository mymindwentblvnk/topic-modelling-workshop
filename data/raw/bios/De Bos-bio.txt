The aim to introduce a new era that upholds the fundamental humane values of honesty and integrity is the new introduction of free voice where words fail, music enable us to speak, so said by "De Boss".
 
More popular by his stage name De Boss, Chinedu Moses Chukwunta is an Independent American rapper, singer, songwriter and producer. His unique music style is an eclectic versatile blend of R&B and Hip Hop, accentuated by Reggae hints. He is one of those few people who haven’t forgotten their roots and always representing with the versatility in all kind of music.
 
After his first album "The Scrutiny" which features top rappers from prominent record labels like G-Unit , HighGrade Family & Rugged Records the independent artist has grown largely in a very short period of time <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/De+Bos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
