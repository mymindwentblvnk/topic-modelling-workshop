Men Of Vizion is a male vocal quintet who made their debut in 1996. They have released two albums thus far "Personal" and "MOV." Their hit songs include "Do Thangz", "House Keeper", "Do You Feel Me? (Freak You)", "Break Me Off." Despite the fact there were some lineup changes, Men Of Vizion are still active.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Men+of+Vizion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
