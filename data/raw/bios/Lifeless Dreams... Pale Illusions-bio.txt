Lifeless Dreams... Pale Illusions is an Atmospheric Black Metal project based out of Deland Florida. A project of M. (Vox) (Starless Night, Sleep Deprived) Abyssous (Guitar) (Cryptic Throne, Whispers of the Black Wulf) Kniwa (Drums & Keyboards) (Concealed Sequence, Cryptic Throne) and Mike Poggione (Bass) (Monstrosity, Capharnaum, Eulogy, Vile (live) ,Serocs)
