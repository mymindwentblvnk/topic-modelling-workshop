Incitatus was a Norwegian band playing a mixture of Nordic black metal and doom. Founded in 1994 in Mørkved / Bodø, Norway they released one self-titled demo in the same year and changed their name to Svartsyn in 1995.

The line-up was:
Ravn - vocals
Jonas - guitar, bass, drums, keyboards
