Psychotomy are a death metal band that formed in Venice, Italy in 2010. To date the band have released two EPs and two full-length albums. Their most recent album "Aphotik" was released on September 28, 2018 through Everlasting Spew Records, and saw the band move away from their more thrash metal roots towards a heavier, more atmospheric death doom metal sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychotomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
