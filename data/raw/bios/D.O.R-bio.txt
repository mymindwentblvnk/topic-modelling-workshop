D.O.R. Hail from Lisbon, Portugal and play black metal. Lyrical themes include, Annihilation, Pain, Satan. D.O.R. stands for "Death Overcomes Reality", DOR also means PAIN in Portuguese.Mortiferus currently preforms all the material alone.There are several albums and splits with other artists as well. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/D.O.R">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
