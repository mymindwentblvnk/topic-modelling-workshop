Cold Cell is a Swiss black metal band founded in 2012. Their debut album "Generation Abomination" released in Autumn 2013 through Gravity Entertainment. Second album "Lowlife" recorded, mixed and mastered in 2014 and it will be released on May 18th, 2015. Their drummer, known as "aW" is the drummer of Swiss Avant-garde/Black metal band Schammasch too.
They were previously active as "Atritas" which was a symphonic black metal band.
