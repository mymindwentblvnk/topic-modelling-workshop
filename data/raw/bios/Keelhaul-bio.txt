Keelhaul is a four-piece math/sludge/instrumental/metal band from Cleveland, Ohio. The members are Aaron Dallison (bass & vocals), Dana Embrose (guitar), Will Scharf (drums), and Chris Smith (guitar and vocals) They have variously described their music as "annoying" and "like a post-hardcore progressive acid rock". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Keelhaul">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
