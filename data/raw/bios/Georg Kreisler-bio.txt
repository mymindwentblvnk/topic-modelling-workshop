Georg Kreisler (1922 - 2011) was an Austrian (jewish) singer-songwriter, cabaret artist, satirist and author, basically known for his black humored and cynical songs - especially for the 'everblack' "Tauben vergiften". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Georg+Kreisler">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
