Wizardzz is a two piece electropop/noise ensemble from Rhode Island featuring Brian Gibson (of Lightning Bolt) on drums and Rich Porter (of Bug Sized Mind) on synthesizer taking inspiration from ‘60s krautrock, ‘70s prog-rock, free jazz and post-rock’s headiest provocateurs.
Their debut album Hidden City of Taurmond was released on March 21, 2006 under the independent label Load. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wizardzz">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
