Bloody Six was a Heavy Metal band from Switzerland (Wetzikon, Zurich). The band was formed in 1983 under the name Witchcraft, but changed their name to Bloody Six in 1984. The band released a single in 1983 under the label Musk. 

After the band changed their name to Bloody Six, they released their debut In The Name Of Blood in 1984 under the record label Camel Records. Some time after they released the album, the band split up.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloody+Six">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
