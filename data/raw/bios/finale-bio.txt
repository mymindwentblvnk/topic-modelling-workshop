There are more than 4 artists that go by Finale.

1. Finale is a Detroit, Michigan-based MC, known best for his collaborations with the late J Dilla, Invincible, Dabrye, DJ House Shoes and others.  In 2007 he dropped Develop, a collaboration with Spier1200 on the Rawkus 50 album, and in the next year you can expect a solo album, as well a mixtape and an album with DJ House Shoes.  http://www.myspace.com/finale 

2. Finale is a Buffalo, New York-based side project made up of Keith Buckley from Every Time I Die on vocals and Steven Riter on guitar(s). http://www.myspace.com/thefinalemusic

3. Finale, AKA Martin Grooms was born in Charleston, SC. Not only is Finale a rapper himself, but also an aspiring producer. He is currently promoting the release of his own album entitled "Ashes to Ashes". Finale is the second half of, as well as vice president of Street Remedy Entertainment alongside his partner in rhyme, Carlos "10 Grand" Whiteside.
