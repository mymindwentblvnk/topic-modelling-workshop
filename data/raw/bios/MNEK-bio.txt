Uzoechi Osisioma Emenike professionally known as MNEK is a British singer, songwriter, remixer and producer of Nigerian descent.

His recent writing and production credits include Duke Dumont, Rudimental, Gorgon City, DEVolution, Misha B, Little Mix, A*M*E and remixes for Duran Duran, Olly Murs, Tinie Tempah, Mutya Keisha Siobhan and more.
