Weevil is an indietronic band whose height was in 2004. Weevil used to consist of three members. In 2004, Weevil only included Jonny Pilcher and Tom Betts. Currently, Weevil is no longer producing songs. Jonny Pilcher recently worked on a new band named "Days That Follow", not including Tom Betts <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Weevil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
