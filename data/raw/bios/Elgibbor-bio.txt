Elgibbor is a solo project done by a member of the former band Boanerges. He is known as Fire. Elgibbor was started in 1999-2000. This project is the result of personal interests, metal music, as well as biblical inspiration and personal experiences.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elgibbor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
