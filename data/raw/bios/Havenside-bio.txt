With a vision of creating a musical outfit, Havenside founder and vocalist Brandon Wells began the process of recruiting a group of musicians who shared the same desire of performing in a gritty hardcore metal band. In 2006, Havenside was born and began cutting its teeth with a full lineup intact. Seven years later the band remains intact with a few lineup changes over the past few years. With three records already under its belt, the group plans to put out a forth album, "T.B.D"  on prominent New York label "Innerstrength Records" -2013 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Havenside">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
