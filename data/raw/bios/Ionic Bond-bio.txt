Ionic Bond is a neoclassical and modern sound influenced  Melodic Death Metal band from Bangladesh. 

Ionic Bond is inspired mainly from Scandinavian and Nordic Melodic Death Metal scene with great bands like Children Of Bodom, In Flames, Kalmah, Norther and many more. Ionic Bond also follows legends like Megadeth and the mighty Iron Maiden. 
Ionic Bond's sound specializes in blending modern sounds with basic melodic death metal and creating a revolutionary genre. There is slight folk metal influence from bands like Eluveitie.
