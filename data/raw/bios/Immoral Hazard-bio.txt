Immoral Hazard is a metal band from Thessaloniki, Greece. The band was formed in late 2009. They have created their own sound, influenced by not only heavy metal, but by other music genres as well.
The band has released a demo entitled “A Minor Innovation” in 2010. They just finished recording their full-length album, entitled “Convulsion” and plan on playing more live shows.    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Immoral+Hazard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
