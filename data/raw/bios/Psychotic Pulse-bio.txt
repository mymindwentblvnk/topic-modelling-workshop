Toronto based Psychotic Pulse has evolved it's explosive metal sound since it's foundations in 2009. Formed by Wes MacDonald the group enjoyed great regional success and faced much adversity as members came and went. Coming together in full with the addition of Alex Rise, Treavor Blakney, and fronted by Nathan Bales, Psychotic Pulse stands stronger than ever, readying their newest set of lethal songs to be unleashed on crowds everywhere. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychotic+Pulse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
