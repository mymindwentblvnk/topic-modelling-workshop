“Soundgeist” is a prog-psy metal band from Heraklion, (Crete - Greece). Originally known as "Devil's Interval" 
The band started as a trio in May 2004 (with Manolis, Andreas and Panagiotis) and after some twists of the second melodist chair, Stelios came to stay. Then Dimitris joined the band. 
As a quintet, the band wrote some lyrics and gave a few shows in 2005 and 2006. 
in september of 2007, the band has finished its first Demo entitled "the fine line between..."
which was finally released as a full length Album by MP records (based in Italy).
the band is currently promoting its first album and creating new music...
