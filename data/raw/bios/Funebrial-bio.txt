Funebrial is a black metal band from California, USA. It was formed by Bleak, as a side project of Bleak, to explore different sounds and inspirations different to those of his main band. 

He released a five-song demo entitled "Dead Autumn Wind" in 2005 via Lifeless. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Funebrial">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
