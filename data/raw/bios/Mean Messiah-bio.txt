Mean Messiah is a progressive industrial metal band from Czech Republic. New album Divine Technology was released on May 5th, 2020 via Slovak Metal Army.

Mean Messiah was formed by the multi-instrumentalist and producer Dan Friml, formerly of legendary bands Sebastian and Apostasy and various projects across genres. Dan is also acclaimed music producer, sound and mixing engineer and studio musician. Other members are Jiří Willander (drums) and David Gabriel (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mean+Messiah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
