ORINOKO was a dance-act created by timo maas, Andy Bolleshon & Martin Buttrich in 1998. They released a couple of singles between 1997-2001. Their best and most successful tune was "Mama Konda", followed by "Vila Nova" and "Island". The project split up in 2001. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Orinoko">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
