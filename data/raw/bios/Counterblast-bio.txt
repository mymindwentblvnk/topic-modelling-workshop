Swedish scene is well known for the Discharge-d d-beat hardcore and fast crust style and the many great bands playing these genres. Counterblast come from Sweden but bring something different: Mixing many genres (crust, industrial, thrash metal, death metal) and using keyboards and samples, they sound like a mix of Amebix, Neurosis and Dystopia but in their own unique style. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Counterblast">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
