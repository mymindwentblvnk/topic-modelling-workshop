Diminished is a brual death metal / grindcore band from Houston, Texas.  They formed in the year 2005.  Their current lineup consists Michael Pinkard (vocals),  Daniel Balle (guitar, vocals),  Brandon Davis (guitars), Shun (bass) and  Rene Martinez (drums).  They are currently signed to Sevared Records.  Their lyrical themes include gore, death, sex, mutilation, torture and society.
