Linfinity are a Brooklyn-based band assembled by vocalist Dylan Von Wagner. Other members include Russ Lemkin, Josh Collins, Grant Zubritsky, Megan Berson, and Omer Shemesh. Their debut full-length album, Martian's Bloom, will be released by American Myth Recordings on 27 April 2010.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Linfinity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
