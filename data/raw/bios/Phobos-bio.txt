There are multiple artists called Phobos.

1) Phobos is an Eastern-influenced new age/ambient project of composer Mark Adams Allison who has also created music under the names The Spirit Level (Neurodisc Records) and Mystiera (Intentcity Records). Mark began writing under the Phobos name in 1999.

2) Phobos is an ambient/dark ambient project of UK musician David Thompson. 

3) Phobos was a black metal band from France.

4) Phobos is an industrial hardcore/darkcore producer from DNA Tracks.

5) Phobos is a DJ and label manager for the label Looney Moon Records.
