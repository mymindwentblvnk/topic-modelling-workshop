Formed in late December 2005/early 2006; Machiavellian play extreme metal with inspirations from the likes of Misery Index, Kreator, Cannibal Corpse and Crowbar. Since their inception the band have played shows with the likes of Job For A Cowboy (Metal Blade), Dry Kill Logic (Reposession Records), Misery Index (Relapse) and SiKth (Bieler Brothers). Machiavellian debut EP 'The Impossibility of Death in the Minds of the Living' is available now in the US & Canada via Year of the Sun Records, and drops November 30th in the UK via Glasstone Records! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Machiavellian">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
