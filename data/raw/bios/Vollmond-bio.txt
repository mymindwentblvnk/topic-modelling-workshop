Vollmond is German for "Full Moon."

Vollmond creates music in the Depressive Black Metal style. Formed in 2005, the name only has one release to date, that being "The Crypt under the Universe" from 2010.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vollmond">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
