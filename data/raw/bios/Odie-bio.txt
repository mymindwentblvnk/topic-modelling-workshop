Odie is the name of more than one artist: 1) Canadian RnB artist 2) 2000's American punk band

1) Odie is a Canadian Nigerian RnB artist, raised in the West Coast. His debut album Analogue released in early 2018, following buzzing Soundcloud efforts. 

2) Odie was a teen punk band in Tucson, Arizona.  Shortly after cutting the CD 'Another Day, Another Banana' in 2002, which was privately distributed, the band then disbanded.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Odie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
