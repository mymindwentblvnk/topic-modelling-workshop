Little Cub was a British dark synth-pop from south London comprising Tootill (synths/keyboards), Dominic Gore (lead vocals), and Adrian Acolatse (synths/keyboards). Dominic Gore subsquently records as DC Gore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Little+Cub">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
