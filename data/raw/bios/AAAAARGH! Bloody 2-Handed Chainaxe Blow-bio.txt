AAAAARGH ! Bloody 2-Handed Chainaxe Blow was formed during summer '99, composed of the following members: Bekal ( vocals, guitars ) and Thaumas Not ( guitars ). Bekal has since departed. He was replaced for the recording of The Musical Body by Fredericksson Vincent Nietzsche ( vocals & bass ).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/AAAAARGH%21+bloody+2-handed+chainaxe+blow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
