Formed by Tony Moore and Mike Flyntz, former and current members of Metal legend 'RIOT', Faith And Fire also includes Danny Miranda from the Queen / Paul Rodgers band on bass and John Miceli from Rainbow, Meat Loaf & others on drums. Tony, Mike and Danny are co-writers on all the tunes.
