Terra Morta is a progressive metal band from the Pacific Northwest. They  take influence from many extreme metal genres, while still creating melodic and atmospheric music . They are currently working on their first full-length album, to be released in 2012. 

Members:
Margaret Pryor - Drums
Chris Jones - Guitars
Shaun Hansen - Bass
Joel Nelson - Guitars/Vocals
