Necropsya is a Thrash metal band from Lima(Peru). The band was formed in 1989. And remains still active by releasing their latest album "Devastated By Time".
These days the line-up looks like this : Gustavo Bermúdez - Vocals, Lead & Rhythm Guitars, César Morán - Bass, Backing Vocals, Luis Sanchez- Lead & Rhythm Guitars, Walter Costa - Guitar ,Jaime García - Drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/NECROPSYA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
