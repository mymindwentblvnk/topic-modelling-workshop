Mutilanova is a symphonic/progressive black metal band formed in Grenoble, Rhône-Alpes, France in 2002. The current lineup consists of M.Svärhandis (keyboard), C.Avskrius - (lead guitar), S. Mørkhød - (drums), Dypentord - (bass) and Karnesis - (vocals). Past/former members were Bjellothorn - (bass, Yldumigus (guitars), Voldr - (guitars) and  Sylvain - (rhythm guitar).

They've released one demo demo in 2005 and a full length album Fragments in 2008.
