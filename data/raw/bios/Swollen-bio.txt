There's more than one band named Swollen.

1. Death metal band: Mads Haarlov, Jacob Olsen, and Thomas Fagerlind from Denmark 

"The Breathless Waiting" was recorded in 1996. Mads went on to join Iniquity and shortly thereafter released "Five Across The Eyes". Thomas joined later for "Grime".

2. 70's - 90's Indie covers band, operating in the Nottingham area.

3. Swedish industrial, collaboration between Deutsch Nepal and Trepaneringsritualen.
