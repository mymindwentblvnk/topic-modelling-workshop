There is more than one Knightmare on last.fm:

1. Knightmare is a Power/Thrash Metal band from Melbourne, Australia. Knightmare was formed in 2005 by Mick Simpson and Luke Besley.

2. Knightmare was a punky HM trio from Manchester, New Hampshire, featuring bassist/vocalist Al Mead of Tearabyte.  They released the 'Mindless Mayhem' & 'Evil Dreams' albums along with several demos during the '80s.  They disbanded at the beginning of the '90s, reforming for a one-off gig on June 16th 2001.

3. Knightmare is a Turkish HM band, responsible for one eponymous album in 2006.
