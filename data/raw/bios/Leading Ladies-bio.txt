"Leading Ladies" is a trio of three current stars of Londons West End. They are (in no particular order): Beverly Knight, Amber Riley and Cassidy Janson. Their first CD "Songs from the stage" was released on November 17, 2017. 
Beverley Knight MBE (born March 22, 1972 as Beverley Ann Smith), is a critically acclaimed British soul singer, songwriter and record producer who released her debut album in 1995. Since then she's gone on to star on stage and screen.
Amber Patrice Riley (born February 15, 1986) is an American actress and singer best known for her role as Mercedes Jones in the hit Fox TV show "Glee". Since then she enjoyed success on the stage, particularly in the London production of "Dreamgirls".
Cassidy Janson (born July 30, 1980) is a British actress and acclaimed West End Star. Among her credits are stints in "Wicked", "Beauty and the Beast", "Footloose" and "Beautiful - The Carol King Musical".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leading+Ladies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
