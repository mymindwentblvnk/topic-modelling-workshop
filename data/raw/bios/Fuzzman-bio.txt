Herwig Zamernik (* 1973 in Wien)  is an austrian musician. In the past he was also active in the bands Naked Lunch and Disharmonic Orchestra. He is also a producer and label owner. He lives and works in Vienna. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fuzzman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
