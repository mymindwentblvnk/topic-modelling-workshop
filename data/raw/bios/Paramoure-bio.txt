Paramoure was an American Power/Thrash metal band which formed in 1987, Pompano Beach, Florida. The last-known line-up consisted of Dave London on Vocals, Caryn "One Evil Bitch" dos Santos on Bass, Don Carr on Guitar, Johnathan Linder on Guitar & 12 String and Marcel dos Santos as the drummer.

Paramoure released a demo in 1989, and then split-up a couple years later. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/paramoure">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
