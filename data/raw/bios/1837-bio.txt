1837, Pagan Folk Black Metal from Québec including members of Souverain and Northern Storm, created in 2010 by I.K. Branmaur and Damon Krieg. The band name 1837 refers to the Patriot rebellions of 1837-1838 in what is now known as Québec. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/1837">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
