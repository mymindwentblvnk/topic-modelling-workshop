молчат дома ("Houses are Silent" in English) is a Post-Punk/Synthpop band from Minsk, Belarus. Their sound meshes infectious Post-Punk guitar/bass melodies with Synthpop style keyboard layers, all rounded out by a drum machine and solemn vocals. The band has been very active in the Eastern European Post-Punk/Wave circuit, playing many gigs. Some of the bands that they have shared the stage with include Sierpien, ДК Посторонних, Ploho, Cold Comfort, gil'otina, Words and Actions, Nurnberg, Safety Dance and Холод.
