Stereoact is a German House - DJ - and producer team from the Erzgebirge. Members are Einenkel Rico (Ric) and Sebastian Seidel (Rixx). The duo founded in 2011 under the name Star Popper, first singles and remixes appeared. Starting in 2014, the duo traded under the name Ric & Rixx. February 2015 was the duo on the hit song 'Die immer lacht' by Heider singer Kerstin Ott attention. The deep house remix was a Kontor Records production and it released in the beginning of 2016.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stereoact">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
