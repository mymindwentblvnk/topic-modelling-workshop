Afterwind is a Russian modern/thrash metal, metalcore band from Krasnoyarsk, formed 
in 2012. Afterwind's music combines roaring and facemelting guitar riffs, melodic choruses, dual guitar harmonies, mighty and jaw - droppingly rhythm section and breakdowns as well as clean vocals and extreme vocals.

In 2013, Afterwind recorded its first single, Tyrants. Also, Afterwind started working on the first album in 2014. In the beginning of January of 2015, Afterwind released their first full-length album called "The "World is "Suffering".
