CUDA was a side project of 2 bongzilla members.
Fast driven stonerrock sounding tighter than any perfectly tuned hot rod engine.
They released a 4 track cd called "Hellfire" on 12th records in 2001.

members:
Cooter Brown - bass
Spanky - guitar
Nuge - drums
