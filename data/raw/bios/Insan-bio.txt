Insan (means human in English), is an indie/garage band formed in Bursa/Turkey by Ergün brothers who in time playing with different drummers, guitarists and bass players. Although the band officialy formed in July 2008, they were creating something before this time. Brothers say their influences are Chuck Berry, The Stooges and The Strokes.

Sessions were hard and they were fighting every time they got but they still managed to record four songs for their first EP. Written by Alper Ergün, Tayfun Ergün carry the weight of guitars in the songs. They still continue creating and writing new songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Insan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
