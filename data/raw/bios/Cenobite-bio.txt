There are more than one artist named Cenobite.

1) Formed in late 2010, in UK, Cenobite are:

Paul "Carps" Carpenter - Rhythm and Lead Guitar, Vocals
Si Slater - Bass, Drums, Rhythm and Lead Guitar, Synth

A fusion of Metal, Thrash, and elements of Black Metal and Stoner Rock combine to form what can only be described as Blackened Metal.

Releases:
July 2011 - 4-track EP "The Black E.P."
October 2012 - 3-track EP "Saint of Killers E.P."

Influences: Black Sabbath, Darkthrone, Celtic Frost, Bathory, Machine Head, Metallica
