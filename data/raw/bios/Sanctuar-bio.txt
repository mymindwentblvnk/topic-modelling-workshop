Sanctuar is a heavy metal band from Timisoara , Romania formed in 2004. 

Members :

ROBERT RISTEIU ( CRETZU' ) - Drums
FLORIN RUGACIUNE ( ZBARLA ) - Bass 
LEVENTE BORZASI ( LEVY ) - Guitar 
ALEX NIEZGODA - Sound Tehnician
