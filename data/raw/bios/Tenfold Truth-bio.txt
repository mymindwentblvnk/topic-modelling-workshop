formed in march 2002 Tenfold Truth started off life as a Hardcore band playing around London and Hertfordshire areas. They recorded their first demo in summer 2002.

After Several Lineup changes Tenfold Truth grew into a more metal style, and then split up a few months after releasing a 2 track demo in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TenFold+Truth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
