Heorot is a Viking Metal band from Mäntyharju, Finland, founded by frontman Teemu "Modsognir" Ollikainen and drummer Mikko Nokelainen in 2002.

Official websites:
www.heorotband.com 
http://www.myspace.com/heorotband

The first full-length album Ragnarök was released in spring 2007.
