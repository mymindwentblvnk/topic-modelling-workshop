Gauntlet Hair was (2009-2013) an indie rock band from Denver, Colorado. The members consisted of Craig Nice and Andy Rauworth. After releasing two extended plays, they signed to record label Dead Oceans and have released their self-titled debut album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gauntlet+Hair">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
