Graveborn is a 5-piece melodic/technical deathcore band from Boston, MA. Founded in the middle of 2012, they have released one EP so far named "A Vile Genesis." Released to positive acclaim, the band finished their lineup in early 2013 and has begun playing live shows in the New England area. Graveborn is currently working on their debut full length EP, which is to be expected near the end of 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Graveborn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
