Genesis of Aggression - Polish thrash metal group founded in 1986 in Siemianowice Slaskie.

In 1988 the band released a demo called Dicit Mors. In 1989 they've released their only album, Disappointment. Despite positive opinions, the band haven't made any more albums and disbanded around 1995. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Genesis+Of+Aggression">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
