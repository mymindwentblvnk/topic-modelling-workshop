Impostor was a heavily Venom-inspired Norwegian old school black metal band formed in 1987 under the name Deathfuck. It featured Mayhem's session vocalist Messiah on vocals and Bard Faust on drums.

Releases:
Abnormal Rape (Demo) - 1987 (as Deathfuck)
Rehearsal of chaos (Demo) - 1987
Little Hitler Illusion (Demo) - 1987
SNS - Still Not Satisfied? (Demo) - 1990
