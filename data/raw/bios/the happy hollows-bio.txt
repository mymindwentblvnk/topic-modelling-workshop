Happy Hollows is a band from Los Angeles that play melodic and experimental noise-pop. The group's members are Sarah Negahdari (vocals/guitar), Charlie Mahoney (bass, synth), and Matthew Fry (guitar).  Their first album, Spells, was released in 2010 on Autumn Tone Records. Their second album, Amethyst, is set for release in July 2013 on Pesky Fruit Records.    <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Happy+Hollows">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
