Beyond The Red Horizon are a progressive extreme metal band hailing from Portland, Oregon who have been bringing their unique blend of classical,melodic and progressive european influences to the Pacific Northwest metal scene since 2007. Since the release of a self-titled demo in 2008 and the "Observance" EP in 2009, the group has been lauded for their finely tuned arrangements and unrelenting dedication to their craft.
