1) Verme is a punk from Milan, Italy from 2009-2012. Band members are Tommaso, Giacomo, Violetta and Iacopo
Their first demo is released between the end of 2009 and January 2010, called "Un Verme Resta Un Verme". Then other 2 EPs, "Vai Verme Vai" and "Bad Verme". Their last work is a split, "Splittone Paura" before they decide to break up in 2012
