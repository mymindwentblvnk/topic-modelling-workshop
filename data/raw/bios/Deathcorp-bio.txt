Deathcorp was an old school death/thrash metal band from Detroit, Michigan. They formed in 1986 and never released a full length, only a demo - "Cold Blooded War" in 1987 and an  EP - "Rest in Pieces" in 1989.  

Deathcorp is currently split up, their last known line-up was:
- Eice (Gene Finelli) - Vocals
- Jake Greed (Joel Burns) - Guitars
- Filth McNasty (Chris Danson) - Drums
