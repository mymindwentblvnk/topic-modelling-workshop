There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Starved is a one-man depressive black metal band founded in the UK in 2018. As of July 2020, Starved has two releases: Starved (2018) and Underworld (2020). Musically, Starved aims to "encapsulate feelings of isolation, depression, and dark hatred through raw primitive black metal."

2) Starved are a Newcastle based metalcore band, striving to take the genre to new peaks. Taking influences from nu-metal and alternative rock, the Tyneside act released their debut single 'Becoming Dirt' in February 2020 supported by live dates up and down the country. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Starved">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
