Ad Sidera is an unsigned post-hardcore/screamo band based in Westchester, NY. The lineup consists of Anthony Kaplan on vocals, Mike Predovan and Bryan Rivera on Guitars, Cedric Obando on Bass and Dan Sweeney on Drums. They were founded in December of 2008 and have been active since. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ad+Sidera">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
