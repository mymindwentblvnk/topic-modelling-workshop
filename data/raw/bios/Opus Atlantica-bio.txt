Swedish heavy/progressive/neo-classical metal band/project created by renowned singer Peter Sandberg (Midnight Sun, Silver Seraph...) in 2002. They released Opus Atlantica in 2003, featuring Johan Reinholdz (Andromeda, NonExist, Skyfire) on guitars. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Opus+Atlantica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
