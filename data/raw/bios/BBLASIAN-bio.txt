Bblasian is a rapper from Louisville, Kentucky. He rose to fame after his song “Change” exploded in popularity on SoundCloud and YouTube. He later signed to Cinematic Music Group, and released his debut album through the label titled “Dif.” on October 25th.

Since, he has left Cinematic Music Group, and continued music independently through his collective “BetterDayz”, He released 2 songs at the start of 2020. “Heartless (Run Far)” and “Who Am I?” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bblasian">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
