THE 6IXTH  Features former members of El Paso's Pissing Razors, Joe Rodriguez, Mat Lynch and Cesar Soto have formed a new powerhouse of groove metal along with Veteran musicians Dan Contreras and Tury Saldana to fill the void of the Razors disbanding.
Why this name?
For a time known as Fueled to Fire, THE SIXTH separated themselves from a growing field of 'Fueled to This' and 'Fueled by That's' with the title of 'THE SIXTH', a reference to the Mayan Mythology of man's demise in the fall of 2012...
