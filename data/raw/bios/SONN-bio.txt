SONN - Steven O Noble started his music playing career as a trumpet player when he was age 7.  Picking up the guitar at 10 he later went on to span multiple music generes including rock, with the band Surreal, Electronic with the band X-Portfolio, Folk with the band Moot Point and his general singer/songwriter acoustic music as SONN. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SONN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
