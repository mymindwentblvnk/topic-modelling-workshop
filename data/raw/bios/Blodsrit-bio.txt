Blodsrit is a black metal band hailing from Västervik, Sweden (Total Destruction), formed in 1998.

Discography:
- 1999 - Dödens Sändebud (demo)
- 2000 - Storm of Immolation (split with Ritual Orchestra)
- 2001 - Supreme Misanthropy (Full-length)
- 2001 - Secrets Unveiled (EP)
- 2002 - ...In Blood (EP)
- 2003 - Ocularis Infernum (Full-length)
- 2004 - Helveteshymner (Full-length)
- 2006 - The Well Of Light Has Finally Dried (Full-length)
- 2007 - Hinterland (Full-length)
- 2013- Diktat Deliberti (Full-length)
