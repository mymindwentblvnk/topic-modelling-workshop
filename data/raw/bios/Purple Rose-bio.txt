Purple Rose is the Slovak hardrock band formed in 1998 in Kosice. 
Members: 
Lead guitar - Jaroslav Gaspar ( Ex - Hey - You)
Vocal - Ladislav Rogozinsky ( Ex - Color Box)
Bass Guitar - Stefan Hundza ( Ex - Sexit)
