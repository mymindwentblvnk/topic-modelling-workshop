Rein is the name of more than one artist:

1) Swedish electro/ebm artist, daughter of Paul Rein, Rein (born Joanna Reinikainen) grew up in Stockholm, Sweden. She has in a very short period of time established herself as a great hope for the Swedish electro scene. Her background as an outsider and her commitment to the injustices of society is an important part of her expression.
Her music is part electro, part punk, part electronic body music. With heavy basslines and enormous attitude she has been the target for a lot of media with her selftitled debut EP released in january 2016.
