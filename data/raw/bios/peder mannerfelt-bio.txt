Peder Mannerfelt is one half of the Swedish electronic duo Roll The Dice and one half of duo Van Rivers & The Subliminal Kid. As a solo artist he released his debut album "Lines Describing Circles" in 2014, followed by "The Swedish Congo Record" (2015) and "Controlling Body" (2016). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Peder+Mannerfelt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
