Ziontod is a Black Metal act from Germany. Formed in 2007, he is still active today. 

Ziontod himself does all the instruments and vocals. His main themes are war and anti-religion. 

So far, Ziontod has released the following:

- Panzerschlag Full-length, 2007  
- Ziontod / Herrscher - Panzerschlag / Lynchtod Split Split, 2008
