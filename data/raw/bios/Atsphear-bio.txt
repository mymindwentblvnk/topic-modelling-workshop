Atsphear is a Melodic Death Metal band from Madrid (Spain), formed in 2001. They had released one demo (Atmosphere X, 2004) and one album (New Line System, 2005) so far. Their style is close to Gothenburg sound but with some innovations. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atsphear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
