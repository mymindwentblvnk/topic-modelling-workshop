Yurufuwa Gang are the new trendsetters of Japanese rap. The duo consists of Ryugo Ishida and NENE, who were both solo artists before meeting in Tokyo in 2016 and forming Yurufuwa Gang. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/YURUFUWA+GANG">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
