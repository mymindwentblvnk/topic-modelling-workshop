Infinite Descent is the newest force in the New England progressive metal scene. The band consists of Phil Provencher, Rob Gil, Chris Robinson (all former Hypersolid), Olivia Berka (former Polaris) and Kendall Divoll (Necronomichrist). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infinite+Descent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
