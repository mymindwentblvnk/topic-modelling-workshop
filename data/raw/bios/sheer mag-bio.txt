Sheer Mag is an American rock band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, formed in 2014. A combination of 1970s rock and punk ethos, Rolling Stone magazine featured the band in 2015 as one of "10 New Artists You Need To Know". In 2017, the band released "Need To Feel Your Love", the first track off their first full-length record, Need to Feel Your Love. On June 19, 2019, they announced their second LP, A Distant Call.
