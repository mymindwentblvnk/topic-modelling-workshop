[b/]Yin Yin (officially written as YĪN YĪN) is an electronic band from Maastricht, The Netherlands that uses influences from East Asian sixties and seventies music. The result is a colorful blend of psychedelic funk and disco. 

Yin Yin is  Yves Lennertz (guitar, bass, organ, stylophone, percussion), Kees Berkers (drums, synths, percussion) and Gino Bombrini (percussion). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yin+Yin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
