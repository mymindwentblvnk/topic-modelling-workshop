1) Triglav was a black metal band from Poljčane, Slovenia formed in 1997. Band split-up in 2001.

Discography:
- 1998 - Triglav - Rehearsal '98 (demo)
- 2000 - Triglav/Črnobog - split
- 2001 - Triglav - Stara Pravda (demo)
- 2001 - Triglav - Padlemu narodu (demo)


2) Triglav (Триглав) started on December 22, 1999 in Kharkhiv, Ukraine. They started working on this project the day after Paulus' birthday. In January of 2006, they signed a contract with the Austrian CCP Records. This is their debut CD, "When The Sun Is Rising Above The Earth". Triglav is a god in Slavic mythology and it means three-headed. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Triglav">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
