A.O.S.O.O.N (A Lot of Something Out of Nothing) are a duo from London, United Kingdom consisting of Marisa Hylton (vocals and guitar) and Manny Folorunso (bass). They focus on storytelling through music that resonates with everyday life. Their first single Skinny Strong was featured on their first EP What Is This About that was relased in  July 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A.O.S.O.O.N">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
