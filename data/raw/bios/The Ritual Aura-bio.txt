Obscenium is a Progressive Technical Death Metal band from Perth, Western Australia...
Founded in late 2011, the band released various demo tracks over the next year and finally emerged into the local scene in early 2013, followed by 6 months of relentless shows.
Recording of the band's debut 'full-length' is currently underway...
