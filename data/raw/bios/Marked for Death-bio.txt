There are several bands named Marked for Death

The most recent Marked For Death were a four piece pop-rock/indie band from Chico, CA that featured former and current members of Number One Gun and Surrogate. The band - Ben Tietz on vocals/guitar, Keith Fegley on guitar, Mike Janke on and Daniel Taylor on drums -  recorded a four song EP  at Old Sailor Studios in Chico, CA with Jeff Schneeweis (Hawthorne Heights, Number One Gun, Brighten) and played around Chico before disbanding in 2010 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marked+For+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
