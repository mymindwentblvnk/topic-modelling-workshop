Joshua Smith (born January 9th, 1995), known professionally as Yoshi Flower, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer signed to Interscope Records. The Detroit born musician independently released his debut track "Movies" on March 9th, 2018 with The Fader calling the song "early Lana Del Rey except if her partying involved EDM." The singer released his debut mixtape "American Raver" on October 12th, 2018 with ID Magazine summarizing it as a "pledge of allegiance gone goth."  Flower toured with Dua Lipa, Epik High, Blackbear, Whethan and others.
