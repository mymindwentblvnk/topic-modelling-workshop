Morbid God was a black metal band from Portugal in 1989, that later gave birth to Moonspell! They released one demotape entitled Serpent Angel and participated at The Birth of a Tragedy, the first portuguese metal compilation. They were formed by Tetragrammaton (bass), Langsuyar (vocals), Mantus (guitar) and Baalberith (drums). Later they were joined by a second guitarist, Fenrir. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+God">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
