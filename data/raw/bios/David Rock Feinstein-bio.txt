David Rock Feinstein is the guitarist for the Metal band THE RODS. He is Ronnie James Dio's cousin, and was the guitarist in one of Ronnie's early bands, called Elf. Previous to that he was in a local band in Central NY called The Cindells. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/David+Rock+Feinstein">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
