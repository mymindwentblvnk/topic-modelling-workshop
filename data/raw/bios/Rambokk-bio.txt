Rambokk is a thrash metal-band from Bærum, Norway. The band was formed in 2003, and they gave out their first demo in 2006.

The band consists of the four members Anners-David (AD) Engebretsen (guitar, vocals), Jonas Eid (bass), Petter (drums) and Sondre B.
