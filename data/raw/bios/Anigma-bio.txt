There is more than one artist with this name:

1.) Anigma is a contemporary band that mixes Rock  with progressive metal, psychedelic rock and Post-rock music.

Their influences include bands like: Tool, Porcupine Tree, NIN, The Mars Volta, Pearl Jam, Opeth and Pink Floyd among others.

2.) Anigma was a speed/thrash metal band formed in Auckland, New Zealand in 1987. In 1989 they supported Metallica and released the 6-track demo 'World of Fear' on cassette. They split up in 1991.
