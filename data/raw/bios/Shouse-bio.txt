Shouse are an Australian-based electronic music duo consisting of Jack Madin and New Zealander Ed Service. They are best known for their 2017 single "Love Tonight", which experienced renewed popularity in 2021 after various remixes of it were used in TikTok trends. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/shouse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
