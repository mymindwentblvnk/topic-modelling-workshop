There may is more than one band named after this movie:

1. A "pure southern booze rock" band from Brisbane, Australia, formed in 2009. In 2011 they became more plain old rock, with less, southern, less booze, and a new singer. Their previous singer now fronts a new project called Death Valley Nights. Their guitarist is now in the band mindjar with their second vocalist.
