There are two artists with the name Stoneburner.

(1) Stoneburner is a sludge/doom band from Portland, OR.  Current members: Elijah, Jesse, Damon and Jason.  Damon (Kelly) is also a member of Heathen Shrine.

(2) Stoneburner is also the name of a solo project by Steven Archer of Ego Likeness and Hopeful Machines. http://www.stoneburnerband.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stoneburner">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
