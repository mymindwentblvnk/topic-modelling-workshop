There are two bands with name Svafnir.

1) Pagan black metal band from Germany. One-man project of Alexander Suplie.
Discography:
- Demo 2005
- Der alten Zeiten Widerkehr (2006, demo)
- Pfad des Nebels (2006, EP)
- Aufbruch (2007, EP)
- The Heathen Chapters (2008, LP)
Official site (archived): https://web.archive.org/web/20060520173136/http://www.svafnir.de/

2) Black metal artist from Italy, a one-man project of Gianmarco Colalongo. Member of Draugr and Sturmkaiser. Released only demo Let The Ravens Come (2003). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Svafnir">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
