Kiesa Rae Ellestad (born 1989), better known by her stage name Kiesza, is a Canadian singer-songwriter and dancer from Calgary who has most recently worked out of New York City and London. She is best known for her debut single "Hideaway". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kiesza">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
