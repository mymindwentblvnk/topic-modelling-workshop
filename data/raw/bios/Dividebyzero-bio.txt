Grindcore influenced band from Perth, Western Australia. Active from 2001-2007. Last Ep 'Bornavirus' was recorded in 2008.
Members are now in other bands including 'Agony' 'Suffer' 'Gunhead' 'The Hunt' and 'Pale As Ashes'

There is also an industrial demo artist using the name of "dividebyzero" (no capitals) based in Sydney. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DivideByZero">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
