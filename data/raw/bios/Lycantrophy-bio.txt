Lycantrophy was a thrash/death metal band from Kuusamo, Finland. The band was formed in 1987 by Jari Aho. Aho played guitar, but on the demos, he plays bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lycantrophy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
