1. Talon is a Heavy Metal band from Bavaria, Germany. Founded in 1982, they released 3 full length albums during their active years. Have since split up.

2.Talon are:

Chandler Mogel - Vocals
Kory Voxen - Rhythm Guitar
Jim Kee - Lead Guitar
Phil Keller - Bass
John Parker - Drums 

Talon are a Californian based melodic rock band, formed in 2002 in Los Angeles.
