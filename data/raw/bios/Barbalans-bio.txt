Barbalans is a black metal band from sabah, east malaysia.

Releases include:

Cindaku (Demo)- 1996  
Thy Shytan (Demo)- 2001
Misanthropic Congregration...The Beginning (Split)- 2008  
True Eastern Sodomy (Split)- 2008
C.U.L.T (Single)- 2009

Misanthropic Congregration...The Beginning was a 4 way split with Labatut / Wisdom / Barbalans / Claw

True Eastern Sodomy was a 2 way split with A.B.M.S. 696

C.U.L.T was re-released with a limited press of 50 copies on CD. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Barbalans">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
