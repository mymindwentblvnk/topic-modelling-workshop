Decoryah was an atmospheric and melancholic metal/rock band from Kaarina, Finland, formed in 1989 and dismissed in 1997. The band has been split up for a long time, and it is very unlikely that they would make music anymore.

Main line-up
Jukka Vuorinen - vocals, guitars, keyboards
Jani Kakko - guitars
Mikko Laine - drums
