Orchidectomy (a synonym for castration, aka removal of the testes) is a brutal death metal band from Toronto, Ontario, Canada.  Their style is fast and brutal, similar to Wormed and Brodequin with more variation than the latter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Orchidectomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
