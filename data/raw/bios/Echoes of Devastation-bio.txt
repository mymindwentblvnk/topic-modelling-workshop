Melodic death metal band from Munster, Lower Saxony, 1995-2006 (as Kobold), 2006-present renamed themselves Echoes of Devastation, currently unsigned, released their first album "My Cold Reign" in 2008 and their second " Salt of your Tears" in 2013 

Echoes of Devastation are 

Phillip Bass (2006-2012), Guitars (2012-present)
Johannes Drums (2006-present)
Christian Guitars (2006-present)
Florian Vocals (2006-present)
Olli Bass (2012-present)
