Krux is a Swedish doom metal band, created by Leif Edling after Candlemass fell apart after the reunion tour. It also features Jörgen Sandström (The Project Hate MCMXCIX, ex-Entombed, ex-Grave) on guitars, Peter Stjärnvind (Entombed) on drums and Mats Levén (Therion, At Vance, Yngwie Malmsteen and many more) on vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Krux">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
