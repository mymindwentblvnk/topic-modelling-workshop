Public Access T.V. are an American band formed in New York City in 2014. The band is John Eatherly (lead vocals, guitar), Xan Aird (lead guitar, vocals), Max Peebles (bass, vocals) and Pete Star (drums, vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Public+Access+T.V.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
