A band formed in 2013 by Joe Russo, the current drummer for the band Furthur, in order to cover the Grateful Dead.

The band includes Joe Russo on drums, Marco Benevento on keyboards, guitarists Tom Hamilton and Scott Metzger, and bassist Dave Dreiwitz. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Almost+Dead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
