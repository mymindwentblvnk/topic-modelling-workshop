ASSAMALLA was already put together in late 1996 by Erki (vocals), Veigo (bass) and Andre (guitar). 
After a force majore pause in 1998 they found a proper room for rehearsals and invited to the band three new members - Meelis (guitar), Hanno (drums) and Kaarit (keys). In 1999 they recorded their first demo (6 tracks, 35 minutes) 'Taamalt toustet toonetarmu' at Stillborn Studio.

Line Up:

Erki - Vocals 
Pichen - Guitar 
Veigo - Bass 
Kaarit - Keyboard 
Indrek - Drums
