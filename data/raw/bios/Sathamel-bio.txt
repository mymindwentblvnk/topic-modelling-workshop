Sathamel are a black/death metal band from Yorkshire, England that formed in 2011 under the name Sheol. They renamed to Sathamel in 2013 and released their first EP 'Sathamel' in 2015. Their debut album is due out in 2016. Kruk (vocals), Baal (guitars), KVN (guitars), Deimos (bass), Valdr (drums) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sathamel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
