Stories From The Lost are a post-rock band from Belgium. They play a blend of heavy instrumental music with electronic influences in the vein of bands such as 65daysofstatic and Maybeshewill. They self released their own ep titled For Clouds in April of 2012. It is available for free at http://storiesfromthelost.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stories+From+The+Lost">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
