Withering Skies is a Hellenic depressive/down-tempo/psychedelic black metal band whitch follows the path of Thorns, Funeral Mist, Necros Christos, Ondskapt, Watain and Deathspell Omega. The band was formed in 2005 by Horror Lord(Guitars, Bass) and King Of Asine(Vocals). They have released one demo called "Knowledge through sorrow" where Velnias(Drums) and Nocturnus Necroaeon(Bass) joined the band, and was released in tape by the ukranian distro "Depressive Illusions". Their new song is called "A leadership in blood" and can be found in their Myspace page or in Youtube. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Withering+skies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
