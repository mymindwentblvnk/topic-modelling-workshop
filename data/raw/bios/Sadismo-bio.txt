Sadismo is a death metal band from Bosnia and Herzegovina. They released only one demo called "Nihilist" in December, 1991 before splitting up. The line-up included Morbid (vocals), Six (guitar), Dragas (guitar), Predo (bass) and Barry (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sadismo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
