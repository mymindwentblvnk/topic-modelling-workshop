"Memnoch" Born Alex Muniz, is an American Rap and Hip-Hop Performance artist from Palmdale California.
 Since a young teen Memnoch's musical preference has been the classical Style of Hip-Hop slowly changing to modern themes and current events. Working with Producer LEO LEGENDARY, Memnoch has carved a groove in Los Angeles' Underground Hip-Hop scene and is a Pioneer of the High Desert Independent Artist movement in Northern L.A. County.
