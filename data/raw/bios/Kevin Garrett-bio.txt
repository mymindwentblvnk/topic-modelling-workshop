Kevin Garrett is a singer and songwriter from Brooklyn, New York. His poignant lyricism 
and musicianship have been showcased in numerous projects over the past several years. 
The Pittsburgh native has spent much of the last decade performing his original work, 
sharing the stage with James Vincent McMorrow, Emily King, Norah Jones, and Maps & Atlases
among others. Kevin recently released his debut single “Coloring,” signifying a new direction in his work 
and showcasing his versatility as a songwriter. “Coloring” earned considerable attention 
since its premiere on Pigeons and Planes, having been featured on Spotifyʼs New Music Tuesday list
