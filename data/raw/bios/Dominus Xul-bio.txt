Dominus Xul was a Chilean death metal band originally formed in 1992 as Dominus. They released a demo in 1992 and an EP in 1997 before changing their name to Dominus Xul to release their full-length album "The Primigeni Xul (I Condemned My Enemies)" in 1998. In April 2009 a retrospective of their material as Dominus titled "Nostrum Antiquos Dominus " was released on Corvus Discos. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dominus+Xul">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
