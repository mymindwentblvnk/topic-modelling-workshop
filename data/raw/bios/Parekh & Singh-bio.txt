Parekh & Singh are an Indian dream pop duo from Kolkata. Consisting of Nischay Parekh (vocals, guitar, synths) and Jivraj Singh (drums, electronics), the band released their debut album Ocean (2016) on Peacefrog, a British indie label. Parekh & Singh's debut music video "I Love You Baby, I Love You Doll", which features aesthetic influenced by the American film director Wes Anderson, has garnered over 200,000 views on YouTube, considered to be a significant feat for an Indian indie band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Parekh+&+Singh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
