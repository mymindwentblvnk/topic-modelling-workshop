Omia is a progressive metal band from West Virginia, USA, formed in 2004. The band consists of Matt Good on vocals, Zack Namey on guitars, and Evan Hoffman and Chris Stout sharing duty on bass. The band's style is heavily influenced by western art music, particularly baroque, and is largely inspired of primary composer Namey's violin study.

The band's official debut album, "Palpitation in the Brumal Wasteland", was released on May 08, 2012 through Hoffman's independent home label Hylopathic Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Omia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
