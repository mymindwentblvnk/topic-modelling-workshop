Black Communion is metal/hardcore band from Portland, OR. The lineup has current and ex-members of Young Turks, Carrion Spring, and Unrestrained. They also feature lyrics based on the Bungie video game, Destiny. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Communion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
