GRINDCORE band active from 1995 to 2009. The four musicians currently live in Zwecking, Bavaria (Germany) and have recorded three albums. Besides grindcore their music is also influenced by other styles such as: death metal, thrash metal, etc. and about lyrical themes, they used to deal about social issues, politics, anti-war, anti-racism, etc. with titles like "Consumption Is Freedom?", "Burn You Nazi Pig" or "Disobedience".
