Syndrome of Terror is a political grindcore band from Osasco, São Paulo, Brazil who formed in 1993.

Discography:

-After Noise the Conscience (1995)
-Emetic Ideology (1999)
-Split Tape w/ Plague Rages (1999)
-Split Tape w/ Crushing Heads, Mahogany & Plague Rages (1999)
-Split 7" w/ Plague Rages (2000)
-¿El Extremo? (2001)
