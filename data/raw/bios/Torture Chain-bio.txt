Torture Chain is a black metal band from the United States with Brendan Radigan (Magic Circle, Mind Eraser) as the only main member. The band has released numerous demos and albums since 2008, with 2019's The Ascent of Deimos being their latest album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Torture+Chain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
