Chicksy Dicks is a alternative metal band formed in 2007 based in Ribnica, Slovenia. The band incorporates many styles of metal, rock, hardcore and some other genres. In the past they had numerous gigs in the Slovenian underground scene. In January 2013 they released their first EP entitled "Obsessed" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/chicksy+dicks">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
