Based in Aberdeen, Upper Hunter Valley, New South Wales of Australia. The Loom of Time is a metal band that was created in 2015. They have trademarked an uplifting and epic sound that is very melodic and bittersweet.

Brad Delforce - Bass (2015-present)
Steven Reid - Drums (2015-present)
Matthew J. Ratcliffe - Guitars & Vocals (2015-present)
