Endless Sorrow is a depressive black metal band, formed by Skeg (Tasmania) and Surtur (Belgium) in 2009. The band signed with Self Mutilation Services and released their first demo 'Hopeless' in 2010.

Surtur is, or was, also active as a (quest) vocalist in several other bands, including Means to an End, Drowning and Eratomania. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Endless+Sorrow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
