Throne of Death was a Canadian thrash metal band formed in 2011 from Newmarket, Ontario. Their first and only album, "Monsterborn," was released for free via Bandcamp on September 9th, 2014, after which the band broke up.

Members:

Brian McCarthy - Lead Vocals, Bass 
Tyler Booth - Drums 
Allan Oakley Langille - Guitar
