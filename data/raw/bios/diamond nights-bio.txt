Diamond Nights is a hard rock/indie rock band whose influences include Thin Lizzy, Queen, and Cheap Trick. They formed in New York City in 2001, and released their debut album, Popsicle, in 2005. The band's song "The Girl's Attractive" was featured in a 2006 Jaguar advertisement, as well as the episode entitled "Nicodemus" from The CW's Smallville and in an Austrian beer commercial.

* Morgan Phalen - Vocals, guitar
* Rob Laakso - Guitar, synthesizers
* Seth Rumsey - Bass
* Tim Traynor - Drums
