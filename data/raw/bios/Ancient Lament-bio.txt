Ancient Lament is paleoastronautic themed old school death doom/funeral doom metal project founded in 2014 by nick name Borgion from Serbia all instruments, production and growl vocals by Hangsvart(ex-Abysmal Growls Of Despair) from France.
In 2015 Ancient Lament release the debut album "Messages From The Crystals" on Frozen Light label.
