Tsar Bomb is an extreme metal band based in the city of Malaga in southern Spain which was founded in 2009 with the idea of a Black-Death Metal fast and aggressive. Since its inception the band has undergone numerous lineup changes a fact that has prevented the holding of concerts and making recordings. In autumn 2011 the band released their debut concert, after which new variations occur in the formation, delaying the release of their first album "Neowarfare" which was scheduled for the end of this year, but eventually will occur October 9 2012.
Tsar Bomb's philosophy revolves around the concepts and contemporary modern warfare refer to the most powerful weapons of destruction created by the hand of man. Its essentially nihilistic and misanthropic thought advocates the complete disappearance of the human race, as evidenced by their letters imbued with hatred and aversion towards life spiced with references to various mythic archetypes and a classic Satanism such feelings of rejection power to everything human.

Tsar Bomb es una banda de metal extremo ubicada en la ciudad de Málaga, en el sur de España, que nace en el año 2009 con la idea de realizar un Black-Death Metal rápido y agresivo. Desde sus comienzos la banda ha sufrido numerosos cambios en su formación, hecho que ha impedido la celebración de conciertos y la realización de grabaciones. En el otoño del 2011 la banda realiza su concierto debut, tras el cual se producen nuevas variaciones en la formación, lo que retrasa la salida de su primer disco "Neowarfare", la cual estaba prevista para el término de ese año, pero que finalmente se producirá octubre 9 de 2012.
