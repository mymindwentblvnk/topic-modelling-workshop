Razamanaz is a hard rock band from the USA, that was formed in 1993, featuring Michael Kelly Smith, Cory Massi on lead vocals and guitar, Joe Bisbing on bass, and Steve Attig on drums. They released a compilation CD, 'Razamanaz' in 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Razamanaz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
