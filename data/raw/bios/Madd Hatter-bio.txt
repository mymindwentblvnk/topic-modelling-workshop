Madd Hatter is 
1. A Heavy Metal band from Houston, TX that released one album in 1984 and has since split up
2. A DJ and Producer from Los Angeles, CA active from 2012-present

The Electronic producer Madd Hatter released a self-titled EP in September of 2013 with the songs 1. Ignite feat. Megg and 2. Where the Party At feat. Chris Ness and can be found on last.fm as DJ Madd Hatter or soundcloud as Madd Hatter (Official) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Madd+Hatter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
