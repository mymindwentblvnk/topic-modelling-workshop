Milkavelli is a rapper from London who has made waves across the English rap scene. He is also known under the aliases Monster Under The Bed and Don Silk. He is closely associated with Skepta, A$AP MOB, Rejjie Snow, Ian Connor, and the 616 collective. He was a member of rap group Piff Gang and is currently a member of rap group Cult Mountain.
