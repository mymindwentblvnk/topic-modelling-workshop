The band was formed in May 2001, supposed to sound extreme and fast, with influences of Death Metal. The lyrics talk about obscurity human feelings, mocking religion and all defending liberty of expression and human sense.

After some changes in the Line-up, the band is now formed by Marcus Borralho (Bass/Vocals), Nanno T. (Guitars) & Hugo M. (Drums). The band released their first demo promo album at 2002, with 2 songs, and in 2003 released a cd demo album with 7 songs, named Through The Lands Of Agony. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Empty+Grace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
