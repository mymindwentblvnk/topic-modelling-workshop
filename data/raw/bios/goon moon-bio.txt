Goon Moon is an American rock band comprised of Jeordie White (also known as Twiggy Ramirez from Marilyn Manson), Fred Sablan (currently also in Marilyn Manson), Zach Hill (of Hella, Death Grips), and Chris Goss (founding member of Masters of Reality , Desert Sessions, Kyuss and Queens of the Stone Age producer). The band released a mini-LP called "I Got a Brand New Egg-Laying Machine" and have since recorded a full length album "Licker's Last Leg", which was released on May 8th, 2007. The album feature cameos from longtime Goss collaborator Josh Homme and Josh Freese, as well as desert rock mainstay Dave Catching.
