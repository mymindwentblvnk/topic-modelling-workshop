Cepheide are an atmospheric black metal band from Paris, France, that formed in 2013. They released their first demo 'De silence et de suie' the following year, and their first EP 'Respire' in July 2015. The EP was reissued on cassette by Fallen Empire Records, and by Ogmios Underground as a digipak CD later that year in limited quantities. The band lineup is Gaetan (vocals, drums) and Thomas (guitars and bass) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cepheide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
