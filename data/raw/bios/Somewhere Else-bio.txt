Electronic production duo Somewhere Else make soulful, atmospheric, dance-oriented pop. Formed in 2016, Somewhere Else features the combined talents of Grammy-winning producer/songwriter Carlo Illangelo Montagnese and producer/songwriter Billy Walsh. Prior to joining forces, they each built up their own impressive array of credits including collaborations with Drake, the Weeknd, Lady Gaga, Alicia Keys, and others. In 2016, they garnered attention with their debut single "Move Together," featuring Canadian R&B duo Majid Jordan. A year later, they returned with several more singles including "Glue" and "All the Way." ~ Matt Collar <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Somewhere+Else">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
