Last Pain was formed in the third quarter of 2005 from the ideas of Yunendra "endra" adiputra and T’naar Kristian Julian. At first, they were planning on making a hardcore band. eventually they tried to gather tallented young lads from their hometown. the first formation consist of Yunendra "endra" adiputra on vocals, Billy and Tnaar on guitars,Indra on bass, and Alvikar "v-kar" Rendy (shot!drivers/Romance Or Lust)on drums. And they choose Black Metal as their basic genre.early in 2006..some changes were made, v-kar decided to leave the band and was replace by Bella, T’naar and Indra also switched instrument. and it became their strongest formation so far. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/last+pain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
