Barbarroja is a  Spanish hard rock / heavy metal band. The band was formed in 2002 in the spanish city of Madrid and  their music, with all the lyrics in spanish, is influenced by bands such as Deep Purple and Barón Rojo. Formerly known as Deskicia, the band recorded only a demo under this name and changed their name to the current one as it fits more with the band’s style.
