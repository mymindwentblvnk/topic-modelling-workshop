Since forming in 2015 the duo Agar Agar have created a catchy sound that resonates in the ears of international listeners. Producer Armand Bultheel layers synth minimal beats complimenting the sultry voice of Clara Cappagli, bringing life back into French electro pop. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agar+Agar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
