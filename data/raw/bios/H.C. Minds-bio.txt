A doom/sludge project from Eugene, Oregon, US by the guys from YOB. Isamu Satu (bass in YOB) and Mike Scheidt (guitar/vocals in YOB) basically switched instruments. The band changed members over the years, and the current members are Isamu Sato (guitar/vocals), Paula Rhodes (bass/vocals) and Sean Schock (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/H.C.+Minds">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
