Nu-disco duo consisting of Timothy Robert Eric Butler (aka Rundfunk aka PYLOT) and John Julian Thomas Parry formed in 2012.

As of August 3rd, 2017, Tim Butler is no longer a part of Televisor. He explained in the Televisor Instagram that he's going to move on. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Televisor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
