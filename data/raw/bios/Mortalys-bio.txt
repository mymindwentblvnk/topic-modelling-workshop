Mortalys is Cesar Grancho, based in Porto Portugal. 

Started with music very young at 9 with piano, then played guitar in garage band at Lamego (Portugal) playing some rock and alt with his friends.

He discover very late the power of the midi(2008) and after he got intestered in psychedelic parties he starting mixing some sets.
