Framingham, Massachusetts, USA (2006-present)

Technical death metal piece straight from Middlesex County, MA. After changing their name form Contagion to Cadaveryne (legal issues) they independently released one demo (2006) and a full-lenght "Fathering the Apocalypse" in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cadaveryne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
