憂鬱 (YU-UTSU) is a synthwave/chillwave/vaporwave/ambient producer likely based in either Japan or the U.S., or both. The name translates to "melancholy." At the time this wiki was written in  July of 2022, 憂鬱 has stopped releasing music and is on hiatus. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/%E6%86%82%E9%AC%B1">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
