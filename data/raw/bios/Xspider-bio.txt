Xspider is a Melodic Heavy Metal band from Japan. Members are:

Luna G. Kitagawa - Guitar, Vocals, Backing Vocals (14th April 1974, Hokkaido, Japan)
Mayuka Nozaki - Vocals (13th April 1977, Tokyo, Japan)
Christian Muhr - Drums, Percussion (27th February 1980, Orebro, Sweden)
