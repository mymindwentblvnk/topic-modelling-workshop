Thy Eternity is a metal band straight out of the Minnesota music scene. 

Influences have been The Chariot, Unearth, August Burns Red, Job For A Cowboy, All Shall Perish, Becoming the Achetype, After the Burial, Zao and a lot of deathcore / grind bands.

"We sound like Job For A Cowboy with August Burns Red drumming and Zao vocals." -Ben

"Ben is wrong we sound like shit mixed with pee"  - Josh
