Darleen Carr (born December 12, 1950, Chicago) is an American actress and singer best known for a recurring role in the 1981-82 television series Bret Maverick.

Other credits include Deep Space Nine and The Waltons. Carr is also a singer and sang in The Sound of Music and in Walt Disney's The Jungle Book. Carr also released an album, "The Carr-DeBelles Band," in 1988.
