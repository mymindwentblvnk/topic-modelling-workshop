Originally started in late 2006 under the moniker "Inner Vision", FEAR CONTROL is a heavy riffin, loud screamin, double bassin audio attack from Austin, Tx. Playing through 2008, the band went through some member changes. Starting fresh in late 2010, the band is back & hungry to destroy live again. Although the only original member, Kash Sarkaria sings & plays guitar, the rest of the group as brought this unit closer as a whole and take the music to another level. Fear Control has shared the stage with established acts such as Within the Ruins, Within Chaos, Hell Within, Powderburn, Indorphine, Critical Assembly, Bangladesh, and MORE...

Fear Control's influences include Metallica, Pantera, Megadeth, Slayer, Trivium, Killswitch Engage, Lamb of God, Unearth, All That Remains, Bullet For My Valentine, Dethklok, As I Lay Dying, 36 Crazyfists, Nonpoint, Avenged Sevenfold, Protest the Hero, System of a Down, Every Time I Die, Slipknot, Machine Head, Darkest Hour, Asking Alexandria, Steel Panther, Anberlin, etc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fear+Control">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
