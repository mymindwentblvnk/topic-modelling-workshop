Opitz is band from Czech republic, founded by ex-members of legendary experimental grindcore band Contrastic. Opitz plays less experimental, with some more hardcore and punk infuences, lyrics are mostly kind of social and humorous. They have released one split with Rubufaso Mukufo and new full-lenght release Globální orgie - Striktní protokol. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Opitz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
