Without Virtue is a black metal band in Eastern Pennsylvania. The members include Jon Svenson, Randy King, Derrick Vahlsing, Mike O'Connell, and Dave Remmish. Without Virtue has a number of songs written, and have 3 of their songs recorded on a demo.
