Nouveau Vélo plays healing pop. They hail from the town of Helmond, The Netherlands.

They released three EPs thusfar -- Courgette (2010), the Moestuin EP (2011) and Daze (2013) -- as well as two full-lengths: the self-titled Nouveau Vélo (2014) and Reflections (2017).
