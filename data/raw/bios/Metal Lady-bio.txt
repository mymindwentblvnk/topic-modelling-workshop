A Hungarian Heavy Metal band, fronted by Biró "Metal Lady" IIona (also a photo model and actress). On her 1st album, Metal Lady was supported by band members of the famous Hungarian Metal band: Pokolgép. On her 2nd album she was supported by members another famous Hungarian Metal band: Ossian.
