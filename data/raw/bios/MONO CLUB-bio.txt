Mono Club are a London, UK, band formed by John Herbert, Jake Bowser, Kyle Hall, Nicky Francis & Daniel Bell John. Names and faces that maybe familiar to some, for Mono Club are the new musical endeavour of the erstwhile Goldheart Assembly members.

They released the track Sky High and Submarine from their debut album mixed by Dave Eringa.
