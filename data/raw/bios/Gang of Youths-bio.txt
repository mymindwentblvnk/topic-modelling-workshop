Gang of Youths are an Australian rock band based out of Sydney, New South Wales. The band is the brainchild of vocalist/guitarist/lyricist David Le'aupepe, who formed the band in 2012. Their debut album, The Positions, was released in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gang+of+Youths">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
