Gaia's Vestige is a Melodic Black Metal band from Italy formed in 2000. They are currently unsigned but have ceased to do tours and are currently active. In 2001 they released the single "Winter Is The Law" from then on impressed the fans. Then, in 2006-2007 "Sekai No Ato (La Traccia del Mondo)" was released. However, albums are still waiting to be released until this one gets a reputable status in the underground Black Metal scene. The lyrical themes are interpreted as nature-oriented Black Metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gaia%27s+Vestige">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
