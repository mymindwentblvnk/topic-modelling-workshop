Since the age of 17, he has been working as a DJ. In 1999, his single "Welcome to the Club" was a success, and he got on with "Living on a Better World." Today he produces techno sounds in a German club, called Kinki Palace in Sinsheim, mostly on Friday and Saturday nights, with other DJ's (DJ Shog, DJ Stewart, DJ Rocco etc.).
