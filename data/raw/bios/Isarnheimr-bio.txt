A Norwegian Black Metal band by "Isarnheimr" (Guitars, Bass, Drums, Keyboards) and "Ravngud" (Vocals).

The band released two demo's (Svartahaf in 2001 and Ask in 2002) and split up after. However, they became active again in 2005 and released their self-titled debut in 2006. Currently, the band is still active but information about any upcoming releases is unkown.
