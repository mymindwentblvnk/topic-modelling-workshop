Keisha Shontelle is known for her versatility as a singer, a song writer and also her ability to deliver direct lyrics as a rapper. Beginning in 1998 her music career began to blossom. She began recording her own songs with original beats. To date she has worked with 9th Wonder of Little Brother, Little Brother, Freeway, Ness, Buckshot and many more <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Keisha+Shontelle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
