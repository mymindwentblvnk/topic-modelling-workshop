Graveheart is a thrash/death metal band from Massachusetts, United States.  They were formed when Blistered Earth changed their name.

In 2007, they released Return of the Curse of the Creature's Ghost on their bandcamp page, https://graveheart.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Graveheart">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
