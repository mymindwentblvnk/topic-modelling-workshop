BOUND TO PREVAIL is a death metal band from Birkirkara, Malta.

Bound to Prevail's brand of Death Metal is influenced by a wide variety of extreme music sub-genres, with each band member bringing their own distinct influences to the songwriting process. Bound to Prevail's core sound fuses aggressive, frantic, pounding rhythmic passages, accentuated by instantly recognisable melodic overtones.
