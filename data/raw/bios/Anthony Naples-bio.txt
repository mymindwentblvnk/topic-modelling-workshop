Anthony Naples is a producer and DJ based in Brooklyn, New York, with releases on Mister Saturday Night, Trilogy Tapes, Rubadub, and his own Proibito label. He has provided remixes for Special Request, !!!, Eric Copeland, Lust for Youth, and Four Tet (with whom he has toured). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anthony+Naples">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
