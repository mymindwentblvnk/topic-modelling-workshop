A heavy metal band that's from Melbourne, Victoria, Australia and formed in 1981.  It was formed by  Mike Logan and Mike Handcock.  Their current lineup consists of  Glen Donaghy on vocals,  Mike Handcock on guitar,  Joe Tilli on bass & guitars,  Bruce Kerr on bass and  Patrick Kuhtze.  They released their first mysterious full-length album in 1983 called Battle Fatigue.  They released their more known 2nd full-length Power Palace in 1985.  They disbanded in 1987.  However, they reunited in 2015.  In the year 2016, they released their 3rd full-length album  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Charley+Browne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
