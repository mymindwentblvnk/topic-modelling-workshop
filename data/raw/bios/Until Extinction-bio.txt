Until Extinction began as a studio project to record songs written by guitarist Jake Thiele, and has since blossomed into what one could call an actual band. Their first full-length album "Lament" has been realeased as of March 12, 2010. Shows and similar mayhem to follow. 

Band members

Jake Thiele - Keys
Josh Spalding - Vocals
Angelic Hobart - Vocals
Kevin Dowler - Drums
Sean Nickrent - Bass
