VoidCeremony is a progressive death metal / black metal band from Ramona, California.  They were formed in the summer of 2013 by Garrett Johnson and Jon Reider after disbanding Portalgeist.  The band consists of  Garrett Johnson (vocals, guitars), Jon Reider (vocals, guitars), I. Mann (bass) and C. Koryn (drums).  C. Koryn originally only played drums live in 2018, but he is now a full-fledged member of the band.  VoidCeremony is currently signed to 20 Buck Spin Records.  Their lyrical themes include dark spiritualism, anti-religion, occultism and philosophy.  For more on them, visit the following links:
