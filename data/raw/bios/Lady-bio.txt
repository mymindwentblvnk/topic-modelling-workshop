1) Lady is a retro-R&B band with soul superstar Nicole Wray. Their self-titled debut album was released on Truth & Soul records on March 5, 2013. The album is produced by soul veterans Leon Michels and Jeff Silverman who have also recorded with Aloe Blacc and Lee Fields. Terri Walker sings on the album and is featured in early interviews, but has left the band in July 2013 to focus on solo work.

2) LADY, born Shameka Brown, is an American female rapper known for the hit singles "Yankin", "Twerk" and "Pussy".

3.) Lady is a Japanese singer. 

4.) Lady (레이디))was a South Korean pop group, noted as the first transgender group from that country. The band consisted of Sinae, Sahara, Binu and Yuna. They officially disbanded in early 2007.
