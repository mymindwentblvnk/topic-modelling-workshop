There are two artists named "Sabi."

1.  Sabi, aka Jenice (Sab-bion) Dena Portlock, was one half of hip-hop duo The Bangz. When her partner in The Bangz suffered a serious injury, Sabi began working on her own, gaining notice due to a number of high-profile featured artists spots with Cobra Starship and Britney Spears, with whom she also appeared on tour.
