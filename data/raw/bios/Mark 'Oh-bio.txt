Mark 'Oh is a German dance producer. Mark 'Oh was born on the 23th of June in Dorsten, Germany. His real name is Marko Albrecht. He is most known for his nineties dance hits "Tears Don't Lie" and "Fade To Grey". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mark+%27Oh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
