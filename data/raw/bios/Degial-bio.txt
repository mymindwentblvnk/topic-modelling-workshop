Degial are a death metal band from Uppsala, Sweden that formed in 2004 under the name Degial of Embos before changing their name to Degial in 2006. They released their debut album Death's Striking Wings in June 2012 and are currently signed with Sepulchral Voice Records. Their third full-length album "Predator Reign" was released on November 24, 2017.

Discography:
- Awakening from Darkness - Demo (2006) 
- Death and Darkness Buries All.... - EP (2010)
- Death's Striking Wings - LP (2012)
- Savage Mutiny - LP (2015)
- Predator Reign - LP (2017)
