Harmahis is yet another side project of Senmuth. It is a two-piece band consisting of Senmuth and russian musician Lefthander. Both band members contribute to the writing process, but Lefthander is in charge of lyrical and vocal duties. Harmahis released their debut album "Occurrence" in 2007.
