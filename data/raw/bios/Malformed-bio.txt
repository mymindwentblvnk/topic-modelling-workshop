There is more than one band called Malformed

1. A 3 piece Brutal Death Metal/Grindcore band from Mexico that features past and present members of  Shoggoth, Vikingo, Traumator, Tronator, Necroincarnate, Wartormentor, Matanza. Formed in 2005 and originally known as Totenkopf (2001-2005), they independently released their full length debut in 2013 entitled, Unholy Genocide. 
 

2. a 5 piece Blackened Death Metal band from Rochester, New York that formed in March of 2009.

Malformed can also refer to:

An old school death metal band from Zagreb formed in 2000.
