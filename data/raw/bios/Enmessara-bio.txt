Enmessara (actually spelled Enmesarra, the Summerian God of the Underworld) is a Gothic Metal band from Sisak (Croatia) formed in 2000. Current lineup includes only two members: Slaven Sencar (Vocals) and Sanijel Saric (Guitars & Programming). They refuse to play live and are only concentrated on recording studio albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enmessara">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
