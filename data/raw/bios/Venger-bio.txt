Venger is the current project of Paul Devine, a Glaswegian singer / songwriter / producer currently located in Cleveleys, UK. The project began in late 2012. 

Originally playing in local bands and acoustic nights around Glasgow, Paul previously released music (including collaborations) under the name Jeemer. 

Venger's music spans a range of music ranges from acoustic to ambient, electronic to industrial. Influenced not only by other bands, but film and other works of art. 

More information is available at Paul's personal site, www.paulietheboss.com/media/, which includes links to Bandcamp and Soundcloud pages. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Venger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
