Biografía:

Barcelona's Groove/Death Metal band Arcanus present their new album, 'Omen', after two years of sweat, road and experience. 'Omen' develops the ideas shown in 'Ashes' (which was qualified as the second best EP of the year by the prestigious magazine Mondosonoro in 2016) and takes them to a darker and more melodic sound.
Estilo: 
Death-groove Metal
