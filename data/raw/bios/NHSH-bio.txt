NHSH (pronounced Nahash) means in its primordial form: to discover, decipher or to attain. NHSH represents the will to knowledge. Faith is the most dangerous virtue of the weak and hope builds the fetters of their eternal slavery. NHSH, symbolized by the serpent, stands for personal freedom and development of the individuality as opposed to all herd mentality.
