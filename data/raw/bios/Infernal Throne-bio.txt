Infernal Throne is a death metal/grindcore band from Ashland, Oregon. They take influence from various grindcore, black metal and brutal death metal bands.
The band played their final show on February 27th, 2011, before going on hiatus while Lucas, the guitarist, goes to Arizona for college. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infernal+Throne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
