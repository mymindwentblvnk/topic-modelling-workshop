Feuernacht are an Austrian black metal band from Tyrol, formed in 2004. The band's name is a German phrase that roughly translates into "night of the fire". The band members prefer to remain anonymous. 

Discography
▶ 2012 - Kampf Macht Frei! (full-length)
▶ 2014 - Wehrwolf Jugend (split with Adoria and Stahlfront)
▶ 2017 - Terror, Tod und Teufel (split with Blutaar and Blutkult)
