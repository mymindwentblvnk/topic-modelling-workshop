WarnerBeast is an album orientated project lead by David Warner and incorporates a lineup of talented Guest and Studio musicians.  Drawing on aspects of many genres, the music introduces a mix that will appeal to both Metal and Rock fans.   The tales of Science Fiction, Fantasy, and Humanity touch on stories both new and old. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Warnerbeast">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
