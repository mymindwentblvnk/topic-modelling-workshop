Aeternus Prophet is a death metal band from Fastiv, Ukraine.  They formed in the year 2010.  They have released two demos and two full-length albums.  They currently consist of Veritas (lead guitar, bass, vocals),  Oberon (rhythm guitar, samples, lyrics) and Dessident (drums, vocals).  Their lyrical themes include hate, psychology and social themes.  They are not signed to a record label.
