"..a piece of genius. The beats, the arrangements, the vocals, the twists and turns. so much better than 99.9% of facile dance by numbers we normally hear it's embarrassing... J.Viewz for President!" Rennie Pilgrem - IDJ MAG, June 2010

J.Viewz is the multi-genre electronic project of Brooklyn (est in Israel) based producer Jonathan Dagan. 
With the collaboration of many diverse musicians in the studio, J.Viewz presents an organic blend of Broken-beat & chill.
