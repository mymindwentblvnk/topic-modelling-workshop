Forge of Clouds are now a 3 piece post-metal, sludge from Silesia, Poland. They released a self-titled full length album in 2011, which is available as a free download @ http://forgeofclouds.bandcamp.com/.

On 2nd November 2013 they released their second album, Ordinary Death, also available on their bandcamp site. 


Members:
Jakub Hanusa - drums
Wojciech Lichota - guitar, voc
Filip Dabrowski - bass
