Ultreia is an amazing Folk Metal band from Madrid (Spain), features Melodic Metal-Folk. Its style of music has been widely recognized on many heavy metal websites in Spain. Ultreia is Charo (vocals), Juan Revenga (guitar), Miguel Sánchez (bass), Claudio Sánchez (guitar) , David Alonso ( bagpipes, flutes) and Dennis (drums). The band's aggressive guitars ,bass and drums find a counterpart in Charo's melodic vocals and David's celtic sounds, lyrics searching for identity or struggling with feelings of life and death,light and darkness, history , philosophy and ecology. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ultreia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
