Dollar Llama is a Hard Rock band formed in 2002 at Lisbon, Portugal.
Their first album, UNDER THE HURRICANE, was released at April 13, 2009.

Tiago Simões - Vocals
Zé Dinis - Bass
João Arsénio - Drums
Hugo Jacinto - Guitar
