Finn Keane, better known by his stage name easyFun, is a music producer and songwriter from the United Kingdom. easyFun is known for his work with Charli XCX, having been credited on 2017's mixtapes Pop 2 and Number 1 Angel, as well as 2019's Charli as a producer and remix artist. 

easyFun's other work has included releases on experimental pop label PC Music, collaborations with A. G. Cook as the duo easyFX, and writing for musicians such as Rita Ora and PC Music's own, Hannah Diamond.

easyFun's first release was a five track self-titled EP, easyFun - EP, in 2013. It is considered to be a proto-version of what would become PC Music's signature sound of bubblegum pop, futuristic riffs and chaotic arrangements. 

Since then, he has released a second EP, 2015's Deep Trouble,  and three singles, 2016's Monopoly (featuring Noonie Bao), 2017's Blink, and 2018's Be Your USA (featuring Iiris), with the largest portion of his output since then arriving in the form of collaborative projects. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Easyfun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
