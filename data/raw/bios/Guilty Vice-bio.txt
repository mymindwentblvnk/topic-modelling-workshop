Thrash metal band from Japan, formed in 1991. Guilty Vice has released their first full-length studio album, titled "Pleasure of Mask", in 1993. In 1997, "Leave Here" followed on Bandai Records. There is not much known about them, except the fact that the band featured guitarist Shinji Tatsugami from Rosenfeld. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GUILTY+VICE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
