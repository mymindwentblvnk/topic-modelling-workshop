Hansen Band was founded for a movie called "Keine Lieder über Liebe". The band consists of Thees Uhlmann (Tomte), Marcus Wiebusch (Kettcar), Felix Gebhard (home of the lame), Max Martin Schröder (der "Hund Marie" from Olli Schulz & der Hund Marie) and Jürgen Vogel (a German actor, who plays the band's vocalist). Hansen Band basically is a Grand Hotel van Cleef (a German indie-label) all-star band. The album was recorded alongside the making of the movie called "Keine Lieder über Liebe".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hansen+Band">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
