Bloody Climax is a heavy metal band formed in Schweinfurt, Bavaria (Germany). They have only one published album - "Back to the Wall" (1985).

Last known line-up:

Matthias Müller - vocals
Frank Heller - guitars (Vendetta)
Daniel Loesch - guitars
Holger Volk - bass
