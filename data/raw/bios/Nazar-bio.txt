There are two artists named Nazar:

1) Nazar is a 26-year-old Angolan producer who grew up in Belgium until his late teens, when he returned after the civil war and is now based in Manchester. 

He is signed to Kode9's Hyperdub records and released his debut solo album Guerilla in March 2020.

2) Nazar (civil name; Ardalan Afshar) was born on the 20th september of 1984 in Therean, Iran.  He's a austrian rapper and musicvideo producer currently living in Vienna.

He released his first studio album "Kinder des Himmels" in july 2008.
