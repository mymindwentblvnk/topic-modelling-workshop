Death Note was a brutal death metal band from Indonesia active between 2005 and 2008 when they changed name to Dazecortica.

The soundtrack Death Note (デスノート, Desu Nōto) is NOT an artist, so please re-tag your music files CORRECTLY.

The 1st OP and ED themes the WORLD and アルミナ (Alumina) are by the Japanese band ナイトメア(Nightmare).
The 2nd OP and ED themes What's up, people?! and 絶望ビリー (Zetsubou Billy) are by the Japanese band マキシマム ザ ホルモン(Maximum The Hormone)
Misa Misa songs are by 平野綾(Aya Hirano).

The anime soundtrack is composed by タニウチヒデキ(Hideki Taniuchi), with orchestral tracks by 平野義久(Yoshihisa Hirano).
