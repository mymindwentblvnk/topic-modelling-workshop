Operadyse is a symphonic power metal band from France that was formed in 2007. Consisting of Damien Marco (guitar), Stéphane Lambert (bass), Emmanuel Colombier (drums), and Bastien Sablé (keyboards).

The band is, as of March 23rd 2011, searching for a new singer, the former singer was Jennifer Lassalle. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Operadyse">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
