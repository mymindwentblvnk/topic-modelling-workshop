There are 2 bands using the name DECEASE:

1. A thrash/death metal band from Romania (2011-present)
2. A death metal band from Estonia (1992-1996)

1. Decease is a politically-themed thrash metal band from Cluj-Napoca, Romania. 
The members are:
Radu Vulpe - Guitars\Vocals
Catalin Vulpe - Bass
George Alb - Drums

The band released an full-length called  "Exhort To Obliterate" 2013.
