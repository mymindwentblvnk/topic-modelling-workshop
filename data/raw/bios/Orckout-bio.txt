OrckOut is a Thrash Metal band from Barra Bonita, Brazil. They formed in 2004 and have released one EP called "Involution" so far. The band plans to record a second CD in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/OrckOut">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
