Visceral Deity is a Glasgow based Death Metal band that was formed in 2006 After a number of line-up changes they found the right members to form a driven, active band.

During this time they have recorded and self released 3 Demos and are currently writing material for an album.

They are dedicated to pushing themselves musically and to always put on an intense and entertaining live show.
