Cinema Red And Blue is an indie pop collaboration led by David Feck with band members from Comet Gain, Crystal Stilts, The Soft City and Pale Lights with help by Gary Olson of The Ladybug Transistor , Hamish Kilgour from The Clean  and Amy Linton of  Henry's Dress  and The Aislers Set . In 2010 they released a selftitled LP on What's Your Rupture? The album consists of original songs as well as covers of The Chills , Julian Cope , Dead Moon , Len Bright Combo  and Vic Godard  songs
