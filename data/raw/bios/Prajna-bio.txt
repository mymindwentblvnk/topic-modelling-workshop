There are two bands by this name; a French dub/electronica band, and a heavy/speed metal band from Colombia.

Colombian Prajna:

Prajna is a heavy metal band from Bogotá, Colombia. It was formed in early 2010 by Andrés Murillo and Sebastián “Rocha” Quintero. They brought Mauricio "Bassman" Tovar and Javier E. Navarrete on bass guitar and rhythm guitar, respectively.

A few weeks later they began recording their first EP.

The band play a mix of classic heavy, power and speed metal resembling groups such as Iron Maiden and Helloween, but with a few speed and thrash metal influences.

Their first EP entitled Lost in the Void was released on 2011.
