This 5-piece metal band from Dayton, USA shows great energy. Their "epic" thrash metal and entertaining riffs draw in lovers of many metal styles. In response to their rapidly expanding fan base, BVD have canceled production on their unreleased EP and are in the process of writing a full length release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brother+Von+Doom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
