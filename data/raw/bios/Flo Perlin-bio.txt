Flo Perlin is a contemporary folk singer-songwriter from London, UK, with Jewish Iraqi and Belarusian heritage. Writing music since she was 9 years old, Flo released her debut album, 'Cocooned' in 2017 recorded in her living room in London and an EP 'Stories' 2019, recorded at The Premises Studio, London. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flo+Perlin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
