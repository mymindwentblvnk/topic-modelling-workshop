Welkin Dusk is an anarchist blackened thrash/crust band with members of Expendable Youth and Abrade. They formed in Chicago in mid 2010. Their influences include black metal, crust, and thrash. They released their first 4 song S/T demo tape in Sept. of 2010 and In June 2011 they released their first full length, Born Into A Dying World. Both can be downloaded for free at http://welkindusk.bandcamp.com. A split 7" with Ramlord came out in 2013. You can hear that at http://stimulationaddict.bandcamp.com/
