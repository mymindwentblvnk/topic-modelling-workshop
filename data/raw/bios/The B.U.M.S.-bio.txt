The B.U.M.S from Oakland are Evocalist & D. Wyze. "The Bums" name was given to them by King Tech. When Tech brought the group in to get signed, D. Wyze came up with the acronym on the spot when asked what it meant. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+B.U.M.S.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
