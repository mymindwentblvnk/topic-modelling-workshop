Children Of Tragedy is a Canadian metalcore band which in 2004 released their self-titled EP. In 2005 they toured with Into Eternity, Byzantine, Baghead, Halo Of Locuts and showed their big music talent. They are known especially thanks to "End of Days" song. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Children+Of+Tragedy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
