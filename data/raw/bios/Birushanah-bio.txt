Birushanah/毘盧釈那 is an experimental sludge/doom metal band from Osaka, Japan. As well as traditional Japanese music - which is a notable aspect of their sound - the band cites Neurosis, Swans, Zeni Geva, and Corrupted (whom bassist Sougyo previously performed with) as influences.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Birushanah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
