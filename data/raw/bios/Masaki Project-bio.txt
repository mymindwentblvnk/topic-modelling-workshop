Instrumental rock/metal solo project from Masaki (Animetal, Jacks 'n Joker, Shy Blue, Canta, Zigoku Quartet). His first full-length studio album, named "Modern Day Crossover", was released in 1999. "Universal Syndicate" followed in 2006. Other band members next to Masaki include: Syu on guitar (Animetal, Aushvitz, Galneryus, Valkyr, Spinalcord) and Katsuji Kirita on drums (Animetal, Volcano, Gargoyle, Metalfolk, Syu, Naoto Shibata, Kariostro, The Cro-Magnons, Kuroyume, Ladies Room, Japametal). Each album feature alot of guest musicians, like Marty Friedman, Kyoji Yamamoto, Circuit.V.Panther, Munetaka Higuchi, Akira Jimbo and many more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Masaki+Project">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
