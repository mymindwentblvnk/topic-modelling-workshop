Hungarian stoner/groove/thrash metal band founded in 2005 on the ruins of the band Riuros when Endre Tarjányi joined in vocals. Guitars: Balázs Kemencei (also member of Wall Of Sleep), Drums: Boldizsár Ifjú, Bass: Szilárd (Troll) Kamarás. In 2006 the band won the first prize of Sziget Festival's Talentum contest.

released stuff:
2005 - Joygrind demo
2006 - Bonus Thrash demo
2007 - Redneck Blues
2010 - The Fellowship Of The Steel EP
2012 - Inherited Fever
