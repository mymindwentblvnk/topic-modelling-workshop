Serenity's Malice was a four piece death metal band from Valdosta, Georgia, USA. Formed in 2004, the band released the album 'Call of the Warrior' in 2006. They blended Celtic elements with traditional death metal. Their songs had lyrical content focused on Scottish Independence, pride, warfare and oppression by the English. In 2010, Serenity's Malice split up.

Serenity's Malice was:

Lord Malphas - Guitar & Vocals
