Dark Leader is Black Metal band which formed in winter of 2009.

Dark Leader is NOT related to any political thought nor religions.

Dark Leader goal is making Black Art, and nothing else.


Line-up:

Serpentine : Vocals

Darkle : Bass

Radin : Guitar

Atron : Drums
