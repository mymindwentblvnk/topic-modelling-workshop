Sharatan was a power metal band from Germany (Vachdorf, Thuringia), formed in 1997. They have released two demos: "Rehearsal" in 1998 and "First Dawn", a year later. The band disbanded shortly after "First Dawn", because of personal differences of its members.

Last known line - up: 

Vocals - Thomas Helm
Guitars - Thomas and Frank
Bass -  Mario
Drums - Manfred.
