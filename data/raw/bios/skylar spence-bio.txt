Skylar Spence, formerly known as Saint Pepsi, is the musical project of Long Island electronic music producer Ryan DeRobertis. He started out making vaporwave and sample-based house music, but has recently drifted towards disco pop music that features his own vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skylar+Spence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
