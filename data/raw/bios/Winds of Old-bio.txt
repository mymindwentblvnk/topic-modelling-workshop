Winds Of Old is a Black Metal band hailing from the United States. Saadvr is the sole entity behind the project. (Saadvr is Justin from Hills Of Sefiroth) Winds Of Old "was formed as an expression of black metal aesthetic not found in Hills of Sefiroth." Thus far there is only a 3 song EP available which can only be obtained through downloading. It is entitled "Enter Thy Ethereal Night." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Winds+Of+Old">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
