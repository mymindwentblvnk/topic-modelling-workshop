There are three bands named Jughead:

1.  Jughead is a rock band consisting of Ty Tabor (guitar), Derek Sherinian (keyboards), Matt Bissonette (bass), and Gregg Bissonette (drums). Jughead's catalog consists of a single self-titled album released by InsideOut Music in 2002.

The band's sound has been categorized as "progressive pop music". As with most of Ty Tabor's music, it is guitar-driven and generally upbeat.

2.  Jughead is also a band established in 1989 in Toronto, Ontario that plays a fast-paced mixture of country and bluegrass.  They bill themselves as "the original motorgrass band" and are known for their "Hockey Song" as well as covers of rock songs such as Ozzy's "Crazy Train", Motorhead's "Ace of Spades" and The Ramone's "I Wanna Be Sedated".

3. Jughead is a acoustic band from Santa Rosa California. They play a style of music they call "punk-grass". Jughead's music consists of acoustic guitar, washtub bass, accordion, saw, and flute.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jughead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
