Hailing from Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Eternal Crossing is a brazilian metal band formed in 2004. In 2005 they released their first demo, intitled "Open the Eyes", which allowed their firsts appearances in the local underground scene. After several line up changes, they put out their first full length album Until The Last Drop in 2008. Following more line up changes and the tour in Minas Gerais and nearby states in support of their debut album, the band started to write new stuff. In 2013 they changed their name to Cauvery and put out a new EP, called WE ARE THE PLAGUE.
