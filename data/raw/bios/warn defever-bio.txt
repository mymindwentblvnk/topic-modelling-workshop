Warn DeFever is the founding member of the long running experimental rock band His Name Is Alive, and was also the founder of the record label Time Stereo (http://www.timestereo.com).

He's known for going under various pseudonyms, and is also a member of numerous other bands including The Dirteaters (The Dirt Eaters), Elvis Hitler, and the avant-garde noise / performance group Princess Dragonmom (Princess Dragon-mom). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Warn+Defever">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
