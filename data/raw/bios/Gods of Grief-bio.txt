Swedish Death Metal. Original take on the 'Swedish style' with quite melodic/doom elements.
Formed by Konstantin Papavassilou and Thomas Åberg (A Mind Confused, Kaamos, Dead Congregation) the band recorded 5 tracks under the name 'Pagan Sanctum' that were never released, after changing their name to 'Gods Of Grief' they recorded 3 tracks for the demo 'In The Shadows' which was released in 1993.

Members:
