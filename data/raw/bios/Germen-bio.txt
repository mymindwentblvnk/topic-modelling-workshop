Germen was an early incarnation of what would later become the well known Spanish goregrind band Tu Carne. Originally formed 1991 in Valencia, Valencian Community, Germen only put out two releases; the "Te Pudres" demo tape in 1993 and  3-way split CD with Tu Carne and Agony Terror System in 2003. It is unknown when officially ended. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Germen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
