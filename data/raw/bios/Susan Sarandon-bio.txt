Susan Sarandon (born October 4, 1946) is an Academy Award-winning American actress.  In 1974, she appeared in The Rocky Horror Picture Show, a cult classic.   Sarandon received four Academy Award nominations in the 1990s, finally winning in 1996 for Dead Man Walking. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Susan+Sarandon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
