Warminister was a melodic power/speed metal band from Dayton, Ohio, USA. They formed in the mid '80s and released only one album ever, the "Out of the Ashes" EP, in 1986 before disbanding. Scott Wallace - Vocals,Benny Bodine - Guitar ,Chris Scott Weiser - Guitar, Keyboards, Matt O'Shaughnessy - Bass, Deron Castle - Drums.
