Prague music band Fire In The Hall was founded in the beginning of the year 2006. Its music style lies on the stint between crossover-metal and crossover-rock. Actual members FITH (after personal changes) in the middle of the year 2009 are:

Tomáš Brtník /Thomas BROWN BEAR (URSUS ARCTOS ARCTOS)/ - kytara (guitar)

Miroslav Čáp /Peaceglory STORK/ - kytara (guitar)

Petr Dvořáček /Peter SMALLCOURTMAN/ - baskytara (bassguitar)

Kryštof Hon /Christopher HUNT/ - bicí (drums, percussion)

Josef Kamiš /Joseph ANHIMA/ - klávesy (keyboards)

Jan Samec /John MALE/  - zpěv (vocal front man)

Zuzana Petrová /Susan PETER´s DAUGHTER/ - housle (violin and thrum)
