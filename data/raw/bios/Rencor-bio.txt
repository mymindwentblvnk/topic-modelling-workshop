Rencor is a progressive/deathgrind band from Lithuania,Panevezys.The band was found in the late autumn of 2007 by Drummer Justinas and Guitarist Paulius.In summer of 2008 Rencor finnaly began rehearsing.Thats when the first songs like (Get Dog,Mes begam kur nereikia,Hate Symphony) were writen.In August,2008 Rencor recorded theyr first demo called “Dark side of the Virgin”.In October the band began rehearsing in one of the local bands studio.As time moved on the band went into more experimental,jazz and death metal influenced sound full of weird song structures,riffs and beats.In late winter of 2009 Rencor started recording theyr first album.The album was re-recorded many times to get the best sound and performances. Now they have bassist Donatas and they rehearsing in new studio.

Rencor yra Lietuvos progressive/deathgrind grupe is Panevezio miesto. Grupe susikure 2007 metu rudens pabaigoje. Ja ikure bugninkas Justinas ir gitaristas Paulius. 2008 vasara Rencor pradejo repetuoti. Tada pradetos kurti dainos (Get Dog,Mes begam kur nereikia, Hate Symphony). 2008 rudeni grupe irase ju pirmaji demo pavadinimu “Dark side of the Virgin”. Gruodi Rencor pradejo groti repetuoti vietines grupes studijoje. Po kiek laiko grupe perejo i daugiau ekspermentiska jazz ir death metal daugumai ju dainu. Žiemos pabaigoje Rencor pradejo irasineti savo pirmaji albuma. Albumas buvo perrasytas begale kartu siekiant geriausio garso ir susigrojimo. Dabar prie ju prisijunge bosistas Donatas ir jie repetuoja naujoje patalpoje.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rencor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
