Teethmarks are a 4-piece sludge influenced stoner metal band from Toronto, Ontario Canada. To date, they have 2 releases; a 4-song ep titled Feral (2012) and a full length titled Survival (2014). Both releases are available @ http://teethmarks.bandcamp.com/ For more information, visit: https://www.facebook.com/teethmarksband

Members:

Graham Christian - Vocals
