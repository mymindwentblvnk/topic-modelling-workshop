Heavy metal band from Itzehoe, Germany. Remaining members of the band Ritual Steel formed Titan Steele after the Ritual Steel -drummer Martin Zellman quit the band and took the rights to the band name with him. So far they have released one full-length album, "The Force" in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Titan+Steele">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
