Amoth is a unique epiphony from Helsinki, Finland. The band combines influences from traditional heavy metal, progressive metal, thrash and even Jazz alongside classical music to their blend of elements that is their debut album entitled "Crossing Over", which was released through Inverse Records 5th of October 2011.

At the moment Amoth is writing and rehearsing new material and also doing gigs around Finland. They now have two guitar players instead of one. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
