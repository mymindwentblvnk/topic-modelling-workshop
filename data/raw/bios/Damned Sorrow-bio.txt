Damned Sorrow is a Tunisian band that plays Death Metal and presisely "The Damned Metal" a style that gathers many other styles, a style that was created by the band! For now the band have a Demo named "Faith To Death" made in 2006, that contains 4 tracks which are: "8 (infinity)" , "Dead End" , "Damned Sorrow" and "Subliminal Aphaisa".
The band was born in 2003.

Current Line-Up:

Mehdi Ayari: Vocals 
Ahmed Chanoufi: Lead Guitar
Aymen Khalsi: Bass Guitar
Aziz Ben Abdallah: rhythmic guitar
Tarek Ghamgui: Drums
