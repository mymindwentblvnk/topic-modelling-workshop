D.V.C. (Darth Vader’s Church) played grinding death metal from Florida. They Released a couple demos in 1989 and their first full-length ‘Descendant Upheaval’ the same year, a follow-up full-length titled ‘Molecular Shadow’ was released in 1992. They released nothing else since and have split-up.

Band lineup was Parker Knapp (vocals, guitar), Glenn Lawhon (vocals, guitar), Alain Rodgers (bass), Todd Thompson (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D.V.C.">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
