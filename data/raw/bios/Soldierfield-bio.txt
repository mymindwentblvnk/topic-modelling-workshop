Soldierfield formed in late 2011 and released the debut EP ”Bury The Ones We Love” in October 2012.
 
Leigh Oates - Vocals (Order of Voices, Rise to Addiction)
Andy Trott - Guitars
Steve Wray - Guitars and Programming (BLAZE, Rise to Addiction)
Wayne Banks - Bass 
Jeff Singer - Drums (Paradise Lost, BLAZE, Kill II This)
