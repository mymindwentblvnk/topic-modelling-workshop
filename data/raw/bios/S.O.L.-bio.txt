S.O.L. are a hatecore / rac band from Poland. The band is a project of Adam from Konkwista 88. (S.O.L. a.k.a. Save Our Land is Hatecore band from USA, it's a different project than this)

S.O.L. is also an alias of german techno/trance producer Oliver Lieb.
