Moon Diagrams is the solo project of Moses Archuleta (drummer and co-founder of Deerhunter). 

He announced his debut album as Moon Diagrams called Lifetime Of Love on May 4th 2017. To be co-released on June 30, 2017 by Geographic North and Sonic Cathedral Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moon+Diagrams">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
