Noah Georgeson (born in San Anselmo, CA, on 19 January 1975) is a Grammy Award-winning American musician and producer. Previously a member of the San Francisco-based indie band The Pleased (from 2002-2006), he is most well-known for his production work with Joanna Newsom (Milk-Eyed Mender), Devendra Banhart (Cripple Crow), Mason Jennings, and Bert Jansch.  'Find Shelter', Georgeson's debut solo album, recorded in San Francisco and Nevada City between 1999 and 2003, was released on 28 November 2006 on Plain Recordings.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Noah+Georgeson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
