Marcus D was born in Auburn, a lower class suburb 30 miles south of Seattle. A melodically adept music producer heavily influenced by early jazz musicians and Japanese producers such as Nujabes and DJ Deckstream, he creates captivating and beautiful music revered by hip-hop enthusiasts and non hip-hop enthusiasts alike. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marcus+D">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
