Miami's First Lady of Soul.
A lesser known Southern soul singer, Helene Smith recorded a handful of songs for various Miami labels, most notably for Deep City. She is often compared to Betty Wright, although Helene's delivery was usually slower, more deliberate. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Helene+Smith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
