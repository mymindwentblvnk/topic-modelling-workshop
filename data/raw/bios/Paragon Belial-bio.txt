Paragon Belial is a black metal band from Germany formed in 1994. They got their name from a Darkthrone song.

Discography

Hordes of the darklands (Demo, 1994)
Hordes of the Darklands (Full-length, 1996)
Believe Was Punished by God (Demo, 2001)
Nosferathu Sathanis (Full-length, 2008)
