There are six groups with this name:

1.) Death / Thrash Metal band from Caracas, Venezuela. Formed in late 1988 with the participation of Enzo Querci (guitar and vocals), Diego Fuentes (bass) and Julio Martinez (drums).  Presumed to be one of Venezuela's earliest extreme metal bands, along with Baphometh.

After their last concert in 1996, the band split-up.

2.) Thrash Metal band from Bogota, Colombia. They recorded a demo in 1993 called "Dies Irae".

3.) Thrash Metal band from Buenos Aires, Argentina, formed in 1986. They recorded a split album in 1990 called «Psicosis Colectiva / El Juicio» with Dr. Jekyll, and a demo in 1994.

4.) Thrash Metal band from Cordoba, Argentina, formed in 1992. They recorded demos in 1994 and 1996.

5.) Death Metal band from Chile, formed in 1989. They recorded a demo in 1991.
