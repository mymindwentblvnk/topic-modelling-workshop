Morbid Path is a melodic death/black metal band from Israel. 

Members:
Cory - Vocals
Yuval - Lead Guitar
looking for- Rhythm Guitar
Ted - Bass
Tomer - Drums


Morbid Path was formed in May 2008. The band released
 their first demo album called "Nightfall" in November of that same year. 
After some line up changes the band started participate in various local metal shows in Israel. 
Today, the band working on their first full length album which suppose to be release in late 2009...stay tuned!
