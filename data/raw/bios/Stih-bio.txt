Stih was a Gothic/Doom metal band from Romania, Suceava, formed in 1996. They have so far released only one Demo, "Shadow Torn", in 2003. The last known line-up consisted of Bogdan Luparu (Bucovina, Piatra), Veronica Luparu (Piatra) and Mihai Bialuca.
