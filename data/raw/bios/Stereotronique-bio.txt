Stereotronique, real name Kevin O’Brien, is a Producer, DJ & Vocalist from Calgary, AB, currently residing in Toronto, ON.
Kevin’s musical background originally consisted of playing in bands as a drummer / guitarist / singer before discovering his love of electronic music production / DJing.

Since 2017, Kevin has been releasing under the alias Kayobe. His music under this new alias is described, “A progressive sonic storm of ambient, evocative music, Kayobe invites both the brave and faint-of-heart to envelop themselves in his bold art, and perhaps in the process, discover something special.” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stereotronique">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
