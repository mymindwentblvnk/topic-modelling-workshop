Black metal from Delft, the Netherlands. Since early 2003 Holthor (vocals) and Miikja (guitar) have been writing and recording heaven-assailing Dutch black under the name of BASTAARD. The first demo cd 'Vorst' came out in May 2005. For gigs, members from Carceri are added to the line up.

Download 'Vorst' for free at Bandcamp: http://bastaard.bandcamp.com/
