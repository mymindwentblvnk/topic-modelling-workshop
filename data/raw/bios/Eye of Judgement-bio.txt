Vegan straight edge band from the Netherlands. Adhere to strong beliefs on the subjects of animal rights and environmental issues.

Their lyrics are characterized by strong views on drug free living, veganism and misanthropy. Their music can be described as metal influenced hardcore not unlike Earth crisis and integrity.
