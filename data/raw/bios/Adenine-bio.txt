Adenine was a Canada based melodic death metal band formed in December 1999 by Transcedence guitarist Philippe Coupal, who was soon replaced with Jonathan Phillips. The current line up is:
Martin Quintal(vocs),
Pascal Charron (guitar),
Michael Kosow(guitar),
Christian Choquette(bass),
Mathieu Groloux (drums).
