God Of Rotors, is a band formed in 2007 from Argy (ex-homoiratus, ex-bulldoza, ex-personality crisis).  The band consists of Constantinos (Sklerotikz), George(ex-Ice In Eyes) and Angelos (ex-homoiratus)

The Band REleased its First EP Part of an EP compilation entitled The Grand Codex: Masonus. In spring 2008 the band went off greece for a european tour in several central europe cities playing with local bands. At the moment they are a step before their second studio release which will include all recorded tracks in an English and Greek Version
