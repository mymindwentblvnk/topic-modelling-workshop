There is more than one artist using the name Zero Degree.

1) Zero Degree is the result of various musical experiments with different kinds of music. As interest shifted more and more form pure noise to minimal, ambient and idm structures, zero degree was born. Deep pulsating rhytmic structures combined with fragile soundscapes invite the listener for a trip inside your own mind.

2) Zero Degree is a Melodic Death Metal band from  Nordhausen, Germany. Formed in 2006, Zero Degree released their first album in 2010 entitled "Surreal World". The band is currently unsigned and looking for a label.
