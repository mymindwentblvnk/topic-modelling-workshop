Hollowhecatomb is the new solo project of Jm Sundown who is known in the Milwaukee black metal scene for his work with Owlscry, Shroud of Despondency  and  The Cold Beyond , though he doesn't limit himself to just metal as he's also involved in the neo folk scene with  Sundown  and a noise/experimental project with his wife called,  Temper .
