Wrath Of Despot are a black metal band from Bejing, China. Along with Evilthorn, they are also one of the first Chinese black metal bands performing on stage with corpse painting and self made arm nails.

In 2007, they released their debut, "Black Tomb" with Mort Productions & have appeared previously on numerous black metal compilations. The most notable being "Resurrection of the Gods III" in 2005. 

Current lineup consists of Lord Darkness on Vocals/Guitar, Liu Yu - Guitar, Lu Xiaofan - Bass & Mai - Drums.
