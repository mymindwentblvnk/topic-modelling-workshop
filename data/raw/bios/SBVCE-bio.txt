Sbvce is a producer rapper and singer known for his work with r&b singer and female rapper Baegod. The duo is known for making Genre bending music that spans, Indie Alternative R&B and Electonic pop to Underground Hip Hop and Trap Soul.  His production is versatile, while usually urban centered it spans contemporary R&B stylings with heavy bass and 808 drums to underground Hip Hop, boom bap, Cloud Rap, Abstract Hip Hop, Future r&b, West Coast and Trap Soul.  He is known for being the main production behind Baegod and artist Nate curry, lending his vocals to songs at time. He and Baegod are the founders of Bedroomtrap a network and record label. The pair have been pioneers of the Trap r&b sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SBVCE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
