Formed in 2000 and finally presenting their debut in 2006, Twisted Into Form include guitarist Kaj Gornitzka (ex-Spiral Architect), drummer David Husvik (Extol), bassist Erik Aadland and original Spiral Architect session vocalist Leif J. Knashaug. Together they fuse jazz and tech-metal <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Twisted+Into+Form">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
