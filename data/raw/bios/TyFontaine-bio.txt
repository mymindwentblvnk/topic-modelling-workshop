Julius Roosevelt Terrell, better known by his stage name TyFontaine, is an American rapper and songwriter in the underground rap scene. He’s a member of the record label Internet Money Records and has many connections with popular artists, such as: Lil Tecca, Nick Mira, Taz Taylor, and more.
