Thrash/death metal act from Japan. Clandestined features Nagahide "Okazzy" Okajima (Voidd) on vocals and guitar, Mari (Crucified) on drums, Shihogerion on bass, and Suzuking (Rightbrain, Dead by Dawn) on guitar. The band has released their first full-length studio album, named "Amok Saints In Sodom", on March 27th, 2010, through Busdeath Records. "Ruinatics" followed a year later, on March 23rd, 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CLANDESTINED">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
