Hailing from Russia, Navjarmaahr formed in 2006, and were previously known as Echos.

Since their inception they have released 1 demo, a split album with Ravendark's Monarchal Canticle, and 2 full lengths.  They are currently signed to Russian label Hexenhammer Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Navjarmaahr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
