Nick Di Paolo (b. January 31, 1962) is a American stand-up comedian and writer. Di Paolo has been featured on Comedy Central Presents as well as other Comedy Central shows including Tough Crowd, The Comedy Central Roast of Pamela Anderson, The Comedy Central Roast of Denis Leary, The Comedy Central Roast of Jeff Foxworthy, and The Comedy Central Roast of Larry the Cable Guy.

Di Paolo is also a regular guest on the Opie and Anthony radio show and on the Howard Stern show.
