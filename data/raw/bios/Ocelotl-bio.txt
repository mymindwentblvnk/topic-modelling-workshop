Ocelotl is a progressive melodic death metal/folk metal band Toluca de Lerdo, State of Mexico. Their name comes from Nahua, and indigenous Mexican language, meaning "panther" or "ocelot". Their lyrics center on pre-Hispanic folklore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ocelotl">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
