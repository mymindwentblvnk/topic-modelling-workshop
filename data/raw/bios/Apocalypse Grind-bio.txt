Apocalypse Grind is a brutal death metal band from Bandung, Indonesia. Starting from Rizky Rera Guna Alam (Bass) and Ardian Pratama (Guitar) who invites Galuh Prasetiyo (Drum) to join the band together. They present something fresh with killertunes that will make everyone headbanging and slamming and they will try to keep this era of Death Metal until whenever. They have released their first promo CD ‘Kandeul Kulit Beungeut' (Promo 2011). The band yet has released their debut EP album, containing 6 track entitled “The Birth of Antichrist” in December 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Apocalypse+Grind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
