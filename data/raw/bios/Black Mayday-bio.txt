Black Mayday, A victorian (Australian) Heavy Metal band. The band consists of two current members and multiple on and off members.
Chris Brown (Lead Guitar, Rhythm guitar and Vocals) - Victoria 
Anthony Commane (Lead Guitar, Rhythm guitar and Vocals) - Tasmania
These are the two current and main members of the band. Black Mayday has been around for almost 2 years now and they will continue to keep on rocking. Black Mayday formed as a highschool band and will soon be available to broader venues
Influences: Queen, Masterplan, Metallica, Escape the Fate, Megadeth, Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Scorpions, black sabbath, Dio and many others <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Mayday">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
