Hurtlocker is from United States of America  (Chicago, Illinois) , formed in 2000 and is currently signed with Napalm Records .

Current line up is:

Chase Morgan - Vocals
Tim Moe - Guitar
Justin Jurgevich - Guitar
Chris Djuricic - Bass
Tony Bettenhausen - Drums

Releases:

Suicide (4 Demo) (2002)
Fear in a Handful of Dust (2006)
Embrace The Fall (2007)
