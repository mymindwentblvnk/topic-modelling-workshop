Richard James Hilfiger (born March 28, 1990 in Manhattan, New York), better known simply as Rich Hil, is an American alternative rapper, singer, actor, co-CEO of Young, Rich & Famous and owner of Heart Culture Clothing who is signed to Warner Bros. Records. He has been steadily releasing mixtapes since 2007 and gained online attention when his singles Girls, Sounds & Colors and Trippy, both featuring Kid Cudi, made their way onto many hip hop blog sites. His father is Tommy Hilfiger, the world renowned fashion designer. He lists his musical influences as Jay-Z (who he grew up around because of his fathers ties with the famous rapper), Jimi Hendrix, Kanye West, The Rolling Stones, Nas and Marvin Gaye.
