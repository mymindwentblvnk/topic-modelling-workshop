Mother Crone is a Seattle based outfit that was conceived in 2012 as a platform to explore the journey of our collective consciousness. With solid riffs, powerful vocals, and driving, unconventional rhythms, Mother Crone offers a tasty blend of heavy, soulful, tumultuous rock. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mother+Crone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
