2. Seasons is an unsigned alternative rock back based in Sydney, Australia. The band features Australian Idol finalist Hayley Jensen, who came 4th in the reality series in 2004. Their debut EP 'A Moment of Clarity' was released in April 2011. As of 2013 they are now performing under the name Silver Cities.
