QUIÑ is an electronic artist and singer based in Los Angeles, California, United States. She is responsible for coining the term "fantasy soul", which is the genre she identifies her music under. Her first album, Galactica, was released in 2016. QUIÑ has also been featured as a vocalist on the song "Think About You" by G-Eazy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/QUI%C3%91">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
