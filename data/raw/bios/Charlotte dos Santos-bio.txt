Charlotte Dos Santos (born Charlotte Hall Dos Santos in Oslo, Norway, in 1990 ) is a Brazilian-Norwegian jazz singer, composer, and arranger, currently based in Berlin. Dos Santos released her solo EP Cleo in 2017 on the label Fresh Selects. 

Dos Santos graduated from Berklee College Of Music (in in Boston, MA) with a degree in contemporary writing and production, jazz vocals and art history. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Charlotte+dos+Santos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
