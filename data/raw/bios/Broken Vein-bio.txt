Broken Vein is a German Metalcore band from Weinheim. Formed in 2012, the band is André Steiner (guitar), Raphael Strauß (drums), Manuel Gottfried (vocals), Michael Sommerfeld (guitar), and Michelle Zimmermann (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Broken+Vein">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
