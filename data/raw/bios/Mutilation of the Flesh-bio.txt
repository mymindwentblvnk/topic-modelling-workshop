Mutilation of the flesh is a  slamming deathcore band from Bad Laasphe near Siegen, Germany. The guy playing the bassguitar, called Schüßler, was ones menber of the wellknown Bad Laasphe hardcore band Since The Day. Mutilation Of The Flesh is part of the evolutional process of  upcoming new styles in german hardcore. So this is the result of mixing hardcore with deathmetal influences. However, hardcore will life on this way.
And so the members of the band act, like hardcore bands act since the beginning: We are not the stars on stage, we are fans among fans! The music is the reason!
