Nuclear Thorn was brought into existence in the beginning of 2008 when Morfeus begun to create material for his solo project. To complement of his vision he need the vocalist and united his strenght with session vocalist Setheroth. The collaboration continued and Setheroth joined the band as regular vocalist and guitarisst. After some reheresals they invited Darken on bassist stand. 
Now the band is looking for live drummer.


Line-up:
Setheroth - guitar, voc
Morfeus - guitar
Draken - bass
Astaroth - session drums
