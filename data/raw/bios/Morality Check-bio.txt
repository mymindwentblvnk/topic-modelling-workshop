Morality Check and its current line up was established in September of 2003 as an all original heavy metal band. Lyrically based on mocking the ways society's portrayal of filth. Using musical hooks, they draw an audience of many individuals.
 
 Morality Check has opened for several National  acts such as Staple, Mindless self indulgence, Dope, Mushroomhead,RA, 10,000 Years, Threat Signal, Darkest Hour, Nminic, and Soilwork.
