The melodic death metal side-project of Taiwan-based multi-instrumentalist Ray Heberer IV, he composed 5 songs for a debut EP in early 2011, while he was still 15.  On September 28th, Bloodred Fullmoon released "Winter Solstice" which featured vocal contributions from Jurgen Haindl (Germany) and was mastered by Thomas Eversole (USA). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloodred+Fullmoon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
