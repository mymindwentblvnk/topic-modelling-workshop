Towards Rusted Soil was black metal side project of Hungarian musician - Tamás Kátai (Thy Catafalque, Gire, Gort, Darklight), who performed all instruments and vocals. Only two one-track demos were released: Forsaken in Fog in 2000 and A Landscape Slumbering in 2003 year.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Towards+Rusted+Soil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
