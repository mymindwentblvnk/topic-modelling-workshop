West Sussex, United Kingdom (2011 - present) 


Derelict Dream is a  four piece British progressive doom metal band formed under the name Raven.
Later the band changed their name to Derelict Dream due to a heavy metal band from the 70s that was also called Raven.

The debut album titled "Ridgewood Lake"  was released in 2013 on Spinonthat Records through online stores such as  iTunes and Amazon MP3

The bands influences include; Opeth, Tool, Dream Theater, Megadeth and Meshuggah.
