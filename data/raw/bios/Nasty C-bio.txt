David Junior Ngcobo (born 11 February 1997), better known by his stage name Nasty C, is a South African rapper, songwriter and record producer.He grew up in Durban where he was raised by his father.His mother died when he was only 11 months due to a car accident. He is most known for the single "Way It Go" by DJ Switch featuring Tumi and Youngsta, as well as the well-received remix to his single "Juice Back", which features Davido and Cassper Nyovest. He was the youngest recipient for an award at the 2015 South African Hip Hop Awards, namely the Best Freshman Award. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nasty+C">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
