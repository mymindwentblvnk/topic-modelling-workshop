Panonski Sindrom is a croatian metal band from Slatina, band is formed during 1994.
During the period between 2001 until 2006 band released series of DIY demo albums recorded, mixed and produced at their home:


2001 - LOUDNESS OF SILENCE
2003 - BLIND
2004 - UNSTABLE 
2006 - ALL IN VAIN
2008 - AMBIVALENCE
2011 - COMPLETION

Lineup (in recent times without Darko)

Darko Novacic : Guitar
Mario Novacic : Guitar
Davor Relic : Bass
Leon Relic : Drums
Igor Brkic : Vocals
