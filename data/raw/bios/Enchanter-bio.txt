There are multiple artists using this name.

1.  Enchanter was a very technical Thrash / Speed Metal band hailing from Chicago, IL. Their songs ranged from Maiden styles in the early years (1985) to PURE speed in the later years (1989). Amazingly the last recordings from this band were recorded before a single member had reached the age of 21. Their full length album "Patterns Of Violence" has never been released. Vocalist Sonny DeLuca went on to front Chicago metal ingenues Cyclone Temple

2. Enchanter #2 is an Italian metal band from Rome. Their music is inspired by symphonic and power metal sounds.

3.  Enchanter #3 was a US Epic Heavy Metal band in the 80's. They changed their name to Longings Past.

4. Enchanter #4 Original heavy metal band from New England since 1991.  Signed to German label Puresteel Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enchanter">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
