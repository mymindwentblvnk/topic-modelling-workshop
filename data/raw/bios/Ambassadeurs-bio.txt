The Brighton, UK born Ambassadeurs AKA Mark Dobson is an uncompromising musician who draws influences from Hip-Hop, Reggae and most Electronic styles of music and continues to push the boundaries when it comes to production.

With releases coming the end of the year, having DJ'd at many festivals including Beach Break Live and Bestival spinning music from Afrobeat to Dubstep and plans for a live band in the works. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ambassadeurs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
