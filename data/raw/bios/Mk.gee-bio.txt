Mk.Gee is the Solo Project of the American Multi-instrumentalist and producer, Junior Michael Gordon. 

Originally from southern New Jersey, Michael Gordon moved to LA in 2016 to pursue a degree in Popular Music at USC Thornton School of Music. His Double EP, Pronounced McGee, which was released on May 18th 2018 was written, recorded, performed, mixed and mastered by Gordon in his Los Angeles bedroom. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/mk.gee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
