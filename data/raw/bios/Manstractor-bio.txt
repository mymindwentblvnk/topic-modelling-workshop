Manstractor was a Brutal Death/Grind from Germany froming in 1998. The band split up in June 2003


Last known line-up

Pöppi - bass
Sebi - vocals
Michael Melchers ("Moshael") - Drums

Former/past member(s)

Grobi - vocals (1998-1999)
Martin - Bass
Hotte - guitars
