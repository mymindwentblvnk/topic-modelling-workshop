The Friendly Indians is a band from Orange County, CA. The band has been a fixture in Southern California clubs since the early '90s. The band performs the  theme for USA Network's newest hit comedy, "Psych" starring James Roday, Dule HIll and Corbin Bernsen. The band include Jason Barrett, Steve Franks, Tim Meltreger, and Gizzy Jackson
