Thrashy Deathgrind from Inazawa / Aichi, Japan! They released a quite well received flexi-EP in 1990 named "Eternal Life". In 1991 they recorded 6 songs for the lethal "Thrashing Deathpower" split-LP compilation which featured Multiplex and Satanic Hellslaughter as well. Gibbed is currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gibbed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
