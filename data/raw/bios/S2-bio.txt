1.) S2 is Korean-Japanese duet consisting of Kim Soo-hyun and Makoto Koshinaka. They debuted in 2006 with EP "Just Like Me".

2.) S2 (에스투) is a six-member girl group under CLIMIX Entertainment. They debuted on August 24, 2017 with their first single "Honeya". Their name is a combination of "S" (for Serendipity) and "2" for the members and fans to meet together and bring 'unexpected luck and love'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/S2">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
