Sludgecore band hailing from Atlanta, GA. active between 1998-2001. They released a demo tape and a full length entitled "Starvation Of Locusts" in 2000, and a split with Sofa King Killer in 2001. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leechmilk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
