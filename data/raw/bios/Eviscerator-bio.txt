Eviscerator are a Thrash Metal band from Colac, Victoria, Australia. The members are Don (guitar, vocals), Trav (guitar, vocals), Nick (bass) and Will (drums). They have one 6-track demo called "And Thus the Terror Begins..." released in 2006. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/eviscerator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
