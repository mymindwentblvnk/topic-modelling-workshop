CamelPhat is a British DJ and production duo, formed in 2008. The two DJs met in 2008 and released their first singles on the label Vice Records two years later. In 2014 they signed with Spinnin' Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CamelPhat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
