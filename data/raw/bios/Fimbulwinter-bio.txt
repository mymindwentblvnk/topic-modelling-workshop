Fimbulwinter is an early Norwegian black metal band from Oslo, Norway which formed in 1992 and broke up in 1994 after the release of their one and only album. Shagrath later went on to the now so famous Dimmu Borgir.
``Fimbul Winter`` is in Norse mythology the first part of Ragnarok - the Mighty Winter takes over the earth and then begins the final battle between men, and gods.

Skoll - Bass (ex-Ulver, ex-Ved Buens Ende, ex-Arcturus (Nor))
Shagrath (Stian Thoresen) - Lead Guitars, Drums (Dimmu Borgir, Starkness, ex-Ragnarok (Nor), Chrome Division)
Necronos - Vocals, Rhythm Guitars
