German Folk/Renaissance Metal band. Estabilished in 2001 in Landsberg. In 2001 their first Demo-LP comes out. The first studio album is published in 2004 under the name Nachtwache. 
In 2007 the album "Stunde der Wahrheit" is published. 

To avoid confusion this is also the name of a Funk-Fusion band from Texas which released an album "47 times it's own weight" in 1975 and re-released on Dusty Groove Records. Best known for the popular club track "March of the Goober Woobers". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cumulo+Nimbus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
