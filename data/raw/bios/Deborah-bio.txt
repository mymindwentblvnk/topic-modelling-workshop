These is more than one artist with this name.

1) Déborah is an all female, Mexican Christian Black Metal band. They released in 2002 an album, "The Song of Deborah". In 2008, the album "Soteria" was released as well. Déborah are: Claudia Cano Martínez (vocals), Miriam López López (guitar), Rocío del Moral Osnaya (bass).

2) Deborah Fraser, South African gospel artist. Worked with Chicco (Selo Twala) and Brenda Fassie.

3)  Deborah sings in Portuguese, and has contributed several tracks to compilations relating to the African dance style "kizomba." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deborah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
