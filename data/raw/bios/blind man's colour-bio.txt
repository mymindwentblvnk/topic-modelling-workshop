Blind Man’s Colour is an indie experimental band out of Saint Petersburg, Florida The band signed with Kanine Records in February of 2009 to release their EP “Wooden Blankets” and their full length album “Season Dreaming.” The band's members are Kyle Wyss and Orhan Chettri.
