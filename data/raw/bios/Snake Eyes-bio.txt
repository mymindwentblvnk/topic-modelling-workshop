Clash magazine said they "blur the edges between noise rock and more straight-forwardly melodic indie rock, a potent brew", snake eyes just call it “grit pop”.

Jim, Nicole & Thomas are a trio of friends that bonded over their love for colourfully scuzzy, nineties grunge & brit pop (you see where they get ‘grit’ from?) & summer 2020 has seen the tip of the iceberg with the drop of debut tracks produced by Thomas himself & mastered in New York by Dan Coutant (Drug Church, Gnarwolves).
