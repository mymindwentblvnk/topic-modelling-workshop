The Seventh Power is a Christian metal band with an old-school metal sound, occasionally bordering on doom metal.  The band is made up of Robert Sweet (of Stryper) on drums and Bill Menchen (of Final Axe, Titanic) on everything else.  Their first cd was released on Retroactive Records in 2006.  A new cd is due out in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Seventh+Power">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
