Sorry no Ferrari is an instrumental rock band. Since its conception in Fall 2004, the band has released two EPs on Alaska Records and Stickfigure Records respectively, undergone two east-coast tours, and played over 60 shows. The band has recently released their debut full-length record titled "Ternary". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sorry+No+Ferrari">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
