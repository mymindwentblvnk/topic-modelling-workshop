Boyd Rice (born 1956) is an American experimental sound artist, archivist, actor, photographer, prankster and writer best known for his pioneering industrial noise music under the name NON and Boyd Rice and Friends. he has collaborated with numerous artists. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Boyd+Rice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
