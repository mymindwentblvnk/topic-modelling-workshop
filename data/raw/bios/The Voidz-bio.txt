The Voidz, are an American rock band formed in 2013 by Julian Casablancas of The Strokes. The band consists of Casablancas (lead vocals) as well as Jeramy "Beardo" Gritter (guitar), Amir Yaghmai (guitar), Jacob "Jake" Bercovici (bass guitar, synthesizers), Alex Carapetis (drums, percussion), Jeff Kite (keys) and Shawn Everett (production)

The band's debut album "Tyranny" was released on the September 23, 2014
The second album named "Virtue" was released on the March 30, 2018
