Woundeep is a brutal death metal band based in Tokyo, Japan featuring the talents of ex-Vomit Remnants vocalist Toshiyasu Kusayanagi.  They currently have two demos out (4 songs total; the 2nd, released in 2005, contains three but the final track is a re-recording of an original track from 2003) and have recently signed with Unmatched Brutality Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Woundeep">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
