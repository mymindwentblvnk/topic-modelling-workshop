Sebastian Grainger of former Death From Above 1979 glory and his new musical endeavour.

He has recently signed, as Sebastien Grainger & The Mountains, with Saddle Creek Records. Grainger is most well-known for having been the singer and drummer of the defunct two-piece band, Death from Above 1979, together with Jesse F. Keeler who played bass. Grainger has also played drums for the project Femme Fatale.
