Worm Cluster is a Death Metal band from southern Germany. The band was founded in 2011 in Ulm by former members of the locally popular bands Havok,  Chaosreign and Tomorrow Comes Crashing and was originally intended as a home and studio recording project. Their musical style can be described as a mix of groovy old-school riffs and progressive elements. The band released their first demo "Wurm Laster" in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Worm+Cluster">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
