Nighttrain are a heavy metal band from Germany formed in 2008, releasing a demo in 2009. The band released their debut album ‘Between Darkness And Light’ in 2011, followed two years later by their second album ‘7 Sins’. The band toured Germany in 2015 supporting Norwegian metallers Chrome Division.
