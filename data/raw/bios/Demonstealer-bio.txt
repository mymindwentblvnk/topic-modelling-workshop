The Demonstealer is the frontman of Mumbai based metal band called Demonic Resurrection. He is also the drummer for Reptilian Death. Under his solo banner he releases various singles online. The music is not confined to metal and almost anything goes. From instrumental rock to hindi death metal to cheesy techno.
