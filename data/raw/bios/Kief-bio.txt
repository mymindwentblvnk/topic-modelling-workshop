Kief was a short-lived five-piece stoner rock / stoner metal band from Rīga, Latvia formed in 2009.

Band split up in 2010. Currently band's members has been involved in Animal Stampede, The Trigger, MonkeyRocks, Totenkopf, and Dūmaparāts.

The original lineup :

Dāvis Mazurenko - vocals;
Rūdolfs Ozols - guitar;
Ēriks Krapivns - guitar;
Uldis Sēnis - bass guitar;
Rihards Jirgensons - drums.
