APOCRYPHON are a psychedelic death metal/grindcore band from San Francisco, California. Their lyrical themes and overall aesthetic incorporate conspiracy theories, psychoactive chemicals, cosmic phenomena, death, psychology, violence, and obscure mythology. The band formed in October 2010, releasing their self-titled debut EP in July 2011 and a split EP with Lafayette, CA-based Fabricant on Horror Pain Gore Death Productions in March 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Apocryphon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
