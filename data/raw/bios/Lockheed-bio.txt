Lockheed is a Thrash Metal band from Campina Grande, a city located at Northeast of Brazil. With an open proposal in relation to Thrash Metal and inspired in the early days of the Bay Area style of the '80s, Lockheed has in its sonority from the Hard Rock to Death Metal.
