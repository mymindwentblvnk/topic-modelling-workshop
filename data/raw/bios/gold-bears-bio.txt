Atlanta, Georgia based “twee-punk” outfit Gold-Bears ) were formed in 2009 by Jeremy Underwood (Plastic Mastery) along with Erich Leudtke, Dave Fortner, and Santiago Archila. 

The band’s first single was released in 2010 via Magic Marker Records and was followed by the full-length debut LP Are You Falling In Love? for Slumberland in early summer 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gold-Bears">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
