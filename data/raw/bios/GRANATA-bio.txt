Alex Granata (known by his stage name GRANATA) is a Connecticut-born rapper who came to prominence with his first mixtape #whynot?  He currently attends the University of Tampa, where he is an avid skateboarder as well as a dope rhymesayer.  His single "What's the Meaning?" has been hailed by music listeners as "that fuego" and currently is the 203rd most downloaded hip-hop song on iTunes.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GRANATA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
