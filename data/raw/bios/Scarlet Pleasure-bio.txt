Scarlet Pleasure is a Danish R&B, soul, funk, and pop musical trio made up of Emil Goll as lead vocals, Alexander Malone on bass, and Joachim Dencker on drums. The formative years of the all-Danish trio were in New York City. They released their 2014 EP Mirage, 2016 album Youth Is Wasted on the Young, and 2017 EP Limbo all charting on Tracklisten, the official Danish Albums Chart. The band is signed to Copenhagen Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scarlet+Pleasure">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
