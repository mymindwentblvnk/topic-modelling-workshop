DieOde, pronounced dye-ode, is a hip hop group from Duluth, Minnesota, USA. Jinx (Douglas Lefebvre, beatbox/writer/emcee) Cense (Brandon Hermsen, writer/emcee/producer,) and Fetus (Jonathan Peterson, writer/emcee/producer), the dope cats who create what is DieOde, share the same hometown of Alexandria, MN. Their first album, Just Another, was released this past September, 2006.
