A Swiss hard rock band. The band was very active in the 80's and reached the pinnacle of their success with their sophomore effort "Energy." Despite touring with Swiss heavyweights Gotthard in 1992, the band soon faded into obscurity. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satrox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
