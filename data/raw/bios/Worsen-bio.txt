Worsen is a black metal band from Charlotte, NC formed in 2012. Since forming, Worsen has independently released one EP titled Blood in February 2014.

Worsen is part of the AC//13 collective and consists solely of multi-instrumentalist and vocalist Rick Contes. He is also the guitarist of Young and in the Way, Votnut, and guitarist/vocalist/keyboardist of Ayr. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Worsen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
