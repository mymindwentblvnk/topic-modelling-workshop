Blood Ritual is a death metal band from Portland, Oregon, USA. The band has released a demo, "Cruciform Antithesis" in 1994 and two albums, "At the Mountains of Madness" and "Black Grimoire" in 1997 and 2005, respectively. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blood+Ritual">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
