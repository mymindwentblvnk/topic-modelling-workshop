Eternity of Darkness is a Black Metal band from Brighton, UK. The sole member Typhon Has released two demo's and a split cd, and was also featured on the tribute to Judas Iscariot with the track "The Black Clouds Roll Under the Parapet of the Sky."  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eternity+of+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
