"Grand Mood was a fierce alliance that recorded two sessions of black metal and eventually succumbed to its own self-fulfilling prophecy."

Airy, flittering black metal from the United States, signed to the label CW Productions. The first demo, Final Urge to March, was recorded in August 2007 and released in December 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grand+Mood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
