JT Bruce is a multi-talented Progressive Metal musician. 
His music is free and can be legally downloaded from his website.

Lush, dynamic compositions with a wide range of moods, tones, and atmospheres make up the bulk of his work and will appeal to fans of progressive rock and progressive metal alike. The heavily layered and technical guitar work coupled with rhythmically complex drumming works together with carefully orchestrated synthesizer to create a unique listening experience. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/JT+Bruce">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
