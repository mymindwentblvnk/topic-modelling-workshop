Fallen Christ were an American death metal band from Oakland Gardens, New York formed in 1992. They released a self-titled demo in 1992, a demo titled "Infernal Majesty" in 1993 and a demo titled "Abduction Ritual" in 1994. In 1996 they released a full-length record also titled "Abduction Ritual" on listenable records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fallen+Christ">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
