Jimi Blue Ochsenknecht is a German actor and singer/rapper. He was born December 27, 1991 in Munich, Germany. His father - Uwe Ochsenknecht - is also an actor, just like Jimi's brother Wilson Gonzales. 
Notworthy movies:
- Die Wilden Kerle 1-5 (engl: The Wild Guys)
- Sommer (engl: Summer)
- Gangs
