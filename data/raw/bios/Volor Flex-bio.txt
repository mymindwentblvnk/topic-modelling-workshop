Volor Flex is the stage name of Russian electronic musician Alexander Frolov, also known for his creative output as Trampique. Highly inspired by Burial, Volor Flex combines ambient, dubstep, 2-step, and future garage. Most of his records have been released via Romanian record label Dark Clover Records.
