P-Lo, born Paolo Rodriguez is a producer/rapper from the Bay Area. He is a founding member of the HBK Gang, which includes the likes of Kehlani, Iamsu!, and Sage the Gemini.

As well as releasing his own music, P-Lo has produced numerous charting singles which include Flo-Rida’s ‘GDFR’, Sage Gemini’s ‘Gas Pedal’ and Yo Gotti’s ‘Act Right’. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/P-Lo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
