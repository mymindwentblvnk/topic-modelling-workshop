Ah-Puch is a one-man black metal band from Mexico City, Mexico. Formed by 
Lord Vestus (Lux Omega Perpetuam,Wolf) in 2009. Ah-Puch is one of many variant names for the Maya death god. Current label mates with Tlacaelel on Necromoon Records. Ah-Puch have 5 demos and 5 splits, including a most recent demo entitled To a Funeral with a Forgotten Woods cover song on it.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ah-Puch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
