Brain Connect is a progressive rock band from Poland, combining elements of progressive rock, progressive metal, and jazz/fusion into a sonically beautiful experience. 

Brain Connect:
Jan Mitoraj – guitars, guitar fx & synth
Marcin Szlachta – bass, synth
Krzysztof Walczyk – Hammond, synths, piano, vocoder
