3B Assylum was a thrash metal band from Bakersfield, California.  The year of their formation is not known and it is not known they disbanded.  Their last known lineup was Tearrence Barzillai (vocals, guitars), Kendall Stoner (guitars), Jeff Dolen (bass) and Doug Pellerin (drums).  Metalstorm Productions was the last label they were signed to.  They only released an EP entitled 3B Assylum in 1997.  They also released a CD entitled Poverty in 1999. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/3B+Assylum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
