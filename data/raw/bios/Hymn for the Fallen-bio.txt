Hymn For The Fallen are a 5-piece Melodic Death Metal/Hardcore band from Northwich/Winsford. They combine a mixture of brutality, melody, and a lively stage presence, and promise to keep the crowd up and moving as we provide captivating, original music.

Hymn For The Fallen consists of:

Saul Ennett - Vocals

Alex Lescionok - Guitar

Gary McNeil - Guitar

Adam Wheeler - Bass

Ben Quinlivan - Drums
