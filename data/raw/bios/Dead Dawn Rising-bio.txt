Dead Dawn Rising is a greek band (Athens, Attica). They're playing some symphonic black metal.
Their line-up is looking like this:
The Grotesque (Kostas V.) - Vocals
Hellseeker (Alex Koutsoudakis) - Drums
Black Candle - Synth


They've got a demo called "Evoking the Tides of Satan" released in 2005 and an selftitled album "Dead Dawn Rising" released in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+Dawn+Rising">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
