There are 3 artists under the name of Martyrdom.

1. Martyrdom were a death core band from Sacramento California. Martyrdom was formed in the Spring of 2007 by vocalist Garrett Reid. Signed to Mediaskare Records on January 1st, 2009. Released 1 full length album Titled - Slaying Giants ( 2010) , And later broke up that same year 

2. Martyrdom were a black/thrash metal band from Holland, formed in 1992. The band released two demos, “Blackstench” in 1994, and “Sodomy and Crush” in 1995 before disbanding.

3. Martyrdom hails from Beijing, China and play a particularly raw style of Pagan Black Metal. In 2002, they released their debut, “Hymns of Pagan Hate” and released their 2nd record, “MartyrdoM II” in August 2003. They have also appeared on the “348? Black Metal Is Plague” split with Ululate and Heartless in 2006.
