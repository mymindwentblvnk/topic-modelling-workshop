Holocross was a thrash metal band formed in 1982 in Pittsburgh, USA.  They played a unique, catchy, but still very straightforward and aggressive style of thrash metal.  They are inactive.  Two full lengths were released under the Holocross moniker.  Hypercaust was originally recorded in 1990.

1988 - Holocross
2005 - Hypercaust

Char R.G. (vocals)
P.J. Macin (guitar)
Max Uzax (bass)
Ray Molinari (drums)
