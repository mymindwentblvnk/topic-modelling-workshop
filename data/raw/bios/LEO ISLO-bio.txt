LEO ISLO is the stage persona of Portland, OR, based producer and singer Matt Pahler. His first EP titled “Modern Fiction” was released in February 2014, and combines elements of Chillwave, Indie-rock, Lo-Fi, and Synth-Pop. The collection was conceptualized on an acoustic guitar while traveling in Nicaragua and recorded in his garage studio during the winter of 2012. LEO ISLO’s second EP, “Hearts in Dystopia” is still in production and slated to drop near the end of 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/LEO+ISLO">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
