Grimaced is a progressive thrash metal band from Tucson Arizona, with blazing guitar work, pounding ryhtyhms and insightful lyrics about political corruption, conspiracies and coverups, they have been through many stylistic and lineup changes but are now established in the Arizona metal name trying to make a name for themselves. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grimaced">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
