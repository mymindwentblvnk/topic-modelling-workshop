English

The band started as a thrash metal tribute band, in 2003, playing songs from Exodus, Destruction and Kreator.
At that time, the band was named P.O.T.T. (which stands for "Pleasures of the Thrash", a joke with the 2nd Exodus studio album).
In the end of 2005 they decided to write their own songs, just for fun
 
Their primary intention is still to have fun. Their second goal is to remember that Thrash Metal style's spirit from the 80s, without influences of another Metal Genre.

The original line-up it's still the same until today:

Alexandre Dantas: guitar
Silvio Senna: guitar and backing vocals
Marcos Ramos: bass and backing vocals
Wagner Andreoli: drums and vocals

In 2008 they released the first demo, called "Same Old Story (S.O.S)" with 5 tracks and a cover song (A Lesson In Violence by Exodus).

Português

A banda começou como um tributo ao thrash metal, em 2003, tocando músicas do Exodus, Destruction e Kreator.
Naquela época, a banda era chamada de P.O.T.T. (que significa "Pleasures of the Thrash", uma piada com o segundo álbum de estúdio do Exodus).
No fim de 2005, decidiram escrever músicas próprias apenas pela diversão.

A intenção principal da banda além da diversão, é resgatar o espírito do Thrash Metal dos anos 80 sem influências de outros estilos

A formação original é a mesma que se mantém até hoje:

Alexandre Dantas: guitarras
Silvio Senna: guitarras e backing vocals
Marcos Ramos: baixo e backing vocals
Wagner Andreoli: bateria e vocal
