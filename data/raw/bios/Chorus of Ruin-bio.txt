British doom metal band, formed in the Bradford area in 1991. They recorded a number of demos ("Les Miserables", "Swan Dive", "Tragedies"), EPs ("Oceans Of Sins") and splits ("Godlike / Last Dance") before splitting up. 

They  reformed to play a reunion concert with Officium Triste on Halloween '09 at the Manville, Bradford. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chorus+of+Ruin">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
