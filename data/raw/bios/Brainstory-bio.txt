Brainstory is a trio from the Inland Empire in Southern California. They self-released their opening EP and then signed with Big Crown Records in 2018 to release their debut record Buck. Brainstory's sound can be described as funk, rock or soul and is undeniably Californian. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brainstory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
