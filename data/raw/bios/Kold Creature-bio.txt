Kold Creature are a groove based, melodic metal band from south Brisbane, Australia founded by guitarist & lead singer Chris Ross in 2013. Lyndon Bauer (guitar), Patrick Stevens (bass), Alan Mahood (drums) rounded out the line up soon after. Kold Creature released their self produced debut album A Weakened State in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kold+Creature">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
