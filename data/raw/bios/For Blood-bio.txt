FOR BLOOD started february of 2006. Various auditions and line-up changes led to the complete and current line-up we have now. From the ashes of past local metal bands, FOR BLOOD consists of Shaun Scott (DEVILINSIDE/HORROR/FALLACY), Kyle Skogquist (REVELENCE/IMPALER), Andy Oltmanns (COLD COLOURS/SECTION HATE/RIVKIT), Jim Haugen (TO BLEED FOR) & Neil Merritt (TO BLEED FOR).
