DECEMBER FLOWER, the German death metal band formed out of the ashes of CHRONICLE OF TYRANTS, has completed work on its first demo, entitled "Moloch". According to a press release, the demo "contains six tracks of finest melodic, but still aggressive death metal."

Check out audio samples on the band's MySpace page.

Labels and promoters can get in touch with the band at decemberflower@online.de.
