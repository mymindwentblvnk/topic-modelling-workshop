Shoreborn is a Melodic and Technical Death Metal band from Italy. They started to play together in 2000, reprising the swedish metal style of bands like Dark Tranquillity or At The Gates.

"Create, Don't Be a Slave" was released in 2005 and it was the second demo of the band, which is searching for a label contract. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shoreborn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
