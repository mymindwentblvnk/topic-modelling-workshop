Formed in 2001 Severed Remains is a Brutal Death Metal band coming from Mayfield Heights, Ohio, the United States of America.
The band splitted after the release of their first and only album "A Display of Those Defiled".
The last known line-up was:
Ben Rubin - Guitar
Andrew Marunowski- Guitar
Pavel Puzerei - Bass
Anthony Biello - Drums, Vocals
