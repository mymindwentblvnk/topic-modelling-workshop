Blackthru is a one man project, established in 2005  in the town of Gargždai, Lithuania. The songs are filled with Blackthru's own feelings, emotions and survivals. It is a Sympho black metal project, which is one of a few bands of this genre in Lithuania, if not the only one. The strongest influence is made by the Norwegian scene, it's nature and bands. 

Band changed it's name to Triangle Of Art when the masterminder's of Blackthru (Justas Balsevičius)  life turned back to the darkside <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blackthru">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
