A Symphonic black/gothic metal band from Japan. 
Not much else is known but they released (at least) 3 demo tapes in 1994-1999 and then demo compilation cd afterwards. They also appeared on "The True ideal realized through the underground" with song 'Moment of Silence and then on the 2nd "The true ideal..." album with 2 songs 'The day opened from pain' and 'Awaken the dawn'.
