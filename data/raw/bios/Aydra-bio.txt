Aydra was an Italian Technical Death Metal band.  They formed in Ancona, Italy in 1993 and were signed to X-Treme Rising and Rising Works. They were previously known as Hydra (1985 - 1993).

Last known line up:
Mauro Pacetti - Vocals
Francesco Olivi - Lead Guitar
Alessandro Marcelli - Guitar
