'My Name is Barry' is a project by Irish Singer Songwriter Barry McNamara. His debut album 'Songs, Some Of Which Are In G Major' began recording on the 22nd of December 2011 and finished fully on the 14th of April 2012. The entire album was recorded in his bedroom using an array of different instruments, Audacity and a Rock Band microphone. 

My Name is Barry's debut 12 song album 'Songs, Some Of Which Are In G Major' was released on the 15th of April 2012 for free download. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/My+Name+is+Barry">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
