a death/doom band from Italy that was formed in 1988 and later on changed their name to VII Arcano half of which later became Theatres des Vampires. They released three demos, "Anteroom of the Hell" in 1990, "Flowers Upon the Grave" in 1992 and a Promo Tape in 1994.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sepolcrum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
