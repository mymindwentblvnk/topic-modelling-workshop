Dave (drums), Fri (bass), Tex (guitar) and Flash (vocals) play together since september 2003.
The sound of the band, influenced by nu-metal and emo core, is the result of the different attitude and influences of the band, and allowed them to won the contest Pagella Rock (Battle of Bands) in 2006 and to play in important context.
The band had in these years the opportunity to play with artists like Africa Unite, The Fire, Tonino Carotone and Arpioni and on the stages of some important Festivals (Meet 2007, Colonia Sonora, Emersione Festival, Sotto18 Filmfestival).
