Kowloon Walled City is an American noise rock (early sludge metal) band from San Francisco, United States, formed in 2007. They take their name from a (now demolished) densely-populated, autonomous region of Hong Kong known for its brothels, casinos and opium dens. 

Members:

Scott Evans - guitar, vocals;
Jon Howell (Puig Destroyer, Less Art) - guitar;
Ian Miller (Puig Destroyer, Less Art, Strangelight) - bass;
Dan Sneddon (Early Graves) - drums.

Former members:

Jason Pace - guitar;
Jeff Fagundes - drums;
Julia Lancer (The New Trust, Strangelight) - drums.
