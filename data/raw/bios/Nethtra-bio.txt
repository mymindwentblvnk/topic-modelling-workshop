Weird black metal made to accompany the still moments and the beings that hide within them.

Formed in 2010, Nethtra uses black metal as the basis for textural exploration and subtle experimentation. Evolution comes slowly, and the music takes time to meditate on the darkness and the creatures that are lost in it. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nethtra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
