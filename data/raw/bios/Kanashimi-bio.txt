Kanashimi is a Depressive Black Metal band from Shizouka, Japan that formed in 2007. The band is a solo project of Misanthropy, who created Kanashimi after the death of the band Samayoi.
''Kanashimi'' (悲しみ) is a Japanese word meaning sorrow or sadness.

Line-up:
Misanthropy - All instruments
