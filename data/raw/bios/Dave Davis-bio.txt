Dave Davis is a DJ, producer and a vocalist from Belgium. He mainly produces trance and house. He is most famous for his first release, Transfiguration (1995) under BMG label. Most of his productions were released by Bonzai Records. He is also known as JD Davis and David Henrard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Davis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
