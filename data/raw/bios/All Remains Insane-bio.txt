All Remains Insane are a Swiss Thrash Metal Band. Formed in 2004 the band have played some gigs in Tessin with bands like Dew-Scented, Vital Remains, Orion, Roots of Death,...
The style of the music mix modern thrash metal (Hatesphere, The Haunted) with some metalcore influences (killswitch engage, heaven shall burn,...)  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/All+Remains+Insane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
