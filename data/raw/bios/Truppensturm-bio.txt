Truppensturm is a black metal band from Germany. 
Lyrical themes: War, Genocide
Line-up: Vangard von Rimburg (all instruments, vocals)
Additional live members: Weigand (Bass; Kermania, Heemat); Alexander von Meilenwald (Drums; The Ruins Of Beverast, ex-Nagelfar)

Formerly called Sturmtruppen (1996-2006). The reason of the name-change was that there is another Sturmtruppen (which is a NS band). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Truppensturm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
