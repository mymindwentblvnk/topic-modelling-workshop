There are multiple artists that go by this name:

1. Ronald LaTour (born September 24, 1984), professionally known as Cardo Got Wings or simply Cardo, is an American hip-hop producer and rapper. He has produced for Curren$y, 2 Chainz, Drake, Kendrick Lamar, Wiz Khalifa, R. Kelly, Jeezy, Jay Z, Frank Dukes and Swizz Beatz, among others.

2. Danilo Cardace (a.k.a Cardo) is a young emerging artist from cesenatico,Italy! in the age 16, Cardo was fully dedicated to creating electronic music. In 2004, he started djing and performing live sets of minimal electro and minimal techno. His sound has always been very dynamic and minimal extatic! He worked for Stereo Seven Plus,ITALO BUSINESS, Autist , Logical Musik and more! Cardo and his friend Elia created "Audio Shooter" , Audio shooter sound are supported of many important dj.
