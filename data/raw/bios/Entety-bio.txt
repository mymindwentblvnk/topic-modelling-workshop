Formed in 1990 in Los Angeles, California, Entety played vile death metal/grindcore often considered as proto-goregrind. Most of the members went on to play in Infamy, another deathgrind band, after Entety split-up in 1994. Their first release was the demo Eviscerated Then Mummificated im 1991. Next year saw the release of a single named Cadaveric Necrogrind and in 1994 it was time for Into the Desolate, their last demo (1994). In 2002 a post-humous full-lenght came out, also named Cadaveric Necrogrind. Most of their discography is available in a compilation named Exhumed Sarcophagous 1990-1994, released in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Entety">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
