Broken Windows are an indie rock band from Massachusetts with members from the Enthusiastics and Empty Phrases. They play loud rock music and enjoy short walks on the beach.

After The Enthusiastics disbanded, Kevin, Ryan and Barry joined with drummer Justin Cole to create some tunez. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Broken+Windows">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
