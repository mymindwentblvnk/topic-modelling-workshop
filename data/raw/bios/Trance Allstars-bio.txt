Trance Allstars were a electronic music project by two German record labels specialized in dance music, Kontor Records and Polydor/Zeitgeist (Universal). The project consisted of renown electronic acts Taucher, Talla 2XLC, Schiller, ATB, Sunbeam and DJ Mellow-D

Year 2000 the group released their debut album Worldwide, incl. club hits such as The First Rebirth and Ready To Flow.
