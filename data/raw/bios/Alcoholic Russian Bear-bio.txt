A brutal death metal band hailing from Moscow, Russia.  They formed in 2014 and only released 2 EP's.  The band consisted of  Vladimirovitch on vocals,  Ochkanaut on guitars,  Maniac on lead guitar and Max Krieg on bass/backing vocals.   Vladimirovitch as Nikita Novozhilov recorded all instruments and vocals on the Siberian Gore EP.  It was released in 2014.  Vladimirovitch  provided bass and vocals on Dead Redhead.  Vladimirovitch and Maniac are the only ones credited on Dead Redhead.  It was released in 2015.  They disbanded in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alcoholic+Russian+Bear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
