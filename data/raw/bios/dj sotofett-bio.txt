DJ Sotofett is one of the minds behind Norway's infamous Sex Tags Mania label. For years Sotofett & his crew have been carving a niche in the underground of dance music just North of the Arctic Circle with their own unique version of Dub, Disco, Afrobeat, House & Techno all self released via their Mania, Amfibia & Sotofett's own Wania imprints. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Sotofett">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
