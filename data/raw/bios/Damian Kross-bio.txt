One-man Industrial Death Metal band formed in 1994 after the Damian Kross moved to Burbank, California (before that he was living in Long Island, New York and was also in another one-man band called Analogy, which released a self-titled tape in 1994). Released a promo "Fist Fuck The World" in 1995 and a full-lenght with the same name the same year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Damian+Kross">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
