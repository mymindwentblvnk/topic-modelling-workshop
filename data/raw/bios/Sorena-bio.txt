Sorena moved to Canada in his early childhood from Iran and had always been surrounded by music; whether it was over the phone hearing his dad play the guitar or with his friends discovering all the tools of FL Studio. It was only in 2015, waking up from a coma after being hit by a drunk driver on his motorcycle, that he decided to finally release his music. It has been a life changing decision! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sorena">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
