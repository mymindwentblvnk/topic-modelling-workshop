Deorc is a Mexican Black Metal band, which were formed in 2007 in Mexico/Mexico City.Their lyrical themes are Madness, Suicide, Depression.Their current label are Azermedoth Records.The band has only released 1 full lengthed cd.The cd was released in 2009 and its called: Negative Cathexis to Life.
