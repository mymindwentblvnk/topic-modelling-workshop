There are two bands sharing the same name:

1.The now defunct Long Pig (correctly tagged LongPig) is old school goth/batcave band from Europe. The term "Long Pig" holds the same meaning as cannibalism.

2.The currently active Long Pig is a death/sludge/grindcore band from Hungary rising from the ashes of Din-Addict. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Long+Pig">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
