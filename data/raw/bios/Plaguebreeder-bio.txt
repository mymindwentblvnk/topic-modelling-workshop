Infernal death/black metal band from Lahti, Finland. Third reincarnation of Plaguebreeder (over the years Plaguebreeder has been Ardeath's one-man sideproject) was spawned in spring of 2010 with full band after their previous band Avafrost disbanded in November 2009. 

First demo The Unbreakable released in 2010 and available below.
Second release Unleash Hellfire Promo 2012 available below.
New release, Loath promo 2013 available here: https://soundcloud.com/plaguebreeder

New video Inferno from Loath promo available here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ePchMgYJC-8
