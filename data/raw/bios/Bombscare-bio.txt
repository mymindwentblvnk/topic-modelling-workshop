Multiple artists of the same name.

1.  Bombscare are a punk band from Adelaide, Australia.

2. Bombscare were a Metal band from Vancouver, Canada.

In 1997 they contributed a song called Stone 314 to a compilation titled GRRRR!! Extreme Music From Vancouver - Volume 1. The compilation was put together and produced by Devin Townsend of Strapping Young Lad fame. Later down the track Bombscare would disband and some members would form the stoner rock band Evenlight. Evenlight features Brian (Beavis / The Beav) Waddel on guitar, who also played in The Devin Townsend Band. Vis, B is the name Brian Waddel is cited as on the Bombscare line-up of GRRRR!! The full line-up is:

Guy, Tuff: bass
Vis, B: guitar
Ashe, Mein: drums
