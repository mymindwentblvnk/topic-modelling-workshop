Music A.M. is a post-rock/electro-pop trio featuring:

Luke Sutherland (of Long Fin Killie and Bows)
Stefan Schneider (of To Rococo Rot)
Volker Bertelmann (of Tontraeger)

More info about the band can be found at:

www.music-am.de

Luke Sutherland is also the sometime violinist for Mogwai and a published author. More information about him can be found at:
