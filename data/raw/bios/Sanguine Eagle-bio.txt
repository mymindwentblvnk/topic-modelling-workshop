Sanguine Eagle are a black metal band from Brooklyn, New York. It features members of Yellow Eyes, Vorde, and Imperial Trumpet. They are a member of the House of First Light. They released their demo 'Individuation' in April 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sanguine+Eagle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
