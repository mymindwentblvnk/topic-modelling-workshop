Helmut Fritz (artist name for Eric Greff) is a French electronic music artist. He's currently signed with the Sony  Music label, and released his first single Ça m'énerve  in March 2009. The single was produced by Laurent Konrad , one half of the electronic music project Discobitch, which released the hit single C'est beau la bourgeoisie in 2008, a song that is very similar to Ca m'énerve. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Helmut+Fritz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
