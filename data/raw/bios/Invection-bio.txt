Thrash metal band located in West Oakland, CA. Formed summer 2007. To date they have released 2 demos, 3 EP's (World Plague 2008, Demented Perception 2009 and Derealization 2010) and a full length album (Facet Of Aberration 2011). Their first full length album, Facet Of Aberration was released November 15th, 2011 on Stormspell Records and is sold via Stormspell Records and the band's webstore.
