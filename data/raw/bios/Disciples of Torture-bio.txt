Disciples of Torture was originally formed by Lindon in 2010 as an internet based project and upon moving interstate, became a fully functioning live band. With the new 5 piece line-up, came a solidification of sound and renewed ambition to create guttural slams and sickening pit riffment. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disciples+of+Torture">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
