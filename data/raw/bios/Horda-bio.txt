There are more bands using this name:
1) Horda is a Hungarian thrash metal band formed by Gyula Pavelka (ex-Akela) guitarist in 1999. They released two demos and a full-length studio album since. There was a pause in the band's story due to a theft in their rehearsal place.

2) Horda is a Polish hard rock band who existed from 2003 until 2007, and appear to be inactive at the moment.
