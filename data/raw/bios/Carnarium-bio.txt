Carnarium A Black/Gothic Metal Band From (Villa Adelina) Argentina that began to play in 1993.
Their current line-up consists in: 
Nestor Ortiz - Vocals
Pablo Dello Valle - Guitars
Luciano Santagapita - Bass
Alicia Del Cerro - Female Vocals
Graciela Biassotti - Keyboards
Pablo Querol - Drums
