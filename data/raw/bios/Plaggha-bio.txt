Plaggha is the name of a black metal/ambient band hailing from Spain, whose sole member has always been Naashahas. Lyrical themes rotate around Draconian Magick. 
To date, three demos have been released [[i]Nekromagick Trance , XI and Serpentiis Mysteriis], along with two splits.
