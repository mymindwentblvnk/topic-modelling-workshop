Biography
Underground deathcore band from Florø,westcoast of Norway!
Cage of torment is a deathcore band from Florø,Sogn&fjordane,Norway!
It consists of Are Liseth(drums) Ove Ronny Kjerpeset(lead guitar) Torstein Aa(bass) Niklas W.Iversen(guitar) and Kristian Løseth(vocals)
On stage we are in the right environment and we love it, wherever it may be!

Cage of torment released deby EP in 2011.
Cage of torment going for full length studio album in august 2012!
