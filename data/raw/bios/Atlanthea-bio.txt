Atlanthea is the progressive metal project of Rodrigo Vieira from Portugal.  Two albums have been released to date, Deliverance To Earth (2004) and Mandragora Tree (2007).  Both are available for free from the bands official website and their MySpace page. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atlanthea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
