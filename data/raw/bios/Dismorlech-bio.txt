dismorlech is a slamming brutal death metal band from chile(quilpue). They formed in 2002.
This band line up with carlos  palma -vocals, camilo olivares - guitar, alberto lobos - bass and jaime jra - drum. They releashed their first demo in 2004 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dismorlech">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
