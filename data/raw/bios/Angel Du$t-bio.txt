Angel Du$t is an American rock supergroup formed in Baltimore, Maryland in 2013, made up of members of Turnstile  and Trapped Under Ice . In 2018, they signed to Roadrunner Records. In 2019, Billboard noted them as one of the most important bands in broadening the scope of what hardcore punk is. Kerrang! included their 2019 album Pretty Buff as one of their "25 Best Albums of 2019" and Loudwire named it one of the 50 best metal albums of 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angel+Du$t">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
