Jack Garratt is an English multi-instrumentalist, singer and producer. He incorporates electric guitar, piano, rich percussion and electronic flourishes into his music. 

He has released two EPs ("Synesthesiac" (2015) and "Remnants" (2014)) and  two singles ("Chemical" (2015) and "The Love You're Given" (2014)). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jack+Garratt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
