V.B.G. (Victimized By Gypsies) was grindcore/death metal band from Bozeman, Montana.  The band released the demos "Staff Infection" and "Auntie Christ" in 1992, before breaking up.  Jason William Walton of Agalloch and Sculptured fame was a part of this band.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/V.B.G.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
