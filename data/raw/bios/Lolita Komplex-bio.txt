Established 2008 
from the Austrian capital of the macabre...

Already at the first shows Lolita KompleX succeeded in combining western and Japanese-inspired elements in a innovative, never seen before way. They are combining influences from a variety of genres, such as metal, goth, rock or even punk. Lyrics in English, Japanese, German and French, merge extravagant outfits and cha-rismatic performances to a total work of art, through which Lolita KompleX have already gained a loyal and steadily growing fan base. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lolita+KompleX">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
