Boltdown are a thrash metal band from Newcastle, UK.

They recently released their debut album "Omnicide" on 3rd March, 2008, on Dark Balance Records. The track 'Deadlock' from this album was also recently included on Metal Hammer magazine's 'Thrash Invasion' compilation disc.

The band's line-up currently consists of:
Leigh Final - Vocals, Guitars
Dan Wilson - Guitars
Richard Ryder - Bass
