Thrashquatch is a five piece crossover thrash band from southern CA. Thrashquatch is all about partying and playing old school Thrash metal music!! With influences ranging from Sepultura,Accused and DRI to name a few.Thrashquatch has recently released there debut full length album on Dead Center Records. The album has had very positive feed back. And is doing well in the U.S,as well as in Europe! The band continues to write music and work towards the next album! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thrashquatch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
