Elijah & Skilliam are Grime Djs from East London, UK. 

They both run popular grime blog Butterz.co.uk, which has interviews, free downloads, and videos with some of the best in the scene.

They have been on Rinse FM since the end of 2008 and so far has had Terror Danjah, Swindle, Rude Kid, Silencer and DJ Spyro DJ JJ and Oneman as special guests.
