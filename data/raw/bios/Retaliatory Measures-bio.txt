Retaliatory Measures is a frantic Death/Thrash-newcomer from Pori, Finland. The band was founded at the beginning of 2009. The band released it's first Demo at late 2009 and it gained pretty good feedback throughout music media in Finland.
After going through minor line-up changes at 2010, Retaliatory Measures founded it's current Line-up.

Later 2010, Promo-cd "MMX" was released and the major goal was to find a good publisher/Record label for possible full-lenght debut album. After a while band decided to sign a deal with German record label, Massacre Records. So far un-named debut album will be released by the end of spring 2012.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Retaliatory+Measures">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
