Enefages is a Swedish tuttifrutti rock/metal band which started in the early 21th century and has performed on many of Stockholms stages. e.g Klubben, Göta källare, Huset and more.

Lineup:
Kalle Hedbom - Lead vocals, keyboards
Viktor Sandström - Guitar, backup vocals (not on band photo)
Olov Nordstrom - Bass, backup vocals
Petter Hedbom - Drums, backup vocals
