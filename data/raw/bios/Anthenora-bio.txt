Anthenora is a five piece heavy metal band which was founded by Luigi, Fabio and Pooma (they were schoolmates) in 1990 in Saluzzo, a small town near Turin, Italy. These three guys were deeply influenced by Iron Maiden, Saxon, Black Sabbath, Judas Priest, Accept, Grave Digger and Helloween. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anthenora">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
