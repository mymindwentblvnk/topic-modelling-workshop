There may be more than one artist named Olden and one of them was born in 1978.  However, the best documented Olden is a melodic death metal band from Tuscon, Arizona that formed in the year 2016.  They consist of Lael Clark (vocals, guitars), Billy Noland (guitars), Kyle Deneen (bass), Benji Semon (bass), Matthew Knaggs (mandolin) and Ricky (drums).  Their lyrical themes include Norse mythology, death and revenge.  They have released one demo, one single and one full-length album.  They are not signed to a label.
