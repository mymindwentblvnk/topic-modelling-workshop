There are more than one artist named Ambivalence.

1.) Ambivalence is a death metal band from Lviv, Ukraine.  They formed in the year 2001.  They currently consist of  Marina Scherbakova (vocals, guitar) and Armen Oganesjan (bass).  They have three demos, three full-length albums and one EP.  Their lyrical themes include problems of morality, frustration, politics and war.  They are currently signed to PRC Music.  Their music is found on Bandcamp.
