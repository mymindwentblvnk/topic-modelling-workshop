Chrome Waves was formed in the summer of 2010 by current and former members of Nachtmystium, The Gates of Slumber, The Atlas Moth, and Twilight.

Line-up:
Stavros Giannopoulos (The Atlas Moth, Twilight)
Jeff Wilson (Wolvhammer, ex-Nachtmystium)
Bob Fouts (Apostle of Solitude, ex-The Gates of Slumber)
