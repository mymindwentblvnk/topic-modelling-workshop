A death metal band that formed in the year 2014.  They are from Costa Mesa, California.  They consider themselves to be an old school death metal band.  Crematory Stench was started by J. Aguilar (vocals, guitar), E. Barajas (guitars),  E. Cruz (bass) and M. Valencia (drums).  In 2016, they released their first EP Crematory Stench.  Barajas left the band at unknown point in time.  J.H. (guitars) replaced him in 2018.  In 2018, they recorded their 2nd EP Grotesque Deformities.  They are currently signed to the label Blood Harvest. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CREMATORY+STENCH">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
