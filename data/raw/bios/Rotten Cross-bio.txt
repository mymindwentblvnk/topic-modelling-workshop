Rotten Cross is a Black Metal band from Las Vegas, Nevada in the United States. The band also experiments with many gothic elements in their music while still staying true to the genre. There is only one member of this band, Listan, who writes all the music himself. The band has self released three albums: spellbound for eternity, architecture of pagan tragedies, and diabolical Cataklysm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rotten+Cross">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
