David Zowie Canada Batt[1] (born April 30, 1981),[2][3] known professionally as David Zowie, is an English DJ and record producer. He is best known for his song "House Every Weekend", which topped the UK Singles Chart in July 2015 and became the first song to do so on a Friday since Adam Faith's "Poor Me" in March 1960. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/David+Zowie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
