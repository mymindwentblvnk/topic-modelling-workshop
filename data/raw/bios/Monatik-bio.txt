Dmytro Monatyk  (Ukrainian: Дмитро "Діма" Монатик; born 1 April 1986 in Lutsk), professionally known as MONATIK, is an Ukrainian singer, songwriter, dancer, composer. 
He opened the first semi-final of the Eurovision Song Contest 2017 on 9 May.
