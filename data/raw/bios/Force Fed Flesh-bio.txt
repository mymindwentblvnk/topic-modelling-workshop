Formed in 2003 in Carroll County, Maryland just north of Baltimore. The band released their 6 song demo entitled "Ripped from the Womb" in early 2004, and then in 2005 released "The Human Virus" EP through Malicious Intent Records.

The band broke up in late 2005 with members getting involved with other projects such as Human Repugnance and Abominations of Desolation.
