Vaginal Stabwound, Death Metal band from Trollhättan (Sweden) Formed in late 2006. 

Band members are: Alexander Olsson Roth (Guitar & Vocals)
Hannes Nystèn (Drums) & Adam Chapman (Bass)

In late 2006, they released their first demo called Gagged. And a new Mini CD is planned to be out in early summer 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vaginal+Stabwound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
