Unending Hatred is Black Metal from Mexico (Nezahualcoyotl, Distrito Federal). Formed in 2008, they play slow Depresive Black Metal at down tempo.

Current line-up
N.H - Guitar, Vocals (Suicidal Anorexia)
Kold - All Instruments (Wounds, Means to an End, Lost Inside, Svartalfheim (USA))

Discography so far :
Alcoholic Solitude Under a Fullmoon... Demo, 2009
Emptiness Cold... Demo, 2009
3 Days of Suicidal Hatred Split, 2010
Into the Cold Path Split, 2010
A Sick Minds Regrets Split, 2010
