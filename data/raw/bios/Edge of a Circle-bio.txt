Edge Of A Circle is a progressive metal band from Lund, Sweden.

The progressive rock/metal band Edge of a Circle was born in late 2004 with the goal to create good quality music. With songs about love, politics and emotions mixed with a tight sound of heavy riffs, crazy guitarsolos and catchy melodies has turned out to be a great concept when people allround the world are following the bands journey.
