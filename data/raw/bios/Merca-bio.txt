Merca is old-school doom metal band from Netherlands, Overijssel. They are heavily influenced by Saint Vitus, Pentagram, and The Obsessed. Excellent guitar work and clean vocals in the style of Saint Vitus. This is slow traditional doom metal.

Alex - Bass
Animal - Drums
Niels- Guitars, Vocals
