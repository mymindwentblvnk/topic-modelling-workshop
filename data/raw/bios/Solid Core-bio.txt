Swedish death metal band from Söderköping/Norrköping. Formed in 2003, put out of its misery in 2005. 

Members: Robin Freijdh (Vocals), Björn Franzén (Guitar), Edward Kåkneryd (Guitar), Johan Karlberg (Bass), Carl Ankarstrand (Drums).
