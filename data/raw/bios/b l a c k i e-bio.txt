Michael LaCour (born 1987 in Houston, Texas), known by his stage name as B L A C K I E...in All Caps, With Spaces, is an American rapper and record producer. His music is an array of Noise, Southern Rap, and U.K. Grime. This combination of influences has led him to be labeled as the founder of American Grime. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/B+L+A+C+K+I+E">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
