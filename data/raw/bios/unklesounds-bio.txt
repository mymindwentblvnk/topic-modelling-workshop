James Lavelle and Richard File, having worked under UNKLE with others, emerged in 2001 with a DJ mix created for Japanese radio entitled Do Androids Dream of Electric Beats? produced under the name of UNKLE Sounds. Variations of name are; UNKLE Sounds UNKLE Soundsystem UNKLESOUNDS. Richard File is no longer in the group Unkle <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unklesounds">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
