Unnatural Helpers are a punk rock band from Seattle, Washington.  The current lineup usually includes Dean Whitmore, Kimberly Morrison (of The Dutchess and the Duke), Brian Standeford, and Charles Leo Gebhardt IV.  Guitarist Chris Martin of Kinski is a former member.  They are currently signed to Hardly Art. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unnatural+Helpers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
