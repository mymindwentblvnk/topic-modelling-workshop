Da Mafia 6ix was the reunited incarnation of Memphis hip-hop group Three 6 Mafia, formed in 2013, comprised of the group's former members, Koopsta Knicca, Gangsta Boo, Crunchy Black, Lord Infamous and DJ Paul. They released their debut mixtape 6ix Commandments on November 12, 2013, featuring guest appearances from frequent collaborators such asJuicy J, Lil Wyte, Yelawolf and 8Ball & MJG. After the death of group member Lord Infamous in December 2013, along with Gangsta Boo's departure in early 2014, the group released their second mixtape Hear No Evil in October 2014, featuring posthumous appearances from Lord Infamous, as well as guest appearances from Fiend, La Chat, Paul Wall and MJG. Their highly-anticipated debut album Watch What U Wish... was released in March 2015; five months after the release of their second mixtape. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Da+Mafia+6ix">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
