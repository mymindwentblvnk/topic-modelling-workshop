Mr. T Experience (or MTX) is a pop-punk band from Berkeley, California, formed in 1985 by Dr. Frank (vocals & guitar), Jon Von Zelowitz (guitar & vocals), Byron Stamatatos (bass) and Alex Laipeneiks (drums).  The majority of their songs are about love, relationships and girls. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Mr.+T+Experience">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
