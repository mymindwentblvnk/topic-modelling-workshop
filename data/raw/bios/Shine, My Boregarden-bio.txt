Shine, My Boregarden is a one-man-project founded on the ashes of dark-electro/industrial formation Darth K.I.A. Cacophonyx. Its music style can be described as a mix of funeral doom metal, dark ambient and blackened doom metal with depressive undertones.

At the moment the discography of Shine, My Boregarden includes the debut album “Siberian Spring” released in February of 2009 and “Monkey’s Fungi” EP released in the summer of 2009.
