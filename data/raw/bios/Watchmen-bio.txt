There are three artists with this name.

1) Watchmen is a hard rock band from Buenos Aires, Argentina. The band was formed in 2003.  The band's discography consists of two studio albums ("Watchmen" released in 2006 and "Nowhere to Hide" released in 2010) and one EP ("Holy Ground" released in 2010).   

2) The Watchmen is a brand new hip hop group presented by Wu-Tang Management. The group consists of Prox, Pro, BlackMask, 7th 7ign and The Cipher. The group released their debut DOUBLE LP Wu-Tang Management Presents Watchmen in May 2010. And in 2011 they released their second LP - Power.

Both BlackMask and 7th 7ign have recorded solo albums, only 7th has released one - 7th Hour (2009)

3) The Watchmen was a hardcore band from Buffalo, NY. They released the 'Disease Of Isolation' 7" on Rorschach records in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Watchmen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
