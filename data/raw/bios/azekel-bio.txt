Azekel Adesuyi, is a Nigerian born British singer-songwriter, musician and producer from East London.

He debuted in February 2013 with That Feeling and A Song to an Unborn Child (Delilah) produced by Nexphase of Tastemaker Music.
A five track EP, the "Circa EP", was released digitally in October via Thunderlightning Recordings. "Raw, Vol. 1" followed on June 2015. It includes the lead singles New Romance , Mad about the boy and Chronophobia. A further track, The War Inside, rounded-up the year. 2016 started by featuring on Massive Attack's Ritual Spirit from the EP of the same name.
