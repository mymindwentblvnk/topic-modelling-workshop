Sepulchral is a Death Metal band from France. Formed in 1988, the group released two demos before disbanding in the year 1993.
They have since reunited in 2005 and have released a demo and a compilation featuring their earlier work.
The band are also featured on the Obscurum per Obscurius split CD, together with Supuration, Putrid Offal, Krhomadeath, Dagon and Nocturnal Fears <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sepulchral">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
