N.W.D. (New World Disorder) is a Venezuelan crossover/thrash band from  San Cristobal, Tachira, founded in 2007. The band was formerly known as Epidia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/N.w.D.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
