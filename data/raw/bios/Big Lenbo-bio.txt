Big Lenbo is an American rapper/songwriter from Maryland. Growing up, he became friends with fellow Maryland rapper Logic. In 2009, Lenbo allowed Logic, who was living under Section 8, to stay in his basement when Logic dropped out of high school. Since then, both Logic and Lenbo became close. Lenbo provided the handwriting on Logic's Under Pressure album cover, and is also featured on Logic's studio album's, The Incredible True Story and Everybody. Lenbo released his first single, produced by 6ix, titled "Protect Your Neck" on February 4, 2016. His debut mixtape, Strange Days, released on July 3, 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Big+Lenbo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
