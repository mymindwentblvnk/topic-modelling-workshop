BLACK AEON are an Ufa, Russia black/death metal band.

Immortal Satan Cult - Vocals / Guitars
Rakoth - Vocals / Bass
Inquisitor - Keyboard
Coldblessed - Drums

The band’s history began in April of 2007 as a 6-piece black metal band.After some changes in line-up band decided to play more brutal music.Now Black Aeon’s style is best described as (symphonic) black / death metal.
