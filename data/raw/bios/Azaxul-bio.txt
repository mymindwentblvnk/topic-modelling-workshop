Azaxul was a sideproject of Gaamalzagoth featuring M.K from Katharsis in session drums, formed in 1997. 
First demo tape was "the arrival of the demonlord",
which was released via Chanteloup creations, limited
to 200 copies. Second tape "...and heaven is crying tears
of fire" is a unreleased demo, only few copies exist.
In 2002 split with S.V.E.S.T released.
