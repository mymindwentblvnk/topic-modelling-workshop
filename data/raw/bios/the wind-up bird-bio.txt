The Wind-Up Bird is the musical project of musician and composer Joseph Grimm, formerly a member of the band 33.3, which is based in Providence, Rhode Island. Grimm is accompanied by Jeff Smith, formerly of Jeromes Dream. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Wind-Up+Bird">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
