Path of Exile is a musical project that consists of Harry Howells (Drums, Clean Vocals, Composing) and Denis Stepancev (Guitar, Bass, Keys, Scream Vocals and Composing) blending the styles of metalcore, rock, acoustic, alternative, film scores and everything else that stands in between in order to create a sweet blend of fresh music.
