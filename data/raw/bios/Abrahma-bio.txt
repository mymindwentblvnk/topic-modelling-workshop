After five years of performing under the moniker Alcohsonic, the French foursome return with a new album, a new style, and a new name: Abrahma. The album Through The Dusty Paths Of Our Lives stands out as a jewel of the heavy rock / psychedelic genre, transporting the listener through a whirl of riffs to a world of obscure swamps where Hindu mythology meets voodoo magic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abrahma">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
