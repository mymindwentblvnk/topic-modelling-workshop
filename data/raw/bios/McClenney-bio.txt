Maryland's McClenney is a 25-year old singer-songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist who makes a forward-thinking soulful blend of Alternative music. Working as both an artist and writer, McClenney has produced for DRAM, Khalid, Marco McKinnis, H.E.R., thatshymn, Dave B and earned a 2018 Grammy nomination for his work on Khalid's "Location". Also noted for turning great songs into infectious dance grooves via his remixes (GoldLink, Joe Hertz and more) McClenney's sound emanates an honesty and organic feel that is truly unique. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/McClenney">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
