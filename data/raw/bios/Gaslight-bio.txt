Gaslight was a British psych-folk ensemble comprising John Drowley (Guitar), Tim Haggis (Guitar), Adrian Ford (Guitar, Piano, Vocals), Robert Swan (Percussion), William Smethurst (Violin, Viola), Richard Soundy (Vocals, Banjo, Guitar), Ian Angus (Vocals, Bass, Cello), and Charles Nuttall (Vocals, Piano, Triangle). They recorded their eponymous album in May 1970, <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gaslight">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
