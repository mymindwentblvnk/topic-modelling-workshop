Baise Ma Hache (/bɛzə ma aʃə/, French for "Kiss My Axe" (or "Fuck My Axe" if slang)) is a French black metal band from Monnetier-Mornex, Haute-Savoie formed in 2013 who were led by John and Thorwald. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Baise+Ma+Hache">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
