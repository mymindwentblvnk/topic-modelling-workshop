WinterRealm was created in 2006 and is the musical endeavor of Aetas, the band's sole member. WinterRealm's music mostly falls under Atmospheric Black Metal, with bit of acoustic and ambient here and there. Recommended for fans of Burzum, Drudkh, Thy Serpent, Abyssic Hate, Nokturnal Mortum, etc... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/WinterRealm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
