Valkija are a female-fronted power metal band from Italy. Their music has  strong influences from 80s metal bands such as Warlock and Chastain. They released their debut album "Avengers of Steel" in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/valkija">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
