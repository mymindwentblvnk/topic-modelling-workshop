U.S. Girls is the moniker used by Meghan Remy. She has released two full length LP's on the Siltbreeze label, 2008's 'Introducing...', and 2010's 'Go Grey'. in 2015, she signed to 4AD releasing her album Half Free in the same year and the follow up  In A Poem Unlimited in 2018.
