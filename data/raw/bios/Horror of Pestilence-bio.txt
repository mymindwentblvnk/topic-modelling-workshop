Horror of Pestilence is a five-piece technical death metal/deathcore band from China,set up since 2007. 
The band's influences come from such bands as The Faceless, Beyond Creation, Obscura ,All Shall Perish, Slice the Cake to name a few. The band have recently played with Unearth at the China tour stage
