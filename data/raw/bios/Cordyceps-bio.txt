Armed with (bass) guitars, loop recorders, effect units and a laptop Cordyceps creates an evolving sound mass of glitchy break-beats, abstract riffs, closed-circuit feedback and layers of droning noises. This two-piece band is currently located in Ghent, Belgium. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cordyceps">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
