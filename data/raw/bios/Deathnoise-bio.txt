Band from Marseille who formed in 1985 playing death/thrash & later changed direction to noisegrind. Were featured on a bootleg split with Switzerland's Fear of God in December 1997 (tracks B1-B7 taken from their Enjoy Life.... demo and B8-B15 recorded live at The Arsenal, 09.09.87 Marseille). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathnoise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
