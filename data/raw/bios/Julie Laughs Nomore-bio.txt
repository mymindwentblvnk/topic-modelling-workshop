Julie Laughs Nomore was a Melodic Death Metal Band from Sweden.

The band took its name from the second track on Candlemass' Chapter VI album.
They were formed in 1995 in Ljusdal, Sweden.

The Band members were:
Danne Carlsson - Vocals
Benny Halvarsson - Guitars, Vocals
Thomas Nilsson - Lead Guitar (Triton Enigma)
Thomas Olsson - Bass, Vocals (Unleashed, Hasty Death)
