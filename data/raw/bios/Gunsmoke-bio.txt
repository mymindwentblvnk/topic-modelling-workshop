1) Western tinged hardcore.

2) Previously an AC/DC tribute band that has also gone under the names Rockafellas and Backseat Rhythm. Featuring: Fredrik Christoffersson (vocals) and Joachim Handin (guitar),  Patrik ”Pata” Tell (drums),  Markus Tell (guitar) and Patrik ”P. J.” Johansson (bass) .   

In spite of their reputation as a live act, they changed their name to  Gunsmoke and recorded two songs, “A Loser’s Game” and “Don’t Steal My Thunder.”  Both songs were released on all major streaming sites in 2019.
