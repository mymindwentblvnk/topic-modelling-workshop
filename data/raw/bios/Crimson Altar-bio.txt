Crimson Altar was a Gothic Metal band from 2003 to 2007. With influences from classic rock / metal through to symphonic and doom metal they released their debut album in 2006. The band split up in 2007 however guitarists chris and allan are now working on anew project winters veil and should have an album ready for release in feb 2012 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crimson+Altar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
