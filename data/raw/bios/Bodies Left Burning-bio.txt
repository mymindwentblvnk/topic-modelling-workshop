Bodies Left Burning (BLB) is a Progressive Death metal band from Lake Havasu City Arizona. They formed in April of 2005 when founding members Blizzard and Thomas met. Today only Blizzard is the original member, but with the new line up consisting of Sean Foster(lead/rhythm guitar) and Derek Ryan(lead/rhythm guitar) new music is being recorded.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bodies+Left+Burning">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
