There are two singers with this name

1) Mavi (born October 7th, 1999) is a rapper from Charlotte, North Carolina who remained fairly unknown until October 18th, 2019 with the drop of his album, let the sun talk. He also gained a lot of attention from a feature he did on Earl Sweatshirt’s FEET OF CLAY on the track "EL TORO COMBO MEAL".

2) Mavi, born on February 6, 2004 in Üsküdar, Mavi Solman, with his stage name "Mavi" is one of Turkey's youngest trap musicians. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mavi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
