Liniker e os Caramelows was a band formed in 2015 in the city of Araraquara, Brazil, and it was headed by singer-songwriter Liniker de Barros Ferreira Campos. Their sound mixes black american rhythms like soul with MPB (brazilian pop music) and often deals with love and human relationships. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Liniker+e+os+Caramelows">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
