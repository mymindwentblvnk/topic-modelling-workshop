Afflicted Convulsion was a Swedish Death metal band formed out of the ruins of a thrash band called "Defiance" and released one official rehearsal tape and two demos under this name. Then the name was shortened to Afflicted. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Afflicted+Convulsion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
