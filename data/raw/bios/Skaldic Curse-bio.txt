British Black Metal band, Skaldic Curse were formed in 2001 and are currently signed to Dark Essence Records. The band consists of five members - Woundz, Astynax, Monolith, Scapula and Vermin and have created several EPs/demos and two full length albums, Pathogen, 2006 (under Forgotten Wisdom Productions) and World Suicide Machine, to be released 23rd February 2009.

Releases to date:

Funereal Eclipse (demo) - 2001
Hated by Matter Itself (demo) - 2002
Contagious Psychic Misey (EP) - 2004, Forgotten Wisdom
Pathogen - 2006, Forgotten Wisdom
