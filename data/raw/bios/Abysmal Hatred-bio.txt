The Death/Thrash Metal band Abysmal Hatred was founded in 2006 by guitartist Markus Hartung in Erlangen, Germany. Later, Thomas Winter joined as the second guitarist and Sebastian Hahn as the drummer.
After a short time, Lukas Gessler became the vocalist.  Bassist Fabian Weitlaner joined in March 2007, resulting in a complete lineup.

Abysmal Hatred played several gigs in underground locations and made some demo-recordings.
