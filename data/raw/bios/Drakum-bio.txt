Drakum is a folk metal band from Barcelona, Spain. The band started in December 2009, after a year looking for the right members.  In 2010 they were immersed in the songwriting and recording their first demo "Around The Oak". In 2011 they were promoting 'Around The Oak "receiving rave reviews, and started the live activity, which has led them to cross their borders, playing festivals in various countries such as Slovenia, Croatia and Ukraine. In 2014 they released their first LP "Torches Will Rise Again". In 2015 begin the tour to promote the album and recording a new EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drakum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
