Godzero was a Thrash/Death/Doom Metal band formed in 1998, in Kėdainiai, Lithuania. The band has self-released a demo called "Promo" in 2001.

Last known line-up:

Bachas     -   bass;
Gedas       -   rhythm guitar;
Michailas  -   solo guitar;
Odis           -   vocals;
Sergėjus   -  drums.
