Thirteen O' Clock, is an unsigned 4 piece Technical Death Metal/Deathcore band from Jember, East Java, Indonesia. Thirteen O' Clock formed in 2010. the lyrical themes is about religion and political criticism.

Members:
    Dio - Drums
    Nuril - Guitars (lead)
