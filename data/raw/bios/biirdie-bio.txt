Jared and richard met kala in Los Angeles on Halloween – have been singing and writing together for four years – their repertoire reflects a serious interest in all forms of music: folk, jazz, r & b, c & w and classical – biirdie are nearly prolific writers but only have one prior album to their credit, 2005’s MORNING KILLS THE DARK – most of the material they perform is written by themselves - their new album, Catherine Avenue, includes “Who Were You Thinkin’ Of,” a Tex-Mex song that was a hit for the Texas Tornados in 1990 - the album is out fall 2007, on Love Minus Zero Recordings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Biirdie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
