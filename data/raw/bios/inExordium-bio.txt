Formerly the doom band Paramaecium, inExordium is crushing new death metal from Melbourne, Australia. Paramaecium had their final show in Oslo, Norway in November 2006, followed immediately by the birth of inExordium. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/InExordium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
