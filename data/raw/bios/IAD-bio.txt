There is more than one band named Iad: 

1. A metal band from Flint, Michigan formed in the mid 1990s and disbanded around 2002. The band had two official releases between 1999 and 2001.

2. Iad is also a spelling of the russian title "Яд" that belongs to a moscow-based avant-punk/math-rock trio and means "poison" in english.
