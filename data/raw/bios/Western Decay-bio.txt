Western Decay are a 5-piece melodic death/thrash band  with a distinct Gothenburg sound based in Brisbane, Australia.

They have played with a number of well-known local Queensland and Australian acts including but not limited to: LORD, Pathogen, A Secret Death, From These Wounds and Infernal Method.

To date they have released a 4-track slipcase demo which is available at shows, and have plans to release a three-way split with the bands Naked Burn and Infinite Thought Process, as well as a mini-album for release in early 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Western+Decay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
