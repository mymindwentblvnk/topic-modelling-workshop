There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Temper is a dance music group that was led by Anthony Malloy. Their only chart entry was with the song "No Favors" which hit #1 on the Hot Dance Music / Club Play chart in 1984. Malloy also formed the group Anthony and the Camp, as well as being a member of Visual, and producing songs for Serious Intention.

2) Temper is a rapper from Chicago, Illinois.
