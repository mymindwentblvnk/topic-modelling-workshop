Burning Flame is a metal band from East Jakarta and Depok which was established in May 2004. Burning Flame has just released their EP Album entitled “Sky of Hate and Damned” in 2010. Their EP Album contains 3 songs were sold on Theories Damn Fest event at IDR 5000, and the money from the sale of the EP Album was donated to victims of natural disasters that occurred in Indonesia. After the print version of the EP Album has sold out, this EP Album was distributed for free via the Internet.
Their EP Album tells a story about the human condition that doesn’t escape the hatred and the wrath of God who created ‘em. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burning+Flame">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
