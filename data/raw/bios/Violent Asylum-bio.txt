Violent Aslyum were a Melodic Thrash-Metal band from Essex England. Taking influence from bands such as Slayer, At the Gates, The Haunted, In Flames. Mixing serious aggressive metal music with a rockn'roll attitude, thier live shows were relentless and thier style unforgettable <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Violent+Asylum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
