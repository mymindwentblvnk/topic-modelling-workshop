Cult of Sorrow is a doom metal band from Cincinnati, Ohio inspired by early heavy metal in it's most primal format. Their self titled, debut EP delves into the world of the Gnostic Luciferian and the sinister tradition of guiding oneself on the path to self-deification. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cult+Of+Sorrow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
