There is more than one artist with this name.

1. Canopy is a six piece Melodic Death Metal band from Stockholm, Sweden. In 2010 they released their latest album MENHIR, which can be streamed in its entirety at last.fm, or http://menhir.bandcamp.com. Canopy formed in 2002 and have since then releases two demo EPs, "During Day One" in late 2004, and "Will and Perception" in early 2005. The band realeased their first full-length album "Serene Catharsis", in 2006. In 2009 a re-recording of "Will and Perception" was done. This album also featured a re-recording of "Common Walls" from the first demo "During Day One" as well as two completely new tracks.  http://www.entercanopy.com
