Burst into the Israeli metal scene in late 2009, the band had managed to build a loyal fan base, due to their crushing melodic hardcore music, and highly energetic stage performance.
After all the copies of their first independently released demo got sold out, the band went on recording their debut album "Legacy", produced by the American producer Mark Lewis who worked with bands such as: Deicide, Devil Driver, The Black Dahlia Murder, Trivium, All that remains, Whitechapel, seemless, Bury Your Dead, Sanctity, Adrift, GodForbid .
