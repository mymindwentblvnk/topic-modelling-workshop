American slamming brutal death/blackened death metal band and brainchild of guitarist Dan Martin. They currently have released one EP, participated in one split and have a new EP entitled Meaningless in the works of getting released for summer of 2017 featuring an all new lineup. Their genre is commonly cited as "blackened slam".
