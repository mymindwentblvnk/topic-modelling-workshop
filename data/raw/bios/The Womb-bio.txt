The Womb is a band formed in 1998 by English musician Alan Driscoll, currently based in Melbourne, Australia.

The line-up and musical style of The Womb constantly changes. All records by The Womb are free to download from http://www.alandriscoll.com/thewomb

If you are new to The Womb, try starting with either of our Best Of compilations. Regular free releases are also announced on the official Facebook page at http://www.facebook.com/thewombmusic

Canada:

In addition to what is mentioned above, there is another band called The Womb from Toronto, Ontario, Canada, who play a blend of metal that incorporates progessive, thrash, punk, and sludge type riffs (just to name a few).  

The Womb formed in early 2006 and have released 2 albums so far, a self-titled in 2007 & their most recent 2009 release This is the Doomlodge.  Songs like "Finding Light", "Arrival", "One with Nature", "Ravaged Wrist Slapback," & "Your House Exploded" belong to this band.
