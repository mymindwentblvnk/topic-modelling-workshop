There are three bands that go by Mortal Remains:

1. Mortal Remains is a thrash metal band from Western Germany, in the style of early thrash from the 1980s.
Official Website: http://www.mortalremains.de
MySpace: http://www.myspace.com/mortalremainsgermany

2. Mortal Remains was also a death metal band from South Shields UK, who released a 12" split with Lord of Putrefaction on Dave Gedge's Nuclear Gore Records. The tracks were Excoriate, Stench of Humanity, Infected, and Embryonic Rupture.

3. Mortal Remains is also a young death metal act from Kennewick, Washington. They released their first, self-titled EP in Summer, 2008 on Discordant Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortal+Remains">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
