Pianist Vijay Iyer's jazz trio, with Stephan Crump on bass and Marcus Gilmore on drums. The trio released their album 'Historicity' to glowing reviews in 2009. The album includes jazz interpretations of Stevie Wonder's 'Big Brother' and M.I.A's 'Galang' alongside original compositions.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vijay+Iyer+Trio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
