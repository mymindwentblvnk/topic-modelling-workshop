SiK is the most Powerful Southern Groove Metal band to emerge from Dallas, TX in a long time. The band is Metal with a broad sweeping appeal to the mass. They are not limited by one style of Metal or Hard Rock; therefore capturing the listening pleasure of all ages and all walks of life.

Developing their chops in various respected Dallas metal bands throughout the nineties, this powerful quartet came together to produce something original and enthralling. SiK’s reputation for genuine musicianship and high energy, smash mouth performances has made them a leading presence on the Dallas music scene. Heavy-formatted grooves crafted with captivating melody are clearly heard on SiK’s new LP, “The Weak Is Broken.” Opening for a national act on just their second performance and making headlining status thereafter. The group’s sturdy dedication is what drives them.
