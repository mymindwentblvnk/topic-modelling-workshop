Thunderclaw are a 4 piece instrumental act from Adelaide, South Australia. They have just released a split EP with fellow Adelaidian instrumentalists SWORDS after hitting the road to Melbourne together. The split is released through Brett Davis'(ex-Love Like Electrocution/My Sister the Cop) new establishment project ENPSYCLOPEDIOPS. Drummer Joel Crannitch also plays drums in Adelaide folk rock band Leader Cheetah. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thunderclaw">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
