Theodore Unit is a Hip Hop collective featuring various rappers from the Stapleton area in Staten Island, NY. The group is headed by Ghostface and features the following core members; Du-Lilz,  Shawn Wiggs, Solomon Childs, Cappadonna, Trife da God and Kryme Life.
 
Thier debut album 718 (2004) was met with a fair amount critical acclaim due to its 'grimey ass' lyrics.
