Dead existence are based in North London and were formed in 2004.

Dead Existence play music that is slow, heavy, and aggressive.  The lyrical themes centre around misery, despair and misanthrophic woe.

They have been seen sharing the stage with the likes of: Brutal Truth, Yob, Pagan Altar, Ramesses,  Dark Castle, Kongh, Necro Deathmort, Ravens Creed, Saviours, Centurions Ghost, Cannabis Corpse, Tombs, The Secret, Conan, Black Breath, Victims and many more.

So far they have released a self pruduced full length and a Split EP with Dopefight and a EP entitled "Born Into The Planet's Scars" out on Withered Hand Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dead+Existence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
