UnterWortverdacht (UWVD) is a german rap group, consisting of Bayz Benzon, Boots Boro, Maz Barak and Cassatlantis (Ca$$).

The Rap-Crew out of Frankfurt exists since 1997. Until 2001 the have mostly done live-shows. Since 2001 UnterWortverdacht releases free tracks in the internet or on various rap-compilations.

In 2004 they released a free-ep called "Dreckiger Sex" on their website, because they didn't want to use these songs for the production of their new ep.

In 2006 they signed with the label 3p. At the 31th of march they released their debut-ep "Aufstand der Aufrechten". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/UnterWortverdacht">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
