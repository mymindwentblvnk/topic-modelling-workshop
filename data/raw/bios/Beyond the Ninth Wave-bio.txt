Beyond The Ninth Wave are a two man Black Metal band from Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada. often referred to as "Suicidal", or "Depressive" Black Metal, Beyond The Ninth Wave play a similar style to that of Burzum and Leviathan. The Canadian band have released one full-length album thus far, which is entitled "Volume 1" and was released in the Autumn of 2005.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beyond+the+Ninth+Wave">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
