Old Throne is a One Man Band Black Metal founded in 2007 by Fernando Count Old in the city of Nova Friburgo "Rio de Janeiro / BR.

His thematic approach: Anti-Christianity, Battle, Honor, Hate and Depression and its musical roots are the bands: Bathory, Burzum (old), Darkthrone (old), Judas Iscariot and Nargaroth.
