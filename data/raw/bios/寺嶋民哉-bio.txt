Tamiya Terashima (寺嶋民哉, Terashima Tamiya), born on April 10th 1958 is a Japanese composer. He is best known for his compositions and musical arrangements for anime, drama and game soundtracks, especially Falcom series like Ys and The Legend of Heroes. One of his latest works is the epic score to the 2006 Ghibli movie ゲド戦記 (Gedo Senki, Tales from Earthsea).
