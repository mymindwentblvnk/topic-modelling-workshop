Nod One's Head is an electro live music project from Berlin. NOH sees itself to be an authentic band without missing out on club culture. In 2012 they released their first singel "UP". There is a diverse range of influences in their music: an outstanding singing voice as well as elements of House, Pop and Hip Hop harmonise in their entirety. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nod+One%27s+Head">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
