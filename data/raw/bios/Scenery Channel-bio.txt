Scenery Channel is a young Finnish band playing progressive rock/metal hybrid music. Formed in January 1999 by Tommi Inkilä and Pasi Hiltula. Scenery Channel released their debut album "Premiere" in June 2006 as independent and sells it through their official website http://www.scenerychannel.com

The current lineup:

Tommi Inkilä (guitar, background vocals)
Pasi Hiltula (keyboard)
Mikko Lankinen (vocals)
Olli Jakola (drums)
