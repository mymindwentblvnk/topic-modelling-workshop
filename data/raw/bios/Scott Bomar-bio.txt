Scott Bomar is a veteran of the Memphis bands Impala and the Bo-Keys who grew up on the music of North Mississippi performers like Junior Kimbrough and fife-and-drum bandleader Othar Turner -- has included plenty of indigenous music on the Black Snake Moan soundtrack. R.L. Burnside, Bobby Rush and Jessie Mae Hemphill all add to the film's texture. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scott+Bomar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
