The Band was formed in 2002 under the name "Sacrasphemy"
The line up consisted of Darius W.(ex Nebular Moon)(Vox), Marek W.(Guit.), Mathias Q.(Synths), Nils L.(ex Via Obscura)(Guit.), Matthias T. (Bass), Daniel B.(Drums). In 2008 the Band equipped a professional reccording studio and due to the grave changes in musical style and quality of the music and regarding to the line-up changes during the years, the band decided to change their name to Project Entropy in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Project+Entropy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
