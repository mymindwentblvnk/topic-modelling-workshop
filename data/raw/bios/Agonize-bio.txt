1) Technical melodic death/black metal band from Tuzla, Bosnia. Current members are: Adnan Hatic (guitars and vocals), Haris Hasančević (bass), and Tihomil Grgic (drums). They formed in 1997 and have released, aside from several demos and EPs, two full-length albums: "The Chosen One" in 2000 and "When Memory Dies..." in 2004.

2) Melodic death metal band from Dresden, Germany. Released first album, "Chaos Reborn", in 2016.

3) Death/thrash metal band from Vejle, Denmark. Started in 89 as Alterator , changing name the next year. Split up after two demos.
