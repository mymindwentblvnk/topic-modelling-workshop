SLIFT is a psychedelic rock band from Toulouse, France.  The current lineup consists Jean Fossat on guitar, synth and vocals, Rémi Fossat on bass and vocals and Canek Flores on drums, percussions and farfisa. They have released one full-length album, "UMMON" in 2020 and two EPs, "Space Is The Key" in 2017 and "La Planète Inexplorée" in 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SLIFT">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
