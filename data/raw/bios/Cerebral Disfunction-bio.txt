One of the earliest death metal/grindcore bands in Italy, Cerebral Disfunction were formed in 1989 in Bologna, Emilia-Romagna. Their only releases were "Sensations of Nausea" in 1989 and a 19-track tape the next year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cerebral+Disfunction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
