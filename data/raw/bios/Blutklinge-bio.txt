German atmospheric / depressive black metal band, Helmrod did all the instruments and Vocals for the band. They were planning to release a full length record this summer. But as of late September 2007 they split up. They have two demos and one EP (Call of the Blackened Woods).
'Blutklinge' was formed in 2003 under the name 'Blackmysk' and released the first demo called 'Once Sent From The Forest'.In 2006 the name was changed in 'Blutklinge'.
