Heavy metal act from Kyoto, Japan, formed in 1984. After releasing two demo tapes in the mid 80's, Excuriver released a full-length studio album in 1988, entitled "In Hard Time". Some members from Excuriver formed Ebony Eyes when they disbanded in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Excuriver">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
