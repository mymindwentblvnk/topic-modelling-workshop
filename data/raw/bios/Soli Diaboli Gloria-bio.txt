Soli Diaboli Goloria is a Russian black metal band from Petrozavodsk, Karelia. Since forming in 2006, they have currently only released two full-length albums. Their music will most likely appeal to fans of Mortuus, Fides Inversa, Sect and/or Deathspell Omega.
