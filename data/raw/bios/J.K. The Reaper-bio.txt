Jabril Kenan, better known by his stage name J.K. the Rapper, is a solo hip-hop artist out of Greensboro, North Carolina.

Self-described as “a skinny nigga with no hopes and a couple dreams”, J.K.’s style is best described as cerebral. He utilizes his creativity to construct both thoughtful and entertaining tracks.

J.K. has released several independent mixtapes including his well-received “I’m A Loser” series. His most recent project, “The X6 Departure”, is a progression of 6 mixtape releases in 6 consecutive weeks hosted by Laced Work | Digital Marketing in Eugene, Oregon and can be downloaded for free. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/J.K.+The+Reaper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
