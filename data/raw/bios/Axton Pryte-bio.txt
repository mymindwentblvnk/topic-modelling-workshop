Axton Pryte were a hard rock band hailing from Quimper, France. The band was comprised of Claude Ziegler (guitar), Ian Proerer (drums), Ludovic Klein (bass) and Eric Annothe (vocals). They released a 3-title demo in 1984, and, two years later, "The Lab", a 5-title EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Axton+Pryte">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
