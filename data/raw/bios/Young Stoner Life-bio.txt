YSL Records (also known as Young Stoner Life and Young Slime/Slatt Life) is an Atlanta-based record label imprint founded by the Atlanta-based rapper Young Thug. The company is a label imprint of 300 Entertainment. Its artists include Gunna, Lil Keed and more. Several contemporary rappers, such as Lil Uzi Vert, Lil Baby, YoungBoy Never Broke Again, Drake, and Lil Gotit have expressed allegiance to YSL, but are not contractually signed. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Young+Stoner+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
