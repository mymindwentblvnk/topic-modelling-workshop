Formed in 2006 in Bern, Berne, Switzerland, Sun of the Blind is a solo ambient black metal project of the Swiss musician Zhaaral also known for his backing vocals and guitar work in Darkspace.

Sun of the Blind's debut Skullreader was put out by Avantgarde Music on May 3rd, 2009, and fans of Darkspace and Blut aus Nord should be head over heels for this release.
