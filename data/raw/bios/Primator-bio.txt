Heavy metal band from Makó, Hungary from 1990 to 1995.

Members:
Roland Bálint - vocals
Zoltán Vékony - guitars
Miklós Völgyesi - guitars
Sándor Szabó - bass
Gábor Farkas - drums

Primator disbanded in 1995, with the two guitar players starting PH, another heavy metal band in Szeged, still active.
