Beard of Antlers is a stoner/doom band from Wilmington, NC that formed in 2009.

Members: 
Will Poole - Bass/Vocals
Victor Marriot - Guitar
Dan Todd - Guitar
Carlos Denogean - Drums
Ryan Wishnia - Vocals

Beard of Antlers has released one EP entitled "The Essence of Wilderness" released June 2012
