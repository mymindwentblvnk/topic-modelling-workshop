Former guitarist and founder of the noise rock duo Harry Pussy (later a trio) Bill Orcutt’s sound is a stuttered reimagining of blues guitar. One can hear familiar Southern folk scales between Orcutt’s jagged solo acoustic phrases, pulling and pushing melodies into unresolved fragments that eventually come unmoored in vast and satisfying note-torrents. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bill+Orcutt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
