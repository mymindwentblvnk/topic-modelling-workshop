2) Electrical Circus are also a band from Belfast, Ireland and they are bringing rockin blues music to the masses. 

3) New wave ambient/gothic duo comprising Pier Rubesa and Shauna. Electric Circus are known for their single album "Hello" recorded in Toronto, Canada and released in 1988.

4) Electric Circus is also a psychedelic/jazz rock band from Italy, they published 1 EP in 2014, called Evoluzione.
