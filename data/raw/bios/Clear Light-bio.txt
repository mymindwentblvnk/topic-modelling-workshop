Clear Light  was an American psychedelic rock band of the 60s. They released a self-titled album in 1967, which has sometimes been reissued as Black Roses. Members included Bob Seal (guitar), Cliff DeYoung (vocals), Doug Lubahan (bass),  Dallas Taylor (drums), and Ralph Schuckett (keyboards). They appeared in the movie THE PRESIDENT'S ANALYST starring James Coburn performing the song "She's Ready To Be Free".
