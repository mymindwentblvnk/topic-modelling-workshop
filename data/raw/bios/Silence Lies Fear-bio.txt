Modern melodic death metal band from Baku, formed in 2008.
Since the band's inception, they recorded one album and one single.
Silence Lies Fear was founded in 2008 by Jakhangir Zeynalov and Asim Ragimov
as strictly death metal band.Their purpose was to write songs with a more
melodic musical direction,so genre softly changed to modern melodic death metal.
Musical style of this band is characterized by the constant use of melodic
lead and rhytm guitars,quite beautiful keyboards and pretty tight drums.
The Vocal is death growl and screaming-style singing,complemented
at times by clean vocals.


Influences:
In Flames, Soilwork, Mors Principium Est, Dreamshade, Parkway Drive, All That Remains, Dark Tranquillity,Between the Buried And Me, At The Gates, From Autumn To Ashes, Insomnium
