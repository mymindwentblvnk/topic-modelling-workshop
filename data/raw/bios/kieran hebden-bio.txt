Kieran Hebden (born 1977 in Putney, London, England, United Kingdom) is a post-rock and electronic musician. Hebden first came to prominence as a member of the band Fridge before establishing himself as a solo artist under the moniker of Four Tet. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kieran+Hebden">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
