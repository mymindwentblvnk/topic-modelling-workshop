Funeral God were a Melodic Black/Death Metal band that originated in Pretoria in 1998.  The band consisted of:

Dwayne Coetzee - Vocals, Guitars
Charl - Guitars 
Ronald - Bass & Keyboards
Riaan - Drums

The band released a few demos and a single, "Decayed Woods" in 2000.  An EP entitled "Misanthropic Drama" was released in 2004.  The band also released a split with fellow South Africans, Morthor.

The band split up and Dwayne Coetzee joined South African metallers, Sacrifist, on vocals.
