Thrash metal act from Japan, formed in 1988. The bands first full-length studio album, named "Growl", was released in spring 1994 through Howling Bull records. In 1996, "Motive" followed. In 1999, the band released a EP named "Hurry Up And Appear Outside". Currently split-up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Displaced+person">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
