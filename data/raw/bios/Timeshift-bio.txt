Timeshift is the name of at least two artists.

Timeshift is the name of a three piece female fronted Croatian progressive metal band based out of Å ibenik. Their debut self titled album was recorded between January 2013 and March 2014, and was released on the 11th of July, 2014.

Timeshift is also the name of a Belgian dance project that features a solo male vocalist called Indy Lee Rogers.
