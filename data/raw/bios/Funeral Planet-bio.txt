Funeral Planet is a Doom metal band from Seinäjoki, Finland. Spawn from the frustrated doomheads of Mika Hankaniemi (ex-God Forsaken, ex-Putrid) and Mika Pajula (ex-God Forsaken) in the year 2003. After several lineup-changes the band 
lineup is now stable: Mika Hankaniemi - Vocals, Tomi Kauhajärvi - Drums, Jani Härkönen - Guitar, Juha Köykkä - Guitar, Mika Pajula - Bass

Their first three-song demo "Born In Black" emerged in 2004.
In 2006 they recorded and released "Planetary Rites."
Third three-song demo "The Chosen Ones"  was released in 2007.
