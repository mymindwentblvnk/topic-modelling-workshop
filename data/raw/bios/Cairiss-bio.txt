Cairiss are a 5 piece female fronted post-metal/black metal band from Southampton, UK. They released their first demo track 'Disgraced' in February 2014 and their first EP Fall in June 2016. 

Cairiss combine clean vocals/black-metal inspired harsh vocals with crushing guitars, powerful orchestral harmonies & choral arrangements to create a unique sound that's rich in beauty and depth. 

Their debut EP "Fall" is available to download from our Bandcamp, iTunes and other popular services now; https://cairiss.bandcamp.com/album/fall
