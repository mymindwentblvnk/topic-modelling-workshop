Joseph Da Rin de Barbera (born 1995), who performs as Young Franco, is an Australian DJ and record producer from Brisbane, Queensland. He is based in Sydney, New South Wales. Barbera has performed at many Australian festivals including Splendour in the Grass and Falls Festival. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Young+Franco">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
