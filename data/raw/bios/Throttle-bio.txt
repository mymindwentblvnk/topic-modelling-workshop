Throttle (real name Robbie Bergin) is a popular EDM producer from Melbourne, Australia. He has released music through both Spinnin Records and Monstercat. He has gained great success with his single "Hit the Road Jack" and his collaboration with Galantis "Tell Me You Love Me".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Throttle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
