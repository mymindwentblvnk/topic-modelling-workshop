1) Bad Neighbors are a band which features Johnny Evil on vocals, Saw-Ha on bass/vocals, Joey Smalls on guitar, and Stauffy on drums. They are from El Cajon which is a city in San Diego County, California. They formed in late 2009/early 2010. They just recorded and will be releasing their demo titled "Chase Avenue" in February 2011. Their music is considered punk, but they have a few other influences that show in songs like "Done" and "Headache" and "Boring".
