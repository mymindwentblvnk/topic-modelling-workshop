Ercandize (Ercan Kocer; born 12th April 1978)) is a German rapper from Kool Savas label "Optik Records". In 2007 he released his album "Verbrannte Erde" (top position in the German album charts: 17). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ercandize">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
