Polish band formed in 2009 in Warsaw, Masovia. In september 2010 they  recorded their debut LP "Juggler". They are playing oldschool thrash, sounds like '80 with fast, heavy riffs, growling bass and wild vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rusted+Brain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
