Pete & The Pirates are an indie rock band from Reading, England. Touring the UK since 2006, their first full-length album, Little Death, was released in February, 2008. The band consists of Thomas Sanders (vocals), Peter Hefferan (vocals and guitar), David Thorpe (guitar), Peter Cattermoul (bass) and Jonny Sanders (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Pete+&+The+Pirates">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
