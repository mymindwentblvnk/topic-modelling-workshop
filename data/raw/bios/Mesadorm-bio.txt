Mesadorm is British musician Blythe Pepino (previously known for her role in London art-pop three-piece Vaults) together with Aaron Zahl, Daisy Palmer, David Johnston, and Jo Silverston. The debut album, Heterogaster, was released in 2018.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mesadorm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
