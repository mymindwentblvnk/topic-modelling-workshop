Anguis Aeturnus was a one-man project. All instruments and compositions are done by Ordeith. On the Black Despondency Demo, he was supported by a guest vocalist/lyricist, Hadrian Sakre. 
Anguis Aeturnus was formed in 2004 in Sleaford, England.

At the end of 2006, Anguis Aeturnus was officially renamed as 'Nihternnes'. This project is still very much active.

Releases:

Black Despondency (2005)
Unfettered (2006)

Unreleased:

Moloch (2004)
Skygge (2004)
The Cult Aeons (2005)

Material from the two demos 'Moloch' and 'Skygge' were rerecorded and released on Unfettered, amongst previously unrecorded material. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anguis+Aeturnus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
