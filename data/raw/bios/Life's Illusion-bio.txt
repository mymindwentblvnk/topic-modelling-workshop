Life's Illusion was a Depressive Black Metal project from Kharkov, Ukraine, formed in spring 2011 by A.Nameless (compositions, lyrics, guitar), later S.(guitar, drums) and N. (vocals) joined her. 
After recording 3 rehearsal tracks, the previous line-up split up. Now Life's Illusion became a solo project of A.Nameless. 
Now split up.
