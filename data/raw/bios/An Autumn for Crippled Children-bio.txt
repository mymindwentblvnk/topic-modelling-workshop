An Autumn for Crippled Children are a post-black metal trio from Friesland, Netherlands formed in 2008. The band consists of three members also playing in other bands, which are kept secret. The band name was taken from a song by EbonyLake.

Their debut, Lost, was released in May, 2010 and their second album, Everything, was released September 1st, 2011. Their third album was Only the Ocean Knows, which was released on September 28th, 2012. Their fourth LP entitled Try Not to Destroy Everything You Love was released on November 11th, 2013.

A free digital EP titled Try Not to Love Everything You Destroy was released on their Bandcamp in anticipation of their fifth album, entitled The Long Goodbye, which was released February 23rd, 2015.
