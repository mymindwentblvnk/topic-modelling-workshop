White Hell was a speed/early black metal-band from Japan. They released one single-EP in 1985, named "Lucifer", which features three songs.

Line-up:
Jim Stiles: Vocals
Ken Lynch: Rhythm Guitar
Katsunori Tabata: Lead Guitar
Caspar Appeldoorn *: Bass
Keisuke Shimizu: Drums

Chorus:
Andy Frick, Rob Stiles, Toshihede Sasaki, Ken Lynch, Jim Stiles
