Profancer is a Thrash/Death Metal band from Singapore

Lineup:
Azman - Vocals
Caduccas - Guitar
Razali - Bass
Dagoth - Drums

Discography:
Rest Without Peace Demo - 1990

There is a second demo which contains 4 tracks titled "Forever Lost" supposed to be released in 1991 but it never happened due to the band split up. The same 4 tracks then was released under the monicker of Beheaded Nasrani titled "Nasrani" demo 1991. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Profancer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
