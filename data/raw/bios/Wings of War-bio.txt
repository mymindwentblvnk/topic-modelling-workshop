Wings Of War is Polish Raw Thrash/Black Metal band formed in 2005.
Members: 
Apocalyptic Winds aka Belial - Guitar, Vocals (Moontower, Demiurg (Pol), Selbstmord (Pol), Pagan Fire (Pol))

Trenhell - Guitar, Bass, Drums

Releases:

In the Glare of Black / War Forever Split, 2006
Battle Hymn EP, 2006
