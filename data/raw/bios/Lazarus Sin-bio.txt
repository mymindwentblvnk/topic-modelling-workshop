Lazarus Sin is a Heavy Metal band from Eugene,Or.
Formed by Joe Tierney in 1985.

Members include:
Joseph Tierney (vocals)
Edward Cross (guitars)
David Thorne (bass)
Bolund Maice (drums). 

Discography:
"Intracranial Mass" (1988)
"Open Up My Mind" (1992)
