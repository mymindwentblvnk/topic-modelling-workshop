Jazzinuf is an Artist and musician based in NYC. He has been making jazz hiphop/ lo-fi beats as a hobby since 2012 and is still using his original logic 9 from 2012 without any updates making his music extra Lo Fidelity. His works are mainly influenced by memories, colors, textures and emotions that he has cultivated throughout his life. Nature is his best friend and he gets many ideas from sharing his life with mountains. His music exudes optimism and one of his soul purposes is to bring more positivity to the society and remind us that we are all one. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/jazzinuf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
