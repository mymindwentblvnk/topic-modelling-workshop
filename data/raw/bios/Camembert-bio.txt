1. Camembert formed in a bedroom in Glasgow with the intention of making dreamy, experimental pop.

Tim, Heather, Joe and Rob came together through mutual friends whilst living in Glasgow. With a love of leftfield music they blend electronic and acoustic sounds incorporating a range of influences from genres including electronic, alternative, folk and post rock.
