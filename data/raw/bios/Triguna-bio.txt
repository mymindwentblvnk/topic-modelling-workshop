Triguna is a Progressive Death Metal band from Algonquin, Illinois. They formed in 2012 and have gone through multiple line up changes since their inception. Triguna has released 3 full length albums and 2 EP's. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Triguna">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
