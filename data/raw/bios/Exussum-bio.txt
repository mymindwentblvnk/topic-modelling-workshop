Exussum is a blackened death metal/grindcore band from Grand Junction, Colorado.  They formed in 2004.  All members of the band left, except for Andreas "Attis" Hollstein (vocals, guitars).  The band is now on hold.   Exussum's lyrical themes include fire, death, weed and corporate America.  They have released one demo, one live album, three EP's and one full-length album.  To the Head Records was the last label they were signed to.
