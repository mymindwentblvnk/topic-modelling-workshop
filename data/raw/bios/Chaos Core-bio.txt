Chaos Core are a Melodic Death Metal band from Italy who formed in 2002. The first demo was released in 2005 and entitled 'The Landscape Of Greed'. This was followed by the 2006 demo 'Destiny Machine'. The debut album 'Born In Silence' was released in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chaos+Core">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
