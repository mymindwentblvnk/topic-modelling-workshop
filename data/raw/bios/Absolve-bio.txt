Alternative metal/nu metal outfit formed in 2000 after singer Adam Morse split from his previous group Trustfall. The band had two releases, an EP in 2001 called The Anxiety of Influence and an album in 2002 called A Cure for Separation, and broke up in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Absolve">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
