Armageddon Awaits is a Symphonic Death Metal Band from Milwaukee Wisconsin. They have been influenced heavily by Horror movie scores and soundtracks as well as metal influences including artists like Motionless in White, Chelsea Grin, and Whitechapel. They are actively touring and currently unsigned and looking. Armageddon Awaits records at 608 Studios in Milwaukee. They have released two EPs and 4 singles and are always working on assembling more music. They can be contacted via Facebook. Their first music video and a lot of additional content is available at their YouTube channel. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Armageddon+Awaits">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
