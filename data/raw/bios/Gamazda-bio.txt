Alexandra Kuznetsova creates piano rock. She was born and lives in Moscow. She started playing piano at 3 years old and later graduated from the Moscow State Tchaikovsky Conservatory. In the spring of 2019 she started her own YouTube channel where she plays covers in her own arrangement. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gamazda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
