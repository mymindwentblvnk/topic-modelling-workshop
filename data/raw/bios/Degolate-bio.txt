Brazilian death metal band formed in 2013 by brothers Victor (B/V) and Caio Angelotti (G) with drummer Victor Nabuco, after the demise of  Cruscifire. A split with Gulag, entitled Consanguineous Fury, was released in early 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Degolate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
