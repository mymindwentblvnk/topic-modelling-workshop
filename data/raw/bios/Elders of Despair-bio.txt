Official website: www.eldersofdespair.com
Elders of Despair is a danish band with it's roots in Hilleroed in the north of Zealand.
The band consists of Michael Buch Christensen (guitars) and Christian Engelbrekt Pedersen (guitars). The recordings contain both bass, drums and keyboards though.
EoD plays melodic metal with elements from black, death, folk, speed and heavy.
