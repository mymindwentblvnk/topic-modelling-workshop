Cranioclast is a duo from Hagen, Germany which produce various artistic endeavors, most commonly music. Usually those two identify themselves as Soltan Karik and Sankt Klario. They have their own label called CoC and were working with a number of like-minded bands, including Kallabris, Fetisch Park and A.B.G.S.. Fantastic experimental ritual ambient. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cranioclast">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
