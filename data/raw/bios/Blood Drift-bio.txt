Amazing Blackened Death from Belgium.

Official Biography:

Blood Drift got founded in 2006 when Aris teamed up with Matt, Elias and Korneel's previous band. They came up with the name Blood Drift in the process, the band started writing tracks at an intense tempo but at the same time being very critical about their work.

In April-May 2007 they recorded their 3-track EP Thrusting Plagues (Death/Black metal lyrically dealing with the Apocalypse). The mixing and mastering was done by Aris and his brother Otis, who later on joined Blood Drift as bass player.
