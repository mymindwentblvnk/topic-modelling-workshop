Toph, aka Trouble Over Tokyo, is an independent musician from London England.  Aside from being a multi-instrumentalist of prodigious talent he is gifted with a truly astounding voice.  His songs reflect themes ranging from alienation and loneliness to love, pain, and pure joy.
