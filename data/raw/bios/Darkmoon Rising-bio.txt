DarkMoon Rising was a Symphonic Black/Death Metal band from Bulgaria, Blagoevgrad. They released a demo in 2002 and had numerous compilation and split appearences, one of which is the great Winter Storms 2001, a compilation with then leading Bulgarian extreme acts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Darkmoon+Rising">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
