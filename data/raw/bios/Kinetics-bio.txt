There are four artists named Kinetics:

1. Kinetics is a 21 year old Drum & Bass producer from the UK. He has releases on Lifestyle Recordings, Extent V.I.P and DNBB Recordings.

2. Kinetics is a rapper from the 845.

3. Kinetics hails from Northern Denmark, and plays electronic rock. The band is set to release their first EP, Zeitgeist, in the spring of '08.
