Sammy Curr band was recently formed in Italy by guitarist/bassist Cliff "Ragman" Whisper and lead singer Leslie Graham; completed by the join of Roger Mockus (drums).
Their style is inspired by Metallica, Black Sabbath, Diamond Head, Blitzkrieg, Sex Pistols, Misfits.
The name Sammy Curr is taken from 1986 movie "Trick or Treat".
