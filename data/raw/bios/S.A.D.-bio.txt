S.A.D. is a Latvian dream pop/nu-gaze band formed in Rīga, Latvia in 2014.

Members:

Anna Freimane - vocals;
Kaspars Pupa (ex-Skumju Akmeņi, ex-Akmeņu Dārzs) - guitar;
Edīte Ķeņģe - guitar;
Ivars Blūms (ex-Skumju Akmeņi, ex-Akmeņu Dārzs) - bass;
Ansis Mežsargs (ex-Tumors) - drums.

Former members:

Lelde Muceniece - vocals;
Kaspars Liepiņš (ex-Oceanpath) - synthesizers;
Krišjānis Purens (Catalepsia, Mono:Tas) - drums.
