Altered Moves Two was a technical thrash band formed in 1988 in Zwanenburg, North Holland (Netherlands).
They released only one studio album before spliting up in 1994: Shifter Of Minds (1992).

Members:
Robert Voogt - guitar 
Jim Buleca - bass 
Geert De Wijngaert - drums 
Bert Ettema - vocals/guitar
