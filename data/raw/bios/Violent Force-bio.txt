Violent Force is a German thrash metal band formed in 1984 in Velbert. The band released  several demos (Dead City, Velbert Dead City, Live at Hilden, Dead City-The Night) and one album in 1987 - Malevolent Assault of Tomorrow. Violent Force split up in 1989 after recording a second LP, which remains unreleased.
