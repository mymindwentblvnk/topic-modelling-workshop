There are currently 3 bands using the name Erebo, detailed in order of year of creation:

1. Erebo is a black metal band from Costa Rica formed in 1998. The band has thus far released 2 albums. Lyrical themes include Paganism, Mythology and Anti-Christianity.

2. Erebo is a brazilian Death Metal band from Pedro Leopoldo, Minas Gerais, formed in 2003. They released a demo in 2005 called "Oath in the Estige's Margin" and are influenced by bands like Morbid Angel, Behemoth and Nile. Band members are: G. Goreon (guitars and vocals), T. Ares (bass and vocals). The band is looking for a new drummer.
