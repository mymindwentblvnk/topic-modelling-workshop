In an album of one minute-long songs, Tulsa native Byron Crenshaw officially releases his first work as “The Growth Eternal” with introspective sentiments on Black identity, love for the environment, social media anxieties, and more. The psychedelic jazz album “Bass Tone Paintings (BTP)” spans seventeen tracks spoken in four languages, recorded primarily with Byron’s bass guitar and vocoder.
