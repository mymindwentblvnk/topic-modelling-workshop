"Takamiy" is the name of the solo project of THE ALFEE guitarist Toshihiko Takamizawa (高見沢俊彦). So far, there are two releases under the "Takamiy" name.
The 2009 single VAMPIRE ~Yuuwaku no BLOOD~ / Yatterman no Uta, and the 2010 album Fantasia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Takamiy">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
