Bird of Prey is San Francisco bay area native Torin Goodnight.  Creating electronic music for over a decade, he has developed a unique and very distinct sound.  His music hybridizes various genres, fusing the enveloping sensibilities of psychedelic sound with hard hitting dubstep and breakbeat elements.  Combining aggressive basslines, twisted leads, and colorful atmospheres to create diverse and immersive soundscapes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bird+of+Prey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
