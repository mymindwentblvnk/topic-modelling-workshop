Isolert is a Black Metal band from Greece formed in 2015 by Panos T. and Nick S.
On March of 2015 Isolert released their first demo called "Isolated Soul" .
On November of 2016 Isolert stopped being a duet and Apostolos K. (Guitars) and Giannis E. (Bass) joined the band.
Their debut album "No Hope, No Light...Only Death" is going to be released by Ogmios Underground on 1 May 2016. 
Members:
Panagiotis T. - Guitars,Vocals 
Nick S. - Drums,Vocals
Giannis E. - Bass
