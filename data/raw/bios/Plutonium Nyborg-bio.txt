PSYCHEDELICDOOMSTONER
Fairly heavy and slightly psychedelic doom rock. Sometimes almost Reverend Bizarre slow, but varies all the way up to mid tempo traditional doom in the vein of the old gods. Raspy, blueslike vocals makes this band sound interesting. All in all a very relaxing sound.
