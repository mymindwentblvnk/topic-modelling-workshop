Mindflayer is an experimental rock band from Providence, Rhode Island, United States, made up of Brian Chippendale (of Lightning Bolt) and Matt Brinkman (of Forcefield) which was formed out of Fort Thunder. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mindflayer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
