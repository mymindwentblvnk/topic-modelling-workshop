Avantgarde/occultism influenced speed/doom metal-band from Chiba, Japan. The band formed in 1985. The bassplayer from the last known line-up, Takami, played in Rosenfeld. Bellzlleb released their first 7" vinyl single in 1987, named "Satanic Metal". After that they released three full length studio albums, between 1989 and 1992. Other lyrical themes next to occultism include horror and satanism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bellzlleb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
