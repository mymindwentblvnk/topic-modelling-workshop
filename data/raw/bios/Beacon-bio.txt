Beacon is an electronic duo of Thomas Mullarney III and Jacob Gossett from Brooklyn, New York City, NY, USA. They started out in 2011 with the release of No Body EP. Their first full-length studio album The Ways We Separate saw the light of day on April 29th, 2013 and is described as a combination of rnb, synthpop, and trip-hop.
