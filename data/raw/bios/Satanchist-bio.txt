The break-up of the group prevented Paul's departure, and the replacement was waiting for him for a long time. After a while she became a drummer Zombie, who brought guitarist Pavel Řezník. Two demos were created with them. In 1992, Řezník left for his own projects, which soon replaced the Wreath Wreath Crusher.
