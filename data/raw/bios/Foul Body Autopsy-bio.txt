Line Up:
Tom - Guitar, Vox, Bass, Drums
Foul Body Autopsy is a one man death metal band from Leicester city. Started by Tom in 2009 after leaving his previous band Love Lies In Ashes. 
Foul Body Autopsy music can be downloaded for free from http://soundcloud.com/foulbodyautopsy.
