Underground heavy metal band from Kent, Ohio, US. During the 80s released one LP "Blessing In The Skies", the cassettes "Death Before Dishonour" and "5 Demons", two 7" shaped picture discs, and a few demos. Most of the material was re-released in the 2000's on the CDs "Blessing in the Skies", "1985-1995", and "Death Before Dishonor". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Axemaster">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
