Ephemer is a Canadian black metal band featuring members of Forteresse and Csejthe. They have released two demo tapes; Guerre et Gloire (2011) and Notre Honneur Immortel (2013). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ephemer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
