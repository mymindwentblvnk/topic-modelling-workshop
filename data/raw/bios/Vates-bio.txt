There seems to be two groups called Vates; one is a thrash metal band from Poland, the other is a reggae band from mid Wales.

Vates (Wales) - Welsh language dub reggae band, formed in Aberystwyth (c2007), feat; Phil Lee Jones (drms), Han (bass), Oz (gtr), Gorwel Roberts (gtr), Dewi Prysor (voc,gtr), Gwyn Jones (keys). Rel LP ‘Ordoficiaid Dub’ (Bos Recs 2007).
