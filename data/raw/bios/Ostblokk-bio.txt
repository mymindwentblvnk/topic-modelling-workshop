Ostblokk are an East German rap formation wich exists since 2004. The main artists are Joe Rilla, Pyranja, Dra-Q, Jamie White, Sera Finale (the only "Wessi") and the producer team Alphabeatz.

They where all friends even before they jointly produced the album "Einmal um den Blokk". Ostblokk is not only a formation, it is also a label.
