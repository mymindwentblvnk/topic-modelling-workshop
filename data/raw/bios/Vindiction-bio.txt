Vindiction are a 4-piece Metal and Punk influenced band from Milton Keynes. Their founding members comprised of local gigging musicians all with a strong passion for Metal, Rock and Punk music. With strong choruses and harmonic breakdowns they draw their inspiration from all ends of the musical spectrum. An intense rhythm section provides a perfect platform for soaring guitar riffs and melodic vocals, which allows them to showcase their technical prowess. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vindiction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
