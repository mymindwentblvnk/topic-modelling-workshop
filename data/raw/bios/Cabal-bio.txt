There are multiple artists named Cabal.

1) A German Death Metal band formed in 1989
2) A Swedish Death Metal Band formed in 1989.
3) An American Death/Thrash Metal Band formed in 1990
4) A Brazilian Rapper who got a breakthrough in 2004
5) A Nicaraguan Brutal Death Metal band that is now known as Gorepoflesh.
6) A French goregrind/deathgrind outfit who formed in 1995 and disbanded in 1998 after releasing a split with Mucupurulent, a split with C.S.S.O. and a self-titled EP.
