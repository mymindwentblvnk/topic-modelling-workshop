Anlipnes started their music in winter 2008. Vitaliy Petrenko(guitars,synths) and Aziz Deniz Babaoglu(vocals,bass) is founder of the band. Their music influenced by darkness, loneliness and silence. Anlipnes means "loneliness" in old English literature. After a while Berrin Yahyaoglu joined the band as 2nd guitarist. 

In March 2009 the first song named "Yalnizlik" was recorded but it was written in Turkish and because of some technical issues it is never released.
