Ricardo Donoso is a composer, percussionist and electronic musician from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil currently residing in Boston. He is currently involved in groups Ehnahre, The Epicureans, Perispirit and Minority Unit along with improvising with a variety of musicians and performing solo, in both the electro-acoustic and noise fields. Ricardo is also sole proprietor of the Semata Productions imprint and event organizing collective which he curates and oversees. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ricardo+Donoso">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
