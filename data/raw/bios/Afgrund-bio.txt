Afgrund is a Swedish/Finnish grindcore band based in Stockholm. Formed in 2006 by Andreas Baier as guitarist and vocalist, the band did few line-up change. In 2006 the band recorded the demo CD "Hjärtslag och Djupa Andetag" and performed few gigs in Sweden. In 2007, they released their debut album "Svarta Dagar" and a split CD with Relevant Few(both on Lifestage Productions) and started playing a lot all over Europe; including a 3 weeks tour and the Obscene Extreme Festival. People says its musical style is similar to Agathocles, Nasum, Kill the Client, Phobia, Mumakil, Assück, Arachagathus, etc.
