Astarium is formed in the summer of 2005 in Novosibirsk(Russia). By the middle of April 2006 debut demo "Monolith Of Abysses" has been recorded. In February 2008 album "On The Edge Of Chasm" has been self-released like promo, and released by label Satanarsa Records in September 2008. The end of June 2009 was marked by the release of the Demo "On The Edge Of Chasm" on label Apocalyptic Art. Now Astarium is ending the record of a new full-length album.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Astarium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
