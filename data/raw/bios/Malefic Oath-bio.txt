Raw early 90's black metal from The Netherlands, the band featured Marco Kehren of Deinonychus and The Unsane of Bestial Summoning. The band only ever released one demo, The Land Where Evil Dwells in 1992.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malefic+Oath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
