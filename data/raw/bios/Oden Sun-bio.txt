Hailing from California's North Bay Area comes Oden Sun. infusing a solid Heavy Metal foundation with Doom & Groove Metal to create a sound that is Heavy and driving. Formed in the summer of 2013 by Guitarist J. Kidd , Drummer Ryan Villegas , Bassist Kelly Gills , Lance Ozanix (vocals ) and second Gtr Jason Wright . <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oden+Sun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
