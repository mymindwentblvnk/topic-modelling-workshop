Manifestator is an underground Black Thrash metal band from Colombo, Sri Lanka. The band consists of WARTEK(Vocals), BLASPHEMOUS WARGOAT, KHAOSHIVA and NUKLEAR TOXIKATOR. They have released thier first Live Demo on Feb 2012 from their live performance at Doom Over Colombo.
