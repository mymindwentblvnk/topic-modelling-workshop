Guttural was formed in 2007 by a man named Johnny Horn as a joke, but soon after words, Guitarist Ian Vandenburgh joined the band it started to get serious about recordings. After rehearsing they recorded their first song which attracted drummer Matt Hammond and guitarist Kyle Kolb. Near teh end of 2007, they recorded the first demo, but shortly after split up due to lack of rehearsal. The members have moved on to newer projects.
