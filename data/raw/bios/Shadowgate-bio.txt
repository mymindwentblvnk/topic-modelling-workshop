Shadowgate is a Grammy nominated Thrash Metal band from New York, formed in 2003 who have thus far released a 3 song demo, a 3 track EP titled "Road of Silence" and a full length LP called "Blood & Guts".  The band has come back strong after a brief hiatus, and are recording a new full length album which should be released by the end of the year.
