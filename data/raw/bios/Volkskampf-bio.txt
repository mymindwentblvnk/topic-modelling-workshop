Volkskampf is a black/death/folk/Aryan metal band from the greater Cincinnati, Ohio area.  The band members have been friends for 15+ years.
They grew up listening to the starts of death and black metal.
All of the band members were born here or around the area.
Our influences are many:  A long list of bands, books, people, nature, etc. goes into everything we do as a band.  
There are four members:
Mike Schoone: Guitars/Vocals/Lyrics
Shaun Hall:  Keyboards/Vocals/Lyrics
Nathan Matthews:  Drum arrangements/Art
Matt Hall: Bass
