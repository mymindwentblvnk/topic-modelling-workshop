Sensum is a project of russian musician Aleksey Kuleshov.
It started in 2000 with fast rhythms of trance, hard trance and house, and then went to another styles - mild sounds, and melodies from inner spaces.
Sensum project is an experiment with the sound. Author is trying to find new sounds, and create an own microspace, own compositions. Music from Sensum is an interlacing of various ambient and atmospheric sounds (from cold and frighting to chill and relaxing) with melody, that comes from nowhere and goes to nowhere.
