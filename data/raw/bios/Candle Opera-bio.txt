There are 2 bands named Candle Opera.

1) Candle Opera was a heavy metal band formed in Toronto, Canada in 1990. They released one demo in 1992 titled "Dream Theatre" before splitting up.

2) Candle Opera is a progressive rock band from southwestern Connecticut, USA. Their first stingle "Mirror Pools" came out in August 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Candle+Opera">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
