Nicole Millar is a singer-songwriter from Sydney, NSW, Australia.

Nicole Millar, Sydney singer-songwriter first gained attention with her soul-melting vocals on the Cosmo's Midnight tack 'Phantasm'.

Having also collaborated with What So Not's Emoh Instead, Peking Duk, Allday and Sun Glitters. Nicole's attention now turns to her solo act - working with an array of the next wave of talented producers across the globe to put together her debut solo EP. Ranking #25 on HillyDilly's Top Australian Releases of the Year for her track 'FALL' (October release) MILLAR has gone from strength to strength and with her ethereal vocals enchanting an army of fans, 2014 is sure to be a big year from Sydney's newest kid on the EDM block.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nicole+Millar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
