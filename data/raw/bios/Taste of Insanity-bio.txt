Heavy melodic guitar riffs driven along by grooving drums, translated into words through singing which varies between harmonic multivocals and violent grunts. Al these elements of taste of insanity are packed in no-nonsense heavy-rock-and-roll-sound, apart from what belongs or should be in the genre. This band from the Northern of The Netherlands (Holland) follow their heart when making their music, combining different (metal-) styles from the nineties with different influences from the past, present and the future.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Taste+Of+Insanity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
