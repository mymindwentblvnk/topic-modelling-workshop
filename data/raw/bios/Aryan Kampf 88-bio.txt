Aryan Kampf 88 is a Belgium (Bruges) based Black metal band whose lyrical themes revolve mainly around National Socialism. The band's name is a clear reference to this ideology : 

     kampf - taken from "mein kampf", Hitler's auto-biography, this term became popular to describe the neo nazi movements and efforts.
     88 - code for Heil Hitler, H being the 8th letter of the alphabet. 
Aryan Kampf 88 - Aryan Struggle 88
    This band has 3 demos recorded, all of them released in 2004 (sieg heil, Belgik Fierté, A nos kamarades emprisonnés)
     
    The band's line-up is: 
Pagan - bass and vocals
K14 Panzer - lead guitar
Infernus -  guitar and synth
Lionel H. - drums and percussions
