Rajan is based in Brampton, Ontario. He's been making music since 2015 with his first album titled 'Broken Home'. Since then Rajan has come a long way then, having major collaborations with people like Tory Lanez on his single 'Maybe', JDSYN on a few tracks, and Sim. Rajan's 'Northside Since 95' track made waves and went viral as a Raptor's Anthem for their playoff run in 2016. Since 2015, his initial release, Rajan has over 55+ songs released on Spotify and more are always on the way. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rajan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
