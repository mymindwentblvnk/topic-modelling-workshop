DVA (Hi:Emotions) is a new alias of producer Leon Smart, also known as DVA or Scratcha DVA. 

Smart released debut album of this alias, called NOTU_URONLINEU, in autumn 2016 on his home-base label Hyperdub.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DVA+%5BHI:EMOTIONS%5D">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
