Edenfire are a Metal band based in Dublin, Ireland.

Initially formed in 2003, after a few different iterations 2010 saw the best line up so far settled upon , , with Dan Monaghan (Guitar & Vocals), Tony Murphy (Bass & Vocals), Paul Murphy (Guitar) and steven Maloney (Drums).
Fusing a combination of melodic and thrash metal, Edenfire are a mainstay in metal gigs in Dublin.

In February 2012, Edenfire completed their first long awaited E.P. in Trackmix Studios, and plan on playing around Ireland and beyond during 2012 in support of it. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Edenfire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
