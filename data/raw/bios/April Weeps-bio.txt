April Weeps is a metal band from Dunajska Streda, Slovakia. The band started out in 2010 and released their debut "Outer Calm, Pain Within" in 2013. In 2014, the band has made it into the final leg of "Spark Fresh Blood" band contest, organized by Czech heavy music magazine Spark.
The style fuses gothic and doomy elements with melodic death metal background. April Weeps are reputed for their charismatic and ravishing live performances.
