Carlo "Illangelo" Montagnese (born July 29, 1987) is a Canadian producer, songwriter, musician and mixing engineer from Toronto, Ontario, who came to fame as long-time collaborator of The Weeknd. His work includes executive production of The Weeknd's mixtapes and compilation album 'Trilogy', along with singles from Wiz Khalifa, Drake and Juicy J. He released his debut album titled 'The History of Man' on August 20th through Brodinski's label Bromance Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Illangelo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
