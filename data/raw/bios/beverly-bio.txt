At least four artists used the name Beverly:
1. Band originally made up of Drew Citron and Frankie Rose (Vivian Girls, Dum Dum Girls, Crystal Stilts). Rose left the project to focus on her solo career and Citron currently tours with Jamie Ingalls on drums and Scott Rosenthal on bass.

2. A punk-pop band from the US who became known for their song "Take My Hand (Let Go)" after their breakup.

3. A female singer from Italy who did a Italo-dance cover of "The Power of Love" in 1994.

4. Beverly Caimen, a Filipina R&B singer who dropped her last name after moving her career to Japan where she signed with Avex Trax. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beverly">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
