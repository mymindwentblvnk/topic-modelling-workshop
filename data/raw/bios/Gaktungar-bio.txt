Black metal band from Vladivostok, Russian Federation. Formed in July of 2011. The band consists of three members - Ammanas (music), Vandal (vocals, lyrics) and Samot (bass), all from Dor Feafaroth. The band's debut album, Goat Perversion, was released on Dark East Productions in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gaktungar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
