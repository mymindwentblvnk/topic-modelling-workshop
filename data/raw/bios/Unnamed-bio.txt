Formed in 1993 in Warsaw, Poland.
Discography:
The Black Monk - Demo, 1994
A Beauty Of Suffering A Poetry Of Dreams - Demo, 1997
Id - Full-length, 2001
Duality - Full-length, 2002
Disbanded in 2003.
Band played a doom (gothic) metal, similar to My Dying Bride, Paradise Lost and early The Gathering.

Last known line-up:
Robert Prach - vocal (Testor)
Konrad Kedzierski - guitar
Piotr Grudzinski - guitar (Riverside, Groan)
Rafal Oskroba - drums
Lukasz Romanski - bass

Former/past member(s):
Piotr Pyrzanowski - bass
Tomasz Machala - bass
