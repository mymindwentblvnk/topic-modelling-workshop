Heavy/speed metal-band from Tokyo, Japan. Formed in 1988, Gilt Face released a 7" vinyl single named "No More Gas" in 1989. They were also featured on the 1991 compilation-cd "Emergency Express III: Declare A State Of Emergency", together with bands like Shell Shock, Rosenfeld, Bellzlleb, Yasha, Lawshed, The Wretched and Extreme Noise Terror. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gilt+Face">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
