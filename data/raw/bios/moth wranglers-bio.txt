moth wranglers is the anti-band collaboration between ld beghtol (flare, three terrors, guest vocalist on the magnetic fields’ 69 love songs) and chris xefos (drop quarters, ex-king missile). Their mission: to collect and present ideas that break everything and everyone (including the creators) down to their component parts — for no reason other than it must be done. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moth+Wranglers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
