GATE 9 is a k-pop girl group that consists of 9 girls from N.O.S Entertainment. They debuted on October 26th, 2017 with the song “Chemical”. The members are: HaeYeon, TaeRa, Haru, Gaga, YeoRi, Kady, Juju, Yoon and Vivian. These nine girls are divided into 3 units which are vocal, rap and dance.
