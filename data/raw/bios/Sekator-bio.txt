Sekator is Polish punkrock band created in Lublin in 2006.
Members of the band: Bolo (1 guitar), Koras (2 guitar), Macia (bass guitar), Szalo (drums). They haven't released any album to this time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sekator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
