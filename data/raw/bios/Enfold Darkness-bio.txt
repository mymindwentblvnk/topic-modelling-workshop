Enfold Darkness is a blackened death metal band from Nashville, USA.
They formed in 2006, and were signed to Sumerian Records in 2009 for their debut full length, Our Cursed Rapture.

Current lineup
Justin Corser - Vocals
Matt Brown (Inferi) - Lead Guitar & Support Vocals
Elijah Whitehead - Rhythm Guitar & Support Vocals
James Turk- Bass
Greg Vance- Drums

Former members
Adrian Perez (Warforged) - Vocals
Malcolm Pugh (A Loathing Requiem, Diskreet, Inferi) - Rhythm Guitar & Support Vocals
Jack Blackburn (Inferi) - Drums
