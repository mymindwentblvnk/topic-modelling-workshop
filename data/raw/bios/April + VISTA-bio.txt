April George (vocalist, pianist, violinist) and Matthew Thompson (producer, engineer) make up the genre-breaking duo April + VISTA. Hailing from the DMV, they pull from an eclectic range of influences--from trip-hop and R&B to jazz and indie rock--and aim to take alternative soul music into new territory. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/April+%252B+VISTA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
