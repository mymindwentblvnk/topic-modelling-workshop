Evil Army is a Memphis, Tennessee based Thrash/Crossover band, who released their full-length debut in 2006. 

The band was on hold due to Rob Evil's incarceration in 2010, but returned with the "I, Commander" 7" in 2013 and recently the new ep "Violence and War" released digitally on Aug 15th 2015 and due on vinyl in September, 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evil+Army">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
