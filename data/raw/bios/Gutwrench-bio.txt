There are two bands called Gutwrench. 

(1) Gutwrench was a death metal band from Bergen/Zaandam, North Holland. Formed in 1992. They released only two demos before splitting up.

Discography:
Wither Without You Demo 1993
Beneath Skin Demo 1994
