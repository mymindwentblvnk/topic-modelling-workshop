Dioid is a solo project from Blackburn, UK created by Bryan Shuttleworth. Within his music he tries to incorporate multiple styles he enjoys such as Post-rock/metal, stoner, sludge and doom. The first release, Dark Observations was released on the 10th of September 2011 for sale on Bandcamp (http://dioid.bandcamp.com) and is a 6 track album, however solid copies include the bonus Electric Wizard cover "Funeralopolis." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dioid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
