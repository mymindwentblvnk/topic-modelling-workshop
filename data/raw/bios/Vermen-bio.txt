Vermen was a Black Metal band formed in 2005 in Lisbon, Portugal. Since that time only two releases were made the 2005 demo "The End of Mankind" and the split with Irae "Odium Generis Humani" in 2009. Vermen also was apart of the six way spit cd "Os Seis Caminhos para a Verdade" along with  Irae, Satanize, Mons Veneris, Cripta Oculta & Black Howling.
