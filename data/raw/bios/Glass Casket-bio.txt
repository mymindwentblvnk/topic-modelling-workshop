Glass Casket was a mathcore band from Winston-Salem, North Carolina. Currently, two of the members of Glass Casket (Dustie and Blake) are also in Between the Buried and Me. Glass Casket employs a series of melodic solos combined with tight death metal riffing and hardcore breakdowns. 

Sid, bassist, now plays in a pop/indie rock band called Yearling.
