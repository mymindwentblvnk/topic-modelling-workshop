1) Saboten was a japanese new wave/art-rock band that made their album debut in 1982 with  Satomi Makino (Guitar, Vocals),  Izumi Imazawa (Bass, Keyboards),  Atsuko Sakai (Drums)  and Masae Fuma (Guitar, Vocals)

2) Saboten is a Japanese rock band formed in 1999.
