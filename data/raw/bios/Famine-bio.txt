There are multiple artists named Famine:

1) Famine is an occult black metal band from Jersey City, NJ, United States. Formed in 2007 by sole member Worthless. Famine self-titled LP can be purchased at http://www.worthlessendeavors.com Facebook page: http://www.facebook.com/pages/Famine-Black-Metal/122200157793975

2) Famine is an electronic music producer from Toronto, Canada, with over a decade and a half of albums and production credits for music production, film, and television, plus several remix and compilation appearances (BCP, Artoffact, Dependent, Tympanik Audio, Noitekk, Geska, Elektroanschlag, Lost Compound, Wounds of the Earth). Also creator and owner of executive.audio.research. Sites: http://www.martyfamine.com - http://chxstfamine.bandcamp.com

3) Famine is a hardcore band from Dublin, Ireland. Signed to indie hardcore label Hurry Up! Records.

4) Famine is a hardcore / punk / grind outfit from Leeds, UK.
