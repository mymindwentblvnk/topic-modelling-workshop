Modesty & Pride is a progressive metal band from Podbořany (near Žatec), Czech Republic.

They are playing since June 2008, when they played their first concert (under the name "Mysterion")

Members:
Luboš Matys - guitar, vocals
David Vopat - guitar, vocals
Filip Matys - bass, vocals
Petr Švihlík - drums
Jan Tolinger - keyboards

Their first album is called "Endless Road pt.1".
