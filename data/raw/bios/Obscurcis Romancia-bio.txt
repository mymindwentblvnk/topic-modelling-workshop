A symphonic black metal band from Montréal, Québec, Obscurcis Romancia is a very energetic group of talented musicians, who have played with the likes of Ensiferum.  Obscurcis have one self-titled album, with another on the way. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obscurcis+Romancia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
