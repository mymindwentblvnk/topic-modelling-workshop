Jessie Ware & Sampha are two British artists signed to Young Turks. They have worked together on many pieces of music. Jessie Ware is perhaps best known for her 2012 single, Wildest Moments. Sampha is perhaps best known for his 2011 work with SBTRKT. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jessie+Ware+&+Sampha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
