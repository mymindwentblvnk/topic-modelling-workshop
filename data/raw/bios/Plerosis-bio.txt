Plerosis is a one man guttural slamming brutal death metal band from Wisconsin, US.  Formed in 2005 by   Neil Ruppert   of Dyscrasia, Plerosis has released music and toured on and off since forming.

Discography:
Gurgle Gag Punishment Demo - Cadaver Cart, 2006
Gore As Plerosis EP - Cadaver Cart
Slaughtered in Seclusion (split with Intracranial Butchery) - Cadaver Cart, 2009
Pulverizing Gore Machine - Cadaver Cart, 2010
Split with Rectal Twat - Cadaver Cart, 2010
