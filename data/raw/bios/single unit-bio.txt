Single Unit is the solo project of Are Mokkelbost. Under this name he makes free form noise electronica that sounds like freaky jazz and extreme metal at the same time. Related artists are Killl and Arm. More info can be found on Are Mokkelbost-archive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Single+Unit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
