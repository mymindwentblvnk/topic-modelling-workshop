Kwamie Liv is a Danish-Zambian singer-songwriter from Copenhagen, Denmark. With a "music-friendly" family, she became interested in songwriting at a young age. Liv works closely with Baby Duka, who produces her work and creates the album art for her releases. Her debut track 5 AM was released on Soundcloud in February 2014 with little information, followed by Follow You and comin THRU before her debut EP Lost in the Girl in August 2014. 

Higher was released in anticipation of a new EP is planned for Summer 2015. Most recently, she worked with composer Angelo Badalamenti to create the title song for Danish film Guldkysten (Gold Coast), Remember Me in Every Cloud of Gold.
