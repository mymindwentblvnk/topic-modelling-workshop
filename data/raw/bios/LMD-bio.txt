LMD is an American hip-hop supergroup, consisting of California-based rappers LMNO, MED and Declaime. The supergroup formed in 2010, featuring on the Madlib Medicine Show series with their debut song “Real Talk”, produced by Madlib himself. The next year, they would also appear on the special edition of MED’s Bang Ya Head 3 compilation, with the song “The Cypher (Two & Pass)”, also produced by Madlib. Their highly-anticipated debut album Flying High was released on September 2, 2022; and was entirely produced by Madlib. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/LMD">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
