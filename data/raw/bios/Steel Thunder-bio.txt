Steel Thunder are heavy metal band from Tetovo, Macedonia formed in 2005.
In 2008 they have recorded a demo called Anthems of the Oscured Chaos.

Current Lineup:
Igor Efkoski (Bass)
Vojislav Stanojevic (Drums)
Martin Ugrinovski (Guitars)
Darko Vucevski (Guitars)
Deni Apostoloski (Vocals)

Past members:
Slobodan Stanojevic (Warnament) (Bass)
