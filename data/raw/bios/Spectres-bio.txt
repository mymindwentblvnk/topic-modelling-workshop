There are 5 bands which have used the name Spectres:
1. Spectres is a post punk band from Vancouver Canada, their sound shares similarities with bands such as The Mob and Joy Division.

2. Spectres is a 'nu gazey noise' 4-piece hailing from Bristol, UK. They draw their influence from acts such as The Jesus and Mary Chain and  My Bloody Valentine.  

3. Spectres was a 4-piece screamo band from San Fransisco, California.
Spectres formed in 2005 and was originally called Maudlin.
Due to copyrights they were forced to change their name.
They became Spectres in early 2006 and broke up in 2008.
4. Spectres was also the name of the early line up which went on to become Status Quo.
