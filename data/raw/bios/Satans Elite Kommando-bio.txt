Satans Elite Kommando are a German black metal band located in Hanover, Lower Saxony, later relocated in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg. Formed in 2004, the band have so far released only one album in at the same year of their formation - Mehr Nägel für das Schwein. It has old-school raw production quality and punk influence on the musical style.
