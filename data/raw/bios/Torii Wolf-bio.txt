Torii Wolf is a singer/songwriter/artist based in Los Angeles, California. Their debut album Flow Riiot was released in 2017 and explored dark trip-hop sounds. Wolf has also collaborated with other artists on EPs and singles, including Mike Shadow, FWD The Man, and electronoir. 

During the quarantine of 2020, Torii Wolf released 20 singles as well as an EP, Gloam. Wolf identifies as non-binary and LGBTQ. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Torii+Wolf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
