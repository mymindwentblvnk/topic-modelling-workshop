There are two artists by this name: a thrash metal band and an electronica group. 

1) Based in Washington state, H.M.P. (Heavy Metal Poetry) plays 80's inspired heavy metal/thrash metal and consists of members Alex Rasmussen on vocals and guitar, Josh Deyoe on guitar, Pete Yore on bass and backing vocals, and Eric Jelsing on drums.  Some of their influences include Megadeth, Metallica, Overkill, Sacred Reich, Testament, and Iced Earth.  H.M.P. has shared the stage with many influential metal bands, including Iced Earth, Exodus, Overkill, Destruction, Metal Church, and Joey Belladonna of Anthrax.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/H.M.P.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
