Emortuus is a Symphonic Black Metal project hailing from Perth, Western Australia. It is solely comprised of Ryan Allan (formerly of Tabryss), performing duties on vocals, guitar, bass, drum programming and synth programming. Influences include of Bathory, Immortal, Behexen, and Emperor.
