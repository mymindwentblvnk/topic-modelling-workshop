A black metal band from Elefsina, Greece. They have only released the "Dancing in the Dark Lakes of Evil" demo.

members:
Tristessa aka Lady Lilith - Bass, Vocals
Nicolas Sic Maiis - Vocals
Stelios Darakis - Drums
Stelios Mavromitis - Guitars
