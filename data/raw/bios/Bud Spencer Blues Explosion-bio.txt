Bud Spencer Blues Explosion are an alternative rock-blues rock band formed in Rome in 2007. The band is composed by Adriano Viterbini and Cesare Petulicchio. The name chosen for the band has two references: the actor Bud Spencer and the punk blues group “Jon Spencer Blues Explosion”.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bud+Spencer+Blues+Explosion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
