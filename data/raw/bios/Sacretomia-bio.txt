Death metal band from the Gothenburg suburb Angered, Sweden, who formed as Chronic Torment in 1991 but changed name shortly after. Only the 'Altar of Sin' demo (1992) was released before they disbanded. In 2006 they regrouped as Chronic Torment. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sacretomia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
