FELIVAND (born Felicity Vanderveen) is a Brisbane-based artist who pairs dreamy indie-pop textures with sophisticated R&B/soul footnotes. She performs with Rhian Cruice, Jye Geltch and Alice Mikkelsen. Felivand's first EP comes out in 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Felivand">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
