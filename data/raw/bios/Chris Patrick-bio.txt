Experienced singer songwriter guitarist with an indie guitar pop past. Fronted Bradford band FAR FETCHED during the 90's.

First proper cd release was 2005's HIT COUNTER an album full of love songs and a predominant lounge music feel.

Sept 2008 saw the release of STOOD UP SAT DOWN a 10 track album of mainly guitar pop.

Fourth album THE RIGHT SIDE OF NAUGHTY was completed in Oct 2010 and contains several different styles of music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chris+Patrick">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
