Ohio-based doom band wytCHord features Dave Jones (lead guitar), Vince Green (guitar, vocals), and Kevin Hartnell (drums). The first single from their debut release "Death Will Flee" was released on July 9, 2016. "Death Will Flee" is scheduled to be released on October 31, 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/wytCHord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
