Vacillation is a Melodic Death metal band from Houston, TX formed in 2006. The band has settled with original members Josh Ellis (Vocals 2006-present), Adrian Socaciu (Guitar 2006-Present), and Nick Cooper (Bass 2008-present) along with various drummers. The band's most recent release is titled "Corridors to a New World" and is released independently. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vacillation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
