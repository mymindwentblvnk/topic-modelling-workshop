Formed in 1993 Spain. 
In 1996, Jorge Elizalde and Santi G. Mendezona broke their relationship with Arise Records. They start to call the band Dormant Soul, and they search a new drummer. The band played in the release concert of Trascëndental´s "Behind".

Javi Martinez started also a new band, In Thousand Lakes. The spanish edition of the Metal Hammer magazine claims that it was a reformation or Dormanth, but it was not. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dormanth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
