Defeatem is an Estonian metal band formed in 2010.
Defeatem has been influenced by many different metal bands, their music can be described as aggressive, heavy and also melodic at times. 

Defeatem released their debut album "Death From A Nightmare" online in December 2012. It can now be downloaded for free from Bandcamp.

Current members:
Mad Anton - vocals, guitar
Tenno Pelska - guitar
Kert Vellner- bass
