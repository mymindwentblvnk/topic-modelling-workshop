Portuguese band formed in 1998 that mixes Metal with Neofolk. They have two albums released, and word is out that they're planning on a third.

Current line-up:
Ricardo Sousa ``Max`` - lead vocals
Nuno Cruz - guitar and vocals (ex. My Iced Beloved)
João Paulo ``Eclipse`` - guitars, mandolin, ukulele, timbrel (Desire, ex.My Iced Beloved)
Ricardo Oliveira - bass
César Feiteira - drums and percussion (Dark Tales, Empty V)

Former/past member(s):
José Ramos - bass (ex.My Iced Beloved)
