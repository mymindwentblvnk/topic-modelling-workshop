Azotth are a Gothic Metal band from Chile. They released a demo in 2006, followed by their debut album 'Die After Pain Arrives' in 2008 which can be downloaded for free from the Official Website. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Azotth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
