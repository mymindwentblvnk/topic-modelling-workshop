Nightly Gale plays a highly original yet hard to swallow mutant doom style. Vocalist Waldemar Sagan, at times, screams his lungs out in pure Marduk style! The music has an ice-cold, oppressed aftertaste and they have a slight resemblance to the old days of Unholy or more recent Esoteric although Nightly Gale are a breed of their own.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nightly+Gale">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
