17.XII is a Bulgarian Thrash Metal band formed in 1993 and disbanded in 1995. They released a single album titled "Capitis Deminutio". They briefly reformed in 2008 but a year later disbanded again. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/17.XII">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
