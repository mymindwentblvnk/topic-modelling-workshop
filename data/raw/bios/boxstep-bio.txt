Boxstep is an alternative country band formed in 2000 in Pittsburgh, PA by band front man Eric Graf.  It began as an orchestral ensemble and its early music is typified by sweeping, powerful instrumentation.  The original band lineup is as follows:

Eric Graf :: vocals, guitar
David Wallace :: guitar
TR Daddario :: bass
Sarah Siplak :: vocals, viola
Erin Hutter :: violin
Greg Cislon :: Drums

Boxstep has produced three albums to date.  
The Faces All Look On, 2001
By Now Even Trees, 2002
Back Roads, 2004
