A black/thrash metal band from Newborough, Victoria.
The Sons of "Newboroughian Darkness" Kakorot and Boof came together in early 2007 to create Altars of Sin. Born out of their passion for Metal Altars of Sin has devoloped their own unique brand of Australian Metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Altars+Of+Sin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
