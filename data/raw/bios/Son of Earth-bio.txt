1. Sometimes known as Son of Earth-Flesh on Bone Trio, Son of Earth is a trio consisting of Matt Krefting, Aaron Rosenblum, and John Shaw.  Their work can be described as abstract low-fi electrified textures, created through the use of guitar, electronics, and various small instruments and objects.
