Belkardt was a Russian black metal band that formed in 1994. They released two demos, one in 1996 and the other in 1997. They also appeared on a 4-way split with Morose, Maleficium and Thanelorn titled "War Against Homo Sapiens". One member also played in the band Fantasmagory and owned the label Blackmonolith's Shadows which released Belkardt's demos as well as those of other Russian underground black metal groups. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Belkardt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
