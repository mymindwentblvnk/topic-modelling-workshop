There are more artists with the name Alice in Wonderland than one.

Alice in Wonderland is a Ukrainian gothic band. They later changed name to Sad Alice Said.
Alice in Wonderland is also the name of a German metal band.

It may also be an incorrect name for a soundtrack by Danny Elfman.
