DETAIL
Origin: Detroit, Michigan
Genres: R&B, hip-hop, rap, pop
Occupations: Producing, singer-songwriter
Years active: 1992-present
Labels: Konvict Muzik
Associated acts: T-Pain, Lil Wayne, Travie McCoy, Lil' Kim, Lil' Jon, Sean Kingston, Ray J, Yung Berg, Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, Brandy, Shorty Mack, Akon

Noel Fisher, better known by his artist name Detail, is a Los Angeles-based producer who rose to fame in late 2005 when he teamed with singer Ray J for most of his Raydiation album. Since then he has also produced tracks for Ray J's latest album All I Feel, Yung Berg's first album Look What You Made Me, Akon's Freedom and the majority tracks of T.H.U.G.S. by Bone Thugs-n-Harmony. He also worked with the Pussycat Dolls, Lady GaGa, Marques Houston, Brandy, Shorty Mack, Snoop Dogg, R. Kelly, Christina Aguilera, Usher, Master P and Tom Green.
