Crypt of Silence is a doom/death metal band from Ivano-Frankivsk, Ukraine formed in 2009. They released their debut full-length "Beyond Shades" on March 10th, 2014 through Solitude Productions. Their music can be compared to bands along the lines of Mourning Beloveth, (early)  My Dying Bride, Before the Rain and Autumnia.
