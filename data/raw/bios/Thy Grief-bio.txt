Thy Grief was a Melodic Black Metal band from Sandviken, Norway - formed in 1995. They released one demo "A Frozen Realm" (1995) and one album "The Frozen Tomb of Mankind" (Solistitium Records, 1997). The band split up around 1998. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thy+Grief">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
