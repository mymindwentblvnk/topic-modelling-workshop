A Sleepless Malice is a Melodic Death Metal band out of Lapeer, Michigan formed in 2009. They are currently unsigned, and released a self-titled full length in late 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A+Sleepless+Malice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
