Buerak (Буерак) is a Russian post-punk, new-wave/Russian-wave band from Novosibirsk, Russia, (Новосиби́рск, Росси́я) consisting of bass player and vocalist Artyom Cherepanov (Артём Черепанов) and Alexandr Makeyev. Buerak was formed in 2014 and is currently still active with five studio albums and numerous singles.
