There are TWO artists known under this name.

1. 

Peruvian raw thrash metal. Founded in 1986.

2. 

Founded in 2002 by Fredrik Andersson. The songs were recorded solely because they did not fit in Amon Amarth. 

All songs written and arranged by Andersson, except "Frail" by Nirbrant and Andersson. 
Lyrics by Andersson, except "Forever" and "Frail" by Leinikka. 
Recorded at Cold Gin Studio fall 2002. Produced by Andersson and Nirbrant. 
Engineered by Nirbrant.
