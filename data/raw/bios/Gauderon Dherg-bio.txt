Gauderon Dherg was a Black Metal band from San Francisco, CA! The members was A.T.B (Horn of Dagoth, Crebain), Abandon All Hope Of Redemption (Horn of Dagoth and Jefeystos! The band released only 1 demo, "Kalt Winternacht" in 1999. According to A.T.B the band was splitted up after under 6 Months! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gauderon+Dherg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
