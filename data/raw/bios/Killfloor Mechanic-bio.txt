KILLFLOOR MECHANIC, the German band founded by the ex-guitarists of INCUBATOR Lasse Lammert and Marcel Wroblewski in 2011, will release their debut album Estimated Time Of Death this spring via Firefield Records. Rounded out by drummer Maik Weinhardt and singer Felix Hochkeppel, a press release describes their sound: “They mix traditional death metal with modern influences and create an unique sound.”  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Killfloor+Mechanic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
