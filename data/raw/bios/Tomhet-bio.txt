There are 3 different bands under the name Tomhet.

1. Black Metal from Mora, Sweden. Formed in late 1999 by Kumulonimbus (Wulkanaz, Semilanceata, Wagner Ödegård). Lyrics concentrate on Dark Germanic Heathenism.

Current Line-up
Kumulonimbus: vocals, guitars
Spitælsker: drums

Past Line-up
John Torch: drums
K.K.: drums

Discography
2001: Demo 1 (demo)
2002: Strid (demo)
2004: Brandbann'r (demo)
2005: Eklipsens Hord Och Karavan (album)
2007: Purpureargotamiceps (demo)
2009: Apokalypsimz Legend (demo)
2011: Fulländad Sfäraförintning I En Onaturliger Skepneskrud Av Pestilenta Djwr (EP)
2013: Samblade Sotkvæden (compilation)
2015: Tomhet 7" (EP)
2019: Tomhet (album)
2019: Arosian Black Mass (live album)


2. Black Metal from Italy. Released one demo in 2001, called "Perdition Forest" .


3. Black Metal/Dark Ambient/Experimental from Canada, formed in 2004.
