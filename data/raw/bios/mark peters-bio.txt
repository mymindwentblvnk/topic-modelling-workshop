Mark Peters (born in Liverpool on 21 July 1975) is a British musician, songwriter and producer who has mainly created music for the band Engineers. As well as solo and remix recordings, he has collaborated with the electronic musician Ulrich Schnauss as Ulrich Schnauss & Mark Peters on the albums Underrated Silence and Tomorrow Is Another Day.
