Obscuro Corvus is a band from Vsetín, The Czech republic, playing experimental metal with many influences (like melodic death metal, doom metal, melodic black metal, thrash metal...). Obscuro Corvus (which means dark raven) was formed in 2005 by Filip Schnaubelt, Karel Malina and Ondřej Petráš.
Current line-up is: Adam Hajda - Vocals, Ondřej Petráš - Guitar, Karel Malina - Guitar, Filip Schnaubelt - Bass, Lukáš Bartošák - Drums
Their first demo is called Born.
