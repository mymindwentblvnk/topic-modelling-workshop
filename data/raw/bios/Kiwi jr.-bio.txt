Kiwi Jr. is a Canadian indie-rock band based in Toronto. Comprising Jeremy Gaudet (vocals), Mike Walker (bass), Brohan Moore (drums), and Brian Murphy (guitar) (who has also played in Alvvays). The band has released "Football Money" (2020), "Cooler Returns" (2021), Chopper (2022) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kiwi+jr.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
