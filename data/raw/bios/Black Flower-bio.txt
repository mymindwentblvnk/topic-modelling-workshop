With a hybrid jazz based on African grooves, Ethio-oriental melodies and psychedelic dub this Belgian five-piece creates an atmosphere where ancient and modern sounds fuse into a hypnotic, groovy, dreamlike sensation. 

Recommended by insiders like Gilles Peterson, The Gaslamp Killer, LeFto… and starring at several showcases (Eurosonic, Womex) Black Flower is increasingly gaining international following.

The team of top-notch musicians (who worked with Beck, Calexico, dEUS, Lee ’Scratch’ Perry,…) piloted by Nathan Daems (sax and flutes), have recorded two full albums, one off-track LP and two EP’s since 2014 and is now working on a third full album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Flower">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
