Letter 7 is Christian rock/metal with hard edge guitars, melodic power vocals and solid driving drums.
The style reflects the old school hair metal generation but with a newer more modern feel.
Created by song writer and musician JD Evans with the help of professional vocalist Tom Collete and engineer Ken Orth of
Alpine Sound.
