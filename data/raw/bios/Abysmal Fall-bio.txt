Abysmal Fall is a Black/Thrash Metal band from Rochester, NY. Formed in the Spring of '98 from the ashes of the band Buried Beneath. They released "Spit in the Face of Christ" in the winter of 1999 and "Bourne Against" in early 2000. The band would broke up in 2002 and reform in 2007. A new cd tentatively titled "Slaughtering The Lamb Of God", has been recorded and is set to be released in 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abysmal+Fall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
