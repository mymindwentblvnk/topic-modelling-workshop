Peace Dogs was formed in 1988 by future Sevendust guitarist and then-drummer John Connolly along with vocalist Greg Anderson, guitarist Mike Grimmett, bassist Kyle Sanders (Bloodsimple) in Atlanta, Georgia. In 1992 Connolly and the the band released their one and only album titled "Exes for Eyes" through Energy Records. The band played a combination of power and thrash metal. A few months after the release of the album Piece Dogs disbanded and the members went onto their future bands. Connolly and Sanders have been long-time friends and have toured together throughout the years <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Piece+Dogs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
