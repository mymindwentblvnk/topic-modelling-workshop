Shrine of Suffering is an up and coming metal band, consisting of David Cowgill(vocals), Art Taylor(guitar), Eric Stewart(guitar), Scott Pletcher(drums) and Wes Studebaker(bass). Shrine of Suffering is located in Columbus, Ohio.
