Veiyadra was formed during July 2011 by Akihiro Muto (vocals) and Go Shimada (guitar). Soon Reichi Tanaka (bass) and Koju Okayasu (drums joined the band.July 2012 Veiyadra released their 1st EP (self produced) including 6 songs. In October 2012 Veiyadra decided to sign with a Japanese label called Bloodcurdling Enterprise to release their 1st full album.The album 'Gehenna' was released in 2014. After the release Veiyadra is aiming for a worldwide appearance in brutal death metal . <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veiyadra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
