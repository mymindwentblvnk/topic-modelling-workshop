Fraunhofer Diffraction is a project by the Russian Stanislav Martensitov (born 1993, based in Moscow). He previously made music for a project called Вечный Ноябрь (renamed to Stahlflüstern → Totenraum → Eternal November), but decided to consolidate all his efforts into Fraunhofer Diffraction.
Stanislav is an active member of the Russian witch house community ВЕДЬМИН ДОМ ™.
