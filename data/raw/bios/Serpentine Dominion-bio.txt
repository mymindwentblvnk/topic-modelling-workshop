Serpentine Dominion is a death metal supergroup comprising of Adam Dutkiewicz (guitar, bass) of Killswitch Engage, George "Corpsegrinder" Fisher (vocals) of Cannibal Corpse and Shannon Lucas (drums), formerly of The Black Dahlia Murder.  They met during 2009's Rockstar Energy Drink Mayhem Festival.  Dutkiewicz wrote songs and asked Fisher to perform vocals over them.  Fisher agreed to do so.   Dutkiewicz got Lucas to be the drummer They released the album Serpentine Dominion in 2016.  It is their only album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Serpentine+Dominion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
