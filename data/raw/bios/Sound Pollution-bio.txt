There are three artists with the name of Sound Pollution:

1) Garage rock 'n' roll / punk / post-punk band from Kumanovo, Macedonia. In 2009 released their debut album "Does anyone know the answer?" for the local label KRAN. www.myspace.com/soundpollutionandcoupleoflesbians

2) A Classic Goa Trance project from Israel. Its memebers are Avi Algranati, Marko Goren and Ofer Dikovsky. They are also known as other famous Goa Trance act - Indoor.

3) Noisy and chaotic Greek Grindcore band who shared a 1989 split EP with Rotting Christ & later became Industrial Suicide. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sound+Pollution">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
