Bastardator was a fast-rising thrash metal group from Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, formed in 2006
The band lineup is:
Jeff - vocals
Pat - guitar
Eric - bass

Ex-Members:
Cody - Vocals
Jo - drums

They have two releases ("Bastards of Mayhem" EP, and the "Identify the Dead" full-length).
Their official MySpace page is:
http://www.myspace.com/bastardator
