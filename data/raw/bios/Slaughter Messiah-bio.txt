Slaughter Messiah is a Black/Death/Thrash Metal band from Belgium. The band was initially founded in October 2008 by Rod "Iron Bitch Desecrator" and Elliot "Sodomaniak" Spencer.
The lead singer since 2011 is LORD SABATHAN (ex-ENTHRONED / SABATHAN / HEINOUS ).
They released 2 demos and 3 EPs before releasing their debut album in 2020.
