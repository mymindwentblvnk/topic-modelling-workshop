FORCE OF NATURE is a Japanese production group consisting of DJ Kent and KZA. In addition to doing remixes for others and releasing three albums of original material, they contributed to the soundtrack for the popular anime Samurai Champloo along with Nujabes, Fat Jon and Tsutchie. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Force+of+Nature">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
