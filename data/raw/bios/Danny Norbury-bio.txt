Danny Norbury lives and works in Manchester. He is a cellist. He has written and recorded two EPs: ‘Dusk’ (Static Caravan), and ‘Jean Cocteau’ (Ono), using the instruments he has around him at home. He has collaborated with The Boats, Library Tapes, Rafael Anton Irisarri, and Nancy Elizabeth. Le Lendemain is a project by Danny and David Wenngren.
