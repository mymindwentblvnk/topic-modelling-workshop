Hero Fisher is a British-born indie-rock singer-songwriter (born to Australian parents and raised in a small village outside of Paris).

Her debut album, "Delivery" was released in 2015. Her second album, "Glue Moon" was released in 2018. 

She opened for The Rolling Stones and Neil Young at Hyde Park, and was shortlisted for the 2014 Glastonbury Emerging Talent Prize.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hero+Fisher">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
