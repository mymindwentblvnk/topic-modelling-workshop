Jessie Reid is a 25-year-old singer-songwriter from Shropshire. She combines fingerstyle and percussive guitar techniques with emotive, fragile and soulful vocal melodies to create a unique style of playing. Her innovative, folk-influenced songwriting has been said to enchant audiences, drawing comparisons with the likes of Ben Howard, Lucy Rose and fingerstyle guitarist Andy McKee. Throughout 2018 she has supported artists such as Ciaran Lavery, Bryde, Dan Owen and Brooke Bentham. Jessie has also featured regularly at Sofar Sounds sessions in the UK and South America. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jessie+Reid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
