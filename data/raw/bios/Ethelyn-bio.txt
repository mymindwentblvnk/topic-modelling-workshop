Ethelyn is a Melodic Black Metal band from Poland. The music is the synthesis of melodiousness, dynamics and agression, wild connections of emotions with rapacity.



Actual line-up:
- Morbideus (drums)
- Wrath (bass)
- Dante (guitar)
- Bogunieslav (guitar)
- Mysth (vocal)
