Band was formed in october 2011 by former members of bands Histera, Democratic Terror and Lobotomy with same idea to combine and mix different styles of metal such as hardcore, nu metal, melodic death, gothic and industrial into unique modern concept... After 6 months of constant rehearsals and hard work, band was recorded first "pre-production" demo and two months later they're recorded their first album, symbolically named, "world without end". All lyrics are based on human relations and harsh reality of modern age... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zer00ne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
