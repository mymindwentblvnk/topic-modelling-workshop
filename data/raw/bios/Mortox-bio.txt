Mortox was a heavy metal band from Lübeck, Germany.  No information on when they formed and when they disbanded.  Not too much is known about Mortox.  They only released 2 demos, one full-length album and one single.  Their last lineup consisted of J. R. Rockfast (vocals, guitars), Buddy (guitars), Chico (bass) and Fast Funk (drums).  Their past members are Der Lange (guitars) and Matthias Meßfeldt (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortox">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
