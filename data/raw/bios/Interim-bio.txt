The name "Interim" can refer to several artists:

1. interim is a four-member sub-unit of the Japanese idol group LinQ, formed in Fukuoka, Japan. Made up of LinQ members Amano Natsu (leader of LinQ), Momosaki Mayu,  Yoshikawa Chiaki and Takaki Yuumi, interim's goal is to create rock and metal songs with an energetic performance. Formed in 2013, interim's first original song "Sweet My Song" was eventually included as a B-side track on LinQ's 2014 summer single "Natsukoi". interim is the second sub-unit to officially debut from LinQ, the first being SRAM.

2. Interim is an four piece indie folk group originating from Flower Mound, Texas in 2010. The band consists of Parker Smith, Alec Wallace, Sarah May and Derek Wenth. The band released their debut EP, Kings In The Corner, on January 1, 2011.
