Fjorsvartnir is Azathil Memfist's one man melodic black metal project. Founded in January 2008, Fjorsvartnir was named and ready to be published. The first serious recordings for the first album 'Praise To Our Ancestors Preponderance' started in the end of 2008, and ended in January 2009. Azathil is now searching for a label to release it on both CD and Vinyl.
