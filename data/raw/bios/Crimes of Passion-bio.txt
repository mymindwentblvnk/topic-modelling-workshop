1. Crimes of Passion is an IKON side-project from Australia. The band is comprised of Chris McCarter, Dino Molinaro, and Michael Carrodus. One album was recorded in 1997. 

2. Crimes of Passion is Andy Lindsay on guitars, Kev Tonge on drums, Dale Radcliffe on vocals and Simon Fearn on bass.

Kris Hudson-Lee played bass up until February 2008 and also recorded the bass on the debut EP's and album.

Throughout 2012, the Sheffield based quintet  performed a number of high profile sets, including the prestigious Bloodstock Open Air festival.
