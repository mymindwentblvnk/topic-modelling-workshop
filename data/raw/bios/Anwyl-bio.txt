Anwyl was a black metal band that originated in Hutchinson, KS  in 1997. The last known members were Blaspheroth on bass and vocals, Krom on guitar and Mikai on drums and guitar. The band has recorded two demos, two EPs and one full-length album titled Postmortum Apocalypse in 2003 with Drakkar Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anwyl">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
