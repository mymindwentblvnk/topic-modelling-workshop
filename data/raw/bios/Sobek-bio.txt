SOBEK is an instrumental melodic (death) metal band from Gorinchem, The Netherlands (http://myspace.com/sobekmetal). Sobek is also the name of a house/ techno production outfit signed to Soma Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sobek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
