....is the result of a collaboration between James Allin of Ikarian/Visionaire and Ian Arkley of My Silent Wake, Seventh Angel and The Other Window (ex member of Ashen Mortality/Paramaecium). The debut 'Awaken' was released in spring 2006. The group is based in England.

Members:
Ian Arkley: Lead vocals, lead guitar, bass, ebow. 
James Allin: Keyboards, guitar, bass, drums, programming, background vocals.
