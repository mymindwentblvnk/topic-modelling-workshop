Memories Of Pain borns in late summer 2005 from an idea of Andrea and Vittorio (ex-bassist). Then, Filippo and Felice joined the band and after some searching, we finally found the drummer: Mattia. Because of school and other stuff, Vittorio left the band and Francesco took his place. Initially we had no vocals but we didn't know Felice's vocals skills that made the band complete. We started covering songs of many bands and then we composed our first songs and recorded our first demo CD: "No Other Souls". After that, MOP deicided to take another musical way, getting more extreme and blackish. So They recorded another demo CD in 2007 and in 2009 finally the first album is out: ".rewind" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MEMORIES+OF+PAIN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
