Nathaniel Willemse, often known simply as Nathaniel, is an Australian singer-songwriter most notable for finishing sixth on the fourth series of The X Factor Australia. In 2013, he signed a recording contract with DNA Songs' label through Sony Music Australia, and released the single "You", which peaked at number four on the ARIA Singles Chart. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nathaniel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
