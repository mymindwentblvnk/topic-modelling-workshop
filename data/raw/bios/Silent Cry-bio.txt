Silent Cry is a gothic doom metal band, formed in 1993, in Governador Valadares, Brazil. The band already made performances with After Forever, Epica, Evergrey, Andreas Kisser and Shaaman.

Formation:
Dilpho Castro – Vocal/Guitars
Sandra Félix – Vocal
Albenez Carvalho – Lead Guitars
Roberto Freitas – Bass
Ricardo Meireles – Drums
Phillipe Dutra – Keyboards
