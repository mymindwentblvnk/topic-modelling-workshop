Thrash, Metal band from Maryland U.S.A. which is located just outside of Washington D.C. This band was part of the D.C. metal scene in the 90's. They giged with "Iron Man," "Unorthadox," and others. They  Recorded their first album on the Hellhound Records label. Hellhound Records is no longer in buisness. The band is currently negotiating the re-release of their first album. 

After several years apart, the band is back together and planning a new album.  Musicians Zack Campbell on Vocals, Lead and Rythm Guitars, Steve Chapman on Lead and Rythm Guitars, Rob Branigan on Drums. Dave Philabaum on Bass Guitar.
