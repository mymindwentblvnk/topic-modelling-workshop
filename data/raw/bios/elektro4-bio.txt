DJ Shadow descendent ElekTro4 - a New York-based producer apparently named Mike, who was born in 1976 - grew up a fan of hip-hop and began producing his own tracks in the late '90s. After a small clutch of singles, he teamed with DJ Signify for Keystroke One, a full-length album released by Bully Records in mid-2005 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/ElekTro4">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
