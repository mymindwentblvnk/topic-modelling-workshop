God-Shroud was formed in 2012 by brothers John & Henry Isaac (formerly of mid 80's cult NYC Death Metal band Arsenic) The music being mostly doom based...with a 70's Metal feel to it and an 80's Death Metal vocals style. Influences include Black Sabbath,Opeth,Paradise Lost,Possessed and My Dying Bride among others.... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/God-Shroud">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
