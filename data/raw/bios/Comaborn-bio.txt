Comaborn is the Blackened Death Metal Solo Project of Keith Pedziewiatr out of Chicago, IL. The first Full Length album "Leylines" Was released in the early months of 2013. Flowed by "Giants Wept". The style consists of highly concentrated Blast Beats and "Machine Gun" drumming, Melodic as well as very Grinding and Chugging guitars, vocals vary from Highs and Lows with occasional cleans and lyrical content is of a religious/ apocalyptic theme. Highly influenced by that of Belphegor, 1349, Dark Funeral, Behemoth, Vesania, Emperor, Dissection, etc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Comaborn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
