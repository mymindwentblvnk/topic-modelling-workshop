Carnotaurus is a death metal project started in 2005 by Travis Helton (vocalist/guitarist). After the band's debut EP/Demo "A Taste of the Carnage to Come" was released, Brad Naratil (drums) and Jason Stephenson (bassist) joined the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carnotaurus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
