Aygan is a rock band from São Paulo, Brazil formed in 2001. The project consists of Marcelo Lane (vocal and electric guitar), Rodrigo Curvo (electric guitar), Bruno Vellutini (electric bass), and Vitor H. Rodrigues (drums).

Songs range from hard rock to heavy metal incorporating many elements of groove. Aygan has released two EPs with 3 songs each, Plastic City EP in December 2007 and Neural Groove EP in February 2009. Their debut album entitled Plastic City was released in October 17, 2010 during Fúria Festival where the band opened for Andre Matos and Sepultura. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aygan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
