Kreisky was founded 2005 in Vienna, Austria by Franz Adrian Wenzl, Martin Max Offenhuber, Gregor Tischberger and Klaus Mitter. The band is named after Bruno Kreisky, former chancellor of Austria.

Official web site: www.kreisky.net
Label: Wohnzimmer Records
