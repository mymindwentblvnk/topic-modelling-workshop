Fornicator was a death/grind group from Portland, Oregon USA. They released one album and split up.
Band members can be found playing in the following bands:  Lord Gore, Whore,  Engorged, Murder Basket, Blood Freak, Maniac Killer and Frightmare  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fornicator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
