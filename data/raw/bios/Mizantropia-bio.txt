Mizantropia is metal band from Ukraine that was founded 02-23-2003. Their music is a fusion of gothic, doom, melodic and black metal.

Current lineup:
Anastasia Kurchenko - drums
Sergey Yasyr - guitars
Evgeniy Suslov - guitars/back vocals
Maksim Kaplenko - bass
Ekaterina Sinegina - vocals
