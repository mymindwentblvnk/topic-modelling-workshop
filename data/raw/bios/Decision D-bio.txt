Technical thrash/death metal act from Utrecht, the Netherlands, formed in 1986. They released two demo tapes in '90 and '91, titled "Testimony of Faith" and "Testimony on Stage". In '92, Decision D released their first full-length studio album, titled "Razon De La Muerte", on Crypta Records. A year later, "Moratoria" followed. Their last album was released in '95, titled "The Last Prostitute", on Dark Horse Records. Fourteen years after their break up the band started again in 2009 with all new members, except the vocalist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Decision+D">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
