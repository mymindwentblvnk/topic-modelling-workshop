The band Iudicium is created by Count Vassilium and Lord George in the beginning of 1995. The demo “The Onset” is released in July the same year. The album “May will be September” is recorded during November ’95 which is released by the Portugal label  “Sword prod.” and the czech Sindy Prod. The album “Nocturnal Nebula” is recorded in 1997.   
Members:vocals:Count Vassilium,guitars:FrostHammer,bass/violin:Lord George,drums:Kerkenekov,verses:Lady Morigan
