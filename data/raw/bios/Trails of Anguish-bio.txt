Trails of Anguish was a Black Metal band from Montreal, Canada formed in 2000. Their lyrics dealt mostly with depression, misery, hate, and emptiness, which is why they are considered a "Suicidal" or "Depressive" black metal band. The band released two EPs in 2001 and 2003 before disbanding. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trails+Of+Anguish">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
