Ajimal is British musician Fran O’Hanlon from Newcastle. Recordings involve different musicians and performers, depending on the desired effect. 

Whilst at university, O’Hanlon began writing his own songs. After graduating, he stayed in London to work and perform. He has since performed alongside the likes of Kathryn Joseph, Lucy Rose, Loney Dear, Lanterns on the Lake, The 1975 and Nadine Shah.
