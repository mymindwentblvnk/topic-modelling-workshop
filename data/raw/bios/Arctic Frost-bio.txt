Arctic Frost is a 3-piece black metal band from Mechelen, Belgium.  they are Lord Argothar (Vocals, guitars) Aegolius (Bass, backing vocals) and Ish Kerioth (Drums, guitars)

The band released the 'Kill Yourself, Express Yourself' demo in 2006 and Exclusion of Hope ep on Old Temple in 2008.
