Duh Predaka is folk doom black epic metal band from Niš (Serbia). They use Serbian medieval history as a themes for songs. 
They recorded one demo with 3 songs 'Smrt Majke Jugovića', 'Kosovka Devojka' & 'Sluga Milutin'.
