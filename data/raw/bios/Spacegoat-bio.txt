Spacegoat, is mexican stoner doom from Monterrey. 

Making its debut in Rock On Bar, March 11, 2010.

The debut EP 'Spacegoat' was released via Bandcamp on December 26, 2012

Formed by:
Gina Ríos - Vocals & Guitar
Miguel Ríos - Lead Guitar
Rigo Vigil - Bass guitar
Rey Fraga - Drums
