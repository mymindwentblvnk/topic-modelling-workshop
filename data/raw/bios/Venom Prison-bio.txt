Venom Prison are a death metal band from South Wales, United Kingdom. Their second album "Samsara" was released on March 16, 2019. The band released their demo "Defy the Tyrant" and EP "The Primal Chaos" in 2015, releasing their debut full-length album "Animus" in 2016 through Prosthetic Records. The band is comprised of frontwoman Larissa Stupar (ex-Wolf Down), guitarists Ben Thomas and Ash Gray (ex-Brutality Will Prevail), bassist Mike Jefferies, and drummer Jay Pipprell (Ninkharsag, ex-Unsanctum). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Venom+Prison">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
