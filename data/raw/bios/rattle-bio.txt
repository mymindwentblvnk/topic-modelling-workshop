Rattle is a band based in NYC comprised of Pat Gubler (P.G. Six), Greg Peterson, Sue Garner and Rick Brown.  The four members began playing together in 2008 and played their first gig together in the spring of 2010.  All four members have extensive histories and continuing activities in the New York City music scene as well as touring the US and Europe.

Rattle is also a Finnish rock band.
