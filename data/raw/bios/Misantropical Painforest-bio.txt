Formed in 2003, Finland by Kutcheck Gorealis as a solo project. Genre: Experimental Black Metal with naturalistic horror as the lyrical theme. 

First album "Winds Saturate with Inhumane Longing" was released in 2005 by Alpha Draconis Records and the second album "Firm Grip of the Roots" was released in 2010 by Descending Towards Damnation.
