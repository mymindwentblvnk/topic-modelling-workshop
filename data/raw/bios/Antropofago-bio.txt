Antropofago is a technical brutal death metal band based in the south of France. The project began in 2007 but only  came into shape in 2009 after a change of line up and moniker . However, our musical direction didn’t changed an inch: a brutal death metal making room for melodies and catchy riffs. Our motto is : effectiveness!! Our line up has been completed in 2009 with the roping in of a new bass player. The first EP entitled ANTROPOFAGO will be released at the beginning of 2010, and features a new vocalist called Rémy. The band is now looking for some gigs to promote their first ep.
