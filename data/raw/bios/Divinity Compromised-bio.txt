Divinity Compromised is Chicago's newest Progressive Metal band featuring members of Novembers Doom and Sacred Dawn. They have built a strong fan base worldwide in the short amount of time that they have been together. Divinity Compromised was featured on the ProgPower USA X sampler CD that was handed out at the 2009 festival. Drawing musical influences from Nevermore, Evergrey, Dream Theater, Symphony X, and Scar Symmetry, their debut CD will appeal to fans of aggressive, melodic, and hooky Progressive Metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Divinity+Compromised">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
