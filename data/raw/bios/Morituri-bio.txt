There are three bands with name Morituri.

1)Morituri is a Portuguese band from the 80's. Their style can be described as Gothic or post-punk. They are no longer active.
https://www.facebook.com/MorituriPortugal/

2) Morituri is a studio project of guitarist/songwriter Roman Samonin and drummer Ralf Goossens. The style can be described as black/folk/viking metal.

3) Morituri is latvian medieval/folk band. Formed in 2004 Riga, Latvia. 
http://www.draugiem.lv/music/musician.php?mid=47059

4) Morituri is a hardcore band from Essen, Germany. 
http://www.myspace.com/moriturifuckfuckfuck
