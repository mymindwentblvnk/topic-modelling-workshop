Sacrosanctum is a gothic doom rock metal band with female vocals.
The band was founded in 1997 in Kroměříž, Czech Republic.

Line up:
Tomas Vojtek - male vocals
Zuzana Andrasova -  female vocals
Michal Andras - keys
Lukas Bartik - guitar
Martin Skrabal - bass
Olda Susil - drums
