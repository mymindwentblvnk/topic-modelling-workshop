Raising the Fawn is a Canadian indie rock band, with its roots in Toronto. Led by John Crossingham, who is also a member of Broken Social Scene, the band formed in 1997 and released their self-titled first album in 2001. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raising+the+Fawn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
