Idolatria is a black metal band from Pordenone, Italy. Established in 2013, the band has released their debut "Breviarium Daemonicus Idolatrum" in 2015, and second full-length "Tetrabestiarchy" in 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/idolatria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
