Time Not Forgotten was a thrash metal band with some punk influences. They were formed in 2004 in Canada. They split up in early 2006 after releasing their only album, If They Hide in the Forest, Burn the Trees in 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Time+Not+Forgotten">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
