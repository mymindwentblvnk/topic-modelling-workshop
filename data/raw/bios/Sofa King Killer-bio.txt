S.K.K. is a Sludge/ Doom/Southern Rock band, started in 1999, in Akron, Ohio.  

band members are as follows: 
Ryan Burgy - Vocals
Chris Chiera - Guitar 
Paul Bartholet - Bass
Brad Thorla - Drums

Discography:
-Stout-Soaked Songs, 2000  
-Split With Leechmilk, 2001  
-Lust, Crime, And Holiness EP, 2002  
-Midnight Magic, 2004
