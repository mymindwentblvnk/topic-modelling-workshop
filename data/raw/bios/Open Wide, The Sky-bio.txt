Based in Newark Delaware and formed in 2010 Open Wide, The Sky consists of Vincent Fedetz on Lead Guitar, Max Gillman on Drums and Rob Saltsman on Rhythm Guitar and Vocals. The band is working on releasing their EP entitled Monolith.
