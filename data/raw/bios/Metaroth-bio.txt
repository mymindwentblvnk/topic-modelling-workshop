Thrash Metal / Metalcore band from Bursa / Turkey.  Band's debut album “Meta non Grata” released on 13th of January, 2005. Band mainly influences for this album were Disturbed, Machine Head, Slipknot, Fear Factory.  

They staged more than 100 shows and joined all the big festivals in Minor Asia. Shows based on theatrical performances and more heavier sound than their album.  They changed their musical style during these performances and started to play their new songs. Neverendinghatred, Poison and other new hits are more old style and melodic. Metaroth reunion the band with original members and back for 2009. They started to work for 2nd album and decided to release their new songs for fall 2009.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metaroth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
