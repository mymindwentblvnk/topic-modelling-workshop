Japanese heavy metal act from Okinawa. The band released four demo-tapes from 1984 til 1987 and one vinyl-only full-length studio album in 1988, released on the Animagawa label. Influenced by occultism and sounding similar to Sabbrabells, Genocide, Velle Witch, Crowley, Mercyful Fate, etc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tabbasa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
