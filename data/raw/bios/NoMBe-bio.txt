Noah McBeth (Born April 27, 1991 in Heidelberg, Germany), better known by his stage name NoMBe, is a German recording artist, composer and record producer based in Los Angeles.

NoMBe began the release of his debut album “They Might’ve Even Loved Me” in January 2017 with the release of his record "Wait", which was premiered worldwide on the popular Australian radio station triple J. The album is inspired by all the women who’ve helped shaped NoMBe’s life: from his godmother Chaka Khan to his high school crushes to summer flings to his girlfriend and his mother.

In April 2017 NoMBe released “Can’t Catch Me” - Pharrell selected to be the theme song for his HBO series “Outpost”.
