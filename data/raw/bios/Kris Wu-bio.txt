Wu Yifan (吴亦凡; born November 6, 1990 in Guangzhou, China), professionally known as Kris Wu or Kris, is a Chinese-Canadian actor, singer, and model. He is a former member of South Korean-Chinese boy band EXO and its subgroup EXO-M under SM Entertainment.

Wu is active as a solo artist and actor in China and has starred in several #1 box office hits including Mr. Six (2015) and Journey to the West: The Demons Strike Back (2017), which are among the highest-grossing Chinese films of all time in China. He made his Hollywood debut in xXx: Return of Xander Cage (2017).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kris+Wu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
