Dark Decade was a black doom metal band from Meissen, Germany, formed in 1992. They released two demos, "...and the Sun Is Shining Black" in 1993 and "In the Land of Disgrace" in 1995. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Decade">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
