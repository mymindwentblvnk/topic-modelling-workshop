Nightflower was a german black metal band. 
Little is known about this band other than that they released one demo named "Promotape '96" and appeared on the second volume of the "...For All Hate In Man..." compilation series.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nightflower">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
