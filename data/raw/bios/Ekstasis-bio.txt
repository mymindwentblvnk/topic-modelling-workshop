There are multiple artists named Ekstasis:

1) Ekstasis is a mellow guitar driven dub group, formed by Nicky Skopelitis, Bill Laswell, Jah Wobble, Jaki Liebezeit, Zakir Hussain and Badal Roy.

2) Ekstasis is a dark folk group from the Pacific Northwest, United States, formed by members of Fauna, Fearthainne, Skagos and Alda. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ekstasis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
