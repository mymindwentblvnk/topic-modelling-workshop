Mortal pain was a Death/Thrash Metal band from Marousi, Athens. The band was formed in October 1989. They released their debut demo with three songs plus the Celtic Frost cover "Return to the Eve". In April 91 they released their second demo. The band put also one song ("The Fear Within") on the Greek compilation LP "Screwing the Scene" out on Molon Lave Records ( MLP 004 ) . In 1992 they entered Praxis Studios with the producer Kostas Parissis to write songs for their 7" EP through Molon Lave Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortal+Pain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
