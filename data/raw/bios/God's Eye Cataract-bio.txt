A decision to create a project with a slow emotional and depressive music with dark atmosphere belongs to Martin Šulc. This project under the name of God’s Eye Cataract has been started in August 2007 after Martin meets Sergey Gordeev, the guitarist of Russian brutal death metal band Fleshbomb. During the autumn 2007 – winter 2008 after a few rehearsals by Martin and Sergey were composed and recorded a four songs demo called “A Sleep So Quiet”. At the moment God’s Eye Cataract is searching for the label to release next materials. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/God%27s+Eye+Cataract">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
