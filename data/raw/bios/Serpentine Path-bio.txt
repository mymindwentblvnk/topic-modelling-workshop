Serpentine Path is a new death/doom band featuring all three guys from sludgy veteran New York doom trio Unearthly Trance plus Tim Bagshaw, the founding bassist/guitarist of vintage UK doom rockers Electric Wizard and the dronier, weirder Ramesses.
