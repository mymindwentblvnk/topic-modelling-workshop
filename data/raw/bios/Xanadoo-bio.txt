Xanadoo is a thrash metal trio from Singapore. They formed in 2006 and have since released four demos, one compilation and three splits. In August 2010 they released their debut album named "Black. Death. Grind. Shit!" 

The band consist of:
Zak - Guitars and Vocals 
Shiva - Bass guitar and Backing Vocals 
Mahesha - Drums
