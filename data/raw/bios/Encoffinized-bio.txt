They're a death metal/grindcore band from Huntington Beach, California.  The year they formed is not known.  They consist of  Chris "Father Mullet" Johnson (vocals, guitar), Jake Ortiz (bass) and Max "Rutan" Baxter (drums).  They are signed to the record label Maggot Stomp.  They released their first demo Interdimensional Abominations of the Dark Abyss in March 2017 and it was released through Headsplit Records.  They released their first full-length album Chambers of Deprivation in 2019 and it was released through Maggot Stomp. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Encoffinized">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
