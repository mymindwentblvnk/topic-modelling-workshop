Prehistöricos is an indie/folk band hailing from Santiago, Chile, formed in 2010 by Tomás Preuss, joining forces with Jessica Romo. they create simple, melancholic sounds that take elements from folk to electronic music. In 2014, Romo leaves the band, becoming Preuss' project only. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Prehist%C3%B6ricos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
