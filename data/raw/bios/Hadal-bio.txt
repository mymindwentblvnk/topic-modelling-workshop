Hadal, a melodic death metal band from Belgrade, Serbia. Formed in 2013. by former members of the bands Forlorn Wisp and Cyreign. The band's genre is mostly inspired by the Scandinavian metal scene, with themes that combine folklore, fiction and mythology, closely followed by horror and mystery. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hadal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
