Founded by Fenrix 'Paul Yarber' and Pyriel 'Nick Nowark', IV Never More is a South Carolina band born out of similar music taste and interests. IV Never More has completed a demo, "A Glimpse Into Blasphemy", and is now in the process of recording their first full length album, "Blaspehmy".
