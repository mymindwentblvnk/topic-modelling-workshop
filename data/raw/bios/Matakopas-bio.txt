Matakopas was a Heavy Metal Band from Rome, New York formed in 1985. They released their first album called "Coming Out Ahead" in 1987, They later realeased a second album titled " coming out ahead 2"  The line-up was: Shane Parry (Vocals), Ray Abel (Guitar), Jim 'Boog' Taylor (Bass), and Mark Madonia (Drums).
