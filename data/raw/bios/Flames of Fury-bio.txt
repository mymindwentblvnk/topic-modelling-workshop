Flames of Fury (FoF) began in the Spring of 2009. Guitarist Jason Perez teamed up with metal vocalist powerhouse, Angelica Vargas. Both veterans of the NYC music scene they set out to create their vision of uncompromising metal. 

The music was purposely designed as a blend of power, speed and musical chops with soaring, melodic vocals in a classic metal format with contemporary influences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flames+of+Fury">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
