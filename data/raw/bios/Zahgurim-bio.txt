Co-founded by Paul Ackerley & William Vince. A Temple Ov Psychick Youth affiliated band. Recorded LP "Moral Rearmament", on the Berlin Atonal label (1985). Following live collaborations with The Colonels, merged to form, Left Hand Right Hand.
