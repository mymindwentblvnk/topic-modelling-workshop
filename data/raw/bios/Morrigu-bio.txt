MORRIGU is a Dark Emotional Metal band from Switzerland.

Formed in 1999; they developed their music-style from doom-death metal into a more distinct, melodic and progressive sound which they themselves label as Dark Emotional Metal. 
Their musical range involves heavy riffs and catchy guitar melodies, breathtaking clean vocals and a full soundscape with an epic atmosphere.
