Rooky is:

1) A Japanese pop group that released four singles between 1998 and 1999. They did some of the opening and ending themes for the anime Super Doll Licca-Chan.

2) Marcus "Rooky" Forstbauer, a progressive metal guitarist from Germany who released his first album Extended in 2007
