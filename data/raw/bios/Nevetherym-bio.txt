Post-Metal from the Central Coast of New South Wales, Australia. Nevetherym released a three track EP in 2007, entitled "Rendezvous".

On Wednesday, February 11, 2009 the band announced via their MySpace blog that they were disbanding. All three tracks from the EP can still be streamed at http://www.myspace.com/nevetherym <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nevetherym">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
