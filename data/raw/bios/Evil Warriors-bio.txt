Evil Warriors formed in Leipzig, Germany.  They formed in the year 2007.  They describe themselves as an "Aggressive Blood Metal" band.   They are actually a black metal band, but they also incorporate elements of death metal and thrash metal in their music.  They consist of Beast (vocals, guitars) Alastor (guitars),  Metnal (bass) and Exesor (drums).  B.S. is a live member for Evil Warriors, he plays bass.  Past members Vent (bass), Hellhunter (bass) and Lunatic (his role is unknown).   The band has released two demos, one EP and two full-length albums.  Their lyrical themes include death, hell and metal.  They're currently signed to War Anthem Records.
