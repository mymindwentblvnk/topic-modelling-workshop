Dissimulation - Lithuanian thrash/black metal band hailing from a small province town of Lithuania - Anykščiai. Band was formed in 1993, and so far has released three full-length albums, "Maras" (2002), "Prakeikimas" (2005) (Maras is more blackish, Prakeikimas - blackened thrash metal, full of hate.) and "Atiduokit mirusius", released in 2008 by Ledo Takas Records.

Band Members:
* Venomous - Bass, Vocals 
* Stabmeldys - Drums
