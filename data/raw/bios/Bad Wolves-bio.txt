Bad Wolves is a metal supergroup, composed of lead singer Daniel "DL" Laskiewicz (ex-guitarist of The Acacia Strain), guitarists Doc Coyle (ex-God Forbid) and Chris Cain (Bury Your Dead), drummer John Boecklin (ex-Devildriver) , and bassist Kyle Konkiel. 
Their original lead singer, Tommy Vext, was dismissed from the band in January 2021 on account of his political beliefs and accusations of violence against him. Their debut album "Disobey" was released on May 11, 2018, featuring the #1 Mainstream Rock hit "Zombie", a cover of the Cranberries' 1994 song.
