Alekhine's Gun is a Metal outfit from Brooklyn, New York. Blending Thrash elements with subtle Hardcore undertones and fierce in your face vocals, AG has made shockwaves in the NYC Metal scene in less than a year.

This veteran lineup consists of vocalist Jessica Pimentel (Everybody Gets Hurt/Desolate), guitarist Jeff Martinez (Desolate), Bassist Dan Martinez (King Hell), & drummer Angel Cotte (Eyes of the Sun, Immortal Flesh). Alekhine's Gun is currently working expeditiously on their EP entitled "Meditations in Wrath".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alekhine%27s+Gun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
