Metakognition was a Black Metal Band from Germany. They have quit the band in June 2009.

Metakognition combines melodic death metal, black metal and oldschool thrash elements. Founded by H.S. and V. in January 2008, two demos, one split album and a full length arose from the ashes of their thoughts.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metakognition">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
