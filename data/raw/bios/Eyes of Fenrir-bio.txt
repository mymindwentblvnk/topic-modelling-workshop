Eyes Of Fenrir is a Japanese Power/Symphonic Metal band and a project by the guitarist Ruriko Azuma.
So far they've released a full-length album in 2009 entitled "Victorious Holy War" that was partly remastered versions of songs that she had made in her solo project.

Members:
Leo Figaro: Vocal and Keyboard
Yazin (Knights Of Round): Guitar
Ruriko Azuma: Keyboard and Guitar

Discography:
