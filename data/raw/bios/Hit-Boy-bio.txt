Chauncey Hollis (born May 21, 1987), better known as Hit-Boy, is an American Grammy-nominated music producer and rapper currently signed to GOOD Music. He is best known for his production on Lil Wayne's 'Drop the World' featuring Eminem, The ASAP Rocky single 'Goldie,' The DJ Khaled single 'I Wish You Would' featuring Kanye West and Rick Ross, Pusha T's 'My God,' Kanye West's 'Christmas in Harlem,' and 'Niggas in Paris,' and 'Clique,' the GOOD Music single featuring Jay Z, Kanye West, and Big Sean.

In 2012, Hit-Boy released "The HITStory," his debut mixtape as a rapper as well as a producer.
