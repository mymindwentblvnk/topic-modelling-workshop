Beez (aka Killa Beezy, Beezy the Boss, Beez Gotti, etc.) is an underground rapper from Florida, known for his collaborations with Eche B, Big J (aka Frothy Fingers), and Taz from the UK.  Currently residing in Gainesville, FL and keeping up a consistently prolific production schedule, Beez is slated to release a solo album and mixtape in early 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beez">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
