Doom side-project of the band Godgory.Grave Flowers Sweden (Karlstad) was formed in December '93 by Matte Andersson as a one man project. They are influenced by My Dying Bride, Katatonia, and Anathema.

Releases:

Demo 1995
Garmonbozia Demo 1997
Solace Me 2000
