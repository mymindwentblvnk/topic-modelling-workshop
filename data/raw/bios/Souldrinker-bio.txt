The sound of beautiful violence

Imagine Pantera, Lamb of God and a dash of Amon Amarth mixed with a voice like Lee Aaron…that is Souldrinker!!!

The riff-driven power of the band and the harsh but melodic vocals of Iris Boanta combine to produce an unique brand of modern metal, which perfectly unites technical finesse and a melodic approach with heavy riffs, a big groove and keen dynamics.  That’s it: Souldrinker!!!
