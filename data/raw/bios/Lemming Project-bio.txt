Lemming Project were a German death metal band. The band released two full-length albums - "Extinction" (1991) and "Hate and Despise" (1992) - as well as a split with folk metal band Skyclad (though only a handful of copies exist).

Guitarist and main songwriter Michael Schmale died on February 17th, 2005. He was 33 years old. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lemming+Project">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
