Angel y Khriz are Ángel Rivera Guzmán and Christian Colón,They are a Puerto Rican reggaeton duo. They work largely with Luny Tunes.

They were introduced in 2002 in the CD MVP, by Hector "El Father" with the song "Cazando Voy".

Los MVP’s is Angel y Khriz’s own CD where they present the listener to soulful hits like Ven Baílalo and Te Quiero Ver Hoy, as well as De Casería (featuring Divino) and the up beat Ram Pa Pan Pan.

In the year 2005, they have come up with the songs "Fua", "De Lao A Lao" (bachata and merengue reggaeton versions), and "Mazacotte". They have been compared to artists like Daddy Yankee, Don Omar, and Wisin y Yandel in terms of fame.

They have also released a single entitled "Carita de Angel" (Face of an Angel) from Echo "The Lab"'s compilation album Invasion.

In 2008 they released their new album Showtime with the first single being "La Vecina" (The Neighbor) and their second single "Na De Na". Rockstar Games chose their song "Ven Baílalo" to appear in one of GTA IV's character trailers (Manny Escuela).
