Sidney Gish is a DIY pop artist from Boston, MA. Her debut album "Ed Buys Houses" was released in 2016 although there are collections of earlier music on her bandcamp. At the start of 2018 her Sophomore album "No Dogs Allowed" was recognized by Pitchfork which resulted in a massive increase in her popularity. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sidney+Gish">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
