Necrotic Trust  is currently the music project of musician Igor Korzhov, based in Kharkiv, Ukraine. Necrotic Trust's music is influenced by metal, alternative rock, electronica, trip-hop and subtly by jazz music.

Necrotic Trust's first album Dry Our Fears was recorded by Igor Korzhov and Serge Tkachenko in 2005 and released in Russia, Moscow in 2007 on the BadMoodMan Music label. Though only 100 CD copies were
issued, Dry Our Fears was an underground success and received positive reviews.

Necrotic Trust's second album is to be released in 2008. It will feature more complex songs, tighter
production and stronger songwriting compared to its predecessor.
