UK-based pianist & composer Simeon Walker has quickly emerged as a leading light in the burgeoning Modern Classical scene, following his initial releases 'Preface' & 'Mono'.

His calm piano instrumentals invite the listener to find stillness, beauty and meaning as much in the spaces between the notes as the notes themselves, as musical stories are woven with each tender, intimate performance.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Simeon+Walker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
