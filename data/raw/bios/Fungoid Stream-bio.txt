Fungoid Stream are a funeral doom band from Buenos Aires, Argentina. 

The band formed in 2003 and takes inspiration from H.P. Lovecraft down to the very name, which refers to the fungoid people called the Yuggoth. The music is ambient funeral doom with both depressing and horror moments. The atmosphere of the music is high compared to other similar bands. For fans of Until Death Overtakes Me who doesn't expect to hear exactly the same.

Discography: 
2004: Celaenus Fragments [Furias Records]
2010: Oceanus [Furias Records]
2014: Prehuman Shapes
