Born of Sin is a Swedish death metal band formed in 2001 and based outside of Gothenburg. Their music is traditional swedish death metal, with a few black metal influences. Born of Sin have released two demos, one EP and one full length album.

Releases:
The beheader - 2002 (Demo)
Hell will walk the earth - 2004 (Demo)
Let it begin - 2006 (EP)
Imperfect breed of humanity - 2007 (Album)
