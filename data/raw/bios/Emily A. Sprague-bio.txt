Emily Sprague is a synthesist, songwriter, and sound designer originally from the Catskill Mountains, currently living and working in Los Angeles. She makes mesmerizing ambient pieces using eurorack modular synths and also fronts warm indie pop outfit Florist.

Sprague self-released four albums in 2011 and 2012. After a 5 year hiatus she returned in 2017 with "Water Memory", followed by "Mount Vision" (2018) and "Hill, Flower, Fog" (2020).
