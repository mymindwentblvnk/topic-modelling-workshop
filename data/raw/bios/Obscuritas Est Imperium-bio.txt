Obscuritas Est Imperium is a Melodic Black Metal band with Atmospheric Black influences formed in Germany. The band was formed in the location of Rathenow, Brandenburg. They would be referred to as - highly obscure and poorly promoted band. All work that is known is the album "Aus brennendem Blut" made in 2001 self-released. Their lyrics are generally related to Scandinavian history and portray an important role in German Black Metal. Although, the band has been considered extremely obscure, they have a considerably acknowledgable sound quality.
