Ironchrist was a thrash metal/crossover band from Maryland, USA that was formed in 1987. They released a self titled EP in 1988 on New Renaissance Records, and their only full-length album "Getting the most out of your extinction" in 1989 on Colossal/Restless Records. The album sold over 27,000 copies worldwide and helped cement the bands place in the growing thrash metal/crossover genre of the late 80s to early 90s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ironchrist">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
