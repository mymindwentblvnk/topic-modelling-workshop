German singer Leepa after having her first viral hit, went straight to performing at Climate Live and Fridays for Future, sharing a stage with superstars like Milky Chance. As if this wasn’t enough already, her latest single "i'm sorry, are you" conquered not only the hearts of international blogs such as Earmilk, VICE or Wonderland, but also those of the German radio landscape: She was nominated for the "New Music Award“ as the newcomer of the year by the national radio stations, where LEEPA proudly won the 2nd place and the hearts of the jury. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leepa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
