Sarakt is a Black Metal band from Bulgaria.It's true old school Black Metal with a lot of Pagan touches, heavy riffs and really dark and aggressive vocals with some excellent dark ambiances. The lyrics are about the glory of Bulgaria under the great Bulgarian rulers Khan Kardam, Khan Krum The Terrible and Khan Omurtag. 
Kiril Nenov / Кирил Ненов (китари/guitars)
Yulian Raichev / Юлиян Райчев (китари/guitars)
