Death/ Black Doom Metal from Karachi, Pakistan. The idea is to paint landscapes of misery and depression, transferring ideas from the mind into a musical format. Embrace the Doom.
Khorne was initiated in early 2013 as a Death/Doom band with influences from Early Doom Metal, Death and Black Metal.
 Vocals: Farhan Rathore
 Guitars/Drums: Faiq Ahmed
 Bass: Daniyal Buksh Soomro
