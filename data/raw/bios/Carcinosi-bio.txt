Carcinosi is a Death Metal Band from Porto Alegre/RS - Brazil since 1996.
The current lineup features the founder member Tiago Vargas (Bass/Vocals), Caio Torres(Guitars) and Alysson dos Santos (Drums). The band released their new album Resumption in August, 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carcinosi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
