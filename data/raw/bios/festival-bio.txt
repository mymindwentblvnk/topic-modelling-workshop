There is more than one artist with this name, including: 
1) Festival was a late-'70s disco "assemblage" created by Boris Midney. 
2) Festival was an American folk band comprising sisters Lindsay Powell and Alexis Powell, plus their brother Mike Powell. 
3) Festival was German techno producer Heinrich Tillack. 
4) Festival was a British band that was one of several aliases used by members of 10cc before they started releasing music as 10cc.

1) Festival was a late-'70s disco "assemblage" created by Boris Midney (producer/arranger of Mi Sound Productions, Inc.); notable for its 1979 dance disco version of the Andrew Lloyd Webber musical Evita, including "Don't Cry for Me Argentina". vocal group, members may include: Kevin Owens, Lucia Suarez, Catalina Sevilla, and Silvia Suarez

2) Festival was an American folk band comprising sisters Lindsay Powell and Alexis Powell, plus their brother Mike Powell. The sound varied from tribal to psychedelic . Their album Come, Arrow, Come! was released on Language of Stone in April 2008.
