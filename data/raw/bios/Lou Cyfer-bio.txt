Lou Cyfer was a Brazilian death metal band formed in 1989. They released a demo in 1990, a demo in 1991, a split with fellow Brazilian death metallers Cirrhosis in 1991 and a full-length album in 1993 titled "Worship Flesh". They were signed to the famous Cogumelo Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Lou+Cyfer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
