George Albert Stone III (born May 11, 1994), known professionally as EST Gee, is an American rapper and songwriter. He is signed to Yo Gotti's Collective Music Group (CMG), Interscope Records and Warlike. He is perhaps best known for his feature on the 2021 single "Real as It Gets" by rapper Lil Baby. His fifth mixtape, Bigger Than Life or Death, released on July 21, 2021, and peaked at number 7 on the Billboard 200. It spawned the Billboard Hot 100-charting songs "5500 Degrees" and "Lick Back". A deluxe edition of the mixtape was released on December 3, 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/EST+Gee">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
