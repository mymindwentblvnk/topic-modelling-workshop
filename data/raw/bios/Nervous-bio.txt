There is more than one artist named Nervous:

1.  Dark ambient project by the Canadian artist who also records as Contra, Strider etc.

2.  Indie rock band from Yogyakarta, Indonesia. www.soundcloud.com/wearenervous

3. Oakland based rock band. Sounds like Unwound covering Mudhoney through an Echoplex. 3 song demo available at nervoussongs.bandcamp.com in both digital and physical (cassette) formats. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nervous">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
