There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Morgain is a hypno doom metal band from Slovakia. 

Morgain are

    * Richard Zajac - vocals, guitars, drums programming
    * Erich "Boboš" Prochádzka - mouth organ
    * Jitka Dobřická - keyboards
    * Andrea Vargová - accordion
