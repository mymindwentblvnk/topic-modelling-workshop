Songs of Green Pheasant is the solo project of Duncan Sumpner, a recording artist and school teacher from Oughtibridge in Sheffield, England. Blending acoustic-based songs with electronic effects, Sumpner's music has been described as psychedelic folk with "warm washes of sound". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Songs+of+Green+Pheasant">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
