Oneiric Realm formed in the fall of 2008 with the goal of melding the primal and atmospheric elements of black metal with the ambience of shoe-gaze and the strangeness of avant-garde.  The band released a demo in September of 2010 with 4 songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oneiric+Realm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
