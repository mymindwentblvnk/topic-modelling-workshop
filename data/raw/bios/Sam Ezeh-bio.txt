Sam Ezeh (born 27 August 2000 in Gothenburg) is a Swedish Singer-Songwriter and a producer based in Stockholm.
Born and raised in Gothenburg, Sweden, he moved to Stockholm when he was 14. He began uploading videos to his YouTube channel in 2013.
He is former YouTube vlogger who was known for his 2chips1cola channel. He gained popularity for his eclectic blend of vlog entries, which included challenge completions and tags.
