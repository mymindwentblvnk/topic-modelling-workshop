Echo Hollow is an American Progressive metal band founded by Tourniquet ex-members Guy Ritter and Gary Lenaire in 1996. Echo Hollow has released two studio albums, Diet of Worms in 1998 and Superficial Intelligence in 2004, then disbanded shortly after. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Echo+Hollow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
