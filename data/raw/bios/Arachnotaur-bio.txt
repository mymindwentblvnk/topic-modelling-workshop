Arachnotaur hails from the UK.  They play a unique style of sludge, with drone playing a large role in the music. On April 11, 2007, they released their debut album "Slow Pertinacious Threnody On The Abnegation Of Succour". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arachnotaur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
