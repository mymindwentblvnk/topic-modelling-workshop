Absolon is an Orlando, Florida based band that plays a style of progressive metal, fused with symphonic and Gothic elements. The band is led by singer/guitar player Ken Pike, formerly of the 80s metal band Malachia.  The band's debut album, Darkness Rising is a concept album inspired by the Oscar Wilde novel The Picture Of Dorian Gray.  Darkness Rising  tells the tale of a heavy metal guitarist named Derek Blackheart that makes a pact with dark forces for fame and glory. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Absolon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
