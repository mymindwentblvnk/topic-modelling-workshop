Brooks' new album "The Axis Of All Things" is out now independently at http://chrisbrooks.com and through iTunes

Chris Brooks is a guitar instrumentalist from Sydney Australia, whose style fuses elements of rock, metal, fusion and prog-rock with melodic and dynamic compositions.

Chris has recorded or played live with members of Yngwie Malmsteen's Rising Force, Black Sabbath / Heaven & Hell, LORD, Steve Paoli's War Machine, Lana Lane, Age of Nemesis, and been featured in major magazines like Burrn!, Metal Hammer, Sweden Rock and Young Guitar.  Chris appears on The Alchemists II and LORD's "Ascendence" album!!!
