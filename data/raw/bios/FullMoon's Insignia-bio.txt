One-man-band from Novgorod the Great, Russia. While on the first demos the music featured mystic atmospheric black/ambient, later it transformed into tragic gothic/doom metal with various vocals and instrumental arrangements. There are three demo works by now. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/FullMoon%27s+Insignia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
