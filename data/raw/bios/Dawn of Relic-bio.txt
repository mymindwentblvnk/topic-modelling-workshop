Dawn of Relic is a Finnish melodic Dark/Black metal band. 

The band was formed in 1993 by drummer Jukka Juntunen in Oulu, Finland. 
Their debut album, One Night in Carcosa was realeased in 1999. This album had two session musicians on it.

The next album, titled Lovecraftian Dark, was released 2003. Their latest release, Night on Earth was in 2005.
