Hicks Kinison (formerly Raijin) is the name of a hardcore metal project featuring Chris Paccou (a.k.a. Aa Kuernito) (Brujeria), Shane Embury (Napalm Death), Roel Klomp (ex-Born From Pain) and Sofia Magalhaes (ex-We Are The Damned).
