Noxagt is a Norwegian rock band from Sandnes, Norway. It started as a Kjetil Brandsdal solo project in 1999, but was expanded to a power trio in 2001. The band have a unique sound. The members are involved with a number of musical projects across different genres.

They are currently not signed to Load Records.

A new record entitled Brutage is to be released in early 2014.

Members:

John Hegre - guitars;
Kjetil Brandsdal - bass;
Jan Christian L. Kyvik - drums.

Members:

Nils Erga - violin;
Anders Hana - guitars.
