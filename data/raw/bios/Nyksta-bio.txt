Nyksta is an experimental/black metal band based in Vilnius, Lithuania. 
Formed in 2013 the act has released their debut full-lenght named "Lieka tik Sienos" in 2014 via Inferna Profundus Records. Often titled as "urban black metal" the band states that their aim is to keep atmosphere and extremes of music as their main creative background within themes of urban decay and vices of post-modern life. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nyksta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
