Northern Ash emerges in 2007 playing an old school blend of black / death / and thrash metal in a unique and uncompromising fashion. This multinational duo is spearheaded by both Felipe Diez known for his work with Sorrowstorm and Encryptor, as well Todd Brandt from Transfigural Form and Eden in Ruins.
