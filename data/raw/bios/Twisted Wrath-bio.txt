TWISTED WRATH are a Heavy Metal/Thrash metal outfit from the west of Ireland.

The band's style consists of Megadeth, Metallica and Testament inspired Thrash metal with hints of Judas Priest, Iron Maiden and W.A.S.P. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Twisted+Wrath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
