Regress was formed in 1996 by Joe Losurdo (ex- Life Sentence), Chopper Stepe (ex- Negative Element), and Anthony Illarde (ex- Rights Of the Accused). In 1997 first 7" "Price Of Power EP" released on Won't Go Flat Records. Played roughly once a year due to Stepe living in Florida and rest of band in Chicago. Second release "Look Who's Pulling The Strings" EP came out in 2004 on Lengua Armada Records. Also two songs on Histeria 2 compilation. Group toured Europe in March 2005. Currently working on subsequent full-length LP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Regress">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
