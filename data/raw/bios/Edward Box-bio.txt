One of the finest rock and metal players the UK has ever produced, Edward Box started playing guitar at the age of 14 and was inspired by like of George Lynch, Yngwie Malmsteen, Michael Schenker, Doug Aldrich and Tony Macalpine. His early exploits with British metalers XLR8R are are hard to trackdown but he did release his first instrumental album, Plectrumhead in 2002 and the follow up Moonfudge in 2006. He is a fiery and melodic player who posesses considerable technique and songwriting skills. Currently he is the main songwriter and guitarist/vocalist in UK metal band Vendetta.www.vendetta-theband.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Edward+Box">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
