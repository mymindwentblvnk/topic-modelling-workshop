Heresy of Thieves is Death Metal/Hardcore/Grindcore band featuring members of Test Subjects and The Ocean. They released an EP entitiled "The Code" and their full length "The Montana Complex" was released in October 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Heresy+Of+Thieves">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
