Spelljammer is a stoner/doom metal band from Stockholm, formed in 2008. Their music, a heavy slab of low-tuned riffs often played at very slow tempos, is most strikingly influenced by classic acts such as Black Sabbath, Acid King, and Sleep.

They are signed to RidingEasy Records. So far, Spelljammer released two EPs and two full albums:

 2010 Inches from the Sun (EP)2012 Vol. II (EP)2015 Ancient of Days2021 Abyssal Trip
