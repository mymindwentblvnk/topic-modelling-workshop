Achsar is slovak Black/Death metal band. The Band's name Achsar came from the name of character in Russian fairy tale. 

Current lineup:
Anton Kalmancai : guitar & vocals
Milan Krištofík : vocals
Rado Botka : keyboards
Michal Ag : guitar & vocals
Ján Nilaš : bass
Martin Sivák : drums
Peter Patai : guitar
