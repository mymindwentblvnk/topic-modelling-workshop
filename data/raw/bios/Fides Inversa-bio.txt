Fides Inversa is a Black Metal band from Italy formed in 2006 that consists of, 
Void A.D - Guitar, Bass
Omega A.D. - Drums, Vocals

They have released two full-length albums entitled Hanc Aciem Sola Retundit Virtus (The Algolagnia Divine) (2009) and Mysterium Tremendum et Fascinans (2014). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fides+Inversa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
