Wooden Arms (http://woodenarms.co.uk/) are a Norwich-based modern quartet featuring: Violin (Jess Diggins), Cello (Fynn Titford-Mock), Piano (Alex Carson), Guitar (Jeff Smith), and 5 part vocal harmonies. Wooden Arms are a subtle blend between classical instrumentation and composition with modern melodies and production.

In April 2013 they released their single - "Separate the Verb" and have since performed as part of Liverpool Sound City, The Norfolk and Norwich festival , and partaken in small national tours.

They are releasing their debut, self-titled EP on the 12th of July 2013 with Bare Feet Records. The EP was engineered and produced by David Pye, credited with working with Wild Beasts, iLiKETRAiNS, Egyptian Hip-hop and more.
