Likblek is a Black Metal band from Gothenburg, Sweden, formed in 2006. Lyrical themes include Occult, Evil, Darkness and Blasphemy. Likblek means "Pale as a corpse" in Swedish. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Likblek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
