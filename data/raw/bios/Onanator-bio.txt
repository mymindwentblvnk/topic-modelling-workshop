Serbian thrash metal band founded in 2010. They have recorded one demo entitled "Circle of Death" (2012) so far, which contains three songs: Psychopath, The Exorcism and Shadow of War.

Members: 
Vocals - Nikola Kuzmanovic
Guitar - Djordje Lukovic
Guitar - Nikola Mijacic
Bass Guitar - Marko Mladenovic
Drums - Srdjan Nedeljkovic
