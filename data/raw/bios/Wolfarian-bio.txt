Wolfarian is a young symphonic-folk metal band from Brno, Czech republic. 
The band uses guitar, bass guitar, drums, violin, flute, vocalists are both a woman and a man, from whom the man also growls.
The lyrics are written in english and concern history, battles and gods, especially of viking and pagan clans. 
Active since September 2012.
