Birthed in late 2019 after a long flourishing friendship between American artist Pierce Fulton and Canadian producer Gordon Huntley, Leaving Laurel have developed a penchant for introspective melodic electronic music. On their formation, the duo writes:   “ Leaving Laurel is a tale of two old friends connecting musically for the first time after almost a decade. Inspired by recording into an old, thought to be broken, cassette tape player, a new project materialized seemingly out of thin air - something neither of us knew we were looking for. ” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leaving+Laurel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
