There is more than one artist with this name: 

1. Auracle was a dutch symphonic metal band with Asian and Arabic influences. Founded by composer/keyboardist Joris van de Kerkhof and lyric-writer/bassplayer Fred Hasselman. Later joined by guitarist Niek van Doorn, drummer Marco Verberne, singer Bryan Ketelaars. When they were joined by female singer Amelie Mangelschots they changed their name into Auracle.

2. Mythica is a celtic rock band, based in Delaware, US. Inspired by artists like Tori Amos, Sarah McLachlan, Dave Matthews and Radiohead, and by traditional Irish and World music, Mythica’s sound is a melting pot of genres, including rock, alternative, ambient, bluegrass, folk and Celtic. The band’s fearless leader, Melissa Cox, has won many awards for her songwriting prowess and powerful mezzo soprano voice. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mythica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
