Hitch are from Kortrijk, Belgium and have released records on Goodlife, Delboy, Vlas Vegas, Latest Flame, Moonlee, Kinky Star and Divot records. They play some kind of rocking post hardcore influenced by bands as Quicksand or Slint. Singer Mich plays in Galatasaray as well.
