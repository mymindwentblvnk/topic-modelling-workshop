Gedwolmann is American Pagan black metal band based in California. The band is solo project of Lord Brosnian  and it's formed in 2001. The band is using drum machine. All the music is by Lord Brosnian. The band got for now only one album - Der nacod Leoht Nipan (2002). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gedwolmann">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
