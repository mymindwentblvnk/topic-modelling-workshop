Hereditary are a metal band from Bonn, Germany, and are heavily inspired by Gothenburg Death/Melodic Death Metal. Founded in 2013, Flo (guitar) and Stevie (drums) were searching for members until they completed the band in the summer of 2014. In August 2015 they released "Angel of Decay", their first EP. In the summer of 2016 their vocalist Fluch left the band, since then their long-term friend Nils Raschke is on this position.
