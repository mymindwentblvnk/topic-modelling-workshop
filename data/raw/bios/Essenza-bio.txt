There two artists that are called Essenza. The first is:

Essenza is best described as a collage quartet, whose sound is the unique culmination of Elegant Electronics, moody guitars, and sultry vocals. Sonically pleasing on every level, Essenza's Haunting Melodies will sink deep into your head and keep you humming from dawn till dreams.

They are a chicago-based band and released their first self-titled EP  in September 2005. 

The second is:

An Italian heavy metal band formed in 1993. The band is made up of brothers, Carlo G. Rizzello (guitarist) and Alessandro Rizello (bassist). Luca Rizello played drums with the band until 2003 at which time he was replaced by Paolo Colazzo.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Essenza">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
