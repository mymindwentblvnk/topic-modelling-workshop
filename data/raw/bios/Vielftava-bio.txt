Vielftava was founded in the autumn of 1998 by Conte Tamasa (guitar) and Hatru (drums). Vielftava is a very appreciated underground metal band in Romania. 
Vielftava is: Conte Tamasa - guitar, backing vocals, Tavar - vocals/bass, Necoranda - keyboards, Hatru - drums.
