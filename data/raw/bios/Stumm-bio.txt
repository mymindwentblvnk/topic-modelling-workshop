Stumm is Nihilistic Sludge Doom band from Turku, Finland. Formed in 2003. Music is slow, primitive and hateful sludge. Stumm I was recorded and mixed in Turku, Finland in June and October 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stumm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
