There are 3 bands with the name Against The Tide. 

1. Against the Tide is a rock band out of Southern Illinois.With deep melodic verses and bone crushing choruses this is a band you don't want to miss!!
Band Members: Mike - Vocals, Cory - Guitar/Vocals, Amos - Guitar/Vocals, Chuck - Bass, Keith Young - Drums

2. A five piece  death metal/ deathcore band formed in mid-2008 from Perth, Western Australia. With release of their self-titled EP and offical music video in 2010, Against The Tide are currently focused on writing a follow up EP, featuring their new vocalist and touring both nationally and internationally in 2012.

3. A Canadian acoustic band from Southern Ontario, formed in 2010. The group is noted for their success starting in early 2011, after the release of their demo ep. They have taken part in a large number of national tours, eventually leading the group to tour Eastern Asia and Austrailia for a tour in late 2011. The group are currently writing their debut album, and continue to tour globally. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Against+the+Tide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
