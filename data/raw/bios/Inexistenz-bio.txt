Inexistenz is a Slovenian one-man band, formed in 2010.

The purpose of the band is to present it's former's perception of life, existence and the inner ugliness of 
mankind.

The album Lebensweg was recorded in 2010, containing six songs.
All vocals, instruments and programming were done by the same person.
