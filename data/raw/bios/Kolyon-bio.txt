Koly P. AKA kolyon is a rapper native to Pompano Beach, Florida. His project titled "The Koly Bible" launched his rap career into the limelight. he frequently collaborates with fellow Pompano Beach rapper Kodak Black and also Boosie Badazz.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kolyon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
