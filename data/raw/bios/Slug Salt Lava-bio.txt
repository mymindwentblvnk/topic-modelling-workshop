Slug Salt Lava ~ Istanbul
Instrumental Sludge

Slug Salt Lava is an instrumental sludge band from Istanbul formed in 2012. The music aims to create an ambient of post- apocalypse world in solitude with slow riffs and doomy atmosphere.
