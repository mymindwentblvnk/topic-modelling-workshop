Acedi was a depressive black metal band from Århus, Denmark, formed in 2007 as a solo-project by N. Lyrics are based on nihilism, anti-religion, suicide and emptiness. The band's third demo, Lonely Soul, was dedicated to 'the old kings of black metal in Denmark'. The band was ended in 2012.

2008 - Glemt (demo)
