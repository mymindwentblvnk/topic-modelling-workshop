Hamburg-based singer with a hybrid of silk-smooth vocals, danceable production and a personal style served straight from the 90's vault. Ace, 23 years old, and her girl gang were immediately referred to as "the new TLC," and hailed as a living embodiment of the 90s R&B revival.
