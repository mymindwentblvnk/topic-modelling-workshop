Deer Creek is a sludge/doom metal trio from Denver, Colorado. Members are John Deer, Paul "Pivo" Vismara and Conan "Condo" Hultgren (ex-Ironkind, also head of Game Two Records). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deer+Creek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
