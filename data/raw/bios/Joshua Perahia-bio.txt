Joshua is a hardrock band founded by guitarist Joshua Perahia. The band has had several members who are well known for their solo works nowadays, such as Rob Rock, Alex Ligertwood (Santana), Steve Fontaine (Uriah Heep), Jeff Fenholt (Black Sabbath, & Jesus Christ Superstar on Broadway), Jason Scheff (Chicago) and Mark Boals (Yngwie Malmsteen). The band has been know by the names Joshua, The Joshua Band, and Joshua Perahia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Joshua+Perahia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
