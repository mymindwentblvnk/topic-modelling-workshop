Alkol is a Crossover Thrash Metal band from Lazio, Rome Italy. there lyrics are mostly about social issues and beer.  The Band consists of Corrado Adamo on Bass, Alex Massa on Drums, Andrea Mataloni on Guitar, and Chopper on Vocals. There debut EP "Beeried Alive" was released on February 28th, 2017.

Alkol is a band radicated in Guadalajara, México. His frontman, Efrén Jiménex, is also the principal lyrics writer. Smart Lyrics and visceral rock with some social preocupation.
