Marcus Morton (born April 20, 2004), known professionally as redveil, previously as DEVI*N, is a 18-year old hip hop artist and producer based in Maryland. redveil's genres and styles include jazz rap, abstract hip-hop, and alternative hip-hop backed by soul and funk samples. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Redveil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
