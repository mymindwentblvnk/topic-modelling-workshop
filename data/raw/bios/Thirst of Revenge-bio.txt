Thirst Of Revenge is a one-man brutal death metal project by Former Human Mincer Vocalist Carlos Mejias. He formed the project initially in 1999 as  Cerebral Torture, however, due to his commitments with Human Mincer it wasn't until 2006 when Carlos began his solo progect once more. This time in the form of  Thirst Of Revenge, Carlos brought together blasting drums, crushing guitars and furious vocals into his brutal death metal, catchy mix of staccato riffs, dodging blasts and bouncy rhythms.

He recorded and released his debut album The Beginning Of The End " through Pathologically Explicit Records in early 2010.
