Amelnakru ("enemy" in Assyrian/Babylonian) is a one-man depressive black metal project formed in Baghdad, Iraq around 2011 and led by Assir also known as Lord Erragal (Erragal on Last FM). 

The project released a demo, three EPs, and two split CDs between 2011 and 2013, and it is currently signed to Rigorism Production. The lyrical themes include wrath, hate, failure, and dark nature.
