Kever were a death metal band from Megiddo, Israel. The band were active between 2007-2013 as Morbid Tendency before changing the name to 'Kever' (Hebrew for 'Grave/Sepulcher'). They released their first EP under this name 'Eon of Cycling Death' on November 8, 2013 through Dark Descent and Me Saco un Ojo Records on CD and 12" vinyl. The band features members of Sonne Adam, Mortuus Umbra and Promiscuity. In 2018 they released the EP "Primordial Offerings" with Dark Descent records, and broke up recently in 2019.
