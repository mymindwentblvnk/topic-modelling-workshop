Akyrviron is a solo project by K. Partier from USA. 

He self released his first debut "Journeys from a New Era" in 2011 and has released his 2013 release "Light of the Ages" via Bandcamp.

Partier released a single "World Beyond" on the 08 June 2013 for digital paid download only. Recently, the project has released their newest full release "Dreamscape Requiem" in 2014. It blends more ambient sounds within heavy electric guitars and programmed drums. All music composed and produced by Kevin Partier. The artwork was made by Johannes Kert Roots.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Akyrviron">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
