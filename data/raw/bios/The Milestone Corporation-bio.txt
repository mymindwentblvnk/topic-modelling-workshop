The Milestone Corporation was an indie pop-rock band from Göteborg, Sweden active between the years 2003 and 2007.

The band was founded by Jonas Karlsson (guitar), Carl Nelvig (born Leskinen) (organ/piano) and Petter Uvesten (lead vocals) in 2003. The complete line up includes Joakim Axelsson (drums) and Tobias Sjögreen (bass). The band was disbanded in late 2007.

Their music blends blues oriented rock music with catchy pop tunes and is heavily influenced by 70s hard rock and 90s britpop groups. In total, 3 demo EP's were released by the band. All music is available for free download here at last.fm
