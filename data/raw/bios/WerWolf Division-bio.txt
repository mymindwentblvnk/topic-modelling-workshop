WerWolf Division was the name used by German resistance groups who continued to fight long after Germany had surrendered during WWII.

Werwolf Division origins from Scotland (United Kingdom) and was formed in 2001. Werwolf Division is no longer active.

The only member was Tyrwaz, who played all the instruments and did the vocals.
