Cats On Trees are a duo hailing from Toulouse, France, made up of Nina Goern (ex Aeria Microcosm) and Yohan Hennequin (ex My Own Private Alaska), created in 2007. They're an indie pop duo, using keyboard and drums, they take their inspiration from animals and colours and have been compared to the Dresden Dolls.

Until now they released :

- Uli EP (2009)
- Tiki Tiki Boy  - Single (2010) 
- Sirens Call  - Single (2013)
