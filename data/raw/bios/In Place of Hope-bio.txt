2) In place of hope is Metal/Progresive/Thrash Metal from Bandung, Indonesia. They formed in Bandung, mid 2008. Their current line-up is Muhammad Husein Ghifari-Vokal, Deni Ramdani-Guitar, Riand Majid-Guitar, Ahmad Habib Azhari-Bass. Their music is influenced by metal bands like Arch enemy, Lamb of God, Threat Signal and many more. The band has so far released five single. Their trying to give different color of Indonesia Metal scene.
