Rock band from Quilmes, Buenos Aires, Argentina. Formed in 1988 playing melodic heavy/power metal, in which time it released 3 albums. In 2009, they released their 4th album, switching to a hard rock style, and in 2018 they released their 5th album, in which their sound shifted towards a more radio-friendly pop rock style. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MATEN+AL+REY">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
