Sword of Darkness is a black metal band from Vietnam.
The band is now located in the United States, more precisely in Minnesota, but they still call their music "Vietnamese Black Metal". Founded in 1999 by the brothers Gary and Kenny Vu, the info talks about influences from bands like BELPHEGOR, DARK FUNERAL and BEHEMOTH for the new material. The band has since split up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sword+of+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
