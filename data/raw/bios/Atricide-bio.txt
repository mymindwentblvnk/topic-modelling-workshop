A death metal band from Jersey Shore, Pennsylvania. Released a demo in 1991 named "The Dead" consisting of 3 tracks, which would be repeated on the B side of the tape. The band had Scott Evans on bass, vocals, Jeff Kreitz as the drummer and Jason Hudock as guitarist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atricide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
