Pyromaniac is Finnish Black metal band hailing from Laitila Birth Happened at year 2001. Pyromanic Lyrics tell about satan,blasphemy and sex.

Members:
Pyromaniac - Vocals & Instruments (Ancestors Blood, Black Blood (Fin), ex-Demon Child)

Live session members:

Fyrdkal - Bass (Ancestors Blood (session), Black Blood (Fin) (session), Inferi (Fin), Kadotus)
J. Nieminen - Drums (G.O.R.E.)
A. Marttala - Guitar (G.O.R.E.)

Former/past member(s) 
Markus Rantanen - Guitar (session)
Black Tyrant - Guitar (live) (Black Blood (Fin), ex-Demon Child, ex-Kadotus (live member)
