Tidfall is an Industrial Black Metal band from Norway formed in 1992. They have released 3 full-length albums: Circular Supremacy [2000], Instinct Gate [2002], and Nucleus [2003]. They are currently inactive. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tidfall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
