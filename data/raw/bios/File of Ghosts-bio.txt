Lo-fi metal artist Sami Minkkala from Finland. File Of Ghosts blends a lot of different styles together, such as black metal, death metal, industrial metal and groove metal. Grey Metal is the third work by this band and was releases in the beginning of 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/File+Of+Ghosts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
