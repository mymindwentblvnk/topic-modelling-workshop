Veil of Winter was a german black metal band, which has been existing from 1993 till 1996. They released one demo in 1996 which is called "The throne of angel-tears". The lyrics focus on antichristianity, war, nietzsche and northern blood and northern honour. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veil+Of+Winter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
