Abhorred Despiser is a brutal death metal band from the city of Jakarta in Indonesia.
For a while they were disbanded, but recently the band has reformed, and they have added new vocalist Hepi Yulianto to the lineup

Line up:
Hepi Yulianto - Vocals
Jameel - Guitar
Novan - Drums
Ujang - Bass
