Juse Ju (Justus Hütter, *1982 in Kirchheim unter Teck, Germany) is a German rapper. He grew up in Kirchheim unter Teck (Germany), Yokohama (Japan) and . He studied theater arts, sociology and Japanese studies at LMU Munich. After that he studied journalism at the EMS Potsdam.
