Sardoniscorn plays music on the violin that has never been heard before. Highly technical, the sounds emitted on the historic instrument are disturbing, haunting, and sometimes grotesquely beautiful. Sardoniscorn tries to turn the thought process of the mentally disturbed into music, thus to the sane, it will sound like meaningless noise. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sardoniscorn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
