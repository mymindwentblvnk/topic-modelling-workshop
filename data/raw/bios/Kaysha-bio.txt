Kaysha is a French-based musician originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo (his family emigrated to France when he was seven years old).  His music is a blend of styles such as kizomba, rhumba, zouglou, zouk, zouk love, calypso, rnb, and hip hop.

Kaysha is also a successful music producer, having produced songs for some of the best-known artists on the Afro-Caribbean scene, including Passi, Solaar, Jacob Desvarieux, Soumia, Lynnsha, Elizio, and Ludo. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kaysha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
