Dark and dismal black metal from Germany, founded in 2003. Finistere Nachtvint, also known as Roac, is the sole member of the band playing all instruments and vocals. He has released two splits, one demo and one full length.  He also runs the label Fimbul Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kargvint">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
