Killer Elite was a Thrash Metal band from Ocean City, Maryland.

they released a demo for the first time in 1987 called 'State of War' as well as one called 'Mad As Hell' in 1988. 'Mad As Hel' was also the name of the full length which was released in the same year as the demo.

Last known line-up
JD Bradshaw - Guitars (Wartanas, Strange Flesh)
Tom Brunner - Bass, Vocals
