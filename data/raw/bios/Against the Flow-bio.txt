Against the Flow is a Christian rock band from Dohna, Saxony, Germany formed with its current members around 2005. 

Band members include:
David Holey (Guitar, Vocals)
Felix Wenzel (Keys)
Samuel Knobloch (Bass)
Stefan Schneider (Guitar)
Jakob Holey (Drums)
