Tico Splinter (24), also known as ticofaces, is a hip hop producer from The Netherlands. Inspired by artists like J Dilla, Madlib and Nujabes he started making beats in 2015. Expect smooth keys, soft synths, mood setting ambience and laid back drums when listening to his music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ticofaces">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
