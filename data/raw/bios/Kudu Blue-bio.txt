Kudu Blue are a Brighton-based 5 piece blending a range of nostalgia-tinged influences from 90's trip hop and electronica through to 00's garage.

Known for ambitious live setups and driven by the challenges which come with translating intricate electronic compositions to engaging and electrifying performances, Kudu Blue are constantly forced into testing their limits as musicians. The resulting sound is one heavily layered with textured instrumentation and vivid, memorable songwriting. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kudu+Blue">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
