Devil in the Kitchen is a folk/speed metal band from the Boston, Massachusetts area.
They formed in 2003.
Current line-up:
Andy Reiner - Electric Fiddle
Stash Wyslouch - Guitars
Bassil Silver-Hajo - Bass (Powerglove, Revengeance (US))
Alex Carrara - Drums
**Andy Reiner and Stash Wyslouch currently operate in the bands Blue Moose and the Unbottoned Zippers, and Fiddlefoxx
