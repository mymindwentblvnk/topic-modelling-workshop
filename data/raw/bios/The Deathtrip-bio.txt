Black Metal project of Aldrahn (Thorns, Urarv, Zyklon-B, Dødheimsgard, Old Man's Child) on vocals and Host 
(Thine) on guitars. Aldrahn left the band in 2019, at which point Kvhost (Grave Pleasures, Hexvessel, Code, Dødheimsgard, Void) joined to replace him. So far the band has released two demos to date ("Demo 08" in 2008 and "Global Exit" in 2010) as well as two full-lengths ("Deep Drone Master" in 2014 and "Demon Solar Totem" in 2019). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Deathtrip">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
