Apoc Death was a "Old School" Death Metal band from Tennessee, USA.
"Justified Killing" was originally released in 1995 on cassette only. in 2005 ''Justified Killing was re-released on CD by Misanthropica Enterprises. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Apoc+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
