United States of America  (Palatine, Illinois) based power/thrash metal band formed in 1985. The band released tow strong demos, as well as appearing on "Metal Massacre VIII" with "Violence Is Golden", however they were never able to recieve a deal to record a full-length. In 2008 they re-released their old material on one best of album titled "Ashes Tell No Tales". The bands sound could be described as similar to Helstar, at times having similarities to Savatage, Hellion, Banshee (US), Glacier and Oliver Magnum.
