1) Railway are a heavy metal band from Bavaria, Germany. They formed in 1977 and released their self-titled debut in 1984. They play a style of music similar to other German bands of the time such as Accept and Warlock. The band released 6 albums before they split up in 1995.
1984: Railway, 1985: Railway II, 1987: Climax, 1991: To Be Continued, 1993: Welcome Tonite, 1995: Persecution Mania. In 2006 they re-united and released "Finally Back - The Very Best Of 20 Years".



2) Railway is the project of Tristan Rodman, a 20 year-old musician living in Providence, RI.
