Death Of Millions is a Technical/brutal Death Metal band from USA.

The band released two Full-length albums,the first one called:Frozen and the second is Statistics and Tragedy.

Current Members

Chuck Salvo - Vocals
Brian Morrison - Guitar
Lee Ribera - Guitar
Brendon Bigelow - Bass
