King Tuff is a U.S. recording artist from Brattleboro, Vermont.

His first album, Was Dead, was released in 2008.  His eponymous album was released on 29th May 2012, charting at  twenty-one on Billboard's Heatseeker Albums chart. The album also entered the CMJ charts at fourteen, eventually climbing to number two. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/King+Tuff">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
