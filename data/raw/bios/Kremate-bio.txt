Thrash metal band from Brazil, formed in 1998. They have released two albums - "Death: In the Name Of" in 2005 and "The Greatest Joke" in 2011.
Current members are.
Anderson Paes  Bass
Rodrigo Mota          Drums
Regi Mota          Guitars
