Originally, TDR was a member of The Floods, alternative - based psychodelic band, formed in early 1996.
Todor Karakolev established himself as TDR in 2005 and began producing and composing electronic music. Despite being a minimal-tech artist at the core, he was heavily influenced by dance and vocal-house music.
As TDR, singer, multi-instrumentalist, and programmer Todor Karakolev makes playful, fractured minimal art techno tracks, based on constant bass&beat design mixing several styles of modern electronic music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TDR">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
