DFC (Distrito Federal Chaos) started to play in 1993 as a punk band in Brasilia, the capital of Brazil. After a few years they changed their lineup and started to play a thrash/crossover with hardcore edges <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/D.F.C.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
