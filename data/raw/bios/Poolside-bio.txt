Poolside is an American "daytime disco" band from Los Angeles, California, formed in 2010 by Jeffrey Paradise and Filip Nikolic. Following Nikolic's departure in 2017, the band currently consists of Jeffrey Paradise with additional members for live shows Vito Roccoforte, Mattie Safer, Brijean Murphy and Casey Butler.

2) Poolside is an electropop/synthrock band consisting of composer Josh Whelchel and singer/songwriter Melinda Hershey.  Poolside formed in 2005 with former member Marshall Reinhart.
