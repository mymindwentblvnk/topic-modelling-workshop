Obsessed By Seth was russian Brutal Death Metal band from the city of Novosibirsk. The band was found by members of Morior and was very popular in Siberia: deep, low growls of Senior Duremar became legendary in Novosibirsk. The band released an EP, Day Of The Final Injection, in 2002, but then they broke apart. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obsessed+By+Seth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
