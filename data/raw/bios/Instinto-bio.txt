Punk band formed in March of 2011 in Barcelona, Spain formed by: Ortega (vox), Aleix (bass), Paco (guitar), Gerar (guitar) and Enric (drums). Members from Instinto are/were also from: Totälickers, Hijxs Taradxs, Afganistan Yeye’s, Infäme, Disundead, Batakazo, Eskupe, Mesqueodi...  They mix d-beat and hardcore/crust punk melodies into their music.
