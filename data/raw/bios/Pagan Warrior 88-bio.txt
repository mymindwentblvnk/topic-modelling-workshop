Pagan Warrior 88 is a Pagan/NS black metal band from Italy.

It was founded in 2000 and is still active today. 

Herr MortuSS plays the bass, drums, guitars and does the vocals.

Pagan Warrior 88 has released so far:

- Waffen SS/Hate XXX/Pagan Warrior 88 Split, 2002  
- Demo mc Demo, 2002
