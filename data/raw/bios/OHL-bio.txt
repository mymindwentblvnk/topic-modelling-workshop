OHL (stands for Oberste Heeresleitung / Supreme Army Command ) is a German hardcore punk band. They started up in 1980 and dissolved in 1987. Since 1993, the have reunited again. OHL played songs against extreme right wing politics as well as left wing politics which was followed by protests in the punk scene. However, they also released anti-fascism songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/OHL">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
