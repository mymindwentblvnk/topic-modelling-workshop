Citizen X is a side-project of The Akashic Mysteries founders Anthony Karnowski and Ron Hubbard. They are based out of Knoxville, TN.

Citizen X is also the pseudonym of WWE wrestler Glenn Jacobs (aka Kane) which he uses for his political podcast, The Tiny Political Show. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Citizen+X">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
