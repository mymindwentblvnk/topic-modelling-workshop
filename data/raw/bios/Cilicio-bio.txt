A 2-piece Christian goregrind band from Sucre, Bolivia, formed in 2006 under the name S6t6n6s Purulento. Later changed name to Cilicio after going through a multitude of name changes over the years - (S6t6n6s Purulento: 2006-2007), (Seixsexsix: 2009-2010), (Cannabis Gore: 2010).

The band dubs their style "Kullugorelectrosplatter".
