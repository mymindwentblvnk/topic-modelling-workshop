Bad Luck is the name of more than one artist:

1) An emo pop punk band from Daytona Beach, FL who now reside in Brooklyn, NY. https://badluckdudesny.bandcamp.com/

2) An indie rock band from Glasgow Scotland.
https://badluck1987.bandcamp.com/

3) An avant garde jazz group from Seattle, WA. Musicians are Neil Welch, tenor saxophone, live electronics
Chris Icasiano, drums 
http://store.badluckband.net/album/three


It may also be an incorrect artist tag for a fictional band from the anime Gravitation, by Maki Murakami. Vocals for the band in the anime are done by Kotani Kinya, except for the song "Bird", which is done by Tomokazu Seki. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bad+Luck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
