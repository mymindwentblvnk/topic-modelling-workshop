Favilla is a raw Black Metal band formed by two members of Skyfire, vocalist Henrik Wenngren and drummer Joakim Jonnson. Wenngren (going by the name Hinreich here) is the composer, playing guitar and synth. They have a downloadable demo on their site http://favilla.hinreich.net/ entitled Spawned From Chaos. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Favilla">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
