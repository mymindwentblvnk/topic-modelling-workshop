Execrable Divinity was formed by Aleq Sheets(Guitars, Vocals, Drum Programming) in March 2012 as a side project. The focus is to make brutal music in the style of Disgorge, Condemned, Defeated Sanity, Deeds Of Flesh, Insidious Decrepancy and many more. "The Consequence Of Existence" E.P. is set to be released late April or early May. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Execrable+Divinity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
