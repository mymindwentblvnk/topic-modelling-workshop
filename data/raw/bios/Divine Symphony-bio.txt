Divine Symphony was formed in Brazil in 2004, and they released a full-length album (Reject Darkness) the same year under the label "Extreme Records"

Current line-up:
Janderson Moreira: Vocals
Kelton Kellyo: Guitar
Carlos Cheman: Guitar
Wilson Amaral: Bass
Alexandre Carvalho: Keyboards
Jonathas Carlos: Drums


Guest:
Isabel Fernandes: Female Vocals
