Grinded Nig is a grindcore band involved with the white supremacist movement. The lyrics deal with white power and violence towards minorities. There is dispute if Grinded Nig's lyrics are actually serious, due to some songs that appear to mock the movement. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grinded+Nig">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
