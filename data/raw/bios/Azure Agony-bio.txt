Azure Agony are an Italian progressive metal band formed in 2006.
The band was born by an idea of the keyboardist Marco Sgubin. In 2006 Gabriele Pala (guitar), Marco Firman (bass) and Carlo Simeoni (drums) joined him to give birth to this project. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Azure+Agony">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
