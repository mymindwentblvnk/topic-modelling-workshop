Craig J. Gordwin (born October, 24, 1994), better known as Craig Xen, is an artist from Houston, Texas. He is widely known for his raw and aggressive delivery in his music. He is well-known for frequently collaborating with the late XXXTENTACION and being part of the artist’s collective, Members Only. Before joining Members Only in 2016, he was originally a member of JGRXXN’s collective in 2015, SCHEMAPOSSE, up until their disbandment. He had even brought the late Lil Peep into SCHEMAPOSSE around the same time he had joined. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CRAIG+XEN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
