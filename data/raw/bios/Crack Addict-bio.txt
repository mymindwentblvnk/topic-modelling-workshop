There are two bands with the name Crack Addict:

1) Japanese Black Metal band, formed in 2002 in Tokushima.

Current line-up:
Takano - vocal
Ryota - guitar
Morimoto - bass
Toda - guitar
Hisashi - drums

Discography:

Demo 2003 
The Funeral Demo 2006 

2) Crack Addict (now known as Shallow Pulse) is a hardcore/powerviolence band from Virginia, formed in 2013. They are known for their use of anime imagery and Japanese characters in their artwork and songs. They have changed their name to Shallow Pulse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crack+Addict">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
