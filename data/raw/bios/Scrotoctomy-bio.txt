Slam death band from Monterrey, Nuevo León, México.
Based out of Nuevo Leon,Mexico, Scrotoctomy was established 2012. Their sick 2013 demo tied down a deal with GHP.
Their demo has been remastered by The Morgue Music Studios in Los Angeles and will be released as the bands debut ep entitled "From Torture To Putrefaction", late spring 2014.
Scrotoctomy main influences include Cerebral Incubation, (old) Ingested and Cranial Osteotomy.
