Dysphorie is a depressive black metal band from France. Their lyrical themes are about suicide, depression and destruction. They released a demo in 2008, titled "Prophétie suicidaire: l'égérie de la damnation".

The word "dysphorie" is French for "dysphoria", a state of feeling unwell or unhappy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dysphorie">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
