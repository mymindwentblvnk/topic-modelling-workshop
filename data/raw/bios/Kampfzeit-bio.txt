Kampfzeit is a NSBM band from Auvergne (France).
Lineup:
Unknown Soldier - Guitars, Vocals
Fallen Soldier - Drums, Guitars, Vocals, Keyboard
Label:
Winter Assault Productions

They released one demo tape called Oath of Loyality in 2004.
