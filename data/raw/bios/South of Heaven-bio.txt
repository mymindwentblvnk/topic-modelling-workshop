There are two bands under the name South of Heaven:

1: Doujin ongaku progressive heavy/speed metal act from Japan, led by Ikuto. The first album, "Touhou Metal Chronicle", was released in June 2008. Several albums were released after that. Official website: http://s-o-h.jp/

2: Dutch band from Arnhem/Nijmegen which also has a connection with Fedchenka. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/South+of+Heaven">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
