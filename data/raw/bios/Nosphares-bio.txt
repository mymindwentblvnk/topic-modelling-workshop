Nosphares is one person band from France, formed in 2002. Will is Nosphares member (Bass/Guitar/Vocals). Nosphares music is like Doom/Death Metal. 

Will is a member of these bands: Nosphares, Funeral Holocaust and Disquiet.

Nosphares have only two recorded albums:
Dark Desires Demo (2003)
