Scholomance was an extreme progressive metal band from Willard, Missouri (USA) formed in 1995. The name "Scholomance" in a folklore means a school for witchcraft that was taught by satan.

Discography:
- 1996 - Promo 1996
- 1997 - 1997 Promo
- 1997 - I am That Which Is (demo)
- 1998 - A Treatise on Love
- 2001 - The Immortality Murder (double CD)
- 2003 - 19967991 (demo compilation)
