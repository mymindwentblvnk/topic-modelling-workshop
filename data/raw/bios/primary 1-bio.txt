Primary 1 (aka Joe Flory), is UK synthpop singer & producer.

His first single, The Blues, featured Nina Persson of The Cardigans.  His Mess Detective album was described, by Pitchfork, as "homemade music with a sophisticated sense of pop melody and arrangement". http://Primary1music.com <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Primary+1">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
