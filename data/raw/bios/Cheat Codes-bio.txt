Cheat Codes are an American electronic music trio based in Los Angeles, California, consisting of singers and producers Trevor Dahl, Kevin Ford, and Matthew Russell. The group achieved international success with their 2016 single "Sex", which samples the chorus from Salt-n-Pepa's 1991 hit "Let's Talk About Sex".
