The Unjust were a speed metal band formed in the mid 1980s in The Bronx, New York City. The band secured a deal in 1987 to release a full-length album - "Hammerhead" has since become a respected underground thrash metal work, with its heavy, evil sound. Lineup:Bruce "Rudy Kane" Ruiz - Vocals, Chris "Ivan" Bernardo - Guitar, Joey "Spliff" Abene - Bass, Joe Joe "Trap" Trapanesso - Drums.
