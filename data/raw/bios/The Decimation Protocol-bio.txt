The Decimation Protocol is a death metal band from Brisbane, Australia playing classic death metal in a modern form, with influences ranging from early death metal to more modern death, black and some other genres.

The Decimation Protocol released its first EP in 2009, entitled, "The Assimilation Directive".

Current band lineup:

Brendan - Guitar 
Ray - Bass
Scott - Drums 
Steve - Vocals
