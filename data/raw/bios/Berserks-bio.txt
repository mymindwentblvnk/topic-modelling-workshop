Berserks is an Italian Heavy Metal Band formed in Udine, Friuli-Venezia Giulia in 1980. Their only official release to this date is the seminal 1982 eponymous album 'Berserks'. They apparently have reformed in the 2000's but have yet to release any official material. Their line-up includes

Giovanni Pignattone - Vocals
Pierluigi Constantini - Guitar
Luciano Toninato - Guitar
Luca Urban - Bass
