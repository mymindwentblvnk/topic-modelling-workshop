Nattaravnur is a Berlin Metal band that was founded in 2006 by Motte, Hacke, Basti and Jen. Two years later, in December 2008, after the band was completed with Gregorius, the flight of the Nattaravnur started. The scream of Nattaravnur is orgasmic melodic death metal. If you want to see or hear our metallic outpour you are welcome to join one of our concerts! If you want to book us, feel free to ask us!
