The Beneath is a six piece metal band hailing from Cincinnati, OH. They have been a staple in the local scene in Cincinnati for the past two years playing with acts such as Protest the Hero, Haste the Day, Between the Buried and Me, Project 86, etc. They released their first record, SO CAME THE DECIMATION in August of 2007, and second one, titled City of Light in 2009. The Beneath blend heavy guitar-driven melodic metal with anthemic harmonized vocals from their two singers
