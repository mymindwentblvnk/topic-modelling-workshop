Naked Shit was a three-piece drone / doom metal project from Edinburgh, Scotland formed in 2006.

Following a series of demos and a split with Scottish doom band Ix, they were signed to Sound Devastation Records. Their debut full length was released in 2008 entitled The Legend of the High Town Crow. They played the final gig during June 2008.
