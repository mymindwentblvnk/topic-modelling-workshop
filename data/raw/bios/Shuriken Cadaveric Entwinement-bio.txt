Shuriken Cadaveric Entwinement is a Brutal death metal band from North Carolina, United States. The band is side project of Jay Barnes and Jordan Varela from Lust of Decay. They formed in 2006 and signed quickly with Sevared Records and released their debut full length album "As the Shroud of Suffering Suffocates the Land". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shuriken+Cadaveric+Entwinement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
