Minneapolis, Minnesota based indie dream pop outfit Bora York is splashing onto the scene after the release of their singles "Close Your Eyes" and "Open Tales". Married couple Chris and Rebekah Bartels are the base of this group, with the help of many friends. Much local acclaim has centered around Bora York, and they look to deliver with their debut album, out March 2013.
