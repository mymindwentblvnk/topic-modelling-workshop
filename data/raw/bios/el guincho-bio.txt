El Guincho is Pablo Díaz-Reixa, a native of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria living in Barcelona, Spain. Also a member of Coconot, Díaz-Reixa rose to prominence with his 2007 album, Alegranza. His musical style relies heavily on the use of sampling and incorporates elements of Afrobeat, dub, Tropicália and Rock. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/El+Guincho">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
