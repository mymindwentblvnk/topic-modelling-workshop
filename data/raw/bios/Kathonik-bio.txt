Kathonik was an American Black Metal band formed 1991 in Houston, Texas.
They started out playing Death Metal and later evolved into A Black Metal band.
The following 10 years the band would release 4 demos and later on dissolve, however the band itself would release the 'Demonic Horde Rising' compilation in 2010.
