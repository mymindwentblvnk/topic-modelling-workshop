Luftwaffe Raid was formed by Warharan as a Black Metal band in 2002. It is about the European Theater of WW2. Luftwaffe Raid is not intended as a political band, you should view it like it was a historical book about the second world war.

The releases so far:
2003 - Wolfsreisszaehne (Demo)
2003 - Empire (Full-length album)
2004 - Paradox (Full-length)
