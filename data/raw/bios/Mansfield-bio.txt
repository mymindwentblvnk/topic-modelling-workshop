The Artist name Mansfield can refer to two artists;

1) Mansfield is the solo project of Masanori Ikeda - 池田正典 , a Japanese DJ who collaborated with, among others, Fantastic Plastic Machine and Cubismo Grafico.

2) Mansfield is James David Morgan, musician, co-founder and designer at the Groundswell Collective, an art and design studio for social change.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mansfield">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
