A Pyrrhic Victory is a 5 piece band from Coeurd A'lene, Idaho that start in early 2007 and have come very far in the few years they've been together. Having recording their highly anticipated full length album "The Preceptor" with Kris Comeaux of (Lorene drive, Elevate: I am and Red Jumpsuit Apparatus) in LA summer 2011 and released October 2011. APV has toured the country and many west coast tours sharing the stage with such act as Attila, Born of Osiris, Motionless in white, After the burial, Blind Witness and many many more. APV consists of frontman Blake Reeser, guitarists Craig taylor and kevin rogers, bass Robert Downing and drummer John Williams.
