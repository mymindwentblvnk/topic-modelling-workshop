Ecthyma is a five-piece deathcore band from Eastern Europe - Rīga, Latvia. Formed in 2009 on ashes of acts Abort of Succubus and Come With Jester.

Current lineup:

Andis Ziemelis - vocals;
Toms Krēsliņš - guitar;
Rihards Adatiņš - guitar (currently also Synodic);
Tomass Beķeris - bass guitar (currently also Dehydrated Goat);
Alberts Mednis - drums.

Former members:

Andis Zvejnieks - bass guitar (currently Sound Machinery).
