Slate Grey are a Metal band from Munich, Germany.
Slate Grey was founded in the summer of 2007. Since SLATE GREY started touring they already played Shows together with DARK TRANQUILLITY, INSOMNIUM and THE SORROW. In 2008 the band released their first EP “Living In Obscurity”, in 2009/2010 their first full length album “Nothing Ever Dies” (Firefield Records/EMG) and in 2014 their new single "Reckoning". Slate Grey is writing songs covering and combining a very wide range of metal music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SLATE+GREY">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
