There is more than one artist named Crows (none to be confused with groups called The Crows):

1) A hardcore punk band from Dublin, Ireland. http://crows.bandcamp.com/

2) London based CROWS produce a spell binding mix of surf rock shimmer and scuzzy garage-fueled rock and roll. Their debut single will be released as a digital download via Howl Club on May 9th 2011. http://www.facebook.com/londonCROWS

3) Crows is a german power metal band formed in 1981, by Ex-members of Wolf Spider, Scanner, Mekong Delta, Sodom and Angel Dust, they recorded a number of demos throughout the 80's and only one album, the dying race in 1991, via Century Media records, the band broke up shortly after. 

4) クロウズ Crows are a Japanese visual kei band.
