1) Tribunal is a experimental post-rock group, founded in 1987 in Petrozavodsk, Russian Respublic of Karelia. Frontman is Michail Kretchet-Potsenkovsky.

2) Tribunal is also a metal band from Montreal, Qc, Canada. They write their lyrics in French and they play a mix of death metal and, depending on the song, folk metal, rock, funk, black metal, etc. The band member often switch instruments.  They have released one demo in 2010, called "Veux-tu un bonbon?". Their first full lenght "ZING?" came out in 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tribunal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
