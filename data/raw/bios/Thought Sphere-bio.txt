THOUGHT SPHERE was a Progressive Metal band from Dusseldorf, Germany. The band formed in 1994 around ex-LOST CENTURY members Andreas LOHSE (vocals) and Jens SCHAFER (guitar). Adrian WEISS joined on guitars and Andreas LOSCHE on drums. The bass position was filled by various members such as Arkos ZIBERE, David LUDWIG and Armin ALIC.

After 2 demos in 1997, the band signed to B. Mind Records in 1998 and released three full length albums for the label, Eden's Shore in 1998, Vague Horizons in 2000 and EOS in 2001.
