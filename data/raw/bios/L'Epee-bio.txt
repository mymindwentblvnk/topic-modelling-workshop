L'Épée is Emmanuelle Seigner and Anton Newcombe on art and hedonism. She's a French film star. He's rock's mercurial madman. Together they're l'Épée, as seductive as Serge Gainsbourg and as druggily alluring as the Velvet Underground. Anton Newcombe and Emmanuelle Seigner, the duo behind L'ÉpéeL’Epee features the combined creative talent of Anton Newcombe (The Brian Jonestown Massacre), Emmanuelle Seigner and Lionel and Marie Liminana (The Limiñanas)

Debut album 'Diabolique' is out the 6th September 2019.
