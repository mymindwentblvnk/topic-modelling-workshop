Dakrua is an italian gothic metal band, formed in 1995.

Discography:
    * 1999 — Inner Wastelands
    * 2002 — Shifting Realities

Members:
    * Eva Rondinelli - Vocals
    * William Quattrone - Vocals, bass
    * Alessandro Buono - Guitars
    * Davide Sangiovanni - Drums
