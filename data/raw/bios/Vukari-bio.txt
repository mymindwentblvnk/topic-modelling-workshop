Vukari is an atmospheric black metal band from Chicago, Illinois. The lyrical themes are based off around conceptual stories. It began as a studio project by Marek Cimochowicz but shortly after became a full band <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vukari">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
