There are two different Lo Pans here:

"Lo-Pan" (this page) is a stoner rock band from Columbus, Ohio.  
http://www.myspace.com/lopandemic

"Lo Pan" is a grindcore band from Knoxville, Tennessee. 
https://soundcloud.com/lopangrindcore.

The "Lo-Pan" band is the tag lo-pan, and the "Lo Pan" band is lo+pan.  last.fm is broken: visiting either tag goes to the "Lo-Pan" biography.
