Two bands operate under this name:

1) Power metal band from Tokyo, Japan, formed in 2005. In June 2007, they released their first studio album, titled 'Falling from the Sky', on Dark Universe Records. Before and after that they released several singles. 'Melodic Crunch', an EP, was released in September 2010.
