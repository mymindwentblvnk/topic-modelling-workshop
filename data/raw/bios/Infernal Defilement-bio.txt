Infernal Defilement is a brutal death metal band from Snohomish County, Washington, consisting of David Christensen (guitar/bass/vocals) and Landon Martin (drums/vocals). After initially recording a 4-song instrumental practice demo in the fall of 2010, Infernal Defilement completed writing and went into the studio for their first release, the "Ceaseless Brutality" EP, in the summer of 2011. The EP was recorded, produced, and mixed by Landon Martin in his home studio. It was released in November of 2011.
