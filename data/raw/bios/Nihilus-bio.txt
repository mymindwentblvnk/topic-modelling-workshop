Nihilus is a Technical-Death Metal band formed around October-2010 by Siddharth and Manu.
They started out with a couple of own compositions which they used to audition for the other band members.
Jason D’Souza joined them on bass after putting down some killer lines for their original, ‘Without Gray Matter’.
Navneeth Shiv then teamed up for the vocalists mantel in early 2011.

They draw influences from bands such as Origin, Necrophagist, Decapitated, Nile to name a few.

It's present line-up consists of :

Navneeth Shiv - Vocals
Siddharth Manoharan - Drums, Backing Vocals
Jason D’Souza - Bass
Manu Manjunath - Guitars
