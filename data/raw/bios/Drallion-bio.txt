Drallion are a six piece metal band from Bournemouth, UK. In August 2022, they released the official music video for their first single "Bloodshot" from their upcoming debut album "New Machine".

Vocals: James 'Boof' Watts
Lead Guitars: Mark Potter (@markpotterguitar)
Rhythm Guitars: Max Beardmore
Keyboards: Matt Smith (@Keyboardistmatt)
Bass: Sam 'Beardy' Beardmore
Drums: Rowan Endacott
