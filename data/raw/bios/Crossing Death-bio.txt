Early 90's death metal from Tampico, Tamulipas. Their debut and only album to date, One Life Is Not Enough, was released in 1992 as a cassette via Avanzada Metálica and re-released in 15 May 2014 on CD via Gates of Hell Productions. The line-up that recorded such album was Roberto Alanis (bass, also part of ArtWar, Empire's Darkness and Sarcoma), Juan Rodríguez (drums, former member of Empire's Darkness), Gustavo Herrera (guitars) and Antonio Rodríguez (vocals and guitars). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crossing+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
