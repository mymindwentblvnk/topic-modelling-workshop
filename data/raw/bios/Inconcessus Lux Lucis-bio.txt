Inconcessus Lux Lucis is a black metal band from Manchester, England.  They were formed by Malphas in 2005 as Whorethorn, later changing their name in 2009. That same year a demo entitled "The Awakening Of Him" was released.  The demo was a rough, incomplete version of the unreleased album; "I - The Awakening Of He With The Strange Shadow".  2012 saw the release of the band's first EP, "Severed From Sephiroth" via Nomos Dei Productions.
