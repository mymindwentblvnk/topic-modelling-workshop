GROOVE GANGSTERS was a dance-act created by the Austrian producers Klaus Biedermann and Stefan Berndorfer. In 1997 they released the song "Funky Beats" which became a worldwide clubhit.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Groove+Gangsters">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
