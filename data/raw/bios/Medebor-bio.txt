Medebor is a Polish doom metal band from Gdańsk. It was founded in November 1997 by Andrzej Bucikiewicz (vocals), and Jarosław Pieczonka (guitars). In 1998 Michał Kijewski (bass), Karol Jankowski (drums) and Maciej Boroś (guitars) joined the band. In 1999 they released their first demo - 'Stars Of Oblivion', in 2000 the second one, called 'Last Dream'. Later in 2000 their drummer left the band and was replaced by Arkadiusz Lewandowski. In 2001 Medebor recorded it's first full-length album - Phantasma, however it wasn't released until 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Medebor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
