DEMONICA is an American/Danish thrash metal band, formed by the guitarist Hank Shermann and vocalist Klaus Hyr, and joined by guitarist Craig Locicero,  drummer Mark Hernandez and bassist Marc Grabowski, with origins from the Bay Area, the Rocky Mountains and Copenhagen, Denmark. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demonica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
