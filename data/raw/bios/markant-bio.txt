markant tells the story of carsten endrass of rosenheim, germany. his sound expresses a deep love for music, a devotion to romanticism and hints of abstraction. most of his work has the captivating intensity of a summernight storm, with unexpected collapsing structures of beauty. since the late nineties markant has produced close to 30 limited 12 inches and a few full albums.  he has also released material under his proper name and the alias muta.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Markant">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
