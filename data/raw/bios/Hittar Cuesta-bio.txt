Hittar Cuesta was a guitarist born in the city of Loja - Ecuador, since his childhood years feels much fondness for the guitar, a hobby that would gradually grow to arise as be his way of life, his profession and for this purpose - to not being allowed entry into the Conservatory of music - because he was a self-taught musician thanks to his innate sense of hearing and versatile fingering that favors further their knowledge. At that time formed his first band with influences from Led Zeppelin, Dire Straits and Eric Clapton.

La historia musical de Hittar Cuesta se inició cuando tenía apenas seis años, pues sus primeros recitales los dio en su escuela. En su adolescencia, sus gustos se nutrieron con bandas como Led Zeppelin, Deep Purple, los solos envolventes de David Gilmour en Pink Floyd pero, sobre todo, le inspiró el guitarrista estadounidense Joe Satriani y la sonoridad Steve Vai. En video comienza con su hijo Randy y la interpretación Another brick in the Wall de la agrupación Pink Floyd.
