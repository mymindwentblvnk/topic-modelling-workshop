Takashi is a heavy metal band from New York City.  They formed in 1982.  They released Kamikaze Killers in 1983.  They disbanded in 1988.  They reunited in 2011.  They are currently signed to the label known as Mongol Horde. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Takashi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
