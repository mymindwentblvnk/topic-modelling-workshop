There is more than one artist wit this name.

1) Sculptor is a band from Serbia. Band's newest promo CD 2008 called the "The Verdict" consists of 4 re-constructed songs from the old demo with different arrangement and the lyrics. Its recorded in Chainroom Studio, Senta (SER). Songs are influenced by Eastern sound and Metal in general. At the moment Sculptor is sculpting conceptual album "Forming..." (working title) and they are searching for right label to release it.

2) Sculptor is a Russian death-doom metal band, formed in 2007.

3) Sculptor was the original pseudonym of a Swedish demoscene tracker later known as Sunsquid. He was active from 1997 until 2003 and composed his works in FastTracker 2. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/sculptor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
