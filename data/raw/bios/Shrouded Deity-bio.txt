Black/Death Metal from Cherry Hill, New Jersey, formed in 1994. Released the demo Omens in 1995. The band's line-up consisted of Jay Lipitz (bass, guitars and vocals, also played in Insatanity and Sapremia, Leo Storniolo (bass and guitars) and Anthony Ipri (drums and vocals, also played in Mortal Decay). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shrouded+Deity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
