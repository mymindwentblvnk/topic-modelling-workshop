A one-man death metal / doom metal project from Arkansas.  It was formed by  Bobby Maggard.   He formed it in 2008.  He released a 4 song demo entitled Bundled Bowels in 2008.  He released a compilation with the same name in 2008.  It contains all 4 songs from the demo and songs that didn't make it to the demo.   He released a split with Vaginal Flytrap in 2011.  As of 2018, the project is considered active. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bundled+Bowels">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
