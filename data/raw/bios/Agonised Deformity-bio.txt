Agonised Deformity from Glasgow, Scotland was formed in Early 2010 and since then the band developed their own brand of Death Metal inspired by a cross of early 90's American Death Metal and a selected few unique European Death Metal acts.


Line up :
Neil Robertson -- Drums
Ross Howie -- Lead Vocals (Ex-Cerebral Bore)
Stuart Higgins -- Guitar
Derek Wright -- Bass & Vocals
Andrew Higgins -- Guitar (Ex-Scordatura)
