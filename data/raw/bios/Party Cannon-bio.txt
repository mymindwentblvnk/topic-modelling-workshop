Party Cannon is a brutal death metal band from Dunfermline, Scotland.  They formed in 2010.  They consider themselves "party slam."  They have an unconventional band logo, which is reminiscent to the Toys "R" Us logo.  They have released 3 singles, 2 demos, 2 EP's, one full-length album and a live album.  They are signed to Gore House Productions.  Their lyrical themes include corporate control, war operation and the decay of Earth. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Party+Cannon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
