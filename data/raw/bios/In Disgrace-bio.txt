In Disgrace was a Melodic Death Metal band with some Black, Thrash and Doom Metal elements.

Formed in 1996 in Nordhorn (Germany) under the name Contempt, in 2000 the band decided to change the name to In Disgrace due to important line-up changes. They split up in 2010. 

Last known line-up:

Acker - Vocals & Guitars
Ingo - Guitars
Raina - Bass
Christian - Keyboards
