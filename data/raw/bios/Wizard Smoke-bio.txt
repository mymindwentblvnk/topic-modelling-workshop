Wizard Smoke is a doom metal band from Atlanta, GA that formed in 2008. The band offers their music up for free download. They released an album in 2009, entitled "Live Rock In Hell", for by a a second album called "The Speed of Smoke", both of which can be downloaded for free. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wizard+Smoke">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
