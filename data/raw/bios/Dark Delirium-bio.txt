Dark Delirium is a Norwegian metal band from Rogaland region. Band members are Simeon Ekse on Rythm Guitar, Bass Guitar and Drums. Halvar Moen on Lead Guitar, and David Jakob Ekse on Vocals and Lyrics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Delirium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
