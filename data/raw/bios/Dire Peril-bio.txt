Dire Peril is the singular vision of guitarist Jason Ashcraft who combined his love for science-fiction with that of power metal. Joined by bassist Jeremiah Bear, guitarist Jacob Grant, drummer Ben Jackson and powerhouse vocalist Norman Skinner (ex - Imagika), this Northern California band is a must for lovers of Iced Earth & Iron Maiden. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dire+Peril">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
