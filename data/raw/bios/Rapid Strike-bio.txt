Rapid Strike is a metal band, formed in Split, Croatia, in 2007 under the name Azazel. They have recorded two demos, in 2008 and 2009. 
Their music can be described as thrash / heavy metal, influenced by the bands such as Metallica, Iron Maiden, Black Sabbath, Judas Priest, Ozzy Osbourne, Megadeth, Kreator etc.
After numerous shows and line-up changes, they changed their name to Rapid Strike and recorded their first album God Take Me to Hell, co-produced with Dean "Clea" from  Osmi Putnik. The album was released in 2011 through the Serbian label One Records.
After the release of the album, line-up was completely changed, leaving the band's leader, guitarist and songwriter Hrvoje Madiraca as the only original member.

Current line-up is:

Ivana Elez - Vocals
Hrvoje Madiraca - Guitars
Nikola Čapalija - Bass
Duje Pelaić - Drums
