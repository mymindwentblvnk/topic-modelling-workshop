Bacchia Neraida was a Pagan black metal band from Greece formed in 1995 and split up in 2001. The band features Marios Papaioannou of Wolfnacht. Lyrics themes deal with Greek mythology and Lore.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bacchia+Neraida">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
