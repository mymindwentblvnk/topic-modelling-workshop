There are 3 Warwolfs:

1) German Heavy Metal band featuring 4 members of Wolfen and Florian Abegg. Their debut album Necropolis released on July 29th, 2022. Self-described as "in the style of Iron Maiden in the mideighties."

2) Fast hardcore from Austin TX with members of employer, employee and sea of thousand.  
They released a 7" on level plane records in 2007
