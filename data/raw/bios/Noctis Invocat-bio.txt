Noctis Invocat is an Atmospheric Doom/Black Metal band from Guatemala formed in 1994.

Current line-up : 
Angel Protervus - Vocals, Bass
Nery Monroy - Guitar 
Nery Rodas - Drums, Percussion 
Willy Monroy - Keyboards 

Former members
Erwin Arenas - Keyboards
Edwin Calderon - Guitar 

+Discography+ 
Eternal Dreams of Fire Demo, 1994  
Depressiva Vox Clamantis Full-length, 1998
