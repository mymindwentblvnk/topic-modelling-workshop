Plastic Earth is a Japanese melodic death metal band. It consists of two people: Yuki (guitars, drums, keyboards) and Yuta (vocals). The sound of the band is similar to that of Gothenburg metal scene. Their only album, S.E.A.M.-01, was released in 2004 via Japanese record label World Chaos Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Plastic+Earth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
