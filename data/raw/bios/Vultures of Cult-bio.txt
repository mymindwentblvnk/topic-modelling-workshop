Founded in 2017, Vultures of Cult are a 4-piece doom, stoner, sludge from Burlington, Vermont. They have 5 releases to date, their debut titled "Cold Hum" in 2010, "Fathoms" in 2012, a 5-song EP "SVRF" in 2013, "Bitter Gloom on a Golden Dawn" in 2015, and a 20 minuted track titled "Pastoral" in 2016. All are available at https://vulturesofcult.bandcamp.com  For More information, visit, https://www.facebook.com/VulturesOfCult/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vultures+of+Cult">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
