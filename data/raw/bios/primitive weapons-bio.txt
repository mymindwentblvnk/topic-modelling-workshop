Primitive Weapons is a post-hardcore band formed in Brooklyn, NY in 2010.  The lineup consists of Arthur Shepherd (guitar, vocals), David Castillo (vocals), Chris Enriquez (drums), and Eric Odness (bass).  The band released their debut album The Shadow Gallery on March 13, 2012 through Prosthetic Records. Follow-up The Future Of Death was released April 15, 2016 on Party Smasher Inc. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Primitive+Weapons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
