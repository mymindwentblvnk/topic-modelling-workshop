Based in Silver Spring, Maryland, USA (1988-1992). 

Progressive Metal, Cyber Metal, Experimental Metal, Fusion.

Members included: Danny McDill (bass & guitar), Jamie Guggino (drums, percussion, clarinet, Moog), Chris Schlesiger (lead vocals, bass, synthesizer), Joe Campi (lead guitar), Danny Kenyon (lead guitar), Josh Kirk (guitar), Dave Moskowitz (guitar).

Related bands: Indestroy, Transilience, LDK, Fearsome Sealife, Exoxen, Wead.
