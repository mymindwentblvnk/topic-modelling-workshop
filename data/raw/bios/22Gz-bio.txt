22Gz is a drill rapper from Flatbush, Brooklyn. He signed to Kodak Black’s label Sniper Gang in 2018, which he is still signed to to this day. He is credited to be a pioneer of the Brooklyn Drill Scene. He released his most successful song ever, “Suburban, Pt. 2”, in 2019, which has amassed over 400,000 scrobbles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/22Gz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
