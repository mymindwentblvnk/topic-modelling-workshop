Malicious Disfigurement is a Brighton based death metal band from the UK.  Their style is a mix of the old-school and the modern death metal genre. Such bands influenced their style like carcass, death, devourment, lividity and the black dahlia murder. In october, 2008 the band recorded two songs for their first 7" vinyl release in the London based "Escape Route Productions" studio. The 7" came out through Tadpole Records, and the songs are "gut excavation" and "smashed skull".  In may, 2009 the band returned to ERP studio and recorded another two songs for the upcoming 7" release on Tadpole Records.
Malicious Disfigurement shared the stage with such bands like Driller Killer, Behold...
