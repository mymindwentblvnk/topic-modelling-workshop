Lammas was a British Celtic-influenced jazz band comprising Don Paterson (guitar), Mark Fletcher (drums), and Tim Garland (reeds), that in the 90s and 00s, featured singer Christine Tobin.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/lammas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
