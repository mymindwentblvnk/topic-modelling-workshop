Dark Heresy were a technical death metal band from London, England who formed in 1989.

They released three demos and one album before splitting up, and are currently inactive.

The last known line-up was:

Arnold - Keyboards/guitar
Hans Stiles - Bass
Kola Krauze - Vocals
