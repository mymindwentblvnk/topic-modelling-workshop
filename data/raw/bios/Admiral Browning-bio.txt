Admiral Browning are an instrumental band from Middletown, Maryland.  They play a self-described "stoner-prog instru-metal" that combines Stoner Metal, Progressive and Psychedelic influences. They were formed in 2002 and have been actively gigging since 2005, playing over a hundred shows mostly in the MD/DC/VA area and up and down the East Coast.  They have put out two self-produced albums: Maiden Voyage (2005) and Dead Pets (2007) and have an EP coming out in 2009 called Magic Elixir.


Admiral Browning is:

Matt Legrow - Guitar
Ron McGinnis - Bass
Tim Otis - Drums
