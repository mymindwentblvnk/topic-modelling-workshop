Swet Shop Boys is a collaboration between Queens, USA rapper Himanshu Suri (better known by his stage name Heems) and London, UK actor/rapper Riz Ahmed (known as Riz MC). They released their collaborative EP recorded in the US ,'Swet Shop', on the 24th of August 2014. 'Cashmere' their 2016 debut album, was conceived with London based producer Redinho and it features 't5' , 'tiger hologram' and 'zayn malik'.
