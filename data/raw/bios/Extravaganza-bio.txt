Experimental Post-black metal band from Lithuania started in 2010. In 2013 released single 'Tai Buvo Mūsų Tyla' (eng. 'It Was The Silence Of Ours') in 2016 released full length album "Priepuoliai" (eng. 'Seizures') and in 2021 released EP "A.N.I.N."
