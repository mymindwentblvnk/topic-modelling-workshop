The band was formed as Module 69 on 18 October 2003 by Marc Dehning on rhythm guitar and vocals, and Dave Dippenaar on drums. By November of the same year Thomasz Sobolewksi joined on lead guitar, and during January 2004 Mario Schutte joined on bass, completing the first lineup.

The band rebranded to PSordid in early 2005. Album released:Bekhor - September 8th, 2016
