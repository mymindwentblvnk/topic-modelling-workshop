Lophiile AKA the producer / turntablist Tyler "Ty" Acord, who was previously a member of the metalcore band 'ISSUES'. He now works on solo music efforts as well as helping ISSUES produce in the studio. He is the brother of Sky Acord, the bassist of ISSUES. Most of his musical endeavour exist on the music streaming service Soundcloud.
