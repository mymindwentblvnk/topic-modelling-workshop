Goathemy is a Melodic Black Metal band from Finland. Although formed in 1993 as a one-man band, they didn't release and official album until 2003, the EP of covers 'Under the Sign of Black Cover'. Recently they had relased their first full-length album 'Frostland'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Goathemy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
