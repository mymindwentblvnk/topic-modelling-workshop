Nhaavah is special studio project of Barbarud Hrom  (ex-Maniac Butcher) & Vlad Blasphemer (ex-Maniac Butcher). Nhaavah plays extremely fast and brutal Raw War Black Metal.

Discography:
Kings Of Czech Black Metal
Detremination - Detestation - Devastation (Split)
