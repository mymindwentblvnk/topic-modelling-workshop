Egyptian Hip Hop is Manchester, UK band formed in late 2008 by guitarist/vocalist Alex Hewett and Louis Stevenson-Miller (guitar). Recruiting their friends Nick Delap (bass) and Alex Pierce (drums). A debut album 'Good Don't Sleep' released on R&S records in October 31, 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Egyptian+Hip+Hop">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
