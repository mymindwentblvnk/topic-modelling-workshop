Valera Zaytsev, known professionally as "DVRST", exploded onto the Phonk scene with his 2021 viral song "Close Eyes". His distinguishable production style has quickly amassed him hundreds of millions of streams across all platforms, placing him among the top Phonk producers in the world. Fans were immediately drawn to the artist's unique ability to blend different styles of music, all while utilizing Phonk production methods, and often, atmospheric textures. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DVRST">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
