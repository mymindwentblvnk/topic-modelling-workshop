S.A. Sanctuary was a metal band from Texas.  In 12 years, the band only completed 1 CD, titled, Abandon In Place. Over the past decade, classic S.A. Sanctuary shows have been released in DVD and are available on Amazon.

They were:
Orlando Velasquez - creator of the name sa sanctuary
Angel Fuentes - vocals/lead guitar
John St. John - bass
Mike Aresco - drums

Their official site is sa-sanctuary.com
