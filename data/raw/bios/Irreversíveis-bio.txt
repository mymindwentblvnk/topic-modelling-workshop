Irreversíveis is a Brazilian alternative indie elektro rockband from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
The bandmembers are: Leo Benitez (guitar, violin, vocals) and Carolina Monte (keyboards, loops, samplers, programming) All songs are in the Portugese language

Their first album "H.G. Wells" was released in 2006. The second album Irreversíveis - Irreversíveis saw its release in 2008.

Their  first full album is available on jamendo, freely (as in free beer), through the creative common licence
