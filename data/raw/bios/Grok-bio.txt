There are 2 bands with the name Grok.

1. Grok (Japan)
Born in Sapporo Japan, 1974. He announces his works worldwide as a member of aerostitch, and produces next generation of electronic music with full of originality.
His originality forms new possibility for a Live performance in laptop-style. He won the Grand-Prix award in the Roland Groove Competition in 2001. and produced the techno pattern of MC-909 groove gear. also play with Ben Sims, Surgeon, DJ Sufflemaster, Co-Fusion, and John Kelly.

2. Grok (UK)
Grok are an improvisational collective based in London, UK.
All Grok songs are improvised and spontaneous, performed or recorded without any prior planning or rehearsal. Grok's approach has a vital, dynamic, unstructured feel. In Grok a chance hook can form the basis for a repeated refrain, or a meandering atmospheric can suddenly burst into a three minute pop song. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grok">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
