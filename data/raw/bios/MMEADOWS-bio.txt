mmeadows is Kristin Slipp and Cole Kamen-Green. The band’s sound is informed by their deep musical backgrounds: Kristin is a current member of indie royalty Dirty Projectors, while Cole has worked directly with Beyoncé on two albums, 'Beyoncé' and ‘Four.' Drawing from their disparate influences, mmeadows sees two people connect in what Paste Magazine calls a sonically “singular” way. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MMEADOWS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
