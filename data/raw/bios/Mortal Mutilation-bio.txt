DEATH-METAL band started in 1990 and disbanded in 1994. They were 4 musicians from Barcelona, Catalonia (Spain) and recorded a debut demo tape in "Drowned Productions" (1992) and 1 vinyl ep with the label "Katie King Records". Before disbanded they recorded 3 tracks more for the compilation "Death-Metal Party". After MORTAL MUTILATION, their ex-members started 2 importants bands in the death-metal scene: ERODED and CORRUPT SOUL.
