Polkadot Stingray (ポルカドットスティングレイ) is a 4-piece indie rock band from Fukuoka.
Members:
Shizuku (雫) - Vocals / Guitar
Harushi Ejima (エジマハルシ) - Guitar
Yuuki Uemura (ウエムラユウキ) - Bass
