There are 2 groups called Downtown Boys:

1. Downtown Boys are an English/Spanish bilingual political punk band from Providence, RI. They're known for their use of two saxophones and their high-energy live shows led by lead vocalist Victoria Ruiz. 

They've performed with nationally regarded acts such as Javelin, Speedy Ortiz,  Chain and the Gang, Filastine, What Cheer? Brigade, Math the Band, Detroit Party Marching Band, and others.
