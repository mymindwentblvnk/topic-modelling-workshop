Tity Boi is the former stage name of Tauheed Epps, now known as 2 Chainz. Until September 2011, Epps released numerous mixtapes under this name, before changing it to be more "family-friendly", following accusations of sexism regarding the Tity Boi moniker. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tity+Boi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
