Naked Giants is an American garage rock trio formed in Seattle, Washington in 2014.

The group consists of vocalist and guitarist Grant Mullen, bassist and vocalist Gianni Aiello, and percussionist Henry LaVallee. In recent years, Naked Giants have become notorious for their high energy live performances. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Naked+Giants">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
