Finnish band Face Of Chaos was formed in the end of 2008.
In the beginning FOC was known as Thrasher but  the name changed soon after they started writing their own songs and playing more gigs.
Musical genre would be thrash metal with influences from death metal and groove metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Face+of+Chaos">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
