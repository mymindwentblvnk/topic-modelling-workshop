Mad Hatter's Den is a Finnish hard rock/melodic metal band. They combine the elements of 70's and 80's hard rock and metal. They draw their influence from Iron Maiden, Deep Purple, Rainbow, Accept and Gary Moore to name few. On their gigs they play own material and covers too.
