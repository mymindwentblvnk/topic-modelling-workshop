Night Canopy magically formed upon recording their debut album Of Honey and Country in March 2006. Night Canopy is recording artist Amy Blaschke and Nick DeWitt (Pretty Girls Make Graves, Dutch Dub). Violin on the album was played by Seth Warren (Red Stars Theory, Rocky Votolato, Suffering and the Hideous Thieves). The live show is organic and often changing and it's line-up features Jenny Jimenez (The Catch) and Seth Warren. The name Night Canopy comes from the book “Slaughterhouse 5” by Kurt Vonnegut
