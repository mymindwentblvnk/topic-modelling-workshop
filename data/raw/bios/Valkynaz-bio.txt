Personally, I despise the term "influence". As one learned in the ways ov the pen would see, "influence" means to control even in its most primordial roots ov the word. Inspiration is a much nicer word to use than "influence".

My inspiration stems from many things, but here is a list ov whats most prevalent in this vestige ov my artistic hatred for man.

Depression
Alcohol
Fine Art
Video games
Hatred
Frozen landscapes
Forgotten forests

There are more elements that influence my voice and my guitar, but I'll mention those later.
