Embrace the Dawn is a Progressive Death metal band with plenty of technical, thrash, symphonic, and black metal elements, formed by guitarist/songwriter Ben Tinker in 2015.

After releasing a promo EP the same year, Ben set about establishing a new line-up of likeminded musicians from around the globe. Denis Landry on bass, Kevin Talley, drums, and Roger Isaksen completing the line-up on vocals.

Members :
Ben Tinker - Guitar, programming 
Roger Isaksen - Vocals 
Denis Landry - Bass
