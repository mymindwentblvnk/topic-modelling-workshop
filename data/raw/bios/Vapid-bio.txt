Vapid are a punk band from Vancouver, British Columbia. They have released a 7” ‘Do The Earthquake’ on Nominal Records and and LP ‘Practically Dead’ on Nominal Records/Deranged Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vapid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
