Blathudah were a Sydney based Thrash/Speed Metal/Hardcore band with roots in Lismore in Northern New South Wales, Australia.

They released 2 demo tapes (their debut, "Blathudah", and the follow up to their EP, "Garbage Daze Re-Revivisected" - Warhead Records) and a CD ep ("Spawnography" - Warhead Records), and a little known Promo cassette before ceasing to be an active band.

They were active between 1992 and 1996.
