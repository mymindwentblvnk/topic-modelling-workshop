Other bands called Scorned

1) Scorned is an Austrian Dark Metal Band from Oberwart (Burgenland). They created the band in 2006 and played their last gig on the 29th of October 2011 but reformed in 2012 without their keyboard player. The 5 musicians were on tour with Six Feet Under and other bands from Hungary, Germany, Finnland and Luxenburg. Their music was influenced by bands like Graveworm,Children Of Bodom, Ensiferum, Dimmu Borgir, Wintersun, Borknagar, Black Sabbath or Behemoth.
