Marjorie Fair is an American band formed by musician Evan Slamka, and based in Los Angeles, California. It currently consists of Evan Slamka (vocals, guitar), Dain Luscombe (keyboard), Scott Lord (bass guitar), and Mike Delisa (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marjorie+Fair">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
