Matthew Sean Leon is a Canadian recording artist and record producer from Ajax, Ontario. Sean Leon has released two major projects and is the founder of the artistic collective IXXI. In 2013, he released his debut mixtape, "Ninelevenne, the Tragedy" which was followed up in 2014 by "Narcissus, the Drowning of Ego". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Leon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
