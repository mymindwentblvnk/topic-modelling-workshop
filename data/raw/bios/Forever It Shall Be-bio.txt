Forever It Shall Be is a Metalcore band from Braunschweig, Germany. Formed in late 2005, they mix up oldschool melodic metal with crushing hardcore/mosh sequences. Their Debut Album "Reluming The Embers" was released in May 2007 on Maintain Records. The new record “Sonic Death Squad” was recorded in the Rape Of Harmonies Studios and will be released worldwide in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forever+It+Shall+Be">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
