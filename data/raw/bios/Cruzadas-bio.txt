Cruzadas (portuguese for Crusades) is a Power Metal band from Brazil. They hasn't released any full albuns, just two demos and an EP called “Guardiões do Apocalipse” (in English, “Guardians of the Apocalypse”). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cruzadas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
