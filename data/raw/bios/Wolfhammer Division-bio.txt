Wolfhammer Division is NSBM band formed in Canada. Year of formation is 2001. Current line-up are four musicians:

Kommander Northwolf - Vocals
Hrungnir - Guitars
Panzerfaust - Drums
Herr Gen.I. Brock - Bass/Guitars

Lyrical themes are about warfare and national socialism, and they are under label of Northern Horde.  See also Fomorii.

Current discography is:
