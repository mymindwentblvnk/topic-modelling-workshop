Hollowed-Out Planetoid is the debut release from the Sylosis frontman under the banner of the Josh Middleton Project.  Completely self-funded and self-released the album features Craig Reynolds on drumming duties. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Josh+Middleton+Project">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
