This band released 3 demos and 1 split before splitting up. They were signed to Metal Blade Records, but were dropped when the label was bought by Capital Records.

Their YouTubes can be seen at http://www.youtube.com/profile?user=VDRP#g/u

Kev Smith - Guitar (Neos, Sludge Confrontations, Mexican Power Authority)
Randy Long - Bass (Jerk Ward) (R.I.P. 1990)
