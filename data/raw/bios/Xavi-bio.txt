Joshua Xavier Gutierrez, known professionally as Xavi, is a Mexican-American singer-songwriter from Arizona. His lyrical sensitivity and heartfelt compositions form a musical bridge between the borders of Mexico and the United States. Xavi truly wears his young heart on his sleeve to showcase a style that can only be described as Tumbado Romantico. On the heels of releasing his debut single with Interscope Records, Xavi is an exciting newcomer revolutionizing Regional Mexican music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xavi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
