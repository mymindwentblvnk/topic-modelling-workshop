A now split-up Swedish black metal band, in the likes of Zyklon-B. The members are also known from bands like Dawn, and This Ending (then A Canorous Quintet, later The Plague).

Niden div. 187 released two full length albums, with a rumour about a third one, already recorded. The band however split up before it ever being released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Niden+Div.+187">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
