There are probably few artists named Grotesk:

1.) A german electronic one-man project "from the Bavarian Forest" of Manfred "Mane" Unverdorben, called "grotesk".

2. ) Band fronted by Muck Groh & Wolfgang Teske, typically jazz-rock twist on the Aera sound one would expect, with the involvement of reknowned jazzer Ulrich P. Lask adding a new slant on the style.

3.) Grotesk is also the name of a rapper from Namsos, Norway.
In 2007 did another Grotesk arise in Norway, a Black/Thrash Metalband from Hamar, Norway, playing metal the old schoolstyle.

4.) Grotesk is death metal band from Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina, formed in 1994.
