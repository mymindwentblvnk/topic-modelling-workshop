Zero 7 is a London, UK downtempo duo, founded in , by Henry Binns & Sam Hardaker. 

They began as studio engineers at Mickie Most's RAK recording studio in London, engineering music for British groups like the Pet Shop Boys, Young Disciples, and Robert Plant. In 1997 they were asked by a college friend, Nigel Godrich (who was busy producing 'OK Computer'), to remix Radiohead's "Climbing Up the Walls". They called the mix "the Zero 7 Mix" which stuck. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zero+7">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
