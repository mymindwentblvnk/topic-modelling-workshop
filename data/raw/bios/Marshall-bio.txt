There is more than one artist under this name: 

1. Marshall is a dance project produced by the SAIFAM Group and best known for a cover of The Black Eyed Peas' My Style.

2. Marshall also refers to a performance by the character of the same name from the TV show How I Met Your Mother.

3. Marshall is also the solo project of Ryan Marshall from the Canadian indie band Walk Off the Earth. He released his debut single I Could Be The One in 2018. 

4.  marshall is the side project of experimental American artist k4yd3nce, primarily regarding their dariacore-inspired series, "goombacore." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Marshall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
