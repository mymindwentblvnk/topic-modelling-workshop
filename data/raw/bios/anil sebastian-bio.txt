Anil Sebastian is a project by Anil Kamalagharant, founder and director of London Contemporary Voices choir (LCV). His debut album Mesonoxian was produced by Anil, his brother Ingmar Kamalagharant and Cherif Hashizume and released on Relative State Records in Autumn 2016. It was preceded by the tracks ' Closer ' , ' Human ' and  ' Living '.
