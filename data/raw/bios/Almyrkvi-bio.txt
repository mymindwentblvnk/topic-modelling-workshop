Almyrkvi is an Icelandic black metal band, the side-project of Garðar S. Jónsson, also of Sinmara. Their debut EP "Pupil of the Searing Maelstrom" was released in 2016, and their debut full-length album "Umbra" was released in November 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Almyrkvi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
