Water Borders are Amitai Heller and Loric Sih from San Francisco, California. The duo  creates music that combines sinister vibes and sonic drone explorations with a haunting haul giving a genuinely brooding gothic effect to their sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Water+Borders">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
