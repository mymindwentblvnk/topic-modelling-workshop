Sinners Bleed is a Berlin based Technical Death Metal band, founded in 1997. Their self-released debut "From Womb To Tomb" was critically acclaimed by the international press in 2003, paving the way to big festivals like Inferno and Party.San in 2004. In 2005 they reached a spot for Wacken through the Metal Battle. After that they started to work on material for the new album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sinners+Bleed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
