Albanian power metal band active from 1993 to 1995. They released one album, "The Orders Executors" in 1993.

Last known line-up
Bledar Seiko - Guitar
Artan Xheladini - Guitar
Alban Laro - Keyboards
Mitat Laro - Bass
Roland Fusha - Drums

Former/past member(s)
Elton Deda - Vocals (Aneroid)  www.eltondeda.com

Additional notes
Elton Deda's musical preferences were in contrast to the other members of Thunder Way as he was into a more rock/pop oriented style. Thus in 1995 he left the band due to creative differences.

They won the first position in the Albanian Rock festival in 1995.
