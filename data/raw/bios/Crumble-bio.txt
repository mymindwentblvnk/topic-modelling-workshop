Crumble is a poprock band from the Netherlands. Their origin lies in the year 2003. Since that year they have written a diversity of numbers with their own sound, varying from funky grooves to raw rock. The four band members have a very wide musical background and taste, wich makes crumble a combination of different styles.

Crumble is:
Marije van Rijn - Vocals
Diederik van de Bunt - Guitar
Rick Fleer - Bass
Daan van Kasteren - Drums
