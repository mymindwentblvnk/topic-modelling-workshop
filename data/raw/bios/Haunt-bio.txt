There are two artists named Haunt.

1. Haunt is a heavy metal band from Fresno California. They were originally the solo project of Trevor William Church from Beastmaker

2. Haunt is a project from the ex-frontman of The Ware River Club, Matthew Hebert.

His recent TV/Film placements include: Anderson's Cross (Illumination Pictures, Inc) and, "How I Met Your Mother" (CBS, Monday Nights).

Haunt's, As Blue As Your Dying Eyes, was recorded at Spirithouse Studios by Danny Bernini (Sonya Kitchell, Reed Fohel, Erin McKeown,) and was released on APRIL 3rd, 2007.
