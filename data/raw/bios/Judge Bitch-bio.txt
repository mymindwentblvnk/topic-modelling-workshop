Judge Bitch (real name Adam Burke) is a Canadian electro musician. His work includes composing original music for the 2011 film "Hobo with a Shotgun", and his recently released album "Viper" which is available on Aphasia Recods' Bandcamp site: http://aphasiarecords.bandcamp.com/album/viper
