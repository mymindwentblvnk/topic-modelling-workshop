Formed in early 2005 by 4 highschool friends in Calgary, Alberta, Norrath is a black/death metal band who have been described as both melodic yet punishing and brutal.

Members consist of

Pat Kiselicka (Vocals/Guitars)
Mark Iocca (Guitars)
Ben Freudenthaler (Bass Guitars)
Griffin Kissack (Drums)
