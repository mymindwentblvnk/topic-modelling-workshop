There are two bands that share the name Beyond Tomorrow.

1. The former pop rock band, Beyond Tomorrow, lead by Patrick Hoydar (Hodar) and Steve Gagnier, was born around 2005 and disbanded somewhere in the 2010-2012 timeframe.

2. Beyond Tomorrow is presently (2014) the melodic, prog rock band founded by Scott Kahn, co-founder of the award-winning prog rock band, Days Before Tomorrow. Beyond Tomorrow cites influences ranging from Marillion, Rush, and Porcupine Tree to Coldplay, U2, and The Police. Members include bassist Pete Blomstrom (of the prog band, Generator, as well as the famous Rush tribute band, Hemispheres), and vocalist Jason Rosenfeld formerly sang for the prog rock band, Nepenthe.
