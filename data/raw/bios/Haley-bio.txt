There are multiple artists known as Haley.

[1] Haley is Haley Gibby, a vocalist known for her work with house producer Kaskade on songs such as Move for Me and Step One Two, as well as the group Summer of Space.


[2] Haley Eleanor Keegan is a singer who plays acoustic covers of various artists. Currently living in Portland, Haley is in the midst of writing original songs and frequently puts out new music. 


[3] Haley are a Philadelphia based punk band formed by Steve Haley, formerly of Trashlight Vision.
