Multiple Crones:

1. German black metal project from Secrets of the Moon vocalist/guitarist.

2. Ambient/drone project from Jeff Caxide, formerly of post-metal band Isis.

3. Punkrock trio, playing singalong, riffalong punkrock tracks.

4. Post-rock project of Anthony Vi.
