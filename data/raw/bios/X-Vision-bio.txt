X-Vision is a groove metal band formed in 1998 in France, based on a huge use of palm muted guitar sounds with aphonical voices and constant double-pedal beating. The lyrics especially deals with the corporate society, the shape of the world to come. Their main influences come from Pantera, Machine Head and Meshuggah.

Line up:
Pierre Pauly - vocals
Arnaud Ferveur - guitar
Nicolas - guitar
Arnaud Marion - bass
Christphe Edricht - drums

Discography:
1999 Radikal
2000 Unreal
2001 Confused
2004 Time of the new slavery
2007 So Close, So Far
