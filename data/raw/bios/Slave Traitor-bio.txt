Making a name for themselves in Seattle's music scene, Slave Traitor are playing an aggressive brand of metal.

Featuring the triple vocals of bassist Marc Burno, guitarist Eric Kempton, and guitarist Jake Willanger along with drummer Steve Hass, Slave Traitor is ready to provide the US and the rest of the world of the shape of things to come from this band of turn-coats and cut-throats.

Slave Traitor formed in early 2005 and released their 1st EP, Black Narcissus, on June 6th 2006. Sharing the stage with bands like High on Fire, Down, Kylesa, and Rwake has showcased Slave Traitor as an intense live act pushing themselves onto unsuspecting audiences.
