A.R.E. Weapons is an electro/punk band from New York City.  They were formed in 1999 by Matthew McAuley, Brain McPeck and Ryan Noel.  In 2002 Paul Sevigny join the band as keyboard player/manager.  In 2004 bassist Ryan Noel passed away.  When drummer Erik Rapin was added in 2004 they achieved their current line-up as a 4-piece. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/A.R.E.+Weapons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
