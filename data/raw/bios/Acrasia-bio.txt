Acrasia are a post rock/post-hardcore/ambient band from the Eastern Suburbs of Melbourne, Australia.
With an already growing list of support slots such as:
Of Mice & Men (USA)
House vs. Hurricane
360
Hands Like Houses
Dream On, Dreamer
Trial Kennedy
And with a live show to leave you wanting more, Acrasia are a band not to be missed!
Check out the 'Music/Shows' tab on their Facebook for their most recent tracks or find them on youtube.
Please tell your friends about them!




Members:
Nico Douglas - Vocals
Dale Freiberg - Bass/Backing Vocals
Nick Ross - Guitar/Programming
Jesse Wilkinson - Guitar
Aaron Beale - Drums
