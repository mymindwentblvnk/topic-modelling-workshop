a doom/death band from Russia that was formed in an unknown year but it's known that they had split up in 2000. Released four demos - "Repent" in 1995, "Alpenglow" in 1997, "At the Pillars of Melcart" as a promo in 1998 and as a full demo in 2000, the latter three with Orphic Mountain productions.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Noah%27s+Flood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
