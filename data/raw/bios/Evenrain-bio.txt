Evenrain started in April 2004 as the reincarnation of the band Ashes Encased when a new singer joined the team. The bandmembers are not new in the scene, for they have already played in many metal and hardcore bands. In their short career they have successfully presented their music in Slovenia, Italy and Austria supporing the gigs of Hatebreed, Pungent Stench, Disastrous Murmur, Rotting Christ, Carnal Forge, Destruction, Dissection, Primordial... Their style is mostly fast and melodic death metal in the vein of Dimension Zero/At The Gates/Darkest Hour. The lyrics are a kind of self-reflection and are more expressive, which is a peculiar contrast to their direct and aggressive musical approach. In December 2004, they entered Studio Sonus and recorded their first demo entitled "Obscured In A World Of Light". After many agonies new songs were born, as well as a new line-up...
