The Loudest Silence is symphonic metal band from Sarajevo, active since year 2010. by Taida and Denijal. Active members are :

Taida Nazraić - Vocals

Denijal Ćatović - Keyboards

Mirza Ćorić - Guitar

Džemal Bijedić - Bass guitar
