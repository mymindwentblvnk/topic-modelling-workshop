It’s special when artists are able to translate their experiences and paint stories that we can all relate to. Mannywellz’s tales of love, heartbreak, and personal discovery pair with his soulful harmonies and African influences to create a unique, genre-melding sound. The Nigerian-born, Maryland-based multi instrumentalist has his eyes set on purpose that is bigger than music, aiming to impact lives. A culmination of his dynamic live performances, songwriting, and production, Mannywellz has positioned himself as one of the most promising rising stars. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mannywellz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
