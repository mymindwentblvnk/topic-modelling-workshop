1- Danish eurodance act consisting of Anja Øjken and produced by Mikkel deMib.
They recorded the album "Mindblowing" and several singles like "Destination Nowhere" and "Show Me (Your Magic Side)" 

2- Young Slovak band Out of Mind was founded in July 2006 by Rudo Šatka, Pavol Chlebana, Martin Šmatlák and Marek Breznický. Since the beginning they have played instrumental and very technical music on the edge between rock and metal. In October 2008 Natália Paulovičová has joined the band as a vocalist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Out+Of+Mind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
