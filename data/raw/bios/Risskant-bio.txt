Risskant a.k.a. "De Woordvoerder"is a Dutch Hip-Hop Songwriting Artist from Dordrecht, he is known for his energetic performances.

In 2006 he released his debut album called "T'is mooi geweest" featuring tracks like "Avond in Dort" and "Knikken".
