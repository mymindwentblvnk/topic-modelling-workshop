Versengold is a medieval/folk band from Bremen, Germany. The band uses medieval instruments like pipes and lutes to create their sound, and are often to be found on Live Action Role Playing events in Germany, as they all actively take part in these performances. Their Webpage is  located at www.versengold.de/. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Versengold">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
