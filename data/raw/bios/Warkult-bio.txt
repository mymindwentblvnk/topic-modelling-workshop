There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Warkult is a raw black metal band from Hungary. So far they have released 4 tapes that are known, including two rehearsals, a demo and a promo containing various material of them.

2) Warkult is a blackened death metal band from France. They have only one self-released album, titled "Kult of Disorder".
