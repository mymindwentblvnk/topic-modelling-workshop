Vorkuta is a hungarian black metal band based in Zalaegerszeg, Hungary. They formed in 2002 and are signed under Paragon Records. Their lyrical themes are darkness, evil, war and Satan. The name Vorkuta comes from a remote region in eastern Russia where many gulag camps were located. 

Lineup: 
Márton Pelényi "Blizzard" - vocals
Bálint Berki "Inmar" - guitar
Márton Berki "Hellspike" - guitar and bass
Khrul - drums

They released four demos, eight splits and one full length album titled Into the Chasms of Lunacy in 2007.

They are formerly known as Fjord.
