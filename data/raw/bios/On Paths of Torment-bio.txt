On Paths of Torment is melodic death metal band with some hardcore influence from Torrington, CT. In 2006, "The Arsonist Plague" was released by Creator-Destructor Records which is run by Light This City drummer Ben Murray. Their influences range from bands like Crowbar to The Acacia Strain. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/On+Paths+Of+Torment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
