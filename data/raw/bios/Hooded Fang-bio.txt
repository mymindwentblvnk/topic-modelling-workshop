In the winter of 2008, six friends, roommates, and lovers decided to start playing music together. Inspired by Canadian literary genius Mordecai Richler – and undeterred by their lack of formal training or the fact that “Barney’s Version” is a shitty name for a band – they settled on "Hooded Fang". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hooded+Fang">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
