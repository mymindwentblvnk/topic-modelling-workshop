Karera Musication is a project collective by most of the members of Japan's Boredoms. Everyone from that band, except ringleader Yamantaka Eye, contributes to the soundtrack for director Takashi Miike's film Ichi The Killer (殺し屋１ or Koroshiya Ichi). Guitarist Seiichi Yamamoto is the driving force here, with much help from drummer Yoshimi P-We. All the elements are there: future-thrill electronics, heavy-handed percussion interludes, big, dumb guitar riffing and surprisingly smooth trumpet lines... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Karera+Musication">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
