Witch Tomb is a American black metal band from Boston, MA.  It was started in 2002 by Ninkaszi (guitars, vocals), Strep Cunt (bass, vocals) and Drath (drums).  C. Dead (keys, effects, samples) joined the band in 2007.  Their sound is somewhat reminiscent of such bands as Blasphemy, Profanatica, Von, Beherit and Black Witchery, blending black metal with primitive death metal.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Witch+Tomb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
