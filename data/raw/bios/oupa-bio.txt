Solo project from once Yuck frontman Daniel Blumberg. Was a collection of piano led “stripped down” songs billed as a side project to Yuck under the name Yu(c)k. Oupa releases his debut LP 'Forget' on August 15 on cassette via his own label Boiled Egg. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oupa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
