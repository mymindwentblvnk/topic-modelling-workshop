Metal act from Seoul, South Korea, formed in '89. Probably best known for having guitar wizard Kim Do Kyoon (Baekdoosan and more) as their frontman. Asiana released their first and final full-length studio album "Out On The Street" in 1990. They are currently disbanded. Later on vocalist Im Jae-Beom had a very successful solo pop/rock career in South Korea. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asiana">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
