There are two bands named Black Winter...

 1) Black Winter was formed back in 2000 in Larissa, Greece and up to 2010 they delivered Infernal Black Metal in their own personal way… Black Winter had also played a good number of shows with bands like Marduk, Septic Flesh, Nightfall, Naer Mataron, Order of the Ebon Hand, Zemial and much more. They headlined many black metal festivals, released 2 demo CD-R’s, A live tape,a split demo tape all released by small distros, a split Cd EP w/ Moontower released by Black Tower Productions and an 7” Vinyl split w/ Nethescerial released by Obscure Abhorrence selling thousands of copies of their overall releases worldwide… They disbanded before they finish recording their album "Threshold of a new Apocalypse". 

Related Acts:
Aenaon, A band whose driving force is the former Black Winter's singer Astrous
Majeste Simphonia, A band created from former members of Black Winter
Naer Mataron, Talos who plays guitar for their shows has been vocalist for Black Winter

2) Black Winter are a Gothic Rock band from Blythe, USA. With influences from Christian Death, Clan of Xymox, Cocteau twins, Chameleons UK, The Mission, Echo and the Bunnymen, The Cult, Fields of the Nephilim.
