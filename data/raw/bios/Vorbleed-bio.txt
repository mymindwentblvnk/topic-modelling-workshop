Vorbleed is a Metal band from Semarang, Indonesia. Formed in 2010, consisting of Piki (Vocals), Ekky (Bass), Arif (Guitar), Alief (Guitar), and Deka (Drums). They want to create heavy music as a whole idea of the band. The band is noted for its inspired melodies and the use of many different riffs, feels and techniques into their music. The band went to record their first demo after one year of dedicated song writing start from 2009. They worked hard on new material and already have four song with different colour for each of their song.
