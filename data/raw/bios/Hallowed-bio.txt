There are multiple artists with the same name:

1)  An incelcore singer-songwriter and member of MSG Support Group from New York. Hallowed first appeared on MSG Volume 3.

2) An Italian heavy metal band from Bergamo, active from 1979 to 1987. Hallowed disbanded after releasing their first and only LP in 1986.

3) An Irish power metal band from Cork, active from 1999 to the present. They released their first album Forgotten People in 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hallowed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
