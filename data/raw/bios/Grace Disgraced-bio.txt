Grace Disgraced is a death metal band formed in Moscow, Russian Federation, in 2005. They released "Natural Death" demo in 2006 and  "Materialized Expectation" EP in 2009. Grace Disgraced style can be described as old school death metal that incorporates a lot of modern 'technical' elements as well as some infusions of other metal genres - black, thrash and even heavy metal. Current line up is: Polina Berezko (vocals), Alexandr Klaptzov (guitars), Dmitriy Belousov (bass), Dmitriy Arkhipov (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grace+Disgraced">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
