Died is a Brooklyn based band formed in 2016, with music that spans the genres of math-rock, post-hardcore, noise-rock, indie rock, and post-rock. Died EP, released December 8th, 2017, is their first EP, following the release of the single "Conk" in May of 2017. Died features guitarists Caio Brentar and Ian White, bassist Alex Geisel, and drummer Matthew Leichenger, with vocals contributed by multiple band members. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Died">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
