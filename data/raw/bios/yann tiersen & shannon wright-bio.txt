"Yann Tiersen & Shannon Wright" is a collaboration album between Breton musician Yann Tiersen and Shannon Wright, a female musician, singer and songwriter from USA. All the music and texts on this album were written by Wright and played by Tiersen and Wright, which is unusual for Yann Tiersen works. This album was recorded in just about twenty days. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yann+Tiersen+&+Shannon+Wright">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
