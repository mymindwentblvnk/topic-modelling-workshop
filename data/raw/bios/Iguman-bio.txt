Iguman, was founded in 2004 and it is the first release of a Black Metal band from Montenegro.
This is satanic underground band and black metal band with only two members : Ikonograf (vocals ) and Zvonar ( all instruments ).
They have released one demo so far called Rogati Kopitar containing 4 tracks.
The most chaotic underground black metal and fanatical satanic worship from the Docleatic first black metal band Total Satanic underground insanity and with conceptual system deeply into Montenegrinian Orthodox Church, Strict Ascentism and total black esyoxia.
This is limited edition about 111 handnumbered copies, recorded in studio Pogano Korito during the year 2006. by Adzove Vrbe Records.
