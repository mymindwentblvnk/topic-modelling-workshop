Italian melodic power metal band who has composed two albums: 'Time For Expiation' (2004) and the 1st ‘The Blood Of The Dragon’ (2001).
Mastered in Sweden at Mastering Room (In Flames, Soilwork, Dimmu Borgir, Hammerfall), Dragonhammer 2nd album ‘Time For Expiation’ mixes epic, progressive and power elements, forms operatic style vocals, multi part harmonies, classically inspired keys, expertly done, and heavy guitars riffs.
‘Time Of Expiation’ wants to deal with more introspective themes, avoiding ‘sword & pride’ clichés. A very particular ‘tormented soul’ (the Devil) is playing the game of life and it brings together past, present and future… <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dragonhammer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
