There are two bands named Human Plague.
One of them is a Brazilian Death Metal band from Santa Maria, having released two albums:
2013 - Marching Through the Fields of Desolation;
2019 - Harvest of Hate.

 The other one is now a defunct, melodic crust band from Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico,
They specialize in creating heavy music consisting primarily of lengthy songs that focus on repetition and evolution of structure and texture. the include a great range of musical influences including traditional Screamo, Post-Rock and Sludge Metal to create a unique atmospheric sound.

Discography:
El Aullido Del Viento; Los Oídos Sordos Del Hombre (Mar 2010)
Y La Muerte Vendrá Con El Tiempo (May 2011)
7'' Split with Apocalipsis (Coming soon)
