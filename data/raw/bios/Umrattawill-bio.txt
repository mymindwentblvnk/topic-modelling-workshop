Umrattawill is a Death Metal band from Chile. Formed in 2002. They released two EPs:

2007 - Defeat the Enemy  
2008 - Mental Disorder 

Current line-up:

Juan Martinez - guitar
Sayruz - Guitar
Tom Evil Hill - Bass


It was recently revealed that Hatred and Jose Strife have left the band and formed a new band called Thy Serpent's Cult.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/UMRATTAWILL">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
