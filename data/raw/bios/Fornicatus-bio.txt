Depressive black metal from Finland, formed in 2006. Line-up consists of Morbid, Deimos and Panzerfaust.

Discography:
2007 - Promo '07 (Demo)
2008 - The Suicide Chamber (Demo)
2009 - Children of Failure (Split)
2009 - Omnium Animalis Terminus (Album)
2010 - Down Again (EP)

Note #1: Deimos and Morbid also participate in the depressive black band Dead, together with the members from All the Cold (Rus) and Black Hate (Mex).

Note #2: Morbid from Fornicatus is not the Morbid from Happy Days and such.
