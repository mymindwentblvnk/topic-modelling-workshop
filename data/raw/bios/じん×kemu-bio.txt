Jin (じん), also known as Shizen no Teki-P (自然の敵P), is a producer whose works are mainly rock. He mainly uses IA and Miku. kemu is a producer, composer and the leader of a doujin group "KEMU VOXX".

Jin is best known for the Kagerou Project series, which got several novel and manga adaptations, as well as an anime adaption named Mekakucity Actors. Jin also composed the opening and ending theme of the above mentioned anime. His notable songs include "Kagerou Daze", "Children Record", "Lost Time Memory", "Night Tales Deceive", and "Outer Science".

kemu has also composed the music for several visual novels, games and anime projects. For his non-VOCALOID work he uses the name Kemu Tsukamoto (塚本けむ).

After the release of kemu's 8th song "Haiboku no Shounen" in May 2013, He mysteriously ceased all activities on his Twitter and piapro account leaving many to question whether he was still active. kemu released a short piano arrange of "Haiboku no Shounen" on Nov. 2015 through ke-sanβ's channel and resumed VOCALOID related work May 2017 with the release of “Haikei Doppelganger”.

kemu is also currently a member of the band "PENGUIN RESEARCH" signed under Sony Music Japan. He goes by the name "Shota Horie" and is the bassist as well as the composer for the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/%E3%81%98%E3%82%93%C3%97kemu">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
