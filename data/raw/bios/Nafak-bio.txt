Nafak are a Melodic Death Metal band from Argentina who formed in 2003. Their debut album 'Melodías del Armagedón' was released in 2005, and a split with Dhak entitled 'Metal Sampler 1 Dhak - Nafak' was released in 2006.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nafak">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
