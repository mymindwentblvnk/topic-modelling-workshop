Gengahr are a four-piece indie group originally from London signed to Transgressive Records. Their debut AA-side single, 'Powder/Bathed In Light' was released October 2014. The band are Felix Bushe (vocals/guitar), John Victor (guitar), Hugh Schulte (bass) and Danny Ward (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gengahr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
