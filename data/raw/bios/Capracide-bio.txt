Capracide started in early summer of '09 after tossing a few ideas around with bits and pieces of written music.With the combined influences of stupid heavy riffs and technicality from bands like Cannibal Corpse, Job for a Cowboy and Necrophagist, Capracide strives for a zen like balance of brutality, technicality, and epic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Capracide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
