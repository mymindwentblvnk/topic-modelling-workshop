KromleK was a Folk Metal band from Bavaria, Germany, founded in 2004. It consists of Nhévann (lead guitar), Alex (bass guitar), Aoife (violin), Forað (rhythm guitar), Hrísdólgr (keyboard) and Galt (drums). They have released three albums this far, "Kveldridhur" (2005), "Strange Rumours... Distant Tremors" (2007) and ''Finis Terræ'' (2011). In march 2012 KromleK has decided to dissolve the band forever.

The music of KromleK is greatly inspired by bands like Finntroll and Ensiferum and other Scandinavian  Folk/Viking Metal Bands. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kromlek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
