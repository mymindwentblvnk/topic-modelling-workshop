AssNynE is a local metal band from the Billings, Mt area. Formed in early 2003 by guitarist and songwriter Dustin Beuning, with David Fredrickson on drums. Over the next few years the lineup had constantly changed with the addition of several different drummers, bassists, and vocalists. As of the time of writing AssNynE consists of David F. on vocals, Ben Meyers on drums, Spock on bass, with new addition Trevor & Dustin on lead guitar.
