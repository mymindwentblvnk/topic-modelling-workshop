Jana McCall is an American singer-songwriter. She played bass guitar in the short lived all-girl band Dickless.[1] After Dickless she worked briefly with Mark Pickerel (formerly of Screaming Trees)[1] and has since released two solo albums; the eponymous Jana McCall (1998) and Slumber (2002). She has also released songs on three compilations albums Up In Orbit! (1997), Up Next (1998), and US: Up Records and Slabco (2000).
