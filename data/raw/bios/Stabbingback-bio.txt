Stabbingback is Metalcore/Acoustic band which was formed in USA in Seattle around 2005. As they are dual-genre band they albums are released in tandem (one metal version, one acoustic version). The bands current line-up is: Gage Sifuentes - Guitar & Vocals, Mitch - Lead Guitar, Jesse - Bass, John - Drums. So far the band has released 2 full-length albums, the first one was in year 2008 named Absolution and the second one was released shortly after with name Redeemer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stabbingback">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
