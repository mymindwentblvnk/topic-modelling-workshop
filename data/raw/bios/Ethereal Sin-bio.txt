Formed in Japan, Ethereal Sin is extremely melodic black metal. They consider themselves "elegiac black metal" due to the extreme melodic nature of their music. Ethereal Sin released new album "...The Abyss will also gaze into thee " in March 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ethereal+Sin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
