There are three bands who use the name Aim For The Head:

1. Four morons from Sheffield creating rad music for your ears. Check them out: http://www.myspace.com/aimfortheheadmusic

2. Leicestershire hardcore band. Now defunct. Four of the five members went on to form Brain Dead.

3. A death/grind band from Calgary, Canada that were active from 2006-2007. Featured members of Iskra, After School Knife Fight, Nusquam Esse. Recorded a single 4 song EP as a four piece: Kevin Smart (Guitar), Scott Browne (Drums), Matt Neudorf (Vocals), Josh McLeod (Bass).
