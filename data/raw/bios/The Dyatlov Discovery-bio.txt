A death metal project by guitarist, vocalist & songwriter Neil Melville. 
Created and established in late 2014, the production of the first EP, A Grave Discovery, begun in the early winter months of 2015. A year later, the project is finally complete, with 6 tracks to blast through your speakers in a death metal frenzy.
During the Spring months of 2016, writing was finished on the debut full-length, currently untitled, set for release in the late summer of 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Dyatlov+Discovery">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
