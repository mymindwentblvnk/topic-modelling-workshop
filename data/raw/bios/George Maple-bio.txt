George Maple is a singer/songwriter from Australia. Her seductive, yearning vocals and instrospective lyrics blend smoothly with downtempo R&B-influenced grooves and light dance beats. Maple first became know for her guest appearance on Flume's self-titled debut album in 2012.

Soon afterward, she sang on tracks by Deetron and Snakehips, and her single "Fixed" was remixed by Maribou State.

In 2014, Future Classic released Vacant Space, Maple's debut EP, which included songs produced by Flume, Kwes., and Slime.

Maple then guested on 2015 tracks by Slime, Goldroom, and What So Not, in addition to a solo single on Future Classic titles "Where You End and I Begin".
