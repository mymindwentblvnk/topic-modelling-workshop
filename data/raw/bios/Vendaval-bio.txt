Vendaval is a Spanish epic power metal band that was created in November 2002 by ex-Nörthwind members Txema Trinidad (vocals), Miguel Fernández (bass) and Fernando Argüelles (drums) with Luis Fernández (guitars) as main song writer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vendaval">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
