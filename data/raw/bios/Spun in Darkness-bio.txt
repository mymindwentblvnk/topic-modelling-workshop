Spun In Darkness Is an old school death metal band from Las Vegas Nevada. The band was started by ex "Goatlord" drummer Jeff Nardone and guitarist Tom Giacalone in early 2005. The band finished recording it's debut album "Birthright" in early 2007, with now forrmer bassist Nick Otvos. Since then the band has added former "Doom Snake Cult" bassist Paul Brady, and a second guitarist Rene Hanpft. Spun In Darkness bleeds old school and is trying to keep the music pure.
