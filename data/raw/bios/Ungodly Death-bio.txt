Death Metal from Malaysia. Formed in 1988 and released their only demo titled "Lost Nations" in 1992 and it's still their only release. Demo was reissued on CD in 2013 by Afterlife Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ungodly+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
