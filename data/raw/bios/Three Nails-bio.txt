"OUR MESSAGE IS IN OUR MUSIC"

A Christian group covering a wide range of musical styles, Three Nails has something for everyone. The group is based in Hanover, PA and has performed concerts within a 500 mile radius. Three Nails Eponymous debut album (released September, 2009) has had a phenomenal impact with internet views in the hundreds of thousands, and although it's still climbing the charts, the bands next album TRUE NORTH is set to be released early to mid spring of 2010.

Influences:God is our inspiration. Our musical influences include: Pink Floyd, Third Day, Casting Crowns, Chris Tomlin, U2, Led Zepplin, Bad Company, Fee... why not throw in a lil Neil Diamond & Jimmy Buffet for good measure.
