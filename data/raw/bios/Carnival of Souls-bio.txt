CARNIVAL OF SOULS is band inspired by both the dewy lushness of Britain's countryside and the grimy splendor of its industrial heartlands in equal measure. They create an urgent yet spacey rock sound from guitar, synthesizer, bass and drums; the lyrics are impressionistic with singalong choruses. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carnival+of+Souls">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
