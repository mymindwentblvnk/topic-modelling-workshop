Popular artist of the 1950s with two top-ten hits and numerous other chart songs. She continued recording into the early 1970s, and was much in demand as a celebrity panelist on game and panel programs (notably  "The Gong Show"). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jaye+P.+Morgan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
