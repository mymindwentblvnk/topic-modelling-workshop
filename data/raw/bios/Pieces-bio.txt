There are three artists with the name Pieces.

1. An experimental/avant-garde pop/indie band from London, UK (http://piecesounds.bandcamp.com/)

2. Collaboration between virtuoso guitarist Buckethead and Primus drummer 'Brain'.

3. A three-piece rock band based in Boston (https://piecesboston.bandcamp.com/)

4. UK Reggae band connected with Vivian Jones.
