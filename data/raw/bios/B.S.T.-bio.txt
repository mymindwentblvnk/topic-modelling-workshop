1. B.S.T. (Blut Schweiß Tränen = Blood Sweat Tears) is a Doom Metal band from Hamburg, Germany, formed in 1994. Their debut album Die Illusion saw the light of day in 2013 after they had eventually found their signature sound  somewhere between Apostle of Solitude and Crowbar.
Early releases: Hamburg City Doom (EP, 2009), vierpluszwei (Demo, 2007).
On April 1st, 2017, the new album Unter Deck was released by Voodoo Chamber Records.
