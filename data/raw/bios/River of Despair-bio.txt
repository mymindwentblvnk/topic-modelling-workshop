Formed in 2008 by the bassist and founding member and guitarist of Skogs Hallen, River of Despair was created to fill the void where music of a different vein was needed. The bombastic attitude of Skogs Hallen, although good, was not satisfying enough. River of Despair was created with bands like Agalloch and Ulver in mind, creating a sound both experimental and black and folk metal influenced <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/River+Of+Despair">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
