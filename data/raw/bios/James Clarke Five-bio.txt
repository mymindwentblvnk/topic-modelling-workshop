James Clarke Five are Liverpool-born songwriter James Hughes-Clarke, and vocalist Gayle Haddock.

James was previously a founder-member of Liverpool duo Exhibit B, whose anthology cd, 'Playing Dead' was released on Favourite Records (Japan) in 2007.  

JC5's many influences include the Beatles, Beach Boys, XTC, Air, Stereolab, Syd Barrett, Tom Jobim, Astrud Gilberto, and Swing Out Sister.

Their debut album, Good Enough for Me and Henry McGee was released in 2004, and included the debut single Get Out of Bed.

Their new album Fly My Pretties Fly is released on 23rd August 2008.
