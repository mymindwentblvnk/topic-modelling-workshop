Domini Nocti is a Black Metal/Ambient project  from Colombia formed in 2008 by Natt-Djevel. His first work of long duration is called "Silentium Aeternus" and was produced by Advoco Sacrilegus Productions from Colombia. 

Its subject is in the profane and the ancestral, having as central axis the knowledge and the transcendence of the spirit as entity of evil. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Domini+Nocti">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
