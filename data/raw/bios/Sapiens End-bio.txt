Sapiens End is a Brutal Death Metal band from the Netherlands, that was formed in 2003. Their current line-up includes Thomas (Vocals), Roger (Guitar) and Tony (Drums). They signed a Record Deal with Murder Records and are currently working on material to release a full length album at the end of 2010.
