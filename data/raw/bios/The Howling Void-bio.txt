The Howling Void is a one man symphonic/funeral doom metal band formed in San Antonio, Texas in 2007. Music can be compared to bands along the lines of Left in Torment, Dreams after Death, Shape of Despair, Anlipnes, Profetus, Ennui, and Ixion.
