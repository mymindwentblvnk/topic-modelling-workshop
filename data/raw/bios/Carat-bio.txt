Carat (カラット) is a 4-member Japanese girl group. They made their debut in 2012 with the single "Koi Indirect" (恋Indirect), and their major debut single "Heart Break" was released in 2015. They are affiliated with Union Entertainment. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
