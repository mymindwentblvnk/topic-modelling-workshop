Savage Thrust were a speed metal / thrash metal outfit from New York City.  They formed in 1984, then disbanded at an unknown point in time.  Then they reunited in 1985 and disbanded again in 1992.  They reunited again in 2001 and then had 2 reunion shows in 2001 at the defunct venue L'Amours in Brooklyn. They disbanded once again in 2001.  They released 4 demos, a single and one full-length album.  Their last known line-up was  Michael Smith (vocals),  Pat Donofrio (guitar),  Rob Antilla (guitars, vocals) and Mike Puch (bass).  You can find out more about them at their official website: http://savagethrust.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Savage+Thrust">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
