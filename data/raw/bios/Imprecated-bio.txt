Imprecated is a brutal death metal band from Murmansk, Russia.  They consist of Max (bass) and Vlad (drums).  Not too much is known about this band.  It is unknown who the guitarist(s) and vocalist are.  It is possible that Vlad provides guitars, since he is a guitarist in Corpseflesh and Neurogenic.  Max may possibly be the vocalist or Vlad may even be the vocalist.  They are signed to the label Ungodly Ruins Productions.  Imprecated released one demo entitled Portal in 2015.
