twocolors is Pierro and Emil, a German DJ/producer-duo who formed the group in 2015.  Both Pierro and Emil were born and raised in Berlin.  Their major-label debut,"Lovefool" (a cover/rework of the the song by "The Cardigans", surpassed 210 million Spotify streams upon it's release.
