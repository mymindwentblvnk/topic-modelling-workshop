Sentient Horror is a death metal band from Stockholm, New Jersey.  They formed in 2016.  They consist of Matt Moliti (vocals, guitars), Jon Lopez (guitars) and Tyler Butkovsky (bass).  In 2019, their drummer Evan Daniele left the band.  Other past members include Ian Jordan (bass) and Ryan Cardoza (drums).  Corpse Ripper (bass) is a past member who only performed with the band live.  Sentient Horror is currently signed to Redefining Darkness Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sentient+Horror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
