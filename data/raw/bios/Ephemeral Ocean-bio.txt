Ephemeral Ocean is Doom/Melodic Death Metal band from Moscow, Russia. They were formed in 2013.
Debut EP, called 'Honour in the Mask' was released on the 5th of April, 2014.
Now they are preparing to release their first LP album.

Members:
Alexey Kostovitskiy – Guitars/Synths
Dmitriy Stempkovskiy – Vocals
Roman Vedeneev – Bass
Efim Burak – Drums/Percussion
Anton Kurochkin – Guitars
