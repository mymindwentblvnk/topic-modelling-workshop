Skeletal Remains formed in California in 2011, originally under the name "Anthropophagy", before renaming to "Skeletal Remains", a name derived from a line in Demolition Hammer's "Human Dissection". The band have released four full-length studio albums to date. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skeletal+Remains">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
