Haunted Shores is a currently re-forming band based out of Washington DC and New York City, consisting of Mark Holcomb (guitars/bass), with studio-help from Misha Mansoor(guitar/bass/programming). A full-length album is in the works to be released in mid-to-late 2011.
Their previous 2 EPs, "Following Ivy" and "Maelstrom" were released under an almost-entirely different lineup. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Haunted+Shores">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
