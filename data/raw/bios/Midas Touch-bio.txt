There are several band projects named Midas Touch:

__1__ Extreme Speed/Thrash Metal band from Sweden.


__2__ A funk/soul/pop band based in London, UK 

Originally from the South Coast of England, Midas Touch are now based in London and are now performing at venues all over the city. Having played in venues such as the Monto Water Rats, The Comedy and The Bowery, Midas Touch manage to impress and capture audiences with the stunning and powerful vocals and the tight, stylistic playing of the band behind it. Consisting of 4 musicians who have influences ranging from Stevie Wonder to Jamiroquai and Maroon 5 to Nina Simone, they manage to create their own unique sound which is a fusion of Funk, Soul and Pop. 2011 sees promising things for the group so are definitly worth catching early and see how their music will keep you asking for more.
