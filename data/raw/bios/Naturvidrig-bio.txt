Naturvidrig is  a black metal band originating from the Swedish countryside of provincial Värmland.  It consists of members Thomsen & Ataraktika.

Their debut ep, Sönderfall, was self released digitally in early 2016 and on cassette by  Ancient Future in June, 2016.

Naturvidrig is currently exploring their craft further for a possible future full-length.
