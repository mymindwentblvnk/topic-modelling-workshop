2-Defier was a finnish thrash/speed metal group formed in 1989. Their first and last self-titled ep-album "Overture of Annihilation" was released in 1990. their music has clear influences of late 80's thrash/speed metal bands like Stone and Testament. The band ended their pretty short career in mid or early 90's.
