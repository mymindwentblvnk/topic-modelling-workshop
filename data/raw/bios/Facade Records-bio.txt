Facade Records is a supergroup, consisting of rapper Domo Genesis and production duo Mike & Keys. The supergroup was formed in March 2020, almost two years after the release of Domo Genesis’ Facade Records EP in 2018. The group released their first two singles “Nate Said” and “Summer 09” respectively in April and May. And in July and August, they released two EPs: Just in Case and Just in Case 2. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Facade+Records">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
