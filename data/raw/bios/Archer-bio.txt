There are several music projects named Archer.

1) If you are looking for Archer from Russia, go to Арчер.

2) American metal trio from Santa Cruz, California, also known as Archer Nation. They play a mixture of heavy metal, thrash metal and hard rock. They say their music is influenced by Black Sabbath, Guns N Roses, Megadeth, Ozzy Osbourne, Judas Priest, Led Zeppelin, Pantera, Black Label Society and many more classic rock and metal bands and artists. The band have released three albums since 2006: Doom$day Profit$, Who's Gonna Save You Now?, and Culling the Weak.
