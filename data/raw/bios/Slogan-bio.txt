Slogan was a pioneering hungarian underground metal band at the beginning of the 1990s. The band was formed in 1988 at Budapest, Hungary and was split up in 1994.

Discography:
Green Air (1988) - demo
Message from/for the human beeings (1989) - demo
Forgotten Moods (1990) - demo
Art of Ego (1992) - demo
Speciál vászontekercseknek és gyurmahabarcsoknak (1994)
Voluntary Swoon (1994)
Nincs (1997) - best of/compilation
