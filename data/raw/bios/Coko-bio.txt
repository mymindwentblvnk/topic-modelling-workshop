Cheryl 'Coko' Clemons is an American gospel and R&B singer, and lead singer of R&B group Sisters With Voices (SWV).

She has 3 albums, Hot Coko (1999), Grateful (2006) and The Winner In Me (2009). The last 2 albums is gospel and her album debut is R&B.  She also released a Christmas album in 2008
