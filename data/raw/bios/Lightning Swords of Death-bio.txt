Raging brutal American Black Metal quartet rising from Los Angeles' Undergound; featuring ENDLESS BLIZZARD member! High-calibre, yet always over-the-top blistering insanity with just a hint of Nordic influence. As a basic starting reference, in the brutal blackened vein of ANGELCORPSE, REVENGE, CONQUEROR, LAIR OF THE MINOTAUR, and [early] NACHTMYSTIUM; plus with Death-Doom heaviness dating back to HELLHAMMER / CELTIC FROST! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lightning+Swords+Of+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
