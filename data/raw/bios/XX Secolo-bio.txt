Black metal band formed in 2016. Location of origin is Abruzzo, Italy. The lyrical themes focus on history, specifically the World Wars and tradition. The band is signed to underground label, Barbatos Productions. 

XX Secolo is Italian for "20th century".

To date, the band has released the following:

2016 - La Battaglia del Solstizio (Single Edit) (Single)
2016 - Foedus Ferreum (Split)
