They're a speed metal / thrash metal band from Highland Lakes, New Jersey.  They formed in the year 1985.  They released a demo in 1986 called Faces of Death.  They intended on recording a new demo entitled No One is Innocent, but they disbanded in 1987.  They reunited in 2005 with all original members, but their original bassist Pat Burns left in 2016.  Their current lineup consists of  John Scherer (vocals),  Shannon Woods (guitar),  Frankie DeLorenzo (bass) and Marc Grossman (drums).  In 2006, they released a compilation album called Thrash Beyond Death.  It features new studio recordings, Faces of Death demo and live recordings of them performing at CBGB's on August 19, 1986. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathrash">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
