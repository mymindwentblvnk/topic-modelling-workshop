Antiquus is a Canadian heavy metal band mixing the raw oldschool roots derived from 80's metal influence with a fresh, melodic, and more modern touch. Vancouver's Antiquus have crafted a sound that can only be described with one word: Heavy! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antiquus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
