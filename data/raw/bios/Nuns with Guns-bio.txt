1) Nuns With Guns was a technical metalcore band from Orange County, California. They wrote songs against commodification of society, apathy, ignorance, and religious contradictions. They had a lot of melodies, a lot of breakdowns, a lot of lyrical chastisement, and a lot of anarchic sensibilities.


2) Nuns with Guns are a Punk Rock band from Dorchester, Dorset, UK consisting of 4 members. Jamie Parker (Vocals and lead guitars) is the influencial member in the group, which is also constructed of James Ousley (Guitars and backing vocals), Joe Dilan (Bass Guitar) and Tim Venning (Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nuns+With+Guns">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
