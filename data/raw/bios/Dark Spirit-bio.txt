The band was formed in 2005 by Spiridon (vocals) and Borislav (guitar).Over time,followed several changes in the band’s line-up.In 2008 Kiril joined the band as second guitarist.After that the band played at several shows in Plovdiv,Bulgaria.A year later,in 2009,the bass player Alexander from End of pain and the drummer Kostadin joined the band and the line-up was finally completed.At the moment the attention of the band is concentrated on the recording of their first album.The band’s desire is to bring together the contemporary power metal and melodic-heavy metal sound of the 80’s. Since the fact that there is no Bulgarian band with heavy and power metal sound the band’s ambition is to propagate their style in Bulgarian rock.
