Seiji Ozawa (小澤 征爾, Ozawa Seiji, born September 1, 1935) is a Japanese conductor, particularly noted for his interpretations of large-scale late Romantic works. He is most known for his work as music director of the Boston Symphony Orchestra and the Vienna State Opera. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/%E5%B0%8F%E6%BE%A4%E5%BE%81%E7%88%BE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
