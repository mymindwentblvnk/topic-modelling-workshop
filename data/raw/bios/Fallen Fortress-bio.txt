Fallen Fortress is a Chilean band which started in 2003 in Santiago. In 2006 Fallen Fortress recorded the first demo "Earthly Purgatory". Sience the begining, the band has gone through lots of changes in it's formation and it's musical attitude. Nowadays, after years of hard work and progress, this band's new energies and compositions have impressively converged into their first LP "Dehumanized".

Current Members:
- Diego Quiroz (Vocals/Guitar)
- Tomás Guerrero (Guitar)
- José L. Fernández (Bass)
- Daniel Delgado (Drums)

Album credits:
- Produced by Fallen Fortress and José Tomás García
