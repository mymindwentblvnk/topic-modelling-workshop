Vallendusk is an atmospheric post-black metal band from Jakarta. Since forming in 2011, they have currently released a demo, a self-titled EP in 2012 and a full-length entitled "Black Clouds Gathering" in 2013 and are currently signed to Pest Productions. Some artists they share similarities in sound with are Winterfylleth, Fen, Wodensthrone, Vattnet Viskar, and Woods of Desolation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vallendusk">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
