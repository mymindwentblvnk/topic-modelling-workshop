Bo Sundström, also known as Bo Kasper, is a Swedish musician and songwriter, born in the town of Piteå 16 october 1961. He is the front man of the Swedish pop jazz band Bo Kaspers Orkester, known for its catchy melodies and witty lyrics. He has also released two solo albums, Skåne (2004),  and Den lyckliges väg (The Way of the Happy Ones), 2007, the latter in collaboration with Frida Öhm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bo+Sundstr%C3%B6m">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
