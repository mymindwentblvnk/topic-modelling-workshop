Reaping Havoc are a young Death/Thrash metal band formed by 5 dudes from London. All members come from different musical backgrounds but all share a love of powerful brutal metal with riffs that make you want to punch somebody in the face. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Reaping+Havoc">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
