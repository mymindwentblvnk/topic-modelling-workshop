Putrefaction was a side project of Grave sometimes around 1989. But the songs were shortly after integrated in the Grave set and so the project was ended. (Copied from Metal Archives)

Putrefaction is also a heavy crust band from Ireland, a technical brutal death metal band from Australia, a death metal band from Brazil and a death metal band from Romania. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Putrefaction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
