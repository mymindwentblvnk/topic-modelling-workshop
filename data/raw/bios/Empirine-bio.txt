Empirine is a duo project between Alexander Edlund and Robin Brinck. The band was created in early 2010 as a small solo side project but later became the duo it is today. Alexander does the Growls and Keyboard, Robin Brinck plays the guitar and bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Empirine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
