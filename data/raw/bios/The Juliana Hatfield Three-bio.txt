The Juliana Hatfield Three is an American band led by guitarist and singer-songwriter Juliana Hatfield and featuring Dean Fisher and Todd Philips.

Initially active from 1993-1994, the band reunited in 2014, two decades after it disbanded, with drummer Todd Philips, and bassist Dean Fisher.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Juliana+Hatfield+Three">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
