Persona Grata is a band bringing new quality to progrock genre. Influenced by groups such as 'Yes', 'Jethro Tull', 'Pink Floyd', 'Dream Theater', 'Pain of Salvation', 'Meshuggah' and contemporary 'Muse'- developed the signature-sound being natural evolution of all above inspirations together with emphasis on melody and complex rythms.
