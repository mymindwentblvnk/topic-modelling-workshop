The members of Home of the Lame describe themselves as "a band consisting of five people living in five different cities" practicing in Hamburg: Felix Gebhard, Ingo Schröder, Alexander Böll, Christian Hake and Marcus Schneider.

The band has so far released two EPs ("Home of the Lame" in 2003 and "Habitat" in 2006) and two full lengths ("Here, of All Places" in 2005 and "Sing What You Know" in 2008). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Home+of+the+Lame">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
