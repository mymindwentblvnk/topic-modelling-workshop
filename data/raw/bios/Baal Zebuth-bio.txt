Baal Zebuth is a Black metal band from Russia, formed in 1997. 
Lyrics deal with Satanism, Death, War, Chaos.
Released 3 albums: 

- Unholy Baal Zebuth (2004)
- We Are Sathanas  (2006)
- Necromysticism - Into The Chasm Of Hell (2011)
