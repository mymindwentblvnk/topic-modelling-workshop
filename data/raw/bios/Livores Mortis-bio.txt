Livores Motris play Atmospheric Melodic Doom/Death Metal and formed in 1995 in Turnov (Czech Republic).
The lineup consists of Josef "Giuseppe" Podzimek (Guitars), Josef „ Pepíno“ Vích (Guitars, also in Pikodeath), Tom "Divoch" Tillner (vocals, ex-Laniena Mentis, Depressive Reality).
Programming, keyboards and drums are done by Libor Fanta (Debustrol, ex-Danger, Syndrom, Kryptor, Harlej).
they released up to now one Demo (Moonfrost, 1996) and 3 Full-Length albums (Vivere Militare Est, 1998; Imperium Equilibrium, 2000; The Garden Of Emotion, 2003) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Livores+Mortis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
