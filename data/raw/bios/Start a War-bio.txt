Start a War is a one-man Melodic Death Metal/Metalcore project hailing from San Antonio, Texas. Founded by Clifton Miles, Start a War has released an EP called "The Inevitable End" in November 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Start+a+War">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
