There are multiple artists with this name:
1- A dubstep and doom-step brazilian girl-producer. .

2- Waverly Hills is an Ambient / Funeral Doom Metal band from France heavily influenced by disembowelment and rigor sardonicous

Band MembersGwen - Vocals
Pierre - Guitar and drum programming

The first album has been released on Faunasabbatha Records, called "The Nurse".

The band is named after Waverly Hills Sanatorium in Louisville, Kentucky, which is reputed to be haunted.  It's even been named one of the top haunted places in the country.  It's not at all uncommon for people to see shadow people, orbs, strange lights, or to hear unexplainable noises.
