Doom metal band from Detmold, North Rhine-Westphalia. Active since 2013, currently signed to Iron Bonehead Productions. Released their debut album Spectres of Revocable Loss in 2014, followed by The All-Consuming the next year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cross+Vault">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
