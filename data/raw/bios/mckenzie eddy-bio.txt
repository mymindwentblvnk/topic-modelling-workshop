McKenzie Eddy is an alternative/electronica/soul artist based out of New York City. She is currently signed to BluRoc Records, run by Damon Dash. Her fist album titled, 'A Prelude To My Next Excursion' was released in January 2011, with features from Curren$y, Wiz Khalifa, Stalley & more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/McKenzie+Eddy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
