As if refracting feeling from a mirror image, Hurtwave bounce bright sonic transmissions off dark emotions. The Orange County, CA duo--Rory Rodriguez and Mike Karle both from the band Dayseeker. Integrate alternative energy and pop ambition into synthwave-spiked soundscapes perfect for a midnight drive. Now, they introduce a 2020 series of EPs with Night Therapy I. In the end, Hurtwave fashion a lasting connection through this sort of honesty. "We want the music to be something that can help people if they're dealing with a difficult time in their lives," he leaves off. "If you need to listen or vent, we're here for you. Hopefully, it can be therapeutic if you're battling through something." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hurtwave">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
