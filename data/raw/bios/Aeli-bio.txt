AEli is a Pulphead artist from Los Angeles. He's a producer, singer, songwriter and occasional rapper.

AEli coined the term "Pulphead", described as a 'mixture of rnb, hip hop, soul, jazz, alternative rock, and techno with a mesmerizing feel to it.' Pulphead artist is an artist that you can't put a label on. You are unable to label the genre, but you can identify the emotion the artist is portraying.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aeli">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
