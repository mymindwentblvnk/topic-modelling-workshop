Grim Forest is a Depressive black metal band from Drusenheim, France. Death4u, it's leader is also active in a lot of other black metal bands such as Negativity, Dark Noise... The band released some demos and a split CD with the french ambient/black metal band Dunkelnacht. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grim+Forest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
