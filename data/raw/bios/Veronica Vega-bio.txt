Veronica Vega is a singer, rapper and dancer. She was born and raised in Hialeah, Florida to a Venezuelan mother & a Cuban father. She is signed to Zone 4 under the mentorship of Polow da Don. In 2018, Veronica joined the cast of Love & Hip Hop: Miami. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veronica+Vega">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
