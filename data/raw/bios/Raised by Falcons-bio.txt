Sweet, tangy and always refreshing, Raised by Falcons is a beverage that is commonly mistaken for a band from the southern Connecticut area. Founder Timothy Scarves established the self-proclaimed "juice empire" in his basement as a way to fund his ever growing love of gardening. 15 years later and over 1 million bottles sold, Raised by Falcons brand juices and juice paraphernalia are steadily becoming Americas most loved beverage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raised+By+Falcons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
