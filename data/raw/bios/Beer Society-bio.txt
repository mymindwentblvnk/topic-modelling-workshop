Beer Society is Slovak band. They play rock'n'roll and metal, or as they say "beer-metal". Few of their songs are about beer and liquor or drinking itself. During concert  can be usually seen with glass of beer.  They have one demo album and in 2008 the released first album "Beeracle".  They were also best band of polish festival Rock May Festival - 3.5.2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beer+Society">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
