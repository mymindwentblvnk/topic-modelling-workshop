A Side-project of members from different pioneer Bands namely Altar, Ungod, and Cabal. The intention was to mix Black Metal with atmospheric Keyboards. The music can be seen as a mix of old Bathory and maybe early Emperor. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Baxaxaxa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
