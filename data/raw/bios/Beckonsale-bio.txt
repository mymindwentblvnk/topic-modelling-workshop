Beckonsale are a progressive thrash metal band from Manchester, England. They were born in March of 2006 and have had their current line-up since July 2006. They recorded a self-funded EP in April 2007 and are working on producing a full album by the end of 2008.  

Line-up:  

Sam Bartlett - Guitar, Vocals
Christian Parkinson - Guitar
Matt Evans - Bass
