Exekrator are a Black Metal band from Denmark.

The band is noteable for the lead vocalist having somewhat outrageously high pitched vocals. Whether or not this works/belongs in the context of Black Metal is likely to divide opinion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Exekrator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
