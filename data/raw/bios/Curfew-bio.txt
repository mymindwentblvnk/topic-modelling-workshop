There are four bands named Curfew: a Belgium indie band, a British jazz band and a British electronica band.

Indie band: https://www.facebook.com/crfwband

Jazz band: Steve Marshall (bass, flute), Martin Lawrie (keyboards), Nick Andrew (guitars), David Bouet (drums).

1960s blues/psychedelic band: https://www.discogs.com/artist/937281-The-Curfew?filter_anv=1&anv=Curfew

Electronica: They create a unique and magical blend of both synthesized and acoustic
soundscapes with thought-provoking and poetic lyrics, beautiful soaring melodies
and infectious rhythms.
Curfew create a distinctive mix of machinery and musicianship.
