Dawn of Silence is a Swedish Power Metal band. They released their 1st album ("Moment of Weakness") in 2006. 
On February 24th their new album "Wicked Saint or Righteous Sinner" will be released.

Line up: 
Patrik Johansson - Vocals
Jesper Johansson - Guitar
Mats Johansson - Guitar
Pelle Johansson - Bass
