Biography:

Disbanded in late 2005, subsequent to the release of Village, their debut album, Eliran Weizman, the band frontman and primary lyricist and songwriter, who formed the band in 2000 along with Oran Bar and Kobi Cherisky, after having spent almost two years performing as a vocalist for Subterranean Masquerade, decided to revive the Rishon Le Zion based band.

The 2016 line-up consists of:

Eliran Weizman - Vocals
Oran Bar - Lead Guitar
Barak Kabilio - Rhythym Guitar
Golan Farhi - Bass
Roi Cohen - Drums
Lior Goldberg - Keyboards

As of October 2016, the band is working on new material, and is planning a reunion show in Israel. Its revised musical style is deemed as Progressive Death Metal with multicultural influences.

Past members:
Omri Leibovich - Drums
Sarel Keren - Bass
Kobi Cherisky - Guitars
