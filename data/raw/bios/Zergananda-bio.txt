The man behind Zerganand is Evgeniy Kostritsa, russian composer and musician. In 2013 he gave up a successful carrer as a software engineer to fulfill his dreams of writing music. As a self-taught composer Evgeniy writes music free of genre boundaries and common techniques. In late 2014 Evgeniy started working on his debut album of instrumental new age music
