Oi! band formed in Sunderland late 1979 until 1984. the original line up was: Steve (vocals), Tony (Guitar), Gaz Stuart (bass) and Dona (drums). The band is inspired for bands such as: The Clash, Angelic Upstarts ,The Press, Red London, Hard Skin or Banda Bassotti. Reformed in 1990 Red Alert is still active. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Red+Alert">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
