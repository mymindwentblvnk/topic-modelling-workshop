Additional notes
Formerly known as BlackReign (US).

There is little info about this band online, as the Balanced Sound Recordings web site has been shut down due to lack of funds. Obskurum has released 2 promo cd's 'In Conniecto Nox Noctis Cultus' and 'The Ceremony and the Descent' in 2003. The band has split up and is now known under the name Bloodoath.
