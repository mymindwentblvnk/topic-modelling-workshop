The are two bands named Cruciamentum. The first is a death metal band from UK which formed in 2005. They released their debut album Charnel Passages through Profound Lore in 2015.  In 2017, they released the EP Paradise Envenomed through Profound Lore.

The other was a black metal group from Norway (Larvik) consisting of members of Dies Irae. They only released a demo in 1993 called Luctus Infinite. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cruciamentum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
