There's a few bands named Crusher :

1)A German melodic Thrash Metal Band founded in the early year of 2002 in Mainz. 

2)A French old death metal band from Mulhouse, split-up in 1994, who released two albums in 1993 : Corporal Punishment and Act II : Undermine. In late 2014 they announced their reunion.

3) A Thrashmetal band from Finland, founded in the year 2005.

4) A brazilian crossover/thrash metal band from Curitiba. 

5) A thrash metal band from Ukraine, founded in 2007.

6) A Death metal band from the Czech Republic, founded in 1991.
