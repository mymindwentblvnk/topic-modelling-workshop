Old School Hardcore Band from Odense, Denmark. The members are Jimmi on the vocals, Anders on the guitar, Lasse A behind the drums and Lasse J (since  summer 06, the old one is in rehab because he had a heroin problem)playing the bass. They formed in 1999 and released the first demo in 2000. After a break from 2000 to 2004, theay reformed and released "UNITY" - another demo - in January 2004. The latest release was the third demo "Better Together". There was an Album announced for October 2006, but it it's not out yet. According to the bands Myspace it will be out on Embrace Records in Juli/August 2007 and will be called "Vision Of Strength". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Struggle+Against">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
