Louis Johnson, better known by his stage name King L or King Louie, is an American hip hop artist from Chicago, Illinois, USA. He has released several mixtapes since 2007, including Drilluminati, Jeep Music, Drilluminati 2 and Tony. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/King+Louie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
