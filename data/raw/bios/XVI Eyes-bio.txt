Winnipeg's XVI Eyes is one of the few modern metal bands who are taking chances and breaking rules. A experimental mix of sludge, death and progressive metal, XVI Eyes is a love'em or hate'em band that will leave an impression one way or the other. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/XVI+Eyes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
