Garwall is a French heavy/black/death metal band formed in 1997. Balrog (Aborted, Genital Grinder, Balrog, The Order of Apollyon) and Olivia Scemana (Aborted, No Return, Balrog) played in this band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Garwall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
