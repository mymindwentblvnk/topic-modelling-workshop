Among The Decayed is a deathgrind band from USA. They formed in 2004 by long-term friends Kevin (Vocals) , Aaron (Bass), Travis (Drums) and Derek (Guitar). 

The band has released;  a demo in 2005 called "A Perfect Day For Terror", a split album "Atrocities Five-Way Split " with Devoured (Idn), GoldenPyre,  Terrordrome and Digging up, a demo in 2006 called "Anatomy of a Massacre " and Debut full-length album "Dead rising" released by Wormblud Records in October, 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Among+the+Decayed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
