Sérigne M'Baye Gueye (born on March 28, 1978), better known by his stage name Disiz la Peste (or simply Disiz), is a French rapper and actor. Born to a Senegalese father and Belgian mother, he grew up listening to rap.

He was a great fan of the French rap groups NTM and IAM. He was discovered by Joey Starr, one of the members of the famous rap group NTM after listening to a sample tape named Bête de bombe.

In 2008, Disiz decides to go for a new music style mixing rap with electro/house, and changes his stage name to Disiz Peter Punk. He released his first album under this name, Dans le ventre du crocodile on May 11, 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disiz+la+Peste">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
