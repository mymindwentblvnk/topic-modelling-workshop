Stonege was a Death Metal band with Progressive and Traditional Metal influences from USA (El Paso, Texas). They released their only album ''Voyage of Ageless Time'' in 2000.

Last known line-up:
Rudy Hernandez - Vocals, Rhythm Guitar
Rick Brooks - Lead Guitar, Acoustic Guitar
Mike Terrazas - Bass
