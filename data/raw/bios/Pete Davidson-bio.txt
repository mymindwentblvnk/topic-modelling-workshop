Peter Michael “Pete” Davidson (b. November 16, 1993) is an American comedian and actor from Staten Island, New York. He is best known for his stand-up comedy, his work on the sketch comedy show Saturday Night Live, and his burgeoning acting career. He has filmed two stand-up specials: “SMD”, which was released by Comedy Central in 2016, and “Alive from New York”, which was released on Netflix in 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pete+Davidson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
