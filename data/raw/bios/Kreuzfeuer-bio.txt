The band was formerly known as Kroizfoier and it was formed in 1991. in Germany as Oi/RAC band. In 1994. they changed musical style to Black Metal and name to Kreuzfeuer. Kreuzfeuer released five full-length albums, with last being released in 2000. In 2010. Kreuzfeuer released split-EP with Polish Pagan Metal band Graveland as a tribute to Conan the Barbarian movie and its soundtrack. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kreuzfeuer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
