Was formed in November 2012 Shadowspawn is a Danish death-metal act with old school death- and thrash metal influences. With a cocktail of traditional death metal, thrash and technical metal, Shadowspawn comes your way with aggressive and powerful riffs in a groovy metal blend. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shadowspawn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
