Artificial Brain are a technical death metal band from Long Island, New York. The band was formed in 2011 by Revocation guitarist Dan Gargiulo and ex-Biolich vocalist Will Smith. Following a demo and an EP, they released their debut full-length album Labyrinth Constellation in 2013 through Profound Lore. The album was recorded by Colin Marston (Gorguts, Behold...
