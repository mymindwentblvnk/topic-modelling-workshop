Devoid of Fate is a Finnish Death Metal band founded in spring 2004 in Jyväskylä. The band  has strong roots in northern Melodical Death Metal.

Jussi Rasi - guitar
Jimmy Salmi - drums
Jarno Ylitalo - guitar, voc
