Harnessing high speed technical prowess, violent circle pit inducing rhythms and a varied vocal attack which spans from the enraged tones found in grind core to the depth charge grunts of the most underground brutal death Resection easily surpass the expectations created by the strong underground buzz surrounding this young German act.
