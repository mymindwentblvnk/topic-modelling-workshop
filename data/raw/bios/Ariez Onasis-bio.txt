Ariez Onasis, the Massachusetts up-and-comer who introduced himself to readers with dual singles "Outta Time" (ft. J. Cardim) and "You Ain't Ready" (ft. Pleasure P), has stepped into the Booth to bring us entry #74 in our DJBooth.net Freestyle Series. On his brand new, previously-unreleased "City Lights" freestyle, the Dice Music Group rapper flows over a Nasty Kutt-produced beat originally featured on Meth & Red's track of the same name.
