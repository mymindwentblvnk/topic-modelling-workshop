The group was formed by three people: Tanat6s, Azathoth and Immortal in 2007 in Kazakhstan, Pavlodar city. In 2007 was recorded first demo "Jesus Beast". Then the band gave much live shows, and by the 2008 was recorded EP "Christians To The Lions!" in sympo black style. Also in the middle of 2008 group create new album "Revelations" in doom style. By the winter 2009 in group comes the present bassist Venom, and the group creates another album "Revelations, opus II" in industrial traditions. After searching for the style and sound, group began recording new album in avantgarde x-treeme orchestral black death metal, that will be out soon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thriumvirath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
