Daniel J is a 15yr old U.K. singer songwriter from London.  After teaching himself to play guitar from YouTube tutorials he began to put videos up for family and friends. This soon developed into a fan base and within six months Daniel had over 6000 subscribers.  He released his first single Beautiful Girls in February 2012.
