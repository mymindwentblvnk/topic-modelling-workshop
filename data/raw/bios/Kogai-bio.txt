Kogai is a Russian death metal band. It was formed in 2005 on the ruins of War Swallowed. Their music is technical and there are so-called progressive death metal elements. Kogai uses different elements of medieval Japanese culture as inspiration of their lyrics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kogai">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
