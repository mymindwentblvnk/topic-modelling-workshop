Lethal Outcome is a thrash metal band coming from Moldova. They released a demo "Reciprocal Hate" in the year 2009, which incorporates nine songs of their own and two covers: "Arise" (Sepultura cover) and "To Live Is To Die" (Metallica cover). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lethal+Outcome">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
