Dark Psychosis is a psychotic Black Metal band hailing from the USA (Lansing, MI) formed in 1999. 

Current line-up: 
Xaphan (Sean Peters) - Vocals, Guitar (Summon, Masochist (USA), Wind of the Black Mountains, Wastelander)
Digger - Bass
Supmur - Drums (Summon, Masochist (USA)) 

Former/past member: 
Nybras (Tim Sever) - Session Drums (Wind of the Black Mountains, Masochist (USA), Genocya)

Discography:
Profane Hallucinations Demo, 2003  
Obsessed by Shadows Full-length, 2005  

Current Record Label:
Moribund Cult
