DÖF (Deutsch-Österreichisches Feingefühl) was a 1980s Austrian-German Neue Deutsche Welle pop band.

It consisted of the Austrian comedians Joesi Prokopetz and Manfred O. "Fredi" Tauchen, and the German New Wave music artists Annette Humpe and Inga Humpe. DÖF published only a few Songs, which were a mixture of Standard German and Viennese dialect. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/D%C3%B6f">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
