Death metal band from New Port Richey, Florida. Their only release is a 1993 demo called "Till Death Do We Part". The band members were Andy Gleason (bassist), Tony Hicks (drummer, also played in Demogoth and Vomit Spawn), Mike Joseph (guitarist) and Pete Joseph (guitars and vocals, also part of Demogoth). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BONE+SAW">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
