Two-piece Italian black metal act from Tirano, formed in 1988. The band members were known as Pentacolar (bass, guitars and vocals) and Satanic Drums (drums). Released two demos: Sexual Lust (1989) and Borealis! (1991). Split-up in 1991. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sagatrakavashen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
