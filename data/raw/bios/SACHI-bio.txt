SACHI is the musical duo of Nick Chrisp and Will Thomas, two young sound makers born in New Zealand. Among their notable works are their EPs Lunch With Bianca (2016) and Nights With Ruby (2019), as well as their various remixes including Lorde’s Green Light and KLOE’s Teenage Craze. They are known for their energetic live show, during which Nick sings and Will and Nick both play live and synthesized instruments. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SACHI">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
