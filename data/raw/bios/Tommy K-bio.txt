Tommy K is an eurobeat alias for Davide Di Marcantonio for Time Records Company.
The project was produced by Giacomo Maiolini and Dall'ora.
Singles: "Desperado", "Break The Night", "Love In Chinatown", "Ready To Fight", "You Need A Lover", "Give Me Back My Money", "Funky Warriors", "Mexico", "Take Me Now", and "Rock On Fire". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tommy+K">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
