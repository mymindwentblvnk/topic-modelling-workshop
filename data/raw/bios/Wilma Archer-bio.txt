Wilma Archer is the recording name of Will Archer, an English composer and producer originally from Newcastle upon Tyne, England, signed to Domino Records' imprint Weird World. Formerly recording and releasing music as Slime from 2010-2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wilma+Archer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
