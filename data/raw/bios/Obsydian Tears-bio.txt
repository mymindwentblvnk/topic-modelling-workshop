Obsydian Tears is a Metalband from Penzance in the Southwest of the United Kingdom.
They refered to their music as Cornish Metal, since they all come from Cornwall.

The band members are Adam Chinner (vocals), Andrew Palmer (bass), James Burdall (guitar), Tim Palmer (guitar) and Chris Tofts (drums).
