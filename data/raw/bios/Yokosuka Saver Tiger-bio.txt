Formed in 1980, Saver Tiger is a heavy/speed metal-band from Yokosuka, Japan. Groupmembers include(d) famous guitarist Hideto "hide" Matsumoto, bassist Toko, who played with Rommel, vocalist Kyo, played which was a member of [artistD'erlanger[/artist], guitarist Rem and drummer Tetsu Kikuchi, who played with 44 Magnum, Zi:Kill, Ｍｅｐｈｉｓｔｏｐｈｅｌｅｓ and much more. The band released a limited pressed 8" flexi EP in 1985, a obscure demo named "For Devil Must Demo" in 1986, two live albums in 2001, and a VHS in 2002.

The band started as Saber Tiger, but band soon had to change their name to Saver Tiger to avoid confusion with the band from Sapporo's Saber Tiger.
