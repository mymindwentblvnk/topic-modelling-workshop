There are two artists who use the name BNQT: 1) A British/American supergroup 2) A Russian punk band

1) BNQT is a British/American alternative supergroup. The group consists of singers Ben Bridwell of Band of Horses, Alex Kapranos of Franz Ferdinand, Jason Lytle of Grandaddy and Fran Healy of Travis, plus Midlake members Eric Pulido (guitar), Joey McLellan (bass), Jesse Chandler (keyboard) and McKenzie Smith (drums). The band's debut album "Volume 1" will be released on Bella Union on April 28.
