1.Kori is an ambient/garage musician from Bristol, England.

2. Kori is an indie rock band band formed by Ori Di Vincenzo.
In 2014 they released their first EP "Darkness Visible" and in April 2015 their first LP "Melancholic Debauchery".
Both albums were recorded, mixed and produced by the band.
