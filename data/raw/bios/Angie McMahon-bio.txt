Angie McMahon is a singer/songwriter from Melbourne, Australia. In 2017, after winning the Josh Pyke Partnership, she released "Slow Mover", her first single as a solo artist. Before that she was active as a singer in the Melbourne soul formation The Fabric. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angie+McMahon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
