A trilingual singer and songwriter who mixes pop and R&B, Sophia Black was born and raised in Los Angeles. She learned French from the city's school system, Japanese from her mother, and music from her mother and her father, who were both professional musicians with eclectic tastes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sophia+Black">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
