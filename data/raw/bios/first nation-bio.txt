First Nation is a group from New York City that sounds much like Gang Gang Dance and Animal Collective, both fellow New York City residents. They utilize guitar, off-beat drum, constant looping, piano, and other instruments belonging to the reign of neo-tribal psychedelia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/First+Nation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
