Trocki (the Polish transliteration of "Trotsky") was founded in 2000 in Wloclawek, Poland. The band has recorded two studio albums and one live album, and has performed concerts in Poland and abroad, including Germany, Czech Republic, France, Switzerland, Spain, Norway, Sweden, Finland, Latvia and Estonia. Trocki is strongly associated with the anarchist and environmentalist movements, and is characterized by radical texts involving the broader issues of freedom and the problems of the world in the era of globalization.
