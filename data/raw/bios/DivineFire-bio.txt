DivineFire is a christian heavy power metal band formed in early springtime of 2004 with the colaboration of Finnish composer and drummer, Jani Stefanovic, and vocalist Christian Rivel (Christian Liljegren) (Narnia). Jani Stefanovic has been playing and touring with the bands Am I Blood, Sins of Omission, and Renascent. Christian Rivel is known for his work in Narnia and run his own record label Rivel Records (Liljegren Records). They also added bass player Andreas Olsson (Narnia, Rob Rock and Stormwind) into the band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Divinefire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
