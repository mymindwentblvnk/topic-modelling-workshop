Blues Goblins is Sam Coomes of Quasi performing experimental arrangements of traditional Blues songs and original compositions. He has released one self-titled studio album, and a straight-to-two-track live album called "As Is". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blues+Goblins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
