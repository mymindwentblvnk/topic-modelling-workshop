Lux Thugs is a UK Hip Hop artist from London, England. The young British MC describes his music as Space Trap. Produced in the pockets of space trapped in between other genres to ultimately create a song that identifies within the boarders of UK Rap / Hip Hop.

The UK rapper stated releasing music in early 2019 and amassed over half a million streams breaking into 2020. His versatility in lyrical flow and production has landed him a place amongst the 'up and comers' of the UK music scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lux+Thugs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
