CLS is the name of a few artists. One is Adam Skorupka, a Polish Drum & Bass producer who commonly works with Wax and has releases on Hospital (Med School) and Critical. Another is Todd Terry, the american House artist behind 'Can You Feel It'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CLS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
