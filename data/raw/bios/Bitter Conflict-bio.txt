Melodic metalcore band with Aaron Sidwell, Rob Austen, Ollie Aylett, Tom 'T-BONE' Buzwell and
Charles Holden. Released a demo CD which included four songs: "I'd Rather A Syndrome", "Blood Runs Black", "One Way Down" and "In The Moment You Know Yourself". Disbanded in 2008. Now working on seperate projects. Sidwell is otherwise known for his role as 'Steven Beale' in the BBC soap, Eastenders. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bitter+Conflict">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
