Isegrim was a German black metal band formed in 1998 first as a solo project of band's bassist/vocalist A. Blackwar. Later he gathered a full lineup around him, meaning guitarists Kvehlor and Zenturio Okkulto and drummer Soldur. They released some material at the turn of the millennium. They covered some Venom and Beherit, but also released their own material; Dominus Inferus Ushanas (released in 2000) being their only full-length and Gloria Deo, Domino Inferi -EP (released in 2001) being their last release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Isegrim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
