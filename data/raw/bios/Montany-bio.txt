Montany, originally formed as Montani, is a Melodic Speed/Power Metal band from the Netherlands. They were formed in 1989 in Wassenaar, South Holland and currently consist of Arendo Marijnus on drums, Patrick van Maurik on vocals, Dirk Hoek on guitar and Ronald Kuyt on bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Montany">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
