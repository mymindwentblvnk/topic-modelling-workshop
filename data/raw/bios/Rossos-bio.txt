Rossos was a heavy metal band from Kiruna, Sween. The band played in the early 1980's and was influenced by the new wave of brittish  heavy metal. They released one demo recording called  "Live In Kiruna" in 1982. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rossos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
