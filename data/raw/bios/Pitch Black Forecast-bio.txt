A four man group from Cleveland, Ohio, Pitch Black Forecast is the next great thing to come from Ohio. With ex-Mushroomhead's Jason Popson face-ripping vocals and Strapping Young Lad's ambidextrous Gene Hoglan on drums, Pitch Black Forecast is a heavy project.

Jason Popson - Vocals

Robert Reinard [HillBobby] - Guitar/Vox

Craig Martini - Bass
