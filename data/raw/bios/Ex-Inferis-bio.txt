Ex-Inferis was a Black/Death Metal project formed in the Colchester/Salem area of Connecticut in 2001. They recorded one demo in 2003 with five original tracks. The band featured Dan Scopino on Guitars, Bass and Keys and Nick W. Baker on Drums, Vocals and additional bass,keys and guitars. The over-all influence of the music are artists like, but not limited to-Darkthrone, Amon Amarth, Death, Dissection etc. etc. The band stopped rehearsing in 2005.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ex-Inferis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
