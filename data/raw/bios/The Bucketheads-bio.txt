Funky house produced by Kenny "Dope". The Bucketheads project became legendary with the immensely popular house anthem The Bomb (These Sounds Fall Into My Mind). The impact that this song had on any dancefloor was amazing, and still is. 

The Bomb is a house track made with samples from Chicago's 'Streetplayer'. Although the cover of 'The Bomb' has (These Sounds Fall Into My Mind) written on it, this is in fact a mondegreen - the original song contains the lyrics "Street sounds swirling through my mind...". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Bucketheads">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
