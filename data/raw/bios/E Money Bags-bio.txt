Brooklyn rapper who collaborated with high-profile rappers such as Kool G Rap, Nas, Prodigy and Noreaga. He released one album. 

On July 16, 2001, after attending a BBQ, E Moneybags was gunned down in Queens, in his Lincoln Navigator. Reports say he had been shot more than 10 times. He was 29 years old. It is rumored that the murder of E Moneybags is said to be a revenge killing for the murder of Colbert Johnson, Supreme's right hand man.
