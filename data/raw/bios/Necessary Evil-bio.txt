Necessary Evil was founded in summer of 2004. The first line up consisted of:

Patric Puola (guitar)
Toni Hildén (guitar)
Atte Santapukki (drums)
Joonas Haikonen (bass/vocals)

By 19th April 2006 1000 Necessary Evil songs had been downloaded a very important milestone for the band.

31st May 2006 first gig at Vehkoja elementary school

12th August 2006 gig at Peräkorpi Rock

Current lineup:

Patric Puola (LeadGtr/Vocals)
Atte Santapukki (Drums)
Toni Hildén (RhythmGtr)
Jussi Liimatta Jr. (Bass)

Necessary Evil is influenced by: Black Sabbath, Children Of Bodom, Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Megadeth, Racer X, Slayer
