Mike Terrana was the drummer of the German Heavy Metal band RAGE(left autumn 2006) and is currently the drummer of the German Band Axel Rudi Pell and at the same time is the drumer of German Power Metal Band Masterplan.
He also recorded 5 albums with the band called Artension and participated on Victor Smolski's solo albums and Kiko Loureiro's project called No gravity (2005).
