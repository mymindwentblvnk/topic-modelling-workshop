Mike Nygard - Vocals, Sam Barrett - Guitar, Matt Neely - Drums, Pete Gelles - Bass, Peter Rutcho - Keys/Guitar

Birch Hill Dam is a heavy rock n roll band, located in the dense forests of Massachusetts. Their first full length album, which is self titled, was released in early 2009. The band has recently released their 2nd full length album, titled "Colossus", and are currently playing out in support of it. Check out the videos for "Boozehound" & "Seeding"!!
