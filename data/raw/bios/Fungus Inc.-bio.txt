In 2004. Fungus Inc. came forth out of the Belgian death metal formation Culpable Homicide.
It started as a side project, however due to the success the first 2 songs got, the project evolved to a band.

Dr. Fungus - Guitar 
Lil' Nop - Bass 
El Hongo Con Cojones - Drum 
The Humongous Fungus - Vocals
