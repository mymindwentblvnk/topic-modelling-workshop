Katorah Marrero (born April 3, 1992), better known by her stage name Young M.A, is an American rapper and entrepreneur. The abbreviation in her stage name, "M.A" (stylized without a period after the "A") stands for "Me Always". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Young+M.A.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
