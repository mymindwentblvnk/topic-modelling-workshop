Axis of Evil is a eight piece ska band from Worcester, MA. They were formed in 2003 and have been busting heads ever since. They finally got out their first CD named Let 'em Drop in 2005.

Band Members: Zach Rudes, Matt Kasputis, Jeff Colombo, Joe Page, Spencer Caddigan, Kevin Kennedy, Ben Howe, Damian Boyar
