Skulldriver was formed in the late summer of 2005, in Liljendal, Finland in the countryside of southeastern Finland. Their first demo, self-titled, was released in the summer of 2006, and consisted of the tracks LocoMotive and Push the Button. They have also made a video of the song LocoMotive, and will soon release their next demo with the tracks Usage and Saviour.

The band consists of Kuli on vocals and guitar, Pede on guitar, Jocke on bass, Tomi K on percussions, and Karda on drums.

As of March 2008, all their songs are available free of charge on the Finnish music site Mikseri.net at http://www.mikseri.net/skulldriver. Their video is available on YouTube at http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AK56QBbQosM.
