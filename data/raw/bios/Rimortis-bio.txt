Rimortis is a power/speed (with strong keyboard sound) metal band from Czech Republic. Their lyrics are Czech. 

Current members: Milan Hloucal - singer, Roman Černý - guitar, Jirka Vrba - keyboard, Vašek Mrzena - bass guitar, Martin Růžek - drums
