There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Inane was a hardcore band from Hatfield, Pennsylvania. They have released a demo in 2002, and a split with Leavenworth.

2) Inane is an Austrian experimental and progressive death metal band.

3) Inane is an Germany Death 'n' Roll band

4) Inane was an anarcho punk band from Louisville, Kentucky that existed off and on from 1997-2005.  They recorded a full length album in 2000 entitled "They Want You."  It was never officially released, but a select number of copies were circulated. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
