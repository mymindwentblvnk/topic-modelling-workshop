Satycarion is a band from Pisek, Czech Republic. It was formed during the somer of 2004. Their music is deeply influenced by the hardrock and metal scene of the 80ies and 90ies but it could be also described as progressive. They play mostly their own songs, but covers of more known bands like Stratovarius, Dream Theater, Ozzy Osbourne or Mettalica can also occur. Their performance is notable for the female vocals and elaborated, melodic songs.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Satycarion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
