Dis Pater is a doom metal band founded in 1993 in rainy and dark Koenigsberg, Russia. The band has gone through several personnel changes throughout the years.

Today's lineup is:

- Rodion - Vocals
- Vano - Guitars
- Alexander M. (Suffer) - Guitars
- Alexander K. (Snap) - Bass
- Victoria - Keyboards and flute
