Gilles Ateyaba Koffi Soler better known as Ateyaba, previously as Joke (born 27 October 1989) is a French rapper from Montpellier.

After the release of the Kyoto EP on November and a sold-out concert at La Maroquinerie de Paris in December, he's considered in 2012 as one of the main promising talent of French rap.  His debut album Ateyaba was released on 2 June 2014 with a number of popular tracks taken from the album charting in SNEP Singles Chart.
