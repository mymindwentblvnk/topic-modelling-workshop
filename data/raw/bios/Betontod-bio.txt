BETONTOD (german for Concrete Death) is a german punk band from Rheinberg, Germany, which was founded in the beginning of the 90's. In the beginning they named themselves Extrem, later the Sniffin' Kills and finally settled on Betontod. To this date they have performed over 800 concerts and recorded four studio albums, along with one live one.
