Darkness Before Dawn are a six piece blackened death metal/deathcore band out of Glendale, Arizona. The band makes heavy usage of symphonic keyboards.

Current Lineup:

Vocals - Logan
Guitar - D.J.
Guitar - Gabe
Bass - J.B.
Drums - Matt
Keyboards - T.J.

They formed in 2004 and built a large local following over the years. The band released a 5 song self-titled EP in 2007, and a full length CD called "Kings To You" in 2009.
