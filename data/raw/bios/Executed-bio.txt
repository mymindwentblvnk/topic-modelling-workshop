Executed, Straight-up hardcore/punk band from Jakarta, Indonesia.
Raw and means fucking hardcore from the biggest punk city in South East Asia, Jakarta.
Featuring member and line up of various Jakarta good bands like Grave Dancer, and Duct Tape Surgery.

Executed played USHC 80's Midwestern style hardcore not unlike the bands like Negative Approach or modern bands like Raw Nerve. They pull it in perfection and mixed well with their straight to point angry lyrics. Lineup consisted of Ari (vocals), Mamas (bass), Mantul (guitar) and Ipul (drums)
