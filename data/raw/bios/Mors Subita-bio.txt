Mors Subita is an aggressive, melodic and groovy metal band from Oulu, Finland. The band combines sounds from the 90's like At the Gates and Carcass with modern aggressive metal. With killer attitude off and on stage, the band is truly a five-piece metal machine.
 Mors Subita includes well-established musicians such as lead guitarist Mika Lammassaari of ETERNAL TEARS OF SORROW, vocalist Tero Piltonen  from DARK FLOOD as rhytm guitarist here, bassist Mika Junttila, drummer  Juha Haapala and Antti 'Hape' Haapsamo,  who performed much of the vocal work on CATAMENIA's 2006 album Location:Cold.

Mors Subita has been around since 2000 and released 4 demotapes. The year 2011 is the year when Mors Subita releases their debut album titled "Human Waste Compression" trought Violent Journey Records.
