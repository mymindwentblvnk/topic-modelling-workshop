"Vakan" is a band created most precisely on March, 2010 in the city of Santa Maria, Brazil. 
Built mostly on Metal influences (such as Black Sabbath, Judas Priest, Iron Maiden, Metallica, Helloween) Vakan's main goal is to work on original songs, allying their many different influences and putting their personality into it. As an independent band, they released their first EP Freeze! on March 2012.
