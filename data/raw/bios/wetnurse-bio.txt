Seventh Rule Recordings owner Scott Flaster describes Wetnurse:

"The urgency of early hardcore; the aggro, off-kilter noise of the AmRep heyday; the raw, outcast power of early death metal; the melodic transcendence of classic rock... All these influences exist within Wetnurse's music, yet theirs is something totally new and progressive." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wetnurse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
