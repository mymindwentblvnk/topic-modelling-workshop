The Curse Of Hail is a melodic metal band based out of Des Moines, IA. Formed in the summer of 2008, TCOH has been working hard to eventually become one of the forerunners in the death metal scene in Iowa. Solidifying the line-up in 2011 with the addition of Ken Payne on vocals and Luke Haffner on guitar. The Curse Of Hail has reached a level of brutality and professionalism that will only take them to the top.
