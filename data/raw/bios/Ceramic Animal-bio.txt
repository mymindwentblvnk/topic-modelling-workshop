Ceramic Animal are “a couple of nasty boys” from just outside of Philadelphia playing “psychedelic garage-yacht.” The band consists of brothers Warren (guitar and vocals) and Elliott Regan (keys and vocals) as well as Dallas Hosey (bass and vocals).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ceramic+Animal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
