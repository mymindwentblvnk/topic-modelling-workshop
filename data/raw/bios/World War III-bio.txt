'World War III' is a Traditional Heavy Metal band formed in Temple, PA (USA) in 1979.
The last known line-up consisted of Tipa Sparrs, Gary "Sledge" Hammer,  Dan Hammer and John DiTeodoro.

Former/past members are: Poodie Taku and Dean Davidson.

After releasing a single, demo and ultimately a full length in 1985 the band split-up.
