There are multiple  bands recording under the name Cabbage.

1. Manchester based five piece serving up an idiosyncratic, satirical attack in the form of discordant neo post-punk.  They are fans of GG Allin.

2. CaBBaGe is a Spanish band that features a mixture of rock, blues, funky and jazz all mixed up with funny and critical lyrics. This dadaist band is intended not to offend but to entertain, which can be proved with their energetic live shows.
