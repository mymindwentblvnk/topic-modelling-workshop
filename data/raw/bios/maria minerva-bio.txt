Maria Juur, alias Maria Minerva, is a psychedelic, avant-pop and lo-fi music producer and artist from Tallinn, Estonia, currently based in Los Angeles, California.

Her concert activities were started in early 2010. The debut cassette Tallinn at Dawn, followed by album Cabaret Cixous, was released in 2011 via Los Angeles' leftfield imprint Not Not Fun.
