Feldhaubitze is a Raw Black Metal band from Friedrichshafen-Lipbach, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. Feldhaubitze means "field howitzer" and it's a type of artillery weapon. They were formed in 2006 have released a Demo in 2007. 

The band is involved in "Schwarzer Kreis Südgermanien" (Black Circle South Germany). It's a club for young black metal bands (mainly side projects of founder Wendul) from the South of Germany. Besides Feldhaubitze it features Wolfsblut, Nebelwahn, Rabenvolk, Uthark, Svartedauden, Sturmgebet, Lieder des Nebelreichs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Feldhaubitze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
