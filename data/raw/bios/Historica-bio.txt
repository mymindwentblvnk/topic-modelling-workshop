2. Defunct Female-fronted death/thrash band from Chieri, Italy that that formed in 2009 and sings about historical events and mythology. They released one EP, Historia Magistra Vitae, in 2011 before disbanding in 2012. Singer Danielle Fiore now provides additional vocals in Italian Grindcore outfit Impaled Bitch. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Historica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
