Perihellium is a progressive metal band in Poland (Tarnów). Formed in 2004, they have released their first album The New Beginning on March 2008 via Insanity Records.

Current line-up:
Marcin Sulek: Vocals
Bartek Bachula: Bass
Seweryn Blasiak: Drums
Gerard Wróbel: Guitar, Keyboard
