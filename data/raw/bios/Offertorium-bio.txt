Offertorium is a Moldovan symphonic metal band with female vocals, using some elements from progressive metal.
Offertorium's vocalist was the winner of the award "Best operatic voice" in a vote-contest for best female vocals Metal Maiden 2004 held amongst bands from ex-USSR countries. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Offertorium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
