Old school death metal soaked in brutality and dark atmosphere in the vein of 90's classics Morbid Angel / Immolation / Incantation. After two albums on local label Grodhaisn Productions american Brutal Bands released the third CD "Dark Future" on the 25th June 2010. However the label haven't supported the band at all, thus Destroying Divinity signed with proven Lavadome productions and delivered contemporary dark death metal opus Hollow Dominion in September 2014.

From south Moravia region, Czech Republic darkness is comming..
