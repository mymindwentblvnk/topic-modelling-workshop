Opossom are a New Zealand trio featuring Kody Nielson and Michael Logie (formerly of The Mint Chicks) and Bic Runga. Their debut album "Electric Hawaii" was released June 1st, 2012 on Dark Summer Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Opossom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
