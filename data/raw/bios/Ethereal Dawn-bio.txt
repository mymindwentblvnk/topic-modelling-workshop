Ethereal Dawn is a one man power metal band from Boden, Sweden. They released a debut EP entitled 'Burning Embers' in 2014, followed by their debut album "The Moonlight Of Gloom" in 2015. The debut album features guest appearances by Tommy Johansson of ReinXeed, who also helped mix and produce the album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ethereal+Dawn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
