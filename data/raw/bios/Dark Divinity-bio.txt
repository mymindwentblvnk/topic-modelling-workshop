Dark Divinity is a melodic death metal band based in Wellington, New Zealand. The band emerged in late 2017 and soon began pummeling Wellington audiences with their brand of ferocious, hook-laden metal. In their relatively short time as a band they have gained a strong reputation locally, have released two well-received singles (Seasons of Dark & Exegesis), and have shared the stage with international acts including Psycroptic & The Black Dahlia Murder.
