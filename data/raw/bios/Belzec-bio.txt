Belzec is a black metal band hailing from Peru and formed in 1998. The band is active as of 2006 according Encyclopedia Metallum (Metal Archives).

Discography:
World Genocide (Demo, 1999)
Holocaust (Demo, 2001)
Belzec/Dark Faith (Split, 2005)
The Art of Holocaust (Full-length, 2005)

Latest lineup:
Lord Bardach - lead guitar (Saram)
Beleth - bass (Goat Semen)
George - drums (Convulsion)

Former members:
Evil Reich - guitars
Marbas - drums
J.C. Muro - bass (Mortem (Per))
Bestialwar666 - drums
Throne of Nergal - rhythm guitar
Landru L. Rophocale - vocals
