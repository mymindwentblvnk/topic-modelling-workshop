Atheme One was a power metal band from Finland founded in 2005 with lyrical themes about fantasy and magic. After releasing a four-track EP in August of 2006, the band disbanded. Members from the band later formed the longer-lasting, female-fronted Amberian Dawn.

Tuomas Seppälä - Guitar and Keyboard
Peter James Goodman - Lead, harmony and backing vocals
Heikki Saari - Drums
Joonas Pykälä-Aho - Drums (backup)
Tommi Kuri - Bass
Tom Sagar - Keyboards
and Sampo Seppälä - Rhythm Guitar

(as cited from their website www.athemeone.com)
