Cynabare Urne is a death metal band emerging from Finland. The band has been active since 2014
and released their first recorded works as “Fire the Torches” digital single in 2016. The band consists
of two members: Jani Koskela & Sameli Köykkä. Their music is dark, heavy and raw death metal. Lyrically the band is influenced by global death rite and dark folklore traditions, as well as hermetic alchemy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cynabare+Urne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
