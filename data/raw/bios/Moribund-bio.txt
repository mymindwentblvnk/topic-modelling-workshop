there seem to be more than one artist with the name Moribund.

1. Swiss Old School Death Metal band that released Oracular Eyes in 1996.
Bass: Roger Welti
Drums: Philipp Eichenberger
Guitars: Luca Amarina
Vocals & Guitars: Pablo Kissner

2. Swedish deathmetalband who recorded two demos in the early 90's. Members of the original band got back together in feb 2007. to be continued... http://www.moribund.tk

3. Power Electronics project of Shawn Royal, owner of sirkut electronics and noted gun enthusiast.

4. Belgian Atmospheric Dark/Black Metal band [RIP] which released 2 demo's and one EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moribund">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
