Thorazine Rush is an American thrash metal band formed sometime in the late nineties in Cadillac, Michigan. The band was independant and managed to release a demo (Force Feed) and an EP (Lost) before splitting up. Details about the band themselfs is murkey and it is unknown if they ever continued on to other projects <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thorazine+Rush">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
