DivahaR is a black metal band formed in Armenia.

The foundations of the band were set back 2009 by Dev. At the end of 2009 Dev (Vocals) and Urubani (Guitars) were already writing their first songs. When Skadi (Guitars) joined the band, Dev focused on the vocals only. The lineup of the band was completed in 2010 when Freya (Bass) joined DivahaR.

In 2014 Belus (keyboard) welcomed to DivahaR .
The band had many concerts both on local and international stages, has played in several famous festivals.
In 2013 DivahaR released a single called an ALIEN and a namesake
music video.
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CFXQl-zfQoM

After long time in October 2014 DivahaR signed a contract with 'Sleazy Rider Records' and released debut album DIVARISE, in which Attila Csihar from Mayhem made guest vocals.

On June 2015 DivahaR released its second music video ''Shadows''
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J-owlsQKa_E

In 2015 Freya (Bass guitar) left the band and Skadi took up Bass.
