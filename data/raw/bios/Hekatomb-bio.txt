Hekatomb is a portuguese Black metal band formed in 2003 in Coimbra.
Hekatomb is the work of one person who goes under the alias of AKM. The band is currently unsigned and has released 4 demos. The first 2 of these demos were self released, the third demo was released through Blitzkrieg and the 4th through Hell War Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hekatomb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
