Pillory began as a project in the summer of 2003. Since then they have performed their vicious style of extreme heavy metal in venues all across New England. Unique Leader picked up the band in 2005, and released their first full length album 'No Lifeguard At The Gene Pool'. The band has now welcomed Ian Jacysyzn into the fold.  Future performance and studio recordings are in the works. 

Pillory is:

Vocalist - Adam Time
Bass - Crusty Chris
Guitar - Metalcore Marcos
Guitar - Sketchy Dan
Drums - Peein' Ian
