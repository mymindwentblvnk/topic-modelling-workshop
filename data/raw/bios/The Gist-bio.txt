There are 2 artists known as "The Gist":

1) nom de plume of Cardiff-born stuart moxham, former member of young marble giants, for 1983 solo album embrace the herd, released on Rough Trade records.

2)Canadian Avant-garde duo, whom write and preform mostly comical songs in a wide variety of genres and styles, provided they are under a minute in length.
