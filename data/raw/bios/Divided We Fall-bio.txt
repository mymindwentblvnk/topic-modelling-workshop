There are three bands known as Divided We Fall:

1) Divided We Fall are a symphonic metal band from Stafford, United Kingdom.

2) Divided We Fall are a melodic hardcore/metalcore band from Wisbech. For fans of; Counterparts, Parkway Drive, Worthwhile.

3) Divided We Fall are a five piece trance inspired metalcore/hardcore band from Worcestershire. They parted ways as a band in August 2011.
