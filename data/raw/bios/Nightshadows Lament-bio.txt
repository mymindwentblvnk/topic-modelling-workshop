Nightshadows Lament are a fusion of Doom, Black and Death metal based in West Yorkshire, UK. Formed in 2005 by Shaun MacGowan (My Dying Bride), the band have 2 EPs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nightshadows+Lament">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
