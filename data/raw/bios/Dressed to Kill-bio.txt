Dressed to Kill can refer to a number of different recording groups:

1) A Kiss tribute band
2) A South Western Ontario Blues band 
3) A disco cover band
4) A Cirencester rock band
5) A rock band active from 2007 - 2009 
6) A speed metal/heavy metal band from Beijing/Tianjin, China. They are very much influenced by major bands such as Iron Maiden and Judas Priest. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dressed+to+Kill">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
