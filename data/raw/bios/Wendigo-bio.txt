Wendigo is a producer and a part of the Spider Gang collective. He focuses mostly on trap type beats but has also produced some guitar-based songs like “where is darkie?”, proving his versatility as a producer. He has one of the most recognizable and unique sounds in the rap scene right now.
He often plays the electric guitars at live shows.
