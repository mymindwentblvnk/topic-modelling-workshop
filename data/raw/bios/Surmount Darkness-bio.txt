Surmount Darkness is a band hailing from Bogota, Colombia. They got a black metal style as well very influenced by nordic folk. The band exists since 2007 and in december of 2009 they released a demo entitled "Venciendo La Oscuridad" which means the same of the band's name. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Surmount+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
