Vesano is a depressive black metal band from Paulínia, Brazil formed in 2009.

Members:
F. - Vocals, guitars
B. - Guitars
Daniel Gerez - Drums

Discography:
2009 - Sangue Seco (demo)
2009 - Eindig/Vesano (split)
2010 - Pedaços de Minha Vida (demo)
2011 - Gritos do Tempo (Full-lenght)
