Furbowl is a Swedish death metal band that formed in 1991 in Växjö, Sweden.  The band consisted of Johan Liiva on vocals and guitar,  Nicke Stenemo on guitars/keyboard and Max Thornell on drums.   They changed their name to Wonderflow.  They disbanded in 1995 when John Liiva joined Arch Enemy.
