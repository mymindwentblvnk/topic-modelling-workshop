The British singer,  Calum Scott  came into the limelight when he blew Britain's Got Talent viewers away on  2015  launch episode and received   Simon Cowell  's Golden Buzzer pick. Calum performed a stripped down, slower version of Robyn's Dancing On My Own in his unique voice. Apart from this, Calum has been part of two other acts. First one is The Experiment, an  Electronic, Dance, House duo, along with John McIntrye. Scott is also the lead of British  Maroon 5  tribute act called Maroon 4. Scott considers   Adam Levine  his greatest influence. He lives in  Hull, England  with his mother and sister.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Calum+Scott">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
