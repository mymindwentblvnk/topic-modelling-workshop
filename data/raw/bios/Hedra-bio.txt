There are multiple artists named Hedra: 
1. Hedra is a Heavy Metal Band formed in 2014, Jim from the band Subvert finally found the band he was looking for after a long 10 years of searching, previous bands were Subvert & Pure Negative, also Jim played Guitar in If Fire Would Fall.
