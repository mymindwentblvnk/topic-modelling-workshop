Ghost Tower is a band from Bellevue, Nebraska who play a very interesting mix of thrash metal and old school doom metal. Their sound draws equal influence from Anthrax and Pagan Altar. Recommended for anyone into thrash or heavy as hell traditional doom. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ghost+Tower">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
