The Sceptic is a brazilian death metal band formed in 2002 in Caxias do Sul, a city situated in the state of Rio Grande do Sul in the south of Brazil.
The band is a project from the guitarists Lander and Maurício Corrêa and have support from Marcelo Nunez on the Bass Guitar. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Sceptic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
