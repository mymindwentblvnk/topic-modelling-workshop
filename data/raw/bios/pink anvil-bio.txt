PINK ANVIL is the collaboration of two members of the band MINISTRY--Paul Barker and Max Brody. In anticipation of the Chinese New Year celebrating the Black Sheep, Barker and Brody have forged a collection of songs only a mother could love.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pink+Anvil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
