David Vandervelde is a Chicago-based, alternative rock musician, whose works and been featured predominantly, along with Brie Stoner's, as background music in NOOMA videos. He performed with The Moonstation House Band (formerly known as Where The Moon Came From band).

All of his albums to date have been released on the Secretly Canadian record label.
