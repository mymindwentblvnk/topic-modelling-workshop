Black Metal from Brazil (Duque de Caxias, Rio de Janeiro). 
Formed in 2007 under  Satanicrew Records label. 
Main themes are J.R.R Tolkien, Paganism and Anti-Christianity.
Rodrigo "Chucky" Lopes - Vocals
Raphael "Chaos" Fonseca - Guitar (Velho, Dark Forest (Bra))
Oton Lamothe - Bass (Poem's Death, Necrose Vaginal, Gutted Souls, Anopsy)
Thiago Splatter - Drums (Necrose Vaginal, Enema, Anal Latex Tool, Anopsy, Velho)
They have one demo - Walking by Forest (2008).
