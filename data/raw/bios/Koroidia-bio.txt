KOROIDIA is an old school death metal band from Hialeah, Florida (USA). Founded in 2013. The band's name is Greek for 'mockery'. They released their first debute EP 'Wrath Abomination' in February 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Koroidia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
