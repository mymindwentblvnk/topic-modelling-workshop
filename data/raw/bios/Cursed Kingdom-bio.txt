Cursed Kingdom is a metal band from Iran was formed by Saman Ali Madadi as Rhythm guitarist and Bassist and Amirhosein Amiri as lead guitarist . Until the summer 2011 Keyvan as a drummer joined the band and Cursed kingdom was working on their songs . They released their first song entitled "Gerdab" with persian lyrics in Thrash metal genre. Cursed kingdom is currently working on a first album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cursed+Kingdom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
