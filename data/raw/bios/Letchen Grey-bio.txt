Letchen Grey formed in Bellflower, California under the name Sexist in 1982.  It is unknown when they changed their name to Letchen Grey.  They were an 80s styled heavy metal band who released one EP in 1986 called Party Politics. Their sound is comparable to bands like Dokken, Lion and Lizzy Borden. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Letchen+Grey">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
