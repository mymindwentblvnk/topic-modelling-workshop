There are some groups called Rising:
1) Rising was a Swedish Heavy Metal band that was active from 1980-1992. They recorded a handful of demos, and 2 singles.
2) Rising is hardcore crossover/alternative/nu-metal band from Gbely, Slovakia.
3) There is  a rock/metal band from Bulgaria aslo called Rising.
4) Rising was a Hard'n'Heavy band from Kiev (Ukraine), fronted by Suzanna Elpy. They recently changed the name to “No Brakes”.
5) Rising is a female-fronted Heavy metal band from Puerto la Cruz, Venezuela
6) A Danish Sludge/Groove metal band formed in Copenhagen year 2008.
