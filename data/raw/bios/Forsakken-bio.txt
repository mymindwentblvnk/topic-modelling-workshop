Founded by guitarist/songwriter Billy Garcia of CBS Survivor fame, Forsakken was born into existence on the mean streets of New York City. Influenced by traditional metal, Forsakken is a Heavy Metal band layered with ruthless riffs and powerful female vocals. Their music has given them the reputation of being New York City's headbanging, horns up, defenders of the metal faith. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forsakken">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
