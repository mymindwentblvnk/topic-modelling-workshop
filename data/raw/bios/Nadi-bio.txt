Nadi is the name of more than one artist: 
1) YouTuber and singer  
2) Indonesian death metal band.

1) Nadi (born on June 20, 1991) is an American YouTuber, singer, makeup artist and blogger known for his fashion, beauty and lifestyle blog Pop Luxe. He also has a successful cosmetics company under the same name. He has released two albums: Black Heart (2014) and Kiss of Fame (2017).

2) NADI formed in the mid - 2010, started by vocalist Konyel and guitarist Jevry, who wanted to make a band project with the two missile a demo song tracks "IDEOLOGI SETAN, and BUNUH". Over time, bassist Jack and drummer Dio joined the band. Their discography consist of Demo (2010), Energi Kebencian Tuhan -EP (2011) and Nadisme (2013).
