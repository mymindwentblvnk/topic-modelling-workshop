Mt. Joy is an American indie folk group. The name of the band refers to Mount Joy in the Valley Forge National Park, Pennsylvania. The members of the band, Matt Quinn and Sam Cooper, are said to have learned how to write songs on Mt. Joy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mt.+Joy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
