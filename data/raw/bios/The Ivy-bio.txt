There are 2 artists with the name The Ivy:

1. Formed in 2016, The Ivy is an indie synth-pop band hailing from Tulsa, Oklahoma.

http://www.wearetheivy.com/

2. The Ivy was formed in the summer of 2007, and has been surrounded by hype and anticipation ever since. It's members comprised of former successful Arizona bands, The Ivy is a collaboration of artists with a variety of music tastes and influences. In a music scene dominated by screamo/hardcore acts, The Ivy strives to rise above the status quo with a pop punk influence masked with heavy guitars and harmonic vocals. This band has only begun it's long life, and greater things can be expected from The Ivy with every passing day.
The band announced January 5th 2010 that they are disbanding. Their final show was January 25th at The Clubhouse in Tempe, Arizona
