Sportlov (Winter break) was a band from Sweden that made parody of black metal by having lyrics about snowball-wars, skiing and other winter activities. Their outfits were skies and pulks with nails.

Members:
Count Wassberg (Steffe Pettersson) on vocals
Dubbdäck Doom Occulta (Kaj Löfven) on guitar
Hell Y. Hansen (Lars Löfven) on guitar
Thermoss (Lawrence Mackrory) on bass
Fjällhammer (Matte Modin) on drums
