BVA MC. The ‘loose tongue style’ inventor from UK, west country boy. Part of High Focus Records familly, participates in many projects: The 3 Amigos (with Leaf Dog and Naive), The Brothers Of The Stone (with Leaf Dog and Illinformed), The Four Owls (with Fliptrix, Leaf Dog and Verb T), collaborated with an impressive array of US Hip Hop legends such as: Krs-One, Vinnie Paz, Sadat X and Reef The Lost Cauze and more. Also recorded his solo album "Be Very Aware" in 2014. UK Hip-Hop revived in the best manner. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BVA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
