At just 19 years old, Lennon Stella's career was already nothing short of impressive. Fresh off a six-season acting stint on ABC's (and later CMT's) hit show "Nashville", Lennon (who played Maddie Conrad alongside her biological sister Maisy)  had wasted no time lauching a solo career. She signed with RECORDS/Columbia in 2018. In August of that year, she released an acoustic version of their original song and released her first song "Live Everybody Else" which rose to #1 on the US and Canada Spotify Viral Charts. She is featured on a Jonas Blue song along with Liam Payne. Stella released her debut EP Love, Me in 2018. Her debut studio album Three. Two.
