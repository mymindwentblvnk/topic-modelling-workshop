Ziggy X is a German trance/dance DJ, who started his career in electronic music in the early 1990's. He slowly progressed playing clubs all over Germany and in 2003 came his breakthrough with the single Bassdusche. He continued with the tracks Geschwindigkeitsrausch and Herzklopfen, released on the label Aqualoop. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ziggy+x">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
