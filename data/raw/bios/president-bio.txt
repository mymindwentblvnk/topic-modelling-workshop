"The President" refers to a band led by Wayne Horvitz (keyboard, harmonica, drum programming),  Bobby Previtte (drums),  Elliot Sharp (guitars), Dave Hofstra (electric bass, tuba), Doug Wieselman (tenor saxophone), Dave Tronzo (slide guitar, national steel guitar). 

The President was alive in the New York City rock music scene from 1985 to 1991.  Bill Frisell sometimes played guitar with this group.
