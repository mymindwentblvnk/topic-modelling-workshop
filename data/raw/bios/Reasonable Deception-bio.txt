Aliens, Conspiracies, Cannabalism, Assassains, War, and Politics pretty much sums up Reasonable Deception. An Extreme Metal Band that consists of Chuck Doner, Chris Fulton, Chris Stack, Ace, and Mike Dickerson. The band became Reasonable Deception during the time that ex-bassist Jason Kashazta was with them, who came up with the name, and it has been that ever since.
