Thamud is a Black Metal (with some Ambient passages) band from Dubai, UAE.
Status: Active
Year of creation: 2007
The one man band started under the name Nekromancer.
"Qowm Thamud is an ancient Arabian tribe that existed in the period of the pre Arabian age of ignorance"!
Thamud is Mudamer (All Instruments, Intros and Vocals), ex-Al-Namrood & قفص.
