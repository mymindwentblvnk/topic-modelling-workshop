Dutch Dub is a side project of Nick deWitt who also plays in the band Pretty Girls Make Graves. Nearly everything done on the self-titled album is done by  DeWitt himself though Nathan Thelen, Chuck Keller and Jason McGer helped on tracks 14 and 15 of the album. The offical dutch dub site is http://www.mistakemachine.com/main.htm <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dutch+Dub">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
