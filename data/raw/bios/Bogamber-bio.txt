Bogamber is thrash metal from Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina. They released one album in 2003 called "Devastacija Sakralnog Objekta" with 14 songs on it. Another album was released in 2007 called "Nevjerovanje" with yet another 14 songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bogamber">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
