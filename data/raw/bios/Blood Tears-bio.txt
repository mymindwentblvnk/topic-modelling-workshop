Blood Tears began their activities in 1998 in Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil, influenced by doom and black metal bands, like My Dying Bride, Dimmu Borgir, Katatonia, Tristania, Hypocrisy, Type O Negative.

The demos "The beginning of suffering"(2000) and "Symphony of dark chords"(2002) got great repercussion from fans and specialized magazines, both national and international.

In 2004 the band releases Demo Songs, Mcd composed by pre-productions of "Nothing but sorrow", the first official full lenght of the band.
