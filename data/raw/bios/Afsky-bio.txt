One-man black metal project of Solbrud's vocalist/guitarist from Denmark. Released a s/t EP in 2015, a single Vinteren bæres ind in 2016, debut album Sorg in 2018 and second album Ofte jeg drømmer mig død in 2020 with Vendetta Records.
