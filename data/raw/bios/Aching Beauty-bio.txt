Aching Beauty - the "Painful Beauty", it is an alchemy, the surprising and effective meeting of multiple horizons from rock to progressive heavy metal.
A complex mix of musical technics and melody, sincerity and experiment.
The band was originaly formed in 1997, in Paris. After an experience of many shows, Aching Beauty released a demo-CD called "One more step" in 2001.
Just after that, they made an unplugged live CD, recorded in Paris.
In november 2005, their first album "L'Ultima Ora" was released on Brennus Music.
