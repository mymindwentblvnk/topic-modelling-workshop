Slow, mournful, dark doom with great poetic lyrics in English, German and French. Steady and solid Death/Doom-metal with occasional female backing vocals. Good idea to check out if you're a fan of early My Dying Bride or early Anathema. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Insanity+Reigns+Supreme">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
