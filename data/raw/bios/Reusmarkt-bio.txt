Reusmarkt was formed in 1997 in Zaporizhzhia, Ukraine. The band split-up in 1999 and then Obscirian (aka Hyozt) formed new project Xul which recorded only one album. Later Hyozt moved to the city of Uzhgorod and there he reincarnated Reusmarkt in 2006. Hyozt recorded all instruments for album "Echo" by himself and Radogor from the band Nagual did vocals. After the release of album Hyozt found musicians for live shows. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Reusmarkt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
