NYC/Brooklyn based indie rock band. Clear Tigers is the work of Nathan Akin. Nathan wrote and recorded "Brutal" over the course of several months in on-and-off recording sessions. Nathan is playing and singing all the parts on the album.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Clear+Tigers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
