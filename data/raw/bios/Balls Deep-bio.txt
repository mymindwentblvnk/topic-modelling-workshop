Since forming in 2011 Stoke-on-Trent based metal band Balls Deep have been on a non stop rampage of the UK. Forming a solid base the band hit the UK underground live scene hard.

 finding their feet they hit the studio in 2012 to record their debut 5 track e.p. 'CHRISTIE'. Since then they have played all over the UK non stop, supporting acts such as EVILE, VIKING SKULL, MERCY HOUSE, BEHOLDER to name just a few.

 Heading back to the studio in April 2013 they finished their debut album 'A THOUSAND NIGHTS' and released it during their set at BLOODSTOCK 2013.

 Balls Deep are currently on an extensive UK tour which will see them playing with SYLOSIS, INCINERY, LIFER and a great number of other bands.

The band have a few more gigs planned once the tour is over which includes a storming show supporting the legendary M.O.D. plus a slot at Out of the ashes festival.
