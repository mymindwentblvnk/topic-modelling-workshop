Morbid Fear was a Death/Thrash Metal band that was formed in 1988 in Söderdala, Sweden. The band has released only one demo "Darkest Age" in April 1990. 
After the demo release the band changed their name to Authorize.

Last known line-up:
Larssa Johansson - Guitar, Vocals
Patrik "Putte" Leander - Bass
Micke Swed - Drums
