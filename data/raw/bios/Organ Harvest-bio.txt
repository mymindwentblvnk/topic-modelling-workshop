There are multiple bands with this name.

1. Organ Harvest is a Brutal Death Metal/Grind Band from Thionville, Lorraine,
France. They are no longer active or together. They were formed in 1999 . They were signed onto Deadsun Records. They have one full length cd titled "Bowels Waltz". Their lyrical theme consists of Gore. Their last known line up consisted of:

Florent ``Double Rectum`` - Guitar 
Yannick ``Larsen Generator`` - Bass
Alain ``Purulent Metronome`` - Drums 
Troll ``Fifi La Mitraille`` - Vocals
Olivier ``Little Woodcross Zombie`` - Vocals
Jeremy - Guitars
