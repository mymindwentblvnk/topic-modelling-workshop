Lifelong is/was a post-hardcore band based in San Diego, California, United States that's featuring members of the band Confide.

Members:
Trevor Vickers - Guitar & Screams (in the band Confide where he played bass, 2010, 2012-2013)
Joel Piper - Drums & Clean vocals (in the band Confide where he did clean vocals and drums, 2009-2010, 2012-2013)

Ross Kenyon - Vocals (in the band Confide, 2006-2010, 2012-2013) (Only on their 2018 release Revive The Masses)
