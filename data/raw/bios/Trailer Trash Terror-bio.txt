Trailer Trash Terror is a thrash metal / hardcore band from Turku, Finland.  They formed in 2005 and disbanded in 2008.  They reunited in 2015.  They consist of Jake (vocals, guitars), Juho (guitars, backing vocals), Alex (bass) and Mace (drums).  Jake was just a guitarist from 2005-2006 and he was a studio bassist in 2008.  Juho played bass from 2005-2006.  They write songs about life.  They are currently signed to Klabautermann records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TRAiler+trash+terror">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
