Leon Vynehall is British producer and DJ, born in Kent, in the south east of England. Vynehall's music combines the "percussive energy of contemporary House music" with "rich harmonies of sample-centric producers". Vynehall lists Aphex Twin, Afrika Bambaataa, Man Parrish and DJ Shadow as some of his major influences. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Leon+Vynehall">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
