The Foetal Mind is a Doom/Black Metal band from Perpignan, Languedoc-Roussillon, France. The band started in 2007. The band consists of 2 members: Lord Trowe and Lord Dreef. The Foetal Mind released 1 demo in 2007 titled "Depressive Way of The Melancholy" and 3 demos in 2008 titled "A Bleeding Dream", "True", and "Severity of Pain".
