Phalogore is a Brutal Death Metal band from Columbia influenced by Retch, Insidious Decrepancy and Dying Fetus. They play with really groovy, fast, new and brutal riffs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phalogore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
