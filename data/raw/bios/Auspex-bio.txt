There are 2 bands named Auspex.

1) Auspex is a French female-fronted symphonic power metal band from Grenoble created in 2001.

2) Auspex was a dark EBM project consisting of Florida-based electronic musicians John Gerteisen and Alan Smith (who both would later join Die Sektor) and vocalist Paul Vermeren. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Auspex">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
