There is more than one artist with this name:

1) An American metal / black metal band that was formed in the late winter of 1997. Founding members Hellspawn (Lead Guitar/Vocals), Talon (Percussion), and Tora (Bass/Vocals) recorded “From The Ashes” a 2 song demo featuring Sarah Gibbs on rhythm guitar. The demo was well received in the underground scene. In 2009 Lisa Holladay later joined their ranks as keyboardist.

2) A hard rock band formed in Minneapolis, USA that features industrial and dance influences, massive beats, mournful violins, raging guitars and intense vocals. Influences include Nine Inch Nails, Marilyn Manson, and David Bowie. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fallen+Empire">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
