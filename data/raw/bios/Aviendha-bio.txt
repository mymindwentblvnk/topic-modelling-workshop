Aviendha is a hardrock band from Bergen, Norway. They started playing together in 2004 and have since then recorded two EPs, Venom Man (2004) and the Art of Possession (2006). A new EP will be out in by the end of 2007, or the beginning of 2008. Check out their homepage http://www.aviendhaband.com. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aviendha">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
