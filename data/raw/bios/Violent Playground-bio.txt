Violent Playground may refer to:

1.) A thrash metal band from New York. They are notable for their mix of thrash metal and blues. They released one album, called "Thrashin' Blues."

2.) An alternative Filipino band. They were one of the first alternative rock bands in the local music scene and even influenced the seminal band Eraserheads. They released one album called "Primordial Soup". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Violent+Playground">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
