1) Sectioned are a metal band from Edinburgh, Scotland.  Sectioned have released 3 E.P's: Wake Up, It's Starting, Monotone and Outlier. Their debut album, Annihilated, was released in 2018. Sectioned have played alongside bands such as Full of Hell, Code Orange Kids, Textures, The Ocean Collective, Aliases and Liferuiner with their song 'Hell Away From Home' being featured on the cover CD of Metal Hammer magazine.

All of Sectioned's music is available for free/pay as you want download from their bandcamp page:
http://sectioned.bandcamp.com
