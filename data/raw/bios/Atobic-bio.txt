Copenhagen death metal band has been active on and off since 1996. The band reformed in 2004, but on their myspace page the band announced in 2009 that the band was already dead and buried.

Current members:

Martin Rosendahl - bass / drums / keyboards / vocals
Brian Eriksen - guitar
Peter Olsen - drums

Past members:

Dave Ingram - vocals
Jans Lee - guitar
Mads Haarlov - guitar / vocals
Anders Nissen - bass / vocals

Discography:

1996 Rotten Symphonies (demo)
2000 Starfucker Corp. (demo)
