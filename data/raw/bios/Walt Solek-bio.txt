Walt Solek was a Polish-American lyricist, musician, performer, and radio show host who introduced English-language lyrics into polka  music in the United States. Solek was known as the "Clown Prince of Polka" and died on 1 April, 2005 at the age of 94. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Walt+Solek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
