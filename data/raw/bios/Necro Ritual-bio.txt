Necro Ritual is a 5 piece black metal band from London, England. Taking influences from bands such as Sargeist, Mayhem, Tormentor and Darkthrone they play dark and hateful black metal. After different line-up changes the band now consists of Corseth on vocals, Necrosis and Skragmor on guitars, Mezentius on bass and Sturm on drums.

Necro Ritual are gigging again after taking time off in later 2005 and early 2006 to find new members and have played gigs with bands such as Abgott, Secrets of the Moon, Watain and others.

The band self released a 4 track demo in December 2003 and then later released a 9 track full length album through Basilisk Productions in 2004 entitled Pure Satanic Slaughter. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necro+Ritual">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
