Gae Bolga plays filthy oldschool Thrash Metal. They originate from West-Flanders, Belgium. 
First demo was released in 2008, soon to be followed by the first EP, both unsigned and called "Drink 'till Death" .
Gae Bolga is:
- John Berry (Drums)
- Danny Copperwire (Bass & vocals)
- Ian MacNamara (Guitar & vocals)
