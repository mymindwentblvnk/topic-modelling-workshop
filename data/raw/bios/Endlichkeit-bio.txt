Black metal project from Czech Republic.
Released three tapes and a 7" vinyl split with Regnum on the analog format label Fallen Empire Records (USA). 
Nine compositions represented by Roman numerals have been released in total so far. 
Amor Fati Productions (Germany) released a compilation CD as the latest release in 2016.
