Hailing from an old folk name for our favorite guy to love,Satan, these northern Virginians know heavy. This 5 piece sludge juggernaut is easily influenced by the area's abundance of traditional doom. The duel guitars and thunderous rhythm section bring a loud, southern hammer of doom to the head. Think old High on Fire mixed with The Melvins. Truly awesome. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ol%27+Scratch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
