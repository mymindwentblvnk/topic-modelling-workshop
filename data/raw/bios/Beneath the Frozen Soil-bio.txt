Beneath The Frozen Soil are a Swedish death/doom metal band formed in 2004. The band plays a dirty, blackish style of doom. The music also has some acoustic breaks, with cleaner vocals, as well as cello parts. They have three releases to date: The First Wreath EP (2005) and a Beneath The Frozen Soil/Negative Reaction Split (2007), the latter being released under the Total Rust Music label and split CD with the legendary Evoken. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Beneath+the+Frozen+Soil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
