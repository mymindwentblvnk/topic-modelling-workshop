Jadu Heart a.k.a. Dina and Faro are a masked duo from London who create a warm, cinematic, ethereal sound incorporating electronic, alt-rock and R&B influences. They were signed to Mura Masa's label Anchor Point. Now they are signed to VLF Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jadu+Heart">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
