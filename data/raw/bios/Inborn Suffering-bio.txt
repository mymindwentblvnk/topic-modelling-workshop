Inborn Suffering was a death doom metal band from Paris, France active between 2002 and 2012.

Inborn Suffering played doom metal in the veins of the Peaceville Three My Dying Bride, Anathema and Paradise Lost.

They released two albums, "Wordless Hope" in 2006 and "Regression to Nothingness" in 2012.

Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/pages/Inborn-Suffering/117955402733
Bandcamp: http://inbornsuffering.bandcamp.com

Members of the band continue to make music in Fractal Gates, Funeralium and Mourning Dawn.
