Brave Irene are an all-girl band from Vancouver formed in 2009 by Rose Melberg (ex Tiger Trap and The Softies). Other members are Caitlin Gilroy, Jessica Wilkin, Amanda Pezzutto and Laura Hatfield. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brave+Irene">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
