Dukatalon is an Israeli three piece Sludge Metal band formed in 2007. In the beginning of their path they found a target audience in Israel and rolled down to tour Europe.
Currently the band has one full length album and is signed in the major label Relapse Records.

Releases:
Dukatalon - Dukatlon (EP, 2007)
Dukatalon - Saved By Fear (Full Length, 2009)
