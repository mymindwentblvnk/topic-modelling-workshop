Mohsen Yeganeh (Persian: محسن یگانه‎; born May 13, 1985 in Gonbad-e Qabus[1]) is a popular Iranian vocalist, singer and songwriter and musician, composer, and arranger. He is one of the most popular singers in Iran and His title is "Mr Genius"[5][6]
