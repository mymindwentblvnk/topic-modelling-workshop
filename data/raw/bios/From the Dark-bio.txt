There are multiple artists called From the Dark

1. From the Dark were a black metal band from the city of Tauranga in New Zealand. They were active in the late 90s, and are now defunct. They released one demo, "In the Last Days of the Sun" in 1997 and one album, "In the Shadow of Kaos" in 1999.

2. From the Dark are a dark, melodic, and heavy power metal from Alabama, formed with the intent of utilizing vocal melody alongside thrash metal and death metal-style riffing in progressive arrangements. In 2013, the band released their first record, "The Opera Ghost", a "story" album chronicling the Phantom of the Opera as well as a few songs about the literature of Edgar Allan Poe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/From+the+Dark">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
