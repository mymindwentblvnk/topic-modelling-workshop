Art Of Mutilation were a Melodic Death/Thrash Metal band from the United Kingdom. They released the 'Mortality' Demo in 2005. The vocalist who performed on the demo Lucy  is now the vocalist for Mechanical God Creation and The Anger. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Art+Of+Mutilation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
