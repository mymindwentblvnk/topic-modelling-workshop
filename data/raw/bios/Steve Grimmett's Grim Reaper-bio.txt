Singer Steve Grimmett decided to regroup his old band Grim Reaper under this new name in the year 2006.  They formed in Swindon, England.  They released one EP and an album under this name.  The EP is entitled Alive and Kicking and the first album is entitled Walking in the Shadows.   On October 11, 2019, they released their 2nd album entitled At the Gates

Grim Reaper featuring frontman Steve Grimmett will be releasing their first live album Reaping The Whirlwind – Live British Steel Festival 2018 under the name Steve Grimmett’s Grim Reaper in 2022 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Steve+Grimmett%27s+Grim+Reaper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
