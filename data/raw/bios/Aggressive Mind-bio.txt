Aggressive Mind is a german Progressive Death Metal Band. Members: Manfred Höher (Bass, Vocals), Florian Fischer (Drums, clean Vocals), Stephan John (Guitar, clean Vocals), Tobias Kretzschmann (Guitar), Benjamin Pietzner (Guitar) & Jörg Zimmermann (Keyboard). Currently they produced only 1 EP, the "Pond of Desperation EP", but they will hit the studios soon, again.
