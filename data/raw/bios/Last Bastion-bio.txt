Last Bastion is a power metal band based out of Seattle, Washington, USA.  Founded in January 2012, the band set out to create a "melodic, epic metal" sound in stark contrast to the typical Seattle grunge scene.  On April 4th, 2014, Last Bastion released their debut album "The Road to Redemption." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Last+Bastion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
