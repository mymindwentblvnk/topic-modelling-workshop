Eastern Lane are an English indie band from Berwick-upon-Tweed. They have released two albums: Shades of Black in 2003 and The Article in 2005. Both albums are on the Rough Trade record label. Their song 'Feed Your Addiction' is currently featured in an HSBC advertisement. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eastern+Lane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
