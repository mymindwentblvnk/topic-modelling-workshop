Not to be confused with the group The Emotions

1- Emotions is an Italian eurodance act produced and arranged by M. Braccagni, Roberto Salsotto and Enzo Gallo Salsotto.
They recorded the single "Feeling Emotions". www.discogs.com/artist/Emotions

2- Emotions is a techno act produced by S. Pagliari. It released the single "Jump To The Sky".

3- Emotions is a Danish pop band that recorded the album "Emotions" in 1997. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Emotions">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
