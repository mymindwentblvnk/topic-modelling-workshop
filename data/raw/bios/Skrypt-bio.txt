SKRYPT is a 5 piece Thrash metal band from Hyderabad. The band has a unique blend of Groovy Thrash metal mixed with Progressive patterns and melodies. The band made its debut at a pub gig on 21 July 2007 after completing its line up in the last days of June. They are known for their intense performances and intricate yet still groovy and melodic style of composing music. 

The lineup is -

Scenic G               - Vocals
Joel K                    - Guitar
Ravi Nidamarthy  - Guitar
Rajiv Kumar         - Drums
Abbas Razvi         - Bass
