Decaarta is a melodic groove/thrash metal band from Chichester, UK.

The band was founded in 2010, and after several member changes, they got underway with writing an album. 2014 saw the release of their debut independent release Exile the Wicked. Early 2017 vocalist Sam left the band and was replaced by Will.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Decaarta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
