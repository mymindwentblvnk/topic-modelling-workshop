Haud Mundus (featuring members of Rebirth of Nefast, Myrkr, Slidhr) is an Irish Black Metal band formed in 2009, that presents insight into the failed spirit of man.
Compositionally, Haud Mundus can best be compared to a mixture of its members aforementioned projects.
