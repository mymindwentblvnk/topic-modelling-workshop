There is more than one artist with this name:

1) X-Odus is a side-project of British Trance producer Stuart James Langelaan, better known as Lange. Only one single was released under this name. "I'm In Love Again". It featured vocals from Xan Tyler, who worked on Matt Darey's Tekara project the following year. Remixes of "I'm In Love Again" came from Kenny Hayes, Mike Monday and Mr. Haywood.

2) X-odus is a black metal from Germany.
