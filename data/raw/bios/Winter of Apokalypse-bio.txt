Winter of Apokalypse is a black metal band hailing from Portland, Oregon in the USA. With former members of Thy Infernal and Demoncy, Winter of Apokalypse are quickly gaining strength in the black metal underground. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Winter+Of+Apokalypse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
