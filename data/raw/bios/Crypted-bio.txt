Crypted are a Thrash/Progressive Death Metal band from Chennai,India. 

Members -
Siva Sbk : Vocals
Arvind Muralidharan : Guitar
Nikhil Bandale : Guitar
Avinash Raghuram : Bass
Vishnu Reddy : Drums

Crypted are influenced by  Death, Slayer, Megadeth, Nile, Gorod, Kreator, Sadus, Gojira, Decapitated, Meshuggah, Cryptopsy, Atheist, Morbid Angel, Cynic, MyndSnare, Cannibal Corpse, Testament.,etc <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crypted">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
