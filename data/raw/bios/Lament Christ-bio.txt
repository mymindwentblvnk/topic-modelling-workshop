Lament Christ is an atmospheric doom metal band founded in 1995 in Lima, Peru. They are one of the earliest examples of funeral doom metal, mixing death growls, melancholic keyboards, violin, and lyrics about darkness, depression and solitude. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lament+Christ">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
