The Gates Of Slumber was a doom metal band from  Indianapolis, USA. They play old school style of doom metal in the traditions of Saint Vitus, Cirith Ungol, Candlemass and the likes. Their band name is taken from the Cianide song Gates of Slumber from the album A Descent Into Hell. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Gates+of+Slumber">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
