Death Metal/Grindcore hybrid from Buffalo, NY, formed in 1991. Released a rehearsal tape the same year. Band members were also in several death metal bands from the local scene, including Tirant Sin (pre-Cannibal Corpse, Carnal Dissection and Grotesque Infection. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Putrefest">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
