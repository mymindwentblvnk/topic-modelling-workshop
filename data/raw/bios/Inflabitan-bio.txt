Norwegian black metal.  Early-to-mid 90’s one man band/solo project by Sigmund 'Inflabitan' Hansen, later live session member of DHG. 

Around 1995, label Ancient Lore Creations proposed a split 10” release with Inflabitan and Lamented Souls, but it never materialized. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inflabitan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
