Vuvr is a band from Czech Republic founded in 1992. The band began playing hardcore punk-influenced metal but later began creating Progressive Death Metal/Jazz Fusion.

Their name is an acronym for "Výzkumný ústav vodních radostí". 

Line-up: Adam Klopar (bass & vocals), Vít Jindřichovský (guitar), Radek Kopr (guitar) and Olda Sušil (drums).

They have released a demo tape "Slaves of Time" in 1997 and a full-length "Pilgrimage" in 2001 on Shindy Productions.
