The band was formed in early 2011 from an idea of dying Poet's funeral Litanies and Restless Wanderer Through the Dark Agony and Gardens of Sorrows, attracted by their passion for the art of funeral doom and depressive gothic beauty, their intent is to go with their verses on the dark paths of this vale of tears called life and their music as a soundtrack of suffering and pain, only half a day to reach the supreme eternal peace. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Trails+Of+Sorrow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
