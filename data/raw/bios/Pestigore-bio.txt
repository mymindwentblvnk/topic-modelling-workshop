Pestigore is a death metal band from Järvenpää, Finland that was formed in 1990.  The band consists of  Kari Kräkin on vocals, Saku Manninen & Teme Grönberg  on guitars,  Pekka Sauvolainen on drums (1990-1993)/bass (2015-present) and Pete Raatikainen on drums.  Past members include Ville Vilpponen on bass, backing vocals and Hannu Laanterä on guitars.  

Members of Pestigore were also involved in a grindcore/deathgrind project named Rotten Bowel. Pestigore released four demos in the years 1991 and 1992.  A rehearsal demo and Within the Dark Mist in 1991.  In the year 1992, they released a promo tape and Twisted Perversions... Unbearable Delight.  They disbanded in 1993.  As time went by, they reunited in 2015.  For more on Pestigore, visit their Facebook page <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pestigore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
