Horned is a band from Marseille, France. Their line up is Jerome Simonin (Vocals), Gaultier Paulez (Guitar), Nicolas Martin-Rosset (Bass) and Mael Taguelmint (Drums). They are currently signed to Rising Nemesis Records. They released their sophomore album Eminence March 20, 2020.
