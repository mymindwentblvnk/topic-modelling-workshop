Mark Curry is an American rock musician and singer-songwriter. He is best known for his single "Sorry About the Weather", a number 20 hit on the Billboard Alternative Songs chart in 1992. He last released an album in 2008.

Curry formed his first band, Crystal Sphere, with "White Boy Larry," Fernando Barrios, Ricky Rutherford and Aaron "El Hefe" Abeyta. In 1992, Curry separating from Crystal Sphere. He released he first solo album "It's Only Time" the same year.

Subsequently based in Sacramento CA and known for his raw raw voice and rugged edge, Curry played with The Hell House Band which released a CD featuring his longtime drinking buddy and step-brother El Hefe, a member of NOFX.
