Sean Sison is a songwriter, singer, and producer from Milwaukee Wisconsin. He plays multiple instruments that you can hear featured in his production. Sean released his first project titled “Bloom” in 2017. the project has reached multiple Spotify playlist and millions on streams. He released the singles “Rome”, and “Break Me Down” in 2018. For 2019 Sean is releasing his second project titled “Good And Bad” the project will consist of 10 of his songs that he wrote and produced himself. The project will also have a music video for the song “I Don’t Wanna” which is directed and shot by Milwaukee filmmaker Damien Blue. All of Sean’s music is recorded, and mixed by Charley Cooks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sean+Sison">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
