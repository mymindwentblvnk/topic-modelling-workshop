During the winter 2008 after dozens of line-ups and decades of throwing their heads on the wall, this orchestra took the very first form. Four individuals (Ville, Timo, Janne, Mikko) were the gentlemen who wanted to escape the blunt and conventional reality with clashing songs written more and more to-the-point. Honest and straight forward metal. At that point they wondered along calling their selves as Treatment. Along came another guitarist (Tuomas) and about the same time the bass player took off. No distress, an old friend (Tommi) who was bound to kick the shit out of a four-string was attached to the band.
