The Hart Valley Drifters formed during the US folk revival of the early 1960's. The group featured a 20-year old Jerry Garcia on lead vocals, banjo and guitar, Robert Hunter on bass, David Nelson on guitar, Ken Frankel on banjo, fiddle and guitar and Norm Van Maastricht on dobro. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hart+Valley+Drifters">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
