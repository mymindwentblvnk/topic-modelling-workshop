Nekrost is a brazilian metal band from Manaus - AM playing a Technical Thrash Metal. Nekrost fuses the tricky riffs of Prog with the speed and fury of Thrash and Death Metal. The band was first known by its demo cd named "All My Hate" released in 1992, the intensity of Nekrost's live performances got the band to win the 2011 Metal Battle Contest and granted his first international gig, on the stage from Wacken Open Air Festival in Germany, alongside with bands like Blind Guardian, Ozzy Osbourne, Judas Priest, Children Of Bodom and many others. 

Actually Nekrost have released his first full length album in April 2014, recorded at Studio Supersônico in Manaus by Beto Montrezol and mixed at the Mr. Som Studios by the prestigious Heros Trench (Korzus bassist). The album contains 9 tracks that resumes all the musical madness and insanity the band has acquired along the years and was released in september 2014.
