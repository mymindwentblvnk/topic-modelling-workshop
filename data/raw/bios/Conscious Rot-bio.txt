Conscious Rot is Lithuanian death metal group formed in 1992.
 Formerly known as Bloody Altar. After the split-up the band reformed as Shadowdances in 1995

Last known line-up:
Raima - Guitars
Juodas - Drums / Vocals
Laimis - Guitars

Former/past member(s):
Omenas - Guitar
Vyga - Bass
Latras - Vocals
Andre - Bass
Makas - Guitars

Discography:
1992 - P.I.T.E. Demo 
1992 - Paranoia in the Evening Demo 
1993 - Blood For Blood Demo 
1993 - Congenital Passivity Demo 
1994 - The Soil Demo, 1994
