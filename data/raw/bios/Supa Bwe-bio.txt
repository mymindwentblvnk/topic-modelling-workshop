Frederick S. Burton, known professionally as Supa Bwe is an American hip hop recording artist, producer, and audio engineer from the West Side of Chicago, Illinois. He released his debut album, Finally Dead on December 6, 2017 featuring Xavier Omar, Saba and more. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Supa+Bwe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
