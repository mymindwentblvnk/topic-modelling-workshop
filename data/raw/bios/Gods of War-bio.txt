Gods of War is an extreme metal band from Liverpool, UK. Their music is a mixture of many genres of metal such as Power, Death, Thrash, and Black metal so that there is something for everyone to enjoy. They take inspiration from bands such as Wintersun, Ensiferum, Carpathian Forest, King Diamond, Sodom, GWAR and Stormwarrior, among many others.

Danny Hell (Lead Vocals, part time Colonial Marine)
Kris Dobson (Lead drums and Alien noises)
Matt Hughes (Lead guitar, lead backing vocals and salsa-related devastation)
