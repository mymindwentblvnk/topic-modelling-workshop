Hate Kommand is a Black Metal project from Fontana, California which began in 2006.

Contact: hatekommand@yahoo.com

Started out under the name Lord Blasphemer, but changed it to Hate Kommand.

Minerva played session guitar for the band at one time.

The band is not NSBM related.

Lineup:
Hate Kommander Blasphemer - Vocals, Guitar, Bass and Drums (Nuclear Desecration, Street Force, Nocturnal Blood)

Discography:
