Panzer X is a Peter's (Vader) heavy metal side project. The first EP 'Steel Fist' 02.2006 was recorded with Pajak from Esqarial, Kupczyk from Turbo, CETI and Vitek from Decapitated. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Panzer+X">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
