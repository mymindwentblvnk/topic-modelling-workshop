Former member of Australian indie pop The Moles, Richard Davies has released three solo albums, There's Never Been a Crowd Like This, Telegraph, and Barbarians.  He has also released two albums as Cardinal in collaboration with Eric Matthews.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Richard+Davies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
