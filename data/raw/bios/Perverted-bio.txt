Belgian Indie-noise-weirdness band, founded 1984. Formerly Perverted by Desire. Latest release Vote Reverend Greed (2010). Group core is drum, bass, guitar, though use of guest artists enables a wide-ranging sound palette, reminiscent of The Fall, PIL, Butthole Surfers and Sonic Youth.
