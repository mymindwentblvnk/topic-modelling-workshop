THUNDERFORGE is a melodic heavy/power metal band based in East Longmeadow, Massachusetts which formed in 2012 seeking to create fast-and-epic, face-rippingly intense heavy metal, inspired by varying degrees of guitar wizardry, old-school madness, and modern metal intensity.

Band Members:
Christian "Siege" Morini - lead guitar/backing vocals
Jules Conroy - rhythm guitar/backing vocals
Adam Morini - lead/backing vocals
John McLaughlin - bass guitar
