A Death Metal band from the Philippines.
The band was active from 1992 to 1999. They reformed in 2005 and released their first official demo in 2008.

Current Members are:
Allan Goldara - Drums
Ricardo Gerri Duenas - Guitar
Andrew Pallarca - Guitar
Eric Caballero - Bass
