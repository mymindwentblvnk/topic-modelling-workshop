There are multiple artists called Ziggurat.

1. Ziggurat are a black/death metal duo from Israel comprised of Tohu(guitars, bass) and Mørk (vocals, guitars).

2.  Ziggurat are a gothic death/black metal band from Turkey, formed in 1994 by Cosan and Çaglayan.

3. Ziggurat are a four piece instrumental band from Austin, Texas, United States of America.
