1. Depressive Black Metal Band from Germany, constisting of Nox and Nekromanteion,
Schmerz released their debut album called Chronika in 2008. Chronika is available as regular jewelcase version or as digipack (limited to 100 handnumbered copies).

2. Polish Industrial/Power Electronics project. One of the members is M. Żentara, well known for the  Mgła and Kriegsmaschine BM projects. 

3. Stylized all in caps, SCHMERZ is a one man dark minimal/cold electronics from Lima, Peru. Their self-titled debut was released in Jan 2020.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Schmerz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
