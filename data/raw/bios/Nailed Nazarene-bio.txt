Death Metal band from Topolcany (Slovakia) started in the begining of the 90'. They have out there first demo "Blood Colours My Thoughts" in 1992. Two more demos ("I'a Y' gs - Othoth" and "Hosanna Tibi de Profundis") was released in 1993 before they split-up. Two members formed later the band Thalarion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nailed+Nazarene">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
