Ocrilim is one of the solo monikers of Mick Barr (guitarist of Orthrelm, Krallice, Octis, etc based in Washington D.C., United States).  Mick's first release under this monkier was the Ocrilorx-1 7" in 2003.

Mick has said the following in comparison to his other solo project, Octis: "Ocrilim is Mick Barr overthinking. Octis is Mick Barr underthinking." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ocrilim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
