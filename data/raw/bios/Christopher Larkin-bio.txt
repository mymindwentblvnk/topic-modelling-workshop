Christopher Larkin is an award winning composer and sound designer from Australia. He does sound for games, film and TV. Some of the projects he has composed for are: Hollow Knight, Pacman 256 and Figaro Pho. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Christopher+Larkin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
