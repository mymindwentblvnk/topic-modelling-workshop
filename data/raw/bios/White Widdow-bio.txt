White Widdow was formed in St Kilda, Melbourne in 2008 with the purpose of playing 80's arena style Melodic Hard Rock, drawing from influences such as Survivor, Journey, Treat, House Of Lords, White Sister, Roxus, De Arrow, Giuffria, Danger Danger, Surgin' and many more.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/White+Widdow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
