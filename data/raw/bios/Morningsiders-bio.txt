Morningsiders is a band composed of Columbia undergraduates. Their genre is "Mumford and Sons-lite."
They feature:
Magnus Ferguson, Lead Vocals and Guitar
Reid Jenkins, Violin and Vocals
Rob Frech, Piano and Keyboards
Vladimir Bernstein, Bass and Vocals
