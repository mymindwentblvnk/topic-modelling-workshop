mSdoS was born in Athens in 1974. From the early age of 12 he was already hosting his own radio show, something which played a leading role in his future. "I feel incredibly lucky to have lived throughout the evolution of the “new” dance scene from the end of the 80s with Acid and Techno music." However the sounds which marked his life were that of Breakbeat, today’s Oldschool Hardcore,Drum’n’bass. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/mSdoS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
