There are five bands called From the Depths:

1. Black/Death Metal band from Cleveland, OH USA

2. From the Depths is an anarcho-punk band from North Carolina, featuring former members of Catharsis and Requiem.  Like Requiem and Catharsis, From the Depths are connected to the CrimethInc. Ex-Workers' Collective.  They released their first album "Germinate" in February 2009.

3. From the Depths is a now defunct technical death metal band from Washington D.C. with touring and ex members of Force Fed Broken Glass, Dig Your Grave, and Age of Ruin.

4. From The Depths is a Progressive Deathcore band from Christchurch, New Zealand. The Band was formed in 2008 by Will Duncan//Michael Brew. They are releasing an e.p. very soon.

5. From The Depths is a Thrash / Death Metal band from Sheffield, United Kingdom. The band was formed in 2008, and have undergone a few member changes in that time. No release's have yet been made. 

6. From The Depths is a hardcore punk/thrash band from Moscow, Russia. They released self-titled EP in 2011.
