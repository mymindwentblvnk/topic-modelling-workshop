Jo Tae-kyoung (Hangul: 조태경), born December 2, 1994, known professionally as About (Hangul: 어바웃; stylised in all caps), is a South Korean singer, songwriter and producer. He debuted as an independent artist in September 2019 with the single "Wither" and released his first EP "Miserable" in March 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/About">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
