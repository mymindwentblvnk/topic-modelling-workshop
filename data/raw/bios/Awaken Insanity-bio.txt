A three man band comprised of Bennett Leung on lead guitar and lead vocals, Brandon Quan on bass and backing vocals, and Jeff Curtis on drums and backing vocals.  

Heavily influenced by Children of Bodom, Norther, Kalmah, Yngwie Malmsteen, X Japan, Dream Theater, Megadeth, Metallica, Protest the Hero, Avenged Sevenfold and many other metal bands, Awaken Insanity continues to develop a sound that blends together the many different sub-genres of heavy metal.

Bennett Leung's influences on the guitar tend to sit on the more neo-classical side of players, including Alexi Laiho, Yngwie Malmsteen, Jani Liimatainen, and Paul Gilbert.
