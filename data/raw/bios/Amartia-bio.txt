Amartia is a French gothic/atmospheric/progressive metal band with female vocals created in 1999. Their music is influenced by bands like The Gathering, Dream Theater, Lacuna Coil or Ayreon. Their latest album "Delicately" has been released in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amartia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
