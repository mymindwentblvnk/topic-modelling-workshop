Ancestral Damnation are a Melodic Death Metal band from Kharkiv, Ukraine. Formed in 2004, they released the 'Live In VIA' Demo in 2006, followed by the 'Versiya Realnosti' EP in 2008.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ancestral+Damnation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
