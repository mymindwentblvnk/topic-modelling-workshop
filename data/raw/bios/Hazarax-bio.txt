Formed in 1988 they play a type of progressive, melodic death metal. Lots of catchy death riffs here, and the vocals are put forth in a frenzied blurr, which is something different. In March of 1990, Hazarax went into the studios to record their debut demo Altered Faith Disorder which contains 3 well structured songs of energy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hazarax">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
