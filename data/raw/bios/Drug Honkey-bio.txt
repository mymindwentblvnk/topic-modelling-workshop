Drug Honkey was formed in 1999 in Chicago, Illinois, USA. They were formerly known as Chronic Illogic and released a 21 track demo in 1996. They experiment with a mixture of psychedelic death/doom metal with signs of dub. The band is currently signed to Diabolical Conquest Records.

Discography:
2002 - Drug Honkey
2005 - Hail Satan
2007 - Quatro de Mayo (live)
2008 - Death Dub
2012 - Ghost in the Fire

Current line-up:

B.H. Honkey - Drums
