Gorgonea Prima is an Atmo Electronic Industrial Black Metal band from Kladno, Czech Republic.

Current members:
Hogath - voices, guitars, keyboards, samples, drum programing
R - guitars

Former members:
Tyrael - guitars, keybords, samples
Loathsome - bass
