Young death metal band that started in 2013 with drummer Grega Cestnik, bassist Lejla Kozlica and guitar players Uroš Polovšak and Žiga Ring. Final line up was made in 2014 when Tilen Kirbiš joined the band as vocalist. At first they played covers and then started to write their own material and had also their first gig, which was in June the same year on a festival called Park spet dogaja in their hometown – Velenje. After that, the number of concerts started to grow. They also played with the most known slovenian group Noctiferia, czech grinders Gutalax etc. They released their first album SLaughterlust in march 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carnifliate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
