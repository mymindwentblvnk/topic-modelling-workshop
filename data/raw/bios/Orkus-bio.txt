Black metal band from Germany (Cologne, North Rhine-Westphalia) formed 1996, but splited-up. Lyrical themes are about death, anti-Christian and paganism. 

Orkus also stands for Jaroslav "Orkus" Konečný, who is Czech Tech-house DJ. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Orkus">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
