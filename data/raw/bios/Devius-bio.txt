Progressive Death Metal band from Cordoba, Argentina. The band was formed in 2006 and so far they released a demo in 2008 and an EP "Infinity Echoes" in 2009.

Current (2010) line-up:
Walter Pidone - Vocals
Gastón Praddaude - Guitars
Fabián Pignataro - Bass
