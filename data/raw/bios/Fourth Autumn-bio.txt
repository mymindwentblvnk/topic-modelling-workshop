Fourth Autumn is a four-piece blackened death metal band from South Wales. Their influences extend to bands such as The Black Dahlia Murder, Cradle of Filth and a slight peppering of Rick Astley for added brutality.


Members:

Julian Cousins - Forest Throat and String Incantation

Daniel Cousins - Coffin Hammers and Moonlit Howls

Jason Robinson - Night Chords and Sodomy
