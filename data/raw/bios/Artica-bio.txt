There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Artica is an Italian gothic rock band formed in 1989. The album "Plastic Terror" featured also some harder tracks with punk and metal influences compared to the more "pure" goth from the beginning of the band.

2) Artica is a metalcore band from Dubois, PA, United States. http://www.myspace.com/weareartica
