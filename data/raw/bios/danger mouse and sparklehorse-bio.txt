Streamable as Danger Mouse & Sparklehorse, this was a collaboration between Americans artist/producer Brian Burton (aka Danger Mouse), multi-instrumentalist Mark Linkous (aka Sparklehorse) and film director David Lynch.

Their album Dark Night of the Soul features  James Mercer (of The Shins), The Flaming Lips, Gruff Rhys (of Super Furry Animals), Jason Lytle (of Grandaddy), Julian Casablancas (of The Strokes), Frank Black (of the Pixies), Iggy Pop, Nina Persson (of The Cardigans), Suzanne Vega & Vic Chesnutt.
