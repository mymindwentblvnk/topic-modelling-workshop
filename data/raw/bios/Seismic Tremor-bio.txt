Seismic Tremor is an alternative thrash/groove metal band from Hamburg, Germany. Formed in 2005, the lineup was consisting of Marco Soumikh (vocals), Sascha Karl (lead guitar, backing vocals), Gernot Heitmann (rhythm guitar), Erik Herrmann (bass) and Torben Neuss (drums).
