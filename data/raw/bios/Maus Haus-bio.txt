Maus Haus is a 6 man ensemble electronic band from San Francisco, California. They are known for their highly eclectic and varied electronic sound intertwined with heavy bass and saxophone for a "sound collage" effect. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maus+Haus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
