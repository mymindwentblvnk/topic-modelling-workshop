Grindcore from Ventura, CA. Recording our full length album for an early 2012 release. Featuring members/ex-members of Fatalist, Dirty Dead, Decrypt, Stump, Burning at the Stake. Neil Burkdoll-Guitars/Vocals, Brad Vanderzee-Drums, Art Hayes-Bass, Dylan Kuhn-Vocals, with help from Chris Moriarty sometimes on Live Bass Guitar.
