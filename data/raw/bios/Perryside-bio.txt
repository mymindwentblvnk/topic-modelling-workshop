Perryside is one of the first  metalcore bands formed in Bosnia&Herzegovina after old school metal scene. They were formed in 1997,when some members of ex-Parricide and ex-Eternal Remembrance came back from Germany. Disbanded soon after releasing their one and only album.

Line up:
Mirza Salihagić - vocals and guitars, 
Džemal Trakić - guitars, 
Mirsad Nalić - bass,
Goran Sakić - drums.
