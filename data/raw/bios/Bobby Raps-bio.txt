Robert John Richardson, better known by his stage name Bobby Raps, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. Born December 22, 1992 in Saint Paul, Minnesota. He has been a member of Thestand4rd, Audio Perm, and Dequexatron X000.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bobby+Raps">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
