1.) WTN (aka: War Torn Nation) was a death metal/grindcore band from Singapore, formed in 1998. They released 5 splits between 2000 - 2005 & the full length Rotting In Pestilence in 2004.

The compilation Black Hearse was released in 2006, around the time of the bands demise.

2.) WTN (Within The Noise) is an infamous podcast/radio show featuring adamdude, AlanJLegend and Smutty.
