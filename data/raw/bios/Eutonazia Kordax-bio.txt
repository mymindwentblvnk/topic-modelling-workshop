Eutonazia Kordax was formed in 2002 in Pervouralsk, Russia. The members are AL (drums), Sagas (guitars), Helgi (bassguitar), Mish_Gun (synthesizers) and Mortis (vocals). They play a style of symphonic black metal, catchy, melodic and poppy yet agressive, dark and sinister. In 2006 they self-released their EP Мечты (Mechty) and in 2007 another one called Конструкция Плоти, which are available for for free on their website or here on Last.FM. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eutonazia+Kordax">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
