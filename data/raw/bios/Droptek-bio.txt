Droptek, real name Lewis Munns, is a electronic music producer, Dj, and live act from Bristol, UK. His sound covers a wide range of tempos and styles such as Electro, Dubstep, Glitch-hop, and Drum and Bass. Releasing on labels such as Monstercat, Droptek is rapidly building a loyal fan base across the globe.

His career started with the tracks ‘Bang’ and ‘Explode’, which caught the attention of Monstercat and gained him a solid grounding within the scene. Munns’ music grabbed the attention of Figure gaining the judge’s vote for the ‘Must Destroy’ remix competition. He continues to turn heads in the industry, remixing for the likes of Krewella and Pegboard Nerds.

Droptek’s name and reputation is rapidly gaining momentum, with numerous releases hitting the top 5 on Beatport and his hard work and determination proves he only has the top in his sights. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Droptek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
