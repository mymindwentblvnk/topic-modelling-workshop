1) Thrash metal band from Slovakia formed in 1986 in Prešov. They've released two demos in the 90's: "Doctor Thrash" (1992) and "Watching Nasty World" (1995). Striga also released a 2015 EP "Prízraky hrôzy". 
Members:
Marián Dráč - vocals;
Ján Šarišský - guitar;
Ján Kavulič - bass;
František Železník - drums.
