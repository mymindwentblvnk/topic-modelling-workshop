Various Artists.

1) Colombian death metal band formed in 1986. The band has been active on and off with different members since 1987. Now called Agressor

They released demos in 1987, 2002, 2003 and 2004.

Participated also on the Rodrigo D. - No Futuro Sampler LP in 1988 and in the tribute to Masacre (Col) with the song (2. Agressor - Sangrienta Muerte (7:08)).

2) Bulgarian doom/death/thrash band which released two full-lengths, Idols of the Suffering in 1993 and A View Back to the Future in 1994. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agresor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
