There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Arkana is the audio-visual fantasy creation of Samuel J Finn.

He invites listeners to engage with his music as the primary thread of a narrative mythology, rather than relegating sound to a subtle supporting role. With a deep understanding of how to paint atmospheres, Arkana delivers musical journeys rich with ethereal soundscapes, mysterious liminal passages, and intricate ear candy that will envelope you in an immersive, cinematic experience sure to leave you wanting more.


2) Arkana was a 1990s Italian dark death doom metal band from Alpignano, Piedmont, Italy. They only released a demo titled "Demoni" in 1996.

3) Arkana is a band from Cleveland, OH, United States. They put out a demo in 2006 and released their first full-length album, "Expelled From Paradise" on November 13th, 2009. 

4) Arkana was an 1980s electro-pop band from Rijeka, Yugoslavia (now Croatia). Participated in the compilation "Metalka 35" with the song "Bolid" and released one album, "Noću mi se često" (HELIDON FLP 05058) in 1986.
