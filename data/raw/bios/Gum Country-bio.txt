Gum Country is Los Angles Based via Vancouver, and consist of the Canadian duo Courtney Garvin (from The Courtneys) as the guitarist and singer, and Connor Mayer on keyboards and drums/drum machine. They combine C-86 indie pop and shoegaze.  Their first album, Somewhere was released in June 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gum+Country">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
