Amputation Spree is a one man brutal death metal from Raleigh, North Carolina. The music is made by Theodore Spencer Van Dyk, and is highly influenced by early to mid nineties New York style death metal such as Suffocation, Immolation and Pyrexia. It is also influenced by slamming brutal death metal bands such as Cephalotripsy and Katalepsy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amputation+Spree">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
