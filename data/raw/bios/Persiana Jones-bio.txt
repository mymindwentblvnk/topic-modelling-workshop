Persiana Jones is an Italian ska punk band formed in February 1988 in Rivarolo Canavese, a village in the Turin North Area. They have appeared on television numerous times and toured countries like Germany, Switzerland and the Czech Republic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Persiana+Jones">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
