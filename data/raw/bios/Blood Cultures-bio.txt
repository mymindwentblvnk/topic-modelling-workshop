Emerging from New Jersey; the nameless, faceless Blood Cultures has caught the internet's attention with his inexplicably catchy dreamy-synth beats. With a persona mirroring that of Jai Paul and clear influences from The Knife, LCD Soundsystem, and Youth Lagoon this mysterious figure is sure to gain even more attention in the coming months.
