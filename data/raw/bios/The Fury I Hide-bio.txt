The Fury I Hide is a Melodic Death Metal/Thrash Metal band from Mexico (Mexico City). 

Their Current Line-up:

Phillip Yepez - Vocals
Oscar Ramirez - Drums
Cuauthemoc Rodriguez - Guitars
Guillermo Don - Bass

They Have an EP, Awaken The Fury out on American Line Productions that was recorded, mixed and mastered at Mat Studios 2006.
