Sodomizer is a Blackened Speed Metal band with old school touchs and high pitched vocals from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
Formed in 1999 and have since released 2 demos, 2 "best-of", 2 full-lengths and 1 split. The band is currently signed to the danish Horror Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sodomizer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
