BoyPanda (aka Dorin Herea) is an up and coming trap music producer. It is hard to put a country next to his name. He was born in Romania but spent most of his youth in Greece living in the capital of Crete (Heraklion). In the recent years, he has moved to Scotland (Aberdeen), this is where he started producing under the alias - BoyPanda. 
Prior to his reemergence as BoyPanda he has been producing under the alias - Domenik.
