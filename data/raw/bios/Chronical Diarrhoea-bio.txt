Chronical Diarrhoea is a Crossover/Thrash Metal band from Germany. They formed in 1988 and split up around 1991. Two members went on to form Bohren & der Club of Gore. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chronical+Diarrhoea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
