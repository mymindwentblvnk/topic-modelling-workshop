Sopor is known by their energyful music and strong guitar sounds. Sopor - russian gothic band. St.-Petersburg...
They were beginning with playing death metal on their 1st album, "Sorry" then moving to gothic metal on "Phlogiston Theory" and then to gothic alt metal on their new single, "Betrayal".

Current line-up
Alexey "Sapper" Spiridonov - Vocals/Guitars
Sergey "Still" Kalnoy - Bass 
Mikhail "Minissstrel" Emelyanov - Drums
Natalia Kalnova - Keyboards
Alexey "Elias" - Violins
