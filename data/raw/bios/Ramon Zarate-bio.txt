Ramon Zarate is a belgian stonerrock band, formed in 2006. They released their self titled album in 2008, and have shared the stage with Ultraphallus, Dozer, Alabama Thunderpussy, Firebird among others. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ramon+Zarate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
