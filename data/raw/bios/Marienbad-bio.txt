There are two musical projects by the name of "Marienbad":

The first is a Doom metal project featuring members from Eisregen. The vocals range from clean singing, spoken word to black metal screeching. Notable is the subtle use of organ and flute in the arrangements. Their first album "Werk 1 - Nachtfall" wasl released on 05/27/2011. The album is presented in both English and German language versions.
