Wachenfeldt is a Blackened Death/Thrash metal band from Helsingland in the north of Sweden. The band consist of Thomas von Wachenfeldt on bass, guitar and vocals, Martin Björklund on lead guitar and Jon Skäre on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wachenfeldt">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
