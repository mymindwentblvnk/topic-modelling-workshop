Havayoth is a gothic/doom metal band formed in 2000, in Sweden. Their sound can be described as gothic/doom metal, inspiring a relaxing but at the same time dark, majestic and grave atmosphere.

Havayoth released their first and only album, called "His Creation Reversed", in 2000.

Their current members are Marcus Norman (Guitar and keyboards), Morgan Hansson (Bass) and Andreas Hedlund, also known as Vintersorg (Vocals).
