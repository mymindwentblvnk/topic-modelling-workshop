Animations (formerly known as labyrinth.pl) is a polish prog-metal-fusion band. It's been started in 2006 by four musicians fascinated by all kinds of progressive instrumental music. 

Animations released their debut instrumental album in 2007 and the follow-up Reset Your Soul, that included Darek Bartosiewicz on vocals in 2009. At this point the band is going through vocalist changes and working on a third album.

Current line-up:

Bartek Bisaga - bass,
Kuba Debski - guitars,
Tomek Konopka - keyboards,
