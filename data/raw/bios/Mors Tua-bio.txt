Black/Epic Metal band from Italy. Formed in 2002, they have released 2 demos and 2 albums. They play short, seemingly unstructured songs, and have a full-time member playing a trumpet. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mors+Tua">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
