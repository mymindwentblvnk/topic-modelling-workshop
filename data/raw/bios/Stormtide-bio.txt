STORMTIDE was formed in the late winter of 2013.

Combining the intensity and fury of the riffs created by expert guitar playing with the epic orchestral scores Stormtide is a force to be reckoned with.

Often lauded for giving an “over the top” experience live, Stormtide has a prolific live history playing alongside major international acts. Country of origin: Australia; Location: Melbourne.

Band Members:
Taylor Stirrat - Vocals
Simon Fragiotta - Rhythm/Lead guitar
Reuben Stone - Keyboard
Jake Pickering - Drums
