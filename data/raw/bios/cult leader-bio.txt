Cult Leader is a dark metalcore/mathcore band hailing from Salt Lake City, Utah. The band formed in 2013 following the disbandment of Sludge Metal act Gaza with three out of four members remaining in the band and former bassist Anthony Lucero taking up vocal duties.

So far the band has released one EP called Nothing For Us Here and two studio albums all through the renowned Deathwish label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cult+Leader">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
