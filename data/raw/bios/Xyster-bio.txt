Xyster is the solo project of Zekenfraut from Anno Domini Mortus. Only two albums have been released. The second album 'Cracks in the shell' contains elements of "Drone", Experimental, Melancholic piano, grim voices and atmosphere. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xyster">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
