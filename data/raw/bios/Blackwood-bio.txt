1) Blackwood is an Italian dance act, that was created and produced by Tony Verde, Marascia and Sandro Murru, also known as Kortezman.

Blackwood had two fronted vocalists: Taborah Adams who recorded the albums «Ride on the Rhythm», «In The City» and «I Am» and wrote hits like «Ride on the Rhythm», «My Love For You» and «I Am». 
In 1998 Taborah was supplied by Sheila Brody, who recorded the album «Friday Night». 

2) BlackWood is Andrea Blackwood-Barnes, a British electronic music producer and singer.
