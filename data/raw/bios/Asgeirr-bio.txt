Asgeirr is a one man (NS) black metal band from France.All instruments are played by Velssiryhn d'Aldarik.The band was formed in 2002 and released its first demo 'Nouvel Ordre Du Nord' in 2003.The latest full length album from 2005 is called 'Diktat Allianz'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asgeirr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
