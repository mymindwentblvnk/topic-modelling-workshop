The austro-swedish combo Mudfuzz managed to cause quite a stir with their two independent EPs 'Mr. Happy' and 'Doglife' before releasing their debut LP 'Mudfuzz' in 2002 and promptly disappearing off the face of the earth.

Their sound - straying away from what was fast becoming a stale genre in 'numetal', was somewhat refreshing and energetic but failed to garner the success predicted for them after their early buzz. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MUDFUZZ">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
