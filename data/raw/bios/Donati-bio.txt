His musical journey is a crossroads that touches on Brazilian music and Jazz, tribal sounds and electronica, and the suggestiveness of dance and the visual arts.
With the "Salgarian" soul of an explorer, his music combines refined impressionism and an innate taste for space. He devotes himself as well, to the fusion between music and contemporary dance. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Donati">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
