Gaia Prelude is a Neo-Classical Heavy Metal band from Japan; their lyrical themes range from love and death to fantasy. Gaia Prelude consists of Hisashi Nishiwaki on guitar, Katsuya Sugiyama on vocals, Koji Okamoto on bass, Ryutaro Okamoto on drums, and Masaki Goshima on keyboards. Gaia Prelude has released one full-length album entitled "To Beyond of the Infinity" in 2006 on YggrDrasill Records.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gaia+Prelude">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
