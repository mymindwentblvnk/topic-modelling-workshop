There are three artists named Infiltrator.

1. Infiltrator is an alias of an American techno DJ and producer Andre Holland, also known as The Infiltrator. He is also known as Chameleon, Chamaleon and is a member of the group Underground Resistance.

2. Infiltrator is a (at the moment) one man band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania consisting of Steve Jansson which plays speed metal/heavy metal.

3. Infiltrator is an anonymous Swedish noise/power electronics group, that released the full length album "Black Light Therapy" in early 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infiltrator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
