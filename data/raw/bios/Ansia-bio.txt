There is more than one artist with this name.

1) Mexican independent band, dark sounds, deep lyrics, great voice.

2) Ansia project was born in Italy due to the will of finding new sounds into the doom scene. The lineup started with Ruben (drums), Steven (guitars) and Ethere (vocals, bass, synth). In the spring of 2006 they begun working in their music, and after a few months the songs I, II, and III were finished, then mixed by Alessio Fagrelli (ex-session keyboards for Deinonychus), who also plays piano on the song I.
After a short period, while the music was complete, Ethere left the band. In July, 2007, Ansia signed to Solitude Productions for a split with the mexican band Majestic Downfall with tracks from the unreleased demo called “I” but with diferent titles.
