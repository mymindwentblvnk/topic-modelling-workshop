Xerpentor formed in 1998 under the name Serpentor.  They were from Benschop, Netherlands.  Initially they were a thrash metal / death metal band, but they just a death metal band later on.  They changed their to Xerpentor in 2001.  They disbanded in 2016.  Their last known lineup consisted of Leon van Midden (vocals, bass),  Philip Nugteren (guitars) and Eric Gijsen (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/XerpentoR">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
