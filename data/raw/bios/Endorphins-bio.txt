Endorphins was a thrash/groove metal band from Toronto, Canada who split up in 2008, thirteen years after being formed, with one EP and this their first and last full-length album under their metal-studded belts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Endorphins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
