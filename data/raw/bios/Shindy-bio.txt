Michael Schindler, better known by his stage name Shindy, (born 7 September, 1988) is a German rapper and producer with greek roots. He is known for his collaboration with Kay One on the album Prince of Belvedair and Bushido. Shindy is currently signed to his own record label FRIENDS WITH MONEY. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shindy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
