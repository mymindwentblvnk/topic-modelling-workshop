Isolation In Infamy is a 4-piece technical death metal band hailing out of Los Angeles, California. They formed in 2006 and released a self-titled EP in 2011.

Current Members:

Andy Nelson - Guitars/Vocals
Shadi Absi - Guitars
Chris Audish - Bass
Mikey Wilson - Drums
