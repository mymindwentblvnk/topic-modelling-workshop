Anger on Anger was a New Jersey groove/thrash metal band formed in 1995. The four members are: MARIO (Singer), DAVE LINSK (Guitar), SEMI QUAVER (Bass), JOHN SMITH (Drums). Anger on Anger has opened up shows for many national acts such as: KORN, GZR, BIOHAZARD, PRO-PAIN, CRISIS, OVERKILL, FEAR FACTORY as well as others. Anger on Anger records and produces their Audio & Video's. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anger+on+Anger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
