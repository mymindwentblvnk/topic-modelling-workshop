ENGLISH. Starting as a side-project by Fernanda Lira (vocals/bass) and Luana Dametto (drums), CRYPTA became a full-fledged band after both left Nervosa back in 2019.  Sonia Anubis (lead / rhythm guitar) and Tainá Bergamaschi  (rhythm / lead guitar) complete the Brazilian/Dutch quartet—which will release their debut album, 'Echoes of the Soul', in June 2021 via Napalm Records. Deeply influenced by old-school death metal—with blackened touches—CRYPTA is the culmination of years of experience by its all-star integrants (ex-members of Nervosa, Cobra Spell, Hagbard, among others).

PORTUGUESE.  Criada em Junho de 2019, CRYPTA é um quarteto Brasileiro/Holandês de Death Metal, cujas influências remetem às raízes old-school dos diversos sub-gêneros do estilo. A formação da banda inclui Fernanda Lira (baixo e vocal) e Luana Dametto (bateria), ambas ex-integrantes da banda brasileira de thrash metal Nervosa, Sonia Anubis (lead guitar) que também toca heavy metal na Burning Witches e Cobra Spell, e Tainá Bergamaschi (guitarra), ex-guitarrista da Hagbard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crypta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
