Fregoli Delusion is a Lithuanian doom/black metal band, but with ideas very bleak and cold in so much of the cases they become different means to achieve the sounds that give unique characteristics; these elements can be doom/black, ambient black, drone, funeral doom, Among other genres, therefore Fregoli Delusion becomes a band of experimental genre. 
The Fregoli delusion is a rare disorder in which a person holds a delusional belief that different people are in fact a single person who changes appearance or is in disguise.
