There are two bands named Necrophobic.

1) A blackened death metal band that was formed by David Parland and Joakim Sterner in Stockholm, Sweden in 1989. The name was taken from a Slayer song. Their lyrics focus on occult, Satanic and anti-Christian themes, while their musical style has been compared to bands such as Dissection and Unanimated. They have released seven full-length albums to date as well as seven demos and EPs. Their eighth full-length album "Mark of the Necrogram" will be released in February 2018.
