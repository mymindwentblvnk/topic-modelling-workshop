Thrashantos is a four-piece thrash band from Cleveland, Ohio. Their current line up consists of Sonny on guitar and vocals, Marky on guitar and vocals, Kyle on drums, and Ammo on bass and vocals. Their demo can be downloaded for free at http://www.mediafire.com/download.php?dzzmm1znzzz. They have a full length entitled "Buy Us Beer" due out soon on SBS records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thrashantos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
