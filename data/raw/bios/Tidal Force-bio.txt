Tidal Force is an old school Melodic Heavy Metal band from Florida. They had only one release, the full length "Will To Power" which came out on Atica Records on 1993, the band split up soon after. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tidal+Force">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
