This is the first full-fledged solo release by Kris Norris, best known for his work in Darkest Hour. The album was co-produced and co-mixed by Cory Smoot of GWAR at his studio in Virginia. The album also features guest vocals by Randy Blythe of Lamb Of God on "Ghostly Shell Removal" and "Remaining Foolish." Magna Carta Records will release a special edition EP "THE GHOSTLY SHELL" on iTunes in upcoming weeks. The EP will be followed by the full-length album "ICONS OF THE ILLOGICAL" release on January 14, 2009 which will be available online and in stores.
