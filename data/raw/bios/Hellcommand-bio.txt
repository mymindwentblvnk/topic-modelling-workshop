Hellcommand is a thrash metal band from Montclair, California.

Current line-up:

Dave Castanon - Vocals, Guitar
Steve Stewart - Guitar
Miguel Rodriguez - Bass
Mike Gross - Drums

The band was formerly known as Midnite Sex, back in the summer of 2009.

The drummer for Hellcommand, Mike Gross, was formerly in the 
thrash band Mutants of War where he was known as Half Pint.

Hellcommand has released one untitled demo, the demo is available for streaming on their MySpace.
