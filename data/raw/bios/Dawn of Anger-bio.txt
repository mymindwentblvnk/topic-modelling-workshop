Dawn of Anger is a band from the south of Holland, they started mid-90's as the band: Forefront, which released a promising demo. After a while they changed their sound towards a more metallic sound and started to call themself: Dawn of Anger. They released 1 demo and 1 full length which were both released by themselves.
In 2001 they called it's quits.
