Justin Tyme was a Canadian band formed in Winnipeg, Canada in 1969. They released two singles on Warner Bros., with their second single, "Child of Dawn, ," peaking at #74 on the Canadian Charts in April 1970. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Justin+Tyme">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
