Crow Black Sky is a cosmic/atmospheric black metal band from Cape Town, South Africa. After a hiatus, the band returned in 2018 with Sidereal Light: Volume One. This release marked a shift in style from the melodic/progressive black/death metal of their debut, Pantheion (2010), to cosmos-themed atmospheric black metal. Sidereal Light: Volume Two is due to be released in 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crow+Black+Sky">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
