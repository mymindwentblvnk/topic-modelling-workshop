Lonely Guest is a somewhat mysterious act on the newly relaunched Berlin-based label False Idols.

July 2021 saw the release of Pre War Tension, a single featuring the voice of Idles frontman Joe Talbot, Marta (who is known for her work and live performances with Tricky) and a verse from Tricky himself.
