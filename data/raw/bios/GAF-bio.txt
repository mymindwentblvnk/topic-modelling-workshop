GAF is a Grindcore band from Finland. They have a record deal with Scrotum Jus Records and they have released two full length albums called 'Mongofied' and 'Cultivate Disdain', both through the aforementioned label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gaf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
