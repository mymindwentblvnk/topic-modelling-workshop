The Gof of War was a Brutal Death Metal/Grindcore band from Thailand that existed between 2008 and 2010. The members were DarK EmperoR (vocals) and Xtechnical Death Shredding 666X (guitar and drum programming). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+God+of+War">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
