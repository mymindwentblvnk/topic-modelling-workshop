A new chapter has begun. "Secluded" is now "The ghost of a campaigner". For the same reason Jean-Claude van Damme left belgium, they decided to go new ways. Stronger, heavier and more intensive than ever before, but still with their well known high-energy live performances. Spread the world and dont miss the chance to join them at their upcoming shows.
