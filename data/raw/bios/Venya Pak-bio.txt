Singer/Songwriter, Youtuber, Actor, Model

Venya Pak was born on November 9th, 1995 in Russia. He was in love with making videos and music since he was in elementary school. He remembers taking his father's old camcorder and talking to it when he was around 7 years old. In his early middle school years, he discovered his passion for songwriting and creating arrangements. Up to this day, Venya writes his journal entries as songs. Having no education other than skills he picked up himself, he started creating and recording music, experimenting and looking for his sound. 
In 2012, Venya decides to move to the U.S., where he is attending the American Music and Dramatic Academy majoring in Musical Theatre.
YouTube became his "internet home". With his daily vlogs and other videos, he expresses himself and shares different life stories and situations with the world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Venya+Pak">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
