Guthrum is the solo project of Mark Wood (ex Ravenage / current Winterfylleth)

They released the first recordings under the name of 'The First Ode to Many...' in mid 2009 and received a high level of feedback thus attaining solid ground to build on for future releases.

The next release will be out during the second half of 2010.

Current members are:
Mark Wood, Phil Wilkinson, Richard Hurst and Jonny Hirons.

Previous members:
Elliot Vernon (Ravenage and Windrider)
