Flesh Feast is a brutal death metal band from Ontario, Canada. They play brutal death metal in the vein of typical NYDM style death metal.

Their discography consists of, as of now :

Fate of Hated Flesh (Demo, 1997)
Flesh Feast (Full-length, 2000) (released with CDN Records)
