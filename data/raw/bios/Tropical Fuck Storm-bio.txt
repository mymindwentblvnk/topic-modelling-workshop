Tropical Fuck Storm (also known as TFS) is an Australian rock band based in Melbourne, Victoria. It features two former core members of The Drones: singer-songwriter Gareth Liddiard and bass player Fiona Kitschin. The band also features guitarist/keyboardist/vocalist Erica Dunn of MOD CON, Palm Springs and Harmony and drummer Lauren Hammel from High Tension

Following four 7" singles in late 2017 and early 2018, the group released their debut album "A Laughing Death In Meatspace" on 4th May 2018.  TFS released their sophomore album "Braindrops" on 23 August, 2019.
