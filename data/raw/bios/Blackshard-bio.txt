Blackshard are a thrash metal band from the north-east of the Czech republic. The band was formed in 2008 by Tomas Tomis (guitars, vocals) and Jan Fikacek (drums). The first demo, which consisted of four songs, was released in October 2009. The debut album called Mankind Disorder was released in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blackshard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
