The one-man Depressive Black Metal project Cragataska has been formed by Kerem Güravşar, in 2008 at Ankara. The first record was an demo named ''Ağustos 17'' released in the year of creation. This album takes 17 August 1999 occured Marmara Earthquake as a concept. This album was dedicated to all people who died, suffered and still suffering in this earthquake. After that, he started for his second record, labeled "Utanç" which was released in 2010. In 2011 he release a split album with Valefor, Mourning Soul and Garden of Sadness entitled "The Wound That Never Heals".


Cragataska got its listeners all over the world and still speaking out to us from the deepness.
