Remuda is a three piece heavy metal band from Maun, Botswana that plays heavy metal in an older vein reminiscent of NWOBHM. The band has a self-titled release on Metal Records that was recorded at Nosey Road Studios. The members of the band are: Cochize on vocals and guitar, Kebadiretse Diako on drums and Chameleon on bass.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Remuda">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
