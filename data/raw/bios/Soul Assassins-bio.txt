Soul Assassins is a hardcore hip hop project of Cypress Hill founding member and producer DJ Muggs. Each album features a plethora of guest rappers and is primarily produced by Muggs; the second and third studio albums see contributions from other producers such as The Alchemist and DJ Khalil. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Assassins">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
