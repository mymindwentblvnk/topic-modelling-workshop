Booze Control is a Heavy Metal band from Germany. They were founded in Braunschweig in 2009.

Band members are:
* David "Daerst" Kuri, Vocals / Guitars
* Jendrik Seiler, Guitars
* Steffen Kurth, Bass Guitar
