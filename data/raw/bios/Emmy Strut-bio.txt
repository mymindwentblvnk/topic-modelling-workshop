Emmy Strut was an American heavy metal band formed in 1981 in Chicago, Illinois. The band released a 5 track demo in 1984 and a self titled EP in 1985 even though the band wasn't signed to a label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Emmy+Strut">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
