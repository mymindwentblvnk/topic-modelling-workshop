Oath of Cirion was a symphonic black metal band from Hyvinkää, Finland, formed in 1998 by Nargath (Tomi Kalliola, who was a member of Wyrd, Valar, Azaghal and many others) and Kalma (Ville Pallonen, former member of Druadan Forest and Azaghal).
The band released a first demo tape titled "King of the Dragonthrone" in 1999, and a full-length album tilted "Dragonmagick" in 2001 and then disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oath+of+Cirion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
