Deer Bear is a danish dark folkpop duo from Aarhus. Members are Lars Bjørn-Hansen (Koldborn)and Anne Hjort. They have released the Album "House Behind the eyes" in 2012 and the EP "Darker Growth" in 2013. The name stems from the surnames : Bjørn meaning bear and hjort meaning deer.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deer+Bear">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
