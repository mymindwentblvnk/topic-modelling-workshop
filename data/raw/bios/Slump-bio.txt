--- Doomy stoner rock band from the West Midlands, UK
Slump formed in 2018 and is primarily based in Birmingham. They play a riff-heavy, bluesy, doomy style of stoner rock. Slump had begun to play their first live shows, including an appearance at Metal 2 The Masses Wolverhampton, before the Coronavirus pandemic hit and the members turned to focus on recording instead. The 'Nothing More' single was released on 22 September 2020 - featuring 'Degenerate' as a B-side.
