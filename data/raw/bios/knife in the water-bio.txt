Knife in the Water formed in Austin, Texas during the Summer of 1997. Aaron Blunt is the band's primary songwriter and lead gutarist, along with Laura Krause who plays organ and often provides back-up vocals. Their line-up also include steel pedal player Bill McCullough and John Brewington on Bass. Their debut album, "Plays One Sound and Others" was self-released in 1998 and garnered them praise within the Austin scene. Their second album, "Red River" was released by Overcoat two years later, which earned them critical attention and an increased fan base. Two EP's followed, released on the Austin based label Peek-A-Boo, and in 2003 their first two albums were reissued on Aspyr Media. In 2003 they
