Paragon of Beauty was a gothic doom metal band formed in Schiffweiler, Germany in 1994, with lyrical themes dealing with love, longing and melancholy. Members are involved with Autumnblaze.

Discography
1996 - Starwoven (demo)
1996 - Snowfall Summerdream (demo)
1998 - The Spring
2000 - Seraphine... Far Gone Gleam
2001 - Comfort Me, Infinity

Members

Andreas Schuler - guitars
Oliver Kron - guitars
