Tatir was a pagan black metal band formed in Athens, Greece in 1994. A demo titled "Dark Autumn Nights" was recorded at Storm Studios and released in 1995.  A two-track promo/demo titled "Fons Acheroni" was released the following year. The cover art on "Fons Acheroni" was by Panos Sounas who has also designed artwork for Necromantia.
