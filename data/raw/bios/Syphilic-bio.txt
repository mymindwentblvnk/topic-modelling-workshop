A brutal death metal band from Detroit, Michigan.  It was formed in 2005 by Brian Forgue.  Forgue records everything.  Marvin Green was the bassist in 2011.  He is the only other member who has been in the band.  It is unknown when he left.  The band has one demo, several full-length albums, one EP and one split.  The band is signed to Sevared Records.  Lyrical themes include sick humor, incest, filth, alcohol, gore, murder and anti-racism. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Syphilic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
