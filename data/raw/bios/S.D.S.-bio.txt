S.D.S  (a.k.a. Societic Death Slaughter) are an obscure, heavy Japanese hardcore/crust punk band with aggressive guitar riffs and pissed off shrieking manic and brutal vocal variation.

Members:

Shibuya Assholer(G)
Hide Coll Bastard(B)
