Rory Campbell is a Scottish musician who plays pipes and whistles. As well as solo recordings, he was a member of Deaf Shepherd (until 2003), Caledon, and the duo Rory Campbell & Malcolm Stitt. He is currently a member of Old Blind Dogs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rory+Campbell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
