The Trend Orchestra or The Marcus Hedges Trend Orchestra is a pseudonym of Marcus Hedges, composer and orchestrator. They are most known for their orchestration of video game scores.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Marcus+Hedges+Trend+Orchestra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
