There is more than one artist using this name:

1) Bloodmoon is a progressive sludge/doom/black metal band from USA. They were formed in 2010 by Peter Tomis (Guitar/Vocals), Patrick Mullholland (Bass) and Jason Goldie(Drums/Vocals). Bloodmoon's songs contain passages of shimmering cosmic ambience accompanied by distant ghostly vocals and reverberating guitar melodies which transition into pavement-splitting riffs and rancid, tortured shrieks. To date they have four releases: Orenda (2012), Voidbound (2013) and a Split record with Trapped Within Burning Machinery (2017), Supervoid Trinity (2018).
https://bloodmoonslo.bandcamp.com/

2) Bloodmoon is a black metal band from Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany.

3) Bloodmoon was a depressive black metal band from Ontario, Canada.

4) Bloodmoon was a deaththrash band from Limburg, Netherlands.

5) Bloodmoon is a Death Metal band from Arnhem in The Netherlands. The band started in 2016.
