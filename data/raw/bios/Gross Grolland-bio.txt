Formed in Kurgan City, Russia in 1995 - Gross Grolland has finally issued it's debut album. Having issued a demo in 1999, the band went on hiatus until a new line-up was announced in 2005. Finally, Gross Grolland has put out it's debut album "What Doesn't Kill Me Makes a Big Mistake" on Metal Scrap records in January. The ten track album also has an official video for the track "Friendship," which follows below the tracklisting and cover art of the album.
