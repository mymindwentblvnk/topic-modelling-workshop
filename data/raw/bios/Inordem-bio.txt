Inordem is born in 1997 when Raúl Pérez (rhythm guitar) joins the project started by Carlos Cabrera (drums) and Sergio Basanta (solo guitar). The band is completed with the arrival of Fede Pardueles (bass guitar) and Hugo Silva (singer). Inordem first performs on stage in September 1998. But already at the beginning of 1999 Fede and Hugo leave the band for personal reasons. Withing few weeks J.F. Torres joins the band as a bass guitarist and after several unsuccessuf attempts to find a singer, Raúl decides to take on the role of the singer as well.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inordem">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
