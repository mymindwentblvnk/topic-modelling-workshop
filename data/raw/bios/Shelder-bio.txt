A Heavy Metal band with a female vocalist Corinne Hacquebey from France
Formed in 1987, currently Split-up. They released only one album, 'God of Vikings' in 1988, which was dedicated to Randy Rhoads.

Last known line-up:
Corinne Hacquebey - Vocals
Daniel Hacquebey - Lead/Rhythm Guitar
Bertrand Cuquemelle - Bass/Vocals
