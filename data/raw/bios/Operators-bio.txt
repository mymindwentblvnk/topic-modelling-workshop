Operators is a band made of Dan Boeckner (Wolf Parade, Handsome Furs, Atlas Strategic, Divine Fits), Sam Brown (New Bomb Turks, Divine Fits), and Devojka. Their second album, Radiant Dawn, featuring Arcade Fire's Tim Kingsbury on bass, was released in May, 2019.
