1) UNDER THE SURFACE (CZ)
Metal band Under The Surface (*summer 2011) is from Valasske Mezirici, Czech republic. They combine thrash-melodeath with modern sample music. They released their debut album "RESURRECTION"  4th March 2013.

Members:
Miroslav Jankot - Vocals
Milan Blažek - Guitar
Pavel Hrnko - Bass
Petr Chaňo - Drums

2) Under the Surface is a Dutch band that was formed in 2015 by drummer Joost Lijbaart (1967), guitarist Bram Stadhouders (1987) and vocalist Sanne Rambags (1994). Their music incorporates influences from jazz, prog rock, folk and tribal music.
