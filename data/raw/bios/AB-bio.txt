AB is the rap moniker of the greatest wide receiver in NFL history, Antonio Brown. He made his hip hop debut with the "Whole Lotta Money" single in 2019. He released his debut album Himmothy in 2020.

With the Pittsburgh Steelers, Brown recorded the most receptions in the NFL from 2010 to 2018. He forced a trade and was released by the Oakland Raiders after numerous incidents such as calling GM Mike Mayock a "cracker". He signed with the New England Patriots, but was released after one game due to a rape allegation. In 2020, Brown signed with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers and won a Super Bowl in his first year.
