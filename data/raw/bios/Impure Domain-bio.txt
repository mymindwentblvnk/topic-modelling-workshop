Impure Domain is an Industrial Black Metal band from Italy. It was born in Italy  (Foggia, Apulia), 1997. So far they have released 3 full-lenght albums, one demo album, and one re-release. The current members are :

Herr Angy-Sstrego - Vocals
Mikheil - Bass
Xhela - Guitar
Psycho Blaster - Guitar

Their releases are :

Progression of Impurity (Full-Lenght)
Vivere di Male/ The Evil within (Full-Lenght)
H.I.V.: Parade (Full-Lenght)
Universal Murder (Promo 2007 - Demo Album)
