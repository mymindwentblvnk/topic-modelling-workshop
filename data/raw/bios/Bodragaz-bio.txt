With their latest album, Awaken, listed by digital music giant Rhapsody.com as one of the top 32 “Most Significant” new releases in the metal genre on June 9th, 2008, BODRAGAZ looks to up the ante with an exciting and innovative 2011 release. Unquestionably a hard rock band at its core, with distinctive symphonic / prog metal elements which easily serve to set them apart from the masses, the band continues to gain momentum and popularity. With live performances at many national shows and a growing catalog of noteworthy achievements, BODRAGAZ stands ready to lead the vanguard in the recent resurgence of melodic hard rock and symphonic metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bodragaz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
