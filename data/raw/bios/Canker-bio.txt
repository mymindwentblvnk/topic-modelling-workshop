There at least two known artists under the name Canker:

1. Canker was a goregrind band from Sydney, Nova Scotia, Canada. Formed in 2002, released splits with bands like Plasma, Bowel Fetus, Pulmonary Fibrosis, etc. and frequently released on the D.I.Y. label, Fecal-Matter Discorporated. It's presumed has split-up but there is no official date regarding when they called it quits.
