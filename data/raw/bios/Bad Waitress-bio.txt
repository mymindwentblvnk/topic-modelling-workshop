Bad Waitress is a four-piece punkrock band from Toronto, ON. Starting their musical adventures as The Nude Dogs, Bad Waitress got  signed to Royal Mountain Records in 2018 and haven't stopped since. They incorporate heavy licks, intense energy, and strong satire. They put out their first EP as Bad Waitress called "Party bangers: Volume 1" on 4.20 of 2018 and are currently waiting on the release of their first full length. As a collective they strive to create an empowering, inclusive and positive environment for people to rage out and have a fucking awesome time with some FEROCIOUS, SHAMELESS, ECCENTRIC PSYCHO MUSIC MANIIIAAAAA. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bad+Waitress">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
