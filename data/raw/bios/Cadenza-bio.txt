There are multiple artists named Cadenza

1)
Cadenza Collective is a band from Kathmandu, Nepal, which plays a mix of jazz/funk/soul/Afrobeat. They can be seen live at Jazz Upstairs, Kathmandu, Nepal on Wednesdays and Saturdays from 8:00 pm to 10:00 pm. They also headline the annual Jazz Festival in Kathmandu.

Their official website is www.cadenza.com.np, you can listen to some tracks on this myspace page www.myspace.com/cadenzakath.

2)
Cadenza is a dubstep producer
