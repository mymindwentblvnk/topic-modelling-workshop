To The Bone is a rock band from Belgium. The band won the Frappant Popprijs and shared the stage with band such as Jon Spencer, The Bellrays, Admiral Freebee, Bassdrum of Death, The Kids and The Hickey Underworld.

Its full length NEXT was released in 2013.
