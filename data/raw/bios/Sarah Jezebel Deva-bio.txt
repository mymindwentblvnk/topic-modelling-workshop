Sarah Jane Ferridge (born February 25, 1977 in Forest Gate, East London, United Kingdom), better known by her stage name Sarah Jezebel Deva, was a backing vocalist in Cradle of Filth, and also fronts her own band, Angtoria.

She also performed with other bands like Therion (on Vovin and Crowning of Atlantis), The Kovenant (on Nexus Polaris), Graveworm (on Underneath the Crescent Moon), The Gathering (on Black Light District), etc.

She has a four-octave vocal range, which allows her to sing from alto to coloratura soprano parts.

The bandmembers of her solo-project are:

AblaZ - Bass
Dan - Guitar
Jonny - Guitar
