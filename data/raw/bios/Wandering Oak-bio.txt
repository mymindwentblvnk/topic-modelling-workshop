Wandering Oak are a folk metal band originally hailing from Berkshire County, Massachusetts, but now located in Rochester, New York. Aside from the band's frontman the entire line-up changed with their move to New York.


Robert Pollard: Rhythm, lead and acoustic guitars, lead vocals, tin whistles, bodhran, field recordings, programming


Monica Finger: Drums, percussion
