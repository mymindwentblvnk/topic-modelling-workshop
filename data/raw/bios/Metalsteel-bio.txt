Heavy metal band from Slovenia, formed in 1999 (band members were 9-12 then). They've released to date; Usoda (2003, means Destiny in english) and Taste the Sin (2005), Bad In Bed (2007) and Entertainment (2011). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Metalsteel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
