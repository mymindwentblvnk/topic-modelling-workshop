Svartr Strijd was a one-man project playing raw black metal by Strijd, who is also in the one-man projects Pagan Winds and Black Circle. Two extremely limited demos have been released and a compilation tape by Total Holocaust Records in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Svartr+Strijd">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
