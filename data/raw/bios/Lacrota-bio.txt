Lacrota are a death metal band from Sheffield, United Kingdom. Originally formed under the name 'Xerxes' in 2011, they changed their name in 2014, and released their debut EP 'Nuckelavee' in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lacrota">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
