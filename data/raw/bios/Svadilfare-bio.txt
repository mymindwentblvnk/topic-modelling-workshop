Svadilfare is a one-man Atmospheric Black Metal project from Bergen,Norway... In the Poetic and Prose Edda, Svaðilfari (Old Icelandic for "The one who makes an unlucky journey") is the male horse that fathers Óðinn's horse, Sleipnir. In the Prose Edda, it is said that Sleipnir's mother is no one else but Loki in the form of a mare. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Svadilfare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
