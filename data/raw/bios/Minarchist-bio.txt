Minarchist is the studio project of Philadelphia musician Connor McNamee. Minarchist's debut album, "In Absence," is due out in Spring of 2016, and features performances by Nick Shaw of Black Crown Initiate, who provided both his wicked bass skills and his impressive producing talent, and Jerry Martin of Alustrium performing his signature harsh vocals. Guest guitar solos contributed by Andy Thomas of Black Crown Initiate and Reading, PA musician Ethan McKenna.
Line up:
Connor McNamee- Lead/Rhythm guitar, clean vocals, lyrics
Nick Shaw (Black Crown Initiate) - bass, drum programming, production
Jerry Martin (Alustrium) - vocals
Guest guitar solos:
Andy Thomas (Black Crown Initiate)
