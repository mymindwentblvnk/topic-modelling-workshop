There are mutliple artists with this name:

1. Wicked Angel is a Canadian Power/Thrash Metal band formed in 1984 that released several demos until their demise. They have just signed to CDN records in 2009 and there are going to be some reissues in the summer with a new album in the fall. Click here for a detailed biographical information

2. Wicked Angel was a Texan Speed/Power Metal band from San Antonio formed in 1985 that released one demo "Chaotic Intellect" in 1987.  Click here for an interview with the band

3. Wicked Angel was a Heavy Metal band from Columbus, Ohio that formed in 1992 and released 2 albums in the 90s. split up. Metal-Archives page
