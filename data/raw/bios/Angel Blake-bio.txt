Angel Blake is a Heavy Metal band from Trollhättan. The band was formed by guitarist Marko Tervonen after the split-up of the Swedish death / thrash metal band The Crown in 2004. 

Tobias Jansson - Vocals 
Marko Tervonen - Guitars
Örjan Wressel - Contrabass
Janne Saarenpää - Drums
Anders Edlund - Bass

Former Members
