Colonize the Rotting was a brutal death metal band from San Jose, California that formed in the year 2008.  They only released one demo and one full-length album.  All of the members left Colonize the Rotting at some point time, except for Alex Colon (drums, vocals).  He was the only member left and then Colonize the Rotting disbanded in 2016.  Alex Colon died the same year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Colonize+The+Rotting">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
