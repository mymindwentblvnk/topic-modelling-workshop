Lilla Vargen is a Northern Irish singer songwriter with a soft, soulful and evocative voice. The name translates as Little Wolf in Swedish, a name which perfectly captures the vulnerability in her minimal, honest and lovelorn songs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lilla+Vargen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
