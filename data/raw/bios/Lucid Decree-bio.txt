Lucid Decree consists of guitar and keyboard shredding, bass riffing, high energy head banging New Age Metal. Members Joey (613) Odin (Drums) and Abe Lox (Guitar/Keys) formed Lucid Decree, bassist Zak Rodriguez, vocalist Maverick Ely joined shortly after. Lucid Decree has many influences from all styles of music and incorporates it all into its own Metal Style.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lucid+Decree">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
