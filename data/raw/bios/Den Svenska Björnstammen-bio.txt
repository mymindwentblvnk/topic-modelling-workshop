Den Svenska Björnstammen ("The Swedish Bear Population") is a Swedish band from Norrköping consisting of Dan Brännvall, Mattias Göransson, Kim Dahlberg, Ambjörn Göransson, Klas Isaksson, Petter Frisendahl and Åke Olofsson.

The band released its first single, Nytt lego ("New Lego"), on YouTube in 2008. In 2010 the group signed a contract with record label Pope Records and have a special distribution agreement with Universal Music Sweden. The band had its breakthrough in 2011 with the song Vart jag mig i världen vänder ("Wherever in the World I Turn"), topping the Swedish charts for six weeks.

In 2010 Den Svenska Björnstammen was one of three bands that made it to the finals of the music competition Metro On Stage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Den+Svenska+Bj%C3%B6rnstammen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
