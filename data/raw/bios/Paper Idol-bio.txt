Paper Idol, real name Matan KG , is a LA based songwriter and multi-instrumentalist.

In a short amount of time, Paper Idol has created a new bridge between dance music and indie rock, reshaping what we've become accustomed to in the indie-electronic genre. His early success is undeniable, as the single "Feel Real Pretty" has amassed over 1.8 million plays on Spotify and he was also featured on Yung Bae's 2019 album Bae 5. Fans of artists like Louis The Child, Whethan, and Still Woozy will surely latch onto his upbeat sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Paper+Idol">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
