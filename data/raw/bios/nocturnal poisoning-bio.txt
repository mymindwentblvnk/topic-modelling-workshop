Nocturnal Poisoning is the dark folk project of Scott Conner after the dissolve of Xasthur. Since its inception in 2010 there has been 3 albums released under the Nocturnal Poisoning project. Scott is currently accompanied by Chris and Robert. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nocturnal+Poisoning">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
