Heavy and nihilistic British sludge/doom band with members from Liverpool, Wigan and Leeds.
They recorded the album They Sang and Chanted for Hours, Then the Locked in Hundreds Set Themselves Ablaze for Dry-Run Records in 2004, but it remained unreleased until 2011 when the At War With False Noise label stepped in.
The band split up in 2006.
Last line up was : Peet Dandy (Narcosis) - vocals, Paul Robertson (They Are Cowards, Black Magician) - guitar, Paul (Like a Kind of Matador) - bass, Paul - drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Redrighthand">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
