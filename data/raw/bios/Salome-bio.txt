1. Salome was a bassless crushing sludge/doom band from Northern Virginia, USA. They've split in 2011, having released two albums (Salome, 2008, and Terminal, 2010) and two splits. Vocalist Kat Katz still sings in grindcore band Agoraphobic Nosebleed. Guitarist Rob Moore has been involved in projects including Auroboros, Thrain, Griefloss and most recently powerviolence group Brainpan. Aaron Deal joined Darkest Hour as a bassist.
