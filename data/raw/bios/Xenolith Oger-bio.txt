Obscure all-female thrash metal-band from Japan, formed in Kanagawa, 1986. The last known line-up consists of Hitomi Takahashi (vocals), Mizuho Muroi (guitar), Akiko Mizuno (bass) and Yoko Ogura (drums). The band released three demo-tapes. "Rehearsal" and "Deadly Sins Worth Death" in 1987 and "Cattle Brand" in 1989. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xenolith+Oger">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
