Malstrom is a progressive death/thrash metal band based out of Brooklyn, NY. They are know for their long compositions and unique melodies, as well as being notorious for keeping their lyrical material "out of the woods" and a disdain for "jazz cud". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malstrom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
