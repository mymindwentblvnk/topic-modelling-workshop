Cythraul is an old Welsh word for the Devil. There are several artists who use this name.

1. Dutch death metal band from Purmerend, North Holland. Formed in 1996.
2. French black metal band from Toulouse, Midi-Pyrénées. Formed in 2000.
3. French black metal band from Sainte-Marguerite, Lorraine. Formed in 2000.
4. American black metal band from Boston, Massachusetts. Formed in 2000.
5. British death metal band from London, England. Formed in 2011.
