Vitam Eternam was a U.S. black metal band hailing from Ottumwa, Iowa. They made only one EP which was released by the legendary, and now defunct, Wild Rags Records.

Line-up:
Ashlar (John Prosser) - Guitar, Drum Programming, Vocals, Synth
Tribulator (Spence Shepard) - Bass, Vocals

Releases:
Blackened Dreams of Nocturn EP
