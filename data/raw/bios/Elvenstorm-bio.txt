ElvenStorm is a female fronted Heavy / Power Metal band from France formed in 2008. 
 Current Members are 
    Michael Hellström   Guitars (2008-present)
    Laura Ferreux           Vocals (2008-present)
    Félix Börner           Drums (2010-present)

ElvenStorm is currently celebrating the release of Blood Leads to Glory
