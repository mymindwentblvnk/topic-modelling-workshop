2. Means to an End is an depressive black metal band that comes from the USA and Belgium.
They formed the band in 2009 and since then they released one album called ''Weathered by Time''
on Pest Productions.
Their lyrics are mainly fed by: Negativity, Despair, Solitude, Hate, and Anger.
Their music is filled with pianofragments and melancholic guitarparts.
