Started in the end of the 80's under the name Bactherion, "Filii Nigrantium Infernalium" is the most cult of the Portuguese Black Metal bands. 
Launched the first Demo in 1993, followed by "A Era do Abutre" EP in 1995. Their full-length cd "Fellatrix Discordia Pantokrator" only came out during 2005.
Current line-up has Belathauzer on vocals and guitar, Andremon on guitar, Helregni on bass and Gorg on drums.
They also count with the collaboration of Pedro Pedra (vocalist from GROG) on the album and at some live concerts. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Filii+Nigrantium+Infernalium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
