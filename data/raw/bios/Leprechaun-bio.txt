Leprechaun is 

a) A Chilean instrumental progressive rock band formed in 1999 in Santiago, Chile, featuring José Luis Santander on guitar, Miguel Fonseca on bass, Martín Sepúlveda on keyboards and Gonzalo Ubilla on drums. They've just released their self-titled debut album.

http://www.leprechaun.cl/
http://www.myspace.com/leprechaunsproject


b) An italian folk-metal project. This project was formed in 2003 in Pordenone, Friuli-Venezia Giulia (Italy), when Damnagoras, the singer and composer of Elvenking had left the band. After returning to Elvenking the band hasn't released any other album besides "the Ultimate Dance" which was a demo, released in 2004.
The members of this project are Damnagoras on the guitar and for vocals. Francesco Bucci on the bass (Outbreak (Ita), Stormlord (Ita)), Isabella (Whisperwind) Tuni on the keyboads and for vocals and Enrico Fabris on the drums (State O' Mind, Raintime).
Whisperwind performed guest vocals on the 2006 Elvenking album "The Winter Wake."
