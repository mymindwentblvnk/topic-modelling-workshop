He started producing House Underground and Ambient music inspired by disco / funk music and the melancholic aesthetics of the 80s in Mexico.

In February of 2018 he released his debut EP called "Busy Nights At Tokyo" in which he defined his more relaxed style that he himself would call "Soft Funk".
