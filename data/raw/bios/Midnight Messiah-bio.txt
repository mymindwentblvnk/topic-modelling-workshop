From the ashes of NWOBHM band Elixir comes a new Classic Epic Metal band for the 2000’s – Midnight Messiah. Featuring the Elixir songwriting team of Paul Taylor (vocals) and Phil Denton (guitars), the line-up is enhanced by guitar maestro/wonderkid Dave Strange, bass player Dusty Miller and drum pounder extraordinaire Darren Lee. 

The band are pleased to announce their debut album, "The Root of All Evil" released 5 April 2013, is now available for purchase at http://www.coldtown.com
