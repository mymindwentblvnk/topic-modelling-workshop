Eisenfeld is a one man Black Metal band formed in 2004, Husum, Schleswig-Holstein, Germany.
Released a demo and a full length album in 2009 so far:
-2009-The Spirit Of Opposing Worlds
-2009-Vita Reducta (Demo)
