Azziard is a Black/Death metal band formed in 2001 in France (lle de France). the band consits of:
Sagoth - Guitar
Zyule - Guitar
Eilkh - Vocals (Atramentum as Vurderlak, Norz Feuer as Yann), 
Khynsk - Bass (ex-Cryoxyd)
Harold - Drums

Past members include:
Vocals-Nörgh, Dräel
Bass-Zorgul
Drums-Tezhrat, Madryal
Keyboards-Sub
