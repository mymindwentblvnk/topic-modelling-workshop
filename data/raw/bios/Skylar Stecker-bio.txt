Skylar Stecker (born April 24, 2002) is an American actress and singer. Stecker was born in Tampa in Florida. Her main influences are Bruno Mars, Beyonce Knowles, Christina Aguilera and Alicia Keys. Skylar's career began to take shape when she performed at the 2012 International Modeling & Talent Association Awards in Los Angeles where she was awarded best singer and most sought after talent. In 2012, she sung the national anthem at Wisconsin Badgers, New Orleans Saints, UCLA, and Green Bay Packers games. She has performed the National Anthem more than hundred times. She performed on stage along with MattyBRaps. In August of 2013, she began actively recording and releasing cover videos on her YouTube channel. In the fall of 2013 Skylar had a guest starring role in the ABC comedy Super Fun Night starring Rebel Wilson. Her first studio album was released on September 25, 2015, it featured the singles "Rooftop", "Rascal", "That's What's Up", and "Crazy Beautiful". Skylar Stecker announced tour dates with hip-hop and pop duo Kalin and Myles in December 2015. In December 2015, Skylar Stecker won a Libby Award for "Best Use of Internet Stardom for Animals."
