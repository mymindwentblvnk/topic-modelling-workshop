Trash Fashion are a 4-piece band made up of brothers Jet Storm (Vocals) and Mason Storm (Guitar) joined by Jimbo Ready (Bass) & Bam Bam (Drums). Current record label, UK: Propaganda Records. Japan: Vinyl Junkie.
