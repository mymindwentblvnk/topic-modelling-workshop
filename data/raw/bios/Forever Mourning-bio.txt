Forever Mourning are a symphonic black metal band from Harrisburg, Pennsylvania that formed in 2001. The band consists of:

Erin Ettinger - vocals
Ryan Chilcote - guitar, synth, drum programming (Ryan was also in the black/death metal band Divine Misery, now split up)
