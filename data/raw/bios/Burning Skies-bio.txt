Burning Skies is a Metal band from Bristol, UK. Their music contains elements of Thrash Metal, Hardcore, Death Metal and Grind. They were established in 2002 and released 3 full lengths on Lifeforce Records, the most recent on march, 23rd (2008). Burning Skies toured Europe with bands like Misery Index, Necrophagist, Origin and War from a Harlots Mouth. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burning+Skies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
