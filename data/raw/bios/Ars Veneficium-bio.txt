Ars Veneficium is a black metal band from Limburg, Belgium.  They formed in the year 2013.  They consist of S. (vocals), Archcaust (rhythm guitar), Ronarg (lead guitar), Y. (bass) and Norgameus (drums).  Past members include Lava (bass) and Sorath (drums).  Past live musicians include Ketter (rhythm guitar) and PerthalgiZ (drums).  Ars Veneficium write songs about Satanism, blasphemy, darkness, evil and death.  The band has released two EP's, two splits, two full-length albums and one single.  They are signed to Immortal Frost Productions.
