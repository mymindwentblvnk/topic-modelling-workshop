Melbourne, Australia-based producer Amin Payne has adopted the Teymori alias to explore a distinctive take on house music. His 2019 debut EP features a host of Melbourne's finest musicians including Blasko (Billy Davis), Silentjay (Hiatus Kaiyote/Sampa The Great) & Horatio Luna (30/70). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TEYMORI">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
