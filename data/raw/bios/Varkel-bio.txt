"Ära valeta!" is the 1st solo album from Estonian heavy-metal singer and musician Sven Varkel. Varkel released the album in 2007 after recording, mixing and mastering the album himself in Eesti Stuudio. He invited some of his friends to participate in the album also and the list included artists like Marko Atso (Metsatöll, Human Ground), Tarvo Valm (Human Ground, The Sun), Mart Veski (House of Games, Herald) and others. Sven Varkel is currently the lead singer in Estonian heavy-metal band Herald. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Varkel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
