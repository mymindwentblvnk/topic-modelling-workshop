Mouth of the Serpent is a new death metal/metal core band from Los Angeles, CA. Having released only one three-song EP in September 2008, they have quickly taken over the underground metal scene in LA. They will be releasing their debut full-length album, "Inception", in early 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mouth+Of+The+Serpent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
