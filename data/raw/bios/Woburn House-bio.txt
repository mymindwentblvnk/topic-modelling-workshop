"Music for adventurers who drink lava, bathe in fire and travel on clouds."

Woburn House is a psychedelic / progressive metal trio from Bonn, Germany. The members are Christian Kolf (guitar, lead vocals), Fabian Fabian (bass, vocals) and Florian Toyka (drums, vocals). 
 
So far they've released three studio albums on Paradigms Recordings / Zeitgeister Music:
Message To Ourselves Outside The Dreaming Machine (2006)
Monstrous Manoeuvres In The Mushroom Maze (2009)
Sleep Summer Storm (2011)
