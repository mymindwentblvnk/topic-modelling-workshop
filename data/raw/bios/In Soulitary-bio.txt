In Soulitary is a Heavy Metal band from São Paulo, Brazil formed in 2000.

Members:
Marcel Briani - Vocals
Danny Schneider - Guitars
Rafael Pacheco - Guitars
Elder Oliveira - Bass
André Bortolai - Keyboards
Matthew Liles - Drums
