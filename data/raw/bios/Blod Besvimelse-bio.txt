Personal project of Sandra "Melse" Bettinger, former wife of A. Bettinger/B.S.o.D..  Featured only on two splits with Grausamkeit and Heroin Makes Happy (another project from A. Bettinger). Sandra unfortunately passed away in 2013 due to unknown causes. B.S.o.D. has been said to have written most of the songs and participated as a session member helping out with drums & vocals. However he was not an official member.
