Aryan Terrorism play national socialistic black metal (NSBM). It is a side project of KV (Knjaz Varggoth) and SS (Saturious) of Nokturnal Mortum. 
Little is known about the status or future of this band, or even if there are other members. They only released one album in 2002, called WAR.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aryan+Terrorism">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
