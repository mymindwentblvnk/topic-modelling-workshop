A melodic death metal band from South Korea. The band broke up in 2005. The last line-up consisted of:
vocals/guitar - Birtcher (ex- Barcass-F, a Carcass cover band)
guitar - Scythe (ex- Sad Legend)
bass - Demian Frost
drums - Namaah (Sad Legend)

Demian Frost was in a brief-lived melodic death project band with Oathean leader Dosu Kim. Sad Legend is on indefinite hiatus and Birtcher and Scythe are currently not involved in the Korean metal scene.
