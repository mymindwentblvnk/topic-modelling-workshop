Darak Figueroa, (b. August 6, 2001), better known by his stage name StaySolidRocky, is an American rapper from Richmond, Virginia, born in San Antonio, Texas. Rocky’s best known for hit song “Party Girl,” which went viral on video sharing platform TikTok. The song caught the attention of rapper Lil Uzi Vert, who ended up remixing the song.
