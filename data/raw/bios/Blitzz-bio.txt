Blitzz a female fronted heavy/speed metal band formed in 1988 in Germany. Formerly known as Prinzz. They recorded an LP in 1989 but it was never released. Band's myspace: http://www.myspace.com/bandblitzz

Last known line-up:

Kerstin (Radtke) Buchfelner - vocals
Tommy Feiler - guitar 
Kai Tenneberg - guitar
Flatzz J. Wahnoff (Jens Hellmann) - bass
