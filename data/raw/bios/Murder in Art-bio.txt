Murder in Art was a Death 'n' Roll band from Äänekoski, Finland. Previously known as Clayforge (1997-1998).

Last known line-up
Pasi Pasanen - Drums (Clayforge, Morningstar (Fin), Plutonium Orange, Swallow the Sun)
Markus Jämsen - Guitar (Rautakello, Swallow the Sun)
Mikko Nurmi - Vocals (Clayforge, Heathen Hoof, Catatonia)
Petri Nousiainen - Guitar (Clayforge, Heathen Hoof, Catatonia, Perkele)
