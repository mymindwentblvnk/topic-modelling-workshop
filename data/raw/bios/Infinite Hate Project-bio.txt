Infinite Hate Project is the side project of Sahil Makhija a.k.a The Demonstealer from India.

Sahil is the frontman of Demonic Resurrection and his side projects are 

1] Reptilian Death [Death Metal & Grindcore].
2] The Demonstealer [Solo].
3] Infinite Hate Project [Death Metal].

Infinite Hate Project were first featured on the 4 Way split album "Defaced And Split", which was released last year and their tracks which were featured are:

1] Blasts Of Hatred.
2] Fuck The Sena Master.

Defaced And Split also featured other Indian Metal bands like Exhumation [Brutal Death Metal], Narsil [Death Metal] & US Death Metallers, Warface and was released by Demonstealer Records, which is owned by The Demonstealer.
