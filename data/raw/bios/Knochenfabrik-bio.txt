Knochenfabrik (German for Bone Factory) is a German punk rock band known for their fun & political lyrics. They come from the beautiful city of Cologne, but from the ugly part of it: Porz. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Knochenfabrik">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
