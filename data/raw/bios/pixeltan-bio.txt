Pixeltan are a Brooklyn based noise rock/electronic band consisting of Mika Yoneta (vocals/keyboard), Devin Flynn (bass/percussion), and Hisham Bharoocha (drums/electronics). Bharoocha used to be in Black Dice, while Yoneta and Flynn are from Plate Tectonics. They are currently signed to DFA Records and are working on new material and playing shows in New York City.
