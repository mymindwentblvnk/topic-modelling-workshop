Needlework is a rock/metal band with a twist of funk, based in Kopervik in Norway consisting of Aleksander Vormestrand(vokal), Morten Fjeld(Lead guitar), Camilo Andres Beltran(Second guitar), Viktor Nordberg(drums), Katharina Sivertsen(bass).
