Founded in January 2011, in the portuguese capital of Lisbon, Wall of Vipers are a new death/thrash metal band. Its creation follows a recent trend on portuguese metal towards uptempo/scream-vocal styled death/thrash metal. Pantera and Switchtense figure among their main influences.
