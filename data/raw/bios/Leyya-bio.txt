Leyya is an electronic pop duo consisting of Marco Kleebauer (solo as Karma Art and part of Ant Antic) and Sophie Lindinger, based in Vienna, Austria. The two grew up together in Eferding and moved to Vienna in 2012 to create music. Signed to Las Vegas Records, their debut EP, Drowning in Youth was released in November 2014. Their LP, Spanish Disco, was released in May 2015.
