MINCE GRINDCORE band started after disbanded 2 bands late 80s decade: Angoixa (punk / hardcore) and Rottest Slag (death-metal). Difenacum seem to collect the musical brutality of Rottest Slag and the lyrical political / social furious of punk band Angoixa. DIFENACUM has recorded 5 albums and several tracks in compilations and tributes. All this stuff always in "Hecatombe Records".
