Wojciech Hoffmann is a Polish instrumental rock Guitarist. He played in bands such as Turbo. Now He started his solo career with the album "Drzewa" He's supported by Ibanez. He plays Ibanez JM model Steve Vai. Influences: Deep Purple, Steve Vai
