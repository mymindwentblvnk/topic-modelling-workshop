An Extreme Death/Gore/Grind Trio. from Cleveland OH. The inception of Corpulate started when Drummer Matt, and Ex-bassist Blake, had left there prior band to go toward a more technical, heavier side of music. Not but a month later did Beast (Matt P.) come into the picture to do Vocals. tired of the cookie-cutter "metal" in Cleveland, Corpulate, Being bent on showing the genre and Ohio that there's more to Cleveland then just hardcore, punk or fashion-core we bring a downright brutal, doomy, technical, mathy sound that Cleveland has been missing for a long time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Corpulate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
