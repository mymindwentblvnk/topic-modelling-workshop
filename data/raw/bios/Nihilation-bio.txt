Nihilation is the name of several artists:
1) West Virginia, USA metal
2) German black metal

1) A band formed in Huntington, West Virginia. Nihilations style can best be described as Metal, as they don't fit into any particular sub-genre of Metal. They draw influence from many bands of many Metal genres including Death Metal, Black Metal, Power Metal, Metalcore, and Thrash. Members include Karl Garret on Lead Guitar, Seth Carey on Guitar, Chris Marshalls on Vocals, and TJ Raines on Drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nihilation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
