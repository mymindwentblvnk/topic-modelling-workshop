Thomas Byron Courtney (born 2 July 1989) known professionally as Tom Zanetti, is a British singer-songwriter, rapper, record producer and model, best known for his hit single, "You Want Me" featuring British R&B singer, Sadie Ama which charted in the United Kingdom, Belgium and Ireland. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tom+Zanetti">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
