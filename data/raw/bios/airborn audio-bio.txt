Airborn Audio are High Priest and M. Sayyid of Antipop Consortium. The group formed in 2002 when Antipop Consortium split up. They are currently signed to the Ninja Tune label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Airborn+Audio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
