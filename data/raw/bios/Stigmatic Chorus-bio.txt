Stigmatic Chorus from Russia (Moscow) was formed in 1999 and where known as Charon before 1999. They play symphonic black metal.

Discography:

Waters of Styx (split with Antaios) (1999)
Autodafe (Full-lenght - 2005)
Gedonist (Full-length - 2008)
Waters of Styx (Full-length - 2008)
Indulgentia (EP - 2009)
