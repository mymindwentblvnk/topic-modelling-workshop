Kazuto (Real name: Kazuto Ishida) is a Japanese Neo-Classical Guitarist that does covers and original tunes, all instrumental.

In 2005 he released his first CD (Single), Greet With Delight/Double Edge, throught his own label "Ben-K Music".

Kazuto competed in the Dean Guitars Shredders Contest in 2008. He came a long way but didn't make it.

He made also an entry for Guitar Idol 2009, with the song "Double Edge", that also appears in his first CD.

Releases: 
[2005] Greet With Delight/Double Edge

He has also made alot of other tunes, but those are unreleased at the moment.
