1) Born in 1996. Engram is Martin Bowes and John Costello. The project has finally landed... What Am I? ep out now album coming soon. www.engrammusik.co.uk

Creating electronic music off and on, mostly off, since the 1980s. Came back to it as a hobby in 2004. Taking it more seriously these days, with an EP and live dates in 2013, and a solo album plus an ENGRAM album scheduled for 2014.

2) Engram is a alternative, post-punk rock band which formed in 2011. in Jagodina, Serbia. The band consist of Vladimir Djurdjevic (vocals, guitar), Nikola Ilic (drums) and Milos Milojevic (bass). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Engram">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
