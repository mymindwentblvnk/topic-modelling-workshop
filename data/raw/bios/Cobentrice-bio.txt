Cobentrice born in 2009 in the city of Pereira Colombia with a proposal Death Metal, Slam and Brutal Death.

start a death metal style and sound over the years evolved into a more Brutal style and Slammer, with lyrics GORE, mental disorders, experiences, entheogens.
brutalizing, nodding and goreando.

in 2009 Cobentrice was formed by an idea of Saul Salazar (Guitar and drum-programming) and Jhonny Marin (vocals).
