Pixieguts is cyber vocalist Marie Craven in Australia with electronic musicians round the world. Part of two duo bands: Cwtch (with Dementio13, Wales) and PIXSID (with Dj Sid, USA). Collaborates closely with Toronto metal dj Crimson Death. Also with Swedish synthpop producer Voide and together they publish an electronic music podcast. Guest vocals for Russian triphop outfit VFSix.

Other releases, tracks, remixes and collaborations with: 
Max Waves (Italy), Adrian Carter (UK), Northcape (UK), SK123 (UK) and many more.

electronica, female vocalist, idm, trip-hop, alternative
