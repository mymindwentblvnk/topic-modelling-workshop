Young heavy-metal band from southern Poland. Four young man who want to do some good melodic heavy/power metal. They are working to make they first demo CD called 'Here comes the Demo(n)'.

Soulcatcher owe it's name to Glen Cook's novel 'The Black Company'. A books' character called Soulcatcher was a main inspiration for receive this name.

Soulcatcher are:
Sylwester Karnuszewicz (vocals, guitar),
Jaroslaw Kucharski (bass),
Marek Franielczyk (guitar),
Pawel Gonera (drums).
