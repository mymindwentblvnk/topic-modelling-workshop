Edensands is a french metal group born in 2006. The band consist of Stefan Tudela: vocals, Yoan Da Costa & Maxence Hugues: lead guitars, Vinz: bass guitar, Benjamin Nogaret: drums. Their first album, Living Kills, is a gateway through which the group members can meet their fans and give them some idea of their universe.
