4-piece depressive/suicidal black metal band from Bjuv, Sweden formed in 2009.

members:  Cipralex - Drums
 Weird - Guitars
 Kaos - vocals 
 Kräk - bass 
 (K) - guitars 

the first demo titled "Ge Upp" was released in may 2010
for contact, questions etc please use http://www.facebook.com/hypokondri



in january 2011 keyboardist player odious left the band, and Kräk and (K) joined. In October Hypokondri played live for the first time, and in early 2012  Hypokondri Entered the studio once again. They are as of now working on an EP or perhaps a full length allbum. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hypokondri">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
