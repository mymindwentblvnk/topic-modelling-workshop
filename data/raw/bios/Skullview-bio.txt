Skullview is a United States, Indiana based Heavy Metal band formed in the winter of 1995. They have released three albums since their inception: Legends of Valor (1997), Kings of the Universe (1999) and Consequences of Failure (2001) on the R.I.P. label from Chicago, Illinois. As of July 2010 the band have released their 4th cd titled "Metalkill The World" which was released on the Pure Metal Record label from Germany.
