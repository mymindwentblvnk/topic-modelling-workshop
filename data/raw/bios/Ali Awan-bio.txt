Ali Awan is a singer/songwriter from West Philadelphia, PA, USA. Ali began playing shows in West Philly basements as a guitarist and bassist at 15 years old. After multiple nation-sweeping tours, as a member of Needle Points and Jane Church, Ali has started to write and perform his own material. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ali+Awan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
