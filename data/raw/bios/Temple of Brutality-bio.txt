Thrash Metal from the USA. Formed in 2004. Legendary bassist David Ellefson,best know for his work with legendary band Megadeth (also Soulfly for a short time) joined the band after he was rejected by Dave Mustaine to join the band again. Stet Howland also a legend in the Metal community joined the band after leaving W.A.S.P.
Together with Killing Machine members they created a brutal sound in music.

Members: Todd Barnes - Vocals
Peter Scheithauer - Guitars
David Ellefson - Bass
