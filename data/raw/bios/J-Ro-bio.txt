J-ro the pacoima native is a member of the departed hip-hop influencing group "Tha Alkaholiks". After many great albums the alkaholiks recently broke up. From there J-ro put together a mixtape 818 antics the mixtape. It has been released in parts of europe but has not released a release date for the U.S. It includes "Ask Her","Party People", and the hit single "Just Rockin". In late 2004 he migrated into Sweden. A fun fact to know about J-ro is that his second born son is following in his footsteps and is planing to drop an album mid 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/J-Ro">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
