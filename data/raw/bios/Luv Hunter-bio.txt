Luv Hunter band was formed in 1990 in Las Vegas, Nevada (United States of America)
The band recorded their only album "Luv Hunter" in the same year the band was formed (1990) under Iron Workz / Azra Records. Short after, the band split up..
The band plays typical Heavy / Power metal of that time which was played in US.
Line-up for the album was :
Dave Milligan - Lead Vocals
Walter Robbins - Guitar
Dave Kauffman - Guitar
Steve Bray - Bass
