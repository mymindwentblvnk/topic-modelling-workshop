Flammensturm is a Pagan/Black Metal Band from Austria which were formed in 2005. They released their first demo "Feuer aus heidnischer Glut" in 2007.

Formed 2005

Origin:  Althofen/Griffen, Carinthia, Austria

Current Line-Up:

Helmar - Rythm Guitar
Ragnar - Lead Guitar
Nordolf - Bass
