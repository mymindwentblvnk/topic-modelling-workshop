Nikki Nair is an electronic producer born and raised in East Tennessee and now lives in Atlanta, United States. He has releases on labels including Pretty Weird, Gobstopper, Scuffed, TRAM Planet, Dirtybird, Banoffee Pies, and Muy Muy Ltd. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nikki+Nair">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
