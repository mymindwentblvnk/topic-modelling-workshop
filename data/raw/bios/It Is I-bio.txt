It Is I released a demo ten years before their album with the title "Evolve". First thing that you see is the participation of Thomas Choi of the legendary  sludge bands Noothgrush & AsbestosDeath. Musically the band walks in doom metal paths, with some sludge and industrial music elements. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/It+Is+I">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
