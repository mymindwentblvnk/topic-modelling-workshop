Ignitor is a five-piece band from Texas. They are a metal purists dream come true, incorporating the key elements that made bands such as Dio, Iron Maiden, Judas Priest, Diamond Head & Metallica, the legends that they are. Guitarists Stuart Laurence and Annah Moore, take you to school & present us memorable, melodic & crunchy riffs. 

Original guitarist Beverly Barrington replaced Annah in 2007 and Jason McMaster (Watchtower, Dangerous Toys) replaced Erika on vocals in 2008.  The band will release a new album called "The Spider Queen" in September 2009.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ignitor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
