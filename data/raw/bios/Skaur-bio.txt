Skaur is a one-man project by NordMann formed in 2003, playing a black metal/ambient fusion. Skaur have released eight demos and one split. Hateful distorted guitars and somber clean guitars fill the air with Skaur on play. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Skaur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
