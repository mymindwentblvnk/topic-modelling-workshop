Lies of Smiles are a north east rock band, born from several bands like axis(rock band, not the r n b band), panama, blackrose, pauline gillan band.
brought together by davy little of axis and pauline gillan band fame in the 80's
.
Straight laced no nonsense hard rock, great sound, and a tight band with mick tucker of tank fame guesting on one of the tracks (Fallen)
new album released december called  'cross and claw'
 this is a nine track cd of pure rock laced with some fantastic keyboards overseen by the impecable fred purser
