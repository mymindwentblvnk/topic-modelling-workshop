NO END IN SIGHT are a fresh young band from Stuttgart /South Germany that knows how to mix Swedish Death Metal and Hardcore. After tons of shows and tours NEIS are a constant part of the the metal and hardcore scene in many countries. 
If you don't know them you have to check them out.

Some new stuff with new shouter will be uploaded soon.

NEW line-up:
Vocals - Dominik Thoma
Guitar - Adam Lasocki
Guitar - Michael Böhm
Bass - Alexander Wendel
Drums - Samuel Schäfer
