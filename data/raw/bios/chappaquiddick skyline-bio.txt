Chappaquiddick Skyline is a project of Joe Pernice, singer/songwriter of The Pernice Brothers and Scud Mountain Boys. Pernice has released one album under the Chappaquiddick Skyline moniker in 1999 on Sub Pop records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chappaquiddick+Skyline">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
