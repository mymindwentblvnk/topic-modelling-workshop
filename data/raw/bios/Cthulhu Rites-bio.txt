Cthulhu Rites is two-men project, inspired by H.P. Lovecraft.
In December, 2012 changed name from Cthulhu Rises to Cthulhu Rites.

Discography:

Pośród Misteriów Koszmaru, Album (June 2014)
Acheront Bezkształtnego Diabelstwa, EP (May, 2013)
Ku Chwale Mrocznych Eonów I, Demo (September, 2012) - Released as Cthulhu Rises
