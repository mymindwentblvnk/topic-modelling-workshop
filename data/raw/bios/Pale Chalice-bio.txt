Pale Chalice hails from San Francisco, California and was formed in 2008. Their debut EP 'Afflicting the Dichotomy of Trepid Creation' was released in March 2011 by Flenser Records. Their full-length album 'Negate the Infinite and Miraculous' was released on June 16, 2015 through Gilead Media. The current band lineup is Ephemeral Domignostika (vocals, bass), Baneist Nonrutin (guitar), Oram Evad (guitar), and Masthantric Nodrab (drums) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pale+Chalice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
