Oghre is a Latvian progressive metal/sludge metal (early stoner rock) band from Rīga, Latvia, formed in 2012. They released the debut studio album Gana in 2017.  It was followed in 2020 by Grimt.

Members:

Oskars Dreģis - vocals;
Kristaps Baķis - guitar;
Toms Gaļinauskis - guitar;
Andis Zvejnieks - bass;
Normunds Balodis (Velns Viņu Zin) - drums.

Former members:

Raivis Bēts - vocals;
Kristiāns Kļava - vocals;
Raivo Balodis - guitar, vocals;
Gatis Krievāns (Lietotas Mēbeles no Holandes) - guitar;
Matīss Kušķis - bass;
Oskars Sīpols (Izslēdzējs) - bass.
