A technical death metal / thrash metal band from Belgium that was formed in 1990.  They released two demos, Brutal Violation in 1990 and Beyond Reality in 1991.  They released two full-length albums,  Fatal Exposure in 1992 and Values in 1994.  Both full-length albums were released by Crypta Records.  They played some gigs in 1997 and disbanded in 1998.  They reformed in 2015 and they are considered active today.
