Max Barskih (born 8 March, 1990 as Nikolay Bortnik, in Kherson, Ukraine) is a Ukrainian singer-songwriter. After graduating he moved to Kyiv where he entered the Music Academy for pop singers. In 2008 he entered the second season of Ukranian Star Factory and became finalist.
