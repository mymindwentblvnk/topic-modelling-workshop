Ronke Owens aka Young Roc was born in Detroit, MI in 1989.
Detroit is  were he learned how to make beats,produce,edit videos, film, write, mix, master and create songs. At the young age of 12 the creative mind used to make songs on a karaoke machine and rap over used industry beats. As the passion for music grew on him he started getting more in to tune with his flow and eventually started to get better. Young Roc has potential to be one of the best rapper/producers of all-time with his creativeness. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Young+Roc">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
