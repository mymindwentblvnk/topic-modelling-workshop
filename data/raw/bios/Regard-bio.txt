Dardan Aliu, professionally known as DJ Regard, is a DJ from Kosovo. His 2019 single "Ride It" is a remix of Jay Sean's 2008 song, which became a viral sensation on TikTok and has since achieved international success. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Regard">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
