Spanish thrash metal band formed in 1988 in Santander, Cantabria. They were very influenced by Megadeth, Sacred Reich, Vicious Rumors and Annihilator. In 1993 they recorded the demo "Censored". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Anger+Tears">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
