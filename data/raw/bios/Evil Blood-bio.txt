Thrash metal band from Croatia. They are formed 1983. 1984. they recorded demo "Midnight In Sodom", but it is released in '89. Later they changed name into Lord Of Darkness, but Italian band Dark Lord threatened them with legal action over the similarities between their names, so they had to change the name again. Finally, they relocated in Scotland as Djinn.In 2007 they created a band with abaddon(ex venom) called beneath but after a few years split up.Now Evil blood is reformed!!with a new lineup and have a new release in the near future.myspace.com/evilbloodthrashmetal <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evil+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
