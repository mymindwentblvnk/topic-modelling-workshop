Contagium was a crust punk / stenchcore band, formed around 2008 in Halifax - East coast Canada. Since their formation, the band has played an endless number of notable shows and embarked on many tours - some of which include their 2008 Canadian September-October tour, 2009 North American tour May-June, 2009 American December tour, and their 2010 American August tour. As of present, Contagium has two releases; Self-Titled 7" EP [Black Raven Records, 2009] and Archaic Full-Length LP [The Total End Records, 2010].
