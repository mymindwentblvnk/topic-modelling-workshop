There are two artists with this name:

1. Green Division is a Hungarian gothic metal project by ex-Nevergreen singer and quitarist Bob Macura. Formed in Subotica, Vojvodina (Serbia), 2004. 

2. Green Division is a seven-piece ska band out of Pickering, Ontario. They have been kicking around the Ontario ska scene since the winter of 2002, and have been blowing away crowds with every show they've played. Though they're mainly a ska/punk band, you'll find elements of metal, reggae, and even some rap in their sound. Green Division has had the honour of playing with bands such as Big D and the Kids Table, Mustard Plug, Streetlight Manifesto, Subb, General Rudie, and The Kingpins. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Green+Division">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
