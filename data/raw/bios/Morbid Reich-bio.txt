Band was started in 2006 under many different names until we finally decided on Morbid Reich from the infamous Vader demo. We have had a stable line up of

Mark Pannenborg - Vocals
Josh De La Haye - Bass
Simon Anger - Guitar
James Andrews - Guitar
Phi McVay - Drums

We play extreme metal, we do mostly originals, but we do a few covers just for fun.

We Have released a mini cd that was given out free at our first gig. It contained Destroyer Of Shadows (Original) and The Levelling Dust (Marduk Cover) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Morbid+Reich">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
