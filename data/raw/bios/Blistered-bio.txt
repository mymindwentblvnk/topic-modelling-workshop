Blistered is a metalcore band from Tampa, FL USA. Taking influences from bands such as Buried Alive, Unbroken, Contempt, Slugfest, Figure Four, Extinction, Racetraitor, Morning Again, Chokehold, 108, Culture, Indecision, Strife, Trial, Arma Angelus, Hatebreed, and Turmoil, they set the bar high for newer bands with the old 90's/early 2000's style. They released their debut 7", "Reject The Shame" on Bottled Up Records in 2013. Hear more at http://blistered.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blistered">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
