I'll Eat Your Face is a two-man grind band from Cork City, Ireland. They formed in 2006 and have toured and gigged at home and abroad with DJs, alternative bands and death metal acts. 
The have released two splits, a 12 track CD and 2 DVDs. The band's debut album 'Irritant' is released for free in Feb 2010 online. Most of their songs are about fat women, snails and laser-powered cannibal ducks. 

If you feel their hot grinding power in your brains you will know life's truth.
