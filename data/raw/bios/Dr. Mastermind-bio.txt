Dr. Mastermind is a heavy metal / speed metal that's from Portland, Oregon.  Matt McCourt from Wild Dogs and former Journey drummer Deen Castronovo started the band in 1985.  According to Wikipedia, they started it in 1982.  A demo was released in 1986 and Dr. Mastermind was released the same year.  In 2005, Sin Sandwich  was released.  The current line-up consists of Dr. Mastermind (Matt McCourt) on vocals & bass, Robert Robinson on guitar and Troy Stutzman on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dr.+Mastermind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
