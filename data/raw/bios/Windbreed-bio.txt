a death metal band from New York, US that was formed in 1992. Released a demo "into the breed" in 1994, a live album in 1994, an EP "pipe dreams" in 1996 and 3 released of an EP named "til the bitted end" in 2011 which were actually recorded in 3 different session from 1995-1996 but were never released back then due to sudden breakup of the band.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Windbreed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
