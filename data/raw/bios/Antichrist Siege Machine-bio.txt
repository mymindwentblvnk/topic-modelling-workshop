A war metal band from Richmond, Virginia.  They formed in the year 2016.  The band consists of Ryan Zell on vocals & guitar and Scott Bartley on drums.  They are currently signed to Stygian Black Hand.  Their lyrical themes are Anti-Christianity, Satanism and warfare.
