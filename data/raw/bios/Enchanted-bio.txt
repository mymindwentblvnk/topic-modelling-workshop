There are several artists with the name "Enchanted":

1) Enchanted is an American musical project headed by MoJoe Nicosia and his wife Dana Nicosia, similar to Pilgrimage and Enigma.

2) Enchanted is a black metal band from Norway that started in 1992 and broke up in 1998. They self-released one demo called Breed My Sorrows that was later released again on professional cassette by Vox Mortiis Records with completely different artwork. Enchanted's line-up included Iscariah before he joined Immortal and later Necrophagia and Dead To This World.

3) Enchanted is an ambient and dungeon synth project from Finland.
