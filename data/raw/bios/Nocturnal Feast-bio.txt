Nocturnal Feast was Black/Death Metal band from Finland (Nurmo).Their lyrical themes were satanism and antichristianity.
Last known line-up
Wrath (Janne Kuru) - Vocals (Enochian Crescent)
Cauldron (Jani Martikkala) - Drums (ex-...And Oceans, ex-Enochian Crescent, Throes of Dawn, The True Black Dawn)
