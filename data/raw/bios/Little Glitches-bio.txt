Little Glitches hail from Sheffield, UK. Tangled up in hazy electronica and folk roots, this four piece's sound is built from intricate layers of guitar and Rhodes piano, over a constantly traveling rhythm section, with four soulful voices raising the call to arms.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Little+Glitches">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
