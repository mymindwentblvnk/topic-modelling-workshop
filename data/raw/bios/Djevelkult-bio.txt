Djevelkult is a Black Metal band from Oppland, Norway and was first founded as Skadefryd in the summer of 2009 by Dødsherre Xarim, but changed the name to Djevelkult in the summer of 2010.
The lineup to this day consists of Dødsherre Xarim on guitar/vocal, T.K on drums, Skabb on bass/vocal and Beleth on guitar.
