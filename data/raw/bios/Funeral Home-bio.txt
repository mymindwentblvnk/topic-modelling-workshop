Originally from Virginia but now hailing from the western suburbs of Chicago  Funeral Home is headed up by identical mirror twins, Taylor & Jamison MacKillop. Funeral Home is their most recent project as their former band Tiger Cub is coming to an end as its members graduated from college and are moving away. 

In the same way as their namesake, the music of Funeral Home lives in those in between emotions... no longer living, but not yet in the ground... drone on my friends... drone on...  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Funeral+Home">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
