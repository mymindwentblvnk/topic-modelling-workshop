There are two artists, using the name Hellrider:
1. Thrash-Death metal band from Burgas, Bulgaria. Formed in 2008 and released an EP called The Face Of Death online in 2011 and on CD in 2012.
2. Heavy metal band from Medellin, Colombia. Started playing in 2007 and released a debut album, called Hellrider in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hellrider">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
