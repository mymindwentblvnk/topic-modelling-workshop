Danish West Coast Electronica

Timescape is a Danish electronic act that has been around since 1996. Timescape 
has released a broad range of electronic music - from ambient, chill out and lounge to techno, psytrance and dance music.

The core of the band today is Tony Andersen, Kent Eskildsen and Tina S. Johansen.
