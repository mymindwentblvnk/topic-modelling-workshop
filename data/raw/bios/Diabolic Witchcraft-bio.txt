One-man black metal/grindcore band from Perm, Russia, formed in 1999 by a man known as Diabolical Necromancer (who also played in a band called Bestial Demongoat and another named Dark Omen). Released a demo in 2003 called "Demonic War Necrocarnage". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diabolic+Witchcraft">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
