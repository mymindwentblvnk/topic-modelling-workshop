Aoora is a South Korean singer, dancer, songwriter, and choreographer. He is one of the members of the popular K-pop group AA.

He started his solo career under the stage name gn.E in 2009, and then joined AA in 2011.

Aoora was born on January 10, 1986

AoorA is a band formed in April 2005 by Mladen Lukic (lead guitarist). One of the rare melodic thrash bands from Serbia reached it's first success when recorded a demo called "Nightside of Mind" in October 2006, which features 4 songs. Band is currently working on new material and album is expected in 2007. Band consists of 5 members including: Mladen Lukic (Lead Guitars), Zoltan Shimon (Drums), Josip Opacic (Bass Guitar), Milan Nikolic (Rhythm Guitars) and Uros Maric (Vocals). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/AoorA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
