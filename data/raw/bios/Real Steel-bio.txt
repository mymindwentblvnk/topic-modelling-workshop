Split-up in 1992, and reformed briefly in 2003, before reforming again in 2007. Currently planning to record and release a full-length album of new material in the near future.

Released "I Rule The Radio" on the Heavy Artillery compilation (Auburn Records), and released the full-length cassette "Real Steel" in 1990 (one Auburn's most obscure releases) as well as a 7" single earlier that year. Also released "Never Mind The Money" on WMMS's "Buzzard Tracks" compilation (1992).
