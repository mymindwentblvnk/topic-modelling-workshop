Thrash/crust metal act from Tokyo, Japan. Thrashing since 1988, the band released several splits, demo's, EP's and full-length studio albums.

Vocals: Hideyuki Okahara
Guitar: Yasunari Honda
Drums: Hiroshi Yoshioka
