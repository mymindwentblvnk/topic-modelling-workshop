Crushing old school death metal featuring the mighty Kam Lee (ex-Massacre) on vocals! Broken Gravestones discharge dark and heavy guitar riffs with growling vocals and an obsession with horror and zombies. "Let Sleeping Corpses Lie" is their debut release and in the vein of Bolt Thrower, Obituary, and Asphyx. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Broken+Gravestones">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
