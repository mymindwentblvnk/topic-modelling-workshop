Izuver is a Russian cyber death metal band from Naberezhnye Chelny, Tatarstan. Founded in 2005.

Current line-up:
Paskuda - bass, vocal (ex-Гады)
Irina - guitar (Butterfly 4, Огнеопасно!)
Lena - guitar (Grim grave)
Ant - drums (studio only) (Alkonost, Eldenhor)

Former/past member(s):
Babay - guitar (Horgas)
Vetrotrav - guitar (Horgas)

Discography:
2006 - Unwished Unnatural Interbreeding
2007 - Defrosen
