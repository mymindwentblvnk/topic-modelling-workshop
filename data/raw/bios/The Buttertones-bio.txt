The Buttertones are a five-piece rock act hailing from Los Angeles, California since 2011. Its current members include Richard Araiza, Sean Redman, Modesto Cobian, London Guzman, and Grant Snyder, the first three originally starting out as "music school misfits".

Their debut self-titled album Buttertones was released in 2013, followed by For the Head and For the Feet and American Brunch. Gravedigging would then be released in 2017 with Midnight in a Moonless Dream (2018) following. The band's most recent album is Jazzhound, released on April 10th, 2020. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Buttertones">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
