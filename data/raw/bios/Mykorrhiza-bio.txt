MYKORRHIZA is the Stockholm based death metal project formulated in 2001 by erstwhile EXCRUCIATE guitarist Henrik Brynolfsson and the SIN and STAIRLAND drummer Martin Karlsson.Cutting a debut eponymous EP in february of 2002 the project was fronted on a session basis by Anders Strokirk of BLACKSHINE and NECROPHOBIC.
The duo employed a whole host of guest vocalists on the 2003 “Shattered Dreams”.In 2006,the band gives the second album “Northern Remembrance”.Even though active,MYKORRHIZA still underground.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mykorrhiza">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
