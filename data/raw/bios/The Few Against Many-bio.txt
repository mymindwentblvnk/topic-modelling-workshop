The Few Against Many is a swedish melodic symphonic death metal band, consisting of Christian Älvestam ( lead vocals, rhythm guitar), Patrik Gardberg (lead guitar), Anders Edlund (bass), Pär Johansson (backing vocals) and Jani Stefanovic (drums). They separate themselves from many similar bands by their Swedish lyrics. The band's debut album "Sot", was released on September 15, 2009, via Pulverised Records in Super Jewel Box format and a limited digipack format edition. "Sot" will also receive an exclusive vinyl version edition release, also via Pulverised Records. It's available in 2 different formats: gatefold-sleeve with black vinyl and as a classic picture-disc vinyl (both formats strictly limited to 250 copies).
