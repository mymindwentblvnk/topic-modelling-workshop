Mutilation Rites is a black metal band from Brooklyn, conceived by Michael Dimmitt and Justin Ennis in January 2009. After various incarnations, hey have amalgamated with George Paul and Ryan Jones. Influences include Darkthrone, Discharge, Dissection, and Weakling.
