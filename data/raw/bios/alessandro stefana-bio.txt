Guitarist and composer Alessandro Stefana, better known as “Asso” (born Brescia, Italy 1981), collaborates with Vinicio Capossela and Marco Parente among others. Debut solo records 'Poste E Telegrafi' is released on the American label Important Records and features Marc Ribot and Leo Abrahams.
