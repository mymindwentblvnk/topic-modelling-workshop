Edge of Attack is a Canadian metal band fusing old-school thrash and power metal with a modern edge. Technically skilled and refreshingly inventive, Edge of Attack has it all. Shredding guitars, haunting vocals and furious drumming are Edge of Attack's stamp of authority.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Edge+of+Attack">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
