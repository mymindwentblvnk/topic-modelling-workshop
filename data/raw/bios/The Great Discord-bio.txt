The Great Discord is, according to themselves, a progressive death pop band from Linköping, Sweden. The band consists of vocalist Fia Kempe, guitarists André Axell and Gustav Almberg, bassist Rasmus Carlson and drummer Aksel Holmgren. They are currently signed to The Sign Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Great+Discord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
