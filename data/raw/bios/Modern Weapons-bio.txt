Metal for gamers by gamers! Modern Weapons is comprised of Jamison Boaz on Vocals, Adam Vex and Aaron Graves on Guitars, Joe Sego on Bass, and Alex Jackson on Drums. Modern Weapons write and record songs with inspiration and influences from many different Anime and Video Game titles. If you love Metal, Anime, and Video Games as much as we do, check out our new album "OST"!  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Modern+Weapons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
