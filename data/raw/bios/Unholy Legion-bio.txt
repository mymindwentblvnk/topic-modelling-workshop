Thrash/black/death metal band from New Zealand. Released the demo "Behold the Unholy Legions" in 2002. Members went on to form Erotic Necrosis and Burning Solitude.

Lineup: Morgan (vocals), Alex (guitar), Patrick (guitar), Daniel (bass), Kurt (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unholy+Legion">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
