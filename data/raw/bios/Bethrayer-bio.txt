Bethrayer is a thrash/nu-metal band from Kojetín, Czech Republic. Bethrayer exists more than 17 years. It is also one of the semifinalist of Czech famous music competition Jim Beam Music. Bethrayer's debut CD is called 'Ray of Hope' from 2004. 

Download new promo CD (2008) and Ray of Hope (2004)!

Bethrayer are:

  - Olaf - kytara, zpěv
  - Tommy - kytara
  - AleK - basa, vokály
  - Sádlo - bicí
 
ex-member: Pegar - kytara R.I.P.
