CAROLINE BLUE is a Melodic Hard Rock Band based in Upstate NY. Our originals have been described as being similar in style to KISS with touches of LA Guns, Type O Negative, Chevelle, Motley Crue, Dokken, Megadeth & more! CAROLINE BLUE is a 3 Piece Band consisting of Wayne W. Johnson (Lead Vocals/Guitar) and assorted hired musicians.
