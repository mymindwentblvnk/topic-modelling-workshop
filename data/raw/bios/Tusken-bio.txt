Tusken is a 4-piece hardcore band from Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Their music is a blend of chaotic hardcore with sludge and metal elements. Their 4- song ep, titled "The More I", was released in May 2008 through Ghouls Records. It is available, name your price @ http://ghoulsrecords.bandcamp.com/album/the-more-i-ep <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tusken">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
