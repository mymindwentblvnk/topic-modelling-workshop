Ikona is now nu metal band from Belarus. Bands earliest EP, called D-Emo, was made in nu-metal/mall emo genre, the second EP, that was named "Derzhat' V Sebe Lezviya" or "Hold Blades Inside" can be characterised as soft mall emo .  Their most recent album, "Nevinosimaya Tishina Minut" or "Unstandable Silence Of Minutes" is pure As I Lay Dying-styled metal. Guys from Ikona have already played with such popular russian bands as Amatory and Psycheya.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/iKONA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
