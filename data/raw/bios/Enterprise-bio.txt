Multiple artists have existed by this name:

1. Enterprise is the title of an electronic music project by Alexander Khorosch, who has also been known as Archivolt. Tracks by this name, having a Psychedelic trance influenced sound, have appeared on compilations for labels such as Spirit Zone and Boshke Beats.

2. Enterprise was a funk and soul group best known for having featured musician Morris Day in his early years. After hooking up with Prince, Day would be lifted from the group and transplanted into the outfit that became known as The Time. Like Prince, Enterprise was based out of the Twin Cities. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enterprise">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
