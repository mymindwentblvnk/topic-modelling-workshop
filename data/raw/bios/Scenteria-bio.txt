Scenteria are a Melodic Death/Thrash Metal band from Halmstad, Sweden who formed in 2002. After recording three demos, they released their debut album Art of Aggression in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scenteria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
