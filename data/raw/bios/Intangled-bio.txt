A death/thrash band from  Santa Rosa, CA , formed in 2006, signed to Burning Token Records http://www.BurningTokenRecords.com, and fronted by bassist Zane Covington, backed by guitarist and singer, Jimmy Kennedy ,and featuring  Shane Sebald  on drums. They have heavy influences from Slayer, Death, Iron Maiden, Pantera, Exodus, Bathory, Megadeth, Death Angel, Warbringer, Cannibal Corpse, as well as many others.
