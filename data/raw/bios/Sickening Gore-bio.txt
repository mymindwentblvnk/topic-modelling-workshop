Sickening Gore was an old school death metal band from Zurich, Switzerland that released only one album called Destructive Reality.  The last label they were signed to was Massacre Records.  Their lyrical themes included death, corruption and politics.  Their last known line-up consisted of Matt Burr on vocals & guitar, Alex Burr on guitars,  Danny Büsch on bass and  Chris Huwiler on drums.  Nenad Đukić was a vocalist, he's the only past member of Sickening Gore.
