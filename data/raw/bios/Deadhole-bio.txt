Deadhole (Trash - vocals, Creepshow - bass, Cancer - drums, Beast - guitars) is a blackened death metal band from USA. Deadhole's music is aggressive and blasphemous; Trash himself is a self-declared satanist.

So far, they released a 5 tracks demo in 2005 called "The Curse of the Ghoul". There's also an unreleased February 2007 rehearsal - check out the free tracks.
