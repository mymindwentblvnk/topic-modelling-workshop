An accomplished, professional musician with multiple talents within many aspects of the musical profession. Eric has been involved with many musicians who can be considered icons to the world of music listeners. The first solo CD (2000) scored 3 "hits" on local and national radio and is still played and requested on many streaming web radio stations. Approximately 10,000 copies of the CD have been distributed. The second CD, titled "Meet My Demons" was released on retrospect records and tells the story of a man with severe anxiety and panic disorder.

Eric has played with noteable musicians such as Tommy Stewart, one time drummer of Godsmack, Bill Adams or "BA" from Sponge and Halloween, Dimebag Darrell of Pantera, Ace Frehley of Kiss and more.

Born in Detroit in August 1967, Eric has been a musician since age 12. He started out on drums, then moved to stringed instruments such as bass and guitar.

Eric now owns and operates "Demon Digital Sound Studio" in Vassar Michigan and works for Acoustica Inc.
