CorpseFlesh is a brutal death metal band from Mandurah, Australia / Russia.  They have been around since 2005.  The members are  Ewza Flesh-Grinder (vocals, guitar)  Sambo Marwick (guitars, bass) and  Vlad Melnik (drums).  They are signed to Ungodly Ruins Productions.  They have released two EP's, two full-length albums and one split.  They write lyrics about gore and violence.  Their music is found at their BandCamp website. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CorpseFlesh">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
