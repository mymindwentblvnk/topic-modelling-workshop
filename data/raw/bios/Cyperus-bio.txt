Cyperus was a Power/Progressive Metal band from Tumwater, Washington. The band formed in 1982, and didnt release their first official demo until 1986. From then they released several more offerings, including the full-length "Sanity Management" in 1992. The lineup consisted of: Sonny Harper - Vocals, Jason Celestine - Guitar, Randy Keller - Bass, Scott Munger - Drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cyperus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
