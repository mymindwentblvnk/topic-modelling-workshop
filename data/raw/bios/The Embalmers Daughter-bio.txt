The Embalmer's Daughter is a metal band hailing from Staten Island, New York embedded with horror movie influences. If you're in the mood to feel as if you're getting chased around by a chainsaw or being fed to an alligator, then The Embalmer's Daughter is for you. 
The line up goes as follows:
Mike "Lord Vigo" - screaming

John Wolfgang von Frankentraks - guitaring
