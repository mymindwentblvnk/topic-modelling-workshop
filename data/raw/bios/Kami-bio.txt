There are several bands named Kami.

1. Kene Ekwunife, better known by his stage names Kami (sometimes stylized as KAMI) and Kami de Chukwu, is an American rapper from Chicago, Illinois. He is one half of Leather Corduroys. He is one of the founding members of Savemoney. In 2016, he was listed by RedEye as one of the "5 Chicago acts to watch in 2017".
