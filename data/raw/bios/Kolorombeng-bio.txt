KOLOROMBENG is Indonesian metal band. KOLOROMBENG was formed in a jail of children penitentiary, Jakarta, 2001. All members are ex-criminals who then promised not to do crime anymore and started to play their music.

KOLOROMBENG are :

KonTL  : vocal
MeMK  : guitar
TeTK    : guitar
JemBT  : bass
