Los Dragula is a sludge/Stoner/Doom Metal band from Buenos Aires, Argentina formed in 2011.
They released an EP Desafiando al Vendaval  in 2011 and a full-length Caminata del Hombre Muerto in 2013.

The current line-up consists of:
Nicolas Rivas - bass
Emiliano Lovotti - drums and vocals
Diego Frandolich - guitars and vocals
