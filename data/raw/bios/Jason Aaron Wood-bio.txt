Jason Aaron Wood, Guitarist of Todesbonden, and Founder, Composer, and Guitarist for Ol Sonuf.

Jason began playing the guitar in 1992, and in addition to beginning what was to later become Ol Sonuf in 1997, he is also a founding member of the band Todesbonden along with his wife and bandmate Laurie Ann Haus, and is featured on both their demo EP "Stormbringer" as well as their debut LP "Sleep Now, Quiet Forest," released through Prophecy Productions in 2008.

In 2010, Jason worked with composer Scott Glasgow, and wrote the music for 2 scenes in National Lampoon's "The Legend of Awesomest Maximus."

Jason also released his 9-song solo acoustic CD "Reflections" on Halloween 2012, and is currently writing and recording for the upcoming Ol Sonuf full-length debut "Glass Idols," as well as for Todesbonden's next release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jason+Aaron+Wood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
