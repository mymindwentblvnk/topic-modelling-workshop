Orthodox black metal from Sweden. They've released only one demo in 2001, entitled Northbound, before breaking up. 
The last line-up: 
Hinze - drums, bass, backing vocals (Ondskapt, Mortuus )
J.Kvarnbrink - lead guitar / vocals (Ofermod, Dödfödd, Mortuus )
