Klingenberg Syndrome is an instrumental power/progressive metal band from Keminmaa, Finland.

The band has one album so far, '... And The Weird Turned Pro' which was released January 2012

Their line up is Henrik Klingenberg from Sonata Arctica on keyboards, Elias Viljanen also from Sonata Arctica on  guitars as well as Pasi Kauppinen on bass and Jari Huttunen on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Klingenberg+Syndrome">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
