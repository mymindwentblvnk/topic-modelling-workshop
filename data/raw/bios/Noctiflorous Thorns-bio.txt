Noctiflorous Thorns was a black metal band from Bulgaria (Blagoevgrad). They released one live album, one demo and one full-lenght album on tape.

line-up:
Krasimir Andonov "Trash"- vocals
Peter Bojinov - bass
Krum Andonov - guitars
Niki Velev - drums 
Atanas Dimitrov "NAKE"- guitars
