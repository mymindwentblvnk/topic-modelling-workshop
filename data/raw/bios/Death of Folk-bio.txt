Death of Folk is a Croatian band formed in 2002 in Rijeka, Croatia. They play epic pagan metal, using both clear female vocals and harsh male vocals.
Their first demo, titled "Neverending at His Neverending Journey", was released in 2002. In 2006, after line-up changes, they started working on a new full-length album.

In Midsummer 2013 the band released a music video for a new song titled "Kosjen's Song" - http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IqC9Qaho1no
