Picture This originate from Athy, County Kildare. The band consists Ryan Hennessy (vocals), Jimmy Rainsford (drums), Owen Cardiff (Guitar) and Cliff Deane (Bass). Rainsford had previously toured with Ryan Sheridan, whilst Hennessy had been a member of another local band. Deane, Cardiff and Rainsford had been childhood friends. Cliff and Owen joined the band when Hennessy and Rainsford asked them to play with Picture This.[ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Picture+This">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
