Slovak band, that plays pagan black metal with lyrics about nature and spirituality. Founded in 2011.
"Krajiny Hmly" means "Lands of Mist" in Slovak. 

Members:
Namtar: lyrics, guitar, bass
Wolkogniv: vocals

Discography:
Keď padnú hmly (EP 2012)
Do hlbín spánku zabudnutia (promo track 2013)
