Brazilian  Female Thrash Metal band that was active mostly in the 80s. 

 Last known line-up: 

Cláudia França - Vocals 
Karla Carneiro - Guitar
Selma Moreira - Guitar
Mila Menzes - Bass
Sérgio Facci - Drums

Split up in 1996.
