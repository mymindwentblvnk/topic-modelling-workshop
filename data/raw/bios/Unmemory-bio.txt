Death-Metal Band from Brazil, forged in the end of 2004, arise by the reunion of Alcoholic Death, Aphasic Hellhound and Scetic Zetetic. Searching a new own sonority, with influences both of the traditionalest Death-Metal "old School" as Morbid Angel, Hypocrisy, Cannibal Corpse, Brutality, Pestilence, etc; and of the tendencies, as Necrophagist, Decapitated, Dying Fetus, Anasarca, Vomitory, etc, but without surrend to the fashioned mainstreans. The band Unmemory recently recorded his fisrt work, one CD demo with 4 musics of the purest Death-Metal, with lyrics about skepsism and atheism, prasing the human reazon against the irrational forms represented by religions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unmemory">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
