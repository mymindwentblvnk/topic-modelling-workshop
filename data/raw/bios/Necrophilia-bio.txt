Several bands use(d) the name Necrophilia:

1: Raw, fast Hardcore / Punk band from Belgrade, Serbia (in that time part of Yugoslavia). They used to play in the 80's and they are featured on the "Buka i urlik" compilation-tape with Herpes Distress and Solunski Front. 

2: Japanese thrash metal-band. They released two EP's in 1995. Currently disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Necrophilia">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
