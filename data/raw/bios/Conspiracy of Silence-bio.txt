There are two known bands that go by the name Conspiracy of Silence. The first Conspiracy of Silence is a story driven, experimental acoustic band out of New Jersey. The band consists of  Ron Reed and Jack Anon. Ron is the guitarist of the band and he composes the songs on his acoustic guitar and arranges the music. Jack is the vocalist of the band, handling all the lyrics as well as taking a part in arranging the various instrumental pieces. Learn more at:
www.myspace.com/conspiracyofsilencenj
http://www.farewellfreedom.com 

The second has been around since the 1980s, but are all very lazy.
