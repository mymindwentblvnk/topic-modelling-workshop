ANGELIZER is a band from Southern France, playing agressive and melodic death metal in the vein of DARK TRANQUILLITY or ARCH ENEMY.

Founded in early 2007 by CEDRIC (voice) REMY (bass) and STORM (guitar) the band first recruits YOHANN (lead guitar) and BORIS (ex E FORCE) on drums, and records a debut EP--to be unleashed by early 2008.
