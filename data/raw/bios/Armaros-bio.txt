Armaros is an epic extreme metal band from Decatur, Illinois.  They were formed in 2005 by  Demoriel (guitar/vocals) and Arioch (drums/vocals).  They released their debut album "Godless Empire"  in 2007.  Armaros recently recruited members Rysiel (bass guitar) and Astaroth (guitar). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Armaros">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
