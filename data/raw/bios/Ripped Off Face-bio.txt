A band combining death metal, thrash metal and old-school metalcore influences. They released one EP, Disease and Medicate, and a full-length album, We Are the State, before splitting up in 2008.

Final Lineup:
Stephen Juliano - Guitar
Danny Michael - Drums, Lyrics
Tim Dawson - Bass
