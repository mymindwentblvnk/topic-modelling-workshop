Timofey Dorofeyev (Timothy Infinite) is a 24 year old, Russian producer from Almaty, Kazakhstan. In the year of 2000, his family moved to Portland, Oregon where he received a classical education. In his early teenage years, his music interest drifted towards jazz causing him to study the greats such as Bill Evans, Miles Davis, Duke Ellington and many more. Today, Timothy is experimenting with genres such as Hip Hop, R&B, Urban Latin, Gospel, Pop, and anything else he can get his hands on. His goal is to make versatile and timeless music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Timothy+Infinite">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
