Biography
Formed in June 2008 by Tom Salazar. Addison, Illinois' I Killed Everyone blends a style of Melodic Death Metal so crushing and impacting one could feel the anger and hostility within the music. Each song tells a story of it's own focusing on topics such as homicide, abolition, and a point of view from an atypical mind. Be on the lookout for I Killed Everyone in your area. Make sure you help promote them by, Purchasing merchandise at shows or online, Come to shows and have a good time, and listen to their tunes.
