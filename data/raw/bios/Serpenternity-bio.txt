Technical death metal band from Kyyiv, Ukraine.  Originally formed in 2009 as Brutal Violence, changed their name to Serpenternity in 2014. Currently unsigned they have released one EP called Collapsar in 2016 

Serpenternity are: 

Sergey Baytal - Bass, Vocals (2014-present)
See also: Jam of Sativa, ex-Brutal Violence
Sergey Klima - Drums (2014-present)
Alex Chikal - Guitars (2014-present)
