Nevelrijk was founded in the winter of 2006 by Nevelgeest. Nevelgeest began recording in begin 2007, and signed to Wolfsvuur Records a couple weeks later. Demo 'Heidens Bloed' released in October 2007 through Wolfsvuur records on tape.
