Guitarist Randy Uchida put out out an EP "Deathly Fighter" under his metal band R.U.G. (Randy Uchida Group) in the early 80's. Randy died of cancer on February 10th, 2001. Uchida and Hiroshima also played in G.I.S.M.

Randy Uchida - Guitar (R.I.P.)
