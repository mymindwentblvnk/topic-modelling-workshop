Garleben is a Pagan/Folk Black Metal band from Germany (Hamburg) formed 2008. 

Current line-up: 
Mike Shredder - Vocals, Guitar
Herr Schwarz - Vocals, Guitar (Metakognition) 

Discography:
Chronos - Demo, 2009  
Hammonia - Full Length, 2010
