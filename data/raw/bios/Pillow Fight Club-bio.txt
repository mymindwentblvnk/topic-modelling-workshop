Pillow Fight Club is a four-piece European (Italian, Spanish, and German) band from the region of Hessen/Germany. They have a record out called "Heart Shaped Bomb" released in March 2005. Typical for the music of PFC is its catchy chorus lines combined with typical rock guitar riffs.  It is their wish to spread their sound across the continent of Europe. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pillow+Fight+Club">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
