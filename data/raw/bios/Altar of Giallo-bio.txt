Altar Of Gaillo is a death/grind band from Spain. The band features members of Gruesome Stuff Relish, Greenfly, and Repugnance. The bands lyrical theme is centered on Spanish/Italian horror films from the 60's, 70's, and 1980's. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Altar+of+Giallo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
