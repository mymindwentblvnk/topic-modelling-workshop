Tiaga was a Finnish gothic metal band. Which  was formed in Finland after Jape left To/Die/For in 2003. They only released a demo album. Then all of the members became part of To/Die/For. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/tiaga">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
