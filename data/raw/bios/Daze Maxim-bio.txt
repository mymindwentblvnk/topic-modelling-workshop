Daze Maxim is a multi-instrumentlist, composer, producer and DJ from Duesseldorf in Germany. He publishes under his own name, but also produced a number of commissioned works for professional DJs. Also notable are his classical compositions (unpublished). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Daze+Maxim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
