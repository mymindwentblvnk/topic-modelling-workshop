In North and South America, KEVORKIAN is a  Death Metal Band originally from Boston, MA and later New York City. In 2003 they released "Immortality In Culture" to underground acclaim and achieved a following. This 12 track CD contains a reworked cover of the Britney Spears track "...Baby One More Time" that has only sweetened with time. 

In 2006, Kevorkian (USA) released a 3 song demo that brought their music to a new level of complexity and intensity. 

In the Czech Republic, Kevorkian is also an awesome Czech hardcore band, their debut album "Relief trough scream" is one of the best Czech HC albums. Dominating part on the record the guitar - played by Robert with a really great HC sound. 

The artwork with the black cover and the handgun is the cover of one of their CDs.
