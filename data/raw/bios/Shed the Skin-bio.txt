Death Metal band from Ohio, USA with doom-ish morbid atmosphere formed in 2013. They have released Rebirth Through Brimstone 7" EP on December 17, 2014 by Hells Headbangers Records. Debut album Harrowing Faith released late May 2016 by Hells Headbangers records (official release date June 24 2016, but was made available to the public May 20 2016).


Line-up:
Ed Stephens - Bass (Ringworm)
Kyle Severn  - Drums (Incantation)
Matt Sorg  - Guitars (Ringworm)
Ash Thomas  - Vocals, Guitars (Faithxtractor)
