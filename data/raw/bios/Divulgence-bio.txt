Divulgence was formed in March, 2008 in Greenville, SC.
the band played together for 10 months before being signed to Bombworks Records.
their style consists of thrash metal mixed with black metal, and with influences such as The Absence, Crimson Moonlight, Drudkh, Testament, Warbringer, Death, Antestor, and Extol.
