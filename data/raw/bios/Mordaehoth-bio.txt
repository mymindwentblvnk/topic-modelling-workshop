Pagan black metal band from Maastricht, the Netherlands. At the moment Sygwyrd is the only member and also performs all instruments. 

Mordaehoth is the union of the words Mor-Dae-Hoth and means (in Tolkien's language ) "The Horde of the Dark Shadow". 

Together with some demo's and a split album with Veinen, the band has released two full lengths; 
- Liederen van het onterfde land (Hymns from the disinherit land), 1999
- Bloedwraak - Verdoem al 't christelijk leven (Blood vengeange - Doom all Christian life), 2003
