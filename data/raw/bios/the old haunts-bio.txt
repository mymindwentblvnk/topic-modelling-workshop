The Old Haunts are Craig Extine (The Serum Greys, Venereal Uppers, Fallout Dregs), Scott Seckington (The Transfused, Two Ton Boa (guest) Ika Panthers, LLP, Sedan, Verticalville) & Curtis James (Excuse 17, Kill Sybil, Popular Music, The Reagan Years, The Stowaways, The Serum Greys), a Punk band from Olympia, WA. They've released two EPs, and three albums(Fallow Field in 2005, Fuel On Fire in 2006, and Poisonous Times in 2008). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Old+Haunts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
