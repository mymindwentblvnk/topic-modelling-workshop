Entorturement is a slamming brutal death metal band from New York, USA.

Guitarist Kirill is now playing in the Brooklyn, NY-based metal band Empire of Dust, also featuring the former drummer and bassist of the hardcore act Indecision. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Entorturement">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
