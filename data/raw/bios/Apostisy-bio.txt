Apostisy is a death metal band from California that formed in 2004. They currently have 1 album released called  "Famine of a Thousand Frozen Years." from 2007.Their music shows strong influence of melodic death metal with some black metal thrown in there, along with some inspirational lyrics.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Apostisy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
