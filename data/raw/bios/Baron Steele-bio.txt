Heavy Metal formed in 1985 from Austin, Texas. Aside from the 1987 Demo they also recorded four unreleased songs: Battle of Pellanor, March of the Mortal Lord, Forge the Damned, The Chamber. Appears on the "Texas Metal Archives Volume One" compilation (Brainticket Records, 2009) with the track "Night of the Wolves". the other recorded songs have been found and and may see a release soon. band members are Kent Taylor Vocals,Joe Szabo Lead Guitar,David Blake Lead Guitar,David Roach Bass Guitar,Kenny Stinespring Drums. a band that had potential but fell apart when things were just starting to happen. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Baron+Steele">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
