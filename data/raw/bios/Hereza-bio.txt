Hereza is a Croatian extreme metal duo formed in July of 2014 who fuse metal black metal elements with old school death metal and crusty punk beats. 
They finished their debut album called "Misanthrope" and got signed by "PRC Music" label from Canada in October of 2014.

Members: 
Slobodan Stupar - guitars, bass guitar, drums, back vocals
Ivan Kovačević - vocals

Guest appearance:
Zvonimir Janković - guitar solos
