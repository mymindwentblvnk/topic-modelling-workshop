Holokastrial is a death metal that formed in Shanghai, China in 2016. They released their demo EP "Horror of Abomination" in July 2018, with their debut full-length album "Echoes from the Devil Vallry" released on May 1, 2019. Both songs from the demo appeared on the album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Holokastrial">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
