Iron Hearse are a hard rock / metal / doom metal 'power trio' that formed in 2001.
They released three demos before putting out self-released EP 'Peddle the Metal' and following this up with their hard-rockin' debut album 'Iron Hearse' on Austria's Psychedoomelic Records.

Line up:

Grant - Guitar, Vocals
Liam - Bass
Stu - Drums

Forthcoming releases:

Vinyl 12" and split 7" through Psychedoomelic Records, late 2009.
