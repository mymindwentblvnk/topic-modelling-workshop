There are multiple artists with this name:
1. Simon Magus is a doom metal band from Toledo, OH, USA.consisting of Wes Allen, Micah Shimborske, Matt Snyder, Nick Shallcross, Mark Reynolds.
They released their debut album The First Year Of Catastrophe in 2015 on Black Swamp Collective 
They are heavily influenced by bands such as Trouble, Saint Vitus, Black Sabbath, Revelation, Roky Erickson, Judas Priest and The Obsessed.
See also their facebook and their bandcamp sites for more infos.
2. Simon Magus is also an alternative rock band from New Jersey established in 2008. The band has only released demo tracks. Their first full length release is scheduled to come out this winter.
