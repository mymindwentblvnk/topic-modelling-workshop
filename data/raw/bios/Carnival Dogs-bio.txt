Carnival Dogs was a high school garage band out of Colorado Springs, Colorado. Its members included Craig Haller (vocals, guitar), and Cobey Hartman (bass), and JJ Lester (drums). The Carnival Dogs started in 1996. Its name was influenced by The Fixx and shared the name with another band which was all but inactive at the time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Carnival+Dogs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
