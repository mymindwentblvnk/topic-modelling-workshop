Eddy Lover is a Panamanian reggae singer.

Eduardo Mosquera is born on March 16, 1985, in Panama City, Panama. Eddy Lover rose to international fame with his appearance on the 2006 hit "Perdoname" by La Factoria. This appearance led to a major label solo recording contract with Universal Latino's Urban Divison, Machete Music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eddy+Lover">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
