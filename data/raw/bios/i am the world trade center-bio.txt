I Am the World Trade Center is an electronic band from Athens, Georgia, comprising Amy Dykes and Dan Geller.  They have had their name since 1999, and are influenced by bands such as The Faint, Ladytron and New Order.

Currently, Amy's moved to the West coast to finish her P.h.D and enjoy married life, while Dan's still in Athens. The future of the band has been left somewhat ambiguous. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/I+Am+The+World+Trade+Center">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
