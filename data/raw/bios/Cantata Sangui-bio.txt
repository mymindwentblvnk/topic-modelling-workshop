A Finnish avant-garde approach metal band that combines elements from various genres and styles of music. No guitars in the line-up, as the parts traditionally assigned to guitar are played with a variety of bass guitars.

First demos published in 1998, debut published via Season of Mist 26th of January 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cantata+Sangui">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
