VRTRA is a blackened doom metal band from Sacramento and San Francisco, California. Formed in 2014, their first full-length release, My Bones Hold a Stillness, was released in September 2, 2016. Band members are simply known as A, Z, M and A. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/VRTRA">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
