Erol, better known as Monomotion is the co-founder of FAKE MUSIC and a miracle-man. In 2013, he almost died in an accident that led him into a 50 days Coma. A year later he released ‘Behind The Moon’, his debut EP, an auto-biographic 7-track, a footprint of his unconscious mindtrip. After playing Dj sets in some of the greatest venues the city of Paris has to offer, he's currently working on a couple of exciting things <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Monomotion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
