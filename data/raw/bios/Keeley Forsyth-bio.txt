Keeley Forsyth (1979, Oldham) is an English actress and musician. She appeared in several television series, including Dalziel and Pascoe, Luther, Criminal Justice, and Coronation Street. In October 2019, Forsyth released the single "Debris" on The Leaf Label, followed by "Start Again", with accompanying video by Andrew Hulme. Her debut album "Debris", released on 17 January 2020, features minimal arrangements that feature her elemental voice and haunting lyrics. The album also features fellow Leaf Label artist and pianist Matthew Bourne. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Keeley+Forsyth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
