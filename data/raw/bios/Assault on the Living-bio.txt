Members of legendary New York Death Metal band Traumaside!!!!!

Assault on the Living's line up has changed as of 2011 with the addition of two vocalists, Dustin Rose and Dan Young and now complete with the addition of guitarist Chris Chapman. The project was started in 2009 by the members of Traumaside in search of a new outlet for crushing rhythm with melodic breakdowns.
