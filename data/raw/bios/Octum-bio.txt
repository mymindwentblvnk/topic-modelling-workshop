Octum is a local thrash metal band from Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.
Members are:
Faisal Al-Alamy (Lead Vocals, Guitars)
Mohammed Kumkumji (Drums)
Sulatan Ridwan (Guitars and Backing Vocals)
Mohammed Mudarris (Bass and Backing Vocals)
