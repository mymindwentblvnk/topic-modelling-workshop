Amethyste is a death metal band from Boulogne-sur-Mer, France, formed in 1993 by Gregory and Patrick Lebecq.

The band has released multiple demos, a split tape and in 2006 their debut album Thrown Off Balance. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amethyste">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
