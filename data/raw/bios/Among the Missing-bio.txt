Among the Missing was a five-piece sludge, doom metal and power violence band from London, United Kingdom formed in 2001.

They mix Black Flag style dirty hardcore with sludgey guitars and doomy riffage to produce a fine chunk of noise. The album Disorder of the Templar was released in 2005 on Withered Hand Records. 

In 2007 Among the Missing played their farewell tour and released their final full-length album Cease to Exist.

Current and related bands involving Among the Missing members include : The Ophelian, Crumbling Ghost, Hygiene, Tim Holehouse, Koresh, Pombagira, and Fantaplastic.
