War for War is a one-man black metal project run by Lord Morbivod, well known Czech black metal musician, participating also in bands Trollech, Stíny Plamenů and Umbrtka. The main subject is war, Morbivod himself calls the music 'panzer black metal', but on the last album the main topic is mining. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/War+for+War">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
