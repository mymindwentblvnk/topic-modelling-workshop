Biography
An Creative band from the boundaries of Québec , Canada , formed in 2009 . Upon Your Grave is a metal mixture of death , thrash , groove , core , melodic metal that is forging is own sound and path into the music world. After releasing are first single '' Haunting Of The Beast '' in 2011 recorded by Antoine Baril we delivered are 6 pieaces demo '' Artistic Freedom '' in June 2012 , we are extremely devoted in achieving are main goals and making music our lifes for the long run.
