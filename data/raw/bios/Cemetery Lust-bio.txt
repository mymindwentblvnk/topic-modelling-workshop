Cemetery Lust are composed of 5 alchoholic perverts who are possessed by Lucifer to play filthy Deaththrash!  They formed in Portland, Oregon in 2009.  They are currently signed to Hells Headbangers Records.  Their lyrical themes deal with necrophilia, violence, drinking and Satan!  The band consists of  Andrew Angeldust (vocals),  Squid Nasty (guitar),  Raypist (bass & backing vocals) and Disgustor (drums).  Nasty Nate (guitars) is the only former member of Cemetery Lust.
