Ballistic is:

1. A Thrash Metal band that consists of Tom Gattis (vocals and guitar), Petio Petev (guitar), Tim O'Connor (bass), Rikard Stjernquist (drums).
The band was formed in 2000 under the name Afterburn but changed it's name to Ballistic in 2001.
