Chainsaw Dissection is a one man brutal death/grindcore band from New Alexandria, Pennsylvania.  Formed by Bob Egler in 2004 has have had quite a lot of underground one man projects in recent years with most of them being in the brutal death metal/ grind arena with gore themes in the lyrics. The most notable bands he has formed over the years are Psychotic Homicidal Dismemberment, Goat Bleeder and Skull Defiler all reaching in different types of death metal. He has also played in thrash and black metal band which notably are Brutish Creation, Morbidness and Cockslush. This project, however, has heavy influences to old school Mortician, Mangled and Impetigo.
