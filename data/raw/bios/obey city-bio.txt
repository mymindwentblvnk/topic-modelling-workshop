Obey City (pronounced "oh-bee city") is an American electronic music producer based in Brooklyn, New York. He has released his material on the Astro Nautico, B.YRSLF and LuckyMe labels. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obey+City">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
