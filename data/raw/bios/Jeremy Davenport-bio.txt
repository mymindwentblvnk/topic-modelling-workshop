Jazz Trumpeter Jeremy Davenport was raised in  St. Louis, Mo  and became a merit scholar at the prestigious Manhattan School of music, Davenport also began private study with revered jazz musicians Raymond Mase and Wynton Marsalis. He went on to study at the University of New Orleans with the patriarch of the Marsalis family, music educator and jazz pianist Ellis Marsalis.

His talents flourished and he was honored to become the youngest performer on four world tours with Harry Connick Jr.`s Big Band. Currently he can be found performing multiple international Jazz festivals and special events with his own band who specialize in New Orleans style jazz..
