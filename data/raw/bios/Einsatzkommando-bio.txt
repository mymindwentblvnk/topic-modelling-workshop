Einsatzkommando is a black/ns act from Germany. Founded in 1999 they are currently split-up.

Last known line-up:
Hitlerian Evil: guitar
Satanis Jesus: drums, vocals, Lagerarzt 

Not to be confused with the homonymous German black/thrash band Einsatzkommando, and other bands with the same name.
