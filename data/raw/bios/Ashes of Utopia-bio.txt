There are 2 bands with this name.

Ashes Of Utopia are a Melodic Death Metal band from Berlin, Germany who formed in 2003. They released their first demo in 2003, a second demo entitled 'Burn To Ashes' in 2005, followed by their debut album 'Slayer's Dance' in 2008.

Ashes of Utopia is also a 2 piece PsyNoise/Drone project from New Zealand. In 2006 they recorded a 4 track Demo titled "Mushroom Smoothie Jam". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ashes+Of+Utopia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
