Black Metal band from Hamilton, Ohio, USA. Released two demos, Total Fucking War and Poisoned Thorns Of Human Sorrow and one EP in 2007 titled Seven Visions into the Epoch of Holocaust

They are now known as Strigoi Vii, and have thus far released no material under the new name.
