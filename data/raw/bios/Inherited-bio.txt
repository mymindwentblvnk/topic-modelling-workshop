Inherited is a two-woman acoustic band comprised of Liz Hayward (guitar) and Amy Connor (vocals). Currently performing covers but working on original material, the songs range from all dates going from the early 90's to the present day; including genres of reggae, soul, country, pop, and rock. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/inherited">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
