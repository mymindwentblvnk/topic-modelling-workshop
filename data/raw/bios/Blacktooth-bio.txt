(For the Turkish southern/sludge/groove metal band, see Black Tooth)

Blacktooth are a four piece extreme metal band from Hamilton, New Zealand. Members are; Kane - drums, Ollie - guitars, Bog - vocals and Matt - bass. Blacktooth wrote, recorded, produced and released their own debut album titled "The Unholy Reign of Metal". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blacktooth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
