Killa Tay is an American rapper from Fresno, California. He is best known for his tenure on AWOL Records, releasing his legendary debut, Mr. Mafioso, and as well as his collaborations with C-Bo. Snake Eyes and Thug Thisle followed two years later; another gang-oriented tale was captured on 2001's Thug Nation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Killa+Tay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
