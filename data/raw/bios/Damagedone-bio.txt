Five tireless hardcore kids from Bandung City, Indonesia try to do the best things in this life. Ichan (eleventh hour), Adi (our pride), Pofa , Gatot, Eggie (our pride)…Formed on 2005 as Damage Done and also play at some local gigs in Bandung City – Indonesia. Our music also influenced by some hardcore bands, and mixed with any rocks and punk beats. Our vision just to bring the spirit on the moshpit and let we make a fun by sing along and do some stage dive…keep struggle in the scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DAMAGEDONE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
