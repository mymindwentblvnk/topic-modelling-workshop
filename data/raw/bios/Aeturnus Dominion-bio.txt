Based on the Central Coast of NSW Australia,  Aeturnus Dominion has been around since 2003 and in 2014 consists of Ian (The Enforcer) Tomlinson - Vocals, Ted (Demented) Foster - guitars/vocals, Tait (Spud) Kristen - bass/vocals and Steve (Ozi) Austin - drums.
