Satyrian call themselves an international gothic rock band, although all members are from Netherlands, combining dark atmospheric lyrics with wave, rock, classical orchestration, piano, and heavy guitars.
They were formerly known as Danse Macabre.

Satyrian line up:
Roman Schönsee - vocals
Kemi Vita - vocals
Judith "Ciara" Stüber - vocals
Jan "Örkki" Yrlund - guitars, programming
Oliver Philipps - piano, orchestrations
Miloš Marićević - bass
