Terror 2000 is a Thrash/Melodic Death Metal band from Helsingborg, Sweden. Formed in 1999, they are signed to Nuclear Blast records.
Members are as follows:

Björn Strid - Vocals
Klas Ideberg - Guitars 
Niklas Svärd (Nick Sword ) - Guitars 
Dan Svensson - Bass 
Erik Thyselius - Drums 


Discography:

-Slaughterhouse Supremacy, 2000 
-Faster Disaster, 2002  
-Slaughter In Japan - Live 2003 
-Terror For Sale, 2005
