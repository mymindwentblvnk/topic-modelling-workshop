Salvus is a Hungarian progressive metal band formed in 2001 in Szeged. They describe their music as "Ákos meets In Flames". They released 2 demos (Álomvágy 2003, Rezdülések 2004), an EP (Imago 2005) and three full-length albums (Minden Kezdet 2008, Önharckép 2011, Lélektartó 2015) up to now.

Members:
Zoltán Pötörke - bass, vocals
Péter Erdélyi - guitar (2011-present)
Krisztián Varga - guitar (2009–present)
Gergely Zsákovics - drums

Former members:
András Kokavecz - guitar (2003–2011)
Péter Gere - lead guitar (2002–03)
Balázs Varga - rhythm guitar (2007–07)
Gábor Pötörke - rhythm guitar (2007–09)
