Iron Dogs is a heavy / speed metal band from Ottawa, Canada that formed in 2011. Their songs contain lyrics about horror, fantasy, 'baddassery', and their music can be downloaded from their official bandcamp page.

2011: Ripping Torment - (7''/tape)
2012: Cold Bitch - (LP/CD/tape)
2013: Free And Wild - (LP/CD/tape)

Current line up:
Dan Lee: Drums, backing vocals
Jo Capitalicide: Vocals, guitar, bass

Former members:
Aidan Donovan: guitar, backing vocals
