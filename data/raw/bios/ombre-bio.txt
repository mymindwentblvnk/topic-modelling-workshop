Asthmatic Kitty labelmates Julianna Barwick and Helado Negro have joined forces under the name OMBRE. Their debut album Believe You Me is out August 27 on Asthmatic Kitty. 

Unlike previous releases from Barwick, which mostly only used her voice and effects, in OMBRE she plays guitar, vibraphone, electric piano, and synthesizer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ombre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
