Czech thrash/death/grind metal group from Liberec founded in 1998 by Josef Vích (guitar), Eduard Bílkovský (drumms), Michal Patrný (vocals) and Petr Řeřicha (bass).

Current lineup:

DINOSAURUS (Martin Zajíc) - vocals
PEPÍNO (Josef Vích) - guitar
MILIVOJ (Milan Tvrzník) - bass
MÍRA (Mirek Habětínek) - drumms
