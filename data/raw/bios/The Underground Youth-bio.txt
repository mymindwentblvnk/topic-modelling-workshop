The Underground Youth is a British band based in Manchester, their music touches on different genres including psychedelia and garage rock. The band is led by Craig Dyer whose musical influences include The Brian Jonestown Massacre, The Velvet Underground, the ballads and writing style of Bob Dylan and the noise of The Jesus & Mary Chain.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Underground+Youth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
