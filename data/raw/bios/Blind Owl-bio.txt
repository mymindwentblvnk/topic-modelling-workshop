"Blind Owl" is an Iranian Dark Ambient band formed in 2008 by Arang (Nima N.) and Morego Dimmer (aka Xerxes The Dark,  Morego,  Nyctalllz,  Nahan,  Ostanes ) The band's name was taken from Sadeq Hedayat's most famous masterpiece. Blind Owl released a demo album called "For My Shadow..." on Amduscias Records and Smell The Stench on May 2008.
