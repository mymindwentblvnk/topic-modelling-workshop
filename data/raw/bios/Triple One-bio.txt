Triple One is a Sydney-based rap group consisting of Marty Bukkake Large, Obi Ill Terrors, Phantom and producer Billy Gunns.

They have released 4 eps; "No Banga Banga's" and "Doozy." in 2015. "The Libertine." in 2016 and "The Naughty Corner" in 2018. They have also released multiple singles and music videos. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Triple+One">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
