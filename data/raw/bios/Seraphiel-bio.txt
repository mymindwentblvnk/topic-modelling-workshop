A power metal band from Helsinki, Finland. They also use melodic death metal and progressive metal influences.

What is Seraphiel?

After taking a glance on this site it is clear that Seraphiel has something to do with epicness and being awesome. 

After taking a listen to the music it is clear that we, Seraphiel are a band, and indeed, an epic and an awesome band. 

We are formed of six talented and experienced people with unique and well crafted skills in both music and life.

We are also known from bands like Tracedawn, Medicated and uniQarma.
