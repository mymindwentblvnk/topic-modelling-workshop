Perverse Imagery was started December 2010 by at the time 16 year old Darryn Palmer.

The band remained dormant until March 2012 when the Carnal, Bloody, Unnatural demo came out, followed by a split with Italian brutal death metal band Dressed for Apocalypse and Spanish goregrind band Vaginal Opening. After the split came Critically acclaimed Necromantic Comedy (FDAU Records) and Total Tits Sextastico Explosion EP's. 

On January 1st, 2013 Perverse Imagery released The Importance of Living (an ambient record) and is hard at work on his second full-length Abstract Melodies.
