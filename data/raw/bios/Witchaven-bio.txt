Witchaven are a blackened thrash metal band from San Bernardino, California. They formed in 2006 and released their debut demo The Vehemence of Attack the same year. From 2007-2009 they released the Faces of Death EP in 2007, the Black Thrash Assault demo also in '07, two compilations featuring past material(but Totalitarian State of War had a few unreleased tracks), as well as a split with Avenger of Blood. After a line up change, they released their debut full length Terrorstorm on 2010 and in 2012 they released the EP Sacrificial Burnt Offering. They are currently working on their second full length. 

Current line up:
Mark Ryder - Bass
Lerby Sanchez - Drums
Henry Montoya - Vocals, Guitar 
Erik Sneve - Guitars (lead)
