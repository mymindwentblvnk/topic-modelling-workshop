Iliad is an instrumental trio that draws from the many feelings of Jazz, Classical, and Rock music.

2. Illiad was also a band from Sydney, Australia.

3. There was also a Melodic Death Metal band named Iliad from Detroit, Michigan, USA. They released a three-track demo in 2006 and split up shortly afterward. In 2011 three of the four members went on to help form the band Konkeror. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Iliad">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
