LEYODE are a two-piece made up of Yusuke Hama and Laurel Wells. They are both originally from Atalanta, although are now based in New York City.

Their debut record Fascinating Tininess... was released in 2007 on Guillermo S. Herren's (Prefuse 73) label Eastern Developments.
