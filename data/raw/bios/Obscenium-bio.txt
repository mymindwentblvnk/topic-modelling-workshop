Obscenium is a Technical Death Metal band from the outer city reaches of Perth, Western Australia...
Founded in 2011, the bands music has slowly evolved into its own take on the 'Tech-Death' genre, gathering influences from bands like Necrophagist, Rings Of Saturn & Blotted Science. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obscenium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
