Embreach is a metal band hailing from Finland. Their sound is melodic and guitar-driven, characterised by tight riffs, and the vocals are a a mixture of growls and clean singing. 

Embreach's debut album, Hues Of Red, will be released on August 17th, 2012.

Their previous releases include the EPs Deafening Silence (2008) and Her Grace (2006), which are both available for free download through Last.fm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Embreach">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
