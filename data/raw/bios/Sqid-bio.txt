SQID is an international Austrian, Mexican and Italian collective of improvisers: Angelica Castello [paetzold flute, cassettes, radios, toys, ukulele], Burkhard Stangl [guitar, devices], Mario de Vega [electronics], Attila Faravelli [electronics through modified speakers]. In 2015 they released their debut album "SQID" on Mikroton Recordings (www.mikroton.net). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sqid">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
