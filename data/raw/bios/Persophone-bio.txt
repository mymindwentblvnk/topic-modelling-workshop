Persophone is an German Black Metal project from Rostock, active between 1990 - 1995, which was strongly influenced by early DARKTRHONE stuff and similar Norwegian Black Metal bands. They released some demo tapes only, well known were the releases "Into the battle" demo and "The past of the nightside" tape.
