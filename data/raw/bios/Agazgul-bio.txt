Agazgul, hailing from Finland, was formed in June 2007, and soon after establishment recorded first demo, "The night of Lucifer". The first demo has been released by Wolfsvuur Records (www.wolfsvuur.nl) TAPE (lim. 66 copies, sold out) Agazgul is influenced by German black metal bands Moonblood and Nargaroth. This project is buried nowadays.
