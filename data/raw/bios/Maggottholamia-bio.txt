Brutal Death/Grind Metal from Turku, Finland. Maggottholamia changed its name to Virulent Blessing in 2004, but after that, some members wanted to keep Maggottholamia alive and formed it again. So, nowadays Virulent Blessing and Maggottholamia are two separare bands. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maggottholamia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
