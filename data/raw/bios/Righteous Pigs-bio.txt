Righteous Pigs were a grindcore / death metal band who were formed in 1987 in Las Vegas, USA. They recorded two albums; Live And Learn (1989) and Stress Related (1990), but split up following the release of the latter album when guitarist Mitch Harris left to join Napalm Death. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Righteous+Pigs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
