Esodic are tharsh metal / death metal band from Jordan, formed in 2000. They released a demo named "Coverless Truth" in 2002.

Lineup:

Mohammed Kurdi - Vocals, Bass
Zaid Amarin - Drums
Marwan Kayali - Guitars
