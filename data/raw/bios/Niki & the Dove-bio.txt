Niki & The Dove is a project from Sweden featuring singer-songwriter Malin Dahlström and producer Gustaf Karlöf.

Prior to being the front woman, Malin was programming for midaircondo, singing lead in now-defunct rock band The Dora Steins, contributing wispy vocals to various electronic productions, and doing solo shows and performance art all around Sweden under the name Disdishdance.
