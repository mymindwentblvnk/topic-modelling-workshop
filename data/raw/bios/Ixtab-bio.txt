There are 2 groups known as IXTAB:

1) Ultra lo-fi sick underground black metal centered on suicide and negativism. Hailing from the Yucatan area.

2) A lo-fi electronic and outsider techno outfit comprising the artists Blood Room and Ovis Aurum. They have released cassette and digital albums on Paris-based label THRHNDRDSVNTNN, Tymbal Tapes of Nebraska, USA, Edinburgh's Czaszka (Rec.) and Latvia's Bezirk Tapes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ixtab">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
