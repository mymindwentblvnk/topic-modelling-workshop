A Power Metal band from Holland. Only released one EP entitled "The Trial" in 1984 before splitting up. The EP displays influence from the NWOBHM and the vocalist has a passing resemblance to King Diamond. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Proud+Existence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
