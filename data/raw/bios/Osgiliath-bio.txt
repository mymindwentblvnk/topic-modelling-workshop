There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Osgiliath was a medieval black metal band from Saint-Etienne, France, formed in 1993. They released two demos, "Ainulindalë" in 1996, and "L'ombre du passé" in 1997.

2) Osgiliath is a black metal from  Mexico City, Mexico, formed in 2011. They released their first demo titled "With Sadness Sheated in Flesh" in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Osgiliath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
