There are more than one act with the name Mr President.

1. Mr. President is a German euro-dance group from Bremen, best known for their hit Coco Jamboo in 1996 which was a hit in multiple countries. The band was originally called Satellite One. It consisted of LayZee, Lady Danii and T-Seven.
Mr. President recorded several albums like "Up 'N Away", "We See The Same Sun", "Nightclub", "Spacegate", "A Kind Of Best" and "Forever and One Day".

2. Mr President, without the dot, is a funk and raw soul project from Lyon in France.
"Out of time breakbeats, JB's / Fela horn section, wobbling bass and biting guitar riffs are the main ingredients of this EP, following Sharon Jones or Quantic Soul Orchestra's steps." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mr.+President">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
