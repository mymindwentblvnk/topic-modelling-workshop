Johnny Flynn  (born 14 March 1983 in Johannesburg) is a British acoustic 
  singer-songwriter and actor. He has also performed and recorded with the English Folk-Rock act Johnny Flynn & The Sussex Wit.

Flynn released his debut album, A Larum, in 2008. This was followed by the Sweet William EP in 2009 and his second album Been Listening in 2010. 'Been Listening' includes the singles album Kentucky Pill, Barnacled Warship and The Water, which features a duet with Laura Marling. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Johnny+Flynn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
