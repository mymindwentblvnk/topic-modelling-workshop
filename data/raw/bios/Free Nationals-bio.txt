Free Nationals are best known as Anderson . Paak's live band, until now. The Free Nationals are bassist Kelsey Gonzalez (bass), Ron “Tnava” Avant (keyboard/vocoder), Callum Connor (drums), and José Rios (guitar). In December 2019, the band released their self-titled debut, featuring collaborations with Mac Miller, Kali Uchis, Daniel Caesar, Unknown Mortal Orchestra, Conway the Machine and Westside Gunn. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Free+Nationals">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
