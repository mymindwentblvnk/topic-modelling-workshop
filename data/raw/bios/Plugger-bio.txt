Plugger's self-titled debut is a heavy, powerful, and melodic collection of hard hitting rock songs, that will make your head spin. Plugger's masterful song structures on "Paydirt" and "Further" to the stoner rock grooves on "Stoken" will leave no doubt that Plugger will tear down the known borders of music.
