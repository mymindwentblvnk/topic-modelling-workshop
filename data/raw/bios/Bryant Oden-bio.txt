Bryant Oden is an American singer-songwriter known for the viral video and internet meme "The Duck Song" (using animation by Forrest Whaley), which has had over 180 million views on YouTube as of March 2015. He later wrote two sequel tunes, "The Duck Song 2" and "The Duck Song 3", featuring the same obnoxious protagonist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bryant+Oden">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
