Yawgmoth is a Black Metal band from Enschede, The Netherlands.

As of 2011, the line-up consists of:

Aegonem - Vocals
Delirium - Guitars (Bellum Animi)
Labyrinth - Bass (Hogwash, Bellum Animi)
