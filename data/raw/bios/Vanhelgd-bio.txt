Vanhelgd (Swedish word for desecrated) is a new wave Old school Death Metal band formed in Mjölby, Sweden in 2007. 

They released their debut album "Cult of Lazarus" in late November 2008.

In the year 2011, they released their second album called  “Church of Death” via Nuclear War Now! Productions
