Jean Claude Ades is a global player, born and raised in Italy, residing in Munich, and a regular in Ibiza. Even though he started as a pianist, at heart he is a producer, DJ, remixer and songwriter. After learning the piano from scratch, he went the way of the synthesizer, keyboards and computers, all the way to become a producer. For the last 10 years the 32-year old has been influencing the international Dance Scene with his remixes, productions and with his highly energetic DJ sets. His early productions, e.g., “Shake That Ass”, “The Colour Of My Style”, “Everything I Do”, and others, opened the door for him to various major companies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jean+Claude+Ades">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
