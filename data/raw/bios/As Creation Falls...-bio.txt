A symphonic black metal band from Finland. The band was formed in 2002 in Varkaus (Finland). They have released one EP (2003). Their music is very symphonic and features many classically-influenced piano passages and solos. Fast drumming and melodic guitar riffs are present as well. The band split up in 2003 shortly after the EP was released.
