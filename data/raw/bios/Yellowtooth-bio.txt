YELLOWTOOTH is a Michigan City, Indiana based power trio influenced by the sounds of BONGZILLA, CLUTCH, SOURVEIN, WEEDEATER, ACID APE, ELECTRIC MAGMA, 16, SOLACE, BONGRIPPER, EL GORDO, FISTULA, BIRDS OF PREY, BLACK COBRA, BEATEN BACK TO PURE along with numerous others of the noise/stoner/sludge guild.  The band members themselves have been involved with music for over +20 years releasing deafening heaviness from their previous outfits & have only been together since March of 2008.  This band released their debut demo "Smile In Disgust" with 4 songs in February 2009 & will be releasing a full length cd by July 2012.  You can check them out at:
