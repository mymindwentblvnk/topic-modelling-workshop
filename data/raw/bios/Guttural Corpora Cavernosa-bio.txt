Guttural Corpora Cavernosa is a Taiwanese Slamming Brutal Death Metal band from Taipei, founded in 2010.

Members:
Larry Wang (Beyond Cure / Gorepot / Coprocephalic) - vocals
Zero (Flesh Juicer) - guitar
Yaya (席卡提 ) - guitar
Jacob - bass
Shuan (Coprocephalic) - drums
