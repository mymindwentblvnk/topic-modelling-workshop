13Rituals is a Hardcore/Groove Metal band from Cluj-Napoca , Romania.
Influences : Deathcore , Crossover , Slam-death , Death Metal
The band was formed in 2008 in Cluj-Napoca.

Members :
Adrian Severin – Vocals
George Chereji – Drums
Vlad Ciumofoaia – Bass
Vlad Bornuz – Guitar

Past Members :
Dobarta Mircea - Vocals (2009 - 2010)
Szekely Csaba - Bass (2009 - 2010)
Mutnean Norbert - Guitar (2009 - 2010)
Mario Ioanici – Guitar (2010 - 2011)
