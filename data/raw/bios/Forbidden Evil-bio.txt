Bay Area metal thrashers, originally formed in 1985 by vocalist Russ Anderson, guitarists Robb Flynn and Craig Locifero, bassist Matt Camacho, and drummer Paul Bostaph. They released several demos, before changing their name to Forbidden. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forbidden+Evil">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
