Abel is Dying was a Metalcore / Melodic Death Metal from Milan, Italy. Formed in 2004 they released three demo's and one full-length album before disbanding in 2010.

Last line-up
Paolo "Paul" Privitera: Vocals
Tommaso "Tommi" Pasi: Vocals
Andrea Franz: Guitar
Giangiacomo Pastore: Guitar
Lorenzo: Bass
Vittorio "Pea" Rosa: drums
