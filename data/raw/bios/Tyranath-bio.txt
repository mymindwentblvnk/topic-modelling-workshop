Tyranath are a US pagan black metal band hailing from Reading, Pennsylvania. Formed in 1994. One of their song titled "Heathen Heart" was featured in the compilation The Night And The Fog Part III: Underground Heathen Hammer.

The current lineup consists of past members of Wotanorden:
Waffenscholtz - drums
Tyranath - vocals, guitars, bass
