(Anti)depressing black metal act from Lithuania consisting of only one member, Martynas. All songs are recorded using real instruments (except the drums), vocal chords, home computer, basic sound editing knowledge and emotions varying from happiness to depression, hence the name.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cheerful+Depression">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
