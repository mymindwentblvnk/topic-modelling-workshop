There are multiple artists with this name:

1) Black Torment is a black metal band from Mexico. They formed in 1997 and have released at this present moment three albums called "Angel of the Abyss" (2003), "Satanic Holocaust" (2007) and Bloody Signs of Devastation (2011) with several compilations, splits and demos in between.

2) Black Torment is a one man black metal band from USA. They formed in 1995 and have released at this present moment, seven albums with "When the Dark Emperor Returns" (1998), "Black Torment" (2000), "To Satanas" (2001), "Obscurum" (2002), "Obscurum II - The Return of The King" (2004), "A Sign of Infernal Death" (2007) and "Nights of the Black Moon" (2009). Also having demos, splits and other releases in between these main releases. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Torment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
