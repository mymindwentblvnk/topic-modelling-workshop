leavv is a Chillhop and Lofi producer from Germany that crafts silky, chillhop-driven lullabies, often blended with gentle piano melodies. He first got Ableton back in 2012 and before that he played piano and guitar. He also took piano lessons when he was younger but quit when he got into his teens. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/leavv">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
