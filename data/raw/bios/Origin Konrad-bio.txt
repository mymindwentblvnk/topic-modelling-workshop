Origin Konrad is a solo project by Vince Hempstead with guest musicians rising from 80s doom band Pagan Altar, Pale Divine from USA and 80s metal band Sacrilege.

Tracks written in 1997 were finally recorded with the help of Brian J Anthony and released in 2013 as an EP named "Forgotten Souls".
