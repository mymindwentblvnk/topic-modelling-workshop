Also known as Raven. Members of Forgotten Woods released their earlier demos under the name of Ravn. There are rumors that Ravn changed their name to Mengele, and are playing but not recording.

There is another Ravn, a black metal band from Virginia, USA.

There is also another Ravn, a norwegian folkmetal band from Trondheim. They combine heavy guitar riffs with a beautiful Norwegian female voice, all inspired by Norwegian folklore.
