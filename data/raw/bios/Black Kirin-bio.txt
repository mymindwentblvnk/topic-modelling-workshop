Black Kirin is a Chinese folk metal band that was formed in 2013 in Changchun, China. The band's original Chinese opera-folk metal style uses double vocals with a down-tuned guitar sound, fast and complex guitar riffs and drumming with amazing opera-folk element which makes their music unique and fantastic.
In 2016, They changed the direction a little bit and eliminated heavy parts and released an instrumental Dark Folk album.
