d-compose (Ben Dainton) is also a hip-hop artist hailing from the Lake District, England. Influences include Braintax & Jehst. Release of the anticipated first album 'Real Eyes' is penned for April 2007.

D-Compose is an international company of five, with members from Uruguay, Poland, Cuba and Venezuela. Combining grindcore ,thrashmetal and punk. Including top musicians like Pablo Minoli (ex-Laberinto) and Liber Torriente. 

D-compose released their first album in 2006 containing seven songs including a cover of the Ramones song 'I'm not Jesus'.
