There is more than one artist with this name:

1. Apocalipsis is a Mexican instrumental metal trio from Mexico City, Mexico. They bridge sludge, doom metal, math rock and post-metal, and end not far off from what an instrumental Mastodon album could sound like. Also the influences from leading post-metal icons such as Pelican or The Ocean are not hard to spot.

The band debuted in 2012 with a self-titled album available via their Bandcamp page.

Members:

Fabian - guitars;
Israel - bass;
Mico - drums.

Former membersp:

Chamaz - bass;

2. Apocalipsis was a Mexican heavy metal trio from Mexico City, Mexico.
