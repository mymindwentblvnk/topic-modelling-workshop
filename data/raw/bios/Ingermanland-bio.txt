Ingermanland is a progressive metal band started by Oddleif Stensland (vocals/guitar; ex-Scariot, Communic) and Erik Mortenson (bass; Communic), the band released two demos, Surface as Ceiling and Beyond Equator, before disbanding and later continuing as Communic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ingermanland">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
