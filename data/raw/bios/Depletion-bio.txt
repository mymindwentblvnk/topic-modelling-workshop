Depletion is a pakistani metal band that plays in a death metal style primarily with some thrash metal and melodic death metal influences. Currently the line up is.

Haider Nawaz - Vocals
Arfraz Mamoon - Guitars
Shahab Khan - Guitars
Waqar Ghayas - Bass
