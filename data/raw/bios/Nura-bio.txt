Nura Habib Omer (Arabic: نورا حبيب عمر‎, born December 24, 1988) is a German rapper of Saudi-Arabian and Eritrean descent.

She is known for being part of Die toten Crackhuren im Kofferraum and SXTN. Following the split of SXTN in 2018, she has pursued a solo career. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
