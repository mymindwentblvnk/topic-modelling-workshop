World of Silence was a Swedish band playing progressive metal in the vein of groups such as Dream Theater and Rush. The line-up consisted of ex-Godgory members Mikael Dahlqvist (Guitar), Fredric Danielsson (Bass), Fredric Danielsson (Keyboard), and included Mathias Sandquist (Vocals) and Bruno Andersen (Drums).

They released two full-length albums, Window of Heaven (1996) and Mindscapes (1999) and were featured on Black Mark Tribute performing a cover of The Beatles' classic Strawberry Fields Forever. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/World+of+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
