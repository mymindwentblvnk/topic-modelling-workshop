Autumn Dawn was a death metal band from the State of Maryland in the United States, featuring ex-Dying Fetus/Sadistic Torment vocalist Vince Matthews and ex-Psychotogen/Pessimist guitar player John Voyles.

The band has two recordings, "... Emptiness" from 1996 and "Biblical Warnings" from 1998, both on Wild Rags Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Autumn+Dawn">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
