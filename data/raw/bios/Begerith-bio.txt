A black metal/death metal band who formed in Vladivostok, Russia in the year 2003.  They moved to Moscow as time went by and then relocated to Warsaw, Poland later on.  They are currently in Warsaw.  Their lyrical themes include occultism, theology, philosophy, cosmos and the psychology of consciousness.   They are signed to Minotauro Records.  They consist of Alexey "Wiedzmin" Bushuev (vocals, guitars), Egor Pavlov (lead guitars), Egor "Slayer" Menshikov (bass) and Evgeny Mazur (drums).  They have released one EP, two full-length albums and one split.
