Lizard Professor are a progressive metal five-piece hailing from Dallas/Fort Worth, Texas. Originally formed in 2008, the band has played with many touring bands such as Animals As Leaders, The Red Chord, and The Faceless.

Members:
Sean Foster - vocals
Gannon McCoy - guitar
Dustin Valenta - guitar
Alex Reyes - bass
Bryce Butler - drums
