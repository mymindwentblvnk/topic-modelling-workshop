Worms Of Vomit is a Slamming Brutal / Technical / Death Metal / Goregrind band emerging from  a small town in Assam, India named Udalguri. Our music has major influenced by some Technical , Slam, Brutal, Terror and Death stuffs. The band has five members and named the band after a High School Party. We have released our new Slamming Brutal Death Metal EP on 23 Aug, 2014 named "Cannibilistic Decayed". And also had released a Technical Death Metal EP "Essence Of Brutality" on 2nd Feb, 2014 containing four tracks. Its a sick Slamming Brutal Death Metal band from India. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Worms+Of+Vomit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
