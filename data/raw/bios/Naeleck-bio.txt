Naeleck is an electronic artist who makes french electro/fidget house. He began his career in Tokyo, Japan working as a DJ but after playing for some time decided to turn to producing his own music. In his promotional photos and while performing, he wears a black hat with two big white eyes and a pointy-toothed smile.

He released tracks and remixed on Roc Trax, TCY Recordings, Coco Machete Records and Den Haku Records, his own imprint.

In 2013 he ranked 11th on LOUD Magazine ranking of the best japanese DJs.
