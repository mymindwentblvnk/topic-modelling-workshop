Crillson was an American Power Metal band formed in Wasilla, Alaska. They released an album in 1993 entitled "Coming of a New Age". 

Last known lineup was:
Jesse Rezendes - Vocals 
Daniel Miller - Guitar
Chris Craig - Drums
