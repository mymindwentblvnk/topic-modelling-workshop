Caroline Pennell from "The Voice" is one of top 48 contestants in Season 5 of the NBC reality singing competition series. She is on CeeLo Green's team.

Caroline Pennell sang "Anything Could Happen" by Ellie Goulding for her blind audition. After CeeLo and Blake Shelton both turned their chairs around, she decided to choose CeeLo to be her coach. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Caroline+Pennell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
