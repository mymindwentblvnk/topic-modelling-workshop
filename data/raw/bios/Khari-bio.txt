Khari is a world renowned poet, spoken word artist, published author, and music producer. He has performed throughout the country, gracing stages from New Orleans to the Nuyorican.  On 2010, he released his groundbreaking, double disk, spoken word CD "VICTORY."  The success of VICTORY cemented Khari as one of the top selling poets/ spoken word artists in the country.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Khari">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
