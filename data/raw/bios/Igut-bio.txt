IGUT formed in 2002 in Zagreb, Croatia. First official demo was recorded in 2003 titled Dreams Are Forever. In the year of 2004 band moves forward and records mini album called More Than Words Can Tell (released on Brand New Place records).

In 2007 Igut recorded first official LP called "Eternities". Album was released on UK label called Mother Should Know records/Lockjaw records in the year 2009. With very good album reviews and cover mount track on Terrorizer Fear Candy CD compilation Igut did UK tour in April 2009.
In 2012 Igut released their 2nd album "Point of No Return".

During their career Igut played a lot of live shows and festivals with bands like Slayer, Hatebreed, Dimmu Borgir, Mastodon, Gojira, 30 secconds to Mars, Darkest Hour, Cameran, The Wage Of Sin, Shai Hulud, Malkovich, etc.
