insanul maut was a black metal band from west jawa, indonesia.

Last known line-up

Acep Abdul Aziz "Malice" - Guitar (Nocternity (Idn), LunA)
Andris Satriya Utama "O.D.A" - Guitar
Yudi Hardhiyana "Yudaz" - Bass
Asep Setiawan "Marco" - Drums

Releases:

Endless Damnation (EP 2000, Bising Records),
Dusk To Dawn (Album 2002, Extreme Souls Productions),
Sabda Zarathustra (Album 2003, Ra Productions).
