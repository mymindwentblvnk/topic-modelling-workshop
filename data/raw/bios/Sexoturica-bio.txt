In 1994 Jason Newsted engaged with Sepultura's guitarist Andreas Kisser and Exodus man Tom Hunting on drums on their oddball Metal venture Sexoturica (a combination of "Sepultura-Exodus-Metallica"). Exodus guitarist Gary Holt also performed live but was not to featuring on recordings. On March 19th 1995 Sexoturica recorded the three track 'SpermogoDemo' sessions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sexoturica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
