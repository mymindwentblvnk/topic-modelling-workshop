KATRA is a  Finnish metal band. Their first album was released 2007, later the band was signed by Napalm Records who released the international version of the debut album entitled "Beast Within" (2008). 

Third album (Out of the Ashes) in stores October 26th (USA/Canada) 27th (Finland/Spain) 29th (rest of Europe) new single at www.katra.fi
