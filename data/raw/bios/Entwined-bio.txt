Entwined are Gothic Metal band from Colchester, England, formed in 1996. 
The band released two demos ("XIII" and "Her Cherished Mask")  before signing to Earache Records to release their first and only album "Dancing Under Glass". 
The last known line up was

Simon - Bass
James Southgate - Drums
Lee James - Guitars
Stephen John Tovey - Vocals, Guitars
