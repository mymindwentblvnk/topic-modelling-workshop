Unfit Ass. (aka. Unfit Association) is a 6 piece death metal band from Nyíregyháza, Hungary. Formed in 1990, have only one full length album: Pulse released in 1996 under Trottel, and disbanded shortly thereafter. Although they did release 4 demos between 1990 and 1993.
In 2011, the publisher Neverheard Distro common CD released by the band's first era of Absurd Reality and  Flagging Water remastered demos.
