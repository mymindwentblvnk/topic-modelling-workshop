Klingsor are an instrumental depressive black metal band from America, formed in 2004. Klingsor only has one member who also refers to him self as Klingsor. They are currently unsigned and have only one demo, Klingsor which was released in 2005, two of the three tracks are available on their MySpace page - http://www.myspace.com/klingsor666 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Klingsor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
