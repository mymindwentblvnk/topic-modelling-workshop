Elishia was a melodic gothic metal band from Puerto Rico that was formed in 1996. It was in 1997 that the band recorded its only demo in the homestudio of the hardcore band Golpe Justo, entitled The Vague Scent of Pallid Mallows. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/elishia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
