Ending Story are a Death-Metalband from Aachen, Germany. They describe their own Music as "Tricky-Death-Metal-Core"(quote of curly). The Band consist of 4 Musicians, Abe (Guitar), Ed (Bass), Fibble (Drums) and Curly (Vocals). There is no Album yet and they are mostly known in Aachen and the vicinity. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ending+Story">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
