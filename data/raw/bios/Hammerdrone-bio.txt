Hammerdrone is a Blackened Death Metal band from Calgary, AB.  Formed in October 2010, Hammerdrone features Graham Harris (ex-Genepool, ex-Rotschreck) on vocals, Vinnie Cardellini on drums, Rick Cardellini on lead guitar, Curtis Beardy on rhythm guitar and backing vocals and Steve Greenough (ex-Terra Cotta) on bass.

Hammerdrone released their debut, a 4-track EP ("A Demon Rising") on July 10, 2012. This can be downloaded from http://hammerdrone.bandcamp.com

March 2013 saw the band release "Wraiths On The Horizon" as a single, and a taster of the forthcoming debut album (as yet untitled). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hammerdrone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
