Diesto is a stoner metal band from Portland, Oregon. So far they have released 3 full-length albums: Outland (in 2000), Doomtown 7 (in 2004), and Isle Of Marauder (in 2008). They have also released a 7 inch  which is limited to 100 copies on red vinyl and contains 2 songs from the Doomtown 7 studio session.

Currently the band is working on an upcoming full-length titled High As The Sun that is due for a late 2009 / early 2010 release, according to the band's MySpace page.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Diesto">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
