Tokyo-based solo female performer. Born in 1989, she started her solo project in Tokyo, spring 2011 .
Tracks are all recorded in her bedroom, simply with one lap top and some keyboards.
Her dance track expresses the space depth beneath the loneliness influenced by various electro music especially psychedelic chill-out.
