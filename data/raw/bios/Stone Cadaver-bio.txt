Stone Cadaver - Aarhus, Denmark

Members: Anders Bech Nielsen - guitar & vocals, Jesper Hauptmann Christensen - drums, Andreas Slocinski - bass.

Stone Cadaver is heavy, 70s inspired stoner rock from Aarhus, Denmark. With lyrics that probe the dark, musty recesses of the human mind, the three piece ensemble plays host to a black mass of punishing guitar, distorted bass and heretical drums <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stone+Cadaver">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
