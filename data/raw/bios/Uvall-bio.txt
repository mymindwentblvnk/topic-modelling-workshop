Uvall was a black metal band from Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States, formed by Todd Paulson (guitars) and Chris Donlon (drums).

In 2006 Todd Paulson founded the folk black metal one-man band Dormant. He is also owner of the label God Is Myth Records, and active in dark ambient scene with the project Cruel Harvest.

Discography:
2003 - The Philosophy of Evil (promo)
2003 - Tales of War (split with Bare-Sark)
2005 - Obsidian Torment
2006 - October Turns... Ruined
