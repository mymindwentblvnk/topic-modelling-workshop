Shallow Intentions Is A folk metal band from England.The band was formed In 2006.Since then the band has evolved from a more blackened approach with a folk twist to Epic,Melodic And Engaging Folk Metal.Shallow Intentions has quickly become an established name both locally and nationally,gaining praise and interest across the land,ensuring a loyal following.Thus far the band has released both a studio and live Ep,whilst also playing countless gigs across the country.
