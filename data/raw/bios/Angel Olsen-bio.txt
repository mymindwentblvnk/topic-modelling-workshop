Angel Olsen (born January 22, 1987) is an American singer-songwriter and musician from St. Louis, Missouri[4][5] who currently lives in Asheville, North Carolina. She has recorded and toured as a backing singer with Bonnie 'Prince' Billy & The Cairo Gang, before embarking on her own career. 

To date, Olsen has released six studio albums:  Half Way Home (2012), Burn Your Fire for No Witness (2014), My Woman (2016), All Mirrors (2019), Whole New Mess (2020) and Big Time (2022). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angel+Olsen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
