Crowlegion is a Sludge/Boogie/Doom band from Gothenburg, Sweden. The members are Linus Pilebrand (guitar/song), Peter Jakobsson (bass) and Joakim Hagselius (drums).
With the goal of combining each band members background in everything from d-beat punk and hardcore to doom metal and 70`s rock, Crowlegion sets forth to bring you heavy and sludgy metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crowlegion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
