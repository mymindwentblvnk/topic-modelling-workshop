The Melodic Black/Death Metal band originated from Penang,northern Malaysia.Was formed by Sophyz,Mizeus and Arrev in early 2006 with the first name,Brabazom Corpse.Brabazom Corpse used to play death metal in that time.In late 2008,the band changed the name and appearence style of music to melodic black death metal when Elphy and Hayzach enter the band.Matt and Whythead joined the band in late 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brabazom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
