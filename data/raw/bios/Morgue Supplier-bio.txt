Morgue Supplier are a Brutal Death Metal/Grindcore band from Chicago , Illinois 

As front-man Paul Gillis says - No gimmicky, trendy bullshit here. Just pure, from the soul, intensity rendered at an expert level.

Chris Mull           -    Guitars (2005-present)
