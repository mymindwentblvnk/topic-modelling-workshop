Black Curtain are a heavy metal band from Phoenix, Arizona. The released their full length debut "Apocalypse Rising" on 21 November 2015. They are:

Bryant "Bryco" Ward: Bass, Vocals
George Bryan: Guitars (lead)
Shane Christensen: Vocals (lead), Guitars (rhythm)
Daniel Christensen: Drums, Percussion, Vocals
