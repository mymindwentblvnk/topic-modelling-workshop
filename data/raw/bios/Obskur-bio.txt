1) Obskur is a pagan black metal band from Germany, that includes members Magog (Ger) and Confident of Victory.  Their debut full length was released on Gjallarhorn Klangschmiede in 2006.

The band uses a dual vocal attack, including clean vocals to produce a strikingly strong and brutal sound.
