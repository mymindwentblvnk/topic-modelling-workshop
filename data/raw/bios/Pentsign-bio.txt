Pentsign is a Black/Death Metal band from Murmansk, Russia. The band has released one album thus far. According to Metal Archives, members also have played in Навь, Dismember Life Body, Nechist' (not to be confused with Нечисть) and Full Decay.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pentsign">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
