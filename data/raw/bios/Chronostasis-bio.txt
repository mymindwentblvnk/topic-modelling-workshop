Chronostasis is the illusion in which the first impression following a saccade appears to be extended in time. Many similarities can come between this general definition of 'Chronostasis' and our musical thought processes. Time has intrigued the four members of this band greatly. What is our goal?... To fuse the infinities of time, space, matter, and music into one.
