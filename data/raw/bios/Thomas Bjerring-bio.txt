Thomas Bjerring has released two Eps on Traum.
He has worked with vocalist  David Skog for the track´s „Ice“  and “2:45” .
Thomas Bjerring is hi-energy, partly progressive and also shows mesmerising pop song quality with his music..  "Ice" has allready been licenced to Sony in Denmark for their: „Sound Of Copenhagen Vol. 6“ compilation  and is being played out by nation radio stations there.

His most recent Track Track "Djakarta" has been been released on Tour de Traum V in december 2012 (http://www.traumschallplatten.de/traum/traumcd28/traumcd28.html) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thomas+Bjerring">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
