Oniromancia is a black metal band from France The band was created in 1998 under the name "Goathrone Prayer". In 2001, when the 2 other members left, Yulkernaath changed the name to Oniromancia.

Discography:
Nokturnal Terror Sickness (2001)
Triste Visage de cette Existence (2002)
