Pietro Lombardi (June 9, 1992 in Karlsruhe) was a German participant in the show "Pop Idol Germany" and the winner of season eight.

In the eighth season of the show "Pop Idol Germany", he reached the title of first place with the song "Call My Name" arranged by Dieter Bohlen. Before taking part in the show he was employed by the crystal manufacturer Swarovski. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pietro+Lombardi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
