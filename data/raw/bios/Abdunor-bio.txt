With their new album planned to be released end of June 2013, Abdunor is ready to show the world that they are back and very serious about raising the bar within the metal scene. Here you will find a taste of this new album. It is recorded by Abdunor and mixed and mastered by Studio Fredman meant to be only a little taste of what can be expected from the future of this band. Abdunor is much more than a simple black metal band. One of the ultimate goals for Abdunor has always been to create uniqueness and always living up to their own standards. They deliver music that encompasses a truly special atmosphere (In both music and lyrics) put together by fast, slow, technical and melodic parts all played with technical perfection by very competent musicians who are truly creative. This combination creates a unique musical experience. Abdunor has plenty of material ready and many interesting ideas regarding the future, so stay tuned!!
