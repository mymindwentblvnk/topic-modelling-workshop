Gales of Avalon are a Black/Death Metal band, from Calgary, Alberta. They have released one self titled EP.

Line up:
Mark Dillon - Vocals, Guitar
Dylan Hansen - Guitar
James Neill - Bass, Vocals
Jamie Gallo - Drums
