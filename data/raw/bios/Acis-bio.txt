Acis was a Swedish Heavy Metal band.
They released a single in 1987 named Lawbreaker/A Wanted Man on Active Records and later fell into obscurity.

Last known line-up:
Johan Andersson - Vocals 
Peder Berglund - Guitars 
Peter Ledin - Guitars (Vanessa, Blackworld)
Marco Malasagna - Bass
