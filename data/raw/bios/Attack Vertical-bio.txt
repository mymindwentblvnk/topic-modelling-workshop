Founded in 1999, Attack Vertical plays a Metalcore which leads towards Death Metal. 

 In 2011, AV recorded their 3rd album « This Glorious World ». The album shows a darker and more energetic side of their music. Around 20 concerts in Switzerland and a Canadian Tour are planed this year to promote this new album. 

 Attack Vertical has been selected in the top ten „Best Swiss album Of The Year 2011“ at the M4Music Awards in Zürich. 

 Slowly but constantly, the Band has now made its name on the Swiss metal stage and shared the stage with some well known Bands such as: Arch Enemy, Darkane or God Dethroned.
