from the epicenter of the Thrash world - San Francisco, California, Yigael's Wall erupted onto the metal scene in 2007 with their debut album, Requiem For The Living. Formed by guitarist, Vincent Vidavici in January 2007, Yigael’s Wall plays fast, captivating Thrash rooted in the classic Bay Area tradition of bands like Exodus, but with a much darker side. 2008 saw the release of the compilation album, Infesting The World 4, from Infest Productions, featuring the track Wages Of Sin. The four members quickly received acclaim from the press and have managed to attract an ever growing legion of die-hard fans.Yigael’s Wall is quickly proving they are one of the Bay Area’s most talented and driven entities by dishing out one brutal performance after another.
