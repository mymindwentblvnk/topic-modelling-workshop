Asethry was a 5-piece metalmachine from Belgium, Oost-Vlaanderen. They played destructive metal/deathcore with a extreme touch of melody, energy and musicianship. Asethry kept pushing the boundaries to the extreme and it's ultimate goal was to blow everyone away with brutality. 

Constantly touring in Belgium, they established a big local fanbase. Their biggest hit was "The Shining Blade".
