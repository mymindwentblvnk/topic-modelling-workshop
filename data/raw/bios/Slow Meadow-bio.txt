Slow Meadow is a solo project of Matthew Kidd, which began when he presented his Aural Method project to Marc Byrd of Hammock, one of his main influences. Slow Meadow went on to become the first signing to Hammock's record label.

The debut, self-titled, album was released in August 2015.
