Mortuous are an old-school death metal band from San Jose, California, that was formed in 2009. Includes members from Necrot, Exhumed, Dekapitator, and Bruxers. They released a demo "Mors Immortalis" in early 2010 with Skeleton Plague records. They released their debut full-length studio album "Through Wilderness" on June 22, 2018.
