Armand Hammer is a hip-hop duo from New York, New York that consists of Billy Woods and Elucid. They released their debut collaborative album, Race Music, on October 22, 2013 via Backwoodz Studioz. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Armand+Hammer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
