The members of a band by the name of Gunther Weezul walk along that thin line that separates creativity and pure insanity.  The group features the haunting vocals of singer Jann Kasey Dorr (formerly of legendary captial area bands including Section 8 and Ill Remembered), the thick grinding strings of Adam Gregoire, the thumping basslines of Jay Watkajtys, and the hammering drums of Kevin Carly.  This band brings a sound to the table that brings fear, confusion, and utter satisfaction <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gunther+Weezul">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
