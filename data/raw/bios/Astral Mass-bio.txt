Astral Mass is an exploratory space rock project from Indianapolis, IN. 

The sound entwines doom, stoner, and metal with a psychedelic twist to initialize sonic pleasure centers. 

Members have played in Apostle of Solitude, Dead Limb, The Hedons, The Heavy Company, Sleepbringer, Necorpharmacon and Giraffes Eating Lions.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Astral+Mass">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
