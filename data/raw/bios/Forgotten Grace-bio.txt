Forgotten Grace formed in Hull, England in 2004 and play Technical Death Metal. they have shared the stage with the likes of Zyklon, Dead Beyond Buried, Nailed, Knights of the Abyss, The Red Chord, From A Second Story Window, Ion Dissonance, Cursed, Annotations of an Autopsy, The Seventh Cross, Heresy of Thieves, Viatrophy, Dead to Fall, Sylosis and many more.
