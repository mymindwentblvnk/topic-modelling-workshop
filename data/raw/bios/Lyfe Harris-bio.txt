Lyfe Harris  (born February 18, 1994) is a singer, producer and songwriter from Atlanta, Georgia, but is now living in Los Angeles, California.

Lyfe's known for the release of EPs like No Nights Off and Ten and for albums like Dysphoria and Watch You Dream 2.  In the summer of 2016 he released the album called After Midnight. He has gained much of his popularity posting his original songs to the music sharing site SoundCloud. Vintage Club Records.
