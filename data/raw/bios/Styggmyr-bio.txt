Black metal band from Norway (Lillehammer). Released two full-length album:
- 2003 - World Massacre
- 2005 - Hellish Blasphemy
and three demos:
- 2000 - Stigma Diabolicum
- 2002 - Summoning the Demons
- 2007 - Sathanas Supreme
