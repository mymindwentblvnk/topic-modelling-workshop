Rox Diamond is an american hard rock/AOR band that formed in the late 80's. They released their self-titled debut album in 1992, it was unsuccessfull, and the band broke up shortly after its release. Rox Diamond regrouped in 2005 to release their second album "Powerdrive". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rox+Diamond">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
