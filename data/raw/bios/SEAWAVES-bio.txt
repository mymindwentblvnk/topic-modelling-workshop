SEAWAVES are a Dreampop duo from Manchester, England. Comprising of Lead vocalist Si Van Brussel and Lead producer Dan Traynor who united through analogous life experiences; paralleled with mutual appreciation of the emotional perceptions obtained within dreams and reflecting those same feelings in both audio and visual form.

SEAWAVES are currently near to completing their next full length release entitled 'The Pistol Star'
