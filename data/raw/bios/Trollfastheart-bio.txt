TrollfasthearT started in 2008 by “the troll” Kadgar from Segovia, SPAIN. After somes demo song TrollfasthearT recording our debut album during July and August 2010 in HeL1S Studio, Madrid. “Only For Trolls” released in November 2010. 8 songs in more of 40 minutes of pure and true troll art. Black Metal by "the troll" dedicated to errants creatures of the nigth. Kadgar also plays bass, vocals and lyrics for the death metal band ABÿFS and the black metal band ICepressIVe. He is known for being a underground metal concert promoter.
