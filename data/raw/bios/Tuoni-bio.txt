Tuoni is a finnish thrash metal band formed in Lahti, Finland in 2003.

Current line-up consists of Aki Hanttu (Aksu Hanttu)  (vocals, drums), Tuomas Leinonen (bass), Jaakko Teittinen (vocals) and Tom Mikkola (guitar).

Heikki Saari plays the drums live.
