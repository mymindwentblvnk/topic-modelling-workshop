Canadian Nationalist skinhead band Battlefront was originally formed in the late 90's. With their upfront, politically charged lyrics and a unique mix of Rock and Oi they were quick to gain a large following with RAC fans worldwide. After various gigs with fellow RAC bands at the infamous Hate Shack they where asked to submit two songs for the Blood & Honour compilation "Fallen But Not Forgotton" Shortly after its release they were signed to American based Panzerfaust Records where they released the full length CD "Into The Storm". 

Most popular tracks include "Skinhead For Life", "String 'em Up", their Ode to American Nationalist "Robert J. Mathews" aswell as the their Skrewdriver cover of "One Fine Day". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Battlefront">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
