Spanish satiric metal band "El Reno Renardo" was formed by Zarigüeya on the summer of 2006. The original group was formed by Zarigüeya (vocals and guitar), Cabra Montesa (bass), and Urogallo The Powerhead (drums). 

Current Formation:
Aniceto Zarzalejo 'Zarigüeya' – Voice and guitar
Simón J. Hayber 'Mapache' or 'T-Map' – Bass
