There are three artists with the name Mola:

1) Mola are a four piece rock band from Dublin, Ireland. The mission is to drag the passion and feeling that existed in the rock movement of the late 60s and early 70s, into the modern world. This is not a trend, there are no creative restrictions. This is about great songs performed by great musicians with all the passion in the world.

2) Mola is a drone/ambient post-rock artist from High Wycombe, England.
