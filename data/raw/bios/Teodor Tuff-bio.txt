Teodor Tuff is  a Norwegian band from Trondheim formed in 2008. They released their self titled debut album in 2009, which was largely hard rock inspired. Their second album Soliloquy has a strong Power / Progressive metal sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Teodor+Tuff">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
