The History of Apple Pie were a band based in London, UK who formed in 2010 and disbanded in 2015.

The band centers on the songwriting partnership of Stephanie Min (vocals) and Jerome Watson (guitar), who are joined by James Thomas (drums), Aslam Ghauri (guitar) and Joanna Curwood. Kelly Lee Owens used to play bass but left the band to pursue a solo career. 

Their debut album, "Out of View", was released on January 28, 2013 which spawned a couple of singles. The sophomore album "Feel Something" was released on 29 September, 2014.
