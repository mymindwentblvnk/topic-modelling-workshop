Anshar is a Thrash/Groove Metal band from Janesville, Wisconsin. They play Groove Metal in the vein of Accuser and Pantera. Defacing Humanity is the only full length album they have released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Anshar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
