Riff Cannon are a 4 piece heavy rock band from Boston, MA, whose influences include Black Sabbath, Torche, Boris, Queens of the Stone Age, The Melvins, Goatsnake, Pentagram, Electric Wizard, etc. They have so far released only their debut album, "Mercury Mountain," in Sept of 2009, with a follow up, tentatively titled "Phoenix" to be release in the first quarter of 2011.

The band has changed its name to Summoner. The album "Phoenix" was released in 2012 under the new band's name. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Riff+Cannon">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
