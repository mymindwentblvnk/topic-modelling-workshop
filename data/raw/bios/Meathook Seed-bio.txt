Industrial metal side-project formed by Mitch Harris of Napalm Death. Released two albums - 1993's 'Embedded' and the follow-up 'Basic Instructions Before Leaving Earth' (B.I.B.L.E.)

The first album featured Mitch's Napalm colleague Shane Embury on bass and Trevor Peres & Donald Tardy from Obituary on vocals & drums respectively. 

On the second album, vocals are done by French singer Christophe Lamouret from Out.
