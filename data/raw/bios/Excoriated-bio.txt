There have been at least two bands using this name, both of them death metal.

1) Death metal band from Malmo, Sweden formed in 2012. Their only release is the demo tape Raped In A Coffin, self-released in 2014 (re-pressed by Old Axe Cult Records in 2015). Split-up.
LINEUP:
Marcus Likqvist - Vocals, Bass 
Nekrofilip - Guitars
Dennis Nilsson (R.I.P.) - Drums

2) Death metal band from South London, UK formed in 2002. Striving in making groove ridden, hook infested metal they constantly push forward. Their only release is [Over-Throne] EP, self-released in 2006, recorded at To Throw Out Studios.
Official myspace: http://www.myspace.com/excoriated
LINEUP:
Jonn Brooke - Bass
Matt Carrington - Vocals, Guitars 
Craig Carter - Drums
