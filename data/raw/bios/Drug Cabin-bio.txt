Drug Cabin is an easy, country-infused, Laurel-Canyonesque band that started as a bit of a spin-off side project but has enough legs and great songs to be a thing. Their second release of 2015, Wiggle Room, is definitely a thing, a twangy chunk of summer to tuck away for a cloudy day. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drug+Cabin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
