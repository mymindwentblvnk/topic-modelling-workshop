Nick J. D. Hodgson is the solo project of the ex Kaiser Chiefs drummer and also Mark Ronson, Shirley Bassey, Rat Boy collaborator. Hodgson released his debut album 'Tell Your Friends' on 26th January 2018 featuring singles such as 'Suitable', 'RSVP' and 'Don't Forget To Go To Sleep'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nick+J.D.+Hodgson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
