Alex Beaupain, born in Besançon in 1974, is a singer-songwriter and composer of music for French films. Former student at Sciences Po1, he composed both songs and soundtracks for films by director Christophe Honoré, especially for the film "17 fois Cécile Cassard" (2002),  "Dans Paris" (2006) and "Les Chansons d’amour" (2007 ). In 2005 his first solo album of 11 tracks, entitled "Garçon d’honneur" was released. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alex+Beaupain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
