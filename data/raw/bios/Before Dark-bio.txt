Before Dark was an American R&B girl group that originated in the late 1990s. The group consisted of sisters Arike Rice and Jeni Rice Genzuk (AKA Jeni G.), and their cousin Mia Lee, all from South Central Los Angeles. Arike Rice was formerly a member of the 90's group The Voices when she was nine.
