C Average is hard rock band with some punk influence. They are a duo, a guitarists and a drummer. Largely instrumental with some tracks containing vocals. Lyrics usually use sci-fi and fantasy as subject matter. Their influences include Maiden, Karp, Rush, Champs, Sabbath, & Blue Cheer
