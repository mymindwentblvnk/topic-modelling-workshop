7 Sins is a Melodic Power/heavy metal band from Malmö, Sweden.
They have released an EP "Angels and Devils"(2005), one demo "Metalized"(2005) and an album "Forever Winter Nights"(2006).
They are currently unsigned.


Vocals: Jonas Olsson
Guitar: Öyvind Andersen
Guitar: Mattias Glans
Drums: Dan Pålsson
Bass: Marcus Thomsen
