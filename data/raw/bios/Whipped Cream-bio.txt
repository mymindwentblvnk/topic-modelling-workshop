Caroline Cecil, known professionally as WHIPPED CREAM, is a Canadian DJ and producer based on Vancouver Island. She is currently signed to Big Beat Records. She released her major label debut EP, Who Is Whipped Cream?, in August 2020 on Big Beat.

In 2012, Cecil attended the Sasquatch! Music Festival where she watched a performance by Active Child that spurred her to create her own music. Using Ableton Live and a Traktor controller, she learned how to produce tracks over the next two years. She released her first single as Whipped Cream in 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Whipped+Cream">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
