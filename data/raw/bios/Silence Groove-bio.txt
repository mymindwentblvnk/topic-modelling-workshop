Silence Groove, a producer from Poland, became interested in electronic music since his youth. His first trancy EP called “Shiny Snow” was signed in 2010 on Unearthed Recordings.Track “Seven” released on Terminal-4 appeared on Richard Durand’s ISOS 9. His latest trance productions are being supported by some of the biggest names of the scene such as Paul Van Dyk, Armin Van Buuren, Andy moor and Matt Darey.
In addition to trance he began drum and bass production in 2011 and he regularly collaborates with producer `Skeletone`. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Silence+Groove">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
