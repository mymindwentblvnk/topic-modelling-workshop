Empatica is a Symphonic Heavy Metal band from the south of the Netherlands. Building their sounds from influences of Nightwish, Dimmu Borgir, Sonata Arctica, Rhapsody of Fire and Epica.

The band was founded by Harald Bouten and Bram Hoogstraten after they quit Inquisition Symphony in the spring of 2010. In the fall of 2010 the line-up was completed and the band moved to the garage of Rick Krause, their new keyboard player.

Empatica is currently preparing for some shows they have planned in the summer to promote their demo cd "Redemption".

Current Line-up:
- Mike Niersmann - Vocals
- Harald Bouten - Guitars, Grunts
- Bram Hoogstraten - Guitars
- Rick Krause - Keys
