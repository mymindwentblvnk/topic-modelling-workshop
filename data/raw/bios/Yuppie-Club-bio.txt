Yuppie-Club is a grindcore band from Germany (North Rhine-Westphalia) formed in 2002. The members are:Lee - Vocals , Bill - Guitar , Jeff - Bass and Ken- Drums. They released two albums with record label Finest Noise / Radar: Pretty Brutal(2008) and Pretty Insane(2009). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yuppie-Club">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
