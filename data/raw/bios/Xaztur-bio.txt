Xaztur was a Black Metal band from Rotherham, United Kingdom that was created in the year 1992. In 1993 the band recorded 2 demos, Frozen Winter and Pagan Rites. Although the band had a very short lifespan they will go down in history as the first British black metal band to use corpse paint.

Last known line-up

Azazel Christ Defiler (A.K.A. Grand Lord Immanis) - Vocals/Bass
Necrentus - Guitar
