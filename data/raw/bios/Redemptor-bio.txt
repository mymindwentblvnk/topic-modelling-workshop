RedemptoR is a technical death metal band. It was formed in 2001 by Daniel Kesler and Lucas Sputowski who are responsible from the start for whole music and lyrical concept of the band. As Daniel said: "Our music can be described as extreme-melodic metal". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Redemptor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
