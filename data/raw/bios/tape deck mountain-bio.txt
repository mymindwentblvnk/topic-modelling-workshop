Tape Deck Mountain is the shoegaze project of Travis Trevisan of San Diego, CA

They self-released a 6-track Sparks EP on cassette tape in Feburary 2009. Their full-length debut Ghost was released November 17th, 2009, via Lefse Records. In January 2011 they released Secret Serf EP on Lefse Records. In March 2013 they released Slow Salvation EP on Lefse Records. Their 2nd LP SWAY was released in October 2013.
