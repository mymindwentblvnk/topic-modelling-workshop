Sadistic Perversion was a death metal band from Austria. The band has released an EP "The Beginning Of The Intemperance" in 2012 before splitting up, however some band members have formed a new project called Pray For Pain. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sadistic+Perversion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
