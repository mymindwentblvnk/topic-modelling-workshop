Hailing from Armstrong, BC, Canada this group of 5 metal souls came together to create the best damn metal and have fun while doing it. Consisting of lead vocal Kasey Klassen, Chris Wynne & Chris Shaver on guitars, Jesse valstar on bass and Jeremy Mclachlan on drums to round it out. They are currrently unsigned, but have self-released the H.O.E Demo CD, recorded from December 2007 - January 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hybrids+of+Exile">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
