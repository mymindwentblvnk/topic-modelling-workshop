Our Final Chapter is a Death Metal/Grindcore band from Brooklyn Connecticut. They formed in 2001 and came out with their full-length album From Hell to Higher Plains through Spare Change Records. In September 2008 they came out with their second full length album Titled Kill the Silence, With there most popular song "pretext for fear".  Which is about the terrorist attack on 9/11, being an inside job.     <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Our+Final+Chapter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
