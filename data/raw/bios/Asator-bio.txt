Asator is a black metal band from Bremen, Germany. The band was founded in 2005 and has independently released a full length album Gezeiten, the EP Zu fernen Horizonten, and two demos (Auf alten Pfaden, Promo 2012). Numerous shows have been played in northern Germany over the years. Originally playing viking metal, the band's sound has matured into their own style of black metal infused with intricate melodies and thoughtful passages. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
