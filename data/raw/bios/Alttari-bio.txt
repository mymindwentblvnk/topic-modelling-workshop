Alttari is Finnish Black Metal Band From Lappeenranta Formed at year 2004.
Current Line-up is Black Longinus - Vokills,Bass(also in Armour,Hammer (Fin), Warpervertor - Guitars, L.H - Drums(Session)
Alttari's Current Label is Werewolf Records and Lyrical Themes are usually Satanic,Hate and Darkness. Alttari Have Released 2 Demons and 1 Split.(2005)Lightning The Torches,(2007)Alttari/Kadotus,(2007)Demo 2
