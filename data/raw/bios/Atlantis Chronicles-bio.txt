Atlantis Chonicles, formerly known as Abyss, are a metalcore/technical death metal band from Paris that formed in 2007. In 2009 they released their debut EP Against the Sea on Realize Records, and released their debut album, Ten Miles Underwater,  in 2012.
  
Members
Antoine - Lead Vocals
Alex - Guitar/Vocals
Mike - Bass
Jérôme - Guitar
Sydney - Drums
