London duo Ten Fé is named after the Spanish for “have faith."  Members are are Ben Moorhouse and Leo Duncan.  Their music is described in NME as having "one eye on the radio-friendly soft rock of The 1975, the other on the on-the-road rock of cult US group The War On Drugs."
