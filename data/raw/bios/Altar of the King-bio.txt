Altar Of The King was a thrash metal/heavy metal band from Oakland, CA, active from the early-to-mid 1990's. Their only official release was a Self-Titled CD released on Rock The Nation Records in 1994. 

Most of AOTK's members were of polish descent; Bogdan Jablonski on vocals, who did background vocals for many testament albums, Bob "The Master" Coons on guitar, Tony "Gutierrez" Anthony on bass & vocals, who now has a solo band called PsychoWrectus (Bob has played guitar in some of the songs on his first CD) and Steve Quartarola on drums, who has played in the 80's heavy metal Black Ice, The Noyze Boyz, The Mighty Chonch Brothers, Press, and also happened to do background vocals for Testament's Soul Of Black. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Altar+Of+The+King">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
