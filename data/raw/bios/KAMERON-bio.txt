Kameron Williams, Born February 4, 1985  is an American musician, singer, songwriter, and  producer. 
Originally from Houston TX, Kameron has played keyboards for many well known bands throughout the United States. He is known for his high energy performing style and keyboard playing talent. Kameron was awarded the KMJQ Magic 102.1 Baddest Band In The Land Winner in 2013. He is similar to Blackeyed Peas, Pharrell WIlliams, Outkast, James Brown, and Prince.
