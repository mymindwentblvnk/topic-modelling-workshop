Vale Arcane is an American metal band formed in 1999. They have released a 3-track demotape Moribund (08/2000), and a full length album titled Death Messages, (03/2015). Mike Van Dyne is also in The Final Sleep, a progressive metal band, and was in Arsis from 2000-2012 (with one brief departure around 2007-2009) and Mullen played live bass on Arsis' 2003 tour. Currently they are active.
