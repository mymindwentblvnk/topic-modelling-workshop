Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur (2008-Present)

Progressive Death Metal band whose members are also involved in other projects such as Sick Society, Incarcerate and In Torment.

The band started at the end of 2008 with only a 3 piece line-up: Dhomfarysa (Guitars), NWar (Guitars) and Faixal (Drums). After several jams and practice for a long time Adib Beast (Vocals) and Kema (Bass) joined.
