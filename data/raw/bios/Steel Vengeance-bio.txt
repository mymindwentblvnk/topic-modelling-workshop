80's heavy metal band. Priest/Maiden style with traces of thrash metal.

Steel Vengeance was formed in 1983 in Michigan. The band is currently recording a new album in Los Angeles, California USA. In May of 2008 a greatest hits album was released on ITUNES entitled "Steel Vengeance - The Collection". Steel Vengeance is in the heavy metal genre similar to Judas Priest and Iron Maiden and they have had numerous accolades throughout the 1980’s such as beating out KISS, Twisted Sister and various other bands for the highly-coveted “Album of the Month” from France’s Enfer Magazine and numerous other magazine awards and heavy European radio play. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Steel+Vengeance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
