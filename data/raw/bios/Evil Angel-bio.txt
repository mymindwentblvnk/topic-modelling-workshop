There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Evil Angel is a finnish old school black/thrash metal band from Lahti. So far they have released a couple of demos and recently (as of March 2007) a full lenght album Unholy Fight For Metal. They have also appeared on a few compilations (Metal On Metal Vol. 1, Outbreak Of Evil III, Don't Burn The Witch). Mikseri.net has some tracks available for download

2) Evil Angel is a DJ duo consisting of Chris Jennings and Marc Lyall. They produce nu-NRG or nu-hardcore tracks. Their releases include the two 12" Evil Angel and Today. They featured on the British nu-hardcore release Bonkers 12. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evil+Angel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
