Krullur are a long-running (formed in 1989) death/thrash metal turned thrashcore/grindcore band from Houston, Texas. Despite having several releases, any of their material is hard to find.

Discography:
1990 - Enormity (Demo)
1993 - Churlish Rubbish (Demo)
1996 - Godvomit Compilation (Split With Tumorhead & Bad Dreams Always & Leech Implant)
1997 - Open Ass Surgery (Rehearsal/Demo)
2001 - Open Ass Surgery (Full-Lenght)
2007 - Demo '07
2009 - Krullur (Instrumental Demo)
