Black Sabbath influenced Doom Metal from Sweden. Founded in 1992, released three demos in 1993, 1996 and 1999. Played their latest gig in 2010, but the rest is unknown. Last known line-up: 

Christian Hellquist - Bass
Kenneth Widjeskog - Drums
Per Wallgren - Guitars
Timothy Griffiths - Vocals
