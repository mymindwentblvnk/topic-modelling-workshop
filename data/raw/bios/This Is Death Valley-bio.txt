A technical make up with a hardcore shell. Heavy deathcore boys from somewhere around Ontario, "This Is Death Valley" are just what you need to wake up your senses, and get you in the right mood to punch your stupid girlfriend in the face, and have a beer. Or, are the perfect place to go to listen to some kickass music.

In a scene where bands spring up overnight, and can play a show, "This Is Death Valley" stands out in the crowd for a few large reasons, amongst various other ones. Being able to put on a crazy show, get the crowd more amped than the headliners, and creating a beautiful mix of death metal, hardcore, and grindcore in some instances, just to name a few of them.
