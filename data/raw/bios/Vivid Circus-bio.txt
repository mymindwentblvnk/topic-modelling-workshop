Vivid Circus is an energetic rock band. Their music includes heavy riffs combined with technical passages, powerful vocals and an explosive rhythm section.

Started at the Enschede Conservatory, the band found its members in january 2010. After the recordings of their first promo album during spring 2010, the band now aims for gigs and writing new songs.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vivid+Circus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
