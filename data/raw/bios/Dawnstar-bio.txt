Dawnstar is a Hungarian band formed in Budapest. The current line-up is the song-writer Attila Wind (vocals/guitars), Balint Hamvas (bass), Anna Mitropulos (keyboards) and Viktor Albert (drums). Dawnstar is one of the bands associated with the BIMS (Budapest Indie Music Scene), being one of several groups that emerged out of the post-punk revival of the 2000s. The sound of the band is generally a mix of flanging and light distorted guitars, throbbing bass, and enchanting keyboard melodies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dawnstar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
