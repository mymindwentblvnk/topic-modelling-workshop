There are many artists called Billy Brown:

1. Billy Brown born 1942 was an Irish musician.  He is probably best remembered as a singer, saxophonist and pianist with one of Irelands most popular showbands, The Freshmen, in 1960s and 1970s

2. Billy Brown. (William George Brown 1929 - 2009) was a Rockabilly artists from West Virginia. He is best known for the songs Did We Have a Party (1957) and Flip Out (1958) for Columbia Records.

3. Billy Brown is a Death Metal/Hardcore band from Tijuana, Mexico.They are best known for the album Cunt Destroyer (2009). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Billy+Brown">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
