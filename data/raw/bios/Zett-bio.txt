A 17 year old independent artist from Rio de Janeiro, Brasil, codenamed Zett. He started posting his first singles on Soundcloud at the age of 15. On December 12, 2020 he released his debut-album "Post-Teen Pop", an album all about Zett's experiences and feelings of love, hopelessness and loneliness that we all go through during our last years of teenagerhood.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zett">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
