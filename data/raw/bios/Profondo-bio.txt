Profondo is a "boerekool thrash and roll" band from the Netherlands. The band weaves multiple styles together like thrash metal ("Useless Hate Song"), hard rock ("Down the Mountain", "Take me in the Back Seat") and punk and hardcore material ("Teenage Yuppie", "Excercise Your Right") among others.

Current line-up:
Colin - Vocals
Thomo - Guitar
Niekk - Bass
