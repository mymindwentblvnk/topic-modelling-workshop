Hailing from the Chattanooga valley area of TN, Coathanger Abortion are the remnants of the "Noiselouds" and were formed in 2001.  Featuring former members of  "Izopin," "Besieged" and "AseraphymN." 

 The band finished recording their debut CD "Dying Breed" in January, 2009 and expect a June, 2009 release.

Largely reclusive, not a lot is known of this band.  However they can be found on the Chattanooga, Bristol, Knoxville, Atlanta and Nashville circuits as well as venues and metal fests nationwide. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Coathanger+Abortion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
