Blacksoul Seraphim is a doom/gothic metal band hailing from Northampton, MA, USA and founded in 2012.
It is mainly another project from Morte McAdaver  (see also Sorrowseed) with Thomas Cyranowski on keyboards and Rick Lowell on drums.
They recently self-released their debut Alms & Avarice.

See http://www.mortemcadaver.com/blacksoulseraphim.shtml for more info.
