Matthew Young may refer to one of several artists:

1) A musician active in the 1980s, performing electronica and eclectic music, including an album of drum machines and hammered dulcimer.

2) A singer/songwriter performing primarily on the Rennaissance Faire and Celtic Festival circuit.  Often booked under the name "The Pride of Ireland", his albums are released under his own name.

3) Matt Young is the founder, member bassist and lead vocalist of British metal band HeKz. In 2008 he founded side project Captain Loveguns with brother Danny Young, Robby Miller & Fergus Shipman. Having developed a keen interest in music business, Matt co-founded independent rock & metal label Metal Dwarf Music in September 2010 with Kris Leech. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Matthew+Young">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
