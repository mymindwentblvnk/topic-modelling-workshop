Scream at the Sun is a Minneapolis based metal band with its feet in many aspects of the metal genre.  The bands many influences darkly intrigue and pull at every corner of the mind.  Evoking shadows from your Peripheral vision, and melodically tantalising your sub conscious.  Their music is true metal for all. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scream+At+the+Sun">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
