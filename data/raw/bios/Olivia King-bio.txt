Born 1993 (April, 19) in United States Olivia is a singer and songwriter known for her unique combination of pop, soul and old-school dance music. She is also an entrepreneur who became the founder of the companies Make Music Moves and RaffleJunkiez. She was inspired musically by Lauryn Hill and Michael Jackson.
