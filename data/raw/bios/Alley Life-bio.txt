Alley Life is an American rapper from Detroit, Michigan most famous for his contribution "Out with the Old" in the video game Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2.

Alley's self-titled debut LP, the inaugural hip-hop release from Farmclub.com/Interscope, is a collection of percolating bass stabs, liquid rhymes and party-starting chants. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alley+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
