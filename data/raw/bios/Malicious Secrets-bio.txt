Black metal from Paris, Île-de-France / Marseille, Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur, France that formed in 1999.

They released a demo and an EP and also participated in the From the Entrails to the Dirt 4 ways split in 2005 together with Antaeus, Mütiilation and Deathspell Omega. The band split up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malicious+Secrets">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
