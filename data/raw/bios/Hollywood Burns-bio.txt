Emeric "Hollywood Burns" Levardon is an electronic music artist and composer from Paris, France.

Emeric Levardon is a freelance composer and video editor. He released his first single Californian Nightmare as Hollywood Burns in December 2015. His First Contact EP was released in June 2016, having been mastered by Volkor X. April 2018 saw the release of the album Invaders on the Blood Music label.
