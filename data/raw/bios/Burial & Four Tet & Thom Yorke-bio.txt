In March 2011, Burial, Four Tet, and Thom Yorke of Radiohead  teamed up to release a 12" vinyl containing "Ego" and "Mirror". Released on Text Records.

In late 2020 the three of them released a single on XL Recordings, 'Her Revolution' b/w 'His Rope'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burial+&+Four+Tet+&+Thom+Yorke">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
