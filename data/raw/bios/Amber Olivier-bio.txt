23-year-old singer-songwriter Amber Olivier is ready to leave her mark on the music scene. Hailing from North London, she’s often described as the magical, musical lovechild of Sade and Aaliyah but Olivier is purely her own — with a quintessentially ‘90s R&B sound that the songstress seamlessly infuses with her own personal elements and contemporaneity. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Amber+Olivier">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
