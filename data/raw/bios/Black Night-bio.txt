Black Night is a hard rock band formed in Lepoglava, Croatia in 2010. The original lineup consisted Hrvoje Funda, Nenad Šprem, Mihael Bračko and Karlo Žulić.

After forming a band Karlo žulić soon left, and then Nikola Zver took his role at the band. Additionally Sebastijan Marković came to band to play guitar.
