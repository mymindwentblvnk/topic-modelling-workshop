Formed from the talents of Hellbastard and Energetic Krusher members, Hellkrusher released several albums in 1991 and 1993 and more recently; releasing splits with bands like DISCLOSE, and also sharing the stage with bands like Doom, D.I.R.T. , Avskum, Extinction of Mankind and many more legends.
