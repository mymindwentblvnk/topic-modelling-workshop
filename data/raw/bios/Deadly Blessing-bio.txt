Deadly Blessing is a progressive power metal band from the United States. Their debut full-length album "Ascend from the Cauldron" was released in 1988. A compilation of old demo tapes and unreleased tracks, "Eye to the Past", was released in 2005. The band members were also in a doomy power metal band called Optimus Prime. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deadly+Blessing">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
