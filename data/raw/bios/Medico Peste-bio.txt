a black metal band from Kraków, Poland formed in 2010, involving the two current live musicians for Mgła and former members of Mord'A'Stigmata among others. Released a demo Graviora Manent in 2011, debut album א: Tremendum et Fascinatio in 2012 with Malignant Voices Records, EP Herzogian Darkness in 2017 with WTC Productions and second album ב: The Black Bile in 2020 with Season of Mist.
