Virginhate is a melodic death metal/thrash metal band from Tokyo, Japan. The band was formed in 2010 and ended in 2014. It was composed of Yuta "ytmn" Okabe on bass and vocals, Sawaken on guitar and vocals and Moyashi on drums. Sawaken has performed vocals for Kissing the Mirror and Moyashi and ytmn have performed with Clockworks Tracer. The official site can be accessed [url=http://[officialsite]virginhate.com/]here.

Discography:
The Struggle for Annihilation (2012 split with Sick of Nail)

Virginhate have also submitted several songs to compilation albums such as Barrage Am Ring and Extreme Hate Feast.
