Dinozauras is a band from Vilnius, Lithuania, formed in '09. Started out as a one-man cybergore sound, it shifted to more straightforward, groovy goregrind, with some sludge overtones leaking out. Dinozauras has 5 split albums and one full-lenght out to this date.
