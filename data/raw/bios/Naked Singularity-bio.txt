Naked Singularity is a rock band from the Washington, DC area.  They put out their first album of original music in 2013 and are currently working on their second.  You can find their music on http://naked-singularity.com

The band is: 
Kevin Boyce (bass, vocals)
Rob Gersten (drums, vocals)
Rich Kelley (lead guitar)
Maggie Masetti (rhythm guitar, vocals)
Joe Nigro (lead guitar, vocals)

Naked Singularity's influences (and favorite bands) include: The Beatles, Creedence Clearwater Rivival, The Byrds, Barenaked Ladies, Guster, and Kjetil Linnes. They've covered everything from surf music to 90s alt rock. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Naked+Singularity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
