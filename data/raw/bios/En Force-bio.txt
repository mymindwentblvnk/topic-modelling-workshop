There were several band that went by the name En Force.

1) En Force changed their name to Warchief is 2010!
En Force is a melodic death metal band from Chivilcoy, Argentina. Founded in 2007, the bands main Influences are death metal . In mid-2007 they recorded the first song 'Enemy Inside' which was widely accepted by fans. Currently they are looking for a record label to release their first album.

Nahuel Martiniello: guitars & vocals
Ezequiel Corrao: bass
Matias Navarro: drums

2) En Force was Power / Progressive Metal band from Baltimore, Maryland, United States.
