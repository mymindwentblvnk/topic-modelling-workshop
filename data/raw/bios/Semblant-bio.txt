Semblant is a Brazilian Gothic Metal band, formed in Curitiba in 2006.

:: Line-up ::


Sergio Mazul – vocals
J. Augusto – keyboards
Mizuho Lin – vocals
Sol Perez – guitars
Juliano Ribeiro – guitars
Thor Sikora – drums
