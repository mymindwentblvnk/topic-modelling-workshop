1. A hardcore punk band from Australia circa the 1980's.

2. Threshold of Pain is a metalcore band with their roots in mXm Antwerp. The band consisted of vocalist Helder Magno but after a period of time, he left again. The bassplayer took over his function as the leadsinger in search of a new one. Other bandmembers are guitarists Dennis and Nathan, drummer Kevin, and Alex (former member of Forgotten Glory) on the four-string bass. 

Starting out in the summer of 2006, Threshold of Pain went looking for a sound that would satisfy the needs of every hardcore-fan. For months, the band crafted their style, working their asses off to create the songs you hear today.
