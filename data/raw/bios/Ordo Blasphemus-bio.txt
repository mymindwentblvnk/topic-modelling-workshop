Ordo Blasphemus is a black metal band from Angers, Pays de la Loire, France. It was created in 2006. Lyrical themes include Satanism, Eulogy, Necromancy, Ancient Ones, Occult.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ordo+blasphemus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
