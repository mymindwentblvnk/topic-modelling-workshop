Porno Graffitti (ポルノグラフィティ, Poruno Gurafiti) is a Japanese two-piece rock band. The band got their name from the album Pornograffitti by the band Extreme. The members are Akihito Okano (vocals and guitars) and Haruichi Shindo (guitars and background vocals).

They came out with the song Apollo in 1999. Two single CDs Saudade and Agehachō both sold over a million copies in Japan. They are also well known for the song Melissa, used as the opening theme of the anime series Fullmetal Alchemist, in 2003.
