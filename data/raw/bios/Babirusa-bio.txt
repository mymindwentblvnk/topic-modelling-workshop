Babirusa is a 5-piece Technical Deathcore band from Brisbane, Queensland - Australia. Members: Rheese Peters (ex-A Night In Texas) - Vocals, Kyle Williams - Vocals, Dean White - Guitars, Tate Senhenn - Guitars, Rangi Barnes - Drums.
