Hailing from Kingston Upon Hull, in Yorkshire, England, Pastel Jack are a Metal band that deal in 
some old Skool thrash with NWOBHM flourishes.

The four piece formed in 2007 and have been touring hard and released their debut E.P "Ghost in the Machine", which was recorded in nearby Leeds at the Silent City Studios.
