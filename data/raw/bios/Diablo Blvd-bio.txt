Diablo Blvd. <==== Fix your tags ! 

 Diablo Blvd  , named after a  Corrosion of Conformity  song, was formed in  2005 , in Antwerp (Belgium).
The lead singer, Alex Agnew, is a well known stand-up comedian in Flanders. 
Their sold-out demo was called 'Scarred and Undefeated', their first album 'The Greater God' was in stores two years later.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Diablo+Blvd">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
