Akraganth is a BDM band from Mexico. Formed in 2007, they released two demo albums: "Declaration of War" in 2010 and "Human Degeneration Part 1" in 2012 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Akraganth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
