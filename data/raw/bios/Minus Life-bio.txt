Minus Life are a melodic death metal band from Brisbane, Australia. Featuring the vocals, guitar, bass, drums and keyboard. They recently despanded in 2006 after recording one album "Eternal Urban Megacide". Which featured the track "bodies on fire" inspired by the "bali bombings" which was always a big hit with the crowds. Most of the band went on to form Silver Ocean Storm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Minus+Life">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
