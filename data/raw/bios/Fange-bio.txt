Sludge band from Rennes, France, featuring members from Huata,  Pigeon,  Calvaiireand Brain Pyramid. 
Their first album available on Cold Dark Matter Records but they were then joined by Matthias, boss of Throatruiner Records, on vocals and started releasing their subsequent releases on Throatruiner Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fange">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
