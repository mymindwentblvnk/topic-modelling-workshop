Gholgoth is a black metal band from Szentes, Hungary since 1998. They released their only album, Somnus Moris Imago on Ordealis Records in 2004. Musically, they sound like Immortal, early Mayhem, and Enthroned.

Lineup:
Zoltán Molnár - Vocals
Tibor Kovács - Guitars (Hollowgram, Diafragma)
László Kliment - Guitars (Megazetor)
Zsolt Temesváry - Bass
Endre Vígh - Drums (Blood Rainbow, Tesstimony, Human Error)
