This heavy metal band is from Frankfurt am Main, Hesse, Germany.  They formed in 1980 and they were signed to Steamhammer.  Steamhammer released an album called Nighout in 1985.  Before that, they had a demo in 1983 called Heavy Rock and then they released a demo in 1988.  They disbanded, but it is unknown when they disbanded.  They recorded another album called Branded and it was released in 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Crack+Jaw">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
