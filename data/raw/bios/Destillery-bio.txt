An ultra heavy act founded in 1993 DESTILLERY are based in Marl, Germany. Added guitarist Daniel Hartelt in 1994 before enrolling erstwhile CRYPTIC VISION singer Florian Reimann. January of 1997 saw ex CRESTFALLEN guitarist Roland Smigerski inducted into the ranks.

The band bowed in with the 1997 mini album 'Interior Fire' issued as a private pressing. This would be followed up by the full length album 'Immortal Sun' released in January 1999 on the Iron Glory label.
