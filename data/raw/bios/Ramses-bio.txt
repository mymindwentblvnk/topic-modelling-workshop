There are multiple artists using this name:

1. A progressive rock band formed in Hannover, Germany in 1972, whose music was in a similar vein to Eloy, Grobschnitt & Novalis.

2. A traditional heavy metal band from Mexico City, who appeared on the first Escuadrón Metálico compilation in 1986 & issued three albums before disbanding.

3. A heavy metal band from Affligem, Belgium, extant during the '90s.

4. A hip-hop artist from Austria.

5. A hip-hop artist from Finland.
