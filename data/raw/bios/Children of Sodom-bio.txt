Children of Sodom is a German extreme metal band from Meinersen, Lower Saxony, founded in 2002. After releasing a demo and an EP, they released their only studio album,  Grind-Flower in 2003. The band remained active till 2004, and afterwards changed their name to Lana in Reverse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CHILDREN+OF+SODOM">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
