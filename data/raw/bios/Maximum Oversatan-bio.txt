Maximum Oversatan is a black/speed metal band from New Jersey formed in 2011. They released a five-track demo titled "Too Evil For Hell" in 2011 with three originals, a Sodom cover, and a Venom cover.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maximum+Oversatan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
