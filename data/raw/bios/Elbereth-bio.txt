Elbereth was a doom metal band from Barakaldo, Spain, founded in 1992. They initially played death doom metal but their style evolved into gothic doom metal.
In 1995 they released their only full-length, "... And Other Reasons". Melancholic music, featuring violin and female vocals that may resemble The Cranberries. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elbereth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
