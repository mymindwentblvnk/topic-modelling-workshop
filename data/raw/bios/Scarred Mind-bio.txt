Scarred Mind is a thrash metal band from Belgium, formed in 1999.
after several line-up changes, they are now thrashing with a solid line-up;

Chris Kaelen - Guitar
Bert Manderveld - Guitar
Tom L. - Bass, Vocals
Ruben - Drums

Former/past member(s) include;

Walter - Drums
Tweeva - Bass
Benny Janssens - Guitar
Jonhy Eyckmans - Vocals
