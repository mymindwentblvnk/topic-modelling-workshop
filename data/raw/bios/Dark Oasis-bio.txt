Dark Oasis was a death metal band from Chicago, Illinois. They released the 'Ode to the Dead' demo in 1995 and 'Lurking in the Darkness' EP in 1997 before splitting up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dark+Oasis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
