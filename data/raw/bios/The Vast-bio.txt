The Vast are a 'Alt Epic Rock' band based in Gloucestershire, UK. Consisting of four members, The Vast offer a unique fusion of modern rock and post rock with the more emotive side of the singer/songwriter genre in an aim to bring together melodic vocal hooks and epic instrumental sections to create an original and moving musical experience.

Originally founded in 2010 when two school friends (Jamie and Oli) who had collaborated on various projects before decided to create a band to combine their two song writing styles. They recruited friends James and Kris and after several months of rejected name ideas, The Vast was born.

The Vast are:

Jamie Giacomelli - Guitars/Vocals
Oliver Foster - Guitars/Vocals
Kristoffer Wheeler - Bass
