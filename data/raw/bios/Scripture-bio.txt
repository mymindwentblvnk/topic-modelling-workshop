Scripture is the solo project of Jean-Pierre Isaac, releasing the album "No Word Needed" in 1998.

Jean-Pierre Isaac (born January 5, 1956) in Belgium, Moved to Quebec (Canada) in 1961. Jean-Pierre Isaac, a French and English speaking Canadian, is a lyricist, Composer, programmer, DJ, Recording studio owner, located in Montreal's Plateau Mont-Royal and a Music Producer.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scripture">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
