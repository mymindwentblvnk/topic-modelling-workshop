There are two notable artists named linked to this profile, Masterpeace which is a melodic hard rock band and Master Peace who is an upcoming London artist who makes Indie Rock inspired Pop music.

Master Peace started out as a rapper who started doing grime sets on London based stations like Radar and has now started focusing on music that comes more from his own influences which he cites as being bands like Bastille, The 1975, and the Arctic Monkeys. Peace has recently released his track Overdrive which is seeing a lot of airplay here in the UK and is set to release an EP featuring the anticipated track 'PDA' and after that he will begin working on an album which he has said will include more rapping. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Masterpeace">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
