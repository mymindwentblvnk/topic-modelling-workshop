A UK Thrash band who released 2 demos, Break the Silence and Distant Screams in the late 80's/early 90's. They also had the song 'Nothing Room' featured on the compilation LP 'A Taste of Armageddon'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/WarpSpeed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
