(1) SHIELDS are an alt-pop five piece based, formed in early 2011, in Newcastle Upon-Tyne, United Kingdom. Their debut album "How Can We Fix This?" was released on 26th February 2016 on Kaleidoscope (Kaleido Music UK).

Previously, SHIELDS put out the "Kaleidoscope EP" which contained 6 tracks, released on Riverman Records Ltd.

SHIELDS are:
Luke Elgie
Tom Larthe de Langladure
John Martindale
Rich Sutton
David Williams
