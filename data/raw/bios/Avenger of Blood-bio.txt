Avenger of Blood is a thrash metal band from Las Vegas. They play Teutonic thrash similar to German bands Kreator and Sodom, as well as the Swedish band Hypnosia.

Avenger of Blood are in the process of signing with Heavy Artillery Records, and a 5-way split is expected to be released, on said record company, featuring 2 Avenger of Blood songs.

Avenger of Blood have released two albums, "Complete Annihilation" in 2005, and their sophomore album, "Death Brigade" in 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Avenger+of+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
