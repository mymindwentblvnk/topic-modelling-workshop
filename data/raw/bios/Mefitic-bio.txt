a black metal band from Italy that was formed in 2004. Presently signed up with Drakkar productions, their discography consists of - 

Rehearsal '06 (Demo, 2006)
Devouring Torment (Demo, 2007)
Paintime / Poisonous Transcendece (Split with Goatreich 666, 2008, with Drakkar prod.)
Signing the Servants of God (Demo, 2009, with Drakkar productions)
Misled Conjunction Of Evil (Split with Necrovomit, 2010, with Drakkar productions)
