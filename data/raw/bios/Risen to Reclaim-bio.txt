Risen To Reclaim is a five-piece metal band from Rijeka, Croatia. It was formed in January 2007, and started playing live a year later. They recorded their debut album Explicit Imagination at their home studio in 2011, and their second album Paradox in a more professional setting in 2014. The songs are riff-driven and melodic, combining both old and new influences to create their unique style. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Risen+to+Reclaim">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
