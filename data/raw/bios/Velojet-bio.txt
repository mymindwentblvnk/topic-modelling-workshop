Velojet is an Austrian indie rock band. Their style is influenced by music of the 60s like The Beatles or Beach Boys as well as jazz-music. In 2006 the band was nominated for the Amadeus Austrian Music Award.

In 2005 their self titled debut-album was released. The singles 'Your Side' and 'Something to believe in' gained much airplay by radio FM4 and gotv.

In June 2007 the band released their second album 'This quiet town'.
