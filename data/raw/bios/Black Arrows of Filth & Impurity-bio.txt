Hailing from Indianapolis Indiana, Black Arrows is an intense outfit combining grindcore, death metal, screeching vocals, and a myriad of beats fast enough to melt the skin off your face. Members include current and ex members of: The Dream is Dead, Worldeater and Demiricous among others. They released a demo in 2005 and their debut album, 1984(eternal) in 2006.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Black+Arrows+of+Filth+&+Impurity">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
