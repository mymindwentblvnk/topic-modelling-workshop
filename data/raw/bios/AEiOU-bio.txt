There are two bands named "Aeiou":

1) One is a Russian National Socialist Black Metal side project of WEWELSBURG members.

2) Aeiou is also the name of a project created by  Porter's  Juan Son and Blonde Redhead's Simone Pace.

3)  AEIOU is a Swiss band project around founder  Oli Kuster  (prog, keys, comp), together with Djemeia (vocals), Jibcae and Kevin Chesham (drums) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aeiou">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
