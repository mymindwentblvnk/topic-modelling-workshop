Flagellum Dei is a Black Metal band from Portugal. Flagellum Dei horde was created in the end of 1998 by Nefastus (vocals) and D. Sabaoth (guitar). In the beginning of 1999 Vulturius (bass guitar) and Byleth (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flagellum+Dei">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
