in October 2006 Bliksem started rehearsing.
For one year they wrote songs but it was very difficult to find the right person on vocals. But now they've found her and she rocks!
The overall sound reminds of bands such as Otep, and numerous other slaying metal bands, but Bliksem entices mostly with their killer live power, adrenaline pumping drums and schreding riffs carried out by the fierce vocals.

Members of Bliksem previously played in:
