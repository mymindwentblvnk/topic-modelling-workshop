Sewn Shut is a Grindcore band from Malmo, Sweden. The three-piece have released one EP and numerous split releases as well as one 2005 complilation album, "Rediscovering the Dead" on Selfmadegod Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sewn+Shut">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
