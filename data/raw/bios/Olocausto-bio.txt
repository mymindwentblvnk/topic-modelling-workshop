Olocausto is a goregrind / grindcore band , formed 2007 in Prato, Tuscany, Italy. Lineup features members of Compost, Trabecular Hepatocellular Carcinoma, W.I.A.P.W.A.C., Rawscum, Human Offal Concealment, Melted, PxMxSxZx, etc.
