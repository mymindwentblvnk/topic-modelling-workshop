Kartikeya is a Russian/Serbian/Canadian post-ethnic extreme metal band, which is heavily influenced by Indian culture, mythology and classical/folk music. The band was formed in Moscow, Russia in 2004.

Line-Up
Anton "Mars" Markov - Vocals 
Roman "Arsafes" Iskorostenskiy - Guitars, Clean Vocals, Ethnic Instruments
Chela Harper - Guitars
Alexandr Miroshnichenko- Bass, Violin
Alexander Smirnov - Drums
Dmitriy "Drevo" Sorokin - Percussion
