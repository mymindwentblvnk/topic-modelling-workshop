Telepathe (pronounced Telepathy) is a Brooklyn, New York based experimental noise pop group, who focus on digital percussion and electronica.

The group was initially formed in 2004 as an aside for Busy Gangnes (from prog-punk experimentalists Wikkid) and  Melissa Livaudais (fellow Wikkid and First Nation guitarist)

In 2007, they released Farewell Forest EP and Sinister Militia 12" through the Social Registry label. Followed by Chrome's On It EP and Devil's Trident EPs in 2008.

Their full-length debut Dance Mother was released in spring 2009, produced by TV on the Radio's Dave Sitek.
