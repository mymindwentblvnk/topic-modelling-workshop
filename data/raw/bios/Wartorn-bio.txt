There are a couple existing Wartorn bands:

One of them is a early 90's style crust/grindcore/power violence punk band consisting of members from Sore Throat, Iron Monkey, Ironside and many others from the UK.  

The other Wartorn bands exist within the United States, some still active and some defunct.  One War Torn (spelled with a space in between the name) is a death metal band from Virginia who formed in 2003 and released a full length in 2005.  Another Wartorn was a thrash metal band from Michigan who broke up and the other Wartorn was also a thrash metal band formed in the early 90's in Maryland but broke up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wartorn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
