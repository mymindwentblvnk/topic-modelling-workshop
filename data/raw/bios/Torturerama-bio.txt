Mid October '08:
Stof (ex-Solipsist, ex-Prejudice) and Raf (ex-Viggen) found young talented Dille (Predatoria) on guitars. The band "Torturerama" was born meanwhile first songs were composed.

March '09:
the band recruited Stefan (Ex-Inferis) on vocals and E.P. "Left as Remains" was recorded

Jan - May 2010:
The joining of Wouter Winters (ex- Signs of Darkness) on guitar
and the release of "Left as Remains"
