Vile Impregnation was started by Aleq Sheets as a side project from his main band Dismembered. The project was started in late 2008 and he released a demo that same year. His main influences are Devourment, Insidious Decrepancy, Viral Load, and Defeated Sanity. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Vile+Impregnation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
