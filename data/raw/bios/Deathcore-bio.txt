Deathcore was a death metal / grindcore band from Speyer, Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany and formed in 1986.  They released a demo called Revenge of the Unborn in 1987.  In 1990, they released their debut full-length album Spontaneous Underground.  Monobrow is their 2nd and final album.  It was released in 1994.  It is not known when they disbanded. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathcore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
