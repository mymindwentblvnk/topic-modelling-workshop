Joshua Burnside is a Northern Irish singer/songwriter and producer. He records all his own music at home, often experimenting with sample based loops and electronics, fused with the traditional sounds of Harp and banjo. His fingerpicking guitar style and poetic lyricism is often compared with the likes of Paul Simon. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Joshua+Burnside">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
