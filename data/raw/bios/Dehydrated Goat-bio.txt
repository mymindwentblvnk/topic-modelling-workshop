Dehydrated Goat is a Latvian grindcore / death metal band from Rīga, Latvia, formed in 2006.

Debut full-length album Genocide Against the Brain Cells spawned through Coyote Records in November 2010. 

Musically influenced by such bands as Cock and Ball Torture, Jig-Ai, Ahumado Granujo and similar ones. Instead of traditional gore / violence topics, lyrically dealing mostly with degeneration, filth of urban / consumer culture and standing against it with the help of nihilism and re-organization of one's value system.

Current members:

Reinis Pētersons - vocals;
Igors Čerņiks - guitars;
Tomass Beķeris - bass;
Filips Batarags - drums.
