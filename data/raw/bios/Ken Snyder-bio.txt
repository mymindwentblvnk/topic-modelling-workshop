There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Ken Snyder was a Christian singer in the 70s who released several albums that are no longer available, including his 1976 release "By Request Only", which became famous on the internet after being featured on several "Worst album covers" lists.


2) Ken Snyder is an american Shred/Neoclassical guitarist from Encico, California. He has released three albums: "One" - 2003, "Nevermore" - 2005, "Progressive by Nature" - 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ken+Snyder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
