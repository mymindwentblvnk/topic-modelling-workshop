Elijah's Tomb are a Christian Metal band from Grande Prairie in Canada, mixing traditional heavy metal with elements of thrash metal, death metal, metalcore and nu metal.

The band was founded in 1998; after several lineup changes the current line-up consists of Nathanael Sterr (vocals, guitar) Marcel Dyck (drums) and Melissa Sterr (bass, backing vocals).

2005 the power-trio recorded their first full-length album "Elijah's Tomb" which was released under their own label "Dovefire records".
