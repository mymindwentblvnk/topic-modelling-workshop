Grinding Death//Oakland, USA –drawing influences from Black Metal and Crust. Formed in 2011, the band released their debut self-titled Demo/EP on Buriedinhell Records in 2012 alongside the sophomore release, "This Black Earth" as a split 7 inch with Oblivionized from London. After a brief hiatus Plague Widow revealed a new EP titled “Despair” comprised of eight unreleased tracks recorded in 2015 on plaguewidow.bandcamp.com and pressed as a limited cassette tape. They have since announced they're currently in process of a new full-length album to be released on Willowtip Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Plague+Widow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
