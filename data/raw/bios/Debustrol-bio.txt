Czech thrash metal band formed in 1986, Mladá Boleslav, Czech Republic. Annoyed by music presented by czech medias these days (there were deep communist totalitarianism) formed guitarist and lead singer Kolinss with drummer Melmus and bass guitarist Charvos a band, whose music was hard to define. Kolinss described it as "ultra mosh". Now is Debustrol one of the most popular czech extreme metal bands. It's also famous because of using chain saw as an instrument.

Line-Up: 

Martin "Kolinss" Kolinský - vocals, guitar, chainsaw
Trifid - guitar
Zed - bass
Skull II - drums
