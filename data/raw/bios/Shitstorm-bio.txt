There are many Shitstorms out there.

1. Miami, Florida's Shitstorm is a 4 piece grindcore band playing in the old school Napalm Death/Terrorizer vein combined with elements of crust, hardcore, war metal and harsh noise. Together they create one of the most abrasive sounding bands ever. 

2. Bay Area, California: Formed in 2004 by guitarist Father Matilda Soulless and bassist Fetus McJesus, Shitstorm are a melodic gore metal band. The group also features Monsignor Recreational Abortion on vocals and Bishop Spumante on drums. They've played shows with groups such as Cannibal Corpse, Skitzo, and Obituary.

3. New York, NY: Shitstorm is a noise band from NYC.

4. Thrash metal based in Cape Town, South Africa
