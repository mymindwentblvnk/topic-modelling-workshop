Formerly known as Corpsegod and Enthralment, Corpsefucking Art is a brutal death metal band formed in 1995 in Rome, Italy. The Band Contains 5 Members Claudio Carmenini (vocals), Andrea Cipolla (guitar), Marco De Ritis (bass) and Marco Riccardi (Guitar). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Corpsefucking+Art">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
