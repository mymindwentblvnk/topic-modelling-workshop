One man south Korean metal artist (Jin-Su Bae) whom recorded a handful of obscure tracks in the early nineties  these rough recordings which would later be released by underground south Korean label Fallen-Angels titled "For This Addicting Pain" the only known "official" release by the artist to date with a run of limited psychical copies made.

The release can be listened through in full through fallen angels bandcamp page:
