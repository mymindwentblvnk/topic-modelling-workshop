There are at leas two bands using this name

1. Barabbas is a German doom metal band from Leipzig formed by 3 brothers  in 2005. They got their name from the Bible's criminal Barabbas who was left free instead of Jesus. Their album Golgotha is  inspired by the book Barabbas by Pär Lagerkvist which tells the tale of Barabbas seeing Jesus put on the cross in a place meant for him. The album is instrumental.

2. Barabbas is also a French doom metal band from Combs-la-Ville formed in 2007.
Line-up is:
