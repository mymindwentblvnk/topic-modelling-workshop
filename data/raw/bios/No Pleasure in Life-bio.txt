Depressive black metal from Jacksonville, Florida (USA).
It was created in 2009.
Members are S. (drums & vocals) and A. (guitars).
S. is also in  Ill-Tolerance, Ives, Talk Sick Earth, Vomikaust, ex-Septic Warfare.
Lyrical themes are depression and negativity.
