USBM From Texas by "Rood"

Rood is the creator of Goat of the Harvest Prod. - distribution, fanzine and compilation.
There was also a demo called "Ocean Stones" recorded in 1994 but it was never distributed.
In 1996 Creepfog recorded song "The Sleepwalker" for Goat of the Harvest Compilation.
