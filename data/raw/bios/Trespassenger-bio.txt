Trespassenger introduces fresh-sounding, dynamic metal music which is a combination of metal genre cross-section and various elements from other genres. This creates a unique style of music, which Trespassenger is known for.

During the past decade, Trespassenger has been active in studio publishing several promo records publishing them in various formats for people to hear. And yet more is coming…stay tuned!

The line-up of Trespassenger is:

Anssi Rissa: Lead guitars & vocals
Lasse Mäkelä: Guitars & background vocals
Raimo Manninen: Synthesizers
Tomi Latvala: Bass
