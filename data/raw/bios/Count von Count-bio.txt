Count von Count, often known simply as "the Count", is one of the Muppet characters on Sesame Street, performed by Jerry Nelson. The Count is a vampire modeled after Bela Lugosi's interpretation of Count Dracula. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Count+von+Count">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
