Tramal is a finnish melodic thrash metal group formed in 2006.  First demo was recorded in March 2008.

Line-up:

A. Härmä - vocals
J. Kanniainen - guitar
T. Pesälä - guitar
J. Koskiniemi - bass
W. Väyrynen - drums
