Romanian old school death metal band formed in 1992. Disbanded and reformed in 2010 with different line-up.

Original line-up:
Alin Comsa - Vocals, Guitar
George Stanciu - Guitar
Mircea Florea - Bass (Necrovile)
Cristian Zaharia - Drums, Vocals

Released 2 demos, Self Runaways (1995) and Malefactory (1996), and the song Thoughts (1997) for the Sometimes Death Is Better IV compilation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rotrave">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
