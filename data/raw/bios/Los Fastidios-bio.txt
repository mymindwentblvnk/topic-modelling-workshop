Oi! and Ska band from Verona, formed in 1991. Their music could be describe as melodic rock with ska rythms. Similar bands could be: Angelic Upstarts, Red London, Hard Skin, Banda Bassotti or The Clash. Lyrics come from the most cheerful street situations to more serious and involved matters which deal with social problems, working class, such as battles against any form of discrimination, for the animal rights, for freedom. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Los+Fastidios">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
