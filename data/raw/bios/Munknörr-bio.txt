Munknörr is a project of Nordic and Celtic music with Shamanic elements created by composer and producer Damián Schneider.

Aethelwyne an amazing singer from Greece its an important part for the project with her beautifully haunting voice.


About the word…Munknörr means “the ship of the mind” in old norse.
Seeking for a pagan sound with a dark and mystic vibe Munknörr´s compositions try to get closer to a natural primitive sound with various twists. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Munkn%C3%B6rr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
