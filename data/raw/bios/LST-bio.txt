LST (lets showtime) are a 6 member South Korean boy group under KN Media. They debuted in October of 2018 with the song ‘I Wanna Go’ as Fife members! In December of 2018 they added a new member. The members consist of SDK, Sunwoo, Jingu, J.N, Kihyun & Kibeom (new member) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/LST">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
