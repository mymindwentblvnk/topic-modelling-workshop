SmokeFishe is an electronic musician from Russia. He writes and releases music in the genres Future Garage, Downtempo, Chillstep, but recently began to experiment with other areas, such as Future Beats, Hybrid Trap, etc.
His musical journey began in 2014. He started as a beatmaker and wrote production for many Russian musicians. In 2015 I began to try myself in experimental electronics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SmokeFishe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
