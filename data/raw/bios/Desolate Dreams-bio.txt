BIO
Desolate Dreams is the project of musician Jake Woodward, from Edinburgh, Scotland. Encompassing many styles of progressive rock and metal, with some hints of fusion and other influences, Jake is just now releasing his debut EP, with influence from Dream Theater, Periphery, Tesseract, Symphony X and many more. Jake also studies music in Edinburgh, and plays with a variety of different musicians on other musical projects. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Desolate+Dreams">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
