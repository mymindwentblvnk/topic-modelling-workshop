1994 formed industrial metal /  cyperpunk band with some techno influences. Former members of Relic (Relication nowadays) Petri and Rami and "new" member Veijo  started to play some material from Relic in Finnish. However they have dozens songs of their own. Veivi  have done several demos and  gigs.   Current status of Veivi is offline, but maybe we will see them in future. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veivi">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
