Maltese Falcon were a Denmark  (Copenhagen) heavy metal band active in the 1980's. Forming in 1983, The band was named by Lars Ulrich (Metallica), as he was a friend of the band. The band released a stream of demos, before getting a deal to record a full-length, resulting in theirt best-known effort "Metal Rush" in 1984. Their bassist - Hasse "Hal" Patino has played in several well known acts including King Diamond, Force of Evil, Michael Denner, Blakk Totem, Palladion, Doctor Butcher, Disaster Peace.
