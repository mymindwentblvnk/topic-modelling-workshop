Bad Boat from Belfast born 2002. 2 demos 'Oblivion Switch' and 'Breathing Space' (DOTM (July 2005) METAL HAMMER). Best live, shared the stage with the likes of Orange Goblin, Hermano and Brant Bjork. The first album 2006's 'Blues Gone Black' is available at most online sites and the rare 2009 E.P 'Lonely Doom' is out on limited vinyl. 2nd LP 'Drown The Phoenix' is out 31 Oct 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bad+Boat">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
