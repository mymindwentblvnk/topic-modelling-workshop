There are multiple bands named Harlot.  Harlot is a metal band from San Marcos, Ca. as well as a 80's style metal band from Los Angeles, Ca.  The 80's band recently reunited and is touring again including shows at Rocklahoma 2009. They have a new CD "Keep the Dream Alive" available from Retrospect Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Harlot">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
