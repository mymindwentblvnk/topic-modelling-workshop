Bruma Obscura ("Dark Mist") is a portuguese band of atmospheric suicidal black metal. Their music is similar to others extreme black metal bands like Ancestral. They released in 2006 "Murmurios da Serra" ("Whispers from the Mountains"). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bruma+Obscura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
