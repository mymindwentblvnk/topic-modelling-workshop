Biotoxic Warfare is a thrash metal band from Heraklion, Crete.The band was founded on October 2012 with Ermis as the lead singer.A month later,Ermis left the band and Mike became the main vocalist.Since then,B.W have played a number of live concerts,not only in Crete,but in Athens as well,supporting legendary bands such as Suicidal Angels and Enforcer.They have also released their first E.P, "Baptized In Blood And Greed" on April 2013. In Septmber 2013, Antonis was replaced by Stelios in the rhythm guitar section. Also, in January 2014, Panagiotis became the new bass player of the band, replacing Manolis and the band started recording for their first full length album entitled "Lobotomized". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Biotoxic+Warfare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
