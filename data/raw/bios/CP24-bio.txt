CP24 is a Metal band from Cologne, Germany. 
Their sound is coined by a vital mixture of melodic parts and powerful rifftng, while the band never loses the focus on the song. Their musical roots are unmistakeably in Power-, Thrash-, and Progressive-Metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CP24">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
