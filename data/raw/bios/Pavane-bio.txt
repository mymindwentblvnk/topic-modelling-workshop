Born Jan. 4th/1981, solo singer-songwriter from Beijing/China, featuring Dream-pop/Shoegazing styled sound. Self-released an album "Beddibye" in 2004, plus a new EP "fine" in early 2005. And now going to university in Denmark, next release is still left unsure. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pavane">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
