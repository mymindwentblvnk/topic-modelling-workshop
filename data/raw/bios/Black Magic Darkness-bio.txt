Black Magic Darkness is Metal band from Colorado. Loud, heavy, melodic songs in the vein of Red Fang, The Sword, Baroness, and a touch of Thin Lizzy. Consisting of Cody Isaman (guitar), Beauvais Mann (bass), ex-ICK member Dustin Chapin (guitars, vox) and the elusive Gringo Starr (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Magic+Darkness">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
