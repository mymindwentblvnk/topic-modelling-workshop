This alcohol-fueled thrash metal outfit came about when a few friends working at a KFC decided to get together and jam. With first members in place, the writing began and soon came a second guitarist and drummer. The booze and creativity flowed... 
The result is a powerful onslaught of thrash metal! Reaching into past styles of metal has brought them much inspiration and skill. Things will only get faster, heavier and drunker! 
Inferus is Shane Leonard, Chris Dow, Eric Horton, Jordan Roberts and Dan Infantino. They are based in Welland, Ontario Canada. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inferus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
