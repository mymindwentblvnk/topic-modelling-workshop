1. Bong is a Hawkwind-worship band consisting of Mike Smith (Drums), Dave Terry (Bass), Mike Vest (Guitar) and Ben Freeth (Sitar).They have a self titled demo, as well as Bethmoora (EP), 7" split with Gnod, a 12" split with Quttinirpaaq as well as numerous live albums.

2. Bong is also an Icelandic electronic band. Its members are Móeiður Júníusdóttir and Eyþór Arnalds, who wrote most of the music together. They released one LP, called Release, and several singles, the most successful being Do You Remember. When the record didn't succeed as well as they had hoped, Eyþór went on to other things and Móeiður started a solo career.
