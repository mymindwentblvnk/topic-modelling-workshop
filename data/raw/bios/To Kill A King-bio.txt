Rock band from London, UK. The name of the band was taken from a line in Hamlet. As Ralph Pellymounter pointed out :"I liked the idea of Claudius killing King Hamlet by pouring poison into his ear – and how that relates to music.”
More information about them @ http://tokillaking.co.uk/
