Formed by Bone Awl’s Marco del Rio, Raspberry Bulbs’ debut LP "Nature Tries Again" was effectively a solo release. He expanded the project into a five-piece for 2013’s "Deformed Worship" (their first entry in the Blackest Ever Black catalogue), and in 2014 they cut "Privacy": an out-and-out masterpiece of bruising punk haunted and emboldened by visions of the uncanny. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Raspberry+Bulbs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
