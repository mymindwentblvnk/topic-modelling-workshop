Unkind (formerly Speedapple) was an influential Finnish hardcore punk band formed in 1997 in Helsinki, Finland.

They disbanded in 2020.

Members:

Tommi Mutka (Radien) - guitars, vocals;
Marko Heikura (Famine Year) - bass, vocals;
Saku Tamminen (Sink) - drums.

Former members:

Janne Mannio - vocals;
Ari Vienola - guitar (formerly bass);
Pasi Lehtinen (Dead in the Water) - guitar;
Perttu Glumerus - bass;
Toni Pihlaja - bass.
