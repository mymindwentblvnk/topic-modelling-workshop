PARiAh ThEORY is:
John McCauley - Lead Vocals
Jason Imes - Guitar
Jole Webb - Guitar, Backing Vocals
Bradley Torrison - Bass, Vocals
Rupert James - Percussion

PARiAh ThEORY formed in Tucson, and its members currently reside in Arizona and Oregon.

Former members:
Rob Ross - Rhythm guitar
Glenn Kurz - Bass

It was rumored in late 2020 that PARiAh ThEORY are in the beginning stages of recording a second album, which will be tracked entirely remotely.  No live performances are scheduled at this time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/PARiAh+ThEORY">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
