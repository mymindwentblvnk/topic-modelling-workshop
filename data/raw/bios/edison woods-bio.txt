Edison Woods has haunted stages from New York to Spain since 2000. The ensemble has released three albums: their self-titled debut (2001), Seven Principles of Leave No Trace (2003), and Nest of Machines (2006). Edison Woods has also scored performances and films for various artists including director/choreographer Katie Workum, dancer/choreographer Niki Juralewicz, and artist/filmmaker Mimmo Paladino. 

Currently, Edison Woods is releasing a new song every month for a series called ''The Wishbook Singles''.  Please visit the Edison Woods website and sign up for the band's email list to receive monthly announcements.
