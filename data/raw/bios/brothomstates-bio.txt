Previously under the name Dune, Lassi Pekka Nikko contributed to the demoscene in a group called Orange. He is one of the most influential Finnish demoscene musicians. Check pouet.net and scene.org for earlier works. (As these songs are in tracker format, the better your module player, the better they'll sound.)
