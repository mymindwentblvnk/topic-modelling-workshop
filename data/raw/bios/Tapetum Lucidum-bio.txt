Tapetum Lucidum is a metalband hailing from Belgium. The band described it's full-length debut 'Machteloos Bestaan' as 'a daring, experimental and progressive mix of black metal and doom metal, with influences from neoclassical music and jazz; combined with a crystalclear production'.
