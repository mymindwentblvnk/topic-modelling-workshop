Solon (born Øystein Sørensen 23 July 1964) is a Norwegian singer/songwriter. Released his first EP in 2002 ”The first day”, and an album in 2003, “It will still be there”.  The single “Vente på sola” (2012), "A good time to remmeber"(2014) and "Vinterpust" (2015) 
He has since the beginning of the 90s, performed and produced performances that mix music, dance, video and poetry. His latest album is "Regnet in Buenos Aires".(2017) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Solon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
