7Zuma7 was a stoner rock band from Eindhoven, The Netherlands, formed in 1995. Its original line-up was: Jerry van Eyck (vocals, guitar), John Peate (guitar), Nick Sanders (bass), and Jacco van Rooij (drums). In 1997 the band released a self-titled EP, featuring five songs. In 1999 followed the band's first and only LP, Deep Inside... That same year 7Zuma7 went through a couple of bass players until Miranda Van der Voort became their bassist. The band disbanded soon thereafter, in 2000. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/7Zuma7">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
