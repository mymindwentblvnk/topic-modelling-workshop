{{{ SUNSET }}}  is an American band from Austin, Texas. Their debut album, "Bright Blue Dream," was released on March 25 2008.The release of their sophomore album titled "The Glowing City" will be on July 5 2008. The band consists of Bill Baird on vocals, electric piano, guitar,and looping pedal. John Kolar on drums, Sam Miller on hazy guitar, Willis McClung on bass,  Will Patterson on Electric piano and organ, Michael Bain on moog synthesizer and Michael Baird on electric piano. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/%7B%7B%7B+SUNSET+%7D%7D%7D">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
