Mavi Phoenix (Marlon Nader) is an artist from Austria. He begun producing, singing and rapping at just 11 years old. In that time, he has developed his own distinct style of music production. Since Fall 2019 Mavi has been outing himself as trans and since then is using male pronouns. On the 3rd of April 2020 he released his debut album "Boys Toys".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MAVI+PHOENIX">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
