Karen Kenedy is a female vocalist from  Colorado Springs, Colorado, US  whose original members of her band included the almost entire Jag Panzer band, of course, with another vocalist. Other members included Doug Riddle from Viktem. She did backing vocals on Jag Panzer's The Fourth Judgement (1997) on the last track Judgement Day and did the choirs on their subsequent album The Age of Mastery <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Karen+Kenedy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
