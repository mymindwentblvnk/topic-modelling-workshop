Hailing from France, Hate Supremacy were formed in 1999 and have so far released one demo and two albums (Under The Reign Of Armageddon and Only Pure Hate). In February 2004, the band became a one man project, with Anton handling all the instruments. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hate+Supremacy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
