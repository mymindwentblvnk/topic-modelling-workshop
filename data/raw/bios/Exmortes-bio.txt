A one-man Dutch black noise (raw black metal + noise) project from Goor, Overijssel, Netherlands, which was active from 1988 to 1991 (and from that year to 1992 under the name Rites of Eulesis). The man behind it, Jan List (aka Verpus), also played in the short-lived death metal band Gomorha. Discography:
