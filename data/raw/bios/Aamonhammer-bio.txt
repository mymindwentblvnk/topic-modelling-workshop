Early Brazilian black tinged thrash metal that formed in 1984. They made a demo entitled 'Funeral' in '87 and then a split with 3 other Brazilian bands entitled 'Warfare Noise II'. Commonly cited as an early influence to black metal. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aamonhammer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
