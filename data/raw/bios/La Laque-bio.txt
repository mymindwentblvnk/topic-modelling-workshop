La Laque (The Varnish) is a five-piece rock band centered in New York City, Brooklyn. The lyrics are in French though no one in the band is French. The girls in the band speak French but the boys do not. Devery is shy about singing in English. Despite the language barrier it's clear what music means.

La Laque released digitall EP via iTunes in 2007 called "La Laque - EP".

Devery - vocals
Leah Hayes - organ, vocals
Bradley Banks - bass
Michael Leviton - quitar, vocals
Ben Shapiro - drums
