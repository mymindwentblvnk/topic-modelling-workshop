Forth Wanderers is a rock band from Montclair, New Jersey. It is composed of singer Ava Trilling, guitarist and songwriter Ben Guterl (Ben Special), guitarist Duke Greene, bassist Noah Schifrin and drummer Zach Lorelli. Their debut 2013 Mahogany EP was followed by 2014's Tough Love LP. In 2016, they signed with Father/Daughter Records and released their Slop EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Forth+Wanderers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
