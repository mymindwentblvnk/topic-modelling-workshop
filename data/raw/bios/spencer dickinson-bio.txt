A collaboration album by Jon Spencer of the Jon Spencer Blues Explosion and the North Mississippi Allstars. The North Mississippi Allstars are a band led by brothers Cody and Luther Dickinson, the sons of Jim Dickinson, the legendary artist in the white blues scene. Jon met the group, they toured together, and now they have gathered up to create his album.
