Led by Fela Kuti's drummer Tony Allen; includes Cesar Anot on bass, Jean-Phi Dary on keys, and Doctor L on samples and loops.  Takes Kuti's afrobeat and strips it to its essentials. Much more drum oriented and jazzy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psyco+On+Da+Bus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
