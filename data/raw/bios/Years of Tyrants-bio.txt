Years of Tyrants is a technical deathcore band from Paris, France formed in 2008 featuring past and present members of Darkall Slaves, Rise of the Northstar, Insain, Drakkar and Resistance. They have released demo Weakening Humanity in 2009 and recorded a debut EP Leading the Blind year later, finally released in 2013 by Kaotoxin records.
