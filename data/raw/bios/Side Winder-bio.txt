Side Winder is a progressive trance project of Aleksandar Lazarevic, a DJ and producer from Serbia. His first release, the Prayer EP was released in 2011 on J00F Recordings. In 2012 followed a second release 7th Dimension, also on J00F Recordings. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Side+Winder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
