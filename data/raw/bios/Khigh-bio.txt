Formed in Los Angeles, California, KHIGH is a heavy rock band formed by 6-string virtuoso Kris, project leader and songwriter of the group filling the demanding role of guitarist and lead vocalist.

KHIGH released Wise Hedonist for Power Play Records in 2005, the follow-up Copylefted Rightovers in 2008 and appear on several compilations like Iron Force II published by Risestar Music. They're performing all over the United States and are currently distributed both digitally on Century Media, Amazon, CD Baby and in more than 3,500 stores worldwide.
