Mahoney is the name of more than one artist:
1) an alias of video game remixer Pex Tufvesson
2) eurodance act produced by A. Santori, DB One, and Flying Dutchman. They recorded the singles "Let's Go On Holiday" and "Soul Shaker Groove Maker"
