Dorian Lord was a heavy/power metal band form Tucson, Arizona. They feature heavy rhythms, high pitched vocals, group shout choruses, and shredding guitars. They stuck around long enough to release 1 full-length album on Silent Stream records titled "Keeper Of The Night" in 1995.
