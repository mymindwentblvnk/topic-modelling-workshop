There is more than one artist with this name:

1). Bloodnut is a stoner rock/sludge band formed Auckland, New Zealand at the beginning of 2012. All members have ginger hair, and most of their lyrics are centred around that theme.

In 2016 they released their debut album, Blues From The Red Sons. Facebook.

2) Bloodnut is a Scottish stoner rock band from Aberdeen, formed by some of the former members of Radio Lucifer. The band comprises of Justin on vocals and guitar, Pete on lead guitar, Oggy on drums and Robb on bass.

In April 2008 they were nominated for the Best Newcomer award at the annual Fudge Awards and won Best Band in Aberdeen 2008.
