Sub-hardcore style band from North Wales (formed Apr15), feat; Neil Crud (voc,gtr), Tracey Howarth (bass), Llion Broadbent (drms). 
Rel début EP ‘How Can You Die When You’re Already Dead’ (15.07.15 on link2wales) and also had that title track on the cover CD of Vive Le Rock magazine (Sep15). Got deported from USA on the eve of their tour to promote debut album 'The Crack Whores Of Betws Garmon'
