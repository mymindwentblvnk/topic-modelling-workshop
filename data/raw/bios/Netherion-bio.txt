Netherion is a Syrian Death Metal band from Damascus, Syria formed in 2009
Netherion means (The Ion of the Nether World)
Released a demo in 2011 (The Journey) 
Lyrical themes : Remorse, Hate, Revenge, Social Issues, Psychotic Tales, Horror.

Members are:

Gilbert (Vocals)
Nihility (Lead Guitar)
Mohammed Al-Shalaq (Rhythm Guitar)
Mohammad Alesseh (Bass)
