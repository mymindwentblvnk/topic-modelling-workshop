D.C. Lacroix was a Heavy Metal / Hard Rock band from USA.

Last known line-up:

Sylvie Lacroix - Lead Vocals, Rhythm Guitar 
Dan "DC" Christopherson - Lead Guitar, Backing Vocals 
Joe Dansereau - Guitar, Backing (Formerly Lead) Vocals
Ed Walker - Bass
Marty Temme - Drums

Former/past member(s):

Jeffrey Urbach - Drums, Vocals (Avant Garde)
Bill Covert - Bass
