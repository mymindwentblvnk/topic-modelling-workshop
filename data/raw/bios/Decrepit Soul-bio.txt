DECREPIT SOUL are an extreme bestial black/death band forged from extreme doom beginnings. 
Formed in 2010 it wasn't until 2012 that the band released 2 demo's and started to play live shows. Their energetic bestial performances earned them the title best local live band for 2012 by Melbourne radio show, Burning Bitumen.
DECREPIT SOUL  perform their brand of Black Art with a nod to originators like Beherit, Archgoat and Autopsy
