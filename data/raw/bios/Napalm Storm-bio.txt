Napalm Storm is a thrash metal band formed in Pedrola (Zaragoza, Spain) in July 2010 by the guitarists Michal Kacprzak and Fernando Tovar, and drummer Alejandro Duarte. Initially the project was called Napalm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Napalm+Storm">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
