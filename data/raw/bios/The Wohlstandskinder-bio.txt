The Wohlstandskinder were a pop-punk band from Overath, Germany. They were founded in November 1995 and existed until December 17th, 2005. Then they played their last concert ever in the "Live Music Hall" in Cologne. They are mostly known in the region around their hometown but can also be called successful on national level. In the ten years of their existence, they released six LPs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Wohlstandskinder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
