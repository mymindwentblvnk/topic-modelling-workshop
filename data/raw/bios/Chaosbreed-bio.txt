Chaosbreed are a Finnish metal supergroup containing members from Sentenced, Gandalf, Amorphis and Moonsorrow. Chaosbreed's brand of death metal pays homage to the early days of the style. Taneli Jarva and Esa Holopainen are no longer a part of the band.
