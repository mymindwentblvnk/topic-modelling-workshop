Hasszorn is a black metal band from Germany formed in 2006. They released an album, Tiefblicke, in 2008. The band has two members, Tim Ziegeler (voice and drums) and Beniamin Forti (Guitar and Bass). They are not signed to any record label.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hasszorn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
