Appalachian Terror Unit is a Huntington, West Virginia (USA) anarchist crust punk band, active since 2006. Members are: Sarah (vocals), Chris (guitar, vocals), Kris (drums), and Matt (bass). The band releases its music through the Profane Existence record label.
