Abserdo was a Philadelphia area crossover thrash band fronted by Keith Lynn, Rory Kobzina, Ryan Fitzpatrick, Christian Bach, and Dylan Zdanavage. They contain elements of a variety of genres, including thrash, death, crust, punk, and grind. Their debut album, "Raising a Pervert" was released on June 1st 2010 on Horror Pain Gore Death Productions. The group self-released 7" EP "Sweet Baby Eagles Coop" in August 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Abserdo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
