There are mutliple artists using this name.

1. Wilder are a indie/electronic band formed in Bristol, England in 2009. They are signed to Rough Trade Records and have just come off tour with Julian Casablancas. Their debut album is due out in the third quarter of 2010. Wilder is also the name of a Heavy Metal band from Springfield Mo U.S.A.

2. Wilder are a heavy metal/hard rock band active during the late 1980s and early 1990s. They released three full length albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wilder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
