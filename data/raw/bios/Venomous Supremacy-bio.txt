Venomous Supremacy is a Blackened Brutal Death Metal band from Houston formed in 2009. Their name is possibly derived from the lyrics of Scorned by Vital Remains from Icons Of Evil. Inspirations come from various Black/Death Metal genres and bands.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Venomous+Supremacy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
