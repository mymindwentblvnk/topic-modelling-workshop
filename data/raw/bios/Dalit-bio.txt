In the western part of Norway winter 2006, Dalit was formed with one sole purpose: Striking the raw nerves of the listener with a portrait of the abused, oppressed and derelict souls. The name Dalit comes from the Hindu word for the "untouchables" of the Indian caste system. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dalit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
