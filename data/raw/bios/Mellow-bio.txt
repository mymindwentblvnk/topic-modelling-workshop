Mellow is a name used for 5 different bands ( a French band, a Japanese band, a German band and an Israeli band). 

Mellow from France is a euro-lounge pop band named in honor of the Mellotron keyboard.  They are known for their work in the Emerica skate video called This Is Skateboarding. They were also enlisted by Roman Coppola for the soundtrack of his motion picture, CQ. They have releases on Emperor Norton records as well as French label Atmospheriques.

The second one メロウ is a Japanese indie-visual kei band formed by 5 members, vo. 康和 [yasukazu]( Sid roadies ) gu. 竜介 [ryuusuke] ( Kra roadies ) gu. 和将 [kazumasa] ba. 文弥 [fumi] ( shulla roadies ) and dr. ヒデキ [hideki] ( mucc roadies ). 

The third one (aka. [mellow]) is a brit-pop trio from Heidelberg, Germany formed in 1999.  Free tracks can be downloaded at their offical website, www.mellowonline.de. 

The forth one is a Blues-rock from Hod Hasharon, Israel. they have published one song called "Route 20".

The Fifth one is an alternative band from Georgia.
