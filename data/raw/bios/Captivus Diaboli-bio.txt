Captivus Diaboli is the Metal band from Italy (Naples, Campania). The band formed in 1996 with 6 members untill they disband. They don't sign with any label.

Their only one full-length named "The Colours of Silence" which contains 10 tracks. "The Colours of Silence" released in Nov. 2002. Beside, they have 2 demos in 1999 and 2002. In this album, Captivus Diaboli played a mixed sounds of Melodic Death Metal with some Black Metal atmosphere and Progressive elements. Their music most Influenced of Dark Tranquillity.

Last known line-up of this band are:

Romano Monero - Vocals, Guitar
Dino Vicedomini - Guitar
Alessandro Uccello - Guitar
Emanuele Ammendola - Bass
Ilenia Vicedomini - Keyboards
