Fitz Ambro$e is a Tokyo based producer originally hailing from Cape Breton Island in remote eastern Canada. A beat maker with deep roots in 80’s Soul/Funk & 90’s Hip-Hop/R&B, Fitz creates stripped-down Hip-Hop filled with various intricacies and unorthodox progressions. Swaggering, popping grooves gel each minimal production together to form tracks busting with trademarked flavor and culture. Throughout his career connecting with a diverse range of collaborators and supporters including Tokyo’s Jazzy Sport crew. Fitz has crafted a uniquely modern sound with a deep individual vision. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fitz+Ambro$e">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
