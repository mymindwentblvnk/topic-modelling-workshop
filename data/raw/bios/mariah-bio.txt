Multiple artists under this name.

1) Mariah is a Japanese band founded around saxophonist, Yasuaki Shimizu. The group would first release, "Mariah Project" before signing to King Records and releasing five albums between 1980-1983.

2) Mariah is an American rock band that would release one self-titled album, Mariah in 1975 before breaking apart. Notably beginning the success of the lead singer, Frankie Sullivan who later joined Survivor. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mariah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
