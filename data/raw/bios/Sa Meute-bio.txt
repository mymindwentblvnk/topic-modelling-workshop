Sa Meute is a project from France with black metal influences.

HISTORY
-------
Coagulated in 2002 from the ashes of late MIRCALLA .1995-1996. They dissolved in 2006.

GEOGRAPHY
---------
In the French Pyrenees Mountains, Sa Meute haunted any kind of abandoned places,
 military training camp, 'Joffre' concentration camp, disused sheds, & decrepit houses with a power supplier, a 800 W sono, and candles.

INFLUENCES
----------
Thor
