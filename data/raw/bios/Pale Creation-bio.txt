Haunting metalcore from Cleveland, Ohio, Pale Creation blend atmospheric beauty with absolute brutal force. While experimenting in all styles of extreme music, Pale Creation have been compared to Cleveland's forefathers of the genre, Integrity and Ringworm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pale+Creation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
