Alice Suki Waterhouse is an English model, actress and entrepreneur. She was born in Hammersmith, London, England 5th January 1992. Her first track ‘Stoned’ was recorded in 2009. She has also appeared on the covers of British, Korean, Thai, Taiwanese and Turkish Vogue. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Suki+Waterhouse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
