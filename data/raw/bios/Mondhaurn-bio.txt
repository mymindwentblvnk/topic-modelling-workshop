Mondhaurn is a Black Metal / Experimental band started in the summer of 2009 by Marius. After years of misdirection Marius has decided to form a project that is more darker in tone and message than any other previous recordings.
Combined with elements of Black Metal and Experimental sounds Mondhaurn is a creative outlet for experimentation, expression and emotion.
