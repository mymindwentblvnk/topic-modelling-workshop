inthelittlewood is the YouTube name of Martyn Littlewood (born 1990). Martyn was a radio host at Fly FM, Galaxy FM and Nottingham Trent University Radio as well as many other radio stations, and has one several awards, such as "Best Newcomer" in his home country of England. Martyn still hosts an internet radio show called "The Wengles Show", found at http://www.wengles.com. In 2008 he started making YouTube videos, mostly song parodies at the beginning. After a while he started doing commentaries of games such as Minecraft and Limbo. His fame skyrocketed when he did a Minecraft themed parody of Lady Gaga's "Born this Way". His song was called "Form this Way" and was featured on the Yogscast, one of the most popular gaming commentary shows on YouTube. Around June 2012, Martyn started YouTube full time. He moved to Bristol to work for the Yogscast at YogTowers as a content creator on August 31, 2012 and still works for the Yogscast to this day.

He is currently dating Stephanie Louise, or "KaeyiDream" as she is more commonly known. On November 28th 2013, the couple moved in together after 10 months of being together and started vlogging (Video Blogging) their lives on their shared channel, InTheLittleDream (appropriately named).
