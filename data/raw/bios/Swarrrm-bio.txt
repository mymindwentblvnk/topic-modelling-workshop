"SWARRRM" are an experimental grindcore band from Kobe, Japan, sometimes incorporating elements of screamo, post-hardcore, power violence, and even rock.  They are currently signed to  "Long Legs Long Arms" and release their material through Bandcamp:https://longlegslongarms.bandcamp.com/music

Members:
Vocals. Tukasa (2007-Present) (ex-Force, ex-Hellchild)

Guitar. Kapo (ex-D.E.A.D)

Bass. Futoshi (ex-D.E.A.D)

Drums. Chowrow

Former Members:
Vocals. Hatada
Vocals. Hidaka
Vocals. Singen
