MW&DWG is a band from Odesa, Ukraine, tending to mix dark noire sounds with an atmosphere of stoner doom adding some blues shade on top of it. The band was formed in 2013 and now preparing to release their first record. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mother+Witch+&+Dead+Water+Ghosts">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
