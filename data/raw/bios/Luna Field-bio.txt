Luna Field is a blackened death metal band from Germany that formed in 1998. Luna Field is made up of:

Alexander Palma - Bass
Benny Rakidzija - Vocals
Tomasz Janiszewski - Drums
Heiko Krahl - Guitars
Marko Sirac - Guitar

They used to have Daniel Sandvoss on drums but he left and was replaced with Tomasz Janiszewski. 

So far Luna Field have released two albums:
Close to Prime (2003)
