Kataplexia is a brutal death metal From Helsinki, Finland. They formed in 2003 and have released three full-length albums since their arrival; "Morgue's Reality" in 2003, "Catastrophic Scenes" in 2005 and "Supreme Authority". The band's sound is highly comparable to technical brutal death metal legends Suffocation but Kataplexia focus less on technicality and more brutality.
