There are multiple bands using the name Medea:

1) A Dutch Casual Silence keyboard player Henry Meeuws who has released two symphonic metal albums so far: "Individual Unique" (2003) and "Room XVII" (2006)

2) A four piece doom / sludge outfit from Little Rock, Arkansas.

3) A gothic/black metal act from Belarus with two albums currently: 2002 - Requiem and 2008 - Dekadans

4) A four piece crossover band from Stockholm, Sweden. Founded in 2011 and released their debut EP "Wolves Night" in November, 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Medea">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
