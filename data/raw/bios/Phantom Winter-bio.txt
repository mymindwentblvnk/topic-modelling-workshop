Phantom Winter is a band formed in 2014 in Würzburg, Germany, by Andreas Schmittfull and Christof Rath – ex-members of Omega Massif. To date, they released the following albums: Cvlt (2015), Sundown Pleasures (2016), and Into Dark Science (2018). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phantom+Winter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
