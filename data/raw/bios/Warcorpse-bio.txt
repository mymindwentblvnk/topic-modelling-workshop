Totally untalented one man D-beat raw crust punk project from Greece, using the d-beat crust noise as a vehicle to express inner fears, thoughts, anger and wrath if is possibe in a few slogan-like lyric lines. Totally DIY home made shit offered for free in bandcamp. Influenced by Discharge, Anti Cimex, Doom, early Extreme Noise Terror, Bombanfall, Disclose, Dischange, Hiatus, Discard, Warvictims, Frigora, Disprove, Final Bloodbath, Besthoven.
