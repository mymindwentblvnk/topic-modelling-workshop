Nyasism is a band from Valkeakoski, Finland. Only the vocalist lives in Toijala, near by Valkeakoski. They play alternative punkrock/metal. This band has played several years, but they threw their ex-vocalist man away and replaced the missing singer with a new young woman. They have trained with the new singer since january 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nyasism">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
