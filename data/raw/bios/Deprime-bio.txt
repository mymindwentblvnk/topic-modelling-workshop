DePrime is a alternative rock, noise rock, punk band from Lakeland, Florida which formed in 2017, with their first single coming out in 2018. The current line-up is a 3 piece including drummer Jon Yorio, guitarist and lead vocalist Kevin Giston and bassist Nick Allison. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deprime">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
