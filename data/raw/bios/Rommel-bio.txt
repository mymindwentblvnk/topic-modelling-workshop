Rommel was a Speed/thrash metal band from Tokyo, Japan. Among several other Japanese thrash metal bands like Rosenfeld and Mein Kampf, Rommel had a Nazi-image for shock value. Rommel released three limited pressed and hard to find demotapes. They disbanded in late 1988. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rommel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
