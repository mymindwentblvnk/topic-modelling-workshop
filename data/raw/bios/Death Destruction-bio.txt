Death Destruction is a new superunit metalband from Gothenburg, Sweden.

The band consist of vocalist Jimmie Strimell (Dead by April) guitarist Henrik Danhage (ex-Evergrey), bassist Fredrik Larsson (Hammerfall) and drummer Jonas Ekdahl (ex-Evergrey).

Death Destruction released their self titled debut album "Death Destruction" in August 2011 on Sony Music Entertainment.
