Sybaritic – sih-bar-uh-tick - adj. marked by a hedonistic lifestyle of self-indulgence and material excess

Hailing from Atlanta Georgia, Sybaritic’s speed-intensive style combines powerful riffs, melodic solos and insightful lyrics focusing on such diverse topics as personal integrity, abuse of power, and the futility of war.

Sybaritic recently released their first full length LP, Being Human, and is currently performing live throughout the Southeast.  Sybaritic has been privileged to share the stage with such international acts as Suffocation, Moonspell, Katatonia, Immolation, and Lust of Decay.
