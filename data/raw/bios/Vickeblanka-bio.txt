Vicke Blanka (ビッケブランカ) is a singer from Aichi Prefecture in Japan. He has a falsetto voice and likes to play piano and pop songs. He won an event called Minami Wheel in 2012 with his song, Wake up sweetheart. He released his first single, 'Ou Boy', with No Big Deal Records.
