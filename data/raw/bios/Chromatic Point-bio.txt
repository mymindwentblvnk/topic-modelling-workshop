Chromatic Point  is an instrumental progressive metal band from Macedonia, formed around 2010 / 2011. 
Abundantly inspired by the Macedonian folklore and rhythmically based on indivisible odd beats that are specific in the Macedonian region, their music is a fusion of many different genres such as blues, jazz, metal, folk etc.
Chromatic Point have self-released their first album Zlaten Presek ("Златен пресек") in 2012.
The band is currently recording the material for their second album.

Line-up:

Alen Spasevski (Alien) - Bass
