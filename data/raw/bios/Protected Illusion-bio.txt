Finnish Death/Thrash formed in Vantaa, Finland in 1988. The band released one tape full-length album, two EP's and some demos, later in collaboration with Timo Tolkki from Stratovarius. They are now split-up.

All their releases are available to free download at their website. There is some pictures too.
