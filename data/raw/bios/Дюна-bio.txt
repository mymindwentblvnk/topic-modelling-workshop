Duna (or Дюна in Cyrillic) is a Soviet-Russian pop band founded in 1987, based in Dolgoprudniy (Долгопрудный), Russia. Popular for it's humoristic song lyrics. Current members of the band are Victor Ribin (Виктор Рыбин), Michael Dulski (Михаил Дульский), Andrey Apuhtin (Андрей Апухтин), Renat Sharibzhanov (Ренат Шарибжанов), Igor Plyaskin (Игорь Пляскин), Michael Udin (Михаил Юдин). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/%D0%94%D1%8E%D0%BD%D0%B0">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
