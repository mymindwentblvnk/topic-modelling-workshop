Convulse is a death metal band from Nokia, Finland formed in 1990. They released two albums "World Without God" in 1991 and "Reflections" in 1994 which took a more "death n roll" approach, they split shortly after releasing this album and then reformed in 2010 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Convulse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
