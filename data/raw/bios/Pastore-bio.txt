After great contribution to Brazilian Heavy Metal singing in bands like: Titanio, Acid Storm (Brazilian Prog Metal precursors), Opera's Noise, Tailgunners, Sacred Sinner, Hamlet's Project (Brazilian Bands Collection) and recently Delpht.

The experienced e renowned singer Mario Pastore presents his new project: Pastore.

This project that takes his name shows a musicality focused on an actual Heavy Metal style, with great creativity and regards to compositions and riffs, showing the versatility of Pastore's voice that synchronizes power and melody.

The members are:
Mario Pastore, vocals.
Raphael Gazal, guitars.
Fabio Buitvidas, drums
