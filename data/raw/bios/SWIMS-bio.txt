There is more than one band with this name:

1) SWIMS is a two-piece, bass and drums instrumental rock duo from Sacramento, CA.  Featuring the unmatched 4-string bass tapping and strumming by Paul Slack coupled with the soulful, meaty drum beats of Mark Rocha.   

2) Swims (formerly The Swims) are a psych/indie/pop band based out of Scranton, PA. Members include:
Brian Langain - vocals, guitar, synth, percussion
Claire Connelly - drums, vocals, percussion
Phillip Reynolds Price - farfisa organ, synth, theremin, vocals, percussion, piano
