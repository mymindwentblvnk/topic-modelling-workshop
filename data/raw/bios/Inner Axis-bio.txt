Started out as Midgard in 1999, but changed their name to Inner Axis in 2008.    They released a few demos before their name change; their first full length album, as Inner Axis, was released in 2011, and it is titled "Into the Storm". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Inner+Axis">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
