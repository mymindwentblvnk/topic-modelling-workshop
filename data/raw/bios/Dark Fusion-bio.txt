Dark Fusion was born in the beginning of the 2010. The band had many concerts in Romania and Hungary. In the autumn of the 2012 the first album was released : “InFusion”. The sound is a fusion between industrial and death metal. 
On 2 Novermber 2013, the band will release the maxi-single ''Dark City'' that contains the songs ''Dark City'',''District13'' and the electro industrial remix of the first.
In 2015, Dark Fusion will release their second album.
Dark Fusion members:
Florin – vocals
Gabi – lead guitar
Alex - guitar
Ted - bass
Adam - keyboards
Teo - drums
