Unexpected is a death metal band from Netherlands (Rotterdam, South Holland). The band has two demos: "Tales of the Unexpected" (1991) and "Thoughts of Wisdom" (1993). Also they took part in a split "Vociferous & Machiavellian Hate" (1994) with Mythos & Expulsion & Mysthical. Currently the band is splitted up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Unexpected">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
