Ingrain is a Post-Black Metal band from Israel, founded in  March 2012.

The band released their debut EP, Aembers , in June 2015. The EP was recorded at Dark Day Light studio, Israel, in June 2013.

About a month after Aembers was released, the band was given a record-label deal by Chinese based record-label, Pest Records, to release  Aembers, probably via CD.

As of August of 2015, the band is said to be working on their debut full length release.
