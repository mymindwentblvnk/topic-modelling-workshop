Nuclear Tribunal was a Deathgrind/Thrash Metal band from Berlin, Germany. They formed in 1996 and released one album entitled, Attack of the Salami - Smokers from Venus before disbanding. After the split-up, R.O.D. moved to Australia, Hardy went to play with Spawn and Andreas "Andy" Grant played with Postmortem and then to Bonestorm. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nuclear+Tribunal">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
