Black Twilight was a gothic doom metal band from Parma, Italy, formed in 1995. They released the demos "The Dark End of a Smile"  and "My Scarlet Rose" (1997), and a full-length album titled "The Solitude of Being" (2000) and then split up.

Last known line-up:
Marco Massarenti - vocals, bass
Ivan Torelli - guitar
Roberto Bianchi - keyboards, backing vocals
