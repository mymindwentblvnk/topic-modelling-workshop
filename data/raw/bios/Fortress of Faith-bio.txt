Vanda Restye - vocals, keyboard; Alex Mezei - vocals, guitar; Claudiu Gal - bass; Ionita Mihai - drums; 
                    
The band was created in October 2005 by Mihai and other former members, but the history of the band really began in December when Alex and then Vanda joined in. During the spring and summer of 2006 the band worked at the new tracks that were composed mainly by Vanda but also by Alex . The first album, entitled "Birth" was recorded in October 2006. The actual bass player joined the band in November. On the 7th of december Fortress of Faith had it's first gig in opening for the band L.O.S.T..
