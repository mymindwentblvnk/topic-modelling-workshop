Demogoth were a short-lived death metal band formed in 1991 in Hudson, Florida. Their only release is the 1992 tape "Flesh Consumed". Their line-up consisted of John Ruff (bassist), Lance Sells (vocalist), Tony Hicks (drummer) and Pete Joseph (guitarist). The last two played in another death metal band named Bone Saw after Demogoth split-up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Demogoth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
