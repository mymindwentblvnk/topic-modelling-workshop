Seahag is a Sludge Metal band from Little Rock, AR. The band (formerly playing under the name of Locust Swarm) currently consists of Jeremy Moore and Metal Dave Grissom on guitar, Bob Rudder on drums, Red on bass, and Alan Short on vocals. They have one release (Our Presence Here Is In Vain) on Hell's War Records.
