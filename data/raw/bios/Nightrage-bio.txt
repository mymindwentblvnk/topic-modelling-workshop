NIGHTRAGE is a melodic Death metal band based in Thessaloniki and Uppsala. Nightrage was formed by the Greek guitarist duo of Marios Iliopoulos and Gus G. Nowadays half of the members live in Sweden. Nightrage has been around making crazy good metal music for several years and is a highly experienced band having played shows all over the world. Their 9th studio album "Abyss Rising" will be released via Despotz Records on February 18th, 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nightrage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
