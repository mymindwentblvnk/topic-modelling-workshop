Extreme Thrash Metal from Gothenburg, Sweden. Formed in 2006. Previously known as Necroticus. Released a demo called "Warmonger" in late 2006. In September 2007 they recorded their second demo named "Napalm". It was later remastered as well. In May 2008 their third demo, "Fracture", was released.

They decided to split up at June 2nd, 2008.

More information about the band can be seen at http://www.myspace.com/perfektor.
