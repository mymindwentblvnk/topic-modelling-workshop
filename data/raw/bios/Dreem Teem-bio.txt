Dreem Teem was a a trio of DJ's and occasional producers, all of whom entered London's club scene during the glory days of acid house in 1988-89: Timmi Magic (b. London; 23 August 1968), Mikee B (b. Kingston, Jamaica; 10 December 1967) and DJ Spoony (b. London; 27 June 1970). No releases are known beyond 2001.
