Roctum is a Finnish Hard Rock band from Hämeenlinna, Finland. Their music combines elements from rock, punk and heavy metal. They are famous/infamous for their elaborate live performances , which features outrageous costumes, stage prop, pyrotechnics and a weird show which combines elements of horror, theatrics and heavy metal/rock clichés.
