Bryndon Cook, who records as Starchild, describes his music as “Champion Music for the Heartbroken.” Drawing equally on electro and R&B, with silky sheets of synth cascading over rubbery bass lines, each song spikes lithe vocal melodies with an undeniable twinge of sadness.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Starchild+&+the+New+Romantic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
