Mule Skinner is a death metal/grindcore band.  They formed in Hammond, Louisiana in the year 1987.  They  relocated to New Orleans later on.  They have released one demo, two EP's, two full-length albums and one compilation.  They are currently signed with F.O.A.D. Records.  Mule Skinner currently consists of Ryan Ashmore (vocals), Michael Howes (guitars), Tony Salisbury (bass) and Todd Capiton (drums).  Past members include Joe Caper (vocals), Bobby Carney (vocals) and Damien Howes (drums).
