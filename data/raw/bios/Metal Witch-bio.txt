A heavy metal/power metal band formed in Wedel, Germany in 1985 that split up in 1987, and did not reunite until 1998. They released their first album "Ready to Burn" in 2002 and followed it up with "Risen From the Grave" in 2008. In 2004 they played as the opener of the 7th "Headbangers Open Air" festival in Brande Hörnerkirchen after winning the "Headbangers Ballroom bandbattle" in Hamburg. Just recently they put out a 12 track split with the band Vindicator from South Amherst, Ohio released on Slaney Records entitled "Outbreak of Metal Vol. 1"

Current Members:

Lorenz Hoppe - guitar
