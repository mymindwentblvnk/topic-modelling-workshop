A.M.Q.A. stands for Apple Maggot Quarantine Area. They hailed from Seattle, Washington. 

The band released one final track on the “Metal Ribbons” compilation in 1991, called "Sinister".

Discography:
Cats are Neat  EP, 1986
Mutant Cats From Hell Full-length, 1988
Senseless Death Split album, 1989
