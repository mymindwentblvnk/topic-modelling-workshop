Divide and Dissolve is an instrumental Doom metal band based in Melbourne, Australia, comprising saxophonist and guitarist Takiaya Reed (who is Tsalagi, one of the indigenous peoples of the southeasternwoodlands of the United States) and MƒÅori percussionist Sylvie Nehill. Utilising drums, guitar, saxophone and live effects, the duo creates music which is designed to empower Black & Indigenous people the world over and dismantle white supremacy. 

The duo's debut album, "Basic", was released in 2017. The album "Abomination" followed in 2018.
