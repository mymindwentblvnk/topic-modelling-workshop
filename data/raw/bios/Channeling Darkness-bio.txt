Channeling Darkness is a death/black metal band from La Verne, California, formerly known as Lordsburg. They are known as "space metal" by their fans because of their use of eerie keyboards and synth and their love for the universe and the cosmos. The band just released an ep titled "The Intelligent Redesign EP."
