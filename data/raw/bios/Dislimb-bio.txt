Dislimb is a Brutal death metal band from USA.

Formed in Ohio, USA in 2001 the band is currently signed to  Comatose Music label and have released 3 demos and one album to their name. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dislimb">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
