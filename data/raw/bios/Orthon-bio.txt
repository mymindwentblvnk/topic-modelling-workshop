Orthon is an extreme metal band in Hong Kong which is found in 2005. Jason and Ben Krimzon started the skeleton of the band in the year of 2002. Forno, Murk, Ronnie, and Bee join the band respectively. The death metal, symphonic black metal and thrash metal lead the fundamental Orthon's style.  Followed by various live gigs and compositions, the band had started to produce their record on 2008. The band participated in some significant live gigs like "the Undergrounds" live shows, and "Hong Kong Extreme Metal Festival" shows, which are held by HKEMO every year. Orthon finished the recording of the album finally in 2011.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Orthon">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
