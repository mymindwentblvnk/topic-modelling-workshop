ALIGHIERI is a melodic death metal band that was formed in Tracy California in late 2006 by a group of friends who love music. ALIGHIERI takes their name from Dante Alighieri. With Brandon Michaels on Guitar, Cohen DeYoung on drums, and Joe Flores Pulling Double duty on Keys and vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alighieri">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
