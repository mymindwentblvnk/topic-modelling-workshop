Little Wings is a musical project started in 1998 by Kyle Field in San Luis Obispo, CA. Field was born in Alabama in 1973, the son of a college football coach, but his family quickly moved to California where he grew up to love surfing. In the late 1990s, Field formed the band Little Wings, of which he is the only permanent member. In the past, the band has had members Adam Selzer on drums, Rob Kieswetter (aka Bobby Birdman)on keyboards, Mark Leece as bassist and various others. As Little Wings, Field has released over a dozen albums, under the Walking and independent K Records labels.
