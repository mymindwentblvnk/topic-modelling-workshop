Scab Maggot was a death/grind band from Gainesville, FL in the mid 90s. Founding member drummer/vocalist Josh Jetty went on to be in numerous other bands including; Vomit Spawn, Cursed Ruin, Queef Huffer, Dead Centre, Saturnine, Bowel Spasm and more. Guitarist Josh Townsend went on to play bass for the bands Impurity and Cystic Dysentery. The band released 1 demo tape that was later re-released on a split cdr with Vomit Spawn. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Scab+Maggot">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
