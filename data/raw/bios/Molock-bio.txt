Molock was the short parenthesis of few months in 2009 between the original three-member sub-band Molock.org and the four-member line-up definitively renamed Just A Ride.

The death of the homonymous website and the new found drummer were pretty huge changes for the band, that found little time for a couple of gigs and to record a self-made demo, Discontinuous Contents EP, before definitely changing their name to Just A Ride. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Molock">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
