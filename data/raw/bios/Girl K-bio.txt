GIRL K is an Indie Rock band from Chicago, Illinois. Weeks before the release of her debut album, "Sunflower Court", GIRL K began playing with a band. Since the album's release on October 6th 2017, this charming group has played out their fun and heartaching tunes in hopes of spreading a message of positivity and passion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Girl+K">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
