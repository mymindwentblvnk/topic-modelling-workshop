Writhing Afterbirth is a four-peice brutal slam band from southern new jersey that has played toegether since 2007. Recorded the 5 song demo you can find on their myspace. Myspace.com/writhingafterbirth. They still play shows almost once a month, and are recording a full-length in November 2009. Check them out and post a comment about them.
