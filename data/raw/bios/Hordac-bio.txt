Hordac is a modern melodic deathmetal band from bremen in northern germany.
Hordac is: Pete(vocals) Andy(drums) Timme(guitar) Chrisch(bass).
Their sound is unique but influences from "in flames" "dark tranquility" or "at the gates" can be heard.
