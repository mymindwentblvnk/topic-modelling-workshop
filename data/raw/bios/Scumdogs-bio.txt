Scumdogs are a 3-piece punk band from Royston, formed from the remians of Edelweiss pirates, a very similar band, and signed to indie label We Call It... 
They sound like a screamier version of Crass playing with the Dead Kennedys only a distinctly British, but non-BNP & anti-Nazi philosophy.
