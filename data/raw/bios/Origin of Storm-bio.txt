Founded in September 2004 in Istanbul, Turkey, Origin of Storm has been creating explosive, heavy and diverse music by culling influence from various genres. Facing several member changes, band has found its permanent members by additions of Ali Berke Hergünsel (2006) and Sinan Ugurdag (2007). After playing some gigs at local clubs and university festivals, Origin of Storm released its first EP called “Internal Measure of Decay” in June 2008. Their style can be described as; modern, catchy and aggressive metal.
