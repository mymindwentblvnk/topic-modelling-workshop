There is more than one artist with this name.

1) Ethere was a one man ambient black metal band from Alsace, France. Released a split with Glaser in 2006 and a self-titled album in 2007.

2) Ethere is a one man atmospheric black metal band from Belluno, Italy, formed in 2010. Released three demos in 2010: "Ethere", "Inni alla morte", "Ethere II"; and a split with Permixtio in 2011.
