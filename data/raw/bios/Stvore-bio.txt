Stvore is an Industrial Metal band from Moscow, Russia. The band was formed in 2008 by vocalist Paul Kirjenin and guitarist Dmitry Fedosenko. The current lineup consists of them, bassist Ilya Purchel, and drummer Roman Ovsyannikov.

The name "Stvore" is derived from the Eastern European title of John Carpenter's The Thing (1982), "STVOR". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stvore">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
