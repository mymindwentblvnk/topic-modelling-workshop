Phlocalyst, real name Michiel De Vleeschhouwer, is a Flemish producer working in Germany as a classical trumpet player. Next to playing orchestra and composing contemporary music, he has a passion for Jazz and Hip Hop, creating Lofi and Jazzhop beats on a regular basis. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Phlocalyst">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
