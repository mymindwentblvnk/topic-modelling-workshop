Negative Voice is a Russian death doom metal  band incorporating some black metal aesthetics into their music. Formed in 2000 in Moscow, Russia, they released the debut album "Infinite Dissonance" in 2013.
Currently signed to Depressive Illusions Records.

Members:
Konstantin P. - drums
Obscurus - guitars, vocals
