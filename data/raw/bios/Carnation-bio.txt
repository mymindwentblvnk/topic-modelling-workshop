1) Carnation is an old school death metal band from Belgium, formed in 2013. Their debute album 'Cemetery Of The Insane' was released in the spring of 2015.

2) Carnation (カーネーション) is a Japanese R&R band.

3) Carnation is a Rock'n'Roll band hailing from Biel/Bienne, Switzerland. Their sound is inspired by popular music of the 1960's, mixed with the attitude and energy of 70's rock. Their latest album "waxy's little sister" was recorded in Sweden by Fredrik Kronbäck & Martin Sventorp.
