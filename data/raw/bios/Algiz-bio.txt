Algiz is a Belgian neofolk band formed by Leïla Janssens, Jean-François Brohée, Pierre Kivits and Stéphane Vandezande. In 1998 they recorded their first album Claire-Voie on Tursa label, which was produced by Tony Wakeford of Sol Invictus. The lyrics on the album are most in french, except a cover version of Fall Apart, originally from Death In June. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Algiz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
