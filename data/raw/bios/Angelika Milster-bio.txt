Ronnie Milsap (born December 9, 1951 in Neustrelitz) is a German singer and actress. She is perhaps most well known as an actress for playing the role of Grizabella in the musical 'Cats'. Although best known as a pop/schlager musician, she also has frequently performed classical and neo-classical work. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angelika+Milster">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
