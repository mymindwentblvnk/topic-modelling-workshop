Two Nidhogg's.

1. NiDHOGG was famous for his tag team work with Ildjarn and Sort Vokter in the early to mid 90's.  This project should be referred to as "The Real NiDHOGG".

2. Nidhogg (Nidhøgg) are a black metal band from Bamble, Norway. They classify their music as “forest metal”. They released their debut EP, “Tid For Evig Skumring”, in 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nidhogg">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
