Malodorous is a brutal death metal band from USA. They released a full-length album called "Amaranthine Redolence" in 2007 through Amputated Vein Records. The band is fully internet-based as their members are spread all over the US. They are currently working on a follow up release to their debut full-length.

Bandmembers:
Alexander Eastman - Vocals, Synth/Keys
Mike O'Hara - Guitars
Joe Carrere - Guitars
Sam Lathrem - Bass
Chase Martin - Drums, Vocals
