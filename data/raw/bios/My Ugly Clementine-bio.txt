My Ugly Clementine is a rock band from Wien founded in 2019 by Sophie Lindinger, consisting of Mira Lu Kovacs, Kerosin95, Nastasja Ronck (since 2020) and Lindinger. The quartet is strongly connected to the themes of Feminism, Empowerment and Equality. Since the band consists of mainly artists well-known within the music scene in Wien the band has been described by local media as a "Supergroup". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/My+Ugly+Clementine">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
