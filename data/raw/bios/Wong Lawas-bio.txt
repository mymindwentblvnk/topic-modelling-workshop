WONG LAWAS is a traditional and melodic heavy metal band hailing from Indonesia. So far, they have released one longplayer: Trivium (2015).

The members are:

Noval Alkatiri:Bass
Bintang Wong:Drums
Waluyo:Guitars
Wiwit Wong:Guitars (lead)
Akma Wong:Keyboards
Wahab Wong:Vocals (male)
