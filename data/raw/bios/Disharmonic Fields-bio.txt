Disharmonic Fields are a Brazilian Power Metal band. Thier demo 'Beyond The Black Horizon' was released in 2001, and their first album 'Killing Hopes' was released in 2005. The current line up is:- 

Nelson Küster Jr. - Vocals
Sandro Dória Barbosa - Guitars
Bruno Monte Jorge - Guitars
Eloi Bilek - Bass
Andre Kisser - Drums
