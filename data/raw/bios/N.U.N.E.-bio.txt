N.U.N.E., the Compton CA emcee, released his debut lp “N.U.N.E.TIME” in June of 2006 through his own PYO Entertainment International label. Continuing the trend, he recently collaborated on a duo album with Shade Sheist (“Movin Units”) and also released the project under the PYO brand. If you are familiar with street albums, then you have probably heard of “Grand Theft Audio” or “GTA.” N.U.N.E. is the official creator of this popular street series that has won several awards in the last decade. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/N.U.N.E.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
