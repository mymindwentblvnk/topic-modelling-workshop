He started playing the Electric Guitar at the age of 14 years old. Few time later, he realized his wished to become a professional musician. Ever since then, he has dedicated all his time and effort to achieve that goal.

Counting with the advice of the best teachers available in the country, always in the form of private lessons, he learned Music Theory in depth, without sacrificing any aspect of his instrument’s execution. In time, he developed a flawless basic technique on both hands, and went further beyond…dominating the sweep picking, all kind of licks and tapings (including the polyphonic) harmonics, the use of the Slide and any other existent technique.

In addition, He play other related instruments, such as the Bronze string Acoustic/Electric Guitar, Classical Guitar, Electric coral Sitar, Oud and Keyboard. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jose+del+Rio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
