Nail Trails is a finnish metal band which have released two demos, "Grief" (January 2005) and "...fall into angels eyes" (September 2005). 

The band consists of Mika "Jose" Karpela (vocals), Mika Koivunoksa (guitars), Kim Eklund (bass) and Juha-Pekka Kaartinaho (drums).
