Bonnie X Clyde (stylized as BONNIE X CLYDE) is an American electronic dance duo formed in 2013 by Daniel Litman and Paige Lopynski. The duo originates from Fairfax, Virginia and is now based in Miami, Florida. The group fuses electropop, synthpop, house and trance music in their sound. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BONNIE+X+CLYDE">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
