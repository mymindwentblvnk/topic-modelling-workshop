About
Best known for her hit single "Yadadamean," she is a rapper with substantial followings on both YouTube and SoundCloud. Her following also extends into Instagram and Twitter.

Before Fame
She released a debut single called "Lean" in January of 2014.

Trivia
She studied at Temple University.

Family Life
She hails from San Jose, California.
