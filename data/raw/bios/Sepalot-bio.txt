Sepalot is a German beatmaker and producer, a prolific creator of hip-hop with a sprinkle of electro - or maybe electro with the soul of a hip-hop DJ, at times really nerdy, at times close to the main stream of pop.
With tours from Johannesburg to Sydney and from Moscow to the far ends of Bolivia, his sound feels at home worldwide. As a band member and producer of the group Blumentopf he released two Top Ten album, 2 Top Twenty albums and sold over 300 000 units.

For more inforamtion see http://www.sepalot.com.
