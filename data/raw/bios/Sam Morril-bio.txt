Sam Morril is an American stand-up comedian from New York City. He attended the Browning School in Manhattan's Upper East side. A former intern on The Colbert Report, Morril appeared on The Late Show with Stephen Colbert on Friday, April 22, 2016. Morril has made multiple appearances on Conan on TBS. He was formerly the host of People Talking Sports on MSG Network and is a regular at the Comedy Cellar in NYC. He has a guest appearance as himself on Todd Phillips'  Joker. His first one-hour special "Positive Influence" was released on Comedy Central in September 2018. His second one-hour special "I Got This" was released on the Comedy Central Stand-Up YouTube page in February 2020.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sam+Morril">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
