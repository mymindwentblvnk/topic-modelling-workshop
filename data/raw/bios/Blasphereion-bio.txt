Old School Death Metal band from Belgium which released one album, "Rest in Peace", one of the first Osmose Production releases (OP003) in 1992.

After the split vocalist/bassist Dan Vanderplas joined the Belgian Black Metal Horde Enthroned as Cernunnos on drums in 1993 until he commited suicide in 1997.  From 1998 another Blasphereion member, Fabrice Depireux also moved to Enthroned as Namroth Blackthorn to replace Dan on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blasphereion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
