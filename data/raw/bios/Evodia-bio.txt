EVODIA is an acoustic ambient experimental band from Tübingen, Germany.
It consists of Charles (Charles F. O’Neil / Piano & Vocals), Seba (Sebastian Goc / Gitarre) and Mitch (Michael Wiedmann / Gitarre, Bass & Violin) and was founded in 2010.

With the release of RECOORDINATE in 2016, they introduced themselves actively to the German music scene and convince audiences with the depth and high quality of their live shows and music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evodia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
