Cleveland, Ohio native Isam Mostafa, or stage name Doe Boy, is a rapper co-signed by Future, Wooh Da Kid, Lex Luger, Southside, Young Chop, etc. He has worked with Lex Luger on the mixtape "Boyz N The Hood" and with Lex Luger and Young Chop on "Boyz N The Hood 2."  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Doe+Boy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
