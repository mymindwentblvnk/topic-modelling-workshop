Abrage is a Trash/Groove from Jerusalem, Israel.
The band formed in 2006 and was influenced by the most successful bands in history such as Pantera, Machine Head, Lamb Of God and Meshuggah, soon they started to preform in different clubs around Israel and write a new matireal. In 2009 their first record, "Abrasive Rage EP" came out. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/abrage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
