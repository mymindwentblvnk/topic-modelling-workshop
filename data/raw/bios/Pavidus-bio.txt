Pavidus is a progressive death metal band from Findlay, Ohio. They have recently released a full length album, "Mascilating the Altered Illumination" on March 20, 2010. You can preview the album on their MySpace page and buy it at any Pavidus show. They released their first full length album "Aspirations of Osiris" Feburary 21, 2009.
