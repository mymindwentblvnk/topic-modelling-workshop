Chicago rapper Kobe Jxrdan is a special talent in saturated landscape which is Chicago rap. Kobe doesn’t consider himself drill or trap artist but just a “wrapper”. His comedic style and potent lyricism shines in his songs while you embrace the intensity of his spirit. His main influence in his music is his life and experiences. Affiliated with a plethora of talent such as Kendrick Lamar, Saba , Frsh Waters, Joseph Chilliams, and Mfn Melo , Kobe has a strong presence that’s being felt.
