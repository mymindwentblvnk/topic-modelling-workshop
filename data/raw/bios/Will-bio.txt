One of the many projects closely related to Front Line Assembly. More than a mere sideproject, Will took certain aspects of the FLA sound, replacing the ebm with martial rhythms, including neoclassical-esque strings and choral sections. This sound was to become a blueprint for Chris Peterson's later - and much noisier - project Decree. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Will">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
