Angkor is a Melodic Black Metal band from Argentina - formed in 1992. They are an ongoing independent act but, aren't always active members as normal, considering they've released two demos and one album. The 2007 album "Infectus I.N.R.I" is mainly typical Metal lyrics with themes of evil and horrors.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Angkor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
