PRIZM is a Dallas-based duo consisting of Krisluv and Danni James. The pair brings their experience from past collaborations to the PRIZM project, adding a professional and polished touch on their love letter to the 1980s. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/PRiZM">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
