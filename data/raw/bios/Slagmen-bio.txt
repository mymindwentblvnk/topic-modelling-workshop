Slagmen is a heavy metal band from Finland. The band's roots are in metal creation process, as some band members have actually worked as slagmen in a steel factory.

Slagmen has recorded six demos. The brand new EP Ironclad consists of 5 new Slagmen songs. Ironclad was released June 15th of 2013 in the gig at Raahe 3" Kuppila.
