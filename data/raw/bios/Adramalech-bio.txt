Status: Split-Up 2009-2012
Project Adramalech formed in January 2009 in Tbilisi, Georgia. 
Participated in the project Veliar (Guitars, Vocals, Keyboards) and Frantic (Bass, Programming).
But because of differences, Frantic left the project and Veliar Responsible for all tools and programming 

Music style of Adramalech is Black Metal / Ambient,
and the lyrics are connected with the Beauty of Nature and Depression. 
Adramalech began his journey with the EP album (Adramalech) which was released in May 2009...
