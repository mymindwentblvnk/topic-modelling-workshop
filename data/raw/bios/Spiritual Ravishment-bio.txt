Spiritual Ravishment is a band from Oradea, Romania, playing grindcore / death metal.
"definitely recommended if you wanna listen some metal music coming from the deep underground catacombs of Transylvania!" (Axa Valaha)
Spiritual Ravishment, the Transylvanian extreme death metal grindcore band, officially announces the release of their debut album "Children of the Atom" via the Romanian label Axa Valaha Productions. (2010)
Members:
Andrei Bejusca - guitar
Stancu Sebastian - vocals
Ali Said - drums
Lorand Deme - bass
