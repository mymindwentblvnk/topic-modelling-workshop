Turbo-Laser are an English heavy rock / metal band, comprising two men from Newbury in Berkshire. 

Formed in 1999, Turbo-Laser play hard rock and heavy metal.

Turbo-Laser released their debut album on 25th December 2010, you can find more information on this including how and where to purchase on their website here: www.myspace.com/turbo-laser
