Mika Vainio (May 15, 1963 - April 12, 2017) was a Finnish experimental electronic musician, known for his works with Ilpo Väisänen as Pan Sonic. Also known as Ø / Tekonivel / Kentolevi / Philus. Vainio and Väisänen also recorded with Alan Vega, (Suicide).

Mika Vainio died in in France, on April 12, 2017 at the age of 53.
