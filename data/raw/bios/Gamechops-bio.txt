GameChops is home to the world's top Video Game DJs. Video game music remixes and cover songs safe for use on Twitch and YouTube.

Founded by Dj CUTMAN in 2012, GameChops was one of the world's first record labels dedicated to video game music. Currently, GameChops focuses on legally licensed electronic covers of the world's greatest video game music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gamechops">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
