Death metal band from Venezuela. They released a demo called "Antropofagia" in 1996 and split up some time after. The band reformed in 2011, with only two of the original members. According to an interview, a new album was on the works.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CRITERIOS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
