Formed in 2004, Motorized originate from Paraguay, they are a deathrash metal band. They are currently independant on releasing all of their material. Their lyrical content covers Pain, Chaos and Horror. 

The band currently consists of:

Cesar Ortiz - Drums
Carlos Ramos - Guitars, Vocals
Javier del Valle - Bass

And they have released so far a demo and ep:

Land of Chaos                                  Demo, 2005
Dolor de Nadie (Nobody's Pain) EP, 2006
