Kiari Kendrell Cephus (born December 14, 1991), known professionally as Offset, is an American rapper who has released music both as a solo artist and as part of Migos, a rap group consisting of his cousin Quavo and Quavo's nephew Takeoff. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Offset">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
