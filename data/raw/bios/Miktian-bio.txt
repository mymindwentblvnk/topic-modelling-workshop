Miktian is the name of Atstec god of death, and this turned to be the name of one man band from snowy Russia, 
Style- depressive black/doom/shaman metal (the musician calls the style musta metalli)
Other projects of this man are: Alone in the Past, Astron, Glory of Misery, Gortaur Grusom.

The first Demo Flowers of Mysanthropy was released on 23 tapes but now they are not available. By the way, the cover contained the mistake - there was written Miktan instead of Miktian
