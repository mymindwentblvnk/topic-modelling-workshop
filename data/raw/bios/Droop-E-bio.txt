Droop-E (born Earl Stevens Jr. on February 18, 1988 in Vallejo, California) is a rapper and hip-hop producer. He is a member of the production team The Pharmaceuticals and the son of Bay Area rapper E-40. 

He has produced or co-produced over 100 released songs since he was 15 years old and worked with a who's who of California rap artists, including Ya Boy, E-40, Snoop Dog, The Umbrella, YukMouth, Turf Talk, Too $hort, Keak Da Sneak, Mistah F.A.B, Messy Marv , Mac Dre, and Mac Mall.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Droop-E">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
