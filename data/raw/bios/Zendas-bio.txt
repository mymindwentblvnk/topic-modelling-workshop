Zendas creates under this name since July 2001 in Barrancabermeja, Colombia.  Over the years the band has consolidated with each if its direct and different presentations, that way obtaining a professional sound.

Their music is a mixture between the overwhelming force of Heavy Rock and the classic style of Hard Rock that was done in the 80s and 90s having in mind the actual evolution of these genres.  Their lyrics are about Latin American Indians, War and Liberty.
