Toumani Diabate, (born June 26, 2002) better known as Smooky MarGielaa, is an American-Malian rapper from The Bronx, New York. Smooky is best known for his 2017 song “Stay 100”. He was cosigned by rapper A$AP Rocky with Smooky’s song “Out My Face”. Smooky was later a standout feature on A$AP Mob’s 2017 album Cozy Tapes Vol 2: Too Cozy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Smooky+MarGielaa">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
