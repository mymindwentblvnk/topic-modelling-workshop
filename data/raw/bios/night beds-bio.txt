Night Beds is a U.S. band.

Currently based in Nashville, Tennessee, the band was formed in Colorado Springs, Colorado, by  Matthew Wilcox and Winston Yellen; they have released three  EPs and an album, Country Sleep.  The current members are Winston Yellen, Ben Kaufman, Aly Holland, Ryan Ellis, Joe Hutchinson, and Taylor Zachry.
