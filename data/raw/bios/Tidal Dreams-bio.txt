Hellenic band coming from Athens, Greece, formed in 2011.
With classic heavy metal passion and epic attitude, they are now releasing their debut album masterpiece, "Once Upon A Tide".
Magical lyrics, aggressive themes, heavy riffs and solos... 
All with vocals that travel you back in time and let you flow with the tide !
The band has supported well known heavy metal bands from Strikelight to Heir Apparent and played to large venues such as Kyttaro Live Club.
