Until The Ribbon Breaks is an electronic project consisting of Cardiff songwriter, producer and vocalist Peter Lawrie Winfield, drummer Elliot Wall and James Gordon. Their debut EP, A Taste of Silver, is available for free download, and their follow-up, The Other Ones, is out now on iTunes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Until+the+Ribbon+Breaks">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
