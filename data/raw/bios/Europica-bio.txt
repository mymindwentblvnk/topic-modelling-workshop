Peter Romanowski is the man behind Europica, a bass player from the UK. Having gigged the pubs and clubs in his home town and beyond he began composing/producing at his home studio which he has  built up over the years. His main genres are Reggae and Electronic Dance Music, but he has been known to venture into different fields.

As well as his solo material Europica plays bass on other artists songs. This has been accomplished through collaborations over the internet. Blues, Rock Pop and Country are some of the genres he has covered.
