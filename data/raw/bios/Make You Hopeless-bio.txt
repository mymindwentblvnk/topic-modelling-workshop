The band was formed in october 2012.  the band play's brutal deathcore, or you can call it technical brutal deathcore. After they got stable members, they recorded the first song and posted it on the internet. Then BrutalWang from BrutalReign Productions found them and decided to help them to release the first EP. they released the EP ‘Desolation Of The Cerebral Labyrinth’.
