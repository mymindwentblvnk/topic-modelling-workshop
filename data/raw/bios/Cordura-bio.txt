Cordura is a punk band formed in Bilbao in 1999. They published their first album, titled Cordura, in 2003, and the second one, La ridícula mecánica de la vida moderna, in 2007. In 2009 they released their third album, El incendio más largo del mundo,  as free download on their MySpace. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cordura">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
