1. LITE are an Instrumental Math Rock band from Tokyo, Japan, formed in 2003. Their members are: Nobuyuki Takeda (guitar), Kozo Kusumoto (guitar),  Jun Izawa (bass) and Akinori Yamamoto (drums).

Filmlets (2006)

For All the Innocence (2011)
