Considered a top influential avant-garde electronic artist with projects such as Heldon, the French composer, guitarist and electronic innovator Richard Pinhas released seven critically acclaimed albums between 1974 and 1978, which granted him the title of the Godfather of Electronic Music in France.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Richard+Pinhas">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
