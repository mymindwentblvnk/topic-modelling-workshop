Californian death metal horde. First demo on Blood Harvest Records and Witchhammer Productions in 2017, split with Desekryptor in 2018 on Blood Harvest Records. EP out in 2018 on Messe Noire Productions, Craneo Negro Records and Nameless Grave Records. Promotional demo out in 2019 on Nameless Grave Records.
