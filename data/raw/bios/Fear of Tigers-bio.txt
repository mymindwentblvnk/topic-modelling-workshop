Fear of Tigers is a dreamwave act from London, United Kingdom formed by producer and big cat expert Benjamin Berry. He released his first studio album, Cossus Snufsigalonica, as a free download in 2009. More recent releases include Zoipa and Hypnerotomachia Poliphili. Fear of Tigers is also known for his remixes, including ones for Kylie Minogue, Ellie Goulding, Does It Offend You, Yeah? and The Sound of Arrows. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fear+of+Tigers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
