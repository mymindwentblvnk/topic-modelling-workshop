Luxury Elite is an electronic music producer making music since 2012 that can be loosely described as vaporwave. She has released material on several labels including fortune 500, Orange Milk Records, Ailanthus Recordings, Illuminated Paths, Suji Co., AMDISCS, and Dream Catalogue.
