Rauf & Faik (Рауф и Фаик) are an Azerbaijani pop duet, formed by twin brothers Rauf and Faik Mirzaev (Russian: Рауф и Фаик Мирзаев) (born July 7, 1999; Izhevsk, Russia). They are best known for their Russian-language #1 single "Childhood" (Russian: Детство), released in 2018. The brothers are ethnically Azerbaijani, and they had lived in Azerbaijan at times in their childhood, but now reside in Russia. They release music sung in both Russian and English, though they also speak Azerbaijani. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rauf+&+Faik">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
