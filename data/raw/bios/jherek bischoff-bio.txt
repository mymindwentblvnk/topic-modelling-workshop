Jherek Bischoff is a Seattle musician with one solo album released on Jason Webley's Eleven Records. His songs are shadowing soundscapes with mysterious lyrics. He also has a new release on Osaka Records under the moniker of 'Ribbons'

He’s served as both full-time member and guest musician/arranger for and the Jason Webley Quartet, as well as experimental pop crews The Dead Science, Xiu Xiu and Parenthetical Girls. He has composed music for dance and orchestral performances, and elegantly produced records for many of his talented friends including Casiotone for the Painfully Alone’s critically acclaimed Etiquette and Parenthetical Girls’ Safe As Houses
