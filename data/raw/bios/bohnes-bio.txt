‘'Have fun. I write the best songs simply while I am having fun.’‘

Bohnes is a solo project by Alexander DeLeon, former vocalist and founder of the band The Cab. His alias was inspired by the catacombs in Paris.

Bohnes was launched in 2015. After releasing multiple singles throughout 2015, 2016 and 2017, Bohnes released his debut album 206: Act I in 2018. An Act Two was confirmed by Alternative Press.
