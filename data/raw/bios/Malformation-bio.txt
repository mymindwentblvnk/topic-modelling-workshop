Founded in the spring 2006 in Rovaniemi, Finland, by two metal maniacs Otto Mikkonen and Ville Viertola in guitars with the help from Iiro Lehtonen in drums. After a year of rehearsing the band put together their first EP entitled Brainsoup that punished the listeners ears with raw old-school death metal the way it's ment to be played.
