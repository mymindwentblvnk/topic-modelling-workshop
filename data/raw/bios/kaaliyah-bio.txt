With a highly distinctive vocal, it´s clear that danish born Kaaliyah gets her inspirations from the late 90s American r’n’b sound, and today’s trap elements. Louis Winding is the Producer behind this project, he previously worked with names like Brandy, Outlandish, LOC, and Burhan G.
In the beginning of March 2015, Kaaliyah publish her single ” Rust & Bone “. Her debut EP, Darkpop – First Sequence, is ready for release by august 2015 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/kaaliyah">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
