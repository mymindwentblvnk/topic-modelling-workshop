Akatechism is a black/doom/death metal band from Germany. They released their debut demo "Dripping Flames" via Bandcamp on May 20th, 2015. Their music will most likely appeal to fans of Abyssal, Irkallian Oracle, Ævangelist and/or Bölzer.
