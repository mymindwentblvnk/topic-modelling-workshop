Sacriphyx of Australia is a Death Metal band in the old Greek style formed in 2007.

Band Members:
~ Anthony Till (Vocals, Guitars & Bass)
~ Neil Dyer (Drums)

Discography:
~ Lone Pine (Demo) 2007
~ Split with Stargazer (Split) 2009
~ Split with Resuscitator (Split) 2010
~ The Western Front (Full-length) 2013
