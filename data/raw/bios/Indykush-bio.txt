INDYKUSH is a hardcore band based in Menton, FRANCE.

INDYKUSH was created at the end of summer 2003.
John (Guitar), Mans (Drums) ans Mo (Bass) from another band named BLIND, met Flib (Vocals) to make INDYKUSH.
During 2 years INDYKUSH worked on their's set including different influences from Power Metal to Post-Hardcore.
In January 2006 INDYKUSH started in HARKAM ASYLUM Studio (with Guillaume SERRA) located in Eze (06) to record the 1st album named "IBOGA".
In september 2006 Manu (Guitar) joined the existing line-up.
