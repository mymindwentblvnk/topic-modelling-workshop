Naeem Juwan, better known by his stage name Spank Rock, is an American rapper and songwriter from Baltimore. He rose to fame with his 2006 album YoYoYoYoYo , which was produced by former group member Alex Epton (XXXChange). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Naeem">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
