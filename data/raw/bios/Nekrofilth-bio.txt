The crossover project of Zack Massacre from Nunslaughter.  The project originates from Cleveland, Ohio (they later moved Denver, Colorado) and formed in the year 2008.  They incorporate death metal, thrash metal and hardcore punk into their sound.  The band has gone through several bassists and drummers over time.  They currently do not have a drummer.  Today, the band only consists of Zack Massacre (vocals, guitar) and Disgustin' Justin (bass).  This project usually writes lyrics about extreme nihilism, death, filth, violence, drug abuse and insanity.  They are signed to Hells Headbangers Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nekrofilth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
