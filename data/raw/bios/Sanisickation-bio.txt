biography 

From the city who no one loves, where metal and hardcore can't see the light, driven by despise the young italian metallic hardcore combo are devoted to kill anyone with kickass death metal riffs and extreme and technical rhythmic section.
In December 2005 All Life Ends Records (GER) and Graves Records (ITA) coproduced and released the mcd Stabbing You Sweety, recorded at Audiostudio(Cagliari) and mixed at Fear Studio in Ravenna (responsible of releases from Slowmotion Apocalypse, The Secret, xReprisalx, Addiction Crew, Purification, Epel Duath, Sentence), that was reviewed with top demo and band of the month at the best italian and european magazines. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sanisickation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
