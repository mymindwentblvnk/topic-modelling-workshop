1) Nepenthe  is a death metal band from Italy.

2) Nepenthe  is an Atmospheric Black Metal band from The Netherlands.

3) Nepenthe  is a Technical death metal band from Poland.

4) Nepenthe  is a blackened death metal band from Kansas, USA. They changed their name to Ligeia in 1996.

5) Nepenthe  is an sludge metal/ambient artist from the USA. (http://www.nepenthesound.com)
