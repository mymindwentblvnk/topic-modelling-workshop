Out Of Channel is a melodic death metal band formed in November 2007 in St. Petersburg, Russia.  They were formerly known as Gangrena.  The band is known for incorporating thrash metal and metalcore into their work.  

Releases:
Проза (Prose, 2008, DF Music Org.)

S.C.H.E. - vocals
Yuri Maxim - guitar
Mars  - guitar
Aleksandr Kuznecov (KuZZ) - bass
Aleksander Chernoskulov (Krokodil, Kro) - drums
