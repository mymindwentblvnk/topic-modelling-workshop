There are multiple artists using this name.

1. A five piece Black Metal band from Ostrava, in the Moravian-Silesian region located in the Czech Republic. They released 3 albums from 1995 to 1998, then disbanded. In late 2001, they reformed and have released two albums since. 

2. ENOCHIAN were a band created in the winter of 2006. Merging a growing love of Techno/Trance with World Music, the first album "The Pangea Engine" was finished after much trial and error. Sampling various native songs and modern ambient arias, they were mixed and produced all at home. Enochian strives to create a bridge between atmospheric dance music and the so-called "New Age" sounds. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enochian">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
