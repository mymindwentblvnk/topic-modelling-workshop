Dantes is an underground rapper from Ljubljana, Slovenia. He released his first and only record called Avenija 8smi dan in 2008. He is also a part of hip-hop group Badale. Some members of this group are featured in his tracks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dantes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
