1) Haruspex was a death metal band from Milwaukee, Wi, which was formed in '91.  In the following years they released two demos, a single and two albums, namely, 'Deep' in '93 and 'Modify' in '96. 

2) Formed in 2008, Haruspex is the work of two deviant tobacco farmers from the Australian desert. Not much else is known about the characters, although rumours abound that they feast on the flesh of backpackers that wander through the area. Their debut album is planned for release in 2009 on Retort Records <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/haruspex">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
