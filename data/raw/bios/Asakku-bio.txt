Asakku, formed in 1994, were a German black metal band playing cold, fairly primitive black metal. After releasing several rehearsal demos, an EP and two splits (Curse (FIN), Moonblood) the band split in 1997. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asakku">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
