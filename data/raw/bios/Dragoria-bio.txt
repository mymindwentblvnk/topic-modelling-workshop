Dragoria, earlier known as Nifelheim and Cień Kruka, is a Polish folk/gothic/symphonic black metal band from Wrocław, founded in 2003. They have recorded a mini album 'Gniew Smoka' and a demo 'Dream Night'. In autumn 2007 the band changed name to Egrimonia. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dragoria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
