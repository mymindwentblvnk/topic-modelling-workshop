HAVENLESS was created in France , in early 2012, by singer/guitarist/composer Fred Blanchard.
HAVENLESS has released its first EP “Architecture of plague”, which was recorded and mixed at ECHOES STUDIO in May 2012.
The band’s progressive death metal sound is strongly influenced by OPETH, IHSAHN, ENSLAVED, and PORCUPINE TREE whereas the lyrics may remind KING DIAMOND ‘s style .   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Havenless">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
