There are two artists with this name:

1) Delano was a three-piece from Portland, Oregon in the mid-90s. They played music that could casually be referred to as indie jazz, much akin to The Sea and Cake. They recorded a five song demo cassette with Brady Grieg at Resin 67 Studios.

2) Delano is a German hip hop-artist.
