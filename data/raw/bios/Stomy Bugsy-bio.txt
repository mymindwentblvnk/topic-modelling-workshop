Stomy Bugsy (real name Gilles Duarte) (born 21 May 1972) is a French rapper from Sarcelles.

Born of parents from Cape Verde, Duarte helped found hip hop group Ministere AMER, however in 1996 he decided to launch his own solo career. Duarte has also had several minor acting roles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Stomy+Bugsy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
