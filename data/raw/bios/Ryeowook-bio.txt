Kim Ryeo-wook (Hangul: 김려욱; Hanja: 金厲旭; born June 21, 1987), more commonly credited as simply Ryeowook (Hangul: 려욱; Hanja: 厲旭), is the third youngest member of Super Junior. Although he is not the youngest member of Super Junior, he is recognized by his fellow members as the "eternal magnae". Even before Kyuhyun's addition to the group, Ryeowook commonly called himself the youngest despite being a few months older than Kibum. Kim is currently studying in Inha University under the Department of Drama and Cinema. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ryeowook">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
