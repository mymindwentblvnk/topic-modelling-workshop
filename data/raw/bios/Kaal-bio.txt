Kaal are an italian rock band, born in Bergamo in 1999. Their debut album "Farfallina snob" was produced by Bagana Record. 
Band members: Michele Clivati (leader), Davide Magris (guitar and moog), Antonio Schettino (bass), Michele Magris (drums).
