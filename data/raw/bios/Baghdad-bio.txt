1. Baghdad is a Doom/Sludge band from The Upper Peninsula of Michigan. The band consists of Ian Soulia on guitar and vocals, Zach Pelletier on guitar, Justin Brow on drums, and Chris Shiner on Bass. They released their debut E.P. in 2013, and released in 2014 a new E.P. and a new single.
