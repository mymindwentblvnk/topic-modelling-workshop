Gruesome Stuff Relish is a Death/Grind band from Spain, that released their debut album "Teenage Giallo Grind" in 2002 on Razorback records, and has released splits with bands such as Engorged, Splatterhouse, Gorerotted, and Gronibard. They have a similar sound to modern Goregrind, but with a more sludgey edge. The band had split-up, but reformed and are working on a new album.
