Hugo Flores is a Portuguese multi-instrumentalist and vocalist. While he has released only a single album under his own name, 2000's Atlantis he has created and released albums with the bands Sonic Pulsar, Project Creation and Factory of Dreams.

On his solo project Hugo plays what he considered to be "A great mystic blend of ambient textures, pop/rock and metal." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hugo+Flores">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
