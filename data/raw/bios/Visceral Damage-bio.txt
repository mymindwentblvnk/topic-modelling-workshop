Visceral Damage is a Brutal Death Grind band from Valencia, Spain. They formed the band in 1992 but has gained little to no notoriety. They are currently signed to Xtreem Music Records. In 2003 they released their debut EP entitled: "The feast of flesh". A year later the full lenght "Garden of Mutiliation" followed, they were both released by Xtreem Music Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Visceral+Damage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
