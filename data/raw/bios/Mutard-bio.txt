Mutard is a crossover thrash metal band from the area of Heidenheim an der Brenz (Germany). Influenced by old school thrash and crossover bands like M.O.D., Exodus, D.R.I. or Leeway, as well as newer bands like Municipal Waste and Dr. Living Dead!, the band was formed in 2013.
