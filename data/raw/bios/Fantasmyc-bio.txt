Fantasmyc is a Brazial Symphonic Heavy Metal Group
They formed in 2004 and have released a 3 track demo called Imaginary World

The band consists of:

B.Dias : Drums
Lucas Marques : Bass
Vinicius Morais : Guitar
Fernando Morais : Vocals, Keyboard
Caio Tassus : Keyboard
Aline Scott : Vocals
