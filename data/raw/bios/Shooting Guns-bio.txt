Shooting Guns are an instrumental psychedelic rock / heavy metal / heavy psych band from Saskatoon, Saskatchewan in vein of Circle, Funkadelic, Elevator, ZZ Top, Pharaoh Overlord, Black Sabbath, and Spacemen 3.
