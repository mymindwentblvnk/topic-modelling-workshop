Shelley Marshaun Massenburg-Smith (born August 3, 1988), formerly known by the stage name D.R.A.M. (ˈdrɑːm; an acronym for Does Real Ass Music), now performing mononymous under his first name, Shelley, is an American musician, singer, rapper and actor from Hampton, Virginia. He is signed to Atlantic Records and Empire Distribution. His debut single was "Cha Cha", followed by his breakout single "Broccoli", featuring Lil Yachty, which peaked at number five on the US Billboard Hot 100. In October 2016, he released his debut studio album, Big Baby DRAM. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shelley+FKA+DRAM">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
