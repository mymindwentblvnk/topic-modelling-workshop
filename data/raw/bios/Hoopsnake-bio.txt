Hoopsnake are a stoner doom band from British Columbia. Their music is known for its crushing grooves and crust punk textures. To date they have released four records - a demo, a self-titled full-length, one EP and a split.

Discography:
2010 - Demo Tape
2011 - Hoopsnake LP
2014 - Curse of the White Widow EP
2015 - Split with IRN

Line Up:
Shane - Bass/Vocals
Oli - Drums
Dave - Guitar/Vocals
