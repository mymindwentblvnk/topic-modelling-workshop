Nudozurdo is a Spanish music band from Madrid. In 2001 Leopoldo Mateos (vocals, guitar) and Felipe Salazar (drums) met. They started playing together in Olmo, where they met Daniel Asúa (bass). 

In 2002 they participed in Popzuelo 2002, they won the first prize and published their first album.  In 2007 they released their second album, “Sintética”.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nudozurdo">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
