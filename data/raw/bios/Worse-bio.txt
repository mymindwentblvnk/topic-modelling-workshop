There are 2 bands known as Worse:

1. Worse is a San Francisco based Power Violence/Grindcore band. They have released two EP length releases, a self titled in August 2014 and To Be Alive is To Be Alone in  February 2015. It consists of Peter Pawlak (ex-Rings of Saturn) -  vocalist, Derek Kanowsky - guitarist, Christian Huft-Robbins - bass, and Jack Lyons - drums.
