"Morbid Axe" is Japanese grindcore band.
Formed in 2004 by Tadayuki & Makoto.
Napalm Death, Metallica , Kreator and many great bands are influencing us.
Our activity is not easy...Because Morioka city is a very small city.
Grindcore scene doesn't exist either... However, we keep grinding!!
