XBXRX is an American noise rock-influenced punk band formed in 1998 in Mobile, Alabama, USA. The band toured Europe in 2001, featuring the rhythm section of Operation: Cliff Clavin. Today the band is a collective, with a revolving cast of musicians. To date the band have had their hand in 25 releases, including seven full-length LPs: Love Songs For The Blind (1999), Gop Ist Minee (2001), "Clear CD" (2001), Sixth In Sixes (2005), Wars (2007), Sound (2007), and Un Usper (2009). Un Usper was released through the band's website, accepting donations as payment. The band is now based in Los Angeles, California. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/xbxrx">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
