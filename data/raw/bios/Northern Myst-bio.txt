Northern Myst was a black metal band from New Jersey, USA, formed in 2005. In 2007 they released the EP Chapters of the Dying Moonlight, shortly before splitting up. Vocalist/bassist Nile Bowie and guitarist Itay Keren went on to form Ossein. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Northern+Myst">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
