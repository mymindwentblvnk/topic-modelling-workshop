Agnes Vein were created in 2001 in Thessaloniki,Greece.They include 2 members of National Pornografik. Current line up is: Foivos-drums,Errikos-bass and Sakis-guitars.

The band belongs to Venerate Industries, a well known and established greek label. They released their first demo back in 2004 (Of Chaos and Law), their second demo in 2008 (Duality) and now they have released their first full-length: Duality.Their stuff and sound is a unique and inspired mixture of Manilla Road,Candlemass and Primordial.They use their influences strategically to create a doom/epic/black monolith.Doom or be Doomed. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agnes+Vein">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
