Coldblooded is a Death metal band from Fryslan, Netherlands. Influences are Funebrarum, Dismember, Asphyx, Hail of Bullets, Obituary, Entombed and God Macabre.










Hardcore band from Bánovce nad Bebravou (Slovakia). Established in 2007.
