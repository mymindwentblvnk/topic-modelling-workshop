Tiger Junkies was formed by Yasuyuki Suzuki and Joel Grind for enjoy play punk rock in 04. In September '05 Tiger Junkies recorded 4 original songs by cheap 8 traxs MTR on Yasuyuki's bed room. It was released by the punk label Destroy records (UK) in 06. The band got good response by this 7'ep and sold out very soon. Tiger Junkies recorded 1st full album "D-beat street rock 'n rollers" in Tokyo and Boston 07. It's contain 10's D-beat rock 'n roll tunes. Cool jacket design by Halseycaust. This LP will be out on Destroy records very soon. Perhaps Tiger Junkies will start live performance in the near future. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tiger+Junkies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
