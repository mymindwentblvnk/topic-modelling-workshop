There are two bands going by the name Incarna;

The first is the solo project of former Tin Flood frontman Zero. It is unknown if Zero is still active. 

-----------------------------------

The second is a post-hardcore metal band from Abingdon, Oxfordshire, England.  Formed in 2001 from a group of school friends they released their first EP 'Incarna' in 2004 and there second 'Abartis' in 2006.  In 2007 they Signed to Casket records and have their first album 'Spires Deceased' due for relaese in February 2008.  They have been regularly gigging and have a growing fan base in Oxford, Birmingham and Wincester.

The band split up in in late 2008 due to disagreements and logistical issues.  No side projects have been confirmed to date.
