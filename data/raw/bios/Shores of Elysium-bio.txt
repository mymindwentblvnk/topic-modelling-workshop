Hailing from Central Ohio is a relentless force to be reckoned with. Shores of Elysium is a Death metal act formed in early 2011. With fast paced and technically savvy guitar work, hammering drums, and incredibly intense vocals. The band will keep you on the edge as you are always guessing what comes next.
