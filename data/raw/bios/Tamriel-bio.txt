Tamriel is a Bristol-based Technical Deathcore solo project themed around Bethesda's 'Elder Scrolls' series. Tamriel's sole member operates under the name Euryale. 

Tamriel's debut EP Blood and Ebony was released for free download on 18th May 2012.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tamriel">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
