Black metal band from Germany, formed in 1995 in Erfurt. Their lyrical themes are based on paganism and satanism. Former members of the band are Engel - Vocals, Vincent - Guitar and Thorns on drums. The band have two full-length albums - Dunkelheit (1999) and Gold Oder Blut (2004). They have also released three demos - Endless War (1996), Land Ohne Gotter (1997), Promo Tape '97 (1997) and two splits - Towards Immortality (1997) and Barad-Dur/Graven (2001). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Barad-Dur">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
