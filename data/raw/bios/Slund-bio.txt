Slund is the solo project of Igor Mortis, the moniker of a Slovenian musician. Slund's music utilizes both the slow, crunching sounds of sludge/doom metal and the tempos and blasts of grindcore. His debut album, "The Call of Agony" released in February, 2017 digitally. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slund">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
