Deepend is a Dutch deep house DJ duo consisting of Falco van den Aker and Robert "Bob" van Ratingen. They gained international recognition after their remix of Matt Simon's "Catch & Release" peaked at number one in five countries and the European Airplay charts. The duo racked in over 130 million Spotify plays and 50 million YouTube streams for the remix. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deepend">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
