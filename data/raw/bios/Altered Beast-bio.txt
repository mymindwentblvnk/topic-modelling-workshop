Altered Beast was a powerviolence band from Lansing, Illinois.  They recorded a hilarious demo, played a few shows in the summer of 2007, and then disbanded.  members went on to play in Grimsrud and Sea Of Shit...long live the beast.

Altered Beast was another powerviolence (and grind/hardcore/post rock) band from Sydney, Australia, that existed from 2002 - 2006. Members went on to play in Massappeal, Mass Trauma, Hee Haw, Crux and Ether Rag.
