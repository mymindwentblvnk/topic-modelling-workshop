ocean jams is a Colombian-American hip-hop producer based out of Spokane, Washington.

In 2012, he began making sample based hip-hop and soon carved a niche for himself in the emerging lo-fi communities of Soundcloud and Spotify. Since then ocean jams’ “urge to create and convey emotions” has seen him release the albums “skits”, “...Drifting” and “junyii. x ocean jams”, alongside several EPs and singles.

He also co-founded the collective radio.wavs and is continuously striving to build a sense of community among the online beat scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/%5Bocean+jams%5D">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
