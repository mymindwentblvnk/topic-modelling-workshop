Arcane Existence is a symphonic black metal/melodic death metal band from San Jose, California.  They formed in 2016.  Their first release is a full-length album The Dark Curse and it was released on January 6, 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Arcane+Existence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
