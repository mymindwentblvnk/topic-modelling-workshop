OBIUS are a melodic/death metal band based in Norfolk, England. Their name is an original representation of the Latin translation for "the act of dying", Obitus. They were formed in 2005 by Vocalist/Guitarist Daz Hannan and Guitarist Joe Clarke. They are currently writing for the release of a Self-titled EP and have two songs already written.

Current Line Up consists of:

Daz Hannan (Lead Vocals/ Guitars)
Sam Baker (Bass)
