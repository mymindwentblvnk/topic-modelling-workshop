CRANIAL IMPALEMENT began in spring 2011 with Eli Alcala on Guitar,Caezar Lazo on bass, Nick Johnson on Drums and Danial Elrod on vocals.Influenced by brutal death / grind, Beer and hard liquor. In the summer of 2012 Justin Bluford stepped in on bass as Caezer Lazo was promoted to a second Guitarist .
