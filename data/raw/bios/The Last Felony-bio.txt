The Last Felony is a band formed in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. The band claims to be  "Montreal-style Death Metal".

Ever since the band was created in 2005 the members have pushed themselves to keep perfecting the extreme death metal style. Now boasting two full-length albums (Aeon Of Suffering, Too Many Humans) the band continues to make their mark in the modern metal world.

Discography:

This Nighmare Begins .... Only To Realise That Hell Does Exist (2006) (demo)
Aeon Of Suffering (2008)
Too Many Humans (2010)
