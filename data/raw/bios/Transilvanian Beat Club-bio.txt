Transilvanian Beat Club is a Metal Music Project of the Eisregen Drummer Ronny "Yantit" Fimmel. Also a member of the band was Theresa Trenks, aka "2T" from Eisregen. She left the band. The "TBC" also stands for tuberculosis. Ronny Fimmel said it sounds funny and then it became the bands name. Their first record "Willkommen im Club!" was released at May 5th in 2006.

Current line-up:

Yantit - Guitar, Vocals
Blutknecht - Bass Guitar
