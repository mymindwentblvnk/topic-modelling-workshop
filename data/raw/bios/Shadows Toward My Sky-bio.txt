Shadows Toward My Sky were a German black metal band formed in 1995 in Eisenbach, Thuringia. Discography:

Forever Lost My Dying Love EP, 1997
Germanenland demo, 1997
Nightwaves demo, 1998
We Walk the Infernal Path (split with Eternity, Luror, and Darkmoon Warrior), 2003
