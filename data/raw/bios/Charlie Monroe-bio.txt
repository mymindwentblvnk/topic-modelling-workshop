Charlie Monroe  (July 6, 1903 - September 27, 1975)  was an American country and bluegrass music guitarist. Charlie performed with his brother, Bill, as part of the Monroe Brothers. He later formed his own group, Charlie Monroe & the Kentucky Pardners. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Charlie+Monroe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
