Alpha 606 has released two ep's, "Computer Controlled" on Dopamine (2004) and "Electrónica Afro-Cubano" on Interdimensional Transmissions (2008) His track "Anarchy in China" appears on the "Subsidence" compilation (2005) and "Nobody’s Perfect" compilation (2006) on Andrea Parker’s label, Touchin’ Bass. His track "Rise of Human Being" appears on the "Ten Volt" Compilation on Transient Force (2007).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Alpha+606">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
