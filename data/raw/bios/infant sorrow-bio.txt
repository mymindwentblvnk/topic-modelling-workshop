Infant Sorrow is a band created for the film Forgetting Sarah Marshall. Aldous Snow (Russell Brand) plays the lead singer. Brand and Aldous Snow also appear in the spin-off,  Get Him To The Greek, released in June 2010.

Most songs were co-written by a variety of comedians including Mike Viola, Jason Segel and Lyle Workman. Jarvis Cocker also appears on a couple tracks. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infant+Sorrow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
