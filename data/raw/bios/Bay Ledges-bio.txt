Los Angeles sibling duo, Bay Ledges – comprised of Zach and Georgia Hurd – first started as a little passion project but as their fan base rapidly grows, it looks as if they will be a household name within the coming years. Relying on each other for creative support has always helped the indie-pop duo connect through music and with the upcoming release of their new EP, featuring chill-out summer anthem, “Safe,” we can’t wait to see where this next chapter will take them!
