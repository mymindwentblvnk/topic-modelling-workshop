He is Aleksi Perälä. He likes dreaming and making music. He was born in Suomi, Finland 1976. Started playing piano in 1983. Bought his 1st syntheziser in 1988 and started making his own music. His first LP on Rephlex in 1999. He also produces under the alias of Ovuca.

Most recently, he’s been recording mostly under his given name.  “The Colundi Sequence” consists of dozens of releases. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Astrobotnia">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
