There are two artists using the name Coma Void:

1) An atmospheric doom metal/black metal/post-rock band from Maryland
2) A death/doom metal band from Oregon

1) Coma Void is the atmospheric metal/post-rock solo project by Okul. The debut album was released in January 2014 via Bandcamp.
