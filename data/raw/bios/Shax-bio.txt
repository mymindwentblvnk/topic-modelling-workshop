SHAX is one of the leading names in the Hard Electro Scene. His tough tracks ride a hard edge between Electro and Dirty Techno, always grabbing the listener by the balls. He's remixed/been remixed by some of the top names in the scene including Rico Tubbs, Blatta & Inesha, DJ Antention, Haezer, Doc Trashz, The Oddword, Donald Glaude, Paul Anthony, The Mastertrons, The Boomzers, Sawgood, Tits & Clits, and more. Owner of SHAX TRAX, a label pushing the boundaries of bangin' Electro and Dubstep music, and co-founder of legendary CRUX Records, SHAX is involved in the scene inside and out. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SHAX">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
