Devastruction is a Thrash Metal band formed in Dortmund, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany in 2011 (as Incivism) and changed there name to Devastruction in 2016. There EP "Alien Thrash Force Attack" was released in 2017, and there first full length "Space Force One" was released in 2019. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Devastruction">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
