Agarwaen was formed in 2006 in Kouvola, Finland, by main songwriter Pauli Hodju. It's horror themed melodic death metal. Their first demo was recorded in 2010 and they are now heading back to studio with new members to record their second demo.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Agarwaen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
