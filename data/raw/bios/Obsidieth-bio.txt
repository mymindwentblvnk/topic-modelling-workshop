Obsidieth are poised to breathe new life into a saturated metal scene. In 2009 they released there debut album "In Loss of All" in an effort to deliver nothing but the hardest and catchiest sounds possible in a difficult niche. In 2013 Obsidieth are back with a 5 Track EP "Winter" Available on iTunes and all major digital stores.

Line-up
Luke - Vocals, Guitar
Chris - Guitar
Bethany - Bass
Jennae - Keyboards
Renn - Drums
