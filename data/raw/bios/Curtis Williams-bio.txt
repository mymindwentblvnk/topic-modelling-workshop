Curtis Williams is a producer & mc from Atlanta, GA. Member and one of the founders of Two9 Pilot Music. Most famous for his mixtape with FatKidsBrotha, Jace (RetroSushi), & Damien Aidoo entitled The Starting Five & The Starting Five 2. Has also done production for Fly Union, Travis Porter, Lil B, cARTer, & more. Release his first solo mixtape "JustCurtis" on cinco de mayo 2011. After receiving good feedback he decided to drop another solo project entitled "TrillShitOnly" and is already talking about a third solo mixtape (Half Forgotten Daydreams).
