Evian Christ is a UK producer by the name of Joshua Leary. He started uploading songs to his Youtube account late 2011, which gained the attention of a number of acclaimed blogs. On 1 February 2012, he released the "Kings and Them" mixtape on Tri Angle, compiling mastered versions of the tracks he had put on Youtube up until then.
