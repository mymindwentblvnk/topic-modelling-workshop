Halfling Thief was a Medieval/Folk/Epic Black Metal band with fantasy themes formed in Athens, Greece in 1998. One demo was released entitled, 'The Legend Of The Hidden Gem'. The band itself consisted of Thanos Tsouanas on vocals, keyboard, piano and guitar, Nick Serganis on guitar and Vili Karagouni who provided (female) vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Halfling+Thief">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
