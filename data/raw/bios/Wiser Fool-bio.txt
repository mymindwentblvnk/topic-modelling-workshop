Wiser Fool is a hard n' heavy rock band which formed in 2011. We hail from Vancouver, BC Canada. Comprising of ex-members of Tobeatic, Meat Of Mankind, Low Life, Evil Survives, Stoned Eyes, Sinews, Grounding.

Jason Schaly - Drums
Spencer Trout - Bass
Troy Horton - Guitar
Jay Beck - Vocals/Guitar
