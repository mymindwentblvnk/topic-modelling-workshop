Ryan  Beevers started blackened technical melodic death metal band  Unflesh in 2014 and delivered their first EP, Transcendence To Eternal Obscurity in 2016. He took care of all instruments other than drums, which were done with Alkaloid/Howling Sycamore/ex-Obscura drummer Hannes Grossman. 

Soon after this release the band was able to attain a full band line-up of experienced musicians and their first full length album will be released May 25, 2018.

 
Unflesh Is:
 
Ryan Beevers - Vocals/Guitar
Chris Gardino - Guitar
Chris Dovas - Drums
