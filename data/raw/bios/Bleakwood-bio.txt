Bleakwood is a one man band located in Sydney, Australia, being Dan Nahum's brainchild. Bleakwood performs abrasive and atmospheric black metal. He's currently signed at An Out Recordings, and released a Demo in 2008 and an EP in 2009 called Blood & Faith. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bleakwood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
