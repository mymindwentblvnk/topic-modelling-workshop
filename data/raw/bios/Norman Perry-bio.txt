Connecticut vocalist-producer Norman Perry burst onto the R&B scene in 2016 with his hit single “Alive”, a thunderous production that puts Norm’s diverse body of talents on display. Norm has collaborated with Grammy-award winning and multi-platinum producers such as Foreign Teck Nick Mira, boi 1da , and OZ, receiving praise from several notable figures in music and pop-culture, including DJ Mustard, Kevin Durant, T-PAIN, and Kylie Jenner. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Norman+Perry">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
