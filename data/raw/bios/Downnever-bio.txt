Downnever is a female fronted metalcore band from Buenos Aires, Argentina. Formed in the year 2000, but they debut album sees the light at the end of the year 2006, "En Silencios Largos...", an EP with 6 agressive songs, with a sound similar to bands like Caliban, Maroon, Walls of Jericho and The Black Dahlia Murder, <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/downnever">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
