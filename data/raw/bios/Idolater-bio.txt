There are two artists with the name Idolater / IDOLATER

1) A black metal band: Through development, research and devotion, Idolater aims to be at the forefront of the evolving Black Metal genre.  Using a complex and anti-minimalist approach, Idolater achieves a new level of intensity while emphasizing dissonant counterpoint and detailed melody.

In mid 2009, Idolater made its name public and posted rough versions of three songs from the Black Sexual Blasphemy album.  The full album was released in early 2010.
