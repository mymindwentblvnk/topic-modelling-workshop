A speed metal/hardcore punk-band from Japan. They appeared on the split-LP named "Far East Thrash Army - Thrash Live in Savagery" (1989), together with Genoa, Airraid, Shell Shock and Vietnam. The band also released one demo-tape in 1989 and a EP named "Shake You Lifeblood" in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drastic+Gunsmith">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
