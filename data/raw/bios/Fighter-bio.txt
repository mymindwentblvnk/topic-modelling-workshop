Fighter was an American heavy metal band from Nashville, Tennessee, who released one album titled No Pain No Gain in 1983.


The brainchild of guitarist Jim Wolter and his wife vocalist Amy Wolter, this band plays 80's style rock with gutsy female vocals and Godly lyrics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fighter">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
