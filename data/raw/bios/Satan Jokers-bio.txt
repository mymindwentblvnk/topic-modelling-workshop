Satan Jokers are a heavy metal band from Paris, France, formed in 1981. The band released their first album "Les Fils du Métal" in 1983- the same year Sortilège released their debut EP. After this the band released the single and then the full length album "Trop Fou Pour Toi" in 1984, folowed by "III" in 1985. Also available are the bands best of collections. The band reformed in 2008 and has since released another full length album. for more info, including album reviews, track listings etc. See the bands page on Metal Archives
