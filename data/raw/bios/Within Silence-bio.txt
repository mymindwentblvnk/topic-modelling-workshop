Within Silence is a Slovakian power metal band formed in 2008 as "Rightdoor," before changing their name in 2014 to "Within Silence." They released their debut album with Ulterium records on May 8, 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Within+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
