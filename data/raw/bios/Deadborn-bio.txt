DEADBORN is a 4-piece Death Metal band founded 2002 in Baden-Baden, Germany. DEADBORN focuses on playing brutal, technically ambitious and diversified metal music. After two years of preparation and several struggles with unskilled studio managers and several line-up changes the band finally released their first four-track EP "Decades of Decapitation" in early August 2004. Their first album "Stigma Eternal" was released on October 19th, 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deadborn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
