There are two artists by the name Concussion:

1) Concussion is a hardcore band from Keswick, Ontario. They recorded their EP, "I Hate Everything I Love", with Kent Sheehey. It was released in November 2010.

Members:
James McCalmont - Vocals
Jon Tomlinson - Vocals / Guitar
Tyler Lewis - Guitar
Kevin Weininger - Bass
Peter Nanacsik - Drums 

http://www.myspace.com/concussion94hc

2) New D-beat/Crust Hardcore band from Los Angeles area. Demo tape out now on World Won't Listen Records. 7" in the works to be released on Hardware Records from Germany.
http://concussionca.bandcamp.com
http://facebook.com/concussionhc

3) Concussion is a thrash band out of Brooklyn, New York, formed by members of Bones Brigade, Atakke, and Haunted Castle. Their first EP, "Dried Blood", was recorded at Dead Air Studios in Western Mass. with Will Killingsworth in early 2010 and released later that spring.

Members:

Blake - guitars/vocals
Hugo - drums
Dan - bass/vocals
Matt - guitars
