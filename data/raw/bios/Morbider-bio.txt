Morbider is a death metal band from Czech Republic that was formed in 1992. They made many demos throughout the 90s, and released their full length debut, When Darkness Returns on Dark Blasphemies records, 2009. The vinyl edition with revised artwork was released on Dark Recollections Productions, 2012. A limited split CD with Abyssus From The Abyss Raised The Morbid was released on Ablaze Productions, 2014 - it features 3 new tracks and 1 re-recording of a demo track and artwork by Costin Chioreanu (artist for Grave, Demonical, Satanochio, etc.).
