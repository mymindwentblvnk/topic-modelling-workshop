Ahab's Ghost is a power trio from Milwaukee, WI. They play a mixture of thrash'n' roll and heavy 70's rock. They recorded their debut full length "After the Fall" at Howl Street Recordings and released it in early 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ahab%27s+Ghost">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
