Nice Nice are a band from Portland, Oregon. The band was formed by multi instrumentalists Jason Buehler and Mark Shirazi. 

The band signed to the prestigious British label Warp Records in 2010, and had their track "See Waves" released on Warp's yearly compilation of new music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nice+Nice">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
