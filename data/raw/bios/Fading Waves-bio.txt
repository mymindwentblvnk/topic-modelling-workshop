Fading Waves is a one-man post-metal band from Rostov-on-Don, Russia. Band was formed in 2008 by Lex Maximuk. In 2009 he recorded his first demo "No Way Home" . EP includes 7 tracks. Music of Fading Waves unite power of Isis, Cult Of Luna's atmosphere and infinite flight of emotions of any post-metal band. This sounds as paint inner waves of modern man, telling about his sickness of being in megapolis crush. Current line-up: Alexey "Lex" Maximuk - all instruments and drum programming. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fading+Waves">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
