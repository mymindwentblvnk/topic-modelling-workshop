Calciferum is a blackened death metal band from France. They formed in 2004 and have so far released a demo "Ancestors' History" in 2007, an EP "Dirge Of Gjallarhorn" in 2009 and their debut full length studio album called "The Beast Inside" in early 2011.

The band is still looking for a label.
