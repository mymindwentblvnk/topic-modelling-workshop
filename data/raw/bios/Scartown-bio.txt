Scartown is a melodic death metal band from Moscow (Russia), that also uses some metalcore elements. Their music can be compared with Children Of Bodom, In Flames, Killswitch Engage. They released their first album "Легенды Большого Города / Urban Legends" in 2006. 

Band members:
Andrey "Leo" Leontiev - guitar;
Stanislav "Joker" Rybalkin - voice;
Maxim "Max" Subbotin - guitar;
Oleg "Stay Puff" Sergeev - drums;
Maxim "AJ" Adjigitov - keys.

In 2007 the band will release their second album.
