Felix Clary Weatherall a.k.a., Ross From Friends is an electronic music producer specialising in his own fine-tuned brand of Techno and House. Hailing from Colchester, Essex, Ross From Friends has had continued support from the likes of Soundplate, BackTheTooFuture and Eton Messy to name a few.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ross+From+Friends">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
