Amandine was an alt-country band formed by songwriter Olof Gidlöf and pianist John Andersson from the small Swedish town Sandviken. They released two full-length albums and some eps on Fatcat Records UK.
Between 2004-2008 the band toured actively around Europe and the US, eventually playing their last show in Cadiz, Spain in 2008. The group disbanded officially 2012 and put out an untitled third album as a free download.

Members:
John Andersson – piano, accordion
Andreas Bergqvist – drums and percussion
Olof Gidlöf – vocals, guitar, banjo, dulcimer
Andreas Hedström – bass, backing vocals
