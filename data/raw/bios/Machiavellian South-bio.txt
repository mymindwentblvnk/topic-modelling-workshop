Origin: Portugal (Lisbon)
Formed in: 1994
Last known line-up:
Guilherme Franco: Vocals, lead guitars (Synapsys)
Sérgio Lérias: Rhythm guitars (Synapsys)
Manuel Azevedo: Keyboards
Pedro Tavares: Bass
Rui Machado: Drums

The band ended in 2002, with members Guilherme Franco, Sérgio Lérias and Rui Machado forming Firewall, which on its turn ended in 2004.

Guilherme Franco and Sérgio Lérias are currently guitarists and founding members of Synapsys, a melodic death metal band.
