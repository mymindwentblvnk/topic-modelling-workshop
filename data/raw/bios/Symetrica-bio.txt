Symetrica is a Progressive Power Metal band from Montebello, CA. Beginning in early 2003, the band has gone through multiple changes in band members and style of music. Currently the band consists of: Andrew Valencia on Vocals, Jason Castillo playing Guitar, Denny Ramirez playing Drums, Zach Calderon playing Keyboard, and Eddie Garcia playing Bass. Despite being around for roughly 3 years, all members of the band outside of Denny(19) and Zach(13), are 16 years old.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Symetrica">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
