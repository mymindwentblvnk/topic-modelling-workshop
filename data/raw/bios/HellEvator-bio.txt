Hellevator is a metal band from Nyköping, Sweden.
Influences from Thin Lizzy, Iron Maiden, Metallica, Deep Purple, Judas Priest etc etc. The band started playing in 2005 and recently won Sörmlands Rockkarusellen.
Band members are: Dennis Augustsson Vocals, Alexander Elmstedt Guitar, Robin Syrjänen Guitar, Jimmy Nilsson bass and Oskar "Tom Twice" Åstedt on drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hellevator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
