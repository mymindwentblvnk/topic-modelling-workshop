DEVIOUS MINE - This is a young English-speaking Italian Power Metal band. Hailing from the city of Pesaro. Formed in 2006, the first live-performance was in 2008.

The group:

Alberto Ambrogiani - ( CA$H ) : voice

Luca Biccari - ( BICCA ) : lead guitar & choirs

Andrea Tarsi - ( DRIU ) : bass

Nicola Faccilongo - ( NICO ) : drums

Alberto Bartolucci - ( TANGO ) : keyboard

The group has two EP.
The first EP was released on 1 September 2010 and is called «Rise or Fall».

The second EP was released: in 2012, with the title «REVELATION»
