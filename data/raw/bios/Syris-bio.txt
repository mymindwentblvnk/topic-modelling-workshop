1. Syris was a Chicago power metal band formed by guitarist Scott Payne.  They released two albums.  After releasing their first self-titled album, the band was set to go on tour, but Payne unexpectedly fell ill and passed away.  After disbanding for the first time, drummer Sean Brown recruited a new lineup. The band had dissoved permanently after recording their second album, Unseen Forces.
