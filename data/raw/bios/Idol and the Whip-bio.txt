Idol and the Whip is "rock n' roll written by metalheads" from Ann Arbor, Michigan.  The band consists of Chris Plumb (vocals/guitar), Adam Tatro (drums), Nik Tyckowski (bass), and Matt Gauntlett (vocals/guitar).  Former members include Dave Kloc (bass) and Andy Zasuwa (guitar). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Idol+And+The+Whip">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
