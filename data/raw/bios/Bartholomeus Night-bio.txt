Bartholomeus Night, started in 1998 by T.K.O.R from Jerusalem, Israel.
by the time he was ready to record the first Demo tape he joined Refahim (The vocalist of the band Dalmerot's Kingdom back than) to the band. After T.K.O.R finished the army the band joined D.Wrath and the way to the first album was set.
"Theosophia Pneumatica" was released at 2004, recorded at Selani Studios, Poland.

Nowadays, Bartholomeus Night are working on their second album.
