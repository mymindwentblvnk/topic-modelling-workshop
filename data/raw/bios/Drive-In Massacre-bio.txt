Drive-In Massacre is a Metal band infused with hardcore influences out of the Monument/Colorado Springs area.  They have been around for about 3 1/2 years and have played all over Colorado opening for such national acts as Hopesfall, 3 Inches of Blood, and 5 Finger Death Punch.  Be ready to see more of them soon.  Their first full-length album, "Upon Forgetful Seas" will be out in early 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drive-In+Massacre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
