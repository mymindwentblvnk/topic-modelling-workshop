Black Stone Ritual carries the torch for old-school metal in the tradition of Black Sabbath, Dio & Judas Priest.  Based in Indianapolis, Indiana, their self-titled debut CD was released in 2006. Line-up includes: Chucky von Bierstein (vocals), Jeff Runchel (bass), Shawn Miller (guitar),  Randy Humphrey Jr. (drums), Scott "Syrus" Benson (guitar). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Stone+Ritual">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
