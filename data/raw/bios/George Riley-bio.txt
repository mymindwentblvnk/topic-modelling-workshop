George Riley is a British R&B/soul singer from West London. Her debut single "HERstory" was released in 2019, with "Move" and "TRIXXX" being released the following year. Riley released her debut projectinterest rates, a tape in 2021, featuring the singles "cleanse me" and "power". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/George+Riley">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
