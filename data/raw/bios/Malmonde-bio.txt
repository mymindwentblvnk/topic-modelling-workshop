Malmonde is a cyber deathmetal band from france. they are 
Influenced by Fear Factory, Rammstein, Samael or even Meshuggah, MALMONDE is building its universe by mixing metal and electro/Indus samples.
The first demo (not available anymore) is really welcomed by french magazines and webzines and will make the band able to promote itself during local gigs.
After having produced its first album " Malmonde " by itself, the band signs with the label OSMOSE PRODUCTIONS for an international promotion and distribution.
The second album " EVA " is produced at the TOWER Studio, and MALMONDE signs a licence contract with MUSICAST for promoting and distributing the album in France. The album EVA was out on october 12th, 2005. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malmonde">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
