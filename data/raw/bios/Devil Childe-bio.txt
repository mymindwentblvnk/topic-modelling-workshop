They were a heavy metal band from New York City and formed in 1984.  The band consisted of Matthew Hopkins on vocals,  Anton Phibes on bass and Lucifer (Jack Starr) on drums.  They only released Devil Childe, a full-length album in 1985.  Their lyrical themes included fantasy and metal.  Pentagram Records was the last record label they were signed to.  They disbanded in 1985. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Devil+Childe">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
