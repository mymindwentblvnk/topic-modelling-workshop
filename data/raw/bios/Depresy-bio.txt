Depresy is a melodic blackened death metal band with classical / symphonic influences from Slovakia.

Their albums were released by Czech label Shindy Productions.

Albums:
1993 - In Embrace of the Dying (Demo)
1995 - ...and There Came the Tears with Christ (Demo)
1998 - A Grand Magnificence (EP)
1999 - Sighting
2003 - Psychomantium Phenomenon
2009 - Morph (Near Death Experiences)
