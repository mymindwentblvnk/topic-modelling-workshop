Fiendish Nymph was a pagan / atmospheric black metal band from Athens, Greece, formed in 1992 by the brothers Pandelis and Spyridon Giasafakis. The band split up in 2000, after which the Giasafakis brothers formed Daemonia Nymphe.

Line-up:
Pandelis - vocals, drums
Spyridon Giasafakis - guitars, bass, synthesizer, flute, backing vocals
