Fatal Influence is a thrash/speed metal band from Orlando, Florida, United States. They are strongly influenced by 80's thrash metal bands like Slayer, Metallica, Megadeth & Sepultura.

They are most known of their Megadeth cover "Hook In Mouth" which was released on "Megaded" Megadeth Tribute Album (1999)

They have also released 4-track EP called "World Unbalanced" (1998) and it's in download section of their official website:  http://www.fatalinfluence.net/

Line-up:
Jon - Vocals
Cal - Rhythm Guitar
Woodzy - Lead Guitar
Carmen - Bass
