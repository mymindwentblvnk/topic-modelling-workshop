Benny Smiles is an Irish synthwave producer based in London.

“When We Touch” marks Benny's fourth release. It follows the "Hotline Miami 2 EP”, which is featured in the cult video game Hotline Miami 2 and in Spotify’s Retrowave / Outrun editorial playlist, both of which have helped to garner an international audience and more than 5 million streams of the EP across various platforms.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Benny+Smiles">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
