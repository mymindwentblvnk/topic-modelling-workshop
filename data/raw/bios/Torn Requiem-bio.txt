Torn Requiem was a metal band from Crystal Lake, IL that founding members Brandon Erker and Brandon Salm started in March 2007.

After several lineup changes, Torn Requiem found a more stable lineup in 2007 with Brandon Erker, Brandon Salm, Shane Hawkins, Adam Salerno, and Dan Williams. They recorded and released their 5 Track Demo (2007) in November 2007. They are currently on an indefinite hiatus with talks of recording unreleased material to potentially take place in the future.
