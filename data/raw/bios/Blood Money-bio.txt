Blood Money was an english NWOBHM and thrash metal band form Manchester, formed in 1984 and after a short lived career split up in 1987.

In their 3 years together, band members Danny Foxx (vocals), Gramie Dee (guitar), Dale Lee (bass) and Brett Avok (drums) released one demo and two full-length albums, Metalyzed (1985), Red, Raw and Bleeding!
