Palmanana is a three piece stoner rock band from Palermo, Italy, founded in 2012.

Current line-up consists of Riccardo Ingrao on guitar, Emanuele Tantillo on bass and Giulio Scavuzzo on drums. Vocal duties are shared between them.

To date Palmanana released 3 albums:

- Palmanana (Origin of the Green) EP (DIY - 2012)
- 21 (Christmas) EP (DIY - 2012)
- Green (DIY - 2013)

through bandcamp.
