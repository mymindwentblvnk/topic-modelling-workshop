Ghost of Echoes is the solo project of Michael Trunks (Silent Empathy). Made an official project in Dec 2010, GoE is a Progressive Metal fusion that brings beauty and brutality together and attempts to explore the various avenues in between. Currently a bedroom project based in Somerset UK. GoE is entirely constructed by Mike using an EMac and Logic.

The self titled debut album 'Ghost Of Echoes' is a concept album that blends a variety of Influences to form something beautiful, heavy, melodic and interesting. Featuring the guest vocals of Louise Body (Zoltar Speaks). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ghost+of+Echoes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
