Infested Blood is a brutal death metal band from Brazil. The band formed in 1999 and has released three full-length albums, the most recent being released in 2009 entitled, "Interplanar Decimation". 

Current band lineup:

Cristiano Alexandre - Vocals
Eduardo Viana - Bass 
Diego Dourded - Guitars
