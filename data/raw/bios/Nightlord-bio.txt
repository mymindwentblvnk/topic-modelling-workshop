Nightlord was a British speed/thrash metal band from Hanworth, Middlesex, who issued the 'Approaching Thunder' demo in 1990.  They reformed for 20th anniversary gigs in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/NightLord">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
