Some Japanese Black Metal fans might recognize the name of this project, as it bears the name of Funeral Elegy's old leader.

For reasons unknown, Hakuja has recently left Funeral Elegy, and started a solo-project. So far he has released a CD-R Demo called "Legacy" in August 2007.
