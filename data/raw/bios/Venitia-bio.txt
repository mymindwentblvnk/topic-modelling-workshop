The band has been created in early 1994 in Roanne (Rhône Alpes county/ France). Since its beginning, the band plays a music made of dark gothic, metal, 80s mixed with progressive structures.The first MCD, "Beyond the light", self produced and distributed has been rewarded by most of the french specialized press for Its strong personality in a music sphere sometimes full of «clichés». 

The ambition of the forthcoming album is to offer a global universe within its various influences. The album has been recorded in Switzerland, and mixed by Paul Kendall (Nine Inch Nails, Depeche Mode, Laibach, Nick Cave & The Bad seeds), the king of electronics Vs acoustics instruments mix.
