Secrets of Separation is a Czech thrash - melodic death - metalcore band formed in Valašské Meziříčí, Czech Republic, in 2012.

Influenced by Born of Osiris, Metallica, As I Lay Dying and other metal bands, Secrets of Separation tries to push their own distinctive style and approach to metal through the music scene.

On March 21, 2015 the band released their first EP Obscuration consisting of four songs, combining thrash/death metal riffs with metalcore/melodic death metal melodies and harsh vocals.

Currently, the band is working on their new records while playing gigs at local shows.

Members:
*Štěpán Fiala (vocals)
*Zdeněk Zajíček (guitar, backing vocals)
*Milan Blažek (guitar)
*Václav Rychtecký (bass)
*Lukáš Machalský (drums)
