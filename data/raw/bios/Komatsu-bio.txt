Komatsu is a super massive rockband from Eindhoven RockCity, the Netherlands. Their music is a mix of sludge, stonerrock and metal. Komatsu released their full-length debut album "Manu Armata" in februari of 2013. "Manu Armata" has been well received by the international press and has gotten raving reviews. Komatsu’s music style has been compared to bands like Queens of the Stone Age, Torche, Mastodon, Monster Magnet and Karma to Burn.
