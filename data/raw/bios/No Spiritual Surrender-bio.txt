No Spiritual Surrender is an Ambient Black/Doom Metal band from Ukraine. The sole member of the band, Ixnay (Alexandr Malinovskiy), also plays bass in the band Terra Impressionum. So far, a demo, full length and an EP have been released.


Discography:
