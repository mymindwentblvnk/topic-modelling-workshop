Born in 1977 in central Slovakia, Smote started playing the piano during his childhood and later the began to play the guitar for the local punk band "Los Ratones Paranoicos". He has been part of the DnB scene since 1998. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Smote">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
