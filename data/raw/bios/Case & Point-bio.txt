Case & Point are an American production duo consisting of Casey Rankin (Case) and Taylor Evans Jordan (Point).
They are signed to Monstercat and released their debut single, Savage. which was featured on Monstercat 016 - Expedition.
After 2015 the duo went quiet except for a few Instagram and Facebook posts that said that they would release more music., but new music hasn't been released.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Case+&+Point">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
