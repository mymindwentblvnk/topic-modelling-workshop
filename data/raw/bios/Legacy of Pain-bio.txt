Legacy of Pain was a Melodic Deathcore Act from Oakland, USA. They started late 2006 and Split Up in November 2007. 
They released one Selftitled EP over Stillborn Records. 
The Line Up was:
Jared (Vocals)
Taylor (Guitar)
Lars (Drums)
Ryan (Guitar)

Jared, Taylor & Lars continued with their Fulltime Band Suffokate.
