Tromsnar is a Melodic Black Metal band from Poland formed in 2000. The band has released three demos until - they become well known and released their 2003 album entitled "Decay of Heaven's Torture". This is their first and final album because, they broke up. However, their album was released on the record label; Apocalypse Productions. The band has an Atmospheric and almost electronic influenced style conveyed in the synthesizers. The lyrical themes include; Darkness, Mental Torment, Existence, Past and Satan. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tromsnar">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
