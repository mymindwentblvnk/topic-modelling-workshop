Norwegian born 7 string guitarist/singer/songwriter in Metal. 
Official Ibanez artist, playing the prototype Falchion 7, the only one of its kind in the world.

The CIC released her first single from the cooperation with legendary PANTERA engineer/producer Sterling Winfield, who mixed her brutal version of PARANOID.
The single is now available as a FREE download here and on her websites.
