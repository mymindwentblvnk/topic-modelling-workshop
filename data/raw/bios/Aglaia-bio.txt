Aglaia is an organic ambient project from Italy formed by Gino Fioravanti and Gianluigi Toso. Beatless sounscapes with warm tones and some hints of acoustic instruments. Soaked in reverb.

Gino Fioravanti is a therapist, writer, painter and musician his touch with electronic music make it human and cosmic/amniotic at the same time. His writings and inspiration, research in different spheres like alchemy, mithology, ortho-bionomy.

Gianluigi Toso is a musician and back-school teacher. He moves through different musical styles, with Gino Fioravanti has relesed many cd for mind and spirit. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aglaia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
