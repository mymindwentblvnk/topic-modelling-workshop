Back War is a black metal band from Brazil formed in 1996. The band released a demo in 1999 named As the Darkness Prevails.

Line-up:
Hammer - Vocals, Bass (Iron Woods)
Holykran - Guitar (Iron Woods)
Guerriera Nox - Drums (Barbarian Warriors in Search of Wisdom, Iron Woods)
