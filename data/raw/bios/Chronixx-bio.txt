Jamar Rolando McNaughton, popularly known as Chronixx, is a Jamaican reggae artist. His stage name replaced the name "Little Chronicle" which he was given because of his father, the singer "Chronicle". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Chronixx">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
