Death metal band from Poland, Zory formed in 1991.

Originally formed under the name Exhaust Death in 1988.
The band changed its name to Vehement Thrower in 1991.

Discography: 
- Worm (Demo) 1991
- I Come in Peace (Full-length) 1993
