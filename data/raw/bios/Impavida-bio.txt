Impavida is a German depressive black metal/ambient band formed in 2005. Currently E. (Vargnatt) is the only member, handling guitar, bass and vocal duties. Herbst (Lantlôs, LowCityRain, Líam) was the drummer for Impavida though he ended up departing from the project. 

Discography:

"Into Empty Spheres" (demo, released April 14th, 2008)

"Eerie Sceneries" (full-length, released November 14th, 2008)

"Antipode" (released 2019)
