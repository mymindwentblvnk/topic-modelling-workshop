Matt Howden is an English violinist that uses his own name for his solo neo-classical work, to separate it from his song-based material, which goes under the name of Sieben. To date he has released four albums.  He has also worked with bands like Sol Invictus, :Of the Wand & the Moon:, Harvest Rain, and many others.
