Pal One is a hip-hop artist hailing from Mannheim, Germany. To this date, he has relased 2 EPs, 2 full solo albums-Palwolf and Fokus Rap- and uncountable features. He has also recorded the EP 'S&J' with Jonesmann and the collaborative effort '7 Tage' with German producer Roey Marquis II, a whole album written & recorded in only 7 days. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pal+One">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
