There are several bands using the name Set The Tone.


1) A British synth pop/funk band from the 1980s. Set The Tone released 2 singles: "Dance Sucker" (1982) and "Rap Your Love" (1983), as well as one full-length album "Shiftin' Air Affair" (1983). Set The Tone featured members Chris Morgan, Kendall Stubbs, Kenny Hyslop and Bobby Paterson. The latter was later a member of Love and Money, but has since passed away.
