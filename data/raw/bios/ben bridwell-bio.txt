Ben Bridwell is a musician from Irmo, South Carolina who now lives in an area outside of Charleston, South Carolina. He plays guitar and sings in the indie rock group Band of Horses. Their debut album on Sub Pop records, Everything All the Time, was released in 2006.  Before Band of Horses Ben was also a member of Carissa's Wierd. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ben+Bridwell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
