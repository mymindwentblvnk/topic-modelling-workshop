Venin Noir is a band of Gothic Metal from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, formed in 2001. Their two albums are "Rainy days of october", released in 2002, and "In Pieces of the Lunar Soil", 2003. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Venin+Noir">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
