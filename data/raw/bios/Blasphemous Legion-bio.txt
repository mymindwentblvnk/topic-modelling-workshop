Blasphemous Legion is a band from Tokyo, Japan.  They play black metal, often melodic, and formed in 2008.  They used to be known by the name Deathscythe.  Currently with Armageddon Records, they have released one split with another Japanese band, Thy Nadir, called J.B.M in 2008.  The line-up is as follows:

Parasugob - Vocals
Hachisuka - Guitar
Phillip - Bass
