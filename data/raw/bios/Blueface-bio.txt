Blueface is an LA based rapper who rose to prominence extremely fast, largely due to his high energy, spur-of-the-moment performances and his tumbling, off-kilter flow. He went viral in late 2018 due to success of songs like “Respect My Cryppin'”, "Dead Locs" and "Thotiana" from his mixtape Famous Cryp. In 2019, he released the mixtape "Dirt Bag" Since then, in 2020, he has released the mixtape Find The Beat. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blueface">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
