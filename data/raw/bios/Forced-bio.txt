Forced are a doom metal band from New York, USA founded in 2011.
They released their debut EP Accursed Share in 2012 on Bandcamp.
Current line-up consists of vocalist Scott Brown also on guitar, Dave Friedrich on bass and Mike Hegarty on drums.
