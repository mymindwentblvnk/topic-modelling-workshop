Windsurf is certainly a product of California, but of its beach culture more than its surf society. Northern California then, not those bronzed and plucked LA parts. The duo—Daniel Judd of Sorcerer and Sam Grawe of Hatchback—create sun baked music not for jumping in or getting wet, but for the wave-gazers and castle-in-the-sanders.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Windsurf">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
