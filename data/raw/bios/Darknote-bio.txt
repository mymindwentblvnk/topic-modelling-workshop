Formed in Iceland in the year 2004 by guitarist Jon Dal and drummer Vilhjalmur Magnusson, the band has grown immensly in song writing and performance. Starting as a progressive rock band, Darknote has slowly transformed into a fast paced metal band, without losing its progressive roots. Influences include Lamb of God, Arch Enemy, Mastodon, Opeth, Meshuggah and Dimmu Borgir, plus many more amazing metal bands. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Darknote">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
