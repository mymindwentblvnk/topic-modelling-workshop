Manditory, a Dallas, TX rock/metal band, was established in 1997 by Rusty Holloway and Brent Wiggins.  Years later they teamed up with Drummer Scott Sabelhaus and Bass player Brandon Kearney and promoted their own "Spread the Madness Tour"

Manditory's first album "Lend Me Your Ears"  has sold over seven-thousand copies and has a a 5 star rating on both iTunes and Amazon.com

Manditory is currently working on their second album "Some of Us Have Nightmares" which is due out in 2011.
