Cold Blooded Promise was formed in 2005 by 3 musicians from southern New Jersey.  After 5 years, multiple style changes and line-up changes, CBP solidified themselves as a popular local hardcore act.  CBP has since split up but talks of a possible re-union in the works have sprung up.
