Rage of Romance is a Gothic / Power Metal band from Greece.  Formed in 2010 and releasing their first album Rage of Romance in 2014. Guest vocals are handled by Vicky Psarakis of E.V.E. and of The Agonist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rage+Of+Romance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
