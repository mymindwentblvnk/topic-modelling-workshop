There are more than one artist with the name Enemigo.

1) Enemigo was founded in 2007 by singer/songwriters Dillon Flynn and Michael Knackstedt as a Eugene, Oregon based project to create musically simple yet lyrically abstract country songs. The group swelled to include folk and rock influences, as well as guitar virtuoso and longtime friend Robin "Eddie" Daugherty and multi-instrumentalists Max Knackstedt and George Gulian. In December of 2007, they recorded Blowing Right Through, an EP featuring three songs from Flynn and three from Knackstedt. They are currently accumulating more material and hoping to tour.
