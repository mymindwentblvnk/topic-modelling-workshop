Malicious Grind was a Hardcore/Thrash Crossover band from El Segundo, CA, active in the mid-late 1980's. The band released a demo in 1987 and full-length called Welcome to Life in 1988. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malicious+Grind">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
