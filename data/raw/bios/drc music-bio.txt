A collective of producers gathered by Damon Albarn visited the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) in 2011 to produce a new album for Oxfam. T-E-E-D (Totally Enormous Extinct Dinosaurs), Dan The Automator, Jneiro Jarel, Richard Russell, Actress, Marc Antoine, Alwest, Rodaidh McDonald and Kwes. all made the trip with the plan - to make an album in 5 days: Kinshasa One Two.
