Volkstrott is a newcomerband from Berlin, playing a lively mix of punk, rock and folk with bagpipes and violin. They have released two mini CDs and have recently been signed by John Silver, with a full length CD coming up in February '07. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Volkstrott">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
