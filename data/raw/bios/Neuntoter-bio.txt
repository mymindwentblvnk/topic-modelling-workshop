Country of origin:   Spain
Formed in:    1988
Genre:    Death Metal/Grindcore


In 1992 they changed their name to Demented Foeticide, who were musically more influenced by grindcore and hardcore. The band split up in 1993 after playing shows with bands like El Kaso Urkijo, Soziedad Alkoholika, Beer Mosh, and after being part of the first Monsters of Rot in Barcelona in 1992, their last concert.
