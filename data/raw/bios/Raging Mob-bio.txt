Beijing-based Thrash Metal. In Chinese their name is 国际联合敲击军团. Their debut album was released in 2008 and in 2010 they won the Metal Battle China final. In between the release of their debut album and the band being awarded the best Chinese metal band of 2010, the line-up had changed considerably. The current line-up is: 

Robert Gonnella - Vocals
Cui Guanke 崔冠可 - Guitar
Jin Peng 金鹏 - Guitar
Liu Ke 刘珂 - Bass
