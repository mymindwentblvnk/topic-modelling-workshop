Cemetery Piss is a black metal band from Baltimore, Maryland.  They consist of Adam Savage on vocals,  Dirck Ober on guitar & bass, Rebecca Chernoff on bass and Josh Gilman on drums.  Their past members include drummers Akai and Derrick Hans.  They have one demo, one EP and one full-length album.  They are currently with the Cricket Cemetery record label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/cemetery+piss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
