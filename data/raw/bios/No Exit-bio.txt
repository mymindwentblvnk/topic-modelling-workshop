There are various bands under the moniker "No Exit":

1) No Exit is a punk rock band from Berlin, Germany, active since 1991.

2) No Exit was a heavy metal/hard rock band from Pennsylvania, USA. They had two releases: "Strung Out" which was mostly an alternative rock/hard rock album and the "Bootleg EP" where they played traditional heavy metal.
