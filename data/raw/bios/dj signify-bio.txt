DJ Signify is a producer and DJ specializing in gloomy hip-hop beats. He was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York. In the past he has worked with many well-established underground rappers such as Buck 65 and Sage Francis. His debut album was released on Lex Records in 2004. His second album, entitled "Of Cities", was released in January of 2009 and features tracks with Aesop Rock and Matt Kelly.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DJ+Signify">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
