Atmospheric/depressive black metal project with Waldgeist (Wintercult) and Sorg. Started as an improvisational rehearsal project, after recording third demo, last material has been reworked and recording first album "Autumn Nocturne". Split-up after this release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Norskian+Anathium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
