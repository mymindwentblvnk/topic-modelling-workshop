Asphagor has been founded in 2007.  The same year the demo "tyrolean hell (demo 2007)" was released (containing 3 songs). After this release Asphagor enveloped new ways.
The founding member "Das Nichts" left in 2010 and "Morgoth" joined the dark forces of Asphagor.
In the same year the band confirmed some open air festivals in Austria and Germany.
They recorded their  debutealbum "Havoc" in 2010.
