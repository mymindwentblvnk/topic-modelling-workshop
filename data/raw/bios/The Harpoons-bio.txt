There are multiple bands with this name:

1) A five-piece post-punk band from Bristol, England, who formed in 1983 and released one 7", "Tunnel Child"

2) An R&B, pop band based in Collingwood, Melbourne, Australia

The band consists of Henry Madin, Jack Madin, Bec Rigby and Martin King.

They have released Faith (4 track EP), via Gaga Digi. In 2011 they switched to Two Bright Lakes/Remote Control and released Walk/Keep You Around as an EP, foreshadowing a full album, later in 2012.

They have supported bands such as Dick Diver, First Aid Kit, SoKo, Dr. Dog, and Adam Green, as well as performing at the Meredith Music Festival, and the Sugar Mountain Festival. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Harpoons">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
