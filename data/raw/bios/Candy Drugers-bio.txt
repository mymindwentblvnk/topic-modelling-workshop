CANDY DRUGERS (キャンディー・ドラッガーズ) is a Visual Kei heavy metal band, formed in 2005, in Nagoya, Aichi, Japan.

Members:
Vocals: Yu Ya
Guitar: Kintaro
Guitar: Masaru
Drums: Okaz
Bass: Osam
