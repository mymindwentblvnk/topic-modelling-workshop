The Secret Machines are a three-piece American alternative rock band. Formed in 2000. They are Originally from Dallas, Texas before moving to New York City, they describe their band as space rock. The original lineup consisted of two brothers, Brandon and Benjamin Curtis, and Josh Garza.

Current Members:
Brandon Curtis: vocals, bass, keyboard
Josh Garza: drums
Phil Karnats: guitar

Former Members:
Benjamin Curtis: guitar, backing vocals
Frederick Blasco: guitar, keyboard, bass
