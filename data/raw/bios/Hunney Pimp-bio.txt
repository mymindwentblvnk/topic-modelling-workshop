Hunney Pimp is an artist from Vienna, Austria. Her music could be categorized as cloud rap, even though she states that she doesn't stick to a certain genre and "just raps or speaks over beats she likes, which happens to be trap at the moment" and could also be described as "R’n’B-Dub-Rap". Her music also contains elements from pop music.
