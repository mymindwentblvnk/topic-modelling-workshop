Girlfriends may refer to:

- A one-man band - Jerry Joiner from Portland, OR who calls himself 'Girlfriends' instead of 'Jerry Joiner'. (http://girlfriends.bandcamp.com)

- Three people from Boston, MA who played under the name 'Girlfriends' between 2009 and 2012 to avoid listing each member's name separately on flyers and releases. This Girlfriends added a fourth member and became Bent Shapes. (http://girlfriendstheband.com --> http://www.bentshapes.com)

- Danish pop group consisting of Simon, Kristian and Martin. (http://www.myspace.com/girlfriendsmusic)

-Two piece pop punk band which formed in 2020 featuring Travis Mills & Nick Gross of Goldfinger. Their self-titled debut album was released on October 23rd, 2020 featuring 14 tracks. It was produced by John Feldmann. {https://girlfriendsxo.com) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Girlfriends">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
