At least two artists go by the name Black Jade:

01) A Dub Reggae producer from the 1970's who put out two full-length albums including the rare 1975 album Contempo and the even more rare Rockers LP and a series of singles.

02) Black metal band from Switzerland (Zeiningen). Now is one-man project of Naragarth. 

Discography:
- 2001 - Holocaust 666 (demo)
- 2002 - Forest of Edoras
- 2005 - ...Of Forest And Fire...
- 2007 - Helvetica Diabolica
