Currituck Co. is American musician Kevin Barker (former member of Indie-pop band Aden and sometime member of Vetiver).

His 2002 debut album "Unpacking My Library" was recorded in Chicago and included a member of The Coctails. 

Subsequent releases include Ghost Man on First (2003), Sleepwalks in the Garden of the Deadroom (2005), and Ghostman on Second (2005).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Currituck+Co.">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
