SoniMinoS is a powerful metal act from NJ, the three piece has a heavy and unique sound influenced by everything from Death Metal to Grind Core. Their furious sound rings loud and true on the stage performing in countless shows with bands such as Dying Fetus, Behemoth, Kiss the Cynic, Black Dahlia Murder, and many others. SoniMinoS is currently in the recording studio with plans for a 2007 SLP RECORDS release. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SoniMinoS">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
