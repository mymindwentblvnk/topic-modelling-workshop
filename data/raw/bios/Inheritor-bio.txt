Inheritor is a melodic death metal band from Bogota Colombia, influenced by bands like Opeth, Children of Bodom, Cradle of filth etc; the band was born at the end of 2008; on 2009 inheritor becomes part of stormblaze entertainment (for booking and management) and on 2010 inheritor releases its album debut "form dust n passion". wich is now on sale!!

Actual members:

Angelica Rodriguez - female vocals 
Alejandro Cardenas B. - Guitar/voacls 
Alexander Gerena - Guitar 
Diego Duque - Bass 
Juan Felipe Morales - Bateria
