Sun Airway is a Philadelphia-based project from ex-A-Sides Patrick Marsceill and Jon Bathmus. After releasing a 4-song EP called 'Oh, Naoko,' they released their first full-length, 'Nocturne of Exploded Crystal Chandelier,' on October 26, 2010.
