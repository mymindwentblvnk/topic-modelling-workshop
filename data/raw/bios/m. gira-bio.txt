Born in 1954, Michael Gira is an American musician, author, and artist. He was the main force behind the now dissolved New York City musical group Swans (1982-1997) and currently fronts The Angels of Light. He is also the founder of Young God Records (YGR website).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/M.+Gira">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
