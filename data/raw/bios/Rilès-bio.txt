Rilès Kacimi, born 4 January 1996, better known by his stage name Rilès, is a French rapper, songwriter, composer and record producer from Rouen (FR).

His music video "Brothers" has reached 56 million views and his music video "PESETAS" reached 78 million views on YouTube.

Rilès released multiple EPs, "Vanity Plus Mind" (2015) and "LVL 36" (2020), and an album "WELCOME TO THE JUNGLE" (2019).
in 2016 and in 2021 he decided to release one song per week every Sunday for a year (RILESUNDAYZ).
