There are multiple artists named Macabre Operetta:
1) Macabre Operetta is a black metal band from Greece. Formed in 1995, they released a couple of demos in the mid-90's and an EP in 2003.
2) Macabre Operetta is a ritual black ambient project from Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil, formed by Demortuus. Released the EP "...in luctu iacere..." in 2008, free download at: https://archive.org/details/BPMF26_Macabre_Operetta_in_luctu_iacere_2008 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Macabre+Operetta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
