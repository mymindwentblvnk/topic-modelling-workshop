Yakamoto Kotzuga is the first solo project by Giacomo Mazzucato, class '94, a young producer from Venice, Italy.
He started his musical career playing guitar and bass at the age of 14 with some local bands and when he was 16 he begins to take the first steps in electronic music.
Until now has released his debut EP "Rooms Of Emptiness" via Bad Panda Records, consisting of 4 tracks. He is currently working on a new ep and preparing his live set that will play with a live drummer. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Yakamoto+Kotzuga">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
