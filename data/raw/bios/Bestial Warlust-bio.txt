Bestial Warlust is a violent war metal band that formed out of the death metal band called Corpse Molestation with a member of Hobbs' Angel of Death. They adapted the style of early Scandinavian black metal. The band became known as one of the most extreme bands of Australia. They broke up after having released two albums with the members concentrating on other projects and bands.
