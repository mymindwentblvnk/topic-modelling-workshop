Zoe Wees is a German singer-songwriter. Born in Hamburg in 2002, Wees wrote music since she was a child and a finalist on “The Voice: Kids”. She released her debut single “Control” in 2019, which reached the Top 40 in France and Germany and the Top 10 in Belgium, while follow-up single “Girls Like Us”reached the Top 10 in Germany, Austria and Switzerland. Wees released her debut EP, Golden Wings, in May 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zoe+Wees">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
