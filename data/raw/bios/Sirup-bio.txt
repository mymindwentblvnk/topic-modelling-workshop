1. Sirup is a indie-pop-rock band from Berlin, Germany. It was founded in 1997. The band-members are Tobias Schade (drums), Holger Räsenhöft (bass and vocals) and Sascha Engel (guitar and vocals).

2. Sirup is the name of a project by Osaka-born Japanese singer-songwriter Kyotaro (2) (styled KYOtaro), in which he collaborates with various R&B/hip-hop music creators to make a new sound. The name Sirup (シラップ) is a contraction of Sing and Rap. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sirup">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
