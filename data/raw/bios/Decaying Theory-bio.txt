Decaying Theory is a one-man band from Sri Lanka that focuses on an instrumental approach to Depressive Black Metal, making use of slow, dense progression similar to Doom Metal.
Releases include a demo entitled "Melodies from 7th Feet" and a single-track named "Sylph Beyond Horizon". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Decaying+Theory">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
