Kornél is a DJ and producer born in Budapest and based in Stockholm, Sweden. He runs the label Studio Barnhus together with Axel Boman and Petter Nordkvist . After landing a residency at the illustrious Paradise club in 2005, he became the host of the P3 Dans show on Swedish nation-wide FM radio. Baby Step, Down Since '92, Szikra and Pantalón are some of his most played tracks. In 2016 he released his first long player: "The Bells".  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Korn%C3%A9l+Kov%C3%A1cs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
