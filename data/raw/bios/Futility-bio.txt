Hailing from Canberra, Australia, Futility are a four piece death/doom band formed in mid 2007.

Their first, self-titled album was released in 2009. Their latest offering 'The View From Here' was released in early 2012.

Disbanded early 2013.

Brendan deBear - Bass/Vocals 
Nathan Youngberg - Guitar/Backup Vocals 
Kurt Neist - Guitar
