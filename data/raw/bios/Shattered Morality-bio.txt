Shattered Morality is a hard rock/ alternative band from Brownwood, Tx. The bands latest line-up was started in November of 2006. The bands current line-up is Donnie Evetts - Guitar/Vocals, Chase Harper - Guitar/Scream, Jeremy Boyd - Bass/Keyboard, and Scott Locke - Drums.
