Depressive black metal band from Germany. Solo-project from Neideck  (Todeskult, Trauer and Kerker). Musically, Todessehnsucht can be compared to Trauer.

Released a demo in 2007 called Stillstand. The demo is limited to 100 handnumbered copies and 10 promo copies; numbered as PR/100. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Todessehnsucht">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
