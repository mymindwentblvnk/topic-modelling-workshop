Trendkill Cowboys Rebellion is the one of heavy metal band from Jakarta Indonesia who love PANTERA so much with Philip Anselmo Influence. They formed in 2005. The first EP sold out in 2007 and now still writing a new material for the first full album

Label: Three Chords Systems
Management: Will Judas Iskariot
Members: Will (Vox), Dechonk (Drums), Daffie (Bass), Aminx (Guitar)
