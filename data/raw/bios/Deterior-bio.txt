Deterior is a one-man sludge metal/post-rock/noise band formed around 2004 by Andrew Arnold in North Canton, Ohio (now in Columbus, Ohio).

Since 2007, Deterior has released several EPs, singles, and albums, culminating in 2020's An Offering.
