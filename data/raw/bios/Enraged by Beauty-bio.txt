Enraged By Beauty are a Melodic Death/Metalcore band from Germany. Their releases so far have been a self-titled album in 2006, an EP entitled 'Words Like Poetry' in 2007, and their second album 'Vae Victis' in 2009. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Enraged+By+Beauty">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
