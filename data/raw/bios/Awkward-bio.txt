Awkward is a beat maker, producer, mc and artist from Bristol, England, UK.

A veteran of the Bristol music scene, Awkward also records under the alias of Grand Prize with his production based work for such artists as Open Mike Eagle, Rheteric Ramirez, Machina Muerte, Vertebrae and more.
