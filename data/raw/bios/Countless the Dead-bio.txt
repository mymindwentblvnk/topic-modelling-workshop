Countless the Dead is a melodic death metal band from Lacey, Washington.  They formed in 2012.  They consist of Zach Page (vocals), Jonah Huffines (vocals, guitar), Zach Sanchez (guitars), Willie Jansen (bass) and Jesus James (percussion).  Zach Sanchez left the band at some point, but he returned in 2013.   They are influenced by bands such as The Black Dahlia Murder, The Faceless and Deicide.  The released their debut EP Forgotten and Rotting in 2015.  They are currently not signed to a label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Countless+the+Dead">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
