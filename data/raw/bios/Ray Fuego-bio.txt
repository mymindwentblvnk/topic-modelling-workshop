Ray Fuego (real name: Rayvell Pieternella, born 17 May 1996 in Amsterdam, The Netherlands) is a Dutch musician, rapper, model and activist. Born and raised in Amsterdam's Zuid-Oost district, he is best known as a member of hiphop collective, SMIB, but he also releases a steady stream of music as a solo artist. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ray+Fuego">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
