Horde Casket are a brutal/technical death metal act from Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and Amarillo, Texas that formed in 2007.  Horde Casket released their first record, "Landscape Of Cadavers", through Pathologically Explicit Recordings in November of 2008.  They will be releasing their second Full Length of Album "Slab of Infinite Butchery"   in October or November of 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Horde+Casket">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
