The semi-progessive death metal sideproject of members of Cadaver and Carnage. They only released a demo, and were featured on the Pantalgia compilation along with Cadaver, Therion, Malediction, and diSEMBOWELMENT. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Hydr+Hydr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
