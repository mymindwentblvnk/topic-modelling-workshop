Hailing from the United States of America  (Herndon, Virginia) ,formed in 1991.

Vocals were done by nobody else than Mark Riddick, Guitars by his brother Mike Riddick and Drums done by Erik Sayenga.

They only released two demo's called;

* Food for worms - 1992
Format / Pressing: Cassette (200 copies)
Label / Year: self-produced - 1992
Sick and gorey death metal with slight industrial tonalities.

* Convulsing Eviscerations - 1993
Format / Pressing: Cassette (500 copies)
Label / Year: self-produced - 1993
