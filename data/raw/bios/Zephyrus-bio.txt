1 - A group of singers, founded and lead by director Dr. Paul Walker from 1991 to 2011, with the aim of bringing the treasures of of Medieval, Renaissance, and early Baroque music to a wider audience. Its members come from a wide variety of academic and professional backgrounds, but share a love of chamber singing and a commitment to excellence in performance.
In August, 2011 Megan Sharp was named Music Director.
