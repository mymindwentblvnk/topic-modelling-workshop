Death's Bride was a death metal band from Hoeselt (Limburg), Belgium that existed from 1991 til 2001. They released 3 demo's and 1 split; Demo '95, Carpe Mortem '97, The Milennium and the 'Unleash Your Dark Side' split. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/death%27s+bride">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
