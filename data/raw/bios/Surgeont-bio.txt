Surgeont is a Guttural slamming brutal death metal band from Nizhny Novgorod, Russia. They formed in 2005 by Sapog (Vocals) , Meshok  (Guitars),  and Schtolz (Guitars). The music is rich russian guttural slam in vein of devourment, Disgorge (US) and Katalepsy. Changed their name to 7 H.Target in 2010. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Surgeont">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
