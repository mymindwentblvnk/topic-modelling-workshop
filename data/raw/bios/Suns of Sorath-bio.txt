Suns of Sorath is a metal band from Denver, Colorado, USA, formed in May 2009. 

Suns of Sorath writes progressive metal laden with astrological and occult themes. Their sound is a mesh of black metal, death metal, and other musical elements. They released their first full-length, Flowers of the Lily, in 2017.
