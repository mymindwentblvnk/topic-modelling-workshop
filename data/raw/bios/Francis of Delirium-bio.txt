Led by 18-year-old prodigy Jana Bahrich, the duo completed by Chris Hewett, 30 years Jana’s senior, make songs that communicate Jana’s intense passion to connect with people intimately.A fascinating mix of 90s grunge and millennial DIY, Luxembourg-based duo Francis of Delirium signed to Dalliance Recordings following the success of debut single ‘Quit Fucking Around’.  Jana and Chris record everything at Chris’ home studio Sonic Temple Studios.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Francis+of+Delirium">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
