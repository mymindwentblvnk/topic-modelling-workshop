Mental Transformation is a band comming from a small town named Salo in Finland. The two who started Mental Transformation was Tuukka Frank, drummer, and Matti Malminen, lead guitarist. 

Mental Tranformation labels themselves as "ChaosMetal", being a very small genre within the metal scene. 

Today the band alsow consists of Kari Laaksonen,  Pasi Virtanen and Larry Lahti.
