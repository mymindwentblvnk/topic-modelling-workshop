1) Auryn is a five-member boy band made up of Álvaro Gango (born 2 October 1989), Blas Cantó (born 26 October 1991), Carlos Marco (born 4 February 1991), Dani Fernández (born 11 December 1991), David Lafuente (born 3 May 1988)  formed in the summer of 2009 in Spain. It was announced through social networks and participation in various TV programs. The five members of the group are vocalists, and authors of most of his subjects. They have been recognized with numerous awards including Spanish group revelation of the decade, and is currently the most successful Spanish boyband has reaped in history

To read more, visit their official website: http://www.aurynweb.com.

2) Auryn is a crust punk band from Pittsburgh, PA.

3) Auryn was a black metal band from Salzburg, Austria. They released the "My Solitary Dreamland" demo in 1995 under the Dreamforce label before splitting up.
