Lanzer is a heavy metal band from Sweden and Germany. Many of their albums contain different guests like Kai Hansen (Helloween, Iron Savior, Gamma Ray), Dirk Schlachter (Gamma Ray), Sascha Paeth (Heavens Gate) or Chris Bay (Freedom Call). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lanzer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
