Thumpers (often stylised as THUMPERS) were an indie pop duo based in London and formed in late 2011, comprising of Marcus Pepperell on vocals, guitars, pianos and keyboards and John Hamson Jr on vocals, bass and drums. The band announced on 15 February 2018 that they were splitting up, with their Lexington show being their last one on 23 February 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thumpers">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
