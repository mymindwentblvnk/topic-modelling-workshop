McArdle is a former member of the Mendoza Line. She left the group in 2007, coinciding with her divorce from fellow member Timothy Bracy. "Summer of the Whore" is her first solo album and chronicles her emotions dealing with the divorce. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shannon+McArdle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
