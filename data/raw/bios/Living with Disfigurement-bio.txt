Living With Disfigurement is a Death Metal band from England (London) formed in 2006.

Band Members:
~ Eddie Spengler (Guitar / Vocals)
~ Kiran Lakhotia (Drums)
~ Tom McKitrick (Guitar)
~ Ed Weech (Bass)

Discography:
~ Living With Disfigurement (Demo) 2006
~ Thrill To The Terror of Death  (EP) 2009
