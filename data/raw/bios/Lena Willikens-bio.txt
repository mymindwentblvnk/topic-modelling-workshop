Lena Willikens is a Cologne based musician and DJ. For the past five years, she has been in charge of long and sweaty Friday night parties at Düsseldorf’s free-spirited club/bar Salon Des Amateurs. As a DJ, radio broadcaster, producer, and Theremin player, Lena is never committed to one style, all is welcome as long as it is twisted the in a one-of-a-kind way. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lena+Willikens">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
