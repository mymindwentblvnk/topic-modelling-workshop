There are multiple artists that go by the name Rilla.

1. Rilla is a Japanese electronic producer. Born in Fukuoka, he is currently based in Kyoto. In 2022 he released his debut album "Yugeki / 遊撃 EP" on the Svbkvlt label, featuring remixes by Howie Lee, Osheyack and Hyph11E.
