Elitsa Alexandrova, also known as Elitsa Yovcheva, is a sound designer and composer working at Ubisoft Sofia, Bulgaria. She was the main composer on the soundtrack of Assassin's Creed: Rogue.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Elitsa+Alexandrova">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
