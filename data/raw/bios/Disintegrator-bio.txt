Disintegrator is one of several successful collaborations between Oliver Chesler and John Selway. They have both since their cooperative work done a career as solo artists and still do techno stuff today. Oliver Chesler’s alias The Horrist had great success with the re-release and remixes of his “One Night In NYC”. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Disintegrator">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
