Whispersorrow (also Psithiros), is a band from Hellas (Greece), formed in the middle of 2010 (April), under the name ''Psithiros'' and is currently based in Thessaloniki. In 2011 Psithiros and Preacher formed a band called Extinction is Forever. Under that name a self-titled album was recorded but never released. Soon the band became inactive and Psithiros took the decision to bring back his personal project. Psithiros changed the name to Whispersorrow. Main influences of Whispersorrow are (Raw / Pagan / Depressive / NS / Atmospheric) Black Metal, (Funeral) Doom Metal, Dark Ambient and also Noise, Punk and Experimental music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Whispersorrow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
