Aenygmist is a symphonic death/black metal band from Drummondville, Quebec, Canada. The band is currently writing music while searching for a vocalist and bassist.

Line-up:
Marie-Audrée Houle - Guitar
Roxane Labonté - Guitar
Jessica Jutras - Keyboards
