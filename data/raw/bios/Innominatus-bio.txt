Innominatus 
Black Metal (Russia, Voronezh)

Founded in 2009 by Deymos (guitars, lyrics) and Sordes (drums, vox). After a cycle of rehearsals all activity was frozen until 2012. At this year Semideus (second guitar) joins to the the project, also new tracks was composed. In the end of 2013  was made decision to record and publish all of material. All work was finished in last days of 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Innominatus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
