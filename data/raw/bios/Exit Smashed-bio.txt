EXIT SMASHED is a five-piece Thrashmetal-Band from Hamburg, Germany; formed as a follow-up project of the band ONLY DEATH DECIDES in 2012.

Band Members:
Rotten Rolf: Voice
Bud: Strings R/L
Joszek: Strings L/R
Pad: Lead Bass
Ken: Drums
