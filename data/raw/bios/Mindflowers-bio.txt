Mindflowers is an hungarian band formed in 2000.
They fuse instrumental prog rock with jazz fusion. Similar in sound to Gordian Knot and Liquid Tension Experiment but with his own style.


Actual members:

Balázs SZENDÕFI - bass, stick
Zoltán SZENTPÁL - guitar
Gergely GÁSPÁR - drums
Bubenyák ZOLTÁN - keyboards

Former members:
Zsolt NAGY - keyboards
