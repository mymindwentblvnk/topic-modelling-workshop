Hatriot is an American thrash-metal band founded by Steve "Zetro" Souza (Exodus, Tenet, Dublin Death Patrol) in 2010. The other members of the band are guitar players Kosta Varvatakis and Miguel Esparza and Steve Souza 's sons Nick (drums) and Cody (bass).
