Real Name:Peter Kremeier
Profile:Losoul, member of Germany's Playhouse label, has great respect for jazz, funk, and soul. Besides their intensity and "explosiveness", he especially admires their ability to unite rhythm and groove with melody, emotion and dramaturgy in view of, however kitsch it might sound, a better world in which peace and freedom, love and friendship prevail. Beauty, finesse, emotion, abstraction: his is gripping groovy music that soothes the soul..
