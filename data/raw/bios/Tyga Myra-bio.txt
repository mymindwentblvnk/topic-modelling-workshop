Tyga Myra was a Heavy Metal/Late NWOBHM act out of Worcester. Their entire catlogue consists of one lonely full-length released in 1986 - "Deliverance". After forming in 1984, the band were awarded a record deal with Ebony and recorded their debut, only to break up not long after. Several of the band members have featured in other NWOBHM acts.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tyga+Myra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
