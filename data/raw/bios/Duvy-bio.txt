Dubbed the only female solo gothic artist in Oklahoma City, the electric songstress, Duvy, creates music that ranges from gothic synthpop to alternative rock. Her music has appeared on several low budget films, video games, and is currently making the rounds on internet radio stations all over the world.

She is currently unsigned.
