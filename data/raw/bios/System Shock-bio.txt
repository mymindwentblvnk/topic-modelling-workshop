1) System Shock is a melodic death metal band from Sweden, formed in 2003 in Norrkoping. Their musical style derives from earlier Gothenburg school with great melodic riffing, rough vocals and with lots of keyboard support.
Debut album Arctic Inside was released in 2004 by New Aeon Media. Second album in 2006 by Mausoleum Records. Third album expected fall 2008.

Current line-up:
Dimitris Ioakimoglou - Vocals (also in Anorimoi)
Lukas Bergis - Guitars 
Jonas Wennerqvist - Guitars
Olle Sundfeldt - Bass
Slathis Cassios - Keyboards
Kim Gustavsson - Drums
