Burning Rain is a band formed by guitarist Doug Aldrich (Whitesnake, Bad Moon Rising, Lion, Dio) and singer Keith St. John (ex Medicine Wheel) in 1998. Joining the group were drummer Alex Makarovich (ex Steelheart) and Ian Mayo (ex Hericane Alice, Bangalore Choir) on bass. They have released three albums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Burning+Rain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
