There are multiple artists who perform under the name City Girls:

1. City Girls is an American hip hop duo consisting of Yung Miami (pronounced "Young Miami") and JT. The duo originate from Miami, Florida, and garnered attention after making an uncredited guest appearance on "In My Feelings," the 2018 chart-topping single by Drake. They are signed to Quality Control Music and have released one mixtape, 2018's Period, and two studio albums: 2018's Girl Code, which spawned the platinum-certified singles "Twerk" and "Act Up"; and 2020's City on Lock.

2. City Girls was a UK pop girl group (originally named "Stiletto"). The founding members are: Sian Charlesworth, Sheena Rowe, Sophie Leniston and Rebecca Doran. They released their first single in 2008, titled O.M.G.. The single reached number 3 in the UK Music Week Pop/Club Charts and reached No. 40 in the Bulgarian chart. In the beginning of 2009, Charlesworth and Doran left the group and were replaced by Claudia Evans. They ony released one album - Debut.

3. City Girls was a duo group from Tawain/East Asia in the 1980s. They have released 6 albums from 1986-1989 and one compilation album in 1990. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/City+Girls">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
