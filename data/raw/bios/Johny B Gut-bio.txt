A young band from Lviv, Ukraine.
«Johny B Gut» was formed  initially in 2010. Final lineup was formed only in the winter of 2012 and then the band started to work on recording material. Guys from «Johny B Gut» were playing southern rock/stoner, but now they turns more and more into direction of traditional doom.

The band has now their first release:
Split EP with ukrainian stoner doom band Bomg
http://soundcloud.com/brutal_hamster
