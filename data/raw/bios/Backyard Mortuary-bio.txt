Backyard Mortuary were a Death Metal band from Sydney, Australia the lineup consists of:
Chris Archer - vocals (formerly drums also)
Dave Thomas - guitar
Stuart Prickett - Guitar (also in Bludgeoner, Decayed Divinity, Elysium (Aus))
Bianca Jamett - bass
