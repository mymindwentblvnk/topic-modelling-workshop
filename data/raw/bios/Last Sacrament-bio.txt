Last Sacrament is a Florida Death Metal band and one of the first metal bands to explore metatonal scales using guitars with unconventional fretting. Began in 2007 by Ron Sword and Mike Maletta. The band currently uses 16, 26, and 31 tone guitars. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Last+Sacrament">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
