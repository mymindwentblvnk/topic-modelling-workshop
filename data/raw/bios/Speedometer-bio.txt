There are multiple artists under this name: 
1) Japanese downtempo artist Jun Takeyama (discogs). 
2) a deep funk band (discogs) originating from the South East of England. Speedometer have four albums of big band brass-section-heavy old school funk to their name. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Speedometer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
