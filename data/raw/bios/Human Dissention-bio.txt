Human Dissention is a blackened death metal / grindcore band from Rochester, New York.  They formed in the year 2014.  Tyrant is the vocalist/guitarist.  Conqueror is the bassist.  Torturer is the drummer.  They released an EP titled Downfall in 2015.  Their lyrical themes include apocalypse, death and suffering.
