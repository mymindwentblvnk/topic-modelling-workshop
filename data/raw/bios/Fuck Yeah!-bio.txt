Fuck Yeah! is a hardcore/punk band from the Northeast of England. The band formed towards the end of 2006 and were known as Stay Bloodthirsty until the end of 2007 when they changed the name to Fuck Yeah!

The band's original line-up was Gary, Lee, Ben, Greg and Gareth but towards the end of 2007 guitarist Ben decided to leave the band and was replaced by Jonny. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fuck+Yeah%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
