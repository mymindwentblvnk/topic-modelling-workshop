There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Stench was a death metal band from Arvika, Sweden formed in 2007 by two members of Tribulation with vocalist Mikael Pettersson. They released two EPs, Reborn In Morbidity (2009) & Stench (2014) and two albums, In Putrescence (2010) & Venture (2014). Split up March 2015.


2) Stench was a Punk band from Wolverhampton, United Kingdom. Stench existed from 1981-1984 in Wolverhampton, UK. Hated by most but loved by the privileged few. You can read the full whack on the bands career in The Burning Britain punk book or better still visit the webpage listed on your left. They have an album called 'CULT STATUS' of remixed unreleased material from the early 80's coming out on Pure Punk Records in FEBRUARY 2008.
