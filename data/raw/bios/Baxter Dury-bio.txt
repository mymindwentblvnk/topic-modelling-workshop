Baxter Dury, son of the late Ian Dury, is an indie-rock singer-songwriter.

In the summer of 2001 Baxter Dury released his debut single, the "Oscar Brown EP". The single picked up rave reviews, which included 'Single of the Week' in NME. It also achieved 'Single of the Week' in the Guardian. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Baxter+Dury">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
