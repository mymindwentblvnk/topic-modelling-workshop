There are three artists that go by the name of Sabnack:

1. Sabnack is a side project of Scorpios Androctonus (Crimson Moon, Akrabu, Demoncy) playing an original mix of ritualistic music and black metal. The lyrical theme is mainly religious (medieval devil worshipping). The full-length Ars Notoria was released in 2007.


2. Sabnack is japanese hardcore/black metal band.
