Slaktare is a melancholic Black Metal Band from Germany. Their first Demo "Misanthropische Isolation" (lim. to 50 copys only) was released in  spring 2006. After a few Line-up changes, now they're working on their second Demo "In Gedanken versunken". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slaktare">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
