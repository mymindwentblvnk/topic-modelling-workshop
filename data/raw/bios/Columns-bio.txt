There is more than one group that goes by the name Columns.

1. Columns is a death metal/grindcore band from North Carolina, featuring members from the bands Glass Casket, One Another, and Hemdale.

2. Columns is a project between Graham (Full Grown Spiders, Tea First Records) and Christopher Glenn (Ghost Box Orchestra) based in Somerville, Massachusetts. Electronic ambient drone.
