Dead Ideas were a hardcore band from Belgrade, (ex)Yugoslavia. They were formed in 1991 and at that time they were the only band with female vocals and the first d.i.y. band that gained some bigger attention of public and media. Dead Ideas played in several anti-war fests in Yugoslavia and Italy (Rovigo, July 1994.), they also toured in Slovenia and Greece. Some members of DI later formed reggae core band called Eyesburn.
