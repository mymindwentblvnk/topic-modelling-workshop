Ryan Dupree is a DJ/Live Act/Producer and Remixer. His homebase is "Stil vor Talent" and he has a lot of releases/remixes on many different labels. He was in few times in the charts of djshop.de and beatport.com. Right now he is in the Indie Dance/Nu Disco Charts with his new EP on "Stil vor Talent" , with a huge remix by Teenage Mutants. And big support from Oliver Koletzki, Kellerkind, Björn Störig, Andre Quasar and many more. He has a lot of gigs within germany, but he also has a lot of attention in other countries, where his music is playing from many different DJ's. So it is only a matter of time, that he will play around the world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ryan+Dupree">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
