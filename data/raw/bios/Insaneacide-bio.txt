Insaneacide was a 5 man thrash band from Salt Lake City, Utah.  Not much is known of them other than the release of a demo entitled "A Multi-Crucial Faze" in 1988. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Insaneacide">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
