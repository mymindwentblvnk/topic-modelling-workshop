Thy Repentance was an atmospheric black metal band from Ekaterinburg, Russia. Formed in 1993 and active until 2003, Thy Repentance created misanthropic atmospheric black metal inspired by the great Ural mountains, nature and shamanism. During the existence of the band they released two full-lengths, three demos and participated on a split record. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Thy+Repentance">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
