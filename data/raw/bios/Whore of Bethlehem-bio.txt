A US based brutal death / black metal band formed in 2009. Their first demo "The Crowning Moment" was released in 2012. Some of the members are also known from bands like Disfigured and Scattered Remains. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Whore+of+Bethlehem">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
