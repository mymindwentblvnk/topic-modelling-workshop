Aitch is an independent British rapper and songwriter hailing from an area called New Moston in North Manchester. He started his musical career like many others, freestyling with his group of friends on the school yard. His skippy flow and crafty lyrics made him stand out amongst peers which then led him to increase his notoriety by releasing a freestyle on YouTube which generated over 100,000 views within 3 months. In Aitch's short career he has already opened up for UK greats like Wiley and Cadet on their respective UK Tours, this has seen many tag him as Manchester's Golden Boy with him still just being 18 years old. The future is very bright for Aitch with his astuteness in many different genres such as grime, rap, hip-hop and even drum & bass, he will surely be an undeniable force to be reckoned with in the coming years. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aitch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
