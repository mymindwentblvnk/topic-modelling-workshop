Formed by Chris Gillespie (ex-Fallen Fate) in 2010.
Based in Middlesborough and named after a former noteworthy football club from the same town.
After settling on a lineup in 2014 they released the already recorded EP 'No Hope' for free download (featuring Gillespie on all instruments) and started gigging.
