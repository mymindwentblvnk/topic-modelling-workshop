Shordie Shordie is a Baltimore artist who is part of the rap trio Peso Da Mafia (which found success with songs such as “Money Man” and “Winning.”) His unique voice and flow, along with his hypnotizing, catchy choruses earned him the nickname “Captain Hook”. In December 2018, Shordie Shordie released his debut mixtape Captain Hook, which featured his biggest song yet “Bitchuary.” <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shordie+Shordie">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
