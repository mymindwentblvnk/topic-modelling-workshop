Born January 8, 1995, Ryan Destiny Irons is an American singer and actress. While she had been working in the music industry since childhood, Destiny first rose to fame as a member of the girl-group Love Dollhouse in 2013. Her smooth yet powerful vocals were noticed primarily in the Fox television musical drama Star.

In 2018, she released her debut single “The Same”, followed by a remix with Tobi Lou in 2019.
