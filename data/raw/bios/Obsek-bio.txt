Band originating from Evain, Abitibi, Obsek started as a Death Metal band. However, it underwent a major line-up change during the two last years and became a Melodic Deathcore band, joining the melodicity of Death Metal and the intensity of Hardcore.

Their music recieved many influences from bands such as The Black Dahlia Murder, From First to Last, From Autumn to Ashes, August Burns Red, All Shall Perish, Whitechapel, Parkway Drive, Winter Solstice, Symphony in Peril, The Dillinger Escape Plan, As Blood Runs Black, Waking The Cadaver, Job For A Cowboy ... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Obsek">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
