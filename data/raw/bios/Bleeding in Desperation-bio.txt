Bleeding In Desperation is a young Band from Göttingen (Germany), formed in 2007. Their music ranges from melodic death metal to metalcore. Due to various changes and problems within the band BID are now ready to go with a new shouter and a 4-Song-EP.

Bleeding In Desperation disbanded in late 2010. They will continue making music as REVERENCE LOST.
