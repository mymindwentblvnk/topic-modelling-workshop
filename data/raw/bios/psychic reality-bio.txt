Psychic Reality is Bay Area musician Leyna Noel Tilbor. She released a split LP with LA Vampires in 2010; her debut album, Vibrant New Age, in 2011; and her second, Chassis, in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychic+Reality">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
