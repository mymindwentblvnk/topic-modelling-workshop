BURNING LEGION was founded in early 2003 to make fast death/thrash metal with progressive melodies. The band has had a lot of problems with bass players, because they couldn't find anyone for this kind of genre. After a lot of line-up changes, the right bass player had came and transformed their music in the style they are playing today. After a lot of live performances around in various clubs and open-air concerts, they decided to prepare material for their debute album. Now in 2007, BURNING LEGION have self-released their EP Controlling Existence and are prepared to unleash the »flame« on earth.
