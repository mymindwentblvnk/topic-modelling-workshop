Dammaj is a Heavy metal band from San Francisco, California. Founded July 13, 1981  and split-up in 1987. They  reunited in 2010, although there were a few one-off reunions in between. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dammaj">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
