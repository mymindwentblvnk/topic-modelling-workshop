Mouthus is the Brooklyn based duo of Brian Sullivan and Nate Nelson.  Super psyche-snarled feedback guitar and overload drum squall with a hep sick-wave edge. A cross of Manowar and New York dustbomb detonation. Total surf skum blooze drone.
