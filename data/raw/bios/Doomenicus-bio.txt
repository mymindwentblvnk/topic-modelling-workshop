Doom Metal band from Foggia, Italy. It started as a one-man band in 2001, but as of 2009 there are 4 band members. They play Traditional/Epic Doom Metal, in the style of Doomsword and Solitude Aeturnus, but at a slower pace.

Discography:
2001 Suffering of Souls (demo)
2007 Promo
2009 Sacred (full album)
