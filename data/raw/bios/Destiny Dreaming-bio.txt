Destiny Dreaming were German progressive metal band, split-up after first album Water Breaks Stone, known before as Synergetic Diversity with one LP Gallery. Sascha Kratzer (V), Andreas Essig (G), Danny Fundinger (G), Sascha D'Agnano (B), Marco Mueller (D). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Destiny+Dreaming">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
