There is more than one artist with this name. 

1) Devour is a hardcore punk band from Raleigh, North Carolina. The band features former and current members of Cross Laws, Gnosis, the Drowsies, Mold, Logic Problem and others. They formed in August 2007 after two of the members' previous group, Cross Laws, disbanded. In September 2008 they released an LP/CD on North Carolina's Sorry State Records.
