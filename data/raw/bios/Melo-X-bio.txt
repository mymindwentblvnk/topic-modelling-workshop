MeLo-X also known as Mustafa was born in Flatbush Brooklyn NY. He is a emcee, DJ, producer, audio engineer, writer, photographer, event/party planner and promoter. He has become a staple in NYC night life and is making a stamp as the new renaissance man of our time. He has worked with countless artists, DJs and promoters and now he DJs for Mickey Factz.
