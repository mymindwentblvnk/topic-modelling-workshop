Sweden Heavy Metal band formed in 1980 and split-up (?) around the 1983-85.
Reformed during 2004 to cut the track 'I Want The Metal Back'. Active again now.

Lissa (Anders Listaniels) - Vocals
Torben Stenberg - Guitar
Håkan Göras - Guitar
Lars Törnblom - Bass
