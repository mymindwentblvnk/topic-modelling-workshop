Natalie Belle Bergman (born February 7, 1989), known professionally as Natalie Bergman is an American singer and songwriter. She has already had a lengthy, successful career as one half of the brother-sister duo Wild Belle. In her debut solo album called "Mercy" released by Third Man Records, she lays it all bare, singing of pain, tragedy, redemption and heal. The album comes after the loss of her father, leading her to retreat to a monastery in Chama Valley. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Natalie+Bergman">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
