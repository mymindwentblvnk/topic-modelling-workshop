The Spellbinders were a New Jersey soul group.  The group made a string of outstanding singles for Columbia and Date between 1965 and 1967, all with the involvement of soul genius Van McCoy.

The group broke up in 1967, with female lead Eloise Pennington moving on to join the Ad Libs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Spellbinders">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
