Thy Hastur is a Melodic Black Betal band, formed in 2000, in Namysłów, Poland.
Current members:
Mordor - Guitar, Drums (programming);
Kawa - Keybords;
Argh - Vocals;

Discography:
- "He Has Come..." - Demo, released in 2004; (with Gregor - bass);
- "Promo CD 2005" - released in 2005;
