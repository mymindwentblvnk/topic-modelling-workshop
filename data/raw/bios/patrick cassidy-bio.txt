Patrick Cassidy (born 1956 in Claremorris, County Mayo) is an Irish classical composer.

He has worked in many various formats, including TV and films. In 2004, he co-wrote the album Immortal Memory with Lisa Gerrard from Dead Can Dance. His aria "Vide Cor Meum" was used in the films Hannibal (2001) and Kingdom of Heaven (2005).
