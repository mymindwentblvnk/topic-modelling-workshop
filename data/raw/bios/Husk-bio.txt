There is more than one artist named Husk:

1. An experimental drone band.

2. An Australian progressive rock band. MySpace.

3. HUSK is an aggressive 4-piece band originating from Oslo, Norway. Although it's core members M. Jozefowicz (vocals) and R.C. Hamilton (guitars) hail from Botswana and Australia respectively. Rhythm section currently supplied by L. Jensen (drums) of Blodspor and D. Flygh (bass) of Aneurism. Hymns EP (2014): Recorded and mixed by Bjarte Lund Rolland (Kvelertak) at Caliban Studios. Mastered by Alan Douches at West West Side Music. Layout by R.C. Hamilton. 

4. A psychedelic/rock trio from Auckland, New Zealand. Members: Ukiah Brown (vocals/guitar), Robert Bruce (bass), Matt Hutson (drums). Facebook.

5. A sludge/stoner/doom metal quartet from Taunton, Somerset, England that changed their name to Sail. 

6. A one-man black metal band from Italy.
