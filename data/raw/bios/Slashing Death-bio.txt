Slashing Death was a Polish death/thrash metal band formed in 1987. They released 3 demos and a live tape between 1988 and 1990. In 1992 their second and third demos were released together by Carnage Records. In 2004 Apocalypse Prod. released all their material but the first demo on one album. Slashing Death were one of the bigger bands of the 80s Polish death metal scene along with Vader and Imperator. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Slashing+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
