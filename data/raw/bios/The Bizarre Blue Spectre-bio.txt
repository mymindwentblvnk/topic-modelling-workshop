Mix Zaraza with oldscool death metal and quite a bit of Ministry-like industrial metal, and you'll possibly end up with this unholy sound. Ranging from mid to funeral pace and incorporating some odd, but nasty industrial elements, really makes this band deserve the label bizarre. Expect cold, sterile, and utterly negative music. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Bizarre+Blue+Spectre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
