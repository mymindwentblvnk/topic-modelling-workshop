Deadpulse was formed in 2007 with the intention of welding the old and new into a new true shape of deathmetal and with brutal force progressing the swedish metal sound. A selfproduced promo has just been released and this heavy hitting bunch means to gain top position on the international metalscene and create some heavy trendkilling noise for a long long time. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deadpulse">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
