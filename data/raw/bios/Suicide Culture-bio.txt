Suicide Culture was a Thrash Metal band out of Seattle, WA. They are now known as the Black Metal band Funeral Age and have opened for such bands as Exodus, Kreator, and Cannibal Corpse. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Suicide+Culture">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
