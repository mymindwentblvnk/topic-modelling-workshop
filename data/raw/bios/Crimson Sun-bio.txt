Crimson Sun is a melodic metal band from Finland. They released their debut album titled Towards The Light on August 3rd 2015. The album is listenable on Spotify.

Members:
Joni Junnila - guitar
Jukka Jauhiainen - bass
Antti Rantavuo - drums
Miikka Hujanen - keyboards
