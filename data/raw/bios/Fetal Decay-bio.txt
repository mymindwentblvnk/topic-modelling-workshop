Russian technical brutal death metal band from Saratov, formed in 1998.
Their lyrical themes are : Religion, Violence, Hatred, Gore

Current line-up:
Dmitriy Orlov - vocal
Denis Akul`shin - guitar, drum programming
Michail Koroliov - bass
