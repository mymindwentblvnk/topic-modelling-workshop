Addaura is a black metal band from Seattle, Washington, United States, formed in 2008. They have released a self-titled EP in 2010, and a full-length album titled "Burning for the Ancient" in 2012.

Current line-up:
Ryan - vocals, electric and acoustic guitars, synth
Jesse - guitar
Heidi - bass
Brandon - drums
