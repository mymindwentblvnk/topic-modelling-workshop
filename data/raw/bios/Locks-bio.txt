Locks is a British skeletal-blues band based in north London made up of Locks Geary-Griffin (lead vocal / guitar), Mike Byrne (double bass), Marian McClenagan (fiddle) and Andy Marvell (percussion). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Locks">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
