Former mc of the greek underground hip hop group Βήτα Πεις (Vita Peis), who now is on solo carrer. Other than the first three Vita Peis albums in which he participated, he has also published a solo album “Από το στυλό στο χαρτί”. Since he has left his former crew he hasn’t published any albums, however he is featured in other greek mcs’ tracks.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/dask">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
