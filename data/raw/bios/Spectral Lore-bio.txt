Spectral Lore is an ambient black metal project founded in 2005 and based in Athens, Greece.  The band is a side project of Ayloss of Divine Element, and focuses on instrumental and often experimental compositions.  Ayloss has released four albums, three EPs, and six splits. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Spectral+Lore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
