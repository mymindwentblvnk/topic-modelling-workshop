Rise Up is a hardcore band from Sofia, Bulgaria.
Current line up: Vasko - Vocals, Ankata (Redound) - Guitar,  Vanyo (Redound) - Bass,Lubo (Melekh, Empty Face) - Guitar & the new member Alex (Redound) - Drums. In 2007 they released their first EP. It consists of 3 songs and one intro. For more information go to http://myspace.com/riseupbg . <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rise+Up">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
