Brother Ares began jamming together in Nashville in 2011. Their debut EP Devourer of Worlds is due out in 2012. Gore hits hard and slow while Blake flies into space aboard the space shuttle pedalboard. Basically they are the coming of Galactus, led by the Silver Surfer.
