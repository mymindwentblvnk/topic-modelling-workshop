Violate are a four piece metal band from Zagreb (Croatia). 
The band was formed in December 2007.
After some necessary member changes, the band began to evolve their sound. In march of 2009. their first EP "Sick Addiction"  to critical acclaim from various forums and the metal crowd in Croatia.
A blend of old school thrash,death metal with modern groove metal, punk, grunge,everything and anything in between with catchy riffs and melodies make this band stand out.
