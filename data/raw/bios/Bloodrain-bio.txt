The invincible symbiosis of black metal and thrash metal wins another war with the artillery of Bloodrain. Intense and catchy thrash riffing teams up with black metal speed and hostility to deliver the mozer of all black thrash bombs. The roughness of the first album already displays the writing skills and the precision pounding of this carpet bombing interspersed with awesome Slayer, Kreator and Iron Maiden covers. Addictive triplazering headbanger of all headbangers!
