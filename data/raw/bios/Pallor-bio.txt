There are multiple artists named Pallor:
1. Pallor is a sludge metal/grindcore band originating from Asheville, North Carolina. Their music occasionally contains black metal and drum & bass influence. They have released several EPs since 2014.
2. Pallor was a post-metal/blackgaze band from Johnstown, PA, consisting of members Jake Dryzal (vocals, guitar) and Kevin Pribulsky (drums). They recorded their only album in 2014, which was then re-recorded in 2019 before the band split. 
3. Pallor is an indie rock band from Edmonton, Alberta, Canada, led by songwriter Erik Hamilton. They released a demo EP in 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pallor">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
