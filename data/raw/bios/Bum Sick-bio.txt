Bum Sick is a grindcore/death metal band that formed in Bristol, England.  It was formed in 2009 by Amputated bassist Andy Bile.  He goes by the name General Ass Piss and he does everything.  Mr. Poopy Pants does vocals live.  Lyrical themes include scat, sick humor and coprophilia.  No conventional vocals are found in the studio recordings, flatulence noises are tracked into the music.  Bum Sick has one split and two full-length albums.  Bum Sick is not signed to a label. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/BUM+SICK">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
