Formed by Egidio Chirinos, Carnagoth is a old-school influenced Death Metal band from Puerto la Cruz, Venezuela. They have released one four song demo titled "Forced to Suffering" produced by Daniel Luces at Infected Studios. Their current lineup is:

Egidio Chirinos: Vocals/Bass
Enrique Lopez: Guitars
