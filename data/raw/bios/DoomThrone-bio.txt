The completely anonymous Doom outfit DOOMTHRONE issued their 'Skeleton Veiled In Flesh' single with the caveat ""No line-Up. No Contact. No Interviews. No Thanks. FUCK YOU ALL!!!" The band recorded a cover of BATHORY's '13 Candles' for a tribute album entitled 'A Tribute From The Hordes To Bathory' released by Black Goat Productions. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Doomthrone">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
