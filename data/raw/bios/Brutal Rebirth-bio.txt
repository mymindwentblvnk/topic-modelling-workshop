Brutal Rebirth is an old school death metal band from Marseille, France. They formed in  2007 and their current line up is Rotpiet (Vocals/Guitars), Darkhyrys (Backing Vocals/Guitars, Bloodshed, Diamond Eyed Princess), Troma (Bass) and Charognard (Drums, Charnier, Nefarium). They play the old style of death metal in vein of Morbid Angel, Suffocation and others of that line. 

The band released their debut album "Hate Over All" in 2008 by Trendkill Recordings and their second album "Death Row" in 2010 also by Trendkill Recordings.
