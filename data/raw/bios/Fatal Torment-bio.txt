Fatal Torment was a technical death/thrash metal from Finland. They released only one CD EP, image of decay in 1998.

Fatal Torment were also a metal band from St. Just, Cornwall, United Kingdom. They released an EP, The Oath of Atrocity before splitting up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fatal+Torment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
