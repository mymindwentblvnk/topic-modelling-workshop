Steeltrooper, formed in 2008, are a four-piece metal band based in Staffordshire, UK. Bringing to the stage an awesome mix of classic and modern metal styles as well as truck loads of enthusiasm, they hope to bring real metal back to the Staffordshire music scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Steeltrooper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
