Bury Me Deep is a German gothic rock band previously known as Die Fuge.
The band features Michelle Darkness on vocals, also singer of well known band End of Green.

Their first record under this name will be released on June 26th 2009 via Silverdust Records and is called "Nearly Down".

Bury Me Deep are:
Leshi Love (guitar & effects),
Michelle Darkness (vocals & guitar),
Carl Lost (drums) and
Pain Pianowsky (bass). 

Discography:
*(2000) "buried love" as Die Fuge
*(2005) "...sleepless sorrows..." as Die Fuge
