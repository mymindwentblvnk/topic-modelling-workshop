Skylord is a melodic black metal band from Moscow, Russia who's lyrical themes often feature fantasy and vikings. Their style incorporates elements of viking metal, power metal and melodeath, focusing more on bright melody than on riffs. Their first album, Frostcraft, was released March 2019 on Bandcamp. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SKYLORD">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
