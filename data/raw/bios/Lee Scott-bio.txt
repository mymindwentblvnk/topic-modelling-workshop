Lee Scott (Mr. Wrong) is a hip-hop artist from Liverpool, UK. He is part of the hip-hop groups Children of the Damned, Hock Tu Down, Mcabre Brothers and Antiheroes. He is also well known amongst the Don't Flop battle scene.
