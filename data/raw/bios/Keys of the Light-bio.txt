Keys of Light are a symphonic gothic metal band formed in São Paulo in 2002. With themes ranging from spirituality, emotion and nature's elements the band combine the aggression of modern metal music with sublime and ethereal tones such as flute, piano and sweet vocals. They cite influences such as Therion, Nightwish and Epica

Keys of Light released their debut, self-titled album in 2006. The band is composed of five members:

Kethelin Cocchi - pianist, singer and flautist
Grasiele Nunes - bassist
Marcio Kishimoto - guitarist
Felipe Rico - keyboardist and vocalist
Carlão Semprini -drummer
