French band playing an electro style of music, abstract hip hop oriented. They made 3 albums : Abstrackt Keal Agram, Bad Thriller and Cluster Ville.

They also often collaborate with the hip hop band Psykick Lyrikah.

Tanguy Destable, aka Tepr has made three albums by himself. 

Lionel Pires is also at the origin of the project "My dog is Gay" and Fortune.
