Dr. Living Dead were the right band at the right time. No doubt about that. Their self-titled album received fantastic reviews across the board. As a result the band was invited to play the prestigious Rock Hard festival and went down a storm with their brand of Metal/Crossover in the vein of early Anthrax and Suicidal Tendencies. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Dr.+Living+Dead%21">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
