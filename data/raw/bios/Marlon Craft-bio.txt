Marlon Craft, the 29-year-old Hell’s Kitchen native & MC, offers an essential mix of new school oxygen and golden era hip-hop. He made waves in NY’s underground scene showcasing his advanced wordplay and technicality through bar heavy freestyles loaded with poignant messages.
