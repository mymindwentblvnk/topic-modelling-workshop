SWIN were a hard rocking band from Turku, Finland. It was on action between 1997-1999. Members of the band were Tomi Korkiakangas (guitars/vocals), Tomi Ollila (Bass) and Timo Kangasaho (Drums). The group recorded 1 studio demo, 2 four-track demos and one Live demo. Also some rehearsal tapes has been recorded. Group did some gigs. The Group later became known as Enerchy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SWIN">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
