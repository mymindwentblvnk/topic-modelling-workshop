Suffercation is the first death metal band who recorded an album in Malaysia. Formed in 1988 at Pasir Mas, Kelantan, Malaysia. Recorded their first album (Day Of Darkness) in 1992 at King Studio under Valentine Sound Production-VSP, second album in 1993 (Cryptic Existence) and third album (In Sufference) in 1995 also under VSP. Their latest album (As We Breathe) was released in November 2007.
