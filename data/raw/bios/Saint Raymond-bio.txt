Saint Raymond is a musical project of British indie singer Callum Burrows (born in Nottingham, UK). He began his career in 2011, releasing on May, 2013 his breakthrough single "Fall At Your Feet" their debut EP Escapade - EP released one day after the Never Fade Records. The album also yielded another single, "Letting Go" released in January 2014.
