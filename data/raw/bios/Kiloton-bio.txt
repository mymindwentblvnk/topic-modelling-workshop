Two acts are making music as Kiloton:

1) Kiloton is an ambient electronica musician operating from Aalborg, Denmark, who has been active on both the local and international electronica scenes with his sincere and soothing soundscapes since 2001. During the 00's he predominantly presented his work in numerous concerts or collaborations like Rumforskning and Knob a Siesta, but in 2010 he finally released his debut album Language Lost on Urlyd.

2) Kiloton is a US based thrash metal band formed in the late 1990's. They have released two studio albums, The Seeds of Genocide (1998) and Knock the Box (1999).
