There are multiple artists using this name.

1. US death/black metal band
2. Swedish choir
3. UK NWOBHM band
4. Filipino symphonic metal band
5. Russian gothic metal band

1. Sanctus was a death/black metal band from Southern California that existed (approximately) between the years of 1996 and 2004.  They released a full-length album entitled "Aeon Sky" through Metal Blade Records in 2000.  In 2004, Sanctus released a self-financed EP entitled "Fateshifter".  It was soon after that release that the band broke up.  

2. Sanctus is  a choir from the southern part of Sweden. Director is Rebecka Nilsson. Hanna Norman is the previous director.

3. Sanctus  was a NWOBHM band from London, UK formed in 1981. They released two demos:  "Thy Disciples" in 1982 and "Gates of Sorrow" in 1983. They played a couple of gigs and split up in 1983.

4. Sanctus is also a Symphonic Metal band from the Philippines. In 2009, they released an EP entitled "Path To Eternity".
