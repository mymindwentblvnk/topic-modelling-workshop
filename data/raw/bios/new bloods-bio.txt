A post-punk experimental all girl band from Portland, Oregon. Since their first band practice in March 2006, the three ladies who make up the New Bloods have dropped jaws and touched off unruly, spontaneous dance moves across the dark and drizzly basement/house party landscape of Portland. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/New+Bloods">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
