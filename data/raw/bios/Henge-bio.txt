Henge is a British group based in Manchester that plays crossover electronic dance music. Their first release was a five-track EP in July 2017, entitled Cosmic Dross. Henge released their debut album, Attention Earth! (stylised in all capitals), on 19 October 2018.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Henge">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
