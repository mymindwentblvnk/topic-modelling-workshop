Calmsound is a website dedicated to natural sounds, guided meditations and relaxing music. This content is often used to aid sleep, for yoga, Reiki and various other forms of meditation. Calmsound create this content by travelling to beautiful locations around the world and capturing natural ambiences. They also compose original music and blend everything to make tranquil albums for people to download to their mp3 players. Calmsound is available at the iTunes store and is noted for the exceptional sound quality of its audio products.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Calmsound">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
