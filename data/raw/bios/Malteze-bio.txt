Malteze was a brash female fronted melodic metal band with tons of attitude and fire. They released only one album through Active Records in 1990. The band consisted of Barbara Malteze on vocals and keyboards, Masami Fujimaki and Steve Cenker on guitars, Kriss Marxx on bass, and Mike Voss on percussion. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Malteze">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
