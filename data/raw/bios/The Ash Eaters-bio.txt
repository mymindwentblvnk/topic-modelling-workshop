The Ash Eaters is the new band from Umesh Amtey and B. Demiurge of Brown Jenkins fame. Playing out of Austin, Texas, the duo have released a demo named "Cold Hearts" in 2011, an EP "The Cruel Side" in 2011, an EP "Ibn Ghazi" in 2012, a LP "Ruining You", also in 2012, and are currently working on another EP devoted to more traditional, savage black metal as well as writing their second full-length LP.
