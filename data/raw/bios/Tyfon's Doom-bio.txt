Tyfon's Doom is the one-man heavy metal project of Tommi Varsala. He plays a classic style of metal, heavily influenced by many of the seminal metal bands from the 80s like Iron Maiden, Mercyful Fate, and Heavy Load. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tyfon%27s+Doom">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
