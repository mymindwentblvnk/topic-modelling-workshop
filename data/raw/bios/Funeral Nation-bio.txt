Funeral Nation was formed in 1989 in Chicago, Illinois. After two demos, three singles, one EP, one album and a best of-compilation they split up in the mid-90s. Funeral Nation is back and have added a second guitarist. They are set for a tour in Germany starting in February 2014 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Funeral+Nation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
