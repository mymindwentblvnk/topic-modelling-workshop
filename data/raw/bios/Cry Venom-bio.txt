Cry Venom is a neo-powermetal band formed by Jacky Vincent (ex-Falling In Reverse) with bassist Niko Gemini, keyboardist Colton Majors, lead vocalist Aleksey Smirnov and drummer Wyatt Cooper. They released their debut album called Vanquish The Demon on December 6th. 2016.
