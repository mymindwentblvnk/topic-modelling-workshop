Drop Hammer was a thrash metal band from Muskegon, Michigan, United States (formed in 1989), they first released a demo back in 1989 called Stimulation of Your Nerves, and a single called 'Electric Skies' in 1990.

After this they released their first and only full-length called Mind and Body in 1991.

Last known line-up;
Scott Carlson - Vocals (Steel Vengeance)
Tracy Kerbuski - Guitar (Steel Vengeance)
Bruce Danz - Bass
Tom Vallejo - Drums (Steel Vengeance)
