Dissilience was started inSeptember of 2008 in Los Angeles, Ca

 It was started from different members from different bands in the Los Angeles area, including Necronazarene, Rott, Sick and Abysmal Chaosphere. Dissilience plays brutal death metal with both European and American Influences. We are currently hard at work to deliver our debut full length album that will include 2 of the tracks from out Inhuman Deformities E.P. Support your local brutal death metal bands.

The Current Lineup cosists of Luis Zelaya(Guitars, Backing Vocals),
Adrian Lira(Drums), Salvador Ramos(Vocals), Daniel Molina(Bass)
