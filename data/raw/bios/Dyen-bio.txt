DYEN, a 21 year old Techno Producer/DJ based in The Netherlands. Inspired by the 90's Old School Techno beats and the hard industrial sounds of these days.

DYEN started with producing heavy techno beats since 2016, he had several releases on Otomo Records and a Remix on Raven sigh. After these releases he was trying to create a own sound inspiring by the 90's and the uptempo hard industrial techno sound from these days. after his first gig at paris he released an EP on Kataklism Records.

After that his journey began with several releases EP's on Labels like Raven Sigh, Eradys records and Coup, with a lot more to follow soon. DYEN has his own unique sound. his sets are energetic makes the crowds go crazy with his hits. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dyen">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
