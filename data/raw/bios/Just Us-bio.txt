There are multiple artists called Just Us:

1. Jim Valice and John Ford who released The U.S.A. From the Air in 1969.

2. Just Us is an American indie-folk band from Asbury Park, New Jersey. Featuring members Thomas Louis & Jenna Murphy.

In July of 2011, the couple released their first single, 'If You're Happy' to an online fan base to promote the up-coming full length. Being featured on AbsolutePunk's 'Free Music Friday' promotion allowed the band to quickly gain a small following. 

Following the release of the single was the release of a two song free EP in November of 2011. The EP has been well received by multiple music communities. Creating a wide interest in the anticipated 2012 release of their first full length 'Basics'
