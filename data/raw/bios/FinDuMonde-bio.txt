FinDuMonde was formed in 2009. The band consists of former and current members of bands such as Jesus Martyr, Avernal and Embodiment.

During 2010 FinDuMonde recorded their debut album "Licantropia" by Sebastian Manta and themselves in La Nave de Oseberg and Home Studio. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/FinDuMonde">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
