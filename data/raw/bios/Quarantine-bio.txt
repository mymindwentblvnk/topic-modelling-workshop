1) Quarantine, from Scotland, was a melodic punk band with crusty influences and dualsex vocals. Formed by ex-members of influential crust punk bands Disaffect and Sedition. Quarantine played catchy, heavy punk rock with strong melodies, harsh vocals and lyrics focused on personal struggles, rather than politics.

Released “Automatic Negative Thoughts” LP in 1996 on Nabate Records (Belgium), and “Junction 10” 7’’ in 1997 on Panoptic Vision Records.
