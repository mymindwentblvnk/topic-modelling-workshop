Gravehome is a Turkish black metal band formed in 2004 by T (bass), Scytodes (drums) and Adyn (session bass).  Lyrics are mainly about hatred and death.

Last known line-up:
Damnare - Vocals (ex-Mictian (Tur), Wrathfist),
Berserker - Guitars.
