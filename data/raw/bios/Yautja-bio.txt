Yautja is an American mathcore band from Nashville, Tennessee, United States formed in 2010. 

The original lineup:

Shibby Poole (Nameless Cults, ex-As Hell Retreats) - guitars, vocal;
Kayhan Vaziri (ex-Legion, ex-Coliseum) - bass, vocal;
Tyler Coburn (Mutilation Rites, Thou, Gnarwhal, ex-Alraune) - drums, vocal.
