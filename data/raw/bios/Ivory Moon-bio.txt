Ivory Moon are an epic symphonic metal band from Italy, with progressive and gothic influences. The band was formed in Summer 2000 and released their first promotional CD in May 2002. They released their debut album, "On the Edge of Time", in early 2004 through Battle Hymns records. 

The band went through some lineup changes in 2005 before releasing "Human Nature" in 2007. Combining the operatic female vocals of Cecilia Serra and male vocals by Sandro Mancione, Ivory Moon create a diverse and distinct musical sound.
