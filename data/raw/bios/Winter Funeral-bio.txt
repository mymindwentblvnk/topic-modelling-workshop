Winter Funeral was a pagan/satanic black metal from Paris, France found in 1996. Hylgaryss always being the only member of the band released a demo in 1999 and  in 2001 the album titled "Bénis Les Ténèbres, Ô Mon Âme". In 2010, the 2nd full-length "Some Thousand Lies" was released. Winter Funeral has split-up.
