Switzerland (2001 - Present)
Time Grid is a progressive metal band that mixes the different influences of the musicians who composed ( metal, jazz,rock,classic,funk etc. ..) signatures complex rhythm and harmonies with a key pop, which makes their unique music.
Mathias Reusser : vocals and keyboards
Laetitia Fontannaz : vocals
Rémi Poussier : drums
Pierre Sottas : bass
Raphaël Sudan : keyboards
