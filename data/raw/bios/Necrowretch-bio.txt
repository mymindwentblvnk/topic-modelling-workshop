Necrowretch are a death metal band from France, formed in 2008. They released their debut album "Putrid Death Sorcery" in 2013.

Discography:
-Rising from Purulence (Demo) 2009
-Necrollections (Demo) 2010  
-Putrefactive Infestation (EP) 2011
-Ripping Souls of Sinners (Compilation) 2011  
-Now You're in Hell (EP) 2012
-Putrid Death Sorcery(Full-length) 2013
