Maledia is a Gothic Metal band from Rome, Italy, formed in 1996. 

Lineup:

Luana Imperatore - vocals
Daniele Pagnotta - Guitar, Vocals
Manuel Romeo - Bass
Marco Righi - Keyboards
Mauro Antonelli - Drums
