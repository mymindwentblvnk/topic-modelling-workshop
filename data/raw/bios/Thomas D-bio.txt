THOMAS D (Thomas Dürr) is a member of the German Hiphop-legends die fantastischen vier who also acts as a solo-artist and was a member of the German rockband son goku. His most successful songs are: "Liebesbrief", "Rückenwind", "Solo" (feat. nina hagen), "Wish (Komm zu mir)" (feat. franka potente) & "Uns trennt das Leben".
