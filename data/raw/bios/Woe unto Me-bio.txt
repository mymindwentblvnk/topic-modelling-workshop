Woe Unto Me is a funeral doom band from Grodno, Belarus formed around 2008.
They released their debut A Step Into The Water Of Forgetfulness through Solitude Productions in 2014.

current line up consists of:
 
Dzmitry Shchyhlinski - guitars
Artyom Serdyuk - guitars, growl vocals
Sergey Puchok - male clean vocals
Olga Apisheva - keyboards
Ivan Skrundevskiy - bass
Pavel Shmyga - drums
Julia Shimanovskaya - female clean vocals
