There are a few bands by the name of Savagery:

1. Savagery is an unsigned thrash metal band from Chicago, Illinois, USA. It consists of Braulio Correa (vocals/guitar), Byron Arriaga (guitar/backup vocals), and Josue Gutierrez (drums). Their self-released demo "BULLET BELT INVASION" was released in late 2009 and is available for download on their myspace http://www.myspace.com/savagesavagery .

2. Savagery is a brutal death metal band from Wilmington, CA, USA. The band is formed in 2004 and signed to Lacerated Enemy records.
