ATOMIC HEAD borns on october 2004 (Santiago-Chile) when Seb starts with the idea of back to the roots of bay area thrash. Inspired mostly in Megadeth, Metallica and Exodus, starts the composition. SEb meets Luis in bass and in april 2005 Diego joins the band. In november, Luis decides to leave and Atomic Head keep going in their musical way. In march 2006, Luchín and Gabo join the band and a debut CD is now ready, but Gabo decides to quit the band and Hugo is the new bass player. The debut EP was launched with INANNA on november 12, 2006 in bar Trifulka, Santiago, Chile. It has 5 original songs.

After the 2 shows in december with Gestos Grosseiros from Brasil, Diego leaves the band. In march 2007, Hugo is fired and then in may Luchín is replaced by SEb's former guitar student Joaco. In 2008 new blood joins ATOMIC HEAD, Pollo in bass and Juanito in drums. Joaco leaves the band in october 2008.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Atomic+Head">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
