Grand Facade is a death metal band from Wichita, Kansas formed in 1998 consisting of members from Manilla Road, Chapel of the Eye, and Sanctus Infernum. They released a demo in 1998, a full length titled Fields of Thought in 2000, and a self titled disc in 2004. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grand+Facade">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
