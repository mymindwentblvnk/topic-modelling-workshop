Darkemist is a melodic death metal band from Chile,formed in 1995.

The current 2009 line-up includes: 

Juan Luis Burquez - Vocals
Ramon Pasternak - Guitars
Rodrigo Godoy - Guitars
Carlos Espejo - Bass
Fernanda Mánquez - Keyboards
