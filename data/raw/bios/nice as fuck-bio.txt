Nice as Fuck (NAF) are a super group comprised of  Jenny Lewis ( Rilo Kiley), Erica Forster  ( Au Revoir Simone) and Tennesse Thomas ( The Like). They have toured with M Ward and released their debut single "Door" on the 14th June 2016 <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nice+As+Fuck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
