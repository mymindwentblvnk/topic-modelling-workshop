Gun Outfit is an American post-punk band led by guitarists/vocalists Dylan Sharp and Caroline Keith. Formed in 2007 in Olympia, Washington, the band relocated to Los Angeles in 2012.

Originally formed by songwriters Dylan Sharp and Caroline Keith, rough, messy indie rock from Olympia, Washington that could get away with being bestowed as grunge.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gun+Outfit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
