Randy Findell, better known as brakence, is a singer, songwriter, rapper, producer, and multi-instrumentalist from Columbus, OH. As a teenager, brakence began to produce his own music, and after dropping out of college, he began to focus solely on his music career, blending styles of pop, punk, emo, electronic, and hip-hop. He released his label debut punk2 in March 2020, featuring the single "dropout". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/brakence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
