Samael Tears was a gothic black metal band from Udine, Italy, founded in 1998. They released only one album in 1999, titled "Infinity... The Desire".
The band split up in 2004 after the death of vocalist Stefano Tomasetig.

Formation:
Stefano Tomasetig - male vocals
Erika Snidarcig - female vocals
Andrea Boezio - guitars
Gimmi Taboga - bass
Federico Iezzi - keyboards
