Tonic Breed is a Norwegian metal band formed in 2006 in Sarpsborg, Norway. Tonic Breed’s line-up originally consisted of bassist Rudi Golimo, rhythm guitarist and vocalist Patrik Svendsen, and lead guitarist Bjørn Myhren. Last in the line up was Daniel Pettersen. Tonic Breed has been through several line up changes, including Bjørn Myhren, Anders Bekken, Thomas Koksvik and Mats Johansen. The current line up is like the original, but with Jørgen Abrahamsen as lead guitarist.

Tonic Breed has released two studio albums, two singles and four music videos, and forty-four singles. The band has collaborated with Beau Hill on a single release. By Norwegian radio program P3 Pyro, Tonic Breed was picked out as one of Norway's most promising bands of 2014.

Current members:
Patrik Kvalvik Svendsen – lead vocals, rhythm guitar (2006–present)
Daniel Pettersen – drums, percussion (2006–2012 and 2015-present)
Jørgen Abrahamsen – lead guitar (2015–present)
Rudi Golimo – bass, backing vocals (2006–present).

Former members:
Bjørn Myhren – lead guitar, backing vocals (2006–2010)
Anders Bekken – lead guitar, backing vocals (2011–2012)
Thomas Koksvik – lead guitar (2012–2014)
