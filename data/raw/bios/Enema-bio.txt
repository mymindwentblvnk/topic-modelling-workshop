Enema is the name of more than one artist:
1) Polish death/grind band coming partially from Chodziez and partially from Rogozno. 
2) Swedish rock

1) The band was created after split off hc/punk ImOdium and rock/metal band called Face Off (now under the name Dying Reason) which had important fact shaping the composition of the Enema band. The begginings of the band reach the autumn of 2005. Since 2005 the band has released "Kill all humanity" demo EP and "Auschwitz" single. Current Enema is working on 2'nd EP without the name yet.
