Two artists share this name.

Auricle (US)
Auricle is a 3 piece Jazz-fusion Metal band from central Arkansas. The music is laced with jazz inspired metal riffs and jazz sections with free improved solos and free jazz jams. This project has been building its line-up for 3 years, and has finally found its current line-up. A new album should be released in 2011.
