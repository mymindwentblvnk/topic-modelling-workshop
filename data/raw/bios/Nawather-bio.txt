Nawather is a Tunisian Metal Oriental band founded in 2013. They provide us a special kind of music. Apart from their influence by many well-known occidental figures ,they are among the first who choose to marry Qanun, the king of Arabic instruments, to metal music <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nawather">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
