Formerly the solo project of one who refers to himself as "S." The project was formed in mid 2008, heavily influenced by the feel of what some would call depressive black metal and the aggression of more traditional bands, Infernal Dysphoria sought to bring a more aggressive sort of black metal onto the SoCal Black metal scene.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Infernal+Dysphoria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
