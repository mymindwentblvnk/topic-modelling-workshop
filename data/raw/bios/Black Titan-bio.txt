Black Titan is a 4-piece stoner doom rock band formed in 2013 in Mobile, Alabama, USA. They released their self-titled EP in 2014, followed by their debut full length, The Bag Is In The River in 2016. They describe their music as "heavier than a fat chick with a mouth full of pork rinds. They won local awards in 2015 and 2016 for best local underground metal band. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Titan">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
