B.H.P. (Butchered Hose Pipe) was death metal band formed in 1991. in Split, Croatia.
Lyrical themes: Gore, Dark Humor
Line up:
- Migi - bass
- Kike - guitar
- Culio - guitar
- Đaja - vocals
- File - drums
