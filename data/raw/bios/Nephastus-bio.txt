Nephastus was a death/trash metal band from Campina Grande in the province of Paraíba in the northeast of Brazil. Their record, Tortuous Ways, was released on the "Whiplash" label. They were the first heavy metal band of the town and played together with famous bands like Sepultura and Kreator. The line-up in the Tortuous Ways epoch was: Gilberto Jr. (guitar), Fábio Rolim (guitar), David (Bass), Tárcio Bezerra (drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nephastus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
