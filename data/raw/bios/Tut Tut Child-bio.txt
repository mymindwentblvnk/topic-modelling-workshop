Tut Tut Child, real name Nick Kingsley, is a EDM producer and a DJ who lives in London, United Kingdom. His music is based on Electro House, Drumstep, Dubstep, Moombahton, and Drum n Bass and most of his music is released under Monstercat. He also has another project under the alias Forest Knot. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tut+Tut+Child">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
