Misshapen Hatred is a raw black metal band from Luoyang, Henan Province China. To date their are two demos and a spilt cd out. The "Chainsaw Legions" demo from 2007, The "Dark Hateful Visions" split with Baal Realm, & The "Black Tornado" demo from 2009.



Members Include:


R. Chainsaw Vocals, Bass, Drums
