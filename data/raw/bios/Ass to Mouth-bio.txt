Polish grindcore band from city of Wroclaw. Playing since 2004 they have toured with such bands as Ahumado Granujo, Parricide and Squash Bowels. Their mini-cd album "Asses for the Masses" has been released on the 16th of November. Full lenght album is expected in early 2007. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ass+to+Mouth">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
