Dissolved is a Scottish Electronic musician from Glasgow, Scotland.
He makes an eclectic blend of Electronic, drone, ambient and experimental music. Currently based in Vancouver. 
Dissolved has released 4 albums on Daddy Tank Records, 
"Snowy Psychoplasmics" "The Beachcombers Scying MIrror","The Amber Surrealizations" and "Surge of the Lucid"
http://www.daddytank.co.uk/disc.php
Dissolved also self releases on his own Dissolvedamberrooms label.
