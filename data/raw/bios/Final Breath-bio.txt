Final Breath is a thrash/death metal band from Gemünden am Main, Germany. 

Current Line Up:
Jörg Breitenbach - Lead Guitar
Jürgen "Eumel" Aumann - Vocals
Heiko Krumpholz - Drums
Dennis Schneider - Guitar
Dominik Merz - Bass


Jörg and Heiko furthermore organise the open air festival 'Up from the Ground' featuring mainly death and thrash metal acts.
