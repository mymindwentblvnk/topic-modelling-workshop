Nex Carnis was founded in Spring 2012 by guitarist/composer Incruent, and soon after Asto Vidatu joined as the vocalist. First demo of the band, entitled 'Death of the Flesh' was released in September 2012. Their first full-length 'Obscure Visions Of Dark' was released in May 2015. Later in September 2019 they released their first EP entitled 'Black Eternity' on 7" vinyl.
Current Lineup: Incruent - Guitars (2012-present). Asto Vidatu - Vocals (2012-present) See also: Creation Of Darkness.
