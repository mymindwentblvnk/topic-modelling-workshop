Flesh Consumed is a brutal death metal band from USA. They formed in 2003 but have had increasingly difficult relations with members, which have led to four major changes, the only one that has stayed from the beginning being founder Corey Athos (Guitars). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Flesh+Consumed">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
