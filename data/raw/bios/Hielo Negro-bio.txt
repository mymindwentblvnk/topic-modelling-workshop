Hielo Negro are a Stoner Rock band from Punta Arenas, Chile. Formed in 1996, they have released 5 albums so far and have toured around Germany and Sweden.

Hielo Negro are: 

* Marcelo Palma: Guitar. 
* Christian Mc Donald: Drums. 
* Pablo Navarrete: Bass
