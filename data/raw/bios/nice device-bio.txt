Nice Device is a Danish house act consiting of Carsten Kroeyer and Maya Albana.
 In 1993, Nice Device had a big hit in Denmark with the song "Cool Corona", which sold 500.000 copies. 
The band existed from 1992 to 1996 and released "The Album" in 1993 and "Get Inside" in 1995.
