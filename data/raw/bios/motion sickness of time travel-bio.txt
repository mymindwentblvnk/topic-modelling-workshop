Motion Sickness of Time Travel is the solo moniker of Georgia, United States's Rachel Evans, formed in 2008. She has accumulated more than 30 solo releases under the name Motion Sickness of Time Travel on a variety of record, CD and cassette tape labels. 

Rachel also plays in the duo Quiet Evenings with her husband Grant Evans and runs the Hooker Vision label with him as well. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Motion+Sickness+of+Time+Travel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
