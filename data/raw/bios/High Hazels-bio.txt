High Hazels are a quartet from Handsworth in Sheffield,UK who released their debut single Hearts Are Breaking via Heist or Hit Records. The band was formed by James Leesley (Vocals), Scott Howes (Guitars), Paul Barlow (Bass) and Anthony Barlow (Drums) in 2013. They released their debut EP ‘In The Half Light’on the 7th April 2014. In September 2014, the band released the first single Misbehave from their debut self titled album. It was followed by Banging on my door. Valencia was released on April 2015 accompanied by acoustic versions of 'Misbehave', as well as their cover of The Kinks' Waterloo Sunset, first played on BBC Radio 2.
