Mahmod Meta is a progressive metal project from Baghdad, Iraq, which was formed in 2012 by Mahmod Hamasi (Fatalogy, ex-Dog Faced Corpse). Mahmod independently released his instrumental debut full-length Echoes of Babylon the same year. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mahmod+Meta">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
