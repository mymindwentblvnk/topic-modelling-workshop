Celtic Blood is a traditional Pagan Black Metal band from Northern Germany that has been founded by Taakesanger in 2002 under the name Grishnakh. Actually, Taakesanger does not only the vocals but also all instruments, but he is supported by the session drummer Gravelord on some of the releases.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Celtic+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
