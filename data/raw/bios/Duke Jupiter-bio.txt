Duke Jupiter is a US rock band which originated in upstate New York. They were active in the 1970s and 1980s. They are best known for their hit single I'll Drink to You.

After the release of their first album Sweet Cheeks in 1978, Duke started touring with national acts, including, ZZ Top, Stevie Ray Vaughan, David Bowie, Bob Seger, Robert Palmer, B. B.
