Forever Wasted is an ukrainian band, founded in 2008, with intention to play sludge doom metal. Later on, after some changes in the lineup, their music became more varied. On May 1, 2011 a 3-way split with grindcore bands Foible Instinct and Skruta was released by an ukrainian DIY-label Trismus Records. In 2016 the band released debut full-length album and broke up. 

Members:
• Oleg - guitar
• Nikolay - guitar
• Kostya - bass guitar
• Sergey - drums
• Anatolii - vocals

Past Members:
• Sergey - bass/guitar (2008-2011)
• Vadim - drums (2008-2010)
