Péterfy Bori & Love Band is a Hungarian alternative rock band, founded in May 2007. Their debut record, Péterfy Bori & Love Band, was released in the autumn of 2007 and became a gold platinum album.

This was followed in 2009 by the second, 2, and then in 2010, the third album, 2B. Due to Péterfy Bori's pregnancy, between January 2011 and May 2012, the band had a break, but the fourth album, Fehér éjszakák, was released at the end of 2012. Their fifth album, Szédülés, was released in April, 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/P%C3%A9terfy+Bori+&+Love+Band">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
