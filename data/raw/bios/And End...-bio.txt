And End... was a Russian depressive/post-black metal project of Roman Lomovskiy (15/02/1991 - 01/06/2013). The only album, "Between Light and Lies", was released on April 14th, 2013, and can be downloaded for free. Roman was also the guitarist for Inhabitant of Darkness and he had another solo project called Unuseful Life. He took his own life on June 1st, 2013. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/And+End...">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
