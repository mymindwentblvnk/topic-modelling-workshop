Sam Gellaitry is a producer/artist from Stirling, Scotland. 

Self-releasing tracks through his Soundcloud page, Sam accumulated Soundcloud 100,000 followers by mid-2015, despite being only 18 years old. 

He digitally released his EP, ' Escapism' through XL Recordings, on 30 November 2015. Volumes II, III & IV were released in 2016, 2017 & 2021 respectively with IV being released through Parlophone Records.
