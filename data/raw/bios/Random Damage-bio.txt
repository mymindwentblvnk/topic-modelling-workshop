Random Damage is a thrash/speed metal band from Vancouver, Canada.
They released two albums:

Random Damage  (1995)
and
Human Flytrap (1998)

The line up is:

Jonas Falle - Vocals/Guitar (Aragathor)
Dean Doucet (now Dean Boland) - Guitar/ Backing Vocals (Aragathor)
John Prutton - Bass
