There are multiple artists using this name:

1. Black Acid is a hip hop record label from Amsterdam, their debut EP ''Beter Nu Dan Nooit'' released in 2016

2. Black Acid is a New York based band featuring Richard Fearless (formerly of Death in Vegas) and members of Dirty on Purpose, Nymph and Coyote.
