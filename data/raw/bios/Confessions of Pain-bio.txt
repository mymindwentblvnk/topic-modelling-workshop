Confessions Of Pain are a Death Metal band from Austria. Originally called Necron. After the death of Dominik Reiterer in May 2007, the band ceased any activities until February 2008 when it started again with the current name and a new lineup. A self titled EP was released in 2010, and a new album is due in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Confessions+Of+Pain">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
