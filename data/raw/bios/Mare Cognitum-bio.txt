Mare Cognitum is a one-man black metal band conceived by Jacob Buczarski.

Let yourself drift into the unknown edges of the cosmos. The most beautiful thing one can experience is the mysterious. Buczarski has released four full-length albums and three splits. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mare+Cognitum">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
