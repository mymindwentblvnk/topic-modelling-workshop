There are four different bands using the name Abracadabra:

AbracadabrA was an Italian doom metal band, disbanded in 2007; Abracadabra is also a Hungarian band; Abracadabra is a death metal band from Latvia; Abracadabra is an Electronic/Tribal band with a self titled album released in 2001 on the German label Hadshot Haheizar.
