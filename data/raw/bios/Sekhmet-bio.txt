Black metal group from Czech Republic, which draw influences mainly from classical black and thrash metal acts.  Until now, they had released three studio albums (Masova ocista cloveka (Mass purification of human), Okularis infernum and Opus zrudy (Opus of the Beast)) and demo (Pomsta pekelnych legii (Revenge of Hell Legions))

Line-up: Set - Guitar, Vocals
                Ragnar - Drums
                Warlord - Bass
                Nekromancer - quitar
                Abaddon - vocals
