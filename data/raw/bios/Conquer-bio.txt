Conquer is a 5 piece Melodic Hardcore /2-Step/ Deathcore Band from Orlando, Florida. Established in March of 2011 Conquer has quickly grown in popularity all around Florida. Doing whatever they can to spread  the word and reach out to new fans. 

Conquer released their Debut 5 song EP "Buried" in July of 2011 since then they have been traveling all across Florida blowing  away fans with their Electric stage presence and Hard Hitting Breakdowns, you really have to see them live to get the full Conquer Experience.
