Porrada is an oldschool black/death metal band
from Brazil (Vitória, Espírito Santo) which formed in 1991.

Lyrical themes are mostly about Death, Society, Politics

last known line up;
Ozy - Vocals (Thor (Bra))
Halex - Guitar
Wilson - Guitar (Thor (Bra))
Poodle - Bass (Thor (Bra))
Nick - Drums
