There are several artists using this name:

1. Kintaro is an alias used by former The Internet member, Jameel Bruner.

2. Kintaro is one of most famous Nitzhonot artists from Israel. His tracks are released in some rare CD compilations in 1999.

3. Kintaro birthed from the bowels of hell in a summer snow storm. Members of Turbo Fruits, MeeMaw, and Hollywood. Based out of a bag of weed in a Nashville basement.
