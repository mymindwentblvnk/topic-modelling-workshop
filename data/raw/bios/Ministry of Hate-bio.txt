This project based act was formed in New Jersey in 2011.Ministry of Hate features Ron Lipnicki from Overkill. The band was started by Mike Cusumano (Guitars, Vocals) and MJ Malinski (Bass, Vocals), both from 80's thrash outfit, Prowler. Extra guitar work comes from Hades axeman, Scott LePage.

While primarily a Thrash Metal outfit, MOH brings an eclectic & heavy mix that is sure to please all facits of Metaldom! <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ministry+of+Hate">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
