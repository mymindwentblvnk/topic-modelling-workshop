There are multiple artists called Oskorei.

1. A death/black metal band from California. Musically similar to bands such as Amon Amarth and Suidakra. Have released 3 demos as of 2009.

2. A noise project from the UK, since August 2007 based in South Korea but mostly inactive. Oskorei has released two CD-R EPs, two split CD-R EPs, two card-sized CD-R EPs, and one online-only EP. All the non-split releases are now free for download at http://oskoreinoiseindustries.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Oskorei">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
