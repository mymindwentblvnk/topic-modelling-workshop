Long Island Melodic Death Metal, with Thrash &  Black Metal influences.  Featuring former guitar for Long Island's The Ambition, Zbigniew Zelga.  Current drummer, Matt McKasty, was the winner of Summer NAMM 2007 World's Fastest Drummer competition, with a score of 907 strokes per minute for Fastest Feet. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/As+Hell+Broke+Loose">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
