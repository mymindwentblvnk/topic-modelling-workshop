Fields of Locust is a post-metal band from Patras, Greece, formed back in 2008. The lineup consists of Mihalis (guitar and vocals), Ilias (guitar), Thanos (bass) and Nikos (drums). Their debut album Subtopia was released in 2010, which was a self-financed work.
