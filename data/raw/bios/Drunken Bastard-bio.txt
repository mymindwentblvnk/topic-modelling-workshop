Drunken Bastard is a band from Germany (Oldenburg, Lower Saxony/Saxony) who plays Blackened Thrash Punk with lyrics that talk about Alcohol, Satan, Violence, and Sex.

Kenny Killfister - Guitar, Vocals

Hellfried Gottlos - Bass, vocals
