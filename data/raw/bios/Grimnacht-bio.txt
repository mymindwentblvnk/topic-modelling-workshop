Grimnacht is the solo project from K. of Winterfiend and the music half of Skogr. Grimnacht is based out of Cedar Rapids, Iowa USA and focuses on nostalgia, nature and dreams among other themes. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grimnacht">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
