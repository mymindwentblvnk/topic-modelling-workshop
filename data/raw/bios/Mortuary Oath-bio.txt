Mortuary Oath is a death doom metal band formed in LeClaire, Iowa, United States, in 1994. Blakk (Maskim, AngelKill) is responsible for all vocals and instruments.

Mortuary Oath released two albums: "Once Upon a Dreary..." (1996) and "Whispers From Her Grave" (2000), a third album, titled "The Gravedigger's Tale", was supposed to be released, but it has yet to see the black of day. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mortuary+Oath">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
