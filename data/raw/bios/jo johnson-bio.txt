London-based musician Jo Johnson has been writing and performing electronic music since 1999. She also collaborates with Plant43 as VLF. She is a member of the Bleep43 collective and co-runs the Bleep43 record label. She has played a handful of live shows in London and played at the 2008 Bloc weekender in Norfolk.

In the early ninetees, Jo wrote music and played guitar in a number of punk rock bands including Huggy Bear and bass guitar in Blood Sausage. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jo+Johnson">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
