Ensnared comes from Sweden, playing Death Metal in the vein of Morbid Angel, Necrovore and Grotesque. Formerly known as Gravehammer but after their vocalist left the band they changed the name to Ensnared.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ensnared">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
