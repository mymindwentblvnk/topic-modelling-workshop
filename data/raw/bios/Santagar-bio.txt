Santagar is Croatian black metal band  formed  in summer of 2008 by Santagar Betrayed and Lifeless.
Name of band is taken from the fantasy books 
Song of ice and fire written by George R.R. Martin.
Santagar is music inspired by universe, deep and hard melancholy feelings, 
ancient knowledge and medieval. 
Santagar music is escape from modern ugly and wild multicultural  world.
