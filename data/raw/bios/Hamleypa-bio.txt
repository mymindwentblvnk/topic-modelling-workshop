Hamleypa is an atmospheric black metal band from Norderstedt, Germany (nearby Hamburg) formed in 2007. They self-released the EP "Aus Augen wie Tau ein Feuer entfacht" in 2010. They are not signed to any labels. At their  facebook  and   bandcamp  streams and download links for the  2010 EP and more information about the EP can be found, including purchases. Same goes for the new full length release in January 2015, "Im Morgen Von Einst".

Hamleypa is a term in Germanic mythology referring to the skill of shapeshifting.
