MOKO is Diane Nadia Adu-Gyamfia, a Ghanaian-British sould/R&B singer who uploaded her first track Summon The Strength in March 2013. As of 2017, she makes up the female half of London based R&B duo NULA. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Moko">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
