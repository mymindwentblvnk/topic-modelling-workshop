About Vialate
Vialate are a four-piece Kent based melodic metal band. Fusing elements of thrash metal and hard Rock mixed with a heavy melodic edge Vialate have built up a solid fan base throughout Kent through their extensive gigging and energetic live shows.

Drawing influence from bands such as Machinehead, Bullet for My Valentine and Megadeth, expect fast beats and aggresive melodies from the Kent metallers.
