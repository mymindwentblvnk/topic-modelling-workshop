Skincrawler is a solo project of Hellhammer style metal/punk from Northeast Texas. This is a solo band of Elektrokutioner (Decrepitaph, Shrouded, Tombstones, Adulation, Foreboding, Bastard Cross) which started in May of 2008.

Official Releases:
Barbaric Expression (Demo, 2008)
This was a 4 song demo released on tape by Maltkross Records.

Lair of the Foul (Full Length, 2008)
A 10 track CD released November 2008 by Angel'sBlood Records.

More releases are planned for 2009 as well.
