Sower are a Thrash Metal band from Colchester, Essex.  Formed in late 2007, they debuted their music with a sold-out headlining performance on November 29th 2008.  2009 sees Sower supporting such bands as Romeo Must Die and Ignominious Incerceration, and spreading across Essex in search of fresh venues and crowds.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sower">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
