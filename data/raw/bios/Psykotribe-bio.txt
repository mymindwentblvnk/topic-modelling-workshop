Psykotribe hails from the Greater Tampa Bay Area known to most as the Death Metal Capitol of the World.  Psykotribe is the brainchild of vocalist Dana Darkly and was founded in 2008,  Although Psykotribe has seen many lineup changes in the past couple of years the addition of:
Chris Demonoid Lewis on Guitar
Jamez Madness on Guitar
Doctor Z on Bass
and 
Adam Z on Drums
Psykotribe has hit the ground running!
Influences include:
Slipknot
OTEP
Arch Enemy
Slayer
Disturbed
Avenged Sevenfold
DRI
Dead Kennedys
