Some By Sea was an orchestral indie-rock band from Tacoma, Washington.

They self-released one album and one EP on their own label, Kringle Recordings. Their latest album, On Fire! (Igloo), was released on SideCho on March 21, 2006.

The group disbanded on July 24, 2006. Their last show was on August 11th at Hell's Kitchen in Tacoma, WA. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Some+by+Sea">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
