A metal band from Toronto, Canada, formed in 2013 by one of the surviving members of the dissolution of The Illuminati. Their DSBM inspired self-titled debut e.p. combines both depressive elements and themes but retains the driving forces behind more traditional black metal.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Levels+of+Abandonment">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
