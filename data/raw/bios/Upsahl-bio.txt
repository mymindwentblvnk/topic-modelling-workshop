Taylor Upsahl, best known by her stage name Upsahl, is an American singer-songwriter. Upsahl gained popularity through TikTok with the song "Drugs" in 2019 and released  the "Young Life Crisis" EP in 2020. Upsahl's debut album, "Lady Jesus" was released in 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/UPSAHL">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
