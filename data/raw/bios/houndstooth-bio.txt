Houndstooth is a Portland, OR-based rock and roll band whose members include Katie Bernstein, John Gnorski, Courtney Sheedy, Graeme Gibson, and Michael Yun (Brette Marie Way played drums in the band until 2012). Houndstooth formed in 2010 and released a 7" (Francis/Beachbummer) in 2011. Their debut LP "Ride Out The Dark" was released on July 16th 2013 on No Quarter Records. "Canary Island," a Crazy Horse, Brit folk, psych rock-inflected dirge is the first single off of the LP.
