Illustria are a power metal band from São Paulo, Brazil, formed in August 2008. They follow the common themes of love and fantasy.

They feature;

Clarissa Moraes - Vocals
Tito Falaschi - Vocals/Bass 
Pedro Migliacci - Guitar
Lucas Bittencourt - Guitar
Samuel Cirelli - Keyboards
Nina Pará - Drums
