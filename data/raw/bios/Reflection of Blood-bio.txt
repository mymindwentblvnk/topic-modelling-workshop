Reflection of blood is a Russian Doom/Death Metal band formed in 2010.
Members: 
 Andrey Bukhtiyarov-(Guitars)
Alexey Uvarov-(Drums)
Alexey Fedotov-(Vocals)
Konstantin Shchukin-(Bass, Vocals)
Vlad Muzyka-(Lead Guitars)
Alexandra Kovalenko-(Keys, Vocals)

After an Ep [ Отблеск крови-2014 ] and a single [ Память предков-2017 ], Reflection of Blood released their first full-length album [ Peccatum Mortale (Смертные грехи) ] in  August 2nd, 2017. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Reflection+of+Blood">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
