Born in London and now splits her time between the south of France and LA, Mileece creates generative audio inspired by natural forms, mixing her SuperCollider compositions with acoustic instruments and vocals. Her grandfather, as erroneously stated in many places is not Max Mathews, but worked with him in the Bell Labs.
