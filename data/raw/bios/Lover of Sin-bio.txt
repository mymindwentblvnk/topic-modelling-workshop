Led by 'Maitri' who is also bassist/vocalist for Christian Death.  Their first album was released as "Christian Death Presents Lover of Sin" and is often (wrongly) considered to be a Christian Death album.  The lead singer of Christian Death, Valor Kand produced and co-wrote this album. Lover of Sin are currently working on a second album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lover+of+Sin">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
