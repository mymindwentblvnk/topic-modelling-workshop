Forgot is a raw black metal band from Komsomolsk-na-Amure, Russia formed in 1995. The lyrics are about war and misanthropy. Wizard is currently the only member of the band.
Discography:
- Doomed to Exile (Demo, 1996)
- Misanthropy (album, 1997)
- Burning Down (album, 2001)
