Dysmorfectomy is a one man brutal death metal band from Carhaix-Plouguer, France.  The band was formed in 2015 by its only member Anthony Guern (all instruments and vocals).  Dysmorfectomy has released three full-length albums, one demo and one split.  The band is signed with Realityfade Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dysmorfectomy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
