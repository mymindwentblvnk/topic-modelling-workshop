Hailing from Long Island New York The band`s gimmick was that Matt was 16, Harley was 10 and Ken was 12 when they recorded their 1st CD.
Dean J. Adams ("Lord Slayer") contributed lyrics. The Style is Old School Death Metal In Vein Of Old Cannibal Corpse And Deicide. Glen Benton even contributed guest vocals for a track on their debut CD. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/DeathKids">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
