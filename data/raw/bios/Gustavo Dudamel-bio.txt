Gustavo Adolfo Dudamel Ramírez (born January 26, 1981) is a Venezuelan conductor. Born in Barquisimeto Venezuela, he is presently the principal conductor of  Los Angeles Philarmonic Orchestra in the US as well as the Youth Simón Bolívar Orchestra in Venezuela. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Gustavo+Dudamel">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
