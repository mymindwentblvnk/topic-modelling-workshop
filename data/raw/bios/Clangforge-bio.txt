Clangforge is a death metal band from Kamchatka, Russia. Formed in 1998, the band recorded two albums 'Flesh Cacophony' (1999) and 'Poetic licence' (2000). In 2016 Clangforge is back with the new furious album 'Nonetheless'. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Clangforge">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
