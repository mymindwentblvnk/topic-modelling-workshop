Grecco are a Costa Rican Metal band founded in 1996, strongly influenced by Thrash, Death Metal and Progressive Rock.

The first demo of the band is recorded in 1998, one second demo in 2000, but it's definitively consolided in 2005, after the entry of Esteban Villalobos, Rafa Torres and Pablo Granados. Pablo leaves the band to concentrate in the producing of bands, being Grecco the first one, which is recording their first album with him.

The current line-up:

Satanachia - Vocals, Guitar, Guitar Synth
Mauricio Madrigal - Lead Guitar
Rafa Torres - Bass
