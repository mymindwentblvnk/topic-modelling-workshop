Nil Ciuró is a composer and sound designer based in Barcelona.
He creates music and corporate sound for TV, advertising, motiongraphics and short film.
He has been working from his own studio since 2012.
Nil started playing the piano at the age of four and went on to study at The Conservatory of Sant Cugat (Barcelona) as well as other well known music institutes such as Fusió, M.Calduch.
He studied music theory, composition and harmony.
