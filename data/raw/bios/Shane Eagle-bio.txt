Shane Patrick Hughes (born 7 June 1996), best known by his stage name Shane Eagle, is a South African rapper. He is best known for making it as one of the top four contestants on the South African television show Vuzu: The Hustle. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Shane+Eagle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
