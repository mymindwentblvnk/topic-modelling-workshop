Argema is Czech pop-rock music group, which was found in 1982 by six members - Zbyněk Horák, Josef Pavka, Karel Holomáč, Slávek Borovička, Olda Gistr and Jarda Pavelka. The group formation has changed a lot of times. Currently the group consists of five members. The band name was given according to the biggest african night moth - Argema mittrei.
