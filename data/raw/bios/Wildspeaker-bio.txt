Wildspeaker is a Blackened Crust band, founded in 2014, from Texas. Lyrical content includes the resilience of nature, downfalls of human civilization, as well as references to Magic the Gathering. Scalding vocals intensify crushing guitar riffs, bruising bass, and pounding drums. 

Releases:
"Revenge of the Hunted EP" (2014)
"Sylvan Demo" (2015)
"Survey the Wreckage" (2015)
"Tranquil Garden EP" (2016)

Members:
Natalie Kahan - Vocals
Nelson Favela- Guitar
Zak Pitts - Guitar
Michael Munday - Bass 
Ricky Rivera - Drums
