PEEPSHOW is a sleazy glam metal band from Edinburgh, Scotland. They released their debut album Out For Blood in 2008.

Mark that in 2011 the spelling of the band's name changed and is now spelled without a space between the words.

On 9 December 2011 PEEP SHOW launched new website www.peepshowonline.co.uk. There, exclusively for registered users, a free download of the new single Let Go from the upcoming album Brand New Breed is available. The website contains links to all social media sites where you can find PEEP SHOW, including a YouTube channel and a store with official merchandise.
