Culminating in the mind of one unusually gifted 17 year old, is the idea of a new pop music. Veela’s unique take on electropop features infectious hooks, potent dance rhythms and alluring lyrics to stunning effect. Driven and focused, she has written,produced and performed every beat, synth, key and vocal of every song herself. Now gearing up to release her debut EP in the Summer of 2010- Prelude – Veela is ready to make waves in the music world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Veela">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
