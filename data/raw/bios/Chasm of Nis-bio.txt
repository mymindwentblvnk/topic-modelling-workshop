Chasm of Nis is a one-man death metal band from Atlanta, Georgia created by Justin Blake Stubbs aka Ghoat (Encoffination, Father Befouled, Festered, Vomitchapel, Winds of Old, ex-Hills of Sefiroth, ex-Lilitu, Bleeding Cross, Stormhammer) in 2012. 

They have released one demo titled "Redolent Of Spheres" released by Archasm Releasing in 2012 on CD-r.
