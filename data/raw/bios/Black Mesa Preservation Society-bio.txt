Black Mesa Preservation Society is a band from the area of The Hague, The Netherlands. It started in August 2010 and performed on stage for the first time in December 2011. Influences are very varied, ranging from Folk metal to Black metal and everything in between, although they tend do do Melodic Death more often than other styles. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Black+Mesa+Preservation+Society">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
