There are multiple artists with the name Rider:

1)  Rider are a US electric blues rock band, lead by singer-songwriter David Thompson.

2)  Rider are a five piece rock band from Leicester, UK.

3)  Rider are a band from New York City whose members blend classic rock, prog rock, and blues. 

4)  Rider are a UK indie rock band, most known for recording a World Cup related song, England Crazy, with Terry Venables in 2002. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rider">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
