• Los Angeles native Antimc has worked with legendary LA hip-hop groups Log Cabin (with Radioinactive, and MURS, Eligh, and Scarub of Living Legends), Project Blowed, and the Westcoast Workforce (Subtitle, Xololanxinxo of Of Mexican Descent)
• Antimc has helped create shows for and toured with Busdriver, Boom Bip, Alias, and Radioinactive playing shows with Islands, Mice Parade, cLOUDDEAD, Fog, Cadence Weapon, and many more.
• A talented multi-instrumentalist (one of those instruments being a drum machine), Antimc played almost every note of music on It’s Free, But It’s Not Cheap.
• Blending influences from punk to hip-hop and jazz to post-rock, Antimc has created an singular aesthetic focused on creating timeless records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Antimc">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
