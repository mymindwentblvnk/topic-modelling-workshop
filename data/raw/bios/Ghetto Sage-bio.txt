Noname, Saba, and Smino have formed a new group called Ghetto Sage. Noname, Saba, and Smino have collaborated on several occasions. The latter rappers joined Noname for “Shadow Man” on 2016’s Telefone. Then, last year, they appeared on Noname’s Room 25 track “Ace.” The trio also played that song on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ghetto+Sage">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
