Ignismei is a Heavy Metal band from Las Vegas heavily influenced by the sounds of Metallica, Megadeth, Trivium, and several others. After years of working on songs together with no success in finding the right band members, Emaan and Fabio finally met the bass player and drummer, both named Rob at the end of 2013.
Ignismei released their 3 track demo in May 2014 which includes My Fire Within, Insurgo and Death Star.
Members:
Emaan Omega - Lead Vocals/Guitar
Fabio Benavides - Guitar
Robert Pagano - Bass
