Necro.K.I.L.L.Dozer was a Moscow death-metal/grindcore outfit. They formed in 1991 and released their debut EP Misunderstood Unicum in 1993 on vinyl disc.

Original line-up:

Vasiliy Taratutin - Vocals
Leonid Boulantsev - Guitar
Mikhail Aljulin - Bass
Vyacheslav Klotnitskiy - Drums
