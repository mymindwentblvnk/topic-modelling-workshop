The band that would become known as Three Days Grace .  The band was formed in 1992 in Norwood Ontario Canada, broke in 1995 and then reunited in 1997.  Bassist Brad Walst's brother is the lead singer of My Darkest Days who won rock search in 2008.

Wave of Popular Feeling is the debut album. "Eddie" was the first single, then "Poison Ivy", and finally "Stare." These are the only three songs that get wide distribution on Three Days Grace fansites today.
