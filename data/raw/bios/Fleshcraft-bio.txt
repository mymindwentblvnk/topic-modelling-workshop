Fleshcraft formed in 2000/2001 under the original name Tyterium, however the band changed the name to Fleshcraft when new members joined the band. Fleshcraft plays blackened death metal and are from Sudbury, Ontario, Canada. Their lyrical themes deals with Serial Murder, Death, Suffering, Darkness and Insanity. A demo called "Deadline Release" was recorded in 2002, and an unreleased live bootleg [2005] called "Live at the Townehouse" was leaked on the internet. In 2013 the band reformed after a nearly 10 year hiatus and recorded older materials as well as newer songs for a full length album which was released on CDN Records on October 31st 2014 entitled "Human Error". 

Lineup:

Justin Rienguette - Guitar
Chris Finlay - Guitar
