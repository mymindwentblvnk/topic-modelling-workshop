TransChamps is a collaboration between the instrumental metal band The Fucking Champs and post-rockers Trans Am.  They have released one EP entitled 'Double Exposure' on Thrill Jockey records, followed up by 'Gold' as The Fucking Am. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/TransChamps">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
