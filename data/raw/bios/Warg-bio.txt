There are several bands named Warg. 

1) Thrash / Melodic Death Metal band from Spain that released their first full length album  Back from the Shadows in 2014. Next release was in 2016 ( In the Dusk Of Men. In January 2019 they will release their third album Dark Tales. You can find the first single on youtube. It´s the only band called Warg still active

2) Black Metal band from the New Jersey, United States that released the demo Below the Misty Mountain in 2012

3) Black Metal band from Härnösand, Sweden that released the EP Vermin in 2011.
