Black Harvest are a melodic / progressive / avant-garde / black metal band who formed in 2002 in Brooklyn, NY. 

They recorded and self-released several demos including  'Shrine of Hours' (2003) & 'Mute' (2005), before signing to independent Massachusetts-based label Oak Knoll Productions for the release of 'White Light Came Down' (2005), 'Ingrate' (2011), &  'Abject' (2014).
