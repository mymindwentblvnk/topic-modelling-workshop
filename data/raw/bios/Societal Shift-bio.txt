Societal Shift are a political-thrash band from Hull in the UK. They formed in early 2008 and almost immediately started gigging, before self-publishing their debut EP entitled Bring Out The Dead in November 2008.

Lineup:
Josh Crosse - Vocals, Rhythm Guitar
Jon Alsop - Lead Guitar
Dan Murphy- Bass Guitar
James Cash - Drums
