The (World Famous) Beat Junkies is an American crew made up of eight hip hop DJs. The group comprises J. Rocc, DJ Rhettmatic, DJ Babu (of the group Dilated Peoples), DJ Melo-D, "DJ What?!", Mr. Choc, Icy Ice, and DJ Shortkut.
