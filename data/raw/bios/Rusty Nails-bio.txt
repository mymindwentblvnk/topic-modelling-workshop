Rusty Nails is a metal band hailing from Pisa, Italy.

Their songs are rooted in the heavy-power-thrash genre with some progressive influencies.

Check out their debut 6-track EP "Mind Control", released in early 2011. The EP has been recorded in Rusty Nails' personal studio and mixed by Matteo Buti (Subhuman). The artwork was created by Lorenzo Bagatti.

Rusty Nails line-up:

Claudio Della Bruna - rythm guitar, backing vocals
Paolo Billi - lead vocals
Federico Viviani - drums, backing vocals
Alessandro Crecchi - bass
Luca Cadonici - lead guitar
