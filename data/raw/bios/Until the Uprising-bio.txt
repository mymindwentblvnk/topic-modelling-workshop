UNTIL THE UPRISING is a deathcore band from Nice, France, formed in 2007, mixing technique , brutality and horror movies sound-alike-melodies. The band records its first EP in september 2008, and has it mastered by Tue MADSEN at well-known ANTFARM STUDIO, Denmark. The E.P, out in march 2009, gets good reviews and allows the band to be invited to radio interviews as well as in several metal compilations, such as the FRENCH METAL 8th and NAWAK POSSE 6th editions.
