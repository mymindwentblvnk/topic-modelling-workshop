Metalpunk band from Brazil. Heavily inspired by Motorhead's heavy riffs and Warfare's vocals. Fronted by Whipstriker, bassist of Atomic Roar and Farscape. Released a heavy '7 inch split with heavy punk rock n' rollers from Los Angeles, Iron Fist on Bestial Invasion Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Whipstriker">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
