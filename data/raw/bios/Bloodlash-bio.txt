Bloodlash are a 5-piece progressive death metal band from Morelia, Mexico. To date, they have two releases; a 5-song titled "Drowning Amidst The Nebulae" in 2012, and a 4-song titled "Rain" in 2015. Both releases are available at https://bloodlash.bandcamp.com.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bloodlash">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
