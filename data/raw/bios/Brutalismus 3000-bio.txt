Brutalismus 3000 is a nu gabber and techno duo from Berlin. The duo started making music in 2020 after they had met on Tinder. Their sound is highly influenced by the Berlin club-scene with a modern interpretation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Brutalismus+3000">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
