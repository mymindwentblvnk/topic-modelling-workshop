He is best known for his 2002 hit "U Know Y", and his earlier hit "The Rock" which uses a sample from the song "Top Billin'" by rap group Audio Two. Starting in early 2010, he became one of a very few people to release music on Deadmau5's record label, mau5trap. On April 13, 2010, he released his first album, We Ar Lyve, on mau5trap. This album features singles released in months prior to the album's release, including "Lyve/Imperial" and "Nyce/Blau."
