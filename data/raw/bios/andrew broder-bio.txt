Most notable for his work in the band Fog, Andrew Broder has also worked with Yoni Wolf of Why?, in the one-off side project, Hymie's Basement.  Andrew Broder's solo material consists of two EP's, "Modern Hits" and "More Modern Hits", both of which recontextualize hip hop, by setting the vocals to varying styles of Broder's making.  Broder has also released material under his own name in the form of a collaboration with George Cartwright, called "Soup". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Andrew+Broder">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
