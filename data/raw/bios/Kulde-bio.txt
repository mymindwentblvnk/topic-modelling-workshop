KULDE is a one man project started in the fall of 2006. In the beginning the project was created as Atmospheric Ambient. Several other compositions were recorded in the same style. However, a number of circumstances brought the project to a halt for some time. To be revived once again in the winter of 2008, with a shift towards Depressive Black Metal as it shall remain.

Lyrics themes: Death, depression, suicide, mystics

Current lineup: Ashkael - all instruments

Discography:
01. Heart of the Northern Depression [2008]
02. The Last Winter Requiem [2008]

Contacts: ashkael@yandex.ru
