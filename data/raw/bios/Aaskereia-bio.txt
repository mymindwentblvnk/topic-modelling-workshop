Aaskereia is a German black metal band formed in 1997. The vocals range from clear singing passages to some sort of howling. The band’s lyrics mostly focus on death, battles that are fought, dark and tragic romance, or the woods and everything that is inside them or happening inside them.

Discography:
- Aus dem vereisten Unterholz... (“From the frozen underwood”; Demo, 1999)
- Rehearsal 2001 (Practice recordings, 2001)
- Live in Bindersleben (2002)
- ...mit dem Eid unserer Ahnen begann der Sturm... (“…with the oath of our ancestors, the storm began…”; 2003)
- mit Raben und Woelfen (“with ravens and wolves”; 2003)
