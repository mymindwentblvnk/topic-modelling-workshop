This is a collaboration between Goodie Mob member Khujo Goodie & producer Jneiro Jarel. Jarel is responsible for Shape of Broad Minds, Dr. Who Dat? and other aliases. They are due to release the debut album Georgiavania on Lex Records in June 2009.

Dave Sitek and Tunde Adebimpe of TV on the Radio have recently been reported to have contributed vocal parts to the album. In return Jnerio Jarel was responsible for remixing one of their tracks on the Read Silence EP. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Willie+Isz">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
