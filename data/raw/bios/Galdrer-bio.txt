Galdrer is a black metal band from Norway.
They have released one split album, Ferdasyn/Trolldomsanger,
together with the other Norwegian band Ulvhedner under the label Nothern Silence.

Current line-up:
Blekjesdrotten - Vocals, Guitar, Bass
Naglapungen - Guitar
Kjeppulv - Acoustic guitar
