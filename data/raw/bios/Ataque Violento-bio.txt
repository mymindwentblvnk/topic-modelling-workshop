Ataque Violento means "Violent Attack" in portuguese. 

Formed in 2007: Ataque Violento is an "Speed/Thrash Metal" band (in the Old School vein) from Campina Grande, Brazil... Singing in Portuguese, with a lyrical context about social realities, anti-religion, anti-capitalism and alcohol...

ATAQUE VIOLENTO is:

Jairo – Bass/Vocais
Symon – Lead Guitar
Felipe Rodrigues – Rythm Guitar and Additional Vocals
Netão Guimarães – Drums

"Let's Drink..."
