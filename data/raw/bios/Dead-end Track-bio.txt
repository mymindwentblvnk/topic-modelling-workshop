Italian gothic rock band formed in 1997 and active till 2007. Since then there is no activity seen neither on their website or myspace.

The band was originally created by Andrea Spinelli (guitar & vocals), Olmastroni Francis (bass), Mark Guardabasso (drums) with Fabrizio Font (guitar) joining them later on and soon after Vito Russ joins as a vocalist. After he leaves the band in 2000, he is replaced by Luisa Quaglia.
