Snuffleupagus is a Death Metal band from Schererville, Indiana.  They released one demo entitled River Thy Eyes in 1995.  They later changed their name to Morthona. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Snuffleupagus">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
