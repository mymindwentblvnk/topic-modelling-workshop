The Ordeal founded in Hannover, Germany, in 2002. They released two full length albums.  

Current lineup:
Oliver Oppermann - vocals
Philippe Candas - drums/keyboards (Fair Warning)
Kai Reuter - guitars (Weinhold, Viva (Ger), Fair Warning, Jane)
Cyborg Haines - bass
