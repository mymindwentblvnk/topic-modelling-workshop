Uranium Club, AKA The Minneapolis Uranium Club, are a four-piece post-punk group from Minneapolis consisting of Matt Stagner (drums), "Teen Man" (guitar & vocals), Brendan Wells (bass & vocals, formerly of Iowa City bands NERV and Solid Attitude), and Harry Wohl (guitar & vocals). Formed in 2013, the band has released two EPs and two LPs (including one live). Their most recent endeavor was the single Two Things at Once, released as part of the 4th volume of the Sub Pop Singles Club. Their style has been compared to DEVO, Pere Ubu, and pre-Pink Flag Wire. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Uranium+Club">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
