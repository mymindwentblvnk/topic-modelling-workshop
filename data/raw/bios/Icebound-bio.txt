Icebound are a melodic metal band from Brighton. Current lineup includes Adam on guitars, Jake on guitars, Marc on bass guitar and Jon on drums.

The band have recently gone through a change of lineup and music style, but are currently without a vocalist. Once the album demo is complete the search for a death metal vocalist will begin.
