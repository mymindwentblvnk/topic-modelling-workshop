Nocternity is an atmospheric black metal band from Athens, Greece, formed in 1996. Besides several splits and EPs, they have released two full-length albums up to date: "En Oria" (2001), "Onyx" (2003), & the third full length album entitled "Harps of the Ancient Temples" (2015).

Current line-up:
Khal Drogo - Guitar (previously also vocals)
W. aka Whyrhd - Vocals
N.S. aka Vriareos - Drums
