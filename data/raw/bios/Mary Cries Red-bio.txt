Formed in 2009, Mary Cries Red is a 5 piece violent metal onslaught hailing from Cincinnati, OH. Mixing a melodic twin guitar attack with sludgy down tuned breakdowns, ear piercing screams with guttural lows, fast double bass with blast beats, and a dynamic stage presence. Mary Cries Red is here to make its name known wide and far.
