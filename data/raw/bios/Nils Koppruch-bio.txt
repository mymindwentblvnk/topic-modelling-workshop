Nils Koppruch (1965-2012) was a singer from Hamburg who fronted Fink until their breakup in 2007. His solo work blended German indie, pop and country to fall into its own style of alt country. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Nils+Koppruch">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
