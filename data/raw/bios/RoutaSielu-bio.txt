Routasielu is a melodic death metal project from Finland, led by Tuomas Saukkonen (Before The Dawn, Black Sun Aeon) . "Pimeys" (english translation: "Darkness"), the debut album from Routasielu was released on February 16, 2011 via Spinefarm Records.
