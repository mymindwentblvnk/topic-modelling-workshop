There is more than one artist with this name:

1) Errata is a screamo/post-hardcore band from Lisboa, Portugal.

http://www.facebook.com/errataband

2) Errata was a Latvian hardcore punk three-piece band from Valmiera, Latvia. In 2002 with support from NEKAC and Hodila Records they recorded full-length studio album Pīta pēriens that have been published in 2009 on Presiite Records.

Errata members were also related with bands as Hope Dies Last and Danga.

The original lineup:

Mārcis Lācis - bass, vocals;
Gatis Jansons - guitars, backing vocals;
Raivis - drums.
