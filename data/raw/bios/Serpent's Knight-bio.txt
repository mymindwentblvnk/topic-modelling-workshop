Serpent's Knight was an heavy/power metal band from Seattle, who played in the early 1980s. Between 1983-1985 Warrel Dane - later of Sanctuary and Nevermore - was the band's vocalist. The group released some demos and one album - called "Released from the Crypt" - in 1983. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Serpent%27s+Knight">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
