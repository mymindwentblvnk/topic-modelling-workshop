Longing and Silence is a one-man atmospheric black/doom metal band from San Diego, California formed in 2013. A demo entitled "Through the Fog" was released on September 11th, 2013 and is available on Bandcamp using the 'name your price' method: http://longingandsilence.bandcamp.com/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Longing+and+Silence">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
