There are multiple artists named Sirrah:

1) Sirrah is a gothic doom metal band formed in 1992 in Opole, Poland. They released two albums - "Acme" in 1996 and "Did Tomorrow Come..." in 1997 - before they decided to split up after a member that was expelled from the band claimed the rights to the band's name in 1999. In February 2013, they announced their reunion on their official website, http://sirrahofficial.net, where their newest single Thrill You can be downloaded for free.
