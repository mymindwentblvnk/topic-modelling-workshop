Herriot was formed by Chen (vocalist) with Chop chaosblood. They recruited Adlan(lead guitar), Azim (drums), Afzad (bass) and Joe Sarcast (2nd Guitar) to complete the band. In year 2000 the band released the breakthrough hugely sucessful EP Celestial Obelisk under the flagship of PsychicScream Entertainment and rip through the Malaysian Metal scene while gaining global respect.
In 2001 the band recruited Solomon (aka Ling) on keyboard and  released their full length album "Transworld Chaos" and sealed their place as metal giants in Malaysia and Southeast Asia. The band split up right after the released of the album. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Herriot">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
