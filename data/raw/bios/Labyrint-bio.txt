There are more than one artist with this name

1) Labyrint is a hiphop/reggae band from Uppsala, Sweden.

2) Czech rock band. The first mention of the musical group Labyrint goes back to 1975, when the the famous plate of Vladimir Mišík Thay cut the little boy’s hair was recorded. Producer of Supraphon label invited Pavel Fort to arrange several songs and lead the whole recording, and because of this Pavel Fort created for the first time his studio group Labyrint, which followed many other famous musicians while recording thier projects. Since the labyrinth itself now almost nearly extinct, Karel Fort decided (son of Pavel Fort) in 2008 to restore, but now as a concert group with its own repertoire and concert show.
