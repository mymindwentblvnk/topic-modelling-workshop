Eli Sostre is an artist/producer from Brooklyn, New York. He’s released his first project “Still Up All Night” in August 2016 which was well received by the public.

Eli delivers a moody blend of turn-up raps and turn-on R&B on productions provided by his frequent collaborator SORIANO. His music has been featured on Pigeons & Planes, XXL, The Fader to name a few.

Less than a year later he released “Sleep Is for the Weak”, his project which displays versatility, while confirming that Eli can do no wrong and that he’s here to stay. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eli+Sostre">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
