Peter Jonsson och Rikard Berglin started jaming in 1999 in Själevad (Sweden). About a year later Marcus Lindman and Chris Häggquist joined on guitar and bass.
In 2004 they started looking for a vocalist and song writer and they found Johan Brandberg. He joined the band and got them started towards a first demo. 
In 2005 Chris was replaced by Johnny Grenwald.  Their first 6 track demo called Trails of Hatred was recorded at Studio Leprechaun in Domsjö and released late 2005. 
Their Second official demo "The Impaler" was released late 2007. Their latest album, released in 2011, is called Undead Supremacy. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soul+Decay">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
