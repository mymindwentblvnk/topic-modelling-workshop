The band initially started out under the name of Helvete in 2001 and recorded a demo entitled "To A Forthcoming Dark Age" in 2002. This was never released and the band split up the same year. The band was re-activated again in 2008 as Impure Worship and released a demo in 2010 called "Goathorns". Impure Worship are based in Athens, Greece.

Line-Up:

Iapetos - Vocals
Fallen Angel of Fornication - Guitar, Bass
Black Priest of Impurity - Drums

Iapetos is also in Unholy Archangel, Kawir and Chaosbaphomet.
