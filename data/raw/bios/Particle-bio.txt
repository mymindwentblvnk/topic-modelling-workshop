There are multple artists with the name Particle:

1. Particle is a jam band formed in Los Angeles in 2000. The current lineup includes Steve Molitz (keyboard), Eric Gould (bass), Darren Pujalet (drums) and Ben Combe (guitar). The music of Particle can be characterized as a combination of rock and roll, electronica, and funk. Particle is currently recording their sophomore studio effort with producer Steve Goldman and engineer Marc Johnson to be released in 2007.

2. Particle is Drum and Bass producer/DJ from South London.  Since his first release on Sebastian Weingartshofer's 'Skankandbass' imprint through to his debut EP on Critical, the 23 year old artist has left a dangerous trail of jungle influenced sonics, gaining early support from the likes of Andy C and Randall along the way. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Particle">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
