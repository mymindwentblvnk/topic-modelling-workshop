Levon Vincent is a producer and DJ based in Brooklyn. A native of Manhattan's Lower East Side, he has been releasing music since 2002, primarily on his own imprints (Novel Sound, Deconstruct Music, now-defunct More Music NY) as well as through others like Ostgut Ton and Jus-Ed's Underground Quality.

His work, including his contribution to the Fabric series Fabric 63, has received critical acclaim from publications such as Resident Advisor, The Quietus, and Little White Earbuds. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Levon+Vincent">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
