The band was created in February 2007 by vocalist Aklu and guitarist Kuba. On expense of years the music style of Sinful Souls was very differential, beginning of rock, through metalcore, finishing on death metal. The group was struggling with personal changes for a lot of time. For now, the band set is stabile. In February 2008 band won record of video clip in music contest "Zgrzyty". Band entered WEM studio to record demo in April 2008.Four songs and intro were recorded.Now you can listen to our record and view video clip.Band is now looking for a record label. In September the band had left guitarist Kuba and bass guitarist Maniek.We wish them well-being and a lot of successes on the new way...
