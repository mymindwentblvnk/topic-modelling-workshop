XES is a new synth-pop/retrowave band formed by Pasha Star and Pavel Saushkin in July 2014. Both band members are from Moscow.

In August 2014, the band released their first single and video "Black Tears" with the help from already well-known artists, such as Japanese singer Origa (OST Ghost in the Shell, Final Fantasy etc), Tommy'86, Folano and Hot Hot Hawk. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Xes">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
