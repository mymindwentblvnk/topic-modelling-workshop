Lethal Energy is a heavy metal band from Salt Lake City, Utah. Formed in 2006, the band has seen several name and lineup changes, with guitarist/vocalist Adrian Eliason being the only constant. Originally rooted in a more traditional heavy metal sound with heavy influence from Yngwie Malmsteen, the band gradually shifted to a more thrash metal sound influenced heavily by bands like Kreator, Exciter, and Exodus. The most recent release, Nuclear Winter was recorded December 2010 with Adrian handling all instrumentation and vocals. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lethal+Energy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
