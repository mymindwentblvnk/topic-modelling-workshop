There are two artists which use this name:
1. A punk band from the United States
2. A rap group from Nigeria

1. Bracket is a melodic punk band from Forestville, California, originally signed to Caroline Records. Their original, smooth sound led them to associate with Fat Wreck Chords, producing several albums and EPs. In 2006, they debuted Requiem through Takeover Records.

2. Bracket is a hiphop duo from Nigeria. Yori Yori is a hit from their album Yori Yori. Yori Yori means happiness, it describes a positive feeling. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Bracket">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
