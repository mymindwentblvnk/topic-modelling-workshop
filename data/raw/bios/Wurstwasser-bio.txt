Was founded in December 1993 under the name "Untamed", but renamed in summer 1996 into "Wurstwasser" (Sausage water) after the liquid staying in glasses after consuming all sausages. First public performance was on "Kulturjahrmarkt Much" on Sunday 08. September 1996.

Only using own material, they published by own distribution:

"Wer ist hier die Leberwurst" (May 1997, MC)

"Keine Angst - Nichts fuer Vegetarier" (May 1998, CD + MC)

"Sie leben in einem Traum" (June 1999, CD + MC)

"Der Menschenhasser" (April 2002, CD + MC)

"Oberberg Sausage Massacre" (June 2004, CD)
