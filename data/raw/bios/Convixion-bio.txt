Convixion, formed in Athens, Greece in 2007, are a side project band of Strikelight's guitarist Nick Papakostas. Having already participated in many gigs throughout the years (including the international festival Up the Hammers II in Athens) they are considered to be one of the greatest hopes of the Greek underground metal scene. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Convixion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
