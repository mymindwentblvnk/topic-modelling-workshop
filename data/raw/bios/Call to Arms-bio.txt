Call to Arms is a 5 piece Street Rock'n'Roll band from the South Bay area of Northern California. Since their inception in 2004, the band has recorded a full length album on Brutus Records (Italy), has been featured on the Backstreets of American Oi! volume II, and has a new EP in the works. Musically, the band cites it's roots in Blues, 77 punk, Old School Hardcore, Classic Rock, and Oi! Always on the cutting edge of their genre, Call to Arms writes music straight from the heart featuring slide guitars, walking basslines, and blistering solos. Apart from musical individuality, the band boasts strikingly poetic lyrics that deal with subjects such as growing up in tough times, brotherhood, substance abuse, lost love, and working class disillusionment among other things. Call to Arms has played about 250 shows throughout California and Texas in the past two years with well known acts such as the Whiskey Rebels, Reducers SF, Pressure Point, Angel City Outcasts, the Boils, Bonecrusher, Monster Squad, Verbal Abuse, and the phenomenauts.
