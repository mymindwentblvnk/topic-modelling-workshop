Małgorzata Jamroży, known professionally as Margaret, is a Polish singer and songwriter. She gained international recognition with her singles "Thank You Very Much" (2013) and "Cool Me Down" (2016).

Her debut studio album, Add the Blonde, was released in 2014 and peaked at number eight in Poland. It contained three singles, "Wasted", "Start a Fire" and "Heartbeat"

In 2015, Margaret recorded Just the Two of Us, a jazz album with Matt Dusk, which was followed by her third full-length release, Monkey Business, in 2017. The latter peaked at number eight in Poland and spawned the singles "What You Do" and "Byle jak". Margaret participated in the Swedish music competition Melodifestivalen in 2018 and reached the final. Her entry, "In My Cabana", charted in the top 10 in Poland and Sweden.
