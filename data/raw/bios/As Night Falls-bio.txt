As Night Falls is a post-hardcore band. They bring melody, complexity, and harmony to the table with their EP, The End of All Innocence. The band creates an intense beauty with their music through strong lead vocals and beautiful female backing vocals.  Previously, they were called Nightfall.  Their bassist left to join Atreyu sometime before or after The Curse was being developed
