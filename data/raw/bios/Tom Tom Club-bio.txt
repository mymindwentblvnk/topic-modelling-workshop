Tom Tom Club is a project by Tina Weymouth and Chris Frantz, who were also members of Talking Heads. They began recording albums in 1981 and have enjoyed some success with classic songs such as "Genius of Love" "Under the Boardwalk" "Wordy Rappinghood" and "Pleasure of Love". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tom+Tom+Club">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
