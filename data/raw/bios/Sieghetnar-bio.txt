Cinematic blackened music with lots of atmosphere from Germany in the ambient and neoclassical vein with a calm orchestral touch, formed in 2005. "Thorkraft" plays all instruments (aside from "Borbarath" and "Bariton" providing guest guitar parts on "Die Melancholie des Schmerzes"). He is also the owner of Nordsturm Productions.

Discography:

Todessehnsucht Full-length, 2007
Asche Eines Geistes Demo, 2008  
Endlösung Demo, 2008  
Erhabenheit Demo, 2008
Bewußtseinserweiterung Full-length
Kältetod Full-length, 2008

Die Melancholie des Schmerzes Demo, 2010  
Sieghetnar / Uruk-Hai Split, 2010 
Endlösung (Remastered demo'08 with added vocal) 2010
