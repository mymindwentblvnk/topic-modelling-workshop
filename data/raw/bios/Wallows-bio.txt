Wallows are an alternative rock band from Southern California.  Members are Braeden Lemasters (guitar and vocals), Cole Preston (drums and vocals), and Dylan Minnette (guitar and vocals). In April 2017, they released their first single "Pleaser". It was followed by singles "Sun Tan," "Uncomfortable," and "Pulling Leaves Off Trees" released the same year.

The [italics]Spring[/italics] EP was released in April 2018.

On February 1, 2019, Wallows released the single "Are You Bored Yet?" from their debut album, [italics]Nothing Happens[/italics], which was released on March 22, 2019. The songs "Scrawny” and “Sidelines” were also released as singles from the album.

During quarantine, the band composed an EP,  [italics]Remote[/italics], which was released on October 23, 2020. A deluxe version of the EP featuring drummer Cole Preston’s vocal debut was released on February 19, 2021. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wallows">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
