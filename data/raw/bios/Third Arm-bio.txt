A metal band with 4 guys from Paris (including a Hangman's Chair member), playing since September 2005. Thrashy riffs, heavy atmosphere with grim melodies, influenced by Pantera and The Haunted.

They recorded their first demo, “A Call To Arms”, in March 2006.
