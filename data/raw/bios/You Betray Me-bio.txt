You Betray Me is a grindcore band from Fort Collins, Colorado.  Formed from the ashes of the Colorado band Onset of Effect in 2004 by vocalist C. with guitarist Ryan Bolitho and drummer Jeff Richardson. 

The band has been inactive since 2007 with the exit of Bolitho and Richardson but new material is being written by C. and former bassist Paul Dykema.
