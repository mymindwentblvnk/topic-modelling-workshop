Aasdgoihasdghexekul is a black metal band from Australia. It consists of Wrellust (vocals, guitar), Tira Ptser (guitar) and Atremore (bass, drums, programming) (also of Oracle of the Void, NeXuS, Inward Escape). They released Demo Noir in 2008. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aasdgoihasdghexekul">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
