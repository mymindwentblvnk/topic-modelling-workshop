Devil Lee Rot is a swedish band that plays black heavy metal and it features members from Autopsy Torment, Pagan Rites, Church Bizarre. It is a project of the Pagan Rites front man Thomas Karlsson (aka. Unholy Pope). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Devil+Lee+Rot">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
