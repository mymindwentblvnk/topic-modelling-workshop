Tony Jay was born on the 2nd Feb 1933 in London, UK  and died on the 13th August 2006 in New York, US to cancer complications. He was a member of the Royal Shakespeare Company in England. He was known for his voice work in animation, film and computer games. Jay's distinctive baritone  voice often landed him villainous roles.
