Blackened Drone Doom/Folk featuring Matt Zuchowski (Absolute Misery, Alone in Silence, Besargian, Dusk Ov Shadows, DoomSquirrel, Funerary Dirge, Neurasthenia) and Smolken (Dead Raven Choir, Wolfmangler). Released a one song demo "The Intransigent Goat " in 2005.

The band eventually broke up and Smolken reused the name for a "crunkcore" project upon returning to Poland. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/GOATBOMB">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
