Foxtails is an American four-piece experimental screamo band from Monroe, Connecticut. Formed in 2015, the band currently consists of vocalist/bassist Blue Luno Solaz, drummer Michael Larocca, guitarist June Benham and violinist Jared Schmidt. They released their debut album This is Not For You in 2015, followed by III in 2017 & their third, querida hija in 2019. In January 2022 they released their fourth studio album   fawn. The band is currently signed to Kitty on Fire Records.
