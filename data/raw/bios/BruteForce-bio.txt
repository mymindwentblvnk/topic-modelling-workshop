Bruteforce was formed in 2005 that have had strong ambitions on playing hardcore music which would later resemble New York hardcore style of music.  All original members are from a small town in Athens, TN. They've had some member changes but now its back to the original members plus a new guitar player. So far they've been on 1 south east US tour and have played a lot of shows in Tennessee, Alabama, Georgia, and Florida. 

Line up as of Jan 9 2009
Cody Novak - Vocals
Jon Moore - Bass
Josh Russel - Drums
Josh Evans - Guitar
