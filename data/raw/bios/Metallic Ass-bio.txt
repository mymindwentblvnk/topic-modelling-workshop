Metallic Ass is a Thrash Metal band formed unintentionally by its two founder [bable and denizone] in Jogjakarta in 2008 and recruited Khemal and Denny as their guitarist in early 2009.
The band was finally released their debut album "Thrash Metal 1983" in April 2011, and re-released / repackaged the album in September 2011.
and the story continues .....

Denizone (vocal, bass)
Khemal (left guitar)
Denny (right guitar)
Bable (drums)
