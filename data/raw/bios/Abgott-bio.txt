Abgott is a Technical Black Metal band formed in Italy in 1997 by German-born front man Agamoth. The band relocated to England in 2001. Abgott have released 4 studio albums (Abgott, Fizala, Artefact of Madness and Godfather in Black), 1 demo (Celtic metal battle) & 1 split. 

Abgott is currently signed to & managed by Helvete & Hate Records. Abgott recently signed to Kucizawa management to release their albums in Asia, and play live shows & music clinics.

Abgott are:

Agamoth - Guitars, vocals
Tanathos - Guitars
Triserial - Bass
Morgawr - Drums, percussion
