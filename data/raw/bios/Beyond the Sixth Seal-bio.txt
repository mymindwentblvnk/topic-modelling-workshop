The melodic death metal band Beyond the Sixth Seal formed in Weymouth, Massachusetts back in 1997.

They released their first demo, Genocide Empire, in 1999 which was followed by The White Demo in 2000. Their first EP, A Homicide Divine, was then released by Voice of Life Records the following year. Another demo was released the next year, called Knives & Guns. Their first full-length production, Earth and Sphere, was released in 2002 by Lifeforce Records.

The band broke up in 2003 but has reformed as of 2006 as a studio project.

Their second full-lenght album The Resurrection Of Everything Tough was released on May 1, 2007 through Metal Blade Records.
