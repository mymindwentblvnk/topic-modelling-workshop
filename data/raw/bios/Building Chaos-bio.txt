Building Chaos was formed in Athens,Greece, in late 2005 by Alex (Guitar-Vocals), Makis (Bass) and Peter (Guitar). Nick (Drums) joined the band in 2008. Building Chaos plays thrash metal in the style of bands such as Pantera, Machine Head and Slayer.
