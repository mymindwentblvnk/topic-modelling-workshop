Bulgarian alternative rock band formed in 1989 in the capital city of Sofia.The band has recorded two albums - Nothing (1994) and Ignored (1997).Pyromania disbanded in 1999 but played a bunch of reunion shows since 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pyromania">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
