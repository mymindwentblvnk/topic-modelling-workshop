Aurelio is a mononym of Honduran musician Aurelio Martinez. He is a singer, percussionist, and guitarist known for his Garifuna music and is considered a Cultural Ambassador of the Garifuna people. According to the UK newspaper The Guardian, he became the leading Garifuna performer after the death of musician Andy Palacio. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Aurelio">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
