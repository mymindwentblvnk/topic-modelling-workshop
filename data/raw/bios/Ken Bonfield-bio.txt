Ken Bonfield is a self-taught fingerstyle guitarist who composes and performs original instrumental guitar music. He currently works out of Magnolia, Massachusetts, USA.

Bonfield incorporates a number of themes found in Celtic, American Folk, Blues, Jazz, Classical, & Rock and Roll into his music. And over the past 15 years he's released 4 solo albums, and 2 duo albums (with Joe Ebel as Bonfield & ebel).
