JJ DOOM was a collaboration between producer Jneiro Jarel and the masked hip-hop artist MF DOOM. Their debut collaborative album, titled Key to the Kuffs, was released on August 21, 2012 via Lex Records. Key to the Kuffs features guest appearances from Beth Gibbons (of Portishead), Damon Albarn (of Blur/Gorillaz), Boston Fielder, and Jneiro Jarel's fellow partner from the duo Willie Isz, Khujo Goodie (of Goodie Mob). Key to the Kuffs was followed in 2014 by the Bookhead EP, with contributions from Clams Casino, Thom York and Jonny Greenwood of Radiohead, Mr Gone, Del the Funky Homosapien, and Beck.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/JJ+DOOM">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
