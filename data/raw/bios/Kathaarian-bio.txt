1.) Kathaarian is a raw black metal band from Germany found in 2004. 

They released the full length album "Cryptic Temples Of The Ancient Cult" in 2005 and a split with Norns in 2007.

Current line-up:
Propagan - Drums
Nysrogh - Guitar, Bass
Alver - Bass, Vocals
Hagalaz - Guitars

Official Website: http://www.kathaarian.com/
