Evilization is a Thrash Metal band based in Pasadena, California. Their members include Beto Lopez (Drummer/Backup Vocals), Henry Recinos (Guitarist), Stas Felton (Lead Vocals), and David Marquez (Bassist/Backup Vocals). Evilization have opened up for Thrash legends Destruction, and Flotsam & Jetsam. Among their primary musical influences are Slayer, Sodom, Testament, Kreator, Suicidal Angels, Evile, Overkill, etc. Their debut album, "Beginning of the End", is set to release in summer of 2014. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Evilization">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
