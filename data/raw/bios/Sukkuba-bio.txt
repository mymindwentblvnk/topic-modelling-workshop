Sukkuba is a goregrind/brutal death metal band from Samara, Russia.  They formed in 2008.  They have released one demo and three full-length albums.  They write songs about sex, porn, misogyny, anti-relegion and sick humour.  The band currently consists of Ash Sociopath (vocals),  DJ Taras (lead guitar) and Dominator (drums).  They are signed to Coyote Records.
