Mother Abyss are a 4-piece atmospheric, sludge, post-metal band from Viana Do Castelo, Portugal. They released their debut album "Burden" in 2013. It is available at https://motherabyss.bandcamp.com  For more information, visit https://www.facebook.com/motherabyssband/ <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mother+Abyss">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
