Spewing forth from dimensions unknown in the year of 2010 Tyrannosorceress is a Blackened Doom Metal band from Dallas, Texas. Building upon a hotbed of doom, drone, black metal and psychedelic rock based influences as well as members prior experience in the Dallas Extreme Metal community Tyrannosorceress comes through with original trance inducing jams. The band is known for their hypnotic live sets among-st the fog conjured forth from a machine. As of this time Tyrannosorceress has one demo out and is working towards a full length release sometime in 2012. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tyrannosorceress">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
