Brutal death metal/grindcore from Luxembourg, formed in 1998 and split-up after the release of "Eat or be Eaten" in 2009. Mars Benett and Nicolas Hieber (owner of Morku Productions, an amateur horror movies company) were the two sole members of the band, having recorded 2 studio albums, 4 demos and a 3-way split with Permanent Death and Boiling Remains in a span of eleven years. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Fetuxion">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
