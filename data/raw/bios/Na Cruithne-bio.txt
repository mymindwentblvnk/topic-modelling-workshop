Na Cruithne (pronounced Na-Cruin-ya) is a six piece Folk Metal group from Galway City, Ireland. The bands style incorporates elements of Groove and Black Metal with heavy influences from traditional Irish, Scottish and Breton music.The Irish language features heavily in the lyrics with almost half of the songs as Gaeilge (Gaelic). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Na+Cruithne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
