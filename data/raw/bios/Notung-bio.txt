Notung is a black metal band from Santiago, Chile. The band formed in 1996 and released their first full-length "Gathering of Swords" in 1997.

Doomicus as the only member recorded a new track during 2003 named "Mother's Revenge".

In "Der Ring des Nibelungen", a series of four epic operas by Richard Wagner, Notung was the name of Siegfried's magic sword.
