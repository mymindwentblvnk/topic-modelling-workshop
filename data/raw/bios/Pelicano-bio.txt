Pelicano is a guitar band from Porto Alegre, RS, Brasil. Formed in April 2005 by Rodrigo Souto (drums), Renan Stiegemeier (bass), Bruno Galera (guitar & vocals) and Douglas Dickel (guitar & vocals).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pelicano">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
