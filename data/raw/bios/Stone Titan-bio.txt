Stone Titan is a three piece band from South Windsor, CT that formed in 2008. The band plays a style that incorporates elements of sludge, doom, noise rock, and hardcore.

Discography:
'Supergiant' cassette EP (2011)
'Stone Titan vs. Gowl' split cassette with Gowl (2011)
'Stone Titan' 7" (2011)
'Scratch 'n Sniff' LP (2013)
'Peace, Love, and Get the Fuck Out of My Face' EP (2013)
