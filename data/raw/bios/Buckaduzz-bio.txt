Buckaduzz were formed in the early summer of 2008 in the Sandvika-area of southern Norway, and consists of four members. They started out with the intent of making heavy blues driven music, and draws inspiration from bands such as Clutch, Kyuss, Pink Floyd and Electric Wizard. 

In 2010 they released "The Big Slow EP" to good critics among several metal and stoner sites on the web.
