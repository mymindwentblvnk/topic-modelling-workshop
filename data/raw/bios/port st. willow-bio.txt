Port St. Willow is the music project of Nick Principe, originally formed in Portland, OR in 2009.  His first album, "Even // Wasteland," was self-released in Fall 2010.

In early 2012, PSW shifted from west to east, traveling back home to live and work out of Brooklyn, New York.  

The first full-length record, "Holiday" will be self-released in Spring 2012.   <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Port+St.+Willow">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
