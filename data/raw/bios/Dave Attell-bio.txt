Dave Attell (born January 18, 1965) is a popular American stand-up comedian and host of Comedy Central's Insomniac with Dave Attell. Attell has been called "one of the most original voices of his generation". He is originally from Rockville Centre, New York and now lives in New York City. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Attell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
