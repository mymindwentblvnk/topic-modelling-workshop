Life Neglected is a (depressive) black metal band from Sweden and was formed and ended in 2005. In this period they released three demo's and a split album with Kyla. The line-up consisted of 'Nachtzeit' (Guitars, vocals and lyrics) and 'Doriath' (Drum programming). The general lyrical theme surrounds "Hate towards humanity".
