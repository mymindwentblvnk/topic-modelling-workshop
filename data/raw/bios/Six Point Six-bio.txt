There are multiple artists using this name.

1.  Six Point Six were a 1980s Heavy Metal band from Germany. They released one full length album title Fallen Angel in 1986.

2. Six Point Six is a polish rock group. Their adventure with music began in 2004, but with current members the band performs since September 2006. Their greatest achievement was the 1st place on Lublin Rock Scene 2007 Festival. The group plays a mix of rock, gothic, poetry and pop. Strong point od the band is a tallented singer - Agnieszka Kasprzak.
