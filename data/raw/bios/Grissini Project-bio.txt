The Grissini Project is a classically trained musician ensemble, also specialized in video game, film and anime music; founded in December 2010 by Romain Vaudé, first as a ''one-man band''. Its members are Romain Vaudé (piano), Lilou (vocals), Bastien Vidal (violin) and Marwane Champ (cello). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Grissini+Project">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
