Danceable, fast-paced and energetic to the bone – Left Alive play punkrock from the heart. These four Dutchies fuse catchy riffs like Green Day’s with powerful, Paramore style vocals and pulsating electronic samples. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Left+Alive">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
