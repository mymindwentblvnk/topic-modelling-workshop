Necrotroopers is an Ambient Drone/Funeral Doom Metal band from Kazakhstan,Karaganda. Primarily a one-man project of Algeroth. On some tracks his sister contributes vocals and piano, but most of the recording is done by Algeroth.
Current Lineup is :
Algeroth : All instruments, Vocals
