Thornus is a metal band located in Nijmegen. The band creates heavy metal and just finished their new demo! Screaming Silence.. (whispers within) contains three songs.
Influenced by the likes of Metallica, Icead Earth and Nevermore they try to create their own mix of heavy metal.

The band started in 1998 but saw many line-up changes over the years. The current line-up consists of:

Dave Relou 
Patrick Janssen 
Aris Nikopoulos 
Michael Maasen
