Acualli is a black/death metal band formed in 2013 by Acatlán Coatlicue, Kampilan and Vahxak in Long Beach, CA. Acualli translates to evil perversion in Nahuatl.

Demo Pact of Possession (2013) released by Crepusculo Negro. Featured on the compilation-tape Tliltic Tlapoyauak. 

Acatlán Coatlicue: bass, composition.
Kampilan: vocal.
Vahxak (Muknal): drums, guitar, composition.
