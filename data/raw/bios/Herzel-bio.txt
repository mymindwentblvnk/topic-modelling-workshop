Herzel is a multitalented artist trained in photography and visual arts. His music knowledge and experience is mainly formed by many years of DJ-ing. As a music producer his work is based on the sound arrangements for different bodies of visual works, part of the photo, video and sound installations. Herzel's concept of music production is to make the crossover between electronic musical genres and audio/visual art.
