BIO
5 piece Deathcore band from Canberra, Australia
What Hunts You formed in late 2011 and have played shows all over Canberra and surrounding areas. In July 2012 we headed to Melbourne to record an EP with Roman Koester at Complex Studios. The EP is called The Epigraph of Annihilation. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/What+Hunts+You">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
