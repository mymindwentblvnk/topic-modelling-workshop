Clamus is a brazilian Thrash/Death Metal band from Fortaleza formed in 1999 which combines a fast and brutal death metal and horn-raising thrash metal riffs with portuguese, english and french lyrics. They've passed through constant changes in the line-up since nowadays.
After several shows they've record the first EP entitled "Clamus" in 2000 and the second EP called "II" in 2001 and after 3 years, Clamus starts "Influences" recording, they first album.

Current line-up:

Joaquim Cardoso & Lucas Gurgel (Guitars/Vocals)
Clerton Holanda (Drums)
Felipe Ferreira (Bass/Vocals)
