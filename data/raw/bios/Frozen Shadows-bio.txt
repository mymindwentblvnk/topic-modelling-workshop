Frozen Shadows was a black metal band from Montreal, Quebec, Canada formed in 1995. Their lyrics focus on satanism, mysticism and northern atmospheres. They released a demo in 1996 and two full length albums: "Dans les Bras des Immortels" in 1998 and "Hantises" in 2004. Myrkhaal owns and runs Sepulchral Productions. 
Current line-up:
Alvater - Guitars
Myrkhaal - Vocals, Keyboards
Namtar - Bass, Guitars
Melkor (Pierre Langlois) - Drums
