Black Infinity is a Vietnamese metal band founded by Nguyen Tien Hung who used to be the vocalist and bass guitarist of an American symphonic death band called Sorrow Decadence. In 2006 Hung quit Sorrow Decadence and came back to Vietnam where he introduced Black Infinity including himself as vocal, Nguyen Tien Man - his younger brother - as guitar 1, Dang Thai Son guitar 2, Tiger Vu as bass guitar, La Cam Man as keyboard and finally Pham Hoang My as drum.

Although their songs are in different genres, including death metal, black metal, doom metal, gothic metal, but Hung said that the main genre they play is Melodic death metal. Their influences from 666, Children of Bodom, Norther , In flames , Lamb of God , H.I.M , Type O Negative.
