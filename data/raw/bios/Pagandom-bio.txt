Pagandom is a Thrash Metal band hailing from Gothenburg, Sweden. Formed in 1987.
Pagandom disbanded in 1996.
They reunited for “Gotheburg Sound Festival”, January 2014, and has since then continued writing music. New album in the works for a 2016 release.
