ILUNGARA are coming from south-west of France and give us some Heavy Metal in the strict sense of the word (that is faithful to its original definition) and not in the general sense we wildly use to describe mid-tempo bands we could hardly define differently.

Grupo de Irún (Guipúzcoa) y Hendaya (Pais Vasco Francés) que comienzo a funcionar a mediados de 1996. Formado por Iván Estévez (voz y guitarra), David Cabanillas (guitarra), Benoit Charles(bajo) y Stéphane Dassé (batería). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ilungara">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
