There are multiple artists under the moniker Torchlight;

1) Torchlight is a dungeon synth project from Salerno, Italy.

2) Torchlight is folk black metal band based in Oakland, California. They released their first two albums, Enchiridion Thaw and Spectral in 2019

3) Torchlight is a Jakarta-based hardcore band that formed in 2010.
