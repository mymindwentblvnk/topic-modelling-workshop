Currently enrolled in the prestigious Clive Davis School of Recorded Music at NYU, Felix Snow continues to trailblaze his way through a plethora of genres with prodigious command. Playing solo, Snow's vocals can be gentle and bold, often simultaneously, while his aplomb with the guitar lends a tasteful backdrop. With a band Snow is equally impressive, capitalizing on the unique talents and strengths of his rotating musical troupe. Either way, Snow's electrifying stage presence is certain to entertain and stimulate. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Felix+Snow">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
