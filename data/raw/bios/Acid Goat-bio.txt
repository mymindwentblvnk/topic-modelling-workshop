Acid Goat is a Stoner/Doom Metal band based in Birmingham, UK.

Formed in 2013, ACID GOAT are a three piece band who play riff-oriented Stoner/Doom Metal.

The band is currently in the process of releasing a self-titled 4 track EP/Demo which is currently being mixed and mastered.

Influences:
Electric Wizard, Black Sabbath, Bongzilla, Weedeater, Sleep, Egypt, Eyehategod, Dopethrone, Conan, Witchsorrow, Alunah among many others.

Interests:
WEED, BEER AND HEAVY FUCKING METAL!!!
