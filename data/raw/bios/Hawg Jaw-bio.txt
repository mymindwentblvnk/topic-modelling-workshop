Hawg Jaw was formed in August of 1996. Over 9 years and 6 line ups later, still dishing out hardcore drenched slop/brutality in midcity New Orleans.

Hawg Jaw have released several self released demo's and 7 ", and have played countless shows with both local, and national touring bands.

Mike Dares - Vocals
