Raushaan Glasgow, known professionally as LSDXOXO (styled in all caps) is a Philadelphia-born, Berlin-based DJ and producer. His style pulls from ghetto house and Baltimore club, and commonly contains nineties influences and percussion, as well as pop vocal samples over hardcore, electro and techno beats.

LSDXOXO's self-releases include the Spit or Swallow mixtape series, BODY MODS and Fuck Marry Kill, as well as the EPs Softcore and Whorecore. His remix work includes Lady Gaga's 'Alice - LSDXOXO Remix', Shygirl's 'TASTY - LSDXOXO Remix' and Kelela's 'LSDXOXO_TRUTH OR DARE_123 BPM'.
