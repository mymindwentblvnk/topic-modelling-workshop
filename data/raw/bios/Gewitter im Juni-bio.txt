Depressive black metal project from Germany, formed by C.C. in 2010. The bandname  ''Gewitter im Juni'' is German for "Thunderstorm(s) in June". C.C. is also part of Corkra Productions and is active in another band called Krähennebel under the name Isrimul. Prior, he had an additional soloproject called Ruinaerus and was part of the band Empathie.
