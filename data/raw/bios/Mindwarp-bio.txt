MINDWARP is a death metal band created in 2015 with in its Line up, members of the band CARCARIASS.
The compositions of the group want to let speak the experiment of the musicians who make up this Swiss band, based in Geneva.In march, 2016 MINDWARP records its first album called « A COLD BLACK DAY », on GREATDANE RECORDS, with 9 tracks.This album was recorded and mixed by DROP (Bassist of SAMAEL and Guitarist of SYBREED) at DOWNTONE STUDIO in GENEVE (Switzerland).
MINDWARP are :
Fred Buschenrieder  : Vocal
Pascal Lanquetin  : Guitar
Romuald Hermanowski  : Bass
