Lakeway is a young indie-pop band based in Kyiv, Ukraine, formed in late 2013 by three best friends. Band is well-known as one of the most prospective young indie bands in Ukraine, a member of biggest ukrainian festivals and radio stations. Lakeway has released two EP albums, both of them get in top ukrainian indie charts.
