There are four bands under the name of Confined.

1) Confined is a Straight Edge Hardcore band based in Buffalo, NY.  SMartin, Artie, Gel Mon, Murderous, Mike Edge.  Est. 2010.

2) Confined was a brutal death metal from Barcelona, Spain. They're now split-up.

3) Confined were a new metal/hardcore band from Teesside, England. 
Formed in May 2008, and disbanded in October 2008. 

4) Confined was the previous Band of Scars of Life. But they was not contented with their kind of music. So they changed from Hard Rock to Metal and Scars of Life was born. Confined published only one album, Silence, in the year 1999. Between 2000 and 2006 they played music as Scars of Life. Now it is Burning Red.
