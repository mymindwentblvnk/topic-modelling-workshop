3rd Stage Alert was a heavy metal band from Los Angeles, California.  They formed in 1982 and disbanded in 1985.  The band consisted of Dave Drury on vocals,  Al Morris on lead guitar,  Guilio Lomma on rhythm guitar, Ron Murray on bass and Mark Edwards on drums.  They were signed to Metal Blade Records and only released one EP entitled 3rd Stage Alert. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/3rd+Stage+Alert">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
