Javan is an AOR band from Germany. The group released its first (and only) album, Somewhere In The Night, in 1991.

Bernd Martin (Vocals)
K.B. Hard (Guitars)
Rainer Herrmann (Bass)
Oliver Schwung  (Guitars)
T.J. Rath  (Keyboards)
Winni M. Shap  (Drums)
