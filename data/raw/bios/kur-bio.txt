Kur is a hip-hop MC hailing from the City of Brotherly Love (Philadelphia, PA). In 2015, Kur's popularity soared after a collaboration with fellow Philly rapper (and Maybach Music Group signee) Meek Mill for Kur's mixtape, "How it Never Was."
