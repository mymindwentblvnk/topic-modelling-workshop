Deadly Pale is a band name attributed to two different bands:

#1: Deadly Pale are a punk band from Quebec, Canada, consisting of Amelie on Vocals, Marieve on bass/ vocals, Steve on guitar/back vocals, Matt on bass/back vocals, Psychopat on drums and Ricky Cutthroat on guitar and back vocals. Deadly Pale have many influences including but not limited to: Aus-Rotten, D.R.I, Iron Maiden, Crass, Minor Threat, Napalm Death, Misfits,  Subhumans, Nasum, Slayer, Brutal Truth and Suicidal Tendencies. Deadly Pale have 2 full length albums and are featured on several compilations. 

#2: Deadly Pale are a death metal band from Dortmund, Nordrhein-Westfalen, Germany. The band consists of the following members Jens on guitar/vocals, Henning on guitar, Dennis on bass/vocals and Benny on drums.
