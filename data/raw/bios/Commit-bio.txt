Commit is a hardcore band based in Worcester, Massachusetts.  Commit had a final show in 2009 but still plays shows occasionally. Members include Nick Malkasian (vocals), Sean Donahue (guitar), Shrew Church (bass 2006-2007), James Rodriquez (bass 2007-) John Alexiadis (guitar), and Zack Ferguson (drums). They released their first 5 song demo in 2007. A later release came in 2008.     <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Commit">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
