Depressive Black Metal from Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. The name is Latin for "deadly way out".

Exitus Letalis released a demo in 2007 which was not distributed but only handed out to friends. In 2008, two demo's where released, called "If Hope Dies...." and "Nothing Remains...".

The line-up consists of B. (Vocals) and M. (Guitar and Drums). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Exitus+Letalis">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
