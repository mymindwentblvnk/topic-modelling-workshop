Catania based death-on-ten-legs HAUNTED, are recently gathered to come out in the wash with their sub-sabbathian disease.

These five banshees are set to release their first full-length LP “Haunted” via Twin Earth Records.

Haunted are:
Francesco Bauso: Guitars
Cristina Chimirri: Vocals
Valerio Cimino: Drums
Francesco Orlando: Guitars
