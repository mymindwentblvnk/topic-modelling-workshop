Rheingold was a German New Wave band that existed between 1980 and 1984. They recorded the three records "Rheingold", "R." and "Dis-Tanz". Their biggest hits were "Dreiklangsdimensionen" and "FanFanFanatisch". Bandmembers were Bodo Staiger (vocals, guitar), Brigitte Kunze (keyboards) and Lothar Manteuffel (lyrics).
