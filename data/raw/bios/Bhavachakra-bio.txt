Bhavachakra are an experimental black metal band from Orlando, Florida that formed in 2014. They independently released their debut full-length album Bhavachakra digitally in January 2016, which was reissued through Translation Loss Records on CD on September 23, 2016. The band has cited Gorguts, Krallice, Death, and 1349 as influences.
