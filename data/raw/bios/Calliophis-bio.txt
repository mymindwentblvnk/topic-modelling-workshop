Calliophis is a Death Doom Metal band from Leipzig (Germany), founded in the summer of 2007. All members played or are still playing in other Death Metal bands. The songs of Calliophis are about human tragedies and old fairy tales.

Line Up:
Thomas - Vocals
Matthias - Guitar (lead)
Martin - Guitar (rhythm)
Marc - Bass
Florian - Drums

Releases: Doomsday (2008)
Cor Serpentis (2017)
