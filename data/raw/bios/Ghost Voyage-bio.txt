Ghost Voyage is a doom metal band from Tampere, Finland, formed in early 2008. Their first promo EP EMBRACE OF THE DEEP was released in January 2009. UNDER THE BROKEN SKIES EP 2010.
The debut album ENDLESS OCEANS released in 2013.
