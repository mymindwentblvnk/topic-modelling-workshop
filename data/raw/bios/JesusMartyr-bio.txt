Jesusmartyr starts as a band on 1994 in Argentina (Buenos Aires city), under the name of MARTYR, records its first demo tape “Fear to be born”, recorded on a four tracks portastudio, which was a relative succes at the moment selling more than 300.000 copies. Immediately the band gets reviewed not only in Argentina but in zine allover the world... <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Jesusmartyr">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
