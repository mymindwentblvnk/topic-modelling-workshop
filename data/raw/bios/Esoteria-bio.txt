(1) Technical Death Metal band from Grand Rapids, Michigan. comprised of ex-members of old school progressive death metallers, Emonday, Connor Coram(Bass/Vox) and Kyle Karafa(Guitar), Esoteria is a crushing improvement. with blistering fast songs and vicious tech grooves to keep you satisfied! recently, Esoteria has recorded a demo entitled "Esoteric Tapes" and is in the process of recording their first full length album "Apocalyptic Metropolis" in mid summer of 2011!  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Esoteria">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
