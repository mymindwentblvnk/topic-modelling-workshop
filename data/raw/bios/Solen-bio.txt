Solen are a grunge pop band from Gävle, Sweden.

Solen is by its characteristic melancholy pop and explosive and captivating stage presence made itself known as Sweden's best unsigned bands. Nowadays it's Sweden's best signed band since Solen in December 2013 merged with Playground Music to begin work for the new album. "Till Dom Som Bryr Sig" was recorded in early 2014 with producer Stefan Brändström in Stockholm.
