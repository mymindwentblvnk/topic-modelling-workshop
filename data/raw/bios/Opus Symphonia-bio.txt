Opus Symphonia is a Gothic Metal band from Sukabumi West Java, Indonesia.

Opus Symphonia is one of the most Gothic Metal bands, (if not the only) Gothic Metal band, in Sukabumi West Java, Indonesia. 
In late 2002 at dialogue studio Bandung, their demo album was released by Rooflessrom Prod. but thats no longer. 
Now Opus Symphonia rereleased by headbanger rec.
