a death metal band from Spain that was formed in 1991. They a released a demo "only death is irreversible" in 1991 with Drowned productions, a demo#2 in 1992 (which was supposed to come out with Seraphic Decay records but it never happened) and participated in a spanish death metal split compilation "dead flesh" with Avulsed, Antropomorfia, Fermento and Spontaneous Combustion (Esp). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Sacrophobia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
