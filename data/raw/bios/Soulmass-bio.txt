Death/doom metal band from Florida.  Their lyrical topics are mostly about the Demon's and Dark Souls series.  They have released two albums, 2014's "Despairing Fates" and 2019's "The Weakness of Virtue". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soulmass">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
