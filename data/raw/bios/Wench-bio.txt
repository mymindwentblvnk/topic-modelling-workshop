There are multiple acts with this name:

1. A collaboration between electronic producer Arca and fashion label Hood By Air founder Shayne Oliver. The duo's projects include the songs Sick and Triller, as well as several Hood By Air runway soundtracks, among others.

2. An alias used by Frances Byrne, through which she released the albums Wench and Satori, as well as the songs Isabelle and Heart of Darkness.

3. A New York-based thrash metal band that lasted from 1987 to 1993, made up of members Allyson Gamble, Meryl Hurwich, and Yana Chupenko. The band released 2 demo tapes and 1 album titled A Tidy Sized Chunk.
