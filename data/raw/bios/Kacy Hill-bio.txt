Kacy Anne Hill is an American model and singer-songwriter. She was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona and had previously worked as a model for American Apparel, as well as a backup dancer on The Yeezus Tour for Kanye West. She released Experience, her first non-album single independently in 2014, with an EP release scheduled for October 2015. The first single from the EP, Foreign Fields, was released in 2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kacy+Hill">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
