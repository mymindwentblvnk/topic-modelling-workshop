Kiriath were a five piece tech metal band from Belfast, Northern Ireland. 

They released one EP; Aniconism. Music can be heard here:
http://kiriath.bandcamp.com/

Antony Moore - Vocals
Adam Irwin - Guitars
Matt MacHugh - Guitars
Conor Agnew - Bass
