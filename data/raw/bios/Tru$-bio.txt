TRU (an abbreviation of The Real Untouchables) was an American hip hop group from Richmond to New Orleans, active from 1992 to 2005.The group originally consisted of rappers on the New Orleans-founded record label , No Limit Records. The members are Master P, C-Murder and Silkk the Shocker. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Tru$">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
