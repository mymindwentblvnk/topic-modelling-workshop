Brannthorde is a black metal band from the Black Forest, Germany.
Brannthorde writes, records and releases songs about visions and wishes. Influenced by and written in the Black Forest, Southwest Germany. The lyrical content and the music is written by Roman Hilser for the most part. Vocals by Giuliano Barbieri.
