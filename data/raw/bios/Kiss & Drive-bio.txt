KISS & DRIVE is the project of an Italian female artist, Elisabetta Spada, living in Brussels and singing in English ...
Alone on stage with her guitar, her ukulele and her inseparable loopstation, she tells us that her folk music could be more electronic and her pop sound rather symphonic
Never the less, her clear-cut songs, her velvety voice, her appealing and funny personality create instant magic. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kiss+&+Drive">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
