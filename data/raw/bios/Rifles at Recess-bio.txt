Rifles at Recess is a melodic metalcore band from chattanooga, tennessee that disbanded after a tour in the summer of 2003.  The 5 member band has a reunion show scheduled for August 2006 in their hometown.  Their music is characterized by heavy blast beats, frequent tremelo picking, and raw biting vocals similar to the styles of the Black Dahlia Murder and Glass Casket.  Their occasional breakdowns, spoken words, and tempo changes blend hardcore into their brutal style. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Rifles+At+Recess">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
