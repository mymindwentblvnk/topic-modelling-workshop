Axattack is a three piece thrash group from Columbia, SC. Influenced by bands such as Toxic Holocaust, Venom, and Motorhead, Axattack is Alec Edelson (guitar), Chris Darlington (drums), and Anthony Oliver (bass).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Axattack">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
