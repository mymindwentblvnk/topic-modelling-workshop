Roberto Di Gioia, the musical force behind Marsmobil  was born in Milan in 1965. He began playing piano at age 4. His favourite music: the Beatles. In 1975 he began his studies at the musicial highschool in Eichstaett and after graduating in 1984 moved to Munich where he began his career as a professional musician. Jointly signed to Kruder & Dorfmeister's label G-Stone, and Compost recordings, Marsmobil won the 2006 contract for a theme song to the FIFA World cup games in Munich.
