Straight Beyond Last is a metal band from Soure, Portugal. they were formed in 2009 and the current members are Pedro (Voz), Joel (Baixo), Shreder (Guitarra) and Paulo (Bateria). They've announced the end of the band at 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Straight+Beyond+Last">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
