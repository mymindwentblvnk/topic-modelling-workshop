Poropetra was Finnish experimental folk rock band. They mixed together the elements of Finno-Ugric folk music traditions, world music, heavy rock and minor influences from different music styles as well. The band existed during 2002-2015. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Poropetra">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
