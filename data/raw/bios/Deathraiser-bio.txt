1. Deathraiser was old schoolish thrash metal band with black metal influences from Seinäjoki, Finland  formed in 2006. Their lyrical themes came from war, apocalypse & thrashing. They released one self-titled demo. All tracks are downloadable from mikseri.net.

2. Deathraiser, previously known as Mercilless (2006-2009) is a thrash metal band from Minas Gerais, Brazil formed in 2009. They released their first full-length "Violent Aggression" in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Deathraiser">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
