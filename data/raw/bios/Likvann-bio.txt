One man black metal band from Hamar.

The lyrics are focused on anti religion, hate, violence and pride.

Formed in 2010 by Skabb, Likvann released:

"Nordisk Blod og Ætt" demo tape, 2010;
"Her jeg blir til jeg dør" demo tape, 2012;
"Vredeskrik" EP on tape/CDr, 2013;
"Drevet Av Raseri" compilation on CDr, 2013;
