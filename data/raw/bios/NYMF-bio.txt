NYMF is a heavy rockband from south Sweden, stationed in the small town of Karlshamn.

The band was formed around 2007 by the singer, Niklas Sjöberg (also the singer from "The Graviators" And the guitarist, Kristofer Stjernquist. Their music idea was to create a mix of the genres they loved the most - Doom, Stoner/rock and Metal. In 2008 NYMF recorded their first demo with Johan Blomström (Kaptain Sun, Spice and the RJ Band, Faith ao). At the Soundpalace studio in Svängsta.
