SKIFONIX, real name Lewis Dodkins, is a EDM producer that was signed to Monstercat. During his time there he released three tracks.
He stopped making music for a while in 2012 and since 2015 he runs/work at a website called Skifonix Sounds that offers music sample-packs to producers. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/SKIFONIX">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
