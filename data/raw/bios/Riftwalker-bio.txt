Riftwalker is a 3 piece extreme progressive metal band based in Vancouver, BC. They have been established since December 2009 and have shared the stage with bands such as Abiotic, Archspire, Benighted and Fallujah. Riftwalker recorded and released their first EP, entitled Wreckage of the Old World, in 2013. 
Riftwalker released their debut album titled "Green & Black" on October 31st 2016 featuring 8 tracks.
Line up:
 Miles Morrison (Guitar/Vocals)
Spencer Atkinson (Bass/Vocals)
