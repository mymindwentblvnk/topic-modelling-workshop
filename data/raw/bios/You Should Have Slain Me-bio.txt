Named after the song of the same name by Hammers Of Misfortune, You Should Have Slain Me is a drone metal project of unknown status by Campbell Kneale (Our Love Will Destroy The World, Black Boned Angel, etc). There was just one release, a self-titled EP in 2005 consisting of a single 18 minute track. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/You+Should+Have+Slain+Me">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
