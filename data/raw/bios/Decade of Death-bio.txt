Decade Of Death is a thrash/death metal band from Szczecinek, Poland. Their music evolved from simple bare metal to more complicated style of this music. The band released an EP "Ritual Mutilation" and, recently, demo "In Murder Claws". Line-up: Marcin Czech - vocals; Adrian Dworanowski - guitar, bass; Paweł Świderski - drums. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Decade+Of+Death">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
