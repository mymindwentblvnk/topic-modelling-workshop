There are multiple artists named Titanic:

1) Titanic is a 1970s progressive rock band from Norway.
2) Titanic is a Christian metal band from Las Vegas, Nevada, United States.
3) Titanic is a heavy metal band from Brno, Czech Republic.
4) Titanic was a heavy metal band from New Haven, Connecticut, United States. Released the album "Then There Was Rock" in 1985.
