Dead Space is a psychedelic rock band formed in Vancouver, Canada. Their original line-up consists of Ian Wood (bass), Cody Bobier (guitar), and Brian McConaughy (drums). Pulling influences from acid/kosmiche rock of the late 60s and 70s, heavy metal, and punk they pull a hard fusion and fuckin' intensity and doom.
