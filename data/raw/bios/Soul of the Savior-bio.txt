Soul of the Savior was a Christian Death Metal band from Tampa Florida, that existed from 1998 to 2002.  Their only release was 4 songs with Dead records, on a compilation entitled "Tools of the Trade." <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Soul+of+the+Savior">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
