Julia Sheer is an American pop/country singer and songwriter. She began her career on Youtube, posting several covers of existing songs, including covers of Taylor Swift, Bruno Mars, and Lady Antebellum, as well as writing and singing her own songs. She often collaborates with Tyler Ward. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Julia+Sheer">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
