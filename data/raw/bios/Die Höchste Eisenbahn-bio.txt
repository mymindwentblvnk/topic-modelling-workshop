Die Höchste Eisenbahn is a German indie band from Berlin created by the songwriters Francesco Wilking and Moritz Krämer in 2011. Later they were joined by Max Schröder (known as one part of 'Olli Schulz und der Hund Marie') and Felix Weigt. As of 2021, they released three albums and zwei EPs. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Die+H%C3%B6chste+Eisenbahn">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
