There are three bands with the name Inversus. 1) Inversus Is a Portuguese hiphop artist 2) Inversus is a Brazilian rock band and 3) Inversus is a Ukrainian gothic metal band from Kyiv.

2) Inversus is a Brazilian rock band. 

Inversus, entre outras coisas, é uma banda de Rock formada por quatro jovens cariocas que curtem desde o mais pop dos rocks até o mais rock dos pops. Em 2006 deu seu primeiro sinal de vida gravando e lançando de forma totalmente independente um EP chamado "Respirando Entre Palavras". O disco traz quatro faixas que, ainda que distintas entre si, traçam bem o perfil do grupo e já dão uma idéia do que está por vir.

Inversus consist of:
 * Gustavo Costa - voz e guitarra
 * Fabrício Oliveira - guitarra e voz
 * Diogo Gomes - bateria
 * Allan Karlus - baixo

MySpace: http://www.myspace.com/inversusmusic
Fotolog: http://www.fotolog.com/inversus


3) InVersus is a doom/gothic metal band from Kyiv, Ukraine. They have released only a demo in 2006 but they're going to sign a deal with local major label soon and release a full-length entitled The First Lightning (already recorded) in nearest time. The sound/style of the new album(as the band members said) is very diverse comparing to the demo released in 2006. An eye should be kept on them. 

InVersus consist of:
 * Natalya "Bast" Kramorenko - voice
 * Ruslan Drozd - guitar
 * Alexandr "Alein" Aine - keys
 * Dmitriy Oleynik - bass-guitar
 * Yulia Zemlyanaya - flute
 * Igor "CacoDem0n" Grabin - drums

Their homepage is: www.inversus.kiev.ua
