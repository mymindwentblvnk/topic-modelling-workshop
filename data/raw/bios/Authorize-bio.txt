Swedish death metal from Söderala. They released a CD on Putrefacion records in the early 90s. Before the CD they also realeased a demo under the name Morbid fear. Back then guitarist Larsa was singing. Later they brought in punkrocker Bizzon on vocals and then also became more "deadly" than Morbid fear. Back then they were more deaththrash.





Bizzon: Vocals
Micke: Drums
Larsa: Guitar (vocals on the demo)
Jörgen: Guitar
Putte: Bass
