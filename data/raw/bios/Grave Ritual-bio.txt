an old school death metal band from Alabama, US that was formed in 2009. As of now their discography consists of a self-titled demo tape released in 2009 with Skeleton Plague records and a full length "Euphoric Hymns From the Altar of Death" released in late 2010 with Dark Descent records (vinyl version) and Razorback records (CD version).

Formerly known as Meathole Infection, GRAVE RITUAL play Death Metal with an emphasis on catchy song writing, dark passages, doom-laden riffs, and a true appreciation for Impetigo, Autopsy, and Incantation, while mixing in their own sound.
