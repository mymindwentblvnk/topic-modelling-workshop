The band formed in 2006 as a project of Hannes. After experimenting with members of PUTRIDITY and EGEMONY, a lasting partnership was formed... in 2008 when vocalist Bazza joined the band.

The pair entered Magostudio in Trento at the end of 2010 to record their debut album, "Deification Of The Grotesque" <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Indecent+Excision">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
