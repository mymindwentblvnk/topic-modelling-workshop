When Blood Burns is a deathcore band based in Brussels, BE, formed in 2006. The band took it's inspiration from the European and American metalcore-scene, and spices up it's music with some thrash- and death metal.

Their current line-up: 

Fabian Bauwens: Shouts
Gaeten Ciselet: Screams & Growls
Benjamin Roulleaux: Guitar
Brian John Reyes: Guitar
Gael Buggenhout: Bass
Jonathan Hanssens: Drums
