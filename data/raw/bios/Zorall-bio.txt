Zorall is a hard rock band formed in 2002 in Budapest, Hungary. They play covers of famous Hungarian songs from the sixties and seventies mainly, mixed with riffs from famous songs, like Walk from Pantera or Seek and Destroy from Metallica.

Members:
Zsolt 'Szasza' Szendrey - vocals (Sex Action)
Attila Barbaró - guitar (Junkies)
László 'Kicsi' Weisz - guitar (Kalapács)
László 'Hangya' Hangyássy - bass(ex-Sing Sing)
Zoltán 'Fenegyerek' Schvéger - drums (Junkies, ex-Mex)
Bálint Bonyhádi - drums (ex-Junkies)
Boros 'Pöpi'  Péter - drums
Albums:
2003 Nem csak a húszéveseké a világ
2005 Randalíra
2006 Live!
2007 Flöss
2010 Cirkusz Világszám
