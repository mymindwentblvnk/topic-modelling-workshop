aeons end is a four-piece post metal project based in Rostock, Germany found in 2015.
 
Their songs combine memorable melodies with impulsive grooves and offer a variety of profound, melancholic, depressed and sometimes promising vibes.
 
The band has released their debut EP "IMAGO" on September 15th 2016.
