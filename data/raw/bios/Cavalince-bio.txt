Forming in 2002, Cavalince is a melodic death metal/metalcore band with electronics and subtle pop influences in the vein of Raunchy. They have three releases in the form of EPs: On the Edge of Madness (2006), Embryonic (2007) and Dead Reflection (2008). As a band they are not currently active but there hasn't been a known announcement regarding a break up. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Cavalince">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
