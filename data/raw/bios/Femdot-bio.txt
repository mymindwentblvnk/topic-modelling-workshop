Born Femi Adigun in Evanston, Femdot is a rapper based out of Chicago. He has established himself as a front runner for the next wave of Hip-Hop royalty coming out of the city.

Fem separates himself with natural display of lyrical ability that is only matched by his eccentric and charismatic delivery. A true student of game, Femdot gives offthe feeling of a seasoned veteran despite his rather young age.

He has been featured in publications across all mediums such as , XXL, Chicago Tribune, Pigeons and Planes and has partnered with Red Bull's Sound Select Program.
