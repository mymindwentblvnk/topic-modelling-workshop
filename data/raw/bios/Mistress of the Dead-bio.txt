Mistress of the Dead is a funeral doom band from the Czech Republic which was formed in 2004 by it's only member Vlad Cristea Vales. The band has quite a large discography consisting of five albums, two splits, a compilation and eleven demos. 

All albums were released as demo on CD-R prior to their official release on CD.
