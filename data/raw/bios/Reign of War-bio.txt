Reign of War is a Dutch death metal band formed in 2008 by Nick Mastenbroek (bass), Jan-Willem Evenblij (drums) and Bruno Bakker (guitar). 

Current members:
Zoran van de Noort - Vocals
Bruno Bakker - Guitar
Eric Rijckaert - Guitar
Nicky Mastenbroek - Bass
Jan-Willem Evenblij - Drums
