Wes Period is an American artist, rapper, and producer from La Habra, California. He began his career working with artists such as Ye Ali and Tommy Genesis. In 2017, Period was discovered by songwriter Justin Tranter via an Instagram Stories post. He is currently signed to Interscope Records. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Wes+Period">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
