LUST FOR DEATH is a French Crust/Metal band formed in September 2011.
http://lustfordeath.free.fr/
https://www.facebook.com/LustForDeathstench

So far, they have released a demo (2011), a 7'' split with Black Code (2013) and an LP, Dust, (2014).
