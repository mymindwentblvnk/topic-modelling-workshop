Heruka is a black metal project from Italy (Erice, Sicily) formed in 2000.  

Current line-up: 
Adranor - Lyrics, Vocals, Guitar, Bass, Drum Programming, Keyboards (Throne of Molok, Legion of Darkness) 

Former/past members: 
Valdemar - Vocals, Guitar
Moha - Session Guitar (on "Leggenda") 

Discography:
Doxa Epicurea Demo, 2001  
Leggenda EP, 2005
