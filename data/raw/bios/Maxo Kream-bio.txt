Emekwanem Ogugua Biosah Jr. (born March 29, 1990), better known by his stage name Maxo Kream, is an American rapper and record producer from Alief, Texas. He has released four mixtapes, Retro Card (2012), Quicc Strikes (2013), #Maxo187 (2015), and The Persona Tape (2016). His debut album "Punken" was released in early 2018. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Maxo+Kream">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
