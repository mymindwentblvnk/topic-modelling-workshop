Anthropic is a grindcore band from Buffalo, NY.  They formed in the year 2016.  They have one demo, two EP's, two splits and one full-length album.  Their current lineup consists of Chris Hull (vocals), Norm Ventura (guitars), Russ Martin (bass) and Jim Santillo (drums).  Brian Pattison (guitars) was a part of the current lineup, but he died of COVID-19 and pneumonia on November 28, 2020.  Sean Smith (bass) was in Anthropic at an unknown point of time and left the band in 2017.  They are not currently signed to a label.  For their music and other resources, go here:
