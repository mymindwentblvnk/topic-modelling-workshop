Path Of Desolation is a Melodic Death Metal band from Lausanne in Switzerland. We are heavily influenced by In Flames, Insomnium and many others. They music is heavy, striking and effective but at the same time melodic. The band is made up of 6 musicians and the first album will be out during the summer 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Path+of+Desolation">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
