Wehrwolf is a NS Black/Thrash Metal band from California, USA. Formed in 2007, the band has released one demo and a split thus far.

It is not to be confused with Wehrwolfe, also from the US, which is an entirely different artist.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Wehrwolf">Read more on Last.fm</a>. User-contributed text is available under the Creative Commons By-SA License; additional terms may apply.
