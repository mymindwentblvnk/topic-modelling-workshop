a death metal band from Finland that was formed in 1992. They released three demos, "A Tomb of Sanity" in 1992, "Season of Sorrows" in 1994 and a Promo tape in 1995. They later changed their name to Ravensfall and then Cryhavoc.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Preprophecy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
