Ultar is a five-piece band from the heart of Siberia, Krasnoyarsk town, that is placed amidst the howling woods and red mountains. Founded in 2011 under another name (Deafknife) as a post-black metal act, the band was reborn in 2016 with a new name, new approach and epicly atmospheric sound.
