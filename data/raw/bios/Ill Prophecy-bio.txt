The first band of german singer Michael Kiske before he joined Helloween. In 1986 they recorded a 5-Track demo. 2 Songs were later re-recorded by Helloween: `A Little Time` and `You'll Always Walk Alone`. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ill+Prophecy">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
