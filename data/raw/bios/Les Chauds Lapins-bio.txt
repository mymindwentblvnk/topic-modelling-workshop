Brooklyn-based Les Chauds Lapins sing 1920s-40s French covers popularized by, among others, Edith Piaf, Mistinguett, Lucienne Boyer, and Charles Trenet. The songs are all sung in French and are exclusively about Love.

Primary band members include: Meg Reichardt on banjo uke, guitar and vocals and Kurt Hoffman also on banjo uke, clarinet and vocals.
