EERA is the nom de plume of Norwegian (via London) singer-songwriter Anna Lena Bruland. Her eponymously titled debut E.P. recorded with Nick Rayner (Farewell J.R.) at his home studio in Cambridge was released on 4 March 2016 via House Arrest Recs.

Eera is also the name of a producer based in Los Angeles, California who has worked with artists such as Bladee, 16yrold, and Jay Critch. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Eera">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
