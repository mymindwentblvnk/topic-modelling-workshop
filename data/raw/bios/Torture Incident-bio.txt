Torture Incident was a Grindcore band from Malaysia that formed in 1994.

Releases include:

Paranormal Activities (Demo)- 1996  
The Deadly Efficiency Of Napalm- 2001  
Smash Hits / Voice Your Disgust (Split)- 2002
Durian is Food Grind is Good (Split)- 2003
The Seven Gates Of Hell (Split)- 2004 
Split Organ (Split)- 2005
Agathocles / Torture Incident (Split)- 2007
