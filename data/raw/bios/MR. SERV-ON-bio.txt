Mr. Serv-On is a rapper from New Orleans who was formerly signed to Master P's No Limit Records. While he was signed to No Limit he released Life Insurance (1997) and Da Next Level (1999) - shortly after the release of Da Next Level he left No Limit to various other independent record labels and came out with War Is Me Pt.1: Battle Decisions (2000), Take-A-Sip (2001), No More Questions (2003), and his most recent and self-proclaimed last album, Life Insurance 2: Heartmuzik (2008). <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/MR.+SERV-ON">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
