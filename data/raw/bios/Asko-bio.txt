[2] Asko (R.I.P) was a raw punk band from South Central Los Angeles. They broke up and formed into Rayos X (Tony) and Tuberculosis (Abe & Gaby). Although short lived during the summer of 2008, they managed to record for the first cassette release of L.A. raw punk label, Silenzio Statico. "Me Das Asko" will be reissued along with Rayos X, Tuberculosis and Poliskitzo cassettes on to a 4-way split vinyl LP. This will be the first vinyl release of Silenzio Statico in collaboration with Discos Enfermos (Spain).  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Asko">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
