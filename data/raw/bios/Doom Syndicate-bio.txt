Doom Syndicate is a multifaceted death metal band that excrete heavy doses of all genres of metal from deathly speed to blackened doom.

Current line-up: 
J.R. - vocals
Scott - lead guitar (ex-Death of Millions, ex-Metagor)
Chip - bass (ex-Baddtripp, ex-Suffusion)
Dan - drums (ex-Murder Majestic)
