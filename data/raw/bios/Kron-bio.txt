Kron is the name of three artists.  
First is an underground Hip Hop artist from Pozega, Serbia. Highly tallented and respected in the underground Hip Hop scene in Serbian west and central part. 
Second is one of the aliases of Reinhard Voigt, who released a number of albums under this name in the late 1990's on Harvest.
