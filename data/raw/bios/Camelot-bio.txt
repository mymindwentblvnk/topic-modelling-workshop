Camelot is the name of several artists. 

1) Camelot is a German eurodance project. It released the singles "Rhythm Of Love" with the voice of Tina H. and "Get In My Life" with Morgane. Both were released under Town records. 

2) Camelot was an italodance project of Italian producer, songwriter and singer Paolo Mantero and singer Silvia Napoli. Released singles were "Ginevra" (2004), "Rise And Fall" (2005) and "Joan Of Arc" (2007).

3) Kamelot before they were Kamelot.
