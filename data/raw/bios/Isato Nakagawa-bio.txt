Isato Nakagawa (中川イサト) is an acoustic guitar instrumentalist born in Osaka, Japan in 1947. Since 1970, he has released 19 albums. He is also a founding member of the group Itsutsu No Akai Fusen (五つの赤い風船) which translates to "Five Red Balloons".
