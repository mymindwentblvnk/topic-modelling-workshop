The Von Bondies were an alternative rock band which formed in 1997 in Detroit, Michigan, United States. The band's lineup often fluctuated, with the only constant members being  Jason Stollsteimer (vocals, guitar) and Don Blum (drums). Other members of the band included Marcie Bolen (guitar, vocals), Carrie Ann Smith (bass), Leann Banks (bass) and Chrissy Hunt (guitar)

The band released three albums, "Lack of Communication" (2001), "Pawn Shoppe Heart" (2004) and "Love, Hate and Then There's You" (2009) before disbanding in 2011. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Von+Bondies">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
