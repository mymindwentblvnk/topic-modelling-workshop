The Avener is a French Deep House and Electro music producer. He is most famous for "Fade Out Lines", a Deep House rework of "The Fade Out Line", a song by Phoebe Killdeer & The Short Straws. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Avener">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
