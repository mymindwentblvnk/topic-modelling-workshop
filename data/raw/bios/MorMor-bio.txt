MorMor is Canadian musician Seth Nyquist (born 1992). He is based in Toronto and mostly writes, records, and produces his work in his apartment. 

His debut single 'Heaven's Only Wishful' was released in 2018. The song quickly gained 1 million plays on YouTube and over 500,000 on Spotify. 

A second song 'Whatever Comes to Mind' was released on 16 April 2018.
