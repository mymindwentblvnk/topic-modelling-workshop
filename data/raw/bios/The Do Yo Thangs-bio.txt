The Do Yo Thangs is the result of many years of soul searching for songwriter/drummer/singer Hugh Rabinovici and his right- hand-lady, vocalist Audrey Powne (Au Dre). The two met some years ago in the drab corridors of jazz school and bonded over a shared love of Erykah Badu and a desire to spread their creative wings.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/The+Do+Yo+Thangs">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
