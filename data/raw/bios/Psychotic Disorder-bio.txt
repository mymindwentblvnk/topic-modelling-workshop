Psychotic Disorder are a 5 piece Metalcore act from Northern Ireland, United Kingdom; formed in late 2009 by Micky Nicholl (Guitar), David Patton (Drums) and Ash Watson (Vocals). After months of writing, and the recruitment of Neil Anderson (Guitar) and Damon Doherty (Bass), the band are set to record their debut EP 

Their music is a fusion of delicately composed melodic sections combined with elements of progressive thrash metal. The band have set their ambitions high, and hope to follow in the footsteps of other flourishing British acts such as Sylosis and Rise To Remain, from whom they have drawn inspiration.
