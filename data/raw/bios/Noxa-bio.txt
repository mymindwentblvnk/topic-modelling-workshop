MINCE-CORE band which was formed mid March 2002 by Robin Hutagol a.k.a Oxen (drums) and Ade Himernio Adnis (Guitar). They play straight forward old school GrindCore[ with socio-political, love & hate story, humanity lyrics, anti-war, etc. They started as a coverband, covering artists like Nasum, Looking For An Answer, Agathocles and old Napalm Death, but quickly started creating original material. They released two albums on their own label, "Noxa Records", in August 22nd 2003 "Noxa" and October 1st 2006 "Grind Viruses". The band signed to finnish Stay Heavy Records in 2008, who will e re-releasing Grind Viruses -album with bonus material on 24.6.2008.
