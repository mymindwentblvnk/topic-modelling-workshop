Vrane is Croatian rock band from Požega. So far they released three albums (Roadrockmania, Moje ime je rock'n'roll and Anđeli i demoni) and compilation of greatest hits (The greatest hits 1997.-2007.).

Band members:
Marko Pavičić - vocals
Željko Mekić Delić - guitar
Renato Pok - bass
Ivan Ivanković - guitar
