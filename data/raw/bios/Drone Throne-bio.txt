Formed in the summer of 08, a 3-piece stoner metal band originating in Gilbert, AZ. The band also includes additional influences from other varying genres (punk, surf, psychadelic rock,...). Drone Throne includes members of the bands Slut Sister & The Prosthetics. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Drone+Throne">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
