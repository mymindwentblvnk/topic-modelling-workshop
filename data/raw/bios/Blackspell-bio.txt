Blackspell is the first Black Metal band from Syria . The band started in late 2008 by Demon of Darkness as a one man project . The sound of the band has changed from Raw Black Metal to Ambient Black Metal and now the band plays Melodic Extreme Metal with influences from other styles/genres  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Blackspell">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
