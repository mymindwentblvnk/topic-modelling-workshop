Luke Buck is a multi-instrumental musician living in Brooklyn with his two dogs.  His influences include Radiohead, Counting Crows, Weezer, Ben Folds, Muse, Norah Jones, and The Cure.  He released his first solo EP "Dark Matter" on Aug. 31st, 2016. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Luke+Buck">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
