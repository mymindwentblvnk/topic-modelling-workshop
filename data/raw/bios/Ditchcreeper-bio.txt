Ditchcreeper is a Southampton based death metal side band set up in 2006 by Iain Muir
The band played a small number of Local shows to warm up in late 2008 before playing DEATHFEAST OPEN AIR 2009, then toured with Malevolent Creation and Vomitory through Europe to aid the release of their album ROTTING REPUGNANCY on Pathologically Explicit Recordings, the album featured artwork by Vincent Locke (Cannibal Corpse album covers etc) <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ditchcreeper">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
