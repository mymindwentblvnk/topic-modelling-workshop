As the mist rolls through the mountains of Vancouver Island, a great thunder tears its way through the valley. It's not the crushing justice of Thor's hammer, it's the sound of local sludge masters Mendozza. A three piece whose roots cover aspects of many styles of heavy music from the groove of 70's Black Sabbath to the brutal power of the Melvins. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Mendozza">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
