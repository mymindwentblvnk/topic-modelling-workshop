Horde Of Worms was formed in Islington, Ontario, Canada in the spring of 1996 by Alexander Erhardt and Brent Assoun with the intent of writing, recording and releasing the most extreme and original music possible. Horde Of Worms have released three full length albums and two EPs on the Bloodbucket Productions label and have detonated live many times over, leaving a trail of corpses their wake. Horde Of Worms is one of the most extreme bands ever from Canada.
