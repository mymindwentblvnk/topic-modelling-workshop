Lost Brethren is a Technical Death Metal band from Peterborough - Cambridgeshire, England. Members: Adam Brown - Vocals, Adrien Crozet - Guitars, Anthony Deane - Guitars, Bass and Drums. The band are a Sci-fi inspired Technical Death Metal outfit. They originally started in 2011 as an online solo project by Anthony Deane and was later joined by vocalist Adam Brown and lead-guitarist Adrien Crozet. In April 2013 the band digitally released their first instrumental recording 'The Forgotten Realm', the following year they dropped the album 'Subatomic Worlds', then they released in 2016 another instrumental record called 'Cosmological Constant'. Their latest album 'The Abduction' - which is the 1st with vocals - has been released at late 2019 through Grindscene Records.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lost+Brethren">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
