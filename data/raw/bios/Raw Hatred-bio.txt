There are two bands named Raw Hatred.

1) Raw black metal band from Fairfax, Virginia/USA.

They split up shortly after the release of the split with Misanthropy

2) Anti-gay metal band who appeared on the Fetch the Rope compilation "Smashing Rainbows".
