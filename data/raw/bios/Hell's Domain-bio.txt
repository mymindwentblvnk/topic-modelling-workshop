Originally formed in 2007 by bassist Lars Knudsen and guitarist Bjørn Bihlet, Hell’s Domain was joined by Anders Gyldenøhr on drums and Alex Clausen on vocals, and the band completed its lineup in 2011 with the addition of guitarist, Andreas Schubert.

Hell’s Domain are:
 
Alex Clausen – Vocals
Andreas Schubert – Guitars
Lars Knudsen – Bass
Anders Gyldenøhr – Drums
Bjørn Bihlet – Guitars
