Zofos is a one-man black metal band from Greece formed in 2007 by Athalwolf of Wolfnacht. So far a full-length and an EP have been released. Lyrical themes deal with nature and gods.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Zofos">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
