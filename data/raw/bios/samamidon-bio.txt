Samamidon is a collaborative effort between Sam Amidon and Thomas Bartlett (Doveman). Together they released one album called "But This Chicken Proved Falsehearted". Sam Amidon later released the albums "All Is Well" and "I See The Sign" under his own name.
Both albums to date are modern interpretations of traditional folk songs (and one by Tears For Fears for good measure.)  
Sam was born in Brattleboro, Vermont, June 3, 1981and currently lives near New York City.
