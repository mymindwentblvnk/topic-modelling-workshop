Moghul are a four piece doom/sludge band from Birmingham UK, formed in 2011 they are set to release a debut EP in 2012. 

Drawing from a wide range of influences their music can most closely be likened to bands such as neurosis, electric wizard, mastodon, kylesa and q.o.t.s.a  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/moghul">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
