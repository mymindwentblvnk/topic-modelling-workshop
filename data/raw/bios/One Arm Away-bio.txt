One Arm Away is a Brazilian Heavy Metal/ Thrash Metal formed by musicians from well known bands in Brazil and World such as Angra, Korzus an John Wayne.
The name of the band represents how near our deaths are to us. In the sense of making life worth while it doesn't touch us for it is at arms length. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/One+Arm+Away">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
