Confessions of Obscurity is a death metal / grindcore band from Belgium. Formed in 1994, they are probably most known for releasing a split 7 inch with Dutch goregrind legends, Last Days of Humanity. The current status of the band is unknown. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/CONFESSIONS+OF+OBSCURITY">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
