Nii Ayitey Ajin Adamafio, also known as Recognize Ali, is a Ghanaian underground hip-hop MC from Accra, Republic of Ghana. He made his first appearance guest appearing on an E.L. track titled ''Switch Up" off the BAR II album. Ali is a Muslim and received International recognition following his collaboration with Philadelphia-based MC and Jedi Mind Tricks frontman Vinnie Paz on their track 'Holy War'. He has since released a slew of albums and mixtapes that have been embraced by the global hip-hop community. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Recognize+Ali">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
