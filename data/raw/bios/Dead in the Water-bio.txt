Dead In The Water is a down-tempo metal band from Finland. They formed in 2000 and have released one 7" EP and one 10"/MCD minialbum in 2002 and 2003, respectively. During 2006 they recorded their debut album and got signed by Firedoom Music. The album named "Echoes... in the ruins" was released wolrdwide in may 2007.
