Started 1984 in Norsjö/Västerbotten when they was 14 years old. Influenced by NWOBHM and bands as Exciter and Nasty Savage. In the beginning they played Heavy Metal, later Speed/Thrash Metal. Split-up in 1989.
Recorded a couple of live and rehearsal demos, and a live video from 1986 with two originals and four covers including Blood On the Black Flag,Killer(Switzerland).
