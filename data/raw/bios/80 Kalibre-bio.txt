80 Kalibre is a Progressive Heavy Metal band, formed in 2007 in Istanbul, Turkey.

"Kurtul Zindanindan" is their first EP released in 2008. They also have some live performances too.

They have released their new EP, "Bitmeyen Gösteri", which is available for free, in November 2011.
