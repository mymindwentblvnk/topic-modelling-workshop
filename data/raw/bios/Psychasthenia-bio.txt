a death metal band from Quebec, Canada that was formed in 1991. They released three demos, "Out of Reality" in 1991, "Guilty of Innocence" in 1992 and "Brain Damage from Glue" in 1994. Before changing their name to Psychasthenia, the band was called C-War. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Psychasthenia">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
