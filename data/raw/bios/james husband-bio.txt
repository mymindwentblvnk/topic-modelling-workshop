Multi-instrumentalist James Huggins a.k.a. James Husband plays drums, bass, keyboards and horns for psychedelic indie pop band of Montreal.  

His solo album, Now Listen was available during the Sunlandic Twins tour and includes 14 original songs and covers of What You're Doing and We Can Work it Out.
