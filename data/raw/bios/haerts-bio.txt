Haerts (stylized as HAERTS) is a German indie pop music duo, formed in 2011 in New York. The duo consists of Nini Fabi (vocals) and Ben Gebert (keyboards, guitars).

The wider group has consisted of Fabi andGebert, together with Garrett Ienner, Derek McWilliams and Jonathan Schmidt. Garrett Ienner and Derek McWilliams left the band in 2015.

The duo's first single, Wings, was produced in collaboration with Jean-Philip Grobler (aka St. Lucia). Haerts' debut self-titled album, Haerts, was released in late 2014.
