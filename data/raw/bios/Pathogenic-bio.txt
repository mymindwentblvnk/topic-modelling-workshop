Pathogenic is a hard working MA metal quintet with an unmistakable sound. Relentlessly heavy, technically proficient and cleverly composed, their music spans the emotive spectrum. With two albums released, and having incessantly gigged the US East Coast, Pathogenic are constantly moving forward. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Pathogenic">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
