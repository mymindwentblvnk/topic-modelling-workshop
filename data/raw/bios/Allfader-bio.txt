Allfader hails from Mo i Rana in Northern Norway, and plays brutal, melodic, dark metal. Allfader is an other name for the Norse god Odin. According to Snorre Sturlason's Edda; Allfader, or Odin, is the greatest of all gods. They are currently on Osmose Productions, where they released their debut album "At Least We Will Die Together" in September 2006. Their latest album shows great development which has enabled them to capture an atmosphere that reminds about the Vikings' gatherings; eating psilocybe mushrooms and drinking mead.  <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Allfader">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
