Born in The Netherlands to Afghan refugee parents, Ferdous, is a musician, singer and songwriter. He writes deeply personal lyrics set to a sonic palette of minimalistic electronics, and alt-R&B.

Ferdous started out in pop duo, Kylne, releasing a self-titled album in 2017. Under his own name he started releasing singles in 2019, followed by two EPs: For My Own Sake in 2021 and Doors in early 2022. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Ferdous">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
