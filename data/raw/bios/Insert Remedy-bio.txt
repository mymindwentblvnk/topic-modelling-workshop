Insert Remedy is a Finnish heavy metal band, founded 2003. It has published a demo and 2 EP's and one full-length album called A State of Constant Change. Homepage of the band is http://www.insertremedy.com. Insert Remedy has also a page at MySpace, check it out at http://www.myspace.com/insertremedy.
