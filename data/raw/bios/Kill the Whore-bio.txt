We are a group of young and talented musicians blah blah blah blah ...........Fuck that shit.Kill The Whore is a Goregrind/Brutal death metal perverted project that was given birth in the Belizean underground metal scene. Originally the project started in the 2011 when Andy" Howler Zombie" Chuc (Lasher Zombie,Flames of apocalypse,Ex lisergia) founded the project with idea of mixing Gore grind,PornoGrind,Slam and brutal death metal. After 1 live show and several lineup changes the band split up.Andy Chuc continued as a one man band and recorded some demos Gore/Porno grind oriented. In the 2013  Brazilian Musician Thiago Santos Cordiero (Never christ,Antropofectus,Crepusculis  Shadow and others) join as guitarist and drum programmer. Kill the Whore officially release there EP "The Art Of Disembowelment" with just 3 Brutal tracks the same year. Kill the whore was the first Belizean Goregrind band ,first in introduceing gore cover art work in Belize  ,Gore themes and brought with them a new level of perversion .In the 2014 Kill the whore release a new full length album "First Stage Of Decomposition" 8 songs of the most heaviest Sound in the Caribbean scene which many have label the style as Slamming PornoGore. In the 2015 kill The Whore participated in a Split name " 5 ways to destroy a cadaver" along with other bands from the world. <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Kill+The+Whore">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
