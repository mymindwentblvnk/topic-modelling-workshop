Lipali is a Polish rock band formed in 2000 by the singer and guitarist  Tomasz Lipnicki after the split of Lipnicki`s previous  band  " Illusion" in 1999.
The band has produced 4 albums including their debut album  Li-pa-li(2000),Pi,Bloo and Trio including  their best known songs such as "Upadam"( Falling Down) and "Barykady". <a href="https://www.last.fm/music/Lipali">Read more on Last.fm</a>.
